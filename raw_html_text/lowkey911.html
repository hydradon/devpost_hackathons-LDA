Inspiration

One day I was at a park, and had to face a crazed individual. I wanted to call the police immediately to avoid danger, but didn't want to call the police in front of him since it might prompt violence. If I could dial 911 in one tap, I could avoid danger.

What it does

Uses Twilio API to send a voice message of name, location, and situation to a number (911 in a real-world scenario).

How we built it

Node.js

Challenges we ran into

Lots!

What we learned

How Twilio works and their API.

What's next for lowkey911

Increasing usability.
