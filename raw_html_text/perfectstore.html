Inspiration

to connect the community and technology. help people to start up the business with technology and data analysis.

What it does

It is a web application (HTML, CSS, and JavaScript) to find the perfect store for people to start the business

How we built it

by calling the google map api we collect the information, and by the back-end algorithm we sort the rent, rating, and competition in that specific business. And report the final perfect address to the customers.
