MemoryPlus

An app for Alzheimer patients.

Features

--- ChatBot - The app is completely automated by a chatbot which acts as a guide for the patient during his memory loss. --- Face Recognition - A features that enable the user to recognize faces that he/she might know. --- Emergency Call - Uses Agora RTC to make calls to their relatives/friends. --- Notes - Has a feature called "My Notes" that lets the patient write stuff he might want to remember later.
