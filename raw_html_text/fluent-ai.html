Inspiration

Many language apps on the market today do not take into account the stage in the user's learning in which they are considered to be intermediate. Our app not only allows for beginner learning, but also provides an enhanced learning environment for intermediate and advance students.

What it does

Leveraging the power of machine learning, we have designed a product that allows users to have a conversation with an intelligent chat bot/assistant in the language they want to learn. Our assistant will help guide and correct the user's grammar.

How We built it

We divided our tasks between front end and backend development.

Challenges I ran into

Nlp was the most challenging part to implement into our product.

What I learned

There is great potential in voice technology in combination with AI

What's next for Fluent AI
