Inspiration

Everyone loves candy! With Valentine's day around the corner, we wanted to make a Valentine's program that finds your true love... of candy!

What it does

The program gives you two candy options and you keep picking your favorite, until your absolute favorite is shown to you.

How we built it

We used tkinter and Adobe Illustrator.

Challenges we ran into

Coming up with a good sorting algorithm.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Working together! And we love the graphics!

What we learned

That it is important to have a diverse array of talent on a team.

What's next for What the Heart Wants

To eat candy!
