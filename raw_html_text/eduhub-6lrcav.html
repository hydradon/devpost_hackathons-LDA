What it does

EduHub is the place to go when you want to learn something new, even if you don't know what you want to learn.

EduHub compiles resources from all across the web and organizes them by topic, learning style and prerequisites. You'll have no problem finding something interesting and an online resource that teaches it in a way that works for you.

Inspiration

The Internet is full of learning opportunities. Across the web, thousands of topics have been taught and each one in many different ways. There is a growing realization that no two learners are the same and people are searching for technology to cater to each learner's style. EduHub is one of the first big steps in that direction. If someone wants to learn something, they can now compare how it's taught on dozens of sites across the web and choose the one that fits them best.

We also want the site to cater towards naturally curious people. The people who get lost in Wikipedia following blue link after blue link on a quest for new knowledge. The site will be able to recommend new learning opportunities based on what you know and what learning style suits you.

At a high level, we just want to make it easy for people with a drive to learn to find new and high quality material.
