Inspiration

My inspiration for this app was as a medical student at UMKC I found that it would be helpful for patients have a way to know whether or not their wound was infected or not. WoundCare allows for that to be a possibility.

What it does

This app does it through the frontend using Xcode and the backend using CustomVision software

How I built it

Through Xcode

Challenges I ran into

I ran into challenges integrating the CustomVision ML with my actual xcode app.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am proud that the app actually came into fruition.

What I learned

I learned how to use both Xcode and CustomVision.

What's next for WoundCare

Hopefully some clinical trials or some adoption by patients.
