• Farm credity...empowering farmers

Inspiration

So we thought about how we can provide a solution that caters for both financial inclusion, empowerment and providing agro-based business access to financial facilities and the idea of Farmcredity came to mind.

What it does

Basically, farmcredity is an online platform for farmers to access flexible and dynamic loans with no collateral. While most farmers usually request for cashloans, the most important facilities that they actually need are those that have to do with their production processes like storage facilities, equipments, production inputs. Farmcredity aims to bridge that gap by prociding directly the needs to these farmers other than the cash loans. This is also a good business model as it reduces the risks on the part of the creditor
