Inspiration 

As this is our first hackathon, the goal for our team was to learn as much as possible in the time we had, so we spoke with each other, and decided that creating a game would satisfy the back-end interests of some of us, and the front-end interests of the others.

What it does 

It's a game.
You make it through the game, and if you have a positive score, you win, otherwise, you lose.
