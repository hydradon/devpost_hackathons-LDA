Inspiration

Bullet Journals are great to stay organized and inspired, yet having to have a paper book with you is not environmental friendly, nor practical. Yet, when we take a look in the store, no Bullet Journal was really doing all that we wanted, nor fgiving us any type of modularity. On top of that, most of the ones you can find are only on mobile, do not permit free-form handwriting (and thus personalisation that most people love in Bullet Journaling) and lack a lot of options.-As such, to have an equivalent to a physical Bullet Journal, you need multiple apps.

What it does

Currently the app is not finished but is cross-platform thanks to Ionic and has a calendar and free-form writing tool.

How I built it

Using Ionic (Angular).
