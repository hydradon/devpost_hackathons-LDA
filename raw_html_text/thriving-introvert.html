Inspiration

I am an introvert and have always had hard times fitting in a crowd, i realized I can not live alone and have to interact with people as i perform my daily activities. I also run a small business and I have always avoided some tasks because I feel inadequate. Since I am the owner of the business, I can not be on the background.

What it does

Skill shares tips on how an introvert can excel in their activities.

How I built it

I used voiceapps platform to develop the skill.

Challenges I ran into

I would have really wanted to develop the skill using Alexa kit unfortunately I lacked the necessary skills. I settled for voiceapps which is almost similar to voiceflow. Outsourcing the development of the skill is expensive for me at the moment.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Completing the skill. Learning a bit of APL.

What I learned

Content is king. All the ideas I have need excellent content which I am working on. I am interested in coming up with multimodal skill. With excellent content, it will be easy to partner with agencies since they can do the development as I come up with the content.

What's next for Sy

I would like to come up with an interactive story skill.
