Inspiration

Every college student has had their schedule out of class a complete mess at some point. We want to fix that.

What it does

Based on your class schedule and priorities, the app divides your free time so that there is enough time in your day for school, friends, and everything in between.

How we built it

HTML, CSS, Javascript, and a lot of google searching.

Challenges we ran into

Most of the team has had no web coding experience prior to. Along with specific difficulties like databases and APIs.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We have a functioning, online website.

What we learned

How to code. That making websites is hard, but interesting.

What's next for Prime Time

Go global.
