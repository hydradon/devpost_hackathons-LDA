As high schoolers, we live in an environment where we are subject to learn and devoted to hard work. As important as it is to do so in real life, one must realize that sometimes, you just have to stop and take time to smell the flowers. Our app, connects allows you to do that. Using connect, one makes their own schedule, shares it with their peers, and the app finds intervals where all parties are free to meet up and just enjoy life together. Not only does this allow for friends to communicate and meet but this face to face socialization allows people to feel the human presence, talk about life, and just relax.
