We decided that the media was becoming too influential in politics, so we wrote a program to determine the bias in news articles. We did this by examining the source of articles and extracting their contents from URL or text to analyze the alignment with left-leaning and right-leaning terms. Articles are classified as liberal, neutral, or conservative via logistic regression.
