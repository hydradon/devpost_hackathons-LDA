Cha Cha Real Smooth

The thought came to me when I realized how amazing it would be if people were to have normal conversations with their chat bots and received normal replies had it been a conversation between two people.This is my second hackathon and I really wanted to give one the projects a go.

How I built it

I am using dialogueflow which is connected to my google account. The entire project is run through the google assistance bot.

Challenges I ran into

A few problems I ran into was first over writing an intent, having to change training words so it does not overlap, and forgetting to make an intent.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I mostly proud of the fact that I was able to complete my project. I was also proud of the fact that I was able to quickly get the grasp of using dialogueflow.

What I learned

I learned the operations of dialogueflow and how to create actions and assign already defined actions to different words to signal google.

What's next for Sing along

This project was made as a nice way to lighten up someone's day. Hopefully if anyone feel like enhancing it to make it ten times better that would be awesome.
