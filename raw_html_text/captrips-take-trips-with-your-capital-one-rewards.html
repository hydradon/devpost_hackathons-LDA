Summary

CapTrips is a web application that helps you achieve your travel goals with your Capital One card. By linking your Capital One rewards account with us, we help you plan your future vacation and achieve it by earning more rewards points or miles.

===

We wanted to practice our web development skills so we decided to do a web service using Capital One APIs (Nessie and Rewards API)

It's a fairly simple web application. We used Google Firebase for the web hosting and database service and we built the front end using google polymer and nodejs.

We also created a server side node app that kept our database records up to date by pulling from Nessie and Capital One rewards API.
