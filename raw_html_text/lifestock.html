What it does

We’ve partnered up with top firms willing to sell their stock for charities. Since you’re trying to improve society, you can avoid paying capital gains taxes on the stock gains and have the option to wave the brokerage fee. Every time you make a purchase, we’ll round it up to the nearest dollar. Watch your spare change accumulate, invest in your favorite company’s stock, and donate to a charity of your choice. Spend away!
