Inspiration

Lisbon's touristic growth.

What it does

The customer can choose among a set of activities according to their preferences in order to avoid wasting time in queles and having the best of what Portugal offers.

How we built it

We used TMForum, Huawei and CML APIs for the backend and a webapp for the frontend. Here are the APIs:

  • TMF Party Mgmt
  • TMF Product Catalog
  • TMF Product Inventory
  • Huawei Customer
  • Huawei SMS
  • Huawei Querying Balance
  • Huawei Deducting Balance
  • Huawei Product Mgmt
  • Huawei Performing Payment
  • Open Data Lisbon

Challenges we ran into

  • There was no documentation about filtering and paging for the Lisbon City Open APIs;
  • Provide a service having 3rd Party Providers;
  • Use plataforms that made available all required functions to support our application;

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Managed to deliver a webapp using three different Open API partners. Designed a beautiful PoC webapp with a very tight deadline.

What we learned

How to integrate with the different Open APIs; Good knowledge about using TM Forum and Huwaei APIs; Good supporting documentation of TM Forum and Huawei APIs; A large amount of Open APIs covering the most important business processes.

What's next for TourHere@Lisbon

To use the following APIs:

  • TMF Product Order
  • TMF On-boarding
  • TMF Billing Mgmt Introduce analytics display in the webapp.
