For some types of the jobs like in manufacturing shaking of hands just a little might not create much damage, while in jobs like brain or heart surgery shaking hands can have negative impacts in the quality of the work. Even though the professionals are qualified for their jobs due to health issues their performance might not be as expected where Shaky will report the issue.

Shaky is a wristband which collects motion data, and predicts if the professional is able to perform the assigned task according the quality standards of the domain.

Texas Instruments SimpleLink dual-band CC1350 is used for motion data collection, IBM Blumix as an IoT Cloud platform, IBM data science experience to analyse the collected data, predictive analyses, and Node-RED for programming the flow of the processes.
