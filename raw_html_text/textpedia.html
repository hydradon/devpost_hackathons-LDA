What is Textpedia: Textpedia is text-based language tool with a purpose to bring the learning experience more accessible to everyone.

Target Users:

  • Users who are traveling or learning a different language.
  • Users who are trying to expand their vocabularies.
  • Users without smartphones.
  • Users with limited cellular data plan.

Benefits of Textpedia:

  • low processing power alternative.
  • eliminates the hassle app installation.
  • not require any internet connection.
  • quick and minimal interactions to obtain information
  • integrates well with messaging apps, you do not need to leave the app.

Future Iterations: This tool can expand to a multifunctional help line service.
