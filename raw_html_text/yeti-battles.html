Inspiration

The inspiration came out of Yeti Butts, the coolers they sell in US and girls posing with them . So we came up with Yeti's the mythical creatures, the workmen/women that surely exist in the North pole. In our game they drive with snowmobiles on a melting ice field, how cool is that!

More about The Story: The Yetis, the workers of the North have been hiding for centuries from the human race in the North pole. They live in a huge ass village Magadan Oblast near the Russian border where they harvest (what they can) and hunt together. One day a Yeti villager called Oblast Magadan VMDCLXV of Magadan Oblast lost it's way and accidentally found an abandoned soviet union army base. He ran away home informing about his findings but the oldest of the village Oblast Magadan X didn't believe him so Oblast Magadan VMDCLXV escaped and ran back with his younger brother to the soviet union army base and they rode back with snowmobiles and the bags full of groundbreaking technology. A snowstorm broke.. the younger brother Oblast Magadan JR. never made it back to the village. After that Oblast Magadan has honored his brother's death with Snowmobile YETI BATTLES.

What it does

1vs1 casual local/online game where your goal is to avoid hitting any obstacles that come in your way and eventually hit your enemy's snowmobile with greater speed that the enemy holds. As time pasts avoiding obstacles and trying to hit your enemy the ice rink melts and shrinks and your goal is to finish the game before the ice totally melts.
