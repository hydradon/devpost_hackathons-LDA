Inspiration

Recent events have made people more politically involved and active, but it is still hard for many people to get the information they need in order to vote on the person they think is right. We wanted to create an easy to read and easy to understand view of information about representatives.

What it does

It gives information about representatives in terms of office, party, contact information, etc. in a more bite sized way so that it is easier for the average citizen to understand.

How we built it

We made the app in Android Studio.

Challenges we ran into

The question of how local or federal we go with the search results for politicians.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Making an easy to digest app that allows one to click and scroll through politicians.

What we learned

We have learned effective ways to use APIs in our app.

What's next for Polytic

We want to add more location based info that will help people around the nation.
