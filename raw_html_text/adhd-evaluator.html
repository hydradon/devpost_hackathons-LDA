Inspiration

Several friends and family members of our team are related to people who are victims of this condition. We want to help these people and others who struggle as well.

What it does

Our website has several purposes. Firstly, it informs users about ADHD. With a detailed home page and about page, users can learn in great depth the reality of ADHD. Secondly, users can take an adaptive test to determine whether or not they suffer from this condition. This test will provide detailed scientific results, and steps for the user to take. Our third part is the game. The purpose of this game is to help users ease their symptoms of ADHD through focus activities.
