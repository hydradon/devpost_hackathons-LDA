Inspiration

I have always enjoyed creating video games, but have never had the chance to create anything using VR. Seeing that this hack-a-thon had a gear VR available, My friend Aaron and I got to work.

What it does

Use the gear VR to shoot as many targets as you can in 60 seconds. The targets will bounce around and not want to be hit.

How we built it

Using Unity.

Challenges we ran into

Creating a polished game in under 24h.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The game is extremely fun to play.

What we learned

How to use VR and Unity.

What's next for High Noon VR

May be published to Google Play.
