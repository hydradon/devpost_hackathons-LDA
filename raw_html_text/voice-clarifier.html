Being disabled is often synonymous to being more dependent on others, loss of independence / freedom Something that motivated us was to us AI to help people regain part of their lost independence. This is why we are trying to make a voice clarifier for people who have difficulty articulating, such as to make them independent and able to make voice calls without the help of someone else, something important in the 21st century where a lot of services are only accessible by phone.
