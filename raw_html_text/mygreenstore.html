Inspiration

Make people healthier enabling them to sell their homemade green products to people that lives nearby.

What it does

Builds a bridge between small local vegetables producers and consumers.

How we built it

We are a team of three specialists in javascript technologies, we split into tasks that include an API a Mobile application and a landing page.

Challenges we ran into

Create a fully functional app in 48 hours with only three people. We didn’t know each other, but that wasn’t a problem and the team accomplished the task working together and well integrated

Accomplishments that we're proud of

API ready to consume. Mobile app ready for use. Good team work.

What we learned

Coding really has no borders, like Ilya says.

What's next for MyGreenStore

Pay your orders in app. Enable product trades
