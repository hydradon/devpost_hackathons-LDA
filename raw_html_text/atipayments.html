Inspiration

Lack of electronic payment methods for public transportation methods in Puerto Rico.

What it does

Allows users to manage their cards an their cards's balances for public transportation usage.

How we built it

Using firebase as backend and Ionic as frontend we developed a simple mobile app for users to manage their cards.

Challenges we ran into

Time constraints, sleep...

Accomplishments that we're proud of

App login with phone number is working well.

What we learned

How to implement firebase phone logins for mobile apps.

What's next for ATIpayments

Adding support for student discounts, completing development, and selling an accessible service to ATI to improve customer experience and improve public transportation usage.
