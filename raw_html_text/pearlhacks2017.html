Inspiration

We wanted to devise a way to encourage people to make small changes in their everyday lives that would help combat climate change.

What it does

Our android app quizzes the user on their consumption of food, gas, and utilities and then provides a score that the user can use as a baseline to try to beat in the future. Our EcoFox serves as a mascot that reacts to the user's score (good = happy, bad = sad).
