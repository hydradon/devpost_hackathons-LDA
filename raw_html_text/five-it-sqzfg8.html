Our world is progressively growing more creative with ways to meet potential love interests but they often come with lots of anxiety, stress, and pressure to impress. We've created an app to platonically introduce strangers while keeping the environment low-stress by having two users engage in an ice-breaking high-five.

The app matches two users who are within 50 meters of each other, own PebbleTime Smartwatches, and are in "Find Match" mode with the hopes of sparking enjoyable conversation. Using PebbleTime smartwatches, the energy behind each high five will be scored and that score gets added onto each user's personal score.
