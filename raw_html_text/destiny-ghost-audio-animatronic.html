Inspiration

I fell in love with the destiny franchise and the ghost companion. I wanted to get my own ghost but ghosts on the market where non-moving “paper weights.” I wanted a ghost that was more life like; enter my project that I have been building during profhacks 2019

What it does

This audio animatronic produces movement and audio, similar to how the game presents it.

How we built it

What we learned

Arduino can Tx to multiple Rx at the same time. For Arduino to successfully Rx, only one device can Tx at a time.

What's next for Destiny Ghost Audio Animatronic

Increased range of motion and voice command interactions between the ghost and a user.
