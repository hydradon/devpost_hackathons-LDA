Lucid - Productive Sleep

Lucid is a revolutionary tool that integrates state of the art machine learning with the classic dream journal. Lucid enables anyone to start tracking their dreams & analyzes the content of their dreams to determine its emotional content. Further, Lucid makes the 8 hours we spend sleeping each night productive & useful.

Inspiration

Tons of people keep dream journals, but let's face it - they're old hat. Writing down your dreams everyday take up time & is inefficient. We used machine learning and text to speech to create an online, easy to use dream journal. Furthermore, we used sentiment analysis to infer emotions from dreams. Using Lucid, people can glean insights into their overall emotional health from their dreams.
