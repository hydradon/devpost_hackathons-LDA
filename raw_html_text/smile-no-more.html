Inspiration

The first step in order for robots to take over the world is by taking over social media.

What it does

This android app makes it so that you cannot take photos of you or anyone smiling.

How I built it

I used Clarifai API v2 to create a model of sad people. When a picture is taken it is upload to the server. The flask app takes the photo and makes a call to the api. Then the response is read by the android app.

Challenges I ran into

Had problems with OpenCV but managed to get the Clarifai API working

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Made a whole app by myself in one day.
