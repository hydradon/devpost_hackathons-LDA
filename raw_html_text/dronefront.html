It was 3AM when we decided to actually compete. We wanted to impress PROS by utilizing the latest technologies available today! We used React, Redux, Nodejs, and Azure.

The goal of this project was to take the user's interest/music and determine where to go locally or globally (Google). From there, it would get the information from Google and Facebook preferences and grab personalized bundles of things to purchase related to the location they were going to go (Amazon).
