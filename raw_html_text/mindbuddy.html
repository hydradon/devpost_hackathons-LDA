• MindBuddy- Bringing Infusionsoft and MINDBODY together.

Inspiration

Health and wellness (gym/studio/spa/salon) small business owners who use MINDBODY to run their day-to-day operations have struggled to use marketing automation to improve and grow their businesses because they were stuck using less than ideal methods to import/export data between MINDBODY and Infusionsoft.

What it does

MindBuddy is a two-way bridge between Infusionsoft and MINDBODY that updates and bridges the most recent contact information, purchases, products, classes, and attendance.

What's next for MindBuddy

We will continue to enhance the integration, bring new ways for the two systems to exchange data and empower the owner to automate more of their marketing and improve their customer experience.
