Wine is complicated. How do you know what to buy? So many regions/varietals/vintages/reserves/wineries/vineyards/soils and so on...

Wouldn't it be great if you tell your google assistant what country, colour, varietal you like, how much you want to spend, and received a great suggestion based on reviews by wine snob who know a lot more about wine than you? Well this day has come! Just use WineSuggest (Patent maybe. Maybe)

Give your google assistant your preferred country, colour, and how much you want to spend, and the WineSuggest will give you a wine suggestion based on value, reviews, or cost.
