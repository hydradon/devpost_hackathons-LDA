Inspiration

Inspired by Apps that let you find music by tapping the beat like Shazam!

What it does

Music Head is simple - it recognizes the music you want and plays it for you. By using Bose AR, Music Head lets the user use only their head to make decisions. Our software identifies the user's intent for music and all other actions like search and provides them with relevant real time decisions.

How I built it

Unity using the Bose AR API and the other partner APIs Music from UMG

Challenges I ran into

Detecting and matching BPM is harder than it seems

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Publishing for iOS and Android

What I learned

Matching BPMs is a real challenge.

What's next for MUSIC HEAD

Music Head lets you find and choose your music by using only you head gestures. We'll integrate other services and APIs
