Inspiration: As a college student in Kent, Ohio that walks to class, I have encountered one major problem. Rain. Therefore I created a jacket that ensures you always have an umbrella with you.

What it does: Jacket that holds a piece umbrella to ensure you are never caught in the rain unprepared again. App also included that allows users to track the rain and purchase more product.

How I built it: I sewed.

Challenges I ran into: Tech partner dropped out.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of: Did it all by myself!

What I learned: You can learn anything with an open mind.

What's next for Covered: Test it out on campus.
