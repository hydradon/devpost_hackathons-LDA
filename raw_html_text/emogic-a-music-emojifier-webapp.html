Inspiration

We were originally inspired by the use of emojis to create mosaic art from photos. We thought it was interesting how emojis are often used in modern communication to replace words. We wanted to explore how emojis could be used to visualize the meanings of songs in a fun and interactive way.

What it does

Emojic takes the lyrics in a song, and translates the words into emojis. Then, based on the frequencies in the music's audio profile, it displays these emojis in a visualization that interacts with the beat of the music. The result is a fun and unique visualization of the song through the use of emojis.
