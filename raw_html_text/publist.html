Inspiration

Inspired by the instant and low cost transaction fees of the lightning network and the difficulty of sharing expenses while travelling with friends.

What it does

allows users to follow each other to view privately encrypted shared expenses. Pay lightning invoices instantly.

How I built it

Built using nuxt.js and vuetify. App is statically generated and hosted on netlify

Challenges I ran into

Had issues with group storage

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Proud of functional nature of the app, its shareability and ease of use.

What I learned

I learned how to work with blockstack and the lightning network.

What's next for Lightzing.me

Create an open source custodial lightning wallet that anyone can host to increase trust in the lightning network.
