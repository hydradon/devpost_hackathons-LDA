What it does

Sister Search is an Android app that connects women entrepreneurs to people with similar aspirations and/or experiences. Any user can create a free account and immediately start looking for mentors, mentees, or just peers that are at similar steps in the process to starting their own businesses. The app is meant to build support through community and networking and keep users up-to-date on information relevant to their business interests.
