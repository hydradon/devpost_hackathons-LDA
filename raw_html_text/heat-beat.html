Inspiration

I am sure that most of you guys are familiar with rhythm games. you press the button on your screen when bars on the screen move down and meet the bottom. You can touch the screen in syncronization with the beat. But, this game is so normal and there are so many similar games.

What it does

This is a rhythm game that uses FLIR ONE. It tracks the heat of your fingers. So instead of touching the screen, you place your hand at a certain position in front of the camera. the camera will recognize your hand movement interpreting it as "clicking the button."

How I built it

Use Android, Java, and FLIR ONE(for android).

What's next for Heat Beat!

It is obvious that the thermal cameras can be used in the future mobile game market. we will play game moving the body like wii.
