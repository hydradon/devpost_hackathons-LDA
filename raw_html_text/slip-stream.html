Inspiration

The platform was inspired by the long-standing tensions between content creators and Youtube's method of monetization and advertisement.

What it does

SlipStream aims to provide an open, decentralized streaming service that puts power back in the creator's hands. By hosting videos on decentralized node servers, and connecting users with content through "ledger" servers, SlipStream offers a flexible platform for content creators that aims to be fair. This is furthered by fostering a platform that is run and maintained by a community of content creators.
