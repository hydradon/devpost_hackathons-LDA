Inspiration

The intention of charities is to help. From people, animals, to housing, the purpose of charity is to provide positive impact within a community. However, after donating to charity, you never really know the distribution of funds and how much is going where. We were inspired to make a product that positively impacts society, and tackles this head-on.

What it does

Built on blockchain technology, Pie is your way of knowing how your money donated to charity is spent. You can see the distribution of how the funds are split, your past contributions, and statistics on your contributions. Blockchain guarantees the transparency and immutability of information.
