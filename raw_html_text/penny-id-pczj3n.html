Inspiration

World today has greater connectivity than ever before. Yet a connection of doing good, empathizing and working together as one has friction. Blockchain & Cyptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum & others has brought us a solution to connect peers of the world with a tangible value.

But Cryptocurrencies' has friction on-boarding the peers and Hence penny is here to reduce the friction by providing value to their social media id by linking their wallet address on penny.id

What's next for penny.id

Apart from tipping extensions, predictor markets and bot-fun stuff upcoming. Penny.ID wants to Connect legitimate NGOs and peers around the world can help/contribute to their causes even a penny directly from their social media.

To achieve this, I am humbly looking for friends/partners/donations to keep the server running and provide value to the world.
