Inspiration

We've always promised each other that on co-op we would spend lots of time together. However, each time we try to plan a hangout, we get stuck with decision paralysis and can never decide where to eat.

What it does

This app takes users' locations and preferences into account to present the best restaurant choices for your group.

How we built it

We used a variety of software including html, css, flask, python, google cloud, and google APIs

Challenges we ran into

Many of these tools were new and this was our first time working in full-stack development, so there was lots to learn.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Our beautiful UI and working functionalities. Although connecting the individual components was difficult, we're proud of how far we got

What we learned

We learned many new tools and practices in full stack development

What's next for You Decide

We want to complete the implementation and share it with the world
