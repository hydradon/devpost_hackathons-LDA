Inspiration

All the times you feel like screaming, but it also feels kind of unproductive

What it does

A simple game for tough times. Scream into the mic and throw an object at your opponent to win the game.

How We built it

We used Javascript and the p5js library to create the game and necessary animations. Through Snap Kit, the player can also access his personal Avatar and have even more fun throwing things.

Challenges We ran into

Calculating the trajectories of the objects that are thrown and positioning all elements correctly on the screen Working with the p5js library

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Making a fun and simple game in less than a day

What I learned

Working with the p5js library

What's next for Screams

Implementing 1 vs. 1 multiplayer
