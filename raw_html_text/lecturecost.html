Inspiration

A lot of my friends and I sometimes feel guilty when skipping a lecture or two and I decided to find out exactly how much money we waste if we ever choose to skip class.

What it does

It takes the user's input for tuition cost, credit hours, and number of weeks to calculate cost per lecture and cost per minute.

How I built it

I used Swift to write the app and Photoshop to make the icons.

Challenges I ran into

I had some trouble with the constraints and the auto-layout features of Xcode 7.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am proud of building an iOS app myself without depending too much on Apple engineers for help.

What I learned

I became more familiar with Swift and the Xcode editor.

What's next for LectureCost

I may add a save calculated data feature where users can access past entries.
