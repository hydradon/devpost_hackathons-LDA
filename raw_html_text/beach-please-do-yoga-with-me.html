Inspiration

Yoga's increasing popularity in America.

What it does

Users are able to connect with their friends via facebook and send them yoga challenges. When you receive a yoga challenge have a friend photograph you performing the pose. Upload your image to Beach Please Do Yoga With Me? to receive an analysis of well you performed the yoga pose.
