Inspiration

Being depressed is not synonymous of elder people, ageing does not mean become useless. But, when our grandparents get depressed their immune system fails and more diseases appears.

We have the responsability to use our skills to improve quality of life of elder people, we have to develop IT solutions to help elderly to be active and feel useful again.

Let's prevent depression. Let's prevent diseases. Let's Improve Elders quality of life.

That's how ActivElderS was born.
