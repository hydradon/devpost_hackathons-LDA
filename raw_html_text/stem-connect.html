There is concern about that the tech field is lacking members from minority groups, many of whom are disenfranchised. It is said that parents are our first teachers.

A website that connects parents with how STEM affects their lives. Through the connection they can teach their children about stem and encourage a STEM education. Most of the time parents do not know about STEM or do not have an idea on how it affects them in their daily lives through this website and phone enabled app they can learn more and spread their knowledge with children.

We used HTML with CSS as well as Android Studio 3.0.

Most of us were completely new to app or web development.
