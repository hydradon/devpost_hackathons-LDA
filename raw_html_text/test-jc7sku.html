-The Problem-

Universities downplay many sexual assault attempts and incidents on campus, building a bureaucratic curtain around the issue. The media has worked tirelessly to expose the prevalence of sexual assault on college campuses, but the hoard of information has yet to be aggregated. Despite the large influx of publically-available information regarding sexual assault and higher education, a university's policies, past incidences, and administrative actions surrounding the issue are scattered across various sources. In essence, the public still lacks a complete picture of a universityâ€™s sexual assault atmosphere.

-The Solution-

Our web application seeks to promote transparency and accountability of sexual assault among universities by publicly displaying articles, statistics, and information relevant to university incidences on a searchable profile page. By compiling all information regarding sexual assault onto one easily-accessible hub, we hope to change university policy for the long-term.
