Inspiration

We just hate filling out paperwork. And if you have ever tried to apply for a visa yourself, you know what kind of anguish it is. We decided to automate this process.

What it does

We made a service to assist in filling out the documents. It can be embedded in your application, web site or instant messenger. So we can fill out any documents. Any complexity. Any online form. Any contracts, forms and other papers.

How we built it

It is java spring + google assistant.

Challenges we ran into

It was very hard to validate different types of field

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We have created a bot that works in Google Assistant and in Telegram. It allows you to create documents and generates pdf. As an example, made 2 types of documents

What we learned

We learned to work with Google Assistant. And also learned a lot about documents

What's next for Hack Docs Bot

In the future development of this project, we want to add automatic recognition of documents. You can simply take a picture of your passport and our service will automatically fill in all the required fields. It is also possible to memorize the context and the entered fields in order to fill the documents faster next time. You can even fill out the document completely without human intervention. In order to simplify the creation of documents, we want to use ML to teach the service to recognize documents and automatically generate questions.
