kontests-browser-extension

Competitive Programming Contests Schedule

A popup extension to give easy access to online judges contests schedule.

Supported sites:

  • Codeforces
  • Codeforces::Gym
  • CS Academy
  • AtCoder
  • CodeChef
  • HackerEarth
  • A2OJ

Credit goes to KONTESTS for providing the contests data through their APIs.

Firefox: https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/kontests Google Chrome: https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/kontests/agpdemlkalmmeenclchlajdcmbcacoea
