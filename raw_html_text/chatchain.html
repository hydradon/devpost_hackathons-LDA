Inspiration

The great forums of the internet; Reddit, BitcoinTalk, Steemit.

What it does

Provides a non discriminant platform for global communication. Since it's based off the blockchain, communication is transparent, pseudo-anonymous and decentralized. This enables open conversation and freedom of expression for everybody. Further implementation of channels expands upon the concept, and allows users to start topic-focused conversations.

How we built it

Blockchain: Ethereum Backend: Solidity, Web3, TestRPC, Frontend: ReactJS + Redux
