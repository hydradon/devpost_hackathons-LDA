What’s Possible? Bitcoin Commercial

Entry Category: Growth Hack

Abstract

Three words, that’s it. This commercial focuses on three real intrinsic (and powerful) aspects of Bitcoin. A large amount of the media in this space focuses on pitting Bitcoin against financial institutions, or “taking back our money”. While there is nothing wrong with this, overly strong opinions or ones that go against the social norm will scare away newcomers. By not directly referencing “banks”, “resistance”, or “conflict” this commercial’s goal is to foster a new demographic to become intrigued with the benefits of Bitcoin. Often ideas with the least amount to say have the most powerful impact.
