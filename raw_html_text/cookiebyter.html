Inspiration

We wanted to learn as much as possible while also having fun, so making a cookie clicker type of game allowed us to achieve both of our goals.

What it does

An accumulative game based on the popular game Cookie Clicker, with a CS twist !

How we built it

Using the Meteor framework, we utilized HTML, CSS, and Javascript to build our application.

Challenges we ran into

Most of our group was composed of beginners in Meteor, CSS and Javascript, with hard-work, willpower, and dedication, we were able to build app that we previously thought was out of our reach.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Being able to implement Persistent Storage, allowing us to refresh or exit the page without losing progress.

What we learned

We learned essentially from scratch, Github, HTML, CSS, and Javascript, and the Meteor framework.

What's next for CookieByter

We are looking to take it to the next level by adding many more features such as, achievements, graphics, animation, etc, and we are currently looking into approaching a startup incubator and seeking investors.
