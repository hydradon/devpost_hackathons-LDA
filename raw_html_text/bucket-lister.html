Inspiration

I've been having interesting weekends lately and it would be cool to see it on a map.

What it does

Whenever you take a picture with the app, it records on the map where you took the picture and a little note that way you get to see all the places you've been and where you have left to explore. The pictures get uploaded to the cloud with AWS.
