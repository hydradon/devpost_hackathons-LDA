Inspiration

ReadyNYC launched a preparedness billboard campaign. It got me thinking, but has been too inconvenient to tackle the planning process with my family. There had to be an easier way.

What it does

Goes to where the user is, communicates over the channel and at the pace the user wants to use. Collects specifics and provides bite-size, habit forming steps and highly relevant information to help users plan.

Aggegates data for emergency manager organizations to make better decisions.

How we built it

Concept prototypes via BotSociety Planning via Mural.co

Challenges we ran into

Limited time of the hackathon!

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Interviews with 14 mentors, and many participants. Made many changes to our project based on feedback

What we learned

Big respect for our national guard and emergency responders. Very big challenges. Small steps can lead to big outcomes, especially in communications and planning. User adoption is a key challenge. How do we design cues around news, events to drive adoption?

What's next for OnBoard

  • Rapid prototyping.
  • Customer development, interviews with potential users
  • Recruiting early adopters.
  • Lean startup iterations towards product/market fit for govt/private sector.
  • Conducting experiments on our business model.
