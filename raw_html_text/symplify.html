" When did the pain start?" * " How long did it last?" *

Has your doctor ever asked you one of these questions and you have no idea where to begin? Do you have the memory of a gold fish?

Well...

HAVE NO FEAR, SYMPLIFY IS HERE!

This app is designed to gather data on the wellbeing of the user from a day-to-day basis over a set period of time and analyze the information to detect if anything is alarming.

Users will utilize the easily comprehensible user interface to submit the status of their physical wellbeing from harmless flu symptoms to serious diseases. If at any point the user wishes to forward their history to their doctor, there is a simple send button to complete the task.
