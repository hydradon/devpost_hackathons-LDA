Inspiration: I work IT at Texas Woman's University and I wanted to make it easier for the fellow students to send in requests for computer repairs.

What it does: Asks about the issues that your device is experiencing and collects the info for the IT person to have

How I built it: Google Assistant, Dialogflow, and FireBase.

Challenges I ran into: Trying to create a function to email the IT people.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of: Making google assistant work

What I learned: how to use dialogflow

What's next for STAR Google Assistant: Email function for IT people, scheduling for the other students
