TunaMelt came about from our team's desire to manipulate images on the go, and have fun learning computer science as well.

By using basic sorting algorithms and leaving room for more to be added, TunaMelt not only offers an intuitive understanding of how sorting systems work but also allows us to create unique art on the fly.

We believe that this can be used in classrooms as a way to closer inspect what's really going on besides a "quicksort".

Our team is comprised of two first time undergraduates. Combined we have created two apps including this one.
