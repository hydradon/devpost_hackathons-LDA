What it does

Contributes money towards a virtual swear jar whenever user enters a swear word. When swear jar accumulates to a predetermined amount, extension freezes Chrome (redirects to custom page) until user pays the amount through PayPal to an account of their choice (charity, etc).

How we built it

Swear Jar is a chrome extension.
