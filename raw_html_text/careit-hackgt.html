As technology advances, apps have become more designed for the younger generations despite the current "Aging World" state. care-it is unique in focusing on creating a pleasurable UI experience for those with visibility and mobility issues, who additionally, need to take regular medication. With Google Cloud's Vision API, the app extracts crucial medication information, displays it largely, and allows the user to connect with his/her family. Whenever the user has not checked off medication, this feature sends SMS messages to loved ones as an extra reminder, in addition to the notifications that pop up timely before and after the user checks off pills. care-it cares!
