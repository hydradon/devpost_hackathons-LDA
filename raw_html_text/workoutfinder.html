Inspiration

We wanted to create a simple app with a clean interface that quickly generates a custom workout that is tailored to your needs.

What it does

by requiring a few simple inputs, it generates workouts that meet criteria for losing weight, gaining weight, or maintaining current weight and living an overall healthy and well balanced lifestyle.

How we built it

android studio/java

Challenges we ran into

lots of bugs and heavy time constraints

Accomplishments that we're proud of

having a running app.

What we learned

android may look fun and easy, but there are a lot of bugs that drag it down

What's next for WorkoutFinder

implement a database login system that allows users to sync their information online from anywhere instead of downloading an offline copy
