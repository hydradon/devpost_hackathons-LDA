Inspiration

The idea for this project stems from our experiences with hackathons. In hackathons we consume large amounts of junk food. We wanted to use our programming knowledge to find out the contents of what we consumed. This app helps regulate your junk food intake and serve as a wake up call to make conscious healthier decisions.

What it does

What’s in My Junk is a Health Tracking app for iOS. The purpose of our app is to track the junk food intake of the individual by taking pictures of junk food they’ve consumed and using Machine Learning to identify the product. Then, the app displays nutritional information of the item to the user and adds it to the daily tally.

How I built it

This was built with swift, designed for IOS utilizing REST APIs

What's next for WhatsInMyJunk

Next, we plan to use CorePlot, a plotting framework that allows for 2d visualization of the information to show graphs over different time periods. This is so that the individual can track their intake of Sodium, Cholesterol, Fats, Calories and Carbohydrates over long periods of time.
