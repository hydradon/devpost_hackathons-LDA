Inspiration

Watching a video on youtube and it was about a gourmet pb&j which required a certain peanut butter. I went through many stores to look for the peanut butter only to realize that my corner 99cent store had it.

What it does

Helps connect deli stores and customers to find certain products more easily.

How I built it

Built with NodeJS, Express, MongoDB powered by Mlab.

Challenges I ran into

Lack of stable and fast internet connection.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned

How to use MongoDB with the NodeJS Express framework.

What's next for Cloud99

Completing the backend/front, adding payment platform, and advertising to store owners.
