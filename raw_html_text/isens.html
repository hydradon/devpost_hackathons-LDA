Identification System for Endangered Species Develop a mobile application that will allow users to capture images of various wildlife, have the application determine the ecological status of the wildlife (critically endangered, endangered and vulnerable), and if so submit the incident to DENR using facebook or sms.

Purpose of the Application To raise awareness among locals and tourists on the status of our wildlife fauna, and to restraint the selling and buying of illegally traded animals among pet shops, traders and vendors selling souvenir items.
