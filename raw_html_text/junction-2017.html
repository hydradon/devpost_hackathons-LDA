The Personal Banker

Logging in to one's bank account can, for some people, be intimidating. The Personal Banker brings your balance and saving into your daily life, without more added effort. It pokes you with important updates, just as a friend would. What's your current balance? Do you need to slow down your expenses to break even this month? Or is it possible to even save a buck or two?

It functions as a thinner interface to your bank account than your bank app, providing an easier way to be more informed for uninterested peoples.

Run bot

node bot/index.js --lt --ltsubdomain junction17
