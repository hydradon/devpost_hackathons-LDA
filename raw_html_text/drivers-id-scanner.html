Inspiration

To facilitate the process of filling out forms or applications.

What it does

Scans a drivers ID and extrapolates the information to automatically fill relevant information in a form.

How we built it

We used Android Studios along with a tool named BlinkID.

Challenges we ran into

Getting the app to properly scan the ID.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting the basis of the app done.

What we learned

Programming is not a one day ordeal.

What's next for Drivers ID Scanner

Further develop its identification abilities and add support for other forms of ID.
