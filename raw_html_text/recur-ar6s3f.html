Inspiration

People need an easier way to manage their credit card finances and so we create a visual tool to help them in making financial decisions.

What it does

Shows regular recurring purchases (like subscriptions for Netflix, Spotify) and non-regular payments in tables in charts.

How we built it

Split the front-end and back-end work accross 2 sub-teams.

Challenges we ran into

JavaScript was a bit challenging at the beginning for all of us.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Finishing a minimal working app.

What we learned

Javascript, Flask, Material-Design, how to cooperate.

What's next for Recur

Start-up expansion
