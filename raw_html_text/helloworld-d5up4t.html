Inspiration

The "Hello World" application makes it easy to party with other people with the same interest, utilising Facebook logins and locating near-by people with GPS coordinates. We can find the chat rooms with your similar interest and also you can locate the near-by chat rooms with the precise location.

What it does

This is the site that we seek someone to share ideas. (나는 가치를 나눌 사람을 찾습니다.)

How we built it

DynamoDB, Linux CentOS 6.5 - Node.js / Shell script , Javascript (React)

Challenges we ran into

It was the biggest challenge to search new things by recognizing the commercial service while we progress some ideas related to that. unfamiliar computer language as well. (새로운 컴퓨터 언어는 물론이며, 우리가 생각한 아이디어를 진행하다가 이미 상용화된 서비스가 있다는 사실을 알게 되어, 새로운 가치를 찾는 것이 가장 큰 도전이였습니다.)

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We met for the first time and found value for a couple of weeks, realized our values, dreamed of new challenges, and offered an environment where a little more people could share ideas with this service. . (처음 만난 우리는 한 뜻이 되어 가치를 찾아서 몇주동안 함께 하였으며, 우리가 생각한 가치를 실현해나가며, 새로운 도전을 꿈 꾸었으며, 이 서비스로 조금 더 많은 사람들이 가치를 공유할 수 있는 환경을 제시하였습니다.)

What we learned

From a simple point of view, We learned the facebook's ideas that we can communicate someone who doesn't know each other. In addition, we have the book which we can consider the situation needed communication and we can have the pride to go forward from little society to big society. What's next for Helloworld

Beyond sharing what is wasteful to me as a minor communication, I aim at the platform that allows users to do things that are mutually valuable. In other words, beyond the material value, the mind value. When I lose something here, when I want someone's help here, there's a platform where communications can help to everyone. That's the future of hello world. (사소한 커뮤니케이션인 나에게 낭비가 되는 것을 나누는 것을 넘어, 서로간의 가치가 되는 행위를 유저 스스로가 할 수 있는 플랫폼을 지향합니다. 즉, 물질적 가치를 넘어, 마음적인 가치. 내가 이곳에서 무엇인가를 잃어 버렸을때, 내가 이곳에서 누군가의 도움을 원할때, 서로 도움이 될 수 있는 커뮤니케이션이 일어나는 플랫폼.
