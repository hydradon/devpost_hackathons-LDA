BankShare

A simple app for sending ethereum to friends without the mess of managing private and public keys. Venmo for crypto with just an email address.

Context

About 1.7 billion adults remain unbanked - without an account at a financial institution or through a mobile money provider. Many well known exchanges allow you to hold and trade funds, but require identity verification, proof of address, social security, etc. just to sign up.

BankShare is a private cryptocurrency storage platform enabling frictionless account sign up and transactions for everyone. By using the Torus framework and Oauth login, the app is designed for easy onboarding of new users onto ethereum, ethereum (and other cryptocurrencies in the future )with their friends.
