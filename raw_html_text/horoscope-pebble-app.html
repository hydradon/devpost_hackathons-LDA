Everyone knows what their horoscope is. But do they know about their companion in the stars? ## Inspiration

Based on answers to a few convenient questions, this app will let you know who is your fated star constellation! ## What it does

JavaScript on CloudPebble. Extensive research on horoscopes and compatibility before moving onto the code. The app determines your horoscope based on your answers and connects you with the horoscope that is most compatible with you. ## How I built it

What is Pebble? What is JavaScript? ## Challenges I ran into

Working together as a team and actually having a finished, working product in front of our eyes. ## Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Everything basic about JavaScript and the CloudPebble platform. We are fairly confident that we can create many more apps for the Pebble Watches to come! ## What I learned

We plan to extend our guessing game to guess your animal spirit, your rival personalities, or anything that you may be compatible or against! ## What's next for Horoscope Companion
