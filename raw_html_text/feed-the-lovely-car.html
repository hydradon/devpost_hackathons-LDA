Inspiration

It may be inconvenient to find the gas station when driving and when the fuel is running out.

What it does

It will prompt drivers to the closest gas station when the fuel is running out if they choose to refuel immediately.

How we built it

If the amount of fuel is at a certain level, the system will prompt drivers to refuel. If the drivers choose to refuel immediately, the GPS location of the car will be collect and the closest gas station will be given and the route drivers will be guided to it. All drives need to do is to press the "direction button" when the prompt shows up.

Challenges we ran into

We found it challenging to get the data of the closest gas station location.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We can finally get the data and provide the navigation service.

What we learned

We learned about a lot of front-end design skills, including html, css and js. We also learned to think the problems from a more realistic perspective and think about a solution to deal with them.
