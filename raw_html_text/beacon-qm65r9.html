Beacon is an offline based application in time of natural disasters Allows users to communicate with loved ones, emergency units, and the public.

We do this by connecting to Bluetooth and Wifi.

For example in times where networks crash or towers are disabled after a natural disaster.

This app has potential to save numerous lives.

Integrates with cell service

Provides Alternative messaging service if cell towers go down before losing service.

Integrates with cell service provided if cell towers stop operating.

Reports where you're at through uploaded pictures, Bluetooth, geolocation, and text.
