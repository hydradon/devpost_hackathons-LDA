Inspiration

Many systems today harvest the potential of individual small elements to create a larger, more powerful collective product by synchronizing the inputs. We used the same concept to synchronize an audio signal across many devices.

What it does

It synchronizes audio signals across all connected devices. Imagine you are at a party, a conference or an outdoor rally, by connecting to the same session on PartyLoud, you can create a speaker-like effect without the specialized hardware by using the collective of individual devices present.

How we built it

We used node.js, javascript, html and css. We are hosting it locally for the time being.
