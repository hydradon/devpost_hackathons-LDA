Inspiration

As friends and teammates who have all faced multiple hours of studying or programming, we agree that our brains, eyes, and bums all suffer from said extended time periods in front of our computer screens. To assuage our pains, we created a website which a user can initialize at any time and send push notifications to remind the user to stretch and keep their blood flowing. This increases productivity over time, as evidenced in multiple reports of companies who sponsor break times in their work environments (e.g., one such company offers nerf gun wars at hour intervals).
