Inspiration

Ebikes are popular recently. Will normal bikes become obsolete?

What it does

A simple DIY addon to any bikes to make it an electric vehicle

How I built it

Arduino Uno board with a DJI ESC board, and a DJI motor. The setup is ziptied due to tools restriction

Challenges I ran into

Wiring of the circuitry

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Finding a spot so that there is enough traction while not grinding the frame of the bike

What I learned

How to use an ESC

What's next for bicycle

Trying to make it more aesthetically pleasing and universal in usage
