What it does

Addth more Tython to your life by replacing every 's' on a webpage with 'th'.

How we built it

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned

Its important to know that websites may be writen unconventionally, and that exceptions have to be made to ensure maximum compatability of an app.
