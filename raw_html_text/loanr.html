loanR

LoanR was conceived as a platform for crowd-sourced micro-loans. We believe in the idea of a free market and that anyone should be able to take out a loan from anyone else using a real time streamlined process. LoanR joins the ranks of AirBnB and Venmo in changing how people conduct business.
