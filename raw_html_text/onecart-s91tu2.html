Inspiration

We are college students from Singapore who just came over to the States at the beginning of August. In Singapore, we faced the problem where delivery orders are extremely high to the point where it makes the cost of ordering a meal for yourself no longer justifiable. We figured that this problem would likely be even bigger over here since a lot of people do not live with their parents and often end up living alone where food delivery becomes a common option for them to settle their meals.

What it does

OneCart allows you to combine the food delivery orders of people living near each other in the same area so that

  1. You will never have to pay that ridiculous delivery fee when just ordering for yourself.
  2. You gain access to promotions and discounts which large orders can get you.
