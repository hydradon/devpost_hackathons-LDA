Inspiration

The desire for appealing visuals.

How it works

Using leap motion, take the roll, pitch, yaw and height of a hand, then use these to choose the colours of strings and output them as you manipulate them.

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Overcame the challenges.

What I learned

I learned about node.js, and programming with leap motion.

What's next for NodeShite

Look into visual representation of more meaningful data, such as data from muse headsets and using leap motion for other things, such as game controllers.
