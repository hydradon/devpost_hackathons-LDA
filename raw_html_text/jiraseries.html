Inspiration

We were looking for an online platform which improves Aptitude and Quant knowledge of students

What it does

It is a timer based test exam where you get random questions designed on the base of functions. Artificial intelligence makes system build 100 questions out of 10 functions using random number generation for series calculation.

How I built it

I used Asp.net MVC framework with cshtml and jQuery for frontend and SQL for database.

Challenges I ran into

The UX was the biggest challenge as I wanted the candidate to focus on questions 1 by 1.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am proud of attaining the Typeform css with our exam

What I learned

learnt Typeform js & css for animation and UX

What's next for Jiraseries

Want Jiraseries to have a bigger bank of questions from verbal and logical reasoning too.
