Friendle

Your Friendly Schedule!

A wonderful schedule and workload management system that permits registered communities (including workplaces) to schedule events that are in need of participants (both volunteers or paid). These events are tagged and classified into different fields of interest. Regular users that are registered in this platform select their fields of interest and hours of availability, and can subsequently see all events that match them. The platform will then use an algorithm to learn of users habits and recommend events, while managing their load.
