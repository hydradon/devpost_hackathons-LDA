Inspiration

Many English speaking people feel as though they aren't welcomed in Quebec, if French is not their first language. They believe that they do not have as many opportunities to form friendships with francophones, due to this language barrier.

What it does

The web-app allows Anglophones to meet Francophones through volunteer opportunities at hospitals and seniors homes in their area. This gives them the opportunity to form these connections while giving back to the community and benefiting the healthcare system.

How we built it

We built it using Javascript, html and css.
