Inspiration

We wanted to enable people to track the food they ate at the Purdue Dining Courts and learn how that effected their digestive health.

What it does

The app provides users with the Purdue Dining Court Menus and allows them to track what food they ate.

How we built it

It was built using Swift for building the app, and a combination of python and java for analyzing the data.

Challenges we ran into

Communicating between the app and the server.

What's next for Purdue Life

Modify and improve the algorithms used to analyze the data in order to provide more accurate results for users.
