Inspiration

Improving safety, security and work comfort in open-pit mines.

What it does

  • Keeps track on every drone in a fleet.
  • Schedules various tasks for drones.
  • Visualizes drone positions using 3D technology.
  • Displays values from drone sensors.

How we built it

We took a cloud of points representing mine terrain and built a 3D visualization of the mine equipped with one drone. This is a mockup.

Challenges we ran into

To overcome lack of physical drones and simulation on site.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We could realize all plans within 12 hours.

What we learned

To think lateral.

What's next for Mining Drone Fleet Management System

Taking potential customers into boat. Turn the mockup into a real system. Testing it with real drones.
