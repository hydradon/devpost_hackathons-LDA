Inspiration

We've been inspired by the choose your own adventure story books, but have updated these to be an immersive audio adventure. We wanted to create a new narrative experience

The Experience

Audventure uses every speaker in the house to create an immersive audio experience where rich soundscapes drive imaginative stories in a completely new format.

How it works

We utilise native HTML5 voice recognition and text-to-speech synthesis to guide the person and allow them to say what they want to do in an audio only environment.

Ambience sounds are played back through the Harman/Kardon OMNI 10 speakers utilising their API.
