Inspiration

Working with experienced workers from the refugee field we realized how crucial the problem of building a secure identity is for refugees.

What it does

It's a secure place on blockchain where a refugee can collect identity data and get it verified by trusted people during her journey.

How I built it

We used Syncano.

Challenges I ran into

Time constraints and managing to narrow down on one of all those problems a refugee faces.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

That we were able to work with people we had never worked with before and that we were able to have fun for 36 hours.

What I learned

Learned a lot about blockchain and the difficulties of refugees and displaced people.

What's next for Digital Grab Bag

We will release it as an open-source project.
