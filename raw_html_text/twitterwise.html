Inspiration

Social Media sites are full of people sharing advice and stories already, and sorting the advice themselves using hashtags. Yet, there is no way to tap into this resource to see the advice others have to give on a particular subject. We set out to try and find a way to tap into this reserve of knowledge.

What it does

Simply enter a hashtag that you'd like to receive advice about, and Get Wise.
