Inspiration

we want to know when my loved are save after entering or leaving a certain location, without constantly tracking them.

What it does

When you enter a zone that you consider danger zone, your phone will send a notification to a list of users that you want them to know about that. So that they can contact you if you didn't answer later

How I built it

We use Radar.io to track user geofences. The app will periodically ping Radar.oi to find out if they enter the zone. We also use Mongo Alas to keep track of user's info as well as user trusted members

Challenges I ran into

Because we try to integrate too many resources, we ran into the problem of combining them at the end.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

This is a project that we have been thinking of doing for our loved one, but we have not had a chance to do so. We are proud that we are able to pull this off and start the project

What I learned

Even though we have tried to keep the project small, our ambitious idea is still not small enough to fit into a 24h hackathon
