Inspiration

To have one less thing on your mind and can fully enjoy that long road trip you've been thinking about

What it does

Utilizes the Microsoft Bing Maps API services to optimize a route, taking into account information about the user's car such as gas tank capacity, mileage, and percentage of remaining gas in tank to calculate when to get gas.
