ayaya

Cutie Hack Discord Bot

This bot has a single purpose: to give cookies to any one who asks for one. All you have to do to send any message containing "cookie". Ayaya will say, "Here, (username), have a cookie!" and will send a cookie emoji after it.

It serves no other purpose.
