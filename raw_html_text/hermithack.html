Inspiration

While brainstorming, we realized that the One thing that all of the team members have in common is the fact that we all met through a hackathon. We quickly became friends. It was a very exciting and valuable experience for all of us, so we decided to make something that allows people who cannot attend a hackathon for geological reasons to have similar experience.

What it does

Hermithack is a hakerthon platform that helps people to host and join an online hackathon. It provides tools and instructions to guide participants to form a balanced team, distribute and stay on tasks, and communicate each other to improve remote team-working skill.
