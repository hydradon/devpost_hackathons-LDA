What it does

How I built it

Followed the tutorial from http://lightshowpi.org/ .

Challenges I ran into

Weren't able to connect the raspberry pi to CMU's wifi network, so we had to use a phone hotspot. Also, getting the lights to work properly on the breadboard was challenging.
