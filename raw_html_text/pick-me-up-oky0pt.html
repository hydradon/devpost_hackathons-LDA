For days you would rather forget, we have created an app to give you encouragement and fill your life with positivity.

Peer support is now at the press of a button in your pockets at all times. User generated support is submitted to be critiqued by users in a reddit style content evaluation system. The best of these support messages are then sent to users when they are in need of a pick me up.

The back end is built on an Amazon Web Services back end and uses node.js to send data to the iOS app which is built in Swift.
