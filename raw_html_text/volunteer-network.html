Main Pitch

This project is made for volunteers who want to action in their community by allowing certain agencies to access our database.

Objective

In using their information for agencies, the agencies will assign volunteer assignments. Each assignment is identified by a company id. This allows agencies to access the social volunteer network while using our Google Maps API to pair them up with assignments close to the volunteer’s location or hometown. Assigning tasks to volunteers will help with time efficiency and urgency.

Processing the Data

Keywords identify the objective on each volunteer assignment. Agencies are assigned to industries. Agencies pitch their goals and objectives to the project creators, on how volunteers can help their community.
