Inspiration

There are thousands of legal resources available to people with their legal problems.

What it does

LawZing curates online legal self help resources, grading and promoting the best sites to get people the help the need.

How I built it

Developed a rubric and designed a webpage

Challenges I ran into

Time to validate the project and back end developer support

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The QRater rubric that objectively evaluates the quality of websites.

What's next for LawZing

Expand the jurisdiction scope, automate the ranking rubric and open source the tool.
