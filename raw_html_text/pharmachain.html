Inspiration

We were astonished to see the problems in the supply chain of lifesaving drugs which are so important for any patient and the amount of counterfeit drugs being supplied.

What it does

A DECENTRALIZED APP 'PHARMACHAIN' THAT SCANS THE PRODUCT'S BARCODE TRACE BACK ITS ORIGIN AND DETECT ANY FRAUD OR COUNTERFEITING OF THE PRODUCT

How we built it

We built it using open ethereum blockchain network where we deployed a smart contract for detecting the anomaly in supply chain and also we use the simple barcode reader which is present in all the mobiles these days. Basically we have made our platform on an application which is easy to use for a layman.

Challenges we ran into

The biggest challenge we faced was time constraint and the integration of front end with back end As it was a local hack day so we had so less time to do all this stuff.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We successfully tested our smart contracts and they are successfully deployed. We have a working DApp

What we learned

Integration of blockchain with drug industry

What's next for PharmaChain

Connecting IOT to blockchain to make it more precise for coldchain transportation.
