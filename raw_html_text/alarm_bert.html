Summary

This is team 37 submission to Idea hacks. In the library and in cafes, students need to go to the restroom and make short errands but don't really need to pack everything with them. This small motion detector allows students to keep track of their stuff and see if anyone moved it via Bluetooth from their phone.

The impetus for this device comes from personal experience from studying and working at the library.
