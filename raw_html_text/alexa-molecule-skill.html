Molecule Alexa Skill

This is a Alexa skill which help Alexa answer simple Molecule details like Bolling point, Melting point etc.

Molecule Props

Description

Closely working with Alexa, we found her a better and smarter skill with which she can help us with some chemistry geniusness. Ah, just trying to make learning chemistry fun, you see. Ask her if you want to know a chemical formula of a complex molecule, or if you want to know what can dissolve a molecule, or even if you want to know what a molecule smells like.

Examples

  • Alexa, Open molecule props
  • Alexa, Ask molecule props what is the Atomic Number for Nitrogen molecule
  • Alexa, Ask molecule props odor for Hydrogen cyanide
  • Alexa, Ask molecule props get the Density for Hydrazine molecule
  • Alexa, Ask molecule props get me the Phase for Carbonyl fluoride molecule
  • Alexa, Ask molecule props chemical Formula of sulfuric acid
  • Alexa, Ask molecule props carbonyl fluoride Chemical Formula
  • Alexa, Ask molecule props does Tetrafluoromethane soluble in Benzene
  • Alexa, Ask molecule props what is the chemical name of CH4
