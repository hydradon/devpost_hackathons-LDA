Inspiration

To get more college students collaborating under clean solar technology and learn about solar innovations.

What it does

Solar shade is redeveloping pre-existing benches on college campuses in to micro co-working spaces for students under clean solar energy.

How we built it

Using pre-exisiting benches on college campuses and innovative solar technology.

Challenges we ran into

We ran in to the challenge of who would be the best fitting customer for this product.

What we learned

We learned the power of solar innovation and the need for it on college campuses with the growing trend of sustainable smart libraries.

What's next for Solar Shade

Solar Shade plans on slowly but surely building out our prototype which we've already figured the COGS for.
