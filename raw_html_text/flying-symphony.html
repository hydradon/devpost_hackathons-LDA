Flying Symphony

Flying Symphony lets you play with a Symphony of birds with your hands free while flying.

Grab birds with your hands. Slap them by flinging your hand. Poke them to make them hoot quack or squeal.

Flying symphony The touchable touch less VR pet that makes a racket.

Inspiration.

I have always wanted to grab flying birds in my hands. Or to turn the act of trying to catch birds in my hands into playing a symphony.
