Inspiration

We don't like clickbait videos.

What it does

We highlight the clickbait videos on red so you know what not to click.

How we built it

We used Naive-Bayes, a machine learning algorithm, to identify them. We labeled 1000 youtube titles as clickbait or not to train it.

Challenges we ran into

Labeling 1000 videos takes a while. Writing chrome extensions can be difficult.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The app is working well and we have submitted it to the chrome extension web store. It is pending review.

What we learned

A lot

What's next for Click Bait Identifier

Use Neural Networks to label the thumbnails as clickbait or not.
