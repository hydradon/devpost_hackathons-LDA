Inspiration

Be foodly and people will be foodly to you.

What it does

A network where people can post events that have free food! a great way to connect, feed the hungry and advertise!

How we built it

We made a Web app using Meteor.js

Challenges we ran into

We had some trouble over writing Meteor's pre-set functions such as front-end styling and database functions that were set on creating a user.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

we are very proud of the free food! Just kidding (kind of) we are very proud of our front-end (Thanks to Phil for mocking up in photoshop) as well as our database functionality!

What we learned

We learned how to really play to our strengths, it really felt like a mini team we had from front-end, back-end, middle-woman! Anyone can code! Let no person think they can't!

What's next for t9-Hackasthon

we are excited for the next T9 and hope for more people across the country! We also love the mini games!
