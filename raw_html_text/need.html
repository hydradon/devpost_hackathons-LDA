Inspiration

Sometimes I need some friends to help me but I cannot find. So I came up with an idea. The main idea is that the users post their needs on the maps and the other users will see each other and they will come to help you. You can also offer some money if you want. Or for instance, there is an emergency and you need blood. You can also post health needs in the app and some good people will see and will try to help you to provide the correct blood type. So briefly, when someone needs something, they can post their needs on the maps. That's all.

What it does

A kind of helper app for all the people. You will post your needs and it will apper on the maps and other people will see and come to help you.

How I built it

Using Android Studio, Google Maps, Google Firebase and of course Java.

Challenges I ran into

Maps is not working appropriately sometimes.
