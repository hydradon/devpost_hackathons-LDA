Inspiration

I use to be a fine arts major and I decided to fuse both worlds

What it does

It takes pictures from your camera and tries to create a pixel art.

How I built it

Using Java and OpenCV

Challenges I ran into

I was unable to properly change the hues, tints, and saturation of all the individual pictures that were suppose to mimic pixels.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I'm proud that I was able to tackle this assignment. Though I am not finished I enjoyed the experience.

What I learned

I learned how to use OpenCV for Java and a lot more about Java's graphics and color library.

What's next for Pixel Art

The real vision of this game is for it to be a mobile app that keeps track of memories by making colleges of your vacations and any other sudden burst of camera activity.
