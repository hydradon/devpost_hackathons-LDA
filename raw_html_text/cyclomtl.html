Inspiration

Bicycles

What it does

Help you visit montreal while biking and giving you stops for bixi or bicycle stands. Makes you stop by water for long rides. You select your start and end location and it suggests interesting stops on your way (or around you).

How I built it

We took the monuments, water stops, bike stands, bixi stands from Ville de MTL and make a day of visit for the cyclist.
