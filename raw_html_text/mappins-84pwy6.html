Imagine a new pizzeria just opened up in your neighborhood, but you have no one to go try it with. Good thing there is MapPins! MapPins is an mobile app that gives users the opportunity to connect with their neighbors. It give the users the ability to setup or join events with their neighbors. The application utilizes the EveryBlock API, which contains information on events occurring in different metro areas, and Google Maps, allowing users to easily see the different events (indicated by pins) occurring around them. Now with MapPins, you don't have to eat alone!
