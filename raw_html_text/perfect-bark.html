Inspiration

All we know that the dogs are animal that take care of us first rather than its own life. Perfect Bark is a web page that try to help the little ones.

What it does

It allows you to play (Corgi the dog), discover (real time chat) and help your friend (analizer of illnesses) to have a complete inmersion in the dogs life.
