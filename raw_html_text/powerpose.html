powerpose

Rethinking dominance in the work environment.

The Problem

The gender gap in the work environment, despite many attempts in the 21st century, is ever-widening. _ Why? _ Where do these internal biases occur?

The Solution

PowerPose. Using Deep Learning on Computer Vision we have the ability to determine in a work environment who is doing a power play over another person-- and what to do with that.
