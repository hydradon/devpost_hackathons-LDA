Inspiration

As a part time job i often lost out on stolen tips so relished this opportunity to change lives... TipAdvisor is awesome

How it works

Beacons detect the establishment you are in, Braintree then makes it easy to perform payments into the staff tip jar.

Challenges I ran into

First Hackathon, Android Studio and GitHub liked to collapse on us, Beacons had limited Android docs

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Genuine sense of accomplishment with what we have created, It works and It is ready to use!

What I learned

How to use Beacons, Braintree, Android Studio and the different style of coding a Hackathon demands

What's next for TipAdvisor

Interesting features to help us infer peoples opinions through tipping habits, gamification and basically the future is limitless.
