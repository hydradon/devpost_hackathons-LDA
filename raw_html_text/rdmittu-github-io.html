Inspiration

Have you ever feel like you deserved a treat but weren't sure if youhad the money to do so? Have you tried to save money but weren't sure how much you needed to save in order to meet your goal? Well we have.

What it does

This website will let you know if you can treat yourself and still be on track financially to meet your goals.

How I built it

We used the capital one api and javascript.
