Inspiration

Snapchat for locations

How it works

You hit "Get Numbie" to get a small (< 4 characters) Numbie. Share with others, they can see your position. Numbies have a limited timespan - so there are no privacy concerns. Ideal to share your location with a group of people.

Challenges I ran into

Syncing the Numbies across clients. esri API.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

First version is running!

What I learned

Meteor.js

What's next for Numbie

Finding lots of use-cases. Possibly sharing other information using Numbies (e.g., your current mood, a tweet, pictures, business cards...)
