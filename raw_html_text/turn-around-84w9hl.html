Inspiration

Why is offline shopping more fun then watching boring pictures or videos? Because you can try the clothes in real.

What it does

With a VR Headset it is possible to have this experience at home at you sofa. Choose a dress or t-shirt an turn around and see you turning around like in a virtual mirror.

How I built it

Dima (our lead developer) is using Unity Framework. Code is written in C#

Challenges I ran into

how to produce 90 to 360 pictures in same light conditions and without a background

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

own picture production API integration

What I learned

VR needs more then only head sensors

What's next for Turn around

producing an app for different platform (oculus gear vr and rift, daydream, android cardboard)
