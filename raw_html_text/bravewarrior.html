• A path to a better life

Inspiration

We want to see a more fit and functional society. Also we are one of the youngest contestants here.

What it does

It encourages people to eat better by offering rewards at their local health food stores.

How we built it

We used javascript and the google maps api for our app.

Challenges we ran into

Early sign up process and finding local health food stores that would be willing to offer rewards to our app users.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We worked together to produce something we hope that can change the world.

What we learned

That app making is a little confusing and more about website building as a whole.

What's next for FutureFitness

We hope to get more users and to also add more local health food stores to our app.
