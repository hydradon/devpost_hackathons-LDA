Inspiration

Whether is getting an expectant mother to the hospital for treatment or getting one that has being bitten by a snake, every seconds counts in saving live in time of medical emergencies. Facilitating timely response by providing a platform where people can access ambulances is what i had in mind when developing unganisha 911. Unganisha 911 is a platform on your mobile phone that helps people to request for ambulance rides is built on artificial intelligence technology to help users conveniently request for these services. While most ambulance service providers rely on calls at their dispatch centers that are processed and received by the operators our uses an artificial intelligence persona(chat bot) available in many social media platforms including Facebook messenger , ussd and sms to do the same. This helps reduce delays as a result of poor responses from ambulance service provides. To achieve this we have roll out plan to work with ambulance service providers such as amref, kenya redcross, ahadi trust among many others. Our Business model is derived from 10% charge fee of all ambulance request our clients make. We are looking forward to raise 10 million Kenya shilling for development phase and roll out in countries.
