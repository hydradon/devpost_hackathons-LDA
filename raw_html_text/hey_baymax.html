Synopsis

This project was to help solve the issue of immediate self diagnosis. Users were to simply tweet @hey_baymax to receive suggestive information regarding there immediate health.

Installation

Simps run python hey_baymax.py

Tests

Simply tweet @hey_baymax key words such as cough , sleepy , etc. and will either provide two distinct responses regarding the status of your tweet.
