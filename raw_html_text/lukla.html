Inspiration

New founders don’t know how to kickstart their idea & launch new company

What it does

An interactive web app for founders aimed at streamlining business management, that makes it easy for entrepreneurs to establish, maintain and grow startups. It helps startups leaders looking to understand and make decisions and introduce an online platform for business insights.

How I built it

A Bootstrap HTML5 web app that utilized JavaScript, JQuery, CSS and GoogleDrive hosted on Microsoft Azure.

Challenges I ran into

Time constraints, HTML5 integration problems
