Are you tired to sifting through Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn and other social media posts just to find the ones you really want to see?

Filty can deliver the most popular posts from amongst your friends based on "Like" ratings, and can also display posts for only the people you want to see posts from in a feed.

Social media is too cluttered, use Filty to filter out, aka sift out all the dirt so that only Gold remains. Create different channels for the types of people in each filter section: Top, Family, Work, etc. Interested in posts about MangoHacks? Make a "Hackathon" filter and pull in all posts across Social Media about MangoHacks. Get Filty !

Filty is built with Ionic JS allowing for quick deployment to iOS, Android, Windows Phone, and beyond.
