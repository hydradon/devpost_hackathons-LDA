RateYourDab

RateYourDab is the first and only truly objective way to grade your dab.

We started out looking for the best dabs, handpicking them from all over the Web to find what truly makes a dab great. We created a website that would allow us to capture images using a webcam on Mozilla Firefox, and enabled ourselves to differentiate between wrists, arms, shoulders, and elbows by interacting with the image. Afterward, we would calculate the relevant angles and compare them to the best and brightest dab we could engineer from an average of all our samples from the Web. Instead of hosting it locally, we opted to use AWS and ec2 to host our hack, making it publicly available to everyone with a webcam and internet connectivity.

But the story doesn't end here. We have plans to continue to develop RateYourDab until it can do things like automatically determine your dab score with no input other than an image or keep a running leaderboard with more comprehensive grading potential.
