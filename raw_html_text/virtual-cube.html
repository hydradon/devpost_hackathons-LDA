Inspiration

We like Rubik's Cubes We wanted an aid to help in learning to blind solve, and found that this does not exist

What it does

  • Simulates a Rubik's cube
  • Generates a sequence of characters that a cuber can use to solve the cube blind
  • Interprets cube notation
  • Scrambles the cube and solves using keybinds

How I built it

By making a virtual Rubik's Cube

Challenges I ran into

Rubik's Cubes are complicated to represent with a simple, convenient data-structure

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Getting it working properly

What I learned

Rubik's Cubes are complicated
