Crision

Giving first responders all of the facts, fast

First Responders put their lives at risk for situations that they do not know the full details of. Although they may not have the time to put all of the pieces together, they have people behind the scenes that can help them analyze situations. With Crision, experts are given photos based on the geolocation of individuals who are in need of help, whether that be by the person in need or a bystander. This will help get help faster to those who need it.

In terms of education, the photos taken can be used by the first responders as a reflection tool to see what they could work on better next time.
