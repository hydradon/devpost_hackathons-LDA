Inspiration

There are many people who resell shoes and highly sought after streetwise at. A lot of research goes into seeing which shoe you are going to buy because these shoes are like an investment. There value increases and decreases with time. The idea behind the website is to give you a quick look at wether an upcoming shoe worth the investment.

What it does

The website tells you wether a certain upcoming shoe is worth the investment. We used the StockX API(StockX is a shoe reselling market) to calculate the risk in investing in a shoe.
