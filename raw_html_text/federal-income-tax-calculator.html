• Simulator version of our app on a iPhone 8

Inspiration

Our inspiration was all of the adults around us struggling with planning their finances and filing their taxes. Our goal was to create an app that will help them along this process.

What it does

This app will take your income and your marital status and use the information to calculate the amount of federal income tax you will have to pay for the year.

How we built it

We used the Swift programming language with its UIKit on Apple's XCode platform to design this app.

Challenges we ran into

Some challenges we ran into were debugging the picker view and connecting its output to the rest of the program. This took up a lot of our time.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are extremely proud of what we made as well as being able to learn a new programming language and build an app with that language in 24 hours.

What we learned

We learned how to use xCode and we learned an entirely new programming language, Swift, in the 24 hours of this hackathon.

What's next for Federal Income Tax Calculator

We want to add more features, improve aesthetics, as well as put tax tips to help users file their taxes. We also want to include State-level taxes to provide more comprehensive information.
