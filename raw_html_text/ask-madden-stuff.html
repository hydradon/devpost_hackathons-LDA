Inspiration

There's nothing quite like playing a nice game of digital football on your console of choice. Having world-renowned football coach John Madden by your side advising you on every play you make. Now you can take Madden everywhere you go and ask him stuff .

What it does

Log onto AskMaddenStuff and ask away. Ask coach about your love life, your financial stability, or even what to do about your recent run with the mafia and he'll give you the right play.

What we learned

The usefulness of JQuery and Git.
