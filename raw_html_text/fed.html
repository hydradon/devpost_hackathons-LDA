Inspiration

The problem of food waste is seen in a daily basis. Having the technology we have available, we should be able to create a system to help us reduce the harm in our planet.

What it does

Fed, will work with a locked refrigerator system, like a vending machine, Fed will generate a QR code for students to have access to food stored in the refrigerator system. Through the app we will be able to provide liability security for both parties, the schools and the students. The app will also receive notification from the refrigerator system when there is food available. It will work through a system of smart scanners that will send the notification when they detect the presence of food
