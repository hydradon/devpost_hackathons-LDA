Ten-X Muscles

Working out has never been so fun and easy.

Despite the importance of working out, keeping track of your progress and making custom exercises is not easy. Taking a notepad can be cumbersome and easy to forget, but many apps either have too many functionalities to understand or don't have the ones we want.

So the Ten-X Muscles team decided to focus on a few key features that simplify and streamline the process of working out:

  • Ease of choice and clarity in choosing a muscle group
  • Freedom to customize workouts and add new workouts
  • Flexibility in choosing exercises during each workout
  • Ability to look back and see progress over time
