Inspiration

Poor college students who would like to find the cheapest ride but don't want to waste time.

What it does

Gives you estimates between uber and lyft.

How we built it

Using ionic frameworks and angularJS

Challenges we ran into

Google maps!!

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Super fast location loading

What we learned

That angular is not easy to learn

What's next for Ride With Me

The team is very proud of the concept and our first beta version of the app. We want to continue to work on it throughout the school year and hopefully put it out for everyone to use.
