Inspiration

The fluctuations of the stock market

What it does

Attempts to predict the unpredictable behavior of the stock market with close probability

How we built it

Built with LUA and Love2D, as well as hard math and theoretical algorithms

Challenges we ran into

The unpredictability of the stock market and more than a few bugs

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Ironing out the bugs and creating a comprehensive algorithm

What we learned

The stock market is ridiculously difficult to predict, but can be observed and analysed with decent accuracy

What's next for Modelling the Future [Lua, love2d]

Real-time stock analysis, better data integration, better, less clunky GUI
