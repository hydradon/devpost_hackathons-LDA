Inspiration: We wanted to make an app that is that is private, giving those who have difficulties talking to others a chance to connect and better themselves

What it does: The app gives daily missions that will help improve the users mental health, and the app also includes an anonymous social network where people can meet those dealing with similar issues, allowing them to know that they are not alone.

How we built it: We build this app using the unity engine, it's simplistic interface made it the prime engine to create the app with

Challenges we ran into: the main challenge we ran into would be the time constraints, however we managed to work around it by simplifying the program.

Accomplishments that we're proud of: We are extremely proud for coming up with a unique way to tackle mental health, and how we were able to implement it within a short time frame

What we learned: We learned how to better manage our time, and also how to think about logistics when creating an app (how it would be funded)

What's next for Vybe: we are thinking of adding more personalized missions to fit the interests of the user in order to motivate them to complete them, we will also finish adding things to the shop so those that complete the missions can have a sense of accomplishments through their new icons and themes purchased through the store.
