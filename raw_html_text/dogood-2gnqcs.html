* Humanitarian Hackathon 2019 * This is a motivation Pipeline and Data Collection Processing Tool to effectively propose corporate initiatives to raise awareness of water risks

Inspiration

We discovered that one of the problems WWF faces is not just the lack of necessary data to make decision but also convincing big corporates to partner with them to support their cause. Thus we came up with this idea of disrupting motivation pipeline and also creating a data collection tool for WWF.

What it does

It helps WWF to engage and motivate companies to involve in WWF's environment sustainability initiatives.

How we built it

React, react-native, NodeJS
