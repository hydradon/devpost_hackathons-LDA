Spin 2 Din

Can't decide on a place to eat?? Enter your location and restaurant/cuisine you want to eat and spin the wheel. Click on the restaurant/cuisine names on the side and it will open a new tab that searches on yelp. Enter the same restaurant multiple times to increase its slice on the pie.

Future Possibilities With more time, we would add a users feature that allows multiple users to add restaurants and allow each user to create their own meal groups.

Created for Hacktech 2018 by Lucy Chen, Rachel Lin, Steve Guo, and Donovan Ye.
