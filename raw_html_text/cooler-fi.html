#How does it work?

  1. when customer pick last bottle from cooler of any product(drink type),
  2. sensors will send signal to particle cloud,
  3. application will then record it in mongo db cloud
  4. application will also calculate current status of product facing in a cooler.
  5. If all the column, consisting of that given product is gone, dashboard will remove product from screen.

# What does it measure?

my project is based on last bottle displacement. It measure the light intensity. If there is any obstacle between light and resister, reading will different.

Does the retailer/bottler/distributor have to do anything different to make your solution work? (if yes, please explain)

solution is only between cooler and sale/marketing team who will monitor facing information.
