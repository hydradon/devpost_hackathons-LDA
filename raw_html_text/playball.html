Inspiration

At times when my friends are not available to accompany me to the ball courts, I wanted to find a way for people to connect with others that are available immediately. Namely, find out how many people are AT the ball courts ready to play.

What it does

Get to know exactly how many people are at your favourite courts. You will receive live data of who's present.

How I built it

This product was built on the inspirations to be a master at a language we were all beginners at. Python with a Flask framework was the tech stack used.

Challenges I ran into

Sending data from the client side back to the server side. Our specific problems attributes to many DOM elements making calls.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

FINISHING, first and foremost. Learning new tech and collaborating with others such that we CAN finish this is a accomplishment I am proud of. Of course, now that this site is live, it is a solution for many athletes and amateur sports enthusiasts actively looking for a challenge.

What I learned

LOTS.
