Democracy must be built through open societies that share information. When there is information, there is enlightenment. When there is a debate, there are solutions. When there is no sharing of power, no rule of law, no accountability, there is abuse, corruption, subjugation, and indignation. Corruption is cancer: cancer that eats away at a citizen's faith in democracy, diminishes the instinct for innovation and creativity; already-tight national budgets, crowding out important national investments. It wastes the talent of entire generations. It scares away investments and jobs. Thus we can say we are in urgent need of transparency between government and public We created an app which focuses to bring all government contracts in a glass box (so the name) which will be under surveillance of common man and can contribute in it We made an android app (handy and easy to use) where all developments, pools, and suggestions will be shared through common database hosted on firebase The actual problem started with figuring out ways to curb corruption and which we discovered by brainstorming. Development was easy in the starting phase but later we got ourselves in huge error in database and we solved it. The first achievement was to decide all problem statements. We are proud to tell we dealt with all technical problems and data synchronization. Further, we can provide this app to the government and add more modern solutions to it and make our nation corruption-free.
