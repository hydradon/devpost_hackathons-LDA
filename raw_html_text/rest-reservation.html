OVERWHELMING!! - a feeling that no one enjoys. On average, any given restaurant in Philly sees 250 customers or more, daily. With that comes reservations, tables, seating, and a lot of organization and coordination. The Rest & Reservation team is here to relieve that tension and make dining out an enjoyable experience, for both the customer and the restaurant owner. We’re beginner hackers who are currently high school students in NJ. Our application employs technology all the way from Twilio to Google in order to: Make reservations by texting the restaurant Ensuring these times are available for the customer Creating an employee directory with employee profiles
