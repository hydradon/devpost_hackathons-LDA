We made it move.

This is our team's very first Hackathon and submission.

Among the free stickers, t-shirts and other awesome stuff given away at the HackYSU event, toy green DUMP TRUCKS were given away.

We made it our job to make it being able to be controlled! The original idea was to use a Photon or an Intel Edison to make the truck controlled via wireless, however, the change to USB was made due to limitations. The goal was to host a web server other hackers could join and input the commands "UP, DOWN, LEFT, RIGHT or STOP." All inputs would be recorded for a few seconds and after that, whatever input had the most votes, the truck would move. We were going to design a course hackers would have to work together to complete, but without wireless it would not b possible.
