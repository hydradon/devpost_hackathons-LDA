Inspiration

With over 600 million credit cards to choose from, finding the right credit card can be overwhelming. We were inspired to create CreditSmarter to help people maximize their cashback with a card that matches their spending habits and credit history.

What it does

CreditSmarter takes spending habits and credit history and recommends the optimal credit card which maximizes cashback to the customer. It also allows the user to sort by certain parameters and explore different credit options in a user-friendly web application.

How we built it

We used Angular6 as the frontend and used Firebase as the database backend.
