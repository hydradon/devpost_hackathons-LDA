Boosted Boards, and Nintendo Switches

What it does

Sentiment analysis of Amazon product reviews. Results in an aggregate view of a company's overall standing based on the analysis of the product reviews. Popular reviews can make use of the Capital One Verify API to verify them selves as a reviewer and the algorithm will place a heavier weight on their reviews.
