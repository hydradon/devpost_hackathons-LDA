Inspiration

We've all been throughout boring friday nights trying to reach friends to squad up and find an interesting activity to do. We decided to solve this problem for the future generations by building HiSquad, an efficient way to find different exciting venues around you and your friends ! Making a plan will never a pain again.

What it does

HiSquad! allows users to join or create groups of friends to explore close by venues while having access to exclusive deals made for outstanding crews. Whether you wanna go drink a beer, watch a movie or go skiing, you'll always find the right match.
