Enviria

Enviria was created to tackle the global issue environmental deterioration and climate change. With Enviria, individuals can compete with friends and the rest of the world to have the best possible GreenScore. Your GreenScore is dependant on your daily habits like taking out the trash, taking your car/the bus to work, riding a bike and the like.

Features

  • Adding/Removing personal environmental impacts
  • Signing Up/Logging in/Signing Out
  • Editing and Adding/Removing history of good and bad environmental impacts
  • Displaying Historical Data of your environmental impact over 7 days, 7 weeks, and 7 months
  • Displaying your Leaderboard Rankings

What's next?

We're going to keep working on Enviria in a non-hackathon environment because of the passion our team members have for the project at hand.
