Inspiration

The use of IT has grown to such an unprecedented stage that it is now possible to do much more than was possible heretofore. It is considered equally important to seek adequate weather condition for planting as it is to utilize the right seedlings. This work is inspired by the vast possibilities that can be achieved with IT and aims at developing a system which monitors, and guides a farmer before, during and after planting.

What it does

It monitors the weather condition automatically and sends proactive instructions to the farmer in regards to the specific plants on the farm i.e. a given location. Some of the weather conditions being monitored include temperature, humidity, wind direction and speed. Soil PH and water content level are also monitored to ensure an enabling environment for plants production. In the case of animal production, PH level of the aquaculture is monitored for fish production.

How we built it

This application is a java desktop application with a robust database containing data-set of climate weather condition for crops and animals. The integration of the desktop application and the hardware part is also carried out. Hardware section consists of a micro-controller and sensors. Assembly language is also used for the micro-controller programming.
