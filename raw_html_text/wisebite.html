wisebite

What we did?

An apple watch application that helps you find out the nutrient information of your meal.

Also acts as a shopping assistant by listing products and their nutritional values.

What makes it special?

Easy to use interface. Just say "what you want to know about your meal" and we'll find it out for you.

Minimum clicks and swipes

How we did it?

Convert what you say to text. (speech to text translation)

Extract food products from the text (sentence parsing)

Do our magic and get you the nutrition report (public APIs)

Suggest similar products for you to buy
