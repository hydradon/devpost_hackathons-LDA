Inspiration

Canada welcomes more than 300.000 immigrants each year and while Canada is a huge country with cities with different characteristics, most of them have no idea where go.

What it does

Helping newcomers to find a city

How I built it

We got the idea to create a website who prompts the user with a series of questions detailing where in the world they would like to live (population density-weather-jobs-language). Then program will then take data from different sources and produce the top city matching the user’s choices
