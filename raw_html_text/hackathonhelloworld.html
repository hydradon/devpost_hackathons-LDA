Inspiration

Recycling takes a lot of energy and is becoming more inefficient day by day. Therefore, reusing is more useful and definitely, easier.

What it does

You type in a material that you have and it gives you ideas on how you can reuse it. For example, it could be art and craft. It provides you with the instructions as well as the picture.

How we built it

We used Xcode.

Challenges we ran into

We couldn't connect it to Firebase. We also had to learn Swift.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We learned Swift and how to make an app with Xcode.

What we learned

We learned teamwork and how to pitch ideas.

What's next for Beaver

Connect to firebase and allow posting of pictures, similar to social media.
