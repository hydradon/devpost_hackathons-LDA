Inspiration

With all the poor quality products available on the market it is very easy to buy an item and regret the purchase. To eliminate the hassle of returns and dissatisfaction we wanted to create an app that would tell the consumer if he/she should buy the product or not based on internet data such as reviews, sales and popularity.

What it does

Take a picture of the barcode and get a simple response if you should buy it or not.

How we built it

Lots of caffeine, little sleep and full dedication.

Challenges we ran into

Spelling Mistakes, Configuring Amazon API, Sleep of Lack, Everything imaginable went wrong.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Learning JavaScript,configuring Amazon API successful.

What we learned

Working effectively in a team under pressure.

What's next for NEEDorNOT

Clean up the design, create a mobile app and pitch the idea to a sponsor.
