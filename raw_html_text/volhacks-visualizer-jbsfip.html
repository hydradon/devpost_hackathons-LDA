Volhacks-Visualizer

An audio-visualizer for VolHacks IV, created using PyAudio and PyGame.

A bumpy road was traversed by our team - we had no idea how much of a pain audio capture and analysis could be. However, with some clever use of WASAPI and PyAudio, we're able to capture live audio output and transform it into a beautiful rainbow-colored plot of frequency data. Much was learned about Fourier Transforms and audio encoding.
