Inspiration

We arrived at the hackathon not knowing many people and it is relatively tough to start a conversation. Having an App that enhances the networking experience by matching people by interests, might foster communication and meetings.

What it does

Hackers can shake their phone to get the profile of other hackers including picture, name, and interests. A simple 'Hit me up!' button is the next step to starting the conversation in the app directly.

How we built it

We fully built it with Swift. We all downloaded XCode and wrote the first line of swift the night before. We also used the Artsy API to get hackers' social interests.

Challenges we ran into

We had a terrible time sorting bugs and learning Swift's basics.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We have a working App!

What we learned

We really learned how XCode works and Swift's syntax and logic.

What's next for Brainify

Next, we hope to get the App working with a real hackathon's database. Also, obviously after enhancing the UI, we would love to integrate some Machine Learning algorithms to find the best match for the hackers based on interests.
