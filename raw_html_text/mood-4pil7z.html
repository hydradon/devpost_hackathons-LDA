Inspiration

In today's era, we turn to social media for our every mood. When we are sad, we tend to read motivational quotes, when we are happy we want positivity and when we are heartbroken, we want to listen to someone that understands us.

What it does

Mood skill in Alexa resonates with your mood and tells you want you need to hear to feel better based on your mood

How we built it

We built it using node.js and the amazon alexa sdk

What's next for Mood?

More customization and inclusion of audio and video features
