mapathon

For ChallengePost "Maps As Art" online hackathon submission

What it does: Visualizes Hackathons around the world (due to Google Maps Geocoding limits, I can only have around 10 to 20 Hackathons appear on the map at one time) when year and month is specified

Currently grabbing data from :
