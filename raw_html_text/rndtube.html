Inspiration

Whilst having nights in at Uni we often play unusual and fun videos on the chromecast. We wanted an easy way of discovering new interesting videos withough scrolling miles through reddit.

What it does

Users can submit media links from common websites (just youtube at the moment). Users can discover new videos in the app and vote wether they like or dislike it.
