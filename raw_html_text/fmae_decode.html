Inspiration

Fannie Mae secondary mortgage house acquisition system

What it does

We have prepared an app for • Assessing a house by a house inspector for damages • Feeding the data to admin (Fannie Mae employee) for review and confirmation • Sending quotes for contractors • Selecting the best contractor fit for the job • Alloting services to repair damages • Final inspection after repairs

How we built it

We built it over the span of 3 day consistently working through days and nights using the above mentioned coding languages to come up with a robust app to function for Fannie Mae secondary mortgage house acquisition system

Challenges we ran into

-mapping addresses to markers on the map -creating the request response between the admin and inspection party -getting the design of the app structure right -detailed use-cases which needed much more attention -conditions of questionnaire

Accomplishments that we're proud of

-Mapping the address to markers on the map -setting up admin requests -preparing a foolproof questionnaire

What we learned

-team work -time management -perseverance and the will to meet a deadline -experience of hacking with fellow participants -global exposure -guidance from experts

What's next for MaePro

-getting it implemented into Fannie Mae's mainstream projects
