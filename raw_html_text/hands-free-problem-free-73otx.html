Inspiration

Our Ipads and some of our are too small, but also too big. How do encounter the problem of them not fitting in our pockets, but also not as convenient as our computers. In addition, it gives users the ability to engage in cooperative activities on the go if the applications are hands free.

How it works

A pebble smart watch sends acceleration data which is then converted to positional data and sent to a smart phone

Challenges I ran into

Debugging, faulty communication between android and pebble, animation using androids canvas.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

This was our first time developing and android application and using pebble

What I learned

Android development, working with pebble, webrtc, using bluetooth

What's next for Hands free, Problem free

We are currently working on a multiplayer interface through peer to peer wify communication
