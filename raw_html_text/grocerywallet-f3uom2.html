Inspiration

University life is really busy and as students, we have to be really careful about our very limited budgets. GroceryWallet helps you save time and money by automatically giving you the best price for the items you need. You no longer need to spend hours finding the best deal for each item. It does everything for you.

What it does

It fetches all the items you need from a spreadsheet and gives you their price and their links.
