• Leave-A-Penny Take-A-Penny

Inspiration

The philosophy of 'pay it forward', and the 'take a penny, leave a penny' tray.

What it does

A 'take a penny, leave a penny tray' for ATMs in disaster areas to 'pay it forward' to those in need. Utilizes 'ADM's (Automated Donation Machine).

How I built it

Implementing Capital One api, Google Cloud hosting, Authorize.net payment platform, Here.com tracking, and DragonBoard for 'ADM' demo purposes

Challenges I ran into

Discovered the Capital One link we were given was to a discontinued api: https://www.programmableweb.com/api/nessie

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Perseverance

What I learned

Adaptation

Helping those in need
