Inspiration

We find it ineficient to apply to every job manually and to fill the same fields again and again.

What it does

You need a summer job or internship but you also need to prepare for exams and stop spending time on repeating the same thing? You can use our Chrome extension to apply to all of this jobs automatically. Use our intuitive UI to specify how many jobs to apply to and what jobs to search for.
