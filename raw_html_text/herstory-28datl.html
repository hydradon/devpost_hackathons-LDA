Inspiration

Earlier today we witnessed the 3rd annual women's march. This inspired us to create a platform in which social movements can carry their momentum beyond individuals days of action and organization.

What it does

Provide curated content regarding relevant news in women's rights history and a social platform for activists to connect with one another.

How we built it

iOS app built in Swift, design done in Figma.

Challenges we ran into

Completely changing our idea late into the hackathon and scrambling to make features work.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Fighting for a more equitable future! We're proud of the technology built as well.

What we learned

How to make apps more engaging, mobile app development, the power of organization.

What's next for HERstory

Get some users and keep this movement going!
