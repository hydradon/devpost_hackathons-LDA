Inspiration

What it does

It's a web site platform for children between 7 and 12 years old, to discuss about their dreams theirs ideas and their course. chatroom16.org, it's for two utilities use, the first it's an interface to watch videos suitable for children between 7 and 12 years old, and the second it's a live discussion interface.

How I built it

Trying to explain web technologies to people who never approach this field in their school curriculum, after i try to make an introduction for children to web technologies with a simple method that is the discussion with other children of their age
