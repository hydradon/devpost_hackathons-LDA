Inspiration

I was looking for ways to incorporate technology into the creation of art. I typically like to either remove or heighten one of the five senses. In this case, I heightened sound.

How it works

A program takes the frequency peaks and intensities and sends that information to an arduino. The frequency peaks are determined to be high, medium, or low, and each control a peristaltic pump. The intensity moves the pumps in the x-direction from an origin.
