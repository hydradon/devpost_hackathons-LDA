Inspiration

We wish to cultivate the idea of food appreciation through a software application. By combining our basic necessity and the modern days' technology, our project enables sharing and communication with food as the topic.

What it does

The main function of this application is to allow users to share photos of food. One of the interesting features of our project is that it can detect the basic food ingredients in the photos. User will be able to see a list of tags of different ingredients right below the each photo.
