Inspiration

Fitness motivation does not come naturally for most of us. We want to remedy this situation by building small (company level) fitness social networks where people can see the accomplishment of others and try to create new fitness records.

What it does

  • On our mobile app, user presses start button to initiate an exercise.
  • MoveSense starts to record activity level.
  • User presses stop button when completing his/her exercise.
  • The app then syncs the activity records from MoveSense, and push the data to the (private) cloud.
  • Results are made visible company-wide to promote other people setting new records.

What it may do in the future

  • MoveSense can also track stress level from employees using ECG measurement. The company can have deeper insight in employees' overall health and satisfaction. Many B2C possibilities such as challenges between family members, friends, kitty groups etc. Isn´t it revolutionising! Integration with A.I and machine learning
