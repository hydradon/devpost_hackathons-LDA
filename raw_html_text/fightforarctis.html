• FightForArctic

Inspiration

To solve the environmental issues today, is what will decide the destiny of the future. A great way to spread the warnings and let everybody know is to create a game that make you fight for Arctis, since the ice is melting and the temperature is higher there than ever before.

What it does

Mean pollutions and burning coals are attacking Arctis, you should grab your glowing ice-balls and save the great north. This will give you a fun time using VR and also learn to understand that Arctis is screwed.

How we built it

We built it by using Unity and writing our scripts with C#. And we used one image from ESA.

Challenges we ran into

Two team members, first time trying out Unity, came up with the idea/started by yesterday lunch, what do you think?

Accomplishments that we're proud of

To finished the game, and to make a great statement (also we love VR).

What we learned

That Unity is awesome and make it easy to accomplish really cool graphics.

What's next for FightForArctis

Combining the game-world with our favourite area: Deep Learning (CNN).
