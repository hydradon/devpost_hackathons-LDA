Hackathon-memelon

We decided to come up with the best domain name and with that domain name we wanted to incorporate a little game. The site loads 4 memes, when you click on them they change, you have to try to match the 4 memes as fast as you can and once you have them matched you can press spacebar to see how fast you were.
