Inspiration

The inspiration behind getaway is a personal experience I had last year where I planned out a trip to montreal for me and many friends. After finally getting there and no one knowing what they were doing, I could see there was a problem to be solved

How it works

It works by a user entering their starting and ending location, and their preferred method of transportation, and it calculates forms of transportation, accommodation, and entertainment for you.
