Inspiration

Working out is really important for your health. However, it is really difficult for a lot of people to be disciplined enough to go to the gym on a regular basis.

What it does

TeamFitness is a collaborative fitness app where users can team up with their friends to form fitness groups. Users can level up their group by consistently attending the gym according to a set schedule. To do this, a user can record their work out session duration (by pressing the starting and stopping buttons) whenever they are physically close enough to the gym.

How I built it

React Native & Firebase (which means that this works on both iOS and Android!)

Challenges I ran into

React Native's community is rather small so it's always kind of hard to look things up.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Completing this in one weekend.

What I learned

More React Native.
