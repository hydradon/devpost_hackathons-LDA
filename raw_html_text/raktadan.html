I was talking to one of my acquaintances one day when she told me a story from her past about how she had to gather courage gathering blood on a day when one of her loved ones was on the surgery table, when I thought about this platform which could connect hospitals to blood donors during an emergency.

Our platform lets blood donors register on our platform and gives them updates about the necessity of blood during an emergency.

We built the platform using react for front-end and firebase for the back-end.

Coming up with a working decoupled platform that won't put a lot of load on the server.

Coming up with the big picture of the entire platform is very important, before one actually starts developing.

Try making it a uniform platform across a country.
