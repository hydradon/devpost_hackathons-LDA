Inspiration

ARGH LABS is a French voice apps design studio. We are a small team of technology enthusiasts! Of course, fond of History and US History :)

What it does

The Before After Skill is a multiplayer game to relive the history of the world. The Before After game can be played alone, with family or friends. The Before After game is simple. Alexa asks to place two historical events between them. Each player has to answer with the words Before or After. Before After is free. You can play Before After with 50 free events. Players can also buy Premium Packs to play with new events.

How I built it

We design it following our ARGH LABS's method. We test it a lot with players. We develop sound design to give an immersive experience.

Challenges I ran into

The production of historical contents was a challenge. Especially because History is specific to each country. The story is written differently according to the nations ;)

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We are pretty proud to have succeeded in designing In Skill Purchase experience which is for us, the first application of real Voice purchase

What I learned

We have improved our knowledge on US History :)
