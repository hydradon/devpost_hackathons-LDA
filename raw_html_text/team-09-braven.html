NPO Name: Braven

Problem We Are Solving: Automating and simplifying the process of matching enrolled students and mentors efficiently. Giving a centralized platform to NPO, students, and mentors to manage their information and matches.

Inspiration

To solve scheduling problem for Braven NPO. It would save them some resources.

What it does

It efficiently matches interviewer with interviewee and schedules their interview. It also notifies concerned people when match is found.

How I built it

We designed and implemented a system in python to schedule student's interviews with volunteers having relevant interest.

Challenges I ran into

Port issue with RDS, Scheduling algorithm and many constraints imposed due to a very special use case.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We could complete all required tasks and more !!

What I learned

Lot of python, aws, flask fast coding, decision making, time management ....
