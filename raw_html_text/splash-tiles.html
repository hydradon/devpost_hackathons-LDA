Inspiration

This is a skill to support our Splash-Tiles cloud service.

What it does

SplashTiles, turn your Fire TV or Stick into a fully customized digital signage display including remote control!

SplashTiles allows you to connect all your Android TV boxes (Fire TV, Fire Stick, etc) to your Splash-tiles.com Cloud account.

Your SplashTiles Cloud account allows you to easily create customized "Screens" which include news, weather, stocks, IP cameras, Twitter, Facebook, sports scores, traffic, radar, photos, and much more. All the best of the Internet condensed into what you want to see!

You can bring up any of your "Screens" on any of your TVs with the touch of a button. You can even display these as PIP over other apps, like when you are watch Netflix!

SplashTiles Screens can be triggered remotely using Alexa Voice control, our Cloud server, 3rd party control systems (Control4, RTI, etc), or other services like IFTTT.

SplashTiles is also the perfect solution for your business Digital Signage needs! Remotely control all your displays with advanced scripting and scheduling built in.

SplashTiles Cloud is a low cost subscription service. You can try it for FREE with our 14 day FREE TRIALs. Signup today at Splash-tiles.com.

How I built it

The skill is built using the Smart Home API for Alexa using node.js.

Challenges I ran into

The Smart Home API is hard to debug (no error messages indicating what is wrong). The syntax on the data you send back must match the examples exactly.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The skill is easy to use and does everything we wanted it to.

What I learned

Not much, this one is very similar to our prior skills in design and implementation.
