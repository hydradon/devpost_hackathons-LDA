Inspiration

Introducing a new dimension into graphs to gain new insights.

What it does

Interfaces with Leap Motion controller to track hand movements to take control of 3D graphs. Hand gestures such as swiping and drawing circles are mapped to camera movements such as rotations and zoom.

How I built it

Uses plotly, Flask, Dash, Leap Motion SDK, Cockroach Labs DB, and Goldman Sachs Marquee API.

Challenges I ran into

Trying to combine apps running Python2, Python3, and Javascript

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Integrating various processes

What I learned

How to interface with hardware such as Leap Motion Controller

What's next for Data At Your Fingertips

Deploying a mobile app
