We decided to develop an Android application that would function as a portal for patients to self-diagnose their own mental well-being by going through a series of questions on a daily basis. The software is designed to evaluate the user's psychological state over time and provide medical advice or suggestions regarding prescription or over-the-counter drugs. We created a system to allow patients to create accounts on the application, so that their answers may be tracked over extended periods of time in order to provide the most accurate recommendations possible.
