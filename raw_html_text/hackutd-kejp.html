Spectral Receipts

Every time I go shopping I am always stuck with receipts that I end up throwing out. No matter where I walk I see receipts littered all around, at a campus, the mall, everywhere... This has been a problem for a long time and statistics online show that:

"Every year in the United States alone, we waste 1 billion gallons of water, 10 million trees, and 250 million gallons of oil on something that’s ultimately going to be thrown in the trash. In fact, paper receipts account for 1.5 billion pounds of environmental waste a year."

This is disturbing so we decided to make a way for companies to implement our app system so that customers can scan a QR Code generated after checkout and will be able to read all the receipts on the mobile device!
