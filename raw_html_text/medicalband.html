Inspiration

Talks and histories of our medical friends about lost elderly people that can´t be helped in a proper manner because of their mental diseases.

What it does

The main goal is to build an Android app that can read NFC bracelets that contain general and relevant medical information of the user and show it in a proper manner. The bracelets are destined mainly to elderly people (although they can be used by anyone) because they have higher probabilities (and higher current percentage) of getting mental diseases.

How we built it

We use android studio to develop the app, using nfc technology and nfc tags for testing.

Challenges we ran into

Being new to the use of nfc technology.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Coming with a good and useful idea accepted by the local medical area .

What we learned

to use new technologies

What's next for MedicalBand

make it available for everyone
