Inspiration

We wanted to redefine the term neighbor to expand to a greater area with our randomized chat system that is location based.

What it does

NeighborChat allows us to communicate with those in close proximity through video and chat.

How I built it

We collaborated using git and Node.js. We all were able to learn new and useful skills, including how to work together as a fluid team.

Challenges I ran into

We had a great deal of trouble synchronizing random accounts, but we think we've finally got it figured out.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Managing to build this project, regardless of the setbacks we encountered.

What I learned

How to use a wide variety of developer tools, and work together as a team.

What's next for NeighborChat

Bug fixes and improvements.
