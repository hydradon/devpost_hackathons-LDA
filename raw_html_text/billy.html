Inspiration We were inspired by oranges and we used our background in chemical engineering to create this beautiful device.

What it does

Rotates Billy the orange using batteries powered by oranges.

How we built it

Arduino, chemical engineering, electrical engineering, computer engineering. Just about everything in one package.

Challenges we ran into

Keeping Billy stable on the platform. Incorporating the hundreds of oranges into the project. Incorporating human touch into the arduino system.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Generating 8.4V and 2mA of battery to power the system.

What we learned

We learned how to use arduino and incorporate chemistry into our hardware.

What's next for Billy

Providing entertainment to all.
