The Problem

UCR current offers this dashboard . It only provides how many spots are open. This is hard to predict how hard it will be to find a place once you actually arrive.

Features

  1. Numbers of spaces available per space
  2. The delta of parking spaces in the last half an hour
  3. The average number of cars in the parking space by hour
  4. A map of the parking location
  5. Tells you which parking space you can park in based on your parking permit

Future Features

  1. Sortable by parking permit type
  2. Subscribe to an email list to send you an email every day with the expected traffic
