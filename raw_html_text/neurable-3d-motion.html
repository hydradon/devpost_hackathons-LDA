Inspiration

We wanted to make a big splash and show off some of latest things that can be done with latest in brain technology. We also plan on applying to the Disney Accelerator, to bring our technology to the masses ASAP. So we thought, what about rotating in Mickey Mouse in 3D in real-time with our brain. That would get their attention!

What it does

We are using the power of our minds to rotate Mickey Mouse in 3D space!

How we built it

We used Neurable's technology with Javascript and BCI 2000.

Challenges we ran into

Connecting all our systems together and dealing with the cumbersome BCI 2000.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Using our own technology and proving the world changing potential of Neurable!

What we learned

The world is at our finger tips and we are ready to make an impact!

What's next for Neurable 3D Mind Motion

We want to keep creating new and amazing brain-technology.
