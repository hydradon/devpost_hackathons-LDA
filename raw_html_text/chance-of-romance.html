Inspiration

We wanted to try something new and fun that could also be a unique approach to educating the player. Sneaking in resource management as a way to progress makes the player have to learn how to balance their finances and social life. Temoc and Enarc were also huge inspirations.

What it does

This is a text-based romance game where the player selects choices. This game also helps with financial literacy. It's a webapp game, runs on HTML.

How we built it

We coded the game mainly in JavaScript. We pulled, drew, edited, and took pictures for the images.
