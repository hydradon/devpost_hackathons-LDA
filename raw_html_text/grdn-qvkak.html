GRDN is a community marketplace for people to list, discover and book green spaces around Boston through mobile phones or the internet. Whether it is a raised based or a vertical garden for a few months, a 10,000 SF land plot for a year, or even private outdoor space for a picnic, GRDN connects people to unique experiences at any price point. GRDN also provides an easy way for people to monetize their outdoor or covered space and showcase it to an audience of users.

Why List

  • Host people on your land or into your home to earn money and meet people in your neighborhood
  • No Fee to List – 3% service fee when you confirm a reservation
  • You’re in Control – you set the price of your listing, your availability and reservation requirements for your guests. You can also set custom prices to earn more during popular grow seasons.
  • Communication is Easy – exchanging messages will help you select guests who are a good fit for your listing. You can review their profiles and the reviews they’ve received for more detail
  • You Have Our Support – we are launching on a localized level solely in Boston

Why Grow

  • Inability to access private green space in a city – re-evaluates that dynamic while making it enjoyable and communial
