Inspiration

We want to help and inspire others who are having trouble with dealing with emotional obstacles. As we grew older, we have all felt vulnerable at some point and wanted to talk with someone. We decided that we would like to help others like us and created an app that would give people opportunities to express their emotions. Many people struggle to find a starting point when finding help, which is why we developed this app and chat bot to assist people to get on the right path to their breakthrough. Takes information on how the person feels. It is supposed to then provide support and free counseling using a chat bot from IBM Watson-Assistance

How I built it

The app is built with Swift on Xcode. The chat bot was built with IBM cloud.

Challenges I ran into

Getting the buttons on app to function properly. Also getting started with chat bot.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Getting the chat bot to work.

What I learned

How to use xcode and IBM cloud

What's next for breakthrough

Improving the app by linking the chat bot to the app to make it more efficient. Adding more features such as a journal that can help the user to keep track of their progress. We would also like to make it accessible to all Americans and make it affordable for those who are struggling financially.
