Inspiration

We both participated in mousetrap car competitions in high school and it is a popular Science Olympiad event.

What it does

Allows users to choose different parts for their car and receive a score. In the future, we'd like to make more complex components and allow students to race their cars against each other.

How we built it

Android studio, blood, sweat, and tears.

Challenges we ran into

We are mechanical and materials science engineers and did not know how to code coming into this competition. Thanks Jason (our mentor) for help!

Accomplishments that we're proud of

As two engineers with 0 CS background, we actually built an app that could potentially be useful!

What we learned

Everything

What's next for Mouse-Mobile

More components, better graphics, collaborative racing, description of physics concepts.
