eduLEAP is an educational platform to leap into the future of learning. It is an interactive way to give students incentives to do well in the classroom. It also builds team work and relationships.

We built this application through Bootstrap, HTML, CSS, Javascript, and hosted on Heroku.

We had trouble making the application look aesthetically pleasing because none of us were proficient with front-end development. However, we are proud of our final product and the skills we learned along the way.

We learned how to leverage an interactive web application with HTML, CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap, and deployed with Heroku.

Our next steps for eduLEAP is to make the application responsive by changing the elements on the screen when new goals are added and when students complete assignments. Furthermore, we would listen on suggestions from users, especially teachers, and add features accordingly.
