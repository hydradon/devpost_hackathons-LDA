What it does

FlameMyo is a wave defense game in which you can summon spells and attacks with the wave of your arm! Don't let the spiders pass. Use spells and fireballs to destroy them. (flamemyo.tech)

How we built it

FlameMyo is powered by Unity 3D and the FlameMyo SDK.

Challenges we ran into

No one on the team had worked with Unity, and there was little experience in working with 3D models. This issue was prevalent, as the team made home made assets with the intention to use them for the game, but difficulties with blender and 3D modelling lead to us using free assets from the asset store.
