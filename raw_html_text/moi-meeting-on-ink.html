Inspiration

Collaboration and brainstorming activity. The use of WILL SDK allows a unique level of collaboration during meetings for better productivity and performance. I was looking for a better graphics representation in our meetings and conferences tool: MeetingRight

What it does

Interactive meeting tools for business and education

How I built it

WILL SDK, Cordova/Phonegap, node.js , S-Pen SD, Android native, Phonegap Plugins

Challenges I ran into

Android 5,0 devices or late. preferred powerful CPU for dual display

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The integration of a large system with many library and tools

What I learned

WACOM WILL SDK, S Pen SDK and events.

What's next for MOI (Meeting on Ink)

  • better network performance to supports hundreds of users,
  • different color for each user,
