Inspiration

Space is really cool.

What it does

It allows the user to experience our version of nightmares

How we built it

Using unity engine

Challenges we ran into

We restarted our project completely multiple times due to technological limitations and have been unable to move the player, so we adapted our project to what we had. Then Unity crashed and our project deleted itself.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to finish our scene and are really proud of the environment we created.

What we learned

We learned how to use new components in Unity, not to back down from challenges, and that we have to decide on one idea and stick to it. We also learned we need to make backups of our project in case of a crash.

What's next for Killer Balloons from Outer Space

Getting the balloons to attack the player and adding levels to the gameplay.
