Inspiration

As decentralized exchanges do not have any central limit order book, historical trade data is not widely available, which puts traders as well as investors at a great disadvantage compared to centralised exchanges.

What it does

KyberCharts aggregates historical Kyber Network trade data as stored on the Ethereum blockchain into OLHCV format and provides a user-friendly data pipeline for visualization and analysis of asset prices.
