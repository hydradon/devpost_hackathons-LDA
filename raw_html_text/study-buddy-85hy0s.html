Inspiration

Juan was sitting in Club Strozier with Andy. They were discussing the upcoming hackathon and potential ideas. After sharing ideas on an app that would do modern physics problems for the poor confused international affairs student in that course, we settled on a study social network. And so, Study Buddy was born.

What it does

Study Buddy gives students the opportunity to meet with other students in the same class as themselves. The user can search for students as broad as which library they are in or go as specific as searching for someone in a specific course. Students can directly message potential study buddies and discuss meeting up and studying together.

How we built it

We built it using Android Studio and a lot of luck. We are a team of beginners and only one of us has any experience with coding, but not with Java, which is what we used. We just did a lot of trial and error.
