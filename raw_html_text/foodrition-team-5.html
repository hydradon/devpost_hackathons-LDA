Inspiration

My wife is 8 months pregnant, last month during her check-up, we got to know that she is suffering from Gestational Diabetes and Iron Deficiency. We were pretty diligent in following a health diet, however there were a lot of things which we were missing.

What it does

It allows the user to take a pic of any food item (Fruits & Veggies) and gives useful insights on them, which helps the mum & baby stay healthy and safe.

How I built it

I used Messenger, pytorch and Nodejs to create the app.

Challenges I ran into

Data, Integration

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Learnt a lot, was able to complete the app

What I learned
