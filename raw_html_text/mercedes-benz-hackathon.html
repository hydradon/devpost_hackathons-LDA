We hate remembering where we park. Yet no simple solution exists. Compass alerts you within 100m of our parked cars, pointing you to where and how far away your car is.

We are paranoid about stuff happening when our cars are unattended. Compass records a live feed and alerts owners when it detects impact to our car. Allowing us to send footage to insurance companies.

We have trouble finding parking in the cities, especially San Francisco. Compass uses the dashboard and prompts us as we near our destination, integrating with Luxe, an on-demand valet service. This lets us meet the valet at our destination to handoff our car.
