Inspiration

Nerd wish fulfillment, be a wizard and throw fireballs in augmented reality

What it does

First, put on the AR headset. Now, your hands are on fire and you can shoot flames and fireballs to melt the approaching snowmen

How I built it

Unity, with effects content built in Houdini

Challenges I ran into

Meta is not consumer hardware and it shows.. Houdini's Vertex Animation Texture implementation had a bug.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Flaming hands feel cool :)

What I learned

How to use vertex animation textures
