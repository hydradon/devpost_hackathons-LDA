Zoom

Intelligent transport options powered by crowd sourced data. Zoom is a simple android application to help get Mexico city residents moving with ease. The prototype implemented includes basic functionalities of city guide to plan your journey based on the traffic, ease of travel, time, bus routes etc. Overall, the project presents a comprehensive understanding of how to realize the city on the go. With the app in hand you have access to all public transport information along with a wide range of transportation options, to help you map your journey in the best and most efficient way.
