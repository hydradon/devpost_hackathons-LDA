Inspiration

In Neuroscience research, the studies of neurovascular according to human feelings are being continued. But the ways are limited because they use methods which have some problems. First, they use measurement tools physically contacts the skin. Second, non-contact methods such as MRI which irradiates in a strong radiation to a subject. Also subjects should not move. The thermal imaging technology is a solution to overcome these problems. That is FLIR ONE. FLIR ONE is safe because it does not emit anything. And it can take the natural movement of the subject. So, if we use FLIR ONE, we can see the human feelings. Then, how could we know human feelings!? We need funny and cool idea!!

What it does

"FACE ONE with FLIR ONE" can show the emotion of the subject by the character , Fofo which named by the first alphabets of the app name. If a face is detected, "FACE!!" message is shown in the left corner of the screen. Then the user can take a picture and the emotion is analyzed by the face temperature, the movement of mouth and eyes. According to the emotional state, this app shows Fofo which have facial expressions - big smile, so-so, depressed. The picture is saved with Fofo and can be shared by SNS.

How we built it

We used Android Studio, Java, Android and FLIR ONE (for android).

Challenges we ran into

Choosing the subject was the most difficult point of this project. We discussed over and over which app can be used easy and entertains users. As a result, "FACE ONE with FLIR ONE" was selected. During the development, we ran into the challenge to define the algorithm that this app to detect a face and analyze the emotion by thermal image. Also synchronizing the detected face and the FLIR’s thermal image to prevent each frame was little hard. However we did it and FACE ONE is the result of these.
