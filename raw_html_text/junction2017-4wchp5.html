SmartLid

Have you ever been in the store with the flour bag in your hand wondering if you have enough flour back home in the pantry or not? We know we have!

By using our smart lid together with our web application you will receive accurate information about the amount of flour back home and you will never be confused in the store again! With our smart lid and web application you will keep track of what you have back home and always know the amount. Our application goes beyond this and offers you your favourite and new recipes based on what you in storage. Integrating this device into your life will make food planing a piece of cake.
