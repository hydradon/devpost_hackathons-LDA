Inspiration

We always wanted to test ourself, and our musical culture, on our music library. Why not using the Spotify library?

What it does

The user log in with his Spotify credentials, he gets to see a list go his playlists, he can select one, and a Blind-Test will be launched, (e.g) 10 random songs will be played, one after the other. The user must recognize the artist or the track's name (or both, for more points) of as many songs as possible!

How we built it

We used node-js as a back-end technology.
