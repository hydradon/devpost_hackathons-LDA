Camping Forecast

Camping Forecast is an Android app that receives the weather for any given location and recommends what camping gear you should pack. Users can choose a location on a map, or input geographic coordinates. The app can extract weather data from both the past and the future from these locations. Camping Forecast will generate tailored gear recommendations for any situation.
