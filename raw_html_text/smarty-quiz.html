Inspiration

My family and I love watching trivia shows on TV, so I decided to build a skill that let's you test your knowledge on different topics.

What it does

I asks you some random questions from different topics and you have pick the right answer. You will have a different experience based on the device you are using.

How I built it

This skill was developed in Node.js and utilizes the new APL components.

What's next for Smarty Quiz

I will be adding more interesting and fun questions.
