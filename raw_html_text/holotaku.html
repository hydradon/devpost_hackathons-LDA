This was an opportunity to explore shared Augmented reality experiences. Right now this is an early concept demo. It shows the fun of doing battle with “drones” indoors— something they would never actually let us do. The biggest challenge in shared Augmented reality is sharing the virtual part in a robust and accurate way. There were many subtle problems just getting a couple of HoloLenses to connect! We were able to get them connected and enable controller assisted virtual combat in the real world.
