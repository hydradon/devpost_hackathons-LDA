CheKins is an iOS app made to provide convenience to students who are looking to find there SI leaders sessions time in a centralized interface. Allow students to subscribe to there classes and thus have access to live session times and locations of the classes respective to the SI leader right from there phone. Would contain updates in case the SI leader has to reschedule or cancel.
