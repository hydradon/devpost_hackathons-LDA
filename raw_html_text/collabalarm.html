Inspiration

We nearly missed our train on our way to Brown. I had to keep calling my friends to wake them up! That is why we built CollabAlarm.

What it does

CollabAlarm allows you to setup an alarm for the entire team and ensure that the whole team wakes on time every time.

How we built it

We built a native Android application with Spring running in the background to allow the phones to communicate and setup a group alarm.

Challenges we ran into

The network operations!

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Putting up a working app at the end of it!

What we learned

Native Android development

What's next for CollabAlarm

Make the group alarms more interesting by using voice messages from the user who first wakes up as a ringtone for the others who are still asleep.
