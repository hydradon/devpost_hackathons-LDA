Candy Battle is a MUMT Game for touch devices especially (at the moment) for Windows 10. Grab as much candies as you can with the Candy-Eaters. Swipe with your finger from the blue or red (two player mode) bar in the direction you want to move your Candy-Eater and hopefully he will grab the candy. Different stages require different strategies to grab the candy. It supports one or two player mode.
