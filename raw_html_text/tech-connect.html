What it does

Tech Connect is designed to connect event organizers, volunteers, and participants together to increase participation in technology-centered events (ie, hackathons, workshops, robotics events, and other hands-one experiences). This kind of technology also has to potential to open up these events to a wider audience, encouraging more diversity.

How we built it

The app was created in Android Studio, written in Java, and used Firebase for the back-end.

Challenges we ran into

A learning curve with Android Studio and Firebase

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Completing the project with time to test and tweak for improvements
