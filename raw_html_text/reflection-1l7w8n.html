Problem/ inspiration: Under utilization of time and space A need to stay in touch with current news and credible news. Government's Smart Nation Plan to move Singapore being a smart nation, to complement this initiative by ensuring rapid information dissemination in times of emergency.

What it does: User or Institutions can choose to reflect real-time information regarding Pollutant Standards Index, MRT Breakdown, Weather, Fire Evacuation Notice and Map, Current News Headlines, and the person looking at it's reflection.

How we built it: 1) Cut a wooden frame via laser cutting to hold our product. 2) Assembled a wooden structure to simulate the time and space wasted in an elevator. 3) Programmed the rasberry-pi and connected it to a tablet. 4) Put them together and Tadaaa

Challenges we ran into: Lacking of technical expertise.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of: A prototype that is up and running soon Working with my invaluable teammates :)

What I learned: Woodworking Laser cutting Rasberry-pi programming Prototyping mindset

What's next for Reflection: Individual Level usage Centralising Information with Various Government Ministries. Institution level usage City-wide scale implementation
