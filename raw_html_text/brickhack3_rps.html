What it does 

One of our key points was to use simple machine learning to build an AI that would learn its opponent and win a majority of the time at the classic game of "Rock, Paper, Scissors". We decided to use Leap Motion Technologies to record a human vs AI, or even Human vs Human. It's an interactive game that ends up being tons of fun.

What's next for Leap RPS 

-Improved AI algorithms
-Collecting and analyzing much more data
-Possibly porting to a mobil application
