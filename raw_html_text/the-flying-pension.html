Inspiration

When people express their political opinions a rough state can cancel their pension. Hong Kong 2019 is an example

What it does

It gives you a annual pension after your retirement. For your whole life!

How I built it

I studied the actuarial science of annuities (technical term for "pensions") and created a simulation with google spreadsheet and javascript. I checked my algorithms against a statistical package in R that calculates annuities

Challenges I ran into

I had missinterpreted a part of the formula and needed to analyse step by step how the R package calculates the annuity

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I showed that a decentralized censor resistant pension is possible

What I learned

A tonn about actuarial science

What's next for The Flying Pension

Finally a solidity version must be implemented. This will be done during Berlin Blockchain Week.
