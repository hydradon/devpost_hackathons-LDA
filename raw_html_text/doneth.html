• Add members to a shared wallet, they can only withdraw their share of the funds

  • Embed the wallet on your website to easily accept donations

Inspiration

After working on another open source project with a tram of four I wanted to add a donation address to the project website. I realized it might be difficult to have a single wallet for the whole project—who would hold the key, manage the withdraws, figure out how much goes to who etc. Thus was born Doneth and that's what Doneth does for you and your team.

What it does

Don.eth is a platform to help you create shared funds that are easily self-manageable. This allows for unique governance directly controlled by the user. Use Don.eth to fund your side project and distribute funds to those contributing to development based on percentage shares.

How we built it

The front end is Vue.js using web3 to deploy solidity contracts and communicate with them afterwards. We're hosted with netlify and all other code logic is hosted on the blockchain. We used truffle to compile and test our solidity code.
