Inspiration

We were inspired by how loud we could get while playing video games and wanted a way to let us know before people complained.

What it does

The device mutes all sound from a device if the user is being too loud, thus letting them know. The volume will become unmuted once the volume decreases to an acceptable level.

How I built it

The setup was built using an Arduino to control the microphone and noise detection while a raspberry pi was used to simulate audio.
