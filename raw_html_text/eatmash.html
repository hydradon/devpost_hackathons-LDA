Inspiration

When you go to the another country, sometimes you find it hard to know what kind of foods are popular and famous actually. EatMash helps you enjoy local foods that color your trip with lots of local cuisine.

What it does

EatMash makes a great plan for breakfast, launch, and dinner to enjoy local foods for tourist.

How we built it

Develop API server with Node.js and mobile app with Ionic

Challenges I ran into

We have never experienced ionic development.

What I learned

How to develop mobile apps with web technologies like HTML5, CSS/CSS3, JavaScript(Angular.js)
