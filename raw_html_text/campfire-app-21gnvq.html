• Campfire logo

We wanted to create a system that would allow people to communicate within their community. We decided to go with the idea of story telling because it allows people to share experiences and see who else in the community has had the same experiences. We chose the theme of campfire because socialization started around sharing stories. We wanted to create a system that can be used by all the age groups and through which they wouldn't feel isolated by listening and sharing stories.
