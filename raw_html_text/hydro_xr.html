Inspiration

it takes up 48 hours to fix a water Leakage issue and this cause a huge water lose per year. the obstacles includes identifying the issue, localizing it, identify the right equipment , apply the fixes.

What it does

A VR solution that will enable the Maintenance Technician to identify the issue when the sensors sense it, display the readings, show the location and help to apply some fixes that will stop the water lose. Basically, it will give the Maintenance Technician all the required information

How we built it

We have created a sample DMA Pipe Network. The user can visit one of the central location and apply the fixes. these fixes is reflected in the physical demo.

Challenges we ran into

  • Understanding the different issues that cause this huge water leakage.
  • Controlling the IoT devices using Oculus Quest (6DoF).

Accomplishments that we're proud of
