bigmarketpricer

I like to search for good bargains on Craigslist and Ebay, particularly computer parts. I have always wished to make this process easier, and not require clicking through millions of listings.

Therefore, I decided to create a webapp to make finding good deals easier. BMP allows users to compare prices on craigslist to the equivalent average going price of the item on eBay, which is in turn, adjusted for the eBay seller fees of 10%.

Additionally, BMP gives direct links to these "good deal" listings.
