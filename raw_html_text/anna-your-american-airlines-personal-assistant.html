What's next for Anna: Your American Airlines Personal Assistant

To continue growth we want to extend the use of the product past the flights needs and deliver a great experience from beginning to end, this can be through city transportation, travel guides, and accommodations. We also personalize it by connecting you to friends, and past experiences. Finally with this data we want to enhance meaningful recommendations and guide you to make transactions through the platform during your trip.
