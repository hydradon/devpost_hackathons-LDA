Inspiration

Be a part of your dog's life in the smart way.

What it does & How we built it

The smartDog connects your dog via smart devices to the internet of things to provide the dog owner with real time information about your dog.

We used Raspberry Pi with sensors to simulate the smart devices attached to your dog. Along with the Microsoft Azure IoT solution and socket.io with nodeJS we developed a prototype of an user friendly smart product.

smartDog sends health critical notifications of your dog and also walk routes and poop spots so you can get there and collect the poop even if your dog is out of your sight. :)

What's next for smartDog

With machine learning algorithms with input data like food intake, daily walk distance, daily walk time, and last pooping time the smartDog will be able to understand the behaviour of your dog to give the owner smart feedback.
