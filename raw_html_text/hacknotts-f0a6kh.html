As students, how we budget our money is very important, but a skill many of us lack. Avoiding your banking app to not have to see how far into your overdraft you are is easy - that's why brandonbot is needed.

BrandonBot doesn't take any of your cr*p. Deliveroo-ed dinner for the 2nd night in a row? BrandonBot will let you know just how angry he is with you via the choice of angry gif.

As well as real-time reactions to your spending, we incorporated machine learning for trends and predictions in your spending, so as well as knowing your current balance it can forecast just how much you'll be spending each day of the week.

We offer support for two popular mobile banking applications: Starling and Monzo (that's right, cross platform users, much wow)
