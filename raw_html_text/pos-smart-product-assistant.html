Inspiration

Not all mortgage products are created equal. Some have more-stringent guidelines than others. This makes mortgage product selection tedious. Our inspiration was to bring out a smart solution that helps customers to fetch the best competitive product.

What it does

  1. Assess the situation of borrowers.
  2. Consider loan options.
  3. Compare lenders and estimate.
  4. Understand loan cost and fees.

Send product requests to multiple lenders and analyze them, finally short list the products and list down the smart products as well as trending product to a customer.
