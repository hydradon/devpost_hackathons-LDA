Our digital world today is controlled by our smartphones. So we thought of applying that concept to our Physical devices at Home by controlling them through our Phones. So the idea of home automation came up.

It uses Arduino and two mobile phones to control Lights, Fans and Door wirelessly.

We planned this only one day prior and executed it pretty well.

Arduino coding, sensors

Control of other devices used at home and finding a way to personalize it according to our needs.
