• Lumbr.net

Inspiration

The original name was inspired by a funny story of my partner wearing a lumber-esque outfit. The project idea came about when we realized how unhealthy eating Ramen every day as a college student could be.

What it does

It helps to manage student lives by simplifying aspects of day to day life.

  • Overall Health
  • Nutrition
  • Planner
  • Sleep Management
  • Financial Management

How we built it

  • HTML & CSS was built from the ground up.
  • Graphics were built from scratch using gravit.io.

Challenges we ran into

  • Building the site from nothing
  • Integrations
  • Color Schemes
  • Overall layout of website

Accomplishments that we're proud of

  • The overall design and concepts
  • Speed
  • Teamwork

What we learned

  • Plan more thoroughly before beginning build

What's next for Lumbr.net
