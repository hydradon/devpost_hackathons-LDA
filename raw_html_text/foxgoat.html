Inspiration

To create a math and physics calculator to assist students by showing a step by step explanation.

What it does

The calculus portion of the program takes in an derivative equation and the program outputs a step by step explanation of the process. The other parts include the solution to common physics concepts such as forces, energy and projectile motion.

How we built it

The project was built using eclipse.

Challenges we ran into

We ran into countless amount of challenges during the process. Such as coming up with logic behind calculating derivatives and implementing the derivative rules.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Although our program may not be fully implemented, we are still very proud of everyone who worked on the program and it took a lot of effort to have created what we have right now.

What we learned

We learned about how much effort it took to create and implement one of these calculators. We also learned about our abilities to come together, share ideas, and work as a group to try to create this program. With this experience, we were able to delve deeper into the concept of Strings manipulation.

What's next for FoxGoat(δ,ε)

To perform limits and integral calculation, implement the math portion of the program with the physics portion.
