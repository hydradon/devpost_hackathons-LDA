What it does

Tracks and analyzes the news websites the user visits to find their implicit bias and allow them to potentially take action by being informed about different companies' views and diversify their news sources. Users can also submit new companies and news sources alongside their political affiliations. These are stored in a firebase database to be manually curated and added to a master database.
