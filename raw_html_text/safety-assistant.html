Inspiration

Travel crash kills more people than flu or gun violence, in VA alone, 750 people die on the roadways every year. To improve travel safety and save lives.

What it does

Intersection dilemma zone warning, Alert drivers to slow down or stop when the signal ahead is about to change to yellow by consuming Signal Phase and Timing (SPaT) data.

Applicable

  • High-speed approaches (> 50 mph)
  • Lots of heavy trucks
  • The intersection is located on a downgrade (sag curve)
  • Or when the signalized intersection ahead has limited sight distance, is at an unexpected location, or a combination of these

How I built it

Javascript, leafletjs

Challenges I ran into

Detailed documentation/specification for signal data attributes

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Better knowledge of open source Signal Phase and Timing (SPaT) data

What I learned

Tons of available open source data, great potential for applications

What's next for Safety Assistant

Further development, workable app deployed in the smartphone
