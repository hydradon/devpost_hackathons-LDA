Inspiration

There are plenty of workout websites, but most just help you track what you already know. Now some sites give you strict workout regiment or give you general workouts for everyone. However, for all their talk about not being static and get to work, that is actually what their suggestions are, static.

What it does

Our site takes that next step and bases the workout and nutrition off of your previous workout data and results, then uses machine learning to help you maintain your progress.

How I built it

The web app use a backend of node.js and was mostly built with React. Then the machine learning is processed and analyzed by a mix of DBscan clustering algorithm. The whole project is hosted using Compute Engine on GCP.
