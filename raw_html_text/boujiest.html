Team: Eileen Buenaflor, Taylor Todd, Charles Vosguanian

As college students in California, the word "boujee" has popped up on our radar due to Migos new song "Bad and Boujee." With that being said, we decided to create a website, containing a short 10 question quiz, to rank your "boujiness". Our website features small and fun images, as well as clever phrases to aid the quiz. Since this is all of our first hacks, we created our website using JavaScript and HTML/CSS. Enjoy!

*created on macOS (some fonts are Mac only)
