What it does

A mobile app that does data capture, processing and communication. Most medical measuring instrument have seven - segment digital display, by taking an image of it and using optical character recognition we are able to extract the data from it. Once the data is gathered it will be sent to a cloud, where it can easily and conveniently be sent to doctors/medical experts.
