Inspiration : We were exploring Drexel University, when we found a place called Hair House. It was a hair salon on campus! Imagine if we could make money based off the small skills people in their college have.

What it does : It is a place to find your school and find the students who are offered a type of service at school, whether that be hair stylist, makeup professional and/or eyebrow threader.

How I built it : I built it using Domain.com.

What's next for Broke College Kids : I would like to add a database with all the schools and all the students in the school offering service.
