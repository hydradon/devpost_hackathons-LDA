Inspiration

Living in a fast paced "Twitterized" world where 140 characters are enough to explain most of the things, Smart Sum Up can give a comprehensive as well as a concise gist of a document, thus saving time and effort. With rapid growth in the amount of information, it has become difficult to access the same efficiently. Thus, Smart Sum Up, in this senario, can help us in a number of ways

What it does

Click a picture of some text and Smart Sum Up will give you a short summary of the same.

How we built it

We built this using Python and Microsoft Azure.
