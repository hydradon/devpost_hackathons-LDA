Tracking your food as easy as a tip on a button. Enhancing your lifestyle has never been easier and more fun than with TrackID. With one single photo of your food, the app computes the nutritions in the photo and gives you customised suggestions to reach your specific goal (may it be losing weight, becoming healthier or gaining muscles). It predicts the ETA to your body reaching full fitness.
