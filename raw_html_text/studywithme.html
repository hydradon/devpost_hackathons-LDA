Study With Me

Study With Me is a student profile web app that brings students together on their campus to find study buddies and study groups to make the most out of their study sessions in all of their classes.

The inspiration behind Study With Me came from the realization that it can be really difficult to find someone to study with in your own class because of the sheer size of classes in most university settings. Along with how busy some students can be, Study With Me takes the work out of finding someone to prepare and study with for classes and exams.

This text you see here is actually written in Markdown! To get a feel for Markdown's syntax, type some text into the left window and watch the results in the right.

Tech

Study With Me uses HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, JSON and Firebase to make it what is it. This helps create a smooth web app with a simple interface.
