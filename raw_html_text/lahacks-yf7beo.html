We used html, css, javascript, google maps API, and google fusion tables to create a website that would bring up locations of bike racks. This project is meant to promote eco-friendly behavior by allowing people to see where the nearest bike racks are located. We've noticed that in our community and especially in silicon valley/ college culture, biking has become an integral part of life. This, however, is sometimes offset by not being able to find parking for your bike, and makes it difficult for people to bike around.
