Inspiration

Story Creation engages all age groups which selected this as our main idea for the competition. Also Introduction of Alexa Presentation Language and to know about how it will accommodated in Alexa Devices and The Price Amount.

What it does

Story Creator is a random story generator. In this skill you can either select a story or Alexa will select a story for you. Alexa will then ask you for 3-5 questions and then use your answers to generate a fun story.

Think fast and speak clearly! If you take too long or stutter Alexa might mishear you and include her own goofy word. This Story Creator skill will provide hours of family fun. Right now we are providing 10 pretty good stories and we will be updating the story line with time.

How I built it

Build it using Alexa developer console, Lambda Function and APL in nodejs

Challenges I ran into

Since APL is a new concept, we came across few issues related to APL like SpeakItem command with the AutoPage command not giving the expected output. Testing was also very hard as developer console was not reliable.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Was able to create a good skill with learning and implementation of APL in short span of time.

What I learned

  1. New concept of APL
  2. Creating a Skill with better User Experience
  3. Developing a Multi Model Skill on All Alexa Devices
