Arrived somewhere new? Need Directions? No Data? You can use our app to get directions, even with no data plan!

But how does it work? Good question! We utilise SMS messages in the background to communicate with our server, to deliver an app experience with turn by turn directions, all without a data connection. Simply enter a destination (And optionally a starting point) and within a few seconds you have your directions!

What if I don't have the app? You can still use our services SMS only, we will just send you back a text containing the directions!
