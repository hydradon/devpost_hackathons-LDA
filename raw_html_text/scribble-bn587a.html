Inspiration

Wanted to design an interactive app for everyone to use.

What it does

You can draw freehand, draw on an image, save to your camera roll, share to twitter, and follow scribble tweets online.

How I built it

objective-c in XCode

Challenges I ran into

Having never made an app before, this app challenged us in many ways. We struggled to add all the features we wanted and had to divert and focus on the major points of our app.
