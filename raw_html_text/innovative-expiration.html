Inspiration

1/3 of food supply becomes food waste, mostly coming from expired food.

What it does

Connects the retailers offering discounted expiring food to consumers watching their wallet. We reduced this mountain of wasted food with the service our app provides.

How I built it

Use Adobe Illustrator to design the user interface. Use Ionic to develop a web or app application. Use Geolocation API to localize where the user is.

Challenges I ran into

Developing the presentation structure. Collecting and displaying enough information for the development platforms.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Developed a three-win situation for the app to be based off of between retailers, consumers, and the app. We're proud we started to solve this serious problem of wasting food.

What I learned

How to think as a team to solve problems. Thinking more outside the box to solve the problems that our community faces.

What's next for Innovative Expiration

Make a negation with several retailers to begin the expansion of our app.
