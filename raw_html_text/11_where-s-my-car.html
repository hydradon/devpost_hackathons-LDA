Inspiration

The struggle of searching your towed car in every detention center and then physically going around looking for your car among thousands of other cars

What it does

it'll search in the database and find you the location of your car.

How we built it

we used HTML, CSS, javascript, node.js, express.js, MongoDB, Mongoose

Challenges we ran into

saving data and extracting data from the database.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

making the web app work as we intended that is solving a major problem and be profitable.

What we learned

teamwork, working under pressure, code in new frameworks, and connect the interface with the database.

What's next for 11_where's my car

convincing the people who run car towing services that where's my car will save a lot of time and paperwork
