Inspiration

We were inspired by the challenge that Tyomarkkinatori set out. As a team this junction we all wanted to work on a problem that affected our community.

What it does

Our service's core feature is helping people get to where they want to go in life. Through our service we want to connect education partners with people with aspirations for self improvement or users we've identified in "endangered careers" onto a path that insures employ ability.

How we built it

We built this using data from two that the productivity tacks partners, ESCO and Duunitori for relevant job data and a bit of algorithm magic to give the data meaningful insight
