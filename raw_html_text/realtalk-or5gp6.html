"I WOULDN'T KNOW WHERE TO GO, TRUTHFULLY. IF I GOT SEXUALLY ASSAULTED..."

Statistics show that 1 in 5 women will experience rape or attempted rape within her lifetime, and that a woman has a heightened risk of experiencing rape while in college (Jozkowski, 2015). Many institutions focus on prevention through affirmative sexual assault education but the path to sexual assault resources and support groups is usually unclear and confusing.

What it does

Gives students a safe space and anonymous "face" to chat with about their experience. Directs students to sexual assault resources.

How we built it

We built our chat bot with php, ajax, and jQuery.

Challenges we ran into

Finding the right framework to use.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Our idea is solving a really critical and important problem that affects many young adults.

What we learned

AI is hard.

What's next for RealTalk.

  1. Improving AI and conversations.
  2. Developing a way for students to report incidents through the app.
  3. Build trust and confidence in the institutions that provide these resources.
