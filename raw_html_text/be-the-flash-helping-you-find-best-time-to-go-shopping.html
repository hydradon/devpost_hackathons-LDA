Inspiration

No one ever said they love waiting in lines for something. Today in the bay area we spend a significant amount of time waiting in lines at grocery stores, restaurants, shopping malls apart from waiting in traffic to go home. After spending 30-45 min of my day waiting - I wanted to create a LIFEHACK that can tell myself or anyone about the best time to go shopping thus saving time.

What it does

It tells when is the best time to visit a store by providing time taken to go there via car, walk, bike and transit

How I built it

Getting busy scores from stores and then showing it using ReactJS

Challenges I ran into

Getting busy times at a particular store. Very hard to get this GOLD MINE!

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Breaking things and fixing them, Helping others find their next travel adventure, Building lifehacks with friends

What I learned

On how to create solutions that benefit everyone
