We like mobile apps

What it does

Our app connects people of various ethnic backgrounds who are looking for help or community engagement with others who desire to better their communities through kindness and helping others. The app provides a start to communication that has the potential to bring about real relationships and a sense of belonging in a community.

The app also provides a helpful page for resources that individuals can view to see the many opportunities to get involved in their area, and takes advantage of Google's vision and translation API to help individuals translate the real world and learn to pronounce things as they see them.

The app is available in 64 languages to provide maximum support to all.

How we built it

We built the app using Ionic Framework to produce a quick, yet sleek app. We used Nest.js for our backend API and stored data in MongoDB. We took advantage of Firebase for push notifications to the application.

Challenges we ran into
