Inspiration

Line cooks in the food industry are constantly creating delicious meals for the front end waiters to deliver to their impatient customers. Traditionally, line cooks would use a paper on a rack that can because many issues in terms of efficiency such as having to re read the paper in the middle of food preparation because the line cooks forgot a specific detail, having to decrypt the piece of paper due to hand writing and losing the ability to use both hands and eyes while cooking and reading an order. OrderFlow provides a unique way to receive these orders in the most efficient and effective way possible, voice. ​
