• Hi there! Baby Yoda, it is me!!

Inspiration

Disney+'s The Mandalorian has taken the internet by storm. Alongside, Baby Yoda has garnered his claim to meme fame. With all the adorable Baby Yoda meme's, I have been inspired to turn myself into Baby Yoda.

What it does

Places a Baby Yoda mask on a recognized facial structure on Instagram through their filters feature.

How I built it

  • Watched a bunch of YouTube tutorials.
  • Tinkered with Photoshop and Blender
  • Uploaded the Baby Yoda image asset that I created onto Spark AR
  • Uploaded the Spark AR filter that I created

Challenges I ran into

  • Making the mask go beyond the facial borders
  • Making the 3D object (Baby Yoda's head)
  • Aligning the 3D object (Baby Yoda's head) with my head

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

  • Finished the project within the time span of the hackathon
  • Learning new tricks in Blender
  • Learning about Spark AR and its functionalities

What I learned

  • Blender and Spark AR

What's next for Baby Yoda, I am

  • 3D facial movements (user opens mouth, blinks, frowns, etc. and Baby Yoda mimics)
