Minesweeper!!!

Description: It is an Arduino based game, follows the same rules just like the Microsoft Minesweeper. Use a TFT touchscreen to display the game. Use a joystick and a pushbutton to control.(flag/unflag, open). Just like the original Minesweeper, touch on the smiling face to restart.

Category: hardware, Arduino, game

Seat: I seat at the table next to the window of the presentation room.
