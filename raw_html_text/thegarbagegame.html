Inspiration

We wanted to let people know that everyone can make a change in the environment! Inspired by Shanghai's environmentally friendly frenzy, we decided that we could also make a change!

What it does

It's not that hard to trash an item into the correct category. The problem is: How could we inspire users to develop a habit of disposing of the trash into the correct category.

How we built it

Unity works Miracles! We used the Unity engine to develop this simple game to provide background knowledge on the types of garbage and how to dispose of them.

Challenges we ran into

There were some collaboration issues and time constraint issues that we ran into. We also did some hardcore debugging to ensure that it worked nicely and smoothly.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

At first, we were proud of mustering the courage and determination to enter the hackathon. But after we got in, we thought differently. Hmmm, it sounds like an accomplishment leaving a positive impact (no matter how slight) on the world!

What we learned

We learned game development and time management skill. Last and not least, hacking is fun!

What's next for TheGarbageGame

More resources, more elements, more fun!
