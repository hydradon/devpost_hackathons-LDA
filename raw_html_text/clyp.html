Inspiration

When sending links to yourself through email is tiring, Clyp can simplify this process.

What it does

Paste your links you find on phone or laptop to our web app or android app, and you can find your links on the other device!

How I built it

I used Android Studio and Java to program the app. I also used Firebase's API to store info on the users, such as link info or account info.

Challenges I ran into

Getting started with Android Studio for the first time, and setting up the Firebase API.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Making the mock app design.

What I learned

Android development.
