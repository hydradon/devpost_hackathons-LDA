Inspiration

I wanted to apply computational methods to gene expression in a brain cancer (glioblastoma multiforme, GBM) that has very poor prognostics. The patient outcome for this cancer has not improved significantly based on literature for the past few decades.

What it does

Predicts the survival status (alive vs dead) of a patient diagnosed with GBM at a particular time point

How I built it

Sweat, tears, caffeine, desperation.

Challenges I ran into

Most of my team dipped at 6 PM, so I had to work on the data processing mostly alone.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned

How to scrape web data and use pandas.

What's next for Gene Prognostics for Glioblastoma Multiforme

Creating a gene signature based on the individual gene data for a more comprehensive prognostic tool.
