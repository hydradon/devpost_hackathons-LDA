How I built it

  1. Identify some buildings which are at least 250 ft.
  2. Check the structural integrity of such buildings. Also check the safety measures for the same.
  3. Identify population density of the region.
  4. Divide the region into small subsets or cells based on population density. For example the New York City should have more such cells.
  5. Now install cellphone antenna in the identified buildings at the minimum height of 200 ft. Give them a Power Supply as well as have array of Battery for backup.
  6. Installing 4 pairs of set of antennas. Each pair should contain One transmitter and Two Trans-receiver and should be at an angle of 2° with respect to y-axis or 88° with respect to earth.
