What it does

Business cards are great, but don't you wish you could do more with them? With our unique cARds, your company can issue cards along with a username and query a top secret database, specific to your business, only accessible through the card.

Simply scan the card, log in and bam, info!

How we built it

App created using Unity / Vuforia, backend with Node.js, frontend with js

Challenges we ran into

Figuring out how exactly the cards would operate. We decided upon a single card per business with a small user ID to be displayed in the lower corner, this prevented the need for a new image per employee as differences in histogram charts would not be significant enough to cause issues.
