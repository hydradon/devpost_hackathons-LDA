We wanted to fill a void in the calculator space. Most graphing utilities do not generate 3D graphs and we saw a feature that is essential to some careers. We also wanted to expedite the process of doing certain calculations in physics, chemistry, and 2d graphing - or solving one-variable equations - so we created this set of programs to do that job. We'll expand more on this in our presentation.
