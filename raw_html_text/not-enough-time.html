Story

We've already seen squarespace, freshbooks and many apps targeted to help small business get off the ground and make their businesses run smoothly.

But what about business cards? The cornerstone of face to face communication. Let's help you design them quickly, and easily and get back to what's really important.

This minimalist application is written using modern web technologies such as ES6, semantic HTML5 and CSS3. Future integrations could include providing printing and emailing.
