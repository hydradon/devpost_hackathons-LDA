Inspiration

We used Andriod Studio and Microsoft Azure Pipelines.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We all learned a lot about everything that we did even though not much went as planned. We leaned to work better with our team and got a prototype working.

What we learned

We learned a lot about sql, microsoft azure and andriod studio.
