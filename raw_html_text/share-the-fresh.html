Inspiration

This project was inspired by my work volunteering with the homeless as well as my experiences keeping track of what I eat.

Want to promote good nutrition while helping feed the homeless? Its easy, just follow these 5 simple steps.

  1. Take a photo of your groceries

  2. Upload your photo to Share the Fresh

  3. Edit your grocery list

  4. Enter your daily nutritional goals, you can use our calculators if you're not sure

  5. Check out your custom meal plans and also see what extra food you bought that you can donate. We'll even connect you with local food pantries and homeless shelters in your area
