Inspiration

We came to this hackathon with no idea and little to no developing experience. But we knew we had the potential for greatness. Assembled within this team are the people most capable of coming up with useless ideas. We decided to put this untapped potential to use in this hack. The bomb simulator.

What it does

It counts down, It beeps, It does everything a bomb does except the part that make a bomb a bomb, the bomb.

How we built it

We used an arduino to make the bomb simulator and used a raspberry pi to add remote accessibility to the bomb simulator

Challenges we ran into

No experience in hardware. At all.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Connecting to the wifi. Making the raspberry pi turn on. Adjusting the contrast on the display

What we learned

Hardware is hard.

What's next for Bomb Simulator
