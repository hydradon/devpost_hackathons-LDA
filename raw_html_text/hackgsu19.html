Wuphf

Wuphf is an app that lets people connect with local animal shelters to find dogs to walk. This app allows users to "check out" dogs for a length of time and care for them. This helps take the burden off of shelters, keeps dogs happy and healthy, and allows users who cannot adopt an opportunity to spend time with a dog.

For more details and images, check out our Github repo .
