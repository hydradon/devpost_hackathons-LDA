Inspiration

Farmers who do free range chickens need to worry about opening, and closing their chicken coops to protect them from predators, and if they can have door management maintained on their own they can be busy with other stuff they need to do in their days.

What it does

It opens up, and closes the coop doors, and also controls the light that calls the chickens home at the end of the day in order to free the farmer up to do other things they need to do.

How we built it

We will be building it with a kit from digi, metal, and electrical switches to open, and close circuits.
