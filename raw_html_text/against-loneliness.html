Inspiration

In a world becoming harder and harder to reach out to others we wanted to approach the topic from a different manner. With our project we are trying to illicit a self-reflective response from the player to think more thoroughly on the effects of loneliness and what they can do to prevent it.

What it does

Many different stories can be played where one play through will affect the course of other stories. It is all played within messenger by sending and receiving messages from a chat bot.

How we built it

We built the project using node.js and hosting on heroku.

Challenges we ran into

Storing state to support branching within a story.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Learning the necessary tools to build the project.

What we learned

Facebook-Messenger

What's next for Against Loneliness

Creating more content and learning more ways on how to leverage the messenger api, whether that be within the vanilla package or building upon it.
