Inspiration

We're living in Morocco , a past few months ago , we had a big disaster , a train accident which resolved in at least 6 deaths and 68 people injured , they transported them to the nearest hospital since the train flipped in a rural area , the bigger problem was that the hospital didn't have enough blood and it was short on blood to start with. Being a developer doesn't mean that you only code websites and web apps , being a developer means solving problems , building things that save people's time or even lives .

What it does

in this particular case , we made a platform that will encourage people to donate blood , a platform that brings together the blood donors and the people in need for a specific type of blood in a specific area

How I built it

we split work my partner and i . i took front-end using vuejs in some parts and apply form . and he took admin section which manage campaigns admins ect ..
