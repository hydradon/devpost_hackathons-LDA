Inspiration

We have two immigrants in our group: one who originally spoke only Korean, and the other spoke only French. This language barrier that they experienced while they were younger frustrated them.

What it does

Translates the chat to your language in real-time.

How we built it

We developed the idea, assigned jobs (front and backend developers), created goal deadlines, and worked on it using a variety of languages and packages.

Challenges we ran into

The node.js did not want to connect with the MongoDB, but we found an alternative: Socket.io. Also, we had a few communication and time management issues.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

A sweet presentation and logo, and learning lots of new concepts and ideas

What we learned

How to format html and javascript properly and how to use node.js

What's next for Gutenberg

Bring to a broader userbase
