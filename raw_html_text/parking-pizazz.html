Inspiration

We noticed a problem in our everyday lives in which we sought to find a solution to. Many young adults understand the struggle of finding a parking spot and an app to allow you to locate and reserve parking spots would help the community greatly.

Challenges I ran into

Most of us are novice programmers and so we had to reinforce of skills in Java.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We are proud of the fact that we collaborated together effectively and each of us participated some amount to create a final product that we are extremely proud of.

What I learned

We learned how to work as a team and increased our overall knowledge of Java. Our skill levels differed but together, we increased our java capabilities.

What's next for Parking Pizzazz

We hope to incorporate it into an app and decrease the amount of fuel wasted when people look for parking. As we are limited in the technology and knowledge we have, we hope this can be a starter that can develop into a parking app major malls and stores use for a cleaner Earth.
