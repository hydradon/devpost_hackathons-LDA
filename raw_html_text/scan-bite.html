Inspiration

We often spent a lot of time in queue in morning for breakfast. We made this app because our daily breakfast was almost the same and did not want to keep repeating our orders to have no meat.

What it does

A vendor + user system to manage food orders. User can scan his regular order at the restaurant and save time.

Challenges we ran into

First time using Swift. And managing backend.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The ios app and web app look great and function good with proper user authentication.

What we learned

A lot of Swift and React

What's next for Scan Bite

First is to complete the full app to showcase its full capability.
