Inspiration

When trying to find items online to buy/sell, it is troublesome to get the prices on it because there are so many platforms to look through. So we made a script that checks through some of the more popular sites such as amazon, craigslist and kijiji

What it does

it looks through amazon. craigslist and kijiji for the item you specify and it gives you the max, min, and average value of it
