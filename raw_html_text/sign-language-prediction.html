Inspiration

We have this inspiration from our classmate who was using a translator to translate whatever the professor is saying because he had a hearing impairment.

What it does

It converts sign language to text live. So, if you are in front of the camera talking in the sign it will be giving you text. So, other people who don't know sign language can also understand. Which will be live.

How I built it

We used GCP(Google Cloud Platform), TensorFlow, training our model to recognize the alphabet, then integrate that model to javaScript to take live Input to convert it and giving feedback back.

Challenges I ran into

Integrating JavaSccipt to python file. Then reducing output delay and etc

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned

Integrating different files.
