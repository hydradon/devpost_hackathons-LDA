Inspiration

The idea came up when I first visited career fair at UMD. It was a chaotic situation with Xfinity center full packed. The top companies Google, Microsoft, Amazon had wait time exceeding 1 hour. I thought why can't this process be eliminated with use of technology.

What it does

It is a digital form of your profile, resume, achievements, projects all at one place accessible through one id. Employer can directly access these respond to you save an entire event with all resumes.

How I built it

I built it using Swift, PHP, JSON, JS, CSS, HTML, JQUERY

Challenges I ran into

Every moment was a challenge as it has an enormous module managing three ends i.e school, student and employer.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Everything it was my first iOS app ever

What I learned

iOS programming

What's next for DigiRez

I plan to expand its network by collaborating with schools and companies.
