Inspiration

The forest app that trees grow as you leave the app open while studying. When you close the app to look at other things, trees die, using "shame" factors to combat procrastination.

What it does

Collects information regarding how much time you spend on non-productive apps (games, social media, etc.), presents the information in the format of % of your day wasted, making the user more aware of their procrastination habits to help them take action to be more productive. Furthermore, our avatar "Slackie" compares the user with other child prodigies to further spur them on in their work.

How we built it

We used android studio
