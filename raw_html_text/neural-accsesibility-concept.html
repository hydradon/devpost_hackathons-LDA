Inspiration

We were inspired by the idea of the world not being as accessible as we thought, my partner coming from a back injury himself had to face many problems himself. Making it hard for him to complete basic tasks. Micheal Reeves a famous youtuber also inspired our project, he used mind control to make a car accelerate https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mPbtR4vorgY .
