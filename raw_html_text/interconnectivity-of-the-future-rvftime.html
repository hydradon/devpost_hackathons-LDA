In a world of increasing interconnectivity, data is all the more prevalent. Those in control of this data are king. Subjective analysis is the current topic of focus with data collection giants such as Facebook and Google, predicting your online patterns. Our revolutionary application, will provide an objective and holistic evaluation of the user.

Inspirations

Looking at the next steps for technological singularity. Time is a scarce resource, as such we needed to optimize it’s value.

What it does

Sync all data between your devices and provide easy access/visualization to it.

What we learned

We learned a lot about querying, managing, and storing different sets of data and ways to visualize/use it.

What's Next for RVFTime

Implement more tools for data analysis/visualization. Provide feedback and advice to improve online routines/habits.
