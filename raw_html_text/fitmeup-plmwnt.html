What it does

With FitMeUp, we bring with us an intelligent friend to help you achieve your goals by remaining fitter. Its a game which lets you compete others, and more importantly, you compete with your own self.

Being Healthy is not just a goal. It is a way of living. There are some things that one need to focus on. Health should never become an obstacle. With a great UI, Machine Learning algorithms and Competitive Environment, FitMeUp is a tool that fits one up.

FitMeUp uses standard and modified machine learning algorithms to tell you the number of days (from the present day) in which you could control your life better. If you deteriorate someday and decide to keep health aside, your aim will be modified according the past performances.
