Nutrition Game focused towards younger children, educating how to have a healthy diet.

Approximately one in every five individuals from ages 6 to 19 is obese. This game is in place to educate children and to prevent future obesity. Obesity can start at a very young age and can be slowed or prevented by having a healthy diet. Thus, we made this project to benefit the overall health and wellness of society in the future.
