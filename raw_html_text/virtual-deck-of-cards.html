Virtual-Deck-Of-Cards

A virtual deck of cards! Play anything anywhere, with anyone

Ever wanted to play a card game with your friends, only to realise that no one has a pack of cards? Felt like playing a round of bridge but none of your friends are around you?

Fear no more, as the virtual deck of cards is here! Simply create or sign into a deck, and play any game with anyone anywhere!

Draw any number of cards from the deck for anyone, reveal or cover them as you wish, or throw them into the pile. With the functionality of a real deck, this virtual deck can be used for many games.

All card images are unicode characters.
