Introduction

Christmas is coming and at some time you sure will want to find your beloved ones some nice gifts. You will maybe want to find them right now standing in the city centre and you won't mind finding the shop with the biggest discount on the item you are looking for.

And that's where we come in. Our app lets you search for items, categories and any cool stuff you can think of and returns the nearest shops to you with the biggest discounts on your requested item. Google Maps will take you there and and, being freshly inspired by Braintree, Paypal will take care of your payments.

Contents

We offer:

  • Android app for the user
  • Webapp to allow shop owners submit discounts to their products to our system
  • Database backend housing all the awesome stuff people may want to buy - especially with christmas coming closer.

We are backed by Amazon Webservices, implemented Paypal API and Google Maps. The customer app is available on Android and for the backend we widely relied on opensource software.
