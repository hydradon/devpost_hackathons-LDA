Inspiration

We have a challenge with car parks in our local markets and usually most people do the shopping after working hours which end up creating a congestion and burning gas. So i want to solve this challenge by creating a Facebook messenger bot to help people pass orders any time of the day and we can deliver and i realized that i will need different type of foods and there they are a lot of people who are trying to do a small side business but they have no time (especially those who work) and they can not find clients to sell to....That the reason i built LakaPercel to aggregate between buyers and the people who want to do a side business.

What it does

Lakaparcel helps users to pass an order of local healthy foods at anytime of the day through messenger and get it delivered think of Lakaparcel as Amazon for local foods.
