Smart Pill Box is a first of its kind product. Providing financial incentives to patients to be medication adherent.

Smart Pill Box connects patients with their medical providers, and notifies the medical providers as soon as patients aren't medication adherent.

Text-Message Notifcations Patients are notified when it's time to take their medicine. Patients must respond to the text by saying that they've taken their medication. Smart Pill Box ensures that patients have indeed taken their medication for the day.

Financial Incentives Patients with exemplary medication adherence are entered into a lottery to win gift cards.

Data for doctors Medical providers have access to patient data, and are notified when a patient is non-adherent so that communication can be established quickly and effectively.

Simple and Portable The setup process takes minutes and can be done in a medical provider's office.
