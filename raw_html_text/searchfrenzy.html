Inspiration

Wordsearch solving is fun, wordsearch solving to beat a friend is even more fun

What it does

Word Frenzy allows you to solve a wordsearch against another person online simultaneously using the same board.

How we built it

We used Firebase for real time synicing of the board, Angular.js for the front end, Wordfindgame.js for puzzle generation and randomwordapi.
