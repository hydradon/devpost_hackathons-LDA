Inspiration

The inspiration was to help city dwellers make a better choice about how to commute.

What it does

It shows your best options for commuting to a destination taking into account parking garage fees, parking duration and uber/lyft fares.

How we built it

We use public data points about garages, uber/lyft fares to predict what would be the cheapest option for people who live in an urban area to travel/commute around.
