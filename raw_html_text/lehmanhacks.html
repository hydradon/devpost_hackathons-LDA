Inspiration

To help thousands of disabled New Yorkers with helpful commuting information.

What it does

Allows users to select NYC Subway stations which are wheelchair accessible and ADA compliant.

How we built it

Using ASP.NET C#, HTML, and CSS.

Challenges we ran into

Data retrieval, deciding on the language

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Able to work long hours towards a common goal

What we learned

We learned how to work with total strangers from different programming disciplines and collaborate in a very short amount of time. We all contributed and built upon ideas to help further our knowledge in CS as a whole.

What's next for LehmanHacks

We are looking forward to the next event, as we feel that we have gained essential experience in our first Hackathon.
