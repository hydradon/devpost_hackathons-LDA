We are realizing that water is becoming more and more scarce, whilst the impact of CO2 becomes more and more important. BuySmart is your grocery shop companion that helps you stay in tune with the planet.

See the real impact of your consumption with the levels of water and CO2 that were required and emitted in order for the product(s) that you were buying to be produced.
