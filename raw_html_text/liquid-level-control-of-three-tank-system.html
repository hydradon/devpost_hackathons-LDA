Inspiration

We are inspired in this project by the use of artificial intelligence “particle swarm optimization (PSO) method” and applications to control “Liquid Level of Three Tank System”.

What it does

Our application is much concerned with the use of artificial intelligence (PSO) method associated with the use of the new technology (Arduino).

How I built it

Our application is pre-programmed by the use of PSO method in order to optimize the parameters in the system to achieve desired value with the use of the Arduino

Challenges I ran into

As you may already know, one of the basic problems in the industries (industrial chemical processing, wastewater neutralization, petroleum, nuclear power generation, food processing industry… etc) is the liquid level controlling system.It was being an important process which has to be maintained at the desired (target) level for smooth running of the process and for better quality products. Our objective is to solve all these problems.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned
