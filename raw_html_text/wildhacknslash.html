Inspiration

Sword duels are a tradition that has fallen out of the mainstream in recent times. I want to revive this with a safer, more fun, virtual version. For the more futuristically minded, lightsaber sounds are included!

What it does

Pair two Android phones with Bluetooth, then, using the accelerometer, hack, slash, stab, and thrust away! Carefully time your blocks to gain an advantage.

How I built it

Android, Bluetooth, and lots of free sounds

What's next for WildHacknSlash

More complex and accurate sword fighting!
