• RoboCom

Inspiration

Fitness and fun at the work place. Split in 2 teams of 4-5 employees and shoot the oponent robot target to get points and be the best.

What it does

The robot drives away and you need to chase it and shoot at the target

How we built it

Arduino RedBot, programmed in C

Challenges we ran into

Missing parts, broken hardware, we used a lot of duct tape and lego .

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Managed to finish the project even if we had missing parts. Strong communication with the team members. We had a lot of fun.

What we learned

To work better in a team. Program new stuff in new languages

What's next for RoboCon

More of them.
