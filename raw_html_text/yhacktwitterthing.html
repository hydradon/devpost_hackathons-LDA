YhackTwitterThing

Thing for Twitter from Yhack 2015

Our app uses twitter API to get the latest tweet about a topic and tells us if the tweets about it are positive talks or negative talk about via an HP API. This app can be used by anyone to look for feedback on certain products or to look how people are responding to certain events or topics that are trending. Data collected from the positive/negative feedbacks of tweets can be used to analyze trend amongst society. Furthermore, our app tells us what topics amongst each tweet has the highest impactive on the positivity/negativity.
