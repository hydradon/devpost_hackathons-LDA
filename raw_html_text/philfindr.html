• PhilFindr

Inspiration

Leaving a lasting impact in our community.

What it does

PhilFindr uses the Pebble to get your geolocation in order to inform you of service opportunities around you.

How I built it

We built it all in Javascript, using the Pebble SDK.

Challenges I ran into

This was our first time building a Pebble Application, so it was a big learning experience.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I'm proud we were able to put together a real app that works, and that could potentially help people on campus do service in the community.

What I learned

I learned how to develop for the Pebble.

What's next for PhilFindr

We hope to expand our database of service opportunities, and move our product to bigger cities like Chicago. As well as we hope to eventually release a mobile version.
