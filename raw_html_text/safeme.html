SafeMe

SafeMe provides women a better and safer way to plan their trips at night.

SafeMe allows women to map out and enter details about various regions during their previous trips at night, including how safe they felt . This data then allows SafeMe to generate a smart route that optimizes the user's preferred safe routes and avoids unsafe routes as much as possible. Women can then take the routes that they are both familiar with and are comfortable with at night, empowering them to feel more at ease at night.
