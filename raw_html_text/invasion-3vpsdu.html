Inspiration

We wanted to provide users with an experience that allows them to view the world from a new perspective while enjoying the thrill of a combat game.

What it does

The user's hand becomes a UFO that must dodge bullets while firing at soldiers and tanks below and avoid getting hit by the attack helicopters surrounding it.

How I built it

We used Unity and assets from the Unity Store to assemble our project.

Challenges I ran into

We had some trouble spawning objects in correct places and syncing everything up together in the end.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We're proud of finishing the project and creating a game that both looks good and has a smooth and engaging game-play.

What I learned

We learned how to work with new technology as a team and use innovation to create a fun shooter game that we're proud of!

What's next for Invasion

We hope to add more levels and environments to our game and continue to polish and improve it.
