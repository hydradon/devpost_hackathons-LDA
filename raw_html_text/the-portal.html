Inspiration

Being school students we have always found that the school lacks in communication, being web developers we found a solution to it by creating a common online platform where teachers can upload their lessons and their assignments and students can communicate with them effectively

What it does

The school starts on by signing on to our platform and then we generate an account for the school, in which teachers and students can create their own accounts and they can start uploading lesson plans, activities for the whole school year and much more

How we built it

We built it on React JS and our backend is google firebase platform

Challenges we ran into

The challenge we ran into was learning a new front end.
