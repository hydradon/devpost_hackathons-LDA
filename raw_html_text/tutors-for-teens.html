Inspiration

We always wanted to tutor people, however, we had trouble finding suitable clientele. We designed this app to facilitate easy access to students and/or tutors with functionality at no cost.

What it does

It helps students find suitable tutors with good qualifications and ratings. They can choose between a multitude of tutors who teach a variety of subjects.

How we built it

We designed the wireframes in Sketch, and then linked them together with InVision. This allowed us to create a functional MVP that is easy to comprehend and use.

Challenges we ran into

We had trouble designing the individual pages and getting accustomed to the new software, specifically Invision and Sketch.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are particularly proud of our UI design and our idea. We think it would be a great addition to students' lives as well as that of tutors.

What we learned

We learned to use new softwares, i.e. Sketch and InVision.
