TechInfoExt

When I was trying to buy a computer a few days ago, even though I study computer science major, I find myself hard to understand the specifications of the computer. For instance, intel 8th Generational, 8GB, 256Gb hardware. Therefore, we wanna create this chrome extension to help people understand the terminology of computers, in a most simple way.
