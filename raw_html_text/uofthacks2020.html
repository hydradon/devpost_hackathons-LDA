Inspiration

As university students with minimal spare time outside of school, a limited budged, and events to attend that require a specific dress code (e.g., formal), we are looking for good, convenient, and sustainable outfits from our closet to wear.

What it does

Provides the ability to manage one's wardrobe virtually, recommends quick/good outfit ideas based on user inputting keywords (e.g., casual, formal), encourages donation of clothing, provides access to community closets containing donated clothing.
