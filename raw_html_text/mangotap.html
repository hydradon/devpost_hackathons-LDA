Inspiration

To bring happiness and fun to every single person in the world through the use of their very own phone to play an interactive game.

What it does

The Mangotag app is the perfect app for children and adults alike that bring them together to play a modern-day hid-and-seek game.

How we built it

By using Xcode to build out the mobile application and use firebase as the database.

Challenges we ran into

We originally wanted to use AWS but ran into problems integrating Amazon Cognito, DynamoDB, and Lambda together

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The app is finished

What we learned

Develop, Test, and cry at night

What's next for Mangotag

Creating a more proprietary QR reading product
