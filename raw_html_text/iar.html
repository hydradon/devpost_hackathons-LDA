Inspiration

Information that we are looking for may be difficult to come by. Imagine if we can use scan commonly used objects to get those information.

What it does

Scanning objects to get relatable information regarding the item. It not only acts as a tool for getting information about an item, it also helps to educate people on what they are scanning about. i.e. Scanning tools for product guide, animal enclosure for species information.

Our key selling point is that users does not need to download any additional apps, as long as they have Facebook or Instagram, they can use our solution.

How we built it

We used SparkAR by Facebook to come up with an AR solution, and assets that were created with illustrator.

What's next for iAR

Expanding scanning ability to other relevant areas. i.e. tools, animals, clothes, etc...
