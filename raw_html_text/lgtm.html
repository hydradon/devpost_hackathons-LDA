Save Me

In a world where drunk driving still causes too many fatalities, there is a stark need for a quick check to determine if you are too drunk. Our product leverages speech-to-text machine learning methods to analyze a person's speech and decide if they're too drunk.

Our prototype is fully working and designed for future growth, with the integration of DocuSign APIs in order to further collect user data and improve upon our models.
