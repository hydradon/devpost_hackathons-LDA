Ecoqo - Future through Community

  1. One-liner: Direct pipeline between volunteers & organizations (charities, bigger influencers)

  2. The idea of Ecoqo is to offer a platform for people within different communities, to interact with each other while doing environmental activities, charity & volunteer work.

Use-case:

  • Your company needs some team building to strengthen company culture ? Join an event, make a social impact, and interact with other communities for a good cause.

  • You've watched that kid on TV about social awareness, and want to give it a try? Come to Ecoqoa and find an event near you!
