Inspiration

Mental health is an increasingly prevalent topic among young adults our age.

What it does

Our app uses sentiment analysis to track journal entries. It gives you personalized data about your mental well being, and helps you with your progress.

How I built it

We used Python and Flask for the Backend, React for the frontend, PostgreSQL for storage.

Challenges I ran into

Our biggest challenge was connecting the different parts of our app; the frontend and the backend.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We pulled together and learned a lot, after countless hours of hard work.

What's next for e-motion
