7am and your hand can't make it to the alarm clock before the voices in your head start telling you it's too cold, too windy, and too wet to go outside. A legion of voices are shouting for your unanimous permission to hit snooze, and go back to dreamland. However, you can't. You must choose the perfect outfit for your crazy, busy, long day. You don't want to get rained on when you step outside, but it is already too late to check the weather AND plan your outfit too! Cue The Sweater Network. Using real time data from The Weather Network, and customized pictures from your wardrobe, The Sweater Network will customize and create the perfect outfit for your day. Gone are the days of being too cold, or too hot, or too wet. Live life to the fullest, and never worry about these issues again.
