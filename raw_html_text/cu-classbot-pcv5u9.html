Inspiration

A devfest talk on Flask bots

What it does

Scraped the online course directory for all three thousand courses, put them into a database where they were will be queriable by the bot that responds to natural-sounding questions from users

What's next for CU ClassBot

Integration with the EZACU and CULPA APIs to expand the scope of what users can ask our bot, with the aim of making it an indispensable resource for CU students.
