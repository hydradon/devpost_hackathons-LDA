Inspiration

We love super cheap/error-priced flight tickets!

What it does

It instantly notifies you in Messenger when a flight that suits your preferences appears with a crazy low price.

How we built it

Using KoalaBot you set up your travel prefences in fb messenger app. In a background, we receive newsletters and emails about this crazy low prices, parse them and extract useful info. If there is a flight suitable your preferences, we send you a simple, quick alert.

Challenges we ran into

How to exploit emails. How to learn from them. How to build a chatbot and interact with a user.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We accomplished all goal we had in front of us to build a MVP in 24hours.
