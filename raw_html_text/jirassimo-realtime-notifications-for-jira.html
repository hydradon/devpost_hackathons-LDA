Jirassimo for JIRA Cloud

Jirassimo is a plugin for JIRA Cloud which enables sending instant messages using your custom templates about your JIRA events to your favourite team-messenger.

Make JIRA a first-class member of your team. Complete integration with your team's messenger

At the moment we support integrations with:

  • Slack
  • HipChat
  • Fleep
  • Email

Try it out https://marketplace.atlassian.com/plugins/net.vacom.jirassimo
