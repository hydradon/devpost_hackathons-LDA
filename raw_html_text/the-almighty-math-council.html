This was a program I made to help me study for my pre calculus class. It generates random sinusoidal functions to graph and triangles to solve, then asks you questions on the properties of the function or asks you to solve the triangle, depending on which function you chose. It offers review if you are getting several wrong answers, and it tells you what the correct answers are once you have entered your own answers. It also allows you to work on trouble spots, such as types of functions you are having trouble with.
