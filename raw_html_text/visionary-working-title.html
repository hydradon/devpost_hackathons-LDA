Inspiration

Inspired by an artwork by Rene Magritte, Visionary (tentative title) is a prototype demonstrating a strange and invisible world revealed by a silhouette of a character that normally is invisible.

What it does

Play the game via regular PC game controls:

  • WASD or arrow keys to move
  • Move mouse to move the camera
  • scroll-wheel to zoom in or out
  • c to crouch
  • space to jump
