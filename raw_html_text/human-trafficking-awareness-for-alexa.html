Inspiration

After I read about the huge problem of human trafficking, I decided to help. I didn't know how big a problem this was in the world until someone reached out to me. I realized that there had to be a better way to raise awareness for this huge problem. Here is the solution: An Alexa skill!

What it does

This skill educates users on the signs of a trafficked person and some appalling facts of the trade. It also notifies the user on what to do if you come in contact with a trafficked person and how to to help them. Hopefully, this will raise awareness about how devastating a problem this is.

How I built it

I built this using the Amazon Alexa Fact Skill Template. I also used AWS Lamba.

Challenges I ran into

I learned that there is a lot of inaccurate information out there about human trafficking to make it sound like less of a problem. I dug in and found the real facts.

What I learned

I learned about the massive problem of human trafficking and how it affects a person's life. Everyone in this world only has one life, and nobody wants to spend it as a slave in the trafficking trade.

What's next for Human Trafficking Awareness for Alexa

I am looking forward to expanding the facts and maybe turning this into a trivia game with Echo Buttons, as I really love this new technology.

I also want to expand this to other social use cases, such as domestic abuse. Hopefully, this can change the world.
