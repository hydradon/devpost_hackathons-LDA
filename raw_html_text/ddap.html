Inspiration

The world of music and our passion regarding medical technology.

What it does

MuseWave lets users listen to music playlists that reflect their current mood without the press of a button.

How we built it

A Muse EEG headband that can monitor brain signals/emotions, Android Studio, Spotify/Muse APIs, and plenty of testing.

Challenges we ran into

Various forms of noise due to muscle movement and other inconsistencies in the data collected from the Muse.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Building a kick-ass application that can use EEG signals to predict the music tastes of users.

What we learned

Teamwork, EEG monitoring, symptoms of sleep deprivation.
