How I built it

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Finishing the design, product, and presentation

What I learned

Engineers don't spend much time making the product, but designing the product. Don't waste your time. Be open to new ideas, because it may actually work, no matter the craziness.
