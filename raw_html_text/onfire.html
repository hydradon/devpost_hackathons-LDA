Fighting wildfires is a hazardous occupation that has recently claimed an average of 18 firefighters lives per year. We've created a firefighter team monitoring platform and a mobile application to monitor firefighters vitals and exposure to dangerous particles to provide alerts when exposure limits are not safe or if a firefighter has collapsed.
