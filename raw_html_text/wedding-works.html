• Start by uploading your wedding photos

  • Your photos will be automatically categorized and turned into a virtual photo album

  • Then you can share your Weddory album with your friends!

Inspiration

We wanted to create a unique, standalone sharing application that used the Clarifai API.

What it does

Weddory intelligently categorizes your wedding photos and creates a beautiful online photo album for you to share with your friends.

How I built it

We use Clarifai's wedding tagging model to get a general idea of what each of your pictures contains. Then we run that data through our machine learning algorithm which categorises it into one, or none, of our 6 predefined categories. Each of photos in these categories are laid out in a digital photo album for you to share with your friends and family.
