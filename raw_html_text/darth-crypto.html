About

Our website allows the registration of users stored in an online Mongo database. Each account goes through a CryptoPhoto registration wizard which assigns a unique image to login using the CryptoPhoto API. Easy and fast to use, just tap the photo on the CryptoPhoto app to securely login using multi-factor 2FA two-step security.

This solution makes it clear to users how to proceed with the login, whereas other integrations may seem more confusing. We've tried to make the UX as smooth as possible and are open to any feedback users have :)

Protect yourself from hackers, viruses, key-loggers, scams, and fraud!
