Inspiration

I was tired of opening and closing my jars, using primitive human powered means. After a great spiritual awakening I realized that in a world of AI, robotics, and teledildonics opening and closing jars is the last great injustice faced by humanity on our path to becoming GODs of the natural world.

NO other problem faced by humanity today compares to our need to have autonomous jar opening, and during this hackathon I decided to take on this challenge and save the world.

What it does

My project dares to dream of world where containers open themselves.

How I built it

I used an arduino uno, a button, and a motor driver. I also designed a part to be 3D printed, for the device.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Being bold enough to solve one of the worst issues humanity faces today. Not world peace, not hungry, autonomous jar opening.

What's next for Jar Opener

In the future the jar opener won't just open jars. It'll be able to open hearts.
