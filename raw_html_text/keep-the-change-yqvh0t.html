Challenges I ran into

Interesting Math problems to overcome the mechanics of saving money on a daily basis and leveling your buckets to achieve the goal.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am proud to say that I have come up with an app that can help people and myself to spend money while keeping track of my debts and loans while also empowering me to chase my financial and personal goals.

What I learned

I learned that using Plaid API I can access almost all information related to my finances. With this information and the challenge to solve problems of loans and increasing debt, it is possible to reduce debt, repay it faster by maintain a sustainable spending strategy.

How I built it

I built it using NodeJS, ReactJs and integrated transactions & income API from Plaid.

What's next for Debtless

Combining 2 or more accounts with the help of Plaid & managing a family's account together in one app to acheive shared goals such as buying a house together.
