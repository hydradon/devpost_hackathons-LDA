What it does

Are you like the millions of Americans that are on several different medications, but somehow you always seem to forget to take medication? MediDock is an all-in-one medication management system that allows you to track and remember to take your medication through a convenient iOS app!

How we built it

Utilizing Swift and Xcode, we developed and designed an iOS app that is easy to use and full of important information about the medication people take on a daily basis.

Challenges we ran into

Originally, we were going to build a scale system that allows you to automatically track your dosage by just taking the medication as you normally do and a partnering app that keeps track of it automatically using a scale system. Unfortunately, the hardware necessary for this was not at our disposal.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We developed and designed a beautiful application full of useful features.

What's next for MediDock

We would like to continue to add features and make it truly the only medication management app you'll need.
