Alexa's Kitchen

The most convenient kitchen aid, Gordon (Alexa's Kitchen) keeps a constant stock of your refrigerator and recommends meals for you based on what you prefer to eat at that time, clearly simplifying the often tedious process of researching ingredients and instructions beforehand. Gordon guides you through step-by-step instructions so you can enjoy the relaxing experience of making a hearty meal; following, Gordon asks you if you would like to restock items you are low in.

Look out for upcoming features, like the ability to create custom recipes, bookmark recipes, and provide in-depth nutritional data for a healthy diet.
