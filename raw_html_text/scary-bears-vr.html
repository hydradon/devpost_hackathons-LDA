Inspiration

VR is cool. Bears are scary.

What it does

Simulates a never ending attack from scary undead teddy bears. Shoot them down before they get you, or die in a swarm of them.

How I built it

Unity and Google VR SDK for Unity. C# for game scripts. Free assets found online.

Challenges I ran into

First time using Unity, Google VR SDK, and C#.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Made a working game.

What I learned

Unity, Google VR SDK, and C#

What's next for Scary Bears VR

More bear variations.
