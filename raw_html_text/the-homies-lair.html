Homies Lair

What is Homies Lair? Homies Lair is a hub for shared accommodation which creates a unique database based on the users house I.D.

From here we created a unique messaging board as well as our main feature a way to monitor transactions from within the house. This can be used to, track who has payed for bills or who owes who money. The messaging board can be used to keep track of chores or any day to day activities or set reminders. But more importantly it can be used for passive aggressive messages.

I hope you enjoy it!
