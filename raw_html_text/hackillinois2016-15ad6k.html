• On hover, our extension provides simple options to learn more about the landmark and plan travel.

  • This is how our extension appears by default on an Instagram photo.

  • Our extension can concurrently handle the detection of landmarks of several different photos.

  • A prototype for a minimally intrusive variant of PicTrip.

Through this chrome extension, general users can see famous landmark information of any photo on the internet and discover how to travel there as easily (and inexpensively) as possible.

The program's enterprise possibilities are endless. Generate even more revenue from advertising travel arrangements in the most intuitive fashion for your website's users. It's so simple, fun, and interesting that your clients won't even notice they are interacting with ads!
