• The Master Crow

Inspiration

As a team full of celery lovers we knew we needed to design a hack to protect what was most important to us

What it does

Lays out the rules for an epic battle between celery gardeners and a murder of celery-murdering crows

How we built it

Blood, sweat, tears, coding node.js, CSS, and html by hand, team coding, lots of whiteboard diagramming

Challenges we ran into

learning how to utilize sockets.io, domain hosting, single server to multiple client integration

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Our UI, use of crows and celery in the same app idea, our humor

What we learned

sockets, how annoying hosting is, the power of team programming, why sleep is a dangerous idea, client-server integration, how to make buttons and voting and decks of cards while controlling state and views for numerous clients

What's next for Crows vs Celery

minor improvements to the rules and gameplay, expansion packs, new themes for the game beyond the initial crows vs celery garden theme, add more beautiful graphics and animations
