ShoppingBuddy

Ever go to the mall and try on clothes, thinking, "Wow! I wish I had another perspective"? Well think no more! Introducing Shopping Buddy! We'll provide everyone with their own personal shopping companion. Together, you can rate each others' outfits on the mall and you'll know for sure whether that outfit you're thinking of buying is hot or not!
