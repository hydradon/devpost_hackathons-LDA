Inspiration

To reduce paper waste and make it easier to go through your business cards and share easily without any cost.

What it does

Platform to easily share business cards by QR Codes and easily manage your collection of business cards.

How we built it

We built it natively in Swift for iOS and Java for Android. For the Database we used Cloud Firestore in Firebase. The backend is Firebase.

Challenges we ran into

User Authentication, complex iOS - Android NFC, Designing Database

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Working demo on both iOS and Android in less than 24 hours

What we learned

Learnt how to use firebase authentication, Cloud Firestore, how to generate and work with QR codes

What's next for Digital Business Cards

Polish the app, provide managing business card feature, NFC business Card sharing
