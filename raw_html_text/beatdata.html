Inspiration

This idea come from our personnal entrepreneurship journey, in digital marketing.

What it does

It help organizors/advitisers to understand what they are and what their environment is, in order to seek opportunities for them.

How we built it

We analyze Facebook Pages/Events/Posts, filter only those music related, define line-up for events, bind with the Musimap API and finally, compute datas.

Challenges we ran into

Connect the Musimap Artist UID with IDs of others platforms.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We can define music styles and moods for a Facebook Pages, based on their events and line-ups.

What we learned

Musimap API architecture.
