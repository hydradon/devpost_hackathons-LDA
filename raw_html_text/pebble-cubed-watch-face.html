Inspiration

I had a Pebble watch and always wanted to make something of my own and put it on my watch.

How it works

My watch face tells time, can tell you the battery level of your watch, and tell you if you are connected to your phone. Another feature on the watch face is if the watch is not connected to a phone the watch face will invert colors.

Challenges I ran into

When i was first making the app i encountered errors that the watch face took up too much space on the pebble device, also i had to realign the numbers, icons, and the background picture to fit the pebble device perfectly.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I got the watch face up and running on my pebble.

What I learned

Making a simple watch face takes lots of patients.

What's next for Pebble Cubed watch face

I want to implement weather into the watch so people can see the temperature at their current time.
