Tech.Declassified

A NLP Classifier for Tech Related Articles

Using NLTK api, we developed a classifier that can tell whether or not an article online is a technology related article or not! It uses sentiment and frequency to interpret a given url as either a Tech article or not. It includes an real time machine learning algorithm to update it's capability to determine articles based on proper classifications in the past. This helps improve future predictive probabilities.
