Inspiration

Someone close to us was affected with Alzheimers and this lead us to wanting to figure out a way to help other people in the same position as us. We know how confusing it could be for the person affected and how helpless a caregiver can feel at times, and that lead us to creating this website.

What it does

Helps caregivers by providing essential resources and information about Alzheimers. The website also allows people who have a family history of Alzheimer’s to delay the onset of the disease through cognitive games and lifestyle tips such as having a healthier diet and exercising more that have been proven by credible sources to delay the onset.

How we built it

CSS, HTML, Photoshop

Challenges we ran into

We coded the website completely from scratch, so it was hard to incorporate all the elements together to make a page with good UI and functionality.

What's next for Alzheimer's Help

We hope to make an app in the future that will serve the same purpose as this website so that it can tailor the tips and cognitive games to each user by having a login and reminders.
