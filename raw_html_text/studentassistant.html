Inspiration

We are obsessed with having the right working conditions, in order to squeeze every drop of productivity out of ourselves.

How it works

A bunch of analog and digital sensors connected to Arduinon and Raspberry-Pi monitor the environment for conditions like temperature, humidity, noise level, light level etc. All this is managed by a low level sensor service. Then this info is processed by a web application which makes it user friendly. The users get to know what's their working environment like and get suggestions about improving it.

Challenges I ran into

Lack of network equipments and struggling with putting together the hardware sensors.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Putting together the hardware and building the low level management algorithm while providing a secured access via a web service. We also have developed a special formula that takes into consideration all the variables and comes with a single result which is the environment index score.
