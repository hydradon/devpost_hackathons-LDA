Have you ever needed to set up a series of interviews? Project check-ins with your students? Auditions for your music group, or simply meet up with an old friend for coffee?

We make it easy for you to do any of these! Create an account, let Rendezvous know when you're available and how long you want to meet for, and it will generate a time-slot interface that you can send out to a list of emails - we make it simple, easy and painless!

Once a user signs up for a time slot, we send both of you a meeting invite so you can add it to your personal calendar. If either of you have to cancel, notify Rendezvous and it will handle the rest.

Let Rendezvous take stress off of you!
