Inspiration

In order to adopt healthier lifestyles, we wanted to create an app that would be able to keep us mindful of the content that we send to others or post onto the internet.

What it does

Wholesome-me is an extension to your favourite messaging application, that calculates the sentiment of every message in a conversation, in order to promote positive thinking among users. The app works as a cyber-bullying prevention method, that ensures only wholesome vibes are circulated on the internet. Our application will also calculate cumulated daily sentiments and yearly emotional trends, in order to encourage all users to pursue a healthy positive lifestyle and reap the subsequent mental and physical health benefits.
