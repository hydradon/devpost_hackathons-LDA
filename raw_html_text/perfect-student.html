Inspiration

Students are always looking for more free time. With Perfect Student, we can organize a students nutritional life and academic life. This takes off the stress of remembering all your homework assignments, and puts more free time in your life.

What it does

Chatbots allow you to input data about your homework and inquire about it later. It also lets you inquire the bots for information about foods and nutrition to manage a healthy lifestyle along with a healthy academic lifestyle.

How we built it

We used DialogFlow API to allow easy conversation between a bot and a human to manage homework and nutrition information. Then, we built a website server and client to work with it and message the bots.

Challenges we ran into

Using DialogFlow was totally new to us, as we had just learned about it in one of the workshops.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of the design and look of the final product, and its functionality.

What we learned

We learned all about DialogFlow.

What's next for Perfect Student

Adapting to mobile (iOS and Android) users and adding more "channels", not just academics and diet.
