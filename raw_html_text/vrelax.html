Inspiration

Thinking of ideas for hours and hours was stressful for this hackathon. We thought of an idea to relax our minds and bodies using a combination of meditation, virtual reality, and humor.

What it does

VRelax takes you to outer-space and guides you to meditate by choosing, listening, and following provided scripts.

How we built it

Unity and C# in junction with Google Cardboard.

Challenges we ran into

The biggest challenge we faced during this hackathon was generating an idea. After 4-5 hours we settled on a few and decided this was the best and most fun to create.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We're proud that we created a fully-functioning application with only one of us having experience in developing VR.

What we learned

VR provides an endless amount of opportunities and we look forward to expanding this application and creating more in the future.

What's next for VRelax

Expanding meditations, visuals, and users
