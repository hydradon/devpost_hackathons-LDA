Inspiration

We wanted to help the Office of Elections solve their problem of registering volunteers and coordinating their training.

What it does

Allows prospective volunteers to register and select their training online without a need to interact with a state employee.

How I built it

We built the application with Node.js, Express, React, React-Redux, Leaflet, Nodemailer, and MongoDB.

Challenges I ran into

We wanted to add a lot of features, but realized we needed to narrow down the scope of the project to meet the deadline. Also, deploying is hard! :'(

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We created a beautiful app!

What I learned

Sometimes, it's just best to keep it simple.

What's next for Votenteer

Adding new features!
