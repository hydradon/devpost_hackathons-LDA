Inspiration

Sometimes reviews are too complex and long to read and you can't have a complete opinion about it just with some stars. Based on that we decide to make it easier to analyze the reviews showing to the user just what people are saying about the product.

What it does

Analyzes reviews for a specific product and categorizes keywords based on the user feeling.

How I built it

It was built using IMB Watson's API to analyze a text and extract relevant keywords and define sentiments related to that keyword. Showing a sumarized list of those keywords to the user

Challenges I ran into

Choosing the rights API's to develop the product

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Ease of use accomplished through a chrome extension and

What I learned

Doing a simple and functional MVP it's more important than fancy features that doesn't work.

What's next for Didn't Read

Today it's just working on the walmart.com website. The next step would be integrating that with other famous review websites (like Amazon, Yelp, TripAdivsor, etc).
