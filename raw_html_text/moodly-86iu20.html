Inspiration

In this busy world, we are often too caught up in our surroundings and neglect our personal needs. To address this issue, we've created an application that allows even the busiest office worker to set aside time to take care of their mental health through the encouragement of meditation.

What it does

The app gives personalized suggestions based on the user's current mood to improve their overall state of being.

How we built it

We used SwiftUI using the IDE XCode.

Challenges we ran into

Everyone on our team was inexperienced with creating an application, especially one on OS. For instance, we ran into difficulties while trying to embed a video and creating pop-ups that allowed the user to make choices that tailored to their needs.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of being able to work around our obstacle of trying to embed a video by providing a direct webpage to the video instead. We are also proud of the overall aesthetics of the app.

What we learned

Not only did we gain valuable experience coding in SwiftUI, but we also learned the dedication and time commitment behind executing a seemingly distant plan.

What's next for Moodly

We hope to implement a small journaling section that allows the user to further explain their emotions and be able to look back on their past journal entries.
