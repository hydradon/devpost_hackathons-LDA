Inspiration :One of our team members love basketball so we decided to make it into reality and we wanted the players to choose their own story so we decided whats better than star wars!!!

What it does: the basketball game provides a hoop to shoot basketballs and the star wars choose your own adventure game provides players the chance to play as the light or dark side on a thrilling quest.

How we built it: we built our program using scratch and we used many skills including if/else statements, loops, and using two computers to work on the same project at the same time

Challenges we ran into: using two computers to work on the same thing at the same time,Although it got the job done quicker

Accomplishments that we're proud of: using our skills together to our advantage and making the best of our problems

What we learned: how to make statements where there are two options to choose from

What's next for scratch games!: Conquering the world!!!!
