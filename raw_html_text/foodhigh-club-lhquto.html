Smoke Cannabis and you are going to eat, why not order both at the same time?

FoodHigh ( www.foodhigh.club ) is a unique food delivery service where you don't tip the delivery guy/gal... they tip you with a mystery gift box. Your mystery box could contain coupons to other restaurants, tickets to events, CBD, Cannabis Flowers (where legal) and other cool gifts.

Foodhigh is built using the ionic framework, nodejs and a postgres DB.

The biggest challenge was conceptually thinking up a way for users to share their meal experiences.
