Decision Maker

An Alexa skill that can give simple suggestions, such as what type of cuisine to eat, what to wear, and what movie genre to watch that day. Currently available on the Alexa Skills Store.

Sample Phrases:

"Alexa, ask Decision Maker what I should eat today"

"Alexa, ask Decision Maker what kind of movie I should watch today"

"Alexa, ask Decision Maker what should I wear today"
