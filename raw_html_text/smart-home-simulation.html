Inspiration

the 21st century life is getting very delicate so home safety has become a major interest of householders so why not making it through connected ecosystems? why not making our homes smarter and safer using internet of things!

What it does

Our technology provides homeowners security, comfort, convenience and energy efficiency by allowing them to control smart devices - integrated in their houses- often by a smart home app on their smartphones. smart home simulation is a collection of software and hardware: mobileapp :for monitoring the hardware devices hardware :for detecting security alerts such as fire cases,water and gas leaks,and the heat augmentation inside the house

How we built it

for the hardware:we implemented arduino technology with starter kit sensors. for the mobile app:we used react native. and then we connected the soft and hard parts using bluetooth module .

Challenges we ran into

due to the lack of material and time we only implemented some of the features unfortunately.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

the ability to work on our project with this kind of support ,to present it and put it onto the light is our greatest pleasure.

What we learned

some of us didn't have a lot of required skills so within the hackathon we developed new skills by the learning by doing strategy and we surely experienced a great adventure

What's next for Smart home simulation

we aim to give our technology the enough intelligence to make it able to control itself using artificial intelligence.
