Inspiration

This app was inspired by the fact that many women are interested in more than just the traditional/conventional hobbies society places upon us. However, it is often hard to go out and try boxing, welding, carpentry, skatebording, etc. when they are surrounded by men.

What it does

Connects women in the area to other women who are interested in the same hobbies that are often dominated by men.

How we built it

We used Android Studio along with Java, the Facebook Graph API, the Youtube API, along with Firebase.
