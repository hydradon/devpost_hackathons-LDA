Inspiration

Many preventable deaths occur due to gas poisoning, particularly in households. People ages 25-64 are most affected by this. What it does

The product is an unmanned electric cart that has ultrasonic sensors mounted on all of its sides for collision avoidance and obstacle maneuvering. It also has a gas detector that measures the concentration of gas particles.
