Inspiration

My Neighbor recently lost her dog. I thought about an app service that would allow people to post about missing dogs. However, i wanted people to post when they find dogs as well.

What it does

The user either enters data about a lost dog or the one they found. Size, breed, picture, tag number, GPS.
