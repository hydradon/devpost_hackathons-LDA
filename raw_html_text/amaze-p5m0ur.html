Inspiration

One of the team members really enjoyed mazes. He wanted to develop this application so that he can re-live his days of being a maze god.

What it does

This app lets you experience a maze in real-time using VR technology.

How I built it

We built it primarily on Unity.

Challenges I ran into

One of the main challenges that the team ran into was transforming the Unity project into the VR headset (Google Cardboard).

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The team was proud to make this app, as most of the team did not have prior Unity/game development experience.

What's next for aMaze

We want to add a way for the player to jump, as well as adding jump scares. We want to also include timed challenges.
