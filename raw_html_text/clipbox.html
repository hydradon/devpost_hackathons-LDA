Inspiration

Have you ever sent yourself email just to share clips of content between devices or with collaborators?

What it does

ClipBox is a smart universal clipboard to copy and transfer clips between devices. Paste content or drop files into the app to make them instantly available across devices. Simple and easy. It is an app you will use every single day.

How I built it

ClipBox is implemented in Clojure as a react-based SPA on top of BlockStack.

Challenges I ran into

Deciding which Lightning SDKs to use.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Pasting images into the browser was released in Chrome today June 30th as we finalized our submission. We are excited to announce that ClipBox supports this functionality.

What I learned

Lightning is rapidly maturing.

What's next for ClipBox
