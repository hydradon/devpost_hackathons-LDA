The idea came from Acorns, we thought building an app that allows users to donate their spare change to support people suffering from disasters/adversities would be a great idea. One of the challenges we overcame was working with Firebase. Understanding how to create a proper linkage between child and properties without overwriting data took us some time to understand, but we figured it out. We're proud of being able to utilize this database to keep proper track of balances and transactions.
