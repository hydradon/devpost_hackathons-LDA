PacTravel

** The ultimate exploration for finding your own future travel plans.**

We are PacTravel, a platform where gaming meets travel planning. By the power of Pacman and the Amadeus API, a user can explore the surrounding environment, visit landmarks, and even fly to new cities. Each landmark visited will be added to an itinerary to be followed in real life, later on. Travel planning remains to be a very difficult task, where information like hotels, flights, and activities are all decentralized. We hope to combine them in a single interactive session, providing a fun way to explore the world!
