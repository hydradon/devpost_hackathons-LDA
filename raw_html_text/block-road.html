Inspiration

We wanted to monetize individual data and allow people to buy data through microtransactions using cryptocurrency.

We created a solution to integrate blockchain attributes into microservice architectures. Now your Lambdas can yield the power of Smart Contracts.

We built a smart contract to hold the data on the Ethereum blockchain and accept microtransactions to access it.
