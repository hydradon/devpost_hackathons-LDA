Inspiration

The inspiration came from Tinder.

What it does

Its matches potential students, so that they can find a studdygroup

How we built it

We first tried to build it as a android app, but in the end we decided to make it a webpage instead. The backend is made as an REST-API with the PHP framework Laravel.

Challenges we ran into

Android was a challenge, and because of lack of time, we decided to make it in a browser instead. We had never uploaded a Laravel-project to a host before, and had alot of issues with this aswell

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The general idea of uniting people in a studygroup on different studies or classes. We made this a bit easier.

What we learned

Dont make android apps when you dont have much time.
