Inspiration

Raising awareness starts from a young age. This is why we're targeting the next generation.

Pitch

Kids are impressionable; kids love games; and kids use social media all the time. With social media playable ads, we can reach kids from all over the world, and give them the basic awareness for reducing plastics.

But that's just a tip of the iceberg. Our idea is a full flow that takes a kid from an ad into an immersive dashboard with more information about reducing plastics and recycling plastics, honing in the awareness for reducing the plastics.

Product

Our product includes

  1. Interactive ads
  2. Dashboard
  3. Chatbot consultation service
  4. Websites with additional information

Business Model

Non-profit organisation. Our plan for funding: Ads, affiliate marketing, social enterprise accelerator, seed funding, government funding, and sponsor funding.
