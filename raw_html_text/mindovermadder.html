BCGameJam2018

Our team is Headshrinkers and we are proud to present our game called “Mind Over Madder.” Our interpretation of “Resolution” in our game is Conflict Resolution. Our vision for this game is that there are two giants having a massive argument above you and you would mediate by shooting negative phrases and allowing positive communication through.

We set the game where the protagonist is visiting a therapist who uses a new technology that has you help mediate a conflict between two people who are arguing about whether games are art. You are put into a dream state and you then find yourself in a mech suit shooting brains out of your arm cannons.

While talking about the idea of conflict resolution a team member suggested having a way for players to learn more about techniques and what they can do. So we included a menu option that has a few tips on helping you with conflicts in your life or those around you. We also included links to help find a mediator or get more training yourself.
