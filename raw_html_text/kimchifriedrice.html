Many businesses are realizing the importance of the subscription model. Which provides them with a continuous, stable income stream so that they can better manage their business risks and revenues.

However, many small businesses do not have enough expertise, money or resources to build this subscription model themselves.

We therefore created an easy solution for them to create a subscription model for their new/existing customers by simply embedding our html into their existing website.

Because we will be managing a lot of subscription based businesses, we can provide them with insight and expertise for the business to succeed as well as target the right audience.

Our application also benefits the customers as they have the ability to manage all of their subscriptions at one place. Recently we have seen an increase in number of customers who forget what they are subscribed to and also face an issue with cancelling such subscriptions. Our application acts as the central hub for recording and managing these subscriptions for customers.
