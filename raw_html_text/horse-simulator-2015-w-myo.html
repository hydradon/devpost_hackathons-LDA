What it does

It does horse with Myo armbands. Inverse kinematics allow us to fully leverage the wearable technology against the modern game development engine Unity. High resolution textures bring the world of horse to life. Neigh.

How I built it

We mapped each Myo to a limb on the horse. Neigh.
