Inspiration: In this political atmosphere, many have a desire to learn more about politics and their rights.

What it does: Politix aims to prevent political apathy. It informs users of bills, current events and how to contact local legislatures.

How I built it: There were two implementations: Web and Android App

Challenges I ran into: We faced many challenges, including our difficulty in parsing through JSON.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of: After long ours of work, we got our Android application to receive HTTP packets. In addition, we were very pleased with our web application.

What I learned: Overall, we gained knowledge through trial and error, personal research and collaborating.

What's next for politix: We aim to complete our mobile application.
