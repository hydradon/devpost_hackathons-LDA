Inspiration

Everyone likes pets. Sometimes, we all want a pet for a few hours and are willing to pay for that comfort. Other times, we have pets but we want to go somewhere and need a sitter. Our app can help you in both situations! We are here for you!

What it does

We help you find people who are willing to sit your pets if you are an owner, or we help you find pets if you are someone who wants to be a sitter. The price is up to you.

How we built it

We used Java and xml using android studio.

Challenges we ran into

We had difficulties merging our files using github. We do not have enough experience with coding, and we had trouble simplifying our ideas to our skill set.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We actually made something and submitted it! Also, we made some great friends!

What we learned

We learnt to use Android Studio and GitHub.

What's next for YoPet

Further development with the database and backend work.
