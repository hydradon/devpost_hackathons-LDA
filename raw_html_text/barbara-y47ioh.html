With 20 years of industry experience, Barbara™ is your personal hairstylist. Using bleeding edge facial recognition technology Barbara™ gives you the best new cut possible, so you can feel great while achieving all your goals.

As the saying goes, "True Success Starts With Having Hair."

How it works: Users submit a photo to be altered. Barbara (the best barber in town) with the help of Microsoft Azure Facial Recognition technology identifies the faces and an incredible hairstyle is applied to each individual. This app lets you find the next killer hair style that you may have never found otherwise.
