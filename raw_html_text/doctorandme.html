DoctorAndMe

ArchHacks 2016 Project

Your personal communication channel with your doctor. We make sure that your doctor has as much actionable data as possible, so that he or she can make smarter decisions, saving time and money.

We also lend a hand in turning a pesky prescription into an achievable goal, with friendly reminders customized to your schedule and your preferences.

We want to make health easier, and lots of good data is our way of making an impact.
