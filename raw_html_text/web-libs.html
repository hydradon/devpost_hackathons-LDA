Inspiration

Once upon a time, a young woman named Danielle Yucht had a dream... to turn news articles into an interactive and silly game inspired by a classic.

What it does

The Web Libs extension scrapes an article for its content and chooses 10% of the words to replace with user-given alternatives, which it then places in the original article.

What we learned

Misspelling Mark Zuckerberg's name has more consequences than expected

What's next for Web Libs

We would like to polish up and launch this extension on the Chrome store.
