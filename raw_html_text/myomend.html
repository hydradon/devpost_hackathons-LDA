Inspiration

MyoMend uses a muscle sensor arm band that collects motion data when patients perform physiotherapy exercises. We use speed and range of the motion data to track recovery progress over time. Patients receive detailed results of improvement. Health care professionals receive more detailed insight of recovery progress and can better understand changing needs of the patient.
