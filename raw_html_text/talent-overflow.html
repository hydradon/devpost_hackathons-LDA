Inspiration

Based on our experience as Software Development college students looking for a co-op job, we found that employers are looking for people who have real-world experience. We are given advice to work building a portfolio of coding projects. We thought that we could find clients in nonprofits to help them with their technology projects.

What it does

Matching people needing experience with Nonprofits and individuals with a limited budget. They can trade their talent or asset with each other
