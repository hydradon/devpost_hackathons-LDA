SSPScheduler

Problems During Registration • Bad registration website interface. • Many possible schedule combinations. • Time consuming

Afterwards • Tons of alternatives for each session. • Gaps between sessions • Many possible combinations

Solution • An application that handles the mess of the course registration process and produces a lot of tables based on the student’s desire as: 1- Minimum days 2- Minimum Gaps 3- Specific doctors 4- Certain days 5- Comfortable Schedule • Another application which is used during the whole semester. It allows the user to manipulate his weekly schedule as: 1- Alternative date and time for sessions. 2- Suggestions for another desired criteria 3- Monitor the schedule variabilities
