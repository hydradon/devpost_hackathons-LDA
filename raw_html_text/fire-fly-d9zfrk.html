What it does

This skill enables users to find out the energy usage and cost for products and vehicles. For example if a user is planning a trip and wanted to find out how much they will spend on fuel, they simply call the skill Alexa ask fire fly to get the gas usage cost for the audi q7 in new york for 75 miles . And the skill will respond with the given amount that will be spent on fuel

Or if the a small business wanted to know how much they will spend on energy on an commercial fridge, they simply call the skill alexa ask fire fly to get electricity usage cost for Grindmaster CTR3.75 1 Swing Glass Door Countertop Merchandiser Refrigerator

If someone wants to compare the mpg of vehicles before they make a purchase they simply call the skill alexa ask fire fly to compare the mpg between the audi q7 and range rover

Want to find out the electricity cost in a State? simply call the skill Want to know how much it will cost to fill up an electric car or want to know its range, simply call the skill

How I built it

This skill users public from various US Government datasets such as Energy star, fueleconomy.gov
