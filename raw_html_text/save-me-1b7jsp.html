Inspiration

We wanted to provide help to people in need for a home as a consequence of a natural disaster.

What it does

It lets you post your message asking for help on our platform and you will be put in touch with someone as fast as possible.

How we built it

We hosted our websites on firebase and we are collecting our data with natural disasters from an RSS feed. Then you can post a message if you need help and someone who wants to help you can see your request. Then, you will be mapped through phone using the twillio API

Challenges we ran into

Databases management.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The integration of RSS feed.

What we learned

We have deepened our knowledge in databases management. We successfully collaborated and created a user friendly interface.

What's next for Save.me

We will put more thought into it, but for the moment we will promote the idea on social media, aiming to raise awareness first.
