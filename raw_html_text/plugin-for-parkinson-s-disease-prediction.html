Plugin-for-Parkinson-s-Disease-Prediction-

A Chrome extension for predicting the typing patterns of people who have Parkinson's Disease We used keyboard strokes through JavaScript to identify the typing pattern of the user to predict whether he is currently suffering from Parkinson's disease or is a future Parkinson's disease patient
