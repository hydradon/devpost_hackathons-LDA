ERGonomic Devices

We created a device that can be parametrically scaled and utilized by individuals with Cerebral Palsy of the hand to help them utilize normal gym equipment such as a rowing machine.

Individuals with disabilities need to stay active and healthy the same as anyone else. Yet, working out with normal gym equipment can be difficult or impossible and special gym equipment is unrealistic for most. To keep individuals encouraged to exercise we aim to create low-cost, low-effort solutions for staying healthy.
