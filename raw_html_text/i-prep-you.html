Inspiration

  • Every day hundreds of thousands of individuals sit through interviews in hopes of getting their next dream job! i-prep-you is the added layer of preparation that can bring you closer to this reality, with our tailored practice interviews that help you gain the confidence and skills you need to ace that next interview!

What it does

  • Visit the webpage and upload the job description to which your applying to, our system will pick a unique set of questions that are custom to your dream job and will give you instantaneous feedback as to how you can improve your answers and ace your interview!

How we built it

  • We leveraged the Azure Cognitive Services Face and speech-to-text to give the user feedback on their speech, which includes words per minute and filler words, along with facial recognition software that informs the user how their expressions may be interpreted by an interviewer.

Challenges we ran into

  • Scope creep
  • Learning how to use React
  • Teaching others how to use React

Accomplishments that we're proud of

  • A finished product

What we learned

  • Don't bite off more than you can chew

What's next for i-prep-you

  • Integration of machine learning to even better customize questions for the end user
