Inspiration

We love to travel, but sometimes we don't know what else to do in the places we visit. The actual websites just sell you the fly and/or the hotel, but no more else. So we created Experiences!

What it does

Experiences is a mobile app that lets you look for new experiences in the place you are visiting. Just Open the app, login with Facebook and automatically locates you and start looking for awesome activities to do nearby. Select the one you love and pay for it. You'll see the instruction to attend the event and have fun :)

How we built it

We built it as an iOS app using Swift and Xcode and an app to create events. We use Facebook API, Sprite Kit and Alamofire
