Home Foods

Using the McCormick API, we created a demo for what we believe is an innovative and purposeful product. In today’s day and age, many people do not know how to cook healthy, may not have access to all the ingredients they need, or simply do not have the time. With our application, we aim to be able to connect users with chefs. Users can select a product from the McCormick recipe database, requesting any local chef to cook their order. The chefs are able to choose which recipe to cook and then will have to deliver the finished product to the user, all within 30 minutes.
