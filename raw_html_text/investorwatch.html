InvestorWatch is a tool to compare FINRA-registered investment professionals side-by-side to find one you can trust. It features an algorithm to rate advisors and firms and allows finding the most trustworthy firms and advisors to pick.

  • Includes a vast data set with hundreds of thousands of investors
  • Make decisions quickly with our rating that takes in account the advisors experience and ethical history
  • Find out which firms are most trustworthy based on their employees
  • Find the most reputable financial advisors with our ranking system
