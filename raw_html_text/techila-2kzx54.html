Building a better mouse trap with Blockchain

Rodent Management industry is a $8.6 Billion industry with over 20,000 firms in the US. They are growing at 5.2% CAGR and expecting to reach $22 Billion by 2026 globally. Though this growth is exciting the biggest challenge that this industry is facing is labor shortage.

There are two big assets that rodent management firms have, their people and their trucks. Getting a mouse trap placed at a commercial facility requires a truck roll to that facility and costs the company about $75. Once the trap is placed, the company needs to send its people out there to check the trap and then remove any rodents on a frequent basis. This second trip to the facility at $75 per truck roll eats into the profitability of the company.

They are looking for on-demand resources who could check the mouse traps and remove the rodents. This is where Techila Chain comes into picture. We provide a way for the rodent management companies to hire certified professionals who could do the work at a much cheaper cost.
