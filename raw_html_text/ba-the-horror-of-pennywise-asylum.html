Inspiration

The game is based off Pennhurst Education Center and Hospital in Pennsylvania. In the 1960s, it was known as a place where doctors and teachers mistreated and abused the youths housed there. This game is an experiment, and the previous 8 guinea pigs have died. You are the player of Project 9. The game’s is of the horror escape room genre.
