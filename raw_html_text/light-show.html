Inspiration 

fun lights for boring bedroom, a companion app for when you're out and about riding, and a fuel gauge that doesn't touch your gas tank :D

What it does

the led's light up in sync with the music, the app displays educational information, as well as provide a health aspect as it shows nearby medical facilities in case of need, and the fuel gauge does what a fuel gauge is supposed to do: tell you how much fuel you've got left.

How I built it 

 connected wires to arduino and breadboard, coded the software to respond to the change in pitch from the music, programmed the arduinos to work with all the extra modules, and wrote an android app with Android Studio.

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned 

 Arduino, android dev, and basics of Java.
