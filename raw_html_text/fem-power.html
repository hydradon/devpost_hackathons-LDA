Inspiration

I wanted to create an app for women which had different subcategories of hotlines, links, and other important resources for women.

What it does

Fem Power (functional for San Francisco) provides 3 main resources for women:

  1. Hotlines (suicide prevention, Planned Parenthood, dating abuse, domestic violence, sexual abuse) and an option to call each one through the app.
  2. A map of all the local women shelters and details about each one.
  3. A map of all the local women's health clinics and details about each one.

How I built it & Challenges I ran into

I originally started with my two teammates on Android Studio, which I was using for the first time. I was able to display the women shelters on a map with Java and the Google Maps API. My teammates left a couple hours after the hackathon started, but I really wanted to continue with the app idea, even if I was the only one working on it. I was having trouble connecting the different activities in Android Studio and was facing various runtime errors, so in order to fit the time constraints, I switched to MIT App Inventor and finished the app there.

What's next for Fem Power

I would like to create an iOS version, add more resources, and expand to more cities in the U.S. and around the world. In addition, I would like to partner with companies such as Planned Parenthood and create an option for women to donate to or volunteer at women shelters.
