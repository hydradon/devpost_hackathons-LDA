Inspiration

Slither.io Papers, Please!

What it does

It is a world of totalitarian where you either hunt down other players for their "corruption" by collecting the evidence they left behind during the bribery or getting hunt down by them. Your money will quickly run out if you not work hard enough. Oh, what if you get caught with undeniable evidence of the corruption? Guess what: you are dead.

What's next for GoodMorningOfficer

Due to the time limit, some features have been simplified or eliminated, including more than one clue collection, and better method of catching the "corruption" and more. Thus, bringing these planned features back to the game will be the next purpose!
