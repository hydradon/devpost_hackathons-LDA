Inspiration

Spring has come, the beginning of a new life. In Japan, Spring is a season of farewells and meeting new people and moving to another location. But we’re usually bone-tired after moving, unpacking of their baggage. This hack is intended to finish moving in a fun and convenient condition. And it is for welcoming our brilliant new life.

What it does

Using MINIKURA API and AR technology, we propose a new unpacking experience.
One of the most common problem of unpacking is user may forgot which boxes of his / her luggage to put in. Just by holding the smartphone and using our app, you can know it.
Our app overlays box info (currently only luggage category, such as "book", "clothing", etc..) on AR marker that is printed on moving box. You can show list of luggages by tapping on AR Marker.
