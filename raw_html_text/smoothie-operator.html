What it does

Customer comes into our shop and orders a smoothie. They say something like:

I’d like something ___ , with a touch of _ __

Where the first item is a description of the smooth like healthy and the second item is a modifier to the smoothie and is usually something ridiculous like Death.

How we built it

We've built this organic experience in Unity.
