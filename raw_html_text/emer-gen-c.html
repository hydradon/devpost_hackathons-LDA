Inspiration

Our inspiration was the the fact that we know a zombie apocalypse is imminent just any second now, so we needed to be prepared.

What it does

It connects first responders, volunteers, government agencies, or whatsoever, with people in areas affected by natural disasters or any sort of emergency. The decentralized nature of it, yet federated result of it, results in getting the same results of a centralized system, without having to worry about it being taken down (since a new instance can always be put up).
