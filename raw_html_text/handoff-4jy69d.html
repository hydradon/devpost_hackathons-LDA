Inspiration

At a moment, we needed to continue our browsing session on laptop, after arriving in the lab quickly and we wasted a couple of minutes, to search again for the page and reload it.

What it does

The application is a handoff tool which allows you to transfer your current browsing session from the phone to laptop, using a simple gesture (a slide upwards).

How we built it

Using React, nodejs and scss 

Challenges we ran into

One of the team members had a small health problem, which made us lose about 6 hours. Furthermore, we encouraged a couple of technical problems and lack of knowledge when dealing with socket.io.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We delivered a functional tool on time.

What we learned

We became even more familiar with React.

What's next for HandOff

The switch from laptop to phone, adding fade in / out animations.
