Inspiration

I wanted to work on a project that would make a difference for others.

What it does

It will sort charities based on previously answered questions that are pressed as buttons and will display the charities that take the items that people want to donata with the closest location.

How I built it

I used android studio with java and xml, and based my design from the website.

Challenges I ran into

How the charities are sorted, and how the nearest location affects which are displayed

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I got the main part to show up as a slide show.

What I learned

Learned to make a slide show that leads to another activity, and place links on buttons.

What's next for Donii

Implementing the sort for the charities, and integrating google maps.
