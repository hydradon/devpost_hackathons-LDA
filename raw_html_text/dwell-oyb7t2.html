Inspiration

Millineils struggle with job searching and home ownership (Myself included). Wanted to make a service that can help solve this problem but also make it fun and not a burden by gamifying it.

What it does

Analyzes Tweets, Companies/Brands followed, Glassdoor data, and Instagram photos to score you on how well your doing as a working professional. job for you. Machine learning is used to help choose a job or pick where to get your grand prize, a house!!!

How I built it

Deployed on GCP, NodeJS express app, MongoDB Stitch Backend, brain.js (tensor flow wrapper) for ML.
