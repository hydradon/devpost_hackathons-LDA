Inspiration

I was wondering how far I could push the interactivity of google maps and I realized that there wasn't a good way to go to a place and interactively see the wildlife that lived there.

What it does

Play a game to test your knowledge of animals around the world by being pulled around to major cities and guessing which animals belong in their ecosystem. You can also search for any location and see the animals there.
