I realized that if you hold your phone to your forehead in front of a cd case, you could form a sort of augmented reality device. The cd case is transparent and thus doesn't occlude your field of view, and is reflective, allowing you to see what's displayed on your phone. The phone and cd case are mounted on a ruler, and held on your head by a duct tape helmet. Finger tracking is implemented with opencv, and is used in one toy experiment, to play AR basketball.
