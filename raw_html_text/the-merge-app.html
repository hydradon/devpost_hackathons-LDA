What's next for The Merge App

Why?

Do you wish there was one place to see all of your social media feeds at once? Well we feel the same way! That is why we bring to you Merge! Come check us out!

When?

We started this project in 2015. It started of as an idea for an app competition, against group who had great ideas. But now we are here and it is alive! Come give it a try!

Where?

We all attend Florida A&M University located in Tallahassee, Fl. We started working on the project at Domi station but we are planing to go Global! Come along for the ride!
