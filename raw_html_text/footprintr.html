Inspiration

Environmental sustainability is one of the fastest growing factor in deciding consumer purchases. We wanted to provide a convenient and reliable web app that can bypass all the hassle and trouble of looking for environmental impacts for different brands and companies.

What it does

Foorprintr provides a platform that can calculate and display reliable environmental ratings to show and compare how eco-friendly some companies are. This allows consumers to choose which companies they want to support based on that. In addition, it would encourage companies to compete and lower their carbon emission and pollution index.
