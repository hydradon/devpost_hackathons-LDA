Inspiration A common problem illustrated to me throughout high school is that we are often too groggy in the morning and wake up tired. Smart Clock will try to alleviate this problem by waking you up in accordance to sleep cycles.

How it works By running an algorithm to determine the ideal time in order to trigger the alarm in accordance to average sleep cycle patterns.

Challenges I ran into Problems were found in the running of the emulator.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of Becoming more familiar in Android studio and learning important lessons on innovation and teamwork.

What I learned Time management, teamwork

What's next for Smart Clock Customized sleep schedules based on personal measurements taken while the user sleeps.
