• Team KeineWaste

Thousands of meals are wasted because small businesses and local producers and not able to connect to the food network

KeineWaste aims to solve the food waste problem by connecting food businesses and volunteers in real-time, converting food waste into food donations.

Converts food waste into food donations with technology ✔︎ FAST [Integrated with Facebook and Spoonocular:v:] ✔ SMART [KeineWaste matching algorithm] ✔ SOCIAL Businesses are enabled to do the right thing and support the local community. And community supports them back with KUDOS!

{WHAT’S INSIDE} ✔︎ Open source API SDK on NPM ✔︎ PRODUCTION READY INFRASTRUCTURE ✔︎ EXTENSIBLE -> ready to integrate Instant Communication, Notification, Analytics, Reviews/Scoring, Smart Logistics
