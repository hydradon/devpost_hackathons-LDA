Inspiration

Transcription is a very tedious process, especially for those of us without perfect pitch!

What it does

Da Capo records you singing or playing an instrument and transcribes it onto sheet music!

How we built it

We built an Android app to record music, then we used a Fast Fourier Transform library to analyze the pitch.

Challenges we ran into

Wrapping our heads around Android development practices and concepts behind Fourier Transforms took a lot of research.
