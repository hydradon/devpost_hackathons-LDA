Inspiration

Do Not Disturb was inspired by seeing my friends and I getting distracted when we try to study. Added gamification to encourage friendly competition and give you motivation to keep studying!

What it does

You join a session with friends or alone, selected websites are blocked, you finally study, and get points when you complete a session without getting distracted. Claim those points and get rewards! If you do not have a session to join, create your own. Select the websites to block, how long the session is, and what devices you must turn off.

How I built it

I created the design and prototype on AdobeXD, and then used HTML and CSS to try and build it.
