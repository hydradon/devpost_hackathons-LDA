Inspiration

I am a musician and I always wanted to pursue a career in music but I don't know how the industry works and who to collaborate with.

What it does

Gives a platform for musicians to find other musicians to collaborate.

How we built it

We took data of similar songs to the user's songs to find who is a good professional fit. We then did a personality analysis from twitter to narrow our search to who is a good personal fit.

Challenges we ran into

Integrating the entire application from start to finish

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We can actually turn this into a working product and a business

What we learned

We learn that we need to manage our time better. We have all the moving pieces.

What's next for Muzify

We want to work on it and start a business
