Your business users collaborate in Confluence. Your customer information lives in Salesforce. The Connector for Salesforce and Confluence lets business users view up-to-date Salesforce.com objects and data without leaving Confluence. As a result, business users have easy access to the information they need without having to navigate to another system.

With the connector, you can:

  • Connect Confluence Cloud to Salesforce instance. Connect to one or more Salesforce instances.
  • Search for Salesforce objects from within Confluence Cloud, using Salesforce Object Query Language (SOQL) and save the search queries as filters. Use the built-in search filters, or build your own.
  • Use the Salesforce Table Macro to display Salesforce data in Confluence Cloud. Wrap the data around charts to visualize data in Confluence Cloud.
