Inspiration

Entering gaming communities with a competitive nature is always a difficult task. It is most certainly harder for amateur players.

What it does

Connects gaming communities, makes them more inclusive and friendly to new players.

How I built it

Using Angular 7 for front end and Firebase for Server and Database solutions.

Challenges I ran into

Making a user experience that can grab a gamer's attention.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Creating an seamless Angular and Firebase app.

What I learned

More complex relations between Angular and third party libraries

What's next for Friendly

Minimal viable product and beta testing.
