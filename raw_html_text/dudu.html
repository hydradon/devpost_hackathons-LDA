Inspiration

We all have grandmas, grandpas, uncles and aunties that we regularly help with digital technology. It's part of being a family, it's part of being Singaporean. Inspiration struck when we asked ourselves, rather than levelling them up to use technology, why not repurpose technology that was familiar to them? Dudu, the recognisable sound that we all make when we call our uncles and aunties regardless of which language we speak, became the name of our latest product.

What it does

Dudu is a services integrator that turns a traditional phone call into your modern day convenience.

How we built it

We used Sketch to prototype.

Challenges we ran into

Ideation, understanding the needs of seniors.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We feel that the solution proposed will impact a huge number of seniors who are falling through the gaps of technology.

What we learned

Think big, start small, act fast.

What's next for Dudu

Pitch to every major tech company in Singapore and grassroots committees organising digital clinics.
