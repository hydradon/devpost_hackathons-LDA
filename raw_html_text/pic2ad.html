• pic2ad

Inspiration

Instagram--ads are targeted based on hashtags, but not always on the images themselves!

What it does

Our app takes in a photo url, recognizes key features of the photo, and gives targeted ads and shopping results based on these features.

How we built it

We used node-Red and IBM's bluemix platform, specifically the Watson image recognition API. We used HTML and javascript, as well as a lot of regular expressions.

Challenges we ran into

We ran out of our free trial number of images :O Also we used completely new languages and platforms.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We came into this not entirely sure what an API was and with no experience in the languages above, so we're proud we even got to this stage.

What we learned

Too much to list :)

What's next for pic2ad

Make our application seamless and more automated.
