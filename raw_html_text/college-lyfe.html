• FSU's Homepage on College Lyfe

Inspiration

Finding a way to help fellow students be involved more in the community as well as a way to improve it.

What it does?

It shows the user dates, locations, and more for activities, restaurants, night clubs and more.

How we built it?

We use HTML, CSS, and Javascript to help build the website from scratch on Cloud9. We used google maps and calendar as the means to show location and times of events.

Challenges we ran into:

Problems with CSS and HTML not functioning as expected. API's not working with plans.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Finding a way to use google maps to show the locations.

What we learned?

API's may not work as expected. API's require lots of underlying code that may or may not be provided with instructions by Google.

What's next for College Lyfe?

Finishing the development and possibly hosting it for future college students to use.
