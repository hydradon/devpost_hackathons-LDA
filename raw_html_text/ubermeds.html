Inspiration

Ever had a day where you're sick in bed, having just finished your last panadol tablet and now face the uphill battle of driving to the nearby pharmacy just to pick up more over-the-counter medication? Inspired by UberEats, we present to you: GoMeds

What it does

GoMeds essentially draws upon the fleet of pre-existing Suber drivers to bridge the gap between pharmacies and you - by delivering the medication you need directly to your doorstep, anytime and anywhere.

Challenges we ran into

Our team consists of freshmen who are first-timers at hackathons, so it was slightly intimidating to delve into the world of hacking! Nonetheless, we learnt a lot and had a lot of fun (:
