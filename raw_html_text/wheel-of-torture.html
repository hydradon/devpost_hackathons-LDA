Behold the Wheel of Torture! Ever felt yourself procrastinating ALL your work? Ya same.

Well, worry not! Wheel of Torture provides a dynamic (and fun) way to get your life together. Simply add the tasks on your agenda to the pretty list (#tableview #iOSstuggles) and their priorities. Add as many as you please. We may or may not judge you.

THEN we will synthesize your life stuggles into a pie chart. And THEN (drumroll plz) you get to spin the Wheel of Torture (read: Fortune).

Spin away, friends!
