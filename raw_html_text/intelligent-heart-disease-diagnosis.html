HEARTGEL

What it does

Provide patients and doctors with a accurate and reliable way to get heart disease diagnosis result online. Display a preliminary visual result of the diagnosis through data analysis, which not only save time for the doctor and the patient, but also reduce the expensive diagnosis fee for the patients.

Challenges we ran into

What project we should make is benefit for human beings.

How to make our website suitable for our potential customers.

How to build our own site and server.

What's next

Cardiovascular disease is one of the most causes of death all over the world. Our intention is to provide a faster way to get heart disease diagnosis data for patients and doctors using artificial intelligence. In the future, we will continue to expand the healthcare data in order for AI to gain more informations. This not only provides service for more patients, but also reduce the heavy burden on doctors. We believe this product will benefit our society and make life better, and we hope that our efforts can make a little contribution to the medical field.
