unlockMe

HackKing's project Unlockme is a dating app designed for students from the same University. The concept is based on a set of personal information that are locked and private to each user. When a conversation is initiated between 2 users, they don't know anything about each other. They can decide to unlock one information of their choice for the other person to see. This is a way to spice things up by forcing people to interact in order to get to know each other. It is different from other dating sites since it is anonymous and pushes you to really talk to someone before choosing to end the conversation.
