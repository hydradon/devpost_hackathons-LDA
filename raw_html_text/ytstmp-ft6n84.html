YTSTMP

A Google Chrome extension that allows you to add timestamps on YouTube videos which creates markers on the video play back bar and also allows you to add timestamps for ad breaks. The video will automatically skip over sections that have been defined as ad breaks. Mousing over the markers will also provide an on screen description of the marker, providing a quick and easy way to navigate around the video.
