Inspiration

I am a very visual learning and I had a hard time with calculus three because it required visualizing 3D graphs. So I thought it would be a great idea to used VR to graph.

What it does

Uses VR to display graphs.

How I built it

Used unity, blender and online 3D models of graphs to build the VR.

Challenges I ran into

Using the internet to extract 3D models because there are not many 3D models out there!

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Successfully doing VR for the first time!!

What's next for MyGraphs

Incorporating this into different subjects! For example, biology students can use it to see and walk around a human body and chemistry students can use it to visually see the bonds and chemical reactions.
