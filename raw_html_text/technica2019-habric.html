We realized on the van drive here that we don't know much about money and finances. One of us had just had our rent check bounced because we hadn't kept track of our balance of our checking account. Another didn't know the difference between a savings and a checking account. And another was interested in investing money in the stock market but didn't know where to start. We all needed financial help and decided to make our own app where all these aspects of finance could be managed and taught to young people. #Technica2019
