Inspiration

DART Technologies is building a hyperlocal media platform for connected cars. This "flyover module" allows passengers to browse and virtually tour physical world destinations, plugging into our Discover browser.

What it does

Demo Storymapping capabilities of ERSI ArcGIS with geotagged Google Earth flyover content.

What I learned

Creating geotagged Storymaps in ArcGIS, and leveraging 3D mapping capabilities.

What's next for Flyover NYC

Create a script to autogenerate flyovers in Google Earth based on destination and populate ArcGIS map with satellite imagery from real-time sources (e.g. Vricon).
