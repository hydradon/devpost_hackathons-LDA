We thought for the first time about our project few hours before the hacking started in the library. At first we thought about a social network to connect students, and allow them to share their notes and solved exercices. Soon we realized it was too hard for us and we simplified our social network. So, the result of reducing the hardest things and simplifying the others to the minimum is our webapp and our bot that uses a database to book rooms for the groups to study.
