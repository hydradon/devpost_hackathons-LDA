Allowing young refugee women to get education on the go while incentivizing their families with small donations to keep their daughters learning. Education on the go via SMS in the form of short classes and lessons. After a certain number of lessons, the family will recieve a small amount of money, to encourage the family to continue the girls’ education. The long term plan is that the girl will develop into a employed woman and repay the money in the long run.
