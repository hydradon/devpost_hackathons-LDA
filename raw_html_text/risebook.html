What is Risebook?

Risebook is a Foursquare-integrated way of booking meetings at Rise Manchester. When you check into Rise on Foursquare, you become available for a meeting with anyone on your friends list. They just drag your face onto a meeting room and click book.

Ideas for the Future

If I had more time I had planned to integrate the Braintree API to allow people to pre-order coffees for their meeting as well as integrating Twilio to notify people of their meeting bookings.
