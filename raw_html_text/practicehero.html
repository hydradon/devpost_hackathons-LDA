Inspiration

Wanting to make it easier for musicians to practice their music, practiceHero does just that by bettering functionality on the Amazon-Alexa platform. Since you can't practice with your bandmates every time, you can used Amazon Alexa for a hands-free way to control the flow of music. Going back a measure, speeding up or slowing down the music, anything you can do with a human, you can do with Alexa. If you decide you need additional practice with a certain part of the piece, you can skip to the chorus, or the intro. Practicing has never been easier with practiceHero!
