Inspiration

Finding the perfect job environment can be tough. Therefore, we wanted to facilitate the process and automatically connect with cultural fit companies.

What it does

JobFit is a cross-platform app which connects to users' social profiles and categorizes their personalities. The app then calculates match percentages and order companies by its cultural fit.

How we built it

Ionic Framework was used to develop JobFit.

Challenges we ran into

Our team faces many challenges related to the use of Ionic Frameworks and Facebook API.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We believe our app achieves its goals and successfully makes easier for users to connect with cultural fit jobs.

What we learned

Ionic Framework and JavaScript

What's next for JobFit

JobFits is working to further develop our cultural fit algorithm and info about companies environments. We believe we can expand our data by analyzing workers' social media accounts, making environment data more accurate.
