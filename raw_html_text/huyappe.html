Inspiration

Looking to the name of the of the company I remembered the expression "monkey see, monkey do", so I decided to create a game that worked with some things like move repeating, player interaction and duels. I also used the mechanics of a brazilian game played in japanese events called "Huya!", which is the word you say when you make a move.

What it does

The leader chooses a move and clicks in any place of the screen. All the players who make the same move of the leader will lose a point - and for each lost point of the players, the point will go to the leader.

How I built it

Socket IO Node js, Html5, javascript, css.
