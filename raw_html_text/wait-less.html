Inspiration

We have been to an emergency department only to find out that there were so many people in front. Every minute waiting there is a minute suffering the pain. It would have helped a lot if we had a way of knowing a better option in advance so that we can see a doctor as early as possible.

What it does

Wait Less can help with choosing which hospital's emergency department to visit, based on a number of factors. It tells us how far the recommended hospital is, how long we need to get there, and more.

What's next for Wait Less

Wait Less currently operates within the state of New South Wales, Australia, but has plans to service the wider community of Australia and the world.
