Inspiration

We wanted to make an app that provides a short info about a beautiful city of College Station so that user can take notes and edit them on the fly!

What it does

Provides a short info about a beautiful city of College Station with all notes taken during the trip around the city.

How we built it

This is an iOS app built for iPhone 6 and 6s using swift and Alamofire netwrking framework.

Challenges we ran into

-Scrollview not responding properly in Xcode -Constrain layout issues

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It actually works! And we took it out to explore the city!

What we learned

How the views work in Xcode and info about College Station.

What's next for TravelDiary

Integration of location, services, food, Uber availability.
