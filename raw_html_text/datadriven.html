Inspiration

There's so much undiscovered data. Let's use this to connect people.

What it does

Aggregates your location history data to match you with potential carpool buddies who share similar routes.

How we built it

Three servers and one react front end

Challenges we ran into

IBM watson was a very interesting tool, but hard to get right. Collecting data was equally as difficult

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We built something aesthetically and functionally appealing. We were able to access data that is difficult to access

What we learned

Data is fun. Connecting people together is fun.

What's next for DataDriven

We can provide Businesses analytics based on data that we aggregate
