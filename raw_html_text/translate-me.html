Inspiration

We thought of this idea due to the fact that none of us are comfortable with french.

What it does

This app connects you with a person nearby that is fluent in french so they can help you translate or interpret what you need.

How we built it

Mobile App built Android Studio with a NodeJS backed with sockets hosted on Heroku.

Challenges we ran into

Setting up the correct type of connection between translators and users within the app.

What we learned

Socket.io does not provide real web socket utility.
