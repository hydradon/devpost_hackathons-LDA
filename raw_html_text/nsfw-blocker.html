NSFW-Blocker is:

A Chrome extension utilizing Clarifai image recognition APIs to block NSFW content in your browser.

Parents in the 21st century struggle to maintain internet boundaries with their kids. With the NSFW blocker Chrome Extension, parents can enjoy peace of mind while their kids are protected from inappropriate content using the power of AI.
