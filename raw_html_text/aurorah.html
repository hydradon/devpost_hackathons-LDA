Inspiration

Our personal experiences with stress and anxiety

What it does

Addresses three different areas of wellness; mental, environmental, and physical. By suggesting nearby green areas, tracking mental health reports, and providing nutritional information we aim to provide a holistic plan to tackle mental health issues

How we built it

ReactJS front-end, which connects to a Go back-end that provides access to APIs such as Google Maps

Challenges we ran into

Many.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Learning the nuances of our chosen technologies and growing as developers.
