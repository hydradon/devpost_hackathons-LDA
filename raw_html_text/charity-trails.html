For ProfHacks 2019, we came up with an app idea. This would be a running app, allowing the user to track their running data. Additionally, there would be in-app features allowing the player to customize their avatar based on the currency they earned from running. Finally, there would be a charity component, allowing the player to donate to charity the more they run.
