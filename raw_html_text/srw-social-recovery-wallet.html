How to try:

  1. Create an account (Enter the password and save QR code) - we will automatically send you testnet ETH
  2. Tap 'Pick friends' and enter 3 nicknames of your friends who are already registered
  3. Wait until they will get notification and will validate that you are a friend
  4. Deposit your funds to your smart contract wallet via 'Deposit'
  5. Right now let's imagine that you loose your QR code (it's a private key encrypted by your password)
  6. Tap 'Restore Wallet' - you will get a new QR code with new public address
  7. Wait until your friends will verify that it's you. (send them a message that you lose your private key for that)
  8. Done!
