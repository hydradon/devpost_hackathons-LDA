• Mapping your browsing habits through data visualization

Description

Meet New.t, your new Google Chrome extension that maps your tab activity to help you visualize your browsing habits. It replaces the classic Google Chrome new tab page with a colorful display of most-likely tabs connected through link histories. At the heart is an intelligent graph-search algorithm, operating on time-frequency analysis and k-nearest neighbors. This ensures that your new tab page always remains relevant no matter what time of the day it is. With a weighted list of pertinent links you’ve visited graphed across the screen, New.t is able to grasp the relationship between the two to improve upon its real-time suggestions. Don’t believe us? Download t and watch it revolutionize the way you organize your thoughts and schedule!
