Inspiration

The idea comes from car cards where the winner is based on the best specs

What it does.

It compares a phones specs with some other phone through a wifi-connection

How I built it

Originally the app should should make a Wi-fi-connection p2p and then tell the other phone which Brand/model they are. During development we had some problems due to our lack of experience with Android development. Therefore we made a prototype which fakes this interaction.

Challenges I ran into

Getting the phones to communicate p2p. Eventually we got this working, but could not implement the rest of the functionalities in time.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We got the p2p connection working

What I learned

Do not start developing in environments that you are not familiar with - for next hackathon.

What's next for Phone Battle
