The problem is the that there are resources available, but they're not always trusted. Our goal is to successfully digitize the word of mouth of the community.

The key features are the following: reliable - community engagement, crowd sourced; intuitive - large icons with little text removes the literacy/language barrier; easy to use - one clic access to target information, mobile friendly website.

The service will have an algorithm that sorts venues based on a category, providing a trust score to the user.
