Inspiration

I saw a man pee on Marta and felt unsafe.

What it does

This app allows you to share your Marta journey with your friends, so they can know where you're headed and how long your trip is. If you don't arrive by the destination time, the app will alert your friends.

How we built it

We used Swift to create an iOS app.

Challenges we ran into

Version control, limitations set by Apple

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We're proud of the UI, idea, accessing contacts to text, and segues between screens.

What we learned

We learned Swift! And good version control practices

What's next for sMARTA

We want to expand sMARTA for other modes of transportation, such as electric scooters, walking, and convert it to an iMessage app.
