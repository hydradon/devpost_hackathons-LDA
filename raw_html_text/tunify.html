What it does

Create interactive music communities through location based playlist tagging. Similar to location based snapchat stories we are creating geo-fenced Spotify playlist that are community driven and made.

Technology

Azure + MS Cognitive + MS Power BI + Facebook Login API + Spotify API CNTK, MS Deep Learning, & Cognitive Toolkit

What's next for Tunify

Personal Playlist - Users can passively share their playlist with others around them becoming a dynamically moving beacon. E Commerce - Driving Users to Spotify Premium + Allowing Business to Develop Sponsored Playlist to be tailored to their location. Customer Created - Businesses can use their playlist to fill up their space. Ex. Gas Stations can have user playlist play at their pump
