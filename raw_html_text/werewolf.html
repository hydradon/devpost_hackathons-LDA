Werewolf

Other than murder and deceit, the essence of the Werewolf game (quite similar to mafia) is the narrator's skills in storytelling. However, you don't always have a deck of cards to distribute to the players for roles and it can get confusing keeping track of the "living condition" of players for the narrator. Hence, introducing the narrator-facilitating web Werewolf, where the narrator has full freedom to control days and night yet can also play at ease without a deck of cards or a tedious spreadsheet.
