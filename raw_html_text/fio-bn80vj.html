Welcome to fiO

As college students, we found that managing bills, deposits, and rent when living with roommates was harder than expected. Not only do you need to make sure you've got electricity, water, internet, etc, you also need to keep track of who needs to pay how much. We've brought everything together to allow for a simple renting experience and plan to make it even easier with tools to find residences and the power to manage even more bills.
