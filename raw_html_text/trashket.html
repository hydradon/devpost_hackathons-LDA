Inspiration

What it does

Track the trajectory of a piece of trash a person throws away. Move in the direction of the launched trash to catch it in a mounted trash bin

How we built it

A car implementing an H-bridge motor driver and a camera that tracks where flying trash heads towards.

Challenges we ran into

Calculating launch trajectories. Debugging hardware issues. Installing python packages

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We implemented a CV algorithm that tracks a blue ball to a high degree of accuracy.

What we learned

What's next for TrashKet

Making the CV algorithm more accurate and versatile.
