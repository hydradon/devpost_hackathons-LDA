Inspiration bricks are a building material that is harmful to the environment in multiple way as in the degradation of farmland and large CO2 emissions due to smelting the clay.

What it does it is a program that lets you enter a ground plan for a building as well as the height and the program tells you how many bricks are needed and how many grams of CO2 are released from smelting the bricks.

How I built it. I built it using algorithms in snap.

Challenges I ran into snaps limited capabilities.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of making a fully functioning program.

What I learned. I learned that snap although easy to use has limited features.

What's next for brick simulator? Nothing it is the greatest program ever to be made!
