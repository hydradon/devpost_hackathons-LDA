What it does: Interactive platform that encourages students to share their ideas and learn through a discussion forum.

How we built it: JAVA, JavaScript, MySQL, HTML5 CSS3.

Challenges we ran into: 1. Pooling of ideas. 2. Time Constraint. 3. Retrieving dynamic content.

Accomplishments that we're proud of:1. Team Work. 2. Exploring domains that we were not aware of, and using it in our product.

What we learned: Weaknesses.
