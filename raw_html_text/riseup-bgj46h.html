Inspiration

There are millions of depressed teenagers and adults across the world. Our app assists people to follow our solutions to help with their depression.

What it does

It consists of a 10 question quiz and leads to effective solutions to overcome depression.

How I built it

We used a software called Kony Visualizer to design our app.

Challenges I ran into

We had trouble with making sure our global variable, count, behaved as expected.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We worked very hard on this project and barely got any sleep.

What I learned

I learned a lot about app design and user interface.

What's next for RiseUp

We want a chatgroup and supportive, interactive community for people who are feeling lonely.
