Inspiration

To eradicate hunger in our community and hopefully the world

What it does

With our application we seek to resolve the current food waste that occurs in food companies and occupy those resources to feed people in need of Morelos. This will be achieved with a model of self-sufficient business where a part of the community (upper class) donates money and other (middle class and upper middle) donates time and / or resources (transportation, warehouse to store food and food) . In this way we join the community, by converting an act of altruism in a fun game with real prizes.

How we built it

We used Android Studio for the main development part, firebase for our prototype, and Microsoft Cognitive Services for our security assurance issues

Challenges we ran into

While Development, figuring out the different modules and how they interact between each other, specially in the Microsoft Cognitive Services Module

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Creating a Viable Project in such a short time, with demos of our main features

What we learned

A lot from android development, as well as the Microsoft Cognitive Services Rest API, leadership

What's next for CommunityPlay

Implement AWS as the main database platform, finish and polish the App
