Inspiration

To find a creative way to use the TRNQL API.

What it does

This app allows you to create 1 advertisement per account, which the users in proximity can see in their advertisement feed.

It lists the advertisements and can be used to see the various services/business in the area nearby.

This app is all about posting your advertisement for free!

The app takes advantage of TRNQLs location based services to pin point the advertisement to the area where it is posted in.

So basically it allows you to post the advertisement in the user's current area. The other users of the app can see the advertisement when they are nearby that location.

You just have to post your advertisement and the users of the app would be able to see it when they'll be in proximity to the location of your advt. For example, suppose you live in XYZ street in California. You open the app and create your advertisement by adding your contact details and name. People who'll be looking for services or business nearby using the app can see your advertisement in their feed and contact you via the details you provided. Simple isn't it ? So dont hesitate, try the app now !

How I built it

Built using Xcode 7 and Swift 2.0 and of course TRQNL api, specifically uses Smart People, Smart Places and Smart Weather APIs .
