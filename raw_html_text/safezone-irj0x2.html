Everyday people are victims of crime. Whether you are suspicious of activity in your area or your life is being threatened, the most important factor in resolving the issue is efficient assistance from authorities. This is what SafeZone allows users to get. At the click of a button on your mobile device, support such as police, ambulance, or fire will be alerted of your need for assistance immediately. A simple to use application with built in features such as alerts, maps to show recent activity in your vicinity, and contact with support, will enable victims reach out for help at crucial times of need.
