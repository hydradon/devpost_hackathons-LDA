What It Does

Our idea is to record a chat between a customer and a seller. We check if it is match with the seller’s script: Has the seller greeted the buyer? Did he offer him products for stock or at a discount? The analysis would reveal sellers who are rude to the customer or forget to offer additional goods. Working with such ‘leakages’ would help retail business to increase profits and brand loyalty, which his crucial in demanding retail businesses.

How We Built It

Technologies: Telegram API + Flask + Yandex SpeechKit
