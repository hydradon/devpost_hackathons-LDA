Road rage is a problem that occurs to everyone of all ages, especially people under the age of 19. We were inspired to make RoadCalm after an extreme road rage situation to one of our group members. To mitigate this toxic problem, RoadCalmprovides a simple, but smart solution implementing text to speech and speech to text recognition and facial emotion APIs.
