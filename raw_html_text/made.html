Inspiration

The Creuna case have inspired us to find the best from online shopping and incorporate it into our project. The best feature from online shopping is the possibility to make thorough research before buying the product. F.x. reading expert reviews, user comments, specifications, and comparing products.

What it does

MaDe is an augmented reality application that helps the user to make a decision while they are at the store.

How we built it

We made it in Unity with the plugin Vuforia.
