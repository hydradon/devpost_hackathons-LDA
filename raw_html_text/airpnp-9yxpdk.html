Inspiration

When nature calls, just pick up your phone. With airpnp, your next royal flush awaits.

airpnp is the next step in optimizing interactions between humans and the public facilities that surround them. Personalized profiles presented in an interactive and stimulating format designed to give you your best lavatorial experience.

What it does

Upon arrival at the landing page, each client is offered the opportunity to "Explore amenities near you." The client is then presented with an amenity within their proximity and important information pertaining to it, such as name, rating, location, distance, and even a bio. If the client taps to the left of the screen, the current amenity is discarded and a new one is presented. If the client taps to the right of the screen, directions to the amenity are given via Google Maps.

How we built it

The airpnp website was birthed with a careful combination of HTML, CSS , Javascript, and our own cheeky problem solving skills. Graphics were designed in GIMP, and domain.com was utilized to secure our clear and concise URL.

Challenges we ran into

Here at airpnp, user experience is paramount, whether on or off the screen. Creating a sleek mobile design without compromising desktop usage was a main priority of ours in order to extend our stream of golden services to the most consumers possible. Rigorous trial and error formatting of each section of the website proved immensely useful in the pristine-as-porcelain presentation through both mediums.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

One of the great hallmarks of this project is the impressive User Interface presented to each user of airpnp. Expert use of GIMP, CSS, and general web design has lead to some of the most visually captivating web pages seen at a hackathon.

What we learned

Through the creation of this project, the airbnb development team has learned that in order to deliver the highest quality of services, you may have to get your hands dirty.

What's next for airpnp

This project can be expanded through more thorough integration of Google Maps, interactive amenity ratings, and indoor directions to the services being looked for.
