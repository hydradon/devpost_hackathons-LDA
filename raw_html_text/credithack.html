Inspiration- As college students not all of us have credit cards and have knowledge about it. We are here to help many among us make the right decision on choosing a right card and maintaining good credit score.

What it does- An app that help consumers, obtain their first credit card or improve your scores in the current card with suggestions and tips we provide.

How I built it- We built the app using Android Studio, with Java and XML

Challenges I ran into- Pitching in the idea was a challenger we ran into. We had many variations on how we wanted to implement this idea such as user-friendly game. But at the end we narrowed it down to profile based app

Accomplishments that I'm proud of- Participating in our first Hackathon and being able to make complete a working app has been our greatest accomplishment.

What I learned-Meeting many mentors, leaning a lot about the sponsors and what opportunities we have ahead of us.

What's next for Credithack-As we go to the same college, it has been a fun journey this weekend and we will continue to do so.
