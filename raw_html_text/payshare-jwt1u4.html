Inspiration

Everytime we head out to a party or go and chill with friends, we have a hard time splitting the bill. With PayShare, this experience can be a thing of the past.

What it does

PayShare is android app that allows you to split money with your friends properly along with a payment system and delivery system. Pricing will automatically change according to people involved with CapitalOne's Nessie API. We used Postmates API to arrange delivery and pricing for businesses around you.

How I built it

We used Android with a MEAN stack backend hosted on a Linode Server. We use Mongo.db for database. As well, we utilized APIs from CapitalOne and Postmates

Challenges I ran into

We had plenty of hard time in designing this app and making the app very smooth and pretty. There were also plenty of trouble in dealing with authentication and real-time updating.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We created a very well designed Material design app. We are also proud of being able to create something we would use on a daily basis.
