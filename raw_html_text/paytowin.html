Inspiration

The majority of top grossing apps on the App Store are games. These games, which all implement a freemium model, are designed in such a way that in order to be amongst the best players, you have to buy in-game items which completely undermines any skill. So why not create a game that is slimmed down to just a platform where you buy points directly via in-app purchases; a mobile game in it's purest form.

What it does

After a user logs in with Facebook, they are presented with leaderboards of high scorers for the day, week, month, and all time. Users can earn 10 points by just opening up the PayToWin app every 24 hours, but users can also pay for points with in-app purchases to drastically and rapidly boost their leaderboard rankings.
