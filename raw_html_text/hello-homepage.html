Inspiration

Inspired by our own busy lives, Hello Hompage is meant to serve as an all-inclusive resource for organizing, prioritizing, and scheduling your day-to-day activities. Receive a quote of the day to give you a mental boost, and an interactive to-do list to stay current on your responsibilities.

What it does

Organizes one's schedule and provides a zen space to begin work for the day.

How I built it

HTML, CSS, lots of googling

Challenges I ran into

We had no CS background and had to teach ourselves html and CSS code.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Proud of creating something of which we ourselves can benefit from as students.

What I learned

Various forms of HTML and CSS

What's next for Hello Homepage

Continue stylizing and add features to increase productivity.
