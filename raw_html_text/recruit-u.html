What it does

Recruit.U is a web application to connect GE recruiters with potential candidates. The application allows applicants to upload their resume, answer some personality questions, and verify eligibility. The application will then extract the applicants name, contact information, skills, and potential interests from the initial application. Recruiters will then be able to upload job descriptions and the application will match the job description to potential candidates that have an account based on skills, interest, and eligibility.
