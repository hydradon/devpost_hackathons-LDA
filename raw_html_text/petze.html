Inspiration

Berlin

What it does

  • Alert others of musicians playing in BVG trains.
  • Be warned by other in which train wagons a musician might be.

How I built it

react-native + kontakt.io beacons + graphcool

Challenges I ran into

  • ~5 react-native beacon libraries didn't work and wasted a lot of time on them

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

  • Tested out the beacons in the wild.
  • Got the beacons working with react-native

What I learned

Beacons can have quite a far range!
