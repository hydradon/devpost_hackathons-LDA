It’s a 360° interactive queer poem created with Viond platform. It’s a nonlinear experience where the user can choose how to continue and end the poem.

The text contains multiple endings and possibilities that happen in a queer breakup and process of grief. With up to 8 different combinations for the poem giving the user agency to curate their own literary experience in that immersive environment.
