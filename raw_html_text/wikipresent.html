• WikiPresent

WikiPresent is here and it's revolutionary. Turn any Wikipedia Article into an easy to read 2-dimensional presentation in reveal.js.

The fun doesn't end there though, with leap motion support, WikiPresent brings your presentations to life, allowing you to control the presentation with your hands. A single finger can act as a laser pointer, and with a wave of your hand, the show can go on.

It will be live at: www.wikipresent.com , but for now you can access it at: sayak97.pythonanywhere.com
