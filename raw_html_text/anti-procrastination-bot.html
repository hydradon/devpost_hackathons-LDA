Inspiration

I procrastinate. A lot. I needed a way to keep myself productive and track my progress towards my goals. To solve this, I created the anti-procrastination bot.

What it does

Every night, the bot texts me and asks me to write a few sentences about my day, and answer a few yes or no questions about my progress towards my goals. Every morning, it messages me a daily update with weather, stats on my progress over the past two weeks, and other info.

It can also be used to track my weight.

It integrates with 6 apis:

Google sheets
Todoist
Twilio
NWS Weather
Facebook messenger
Ifttt
