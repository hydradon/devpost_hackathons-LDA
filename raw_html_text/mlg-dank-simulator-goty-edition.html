• Starting Screen for MLG Dank Simulator GOTY Edition.

Inspiration

We wanted to build the dankest text-based adventure game to allow our audience to experience the skill needed to rescue a race, and one day, use it to their advantage to save the dankest meme.

What it does

You are placed in a randomly generated dungeon trying to level up to beat the ultimate Illuminati leader. Simulations include a built-in difficulty level set by the user.

How I built it

We used a C++ editor, a lot of time, food, and caffeine.

Challenges I ran into

We ran into challenges with bracket placements along with many syntax errors.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am proud of our health point and leveling system. I am also proud of our game's ability to randomly generate a dungeon.

What I learned

I learned how similar C++ is to Java. I also learned a little C++.

What's next for MLG Dank Simulator GOTY Edition

We will be able to make a functional GUI version along with many other bug fixes.
