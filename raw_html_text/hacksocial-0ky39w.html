Have you ever REALLY wanted to enter a hack-a-thon or programming competition, but have a lack of hacker friends to make a team with? Well, then you need to get Hackified! Hackified is an online social platform that allows those in need of hacking teammates to reach out to skilled programmers, and gives those skilled programmers the resources to search for upcoming competitions to join. This website, alongside helping with getting teams together for hack-a-thons, can also get passionate programmers with similar interests together, creating lasting friendships that might not have been possible otherwise. Just remember; when you need a hand, get Hackified!
