Inspiration

Another version on the word game Fizz buzz: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fizz_buzz

What it does

You play with it. You will need to reply with a BOOM at on any number that is a multiple of 7 or contains the digit 7. The word BOOM changes from time to time, and also the number, 7 changes.

How I built it

Using Python and Lambda.
