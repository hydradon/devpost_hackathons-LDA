Inspiration

Leah is very creative and loves to write her own stories. We wanted to share some of them with other Alexa users.

What it does

The Story Fairy tells you the names of the stories in the Fairy Library, and you can choose one for her to read to you.

How I built it

Using the Alexa SDK for node.js

Challenges I ran into

We went through several iterations of ideas before settling on this one due to time and technical constraints.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Submitted a working Alexa skill!

What I learned

The basics of the Alexa SDK and node.js

What's next for Story Fairy

Keep checking back for new stories!
