I am building this for my senior research project and originally an idea I had with my roommate to help us keep track of our lifts. The goal is to give the user a hands free experience at the gym with integration of the Apple Watch, Fitbit and the Pebble.

Challenges

  • Learned swift to do this project, only had practice with one other project that was very simple
  • Creating table views to connect to other table views and pass their values correctly
  • Drawing the user distance on a map with colors to specify their speed based on the CLLocation values
  • Never used the Apple Watch so learning WatchOS was a new experience
