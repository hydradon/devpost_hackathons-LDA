wildhacks2015

We are very interested in finding out how the current affairs are tied up with the Internet search. We all understand that big events (locally or globally) always attract people's attention. One of the most direct way to reflect people's interest is they go online and try to find out what's happened. With this mind, we think we might be able to find some interesting facts by observing how a key word trends on the Internet. We believe that any anomaly in the trend should correspond to a event happened in the history and that's what we are presenting in this project.
