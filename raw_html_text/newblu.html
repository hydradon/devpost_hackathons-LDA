Inspiration

Being able to work with cars and develop new technologies to be used in them.

What it does

It would be connected to your car via Bluetooth and could handle multiple device connections via Bluetooth (up to three). A lot of cars can only support one Bluetooth connection at a time, whereas with our device you could easily switch between several devices within a car.

How I built it

We used Java to model the basic operations that would be completed by our device and 3D printing to create a basic model of what our device would look like.

Challenges I ran into

3D printing was an issue for a little bit, and we had some trouble thinking about how to model the operations of our device with software.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We think that we created a pretty cool idea!

What I learned

3D printing, the capabilities of NX, and abstracting the operations of a device.

What's next for NewBlu

Being able to connect our device to a universal set of cars with Bluetooth.
