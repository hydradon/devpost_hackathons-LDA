Inspiration

Our product hopes to be as efficient as Chef Curry's automatic three-pointers

What it does

Using Alexa, we initiate a conversation with a customer to record their order and upload the information to a database.

1. Quickly adopts new restaurants to the platform via a Menu Converter.

2. Cuts costs and minimizes workload for waiters.

3. Dynamically shows order information to both restaurant and customer and allows easy payment through Capital One API.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Completing and integrating a multidimensional project with a such a small group of developers.
