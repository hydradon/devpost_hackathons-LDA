Inspiration

The death of our lord and savior Harambe, and the scary face of Hurricane Matthew.

What it does

It's a playable game where you, as our savior Harambe attempt to save Florida from Hurricane Matthew while trying to stay away from Hurricane Matthew and collect some bananas.

How I built it

I used Processing to make the gui elements simple to work with

Challenges I ran into

Getting sound to work and stop the game once Harambe succumbs to the storm presented interesting challenges.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I'm proud of how good the images look, and how it will honor Harambe.

What I learned

I learned the changes from processing 2 to 3, as well as how to use sound, images, and loop manipulation.

What's next for HaramMatthew

Improve the looks and scalability.
