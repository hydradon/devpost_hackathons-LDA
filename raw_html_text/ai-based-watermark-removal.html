Inspiration

Showcase the bleeding edge research of how machine learning systems can be used for content piracy

What it does

A demo that attempts to remove a watermark from an image without making it obvious.

How I built it

I used pytorch and a server to train a neural network to learn from 1.9 images. It tried to remove watermarks again and again till it succeeded.

Challenges I ran into

Servers are expensive and I could not afford a higher memory one to experiment with better possibilities.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I managed to make a working proof of concept.

What's next for AI Based Watermark Removal

Develop secure systems to prevent my system from working. A 'good' ai that tries to undo what my project is doing.
