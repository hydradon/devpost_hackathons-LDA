here's the presentation: BikeSafe

Inspiration

We wanted to tackle a pressing issue in our city, thus we chose the unsafe streets for biking.

What it does

Our data would be used to help the government find ways to improve the commute system to accommodate bikers and it would be used ass an app for the public where people would be aware of dangers in their path.

How I built it

We used tableau to collect our data. We used google slides to pitch our idea.

Challenges I ran into

We didn't have that much time to implement our idea which really took away much of our ideas potential.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We are proud of our idea because we believe it could really make a difference in the city.

What I learned

I learned a little more about how to compile data and to analyze it.

What's next for BikeSafe

The next step is to implement it.
