The Blind Date Matchmaker simplifies the process of setting someone up with a blind date (as a matcher), or getting connected with a blind date (dater).

Matchers sign up to create a "list" and receive a randomized code, which is shared with friends (daters) who want to get set up with a blind date. Those who sign up with that code enter a pool of people who are also looking for blind dates. Matchers then get to choose people to pair up!
