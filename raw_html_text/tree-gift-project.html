Inspiration

Planting trees and writing a thank you email has been a method I've slowly adopted as a way of thank you to friends and strangers who've invested in me.

I wanted to build something gave the deserving experience to feel great while planting trees!

What it does

You plant some trees, write a message, and bam ... you can see your beautiful trees, and also share that gift with your friend.

How I built it

React, Express and Node Firebase, ThreeJS

Challenges I ran into

Managing the ThreeJS layer while using React was tricky, but it was a lot of fun to be honest!

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It's live, it works and that's what matters to me. Finishing something that works (and anyone can use)

What I learned

ThreeJS!

What's next for Tree Gift Project

More Tree visualisation
