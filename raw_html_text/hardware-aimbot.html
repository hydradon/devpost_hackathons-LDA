Inspiration

bongo

What it does

this hardware system uses open cv to find a target in siege aim for a perfect headshot and fire almost better than a human could; without being banned for using a trackable software aimbot.

How we built it

Pain and Redbull.

Challenges we ran into

Getting the OpenCV to use the capture card; along with with accurately detecting its intended target rather than literally every other object in the room.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

we lived ....... also by then end, we had created a semi-accurate aimbot capable of hitting its target most of the time. Also, the incorporation of an Arduino Leonardo as an aim assists; making the device untraceable by the game's cheat detection software.

What we learned

the conceptual and semi-practical application of OpenCV in gaming assistance and tracking.
