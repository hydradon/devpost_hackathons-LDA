Inspiration

While thinking about the troubles of our daily life, parking is one of the biggest obstacles that we face in a seamless, interconnected society. It takes much time to find a parking spot, and we always have to rush back to our car to add more money to the meter if we have to stay longer. We were thinking of solutions to make the process of parking and paying, and we came up with Parkr.

What it does

Parkr offers a sustainable solution that not only finds available parking spots but also eliminates the need of having to go and pay at a parking meter by automating the process entirely. It will also allows you to worry less about unforeseen circumstances such as vehicle theft, natural disasters, auto insurance claims and other causes as there is a live video feed that is maintaining the safety of your vehicle.
