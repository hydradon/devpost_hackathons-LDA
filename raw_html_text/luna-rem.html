Luna-REM Sleep and Relaxation Aid

Inspiration

A twist on the classic sleep mask, created to help insomniacs, and those who find it difficult to relax.

What it does

Blocks outside sounds and visual cues, by supplying a stream of soft lights and sounds. This combined with state-of-the-art noise canceling technology.

Hard work and determination

Accomplishments that we're proud of
