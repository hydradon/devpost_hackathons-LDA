asOne builds a support network of close friends for the night to take steps to ensure everyone’s safety, fighting bystander inaction.

What features differentiate us?

  • Periodic pinging of geolocation ( updates ) geofencing to be added,
  • Battery life level notifications: when battery is at 5% send location and push notification
  • use case is broad and regardless of gender, help protect and take action for helping women and keeping men accountable for their behavior when drunk
  • Audio recording triggering by friend in group in case of emergency (status not updated in a while, location elsewhere, phone about to die and not responding), but this audio will not be streamed or leave the user’s device. Can only be shared/released by the owner of the device, to combat he said / she said
  • Ping (Soft push or loud sound played)

Future features:

  • I’m home automatic ping *verification using home location / and their home address geofencing
  • Maybe Uber integration, if phone dies while drive, we can use the Uber API to let the group know their friend has gotten home safe
