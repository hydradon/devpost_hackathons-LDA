Inspiration

Why doesn't my calendar fill the empty spots with stuff I would like to do? It should go and find the concerts I like, and put them in my calendar. This way, I don't have to search to find things I like: they come to me, straight to my calendar.

What it does

It looks like a calendar: it shows you a plan for the day (and then a plan for the following days).

In this calendar you see your planned activities as well as events that are suggested for you.

You can swipe away activities you don't like, and you will get new plan with other activities. Or, if you like the activity, you can reserve or buy a ticket right there.

It plans the locations and times. It even tells you how to get there.

How I built it

It is currently built using the Ionic creator. The dynamic parts are coded in angular/javascript. It uses the TomTom API, to find and display routes between the locations.
