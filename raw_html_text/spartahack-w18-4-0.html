Pet Recovery

The mission is to match lost pets with their owners. This can be especially beneficial after a natural disaster like a hurricane.

When a user finds a pet, they make a profile for their lost friend (filling out as much of the profile as possible). Owners who lost their pet use the app to swipe through all the lost pets to find theirs. If a user knows the dog they can swipe right and get the contact information of the user who has their pet.

Future Goals

Port this to a mobile app with built in messaging to connect owners with their lost pets. Additionally, update the GUI and theme. Eventually, we want to have Facebook login, built in camera, remote updating of profiles, and more.
