Inspiration

Hill food is really not that edible, especially those from B-plate. However, the debate regarding whether hill food is edible goes on forever. So, I decided to settle this using Google's Natural language processing API.

What it does

It gets dining hall menu data, feed it to Google API, and say whether it is edible or not!
