OnlineBhamTimetableConverter

Will automatically add your University of Birmingham timetable to your google calendar (possibly supporting others in the future).

Details

This automatically logs in with provided details and gets your calendar in a format ready to upload to google calendar. Ideally, it will soon also be able to upload this for you but this introduces potential security issues.
