HELP.ME

Seeing the many cases of high school harassment happening lately, we decided to built a framework that would let users view anonymous data , post their own stories and denounces, and connect them directly to institutions that would have people ready to intercede and help the people in need of aid.

we built it in php, javascript, and with a "WIP" android app.
