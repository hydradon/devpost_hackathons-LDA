Inspiration

We wanted to find out if question scores of discovery value, actionability, correctness/confidence, and level of expertise correlated with the measure of scientific productivity Relative Citation Ratio (RCR).

What it does

Graphs data points including conditional probabilities.

How we built it

Least Squares Regression

Challenges we ran into

Relative Citation Ratios (RCR) values are a relatively new metric that are used for biomedical articles, so only around 280 manuscripts were used as datapoints. Difficult to find a model.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Data visualization.

What we learned

Scores are not a good predictor of RCR values, but we managed to detect different behavior between readers of across levels of expertise.
