• Slick and Sleek Single Page App

Inspiration

Love of literature and music.

What it does

Converts blocks of text into sequences of piano notes allowing you to see the differences in sentiment between different authors, genres, and writing styles.

How we built it

LAMP, HTML/CSS/JavaScript, Amazon Web Services

Challenges we ran into

python. note generation algorithms. cross browser support.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We're hot and live.

What we learned

AWS, JavaScript, teamwork, natural language processing, synergy.

What's next for Music, Literally

Scaling. In-app purchases. Music trans-vocalization analytics.
