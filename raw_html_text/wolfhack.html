Wolf Hack

Project for ArchHacks hackation

Problem Statement: People need support groups for the health issues and to reach their health goals.
  • Preventative Healthcare / Goal Setting
    1. Build a health profile
      • Get wellness data from doctor
    2. Set health goals
      • specific , measurable, small
    3. Progress is represented by numbers which make progress bars
    4. Stop health issues before they arise
  • Join support groups - Support Group Platform
    1. See local groups for their specific problem, when/where they meet
    2. Join a group online
    3. Making goals public, allow people to share goals with others.
      • Gives motivation to achieve goals
    4. What happens if they are not meeting their goals?
      • App suggests more reasonable goal for them
