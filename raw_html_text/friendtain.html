Inspiration

Want to keep in contact with old friends but don't always get the chance to with our busy lives.

What it does

Friendtain helps you stay connected with old friends by randomly texting them from predefined greetings. You can also add your own greetings to the possible list of messages.

How we built it

Blood, sweat, tears, sleep deprivation, Java and Android Studio.

Challenges we ran into

Setting up/learning APIs on the fly

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting sms texting functioning, the UI design of the Friendtain

What we learned

Good to research and practice APIs prior to the hackathon as well as know which one(s) you'll be using.

What's next for Friendtain

Sentiment analysis of the responses from friends to determine your relationship with them (bad, neutral, good)
