Examples

Get all Elite cards from the Blackrock Mountain card set:

http://hearthapi.com/v1.0/cards/?cardSet=Blackrock%20Mountain&elite=true

Get the Angry Chicken card by its name:

http://hearthapi.com/v1.0/cards/by-name/Angry%20Chicken

Get the Angry Chicken card by its ID:

http://hearthapi.com/v1.0/cards/by-id/EX1_009
