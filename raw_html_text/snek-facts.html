Inspiration

Kids want fun skills that they can giggle at, in order to encourage them to interact with Alexa. Surprisingly, it pronounces even made-up words like 'snek' very well. I pulled together a bunch of snake facts, but swapped out the word to make it a more endearing skill.

What it does

Provide various facts about sneks (snakes).

How I built it

AWS Lambda function running Node.js.
