Phishing has become a bigger threat to people recently, and we wanted to protect businesses and general people against it. With GrizzlyBear, you can query the domain you want, and it will find all of the domains similar to it within an index. Our web service should help people be safer online with just a quick search on our website. In the future, we plan on making a browser extension to make this service even easier!
