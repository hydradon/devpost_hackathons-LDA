Go Bananas is a side-scrolling adventure game designed for the Pebble Watch.

You play as a monkey who awakes to find his bananas are stolen, and the only natural solution is to use his bananas and shoot his way to find the culprit.

You collect bunches of bananas to replenish your ammo and battle through blue monkeys and gorillas.

It's a completely silly experience.
