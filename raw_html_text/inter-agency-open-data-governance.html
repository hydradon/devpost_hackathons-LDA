Solving for procurement

At present, there is lack of a common process management framework for government agencies and private service providers to operate efficaciously in concert.

For NYC ACS and related human service agencies, blockchain functions as a universal ledger that enables all stakeholders to keep track of processes (maximal organizational transparency) while maintaining data protection and integrity at the user level (maximal individual privacy).

Open data governance

Significant data liberation (extracting data from PDF and other non-machine-readable files into machine-readable formats) would be required, to be conducted in collaboration with Beta NYC (New York's Code for America Brigade) and other civic technology partners.
