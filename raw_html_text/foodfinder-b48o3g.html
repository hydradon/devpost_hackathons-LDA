Inspiration

Everyone gets hungry. Everyone gets lazy. The solution to all your problems. FoodFinder.

What it does

Currently, FoodFinder helps you find a place to eat.

What's next for FoodFinder

Next, I want to make FoodFinder smarter by allowing it to get the users location and find places to eat around the user. I also want to develop different food categories that the user can select from also based on the users location. A favorite food category is also a update that FoodFinder will get soon. These are a few of the ideas I have to improve my app, FoodFinder.
