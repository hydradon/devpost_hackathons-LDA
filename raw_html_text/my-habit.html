Inspiration

There are many habits that I want to build or get rid off. It was necessary to keep track of how long has it been since I have started the habit for motivation to keep going.

What it does

The skill keeps track of how many days, hours or minutes has it been since you have started your new habit. Whenever you ask about your habit, it tell you how long has it been since you have started that habit.

How I built it

Using AWS-Lambda functions, node.js, and dynamoDB

Challenges I ran into

How to take in custom slots.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Completed and published the skill successfully.

What I learned

How to make an alexa skill with custom slots.

What's next for My Habit

Adding multiple habits at the same time.
