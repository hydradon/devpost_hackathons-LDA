Inspiration

Today it is hard to find the human resource and people are wasting times in the games which will not help them in the real world

What it do

A new way of hiring hunters to get great human resources. People can learn the things while they are gaming and also they can gain the certificate after taking the test online

How we built it

We used Unity and C# to build the project. We also did an IOS app in Swift

Challenges we ran into

The camera of Unity always change its size when we are switching the scene, therefore we changed our camera to follow the player which the camera size will not be moved

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We can move our character around and change from one scene to another.

What we learned

We gained more knowledge in C# and Unity. Some skills in IOS programming

What's next for World Script

Connect the IOS app to the game The player room can be modified Create 3D, androids, VR platforms Get more companies to be involved Let users have more efficient time Able to exchange points with real-life marketing supplies in- store and onlinesas
