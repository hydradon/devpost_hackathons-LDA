Inspiration

Making recitation of azkar easier

What it does

Plays audio of morning and evening azkar by Meshary Alafasy

How I built it

It was build with NodeJS that is running on Amazon AWS Lambda.

Challenges I ran into

figuring out how to make Audio Player works with Alexa and being able to play a Long Audio File

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Making a working Alexa skill that can be useful for a lot of people.

What I learned

Learned how to develop a skill with different intents and linking it with Amazon's AWS Lambda

What's next for Azkar Reader

Adding more azkar that are part of a Muslim's everyday life
