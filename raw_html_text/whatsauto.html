Inspiration

I had a car driver honking in order to tell me lights were off. I didn't realize until i got home that my lights were and that's what the driver was trying to tell me

What it does

This app allowing everyone to register with their Licence plate number and communicate via this plate number

We build the app with swift and used Parse for back-end
