Inspiration

Learning and collaborating through VR/AR games.

What it does

Connects two users to perform a shared task in VR.

How we built it

Used multiple Kinects to capture the two different model movements as well as relatively precise fist detection and fire shots with it. Use Rift to track the model/spacecraft movements through the game.

Challenges we ran into

Setting up Rift and tying it up with two different versions of Kinect V1 & V2. Making the scene look decent enough in Unity (since none of us are Graphics folks!!).

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting 3 different hardware with differnt SDKs to work in parallel controlling a model.

What we learned

Learnt coding with Rift as well as generating, modifying and combining different VR scenarios.

What's next for KinectOc Arena

Extend it with more focus towards benefiting the community by developing collaborative medical/rehabilitation games/apps for betterment of the humanity. Develop better and more immersive games with >2 users collaborating.
