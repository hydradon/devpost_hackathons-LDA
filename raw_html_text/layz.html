Inspiration

We sit in a stationary position for an unhealthy amount of time without noticing.

How it works

Once you sit down, it tracks your movements using an accelerometer. Then, it begins a countdown timer from 30 mins - 1 hour and send push notifications if the movement requirement is not met. If movement is meant anywhere in the timeframe of the countdown, the timer resets.

Challenges I ran into

We had many challeneges in setting up the accelerometer and push notification to activate when needed.
