Inspiration

We found out that, in the part of the world where we live, it's very hard to get employment. Even with the right requirements and qualifications, it is still very hard. We've come to realise this isn't a problem for Africa only, it's a global problem. What we are working on is, a solution to connect people with the right employers/employees. We're helping people to connect with the right opportunities, form better communities and bring the world closer together.

What it does

Job It, is a platform for Job seekers to find and connect with their dream jobs with ease. Employers can also look out for amazing talents to employ by posting job vacancies.

How we built it

We built this solution with Android Studio and Firebase for the backend. We implemented Facebook Log in api for the user authentication. Facebook graph api to get locations of users.
