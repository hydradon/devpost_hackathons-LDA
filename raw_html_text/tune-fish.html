Inspiration

Because of the disappearance of the headphone jacks and the ever-prevalent Bluetooth connectivity issues, sharing music between friends requires playlists. What if a playlist was automatically generated based on your and your group's listening habits?

What it does

The last 50 songs a user listened to is accessed and processed to analyze its compatibility with the playlist. If our algorithm deems the song a viable candidate, it is added to the playlist and every group member can access it.
