It is often difficult for non-legal people to understand the legalese in documents and contracts that are often directly ##affecting their lives.

Insert your contract or document into the editor and it will parse the body of the text and return grammatical errors, ##run on text, and paragraphs which are too large. It will also swap out legalese words for plain text English making it ##easy to read and comprehend.
