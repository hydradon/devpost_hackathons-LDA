Inspiration

The user of bank applications don't like to look at the boring charts and statistics. We decided to change that!

What it does

We bring gamification aspects to the bank applications. Users are inspired to manage their finances more responsibly.

How we built it

Front end is in vue.js, back end is based on serverless technology and gcp.
