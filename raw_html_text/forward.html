Inspiration

Less than 20% of the sexual assaults were reported to the police Report of the incidence is often delayed 45% of victims are reluctant to deal with the police

What it does

Forward is an online form for anyone to record their sexual assault, regardless of when and where it took place. It is completely confidential, as the content is saved only on your device. Our aim is to empower victims at their most vulnerable state.

How we built it

Forward is a web application built with Javascript, HTML, CSS, with integrations of APIs for Google Map, speech to text.. etc.

Challenges we ran into
