Inspiration

Patients usually don't have any idea about how much their care will cost until they have to pay for it.

What it does

HealthHere shows users published average costs for different procedures, giving them an idea of how much their care will cost.

How we built it

We parsed published data on average procedure costs by the three major Cleveland area hospitals and display it in an intuitive format for users.

Challenges we ran into

Parsing the data and structuring it.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It works

What we learned

Medical procedures are expensive
