Inspiration

In high school, volunteering is a difficult but important task, and we wanted to make it easier and more effective

What it does

It shows you volunteering opportunities around you, and you can easily sign up for them. It also gives you a chance to host your own events

How we built it

We used some HTML, CSS, Photoshop and invision.

Challenges we ran into

One challenge that we ran into was being able to make everything flow seamlessly, after about 2 hours of work, we were able to make that happen. It was also difficult to think of an idea that hadn't already been taken.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud that the functionality of the app is working seamlessly while providing the user with the best experience.

What we learned

We learned that great ideas aren't easy to come across and that it takes time and effort to come up with a great idea. Sometimes the idea is more important than the execution.

What's next for ConnectX

We hope to make this a real website and we hope to expand our horizons to helping the whole country.
