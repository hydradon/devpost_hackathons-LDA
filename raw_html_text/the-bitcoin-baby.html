The Bitcoin Baby features baby Skye, a member of ‘generation crypto’, and her thoughts on why bitcoin is the future. Skye’s generation doesn’t trust the banks (they learned from the '08 financial crisis), and believes in the values of decentralization. She and her pal, Satoshi, encourage everyone to get ‘in’ on this growing bitcoin economy ASAP.

The Melrose PR team, led by CEO Kelley Weaver, is the visionary behind this project. The trailer stars Skye Weaver, Kelley’s six-month-old daughter, in her acting career debut.

The team learned that the core aspects of Bitcoin really can be conveyed in 60 seconds. To the moon!
