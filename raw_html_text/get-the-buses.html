We can't evacuate people if we don't have the vehicles in time to transport them.

Our solution matches buses with an extended database of possible drivers in realtime to provide the optimal resources to an evacuation event.

We've learned a ton from the mentors on the ground to help formulate the problem we are attacking.

We will combine the net new functionality of driver repository and realtime matching between drivers and buses with existing Rally tech for dynamic routing and tracking buses, drivers, and riders.

We've kept the technological scope lean to simplify solution development toward getting this to market faster.
