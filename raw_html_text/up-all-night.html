Inspiration

While thinking of ideas we wanted to plan ahead for the weekend and stay on task throughout the night, and we came up with the idea of making a website which people can schedule and manage all-nighters. It's really relatable to us as high schoolers because we stay up all night all the time!

How it works

We have people input their tasks for the night and form a schedule, keep track of time, and give notifications of when they shoud take breaks so they can be the most efficient worker.

Challenges I ran into

We had some troubles getting the input to show up in the boxes that formed our schedules.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We're proud of the amount of work we finished in the amount of time we had considering this was the first hackathon we had each done.

What I learned

We learned how to collaborate and manage our time really well.
