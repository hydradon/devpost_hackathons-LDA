Two students with some experience and one beginner, tasked with the impossible job of eliminating forest fires through machine learning and app development.

The app is intended to be used by forest park rangers & firefighters. The app gives the user a notification once a fire has been detected from satellite view snapshots. The snapshots are put through a model which we trained to recognize fire/smoke areas. It then shows the user, on a different tab, a pinpoint location of the danger area on a map.

Thus, it gives the user knowledge of possible at risk locations, prompts them to go search the area and ultimately, minimizes future fires.
