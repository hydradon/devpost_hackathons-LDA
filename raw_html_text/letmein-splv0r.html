• LetMeIn

Inspiration: Not having to deal with spam calls when applying for jobs and internships.

What it does: It screens calls by generating and prompting a sms on the caller.

How we built it: Spaghetti.

Challenges we ran into: Not knowing how telecommunications programming worked.

Accomplishments that we're proud of: Having builds that compiled.

What we learned: We should move away from our current telecommunications infrastructure.

What's next for LetMeIn: Making this work over a single phone call, through a RNJesused extension.
