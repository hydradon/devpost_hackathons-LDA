Inspiration

We want to make the world a better place. To have a sustainable use of resources and start an empathy culture by sharing the food that you are not going to eat.

What it does

Connects people that wish to give food because they are not going to use or eat, with the people with more in need.

How we built it

We built using the bot technology of Facebook Messenger and the ability to create a type of artificial inteligence device with witai

Challenges we ran into

*Get to know the facebook messenger bot environment *Build the message from scratch *Teach the bot a NLP able to understand spanish and people from South America

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to establish the intelligent of the bot to make it able to understand any user in South America and connect it with Facebook Messenger in order to have an easy accessibility for the users.

What we learned

Learned the facebook messenger developing environment How to create a bot intelligent How to teach a bot commands How to connect the bot with facebook messenger

What's next for Food+

The next step for Food+ is to change the mentality of all the people in South America, to create an culture of empathy; where food is not wasted anymore
