A user friendly app using graphs to assist Capital One account holders to easily track their transactions to assist in budgeting their expenses.

Using d3.js for the graphs and the Nessie Capital One app for account data

Connecting backend and front end applications issues were our biggest obstacles, making all the pieces come together.

We learned and grew by using d3.js, built a bond with team work and working through obstacles

In the future we would like to expand functionality, add the Twilio API to send SMS alerts when user designated thresholds are met
