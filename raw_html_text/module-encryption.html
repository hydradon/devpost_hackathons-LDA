Inspiration

How do you do Cryptography without keys? How can you avoid the problems of someone stealing your keys?

What it does

A new method of cryptography that can be both open source and secure.

How I built it

With a lot of time, effort, headaches, and coffee. Also, through extensive consulting of the unicode website because ASCII characters are absolutely insane to deal with.

Challenges I ran into

It is very hard to change a String that is already in octal configuration to a hexadecimal configuration, and then back.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

All of it - the entire idea is unique. I also put a lot more time and thought into this to make it function quickly as well as efficiently.

What I learned

Encryption is hard. Very. Very. Hard. I do not exaggerate this at all.

What's next for Module Encryption

More and more modules. Also, I would hope that this technology could advance sufficiently that it may be used without worry of any brute force assaults.
