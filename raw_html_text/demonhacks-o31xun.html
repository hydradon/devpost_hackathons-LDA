Ventra 2.0

A new and improved Ventra mobile application built in Android! Idea: Creating a new version of Chicago Transport Authority's Ventra to include an interactive map of train locations, as well as time tables to include arrival time and time remaining

We implemented a bunch of cool features!

  • Real time train tracking,
  • Real time train location marks on google maps
  • Emergency Reporting with camera uploads
  • Ventra Card Access
