Inspiration

Increasing Transparency in Finance using Blockchain and AI

What it does

Allows title assets to be digitized and shared on blockchain. This allows for easy tracking of loan payment and release of the title once loan payment is completed. We also incorporate social media posts to understand the problems faced by the customer and provide the solution in realtime. We also remodeled the reward system to understand customer spending patterns and provide rewards more accustomed to the customer. We also provide the customer with optimal spending advice based on their spending pattern.

How We built it

Sugar, Spice, and Everything ... Just Kidding. We used blockchain to make a permanent ledger on which all transactions are posted. The frontend was made in Javascript and backend was made in Python. Pushing data to the blockchain was done in node.js.
