Inspiration

We wanted to make a site where people can be informed about the Women's Rights Movement, and can let out their emotions.

What it does

The website has info on issues that the Women's Rights Movement fights for.

How we built it

Each of us coded different parts of the website. We went to family site of Anagha and Pavana, and put it all together.

Challenges we ran into

We originally wanted to make a discussion page, but the global comments weren't syncing. So to fix that problem, we made a rant page, where people can go to let out their thoughts on the subject.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Definitely making the carousel, and getting everything done in a clean, clear, and timely manner were the few things that we are most proud of.
