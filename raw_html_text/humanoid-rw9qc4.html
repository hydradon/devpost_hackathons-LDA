Working principle

When there is a road accident there will always be an impact on the mobile along with its owner . This app senses the impact using accelerometer and sends SMS to user selected contacts and data to our fire-base cloud. This app will send message before mobile gets damaged The data obtained at fire-base can be used to sense the accident and send immediate help and also helpful in analyzing the accident prone areas and speed at which accident occurred(this data can be used by Govt. or organizations like GMR 108 service and Traffic Police Departments It also have the regular panic button

How it works?

_->If you are a pedestrian/cyclist/motorbike rider: while walking if you had an accident then obviously you will fall down .Then this app sends message even you gets unconscious ->If you are travelling by car: Then you must carry your mobile in your shirt pocket. If you met with any accident then your mobile also will feel the shock along with you and send the message _

What it does - This app can be used to Prevent, Detect and Analyse the probable cause of accidents

Sends SOS message to friends/family contacts and also to online data base Automatically It sends AUTOMATICALLY emergency SMS containing location of the person to 3 contacts that are selected by the user and also send that location and speed at which the person is travelling(GPS Speed) to our Fire-base server. This app also have a button to do the same when help required(just like other panic button apps)
