Inspiration

Human Beings eat three times a day with some in-between meals :), but we often face these problems:

  1. What can I make to eat today
  2. Do I enough stuff to make those.

What it does

This Device will be placed in your kitchen and when you enter your kitchen you can talk to it, ask for suggestions for what you can make for that day.

How I built it

Built frontend using HTML5, CSS. Used Microsoft Cognito services for text to speech and speech to text conversion. PHP for backend services. Mysql database
