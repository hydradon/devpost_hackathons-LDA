Inspiration

Our project's inspiration is the safety of our friends and family.

What it does

Help Me sends the coordinates of the users location at a buttons click or through the app or through the dialer to trusted contacts during times of danger.

How we built it

We built the application using Android Studio. Target API 9.0 so that it can target a large audience.

Challenges we ran into

  1. Setting up Listener for the Power Button.
  2. Setting up Listener for the Dialer.
  3. Storing the contact details.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Sending the users location just by pressing the power button twice. Immense knowledge gained.

What we learned

Use of BroadcastReceiver, Services, LocationManager .

What's next for Help Me

Developing the iOS version of this application. Setting up the service for PowerButton.
