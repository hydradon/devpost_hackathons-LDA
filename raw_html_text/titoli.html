Titoli allows you to automatically add subtitles to a movie of your choice. By using your Android phone, simply hold the phone close to the movie for recognition to occur. Subtitles will automatically start playing from the current point of the movie.

This app has amazing potential for use by the deaf. Rather than waiting for a movie to come out on DVD or having to go to a select few showings of closed-captioning enabled theaters, they can simply use their phone during the movie to play the subtitles at their hand, so they can enjoy the movies on the big screen.

Main theme we are competing in is Empowerment.
