Inspiration

I was inspired by the need for students to be able to calculate their grades in anticipation of a final exam.

What it does

Gradinator calculates what you need on a final to get a desired grade in the class. Gradinator considers test, homework, quiz, and participation grades, along with extra credit. Simply type in your grades and click the "Grading Magic!" button. After that, scroll down to view a study tip!

How I built it

Gradinator is a webapplication made with HTML, CSS for design, and JavaScript to calculate grades.

Challenges I ran into

It was difficult to figure out the alignment and page layout.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I finished it. :)

What I learned

How to autocomplete forms.

What's next for Gradinator

In the future, Gradinator could autocomplete with more classes. The ones I used are some of the ones I'm taking now. An API with grade weights for certain classes could also be used. Additionally, extra grade sources could be added if needed, such as term papers or projects, which don't fit in the available sources.
