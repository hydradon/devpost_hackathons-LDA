Inspiration

For someone who lives in one city but is a fan of another city's team, it can be tough to find fellow fans to enjoy baseball games with. Likewise, fans who are traveling might enjoy finding a watch party for their home team.

What it does

The user enters their team and then sees a map of places to watch that day's game with other fans of their team. The user also has the option to submit a new watch location.

What's next for Enemy Territory
