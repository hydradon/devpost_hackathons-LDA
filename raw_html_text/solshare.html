Inspiration

Trying to utilize more of the available solar power potential in the US

What it does

SolShare connects people and businesses who are interested in investing in solar energy but can’t with people and businesses who have available real estate (residential and commercial) but can’t afford the initial capital cost. Listings are posted and funded in a crowdsourcing format.

How we built it

Backend - Django Python, Frontend - React JS
