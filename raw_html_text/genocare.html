Inspirations

Genomelinks Genetic Insights defines who the user is. Some of this Genetic Insight for some users may need to be worked and improved on to make the Genomelink user a better person in the society.
Here our application will be using Genetic Insight Diary and Calendar System to allow users to input and view various events on how they want to work and improve on their genomes

What It Does.

Become a Better Person, Have a Better Look and become Healthy by leveraging Genomelinks Genetic Insights. In this Digital World, Your Genomelinks Genetic Data literally defines who you are and what might be good for you.

How you want to look, The kind of life or Person that you want to be and Kind of foods that you want to eat are determined by Genomelinks Genetic Factors/Insights.

Genomelinks Genetic Insight has done everything already by heling you define your Genetic factors as regards to Body Traits, Intelligence, Personality, Sports and Food & Nutritions etc.

. Its now up to you to work and improve on those genetic Factors/Insights that you do not want in your life, those genetic factors that are causing Nightmares, Headaches and troubles.

For instance under "Body Traits" , Your Genetic Insights (Genetic weight) may indicates that you have higher Body Weight(Obesity) . This may become a nightmare for you since you may like to keep your Body Weight and size to a Minimal/Normal Weight.
This app will help you improve your "Body Traits(Body Apperances)" on Daily basis..Eg. You can say okay I will like work more on my Body Weight . To this effect, I will embark on Taking my Medications, Doing Excercises, Eating Sliming Meals, Medical Appointments,Readings etc..)

This app has a Body Traits Diary where you can input and note down data regarding the way and methods you want to apply on improving your Body apperances on Daily Basis. It also has a Body Traits Calendar that keeps you reminded of those events/activities based on date and Time.

Now under "Personality" , Your Genetic Insights Anger may indicates that you are More easily angered . This may become a headache for you since excessive anger can lead someone to disaster. You can say okay since my anger is bad, let me work and improve on my anger tendency in other to become a better person in the society. This app will help you improve your "Personality(Personal Character)" on Daily basis..Eg. You can say okay I will work on my anger by Ensuring that I smile with everyone when am angered, Going for Counselings, Leaving the scene whenever am angered etc.

This app has a Personality Diary where you can input and note down data regarding the way and methods you want to apply on improving your Personal Characters on Daily Basis. It also has a Personality Calendar that keeps you reminded of those events/activities based on date and Time .

Now under "Food & Nutrition" , Your Genetic Insights Carbohydrate-Intake may indicates that you are More a carbohydrate seeker . You can use this app to better Check and determine the nutritions that is good for your Body based on genetic Insights so that you can better plan your daily meal and Food Timetables.

This app has a Check Nutrition Button that allows you to Check and see all the Nutritional Genetic Sources to effectively determine the nutritional percentage content in your Body and to check the nutritions that is good for your geneome based on Genomelinks Genetic Insights.

The app also has Nutritional Diary where you can input and note down data regarding your Daily Meals Intakes and for building Foods Time-tables on improving your Nutritions on Daily Basis. It also has a Nutritional Calendar that keeps you reminded of those Daily Meals/Food Timetables based on date and Time.

How we Built It.

1.) We use Genomelinks Authentication API to get authenticated Users Genetic Data by using Genomelinks Nodejs Sample Project API which we hosted on Glitch.com
2.) We then Forward the retrived content to another site Ogachijo.com so that we can save the Users Genetic Data to our database runing PHP and Mysql.

Issues Encountered and How we Resolve it.

Genomelinks requires entering the "Client Id's, Secrets and Callback URL" in a terminal or Command prompt. This will become a problem when launching the application online because nodejs will go off once you close the termial. To mitigate this issue, I have to create an external environment variables files .env and then teweak Genomelink API Nodejs code to integrate the enviroment variables thus making it as part of the Genomelinks API. No more entering of Client Id's, Secrets and Callback URL" on the Shell prompt, Terminals or Command Prompt.....

How to use the app

1.)Login using Genomelink.
2.)Create a Temporal Geno Account(Just Username) and then login using temporal username. This used because the Genomelink API does not provide us a way of gettin authenticated users username that we can use to create further sessions
