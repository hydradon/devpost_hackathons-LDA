Inspiration

We were inspired by online games that utilize logins and passwords to keep track of progress. We wanted to use Firebase to manage our user database to store login information.

What it does

Our app allows you to play tic-tac-toe locally and create an account.

How we built it

We used Android Studio and Firebase.

Challenges we ran into

Integrating Firebase into Android Studio proved to be very difficult due to very rare errors specific to our app. We'd like to thank the Firebase team for helping us solve the problems we faced.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud that we created a working tic-tac-toe game. We were also proud of being able to create accounts, store login information using Firebase, seamlessly keep them signed in even after app shutdown, and being able to sign out and switch accounts.

What we learned

We learned how to utilize the Android API. We also learned how to use Firebase and send information from an Android device to an online database.

What's next for TripleT

We plan on implementing online multiplayer and an Elo ranking system soon.
