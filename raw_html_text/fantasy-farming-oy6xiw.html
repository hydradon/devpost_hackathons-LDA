Inspiration

Challenge #1, fantasy football, and TOM!

What it does

Fantasy Farming gamifies harvesting through a fantasy football-like point system. Gain points and level up by using the data at your disposal and optimizing your combine efficiency. You can also share your yield and take advantage of educational components of the system.

How we built it

We used python for data processing and alerts. We used plotly to graph our real machine test dataset and potentially real time data, as well. We used balsamiq to create the app's User Interface.
