With a Chem 30 Diploma coming up in a week, this project was designed to help students study organic compounds.

What is ChemARstry?

ChemARstry is an AR program that creates 3D organic compounds in the real world! By aiming your phone camera at a jack of spades, an organic compound appears out of thin air. The compound is able to be edited by converting atoms to carbons, hydrogens and oxygens.

Its Creation

ChemARstry was created using Unity and Vuforia augmented reality.
