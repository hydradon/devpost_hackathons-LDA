Inspiration

Waiting in line for lunch on Saturday made us realize how easily bottlenecks form when ordering at food trucks. We created NoLine to solve this problem.

What it does

NoLine is a solution to make the food truck experience smooth and quick. Instead of searching online for food trucks near you, driving there, waiting in line for 10 minutes just to order your food, you can use NoLine! NoLine finds food trucks near your current location and lets you view the menu and order straight from the website. We use Braintree to process your payment and then issue you an order number. All you have to do is show up and pick up your food and you're good to go.
