Inspiration

Thinking about the best experience in a hotel and the technologies, in the efficiency, in the comfort, basically in the facility when staying in a hotel.

What it does

We have made a set of two applications, one for clients and one for the staff. Happy Guest is used to enter your room, to request a taxi, to eat, to control the air, the lights, to talk with the staff, to see the schedule of the hotel ... In short, the whole experience in a single click. Happy Staff is the side of the hotel employers, from where they will receive all kinds of notifications from clients about any doubts or needs they have, as well as administrative tools to give customers access to the rooms.

How we built it

We have developed the two applications with flutter and firebase
