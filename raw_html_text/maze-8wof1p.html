Inspiration

At first the interest was geared towards VR . The team decided to incorporate a musical application with VR through Unity or Unreal-engine. However, over the course of time, the team decided to shift the focus of the application within the VR to a maze game that will function using the Samsung Gear VR.

What it does

The Unity application places the player in a maze inside a virtual white world. A cube serves as a step in the direction of the multiple challenges the team wanted to implement within the game, however, the team lost the race against time.

How we built it

Using Unity! (We tried testing it out through unreal engine, but found issues with the samsung Gear VR controller)

Challenges we ran into

The first challenge: Building and running the Unity application on an Android Phone to map it out on the samsung VR. The Second Challenge: To understand how to use unity from scratch.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Creating a virtual world. Implementing physics within unity. Not giving up.
