Inspiration

Looking up where you can recycle various items is a pain - there are websites that will do it, but it's much quicker and much more natural to just ask the question out loud.

What it does

Recycling locator is an Alexa skill that will quickly tell you, based on your current postcode, the closest place where you can recycle different types of items - eg. batteries, textiles, glass bottles, etc.

For those with an Echo spot or show, it will also show a map of said location, and the map can be zoomed in or out.
