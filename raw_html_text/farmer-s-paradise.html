Inspiration

In suburban areas with a lot of farmland around, there are often farmer's markets with no business that have to close down, and in a lot of places with dense amounts of people or homes there is no farmer's market in a convenient location. Farmer's markets are a great way to directly help local farmers as well as get fresh food to consumers. We wanted

What it does

It has functionality for farmers/users and organizers. If you're the former, you can type in your location and see nearby farmer's markets and filter them by day. If you're the latter, you can choose specific criterion and we'll find an area right for you to create a new market in.
