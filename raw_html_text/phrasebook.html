phraseBook is a web app that takes in a phrase about your day, and returns books, music, or movies that match the emotions in your phrase. If your phrase is happy, we'll return you happy songs/books/movies! Built mostly with HTML, CSS, and Flask, our database was automatically and manually created with an extensive list of media. By running a sentiment analysis on this media, we find the best matches for your current emotion.
