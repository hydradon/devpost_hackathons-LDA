Inspiration

We were inspired after several people failed a math test, and needed help. We decided to create a platform where one can find study guides, study in groups by group shared study guides for people to study together and create and submit study guides of their own.

What it does

StudyShed is a website that is a platform for a collection of study guides. Here, you can access several study guides, and group study with others from around the world.

How we built it

We used HTML/CSS/JavaScript to code our website.

Challenges we ran into

It was very difficult to get the grade calculator to work out well.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Our site has reached 9th-10th graders in various schools in Fremont. We were able to replicate our drag and drop site into a coded website, and even added more features, such as a grade calculator.

What we learned

We learned how to code websites from start to finish.

What's next for StudyShed

We need to give people the ability to submit study guides onto our site, as well as create personlalized study plans for each person that comes on the site.
