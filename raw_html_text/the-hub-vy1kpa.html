Inspiration

We have struggled with using multiple platforms to successfully communicate within our lodges and wanted to streamline the communication experience.

What it does

Centralizes communications such as calls, texts, emails, and social media to a central HUB.

How we built it

We based our ideas off of the sharing capabilities of many social media sites.

Challenges we ran into

We are not technologically inclined. We have ideas but don't have the knowledge to make it happen.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We definitively created something that would benefit the organization and the lodges.

What we learned

Though it may take an exceptional amount of effort by the Order of the Arrow, with innovation we can seek to excel our means of communication.

What's next for The HUB

Get implemented by the Order of the Arrow.
