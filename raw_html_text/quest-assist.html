• Quest Assist

Inspiration

The inspiration: it is not easy to find basic essential information among the mountains of online content at our college. So, we decided to start by assisting with the student-life experience in several meaningful ways.

What it does

Finds student resources on campus for useful things like cheap food and tutoring.

How we built it

We started with a decision tree, then moved into the Alexa Developer Console, and then the lambda.

Challenges we ran into

The biggest challenge was how fast we went from zero to here. A last minute challenge was wading into the json, but it was helpful to track down those slot id errors.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We did a really good job and finished the project. We were a perfect group, and it turned out that each of us have unique strengths that contributed to our overall success.

What we learned

Alexa made learning AI fun and useful.

What's next for Quest Assist

Learning Pathways, Geolocation, Cards
