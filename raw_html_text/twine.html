Inspiration

We wanted to create a new social media site that combines the ideas of Instagram, Twitter and Vine all in one.

What it does

Allows users to create and share short (<6 seconds) videos and also append to the end of other videos too. Users can also view and follow other users.

How I built it

React Native front-end

Challenges I ran into

Not knowing React native and having to learn during the hackathon.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Creating a mostly functioning app.
