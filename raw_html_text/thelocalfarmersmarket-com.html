It's difficult to find current information about Farmers' Markets in Hawaii

The only map of farmers markets in hawaii is out of date.

Some organizations, such as hfbf.org, have updated information, but only for their markets.

There is no one stop shop for all the information in a form that can easily be accessed on your phone.

Discover Hawaii Farmers' Markets at TheLocalFarmersMarket.com

Our mobile app makes it easy to discover your local markets. Users can:

  • Use geo-Location to find the nearest market
  • See a list of market vendors and their goods
  • See a map of the market

Administrators can:

  • Update market vendors
  • Change market times and locations
  • Assign vendors to specific booths for markets that support it (KCC only for how)
