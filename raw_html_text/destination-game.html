Inspiration

While trying to connect to the internet Jeremy was inspired by the Google offline dinosaur game... With some Destiny in the back of our minds we made a platformer.

What it does

The game has the player racing a bike over broken platforms, you score each time you pass a platform.

How we built it

With Android studio as our base and a lot of Redbull...

Challenges we ran into

Everything was a challenge, for our first time- getting the art to synchronize, getting the emulator to respond, and just over all simple syntax mistakes- made it a challenge

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We made something. That was big for us.

What we learned

Too much to type in 3 minutes, but a few are debugging, making gravity physics, creating linear game movement, and intro Android development.

What's next for Destination game

More controlled jumps, newer graphics, an intro screen, and more animations!
