Inspiration

LEGO Bionicle is a big part of my childhood. I don't have money to buy them now since I need money for college. It would be nice if I can still play with them somehow.

What it does

It's a repository of many LEGO Bionicle parts. The users can use them to assemble the LEGO model that they want. LEGO also has a program with a huge repository of parts but after you build with it, the model mostly static, you can't interact much with it. Verionicle solves that problem.

How it was made

I made all of the parts with SolidWorks

What I like about it

The parts were more beautiful than I expected them to be. And I can design my all Bionicle parts.
