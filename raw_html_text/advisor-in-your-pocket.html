Inspiration

The ability to realistically see different career paths, without making an advising appointment!

How it works

Paths manages an account for you that lets you compare your major to another major that you want to switch to. It allows you to see how many credits will not transfer and how many are left to take.

Challenges I ran into

Android development was tricky for our team which was composed of first time hackers, and first time Android development users.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Creating a clean, functional application that helps our fellow students see multiple paths for their future career.

What I learned

The ability to use Android development front and back end.

What's next for Advisor in your pocket!

Hopefully additional feature's to allow more majors and schools to be able to use our app!
