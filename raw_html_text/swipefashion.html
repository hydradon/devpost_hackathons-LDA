Inspiration

Sometimes we see people on other social media apps are wearing clothes we want but we don't know where to get them. Also, some of our friends have a hard time knowing what to wear and what to buy in the season.

What it does

Helps people who don't know what to wear in what season and don't know what colors to wear with what.

How we built it

We used the app Marvel to build it. We used hotspots and images to create our app.

Challenges we ran into

Sometimes, the hotspots wouldn't save or Marvel would glitch. Also, we have never created an app before so it was difficult to make one for the first time.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud that we had successfully created an app on our first time at a Hackathon and our first time using Marvel.

What we learned

We learned how to make an app, good teamwork skills, and how to work Marvel.

What's next for SwipeFashion

Hopefully, for it to become a new social media app that could be used to benefit young women.
