Inspiration

Had a medical issue that kept getting misdiagnosed and went around for weeks until finally reaching the right provider.

What it does

Virtuoso is an app that combines predictive tools and telemedicine to give providers the right context so they can feel comfortable about routing patients fast and correctly.

How we built it

We use ML to analyze patient symptoms, get a proper initial diagnosis, and share that diagnosis with an NP, PCP, or Pharmacist that you can talk to within minutes.

Challenges we ran into

Getting used to working with a new team under such a short amount of time.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Being able to complete our first hackathon!

What we learned

It’s important to come to hackathons

What's next for Virtuoso

Routing patients properly Learning more from patient symptoms Integrations Securely storing healthcare information
