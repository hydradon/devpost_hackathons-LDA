Inspiration

Midnight, lonely tinder swiping inspired me to find love in music; I love music, so why not blend the two?

What it does

Sources sounds directly from SoundCloud and presents them to you; you choose to swipe left or right on the song, and a list of liked songs are curated for you.

How I built it

At PennApps, with much help from the Apple.

Challenges I ran into

Data structure issues, layering of the view controllers, etc.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

First time me and my teammate have used Swift - think we did a decent job given our lack of experience

What I learned

apps are hard to build really hard to build

What's next for Minder

more development, using crowdsourcing data to better the experience
