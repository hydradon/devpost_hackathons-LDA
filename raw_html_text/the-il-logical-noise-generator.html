Inspiration

Unorthodox Synthesizers and also Hackaday.

What it does

The 4046 Phase-locked loop chip receives a voltage between 0 and 5 volts. The input voltage is modulated by two hex schmitt-trigger inverters in a loop with 100K potentiometers. One loop controls the voltage of the oscillator, while the other controls switching frequencies, which is implemented by a bilateral switch.

How I built it

We implemented the circuit on a breadboard and 3D printed a stand to hold the two potentiometers. The circuit is powered by a 3.3v wire from a PC PSU.
