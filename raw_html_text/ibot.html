Inspiration

The digital Insurance Track and OF COURSE our big interest in AI, especially chatbots.

What it does

iBot is a chat application that helps you find the insurences that fit you best. Just have a talk to iBot and get your insurences recommended.

How we built it

After long consideration we chose SAP Conversational AI as our natural language processor. Alongside we have a Spring backend as well as an Angular frontend application to use our bot.

Challenges we ran into

We had to learn how Conversational AI works and how Bot APIs work in general as well as how to connect those features to our own logic.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are happy to get a pretty much functional application including custom user interface as well as a backend with database integration and also a working chatbot that recognises user intents and personal information.

What we learned

How to draft a full application and divide into more and less important part as well as share the work in a team. Time management and of course new technology like AI.

What's next for iBot

Better language recognition, more detailed dialoges, bigger frontend and extendable backend logic.
