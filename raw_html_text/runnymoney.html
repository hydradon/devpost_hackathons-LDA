Inspiration

We wanted to create an app that motivates people to stay active and stay connected with their friends.

How it works

Users sign up online and download the app. A group of friends can each put in an equal amount of money, to create a pool that the user with the highest step count at the end of a certain period of time wins. The app uses a pedometer to keep track of the user's steps per day and it compares it to the number of steps of their friends over the challenge period.

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned
