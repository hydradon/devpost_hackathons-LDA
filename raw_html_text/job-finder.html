Inspiration

Struggling to find a job that's right for me.

What it does

ThingimaJob is a web app that skims your resume for keywords that will help you find a job that is perfect for you

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned

Don't do a hackathon in languages you do not know and expect to be able to pull it off in a few hours.

What's next for ThingimaJob
