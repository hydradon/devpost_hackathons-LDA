Inspiration

Rogers' vision of integrated connected living solutions that are simple to use and attainable for everyday life.

What it does

Our solution is platform agnostic, not specific, it would be available through Google Assistant, Alexa, Siri. Time to market would be longer

How we built it

Using the Dialogflow API, we built solutions for several situations and then connected it to our google home.

Finding a prototype solution that can be implemented within one day. We then came across Dialogflow and had to learn how it works and how it can be implemented for our case using a google home.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Having a prototype virtual assistant technology and working with conversational platforms to accomplish our goal.

What we learned

We learned about new emerging technologies and how they can be implemented into the telecom industries.
