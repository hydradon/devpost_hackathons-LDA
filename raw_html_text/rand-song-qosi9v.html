rand(song)

The world is full of many important problems, but we couldn't solve any in 24h with limited connectivity :(

However, we did spend a lot time guessing songs played on speakers around us, so we built a game to make it more fun: rand(song) !

You only need:

  • A group of friends
  • A few smartphones
  • A Bose Sound Touch 10 speaker

You get 10 points every time you guess the song correctly. Good luck!
