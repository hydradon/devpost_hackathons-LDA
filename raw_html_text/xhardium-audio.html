Inspiration

The idea was to manage analog audio signals without a mixer or computer.

What it does

Thanks to this concept, I can mix, route and memorize audio mixing sequences. So it is possible to create an audio show that is synchronized with the lighting The interface has 8 inputs and 8 outputs, and several interfaces can be linked together to have more channels, so more creativity.

How I built it

To do this I created an analog audio interface that is controlled in DMX512.

Challenges I ran into

The challenge was to find the money to create prototypes and test them.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

My system allows you to create Dolby Atmos without a computer.

What I learned

Always keep your goals in sight, be bold and do not let go
