HackPR-El Nuevo Dia News Bot

A news bot that consumes data using the El Nuevo Dia GFR Media API.

Commands

The commands are for the bot are created in a way that allows easy integration/removal of commands. Available commands:

  1. Horoscope
  2. Latest
  3. Lottery
  4. Opinions
  5. Social Feed
  6. News
