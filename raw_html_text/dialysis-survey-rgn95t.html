Ms. Lin told us about how her sister-in-law was a nurse and she wanted an app that would ask her patients some questions before they came for dialysis. We decided to make that app.

This app asks some questions about the patient's health and moves to a database so that nurses can see how a patient is doing before they come in or they can track a patient's progress over time.

We built the app using Thunkable and the Firebase database.

One challenge that we ran into was connecting the Firebase to the app and making it record data properly.

We are proud of making the database work and getting it all working properly.

We learned how to connect Thunkable apps to Firebase. We also learned a little bit about JSON.

We might launch the Dialysis Survey on the App Store or we might sell it to a clinic or hospital.
