fyeah.co/cktails

fyeah cocktail is a visual concoction of cocktail mixes for your viewing pleasure. Displayed in a grid like manner, you have the ability to scroll through the selection and decide which drink catches your eye. Using Yummly’s API, we have aggregated the most popular cocktails and their ingredients. Plus, you’re able to search and filter by type!
