Inspiration

Parking is hard and annoying especially in Chapel Hill.

What it does

Allows users to put up their parking spots to sell for short periods of time.

How we built it

We used Ruby on Rails and utilized the Esri API.

Challenges we ran into

Not knowing Ruby on Rails or how to use the Esri API.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting some semblance of a working app.

What we learned

Debugging is hard especially in languages and frameworks you don't know.

What's next for Metr

Deploying it properly, a lot of debugging, and adding payments.
