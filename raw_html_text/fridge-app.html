Inspiration

We were trying to make an app that could help people with the day to day issue of making food and what recipes to follow and make.

What it does

It was intended to scan a receipt or barcode and store the data of the returned product in a personal fridge for the user in the cloud. That way when the user uses the app to find recipes our app knows what the user has in his/her fridge and can suggest recipes only based on what he/she has to work with. No more getting half way through a recipe before realising you don't have a crucial ingredient.

How we built it

We were only able to get as far as producing a barcode scanner.
