Inspiration

Inspiration from our frustration of queueing that every thirsty person experiences at the crowdy bar/pub. Or even a fail and embarrasement because you couldn't get a drink for your date.

What it does

A smart, fast and simple way to make your order and pay without clunky new app downloads - using just natural language and Facebook Messenger, while the order is automatically updated on the POS.

Best of all, the bi-directional communication channel between a business and customer remains open for the future marketing and in-store experience enhancement. Brings smiles to customer's faces while making local business shine.
