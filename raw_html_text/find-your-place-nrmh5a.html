Inspiration

Whenever we go traveling we have a difficult time figuring out what to do in such a short period of time.

What it does

After selecting a location, the top 3 places to visit in the location with information on it.

How we built it

We used Codepen and Squarespace to be able to select a link on Codepen and then have it redirect to our Squarespace website, where the actual information of the places are shown. The map of Europe and links to the information are located on Codepen.

Challenges we ran into

We found it difficult to learn new languages in a short period of time.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of learning how to use API and learning a little bit of CSS, HTML, and JavaScript.

What we learned

We learned a little bit of CSS, HTML, and JavaScript.

What's next for Find Your Place

It can be turned into an app for Android and IOS to be accessed more easily.
