Inspiration

Skyrim is fun, make it more fun.

What it does

Changes the built-in sound effects assigned to different animal and plant NPCs.

How we built it

Using the Skyrim Creation Kit, along with recording and editing our own sound effects (91 total).

Challenges we ran into

NPCs behave differently and so have different places for different noises. Organizing all of them was a tricky task. The creation kit had also never been used before, so that was learned. Creation kit is also outdated and crashes often due to a bug on newer systems.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Making this mod while having no experience with mod making or the creation kit at the start of the hackathon.

What we learned

How to create modifications for Skyrim.

What's next for Nature's Voice: Skyrim Mod

It will likely be uploaded to the Skyrim mod nexus for all players of Skyrim to enjoy!
