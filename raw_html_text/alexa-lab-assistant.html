alexa-colab

Not enough TA's to guide your lab section? Working with dangerous materials and can't conveniently take notes? Don't fret. The perfect lab col-LAB-orator is here.

Alexa Co-LAB is a speech interface lab assistant that keeps track of instructions and data for educational science labs and streamlines the data-collecting process for students.
