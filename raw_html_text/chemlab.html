Inspiration

our goal is to combine education and technology to meet the quality education's goal by targeting chemistry and using AR technology.

What it does

It visualizes the laboratory experiments and provides access to laboratories.

How we built it

we used C# as the programming languages and unity3D as the development environment with Vuforia framework.

Challenges we ran into

developing it with AR and setting the chemical reaction rules.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

completing a prototype within 3 days for an idea that was generated withing only this period of time.

What we learned

team work, working under pressure and in short time, developing using trending technologies and many more.

What's next for Alchemy

further improving the performance and virtualization of laboratories in many other fields.
