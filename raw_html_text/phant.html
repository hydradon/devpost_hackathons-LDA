Inspiration

We wanted a community to be able to provide their hobbies as services to unite a neighborhood and created a mini economy.

What it does

Users can post either a service they are willing to provide or post a request for particular service they are looking for. Users can browse the Services if they need something, or they can browse the Requests and see if they are able to help someone out. Basic information is provided, such as hours of operation. On Services, users can leave reviews and ratings, creating a reputation system to encourage users to provide the best service they can.
