Inspiration

Engineers need to appreciate art to get a better understanding of the world around them. To understand art, it's best to not understand it all. This developer-art-piece was inspired by Magritte's famous painting: "Ceci n'est pas une pipe"

Ceci n'est pas un website

To spoil the fun, Magritte's painting "n'est pas une pipe" ("is not a pipe"). It's a painting of a pipe. You will see that Ceci n'est pas un website , "this is not a website" . Indeed, it's not; it's a domain that points to a website. Welcome to the wonderful of modern art. The surrealists verb you.

(_ The hiccup _

The domain was registered to point to my student website which contains a custom-designed site, but domain.com was having issues registering my university's host.
