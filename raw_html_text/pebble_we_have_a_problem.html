A hack to help people become more aware of what's up!

We want to take full advantage of the many features the pebble provides and in the process bring a lot of useful information to the many stargazers among us.

From tanking advantage of the GPS capabilities the pebble inherits from the phone to display local weather information, to displaying the current position of the International Space Station.
