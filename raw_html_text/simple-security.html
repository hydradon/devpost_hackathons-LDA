What it does: 

Simple Security utilizes a phone's camera to take pictures of any intruders. By tracking the changes in picture frames, the app will take snapshots of the current scene. The app will then immediately begin to upload the files to a web server, to make sure that the data is not lost in case the phone is stolen or destroyed.

What we learned: 

Projects work best when people specialize in different areas.

What is next for Simple Security: 

We hope to add image compression features in order to save data when uploading pictures.
We also hope to bring this technology to developing countries to utilize, especially when the cost of security cameras is so high.
