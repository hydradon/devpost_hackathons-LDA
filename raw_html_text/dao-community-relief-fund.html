Problem : Process to release funds from donation pools is inefficient. Solution: TRANSPARENCY AND DECENTRALIZATION: By empowering every single member of donation pool to engage in the process. How? By leveraging blockchain we allow the following to address this: Authorization of release of funds (through voting) Tracking of transactions Low cost hardware (< $10) to use this platform where access to smartphones\PC is limited Potential use cases: Community funds, University funds, Refugee camps etc
