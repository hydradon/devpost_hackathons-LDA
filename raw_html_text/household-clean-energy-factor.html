Problem: CCAs and others need to engage customers into load shifting and energy efficiency through sharing of smart meter data.

Solution: Show customers when energy is cleaner and costs less under their new time of use rate.

Why: Reduce peak demand and curtailment.

How: Offer a weekly clean energy forecast with actionable, relevant insights. Leverage Utility API portal to provide normative report to encourage load shifting.
