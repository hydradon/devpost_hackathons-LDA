What it does

The aim was for people to be able to join lobbies and decide on music by voting and adding music to a queue. Additional features include a music visualizer and "Chat-chat" box.

What I learned

That software development, while fun, can have some serious factors that you need to take into consideration throughout the development process. We had to iterate, learn, and think through our processes very carefully to create an end product that: A) fit the requirements set out in the initial plan, B) is extendable and flexible, and C) sparks inspiration from others.
