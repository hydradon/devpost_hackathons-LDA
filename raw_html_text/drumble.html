Inspiration

Air drumming too hard and learning about physical therapy techniques.

What it does

Lets you air drum using "fake" drumsticks on a virtual drumkit. We show patterns to the user to copy and judge their score based on accuracy

How I built it

We send a video stream over socket.io form browser to a flask/python backend and process the images via openCV

What's next for Drumble

More sophisticated drum patterns, better score predictions, partnerships with physical therapists.
