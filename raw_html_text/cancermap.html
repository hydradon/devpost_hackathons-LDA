Inspiration

Recent Cancer research, general interest in forecasting trends

What it does

Cancer trend forecasting and visualization

How we built it

Data analysis in python and minitab to forecast future trends, stored large dataset in firebase and presented data in interactive web application displaying past cancer statistics to allow researchers and professionals to visualize how cancer has changed over time in the us

Challenges we ran into

New languages, new analytical methods, storing and retrieving large amounts of data, forecasting with limited information

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Learned new technology and analytical methods

What we learned

how to intuitively present data to non technical users

What's next for CancerMap

More data means better predictions in the hands of our consumers
