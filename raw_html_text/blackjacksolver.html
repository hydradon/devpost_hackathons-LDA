• Blackjack Genie

Inspiration

We felt it would be an interesting way to implement our mathematics and computer science abilities to a real world application.

What it does

Based on certain card inputs, two percentages are returned.
One will be the percentage of not busting, and the other will be the percentage of getting a perfect Blackjack.

How we built it
