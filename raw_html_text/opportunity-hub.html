A platform to collaborate NPOs and volunteers who want to give something back to the community. A market place where NPOs can post their requirements on and a platform for volunteers who can get the job done for the NPO. Open source platform which can be deployed by any NPO, civic organizations. NPOs can do 1) Post their requirements as a project on the platform. 2) List down the volunteers who has applied for the job. 3) Pick a volunteer to get the job done 4) Provide the feedback on the work that is done by the volunteer 5) Mark the project as completed

Volunteers can do 1) See projects that are posted on the portal. 2) Apply the projects and give their breakdown for the project they are working on 3) Update work status on the project.
