Spawned from frequent usage of Youtube as a source for music, we designed an efficient google chrome extension that allows for users to make playlists on the fly. Permanently resting at the bottom of the screen it is nearly hidden until it is hovered over. The user can skip songs and pick any song to play from their playlist which runs on its own in an embedded html5 video tag.
