Inspiration

I wanted to take something that is hard to learn in 2D and that most people don't know. This lead me to creating a Mass Spectrometer tour/learning video in a mobile VR headset.

What it does

Teaches you in a whole new way, learning in VR is so much cooler.

How I built it

With lots of patients because 3D modeling and animation is a hard thing to learn. But all with 3DS-Max and Unity.

Challenges I ran into

I have no artistic skills and I can barely draw in 2D

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Getting everything to ultimately work and having an awesome demo to show

What I learned

To watch the entire tutorial video and not skip through it.

What's next for VR Science MS

Another cool subject or idea that would be cool to learn in VR.
