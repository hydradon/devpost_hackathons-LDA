Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of the custom search algorithm we had to develop in order to find an optimal path given modeled exchange rates over time. Many search algorithms, such as Bellman-Ford and Dijkstra's, solve individual requirements, but there are no well known algorithms that solve a widest-path, time-sensitive search problem with multiple goal states.
