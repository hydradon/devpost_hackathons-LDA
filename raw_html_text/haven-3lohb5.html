Inspiration

The elderly are defrauded out of $36 billion each year. When the elderly are defrauded, it is a very painful experience.

What it does

This app prevents fraud and ensures financial security in dementia patients. It allows elderly patients to designate a child or trusted friend who is notified if there is irregular spending on the elder's bank account that could cause the elder to not have enough money for care.

How I built it

node.JS, html, javascript, css, twilio
