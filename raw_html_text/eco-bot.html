What is Ecobot?

Ecobot is a Messenger chatbot that measures your personal ecological impact. Through an initial survey, it calculates your CO2 emissions based on formulas, measurements, and statistics from the EPA . It then gamifies the process of improvement by compiling standings amongst friends that have also messaged the chatbot - standings of people having the least CO2 emissions. Finally, it'll send weekly reminders on actionable improvements, like weekly miles traveled with your car.

How we built it

We're using:

  • Node.js for our chatbot's backend server
  • Firebase to store data
  • npm package messenger-node for building the bot
