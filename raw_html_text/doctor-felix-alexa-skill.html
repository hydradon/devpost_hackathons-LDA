Inspiration

I have a lot of diabetic people around me including my wife and i can tell you that it is a very heavy illness. It is very important that diabetics log all this information to help their doctor get a better view and make the necessary changes.

Doctor felix skill helps you to log all the important information including blood sugar level, insulin and carbs intakes, sport activities and mood.

it can also send all this information to you in a csv files format.

The idea came to me when i meet a blind women who has diabetic, she explained to me how difficult was for her to keep track of her reading.

What it does

Doctor felix alexa skill Allows Diabetics to log all the important information including blood sugar level, insulin and carbs intakes, sport activities and mood.
