We liked Johnson and Johnson's One Touch API because its networked recording system allowed us to visualize data in a dynamic and persistent way that is uncommon for data related to personal health or wellbeing. We are often concerned about our awareness of our own health, but we especially feel that the One Touch may be a promising step toward encouraging young people to be more interested and engaged with their conditions.
