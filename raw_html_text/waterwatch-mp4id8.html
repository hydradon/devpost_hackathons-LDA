Inspiration

Absence of effective tool for global water bodies and their dynamics.

What it does

Calculates the extent and volume of water bodies globally.

How we built it

We created the product by using two datasets: 1- Satellite observations of the reflectance 2- Satellite observations of elevetation (topography of the earth)

Challenges we ran into

The data size is huge (13 TB) so we needed to think of eefficient ways of dealing with this large dataset

Accomplishments that we're proud of
