Inspiration

Inspiration comes from watching how children can impact the world around us, that we have borrowed from our coming generations, not inherited from our ancestors.

What it does

Some simple games for kids that could help them get a gist of how to effectively do environmental conservation through different activities.

How I built it

Using GameMaker 2 Trial

Challenges I ran into

Challenges included, agreeing and developing on this platform we used for the first time.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We could translate our design concepts into simple games that can leave a long lasting impact on the mind of the children who play and understand environmental conservation.

What I learned

A lot of concepts from psychology, design, coding, teamwork, ideating, hacking etc.

What's next for Go Green Game

Integrating AI & Semi-AI Graphical as well as problematic arcade features to make the game more involving and more interesting.
