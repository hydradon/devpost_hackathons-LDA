Inspiration

Mealy.me is a novel way of dining. It utilizes meal-sharing technology to improve the dining experience at UMass Amherst. We utilize an Android app and IBM cloud services, to allow users to share their meals with one another. Our project will make dining cheaper for people who do not have meal plans and will save money for people with meal swipes.

How it works

Ever ran out of meal swipes? Or had too many? We're here to bring back the balance and provide a platform to sell your extra meal swipes to hungry souls around campus. Yes, you're welcome.

Challenges I ran into

Most of us are pretty new to web server design and development. There were also issues we ran into when integrating the app with the web server. One of the major challenges involved writing web server code from scratch. We used python, databases, bluemix and google APIs for the first time!
