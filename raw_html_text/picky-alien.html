Inspiration

Rocket League meets Simon Says meets Area 51?

Story

For some strange reason you are a servant to your alien overload at area 51. He demands you bring him things (right now only cacti) before the time runs out. You do this by obviously ramming your car into the items and drag them around the map to the alien.

How I built it

Unity

What I learned

Playtest often.

What's next for Picky Alien

This game can be easily made more complex by adding different items for the alien overload to demand from the player.
