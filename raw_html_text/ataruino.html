Inspiration

Pong is pretty cool, and so is Retro!

What it does

Play pong wirelessly with a partner. Move your paddle by tilting the controller forwards and backwards!

How we built it

Arduino, accelerometer, LCD screen, bluetooth modules

Challenges we ran into

Driving the LCD screen, calibrating the accelerometer, communicating via bluetooth

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Built with minimal components. A new twist on a classic game.

What we learned

Wireless communication is never as easy as you think it is.

What's next for Ataruino

Additional games, a single player AI, and a touch screen.
