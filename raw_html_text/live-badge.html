Inspiration

Patrick came up with this idea one day trying to figure out how to implement the Star Wars AR app but with concerts.

What it does

It reads tickets and makes cool stuff appear. Festivals can use it to promote their events and have people interact with their badges.

How we built it

We used a bunch of MacBooks and a couple iPhones to make this.

Challenges we ran into

Stefan making up ideas on things we should implement.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We didn't declare an ICO.

What we learned

We learned how to make an AR app in 24 hours.
