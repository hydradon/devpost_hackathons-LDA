Sir Gregory Williams, Baron of Love, Archduke of the Cosmos, has selected a unique few to take on life-threatening missions in a valiant effort to better their communities. Unfortunately, when he and his friends joined together, they were stumped. They didn't know where to start. So Sir Gregory Williams and his friends created Zenith, an easy access app that'll quickly enable those like-minded to start on their journeys. Join Sir Williams in his quest for a better tomorrow.
