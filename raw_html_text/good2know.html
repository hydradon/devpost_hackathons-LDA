Inspiration

I have 100s of notes about everything. It's difficult to keep the notes accessible since a note might belong to several projects. Found out companies have the same problem of sharing ideas and notes between team members. Currently working on company features for g2k.

What it does

good2know is your second brain ... so you can forget stuff. Unlike all the other note taking apps. It's like Twitter for your thoughts, but:

  • there is no character limit
  • it's private .. unless you share your entries / topics with someone
  • offline support (on mobiles)
  • learning feature for languages and other new skills. Repeat what you learned and get better at it.

A thought, a picture, an event .. good2know knows it all. And applies AI to suggest you tags for websites, pictures and texts you store in it. To make finding your information as easy as possible. You can also follow public topics and gain knowledge from others.
