Inspiration

--A digital world where every thing is digitalized and we are making a platform to secure every single identity.

What it does

--It creates a unique and authenticated identity of every single personality. It provides a platform where any one can upload his/her documents and get a portfolio.

How we built it

-- we have made use of django to handle the back-end and some front-end libraries like materialize.

Challenges we ran into

--The biggest challenge we faced is to get the documents authenticated from their respective organizations. For now we have found goverment organization aadhar's API but we are having some issues in integrating with our project.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

--we have created a dynamic website with the database connectivity.

What we learned

-- To learnt how to make use of django to deal with the back-end and so many new things.

What's next for Digital Identification

--We are going to become a parameter for judging the authenticity of a document..
