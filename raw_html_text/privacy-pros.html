Inspiration

My relative has had multiple breaches of his data, which has cost him losses in the ranges of the thousands of dollars. I would like to make sure that no one else has to go through that horrible experience, hence we are creating this project to inform the general populace on how to better protect their data.

What it does

This website gives in depth information on how to protect your data, and also allows users to test their knowledge of data privacy as well as includes a password enhancing feature.

How we built it

This project was created through the use of Java as well as Weebly for our website.

Challenges we ran into

Two of the greatest challenges we ran into were thinking of an idea and implementing the password enhancer.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Managing to fit everything into a coherent website.

What we learned

We learned how to successfully integrate Java into a website to create a seamless transition and further the experience of the user.
