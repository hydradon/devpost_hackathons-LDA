Inspiration

We thought "Tinder for dogs".

We decided to make a social good hack that would enable animal shelters to simplify the process for those looking to adopt pets.

What it does

People can browse animals available at their local shelters and make inquiries directly with the shelter.

How I built it

We used Android.

Challenges I ran into

Limited experience, the .idea folder and loading issues.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We finished it!

What I learned

Find a team before going to a hackathon.

What's next for Fetch.Me

We hope to reach out to local shelters and hope to build a full fledged platform.
