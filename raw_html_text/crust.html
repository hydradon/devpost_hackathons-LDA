command-line pizza

Coding away at CarlHacks 2016 and feeling the need for a warm, delicious, Domino's pizza at 1:40am? Simply enter your command line/terminal, and enter "python crust.py". After a few quick steps, your order will on your nearest Domino's cash register, and your cheesy slices of excellence will be on their way shortly.

the future of crust

favOrder( ) -> save an order after you make it, and order it again with a few keystrokes the next time you use crust

payNow( ) -> pay using a credit card in app

newMenu( ) -> expanded options (cheesy bread, parm bites?)
