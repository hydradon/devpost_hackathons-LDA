Inspiration

We enjoyed Trouble in Terrorist Town for Garry's Mod so much we decided to make a couch multiplayer 2D variation of it.

What it does

One of you is a traitor. Who? That's the point of the game. Try to find it out and kill the traitor before he kills you. Or maybe YOU are the traitor!

How we built it

Pizza + coffee goes in, Unity C# code goes out. That's basically the whole proccess.

Challenges we ran into

Getting multiple gamepad inputs to work nicely was a bit of a pain, but we dealt with it eventually.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned

We deepened our Unity knowledge, learned more about collaboration on the same code and how to make it not hurt.

What's next for Traitor Baitor and the citizeens

Network multiplayer, smartphones as more advanced controllers or simly a mobile version. Lots of money and fame, obviously.
