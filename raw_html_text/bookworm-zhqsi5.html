Inspiration

As book lovers, we are always looking for new stories to read. But finding a book that we know we will enjoy is always a struggle.

What it does

In our app, users can enter the title of a book they love, and they will be given a similar or related book for them to indulge in.

How we built it

We used Android Studio and Google Books API to create this app.

Challenges we ran into

We ran into a problem with loading the large amount of data in the JSON file, but figured it out by researching solutions.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to effectively use the Google Books API, which was a new experience for us.

What we learned

Running into countless bugs and errors is part of the process of creating an app! We learned how to stay calm and focused throughout our journey.

What's next for Bookworm

We hope our app will be used by anyone looking for a story to get lost in and find their love for reading!
