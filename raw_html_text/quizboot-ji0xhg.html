• QuizBoot

Inspiration

Quizboot was inspired from the need for me to challenge myself while socializing and to learn alongside.

What it does

Quizboot is a social learning platform that is focused on the assessment of knowledge through quizzes, thereby providing people (student, firms, interview applicants) access to pool of rich quizzes where they can challenge themselves or friends, track their performance and also have fun while learning.

How I built it

I built Quizboot with django and python initially until i got to experience the awesomeness of graphql and react.

Challenges I ran into

Some of the challenges i ran into then was to track cheats then in the system, hosting on a reliable server.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I happy we have over 400 users

What I learned

I was able to improve my Django, Python and JavaScript skills. Also learnt some devOps

What's next for QuizBoot

Promotion and getting more people to engage on quizboot
