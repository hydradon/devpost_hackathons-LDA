Inspiration

We know there is going to be an impending recession. That is why we want to make sure every American is ready for retirement. More than 60% of Senior citizens have not enough savings to survive their rest of their lives without any help from others.

What it does

It makes sure that right before someone buys something from amazon it prompts the consumer to save money, invest it or donate it. It also has a handy calculator to see what the money will be at retirement.

How we built it

Python Google App engine and Linear Regression

Challenges we ran into

Google App Engine does not like python and javascript

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Having made this product in a day with a website and google chrome extension deployed

What we learned

Everything

What's next for MoreMoneyForU

We will take it to market after some polishes of course.
