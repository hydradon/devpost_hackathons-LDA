Inspiration

Enable up and coming artists to grow their fan base and make connections for Record Label signing opportunities

What it does

Livestreaming platform where artists livestream their shows and fans can view and give accolades. Based on the amount of views and accolades, these artists can be recognized onto the Tempo 'Hype Leaderboard' Once they meet additional criteria, A&R from Record Labels can proceed to set up a meeting to discuss on 'signing' opportunities

How we built it

HTML, CSS, Javascript, Tivo, Spotify

Challenges we ran into

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Ability to develop a platform to address user needs from 2 user groups: Fans and A&R Music Labels

What we learned

Learned the music industry ecosystem

What's next for Tempo

Assess incorporating integrations with our music platforms so Music Labels can have all the metrics they need to make decisions for signing an artist.
