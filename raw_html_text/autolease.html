Inspiration

Prompts from Interac and Smartcar, and the exotic nature of the Tesla Model S

What it does

Connects owners and dreamers on a marketplace of short-term (luxury) vehicular leases.

How we built it

We used Flask as a backbone framework, jQuery, core Python, JavaScript, HTML, Bootstrap.

Challenges we ran into

Defective APIs provided by the sponsors, and limited assistance.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We created a slick UI/UX and a working front-end client.

What we learned

Selection of codebase in a team of limited resources is important. It may have been easier to start in JS.

What's next for AutoLease

We'll explore other financial/vehicular APIs for further development.
