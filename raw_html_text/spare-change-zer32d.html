Inspiration

For busybees out there, carving out time to get involved in the community and giving back isn’t easy. But you want to do something with high impact. Spare Change helps you care about local or global non-profits by allowing you to incorporate giving back into your daily routine.

How I built it

ReactJS front-end. Dozens of ReactJS components pending. Additional mock-ups forthcoming. Component-level styling uses Sass, SCSS, or CSS with CSS Modules. Webpack v2 Babel to compile ES2016+

What it does

Search for a charity of your choice. Connect the accounts and cards you use to make everyday purchases. Donate your change. Track your donations on the dashboard and see the true impact of your dollars.
