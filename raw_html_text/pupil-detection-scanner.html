Inspiration

Our friend Gavin came up with the idea, to be able to order glasses online without having to use body-measurment tape to try and find the distance between your two pupils.

What it does

Using opencv.Js, we used facial recognition and edge detection to reference a credit card to then find the measurements from the users Webcam to digitally map the purple distance and automatically updating the order profile for the user.

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned
