Inspiration

As high schoolers, we face stress on a daily basis due to the work and test load, and peer pressure.

What it does

Provides stress free games, advice, and a questionnaire to relax the mind. It encourages the user to figure out his or her main goals in life and prioritize them.

How I built it

We worked as a team, collaborated, and came up with a paper prototype. We considered factors that help us, as individuals, relax and reduce stress and combined all the factors to make something unique and beneficial to teens.

Challenges I ran into

It was challenging to change the java and html softwares to produce a smooth user interface.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We are proud of the fact that we were able to create several games in the limited time. For our first hackathon, we believe we got far, and have learned a lot throughout the experience.

What I learned

We developed life long presentation skills through the "Improve your presentation skills " event, and learned more about machines, and artificial intelligence. The events held were intriguing, and have motivated us to discover more about the vast field of technology.

What's next for The Stress-Less Zone

We plan on enhancing our project and adding more games that are stress free. We want to add a meditation lesson as well. We will continue to participate in hackathons, and will strive to make apps that benefit our society and environment. We will continue to learn from our mistakes, and build on what we learn.
