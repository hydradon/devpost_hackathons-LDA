Inspiration

A way to request music at parties easily.

How it works

Every "Dj" has their own page that streams YouTube videos. Using the MEAN stack, songs are dynamically searched for and added without refreshing the page. Every video that is added is put on the priority queue, which can be voted on and manipulated by party-goers.

Challenges we ran into

The MEAN stack was not as straightforward as planned. Also, sleep was our enemy.

Accomplishments that we are proud of

Creating a working website!

What we learned

Dedication, MEAN Stack, syntax (if only)

What's next for djQ

User authentication as well as automatic streaming.
