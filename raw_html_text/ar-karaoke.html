Inspiration

Karaoke is popular. Face filters are popular. Many social media users rap, sing, and lip sync to music

What it does

Users words are an input. As they recite the lyrics to a song, some of which are left blank, they score points for each word they get correct .

How I built it

Unity with the ARCore SDK. IBM Watson for the speech to text.

Challenges I ran into

Aligning and and scaling the face filter objects took longer than I expected. The speech to textservice can be slow and inaccurate

Accomplishments that I'm providing, of

Interesting way to interact with popular music

What I learned

A lot of new and interesting things can be done with new tools that are becoming more available

What's next for AR Karaoke

Multiplayer. Body tracking, for dancing, to extend the gamification idea
