Inspiration

We were sick of wasting time trying to figure out what to wear everyday, so we wrote a fix!

What it does

You can access WhateverWeather on the webapp, or using a google home mini to get the weather for your location and determines what you should wear

How we built it

We made an express node js server to host our RESTFUL API.

Challenges we ran into

Asynchronous calls are very annoying. Also it was hard to determine an algorithm for picking the perfect clothes for specific weather conditions

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Our restful API can be used by anyone with a few GET requests. As well, our UI's simple design allows users from all walks of life to use our app.

What we learned

What's next for WhateverWeather

We want to be able to allow users to enter their entire wardrobe to get specific clothing choices.
