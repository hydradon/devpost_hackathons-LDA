Inspiration

As Highschoolers with cellphones, it is often difficult to stay dedicated to one task and finnish it without the distraction of technology.

What it does

Maybe later locks you into a timer that you set to complete a specific subjects homework. If you want to exit the timer and use your phone, you have to take a quiz on that subject and answer all the questions correctly.
