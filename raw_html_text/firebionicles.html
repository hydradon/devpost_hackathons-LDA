Inspiration Our main inspiration came after the sad story that happened with young soccer players of Flamengo, a Brazilian soccer team. With that in mind, we saw the necessity to provide people a new and smarter way to flee from a building.

What it does

It detects if there is a fire happening in a corridor, and notifies our main control board. It then plans the best possible way out and signifies it to the user.

How we built it

We used Particle Photons, electronics, and a breadboard to make our first prototype.
