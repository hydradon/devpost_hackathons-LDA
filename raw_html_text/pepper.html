What is Pepper?

Pepper is a personal banking assistant. You can either use our iOS app or text message number to access Pepper. She can do things such as:

  1. Check balance, and specific accounts
  2. Check ATM locations
  3. Check bank branch locations
  4. Check last transaction
  5. Check all transactions in a category
  6. All spending for the month
  7. Check your bills

Why Pepper?

Even with ATMs being at every corner of the city, people still elect to go to local bank branches because of the personalized experience they get. We built Pepper to work alongside current bank apps (Capital One) as a personalized "mobile banking" solution. Pepper's learning curve is also much smaller than a mobile app, as you simply use language to communicate with her.
