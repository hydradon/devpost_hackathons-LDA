Key Features

  • Safe Ride: When you feel like you aren't safe, tap three times and police will be notified. Swipe when you feel safe again. The dark screen is meant to also provide you security when you aren't feeling safe, as potential attackers or harassers won't be aware that you have safe ride on.
  • Anonymous Reporting: Allows riders to report their bad experiences to local bus managers without fear or retaliation
  • Bus Map: Locates the nearest bus stop to you and lets you know the progress of the buses along your route
  • History: Keeps records of all of your rides

Inspiration

I've ridden public buses before, and I know what it feels like to be uncomfortable in a situation. Men and women need this app so that they can commute safely and stress free.

Why the name?

I was really craving chocolate. And hot cocoa usually makes you feel good inside, like this app does :)
