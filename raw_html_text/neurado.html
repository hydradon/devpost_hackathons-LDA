Inspiration

In the age of IOT, we have acquired increasingly more connected devices in our lives. In the center of this connected net of devices, our mobile phones are the ones closest to us. Neura provide intelligence on user behaviors. Wouldn't it be nice to connect our devices closer to our lives? NeuraDo aim to experiment with this idea. With it, you can build IFTTT like recipe that connect your live with your devices. For example, you can set up a recipe that send your SO a text message when you got out of work, or you can set up a recipe that mute/unmute your phone when you get to/off your work. Also, since the project is open source, you can build your powerful custom automation that run on your phone.

What it does

It let you build IFTTT like automation by connecting Neura's intelligence and pre-coded action.

How I built it

I built it by hand.

What's next for NeuraDo

I think it will be more useful for NeuraDo to support dynamic scripting so people can build their custom automation by uploading script instead of coding on top of the current app.
