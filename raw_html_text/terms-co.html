Inspiration

In today's world, with our short attention spans, a lot of people don't bother to read over terms and conditions, which can be very dangerous. People fail to see things like hidden fees, penalties, service charges, and the like. We'd like to solve this problem with a website that can summarize the long and tedious terms and conditions for you in simple, clear, and concise phrases. This way, you'll never have to be screwed over by the fine print ever again!

What it does

The website takes the terms and conditions of a service in the form of a URL, PDF/image, or pasted text, and summarizes it in a concise, clear way which makes it easy and quick to read. There are also advanced settings that allow you to filter out specific sections of the contract.

What's next for Terms.Co

Next, we would like to further develop our learning algorithms for more concise and accurate summarizing, and develop a more aesthetically appealing and functional website.
