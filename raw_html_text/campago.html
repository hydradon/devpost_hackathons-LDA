Inspiration

From our personal experience, we met extensive barriers while applying for summer camps. These paper forms, booklets, and long pages of text really ruin the effectiveness of finding the best camps.

What it does

This platform would essentially create a database of camps and their details (location, price, program type, date/time), which would be easily accessible from a mobile app. Users can search for camps by keyword or characteristics, and can filter results as well. Users may also provide ratings, reviews and other contributions such as photos to enhance the platform's utility. Calendar and map views are also available to find compatible schedules and proximity.

How we built it

Adobe XD, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator and Invisionapp were used to create the page demos.

Challenges we ran into

The time limit made it difficult to incorporate all proposed features.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Fluidity and aesthetics of the UX.

What we learned

Adobe XD and user-friendly design.

What's next for Campago

Actually developing it into a fully-functioning application, available to the public.
