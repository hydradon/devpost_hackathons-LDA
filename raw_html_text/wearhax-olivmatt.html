What it does

Kinect game where you walk around a city destroying as many buildings as you can before time runs out. You need to swat missiles out of the sky as you stomp through the city.

How we built it

We take Kinect input for the hands, Occulus head tracking for the head and to simulate spinning around, Xbox Controller to walk and strafe. Runs in the Unity game engine.
