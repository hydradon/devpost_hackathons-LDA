Inspiration

We believe everyone deserves to be able to sleep, eat, refresh and repeat!

What it does

Find resources nearby, no personal technology or money needed.

How we built it

We created a prototype that connects the providers with the needs of the homeless population.

Challenges we ran into

Not enough time. It above our skill set, additional tech support needed.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We worked together as a team to create a solution to provide easier access to exisiting resources for the homeless population.

What we learned

We learned that homelessness is a much greater and complex issue in our community than we were aware of. We learned about some of the challenges that homeless individuals experience on a daily basis.

What's next for ACCESSi

-Community trial and feedback -Additional resource categories -More accessibility for different abilities and languages -Cloud-based document scan and save with ability to charge your devices (charge locks) Smartphone and Web Version
