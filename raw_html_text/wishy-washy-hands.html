Inspiration

My son's teacher taught the class that if they sing the ABC's while washing their hands, they could significantly reduce the spread of germs. Ever since then, my son sings a song every time he washes his hands. I thought it would be nice to be entertained and reminded how important it is to wash your hands.

What it does

When activated, Alexa says some entertaining information about washing your hands

How I built it

Modified the Alexa fact skills blueprint

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Having my son help me come up with the phrases that are expressed to the user

What I learned

It's important to plan a head and I also learned how to quickly import, edit, and export an iMovie vido

What's next for Wishy Washy Hands

Incorporate some songs that Alexa sings to you while you wash your hands.
