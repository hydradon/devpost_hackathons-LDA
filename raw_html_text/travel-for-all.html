• Challenging today’s conventions by creating mobility system and travel matching service.

  • By Hacking the CAN system, car can be controled remotely.

  • In addition, you can update your tour with your own hands at any step during the tour..

  • When the car is approaching, you can unlock from your smartphone or wearable devices.

  • You can easily open the car door by tapping.

  • Just by setting your favorite theme and preferences, you can make a perfect tour wherever you are.

Inspiration

Absence of persons with disabilities in foreign tourists and holding Tokyo 2020 Paralympics.

What it does

Automobile remote controller (+ Tourism matching) service especially for the people with disabilities.

How I built it

By hacking CAN system, remotely control the function of the car from the smartphone.

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I’m proud of

Not only provides a solution but also propose the system which helps the people with disabilities to have the motivation to go out.

What I learned
