What it does

Pinch The Penny is a fun interactive way to allow other people to donate to us one penny by; you guess it - Pinching the penny. Yep, thats it.

How I built it

Pure HTML/CSS/Javascript with bootstrap as our CSSframework and some JavaScript libraries.

Challenges I ran into

CSS/Javascript Animations and turning an arm into a pinching cursor.
