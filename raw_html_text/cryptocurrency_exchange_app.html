Happy Hacking for Caltech 2018 Hackathon

This is a test repo for cyptocurrency exchange simulator application.

Recently Bitcoin and blockchains become very popular in the world. Many people earn money by buying and selling bitcoins and other kinds of cryptocurrencies. We built the platform for people to train their skills on exchanging bitcoins and predicting pricing. Also, we want to deploy some auto-trading bots on the website by and train them which use algorithmic trading and deep learning models for making more money.

We provide both real-time and historical pricing data for Bitcoin and other types of cryptocurrencies.

Please have fun to visit our website: cryptoground.herokuapp.com/
