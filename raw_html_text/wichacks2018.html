Inspiration

Cats.

What it does

Symphony of memories is a short unity game that plays through a tale of twelve days shared between a ghost and the cat that keeps her tied to her home.

How we built it

unity and a cumulative 1,297 milligrams of caffeine.

Challenges we ran into

Learning unity took the longest, second only to dealing with merge conflicts involved in using git to manage a unity project.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The art, staying up all night, the music, the staff of energy drink cans, and the ability to complete a whole game in 24 hours.

What we learned

Don't use git for unity, and don't commit your scenes. just assets and scripts. Good god.
