Inspiration

SwissQuote Challenge #2 High risk high reward nature of the stock market

What it does

The player has to collect green arrows while avoiding the red ones. The player loses if his balance reaches 0 at any point during the game.

After 30 seconds, there is a market crash where only red arrows fall in the "Stock Shower" and the goal is to take as few of them as possible.

How I built it

Using Processing

Challenges I ran into

Tweaking the game mechanics to make it resemble the stock market to its best and have good difficulty curve.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Using memory efficiently The game is fun

What I learned

Basic game design and thinking about the user experience

What's next for Stock Shower

Compatibility with different screen resolutions Better graphics (using images) Sounds Adding names to high scores
