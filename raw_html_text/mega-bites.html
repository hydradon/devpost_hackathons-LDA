Human beings are habitual creatures, but creating good habits and breaking bad ones have never been a straight-forward task. With Habit Breakr, our team has created an interactive web-application that allows users to achieve a better health style by helping them to manage their daily tasks and and be the judge of their own performance through interactive graphs and an and intuitive user interface.
