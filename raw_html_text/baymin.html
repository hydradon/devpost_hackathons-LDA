Inspiration

People often emphasize the importance of teaching kids how to stay healthy physically, with no regards to mental health. Mental health awareness is important at any age, but we believe that encouraging children to be aware of their own mental health earlier on is important for their development.

What it does

BayMin encourages kids to learn how to talk about feelings; it includes information understandable by young children, and fun activities such as challenges to make drawings based on prompts about how the user feels.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Creating a kid-friendly interface. Focusing on a target audience who could benefit from the app's experience.

What's next for BayMin

Adding more interactive, kid-friendly games. Expanding activities, such as a wider range of prompts for challenges.
