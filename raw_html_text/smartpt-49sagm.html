Inspiration

When talking to collegiate athletes they expressed interest in a better way to analyze and present already widely available medical device data (Apple watch for example).

What it does

Takes data from athletes such as heart rate and oxygen consumption during a workout to provide them with real-time feedback on how intense their workout is as well as trends on their habits and exercise. The athletes personalized data can then be taken to a professional such as a personal trainer who can then use their logs to provide a quicker and more accurate assessment of what's going on.

How we built it

Swift, Xcode.

Challenges we ran into

FDA regulations on medical devices, time constraints.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

App framework and design, teamwork, research on device regulation.

What we learned

More about app development from back to front end, planning, compromise, teamwork.

What's next for smartPT

App development, improved algorithms, customer surveys, FDA medical device approval.
