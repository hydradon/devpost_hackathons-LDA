Inspiration

One of our team members' remembered a story from their chilldhood:

A long, long time ago in a land far, far away there was a desperate nation that needed salvation from boring bureaucratic documents. Four brave wizards were called upon to save this world on the brink of destruction. The rest is history.

What it does

Simple SAR brings you the most important information from the Student Aid Report. Packaged in a sleek look that will catch your eye, Simple SAR is the living proof that official documents can be sexy.

How we built it

Docker and JHipster alongside AngularJS, HTML, CSS.

Challenges we ran into

Integration of backend with frontend, Angular integration.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The sleek single-page website that contains all the information the student needs to have access to right away. The beautiful UI elements combined with the essentials of a hopeful student.

What we learned

Staying focused for 28 hours straight, eating artificially flavored candy and crispy cookies.

What's next for Simple SAR

Taking over the world of boring education documents and disrupting the undisruptable.
