More than 400 people lose their lives everyday in india on the road due to accidents resulted by human errors, potholes and weak emergency response and a lot more lose someone close to them.

So we have put together some lines of code attempting to make the mobility safer.

We have an app that enables you to have vital medical information and SOS contacts on the top of your android lock screen to make it easier to get help if the inevitable strikes you.

You can share your location in real-time with your loved ones to make sure you there is always a watching eye on you.

SOS sms will be sent to selected contacts can be invoked from app or even by pressing headphone button 5 times if the phone is in pocket or bag.

Well more than 80% accidents happen as a result of human error and so have enabled non-distracting audio alerts even through your headphones or infotainment system to warn you of hazardous driving conditions, cycling and pedestrian zones to enable you to take responsibility of road equity.

It can also connect to an OBDII bluetooth interface to set overspeeding alert on that rented Zoomcar to avoid overspeeding penalty or before the coolant in the car starts boiling.

The app can also talk with mediatek’s linkit one and another dedicated app can talk with Ti’s CC2560 sensor tag (link in YouTube description) to read values of accelerometer,gyroscope and magnetometer over bluetooth, fuse it to auto detect accidents and road conditions and spread the information through the network.

We have also put a tiny safety news feed where everyone can contribute via social media on #iot #connectedcars #drivesafe #roadsafety #cycling and we will put the best ones in the feed everyweek.

Take responsibility of safety and spread awareness
