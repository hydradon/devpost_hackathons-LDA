Inspiration

VUCourse idea pops up when we think about an app that is both useful and easy-to-do.

What it does

VUCourse can help you schedule your classes in the upcoming semesters with a few click. You just need to tell the app your major, your year and classes you have taken. The app will give you a schedule that helps you graduate on time without torturing yourself into 18-hour semester

How we built it

We use Node.js and MongoDB for back-end and Swift for the front end

Challenges I ran into
