Inspiration

Do you ever go to the grocery store, wondering "What do I need and what am I missing?" Well, this is the smart home application for you.

What it does

Essentially, a camera is set in your fridge which logs the contents every moment you close the fridge. It will have all items ever registered in the fridge and the amount of those items within it.
