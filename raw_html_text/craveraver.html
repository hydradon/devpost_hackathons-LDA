Inspiration

Many times my parents are always confused about what to make for our meals. They usually come back home beat from work, and they are too tired to make us anything, let alone think of a meal. Not only them, but students also get too lazy to cook actual food. As a result, they resort to unhealthy fast-foods. I've seen many times if folks get the inspiration for a meal, they'll cook out their vision and enjoy an actual meal for once.

What it does

CraveRaver allows the user to lean towards a genre of foods. After that, the possible foods for the user's preference are randomly displayed.

How we built it

We built it using Python and it's inbuilt modules.

Challenges we ran into

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned

We learned what ways to not practice in order to work efficiently.
