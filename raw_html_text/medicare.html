What it does

MedicARe is an android app that uses augmented reality to scan bottles of medications and record each intake. User will scan the bottle of medication and click "take pill" to record. A medication history list of last and the amount taken for each medication is shown. A page of circular progress bars are shown to keep track of how many dosages has been taken for each day.

How we built it

We used android studio, google cloud vision and google AR core to build the app.

Challenges we ran into

We ran into issues with the placement of AR anchors.
