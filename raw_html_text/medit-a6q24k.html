Inspiration

Our inspirations came when we realized that we can use SMS as a reliable source of data transmission between callcenter operator and an injured person in need of help

What it does

It is a multimedial app designed to ease out actions that are necessary to presere someones life It also looks after people that are either elderly or have serious health conditions when risk is detected and is able to send information about them to the call center operater which then can based upon their conditon send help

How we built it

We used android studio, coffeinne and power of will
