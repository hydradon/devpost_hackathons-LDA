Inspiration

We saw that there are not convenient way for student to find out about on-campus events that occur through-out the year, so we decided to make one for students to post events and share events that are happening around campus.

What it does

This app shows Campus Events at selected colleges. Also allows users to add new events and categorize them.

How I built it

We used android studio to built the front-end and back-end of the app.

Challenges I ran into

Challenges we arn into: I think one of the biggest challenge was to collaborate and built something to scale. Also the time constraint didnt help either

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Proud of working ALL day on a project and coming out with a functional prototype.

What I learned

We learned that it's best to have a structured plan before diving straight into a project to avoid issues down the road.

What's next for SCE

We are missing other parts to the project such a sign in and sign up.
