Inspiration

I wanted to book a trip that took all the decision making away from me and give me a package that gets the best from my trip based on what I like to do.

What it does

TripMe books a trip, and that consists of hotels, restaurants for all three meals, local attractions and Lyft rides between all locations using our AI engine.
