Inspiration

Chatbot & voice command app will require a device, like smart speaker & smartphones. But what if there are peoples who can not afford smart speaker, or disabled peoples who can not use smartphones but keypad phones, or peoples who don't have good internet to use those app.

What it does

You make a phone call. A bot will answer. You make a booking for hotel or car. Bot will book for you. This is just a proof-of-concept, we're only using BookTrip sample bot provided, no booking API is called when you do booking.

How I built it

Magic! No, using NodeJS, and alot of magic.
