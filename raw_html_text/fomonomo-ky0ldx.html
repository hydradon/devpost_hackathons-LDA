Inspiration

FOMO. We miss out on things a lot. What if there's a way to see things that happened in a certain area when you enter it? FInd out who of your friends visited the area and see from not only their perspective but also the perspective of others?

What it does

Fear Of Missing Out? Now you don't have to worry, with our mobile app "FomoNomo" - Fomo is nomo. It takes your current location and allows you to see the videos and pictures that has happend in your current location through video replay and/or AR (Augmented Reality) technology. Just whip out your phone and scan across your location and see what live events happened there.

What makes us different is that we're focusing on building a community through the experiences of a collective group of people who are potentially strangers - and share each other's perspective of events. You never have to miss out on events because you are too busy anymore - you are assured that you will also get to experience the perspectives of those who do go to the events (friends or non-friends - let's make friends and make sure no user is ever left out!)
