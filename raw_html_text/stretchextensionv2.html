Inspiration

Intel's best health and wellness hack and motivating people to be aware of their mental and physical health

What it does

Our Chrome Extension reminds people to take a 1 minute break every 15 minutes they browse the Internet on Google Chrome.

Problems we encountered

It was our first time making an extension, and we had trouble figuring out how to make the notification pop up, no matter what website or tab that the user was on. The timer had to constantly running in the background.

Accomplishments we're proud of

We are proud that we made the deadline and have a fully functional and helpful product on the Chrome Web app store to share with our friends and family.

What we learned

We learned how to work under pressure, get our domain name to connect to our github repository, and how to build a chrome extension.

What's next for Strexercise

We would like to add more options for users to choose when to take breaks at, such as 25 minutes or 30 minutes. We would also like to make it more efficient by having the timer start when you dismiss the alert. Suggesting different exercises would also be cool.
