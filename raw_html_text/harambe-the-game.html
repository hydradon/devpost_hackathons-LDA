What it does

"Harambe - The Game" is a real-time multiplayer game where one person plays as Harambe, and up to three other players can play as zoo keepers. The idea for our gameplay was an asymmetrical game of capture the flag where a young boy is the flag and is inside Harambe's pen. The zookeepers must cross the line into Harambe's pen and rescue the boy (capture the flag) before the clock runs out. Harambe is capable of punching the zookeepers to defend himself, which each play given a specific amount of health. In retaliation, the zookeepers can shoot Harambe.

How we built it

We used JavaScript (Node.js), websockets (Socket.io), and the Phaser game engine.
