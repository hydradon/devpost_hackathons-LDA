Inspiration

Words are neat, Google Translate isn't very good.

What it does

It's a list of some words with their translations.

How I built it

I looked at a list of some of the most frequently used nouns and verbs, and translated them into other languages using prior knowledge of languages and some google translate, checking the google translate outputs for accuracy.

Challenges I ran into

Some words don't really have good translations.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of
