The Japanese pseudoscience of Reiki inspired us to create an app that promotes the balance the four aspects of life- physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual- for a more fulfilling life

Users do different activities (health, academic, artistic, and social tasks) to improve the different "environments" that are given in the app.

We ran into issues concerning web-based API and re-sizing design elements between the Android and iOS versions.

We are especially proud of our mission, design, and dedication.

We learned how to collaborate better as a team by using our diverse set of skills to create a beautiful, easy-to-use app.

We plan on creating a more extensive list of tasks, and more specific details for each task.
