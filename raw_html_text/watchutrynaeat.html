What it does

This program is based on the person's allergy problems diagnosed by the doctor. Our design helps you avoid food with allergens. This tracks dietary habits with the experts' advices and best recommendations tailored for your needs.

How we built it

We used HTML and CSS the skeleton of website and used JAVASCRIPT to create more complex web tools and used ajax to connect to a mongodb database to retrieve user and food data.

Challenges we ran into

Most of us didn't know html/css little to no experience to ajax and mongodb to connect the backend and frontend database.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We successfully created a website with a huge potential which fulfill the needs of contemparary human adults suffering their medical and allergic conditions.

What we learned

We learned css, html, javascript and mongodb, and ajax and how to implement apis.
