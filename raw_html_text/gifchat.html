Inspiration

The increase in the use of GIFs and the messages that they convey insipred us to use it as a medium of communication.

What it does

Recognizes your voice interprets your speech and returns a reply in the form of a GIF. We have implemented a bot that can also serve as a second party in the two way communication.

How we built it

We used python as our back-end language, various APIs to parse the speech to text and vice versa. return gifs

Challenges we ran into

Getting the browser to record the sound was one of the major challenge.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Got the bot to work.

What we learned

Use of different APIs.

What's next for gifchati

Even more contextual conversations.
