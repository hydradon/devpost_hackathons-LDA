Inspiration

We wanted to build a Fitbit-quality product to help the two-thirds of Americans who take medications. Here it is.

What it does

Pillsy automatically tracks when you take your pills, and sends reminders when you forget. You can also share data with your family and healthcare team.

What's next for Pillsy

Pilots with customers and partners in healthcare
