• app2bat

Inspiration

To allow baseball game spectators to interact and connect with other spectators at the same game.

What it does

Allows users to guess specific player actions during the baseball games alongside other app users inside the stadium.

How we built it

App2bat was built with React & Flexbox.

Challenges we ran into

Challenges we ran into were scaling down our app to fit inside the time allocation.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of what we were able to achieve in a short amount of time. We were able to conceptualize our idea, design proof of concept and execute with code within the span of 5 hours.

What we learned

We learned that we work well together when we utilized our individual strengths (design, code, project manage) to work as a cohesive unit. And that communication is key.

What's next for app2bat

Future iterations of the app will include a leaderboard and a point system that will allow users to redeem their winnings with concession stand items.
