Inspiration

school shootings are too common, and a tragedy. we come from florida, which had a tragic such incident just a few days back. we can use technology to lower risk, and save lives in these situations

What it does

detects guns in pictures, (we dont have guns so it detects bananas)

How we built it

azure cognitive services

Challenges we ran into

we dont have guns, and bananas dont really look like guns

Accomplishments that we're proud of

it detects guns in stock images, and detects bananas when we hold one

What we learned

emergency procedures are a labyrinth

What's next for securecampus

more features, testing in lifelike simulations
