Why we built Retrievr

We all lose something every now and then. Retrievr helps you get your lost items back.

How it works

Users log into the app using Parse. Once inside, they can scan in their NFC-tagged items (e.g. wallet, backpack), which are then stored in the Parse backend. If the user happens to lose an item, other Retrievr users can find and scan in the item--the two users are then put in contact with each other. Simple as that!

What's Next
