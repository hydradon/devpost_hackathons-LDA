Inspiration

Between 2016 and 2017, there has been a 52% increase in female solo travelers. However, females/minority woman are statistically proven to be more at risk when travelling alone. While other travel websites and applications cater to a general population with lengthy blog posts, travelher is a concise, streamlined platform where woman can easily identify destinations where they would feel the most safe.

What it does

The main functionality of this app is a rating system, where woman rate how safe they feel while travelling to various destinations. Additional features of the app include, emergency services, and currency exchange information.

How we built it

With the 12 hour time frame, our team was able to fully design the User Interface of travelher by designing wireframes with sketch and photoshop.

Challenges we ran into

Not having the backend experience to program what we want, and working with other UX tools we were unfamiliar with!

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Our team is most proud of the uniqueness in the idea, as it identifies a gap in the Travel Industry

What we learned

Learning Invision and Python!

What's next for travelher

Expand to Website and Android platforms, become the "one stop shop" for female travelhers, and emergency alerts
