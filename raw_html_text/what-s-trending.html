Inspiration

Everyone wants to stay updated with the topics they are interested in. But in today's busy life we are some where lagging behind of others. Just because we lack knowledge of that field. You might not always get the time and patience to look up something on google and then browse through the pages to the news you wanted. But with What's trending you'll be able to get what you always wanted.

What it does

The skill will provide topics based on the query requested by user. This skill can also tell what's on some top charts (these charts are limited but based on the searches of people all around the world.). User can ask for topics related to some keyword provided by the user.

How we built it

We'll be using pytrends for this purpose, and we'll use Python as our runtime environment for our lambda function. We'll use _ slots _ and _ Dialog Directives _ for our skill to be more efficient and user friendly.
