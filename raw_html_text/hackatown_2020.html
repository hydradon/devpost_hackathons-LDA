Inspiration

Inspire communities to take ownership of their environment.

What it does

It gives a platform for local communities to pinpoint areas with unmanaged waste or littering and plan social events to clean them.

How we built it

We separated tasks between back-end and mobile app in hopes to get a working POC as soon as possible.

Challenges we ran into

  • Find the best technologies for our needs (for mobile)
  • Integrate said technologies

Accomplishments that we're proud of

  • Practices adopted during development

What we learned
