Everyone has experienced the process of finding a place to rent. A simple Google search produces an overwhelming number of websites where you have to figure out the credibility of the site and whether the landlord is reputable. You also have to constantly input the search criteria with each different site. After finding some houses you end up losing track of it all. With our App, HouseHuntr we make everything easier with a single tap. HouseHuntr provides users with the ability to view a landlords rating, quickly filter out houses that don't meet your search criteria, chat within the app and keep your favourites all in one place.
