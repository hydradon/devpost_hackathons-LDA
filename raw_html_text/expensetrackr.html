expensetrackr

expensetrackr is a Cisco Spark based expense tracker bot. Talk to it! It is very friendly :)

Smart-Contracts is a breakthrough technology which enables us to execute custom software programs over a Blockchain. expensetrackr is built over Ethereum Smart-Contract technology to help you invest for your retirement or any other investment like buying a home. You make your own plans and the machines do all the work.

expensetrackr can track your expenses and manage your investment plans using cutting edge Smart-Contract technology backed by Ethereum Blockchain.
