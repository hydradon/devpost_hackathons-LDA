Inspiration

Experiences and friends' experiences. The internet is a scary place, and you never know when you will encounter an image that you're uncomfortable with, or triggers your phobia.

What it does

Censors images you are sensitive to online.

  • Can be expanded to have each image optionally toggled to not be sensored
  • Can change replacement images
