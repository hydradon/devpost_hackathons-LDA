Inspiration

Not knowing what a product is frustrates us, and it would be awesome to know what it is by simply putting it into your camera's view.

What it does

Recognizes image markers on products.

How we built it

We used Vuforia's image recognition technology using Unity with an Android SDK.

Challenges we ran into

Integrating Tango with Vuforia

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Finishing our product within the narrow time limit.

What we learned

The importance of convenience along with speed and reliability.
