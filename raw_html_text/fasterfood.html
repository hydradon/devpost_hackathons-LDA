What it does

It automates the task of filling out pesky surveys for fast food restaurants for redeemable offers. At the moment, it supports Jack in the Box and Subway.

How I built it

The Android app takes user submitted info from a fast food receipt (at this moment, just Jack in the Box and Subway), and posts that data to a phantomjs webserver. This webserver is responsible for automating the survey submissions.
