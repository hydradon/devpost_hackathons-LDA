Inspiration

We are space exploration fans and we suffer every setbacks that happen.

What it does

It reduces the risk of failure in high-pressure tanks.

How I built it

By implementing digital twin and 5G

Challenges I ran into

Lack of a Python library for MindConnect IoT Extension, hence we made one such that others wanting to connect their sensors to MindConnect have an easy to use, high level communication library.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned

Outcome

This hackaton resulted into two new open-source projects released under the MIT licence:

  • https://gitlab.com/Addono/mindconnect-iot-extension-python A MindConnect IoT Extension client for Python, offering a high-level API to send data to the MindConnect platform.
    • https://gitlab.com/Addono/iothon-thingspeak2mindconnect A tool to forward sensor data from the ThingSpeak platform to MindConnect.
