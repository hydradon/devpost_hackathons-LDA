• FIND THE TREASURE

Inspiration

We love games - especially ones that we can win money on.

What it does

There's 3 clues placed in locations around your city - and a treasure chest. You find clues that lead to clues until you find the treasure chest, then you win $100! You have one hour to complete the treasure hunt!

You find the clues by holding up your phone and searching for them in AR.

How I built it

Unity3D + ARKit2 + Firebase

Challenges I ran into

XCode had to update to use ARKit2 and in order for XCode to update, I needed to update my OS, in order for my OS to update I needed to clean my SSD because I was out of space

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Just placing an AR item in the world. I've never done anything with AR so it was exciting to get that working.

What I learned

A ton - ARKit is awesome.

What's next for AR Treasure Quest

We launch on the app store! After some refinements
