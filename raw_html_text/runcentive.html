RunCentive

RunCentive is an app to incentivize yourself to be healthy. It tracks the calories you burn by running, and transfers money into your checking("spending") account from your savings account based on how much you ran. Have a pricey item you want to buy or a concert you want to attend? Go for a run first so you can spend that money guilt-free and be healthy doing it!

The app uses:

  • The Capital One API to manage your bank accounts and transfer money between them.
  • Android Geolocation to track the distance you have ran. It uses your location and speed to calculate the calories you burnt using a standard medical formula.
