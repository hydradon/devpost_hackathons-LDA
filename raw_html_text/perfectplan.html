Inspiration

Tired of losing days planning travel routes we decided to get to work and design a tool to make life easier for those who waste their time planning trips. Perfect Plan gets a perfect combination of destinations, activities to do, hotels and flights getting travel plans that will fit to your decisions.

How it works

Perfect Plan combines three different ways to obtain a complete travel plan:

  • Through natural language, choosing what you want to do or where to go.
  • Choosing your favorite destinations and on what to spend your time.
  • Selecting plans shared by other users.

A travel plan is created from this premise. Only add dates and duration of the trip. A list of flights, hotels, activities will be generated according to your selections. We find the best option.

Our project lets you create a complete road trip in only one step.
