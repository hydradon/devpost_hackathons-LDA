Inspiration

Our story: We wanted an easy way to find tutors in our area. Tinder makes things easy, so we combined the two.

What it does

Matches you up with tutors who teach the subjects you're interested in learning about. Or, if you're a tutor, it connects you with people you can teach.

What's next for Tinder4Tutors

We want this web app to sort by location and have a better algorithm for matching people up. We also want to expand our target group beyond just college students.
