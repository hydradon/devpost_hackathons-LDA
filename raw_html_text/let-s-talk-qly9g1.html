Inspiration

In my personal experience, sometimes it was difficult to open up to my friends about certain situations just because I felt embarrassed. Often times I wished for someone that was going through a similar situation just so I wouldn't feel lonely.

What it does

This app creates listings for people near you who are going through a tough time, potentially facing an issue similar to one that the user is facing. From this app, you can choose what pieces of information you'd like to show to everyone else and create 'status' updates for certain problems you encounter in life.

What's next for Let's Talk

We're going mobile! The next step for Let's Talk is to expand onto mobile platforms, both IOS and Android.
