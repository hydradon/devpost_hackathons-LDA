Ardaigh Education

Inspiration

We had always had difficulties finding useful materials to help us study. So when we made materials ourselves, we wanted a way to share them.

What it does

This platform allows for uploading lecture notes as well as organization by school, class, professor, and subject. Although currently you cannot find notes through anything but the notes search tab.

How we built it

Using the great jHipster framework we were able to put this website together.

Challenges we ran into

Only one of our team members had much programming experience at all, and he didn't know anything about jHipster. It was a massive learning experience for the whole team.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The website runs, we're amazed by that fact more than anything.

What we learned

The great power that strong frameworks like jHipster can give a developer, allowing you to focus on the important design aspects as opposed to technical problems you may encounter.

What's next for Ardaigh Education

We plan to continue developing and eventually launch this product.
