Inspiration

A lot kids love TV shows about dinosaurs or read books about them, so I thought why not bringing it to Alexa.

What it does

It tells the user facts about dinosaurs.

How I built it

I used the alexa skills kit with node.js.

Challenges I ran into

Creating a skill in english was not that easy, because I am german.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Making a voice experience, that hopefully some kids will enjoy.

What I learned

Of course facts about dinosaurs. But also running through the skill certification process.

What's next for Kids Dinosaur Facts

Maybe I could add more facts or create a new skill with a more flexible voice interface, that it won't be limited to telling facts.
