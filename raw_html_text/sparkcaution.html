Whether they happen in school or at home, intentionally or unintentionally, fires set by children are always cause for concern. Most school counselors will, at some point in their careers, be called on to work with a child who has started a fire or played with fire. Even experienced counselors may worry about their ability to help a child who has set fires, believing that highly specialized skills are required.
