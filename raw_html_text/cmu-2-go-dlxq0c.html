Inspiration

Our inspiration derives from the frustration waiting in line on campus and living far away from campus. Our friends or other people often are on campus at times we aren't so making a connection for anyone on campus to help each other out and reduce wait times for dinning is why we built CMU 2 Go

What it does

CMU 2 Go is a platform in which the entire dining process gets changed. Everyone orders through this platform and all the servers receive orders through this platform making it a simple and easily coordinated process. This platform makes it easy to get your orders accurately, quickly, and efficiently in addition to making it possible to order food remotely and have it delivered. This platform is designed to reimagine dining to make it easy.

How we built it

Xcode, Android Studio, and Firebase for the server.

Challenges we ran into

Learning swift syntax and implementing location services.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Synchronized location tracking, cross-platform compatibility, and server authentication with password protection.

What we learned

How to implement location services and how to create an iOS app.

What's next for CMU 2 Go

In-app Payment with dine-x or regular money, implementation throughout all dining locations on campus. Giving reviews to the deliverer and incentive for more people to deliver.
