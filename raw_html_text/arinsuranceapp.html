Inspiration

Statefarm is an insurance company. AR has changed our lives in numerous ways. It has made possible for us to visualize things hitherto impossible. With the power of AR, we thought of building a product that truly recognizes the potential and can be correctly applied to the domain of Insurance.

What it does

The AR-based application takes in the live capture/pattern and compares that with the database of collected images (laptop, electrical fixtures, dollars, etc). If there is a match, it displays the potential risk and result.

How we built it

We used Unity IDE and C# programming language for the same. It enables us to get an Android, ioS application.

Challenges we ran into

  1. 0 Starting knowledge of AR and Unity.
  2. Getting the text on the screen dynamically updated
  3. Virtual buttons for better interactivity

Accomplishments that we're proud of

AR implemented with near perfect accuracy

What we learned

  1. Using Unity-based approach of developing AR applications
  2. Understood the problem scope of insurance companies

What's next for ARInsuranceApp

Incorporate more detailed insights about the potential risks and comparative study with the competitors.
