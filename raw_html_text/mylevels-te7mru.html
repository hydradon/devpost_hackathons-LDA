Inspiration

To make an individual's life easier by keeping track of their health data and giving recommendations on how to manage their health lifestyles such as blood glucose and blood pressure levels, etc; in order to prevent, well in advance, from suffering any malignant health problems.

What it does

An iOS application that provides the user with comprehensive health information through a direct link of the URL and a service to immediately call 911 in case of emergency health issues.

How we built it

We built this using xCode and Swift with a MAC computer.

Challenges we ran into

It was difficult to produce a solution by identifying a well-defined healthcare problem within a short time. During developing, the Segues between scenes and as well as the embedding the relevant health information URLs and integration of the scenes' code to the user interface was challenging.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Providing a solution for the user by giving them better accessibility of trustworthy health information through open data.

What we learned

We are glad to have explored the new ideas and identifying the problems on the accessibility of relevant health information towards a better understanding of it. Plus, we learned to use xCode and Swift Programming resulting in an improvement of technical skillset.

What's next for MyLevels

The next level for MyLevels is becoming a part of the Smart IoT Healthcare Systems like Smart Ambulance. Trustworthy healthcare information for the public's understanding will be instrumental in the future of Smart Healthcare system. A well-known health condition of a user, in advance, will be significant in taking countermeasures for their respective health issues as time is a critical parameter here.
