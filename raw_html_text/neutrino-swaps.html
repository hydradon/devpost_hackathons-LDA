Inspiration

Compound currently has volatile interest rates, we wanted to give more stability to the lending market for the good of humanity.

What it does

Creates a market for interest rate swaps on blockchain.

How we built it

We used cosmos and a lot of coffee.

Challenges we ran into

Serialization issues in Cosmos. Needs moar logging

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We have a solid design and framework for the project. We met each other.

What we learned

Learned about how Cosmos works and its flexibility. Learned about Oasis Labs' privacy features.
