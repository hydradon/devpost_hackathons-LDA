Hack @hackmty The "Food" app is an app for iPad thought for undecided chefs. It will allow you to select the food you have and give you options as of what you can create with those or select random recipes that will satisfy your tastebuds. The app is powered by Amazon-Web-Services, which allows a huge database storage with identity verification through Facebook and/or Google. This gives the user the opportunity to retrieve all the users favorite recipes that are already in the cloud. The app will give the user step by step specifications to create Food masterpieces. It includes 4 stopwatches to allow the user to have precise information on the status of their creation, naming the step they are on the specific countdown. The platform includes a checklist so the users can easily know what they have and what they need to cook their choice of meal. The app is an new interactive way to cook, yummy made easy.
