We live in an age where the president of the united states, one of the most influential men in the world, has the power to send stock markets into shock at will by naming a company in his tweets. By analyzing the effects of his tweets on stock market prices, we can create data trends into the future to make predictions about what effect he potentially could have on industry stock were he to tweet about them again. Potentially, this could be used for a competitive edge in stock trading, as by being able to predict roughly the trend that stock prices could follow, buying and selling at the right time could lead to large profits.
