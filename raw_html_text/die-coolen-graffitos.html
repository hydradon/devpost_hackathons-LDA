Inspiration

House owners hate graffitis, some people really like it but perhaps don't want to vandalize walls in a destructive way. And that's where we come in.

What it does

Tag walls with QR codes, scan them and see what other people have left behind.

How we built it

Wikitude AR for android and pyhton for the backend.

Challenges we ran into

Everything. Seriously.
