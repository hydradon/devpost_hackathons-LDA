Inspiration

The members of the group are all children of immigrants. Some of which have parents that are highly skilled but do not have transferable education.

What it does

Brampton's Employment Hub is a website dedicated to helping immigrant job seekers to find a career relevant to their studies. It attempts removes the difficult job search barrier that prevents hard working immigrants from finding employment.

How we built it

Employment Hub is a website built with the Django framework using HTML/CSS and Python.

Challenges we ran into

Utilizing Django's full features. We found it difficult, due to time limitations.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Our team is proud of working together to come up with a solution to relevant problems that we find in the community of Brampton. As well as completing a demo that can be showcased.

What we learned

Our team learned a lot about how immigration. Such as the struggles of new immigrants, and how difficult they found the job search. A lot of families come to Canada expecting a better future, but are limited because of their education in their country.

What's next for Employment Hub

Our team would like to improve the website, and enable functionality, such as database integration, and accounts.
