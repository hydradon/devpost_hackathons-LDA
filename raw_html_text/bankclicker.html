Inspiration

Currently investing money often feels troublesome and boring.

What it does

BankClicker solves this problem by gamifying the aspects of investing with a simple, social game that's connected to online banking services.

How we built it

React, canvas, express.js

Challenges we ran into

Time management.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The visual outlook of the game and the fun aspect.

What we learned

The importance of planning ahead of time.
