Sixteen

#ArchHacks2K16

Encouraging both younger and older users to get out of the house and be active. Sixteen builds a profile around each user that encompasses preferences and evolving interests. Harnessing community data as well as past user feedback, Sixteen suggests new activities that a user may find interesting.

We built this platform's back-end on Django with a front-end supported by Bootstrap.

This project is far from completed, as we look forward to adding Google Maps integration so users can get a better idea of what exactly is around them. Furthermore, user reviews of specific activates as well as "temporary" activities such as festivals and concerts would also become part of the platform. Finally, we aim to make meet-ups a critical part of Sixteen. Matching users of similar activity preferences, we hope to encourage users to not only pursue an active lifestyle, but also meet people with similar interests.
