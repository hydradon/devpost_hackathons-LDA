Inspiration

People don’t know what's out there and how easy it is to contribute to their favorite causes. There are so many apps that can help them contribute, but they are too difficult to find.

What it does

Lola provides simple things that the everyday person can do to get involved. The user is prompted with an abstract question, and we use their result to present them with apps they may be interested in.

How we built it

A combination HTML, javascript, brainstorming sessions and hard work.

Challenges we ran into

We didn’t know where to start and what to do-- both implementation and idea-wise.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We created something and we are excited to share it!

What we learned

There is so much you can learn on the internet.

What's next for Alola

Refining it’s algorithm to make better app selections, and adding new features for the future.
