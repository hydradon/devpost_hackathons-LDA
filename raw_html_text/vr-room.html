Inspiration

Making an education room that has the ability to have multiple headsets in it with wireless ability but full room tracking

What it does

Uses the HTC VIVE IR sensors with a Terasic FPGA, and ESP8366 wifi card to send position data calculated by the fpga via sensors on the headset to a host computer using UDP and update camera pose in a unity experience
