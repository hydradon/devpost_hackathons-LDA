What it does

Takes a picture of your business card and uses OCR to rip the text from the image. The data is then parsed and entered into a Contact file in the app for future reference, networking, and sharing.

How we built it

With Android Studio, Java, and Ritwik Gupta for mentorship.

Challenges we ran into

We had no idea how to build libraries an include them into our project, so we had needed the help of Ritwik Gupta to walk us through it.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We finished our first Android app that actually can be used in the real world.

What we learned

Android development, along with how to use Java in it and specific quirks about Android.
