Description

WikiMap lets you view your world through Google Maps and StreetView.

Inspiration

People need an interactive way to explore the world around them along with Wikipedia.

How it works

Just explore the world via Map View or StreetView and learn! The back-end is using ClusterPoint to host a dump of Wikipedia with latitude/longitude, and it is using ClusterPoint's GeoSpatial search.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Integrating ClusterPoint! It was a breeze!

What's next for WikiMap

Expanding to Virtual Reality goggles!
