• FakeChat

Inspiration

**We thought about Snapchat and we made this based off of a part of what you can do with/in Snapchat.

What it does

It takes pictures using your phone, and it lets you color over them. You can select between red, green, or blue colors. You can also retake the picture, and or redo your drawing on the picture.

How we built it

We used MIT App Inventor 2 (and QR reader), and used block coding.

Challenges we ran into

We didn't know how to combine the picture with the drawing, meaning we didn't know how to make it draw on a picture.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We feel accomplished that we made something that we could normally do also on Snapchat.

What we learned

We learned how to make something (App) on android.

What's next for FakeChat

We are thinking of adding more features like sounds, filters, etc. like there is on Snapchat.
