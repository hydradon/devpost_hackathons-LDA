Inspiration

Why should only premium cars like Tesla have advanced detection system? Everyone deserves to be safe on the road. Hence ARSenses was designed.

What it does

  1. Detect the person, vehicle or obstacles in driver’s blind spots and notifying it on the AR before it’s too late. [Check first image slide]
  2. Indicates you about other vehicle’s next turn if you are in their blind spot region & alerts you to take actions to avoid a collision on AR display. [Check second image slide]
  3. Potential collision? ARSenses saves the day.
