Inspiration

The audio level in a particular area can tell you a lot about what is going on, including the number of people, the need for assistance, and more.

How it works

Audio Vigilance uses the following technologies: PubNub for data streaming, Sinch to deliver messages, and the Tessels to measure ambient audio activity. When there is enough noise to trigger Audio Vigilance to deliver a notification, we automate a text message and update our Audio Vigilance dashboard to reflect the activity.
