Inspiration

Like the movie Gravity, Moon Alone explores the fragility as well as the strength of human life under total isolation.

What it does

Moon Alone is a Sci-Fi game that explores the concept of surviving on the surface of the moon alone. The player is stranded on the moon with low Oxygen Level and receives almost no communication from outside. They must solve a Maze in order to gather as many oxygen tanks as possible while waiting for the potential rescue to come.

How we built it

We built it with Unity.
