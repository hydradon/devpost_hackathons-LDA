Inspiration

We spent some time with LULAC representatives and they explained us the gravity of challenge 4. Hence, to make lives better at TAMU, we took up this project.

What it does

It has a 3D model of building that we propose as cultural center for Latin American students and faculty and a website which has a description of all resources available in that center.

How I built it

We used Sketchup for 3D modelling of a building and used HTML,CSS, Flask and Python for building website.

Challenges I ran into

Software installation issues, design issues.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Planned and successfully created a 2 storied model of a building having everything ranging from information center to museum.

What I learned

Sketchup, Python, Flask, design principles.

What's next for The Innovador Trio- Latinx Cultural Center

Organizing events at the cultural center and displaying their information on website.
