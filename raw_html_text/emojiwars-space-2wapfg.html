    Emoji Wars: Space in action

What it does

Emoji Wars turns your chat into a multiplayer game where your emoticons become character and weapons of an arcade fight

Inspiration

We liked the idea of turning your chat into an arcade gamepad so we decided to work on that!

For the game style, we got inspiration from Worms !, the world famous artillery strategy computer game from 1995.

Challenges I ran into

We had the idea to send visuals from the game into the chat so users could play even if they were not in front of the computer. One of the many challenges we faced was to generate GIFs to summarize the best moments of the game's actions.
