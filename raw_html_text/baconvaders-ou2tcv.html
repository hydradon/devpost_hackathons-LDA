Inspiration

What would the job of a butcher become if pigs could fly?

What it does

Uses Makey Makey to control a video game with gloves.

How we built it

Connected Makey Makey to gloves, wired the inputs to a video game we created.

Challenges we ran into

Properly wiring the makey makey, traditional game development

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Creating something that always makes people go "Whoah, that's crazy!"

What we learned

Teamwork, that sometimes you spend 5 hours coming up with ideas that go nowhere.

What's next for Baconvaders

Turning the game itself into an app.
