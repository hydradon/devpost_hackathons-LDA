Inspiration

Diabetes is one of the biggest common diseases in the world. Unfortunately, many do not take the disease seriously. Children are mostly affected by type 1 diabetes. However, the number of children who develop type 2 diabetes due to obesity as a result of malnutrition and physical inactivity is also on the rise. Early education and prevention in school and kindergarten should help to prevent this.

What it does

This Alexa Game Skill is asking questions about diabetes and educates the player.

How I built it

Alexa Skill Kit with skill-sample-nodejs-quiz-game template.
