Inspiration

Whenever you want to use the map on your phone/tablet during driving, it's distracting to look for buttons on the touchscreen, or have your hands block your view. It would be ideal if these tasks can be carried out with a unified, streamlined, interface.

How It Works

Provide a gesture-controlled platform that enables drivers to perform common tasks when driving, taking advantage of a virtual control interface and the interconnectivity of devices to achieve cross-device operations.

Highlighted Features

*Change the volume with a swipe.
*Grab music from your phone to your car.
*Pull up display of information on your car with a pinch.
*Zoom in/out of your map by rotating your hand.
