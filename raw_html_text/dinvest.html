Inspiration

Kitchener-Waterloo has a vibrant community of amazing tech startups that are developing technologies, products, and services that can benefit millions of people of around the world. Often times many tech startups in this community does not get the traction from investors they need to further develop their products and increase their customer base.

What it does

DInvest is a platform that allows investors to understand the tech ecosystem of the KW region and make investments into startups based on their criteria

How I built it

Bootstrap and JavaScript

Challenges I ran into

Adding different visual elements on the platform

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Being able to make a potential platform that can serve as a tool for investors to invest into KW and for startups in the region to thrive.

What's next for DInvest

Adding more startups onto the platform along with key information about them.
