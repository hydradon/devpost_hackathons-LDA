Inspiration

We often found that we would go to a location and forget to do something, like pick up groceries nearby or pick up an exam at school. This app changes that.

FanTaskTic changes the way people complete tasks by adding location data into the mix and offering a simple and fun interface.

What it does

FanTaskTic polls for your location continuously, and notifies you when you're near one of your to-do list items' locations.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of
