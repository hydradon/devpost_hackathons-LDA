Inspiration

RL solutions inspired us to make an app that will reduce wait times at health institutions around the world

What it does

With InstaClinic , you can make medical appointments with a click of a button. No more waiting in line at waiting in line when you have that annoying cough; Book your appointment, show up, and a doctor will be ready to see you within minutes. InstaClinic is a dual-interface app that let's you sign in as either a normal consumer or a verified walk-in clinic. As a consumer, the app locates the user using GPS location and Google Places is used to find walk-in clinics near you. The user can filter walk-in clinics by distance or city and then choose a convenient time for his/her appointment. All the clinics unfiltered by the user will receive a notification in their app of this request and the first clinic to accept the request will receive the consumer. Cancellations of appointments are available until 3 hours before the scheduled appointment. The consumer will pay a deposit fee of $5.00 that will be given back when they show up to the appointment on time. Walk-in-clinics will have to download the institutional version of the InstaClinic app to accept client in accordance with their own availability.
