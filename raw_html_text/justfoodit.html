Inspiration

Our inspiration was to create an app that would help kids be healthier. We also got inspiration from the achievements available on khan academy.

What it does

It encourages kids to exercise and eat healthy to earn points and to combat the issue of childhood obesity.

How we built it

javascript, html, css, sql

Challenges we ran into

Creating out database and formatting front end with css. Another challenge was working together to narrow ideas down to the minimum viable product and assigning tasks.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The database and the css.

What we learned

Learned how to work as a team.

What's next for justfoodit

We will continue to add more pages and create more ways for parents to track their kids' progress.
