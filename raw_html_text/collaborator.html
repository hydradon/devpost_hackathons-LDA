Finding right teammates for your project is extremely critical and it also the difficult part about any project. With this app you can find the right people for your project by matching the right skills, location and personality.

How it works

The matching algorithm will will map your profile with other users in the group and if there is a match then a matching number will be generated. If the matching percentage is more than 70%, then you will have the ability to directly call, message or find the particular individual on Facebook, Twitter and Linkedln.
