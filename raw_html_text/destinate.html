Inspiration

Nomandlist.com lets your filter which city you want to live based on your preferences. We took it a step further and used the IBM Watson to analyze your personality traits based on your tweets to automatically match you to your perfect city.

What it does

Destinate aggregates your personality traits based on twitter to tell you which city is best for you to live in
