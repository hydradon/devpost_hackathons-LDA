Inspiration:

To help life go right, something must be wrong. Based off the Angel Shot idea, we wanted to give people an easy, safe way out when a date or any situation goes wrong.

What it does

Our idea consists of a smart bracelet that syncs with a phone app. This bracelet will allow the user to get out of any situation, in any degree of danger. She can set up a fake call to excuse herself from an unwanted conversation. She can alert her friend with her location so they can come to her rescue. She can call for a Lyft or an Uber. She can also call the police if things escalate to that level, all with the touch of her bracelet.

How we built it

We created an Andriod app that will sync with the bracelet via bluetooth or wifi.

Challenges we ran into

Andriod app won't run. We also had trouble compiling the code for the hardware and the Andriod Studio.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Out idea fills a desperate need and we did the best we could.

What we learned

We learned just how many parts goes into a single app development, a lot of Andriod app development- mostly what doesn't work.
