Inspiration

I was filling out an internship application and the last question asked "If Kanye West, Elon Musk, and Hilary Clinton co founded a startup together what would the company be called and what would it produce?" It was my response that inspired me to build a website that helps people who wants to rule the world.

What it does

Throughout history, we have seen great rulers rise and fall. Let's learn from their mistakes. There are two parts to the site. The first part are guides. One must familiarize him/herself first but ruling the world. There are specific steps and actions that needs to be taken in order for this journey succeed. The second part builds on the first part.

How I built it

Registered for a free domain. Used Weebly to make and publish my site. Google Images provided pictures for the site.

Challenges I ran into

Finding the perfect picture and writing caption to go with the picture.

What I learned
