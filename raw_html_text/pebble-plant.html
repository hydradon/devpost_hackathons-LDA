Inspiration

There's cute apps that track your water consumption, but how about your steps? We wanted a fun and cute way to combine "pet nurturing"-type games with fitness-tracking games.

What it does

As you walk, you accumulate steps, which you can apply to grow your plant.

How we built it

PebbleCloud, Inkscape SVGs of plant
