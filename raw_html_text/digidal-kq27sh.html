Inspiration

The lack of convenience of carrying around a student ID card to get dorm and room access

What it does

A user can enter their ID card and be able to use NFC to scan a RFID access point with their phone instead of their card

How we built it

We used android studio to create an android app

Challenges we ran into

NFC is difficult to implement

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We had great teamwork and sharing of ideas

What we learned

We learned to plan first and implement after

What's next for DigIDal

We would like to allow users(students) to be able to login to their .edu account and automatically have their ID card registered and ready to scan.
