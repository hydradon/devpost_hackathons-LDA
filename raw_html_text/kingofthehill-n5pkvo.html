KingOfTheHill

A mobile king-of-the-hill game

KOTH is a simple, engaging Android app meant for play with a group of friends. Users create a game id and distribute it to their friends, and gameplay immediately begins. At any time, only one player, the king, can score points. The king is designated as the person who has most recently pressed a button on their version of our app. Gameplay persists and scores stack for a user-defined time, and the ultimate winner who was KOTH the longest is crowned victor.

In layman's terms: Our game is the embodiment of the "Facebook poke wars" only, our game has a winner.
