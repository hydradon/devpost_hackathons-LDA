Inspiration

There are fewer women who know how to manage their finances and fewer women entrepreneurs in India. Women have business ideas but don't have the resources to scale it to the next level. Often, they don't have access to guidance and fundings.

What it does

It is a personal finance and business finance management application. Personal Finance: 1. keeps track of your spending. 2. keeps track of your earnings. Business Finance: 1. keeps track of your sales records. 2. Inventory management of your products. 3. keeping track of your employee salaries. Also, connecting women entrepreneurs and investors / NGOs interested.(chatting facility is provided)

How we built it

We built it in Android studio and firebase database.
