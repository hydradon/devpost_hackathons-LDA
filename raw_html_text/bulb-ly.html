Inspiration

One of our team members went through the experience of being hospitalized due to the very poor condition of the street light, especially in poor weather conditions that reduce visibility.

What it does

IoT device will measure the current brightness of the surroundings, send information with API and change the brightness and hue of the bulb accordingly.
