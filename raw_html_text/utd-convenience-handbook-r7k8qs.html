Inspiration

Helping students like us get quick, useful information

What it does

It is a website that has campus info on one page, with quick access the different parts. It also adjusts to different devices.

How I built it

I used a bootstrap template maker, HTML, and CSS

Challenges I ran into

Our team has never used HTML or CSS or any other web dev tools. We pretty much started from scratch and made prototype we are proud of in 24 hours.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We are proud of what we made with the previous skills and the time we had. We are also proud of the idea and think that it will help many student on campus, potentially.

What's next for UTD Convenience Handbook

We plan to make a pebble/pebble time app to get access information wherever you are from your wrist. We also plan to make a mobile app
