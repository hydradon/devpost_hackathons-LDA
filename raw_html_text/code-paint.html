Inspiration

As I am running Girls Who Code, I feel a need for the girls to understand the logic behind the codes instead of just writing lines of codes.

What it does

The girls can click on blocks to build their programs for the drawing. Each level will have a different mission they have to complete.

How I built it

Interface by Android Studio. Game demo by Scratch.

Challenges I ran into

All members are new to Android Studio and we have difficulties incorporating the game in the App.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We came up with a good idea, and we learned something about app making.

What I learned

Creating user interfaces in Android Studio
