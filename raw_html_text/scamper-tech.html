Inspiration

The presentation for the CEISMC club at Georgia Tech that helps with STEM education in K-12 schools.

What it does

It's a website that uses activities to teach students how to solve certain problems. Part of the purpose too is to encourage students to use the SCAMPER method to help students challenge themselves and understand material better.

How we built it

The website is built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Challenges we ran into

Building out the activities while making sure that the SCAMPER method was used without stifling creativity.

Accomplishments that we are proud of

Design of the site, planning, custom artwork.

What I learned

Leanred Bootstrap, more JavaScript functions, TypeScript

What's next for scamper.tech

Possibly reaching others by building an app, and also more activities
