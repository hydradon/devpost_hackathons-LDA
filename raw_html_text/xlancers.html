Inspiration (Problem I solved):

Posting, advertsing and Renting of Real Estates Apartments on a website has not been easy at all. Consequently, Tenants/Buyers needs to know and access the Portfolio of the building or propertises they want to rent or buy and such Portfolio may includes 1.) Building Informations
2.) Video Demonstrations of the building
3.) Series of Image display of the building
4.) Tenancy Agreements
5.) Prices, Cities, Location Address, States.
6.) Real Estates Norms and values. Estates Likes and Dislikies etc.

Another Major problem is the issue of managing your Tenants Payments, Checking Dumorages, Renewal, managing Reports from Tenants, Accessing and management of your Tenants data.

Another Problem facing Commericial Brokers is the issue of real-time Communications and files sharing with their respective Tenants.

Another Problem with Estate Renting is that some Tenants will try to evade Payment when their trenting periods expires and I got that covered....

Here is how I solved this problem. I created an application called Xlancers or PropTech Lancers:

A Realtime Freelancing Site for Real Estate Posts Updates, Tenancy Managements, Property Rentals, Buying and Selling.
Tenancy Payments Managements and Renewals. Dumorages Checking. Connecting Brokers, Landlords and Tenants with the ability to build your Tenants Contact List, Chat List and real-time communiction management throughout the transactonal process, Tenant Reports etc.. This application also introduces Transactional System for tracking of Tenancy Payments Dates, Renewal Dates, Dumorages and Tenancy Expiration Dates etc..
