Inspiration

Huge number of untrained teachers in informal sschools

What it does

It simplifies the process of accessing loans by these teachers thus saving resources such as time and other expences such as money for travelling to and from banks during loan application

How we built it

We're using USSD for normal mobile phones and Cross-platform mobile application( Intel XDK) for Smart Phones

Challenges we ran into

Complexity of the processes involved as there are quite a number of partners involved

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Efficiency and Simplicity of accessing loans

What we learned
