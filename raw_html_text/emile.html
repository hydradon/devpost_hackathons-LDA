Inspiration

I’ve always loved listening to the music of Pamela Z—this setup is loosely based off of her motion and gesture controlled interface for live processing.

What it does

Takes samples of your voice and allows you to add effects, while simultaneously playing multiple other electronic instruments built into the setup.

How I built it

Little bits synth kits, plus some miscellaneous parts.

Challenges I ran into

It needs very long cables to be a functional set up, which were not available to me today.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I completed a fully functioning vocal processing and electronic instrument setup which I can perform with.

What I learned

Being able to perform with electronics can be much more user friendly than we are led to believe—it’s all about finding the right system for you.

What's next for Emile

Addition of an infinite sampler loop, plus the capacity to remember and edit/live process multiple samples at once.
