Inspiration

Saw random youtube video when thinking of what project to do. Big part of my childhood and thought it would be fun

What it does

Reels spin and wins are calculated accordingly via a certain multiplier of the stake they chose to play. 3 bonus games each have a 1 in 20 chance of appearing win extra cash. There is "Guess the friend", "flip the patties" and "Driving test". The game also has a create account/login system to keep a track of the money you've earned and will save for future times paying.

How we built it

Used win forms to create the backgrounds and set images to picture boxes. Programmed the changes and calculations using c#

Challenges we ran into

One page randomly deleted all its functions and when "re-enabled" still acted as though it had no function. Also had trouble calculating the total win multiplier when bonus games were played due to how the variable was being passed.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Actually completing (well mostly) the project despite spending all night awake and being very tired
