we wanted to allow for students to be able to find projects faster and for projects to find people faster. One of the hardest thing about being a young CS major is trying build a resume and find cool things to work on. This app fixes all of that. Our password-less authentication was inspired by Bop-It, in the way that we use gestures to log in to out app.
