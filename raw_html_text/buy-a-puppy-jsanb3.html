I recorded a video explaining the project. You can watch it here: https://youtu.be/moFA-NBBPk0

1. How long did the assignment take? Around 20 hours total

2. Did you learn anything new in the process? Yes I did. I’m pretty used to working with ruby on rails + slim + scss, or node + jade for complex projects, but I thought I could waste too much time with setup, so I started looking for alternatives and came across a pretty cool app caller Hammer that allows us to use slim/scss/bourbon/neat really easily. I really liked it, and I'm probably going to use it from now on for simple projects.

3. What was the hardest part? The tight deadline, for sure.

4. If you could go back and give yourself advice at the beginning of the project, what would it be? "Krystal, I know you are really excited about this hackathon, but you really should get some sleep"
