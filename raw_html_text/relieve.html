Inspiration

We wanted to communicate people the problems in the world and be more solidary with disasters helping people.

What it does

Relief shows all the disasters around the world and let you to make micro donations.

How We built it

We used :

  • .Club for therelief.club domain
  • Parse for saving the data
  • ESRI GIS for the maps
  • Twilio for the sms code auth
  • Master Card for the donations
  • IBM Watson/Bluemix to recover news about the disasters
