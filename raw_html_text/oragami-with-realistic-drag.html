Inspiration

I had a leap motion and I needed something to do with it

What it does

It presents you with a piece of paper that's a high fidelity cloth simulation of how you fold paper. You use the hand Tracker to fold the piece of paper to whatever shape you'd like then press q, grab it, then w to throw it, and a high quality air resistance simulation occurs that reflects the flight path of the shape created.
