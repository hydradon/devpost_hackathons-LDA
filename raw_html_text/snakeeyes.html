Inspiration

Keep hikers safe from snakes on the trails of Tallahassee.

What it does

Tracks the user's location and allows them to collaborate with other hikers to add pins when they spot snakes on their hike.

How we built it

Using Swift 2.0 APIs, Open Source documentation for ArcGIS mapping.

Challenges we ran into

Learning Swift on the fly; Taking on the challenge of implementing a custom map interface directly in our app.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

A working map that tracks your current location; the ability to add custom pins when the user spots a snake; A fully native app written in Swift; The ability to contact emergency personnel.

What we learned

Learned a lot about Swift and how to integrate custom maps and quality control.
