What it does

Sourceably is a comprehensive supply chain data solution that ensures standards and efficiency in reporting. Sourceably integrates with businesses reporting, production and supply chain data, and stores this information on a blockchain for security and transparency. This data can be leveraged for certification and safety compliance, analytics and metrics and for consumer-facing marketing and brand security.
