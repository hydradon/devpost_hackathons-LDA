• Features

Have you ever walked into Wegmans and thought... I need some coupons? Well what if you could open your Steps2Save app and step your way towards saving some money! Navigate Wegmans in a fun, healthy, new way with your friends and earn coupons as you shop. Imagine yourself in the ginormous Pittsford Wegmans with your shopping list ready to go! Every 300-500 steps automatically earns you a coupon towards something on your shopping list. We also inform future purchasing, alternative healthy options based on your tastes and coupons using machine learning. There's also a community option where you can compete for who walks the most steps amongst your entire community. Get healthier, save money, and bring your community together all at the same time! Food you feel good about, now with a healthier you.
