Inspiration

When you're searching for a cruise, you buy based on dates and price. But when you get off the boat, what you remember is either fun in the sun or non-stop queasiness.

Why do we not think about the weather? Well, now you can.

What it does

Search major cruise lines for dates and pricing, and correlate that with reliable weather data.

How we built it

Pour over massive amounts of data and write scripts to organise all of it.
