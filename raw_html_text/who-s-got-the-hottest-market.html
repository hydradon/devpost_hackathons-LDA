What it does

Uses commercial real estate data from 2017 to determine the real estate markets with most buying power.

How we built it

Used a software called Tableau to analyze and visualize the data. Excel file contained the data.

Challenges we ran into

Many zip codes had few data points, thus the meaning extracted for those localities may not accurately reflect that status of those localities.
