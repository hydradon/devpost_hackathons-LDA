Inspiration

The banking friend that simplifies operations and saves time.

What it does

The assistant streamlines all banking transactions and detects fraudulent actions taking necessary security measures.

How we built it

Challenges we ran into

We decided to integrate the assistant within the bank app to avoid security conflicts and scrap our idea to have a single assistant for all bank accounts of a user.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Since this was our first hackathon, we are excited to end up with a working model at the time of submission finding development issues with the idea along the way. The best part was turning our idea to reality.

What we learned

Team-work. Importance of brain-storming. Collaboration. Impact.

What's next for Eve

Eve can be a one-stop-shop for everything related to finances such as investment suggestions, bill payment reminders and scale up in terms of features along with the banking app
