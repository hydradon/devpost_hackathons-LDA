Inspiration

We seek to deliver a solution by giving our customers access to all of their Smart Home sustainability efforts all in one place. Our easy-to-use, interactive user interface guides the user through their energy-saving IoT devices, smart meters, and energy consumption data. We incentivize sustainability among Smart Home users with our system of badges and rewards and by clearly breaking down their improvement in energy cost savings over time.
