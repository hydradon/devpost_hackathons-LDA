What it does

Meal share connects food hosts with guests

Feel like cooking tonight? Set a few more plates and create an event on MealShare where people around you can view your post and buy in a spot on the table.

Don't feel like cooking? That's alright! Search through different events in your area and have the cuisine of your choice.

Cultural Impact

Learning about new cultures has never been so delicious. MealShare helps you experience different cuisines in your neighborhood and also offers an opportunity to express and share your cultural identity with those around you.
