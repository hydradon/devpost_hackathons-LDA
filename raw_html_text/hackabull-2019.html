Donate-a-bull is a forum that allows users to navigate through the site in order to find potential organizations that they would like to donate to. Ranging from a wide variety of undergraduate research ton wildlife preservation, Donate-a-bull can help find the right organization to make clean and efficient donations to. By using React.js to style and functionalize the site, we were able to create this questionnaire in order to match users with their most desired non-profit organization.
