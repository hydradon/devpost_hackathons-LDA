Motivation

Video consumption has dramatically changes media industry. Youtube and Twitch have become so popular and live streaming is on the rise. A new york times survey showed 81% watched more live video in 2016 than in 2015. Live streaming is great for viewers to communicate with the streamer via live chat. However, viewers don’t have access to the streamer’s environment or have control over the stream content. The interaction is one-way and the viewers can only watch.

What if viewers can also control what they are watching? This is the aim of our project - break the boundaries of one-way streaming by giving users the control over parts of the streamers environment and creating a highly interactive streaming experience.

Nekoh

Introducing Nekoh, an interactive live stream of our cats. (Potatoh and Gelatoh). Nekoh is a chatbot that complements the live stream. You can ask nekoh more info about our cats and you can also interact with them by sending them treats right from facebook messenger!

How we built it

  • Particle spark
  • Lambda
  • Lex

Features

  1. Ask about the cats and when is the livestream starts and link to it.
  2. Feed the cats in realtime
  3. Play with the cats in realtime
  4. Get some random info / background about the cats

What's next for Nekoh

  1. Possibly useful for animal shelter for donations.
  2. Add more interactions such as moving toys etc.
  3. Possibly useful for marketing cat cafe
  4. Possibly integrate additional sensors (check temperature if cats are fine etc)
