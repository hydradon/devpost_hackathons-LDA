Inspiration

I became frustrated with how many domains have been taken by failed startups and domain squatters looking for an easy cash out. When I discovered that ICantBelieveThisDomainIsntTaken.com wasn't already taken, I was in ecstasy.

What it does

Simply put, this website shares a reenactment of how I felt when I discovered that ICantBelieveThisDomainIsntTaken.com wasn't already taken.

How I built it

I made this website with HTML, CSS, and my iPhone's front facing camera.

Challenges I ran into
