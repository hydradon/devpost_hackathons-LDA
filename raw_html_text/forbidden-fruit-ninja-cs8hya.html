In a world, where kids are eating tide pods, one game emerges to satisfy the quench of the tasty, delictable, creaminess of Tide's laundry degergent pods.

In Forbidden Fruit Ninja you are tasked with cleaning up the streets and saving the corrupted youth from an early grave.

Slash the pods, stray away from the dirty FDA, and Unleash your true potential as a hero.
