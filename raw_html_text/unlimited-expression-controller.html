Inspiration

Existing controllers with a lot of freedoms are very expensive. I wanted to still show the musical expression, but pay way less than 500€.

What it does

It is a custom set of custom hardware and firmware which adds additional degrees of control to musical synthesizers and effects.

How I built it

I made the hardware and the firmware
