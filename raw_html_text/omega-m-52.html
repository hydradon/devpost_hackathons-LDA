Inspiration

Deeply touched by the story of our teammate, Carlos, whose mother love extends to all those dogs that have ran away from home and got lost, and to all those helpless stray dogs who can't find a warm and comfortable place to call home, we decided to create a mobile app solution to solve the problematic.

What it does

Our mobile app unites dog owners and lovers into a community of helpers. It lets owners who have lost their furry partner to make a report so that other members of the community can help to locate them. It also allows people to report dogs that are in need of a home, so that they can find a human friend and create a family.
