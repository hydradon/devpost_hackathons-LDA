Inspiration We were inspired by Buzzfeed's layout which appealed to millennials, and sought to apply a similar model to a more serious news outlet.

What it does

Connects Giphy's interface in an intuitive way with the NY Time's articles to present the reader with a more engaging platform to access vital information.
