Inspiration

Watching the residents in my building trying to decide whether or not a party/event was worth going to.

What it does

PartyMap allows users to see what events are taking place near them and displays information about the size and type of the gathering.

How I built it

This was built primarily with python, flask, and javascript and a lot of trial and error.

Challenges I ran into

A variety of errors in sections of the project. We also had difficulty combining the various elements into a complete project.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I'm proud of what we were able to create in such a short time and the opportunities for expanding the project later on.

What I learned

I learned what is required to build a web application as well as how difficult it can be to implement ideas.

What's next for PartyMap

We are planning on adding a variety of features including the clustering of people at the same event under one icon. We are also considering adding a preview feature that allows people at the event to share short video clips and share them under the icon.
