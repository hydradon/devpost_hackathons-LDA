Inspiration

The music experience for drivers is fragmented. Music is a huge part of the driving experience but it isn't used effectively for marketing itself.

What it does

Mobile app that leverages Ford SDL vehicle telemetry data and industry APIs.

How I built it

Android application

Challenges I ran into

Getting setup with some of the sponsor APIs took a lot of research and patience with help from on site mentors we were able to successful debug all issues.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I'm happy to have competed in my first Capitol Royale Hackathon I was impressed by the energy and creativity of all of the participants.

What I learned

Learned a lot about Capitol Records and how Artists and Record Labels work with one another.

What's next for Lantern

Deploying to Google play store and then the iOS store.
