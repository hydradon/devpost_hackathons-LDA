Inspiration

Our team really wanted to use the Gear VR in our hack.

What it does

Our game allows you to experience an Escape Room in VR! Maneuver around the space and solve a puzzle in order to unlock the door.

How I built it

We used Unity.

Challenges I ran into

None of us have prior experience with game development, Unity, or VR.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We created something in VR! Also learned how to use Unity.

What's next for Escape Room

Hopefully more functionality and more puzzles if we get a Gear VR for ourselves!
