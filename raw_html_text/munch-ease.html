Inspiration

I`m a Texas sized man who only eats at steakhouses. Despite our best efforts, our car ride down had a vegetarian. Where should we eat!? Where can we eat... If only we had a conveniently available mobile application built with react native and firebase to help us decide where to eat?!

What it does

Utilizing data gathered from Yelp, we can use advanced unsupervised machine learning algorithms and hard coding to give you the next generation of stomach satisfying suggestions. We aggregate all of our different tastes and picky habits by looking at our own reviews.
