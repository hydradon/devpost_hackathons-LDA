Lend-A-Space

Inspiration Parking lots are oftentimes filled to maximum occupancy at large events. We sought out to solve this problem by making it easier for vendors to deal with lack of parking availability.

Our Solution Venues hosting large events are usually around other businesses that restrict extraneous parking space to only customers of said business. We attempt to create a compromise between the event venue and the business/lot owners by creating a marketplace to rent out the vacant parking lots.
