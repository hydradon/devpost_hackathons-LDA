Inspiration

Doing research for colleges during highschool was tedious and there was no central platform to gather data on colleges.

What it does

Digitalizes a highschool guidance counselor's job and provides a platform for students to do research on colleges.

How we built it

We used reactj, python, django, mongoDB, College ScoreCard API.

Challenges we ran into

Completing the program, deciding the critera for surverying the students.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Finishing the program.

What we learned

A lot about ourselves and the real treasure are the friend's we've made along the way.

What's next for Pathway

Scaling up to have this platform avaiable for the entire US and not just for NYC.
