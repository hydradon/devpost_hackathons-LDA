• Remin makes us happy

Inspiration: 1900 bus stops accross London

What it does: add alarm to London bus travellers when the bus approaches the bus stop

How we built it: phonegap hybrid technology downloadable on android and iPhone

Challenges we ran into: 2.4 billion bus passengers, school kids, dementia patients, older gen more than 50 years old

Accomplishments that we're proud of: the remin mobile app at beta stage

What we learned: there is a huge market lies there to validate the idea that remin is useful and freely downloadable for the target audience to get a friendly iterated alarm reminder

What's next for Remin: Once the remin app is available to download on iOS and android, more free downloads will make it more popular and funding approaches will be then established.
