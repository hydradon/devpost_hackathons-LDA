Inspiration

We found sometime it is hard to assemble a furniture. So we decided to make an app to help people assembling their furnitures.

What it does

Show how to assemble a furniture with animation. Simplify the process of assembling furniture.

How we built it

We use unity and C# to build this app.

Challenges we ran into

The tutorial animation part is most challenging.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We sucessfully made the app run!

What we learned

How to use unity3d and maya.
