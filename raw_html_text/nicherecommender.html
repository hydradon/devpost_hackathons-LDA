nicheRecommender

Ideally provides niche, lesser-known places for people to visit, explore, eat at, etc.

Our minimum viable product allows you to search cities for places that aren't the popular assortment you'd find on the front pages of a Google search. By looking for establishments rated highly but reviewed infrequently, we allow users to explore and discover places they may never have considered and found themselves.

The program is built from a mix of Python and R and retrieves data using the Yelp API.
