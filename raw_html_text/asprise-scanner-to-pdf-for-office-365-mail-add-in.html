• Asprise Scan for Office 365 Outlook Mail Add-in: scan documents from scanners as PDF attachment within the browser

Inspiration

Attaching documents from scanners is tedious. There must be a better way to do it.

What it does

Scan documents directly from scanners into Office 365 Outlook without leaving the browser.

How I built it

Using JavaScript, Office 365 API (Office.js) and Asprise's scanning utility library.

Challenges I ran into

Make a functional UI.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It simplifies the scanning process and make it a much more enjoyable.

What I learned

Creating an add-in is easy.

What's next for Asprise Scanner to PDF for Office 365 Mail Add-in

Will publish it to the official Microsoft store soon.
