Available soon, “QuotEm" is a social media platform that enables you to capture fun moments by quoting the people around you. Users of the app build each other's profiles by sharing and reacting to quotes.

  • Has someone ever said something really nice to you?
  • Have you ever wanted to pass on a compliment?
  • Has anyone ever told you something so funny that you just had to share?

Well now you can share with the world Just find that person on “QuotEm" and... quote 'em!

“QuotEm" users can also interact with collections. Collections are a way to share your quote with a specific audience. Start a private collection with your friends or join your school's public collection. Adding a quote to a collection will allow your quote to reach more people.

Does your school, team, or company have a collection? Be sure to start one if not!
