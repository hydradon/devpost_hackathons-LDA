Inspiration

Most medical insurance plans do not include dental services even though poor dental health can lead to detrimental health outcomes. Furthermore, while students who are under their parents health insurance plans are covered under the age of 26, they are no longer under their parent's dental insurance at 23 years old. This can be problematic for students who decide to pursue higher education and struggle to find and pay for dental insurance.

What it does

This web application navigates and chooses through various dental plans based on the user's current needs and preferences.
