Inspiration

To help others in a seamless activity that we all do regularly.

What it does

Every link you click, create, or share helps raise money in hopes of building online curriculum, acquiring teaching facilities, and supporting local non-profits. How do we do this? Each link is monetized so that it raises a fraction of a cent per click. Each impression also is supported by donors who want to support our cause. Thousands and hopefully millions of clicks later, this can do incredible good for communities in need of STEM education.

How I built it

I built it with HTML, CSS, and PHP + of course MySQL

Challenges I ran into

Integrating an admin panel into the site to manage all links.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am proud of building the site as I did not think I could do it before. It has changed my life

What's next for WhyURL

Helping the next generation of coders, by building out an app for the site that helps do this seamlessly.
