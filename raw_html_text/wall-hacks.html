What it does

This is a very life-changing application. It is online, and you are able to battle in top-down fashion. The gameplay is simple, defeat other enemies (Your friends) with your guns, and you are able to move up through different weapons. If someone kills you though, you are reset back to the basic weapon. If you run out of ammo, your only option is to use your handy whip to kill and steal ammo from enemies. One more thing, the walls are close, and they can assist or they can hurt you! The walls are a strategic tool to be used at your disposal! Play and find out!
