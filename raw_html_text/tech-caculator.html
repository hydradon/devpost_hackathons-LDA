Inspiration: How many gadgets do you have? (computers, laptops, tablets, google home ect.) Do you know how much it all cost collectively? How valuable it all is? Well techeck.org is here to help

What it does : 1. Calculates how much all your gadgets cost. 2. Finds the prices of everything online. 3. Gives you a combined price from everything you own. 4.Displays a number for you. 5. Tells you what items are valuable and still popular.

How I built it : html5 and css3

Challenges I ran into : Coming up with the idea and getting something done between the hours of 4 and 6 am.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of : Learning a new few new things and positive feedback.

What I learned : I learned more html5 and css3
