Inspiration

The inspiration was the lack of readily-available, comparative information regarding price and nutrition of food in our area.

What it does

FoodUP offers a quick and simple way to search for and find the best food nearby that fits not only your budget but also your nutritional needs!

How we built it

Challenges we ran into

The sorting algorithm that considers multiple nutrition parameters and price was a large initial hurdle. Implementing geo-coding for the location service also proved to be difficult.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned

What's next for FoodUP

In the future we want to add support for more nutritional categories as well as dietary restrictions, such as Vegetarian and Gluten-Free items. We also plan on tailoring the individual user experience further by using height and weight data to provide more accurate daily nutritional value estimations when suggesting the best nearby options for your needs.
