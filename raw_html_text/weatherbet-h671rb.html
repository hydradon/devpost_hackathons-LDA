Inspiration

We were inspired by SportX, a crypto sports betting exchange. Since the weather is a conversational topic popular among almost all demographics, we recognized a large addressable market. We realized that the addition of crypto betting, combined with a simplistic UI, would interest existing wallet users, as well as provide an opportunity to attract new users to dApps.

What it does

WeatherBet allows users to bet on the weather with ETH.

How we built it

We built the smart contract/back-end on Solidity, built the front-end with React, used Chainlink to get weather data, and prototyped on Figma.

Challenges we ran into

Laptop battery life management difficulty.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We're proud of Christian for learning Solidity.

What we learned

Solidity.

What's next for WeatherBet
