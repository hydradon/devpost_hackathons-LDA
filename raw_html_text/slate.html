How we built it

A bulk of the project utilized Firestore technology to store and share data across multiple devices. This was done through sharing new lines over the database, then redrawing them on other device to keep them in sync. From here UI improvements were made to make user experience more satisfying.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Live updating across multiple mobile devices, having ability to collaborate with up to 4 people, creating an intuitive design, and growing our knowledge of firebase and android applications.
