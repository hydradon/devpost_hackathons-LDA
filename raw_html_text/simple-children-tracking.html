What it does

The app detects the activity and location of children's phone. The parent can view that information on their phone to ensure your children is safe.

How I built it

The main problem I need to solve when do the app is detect activity and location of user. Fortunately, it is easy with the support of the Trnql Sdk. So, the rest part is make all codes work well together.

Challenges I ran into

Time is the most challenge I ran into. The end of year is the busy time with me. I do not have enough time to make it more perfectly.

What I learned

The use of Trnql SDK.

What's next for Simple Children Tracking

New features will be added in the near future: new setup way (Bluetooth, NFC,...), children activity history,...
