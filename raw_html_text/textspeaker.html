Inspiration

When we are on our phone, we always listen to songs. We can be more productive with audio players and we decided to make a text files and images speaker. So we can have news articles and research papers played in our phones.

What it does

TextSpeaker reads images and pdf files, and then transform those to audio files.

How we built it

We used TET library and AWS polly translate text to speech.

Challenges we ran into

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned

Using different API and iOS development

What's next for textSpeaker
