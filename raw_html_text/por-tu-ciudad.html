Inspiration: The constant poblems that people face when walking on he street that can be avoided with the help of the people.

What it does: It's an app that groups the problems seen on the street and sends it to the adequate government administration to be resolved. The user wins points for each time he sends and confirms a problem.

How I built it: with help of App Inventor and Balsmiq for the visual experience.

Challenges I ran into: how to programm properly with the tolos we were using.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of: Completing the balsamiq.
