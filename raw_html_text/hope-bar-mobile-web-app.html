Inspiration - A nonprofit nutritional bar company needed help integrating its online web presence with a more user friendly and accessible application. We were inspired to help by the cause they presented of curing world hunger and improving the lifestyles of others.

How it works - The website we created puts together all of the existing websites in a clear and concise manner. We also supplemented it with an app design which further simplifies the information and donation process.

Challenges I ran into - Not knowing much web/app development. Being unfamiliar with UX and app/website design and marketing. Our text editor couldn't compile, so we couldn't make any syntax errors.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of - Making a full-fledged and operational website, creating a design and backbone for a functioning mobile application

What I learned - HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery and Invision Design Software

What's next for Hope Bar Mobile Web App - Going Global
