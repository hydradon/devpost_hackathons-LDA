Inspiration? We were inspired by conductive thread, fur, and interactive experience with clothing.

What it does? Our garments turn on lights and music through the wearer's touch

How we built it: We sewed garments and embroidered conductive thread, printed fabric, printed buttons and tied it together with incorporating the ardinuo to activate different commands

Challenges we ran into: Wiring the thread

Accomplishments that we're proud of: Learning things about different majors

What we learned: Smash Mouth has a bomb remix of All-star

What's next for Grandad's Coat? The possibilities are infinite.
