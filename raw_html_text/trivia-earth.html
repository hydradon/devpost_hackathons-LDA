Inspiration: We were largely inspired both by the impact of environmentalism in our current society, as well as the power of education. So we attempted to combine the two, and add a fun trivia twist to it.

What it does: The educational game intends to save the world one tree at a time, while helping people improve their mental math skills as well as increasing their range of knowledge.

How we built it: We programmed it using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Challenges we ran into: Attempted completely random changing questions.

Accomplishments that we're proud of: Linking the 4 pages together, overcoming the complicated formatting process, getting the game to run

What we learned: Patience + teamwork = success
