Inspiration

SFU students are suffering from terrible and erratic weather (like this weekend) on Burnaby Mountain. We do need an app to keep track of the weather on the mountain from time to time. This app will help us make plans for the up-down-mountain trip.

What it does

Our web app, Mountain Pulse , connects with some sensing cameras deployed on the mountain, taking photos and recording environmental information automatically. It will tweet pictures with temperature and humidity regularly.

We are proud of

Incorporating weather data into IoT by sensing cameras to make us build smart Burnaby Mountain.
