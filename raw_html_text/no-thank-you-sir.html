Inspiration

Have you ever been asked for a phone number and didn't want to give it out, but didn't want to hurt the other person's feelings?

What it does

Just give someone the number (415) 523-0057. Any text to this number sends back a polite reply telling the person who texted that they're not interested. If they call, the get a robot voice telling them a similar message.
