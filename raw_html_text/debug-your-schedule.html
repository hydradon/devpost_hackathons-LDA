This program is in the running for the UO/UX Hack category.

Our inspiration was to help other students organize their time in order to complete their assignments in a complete and effective way.

It was built with python and was staged with the mock up site balsamiq.

The team had to expand our knowledge of the Python language after only 3 weeks of exposure to the language. We also had to learn how to use balsamiq from scratch. It was also our first time experiencing and participating in a hackathon.

We were given the opportunity to embrace the computer science community. We made a product that runs successfully and has room to expand.

We learned how to merge collaborative work.

Hopefully, it will go on to assist every student that needs help managing their time or learning new study skills.
