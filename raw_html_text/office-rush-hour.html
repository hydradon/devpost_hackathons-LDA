Inspiration

Office hour can be long, one professor can teach about 3 courses. To help professors to handle many students at the same time, this application will help people to group together by similar questions.

What it does

Professor can create / delete office hour schedule. Students can join the line by adding topics / joining existed topics.

How we built it

We wanted a fast way to demo our worked. We decided to use Firebase for our database and Flask running on Google App Engine to quickly deploy a back-end server.

Challenges we ran into

We needed more hands. I also ran into the problem that it is the first time I register a domain on domain.com, I had trouble giving this domain to Google App Engine.
