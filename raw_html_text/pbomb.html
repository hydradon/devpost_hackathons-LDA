How it works

PBOMB is controlled by an android application using bluetooth. It receives Instructions and performs tasks based on those instructions.

Challenges I ran into

During PBOMB's creation several electronic components were broken, which required us to solder motors together ad replace PBOMB's transistors. Also, getting the arduino and android app to communicate proved difficult in the beginning.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

PBOMB is voice controlled

What I learned

We learned how to use both the appinventor and arduino IDE and about cross-platform communication.
