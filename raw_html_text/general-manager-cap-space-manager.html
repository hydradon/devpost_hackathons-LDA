Inspiration

As the NFL free agency season sits right around the corner with many trades taking place already to kick of the offseason, it was only right for me to make a cap space manager where you can manage your virtual team, add players, and use a GUI.

What it does

It simulates a general manager's office with a list of players and contract details, you can cut and add new players to the roster.

How I built it

Using Java, not a very advanced program.
