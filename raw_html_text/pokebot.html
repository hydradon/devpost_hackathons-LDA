What it does

Play's competitive pokémon matches, learning and improving its play as it goes

How we built it

We used a webdriver through python to control the online pokémon showdown interface. A neural network was implement to control our game agents moves in a match

Challenges we ran into

Finding and interacting with elements on a webpage. Figuring out whether or not our AI won a match (there is no pop up "you win" dialog box)

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Implementing a system that allows multiple AI agents to play against each other and iterate on their decision making

What we learned

Machine learning stuff and how to programatically control a webpage

What's next for Pokébot

Designing its own teams and operating outside of the ranom battle environment
