Inspiration

We are all science students who are interested in healthcare, and improving the system is beneficial for all.

What it does

Integrates health records in a singular database that can be securely accessed from any registered healthcare provider.

How we built it

Using Figma and HTML, we designed a prototype website that serves as the main site of AMI and a design prototype that represents the ideal version of the website.

Challenges we ran into

Coming up with a solid idea was our biggest roadblock as it took the most amount of time. We wanted to find a market niche to fill but could not think of one that would be easily filled.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Learning lots about HTML and Figma and being able to work with these programs with limited experience.

What we learned

How to use Figma and HTML more effectively. Also how to effectively pitch an idea or product in an a concise way.

What's next for AMI

Further development of website and eventual pitching to the government to be partnered with them.
