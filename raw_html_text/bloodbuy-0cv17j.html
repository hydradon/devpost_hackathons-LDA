Historically, the Blood industry has been extremely geographically fragmented, with blood products not moving effectively from region-to-region. This dysfunction gives rise to acute spot shortages, broad dislocations in supply and demand, and tremendous price variations of uniform blood products between, and within, U.S. regions.Bloodbuy was created to solve these issues.
