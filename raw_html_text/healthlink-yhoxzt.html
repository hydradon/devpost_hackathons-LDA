As two young women constantly surrounded by problems occurring around us, we decided to take a step back and think in simple terms. What are some problems we experience everyday that effects our life? The answer is, our health care system. For many years, physicians have struggled to connect with their patients due to a lack of communication. We have created an application: HealthLINK that will help bridge this gap and increase patient engagement in their health care experience. With a user-friendly interface, we have developed an application that is easily accessible for people of all ages. With voice commands and easy guidance, the HealthLINK application is the solution to bridging the gap.
