Our software equips Foreign Born Individuals with tools for success in the American Economy, by pairing an Individual up to an experienced Mentor who has been through the same process and is knowledgable of the challenges facing Foreign Born Individuals, and is willing to help others accomplish this challenge as well. We use a network of Advocates and Mentors to build an individualized roadmap, complete with day by day, and weekly goals in order to break this daunting task down into easily achievable goals. We start by looking at the most pressing issue for most foreign born individuals, finances, and break that down into getting a credit score, and build up to a point where they are knowledgable enough to achieve financial stability and to mentor others.
