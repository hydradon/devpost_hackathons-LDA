Ever want to follow a bunch of players at once and not want to open 12 ESPN tabs? Wanna keep track of your fantasy stats at work with Yahoo! or ESPN blocked? Go to StatMe! and watch who you want to watch, and only pay attention to who you want to pay attention to!

Our app at http://statme.org is currently replaying the Suns/Grizzlies and Nets/Jazz games from last night, so choose players from those teams and watch their stats update as if you were watching last night. Note that if a player is on the bench, their stats will naturally not update. For someone who likely has some playing time, take a look at PJ Tucker or Gordon Heyward.
