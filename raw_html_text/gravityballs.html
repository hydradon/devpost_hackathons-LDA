The Game

A multiplayer game where you can adjust your size in order to change your gravitational pull and control your movements. Play sumo against your friends on different devices and choose whether you want to challenge each other to hit the side of the ring the most or the least.

Number of players

You can have as many players as you want! Just make sure you don't press the start button until all players have loaded the page otherwise they will only be able to watch.
