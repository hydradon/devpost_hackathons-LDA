• Showing same product from E-commerce sites next to article content

Imaging you are browsing the app of your favorite fashion magazine and you love the jacket Brad Pitt is wearing or you are watching a youtube video where you like the bag that Alia Bhatt is carrying. Would it not be cool if you could get a link to the exact same product on ecommerce web-sites like Flipkart etc !

We have used artificial intelligence and computer vision to build a pilot of a solution which can empower publishers to show contextual, relevant and interesting advertising.
