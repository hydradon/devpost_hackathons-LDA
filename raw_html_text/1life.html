Inspiration

Create a something that's fun for the community...relates to road safety...and uses data about road accidents...?...Okeh... Community = Lots of people Fun = ...Fun

...hmmm, what would be enjoyable for a wide variety of people, and fun...oh wait...a game.

What it does

It's a simple game, the user holds the left or right of the screen to navigate left and right on the road (respectively) if the user crashes into an obstacle a question appears, if they answer the question wrong, they die, if the answer right, they proceed. With only one life you strive to go as far as your knowledge or skill can possibly take you...welcome to 1life.

How we built it

We used java in android SDK.
