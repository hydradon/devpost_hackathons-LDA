Inspiration: Not all tasks can be memorized. Car takes us everywhere. So if car reminds us, we cannot forget!!

How it works: Sync with calendar to display list with location and time of the appointments. User can select the appointment and car will show the route to the destination, also find a parking spot. It can share route with others.
