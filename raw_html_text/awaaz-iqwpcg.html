Inspiration

Special or disabled people have problems and really big issues to live normally and interact with normal people. Similarly, normal people don't know how to understand them.

What it does

Focusing on deaf people, AWAAZ bring solution to overcome gap between normal and special people, by eductaing both of them sign language.. And by providing online academics for special persons so that they can afford quality education.

We built an android application, using youtube apks and media player integration
