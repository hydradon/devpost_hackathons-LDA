VandyHacks

We were inspired to create this project after discussing making music accessible to people who otherwise wouldn't have a chance to play due to physical disabilities. We learned this weekend how to Code in C#, as well as skills in Unity. We faced a number of challenges like time, syncing joysticks, and exporting in VR.
