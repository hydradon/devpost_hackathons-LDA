Code Name: "Thorold"

Aimed to be a simple demonstration of a operating system that boasted having features that would benefit command-line developers, it could not be completed in time due to linker issues that could not be resolved in the alloted time, and a multitude of rewrites. Utilizes the GRUB bootloader to call a kernel written in C++.
