Wonder why Diagnozer_OO ?

Tremendous increase in the tech industry has made life simple for many but not all. There are people who still face issues catching up with the tech growth.

It becomes hard for the doctors to diagnosis certain problems of a patient without their constant monitoring of the patients activities. This application help them monitor their activities in a simple and cheastest way which helps the doctor to find some crucial insights.

What it does

Using Android apps captures the hard paper sheet which is filled out by patient and sent it to the firebase database, and from the backend its downloaded on the system and the pattern on the sheet is recognized to provide some analytics to the doctor, so that he can track the patient's activities to provide insights to the doctor.

How we built it

We divided the work into three modules, android app firebase database and fetching one part, second part analyzing data and the last part was result in the form of data visualization.

Challenges we ran into

Open CV and Text recognition, image processing, to use the distorted image and to analyze the data from that. Various symbol detection was one tough job.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Text recognition and pattern recognition putting it to firebase fetching data from the Android mobile app.

What we learned

How to detect text recognition, firebase database, NoSQL, Machine learning concepts.

What's next for DiagnozerOO

Diagnosis of larger datasets and provide it to several doctors.
