Inspiration

Sponsors are often scouting for talent at Hackathons, but it can be hard to identify jobseekers and their skills. HackathonJobs simplifies the process by allowing sponsors to see who at the hackathon is looking for work, and what their skills are.

What it does

Sponsors can meet jobseekers who are attending hackathons they have sponsored, and arrange to chat with them on the day.

How I built it

ASP.NET MVC 5 + Entity Framework + Azure

Challenges I ran into

Learning React + Parse quick enough.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I threw the final site together in 2 hours.

What I learned

At a hackathon, stick with what you know to get things done quickly

What's next for HackathonJobs

Probably nothing
