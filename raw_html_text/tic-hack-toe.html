Tic-Hack-Toe

Bored, tired, or just need a break? Play a quick game of tic tac toe against your computer in the command line!

Programmed in Haskell with :heart: at Hack the North 2015.

Usage 

$ cabal update
$ cabal install Tic-Tac-Toe
$ Tic-Tac-Toe
