Inspiration

After seeing Yodaspeak available as a free API online, we knew we had to do something with Yodaspeak. As Star Wars fans, we wanted to put a twist on apps that utilize picture recognition that is both entertaining and practical.

What it does

As you go through your day, you can take pictures of any object in the vicinity, and using picture recognition, the app will recognize the object and its surroundings, and Yoda will expertly tell you about it in his cryptic, Yoda-like ways.

How we built it

Using Android Studio and Git, we built the app from the ground up with the help of APIs (Yodaspeak, OxfordDictionary, and Clarifai).

Challenges we ran into

Integrating APIs into the program and JSON parsing

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Yoda speaking to you

What we learned

Cross-language barriers amongst APIs and IDEs are brutal, and XML

What's next for Pocket Yoda

Developing a Yoda voice and other Star Wars voices and personalities
