Inspiration

We noticed that environmental issues are becoming more prevalent now as they are all being shown through several media outlets. Therefore we decided an app would be the best way to reach out to the younger generation and our peers to inform everyone in an interactive way on how to get involved in helping the environment.

What it does

This app informs everyone in an interactive way on how to get involved in helping the environment. It includes a chatbot, games, map with volunteering opportunities, and a shop to purchase eco friendly products.

How we built it

We used multiple apis

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned
