Background

Tiger was in a lecture with 499 other people. He did not understand what the professor just said, but he did not raise his hand to ask the question. He felt very pressured to ask questions in front of those 499 people.

Never Afraid to Ask

We as a team of half-way-in university students, we deeply understand the struggles of students in lectures. Our goal is to give the students' the ability to never afraid to ask a question in their lectures!

Materials

Single Page Application built with Typescript, html and css for the professors to use during lectures which will display the questions that the students ask. Students are able to use the mobile app built with Java to send in questions for the professors. And the question will show up on the projector!

Goal

Greatly improves students' study experience. Help students' to do better in school and have a better future.

Now go on and ask.
