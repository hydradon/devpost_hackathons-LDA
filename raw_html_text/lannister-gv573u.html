Inspiration

We've started building Lannister because we needed a way to keep track of our holdings both in crypto and fiat (banks, deposits, savings, etc).

What it does

Lannister allows anyone to keep track of their holdings, follow its progress and easily analyze their distribution. It's simple to use for anyone who wants to take control of their financial life.

How we built it

We're building Lannister for iOS and web. Both platforms will be able to sync information via Blockstack's infrastructure and keep information securely stored.

Challenges we ran into

Building a financial product is a difficult challenge because we need to make sure it's secure, always up-to-date and relevant to all users. This is why we're building Lannister to be the simplest platform to manage your assets in a secure way.
