Inspiration

Cyber Bullying and online depression is a huge issue for teens in our generation where social media is so prevalent. Our team decided to find people on social media who seemed like they were being cyber bullied or depressed using sentiment analysis. Runs sentiment analysis on tweets to determine if a tweet indicates either depression or cyber bullying.

Using google cloud platform natural language processing Lots of issues with express js. training our own neural network for specifically cyber bullying. Lots about neural networks and GCP. Cleaning up UI and node.
