MangoHacks Submission: Quizlit

A Google Chrome extension which will aid students in studying by automatically presenting them with answers to questions.

It currently integrates with the Blackboard system to automatically record the answers a user chooses for his/her assessments. As more people use it, the database of answers grows and becomes more and more useful.

The Blackboard technology streamlines the process of creating tests, and ours streamlines the process of taking them!
