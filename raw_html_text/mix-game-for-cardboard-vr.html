Inspiration

Unity's breakout game tutorial

What it does

A VR breakout-like game where you need to hit the upper surface of the cube with the ball that bounces off of the paddle and walls. The ball starts its bouncing journey when you trigger the cardboard trigger. Then you need to keep the ball in play and not lose by letting the ball pass the paddle and drown in the water. The paddle is controlled by your head movement. Left, right, up and down.
