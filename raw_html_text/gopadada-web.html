Back story

I am a digital nomad and I travel a lot, to many places, often without much planning. And I like me there is a growing number of people. You might be one or know someone like me.

We want things to be easy & instant.

We don't want to waste any time in understanding how a ticket machine works, how to pay for the ticket, what ticket to get --- I am sure you will agree the user experience is rarely pleasant, unfortunately. Even if there is no complicated ticket machines, we can find strict rules, for example you have to pay a fixed amount of cash to the driver for the ticket and you find out you don't speak a common language, or you don't have enough money.

I believe this problem can be solved. We started on this road in the past day.

What it is?

GOPADADA is an easy to use mobile app where you can handle all your public or shared transportation needs in a pay-as-you-go system, using debit or credit cards.
