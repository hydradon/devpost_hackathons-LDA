Inspiration

Involved in clubs, making schedules and organizing team events is challenging and time consuming

What it does

Allows users to join and administrate teams with event boards and notifications

How we built it

We built it in a MEAN stack. I worked on backend apis in nodejs and expressjs, while my teammates worked on UI and frontend controllers.

Challenges we ran into

Multi page site seemed a bit ambitious for a short time span. Ran into issues with a lot of routes and designing and developing different pages.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Managed to get most of the functionality working while maintaining clean code, a good looking UI and secure backend

What we learned

Take better consideration of time constraints. We put too much time into being nitpicky and into planning leaving us scrambling to complete.

What's next for Tethr

Continue working to implement features which we could not reach, such as email notifications and user chat
