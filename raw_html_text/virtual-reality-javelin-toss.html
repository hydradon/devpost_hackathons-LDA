Inspiration

A couple guys met with two others at the beginning of the night. We instantly connected with our love of gaming and our enthusiasm to try VR.

What it does

A game that uses the Myo armband and oculus rift to provide a unique javelin-throwing experience.

How I built it

Unreal Engine.

Challenges I ran into

Inexperience with Unreal Engine, oculus, and myo.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We got a game going. We're very proud of what we were able to do, given that we had planned it all when the hackathon started.

What I learned

Unreal Engine programming, using the myo armband and collecting sensor data, and setting up the Oculus Rift with UE.

What's next for Virtual Reality Javelin Toss

More game modes (Hit the target, toss as hard as you can, bigger explosions)
