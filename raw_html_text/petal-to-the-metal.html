Inspiration

Late at night, in the midst of Andrew's dreams, an idea came about: dandelions in a field blowing in the wind. Upon waking, Andrew thinks, "This would make a great game." Thus, "Dandelion" was born.

What it does

It's a puzzle game based on the concept of how dandelions spread their seeds. A turn consists of pressing up, down, left, or right and any tile that has a dandelion in it will blow its seeds in that direction. However, dandelions can't grow on stones. Your goal is to fill the field in as few moves as possible.
