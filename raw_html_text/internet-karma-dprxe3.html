What it does

Internet Karma calculates a "karma score" for your internet presence. Internet Karma takes engagement data from Facebook, Twitter, GitHub, Instagram, and Reddit to provide you with the total number of likes from across all your social profiles.

Inspiration

Playing around with interesting metrics such as Reddit's Karma Score made us wonder who had the most karma on other social media, leading us to this hack.

How we built it

The backend was built with Node.js/MongoDB, with the Android client built in Java, and Pebble app built in Javascript.
