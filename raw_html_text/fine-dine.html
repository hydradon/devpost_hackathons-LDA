• Your food, your way!

Inspiration

We wanted recommended healthy full meals based on ourselves.

What it does

Recommends full meals based on age, weight, activity level, etc.

How I built it

We built it using an online IOS app developing software named Thunkable.

Challenges I ran into

The UI does not allow us to add all of the options that we wanted to such as macrotracker and filtering by food allergies.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We were able to create a visually appealing app that was user friendly with a minimalistic interface.

What I learned

App development is difficult.

What's next for Fine Dine

Filtering meals based on food allergies, dietary restrictions, restaurants around the area, compatibility with food delivery apps.
