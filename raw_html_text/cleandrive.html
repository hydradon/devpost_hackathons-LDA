Clean Drive

We live in a world with a huge pollution problem which have a very negative imapct on our daily lives. Fortunately, we got the access to different data providers which give us the most useful data to assist us to find the best solution for our tomorrow Clean Drive.

We developed an IOT Camera based soultion to be installed by the Government to detect License Plates using image processing and deep learning techniques.

On the user side we developed a Smartphone App which is linked to the Databases containing the user data and linked to the provided APIS such as AI Park and the German Government Fine Dust Sensors API.

The Use Scenario will warn users who have old cars which increase the air pollution and provide some alternatives to park for free or to take public transport. Otherwise, the user will be fined and pay this fine through his taxes.
