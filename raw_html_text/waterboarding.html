Inspiration

We want to build something we are very bad at; hardware, water and plants. Also, we want to create something every students need: a very spoiled plant that takes care of itself. Better than a pet.

What it does

The smart green house: Senses water level, humidity, temperature, light. It sends real time video of how it is doing; is it happy? Dry? Does it have a waterboarding need? The system can act on the sensor data; it will water the plant with a 3D printed waterpump and provide light when the night is dark and full of terrors.
