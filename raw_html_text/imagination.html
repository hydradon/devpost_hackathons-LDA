Inspiration

In our daily experiences, we always need apps of Map to guide us to the destination. For food fanatics, we already have the "OpenRice". For drivers, we now have the "OpenDrive". Hong Kong is a densely populated city, drivers always encounter difficulties on instant road information which include location of nearest car parks, petrol stations and meters. Our comprehensive online map is designated for Hong Kong drivers.

What it does

-mapping instant car-parking information -e-routing to meters and car park nearby, which can be sorted according to the distance and required updated fee of that parking services. -info for refilling the gas, the instant price of gas provided by the petrol stations -provide the emergency contact for drivers -info for instant road news (e.g. road block, car accidents) -user-to-user function :setting up a platform for drivers to upload some available parking space

How I built it

We write the mobile web on html and then use Github to upload it to azure for hosting.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of
