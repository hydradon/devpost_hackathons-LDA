Inspiration Our good friend Alex has always had an interest in reselling items, from starting his own sneaker/clothing resale business when he was 12, to reselling cars and electronics for friends. “The mentality of making a deal, and having the deal fall through, is an addiction.” With specialty of using various selling platforms, we will do whatever it takes to find a buyer for your item.

What it does we created an app that enables users to post items they wish to sell on various ecomerse websites so sellers can compare the best offers easily.

How We built it we used various languages to make an app and admin website to allow the sellers to sell their app. We learned various languages along the way as well as how to integrate an app with a website. We had to figure out how to create navigation, authentication, fetching from a database for posts, and more.

Challenges We ran into many coding errors and struggles.
