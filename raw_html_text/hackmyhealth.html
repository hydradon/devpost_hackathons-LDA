Inspiration

Healthcare is becoming expensive each day. In order to serve the long term and remote patients, there is not much a hospital can do other than just looking after them and having a surveillance. Wanted to solve this by reducing cost.

What it does

Monitors patients body conditions remotely and uploads to a server. The doctor when notified, diagnoses the patient condition and generates a report with which the patient knows what to do next.

How we built it

Had few medical sensors already. Just hooked them up with the famous arduino and developed a portal for the same.

Challenges we ran into

Minimizing the hardware resources

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Utilizing new platforms (syncano and processing) that offers a great service

What we learned

Programming issues, market issues

What's next for HackMyHealth

Support for more sensors and develop APIs for anyone to use this platform.
