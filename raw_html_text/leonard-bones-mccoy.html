Inspiration

  • few doctors
  • many people visit doctors for very minor issues
  • both patients and doctors spend a lot of time with issues that could've been treated at home

=> provide basic health services using a Google Home voice assistant, by giving proper tips and treatments for issues

What it does

It analyses the user's minor health issues and provides high-quality tips to help cure these issues.

How we built it

With Python, Google Cloud Platform and love.

Challenges we ran into

Getting the bot running on the Google Home and continuously deploying working versions.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Parsing the NHS page with a custom parsing approach: structuring by tags.

What I learned

Dialogflow has a V2 API, apparently.

What's next for Leonard "Bones" McCoy

Providing basic health assistant on other surfaces, e.g. the Google Assistant app.
