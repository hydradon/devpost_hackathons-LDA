Inspiration

To improve the data collected at our shores and provide a community around this crude data that will keep improving it.

What it does

It allows for collection of data and its analysis.

How I built it

An Android application is used to capture the data which is used to populate an online community of experts

Challenges I ran into

  • Lost my AWS instance.
  • Time constraints.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned

The fisher folk along the coast of Ghana are riddled with so many problems. The use of technological devices will definitely pose a challenge in a sector dominated by over 50% illiterates. By employing apps and technology that incentivize the fisher folk we will be able to use them as major data collectors to enhance our knowledge about fisheries in Ghana.

What's next for haddock

Making it a fisher folk tech staple.
