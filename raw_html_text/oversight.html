Inspiration

Easier, more well designed way to browse and keep up with current Congressional affairs.

What it does

Oversight pulls data from the Sunlight Foundation's Congress API, and presents it through Material Design. Oversight also allows users to connect with others over Twitter by using a shared hashtag relating to each bill.
