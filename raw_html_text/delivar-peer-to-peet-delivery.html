Inspiration

Not every shop offers deliveries. Sometimes, we're just too lazy or busy to go out to buy the goods that we strongly desire of.

How it works

  1. Post your order on our website detailing the purchase location and your home address.
  2. Someone who is travelling home logs into our app and see your order which matches his route will be able to help you out, with small tips.

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I'm proud of
