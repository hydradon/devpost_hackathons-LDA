Inspiration

Ever forget to log how much water you drank, or your weight? Well now you can log through your voice

What it does

It allows you to log for your fitbit through your voice, as well as use the chrome extension to add a recipe's nutrition on AllRecipes as a custom food on your fitbit account

How I built it

It's a chrome extension and Dialogflow nodejs app

Challenges I ran into

Chrome Extensions are weird, grabbing the page source and importing jQuery wasn't traditional.
