What it does

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Business management, Strong technical background, best android and web developers, learners and self-motivated.

What we learned

We learned to solve practical problems, critical thinking, establish and grow business along with technical skills in our field of interest.

What's next for ForiTaleem

We are planning to deploy it on national and international level with help of authorities to make it accessible for every student especially in rural areas by which every child can be educated with best quality in minimal cost.
