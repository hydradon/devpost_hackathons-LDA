Inspiration

Online shopping often leads consumers to mindlessly purchase products. This behavior leads us to increase our consumption, leading to us harming our environment.

What it does

Our project is a Google plug-in that recommends more eco-friendly products to consumers when they are shopping online. The pop-up displays the top three alternative products ranked from the most eco-friendly to the least.

How we built it

We used HTML to code the plug-in and used google extensions.

What's next for ECOnsumer

Allowing the program to scan the web for alternative products and then comparing them to their companies' eco-friendliness scores.
