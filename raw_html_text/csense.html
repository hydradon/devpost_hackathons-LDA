Inspiration

Our eyes hurt when we look at screens all day. That's not good. Let's do something about it.

LEDVANCE supplies us with the means to establish human-centric lighting in the working place.

What it does

The color of the ambient light will adjust to the main color of the page your web browser is currently displaying, so that browsing websites at night becomes even more fun.

How we built it

We developed a fancy chrome extension.

Challenges we ran into

Resetting the LEDVANCE smart lights. CORS.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Non-linear lightning. Usability. Cool idea.

What we learned

Making a Chrome extension, some REST, some JavaScript, to bring mouse and keyboard even when you have a laptop with you.

What's next for CSense

Adaptive Christmas lights that learn about your web browsing habits. Share your lightning ideas with your friends and family.
