Inspiration

Ethnic and racial tensions is something we still see today. Specially in major multicultural cities like Miami, we must ensure that everyone in the city has the chance to share and be part of the resources fairly.

Sometimes this type of tension comes from something as simple as a language barrier, leaving people with a fear of the unknown and therefore lack of understanding of other cultures.

This project takes from our experiences in a community that may not be as close as desired. This is our way to help solve the problem.

What it does

An App that allows people to ask questions for the community to answer.

Auto translates according to the language selected at sign up.

Everyone is free to ask, share and respond in their own language and everyone will be able to understand.
