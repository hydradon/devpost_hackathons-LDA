Inspiration

Residential windows are dumb, don't connect to the internet, and are a massively overlooked part of energy efficiency and home comfort.

What it does

Measures the value proposition of using windows to passively control temperature in the house.

How we built it

Python if statements and coffee.

Challenges we ran into

Finding good temperature data and making the window behavior realistic.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The UX/UI design of the app is really slick and simple, and the value proposition of passively controlling temperature is real.

What we learned

How to do things quickly with purpose.

What's next for BreezeBoyz

Keep working on the device and app!
