Inspiration

There's a dearth of scientific rigor applied to quality assurance in solar installations.

What it does

SunKaizen uses data science to find millions of dollars in savings for solar installation companies through quality improvement.

How we built it

Using a design-oriented approach and mixing it with data science, our team combined a variety of tools to produce a proof of concept.

Challenges we ran into

We did not want to shortchange our product and built a lot.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Building a functional and aesthetically pleasing product in an exceptionally short period of time.

What we learned

There is a lot of money to be saved in solar through appropriate quality improvement.

What's next for SunKaizen
