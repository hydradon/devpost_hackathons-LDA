Inspiration

Stylicious was brought to life by a desire to improve the self-confidence and style of men across the world.

What it does

Stylicious serves as your go-to source for pro style guides and fashion tips tailored to you. Our carefully curated list of hair, fashion, and style guides show you just what you need to build your personal brand. With Stylicious, you can take your first steps to a more stylish you.

How I built it

Stylicious is built from scratch using HTML5 and CSS.

Challenges I ran into

The greatest challenge faced to bring Stylicious to life was the hosting of the site.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Stylicious is built completely from scratch and supports information for all body and hair types.

What I learned

Team collaboration, web hosting, HTML5 and CSS.

What's next for Stylicious

We are looking to expand our information base to include women’s fashionwear and style.
