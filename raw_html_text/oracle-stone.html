Inspiration

We wanted to help students and their respective colleges with building schedules. College requires a lot of time and resources, so anything that can let us focus on studies more is a big plus.

What it does

Oracle Stone, our schedule building program, simplifies the schedule building process by showing a major's required classes. They then pick the classes they want to take based on their needs and it creates a schedule for them.

How we built it

Using PHP, with some CSS, Oracle Stone is a 4-page website.

Challenges we ran into

The strain of time made us panic a lot more than we needed to. However, we came together and pulled through in the end.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Learning PHP and CSS together as a team. Originally, there was one person on our team who had light experience with PHP; now, every member of our team has better learned both languages.

What we learned

We learned how to overcome obstacles as a team. Each member had their own strengths that helped teach the other's to overcome his weaknesses.

What's next for Oracle Stone

We want to automate the schedule building program to where it will only take the classes they want, and the program will automatically build a schedule for you. It will also be editable to fit their preferences.
