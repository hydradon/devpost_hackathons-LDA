Inspiration

Our idea started from a problem. 30% of patients don't show up to their medical appointments.

The current solution to this problem is just sending an SMS reminder to patients, however, this method costs money but is not producing any results.

We found a dataset of 300K medical appointment records containing information about the patient showing up and the variables involved. So, we decided to analyze this data and to find some useful insights and lead us to a better solution.
