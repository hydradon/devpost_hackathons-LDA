Optime

We're students, and we also love hacking, hanging out, and do many things. It's difficult for us to manage all of our time. This is why we created Optime , a tool to Optimize your Time. The app is based on the Last Planner managing tool for projects. You can add your tasks, set the time and the day you want to do it. The app will organize it in order to reach all your goals at the end of the week. You'll be able to change the tasks to another day if you couldn't finish it, and provide a feedback.

It has also integration with Google Home!
