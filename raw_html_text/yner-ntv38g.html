yner.net

We believe that podcasts shouldn’t be valued on size but on the quality of content.

Therefor, we want to empower all podcasts to get sponsorship.

Imagine you want to feature your amazing but very specialized products you developed this weekend on podcasts and leverage the strong community behind small niche podcasts. You would spend a lot of time searching, addressing and bargaining with many different podcast hosts. But imagine it would be as easy as clicking once/ one click to advertise on a variety of perfectly fitting podcasts for your product.

That’s why we build http://yner.net/ .

yner is a platform that matches advertisers with suitable podcasts. How do we do that?

  1. We connect both – podcasts and advertisers – on ONE platform
  2. We identify key characteristics of podcasts and bundle those with the same target audience
  3. We match you as the advertiser with the best combination of podcasts according to your product. To do so, we use an AI-based technology.

Our technology

  • Extracting keywords from company website to search for matching podcasts
  • we use python and angular
  • Outlook: ML to deeply understand companies & podcasts profiles https://github.com/gschelter/slash-19-backend
