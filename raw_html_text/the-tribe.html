What it does

Captures employees questions with Google home, posts them to the company slack channel. The questions and any responses are written to a wiki page. Answers will be spoken back to the asker when they are answered, a

How we built it

Node express server hosted on firebase functions. Firestore to store questions and answers. A google home mini skill to capture questions and speak responses. Angular to display questions and answers as a wiki.

Challenges we ran into

Slack API, Google home skill, angular routing
