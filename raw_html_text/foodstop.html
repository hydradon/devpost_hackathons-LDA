Inspiration

We decided that searching for food in specific locations was inefficient for those on long road trips or those who don't know the area well enough, so we built an app that does it for you.

What it does

It takes a starting location and an ending location and creates food suggestions that are on the way from start to end.

How I built it

We built this app using Meteor, javascript, MongoDB, html, css, polymer, the Yelp API, and the Google API .

Challenges I ran into

Ran into challenges with staying focused even when deprived of sleep.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Learning new frameworks and APIs and being able to hack something together with it in under 36 hours

What I learned

Learned how to work as a team and new frameworks.
