Inspiration

We wanted to create a way for folks to get the beer they want, as soon as possible!

What it does

You just order the beer you want and we handle the rest! Your order will be sent to a local driver who will deliver your beer to you, as long as you are 21 or over.

How we built it

We used Flutter for the front-end and Firebase for the back-end

Challenges we ran into

Getting flutter to work properly, implementing APIs.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Learning Flutter, getting our back-end to work properly.

What we learned

Flutter, Firebase, and Dart.

What's next for Beer Run

Expansion into other types of alcohol delivered. Partnerships with local stores and BevMo.
