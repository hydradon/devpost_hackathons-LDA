Inspiration

It's lunchtime, you're starving , and you got 45 minutes until your next meeting. With LunchCrunch, planning your lunch break has never been easier or more time-efficient!

What it does

LunchCrunch evaluates all of the popular lunch spots nearby and shows them to you with a estimated wait time and projected busyness. From there, you can determine what fits into your schedule!

How we built it

LunchCrunch is an iOS app using the Flask framework and sourcing it's data from the NCR API.
