Inspiration

Thought the hackathon was a cool idea and I wanted to put out a utility people could use to earn Bitcoin, without permission.

I always thought Earn.com was is a pretty cool idea. It's certainly validated in the market, but it's a permissioned platform owned by Coinbase :vomit:

We can do better.

What it does

It's a paywall for your Inbox.

  1. Set a mailbox filter for topics/senders you want to Paywall
  2. Charged Mail will auto-respond with a lightning invoice, bitcoin address and a custom message.
  3. Once the paywall is satisfied, the message will be marked as paid and delivered to your Inbox.

What's next for Charged Mail

If there's sufficient interest, we'll provide this as a hosted service and add support for other mail servers.
