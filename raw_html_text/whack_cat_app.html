At Wellesley, we often focus on academics and forget to prioritize our health. But it is important to care for yourself! Our app encourages users to engage in self-care by combining fitness and health with adorable virtual cats. Go to sleep with Catnap! Catnap is a pebble app that counts the user’s hours slept, steps taken, and reminds you to hydrate. Enjoy a restful night with your furry companion as your cat falls asleep and wakes up with you the next day. Enjoy a nice Wellesley power walk as your cat walks by your side and even reminds you to stay hydrated. As you take care of yourself, you are also taking care of your new kitten! As you sleep, hydrate, and exercise, take care of yourself AND your cat!
