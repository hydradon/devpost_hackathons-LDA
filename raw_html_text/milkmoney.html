Inspiration

To bring hope and to make a difference in improving the quality of life with investment in love and kindness. To build a cross platform mobile application to make it more easy for the volunteers/donors/company.

What it does

Provide a cross platform mobile application which maps the volunteers with opportunities/events. It sends notifications on new events. It alerts the users based on their interests. It allows a company to partner with an event. It allows a program partner to collaborate with milkmoney and post an event Allows Milkmoney organization to help keep track of volunteers/donors for each event
