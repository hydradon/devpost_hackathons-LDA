Inspiration

What can we do with the sensors available? Build a sensor for a car-- bike, we have bikes available.

What it does

It sends acclerometer, temp and light data via wifi to a server, which is then processed to velocity and displacement and displayed on graphs. This data is displayed on a website to be viewed by the cyclist. Also some decisions are made to show the user whether or not to have their lights on.
