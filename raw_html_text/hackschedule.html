Inspiration...

Registering for classes at USC is currently a grueling, inefficient process. After spending countless hours rummaging through a mess of RateMyProfessor, Calendar View, Coursebin, and scheduling advisement, you end up with an ugly, sporadic schedule that FINALY fits the bare requirements for the upcoming semester. USC itself recognized this issue last year, and hosted a competition to ameliorate the scheduling mess. However, its product runs into the same core issues, albeit with bigger buttons and a prettier schedule display. Many students, including myself, still use the old platform because the improvements are minor and actually make certain features harder to use. It’s time for real change.
