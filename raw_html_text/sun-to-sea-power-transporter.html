Inspiration

Inspired by the current electricity struggle in Puerto Rico, we want to provide electricity to affected areas quick and efficiently.

What it does

The Space to Sea Power Transmission system takes solar energy and uses it to provide power to victims in coastal cities after natural disasters.

What's next for the Space to Sea Power Transmission

Going further, we plan on finding more ways to help survivors of natural disasters. This system can be adapted to work for inland situations for survivors of tornadoes and other land based disasters.
