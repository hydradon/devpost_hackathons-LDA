Inspiration

We wanted to create a way to reduce healthcare costs to make healthcare affordable for the less fortunate.

What it does

It allows users to get connected with doctors in third world or developing countries. This allows the patient to get treatment through doctors in places where living costs may be lower, thus making fees for the service cheaper. Due to our platform, the doctors would also have competitive prices to achieve most number of patients.

The patients will also be able to leave reviews and comments on the service the doctor provides.

How we built it

Our back end uses Go and Firebase. Our front end uses Angular and HTML/CSS.

Challenges we ran into

Coming up with the idea, getting our front end completed.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Going to the zoo.

What we learned

Always use node.js and make sure to recruit a UI/UX team member.
