What it does

Shakespearean poetry is hard to write. This tool will aid you in your endeavor to write rhythmic and interesting poetry.

How it was built

We used Shakespeare's sonnets and broke down the words into its part of speech, syllables and phonics to train the model. Using the model, we used a webapp to allow users to input words that the model can read and provide top results that match the rhythm and format of a poem.

Challenges

Gathering enough training data, merging backend with frontend.
