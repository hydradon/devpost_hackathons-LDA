Inspiration

We wanted to create a way to help immigrants become more integrated into their communities

What it does

Pairs immigrants with locals that speak the same language. Immigrants can request help and recommendations for everyday tasks such as: Grocery shopping, booking appointments, getting around, and even interpretive services.
