THINKTogether Problem: All employees must log into many different sites to get the information they need to run after school programs. Site coordinators must print out daily curriculum for Program leaders. This process is inefficient, expense and bad the the environment.

Proposal: Create a portal that integrates all of the necessary resources in one location, accessible with one login. This will save time and money, helping THINK Together to do what they do best-- educate and inspire students!

Inspiration The programs are inspirational themselves- this organization does amazing work for kids in California.
