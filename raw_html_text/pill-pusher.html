Inspiration

According to the Center for Disease Control and Prevention 32.5% of American's are actively prescribed to three or more prescriptions at one time. Keeping track of when to take which pill and when to refill can become confusing and frustrating for patients. In addition to this

What it does

We wanted to simplify this process by giving them a centralized platform to get their medications from that would know when and which medication they would need to take and would alert them via push message and login. The app also communicates with the patients doctor and has a place to note side effects of medication, schedule appointments , etc. Additionally, a security feature would be implemented via fingerprint scanner (if feature is wanted) to make medication difficult to steal/spill.
