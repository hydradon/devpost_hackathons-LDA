Inspiration

We want to combine wearables with real versatility (not just watches/bands/necklaces) and allow users to feel safer.

What it does

Provi is the companion mobile app for a wearable device that allows you to quickly ping your friends and family at the push of a button when you feel unsafe.

How we built it

Our team's very first iOS experience! Because we know most of our target audience owns iPhones, we built Provi with Swift and Xcode. Next step is to integrate it with a bluetooth device!

Challenges we ran into

Setting up a Twilio texting function, learning curve for iOS/Swift

Accomplishments that we're proud of

  • now more people can feel safer while on-the-go
  • we can start to democratize wearables even more!
