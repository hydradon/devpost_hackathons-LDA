BLOW is a survival game.

Ball are dropping from the top, use the fans to blow them to the right basket.

Three kinds of balls are there:

1) Colored Balls, put same colored balls to the same colored basket. 2) All Colored Balls, these balls are changing their color constantly, you can put those balls into any basket you want. 3) Cleaning Balls, Big blue flashing balls are Cleaning balls. What they do is they clean the basket in which they fall. Use them to manage your basket space.

Its on you how you manage them. With time it will be more about focus and fast reaction because balls will drop faster and move faster when blown.

Don't underestimate the power of BLOW.
