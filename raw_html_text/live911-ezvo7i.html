Inspiration - The current state of safety in the United States.

What it does - Emergency button then chat with dispatcher at safetrek to report the authorities. App asks for Room Number and Building Name to provide live incident reporting to peers.

How I built it - In android studio with safetrek api and google firebase for databasing live alerts.

Challenges I ran into - having two laptops not working.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of - getting this done in less than 6 hours.

What I learned - patience.

What's next for Live911 - Prank Prevention and Image recognition.
