What it does

You can talk to and use gesture to control the mirror to provide useful information, such as weather, calendar, directions, games, and a great Easter egg.

How we built it

We integrated AWS Lex for voice recognition and natural language processing, leap motion for touchless gesture input, and many many APIs for additional information.
