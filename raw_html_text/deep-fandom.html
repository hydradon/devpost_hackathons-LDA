Inspiration

Improve interaction between artists and fans.

What it does

It learns the Soundcloud comments for 10000 hot tracks and predicts what YOUR track could get as comments and where they are positioned on the waveform.

How we built it

Deep learning in python with Theano and Keras, visualisation using d3 with flask backend. We crawled the Soundcloud API.
