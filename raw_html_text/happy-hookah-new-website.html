Inspiration

We were tasked to create a new site for the Happy Hookah business with a very short amount of time before the deadline. The process was arduous, but we persevered and created the skeleton of a (semi) functional site!

What it does

It has lots of helpful information about a local business here in Atlanta. You can find all the important details about Happy Hookah on this site.

How we built it

We built it with Atom.io and lots of patience. It was an entirely new experience for all of us and on such a short frame of time to build the website, it was a fast-paced adventure.

Challenges we ran into

The timeframe was definitely constricting and prevented us from finishing the project to match our standards. However, with very little experience between all of us, what we accomplished is satisfactory.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Considering the severe lack of experience we all have, it is an accomplishment we have a site at all. The programming was tough, but the team-bonding we experienced is truly irreplaceable.

What we learned

We all learned a considerable amount of html coding and the Atom.io text editor. We learned to work as a team and find alternative solutions to seemingly impossible problems.

What's next for Happy Hookah New Website

We hope to refine the site to a point where it is acceptable to publish online for the Happy Hookah business.
