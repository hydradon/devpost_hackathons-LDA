Inspiration

Volunteers at UNHCR receive thousands of emails each day from people who want to help.

Challenge given

Utilize AWS to enhance the UNHCR website with the ability to complete two-way communications that inspires interest, promotes empathy and allows Americans to more easily engage on refugee issues.

Product

We used AWS lex bot to create a Chatbot to add to the existing UNHCR website and for Alexa.
