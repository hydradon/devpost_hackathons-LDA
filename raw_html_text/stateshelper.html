Inspiration: I really saw technology develop and I could see myself programming and make the world better. Therefore I participated in this challenge as a stepping stone for the future

How I built it: I spent many hours during my vacation to finish this code. Additionally, I used google as my resource if there was a problem I had to face

What I learned: While making this code, I learned so many state animals, as well as popular state landmarks. Also, I learned that I can do anything if I put my heart into it.

What's next for StatesHelper: In the future, StatesHelper will be made as a quiz in order to make it more interactive.
