Ritual.

What it does

Allows users to order items at their favourite restaurants through text message.

How we built it

StdLib and MessageBird for texting, Flask and Postgres for the creation of users and restaurants, Vue for the frontend.

Challenges we ran into

Node.js and firebase.... and Node.js

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Being able to change the food industry.

What we learned

StdLib and MessageBird, Node.js

What's next for Munch

Allow payment through text message.
