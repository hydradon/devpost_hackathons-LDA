Inspiration

People would like to send out newsletters

What it does

Open the app, compile your newsletter by selecting categories and then share the newsletter through Email, Facebook or Twitter. When you select the categories, the newsletter is created at a web address and contains the top three posts of the categories you chose. It updates content automatically from RSS feeds

How we built it

We built the app on android studio and used Microsoft Azure as our backend. We used the Facebook and twitter API to share the newsletter on the two social media platform.

Challenges we ran into

Lots of errors in Android studio with integrating libraries

Accomplishments that we are proud of

Solving the challenges by Googling them

What we learned

We learned how to use the Microsoft Azure platform to host the backend of our app

What's next for Letters

We plan on refining the project and releasing it on the App store
