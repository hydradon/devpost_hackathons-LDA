InfojobsHackupc

For this challange, the proposal was:

Build your perfect job seeker. We encourage you to propose any change or improvement in our registration process, CV, job search... In other words, everything related to have a more suitable InfoJobs for you and your needs.

Our proposition consist in how to make easier, faster and less boring the choice of offers.

Our main idea is to bring to the user the nearest experience on job searching, that means, less thinking, easy recognition of interesting offers for you, and most important demystifying that looking for work is hard.

That's why we always will show you how interesting/related this offer is for you!

With you CV and the different offers, you will not search for a job, a job will be offered to you, which is totally different concept.
