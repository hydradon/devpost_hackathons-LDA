Inspiration

Often elderly are prone to sudden medical catastrophes all on their own. Existing products put the responsibility of sending an alert on the victim, who may not always be in a condition to respond. OneStep is an easy-to-use app that notifies an emergency contact of potentially dangerous behavior and allows for prompt responses in cases of emergency.

What it does

Keeps track of activity level using phone's pedometer and sends periodic text message alerts (including location) to emergency contacts if the desired activity is not maintained for a specified period of time. If prolonged inactivity is observed, then a phone call is automatically placed.
