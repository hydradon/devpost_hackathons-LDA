What it is

Unacceptable is the latest in fitness technology. Unacceptable ensures you offset your guilty pleasures by prescribing exercise. Unsure of whether or not you should have that last slice of cake? Not a problem! Unacceptable will make you do penance in the form of exercise.

What it does

The steps are simple:

  1. Select your guilty pleasure. Ice cream? No problem. Cinnamon buns? We gotchu. Beer? Yup, that too.
  2. Choose an activity, such as running or cycling.
  3. We will tell you exactly for how long you need to exercise!
  4. On top of that, we will suggest a nearby park to jog or a local route to ride.
  5. Get on it!
  6. Receive an "Acceptable" rating from Vladimir Putin
