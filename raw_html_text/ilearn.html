Inspiration - Due to many taboos and myths prevailing in our society, people are not able to discuss openly about some important issues, such as Sex related Issues. Also many citizens of our country are not aware of basic rights they posses.

What it does - Our application aims to create awareness among its users about above mentioned issues.

How I built it - Using Android Studio as platform, Java for backend and XML for UI.

Challenges I ran into - To incorporate all our ideas an brains in one application was a bit challenging.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of - I have made several easy-to-use, user interactive android apps which solves certain basic problems.

What I learned - I learned how in today's world we are just a click away from the solution of the most difficult of problems, and how i can build that solution.

What's next for ILearn - Our Application comes with regular updates. With every update of the application it becomes better and more interactive than before.

Future update of our app plans to introduce Notification feature, wherein users can register themselves on the app to get notified of the various events related to our issues. The users can also continue as a guest if they do not wish for regular notifications.
