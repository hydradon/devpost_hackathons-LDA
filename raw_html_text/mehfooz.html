Inspiration

Our inspiration is life saving and humanitarian work. The real use case is Rescue 1122 and other emergency services.

What it does

It minimizes the response time based on locational intelligence of caller and ambulance.

How I built it

 Web Application is developed in Laravel for control room monitoring  Android application is developed for ambulance drivers  Tracking of vehicles through already installed TPL trackers in the vehicles

Challenges I ran into

The main challenge was to convince the Rescue 1122 authorities to accept this system. At the moment, they are fully convinced and ready to implement the system.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Convinced the District Emergency Officer to implement this system

What I learned

The government way of doing things

What's next for Minimizing Emergency Response Time?

Scalability to other district and provinces
