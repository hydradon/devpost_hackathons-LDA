Inspiration

Raybent was born from the boredom of the exam period. The game idea was firstly a mental exercise which later grew into a proof of concept and is now being converted into a fully fledged game.

How it works

You manipulate blocks on the board to bend and contort the lasers to satisfy all goals.

Challenges I ran into
