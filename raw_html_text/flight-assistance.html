We were inspired by American Airlines' challenge in making a flight easier and efficient. In order to communicate with attendants on a flight, you have to press the call button so we build a program that is a digital chat server between the passengers and flight attendants. The passengers first enter their seat number and proceed to make selections of what kind of assistance they need. In our case, we are primarily focusing on drinks and food so the customers give their seat number and what they want and the flight attendant could just get that and arrive at the seat. It takes less time and is more efficient. We had a hard time implementing databases as we are new to coding so we did not have much experience going into it however, we tried our best to make the chat server.
