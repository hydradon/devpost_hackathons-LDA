This solution dedicated to solving the current discrepancies in the operational and tracking methodology of the wildlife enforcers and the respective government. This solution aims at bifurcating the elephants who are vulnerable to trafficking from the ones who are not. These solution will also digitalize the entire tracking and identifying framework and will remove any room for unethical processes in the system.
