Inspiration

We found ourselves inspired to attempt to fix the frustrating and fruitless experience of mobile dating apps.

How it works

The idea seems simple - but yet its eluded us until now. You sign into this dating app by taking a photo - your face is your password. There is no Facebook sign in, no old mysterious photos, no stale data, no sex bots, and most importantly - no more catfish. Just a nice live stream of people's true faces near you.
