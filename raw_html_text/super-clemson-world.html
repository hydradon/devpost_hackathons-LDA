Inspiration

We just wanted to create something fun and interactive as well as learn new skills while we tried to survive staying awake for 23 hours

What it does

You play as the Clemson Tiger while you dodge school mascots, facing Nick Saban and the Roll Tide in the final level all while trying to please Godly Dabo!

How we built it

We used pygame and python

Challenges we ran into

Making our character jump as well as registering collisions with enemy characters!

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The whole game! Both of us came in with no Python experience and we created something to show our friends and family, as well as the judges!

What we learned

How to handle frustrating errors and lack of sleep/motivation, as well as general programming concepts with Python and pygame
