Inspiration

This idea came to us after our trip to Miami over winter break where, whether it be for the hotel or the bike rentals, everywhere we went someone ended up paying for the group to make things easier. It became complicated keeping track of everything and seemed inefficient making so many Venmo requests.

What it does

Our Android App allows users to input their expenses throughout the duration of their trip and, afterwords, be able to resolve all their "I owe you's" with one button. Rather than keeping track of all the receipts or constantly making charges and payments on Venmo, Split It will do all the work for you.

How we built it

We built our application for Android and used Firebase as our backend, allowing us to communicate via devices and login via Facebook.

What's next for Split It

Hopefully we'll be able to finish polishing the app and use it over Spring Break :)
