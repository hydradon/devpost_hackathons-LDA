Inspiration

Based on popular peer-to-peer payment and communication apps, we wanted to shortcut part of the Bill.com workflow to connect two users quickly.

What it does

This app encodes a contact (Name, phone, email) filled out on a customer's device into a QR code for the vendor to scan.

How we built it

Our iOS app was built on the Objective-C framework in Xcode, using a CocoaPod to enable the Bill.com HTTPS API.
