Inspiration

https://twitter.com/eiaine/status/965022911701557248?lang=en

@elaine video was the inspiration to make the project. I wanted to learn how to control a motor via a raspberry pi. And I wanted a way to view chickens and give them a snack without having to go outside

Learned

  • Control a motor with a raspberry pi
  • Live streaming videos
  • Accept LN payments, and keep DB in sync

Challenges

  • stream video w/ slow internet connection
  • getting motor to dispense proper amount of food
  • securing dispenser from animals
