Inspiration

Baseball scorecards, invented by Henry Chadwick, are a staple of baseball tradition with intricate interfaces. Our desktop clients bring order to this, with the richness of a native desktop client.

What it does

Logs the state of every play in a baseball game.

What's next for Chadwick

Test interface with actual games. Android and iOS versions. Actually implementing the Javascript/Firebase syncing part.
