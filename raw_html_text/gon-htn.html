gon - Hack the North 2015

gon, a play on words for "go on" and "gone", is a useful Android application for event--planning! gon utilizes the Facebook API for easy login and importing of upcoming Facebook and personal events. It strives to offer recommendations for things to bring to your next event, making event/travel planning an easier and more streamlined process.
