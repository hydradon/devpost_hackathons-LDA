foodClues

We want to help individuals find local EBT-accepting establishments. We built an SMS platform to enable those with "dumb" phones find where their next meal comes from, and web platform allows people to find nearest location via public computer (i.e. library, coffee shops with public desktops). Hoping to expand services to include mental health and legal clinics, ultimately focusing on integrating recently incarcerated folks back into society.
