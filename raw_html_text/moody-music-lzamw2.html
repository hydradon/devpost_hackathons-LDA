• MoodCat, Mascot for Moodisic™

Inspiration

I have always wanted my device to play a song/music for me, and an app will choose the song/music for me that is relative to my current state of my mood. In this way, I don't have to choose a song/music. For example, it will feel like a movie in a scene where there would be a heroic victory, and an appropriate song/music will be played in the background. Essentially, I just wanted a song or music to be played depending on my mood or the environment/atmosphere around me, but the song/music has to be very fitting and accurate toward my mood or surrounding environment/atmosphere.

What it does

This is an app that plays a song that is relative to your current mood. There is a list of option of moods to choose from, and when you choose your mood, a song plays.

How we built it

This hack was made on android studio with the help of some mentors.

Challenges we ran into

Time and finding ways to implement ideas

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What's next for Moodisic

We can add custom tags, the addition of facial recognition, and a database of songs, the ability to switch songs from your song library. Also, we can add this into muse head bands.
