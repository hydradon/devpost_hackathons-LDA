Inspiration

I didn't get to grow up with my parents so what if I can have a bigger role in their lives by sending them animated drawings I made as a kid?

What it does

Currently, only sends snapshots of the pictures to parents; however, once Lottie can fix a pod conflict it can send animations too!

How I built it

Swift, RxSwift

Challenges I ran into

RxSwift is rough. Swift drawing is rough. Distinguishing separate "characters" was rough.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

App is fully set up and complete and can easily be done with animation. I have the json ready to do the animation.

What I learned

Cool animations and drawing on swift.

What's next for DokiDoki

Fully integrate with Lottie API.
