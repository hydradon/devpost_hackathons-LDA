Inspiration

To help others and make their lives safe and more secure #makeadifference

What it does

Ignis is a device which helps you and your neighbour to be alerted in case of a fire. This device also keeps you up to date if your house temperature has increase. Also it will notify you and the Emergency Services using our specialized app.

Challenges we ran into

With our code, and temperature sensor, and creativity to innovation were all the skills we need.

What we got from this hackathon

We learned about creating and solving the problem, and that too with a great group and a positive attitude. We developed upon several new skills, and meeting new people were the things we could take away from here.

For next time

New app and better-integrated app, with more sensitive sensors.
