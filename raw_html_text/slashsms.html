SlashSMS

Send SMS from Slack using Nexmo. Add SlashSMS to your Slack team and start using the /sms command.

Type /sms +1-202-555-1234 Hi, how are you doing? to send SMS to a phone number.

Type /sms @username Hi, how are you doing? to send SMS to a user, provided they have updated their profile with a phone number.

If you don't have a Slack team or don't want to add SlashSMS to your Slack team just yet, click here to get automatically invited to the SlashSMS Slack team, where you can say hello to me and test the command.
