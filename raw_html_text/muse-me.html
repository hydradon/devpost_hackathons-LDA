Inspiration

Often you go to clubs or bars and the music just isn't what you are looking for. Our app will find the place to go, based on what kind of music you like to hear.

What it does

What the MVP would do is, based on your Spotify liikes, provide you with the nearest location where they play your favourite music. If you prefer, you can specify a song or artist particular and the app will give you a list of locations where they play your favourite music.

How we built it

It's a web-app with Node-Express in the back-end and a regular HTML/CSS/JS/Boostrap in the front-end

Challenges we ran into

We were not familiar with either the touchtunes or spotify apis.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Managing in such a short time to learn two new APIs and strap together a functional web application.

What we learned

How to work with the Spotify API and the Touchtunes API

What's next for Muse Me

Convert all the hardcoded stuff into an actual fully-functional app and add any additional features deemed necessary for an MVP.
