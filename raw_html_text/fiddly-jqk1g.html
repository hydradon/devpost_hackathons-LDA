Fiddly happens to be one of the nifty little tricks that every web developer has up his sleeves to make his life easier & make himself look like a wizard. Packaged as a simple single click app, fiddly helps you to remove/edit a page & persist the changes until your next visit.

About Fiddly : Have you ever wanted to hide a particular section of a website, forever? Did you ever wish to edit a part of the page before taking a print out?

All this was always possible for developers from the dev toolbar. But for everyone else, there is Fiddly. Click the Fiddly Icon & start fiddling with the page.

Fiddly as an Ad Blocker : Traditional Ad blockers try & remove the advertisements from loading from the source itself. Although this saves very little of your bandwidth, but creates a major loss for the websites & publishers. Using fiddly, you can choose to remove the ad containers whenever you want to.
