Inspiration

Stocks reacts very quickly to news nowadays, so we want to inform investors with live updates on news that might have a major impact on the stock price.

What it does

Live Stock alerts the user of any major news regarding a stock they are following and may provide a recommendation on what to do if appropriate.

Challenges we ran into

Accomplishments that we're proud of
