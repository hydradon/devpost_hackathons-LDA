Inspiration

Mark Zuckerberg has Jarvis tell him who is at his door, and we wanted that for ourselves

What it does

When you ask Alexa who is at the door, a picture is taken of whoever is at the door and runs facial recognition from your Facebook friends, and so Alexa can tell you who is at your door

How we built it

We integrated Alexa, Node.js application running on a raspberry pi, and Facebook image recognition to find out who is currently at the door

Challenges we ran into

The Facebook API was very hard to work with, so we had to create a workaround to get the facial image recognition.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

While this is not the first time this app has been made, our code works instantly and immediately out of the box, rather than using training models which take multiple visits to learn

What we learned

We learned how to work with the Facebook API as well as some very important computer vision techniques.
