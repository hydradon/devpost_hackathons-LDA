Inspiration

As I came to know that there are many small hospitals which don't have any proper management software due to which patient face problems in fixing the appointment with the doctor.

What it does

What our project does it that it manages all the appointments of patients with the doctors in the priorty order by saving all the necessary information of the patient in the it and if the patient completed the appointment then it deletes the information of the patient and points on the number of next patient.
