Inspiration

Porridge was built by students for students to meet new people and make new friends. Our app accomplishes this mission by connecting groups at dining halls through a series of fun and engaging games.

How we built it

Ruby on Rails iOS Android

Challenges we ran into

Logistical details associated with groups meeting.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Built the app in both iOS and Android.

What we learned

Broadened our knowledge of Ruby on Rails, iOS, and Android.

What's next for Porridge

  • Add more games
  • Display a map of the users as they're walking to the event
  • Add the ability to display multiple location possibilities
