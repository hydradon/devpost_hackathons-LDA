Inspiration

to help people to detect the type of skin cancer without going to doctor

What it does

This application provides a free platform for us to check whether we have malignant or benign skin cancer . How it works is you can upload a photo of your skin that might be malignant or benign; or anything that appears on your skin and it concerns you. In this application, we also can chat with a doctor to check the result of the image.

How we built it

we built it with keras and trying to integrate it with django
