Inspiration

News takes too long for it to be processed.

What it does

Its like waze but for all alerts, we're talking anything from neighbourhood stalkers, to mass shootings, to traffic issues, to anything that can effect the user.

How I built it

I built the app using Xcode and Swift 4.2, with integration of Firebase realtime database as well as Facebook login.

Challenges I ran into

Soooo manyyy to even count. Anywhere from autolayouts to writing to firebase.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It can change so many people's lives.

What I learned

How small details can make a huge impact on the users experience.

What's next for Alert

Further improvements as well as integrations of other API's
