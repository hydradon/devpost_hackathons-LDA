Inspiration

There are times when you get confused what to buy in a store, be it a book, or a bed or just a simple gift that you want to give your special one. How cool will it be if your close friend can help you choose?

We had that in mind when we created Shelf VR. This virtual reality website lets an individual connect with their facebook friends to help them shop better and make them closer.

What it does

It helps people connect to each other through shopping as they share their preferences.

How we built it

We used aframe for webVR, javascript and php for interactions. And the star of the day, almighty Mr. HTML .

Challenges we ran into

Rendering the images.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Now, we have a 3D store that you can move around in. B)

What we learned

A very nice blend of aframe and php.

What's next for Shelf VR

Share the scene that the individual is touring. Connect to a database to link to show product details
