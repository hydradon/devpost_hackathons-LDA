Inspiration

We wanted to find a way to sift through the tons of organizations asking for donations.

How it works

We give you a simple questionaire that assesses your personality. We then match you to a handful of nonprofits among hundreds. We use IBM's Tradeoff to find the optimal among several competing factors.

Challenges I ran into

Working on a tight schedule on an ambitious project

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Collecting data from a variety of non profit databases.

What's next for Charitas

Hopefully expand and polish our services using Machine learning APIs to offer better recommendations.
