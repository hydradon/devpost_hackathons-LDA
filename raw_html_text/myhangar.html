MyHangar

Android app that allows a user to have their own personal aircraft hangar, using actual airline tickets as a prompt.

Users can track which aircraft they have flown, based on their ticket. The app will recognize the pdf417 barcode on airline tickets, and will extract necessary information to recognize the specific aircraft type and registration.

Users will be able to get further aircraft information if available, such as previous incident history of the aircraft, and image of the aircraft.

Achievements are also available based on how many times they've flown, types of aircraft, etc.

Further implementation will include the travel map, where the user can visualize the places they've flown.
