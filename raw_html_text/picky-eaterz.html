It's a Friday night; you decide to hit your friends up for dinner.

Dinner? Everyone agrees. Where to eat? The hardest part.

But it doesn't have to be. With Picky Eaterz, the decision of where to eat is made trivial by narrowing the hundreds of options down to two at a time. People in a group can join a room and choose between restaurants closest to a given location and within a certain price range.
