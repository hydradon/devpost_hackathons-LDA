What it does

Allows you to schedule motivational media for you and your friends.

How we built it

Da Motivators used a flask web app to fight against unmotivation that attacks cities.

Challenges we ran into

One of our teammates got sick, but even he persevered through it.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Coming to PennApps with no teammates in mind, and leaving PennApps with 3 new friends!

What I learned

Definitely take the time to get to know your teammates, and always get some sleep!! Also, don't disregard UI/UX and documenting what you want to get done, don't just jump right in, otherwise you might miss out on a lot of cool ideas.
