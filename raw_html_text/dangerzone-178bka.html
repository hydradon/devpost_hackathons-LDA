Inspiration

We found a lot of information about being prepared and dealing with natural disasters that most people would not consider in those situations.

What it does

This app provides information on how to prepare and survived natural disasters. By providing them with the necessary information of recognizing the signs to emergency kits to have made beforehand.

How we built it

We used Android Studio to create our very own Android app and edited all our graphics in GIMP.

Challenges we ran into

At first, we ran into complications surrounding the styling of buttons in Android Studio through their respective xml files. Implementing a navigation drawer and fragments.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It works and provides the needed information in categories for better readability.

What we learned

How to do workarounds to get past giant hurdles. As well as not to implement things that we are not sure how they work.

What's next for DangerZone

Human and biological disaster preparation.
