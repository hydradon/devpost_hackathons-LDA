Inspiration

We are 2 heavy users of the site and wanted to propose a redesign since we are close with the site owner.

What it does

Connects like-minded people interested in public policy advocacy. We provide a community-based engagement platform that facilitates the collaboration of policymakers.

How we built it

Used JustInMind for the prototype, powerpoint and iMovie for the presentation aspects.

Challenges we ran into

Just about every aspect of where to put what on the site.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

This is a layout we would actually use and are confident that the site administrators will like it also.

What we learned

How to use JustInMind and iMovie.

What's next for LawAtlas Redesign

Hopefully, we can get a stamp of approval from the Law Atlas team and see these changes take effect.
