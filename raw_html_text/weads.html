Inspiration

We all have seen different ad boards that have nothing to do with us or the same ads are repeating all the time. On the internet, we have directed ads to the potential customers. From now on, Street ads can have a higher value with the potential customers.

What it does

Display the Ads based on the interest of the users.

How we built it

First, the users have to register their devices through their mobile applications. These information will be stored on the databases on the cloud. Beside the advertisement board, the Raspberry Pi will communicate with database to retrieve information and display the ads based on the location of the users.

Challenges we ran into

Hardware integration. Database connection
