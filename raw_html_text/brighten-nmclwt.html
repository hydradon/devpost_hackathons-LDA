The goal of Brighter is a to create a fun and interactive learning environment that empowers individuals to take action towards building 100% renewable energy in their communities.

Brighter's educational platform enables users to learn about energy, earn points, and connect with others to collaborate on community-based renewable energy projects.

We're proud of the vision and mission we identified, as well as the user platform that we've created so far.

Our biggest challenge was building a robust tool/application without a developer.

Our next step is to identify a developer and/or organization that wants to help us build this!
