Inspiration

Frustration with poor team experiences.

We've all been in teams where either everyone is highly skilled in one field and not the others or lack the required skills. Working with our friends can often work in our favor when allowed yet it's not the case for most university assessments. Random allocation is hence, not an efficient way. There is a need for a structured system.

What it does

Aims to allocate team member automatically on the tutor side using skillset-matching algorithms to find the balanced or best fit for optimum team performance and learning.

How we built it

React and Firebase

Challenges we ran into

Mostly issues with technical frameworks

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting the algorithms to work and building a system that is more than just random allocations and considers various metrics for team formation.

What we learned

Flesh out ideas more beforehand

What's next for Collaborator

Adding more features for team allocation and improving the algorithms.
