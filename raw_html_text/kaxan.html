Inspiration

Very often we see that international firms use indigenous designs for their products, without acknowledging the tradition and cultural background of it. Additionally, these indigenous communities, have high unemployment rates and economic problems, when they migrate to urban cities looking for better opportunities, they found themselves at higher disadvantage, as often they do not speak even the official language or do not have the education to get a job in the city.
