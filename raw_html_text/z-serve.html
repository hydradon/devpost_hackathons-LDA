Inspiration

Service providers today misuse private data and are unable to keep it secure. I believe that private data belongs to the one who owns it and this fact should be realised in the real world.

What it does

Z-Serve allows users to verify private information that they possess with a service provider (eg. e-commerce website, financial institution etc.) without revealing it

What's next for Z-Serve

I plan to look into market validation by contacting government agencies and see how receptive different industries are to this idea as well.
