• Make the most out of your meet up with weather, restaurant, direction, and location updates all in one page.

Inspiration

Irresponsible people ruining meetups.

What it does

Meetifi makes planning meetups with your friends effortless. Easily invite your friends to your group and you will be able to share locations with each other, see weather forecasts, public transit directions, and local restaurants at your meetup spot.

How we built it

HTML, CSS, Bootstrap & Flask and SQLite3

Challenges we ran into

Not being able to sleep.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

A lot of functionality and nice looking website in a short time.

What we learned

Database management, how to make better looking websites.

What's next for Meetifi

Transitioning to a mobile app
