Inspiration

There is a lack of good software that allows gamers to connect with each other and organize tournaments for their favorite games.

What it does

Our application creates a framework for both finding and creating your own gaming events, as well as offer a way to meet up with local gamers in your area.

How we built it

We have been prototyping the application for a long time using Figma, and we have tried to bring a small portion of it to life with Flutter and Android Studio.

Challenges we ran into

A majority of our team did not have any previous Flutter experience.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to develop a way for users to create new events and have them show up on a Firestore database.

What we learned

Learning a new programming language in 48 is a daunting but rewarding task.

What's next for GG Tournaments

We will continue to develop our initial prototype using Flutter, and hopefully port it to a more native implementation later.
