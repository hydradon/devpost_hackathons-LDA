Inspiration

Ourselves and other students plagued by a lack of superior speaking skills

What it does

Converts your speech to text, and allows you to get your personality from it, save it, send it to other people via chat, and even speak it to other people in real time

How I built it

With Bluemix & Moxtra APIs

Challenges I ran into

Learning how Node.JS works and various issues of making a web app

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Overcoming the aforementioned challenges

What I learned

How to work with Node.JS and make a web app

What's next for SpeechHacks

expand it to further than students, use it for professionals and other people
