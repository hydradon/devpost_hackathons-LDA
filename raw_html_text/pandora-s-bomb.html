Inspiration

We always liked the concept of Escape Room, so we decided to fit this idea into a box.

What it does

It's a puzzle game. You have to solve a complex puzzle. It requires reflex, observation and logic.

How we built it

We used Arduino and other materials like a Styrofoam box and basic materials from an Arduino kit to build it

Challenges we ran into

We had limited knowledge of Arduino, hence we took a long time building it. Also some of our equipment ended up malfunctioning on the day itself. We realised that the LCDs were faulty, and ended up wasting alot of time trying to fix it.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We managed to build a relatively complicated project, for our level, within a day.

What we learned

How to use a Servo Soldering Arduino coding Wiring and designing

What's next for Pandora's Bomb

We can include more complicated puzzles into the game.
