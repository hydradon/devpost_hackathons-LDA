Inspiration

Students far away from their family are homesick. To alleviate their stress, we have designed an app to be used by university care package stores which delivers to students in campuses.

What it does

Parents and friends can use it to deliver customized care packages.

How we built it

We have prepared a mockup using Balsamiq wireframe tool.

Challenges we ran into

Balsamiq cloud application does not work properly (deleting or moving items randomly). It is not proper to use for real time collaborative design.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Our achievement is our cooperative work and finishing it in the time frame.

What we learned

-How to convert initial idea into a design -We learned how to utilize from empathy map and innovation canvas to get a clear idea of our users and create a feasible scheme -Challenges that arise from trying to build a business and design an app for it

What's next for ShareCare

-Implementing and testing it so that we can improve user experience -Surveying users to collect feedback
