Inspiration

Kids learn most effectively with visual and audio.

What it does

Word Match is the classic game of identify the photo where you have to select the correct word from 3 choices that describe the photo

Photo Match asks you to select the correct photo from 3 images that describe the word

How I built it

Used Google API to collect hundreds of words and public domain images to create a diverse mixture of choices. For Alexa, used SSML, Interjections, Time Delays, Body and List Templates.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

the Easter Eggs was accidentally discovered one day during testing when 2 of the choices are both correct. Some photos contain a mix of different words in the list.

What I learned

how to use Templates for Echo Show!
