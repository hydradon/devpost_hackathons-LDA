Inspiration

Stephen Hawking, "Bond, James Bond". We were thinking about spy gear and equipment for disability support at the same time, and realized we could put them in one.

What it does

Telepathy*™ allows you to communicate through a series of movements, which can be as small as EEG signals and breathing, and as much as hand gestures. They work in hand with either visual or audio cues to help you add words better.
