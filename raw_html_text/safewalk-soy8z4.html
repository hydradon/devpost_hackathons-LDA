Inspiration

Two of our teammates felt like safety information at Berkeley could be distributed in a more efficient way.

What it does

We integrated the Google maps API with data from the city of Los Angeles to provide interactive crime information about the routes you can take home. We developed an algorithm to quantify the likelihood of crime on each route to help users select a safe way home.
