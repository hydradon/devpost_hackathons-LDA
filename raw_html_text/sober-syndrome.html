Inspiration

We were inspired by one of the speakers on the panel that surprising said drug abuse is the leading cause of death. As a result, we believe this app will leave a be an impact in helping people find an easier way to reach out for help.

What it does

If you are looking for help, if you get matched up with another person. If you follow the program, your sponsor will help you. If you are matched with someone that has the same issue as you, you will become friends and help each other out.

How we built it

Prototyped an algorithm that matches people up fairly.
