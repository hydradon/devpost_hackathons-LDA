Inspiration

As a meeting facilitator, I want to start a new meeting so that I can collaborate on a new topic with a new group of people.

What it does

Collaboration JAM asks a question to a group of people. They all respond, but Collaboration JAM keeps their responses anonymous so that one person's answer doesn't influence others.
