Inspiration

The thought of being in the shoe of the client that is encountering poverty that limit himself/herself from approaching for help in person.

What it does

The app connects user to resources, to seek help putting foods on the table, able to search for jobs and housing.

How we built it

Android Studio, API's from free sources online.

Challenges we ran into

Integrating API's to keep it as simple to the user as possible.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Building prototype in a short amount of time. "Winning the hackathon!!"

What we learned

Collaboration with Githubs, teamwork, researching API's

What's next for UP Opportunity

Implement everything that we didn't finish at this hackathon.
