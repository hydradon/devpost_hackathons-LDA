Inspiration

Team members bickering to each other, realizing that within our own bickering, we might be too aggressive, which gave us the idea to target these instances for a more wide-spread audience.

What it does

Wearable IoT machine to help people be aware of their own potentially problematic speech patterns.

How we built it

Researching IBM Watson's capabilities, as well as understanding interesting data flows from IoT to the cloud by messing around with a Raspberry Pi.

Challenges we ran into

The Raspberry Pi was giving us a ton of issues, but through them were given inspiration for our idea.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Using our own differences to build our project idea

What we learned

More Python, consuming IBM's cloud services, and Node red as middleware

What's next for Mood Ring

Data Analytics, body language detection, 3D printed proto
