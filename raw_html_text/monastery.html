Inspiration

There is an entire space of information that doesn't come from textbooks, but from experience and spread of wisdom. While you can attain some of this knowledge through a good conversation, another medium are blog posts.

And, so, this hack provides a minimal (in amount of data available) search engine for asking some of these questions. 

How to do fundraising?

Googling will give you spammy results and Quora doesn't provide answers on a more solid level. However, Monastery provides a quote summary and link to a blog post by Paul Graham, which excellently answers the question. 

What do my instincts mean?

For the midlife crisis crowd, Google returns dictionary definitions and Quora considers the question synonymous with the phrase "following your instinct". However, Monestary will provide you with a LessWrong blog post that discusses instincts as well as purpose of life.
