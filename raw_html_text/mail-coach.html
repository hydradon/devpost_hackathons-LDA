Inspiration

I wanted to make use of IBM's watson technology in some way, and I personally have trouble writing emails so I thought that it would help if it was more interesting, and I had deeper insights about the mails I am sending.

How it works

Shows you you have interacted with your correspondence in the past using outlook to retrieve email and IBM's personality profile for data and visualization. Shows you trending keywords to make your email more engaging.

Challenges I ran into

-Interacting with the API -Almost lost all my mail from Gmail

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I was able to make this in less than 24 hours!!

What I learned

-Help is very helpful, I am glad that developers from respective companies were here for support. -Sometimes taking a break can help you go in the right direction, don't be hasty. -Working alone isn't as bad as I thought it would be.

What's next for Mail Coach
