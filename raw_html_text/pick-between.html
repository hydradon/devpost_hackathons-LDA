A fun way to make a hard but non-important decision! For example, if you can't decide what pizza topping you want, or maybe you cant decide whether to play video games or watch YouTube. No worries, Pick Between's got your back. Note: if Alexa doesn't respond right, try flipping the order that you say the two options. Also, Pick Between is not responsible for the outcome of the choice it makes.
