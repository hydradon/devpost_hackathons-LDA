Inspiration

School bell rings, everyone is running towards the door, it's crowded, I'm tired, I can't find my bus. There was no efficient way to locate your bus at the end of the day especially if the school is overcrowded. This is due to some of the following reasons:

  1. tiny bus route signs
  2. people in the way
  3. bad eyesight
  4. the white-board that shows the buses is too small
  5. it's raining and you don't want to wait outside.

What it does

A layout of the bus loading zone is interactive by Admins so they can input the locations of buses. Only the Admins have permissions to update the map but Students have the ability to see where their bus is on the map. The purpose is to reduce amount of time spent looking for a desired bus.
