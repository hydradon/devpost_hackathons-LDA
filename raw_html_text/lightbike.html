Easy Turn Signals

LightBike includes prominent turn signals activated by simple buttons on your bike's handlebars. Stay safe on the road by letting other drivers know your intentions.

100% Green

LightBike uses your bike's movement to generate electricity in order to power your phone and the built-in turn signals. By supporting LightBike, you're supporting our environment and joining the fight for conservation.

Power Your Phone

Keep your charge - LightBike includes an attachable phone holder that connects to our electric generator to power your phone while you're on-the-go. In addition, use the phone holder to easily access map directions during your trip!

Fits to Your Bike

LightBike easily attaches to your bike - just a quick setup and you're biking like a pro!
