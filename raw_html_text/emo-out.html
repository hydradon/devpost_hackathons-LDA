Inspiration

Emojis are fun, and people are too lazy to write a diary. Why not?

What it does

Hold down the microphone to tell Emo-Out something that happened to you, and Emo-Out will post a list of Emojis.

How we built it

We turn speech into text and text into emoji, and we spent half of the time on the interface.

Challenges we ran into

It was really challenging to get API to work. At the same time, we have to learn Swift from scratch.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It was the first time coding in swift for both of us.

What we learned

Coding gives you a really bad back pain.

What's next for Emo-Out

We want to add emotion calendar for the App, which allow user to track their moods through the week and link back the events from the emojis.
