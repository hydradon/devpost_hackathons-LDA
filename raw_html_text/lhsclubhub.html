Inspiration

We wanted to make it easier for students to find clubs they are interested in.

What it does

Creates a use friendly platform in which students can search, organize, and follow various clubs.

How I built it

We used a combination of HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and JQuery, along with various APIs.

Challenges I ran into

Finding and learning how to use APIs was a huge challenge we were presented with.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We successfully created a live working website at our first ever hackathon.

What I learned

We learned a great deal about HTML and CSS, two languages none of our team members had much experience with.

What's next for lhsClubHub

We plan to finish unimplemented methods and to present this idea to our school for student use.
