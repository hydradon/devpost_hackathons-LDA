Challenges we ran intoProblem

The current state and platform of the UW evaluation data set is not efficiently used. We believe that the data used is important, but the manner in which it is being communicated is not effective or beneficial for its stakeholders. The current state is unfriendly containing a poor user interface and information architecture, not widely accessible or available, and not useful. Direct Stakeholders: UW students UW faculty UW professors Indirect Stakeholders: Future Employees Future Students Faculty and TAs

Solution

We believe that the stakeholders should be communicated an easy friendly, accessible, and useful data user interface with simplicity in efficiency and availability.

Accomplishments that we're proud of
