Inspiration

Have you ever been stuck in a conversation with no apparent end? I wanted to make a way to easily and smoothly remove myself from conversations.

What it does

Sends fake phone calls to your phone to allow you to have an excuse to easily remove yourself from a conversation.

How I built it

I used Node on the backend to handle the incoming requests from Yo. MongoDB is used to store the phone numbers and Twilio makes the call to the users phone.

Challenges I ran into

Ensuring that the endpoints are secured so that no unwanted calls could be made via the URL enpoints of the application.
