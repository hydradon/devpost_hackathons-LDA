CyberAdventure

CyberAdventure is a simple choose-your-own-adventure game that teaches young children what it means to be a cyberbully. We hope that by understanding what cyberbullying is and how it could hurt a person, children will grow up to avoid doing this, even when they're upset at a friend or going through something hard in their lives.

How we built it

Our team came into this challenge not knowing very much python, but we stuck with it and figured out enough to build a game we're very proud of!
