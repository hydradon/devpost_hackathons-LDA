Inspiration

PDF is one of the most popular document formats. Organizations are using it for their contracts, invoices & many other documents. Supporting the PDF-related process automation, there are currently available activities for UiPath Suite to Read, merge & extract PDF files. But PDF process automation is not limited to those activities as companies also would like to:

  • Export PDF files to other formats: Excel, Word, PowerPoint, html, xml...
  • Correct rotation & convert scanned PDF to editable text & images.
  • Fill PDF forms.
  • Search text in PDF files.
  • Delete, insert & move pages.

What it does

This package of "Acrobat DC PDF" activities provides the solutions for the points mentioned in the "Inspiration" section.

How I built it

The package is built with VB language on Visual Studio.

Challenges I ran into

As I have just started learning VB & got myself familiar to Visual Studio, the challenges I ran into were relating to coding the activities on VB on Visual Studio.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am proud that I have completed the custom activity package.

What I learned

Coding VB on Visual Studio and how Acrobat DC works.

What's next for Acrobat DC PDF activities

More activities (eg Annotations-related activities) can be added later to the package.
