MindStorm

nwHacks2017

We believe that the best ideas are produced when creative people are able to participate in the free exchange of knowledge. By time-boxing the idea generation period to the first 5 minutes of a session, we reinforce the notion that there are no bad ideas, and every contribution is valuable. From then on, collaborators in the MindStorming session have all the time they need to groom and organize the thoughts that arose during the first 5 minutes.

Our realtime collaboration solution brings people together, regardless of location, helping your team achieve their full potential.
