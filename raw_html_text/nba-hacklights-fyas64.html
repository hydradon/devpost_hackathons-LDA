Inspiration

The need for a better NBA highlight-watching experience.

What it does

It helps you watch videos from the official NBA website, especially the game recaps.

How we built it

Using a selenium back end and a tkinter front end.

Challenges we ran into

The biggest challenge was getting the videos from the official NBA website, from the GET request/responses

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The way it gets videos is unique and was difficult to construct, needing to bypass a few issues when working with dynamic websites

What we learned

Dynamic websites are really hard to crawl (but rewarding)!

What's next for NBA Hacklights

Making the process more seamless by utilizing GCP for the backend. Maybe we can also do a similar process for other websites and content
