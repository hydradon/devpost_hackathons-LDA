Inspiration

We are international students. While grinding through tough eng courses we still have to learn a second language. And it's dam hard some times.

What it does

learn-that-lang is a real-time web-app that let friends improve their second language together independent of their mother language.

How We built it

Everything is on top of Google AppEngine, Translate, Firebase....
