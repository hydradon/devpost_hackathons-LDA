Car sharing, ride sharing, ride hailing, car rental. So many technical terms that aim to describe the same basic thing. To get from point A to point B. In our project we aimed to create an app, that attempts to seemlesly integrate all forms of mobility needs into one place. Making it as simple and intuitive for the user as possible. All you need is a Sixt account.

The user interface consists of a map and three buttons, giving the user the option to state his prefered form of transport. The user can either choose to rent a car from an available Sixt office, unlock a Sixt car they see on the street or hail a ride from another friendly commuter, who himself is driving an unlocked Sixt car and has decided to accept ride hailing requests. Accepting a ride request would granst the person "points", depending on where he picks up the other customer, where he is driving to and where the other customer is driving to. These points can later be used to recieve discounted rides in the future.

With our project we sought out to combine all modern foms of mobility into one easy to use interface, to create a simple and seemless experience for the user. Changing the way people think about transportation and even ownership over a car.
