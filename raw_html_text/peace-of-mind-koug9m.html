Peace Of Mind is an app that allows people to connect with individuals who believe in the same rights and causes as you. Keeps you updated with events like rallies, marches, petitions around you. It also allows the users to find places to donate money and necessities to other second world nations, or countries in dire need.

PARTS: An App where you can find things you believe in and want to support: Explore Page/ Petitions Rallies/Marches Calendar Local
