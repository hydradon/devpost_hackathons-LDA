Inspiration

The digital age is here, and frat parties will be at the forefront of this revolution. Paper bids are old. Outdated. Easily forgeable. This is a major issue facing individuals (at Berkeley) all over the world (but mostly Berkeley). We introduce to you: FratPass.

What it does

FratPass offers a minimalistic yet incredibly elegant for digital permissions and sign-ins. Users can easily distribute bids or use them for themselves. Sign-in is as simple as a QR swipe.

How we built it

The app is built from the ground up using React Native technologies.

What's next for FratPass

We plan to add features to distribute bids in app, secure bids against screenshotting, and offer real-time data analytics to frat party organizers!
