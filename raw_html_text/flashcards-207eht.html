Inspiration

We wanted to make an app that combines the fun of charades with studying.`

What it does

It is a heads up type game where one person tries to act out one of the terms while the other guesses the term.

How we built it

We were originally planning on using android studio, but then we switched to MIT app inventor

Challenges we ran into

It was very difficult to figure out what we wanted the computer to do. Once we had an idea, we struggled to actualize it.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

There were many problems that we ran into that, after a little effort, we were able to solve.

What we learned

We learned some of the basics of using Android Studio and MIT app inventor.

What's next for FlashCards

We want to develop the aesthetic appeal of the app by improving graphical design.
