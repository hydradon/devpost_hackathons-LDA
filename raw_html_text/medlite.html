Inspiration

Willing to help people

What it does

MedLite allows people to call nurses to their homes

How we built it

Client-server architecture: Android app (written in Kotlin) and back-end (written in Java (Spring)).

Challenges we ran into

The main challenge is to try not to sleep

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We accomplished 2/3 of planned work. Besides, we made some contacts with people from around the world. This is the most valuable part of the whole Junction 2016 experience.

What we learned

During work on this project we got familiar with new technologies (like Kotlin, MyBatis).

What's next for MedLite

  1. Premium-accounts for Junction 2016 organizers :)
  2. Payment by cards to enhance customer experience;
  3. Adding filters in order to simplify decision making process.
