• Wakeup Challenge

This skill is designed to help users wake up early in the morning. It incentivizes users by challenging them through Alexa. If the users wake up on time, one point is added to their streak, along with a success hurray on their facebook wall. However, if the user fails, Alexa will make the streak zero and post an amusing and slightly embarrassing post on your facebook wall.

It is a fun and engaging skill to improve your habit of waking up on time. You can modify the time or opt out of the challenge any time you like.

Say, "I am ready to take up the challenge" to start it. "I want to wake up at six AM" to set the time. "I want to change the time to five AM" to change the time. "I give up" to opt out of the challenge. "What are the embarrassing posts" to know what will be posted on your wall.
