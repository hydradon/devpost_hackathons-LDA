Inspiration

Experience the Power of SMS and Email Notification Systems in solving traffic Jams in every city of Sao Paulo. Sao Paulo comprises of about 635 cities. Being one of the largest in Brazil. Controlling Traffic Incident requires both co-operation of both the Government and individuals(Commutters).

Controlling traffic begins by passing the right informations at the right time. And This is where SMARTPAULO APPS comes in. SmartPaulo works by Out sourcing Road Traffic informations from Sao Paulo Road Safety Officials and from Individuals, Drivers, Cyclist etc and automatically leverage SMS and Email Components to broadcast the Traffic events to all users of the app in Sao Paulo. When road traffic incident is cleared from a particular city based on the Users Inputted data, the app auto re-program itself to send another SMS and Email notifications Alert to inform all Sao Paulo commutters that the road has been cleared and free to move on. This will help to increase Productivity, minimized environmental Pollution and reduce Petroleum consumption in Sao Paulo Metropolis.....


SmartPaulo App will send to you an SMS and Email Notifications Alerts on any Road Traffic Jams, Accidents and Road Block..etc that will occur in any city in Sao Paulo Metro-Polis.

Key Features of the App & How the App works

1.) Share Roads Reports
This components allow Users and everyone to share any traffic incident, traffic jam, Road Accidents that occurs within any city in Sao Paulo Metropolis. Users are allowed to share pictures of the traffic events, the location of the traffic and what actually caused the traffic. The app automatically leverage those informations and broadcast it to everyone in Sao Paulo via Email and SMS notification Protocols.

when the traffic is cleared, the User can simply goto -->Edit Information Menu--> Edit My Road Reports Broadcast to make an update.Once update has been made, the app auto re-program itself to send another SMS and Email notifications Alert to inform all Sao Paulo commutters that the road has been cleared and free to move on.

2.) Check Road Condition
Take for instance when you woke up in the morning and wants to travel, you will need to check your mobile phone and Email address to know whether the app has already sent you an Email and SMS notification alerts regarding any road traffic event in any city in Sao Paulo. If not, Check Road Condition enables User to check every city and route in Sao paulo for traffic Jams. If any are found, the app automatically detects it and alert the User of any incident. The user may decide to go to work by Public Transport thus minimizing the number of vehicles along the road

3.) Live City Commutters
Whenever you wants to travel from one city to another in Sao Paulo, the app requires that you updates your traveling/commutting route. You will need to enter the vehicle you are traveling with eg Car, Bicycle and your Destinations. The app leverage all those data to makes an updates and will alert you via an SMS and Email if any Road traffic Jams, Road Accidents happens to occur on your application route. This will help to reduce excess Carbon mono-oxide(CO) in the air and save you wasting your time and petroleum resources.

Once You arrive to your Destination city,You will need to inform the app that your journey has been completed so that the app will count you off the road. Hence you can simply goto -->Edit Information Menu--> Update My Commutte/Traveling Route to make an update.Once update has been made, then your journey has been completed successfully.

4.) Parking Space and Direction
The app automatically Provides you with all the available Parking Lots in every Line Stations in Sao Paulo. To access any, you can click on it to view details of each and every Car Parking Lots and then click Get Map Direction Link to navigate to Parking lots Direction on the map.



5.) Car Breakdown
Assuming you are traveling to any city in Sao Paulo Metropolis and your Car breakdown, your car is Automatic and is blocking the road , you can easily navigate to Car Breakdown -->click on "Vehicle Breakdown Geo Detect & Report. The app will utomatically mapped your Car Parking Longitude and Latitude and other informations like your Car plate Number and forward the Message to appropriate authorities and other app users for immediate help via SMS and Email Alert notifications. You and everyone can access your car parking directions from the map.

5.) Chat Conversation
To make conversation easier, the app provides a 1 to 1 chat System with traffic events photo sharing option.
