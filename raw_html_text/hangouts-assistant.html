Inspiration

Most of us usually hangout to the same place every time and struggle to find a new place to hangout to. This is why Hangouts Assistant was born.

What it does

Messenger bot that help users not to hangout to the same place every time and find a new place to hangout to. Ask the people with the same interests for the recommendations.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

  • Bots for Messenger Challenge Finalist
  • Featured in Egypt Messenger Discover tab

What's next for Hangouts Assistant

  • Organize the events for the people in the same community.
  • Search for trips.
  • Reservation capability.
  • Show communities' suggestions according to the interactions with the bot.
