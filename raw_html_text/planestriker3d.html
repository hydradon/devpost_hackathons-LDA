Inspiration

Love games that are first person shooter

What it does

You play as a character who shoots projectiles at airplanes in the sky. You must have a certain number of points to complete each level before timer runs out.

How I built it

C#, Blender, Unity3D and monodevolop

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Getting a good running game

What I learned

What's next for PlaneStriker3D
