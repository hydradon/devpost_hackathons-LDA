Inspiration

I watched FalleN, a professional Counter-Strike player who is widely considered the best in-game-leaders in the world, use a simple flow chart to try to teach his viewers. I thought I could make a tool that lets people learn CS strategies easier.

What it does

Lets you and your friends pretend to be pros at CS.

What's next for CounterStrat

  1. Saving strategies to localStorage so you can have multiple strategies
  2. Publishing strategies
  3. Commenting, favoriting, and liking strategies
