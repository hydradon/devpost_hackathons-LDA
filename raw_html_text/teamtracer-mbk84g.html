Inspiration

To be a big brother, I always worry about my two young sisters. Where they are? Are they in safe?

What it does

When my family in danger, they can use TeamTracer notify me with their realtime location. And then I can use the map to get them and protect my family.

How I built it

  1. Use QR Code to add a new friend.
  2. Use Firebase to maintain friends list and update location.
  3. Use Notification to notify friends.
  4. Use Map to track your friend who need you.

Challenges I ran into

All the functions are challenging to me, but I found good tutorials and have great instructors.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I built several software for my customers before, but it's my first product that can help more people not a private company.

What I learned

Learn a new programming language, how to design an app, how to presentation my app and more proficiency in the stage of the product cycle.
