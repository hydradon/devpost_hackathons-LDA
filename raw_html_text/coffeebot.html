Inspiration

CoffeeBot was originally conceived for our teammate Shree who has a caffeine addiction.

What it does

CoffeeBot approaches the nearest human and offers it coffee (or medicine, food, etc).

How I built it

Open source image processing Open source communication Lots of vex

Challenges I ran into

Integrating the computer vision and perception with the deliberation of Arduinos and motors.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Building CoffeeBot in 24 hours.

What I learned

Basic computer vision Autonomous agent architecture

What's next for CoffeeBot

There are so many potential applications for CoffeeBot-- distributing medicine in hospitals, feeding children in Africa, etc.
