Inspiration

I wanted to gain experience with XCODE and iOS development, so I came up with the idea for an app that incites friendly compeition for running, regardless of technology that somebody owns.

What it does

It would take data from the major running apps, such as Nike+ Running, the Health app, FitBit, or Runtastic, (to name a few services), and allow users to bet other users that they can run more. Users would be able to accept or decline a bet, and if they accept, the competition is on.

How I built it

A lot of googling how XCODE stuff works

Challenges I ran into

Not knowing much about XCODE

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned
