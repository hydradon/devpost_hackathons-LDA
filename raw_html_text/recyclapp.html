This app was born to encourage people to start recycling and reusing more. A common question is “what can I do with this can?”, or “this bottle… “. We waste a lot of material every day and maybe they have some interesting applications, who know? So we thought that we can help each others with our creativity creating a community of people who want to save our planet starting with our everydays rutine. In this app you can search for useful ideas or post your own ones to share with this community of people who wants to change the situation. Also you can earn some money if your ideas are great with the money from the advertisement that we can collect.

Everything explained in this webpage: https://recyclap.freesite.host

Hope u enjoy it!
