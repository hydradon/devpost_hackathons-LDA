What it does

Electromotivated parses images of hand-drawn electrical circuit diagrams, extracting features including batteries, switches, inductors, capacitors, voltmeters, and the connecting wires. It then is able to render and analyze, and share these circuits.

How We built it

We used OpenCV and Django.

Challenges We ran into

Parsing hand-drawn stuff is hard.

Accomplishments that We're proud of

It works.

What We learned

We learned a LOT about OpenCV and embedded analytics.
