Office add-in Shaders allows you to insert graphics shaders into the PowerPoint presentation. Such shaders can be used as a beautiful animation, as well as to be interactive.

Shaders computing in the graphics processor (GPU) that exempts process load of the central processor (CPU). It allows you to show great effects even if they are used on slow computers.

Shaders allow you to animate your presentation, whereby contrary to the template video such shaders can interact with the user.

Used technologies:

  • Office add-in
  • AngularJS
  • WebGL + ThreeJS framework + Shaders GLSL
