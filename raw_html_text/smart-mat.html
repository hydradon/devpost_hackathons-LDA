You order things online all the time. While you're away from home you want to know your package is ok. What if your doormat can send you a text along with a picture of when your package arrived and who dropped it off? Smart Mat can do that for you.

When Smart Mat senses that something has been placed on it, it will take a picture with a wirelessly connected webcam and send you a text along with the picture to notify you. It will do the same if your package is lifted off the Smart Mat.

Smart Mat will allow you to keep an eye on your package and give you peace of mind in return.
