Inspiration

An app by runners for runners.

What it does

Allows one person to keep the overall times and splits of multiple individuals.

How we built it

Using android studio and a lot of tutorials.

Challenges we ran into

Gesture control and communication between views and models

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Swiping functionality for splitting and stopping the timer.

What we learned

Fragments and array adapters

What's next for Coachs_Timer

Expandable individual view, save and load, splits on stop, remapping volume rockers to timer controls.
