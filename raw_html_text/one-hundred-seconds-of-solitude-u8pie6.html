Inspiration

The idea of the project is to display the world in the perspective of autistic children.

What it does

Users wearing our device can explore the VR by looking around.

How I built it

We applied ML to train the computer to study the paintings made by autistic children and then stylize realistic photos. After stylization, we transferred the stylized photos to our VR device and implemented gyroscope to achieve the rotation.

Challenges I ran into

VR hardware connection, mathematical computation, ML training

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

learned android and unity VR in less than 36 hours :)

What I learned

convoluted neural network, virtual reality, android studio programming.
