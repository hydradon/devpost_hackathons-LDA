Inspiration:

I love the idea of users sharing information through Amazon Alexa to help personalize the experience. We typically use Alexa for gaining information, but not for connecting to other people. I think there is a huge opportunity for growth in voice technology for this type of connection.

What it does: Alexa shares embarrassing moments or hilarious secret confessions.

Users visit ConfessedSecrets.com and submit an anonymous secret. Secrets are added to the skill and heard by users all over the world.

How I built it: I utilized a team from Voice First Tech to help me develop the skill.
