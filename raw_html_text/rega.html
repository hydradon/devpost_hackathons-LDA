Concept

Have you ever been stuck in the middle of the woods with no internet, and wanted to find out what the square root of 16 was? If so, then this app is for you! Simply text any question you have to Rega at +1 (503) 897-9468 and we'll retrieve the data for you.

How it works

Rega retrieves data from Wolfram Alpha and texts it to you, free of charge!

What's next for Rega

We plan to make Rega smarter about answering questions, with capabilities such as giving directions when you're lost and translating phrases and sentences.
