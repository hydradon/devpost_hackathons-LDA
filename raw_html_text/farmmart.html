Farmmart

FarmMart is project build during 2018 Developer Circles Community Challenge Yola and it's a solution to a problem based on farming sector of our community.

FarmMart solve simple man power problems for example, it solves the problem of taking farm products to the market because of lack of adequate transportation infrastructure and high cost of transporting farm products from rural areas to the cities, the farmer can just post his products and people will access and make orders.
