Inspiration

How to teach new students about ethics in research.

Public

Our app is a game for people who are willing to learn more about ethics in research.

What is the game

It is a question/answer based game, the player win points and experience while he plays it and unlock new categories.

Features

It also selects the best categories based in your major.

How we did it

We used mysql to do a database of questions and categories, all done with laravel framework with php. We also used Bootstrap, html5 and css3 to do the web page (that also works well in a mobile) and the design.

Results 

  During this hackathon we learned a lot about teamwork and databases.

Expectations

We are expecting a good reaction from the judges, since that as a problem that the university was facing.
