What it does

Our product is a device that tracks room conditions and help people maintain them on the good level for sleep.

How we built it

It consists of Arduino and several sensors such as temperature, humidity, noise and light ones. It constantly tracks the room conditions and sends this data to the user’s mobile application. Mobile applications shows all data to users and help them to improve it.
