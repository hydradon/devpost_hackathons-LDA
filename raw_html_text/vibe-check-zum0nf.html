Inspiration

I'm a big movie watcher and I thought it would be interesting to analyze scripts to see trends.

What it does

Our project analyzes the tone of movie scripts from beginning to end using IBM Watson Tone Analyzer. Users search for a movie, then we visualize the tone data for that movie script.
