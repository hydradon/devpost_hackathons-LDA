Inspiration

Wishes should be easy to fulfil, system should be scalable on peak days like Christmas. Our application targets all these problems.

What it does

Wish Bringers will help the organization to donate by

  • buying wishes on this app
  • View stats like how much donations they did
  • Upload wish data in database via this app
  • Take care of scalability
