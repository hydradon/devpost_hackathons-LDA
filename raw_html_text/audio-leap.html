Inspiration

Something I have always wanted was to be able to control the media controls on my laptop with just a wave of my hand. Even though the leap motion would perfectly fulfill this role, it costs $80, whereas, an Arduino board and the distance sensor is only $24 This project fulfills my dream at an affordable price.

What it does

Allows you to control your media settings with the motion of your hand.

How I built it

I wrote the functionality in Arduino, the serial outputs were sent to Python to access hotkeys.
