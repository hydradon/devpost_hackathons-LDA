Inspiration

We missed playing with our Tamagotchis and Neopets, so we decided to bring it to the 21st century by integrating it with Facebook Messenger.

What it does

It's a Facebook page that you can message and interact with, like a pet. You can feed it, play with it, bathe it, give it treats, and more. Your Stitch needs to be fed, receive attention, and be kept healthy or else it will die!

How we built it

We used Python, Flask, Heroku, and the Facebook Messenger API.

What's next for Stitch

We're going to personalize the character even more, and use images or gifs to represent it.
