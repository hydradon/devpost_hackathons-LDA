Inspiration: Our inspiration came from the recent hurricanes and other natural disasters.

What it does: It is a social media that connects people that are in natural disasters to people in their community and the emergency services.

How we built it: We started out with a web app made from html, bootstrap, and css with django and flask as our backend. We then made a mobile app using Xcode.

Challenges we ran into: Some of the challenges we ran into were integrating twilio and learning a new language in a short amount of time.

Accomplishments that we're proud of: We built an app as well as a website.
