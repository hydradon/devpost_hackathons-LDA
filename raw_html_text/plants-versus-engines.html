What it does

It shows the ecological heatmap of Barcelona.

How we built it

We got several datasets from the OpenData BCN portal, extracted the relevant data and transformed it to our needs. We also read the articles on photosynthesis to figure out the right formulae and estimates.

Challenges we ran into

The data was quite diverse and it took a lot of effort to make it well-aligned.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We have managed to apply biology and IT to a real world problem.

What we learned

To work with various geospatial data.
