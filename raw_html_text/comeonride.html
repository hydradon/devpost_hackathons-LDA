ComeOnRide let's connect with nearby people that are going to the same place as you in real-time.

you go to parties right? usually you don’t go alone, that would be creepy so you’re new to Berlin, you want company for a club, what do you do?

you go right to ComeOnRide , enter the place you’re going get a list of people going there, you select the ones you like you ping them via Facebook!
