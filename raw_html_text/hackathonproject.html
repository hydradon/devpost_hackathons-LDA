Inspiration

As travelers (and even college students), we often find ourselves to be in different time zones. Our goal is to create a site displaying popular areas in which users can choose to customize which clocks to display.

What it does

Our web application shows the timezone in real time based off of the user's current time.

How I built it

We used Javascript to get and alter the time; we also incorporated CSS and HTML into our design.

Challenges I ran into

Time zones can be very difficult to convert from 24 hour time and to test for all possible issues.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We are proud that the time zones worked at the end of this project. We are also proud of learning Bootstrap to incorporate design

What I learned

We learned the definition of cookies and how they can be implemented, using jquery.

What's next for HackathonProject

We hope to fully implement our cookie feature for purposes of display.
