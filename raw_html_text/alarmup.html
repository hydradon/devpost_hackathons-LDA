Inspiration

We just wanted to see what we could do with some of the technology presented with us.

How it works

AlarmUp is a smart alarm that keeps you updated during your morning routine, IBM Watson wakes you up and gets you up to date. It shows you what's going on in the world, shows your local forecast, and plays music based on the current weather. You can also snooze or turn off the alarm with hand gestures (Leap Motion).

Challenges I ran into
