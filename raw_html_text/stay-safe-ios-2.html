Stay Safe

Rationale Jamaica's children are going missing and most are never found. A chief reason why they can't be found is that the public is generally unaware and can't keep a watchful eye on our future. This application will provide detailed information about our children at the convenience of each and every Jamaican with access to a smart phone and an internet connection.

Features

  • Search and view missing children
    • Be able to view a child's photos along with valuable information that would aid in the identification of the child(e.g physical attributes)
  • View the last known location of a child on the apps map view
  • Users will be able to submit reports about missing children that would show up on all devices in realtime
    • Users will have the option to do this anonymously
