Inspiration

I want to share a selfie, but pictures are boring. And video is so sluggish. Thankfully there are Gifs!

How it works

Giffer makes it easy to make a Gif of yourself (Giffy), and easy to share it with friends!

Challenges I ran into

I originally wrote this to work with Android. After some technical issues, I decided it'd be best to make it as a website.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I made my first website that can turn things into Gifs.

What I learned

The Android camera can be a pain to use. Developing/Prototyping on websites is it a lot easier than mobile apps.

What's next for Giffer

Make it available on MOBILE. This will be vital in making it more accessible to people who take selfies the most.

Warning: Currently works on Firefox only.
