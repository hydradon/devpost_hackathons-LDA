Inspiration

Over 30 million Americans suffer from diabetes, and tracking blood sugar is a crucial part of staying healthy and avoiding emergencies.

What it does

The app measures and plots blood glucose levels, allows you to track meals eaten, and allows you to talk to a bot or doctor with questions.

What we used

xcode, swift, dialogflow, slack

Challenges

Time restraints and unfamiliarity with swift

What we're proudest of

Our chatbot!

What we learned

How to properly integrated different core parts to create an application

What's next for SugarTrack

AWS for user credentials
