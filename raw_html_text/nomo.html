Do you ever feel like you don't know what to do? Do you ever feel like everyone else is having more fun than you are? Well wonder no longer. Nomo or 'No One Misses Anything' is our web app solution to this problem affecting millions of people around the world. With Nomo you are able to find all types of events in your area from concerts to parties with a just few clicks. Nomo also allows you to input your own events that you want others to join and help the cause. No one should ever feel like they are locked out of all the fun, so use Nomo its key emoji .
