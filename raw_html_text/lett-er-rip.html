Base Mechanic

Connect close letters to make and explode letters! Here are the completed game modes:

Action

Collect as many of the bouncing around letters as you can before time runs out!

Puzzle

Find the themed words hidden in the grid!

Adventure

Guide your character to the top by using bridges of words!

Strategy

Strategically place explosions to build long words and get more power-ups to place!

Tower Defense

Make words of the attacking letters before they take your tower!

Zen

Relax with slowly floating letters and no time limit!

Survival

Letters grow until you collect them, don't let the screen fill up!
