What it does

Inhaled allows asthma patients and their doctors to track their medication and respiratory well-being, providing the ability to create the most effective regime for each patient

How we built it

We used Vue as our front-end framework and firebase for our back-end. We integrated weather APIs, to provide real-time data for asthma patients and outside conditions.

Challenges we ran into

Setting up the project and designing the UI was difficult
