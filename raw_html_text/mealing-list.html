Inspiration

Coordinating who brings what for a dinner party via email or text can cause great frustration. We were motivated to find a shared tool to avoid repeat dishes, push flavour boundaries, and make ingredient purchases simple.

How it works

Add an event, create a dish and add your ingredients to the dish. Share with friends, purchase ingredients and collect money online using Paypal.

Challenges I ran into

Finding out what was possible in a short time and looking the best personal scenarios.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The dedication of the team in trying to make this project come to life.

What I learned

Simple ideas can become complex in the making and taking shortcuts don't help in the long run.
