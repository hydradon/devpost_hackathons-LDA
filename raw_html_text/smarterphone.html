Inspiration

Making my life easier is quite a thing I like, considering that it helps.

What it does

Tells you when your phone is charging, when its is not and how much percent there is.

Helps you do not disturb when charging. For example, I just came home from school, I charged my phone and went to sleep. Anybody who messages me will be automatically replied with a message, telling them that I am not available at the moment.

How I built it

Using autoapps on my phone.

Challenges I ran into

Sometimes the "Phone disconnected" message does not work.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Managed to really do it.

What I learned

It takes alot of work to complete it and fix the bugs.
