Inspiration

Too many free snacks, too much temptation, can't keep track!

What it does

Categorizes what you eat so you can see how many carbs or fruits or drinks you're eating on a regular basis.

How I built it

I used Android studio to build the app and the buttons and access the camera. The plan was to use FIREBASE's ML KIT in order to determine what the snacks were and categorize them. But I was didn't get there in time.

Challenges I ran into

Time crunch, and not being able to save the pictures to access them.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Being able to take pictures and save them

What I learned

To work with Android studio

What's next for SnackrTrackr

Add something to not only pick up what type of food it is but also keep track of how many calories there are in each and keep a running total of your calorie intake which you can then keep stats on over time.
