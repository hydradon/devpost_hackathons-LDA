What it is

Bushele connects farmers to their crops. An innovative webapp that can be used to aid farmers and growers of all kinds keep their business running smoothly. Bushele allows a farmer to be on the farm even when he's not physically there. By giving him a dynamic overview of the current crops and their status, the grower can decide which crops or fields to give a little extra TLC. Bushele saves time, money, and cares about the most important thing: Growth.
