Have you ever wanted a quick introduction to Wellesley's campus and culture? Do you love cats and popstars? Wellesley for Wendys: Feline Edition is the game for you. Built in Ren'py visual novel creator, this point and click novel will take you through the ins and outs of Wellesley student life, while still being a fun, cute adventure!

How to play: Click or Space Be careful when grasping object! Wendy is not great at holding things, so you may have to click them on the right spot!
