MemeMap

We aimed to bring cultural content to your location. We wanted to use the facebook API to mine memes/pictures and tag them with relevant keywords from the captions, and geotag those images to certain areas around college campuses. The whole project was built as an app in the Expo XDE, written in Javascript with a React-Native framework.
