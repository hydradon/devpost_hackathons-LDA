Inspiration

We were looking to try out something completely different - usually our projects have revolved around healthcare or educations. Now we decided to pick something completely new and out of our expertise.

What it does

Platform that rewards normal users for advertising.

How we built it

We built it in two teams; one team took care of the Front End and the other team worked on the Backend.

Challenges we ran into

Database modeling.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting the application working as it should from start to finish. The platform works with the exact requirements that we set for it and just in time. Also during this time we all worked as a team while having a lot of fun.

What we learned

Importance of focusing when it's time to focus. Even though Hackathons are for fun and it was a lot of fun, we still managed to focus on our tasks that we had and managed to complete our application just in time.

What's next for adrev

Getting more providers for the platform - now with Zalando taking the lead and already integrated to the platform.
