Inspiration

Seeing our classmates struggle to use basic installers and not know where their private data goes. Our main goal is help deal with digital illiteracy.

What it does

It parses data from online privacy policies and condenses them from Lawyerspeak to English.

How we built it

We used FastText and Summa in Python.

Challenges we ran into

Machine Learning is hard.

What's next for PPS (Privacy Policies Suck)

We plan on making this an open source browser extension.
