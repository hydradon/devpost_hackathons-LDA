Inspiration

As fans of the popular show Rick and Morty, and as robotics enthusiats, we wanted to build the famous exsistential butter robot.

What it does

It rolls around, passing butter

How we built it

We connected an ultrasonic sensor, servos, and motors to an Arduino Uno and then built a casing around it.

Challenges we ran

-We had limited supplies, and no way of acquiring more, so we had to improvise ~a lot~

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We built a complete product that spans all the major engineering disciplines (mechanical, electrical, computer science)!

What we learned

-How to program an Arduino -How to integrate different subgroups to complete one final product
