Inspiration

For women to be safer in the outdoors (streets) since women can easily become a target.

What it does

In case of emergency for women, this website can provide help for them. It can make a loud sound, call 911, and get google map for women.

How I built it

google maps, html, css

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned

I learned we need to work in groups, and I learned how to design the website.

What's next for Women Street Safety

We can expand the website with more emergency information.
