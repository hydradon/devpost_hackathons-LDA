• Swip to join a movie

Inspiration

On a nice Friday night you want to see a movie with a group of people. You swipe on movies you want to see.
People will be grouped and will enjoy the movie together.

What it does

The Inference Engine (Tensorflow) is not working ... but ... The inference engine learns your preferences and suggests a group to join for a movie.

  1. I rate movies by simply swiping right or left.
  2. Once I have a movie and a group I can get tickets.
  3. Then join my group at a local meeting place to discuss the movie.
  4. Finally I can provide feedback to the studio about what we thought. The feedback is also used to train the inference engine allowing better suggestions.

How I built it

The Tensor is not working. The Android mobile app is functioning with artificial data.
