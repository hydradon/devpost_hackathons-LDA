Inspiration

January 28th 2019, small farms must be FSMA compliant.

What it does

Helps farmers and small business owners become FSMA compliant. Not unlike Turbo Tax to file with the IRS and become compliant, Breadbox is easy to use enterprise cloud-based SaaS to be compliant with FSMA and the FDA.

How I built it

React.js frontend and a node/express back.
