Inspiration

FireStorms in San Diego are a real problem, especially for the folks who need to evacuate and get out of its way. Many times, folks can avoid fires, and properly evacuate if they had known about the problem quickly and accurately.

What it does

ACT is our system of allowing citizens to be informed of the fire, and also allow them to report of the situation.

How I built it

A backend system of collecting incident reports, along with user profiles that would allow those incidents to be ranked, and verified by officials.

Early responders and emergency groups would use either a web site or mobile application to have a situations awareness of their community. Each citizen can also view and report of incidents using our ACT smart phone application, or our web site.
