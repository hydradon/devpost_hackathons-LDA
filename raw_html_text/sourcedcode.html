Inspiration

Our modern devices are incredibly complex, sourcing components and raw materials from around the world. It is increasingly difficult to understand the ecological and humanitarian effects of the devices we use.

What it does

SourcedCode is a database of devices and their source materials. In addition to providing information about the various components, the app assigns each device a "responsibility score", represented by an understandable emoji scale.

How we built it

SourcedCode is a native app built for iOS built with UIKit and Swift. We used CoreData for our database backend.
