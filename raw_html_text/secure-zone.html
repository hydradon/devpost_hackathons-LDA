Inspiration

We were inspired to create Secure Zone in order to better improve the safety of individuals by identifying potential threat.

What it does

Secure Zone utilizes the camera to capture image and identifies whether the person is listed in the public criminal database.

What's next for Secure Zone

We hope to add additional features such as a safe word and pace tracker option; the safe word option would allow users to customize specific safe-word sent and the response. The pace tracker option would allow for them to alert authorities if user pace suddenly increase.
