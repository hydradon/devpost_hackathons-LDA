Inspiration

We were thinking on project with a social connect.While brainstorming we got the idea to help the struggling dabbawalas who dont have enough resources to expand their business.

How it works

It's a Database Management System that connects customers to the dabbawalas.Dabbawalas could make their account on the portal and customers could check them out filtering them.

Challenges I ran into

Actually we were going to make web app for Android Os and Firefox Os but the problem came when the homepage of the site was'nt functioning properly on the server when we put it for testing on the respective Os.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

This was our first project for a hackathon and indeed our first hackathon . so we have made a good attempt.

What I learned

We learned mainly that how backend developers work with Frontend developers and get to know that both are equal parts of web development

What's next for Dabbawala

We are looking forward to add some features like Order, notifications and a chat client.
