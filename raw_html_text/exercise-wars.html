Inspiration

*My struggle with encouraging my family to get active
*Our coworker workout calendar

What it does

Helps people...
*Get motivated to exercise and build a healthy habit
*Engage in a fun, incentive-driven competition with people you know (e.g., family, coworkers)
*Participate with any level of fitness

How we built it

*Using Figma

Accomplishments that we're proud of

*Coming out to the hackathon
*Brainstorming impactful ideas
*Building the primary user flows for the app with high fidelity mockups
*Achieving an MVP prototype
*Collaborating and working hard together

What we learned

*It's challenging to brainstorm, select, and build an app, and create a presentation in 1.5 days with 2 teammates!
