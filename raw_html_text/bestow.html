We wanted to connect those who have excess resources to those who are in need by providing a convenient platform for these donations to occur. Our app provides a donation platform. We built using the android studio.

Google vision was very difficult to utilize and the Indico was not usable with android studios which were very upsetting. We got firebase working and the visual was adequate. We learned to utilize firebase fluently in addition to utilizing vision api. Also this project helped us gain valuable teamwork skills.
