Inspiration

We wanted to show children how important it is to avoid pathogens, and teach them how they can gain protection against them.

What it does

It is a game in which the kid need to collect the fruits, vaccines and antibiotics while avoiding bacteria and viruses with increasing difficulties level-by-level.

How we built it

In Unity

Challenges we ran into

Learning how to use Unity, updating our project continuously while developing; trying to model the immune-system and healthy lifestyle in a game; team management; lack of coding computing student

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It ended up being a better game than what we imagined

What we learned

Game design.

What's next for Imm-Unity

Develop additional levels and challenges.
