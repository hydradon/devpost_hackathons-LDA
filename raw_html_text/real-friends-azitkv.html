Inspiration

For all those moments where you need your phone to save you in those awkward social situations. And Kanye for everything

What it does

A texting service that texts you certain responses for certain situations. For example, if you need to quickly get away you can text the number to get a response that will allow you to slip away with ease. Or if you're out with friends any everyone is on their phones, you can get texts too so no one will know how lonely you really are.

How we built it

python with twilio api; html for the website

Challenges we ran into

maintaining a conversation is much more difficult than we initially thought. also the website acts as a proof of concept as we didnt know how to have the texting service add numbers to it. also coding in general prob

Accomplishments that we're proud of

we used the twilio api and successfully made a functioning program that texts back

What we learned

we further developed our knowledge of python, terminal, how to use other companies apis, and gained a ton of experience

What's next for Real Friends
