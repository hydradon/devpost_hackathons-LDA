What it does

It is a maze. You are the black dot. The goal is to get to the right side of the canvas without touching any red dots or the walls (besides the right wall). By hovering over red dots, the black dot grows in size. You can still get to the other side, but if you grow too much, the dot will touch other red dots and you will continue to grow and eventually you will touch a wall. Once you touch a wall, if it is the right wall, you win and can go to the next level. If it isn't the right wall, you lose and must start over.
