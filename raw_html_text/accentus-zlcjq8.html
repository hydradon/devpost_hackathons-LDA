Inspiration We realized that most teenagers face a large amount of stress on a daily basis and wanted to help this problem, that we also face at times.

What it does Our website makes teenagers log how they are feeling everyday and log what activities you did that day. We then analyze this data over time periods (week, month) and find trends in the activities that make them feel better and reduce their overall stress.

How I built it We used HTML and CSS for the website and for pushing the website, we used Github and created a repository.
