Inspiration

We were inspired by our personal struggles in math and the struggles of the people around us in simplyfing really tedious calculations in math.

What it does

EZMath evaluates calculations of all size and can solve one variable equations.

How we built it

We built the mobile app using android studio, myscript api and mathpix api.

Challenges we ran into

After we have split up the work and made the different components of the app, it was hard for us to merge all of the parts together.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of our final out come which is successfully able to solve all calculations.

What we learned

We learned how to merge code efficiently.

What's next for EZMath

We plan to have an additional feature to take a picture of a math problem and have the app solve the problem and show the steps.
