Making time to meet with people has become increasingly difficult in our busy lives, so even scheduling time to cross reference free periods has resigned to being too much of a hassle. With Calendar-Catch, you and your group of friends, peers, or colleagues can simply select expected free periods in the coming days and an optimal date will be selected and synced with your Google Calendars, along with the people expected to attend.

Front End: HTML CSS with Bootstrap Javascript

Back End: Python, using the Django framework

Design: Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop

Hosting: AWS EC2
