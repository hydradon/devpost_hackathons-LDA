• Surprise Photo Booth

In today's day and age, everyone loves to take pictures! Whether it'd be outside or indoors with friends and family, there's always a picture moment ready to happen. So why not build a portable touchscreen photo booth you can take with you anywhere that is cost friendly and easy to make. This photo booth however, also has a unique surprise for whoever dares to try and take a picture, you'll just have to wait and find out what that surprise is!

Built With

  • pi-camera-module
  • raspberry-pi
