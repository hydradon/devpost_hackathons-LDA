Inspiration

Scalable prisoner's dilemma.

What it does

Lets you stake money on a game of prisoner's dilemma with many players using taxa.

How we built it

Frontend - Vue Secret guess contract - Taxa Live updates - Node.js/WebSockets

Challenges we ran into

Deciding on privacy implementation, getting taxa set up, managing state over multiple backend components.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting taxa working. Combining multiple ideas into one. Teamwork and communication.

What we learned

The difficulty and wide variety of privacy options for smart contracts. How to deploy contracts on Taxa. The value of non-technical skills.

What's next for SweepStake!

Go live! And then #1 spot on the app store!
