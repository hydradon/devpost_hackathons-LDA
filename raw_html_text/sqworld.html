Inspiration

We wanted to tell a compelling, yet amusing narrative.

What it does

It lets the user go through the story and make choices to determine the outcome of the squirrel's life.

How we built it

MIT App Inventor

Challenges we ran into

There were different ideas during the brainstorming phase that we had to scrap. We were almost for sure going to make a different app, but we decided against it. It was also challenging to decide how to make our idea a reality because we are all beginner programmers who have merely taken one java class.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We made a game that works.

What we learned

Making a game is hard, and we also learned a bunch of random facts about squirrels.
