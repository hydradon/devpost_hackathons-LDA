Inspiration

I wanted to decide which paper I should work on for ICLR Reproducibility Challenge . Soon realized there's a lack of resources out there for figuring out what topics you could work on, which papers could use verification and where could you find teams and mentors.

What it does

Topic models all papers for ICLR. You can search papers by inferred topics, popularity on twitter/hacker news, credibility of authors, etc. Intended to be a platform for not only looking up papers that you could potentially work on, but also finding people who you could collaborate with.
