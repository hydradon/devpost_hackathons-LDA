Inspiration

People always need pick-me-ups throughout the day so why not use nexmo's api to automate a phone call telling a joke.

What it does

After entering a phone number, the site executes a script to call the phone number listed and reads a listed joke. That's it!

Note

It only works with US numbers at this point so don't spread the funny with all of Africa just yet
