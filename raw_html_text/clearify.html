Inspiration

Due to the quality of air in the city it is very difficult to do outside activities like running, walking. But not all parts of a city is filled the smog, there are what we call little green zones where the quality of the air is better, but there is no application outhere that can help you identify those zone, that is where Clearify comes in.

What it does

Clearify is a application that measures the level of pollution in the user's area and recommends him a new location with less pollution based on the activity he wants to do.

Target public: -people who lead a sporting lifestyle -people concerned about their health -economic vendors who want to use pollution data to make different decisions(where to build apartments, where to have public activities, etc).

Motto: clearify smog, any time of day

How we built it

Using Node.js, Express.js, passportjs, Mongo, MariaSql, HTML, CSS JavaScript, MaterializeCSS, Google Maps API.

Challenges we ran into

Making the design responsive for mobile devices.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We have our own API that integrates any kind of sensor into our pollution/smog monitoring system.

What we learned

To think outside of the box.

What's next for Clearify

Integration with other devices:smartwatch, fitness trackers, etc.

Integration of our API in existent pollution/smog monitoring systems.

Gamification system for users to motivate them to go outside.
