Inspiration

Trying to reduce preventable harm by providing easier access to healthcare

What it does

Enable community based access to care in a place where there is a lack of availability and care as a solution to preventable harm as well as reduce poverty by increasing employment rates in the region

How we built it

We built it by interfacing a Qualcomm board with and Arduino as a basis for communication.
