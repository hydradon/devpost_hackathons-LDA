Inspiration

Websites like Doodle.com or when2meet make scheduling meetings easy. Why not lunch outings?

What it does

Spork helps you pick the best places on Yelp and share them with your friends. Easily plan a lunch or dinner date by suggesting Yelp businesses and letting your event participants vote on them! Finally, an easy way to choose somewhere everyone will want to go.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Spork's integration with the Yelp API and instant search capabilities make finding a great place to go a breeze. We're especially proud of the user experience of our product. We set out to make something simple that people will want to use, and we think we've accomplished that.

What's next for Spork

In the future, we hope to create a Slack integration that will allow you to plan team outings.
