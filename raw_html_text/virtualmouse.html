Inspiration

We got tired of not having touch screen computers!

What it does

Controls the mouse of your computer using the webcam and your fingers

How we built it

Learned OpenCV and JavaCV from online tutorials and did a ton of trial and error.

Challenges we ran into

Complications with outdated OpenCV and JavaCV versions!

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Actually finishing and submitting the project

What we learned

Nothing goes according to plan!!!

What's next for VirtualMouse

Include implementation into iOS and Android devices and include more commands that can be done.
