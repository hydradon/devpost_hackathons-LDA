Stimul8 is a web site to help people get motivated and do what they always wanted. There are a lot of people that have a very big barrier that stops them from doing what they want to do. For example, there are people that want to commit suicide because they get bullied all the time. We want Stimul8 to give people motivation to over come that barrier. This will help the society because that way people can feel good about themselves without worrying who knows their secret. People all over the world will connect to each other all from this website. This will make the world stronger and better. It will keep people motivated and structured.
