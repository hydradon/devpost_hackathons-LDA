Excess weight has reached epidemic proportions globally, with more than 1 billion adults being overweight in 2003 according to Wikipedia.

Losing weight and maintaining your weight after a diet is extremely challenging. There are lots of contradicting information out there, so how do you know what actually works for you?

Wouldn't it be great if you can have a personal dieting coach to tell you anytime if whatever you are eating will cause YOU to gain or lose weight?

Now you can with Diet Coach Bot !

With Diet Coach Bot you record your weight and what you eat daily and your Bot will use machine learning to predict how each food or drink is affecting your weight. The more you use your coach the more accurate it becomes.

By knowing in realtime how much the food you are eating is affecting your weight, you can now make daily adjustments in order to find a diet that you can enjoy and sustain!

Diet Coach Bot is built with Cisco-Spark for messaging with the bot, IBM-Watson for understanding natural language and advanced machine learning algorithms to make predictions.

The Radix domain dietbot.online is used to host our bot.
