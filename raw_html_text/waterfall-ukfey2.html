What it does

We give users a simple interface with one single stream of tasks like a waterfall. The key feature is instead of letting the users staring at millions of tasks they have, we auto-sort the tasks users on hand by due time, importance, and estimated time. All users need to do is follow the order of the tasks we give them and conquer them one by one. No more panic and hesitation of what to do first.

How we built it

We are making a ios app using swift language and interfacing the app with mongoDB.
