What does it do?

The project gives the user an intuitive user interface to keep track of their school work and assignments. The program will then automatically create events in their personal calendar and block out time for them to complete their work when they have free time, between their working hours of course. Instead of going through your calendar everyday and manually setting up blocks of time, why not let a computer do the work for you?
