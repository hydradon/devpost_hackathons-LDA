Inspiration

To make learning from your mistakes really easy while playing competitive games.

What it does

Our web application gives player an overview of how the game progressed throughout the entire round. After the round has finished, the player has the ability to see the an arial recap of the match. The player can learn how all the players in that round moved, eliminated hostiles etc, so he/she could watch and learn from the tactics of the opponent and do better the next round.

How we built it

We built it with React and Java8. All the data was provided by the Kamu.

Challenges we ran into

Cutting data into pieces and finding information that could be useful.
