Inspiration

It's likely gamers have collected trillions of dollars in none value gaming points, power ups and badges. What if we can replace gaming points with a real world micro currency, thats perfect for gaming.

What it does

Gamerholic is a cross platform, cross game, gaming cypto currency.

How we built it

Gamerholic is built using ionic2 and angular2, along with a postgres db

Challenges we ran into

We implemented gamer to gamer rides, a service to meed the demand of out and about gamers collecting pokemon, bitcrystals game cards or gamers who'd simply prefer to get a ride from another gamer, in exchange for Gamerholic coin.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We built an MVP within 24 hours
