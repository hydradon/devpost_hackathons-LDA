In order to be an informed investor, knowing the history of the market is invaluable in making decisions for the future; knowledge is indeed power.

To put this power in the hands of the average long-term investor, we've developed a single page web application to show users how their investments would have done, which they can use to make informed decisions with their hard-earned capital. While other web apps offer similar functionality, what they lack is the more in-depth analysis of dividends (and reinvesting those dividends). With Sentinel, users have full control over how they spend their money, just like they should, with our easy-to-use and sleek, simple design.

We believe that Sentinel provides users with masterfully intuitive and gives users full control over how they'll invest their money.
