Occasionally, you really want to know if you're thinking of the correct screw, or you want to describe a screw to someone else - even see a picture of this screw if the device allows it.

Screwdrivers will tell you details, and show you a picture of a particular screw type.

If you're feeling particularly adventurous, you may wish to just ask the app to tell you about a screwdriver at random. It'll pick one to brighten up your day.
