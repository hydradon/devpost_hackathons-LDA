Our Inspiration

The desire to give people, who want to donate to their beloved charities , the ability to do so by giving them the ability to use their time as their currency, and also teaching them about other causes they might not know about.

What it does

This website is the introduction to the app known as Charitable Education, and the app as created by Android studio allows for our vision to be realized, through creating a user friendly interface that connects users with videos that are followed by a quiz, which allows the user to donate to any cause of their choice with the coins that were earned.

How we built it

For the website we used HTML5 and CSS. The app was created in Android Studio and coded in Java.

Challenges we ran into

Their were challenges on both stages of the project from layout on the website to bugs in the code for the app.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of our result, we worked very hard to bring the product that we created today and present it. It was a challenge but it taught us so much.

What we learned

We learned more about web design and creating and app. The design that goes into one, the planning the grueling amount of time to code it.

What's next for Charitable Education

We hope to branch out into ios systems and obtain donors to fund our app so that people who are unable to support the causes they believe in with money can support it with time while learning new things the whole way.This more people can be made aware and help with situations around them
