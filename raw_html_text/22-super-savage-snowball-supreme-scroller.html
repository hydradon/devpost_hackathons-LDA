Inspiration

Megaman game

What it does

A game where you shoot snowballs at a moving enemy in a 3 x 6 grid, where the enemy retaliates.

How we built it

We used Unity 2D

Challenges we ran into

Figuring out how to destroy an object after its animation ended, collision and physics errors

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We made a game in 12 hours with zero knowledge of Unity.

What we learned

How to work with Unity in a more streamlined way, the importance of teamwork, and programming skills
