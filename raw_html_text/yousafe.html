Inspiration

Saving people from emergency situations like accidental falling. Help your close one's who are in need with a single click.

What it does

Alerts the emergency contact with an email and SMS immediately during emergency ().

How we built it

We used Amazon iot dash which when clicked will alert the emergency contact. We used pebble smart watch to detect unusual fall pattern and allows the emergency contact.

Challenges we ran into

It was a challenge to integrate pebble with android phone. Fall detection itself is a big area of research and we were not able to accurately predict the fall.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to properly integrate pebble with android phone and also Amazon dash device with AWS. We were able to properly send alerts.
