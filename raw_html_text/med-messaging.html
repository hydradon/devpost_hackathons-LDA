Patient Clinician secure photo and file messaging for healthcare. With the increasing penetration of digital mobile devices, mobile is emerging as a new channel for patient-clinician communication. However, there is always concern about securely sending photos & files by the patients. Our app use Send Anywhere API to accomplish that. We have enhanced our existing care coordination messaging platform and added this new feature for patients to securely send photos & files to the doctor on a smartphone.
