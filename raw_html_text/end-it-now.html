Inspiration

Since the advent of modern social media and regular connectivity between people of all ages, persons have becomes victims of malicious activity. It is adamant that we allow for a platform for survivors and activists against online harassment alike be able to share their stories of positivisty and campaigns for others to share and connect through the similarities.

What it does

It allows users to sign up using the facebook login and permissions are given to the web app. As a result when we post anything with the "#enditnow2017" string the feed is updated on our platform and other users may connect with each other to solve problem in theie local community.

How we built it

Using Azure, CockroachDB, Facebook API.

Challenges we ran into

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned

The importance of project management (prioritizing), SQL and Node.js syntax, Facebook API, navigating Azure
