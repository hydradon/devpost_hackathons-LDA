What it does

Bluma (Business-Level Universal Machine Assistant) is an intelligent business AI that hopes to reinvent how recruiting is done. Bluma also provides assistance for HR managers regarding scheduling, as well as business analytics (under zero-training). All she requires is simple conversation. If you're a candidate applying for a job, you can ask Bluma questions such as, "I'd like to reschedule my job interview", and she will reply with, "I've found some available slots: xx". How about sending a candidate a personalised package? Bluma will take care of that via an SMS message. Unlike standard chatbots, Bluma provides a human-like experience for candidates, clients, and internal staff alike. With Bluma, you'll never have to worry about falling short on consistent corporate image and on-brand messaging.

Core features: -Talent Development -Employee Assessments -Candidate Assistant -On-site Concierge Service
