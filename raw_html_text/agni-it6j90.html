During last Diwali break when i went to my hometown while watching TV I saw a news which was regarding a farmer named Santosh Shinde, said, “Since we had got decent prices last year, we grew tomatoes on 3 acre this year too. We were expecting a good price for the produce. I had invested more than Rs 2 lakh on it, but I have not even earned Rs 1 lakh. How will I repay my loan now?”

Since my grandfather also belongs to agriculture field and he was also facing the same situation so I decided to take an initiative toward the backbone of our country FARMERS while thinking some day I got an idea which I implemented today.
