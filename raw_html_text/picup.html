Bringing communities together, PiCup creates and spreads local pick-up sports games.

As a young soccer player, Jonathan always wanted to play with his friends after school. However, as parks became dominated by league sports, Jonathan's friends and him couldn't find time to play simple pickup soccer! PiCup changes all of that. By connecting strangers and friends to play sports (football, hockey, soccer, basketball, etc), athletes can have more fun in a busy world.
