What it does

It's a chrome extension that turns any input into a collaborative editor.

  1. Right click an input field
  2. Click "Mirror™ This"
  3. Send the Mirror™ link, that was just added to your clipboard, to a friend.
  4. Rejoice in a collaborative text editing experience! Real-time. No brand cuffs.
