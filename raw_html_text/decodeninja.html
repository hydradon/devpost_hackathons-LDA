Inspiration

Countless software projects depend on understanding complex binary protocols. DecodeNinja makes it easier to decode and reverse engineer that data.

What it does

DecodeNinja lets you apply analysis to your data and share that analysis with friends and collaborators, making it perfect for communities developing free software.

How I built it

This tool is built as a web app. Data is saved to the cloud, making it instantly available to those you collaborate with.

What's next for DecodeNinja

Now that the proof of concept is done, DecodeNinja needs more analysis and commenting tools to allow better communication between teammates.
