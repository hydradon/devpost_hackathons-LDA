headbang.js

Play a song based off the bpm of your headbang

** How it works** Uses tracking.js to monitor your face's y coordinate. Detects the bpm of your head nod over the span of 5 seconds Uses that bpm to find songs with that bpm with echonest api Uses the song artist and title to play the song with youtube api in an iframe
