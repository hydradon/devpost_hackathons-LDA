Inspiration

After hearing so many business travels trying to spend time making the most of their weekend, we thought of making that process easier for them and giving them a better overall experience

What it does

When a travel agent enters flight details for their client, they are prompted with weekend flight and event getaways.

How we built it

We build the app using Angular, Node, Express and Sabre API

Challenges we ran into

Two of our team members we just starting to learn Angular and this was an amazing platform to practice those skills in the real world

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Working on making the UI better than just a working prototype thanks to the implementation of Google's Material UI

What we learned

All of us got a chance to apply a skill we were all learning and we all learned to work together complementing each other's skills

What's next for Upsell Travel

Integrate it in the Sabre Red Studio for seamless travel agent experience
