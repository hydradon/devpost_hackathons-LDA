Tpumps is a popular bubble tea shop in California. It stands out from the crowd because of the diverse array of flavors that customers can mix and match.

Often, customers who visit Tpumps are overwhelmed by the number of choices and can't decide on a combination of flavors to try. To solve this problem, the Tpumps app can be positioned on a tablet at the register of Tpumps for customers to interact with and get suggestions for flavor combos. The app is cross platform, so it can be run on both Android and iOS devices!
