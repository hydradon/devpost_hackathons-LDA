Overview

IMPACT allows anyone to get enact political change by impact and engaging with local legislation.

What it does

Impact allows anyone to search local policy by popular or controversial issues and see how their local legislators voted on particular issues. It empowers individuals to directly engage with their local representatives with the ability to call or email them directly from the app.

How I built it

We leveraged FiscalNote's API to obtain legislative and legislator information.
