Inspiration

We wanted to make sure that everyone in emergency will receive a proper care.

What it does

It monitors current status of city traffic (or other provided data) and handles both long and short-term optimisations regarding emergency infrastructure.

How we built it

With our bare hands and sheer luck and coincidence - also, Python.

Challenges we ran into

Teamwork, overlapping domains of competence and frontend.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Swift work despite the challenges we've faced, and our frontend came out pretty nice too afterall!

What we learned

To better prepare ourselves for work under the pressure of time.

What's next for our project

Deep and painful refactoring, then maybe further development in other areas such as resource management in telecommunications or general flow control problems.
