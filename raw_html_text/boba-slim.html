• #BobaSlim

Inspiration

A friend would always struggle when he bought large boba drink and took them to go but they wouldn't fit in his cup holders.

What it does

Allows large boba tea cups to fit in car cup holders.

How I built it

I used Pro Engineering WildFire 5.0 to CAD the piece

Challenges I ran into

The 3D printed version of my piece was too weak and it broke when the engineers removed it from the machine

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am just glad that I was able to make a piece that I can test out and I can improve upon.

What I learned

I learned how to design again because I hadn't worked with it in a while.

What's next for Boba Slim

Tests and improvements.
