eduMD is a platform allowing health care proffesionals to share important health care education resources with their patients, in a secure and convenient web-app. At present patients have access to a disparate range of online resources. These resources can be inaccurate and sometimes do not apply to the patients actual ailment. Using eduMD doctors can curate and select particular health care educaiton content to share with each specific patient. This robust platform allows doctors to select and share a wide range of important health material catered specifically to the individual needs and diagnoses of their patients and then share these personalized pages with their patient. This should improve patients' access to quality information and improve the information exchange between the health care provider and their patients.
