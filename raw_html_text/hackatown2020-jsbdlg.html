Bicycle

Bicycle is an app to ease the use of bicycle in the city.

The Bicycle app will display the closest repair stations within a radius of 1km if a rider's bike is in need of repair.

If the rider is unable to get to a station or has no station near him, the Bicycle app offers the option to connect the rider with other nearby Bicycle users to ask them for help. When the user asks for help, nearby riders receive a notification that another rider needs help. At this time, the rider can view the location of the rider in need and decide whether or not to go help him. If he decides to offer his help, both riders will be notified of the position of the other.
