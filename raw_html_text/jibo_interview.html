Inspiration

The interview process can be intimidating. Sometimes, nerves can obstruct you from performing your best on an interview, hurting your chances of getting the job of your dreams. As CS students and as adults entering the job market, we can empathize. So we decided to create something that could prepare you for the hot seat, and who better than Jibo, the adorable social robot?

What it does

Jibo asks a series of common, yet challenging interview questions for the user and provides encouragement after each answer.

How we built it

We used the Jibo SDK.

Challenges we ran into

Getting the activation phrase to work as expected. It originally skipped over the activation phrase and went straight into asking all of the interview questions at once. However, after a review of how Jibo's Natural Language Understanding and branch structure works, along with a lot of trial and error, we were able to fix the issue.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Fixing the activation issue.

What we learned

Coffee is amazing. Also, speech recognition is extremely complex.
