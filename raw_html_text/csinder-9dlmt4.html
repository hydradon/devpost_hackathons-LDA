What it does

A social media site for like-minded CS students.

How we built it

HTML/CSS/JS front-end with PHP/MySQL backend

As a team, we all wanted a stronger computer science community at our schools. We noticed that most students didn't talk to each other or work together on projects outside of class, and we believe that the reason behind this was a lack of a resource that specifically connected same majored students together. Therefore, we wanted to create a website called CSinder that would help make this task much easier. By developing CSinder, we can now easily find teams to work with at hackathons or even on side projects and create connections with people you have similar interests with.
