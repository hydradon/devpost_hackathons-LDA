Inspiration

Tired of the same old boring security questions? Do you just want to get into your account, but you can't remember the answers that have changed in your mind? Are you just plain old bored of having to come up with these things all the time? Fear not, for with this, those days are over! With this new design, it's never been more fun to get into your account! Everybody has that one tune that sticks around in their minds, and now you can make that your answer! Just pick from a range of audio options, and you'll be able to hear that little melody you've created when you need to get in!
