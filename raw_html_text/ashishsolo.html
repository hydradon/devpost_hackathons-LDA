Inspiration

N2N solution to help outreach and non profit organization to the get exact location of the homeless people so that outreach people can reach out to them. Also,Mental disorders keep thousands of homeless on streets Also,THOUSANDS of people with mental illiness end up homeless , BUT THERE ARE APPROACHES THAT CAN HELP THEM OUT.

What it does

Part 1: Client side: People can send real time detailed information of people in need so that outreach team can help them. Donations are made by people and are saved in block chain so that homeless people don't misuse the donation and people get notified in real time. Outreach Team: They get the exact location of the homeless people.

Part 2: Facial recognition system to fight mental issues
