"How can we better measure sustainability importance in the private sector?"

Was one of the three questions asked at UWEnergyHacks which we chose to answer. Our idea was to implement a means of comparing companies based on their sustainability. We chose to use a paper (found here link ) which identified a correlation between economic output and equivalent carbon emissions.

From this idea we created CETA the Carbon Emissions Tracking Application.

CETA placed 2nd in the Top Design category at UWEnergyHacks 2019
