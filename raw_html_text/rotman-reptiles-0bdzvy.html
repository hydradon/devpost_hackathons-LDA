Inspiration

We were inspired by seeing growing trends in individuals knowing that they want to support community causes but not know how.

What it does

This product is a credit card for those who want to give back. Instead of getting cash back directly to your account, individuals with the TD Spark card get 2% cash back to donate to a social cause. The credit card is designed to have a characteristic look and feel to have a brand that is as impactful as the people who use it.

How I built it

The product is built using Angular 7 and a node Express server. The product integrates with the TD API as well as the Kiva.org API to pull projects to support.

Challenges I ran into

Technical complications with API integration.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Our team was cohesive and worked well as a collaborative unit to design and develop the product.

What I learned

How to combine the expertise of product developers and engineers to build something to help society.
