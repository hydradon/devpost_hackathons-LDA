Inspiration

The Layer2 Feed in the ConcourseQ community. https://www.reddit.com/r/Layer2/

What it does

Toolkit so anyone can start and run their own token-curated feed and distribute to multiple channels.

"Curcur is a Curation Toolkit. Start a feed about what you love. Name your own token. Invite your friends to curate with you. Build your audience. Grow your curation community. Let advertisers sponsor posts and watch your community sell their earned tokens for real ETH."
