Inspiration

Born out of necessity. Divorce, trauma, and modern family structures make it almost impossible for families to save in traditional standpoints. Traditional government aide fails to address the value of wellness, whole family, and education.

What it does

Easy smart contracts to create a record of the relationship. Creates a non-fungible <3 for each family, general <3 tokens can be given to families. Hearts can be broken, ending the smart contract and removing breaking party as wallet owner. Families may post the link to their wallets to receive aide. Funds are monitored by central custodian, only approved expenditures can the <3s be used for.
