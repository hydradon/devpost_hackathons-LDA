Inspiration

I hold bananas A LOT. I don't always remember if I'm holding my banana or not. As a result, sometimes, I take bananas to odd places or forget to take them with me.

What it does

Alexa will tell you whether you're holding a banana or not. Simply say, "Alexa, ask the banana man if I'm holding a banana."

How I built it

We built it using the alexa skill kit. It randomly decides between the two options of whether you're holding a banana or not.

Challenges I ran into

We were going to do something else, but another group had that idea so we didn't do that. Alexa likes to apologize about not knowing how to answer questions.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

  • We're new to being Alexa developers.
  • Inventing the banana man.

What I learned
