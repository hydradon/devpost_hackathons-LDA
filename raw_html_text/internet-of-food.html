Inspiration

I excited about the Internet of Things. I wanted to make an Internet of Food.

How it works

We connect a wire a cup, utensil, or food. All of these are conductive. We attach a wire to the person so are ground. When the food touches a person's lips, the computer records that a bite has been taken. Nutritional value is looked up and displayed. This system helps a person keep a balanced diet.

What I learned

I learned about pull-up resistors and implemented a moving window averager.

What's next for Internet Of Food

Keep logs of what what you eat and recommend food for a balanced diet.
