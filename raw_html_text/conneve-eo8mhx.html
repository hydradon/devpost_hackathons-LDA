Inspiration

Seeing as people are looking for public compatriots to participate in activities with. This was especially noticed during orientation activities where people were introducing themselves to a crowd of people and talking about their interests and hoping that they would find someone else in the crowd with similar interests.

What it does

This is a very personalised social networking cross platform app in which you make event based aquaintances. No matter who your, you can look for an activity and if it already exists then you are a part of it. For using app user simply type the details of event he is looking for and based on the search he finds other people who are looking for the same kind of event.

How we built it

We have created a cross platform module/app based on HTML, CSS, JS and PHP. We sought to think about different ideas and finalised some and then finally came down to this.

Challenges we ran into

We had problem in some areas while rethinking logic or coding some parts.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We could start from a concept to a fully working module in less than 36 hours.

What we learned

We learned the benifits of working in a team to provide a web app.

What's next for Conneve

Conneve is at a very embryonic stage right now, and its potential can includes a more intricate event matching algorithm, an interface with more layers, and the possible addition of an activity control center where there can be widgets and more information displayed.
