Inspiration

Wanting to help other people deal with their cardiovascular issues after a close family member passed away suddenly and unexpectedly from a heart attack years ago.

Elevator Pitch

Heart.AI is a modern, intuitive way for patients and doctors to easily monitor, manage, predict, and communicate cardiovascular vitals and issues through a web app, mobile app, and machine learning!
