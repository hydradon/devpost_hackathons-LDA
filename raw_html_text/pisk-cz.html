Inspiration

Presence of high level corruption in daily lives of people in Czechia and Slovakia.

What it does

Creates a secure, encrypted, private, and anonymous communication channel between journalists and their sources/whistleblowers. Establish highly trained group of journalists in cyber security and OPSEC.

How I built it

We form team of skilled System Administrators, Lawyers and Developers to deploy solution and prepare for future development.

Challenges I ran into

Technical set up was the most challenging part in the given time.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Creation of PISK team and PoC of SecureDrop for Czechia and Slovakia.

What I learned

Current state of security and security awareness in CZ and SK is worse than perceived.

What's next for Písk.cz

  • translations of service + SecureDrop documentation
  • creation of OPSEC checklist for journalists and whistleblowers
  • kick-off meeting with NGOs and media orgs
