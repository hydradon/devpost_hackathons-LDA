Inspiration

I have a grand dad who has been diagnosed with a cardiovascular disease. Since, I am busy with my work I cannot monitor his food intake all the time.

What it does

You, as a son, need to add Watbot and your grand dad in a Whatsapp Group. All your grand dad needs to do now is upload time-to-time images of the food he's eating. Watbot would then try to recommend the type of food you should eat based on your age, disease and personality.

How we built it

It's built on a Whatsapp backend using a python framework. Uses personality insights from Watson, Text-to- Speech API of Watson and food nutrients API

Challenges we ran into

Image processing of the food to analyze it

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Image processing of the food is accurate!

What we learned

Never give up

What's next for watBot

Extend to multiple diseases and send timely reminders /calls to the concerned person.
