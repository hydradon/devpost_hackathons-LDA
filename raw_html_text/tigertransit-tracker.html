Inspiration

The existing eta prediction functionality was dangerously misleading, but only if you wanted accurate information. So, we decided to do our part in the fight against the post-factual-era, and applied machine learning to predict bus ETAs. Afterall as Steve Jobs said, "If you can't trust your bus, then you can't trust your wife." And I think that's beautiful.

What's next for TigerTransit Tracker
