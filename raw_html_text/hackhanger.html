Inspiration

The aim of HackHanger is to ease the workers to locate their products in a significant space. It also helps the companies to predict the next trends by knowing the most sold products.

What it does

HackHanger gives information such as the number of hangers in stock, the quantity of misplaced hangers,the type of the cloth (jeans, t-shirt). The object also counts the frequency the hanger has been removed in a period.

How I built it

HackHanger is made of plastic printed with an 3D printer and bread board.

Challenges I ran into

Create the hardware, succeed the communication between the databased and HackHanger.
