Why SquadShare? SquadShare is a unique application that resolves the real problems of shared expenses. Your information is secure! Supported and monitored by Capital One, one of the largest bank in US! Everyone is risk free! No more one roommate pays for everything, a virtual joint account takes care of you all!

Who are we? Our names are Nick Clark, Zachary Geier, Neal Mangaokar, and Jiayi Wang. Four passionate Virginia Tech Computer Science students. As juniors and seniors, we love and appreciate the VT community. We want to use the opportunity of VTHacks to build an application that truly benefits everyone. That's how SquadShare was created! Our take on a much needed financial app, especially among college students.
