Inspiration

We wanted to create a strong college network that wasn't diluted by others not a part of our college.

What it does

Connects alumni and students through a common bond of school. Allows any student and any alumni to message each other and view each others profile. Displays work positions and past experiences because username creation is dependent on linkedin data.
