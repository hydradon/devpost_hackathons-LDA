Inspiration

I've had a keyboard in my house for many years and have always wanted to make something with/for it.

What it does

Multiple modes including play, learn, and a DDR style mode allow a user to learn or just have fun. Users can fight off stage fright by practicing in crowded venues or ignore an audience by loading in a solitary setting like a peaceful room or even mars.
