Safe Water

Drink pure water at proper times

An Android application which calculates daily water amount for you and reminds you when to drink water based on your fixed schedule. If you are not sure your bottled water is safe to drink, it can help you with that. This application has data about more than 50 water brands and more will be added soon.

Data source: Quarterly report (Jul-Sep, 2015) on bottled water quality, Pakistan Council of Research in Water Resources ( www.pcrwr.gov.pk )
