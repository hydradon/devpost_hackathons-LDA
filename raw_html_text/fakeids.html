FakeIDs

FakeIDs helps you keep record of the fake personal information you provide online. When a spam or harassment call finds you referencing fake information you provided in the past, you can find out who you submitted the information to, which is likely the source of information leakage.

Usage

You need Sqlite3.exe to operate the databases. Download sqlite tools from: https://www.sqlite.org/2019/sqlite-tools-win32-x86-3300100.zip

Configure the PATH variables in FakeIDs.jl before running. FakeIDs only works for Chrome-like browsers. In case of Google Chrome, set BROWSER_DB_PATH to %LOCALAPPDATA%\Google\Chrome\User Data\Default\Web Data

Run FakeIDs by Julia FakeIDs.jl and follow the instruction on screen.
