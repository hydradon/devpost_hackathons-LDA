Inspiration

At Space Nyx, we believed that every active android gamer deserved to experience space as a Space Destroyer!

What it does

Space Nyx is a multiplayer Space Inspired app which allows 2 players to destroy asteroids as they fly through the galaxy trying to beat family and friend to be ultimately called "Destroyer of the Galaxy.

How we built it

Space Nyx was built with the crucial use of Unity and the programming language C#.

Challenges we ran into

Getting the game to implement the Multiplayer function as well as creating the GUI was a great challenge for our group.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We believe that accomplishing the overall idea of our game and making it into reality was a great accomplishment.

What we learned

We learned that coding is an extremely precise and important tool needed for the future of our world, as well as space of course.

What's next for Space Nyx

In the future, we would like to eventually include compatibility with Oculus and other VR headsets, not just Google Cardboard. Eventually, we also would like to implement a Bluetooth 4.0 functionality so that Space Destroyers can play the game offline as well and enjoy it to its full potential.
