Inspiration

Promote reading and recycling. Every time you donate a book or a book is taken a tree will be planted by either you or anybody!

What it does

Provide an easy and quick way for students to find used/new books around campuses, accessible locations where public shelves can be found

How I built it

using web technologies like Node, React, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.

Challenges I ran into

How to make books accessible to find and share.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The platform will allow you to submit a book manually using a form Every book in our database will be listed immediately A map is provided to locate specific shelves across campuses.

What I learned

Work with great people and learned amazing skills

What's next for Treenating

A lot, build shelves with a scanning system to upload books faster and more efficient. Provide messaging services between students to communicate Promote trees! We love trees right?!
