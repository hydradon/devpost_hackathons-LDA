Inspiration

Across the world, people buy and own rental homes in hopes to have stable assets and passive income; however, rental homes can become highly expensive and time intensive to manage. One of the most painful problems for independent rental home owners is to fix a broken air conditioner. This expensive and time consuming event can be easily avoided if the tenants change the air filters regularly, but it is hard to motivate tenants to do so.

What it does

With AC Angel, you can manage your properties, see the current state of your air filters, and schedule for regular shipment of new filters.

How I built it

AC Angel is built on angular with bootstap 4.2.1 for styling.

Challenges I ran into

We had issues getting the framework setup for the both of us, cutting into about half of our total hack time.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We are proud of the way it looks and the fact that we were able to host it on live.

What I learned

Not to use visual studio for team projects.

What's next for AC Angel

Set up auto payments, manage home lawns, lightbulbs, and wall paint.
