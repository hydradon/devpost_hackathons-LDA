Inspiration

Wanted to create the most innovative bomb and put up a combination of gyroscope aided maze solving on a phone :)

What it does

Let's you solve a maze by using the gyro of your phone. However you don't see the maze only your friends with the instructions got that info. So you have to communicate with each other to solve the challenge quickly!
