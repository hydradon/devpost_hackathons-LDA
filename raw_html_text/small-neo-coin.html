Inspiration

Small NEO is an NEP-5 token buit on the NEO network that only has 1000 coins in its entirety. 

We created Small NEO to learn more deeply about building a cryptocurrency from scratch. We have extremely 
limited supply. We built our token with only 1000 coins to build in scarcity.

What it does

Smart Contract ICO, Mining, buy/Sell Coins

How I built it

Neo Node.js

Challenges I ran into

Creating Blockchain on private Net to TestNet, hosting on Google Cloud Service. Setting up SmallNeo Wallet and Coin distribution.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Creation of a new Cryptocurrency.

What I learned

Everything.

What's next for Small-Neo-Coin

Possibility of inflation or staking Structured incentivization for coin holders Potential to integrate masternode payout system for charity into the coin.
