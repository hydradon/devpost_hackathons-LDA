Inspiration

with the growth of green feeling, people are more aware of environment issues. New devices let us read contents but there are still book lovers. How can we help them enjoying reading books, while protecting the environment ?

What it does

Our solution helps people to share their books in a geographical area, by exchanging a token. At first you have to buy a token that let you take a book. Every time you give a book you are given a token.

How we built it

We have a front with react, Ox is used to exchange Ether with our tokens.

Challenges we ran into

We have been changing our ideas several times because the use of blockchain was not necessary. We have been exploring many technologies, and once we chose our project, we realized that all of them were not needed.
