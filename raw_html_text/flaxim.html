Flaxim aims to make allergies safer. The product is a range of adjustable wristbands embossed with the name of an allergy, a switch the allergic person can push in case they've been exposed to the allergen or are experiencing an allergic reaction and are unable to communicate the situation, and a QR code bystanders can scan in an emergency to get information on the allergy and contact details for parents, friends and/or emergency services.
