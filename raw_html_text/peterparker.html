Inspiration

What's the most stressful thing about driving in Montreal? Finding available parking. Clearly.

What it does

-Gets your location and selects the nearest parking spot for you.
-Gets the ID from an NFC tag to identify in which parking spot you are.
-Can send you notifications if you have to move your car for whatever reason (weather, towing, closing hours, etc.). (Not fully functional)
