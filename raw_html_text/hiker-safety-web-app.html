Inspiration

Over 120 people go missing in US national parks alone, and we wanted to stop that.

What it does

A hiker (or group) will register with our app, and we will take in their basic contact information, emergency contacts, and trip dates. If a hiker does not check out by their date, a missing person report will be filed and their emergency contacts will be notified.

How we built it

We built it using Flask, Angular.js, and 3 hours of sleep.
