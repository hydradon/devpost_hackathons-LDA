• Caution alert popup on HUD display when driver has driven over 5 hours consecutively.

Inspiration

Toyota HUD displays

What it does

After driving 5 hours straight, an alert popup appears on the HUD advising drivers to pull over to rest.

How we built it

Parse and analyse Toyota data in Kibana and web application for advised times to rest, SDL/HMI virtual box for HUD cautionary alert popups.
