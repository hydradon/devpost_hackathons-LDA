What it does

CardiOlympics uses your heart rate to race with your friends by matchmaking with Game Center. The faster your heart rate goes, the faster your character runs.

How I built it

  • Heartrate came from Apple Watch
  • Matchmaking using Game Center
  • Sprite Kit for gameplay

Challenges I ran into

Coordinating multiple iPhones and Apple Watches to create gameplay conditions

Built With

  • iphone-sdk
