Inspiration

Seeing too many smaller organizations fall by the wayside due to lack of incentive to keep going.

What it does

Provides a platform for small organizations to showcase their outreach to an ecosystem of other organizations with similar philanthropic interests.

Challenges we ran into

A team member's laptop went down during the first night. The app deploys but did not feel like loading into app-root.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are most proud of the fact that we came here without an idea and we were able to brainstorm on the fly and not only come up with an effective solution to a prevalent problem but also complete the implementation.

What we learned

We all worked with technologies we have not worked with before. As such, we learned from HTML to frontend with angular 2 to backend with firebase.
