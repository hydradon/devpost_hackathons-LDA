Inspiration

I have Magic Leap AR Glasses and I wanted to integrate an Ethereum Management Interface to: Eth Price in various Currencies Check Eth wallet balance Send and receive Ethereum money from various wallets to various wallets. Create Ethereum Private and Public Keys Sign Private Image Data messages with Ethereum keys. Sign Private Text Data messages with Ethereum keys. Open an Ethereum Wallet Send and Receive Ethereum from Wallet
