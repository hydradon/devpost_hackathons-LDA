Education is the only way that kids in underprivileged areas can change their lives as well as their community. However, many compassionate people around the world cannot find a solid way to lend out a helping hand, due to the lack of supporting channels and trusted social institutions. Founded by Henry Hu, a Computer Science student from U of T and Peter Xie, a Business graduate from Ivey, HopeDapp utilizes its own cryptocurrency to provides a trusted, traceable and peer to peer channel to deliver people’s help to these kids.
