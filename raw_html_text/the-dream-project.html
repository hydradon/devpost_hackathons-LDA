As part of the International Development Hackathon, we tried our hand at creating a prototype that we could eventually deploy in actual Dominican Republic classrooms as part of The DREAM Project. Given the exhaustive amount of manual work that currently goes into digitizing attendance and performance records from the Deportes Para La Vida program, our work could have an immense effect on the programs' efficiency. As a result, we used React Native, JavaScript, and Sketch to create a mockup of an app that could digitally record in-class records and then push it efficiently to a central database.
