Challenges we ran into

  • We accidentally broke wires and had to solder them back in place.
  • Everything broke.
  • We cannibalised a robot.
  • We were unable to provide enough power to the servo motors from the GPIO pins on the Raspberry Pi
  • We had to run instructions from the Raspberry Pi through a Serial interface into the Arduino, which we then used GPIO to power the servo motors.
  • University firewalls blocking IRC!?
