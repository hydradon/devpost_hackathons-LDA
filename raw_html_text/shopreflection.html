My wife loves shopping, I hate it, I wish she did it online but according to her, online shopping is a frustrating and boring experience. => we had to solve that.

The word "reflection" refers to the mirror reflection when you find yourself beautiful. It captures this very special moments for any shopping lover. [dot]club brings the intimate social finish that you get when going shopping with your friends..

ShopReflection.club re-invents the online shopping experience, bringing social back into the picture and letting users try clothes or shoes online through a face-mapping technique. ShopReflection.club also disrupts the delivery and return experience by making them seamless. ShopReflection.club reflects the true process of shopping.

We built ShopReflection.club using the classical web stack (jquery, js, json, css, html), plus Magnet api for chat, Zalando and Walmart for the content, IBM-Watson for face recognition, and Clover for the payment system.
