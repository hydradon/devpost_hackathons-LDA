Inspiration

Hands free instructional cooking, like a cooking assistant helping you with the recipe.

What it does

Verbally tell the app what ingredients you have and get recipes for that ingredient.

How we built it

Using Android Developer/Java and Nuance API.

Challenges we ran into

Verbal ingredient identification, some ingredients did not get picked up correctly.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting it to work.

What we learned

What's next for Voice 2 Food

-Step by Step verbal instructions for cooking. -Inventory system that keeps track of your ingredients in your pantry and displays recipes that can be made with those ingredients.
