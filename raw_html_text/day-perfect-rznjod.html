Inspiration

My mom always reminds me of drinking more water even if she is thousands of miles away from me, so I thought why can't we build a simple app that does things much easier (with proactive health tracking and hardware integration)

What it does

It keeps track of water intake for one person in a day and 4 other healthy habits with a man shaped figure

How I built it

Originally started with an Edison board and many sensors, but eventually the project was reduced to an single iOS app with HealthKit integration due to hardware failure

Challenges I ran into

Getting data from HealthKit Making subtle animation hardware issue coding for super long hours giving up good ideas for a on time submission

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I learned HealthKit from scratch and successfully made an HealthKit supported app I survived my first hackathon

What I learned

There is never too much to learn, and never too many people to learn from

What's next for Day Perfect

new features, better design, more software oriented, publish to the App store
