TheHive

A peer-to-peer big data processing network - get paid for renting out your PC when you're not using it This is more of a concept at the moment, however the fundamentals are all in place

Create an account on the website and either download the app to rent out your computer, or choose your plan and submit the program to run

The idea is to create a 'hive' network of workers who can run small threads from large programs and get paid to do it. The developers then pay the hosts for their service
