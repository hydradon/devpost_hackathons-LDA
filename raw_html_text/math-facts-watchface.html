math-facts-watchface

We wanted to create the perfect Pebble watch-face for every mathematics nerd. Each minute, our product displays math trivia associated with the current time. So at 9:41 PM, our watch might read "941 is the smallest number which is the reverse of the sum of its proper substrings," and 9:42 you might find out that "942 is the smallest number whose cube contains five 8's." With our innovative watch-face, every minute of your day can become a learning moment.
