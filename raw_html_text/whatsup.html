Inspiration

How people second guess what they tweet because of scrutiny, and impact social media has in our day to day lives

How it works

The user's tweets only show up to him or herself. As a way of remaining independent of the public. Effectively personalizing the social network without taking away from the experience

The user also recieves personal reminders to help them keep track of their day to day lives and has shortcuts to instagram, facebook, phone calls, and text messaging.
