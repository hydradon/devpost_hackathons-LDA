Inspiration

Inspired by the difficulty and creativeness of Flappy Bird and Cat Mario

What it does

A challenging game that inspires the users to keep on trying and maintain focus and concentration. It also tests the limits of human coordination while training the users' patience.

How we built it

Using Pygame library with Python
