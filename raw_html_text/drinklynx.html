How it works

The android app determines the 4 corners of the view and then gets the bars and their locations from the rails backend. Then it gets the specials for each bar.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned

How to build a rails api, our team, and to be successful in a hackathon

What's next for DrinkLynx
