Inspiration

In today's world where banks incur loss when their customer who takes wrong decision in investment and goes bankcrupt, it is very essential for the bank to know which loans will be possibly repayed.This is also for the bank which deal in multi domain like investing in property which gives them long term profit.

What it does

The system predicts which property investment will be beneficial and which will suffer loss. It also gives recommendation about investment which will maximize the profit

How I built it

Built it on jupyter using python and scikit learn

Challenges I ran into

Challenge to bring high accuracy in a real world and complex data.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

If this idea will be implemented, it will avoid lot of banks wrong decision to suffer loss
