• Bubble Breaker in action, adding alternate views to a Breitbart article

Inspiration

The election and the following shock.

What it does

While reading political news, Bubble Breaker will suggest articles from different perspectives.

How we built it

Built a chrome extension with a database of news sources with known political affiliations, queried when the user opens a webpage.

Challenges we ran into

Waffling on an idea until 3pm Saturday caused some issues.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Building something with real relevant impact on the country

What we learned

To man up and choose an idea.

What's next for Bubble Breaker

We want to add Facebook news feed changes in a way that doesn't excessively disrupt the regular news feed algorithm.
