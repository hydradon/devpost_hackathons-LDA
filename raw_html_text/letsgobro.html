What it does

LetsGoBro generates your next trip to a random country; by combining SkyScanner, Google Maps and Yelp we're able to generate the most interesting trip so you don't have to!

Why?

Sometimes we feel like exploring another country but we may not have an idea where to start. That's how this concept was born -- a product that fully generated a trip to an unknown and exciting place including places to visit!

How we built it

Node.js on the back and bootstrap in the front.

Challenges we ran into

Combining all the APIs and reaching their limits too easily.
