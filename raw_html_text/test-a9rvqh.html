Ever needed to send something to a friend, but didn't want to pay high shipping fees? What about finding out someone just came back from somewhere and thought to yourself, "man, I wish I'd known, I'd have sent something with them"?

With Deliver Me , you can connect with others going where you want things to do.

Deliver Me is a blockchain based courier client leveraging and combining individuals free movements with the Gig economy.
