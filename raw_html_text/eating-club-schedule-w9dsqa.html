Inspiration

It is often tedious for Princeton students to discover the schedule of events of the eating clubs on specific nights given that the status changes on a nightly basis.

What it does

My site/app should in theory enable Princeton students to discover the status of eating clubs on any given night. It will require cooperation from eating club officers.

How I built it

I built this project using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, and Google software.

Challenges I ran into

A challenge in realization of this project will be cooperation from eating club officers. I ran into difficulty importing the values of status from Google Sheets with JavaScript into the web page.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am proud of the aesthetic appearance of this site. It is also mobile-optimized and can easily be realized in mobile application form.

What I learned

I improved upon my JavaScript skills and learned how to use the Google API.

What's next for Eating Club Schedule

I look forward to deploying this project with the cooperation of relevant campus and eating club leaders.
