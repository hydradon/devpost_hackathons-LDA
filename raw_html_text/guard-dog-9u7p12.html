Inspiration

Keep life's dangers in the Dog House

What it does

Runs in background, monitoring frames of accelerometer data. Runs these frames through a pretrained machine learning algorithm. If it determines that the acceleration in the past few seconds is cause for concern, it will prompt you with a dialog box asking if you are okay. If you fail to respond, it will reach out to your "Guard Dogs" – people who you have registered with the app as those you would like to keep informed of your safety. It can also place a phone call to one emergency contact.
