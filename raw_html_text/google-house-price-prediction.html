Inspiration

My uncle had to sell his property in a haste due to medical emergency due to that he had no time to scan market for how much worth his property had due to that he sold his property at way less price.

We looked at that scenario and tried to come up with an app that can easily predict price of your house in seconds. We did this by preprocessing kaggle's house price prediction dataset and training it on colab using tensorflow and keras. we didnt had the time to complete its implementation on dialog flow but we will complete it in future. we got a testing accuracy of 93.5%
