Inspiration

We asked if companies could love, and a robot told us the answer.

What it does

This tool grabs a corpus of company posts from facebook's graph-api and performs text analysis to determine the overall sentiments of the company's online presence. Then a simple algorithm is applied to determine if the company can "love".

How we built it

Pain and hardship.

Challenges we ran into
