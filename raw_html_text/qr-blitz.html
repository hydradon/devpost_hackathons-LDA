A fun exercise/math focused game made for young kids.

We believe it would be a lot of fun if added to City of Ottawa parks and other public spaces.

Choose a difficulty, click start, and you'll hear a voice command for which QR code to scan.

After you scan each one correctly, you'll receive instructions on which colour/number to scan next.

Compete with your friends for the best time!
