Inspiration

We had a mini projector, a face shield, and a dream, that one day no one would ever bike map-less again.

What it does

Heads Up is a hands-free GPS and speedometer for bikers.

How we built it

We attached a mini projector to a helmet with a Raspberry-Pi to display a web-app and connect to your phone through Bluetooth to gather information about speed and location.

Challenges we ran into

The face shield being too clear. Angling a projector just right. Never being able to connect to the internet.

What's next for Heads Up
