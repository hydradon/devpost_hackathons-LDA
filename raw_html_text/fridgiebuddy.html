Inspiration

Have you wondering what to cook for today but don't know what's in your refrigerator? I have. So, I want an app that could help me keep track of food in my fridge and also suggest me what food I could make out of them.

What it does

A app that tells you what you have in your refrigerator and suggest you with some recipe

How we built it

We build it with android studio.

Challenges we ran into

Get data from wegmans API and store them in sql.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We spent a lot of time making the UI. Also, we learnt how to build a sql database.

What we learned

How you use wegmans API, how to edit database in sql.

What's next for FridgieBuddy

Add functions that let user buy all the materials they want from wegmans by one click.
