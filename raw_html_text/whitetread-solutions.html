WhiteTread Solutions looks forward to keep a green-production.

What it does

Through the Pro-Green Certification, we verify that the consumption of resources is the minimum necessary to operate.

The benefits for companies with a Pro-Green Certification are:

  1. Tax reductions
  2. Special prices for replacing old machinery
  3. Special discounts on energy bill
  4. Constant Monitoring of your resource expenses
  5. Positive impact in the environment around them

How we built it

Also, we can help your company to reduce the cost of production by constantly analyze and detect anomalies from data gathered on demand from your machinery.

All this is possible using industrial on demand software PREDIX, provided by our main partner General Electric.
