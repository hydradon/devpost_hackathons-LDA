Inspiration

A suggestion made at this year's CSSoc annual meeting was to create a mobile app for the forum. I was also slightly interested in learning about development on the Apple platform.

What it does

Loads the UOIT CSSoc's forum website and can navigate around

How I built it

Using Swift, compiled with Xcode 9.1 on macOS Sierra 10.12.6

Challenges I ran into

It was my first time coding with Swift, let alone creating an iOS app

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Finished a really simple version that works (for now)

What I learned

A very tiny bit of iOS Swift programming in Xcode

What's next for CSSoc Forum iOS App

More features/functionality, better app design/UI and navigation?
