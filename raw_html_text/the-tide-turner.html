Inspiration

The theme of sustainability and the ocean made me think about a program that could help make the oceans cleaner.

What it does

The Tide Turner is an informational and interactive map of California that displays a few of its beaches. The user can click on location markers that mark these beaches and find out the average pounds of trash that's cleaned up there per year, which organization is responsible for cleaning that beach, and the organization's cleaning schedule and contact information. Then the user can simply tap anywhere to return to the map of California.

How I built it

I used Unity and objects within Unity to make a "game" which is really just an interactive map. The location markers for the beaches use scripts written in C# to display the information to the user.

What's next for The Tide Turner

The Tide Turner would definitely expand past just the coast of California. Ideally, The Tide Turner would be applicable to anybody who is willing to help clean the nearest shore or even other bodies of water. In addition to providing data, The Tide Turner would also display a live feed of the quality of respective body of water so that the user can see the amount of bacteria present and see if it is safe for people.
