• Salaam - Your voice matters!

Inspiration

I was walking down the street when suddenly I heard somebody coursing. I turned my head. He was sitting in the front seat pointing his middle finger at me and wishing me to die. I stayed relaxed and ignored him. But I had a fire burning inside me and I really wished that I could scream that moment to the world. That was when I come up with Salaam. A platform for the victims of Islamophobia and racism to share their experience and let their voice be heard anonymously.

What it does

Salaam is an app that users post about their personal experiences with racism and Islamophobia anonymously to rais awareness about racism and Islamophobia.

How I built it

I build in a course of 3 weeks using swift and firebase.

Challenges I ran into

The hardest part was making the posting code work.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am proud of what I have a learned from this experience and from the App that I made.

What I learned

I learned about Swift and Firebase and the App Store process.

What's next for Salaam

I will be adding more features like texting and commenting
