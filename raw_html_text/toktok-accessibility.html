Inspiration## Inspiration

TOKTOK Accessibility for ALL: To increase knowledge and inclusiveness on FINANCIAL LITERACY, EDUCATION & ENTERTAINMENT. . Nearly Two-Thirds of Americans Can't Pass a Basic Test of Financial Literacy These findings come from the National Capability Study by the FINRA Foundation.

“This research underscores the critical need for innovative strategies to equip consumers with the tools and education required to effectively manage their financial lives,” said FINRA Foundation Chairman Richard Ketchum.

In the Accessibility Community unemployment rate, joblessness overall is higher than any other.

We live in a visual world; a world, where information dissemination by financial institutions are more in pictures than in words.

This situation leaves very little opportunity for the visually impaired/blind people who are also need to know financial products, services and banking.

Simply put, individuals with disabilities are twice as much to live in below poverty level than their non disabled colleagues.

Well informed and educated individuals are more prone to make better decisions, manage assets and their lives.

Globally, it is estimated that approximately 1.3 billion people live with some form of vision impairment.

TOKTOK will expand to Entertainment as well. Just imagine the Baseball Game and now eSports.
