The idea of Sugoi Smash birthed while playing around with borrowed hardwares from SDHacks 2015. With the interest to further explore the Oculus, Sugoi Six took the courage to do something none of the members had experiences with, virtual reality. We have combined the Oculus Rift and the Leap Motion to create a virtual relity system with 3D motion control which will bring your gaming experiences to a new level.
