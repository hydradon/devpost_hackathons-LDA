Inspiration

This summer, after work one day, my friends car battery had died. Neither of us knew how to jumpstart a car and had to call my dad for help. We had the tools, but we didn't have the knowledge.

What it does

This app provides information about symbols on the dashboard, who to contact when in need of help based on location, and will soon include games that walk the user through basic vehicle repairs such as changing a tire or jumpstarting a car.

How we built it

Android Studio

Challenges we ran into

Displaying images and messages that would pop up for each icon.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Building this app and figuring out how to overcome the challenges faced.

What we learned

How to create an app in android studio.

What's next for Guide to Gears

-Build out the help page more -Create the games -Add more icons
