EvoHub

EvoHub was built to help electric vehicle owners to cater for their vehicles . It gathers list of available charging outlets and technicians. It also provides maps to users and even an emergency service to be used in case of unforeseen occurrences.

EvoHub also tries to assist owners on the working condition of their vehicles by providing critical information while using the app . Info such as Battery status , Weather Condition , Date and other telematics . Mongodb helped greatly in building the backend of this app as it provided several services that were used .

Note : This project is yet to be completed hence the app might contain some unseen bugs
