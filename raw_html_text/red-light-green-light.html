• Play the family game red light green light, for hours of fun.

Inspiration

Saw the invitation on Saturday, January 6, 2018. Wanted to start creating Alexa skills. Thought of a game that my 5 and 6 year-olds could play. Ask them if they would like to play red light green light. They were excited and said yes.

What it does

Guides children as they play red light green light in the home. The game reminds children to walk in the home.

How I built it

I used Alexa sample skills to come up to speed and see what type of commands were necessary.

Challenges I ran into

Being new to the Alexa skills development space, coming up to speed on the commands.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Started and completed the skill.

What I learned

I learned a ton. I also learned that I have a ton more to learn.

What's next for Red Light Green Light

Make it better.
