What it does

Using the Amadeus Travel API Catalog we're able to find the most popular and cheapest flights anywhere in the world that are within the budget you set yourself.

What's next

After selecting where you're going we suggest hotels within your budget and food/attractions nearby. Selecting each item adds it to your itinerary and detracts from your budget. Once you're happy with your trip, just click book!
