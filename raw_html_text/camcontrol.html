Inspiration

The latest craze in Hollywood is KIRA: a state-of-the art camera robot that is responsible for everything from Thor: Ragnarok to Dr. Pepper commercials. With precision and specs enabling KIRA to take shots that no human physically can, everything about the robot is cool except for its wildly unaffordable 250,000 price tag. We wanted to create a way for mere mortals - from small businesses to nonprofits to wedding photographers - to be able to create Kira-like footage for a fraction of the cost. Our project Cam Control leverages the same cutting-edge robotic tech behind stunning, professional photo shoots and makes it accessible to anyone with a smartphone.
