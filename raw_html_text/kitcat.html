Inspiration

You love your cat (maybe too much). But what do others think about him? Everybody knows a person that has a cat. Why not put them in touch? May the cutest win your heart!

What it does

Comparing one cats to another. Choose the one you prefer Get feedback on your pet (is he the cutest?) Connect with the master (maybe the perfect match!)

How I built it

AndroidStudio Firebase

Challenges I ran into

Connecting the app to firebase Fetching pictures from the web directly from the phone

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

My very first hackathon!

What I learned

How cool is programming!

What's next for KitCat

Let cat owners create their own account in order to upload their own cat pictures and see their cat rating. Let cat owners chat together and maybe find love!
