What it does

"Tewnty One Cards" presents 21 Blackjack. You can wage bets or play friendly games. Alexa will be the dealer of the game. You will be given 1000 gold coins (virtual) initially when you activate the skill. You can now wage bets and increase your winning streak and coins collection! You being a player will "Hit" the cards. The player to reach 21 points first, wins the game. You can stop at any moment, just say "Show" to reveal all cards. Now keep a track of the total gold chips won, rank on the leader board, richest leader board, games won, total games played, longest winning streak and more!

Sample Phrases:

  • Alexa, open twenty one cards
  • Wager a bet
  • Fifty percent / Ten percent / All in
  • Play practice game
  • Hit me a card
  • Show all cards
  • What is my rank?
  • Start a new game
  • Help me with the rules
  • Stop
