Inspiration

We, as students and coders are always looking for motivation. However, sometimes, it is hard to find one. We decided to create something that would help in our everyday life.

What it does

This is an application that breaks down tasks into smaller ones, so users feel less pressure in big projects. For example, instead of seeing a big essay, they will see 10 small tasks that they will be rewarded for.

How I built it

We used Javascript, Html, and CSS for our website and Java for the Android Application.

Challenges I ran into

We had very little experience with Javascript and CSS in our team.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We built a great variation of webpages. The whole team learned from the workshop.

What I learned

Everything that we did we learned throughout the course of the project.

What's next for Goals

Taking what we learned here and creating side projects in the future
