QuickSwitch

A tool to protect your privacy using google's face detector api

Basically it opens a "safe link" whenever someone tries to snoop on your screen. An easy way to protect your privacy when looking at sensitive information.

Easy to use and non-intrusive to your focus, this app silently keeps you protected

Features include:

toggling functionality -
personal url block
delay to close (in case a page is stuttering, increasing this value should help)
Number to abide - the number of people that are allowed to be looking at your screen
Exact abidation - exclusively that number of people to abide are allowed (no more, no less)
Closeness sensitivity - requires a minimum distance from the camera for your screen not to be blocked
(to turn this feature off, just set it to 0)
