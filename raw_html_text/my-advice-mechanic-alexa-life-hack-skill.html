• My Advice Mechanic - Alexa Skill

Inspiration

Car troubles are annoying. Wouldn't it be nice if Alexa could help?

What it does

My Advice Mechanic is a voice decision tree application for Amazon Alexa. With just a few Yes No questions, it'll find out what may be causing common car problems, like starting engine troubles, noise, and smoke.

How I built it

Amazon Alexa Skill Kit, JSON coding for intent capturing, and Javascript coding on Amazon Lambda

What's next for My Advice Mechanic - Alexa life hack skill

Maybe invite local auto shops to add more value-add content and services.
