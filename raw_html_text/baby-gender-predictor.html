Inspiration

Lack of a great experience for predicting a baby's gender in the Alexa Skills library

What it does

Predicts the baby's gender provided the mother's age at conception and month of conception (not 100% accurate) but is based on the ancient Mayan Gender Predictor Chart

How I built it

Using ASK-CLI and Alexa Presentation Language (APL)

Challenges I ran into

Different templates and solidifying the user experience to deliver an engaging skill using APL and voice

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The ability to publish an Alexa Skill with APL and voice support using the ASK-CLI

What I learned

How to develop voice and visual experiences for Alexa-enabled devices

What's next for Baby Gender Predictor

Publish it to the challenge and make it publicly available as an Alexa Skill
