Inspiration

Today, interactions with people close to you are harder to happen, due to physical and virtual distance. What if you could have a better interaction with your near neighbors? What if you don´t have to go much far to solve a problem, that someone close to you know how to do? What if ... whatever you want?

There is a huge need to increase interaction, markets and promote businesses for people nearby. That´s what we want to solve.

What it does

The Social Strata brings a quicker way to interact in communities, increasing their social Interaction with neighborhood, a closer interaction between tenants and landlords, unify groups in a closer area and promote events, business, news and notices.

How we built it

An application was built, using Ionic, Angular, Sass, Cordova accessing services built with C# WebApi, hosted in Windows Azure and with data store in Azure based Sql Server.

Challenges we ran into

In a small amount of time, we realized there were a huge scope, and we had to be extremely focused on designing and coding.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

A great design, and a prototype covering all the areas required, with few working functionalities.
