The idea came when one of our teammates, Shivam Karga, mentioned that he wanted to collaborate with other students in a building, but had no idea who was around. From there we created Proxichats.com. Using Here.com location software, we track your location and allow you to view other people within a short distance. This is extremely useful in study situations, talking to professors, hackathons, and even the outdoors. With this app, we unlock the ability to communicate with anyone nearby. We hope enjoy our project!
