We are college students who understand the struggle to stay awake during the day after long hard nights of studying and simply just love coffee. We also understand the struggle of being able to afford our needed caffeine fix. Did you know an average college student spends up to $30 a week on coffee? Well with our membership program we want you to be able to get the right amount of coffee at the right price. What's the catch? Be Loyal. Drink Coffee. We found our solution by using buildfire to create an app that keeps track of the amount of cups you drink with bundle deals so students can create a "Pipe'N Plan" to budget.
