• Jelly

Inspiration

Liquidity is an issue for many exchanges especially new ones.

What it does

We provide a solution to tap into other exchanges for more liquidity. These include dexes such as Kyber (on-chain solution) and 0x relayers.

How we built it

We built a trading view specifically to show how we can leverage alternative liquidity markets

Challenges we ran into

Testing Kyber on Kovan, building a depth chart, time (we fleshed out or figured out what we were going to show late)

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned!

What we learned

Better understanding of how relayers work, how to use Kyber, how MakerDai is used and created, and the engineering challenges to building a trading view / exchange.

What's next for Jelly

Integrating with more decentralized exchanges. Provide more stable coin swapping options.
