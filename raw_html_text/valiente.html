Inspiration

Allows others to speak up for themselves

What it does

On this platform emplyees can post anonymously their stories of discrimination or sexual assault/harrasment of any kind. In addition to this, users may identify the company or organization where the inncident occured. This way, companies and organizations will be aware and be pushed towards making a change or helping your case.

Valiente means brave in spanish because it is giving employees a chance to show their bravery by standing up for themselves without any consequences. More than half of people who go through sexual abuse in the workplace are too embarrassed or scared to speak up. Valiente can provide a feeling of security and strength to these victims.

Many people who have gone through sexual assault in the workplace change jobs but must live with their trauma. If 25,720 rapes and sexual assaults happen every year in the workplace then how many of those people do you think feel fear free of going back to work in an office or building? Employers can give these employees a feeling of security in working with them. This is why Valiente allows companies to make their own profiles where they can be notified when their company’s name is being used on the app. This gives them the chance to make a change not only in their company but in the rape culture that is based around areas of work.
