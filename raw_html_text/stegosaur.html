What it does

Typically, when you take a screenshot of an app— the things on the screen are frozen. What if it were possible to hit "play" on screenshots— and resume whatever app the screenshot was taken from?

How we built it

By adding a small Javascript library to your web application, you can hide your application state visually in the app. This information is preserved when a user takes a screenshot.

A special browser extension notices when you load a screenshot with an embeddable, resumable application and transparently loads it when you hover over the screenshot.
