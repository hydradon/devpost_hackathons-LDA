Overview

Ingo is a smart voice assistant based on Google Assistant and is aiming towards simplifying communication between local governments and citizens.

In the hackathon, we developed a voice assistant which helps users report vandalism in their community.

To get you a better idea of what we have been building for these past few days, please have a look at these prepared slides: https://drive.google.com/file/d/1N-pQ_g20AgIkvyRo06NBNpapgxVa2LD5/view?usp=sharing

Also, please note, that we will be submitting 2 prototypes!

We built a non-technical prototype to illustrate both our unique user-centric features and the business value behind Ingo. It will be the central part of our pitch.

Moreover, we would like to submit a technical-driven prototype (see link above)! We are excited to share the progress we have made!

What is next?

We used the "Hackadon" to kickstart an idea. After 1 weekend of hard and dedicated work, we are more excited and proud of the product we are building. Therefore, we are definitely looking forward to pursuing it more further.
