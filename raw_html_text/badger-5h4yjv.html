Inspiration

We want to nag our the person that did not submit their timesheets, did not clean their coffee machine, forgot the meeting invite.. Its a hackathon eh!!

What it does

Alexa to submit a request to nag the person in your office, and the task that they "forgot". Once submitted it will send a SMS to nag them to submit..

How we built it

Javascript, alexa-sdk, lambda, dynamo-db, SNS (to send SMS)

Challenges we ran into

Alexa UI changed and the workspace changed.. Old alexa builder no longer worked..

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Work with new Alexa Skill builder, SNS notification

What we learned

New alexa skill builder.

What's next for badger

Add a stop nagging functionality, configurable retries.
