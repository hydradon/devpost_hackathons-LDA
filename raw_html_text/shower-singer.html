Inspiration

I was in the shower, and I was like, I wanna sing right now but I have terrible memory and a Pebble. Hmmm.

What it does

Beams song lyrics right to your smart watch while the song is playing on your phone.

How I built it

Android sends notifications to pebble smartwatch on timed intervals to match up with song lyrics.

Challenges I ran into
