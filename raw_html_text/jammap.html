Inspiration

We've met some of our closest friends at Brown through informal jam sessions that took place in our lounge. We want to expand this musical experience throughout the entire Brown campus, meeting new cool cats, and learning new styles of music.

What it does

App that allows Brown musicians to schedule jam sessions and invite other students. Each user will have his/her own profile with their music background, music tastes, and videos of their performances.

How we built it

Swift/Xcode and Google Firebase.

Challenges we ran into

Not sleeping / adapting to Apple MapKit API.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Learning Apple MapKit and setting up Firebase

What we learned

Coding is hard. And rewarding.

What's next for JamMap

World domination. Kidding. Making individual profile accounts and allowing users to see each other's music tastes, interests, and experience.
