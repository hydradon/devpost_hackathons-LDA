iris

Making the world a more colourful place. Iris is a voice assistant for the visually impaired.

With the help of Amazon Alexa, iris is a voice assistant for the visually impaired.

Today, she can help identify which clothes to wear. Tomorrow, she could pick your whole outfit, tell you when to stop pouring water, and finding things around the house.

Simply invoke iris by saying Alexa, ask iris...
