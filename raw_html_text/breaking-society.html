We will use crowd sourced data to build profiles for organizations to help provide resources on a local level. The data inputted will be used to provide world wide statistics. Breaking Society will showcase this data to put pressure on local authorities and schools to make principle changes to reduce the occurrences and provide justice for the victims. Violence and sexual assault claims are often left unresolved due to social barriers and lack of support. Women and men are left helpless and often deal with mental health issues.
