Inspiration

I like anime and I really wanted to incorporate a real-life group game into an online game. I chose assassins because it sounds cool and fun.

What it does

Each player can move around and their goal is to assassinate their target. At the beginning, everyone's faces are covered so it is impossible to determine who is who. Once faces are revealed though, the assassination begins. Also, it is important to be able to memorize who's name matches who's face.

How I built it

node.js, socket.io and javascript

Challenges I ran into

node.js was har for me to use to be honest. I kept finding "mistakes" that slowed me down a lot.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Managing to get it online.

What I learned

Probably should learn more about a language before I decide to use it. Also should analyze mistakes more carefully.

What's next for Anime-Ssins
