Inspiration

When doing house work breaking out a tape measure was tedious and for long distances required 2 people, one to hold the end of the tape and the other person on the other end.

What it does

Using the hololens and spatial mapping, the app gives users the ability to measure distance, find mid points, or even fold paper perfectly in 1/2,1/4, etc....

How I built it

Used unity along with hololens

Challenges I ran into

Getting the development end up for hololens was difficult!
