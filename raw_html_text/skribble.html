Inspiration

We've always needed an easy way to write things down and sync to the cloud. Unfortunately, it has always been either one of those or the other.

What it does

The idea was to have a camera connected to the Android Things board on the notebook and sync it to a phone over Bluetooth, where it would use Google Cloud Vision to work with the information in the notebook with machine learning.

How we built it

Android Things, Swift, and kettle stitch bookbinding.

Challenges I ran into

Unfortunately, we couldn't get the Android Things board working right, which mostly derailed our whole project.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting all the hardware on the notebook!

What's next for skribble

Getting it to actually work.
