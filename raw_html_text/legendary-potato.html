legendary-potato

Overview

LegendaryPotato is a game designed to inspire anyone from children through adults to improve their math skills through an ELO system and leaderboards. In the same way that apps like Duolingo motivate people to learn languages through an increased challenge and weekly rankings, LegendaryPotato aims to push people to their mathematical peak .
