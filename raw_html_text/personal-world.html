Inspiration

It is so important to remember your close circle's birthdays hands-free. Our skill allows you to update, search or remove people in your "personal" world. It is easy, convenient, confidential and fast.

What it does

Adds, Removes and Searches for birthdays of the people you care about. Users can create their worlds by simple voice commands. The commands include "Birthday of Name", "Birthday for date", "Add birthday", "Remove Birthday For" and "Remove Birthday of". The skill also greets you, using your Amazon identity federation name.

How I built it

Inspired by Alexa Echo and Spot to simplify everyday tasks

Challenges I ran into

Certification, dialogs, federation authority, synonyms and confidentiality.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The skill helps real people from all over the world

What I learned

Slot disambiguation, Dialogs and mastering the latest Alexa SDK features

What's next for Personal World

Monetizing, expansion and world popularity
