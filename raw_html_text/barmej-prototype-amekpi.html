There is a lack in the availability of skilled computer scientists, programmers, and software developers in the Arab world. There is not much awareness about the importance of these skills neither in educational systems nor in businesses and organizations. Moreover, there are a few resources available in Arabic to help Arabic speakers learn programming, algorithms, developing software, etc. This puts the development and progression in the Arab world at a disadvantage with the rising potential and utilization of software in the rest of the world.

Barmej is a web application that provides the appropriate educational resources to schools, businesses, and organizations that lack the resources to computer science education. Our job is to introduce them to the great potential of software and help them incorporate it in their educational, administrative, or organizational systems.

We buillt a prototype of what we envision the finished product to look like using Adobe XD.
