How do hackathons affect the human heart?

We expect our hearts to work without any repercussions, yet heart disease is the leading cause of death for both men and women. Every year about 735,000 Americans have a heart attack.

From Red Bull to Five-Hour-Energy shots, from Potato Chips to Soylent, hackers' hearts are constantly overstimulated for 36 straight hours. GitPumped was conceived as a public reminder to the importance of our hearts.

Please keep your hearts safe.

This project was inspired by a recent heart attack suffered by an unsuspecting family member.
