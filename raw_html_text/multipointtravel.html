Inspiration

The Frustration involved in orchestrating every step of long trips

What it does

Hooks you up with the best connections from your proximity to the proximity of your destination

How we built it

With JavaEE, some web APIs such as Kiwi and Blablacar, and finally Vaadin as a frontend

Challenges I ran into

JGraphT, Integration and implementation of a graph model. API querying

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Installed Postgresql

What I learned

The difficulty of integrating several APIs in into a single service

What's next for MultipointTravel

Mashing up more and more services to give even more options to users
