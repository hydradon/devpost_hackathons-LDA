Inspiration

Two of the most unique and identifiable icons in the technology industry can't be kept separate forever.

What it does

Generates an out of this world image with you (in bitmoji form) and octocat by connecting to your Snapchat account.

What I learned

I learned how to use the Snapchat API and Oauth.

What's next for Gitmoji

A more general photo generating web app would be cool.
