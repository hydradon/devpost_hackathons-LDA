Inspiration

Tab for a Cause, FreeRice

How it works

Tap an object and level up as much as possible. To help level up, collect speed boost bonuses by looking at ads that support the charity of your choice.

Challenges I ran into

The time constraints made it difficult to ship a finished product. Several team members did not know how to develop any sort of app.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

None of the team members knew how to code an app at the beginning of the project. By the end, we had a completed app and website.

What I learned

We learned about how to manage a team, work with Arduino, and develop for the web and android.

What's next for CharityTap

The next step is to implement graphics into the game like are seen in the demo images, and add more involved mechanics to make the game more engaging.
