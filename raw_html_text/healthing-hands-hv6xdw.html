Inspiration

With the new year coming to a start, many of us have resolutions to become healthier individuals. We know its hard to tackle these goals alone, so we wanted to create something that would help us along our journey.

What it does

Our app allows people to connect with individuals around the world with similar goals surrounding fitness, healthy eating, mindfulness, and sleeping habits. They can find like minded people who they can connect with and start a conversation regarding their health path. Not only will they be able to keep each other accountable, but also learn new skills and develop healthier habits.

How we built it

We used Figma to design low and high fidelity wire frames combining user maps and flow.

Challenges we ran into

With a team of inexperienced developers, it was difficult to create a feasible technologically driven product. With the help of workshops, mentors, and each other, we learned some new applications!

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of designing an app that hopes to solve a current issue in society, and also learning a whole new application that we will definitely use in our futures.

What we learned

We learned that despite having limited knowledge in this field, with grit, perseverance and a willingness to learn, it will allow you to accomplish anything! :)

What's next for Healthing Hands

We hope to gain more experience in back end development to bring our idea to life!
