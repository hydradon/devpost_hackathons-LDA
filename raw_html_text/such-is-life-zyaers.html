The game, ‘Such Is Life’, is designed to spread knowledge in an exciting way, where kids from age 10-13 yrs old can enjoy learning morality without knowing. "Such Is Life" is a story mode game that teaches kids to make the most moral or immoral decision has an effect in the long run. It consists of five certain endings that may be good or bad based off the decisions you make during this game; the levels can range from millionaire to hobo and everything in between.
