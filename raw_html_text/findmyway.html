Inspiration

Solving problems for the rural population and coming up with some innovate idea concerning transportation was our main inspiration.

What it does

It let users customize their routes from an origin to a destination and then submit this customized route to a virtual transport marketplace. Interested parties like transport service provides, ride sharing services and private transport companies can subscribe to these submission and then contact the user in need and proceed accordingly.

How we built it

We used html/css/js front-end and php with mysql with as our back-end.

Challenges we ran into

WE HAD VERY LITTLE TIME. We learned about it late night on Saturday from a partner representative and thought we can present a MVP and a solid idea.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We came up with a MVP on such a short notice.

What we learned

A lot or almost everything relating to public services has tons of data available free online. Google maps integration and php.

What's next for FindMyWay

This is just a MVP. It has bare minimum functionalities just to present our idea and hence it can be extended into a fully functional product.
