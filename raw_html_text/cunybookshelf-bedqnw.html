Problem

CUNY Websites Suck! and the Library Database is No Exception! As a Lab Technician I have had multiple students ask me, "how exactly do I use this library Database" This! is where the problem lies! The use of the Database isn't intuitive moreover, it Lacks Responsive Design.

Our Solution

Seek No More!! Introducing CUNYBookshelf A mobile platform (iOS and Android) that allows students to Search books across all CUNY libraries, public libraries AND users with accurate status of the books in one stop It offers a lucrative option for students that are trying to sell their books to other students! Buy books from any available sellers & an option to purchase on Amazon.
