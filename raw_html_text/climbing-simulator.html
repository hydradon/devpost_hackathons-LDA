Inspiration

QWOP, Surgeon Simulator, Goat Simulator, I Am Bread, Ancient Frog, and things that are fun to do at the gym.

What it does

It's a fun game! You have to touch or click the character's arms and feet and move them to the higher holds in the wall. The wall keeps moving down so you have to keep up with it.

It's best on iPad, but it works on iPhone and Android.

How we built it

Unity, C#, Xcode,

Challenges I ran into

The Perlin noise was tricky

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Set up and used source control all by ourselves :)

What's next for Climbing Simulator

More features & polish. I think it's fun.
