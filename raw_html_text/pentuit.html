Inspiration

Everyday we experience small stints of time with nothing to do. Just to avoid the void, we find ourselves taking out our phones and scrolling through facebook or watching videos on YouTube.

Whether we are waiting for a bus, or just taking a break from work, this time could be used for something for more advantageous.

With that in mind, we created Pentuit with the purpose of providing "Five-minute lessons" that provide a medium to build up these short stints of time into comprehensive learning periods.

With a hierarchical system that prioritizes highly rated "approaches" for a single concept (based on community ratings), one can take steps on certain course paths to advance their knowledge in a particular field of study.
