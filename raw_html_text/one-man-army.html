• Quick Job

Inspiration

I was inspired by indeed.com and how it helps people find jobs.

What it does

This app shows people small time jobs they can get to earn extra money based off of what they like.

How I built it

I built it as a single page application in Backbone.JS and Bootstrap as well as other common web technologies such as javascript, css and html.

Challenges I ran into

It was hard for me to implement canvas animations becasue the loading of different parts of the application in Backbone.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am proud of the speed of the app as well as the Phone Gap compatibility.

What I learned

I learned that building a single page application is very important in making Phone Gap applications.
