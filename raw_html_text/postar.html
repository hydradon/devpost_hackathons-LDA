Inspiration

We wanted to create an "art gallery" with Augmented Reality Motion Art.

What it does

Augmented Animated Poster art on a phone app as well as Magic Leap

How we built it

We built the experience in Unity for MagicLeap and also a mobile phone version in EyeJackApp.

Challenges we ran into

  • Making sure the MagicLeap wouldn't play all the posters at once (because sensory OVERLOAD)
  • Not being able to use zero iteration on ML development due to use of the camera
  • Since ML is very new technology there wasn't a lot of use cases or blogs online to help with development
  • ML doesn't let let you record through the camera if you are using the camera for image recognition :(

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We got it working in the ML (which was our original stretch goal)

What we learned

  • image tracking in the ML
  • new animation techniques
