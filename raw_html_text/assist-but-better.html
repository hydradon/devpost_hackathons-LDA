Inspiration

We struggle to find transferable GE's at affordable prices, so we wanted to create something that would help complete this task easier.

What it does

It compares classes and prices and how they will transfer to your school.

How we built it

We wanted to make an Android app that looks nice and is easy to use. We attempted to web scrape for the data, but that didn't work out too well for us.
