How I built it

We utilized the Design Thinking approach to decide the idea and finalize prototype.

Step1: Empathize

We were inspired from the hackathon challenge areas and related with the areas. We were responding to the challenge from Northrup Grumman - Model Based System Engineering & IoT

Step 2: Define

Used creative techniques to create a communication hub for 2 or 3 sensors which can be controlled by an application on a central node within one day

Step 3: Ideate

  • Study break → Timer? --> discarded
  • Trash Can Level of trash Amount of trash Frequency of trash Gamify - competitive recycling/ gamifying / social elements, leaderboard - between friends, UC applications; comparison between trash & recycling; most popular spots on campus/cities

Decided to go with the idea of trash can

Step 4: Prototype

Tried integration sensors - temp, humidity, photoresistor, infrared, motion sensor, light Managed to achieve photoresistors, PIR + led , ultrasounnd sensor 2 approaches: one only with serial monitor; one with hosting to web server

Step 5: Test

Tested with button, photoresistor, PIR, ultrasound and infrared sensors
