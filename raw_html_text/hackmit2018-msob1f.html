MIT is a complicated place with a lot of buildings (most only labeled by their number) and increasingly more rooms, and the only way visitors and new students can get around is by a static map littered with numbers. This project works as a more advanced way to get from one place to another. When given a starting building room to a final building room, it finds the shortest route and gives directions.
