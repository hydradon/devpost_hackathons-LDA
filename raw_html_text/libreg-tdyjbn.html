LibReg

In this project, we use a raspberry pi to capture video and stream video to the server. Our server will extract every single frame and perform objection detection on this frame. One we found there's a person in the frame, we will try to perform face recognition on this person

Once an event is triggered, for example, one object is put down. Our system will try to judge who put it there and relate the person's identity to the object.
