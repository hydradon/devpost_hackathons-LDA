ImageX is an exchange which allows anyone to lease satellite imaging services anonymously. ImageX uses the zero-knowledge based private payment protocol of Quorum on Azure to create privacy preserving contracts between buyers and sellers of satellite imaging. The ImageX token, app and api protocols allow satellite owners to optimize their capacity without having to deal with payments and public buyers to purchase imagery time and spectrum without revealing their identity or activity.
