Inspiration

Cheap, environmental, and efficient way to exchange information with your fellow coworkers.

How it works

Uses NFC to send a virtual business card in the form of text data to another phone, and the other phone adds a contact with the first person's information.

Challenges I ran into

Sending NFC data between phones, accessing sent NFC data, and uploading a contact onto a phone through an application.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Efficiently sends NFC data back and forth and uploads that data onto your phone.

What I learned

NFC in Android, as well as phone-to-phone communication in general.

What's next for EzCard

Implemented in companies and corporations.
