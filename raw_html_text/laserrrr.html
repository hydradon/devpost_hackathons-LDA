Painting Walls with Lasers

What it does

A camera-attachment that emits a moving-laser to draw patterns. 

open_camera_shutter()
draw_with_laser(coordinates)
close_camera_shutter()

Inspiration

Dark night photography like

  • these rocks
  • this spiral
  • and this circlular blaze
