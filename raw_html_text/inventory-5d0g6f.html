Inspiration

Long story short, three kids in rural Texas struggled to find basketball nets in surrounding stores.

What it does

Its a product finder app! Given any item, the application will find the nearest store that contains the item, relative to the users location and price preferences.

How we built it

We used quite a number of API's to get the job done. I won't go into too much detail but we first prioritized getting really reliable data.We then focused on parsing and packaging the data, finally we worked our UI/UX magic.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Taking a stand against Amazon's online savagery.

What we learned

Photoshop, API, how to sit for long periods at a time, and much more.

What's next for Inventory
