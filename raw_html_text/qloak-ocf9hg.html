Inspiration

LGBTQ+ youth make up one of the largest percentages of the homeless population. And today's political climate poses a huge threat to the trans community. Imagine trying to call a hospital to see if they support transitioning under that pressure.

What it does

Qloak helps tackle these issues by integrating the relevant resources for the LBGTQ+ community, promoting access to and visibility of queer inclusive spaces.

How I built it

I built this app using react native for the front end with plans to add node.js functionality to the backend

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I'm proud of the navigational capabilities of the app

What I learned

This was the first time I attempted to fully build out an app with multiple necessary pages and data points, and I learned how truly difficult all of that can be.

What's next for Qloak

I'm planning on adding Node.js functionality to the backend to incorporate business data into the app, and from there, I'm currently working with a team to get the app fully launched and functioning
