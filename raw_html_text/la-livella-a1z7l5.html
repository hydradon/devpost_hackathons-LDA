Inspiration

Inspired by a great actor and poet: Antonio De Curtis

What it does

It leads you into the life of Antonio De Curtis and tells you many thing about him and what his contemporary thought about him. It also lets you listen to his most famous poem, "A Livella", and some of his other poems also. Naturally it has images and sounds.
