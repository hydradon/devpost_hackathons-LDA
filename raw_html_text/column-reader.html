Inspiration

A lot of distractions while reading online (Ads, subscribe info, related etc), and too much scrolling for long articles, alot of white space for wider screens, Need an easy and simple and lightweight way to read things online comfortably

What it does

A simple toggle button to declutters, simplify and maximize the total reading space of a user's screen to enable a more pleasant viewing experience. Easily revertible to original state.

How we built it

We based our project on the open-sourced Readability API from Mozilla Firefox and subsequently implemented our various features in javascript, html and css.

Challenges we ran into

Accomplishments that we're proud of
