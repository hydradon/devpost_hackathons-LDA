Inspiration

I'm currently having a tough time searching for someone to sublet my place for the summer.

What it does

Provides a platform to search and sublease your apartment

How I built it

We used a mongoDB database to store the users and the listings. We used freemarker as the template engine and jetty with spark to serve the webpages

Challenges I ran into

Integrating front end and backend

What's next for Sublease

More feature, better search, adding pictures
