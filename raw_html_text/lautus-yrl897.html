Our mission is to make it easier for the average citizen to practice proper waste management.

Did you know over 26% of recycling goes to wastelands in Toronto alone? People either don’t know or don’t have enough time to recycle. It's hard to find garbage in piles of recycling and it creates pollution and harms the environment.

Our solution is the Lautus sorting system; a guide for the user, helping them correctly separate their trash, recycling, and compost using the power AI assistance.

Benefits of using AI to sort garbage:

  • Quick and Easy, promoting correct sorting
  • Less garbage goes in the wrong place
  • Saves the environment
