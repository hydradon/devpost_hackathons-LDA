Turn-Right-AR

OverView of the Game

Turn Right AR is a 3D game with the support of AR and a beautiful gameplay with cool graphics and amazing sounds.

Game Instructions

If you touch the screen anywhere slightly then the car will turn slightly right but if you continuously touch the screen then the car will move in a circular path in the right direction. And don't step on the grass or go outside the tracks as you will lose. Make a HighScore and brag about it.
