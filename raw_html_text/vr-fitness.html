Inspired by 80s jazzercise videos, VR Fitness aims to motivate players to move their bodies and exercise. And it looks like an 80s screensaver! Also includes a seated mode for those who cannot exercise standing up. Obesity is a major challenge and making exercise fun and accessible is a good start.
