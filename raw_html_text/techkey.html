Inspiration

This project was inspired by the frustration that young people have when helping their parents and grandparents with simple technology. We found that there isn't one place to consistently get help with household technology, and a part of the trouble is finding that information effectively.

What it does

This web application would work on computers and mobile devices to provide an image centric hub for technology help. This way, people who do not know a lot about technology can find the help they're looking for quickly and easily.

How we built it

After some struggle with ReactJS, we decided to submit Wireframe models made on Figma to communicate our idea. There is also a mockup of the homepage made with HTML, CSS and Bootstrap.

Challenges we ran into

Our technical inexperience with building webapps hindered our ability to make a real, functioning mockup.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We're proud of the many things we've learnt over this weekend.

What we learned

ReactJS, Figma, collaboration.

What's next for TechKey

Making it an actual app, and including machine learning and computer vision to make it even more user friendly. Also, connecting with product manuals and making diagrams interactive.
