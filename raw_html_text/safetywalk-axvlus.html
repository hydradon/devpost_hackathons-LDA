We’re from Advokit and we’ve built a platform to empower victims of online harassment to take action when they’re abused.

  • 40% of internet users experience online harassment, particularly bad for women
  • women are almost 4x more likely than men to be stalked online
  • 2x as likely to be harassed online
  • far more likely than men to experienced sustained online harassment http://www.pewinternet.org/2014/10/22/online-harassment/

People deal with this stuff in different ways, from completely ignoring it to wanting to take serious legal action. The point is, this is a very personal subject matter. Our app gives victims of online harassment the tools to deal with their abuse in however they see fit, from documenting abuse all the way to talking to counselors or preparing for formal action.
