Inspiration

Watched a video with people playing One Night Ultimate Werewolf and did not want to spend 20 bucks on the cards. We also wanted to play games with friends easily.

What it does

Allows users to play Ultimate Werewolf through SMS and spectators can watch through the website.

How we built it

Website: HTML5/CSS3/Javascript & JQuery Game: Twilio, Heroku, Python

Challenges we ran into

We had to figure out how to update the users every time someone texted, update it on our end, and then send the results back to them.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to receive texts instantly after we texted the Twilio phone number. The website has a minimalist design. We were able to link up Twilio and Heroku.

What we learned

We learned how to use Twilio api.

What's next for Game of Hounds

Fix bugs, have more possible roles, and make it user-friendly.
