Welcome to the ORCOS Platform. It stands for Opioid Recovery Circle of Support. ORCOS platform is a complete ecosystem of support for patients receiving medication-assisted treatment for opioid use disorders. The platform consists of an innovative mobile app and services that the community needs. It has three main components – ORConnect, Find Information and Education.

“Find Information” provides patients receiving Medication-assisted treatment information about possible side effects and drug interactions in a format that is easy to understand and access. It provides information on common side effects that patients receiving Methadone, Buprenorphine, or Naltrexone treatment experience, how to deal with side effects, and when they are expected to subside.

“Education” provides educational tools and materials for opioid recovery support. e.g., time management, parenting skills, effects of drug use on family, etc

ORConnect is a peer support social network that helps you connect with people going through similar challenges. It provides a safe, secure and simple way to connect and stay connected with your friends and family. It is free to join. Not everyone has a support system. But “You are not alone”.
