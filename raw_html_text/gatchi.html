Inspiration

Tamagotchi with mini-games.

What it does

The player takes care of a virtual pet. They can play mini-games against the pet to gain money, then use the money to buy more pets or other accessories.

How I built it

Android studio.

Challenges I ran into

Originally we wanted to make the pet get sad or happy depending on the results of the mini-games; however, we had a lot of glitches with intents.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It's a finished game, and the mechanics work.

What I learned

How to make a game that is somewhat entertaining and efficiently working with others to finish it.

What's next for Gatchi

Improved UI, more shop items, more mini-games, push notifications, sound effects
