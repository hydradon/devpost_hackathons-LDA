Inspiration

We are all musicians and enjoy playing music. We wanted to allow musicians to connect and rate each other's performances online and use the feedback to improve in the future.

What it does

Allows musicians to review others' performances and receive feedback.

How I built it

PHP for backend, HTML/CSS/JavaScript/Bootstrap frontend, MySQL database build on Google Cloud

Challenges I ran into

We ran into challenges deciding on a database design.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We accomplished making a forum by hand and allowing upvoting/downvoting of posts.

What I learned

I learned more about database design and good practice for PHP connection.

What's next for MyEnsemble

Allowing users to have a karma score based on the upvote/downvotes of their posts and comments.
