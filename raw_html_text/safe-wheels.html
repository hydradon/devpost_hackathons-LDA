Inspiration

What inspired us to do our project was the intention to generate something innovative to help improve the lives of people making use of information technologies, as we realize that car accidents are a major cause of death in Mexico

What it does

The basic function of our application is to detect high impact accidents generating an alert for the services required to care in the shortest possible response time

How we built it

The application was made in the Xcode environment using Swift language

Challenges we ran into

The challenges we had was the development of the application through the Swift programming language, since it had to be learned from scratch. Another challenge that the team had was to devise a way to generate an alert as quickly as possible when having an emergency.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The team is proud of the result obtained, since in less than 28 hours could be generated an executable application and ready to show the client

What we learned

We learned to work as a team, a new programming language and new challenges

What's next for Safe Wheels

Obtain the investment for the development of the project and implement in the different platforms of mobile devices
