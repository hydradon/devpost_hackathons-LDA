What it does

The program is a game for people to understand what it is like to live on a minimum wage. It includes making hard decisions and separating wants and needs.

Challenges we ran into

It was hard to find an idea, but after trying many different programs, we finally found this one.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of matching a minimum wage to the state that the user inputs.

What's next for Money Week

We hope to make more problems and different pathways that the user could take.
