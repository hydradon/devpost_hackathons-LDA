Inspiration

Inspiration came from monopoly which teaches investment and if one can't invest properly, designing something meaningful that would help him or her invest become priceless.

How it works

My Target audience were employees that find it difficult to save , so on first time registration and signup it asks the user for its current salary then prompts the user daily to insert his or her expenses then tracks it.It also provides Investment option and an opportunity to seek advise.
