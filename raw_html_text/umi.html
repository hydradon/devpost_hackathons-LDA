Inspiration

Food is expensive. If you buy food at a restaurant it's either cheap, unhealthy, or expensive. If you cook food yourself its time consuming. We want to make a service that will help both parties out.

What it does

Umi is a service that allows home cooks to sell their awesome food to nearby neighbors and friends.

How we built it

We used HTML, Javascript, and CSS to build our web app.
