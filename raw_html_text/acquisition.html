ACQUISITION is a game of strategic conquest. The victory conditions are simple: one player will be crowned the victor by gaining control of every tile on the board. And for the loser? Shame. Can you acquire the entire battlefield before your opponent? Only your wit and cunning will allow you to survive!

The Acquisition team would like to be considered for all prize packages.
