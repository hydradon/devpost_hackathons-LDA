Fun and simple 2D game, that allows you to fly around and seek for a new high score! Game was made to be enjoyable and easy to play, I hope you will find it likeable. How to play: Goal of the game is just to collect as many coins until a certain amount of coins appear on the game screen. Game features: 3 Classic game modes with different high scores Custom level selection Enjoyable music
