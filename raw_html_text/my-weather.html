This is an android application that shows weather data for next few days. But the best part about this application is that it gives suggestion based on the weather of the day (example below). The application auto detects when the person is moving out of the home network and immediately brings up a notification with the list of things to be taken on that day based on the weather conditions.

For example:

  • On a rainy day, it will give you suggestion to carry umbrella when you leave home.
  • On a sunny day (let's say over 30 C, it will suggest you to carry sun cream
