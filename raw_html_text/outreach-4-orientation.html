Inspiration

Our inspiration was personal experiences with not being able to find a reliable source to discover the information we provided on the website.

What it does

Our website gives likes to our social media links to contact us, give information about LGBTQ and other resources and hotlines.

How we built it

We used glitch.com in order to build this website. We used HTML, CSS, and a little bit of JavaScript.

Challenges we ran into

Trying to set up a chat room, we ended up not being able to find a good tutorial. We also had problems with typos and formatting.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of our social chat rooms and our resources, we are happy with the way our website came out.

What we learned

How to type and add paragraphs, we also learned how to how to add links and images.

What's next for Outreach 4 Orientation

we hope to expand our website and social media to gain popularity and hopefully help some people find their way.
