Inspiration

A dislike for the necessary evil of running.

What it does

It senses how fast you are moving, and accordingly tells you how close the virtual zombie chasing you is to catching up.

How we built it

We used MIT App Inventor.

Challenges we ran into

Setting up a synced clock and pedometer functionality.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Completing it on time.

What we learned

A wide variety of functions and troubleshooting in the app inventor.
