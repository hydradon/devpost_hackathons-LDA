Inspiration

Our inspiration for the project is to be able to find a local restaurant with varieties of choices and quick access.

What it does

This application finds 3 of the closest restaurants of a certain category of food of your choosing.

How we built it

We used the website called Marvelapp.com to make a prototype

Challenges we ran into

Some challenges we ran into would be finding an idea and making it work, and very unfortunately the website we were using went down for maintenance in the middle of our project. This really set us back but once it came back up we went hard to work and completed what we needed to finish.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Some accomplishments we are proud of are that we actually made a prototype. We are also proud that we were succesful in finishing the project.

What we learned

We learned to implement new things that contributed to our project.
