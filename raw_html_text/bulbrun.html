With Bulb Run, we are trying to teach students about light bulb efficiency and costs. We have developed this game that integrates the bulb life and costs associated with 3 different kinds of light bulbs in order to finish the game. You will be given the chance to pick among 3 different kinds of light bulbs with different costs ad efficiency rates. The challenge is to strategically use the fixed amount of money adequately while choosing the best light bulb. So with that said, LET THERE BE LIGHT!
