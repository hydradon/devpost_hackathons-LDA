I want to remain as sustainable as possible with what I buy at the grocery store. It takes much more energy to get out of season vegetables than in season ones. So I decided to make it easier to pick out the most sustainable produce.

Tells you depending on the season or month you chose what to pick out at the grocery store for maximum sustainability.

I built it with the homemade database of fruits and vegetables the season and month that they are the ripest.

This is the first database backend Alexa application I have ever made which was challenging but I learned a lot.

Adding functionality to add certain things to your shopping list while your listening. Potentially buying that produce from Amazon straight from the app.
