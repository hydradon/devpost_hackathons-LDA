We are here at a Hackathon and have embarked on the sleepless quest of creating a project. We want a quick tool to find average mile times if you know your 5K time when we run, but also a tool to be able to find variables in RSA when you know other variables in a no-hassle way.

This hack is made as part of our local Hack Club, and we want to inspire more of our members to compete in hackathons, and to do that we have made a short documentary covering this hackathon. Editing takes a while, so the video is our work in progress/demo.
