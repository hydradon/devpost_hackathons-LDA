Inspiration

The desire to listen to my Quizlet cards like a song.

What it does

Sound card lets you chooses a Quizlet note set and mash it up with a song from Soundcloud

How I built it

I started by first building a script to convert the cards into a voice mp3 with google text to speech. After that I grabbed a song from Soundcloud to mash up the two!

Challenges I ran into

The whole way was challenging

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Everything works!

What I learned

Working with mp3, Quizlet's api, and Soundcloud's api

What's next for Soundcard

Machine learning for creating a more human like voice for the quizlet card reader.
