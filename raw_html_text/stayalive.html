Inspiration

** We hate heart attacks *** Since getting proper help can take too long, and someone suffering a heart attack might die in that time.

What it does

We implemented a system which allows certified CPR certified users to help those who are suffering a heart emergency.

How we built it

We used a website and an app using swift, php and html to connect emergency responders and victims of heart attacks.
