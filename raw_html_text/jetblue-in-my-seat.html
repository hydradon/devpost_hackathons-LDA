Inspiration

With JetBlue's offer of free wifi, we decided to take advantage of that and extends jetBlue's care to it's passengers

What it does

Our program is an in flight app that allows passengers to check the status of the restrooms. It also allows for passengers to create and join a virtual line.

How we built it

We built it using Java and Android Studio

Challenges we ran into

We ran into challenges with connecting the frontend to the backend. We could have fully debugged it with more time

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud to have learned new concepts in Java and get it to basically work

What we learned

We learned new interactions within android studio

What's next for JetBlue In My Seat

Jet Blue has free wifi, lets take advantage of that by enhancing the travelers experience with an app. Don't line up for the restroom at the back of the plane. Do it viritualy from your seat and only get up when its guaranteed to be your turn.
