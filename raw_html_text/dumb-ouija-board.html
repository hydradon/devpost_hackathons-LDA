What it does

Dumb Ouija Board lets you consult the spirits for answers to your deepest questions. Simply type out your question, and your phone will channel the spirit realm to find you an answer. Also we have some crystals and pyramid schemes to sell you.

How we built it

It's literally a random alphanumeric string generator. We built it over lunch to ask the important question: What is for lunch? The answer surprised us as it was not what was for lunch. Well how smart could the spirits be if they're dead?

Challenges we ran into

We ran into the challenge of integrating a dictionary so that the system could form words. We solved this by not forming words and skipping the issue altogether. The output text will make no sense.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

This project is nothing to be proud of.
