Inspiration

I wanted to do as much of a live performance (DJing, VJing, electronic live performance) as humanly possible, while maintaining the dynamic flexibility of a DJ set reacting to the crowd.

What it does

Pretty sure it does more than what big name artists do together with a whole production crew on massive stages, but I'll let you be the judge of that.

How I built it

With sweat, caffeine and insomnia.

Challenges I ran into

Minor compatibility/communication issues between the various software tools, and the sheer complexity of the whole thing.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It works! Veni, MIDI, vici.

What I learned

Sometimes 1 hour of sleep beats 4 hours of tired hacking.

What's next for #unrealDJing

As Bill O'Reilly said: WE'LL DO IT LIVE!!
