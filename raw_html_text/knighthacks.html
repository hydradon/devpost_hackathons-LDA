KnowYourU

A website that allows students to participate in polls anonymously so that campuses can get a better understanding of the student body's opinion.

Why KnowYourU?

-It allows students to honestly express their opinions and sensitive topics -It can take a census to improve campus quality. -Teachers can poll students in regards to their learning style to adjust their method of teaching.
