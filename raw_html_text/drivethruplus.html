Inspiration

The inspiration was the countless visits to drive thrus, and noticing the lack of efficiency and slowness.

How it works

Customer enters restaurant and car navigation system prompts for application to launch. After application launch, customer places order through app. Passengers in car can connect their phone to the session. If help is needed, customers can connect to a representative which will connect over Bluetooth. Customers pay separately and receive their orders in separate baggage. Customers receive order and enjoy.

Challenges I ran into

Learning curve with Firebase, and voice connectivity issues.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Revolutionizing the drive thru industry.

What I learned
