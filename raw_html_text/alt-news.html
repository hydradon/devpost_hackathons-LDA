Inspiration & What it does

Fake News are all the rage right now. Inspired by that, we created Alt-News, a Fake News Generator!

How we built it

We use Twilio to receive calls just like 911, then provide the transcription into IBM Watson's Language Tools and do some magic with their NLP tools. The output is then sent to our twitter account at https://twitter.com/altnewsdotpress

Our amazing domain name is powered by Radix!
