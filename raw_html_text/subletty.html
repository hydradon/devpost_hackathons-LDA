Inspiration

As Co-op students in Waterloo, we face the challenge of having to move places every four months (on average). The search for sublets can often be stressful and confusing which is why we came up with the idea to create Subletty.

What it does

Subletty is a website that connects people who want to sublet their places to people who are in need of sublets. Once you log in, there's a page dedicated to all postings where you can choose to contact the owner of that place.

How I built it

We constructed the front-end using React and Bootstrap while the back-end incorporated Firebase and node.js.

Challenges I ran into

The main challenge that we found was incorporating Firebase to React while storing, uploading and downloading the data that our users input.
