Inspiration

Our inspiration was to have a condensed platform to access information on candidates for public office in the United States.

What it does

TrueRep is an android based application that aims to serve as a resource for citizens to learn about the candidates running for various political positions.

How we built it

We built it through android studio.

Challenges we ran into

We had trouble creating the database of information to access.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

TrueRep.

What we learned

We learned about FireBase and android studio.

What's next for TrueRep

To expand to include candidates from states across the United States.
