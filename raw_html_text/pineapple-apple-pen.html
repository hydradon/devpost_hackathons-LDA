Inspiration

Often times we find ourselves in a situation where we need to sign a receipt or document, but our hands are dirty, or it would be unsanitary to touch the document.

What it does

Pineapple Apple Pen allows a user to sign their signature in the air, without touching anything.

How I built it

This was built using a Leap Motion to detect the location of a user's finger which they can use to sign the pen.
