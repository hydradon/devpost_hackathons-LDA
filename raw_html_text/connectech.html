Inspiration

We are inspired by innovative ideas! Rogers is currently developing solutions for customers in a rapid growth field - Smart Home Monitoring. We think that this field is extremely interesting because it presents key opportunities where customer experience can really make the difference. Committing to Smart Home technology is not "necessary" or required in any way for customers, so in order to do so they must have keen interest and comfort in their technology provider. Your home is your most precious commodity, because it houses your family and all other items. For us to work on improving Smart Home Monitoring by focusing on customer experience through IoT, we think that this creates limitless opportunity for growth for Rogers.

What it does

Connects the dots, real-time, for customers with their Smart Home system - allowing Rogers to provide proactive solutions tailored to meet customer needs in a self-service forward fashion.
