Inspiration

FireSafe is a system of Sensors and Applications that when working together would notify residents to leave their homes so they will be out of the danger of fires.

What it does

Each individual has their own FireSafe, like an indoor fire detector. Only it is made up of three main parts, the sensor, the Wi-Fi module, and application. The sensor reads data in the air and relays it to the W-Fi module indoors. The Wi-Fi module then relays the data to a network and to the users App on their phone. The data on the network is cross referenced with live wind and weather readings to help determine where a fire might be.

How I built it

I built the application through Thunkable and was working on getting a board to simulate the sensor module and Wi-Fi module.

Challenges I ran into

I could not figure out how to transmit data through to a host website.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned
