Inspiration

What it does

It's kind of like a tinder for fashion, where your friends or other people can tell you what they think about your outfit

How we built it

we used xcode do develop the app in swift 3, along with google firebase which held our database.

Challenges we ran into

Lack of knowledge in app development, and we only had two people in our group, so lack of manpower

Accomplishments that we're proud of

competing in our first hackathon, and hopefully being able to submit a project.

What we learned

we learned a good amount of swift code, and UI development, and database management.

What's next for Fashion University (FU)

we would like to improve it so we could make it more appealing to use, and eventually make an android version of the app
