Inspiration

We are new in this hackathon world and we were a little lost. So we decided to make an app to help new people organizing their own travel.

What it does

List of upcoming hackathons. Their position on google map. Show hotels and restaurants near the hackathon.

How we built it

Using android-studio and google APIs.

Challenges we ran into

Learn how android works and deal with APIs.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We learn android and the app works.

What we learned

Android and google APIs behaviour.

What's next for HackWorld
