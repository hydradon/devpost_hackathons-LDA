Inspiration

At a young age I was oblivious to why some cans/bottles weren't bottle depositable. Later on in life I eventually figured out why but I felt as if other people could be just as ignorant as I was.

What it does

It's web app that scans your can/bottle's barcode to determine whether or not it's depostiable. Also tells you which states and the return value if you have them in bulk.

How we built it

HTML,CSS,Javascript, Flask, Python, Axios

Challenges we ran into

Working with Flask, serving public file

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Overcoming the challenges, making JSON.

What we learned

Flask, JSON

What's next for $can4Cash

Map of locations to recycle, store scanned items, accounts
