What it does

Bringing a solution to the problem of limited and expensive health care options for the Elderly. This hassle-free App alerts Emergency Contacts and Emergency Personnel via a text message and location when a fall is detected.

How we built it

Swift, XCode

Challenges we ran into

We ran into issues with integrating Twilio API into our application because of issues with packages. We also had to figure out a way of detecting a fall by utilizing the phone's accelerometer data.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We're proud of building a working prototype of our product in 24 hours!

What we learned

Swift, XCode, Twilio API

What's next for Fall Detector

Improving fall detection algorithm
