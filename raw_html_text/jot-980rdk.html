Inspiration

We got a concept of a personification of a journal, where you can talk in natural language and be remind you when to make an entry in your journal. Is almost if your journal were sentient and acted as a interested friend.

What it does

As a chat bot, it uses your Facebook account to communicate and help you express your feelings, and anything else you want to talk with it. Can be used as a tool on psychological treatment of depression, or just help you know yourself better. Also allow the user to share their thoughts with family and friends more easily.

How we built it

We used an platform called Chatfuel that creates and setup the facebook Messenger API.

Challenges we ran into

Learning the AI concepts and finding a matching idea to the technology was difficult.

What's next for Jot

We want to make Jot smarter and broader, making it capable of different questions and in a customized way.
