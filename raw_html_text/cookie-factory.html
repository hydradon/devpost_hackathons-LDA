Inspiration

Contemporary internet authentication systems suck. They are super inflexible, do not generalize across platforms, and place undue burdens on their users.

What it does

We make it quick and easy to pass authentication tokens to friends. Now you can share your Netflix account without telling your friend the password!

What's next for Cookie-Factory

Making it even more user friendly, because auth should be easy for everyone.
