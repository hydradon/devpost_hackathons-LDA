Inspiration

A necessity for our college's radio station and they came to us with the problem hoping to find a solution.

What it does

It streams the live radio, has links provided -- for their Facebook, website, a morning talk show host's twitter, and a list of concerts in the metro(Omaha/Council Bluffs) area.

How we built it

We used the MIT App Inventor 2 to create this app.

Challenges we ran into

Making the interface of the application aesthetically pleasing and screen rotation friendly.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of how user friend the application we created is, especially compared to what the radio station has on their website for online streaming.

What we learned

We learned how to use the MIT App Inventor.

What's next for The River Radio Streaming App

Receiving feedback from the radio station to see what else they would like on it. Possibly looking into improving their website or making a database to show the name of the song while they are playing.
