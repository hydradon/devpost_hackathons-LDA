Inspiration

I noticed that there was someone that continuously built small platforms for us to cross on in a tunnel. Without the platform, students would be walking through the tunnel and getting their shoes wet. I wanted to thank whoever made these platforms and thought of this idea. A platform where you can drop compliments for the recipient and everyone else to see.

What it does

Kindr is a simple app that allows you to share kind posts on a map.
