Inspiration

There is a major safety concern, but also necessity involved with children riding the city bus unaccompanied. We seek to mitigate those concerns while meeting the needs of parents.

What it does

BusSafe allows a parent to remotely pay fare for their children and keep track of their bus routes.

How we built it

XML, Java, Android Studio, SnapKit, SQL Database, and generation of assets in FireAlpaca.

What's next for BusSafe

Integration of SQL server, custom Google Maps pins indicating National Safe Place locations
