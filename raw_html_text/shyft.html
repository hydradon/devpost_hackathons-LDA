What it does

Shyft is a marketplace that gives refugees the ability to perform online work from employers in the US.

How we built it

With React-Native

Challenges we ran into

Finding a unique solution that provided economic value for women in the area

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Finding a unique solution that allows refugees to be able to improve upon existing skillsets.

What we learned

The life of a refugee and the result of a lack of education and opportunity.

What's next for Shyft

We want to launch a beta of the product and see what kind of traction we get. From there we would love to leverage Chime For Change, Facebook, and Coursera to be able to make this a reality
