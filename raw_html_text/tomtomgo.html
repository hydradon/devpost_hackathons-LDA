• Get Set TomTomGO!

Inspiration

It was the everyday hassle to find someone who can do a quick fix for my shower knob, that broken bike I left on the bridge or even distributing flyers for brand awareness!

What it does

Get, set, #tomtomGO! Our platform fulfil all your on-demand service requirements hassle free, quick and easy on the pocket.

How I built it

We used tomtom API's provided for the challenge to tackle the inbuilt navigation and maps.

Challenges I ran into

TomTom wasn't 'GO'ing due to internet connection.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We've a working prototype and business plan to validate our hypothesis.

What I learned

Working with diverse team from Singapore, India and Romania has been an amazing experience and great learning so far!!

What's next for TomtomGO

Validating our hypothesis, usertesting, iterating and launching beta.
