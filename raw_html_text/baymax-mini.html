Inspiration

We wanted to create something that both adults and children alike could use in order to help with their medication reminders, and tracking what effects (desired or otherwise) those are having on them.

What it does

It is a mini Baymax, the healthcare companion. He reminds you of your daily medications, the doses, and at what time to take them. A survey helps to do this, and sends the data as a text file into a graph generator. You can also find the different medications contraindications and side effects in here.

How we built it

We used Amazon Web services, including the build an Alexa Skill. We used mainly python in Lambda.

Challenges we ran into

Learning how to use the different Amazon Web Services, especially Alexa.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We got to finish our first Alexa skill! And we are very proud of it.

What we learned

New AWS's, more python.

What's next for Baymax Mini

The app! And hopefully direct connections with pharmaceutical companies.
