This app is designed to improve your safety on road and also helps you to prevent accidents.

This app makes the mobile informs you By saying "You are over speeding" and sends the location and speed to cloud when you over speed the limit or. When you travel with more than 60 km/hr speed, it automatically saves the data online. When your mobile fell and broke in an accident, it automatically sends the SMS to emergency contact number you have had feed. Along with sending SMS we can have the data of all the people and their location and SPEED of impact online. This info can be used by traffic police dept/medical dept. to analyse accidents, and respond immediately.
