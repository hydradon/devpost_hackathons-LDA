Table 35 With so many social outlets in this day and age, it can be difficult to find a seamless way to share contact information. Contap solves that problem. When a realtor has an open house, they can open up Contap and set their phone up as an iBeacon through the app. Through the iBeacon, other users nearby can quickly connect with the realtor. Another way to share is through simply tapping users phones together. After tapping, each user's information is sent to the other via email.
