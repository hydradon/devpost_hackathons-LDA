Inspiration

Detroit human was a text video game that taught us the importance of our actions.

What it does

Our program goes through the life of a little kid and gives them the ability to choose their decisions. It shows them the consequences of their actions. It teaches morality.

How we built it

We used switch statements and objects in Java to create the code. We heavily used Github to collaborate and share our code.

Challenges we ran into

Github was hard to grasp, BUT we learned it!

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Creating something out of nothing and working very well as a team. We completed our first hackathon!

What we learned

We learned more Javascript, Json (although we didn't get to use it in the final product), and Github!

What's next for Good to the Bones

Good to the Bones should have more scenarios and let the users choose the locations that they want to go.
