Inspiration

Pariveda's theme of making an application for social good inspired us to make the Anxiety Reliever.

What it does

It is a chat-bot that can detect moods of people, and counsel accordingly. Also, in severe cases, the chat-bot can message or call people close to the subject and notify them of the morbid attitudes of the subject.

How we built it

We built the chat-bot using rivescript and implemented the sounds and speeches using p5.speech. The website was made using HTML, CSS, Javascript, AngularJS and NodeJS.

Challenges we ran into

Incorporating all the languages and dependencies in a few web-pages was certainly a challenge. While we may have had our difficulties making everything work, we are happy to have learned all that we have from the bitter experiences.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Anxiety reliever is a website that can help people cope with their anxieties. We are delighted to have made a web-app that contributes to social good.

What we learned

We were punished at times we did not follow programming convention. We have understood the value of proper commenting techniques and look forward to improve our coding behaviors.

What's next for Anxiety Reliever

We hope to add more functionalities, improve the natural language processing, refine the sentiment-analysis capabilities, and improve the bot as a whole.
