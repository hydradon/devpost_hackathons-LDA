Inspiration : Reading music is still not very popular among music lovers around the world . Though it has power to explore a whole new world for them. Being a music and technology lover myself, this is a way to make a tool for easy music language learning. You`ll need headphones or a sound box.

What it does: It starts with basics of music language. Then goes with explaining the Staff, Note Values, Time Signature. Examples and interactive demo makes it very easy for player or operator to get a good idea of reading music sheets.

How I built it : Amazon Polly, Amazon Cognito, Sumerian

Challenges I ran into: Importing of FBX model to Sumerian. Animation of characters. Camera controlling scripts. User interaction scripting.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of: Founder of ‘Delhi Technology Club’ ( https://www.delhitechnologyclub.com/ ), Guitarist, Initiator of ‘Music for Life’ community ( http://delhitechoclub.wixsite.com/musicforlife/video-reel ).

What I learned: Working with Sumerian has been great in terms that production time is very less. It enables projects to make and instant share, with no need of any particular set up or deployment.

What's next for Musicolge: Musicolge plans to give music learning with technology to a new level with VR, AR & MR.
