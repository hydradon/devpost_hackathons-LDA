Inspiration

We were inspired to build Path Hero because we noticed that people, at the start of each semester, often scramble to find a good route to class. The app is mostly aimed towards incoming freshmen, but anyone can use it to find an optimal route to their classes. We even added shortcuts - it takes you through buildings!

What it does

Optimize your route to class, just input your JHU class schedule.
