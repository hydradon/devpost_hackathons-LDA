What it does

Users can reserve parking spot for an hour, starting from when they plan to arrive, the parking spot light turns red to show it is reserved. The users receives the number of the place they have to park in.

Sensors in the parking spot detect arrives. The user receives a notification saying their spot has been occupied. The user then has to either confirm they have arrived and occupied the place. They can do this once they have parked, and are going to the train! If someone else has taken their spot, they can park in a green spot and notify the app someone has taken their spot.
