Inspiration

We were inspired to make this website after hearing about students at our school, Irvington High School, are dealing with mental disorders such as depression and anxiety.

What it does

This website allows you to get the best tips on how to cope with stress and you can talk with one of us if you are dealing with a mental disorder

How we built it

We used html5 with a few plugins such as a one on one chat.

Challenges we ran into

Because we are beginners at html, it took us a while to learn and figure out what to do.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud that we had a smooth workflow and got everything done on time with breaks.

What we learned

We learned how to use html and how to work together as a team

What's next for Mental Disorder Awareness

We are hoping that this website will be able to reach as far as the entire world so that everyone dealing with mental disorders will be connected and assisted
