Inspiration

Harassment and Assault had become a new normal for us. I built this app aiming that it would help society to be aware of what's happening around the world. And help to help them who are in danger and hopefully save them from life threating situation

What it does

It shows the view of where the crimes are happening. It Helps to deploy SOS message when the need it most. I record the video of the event. It helps people to know about dangerous situation and makes them aware of Mental health issue etc

How I built it

I built is using android studio (Java), PHP, SQL, HTML5, WEBRTC.

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned
