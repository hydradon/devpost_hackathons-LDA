The inspiration was to make something that helps people to be better.

To achieve that it records how you behave every day, to be aware of your behaviour, so you can try to make it better.

I built it with Litexa, a new approach to make Alexa skills, that generates a nodejs project.

That was challenging because it was the first time that I used Litexa, and also the first time for ISP.

I'm very proud of making that skill, I was very close to not finish in time, due to some complications with Litexa, it is still under development.

I have learned a lot about Litexa, ISP and Alexa (new ways of state management).

The next step will be to add more complex and rich interaction with the user.
