Inspiration

Our major inspiration is the recent happenings as well as the events that has happened around the world and has proven us disabled to perform and do anything that can support us in anyway.

What it does

It basically creates a social platform to all those enthusiasts and sensible people who all want to perform and make a change in their environment and surroundings.

How we built it

It is build using different languages which includes php, java, javscript, phpmyadmin, html, xml and css.

Challenges we ran into

We weren't able to push the updates on Google Playstore on time for publishing as our pace for fixing of bugs was a bit faster.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned

We learnt that its not a task for one person to be able to develop something that can perceived in a positive and constructive manner to the society and time management, co-ordination and planning is a must.

What's next for Apricate

We have thought of including a bunch of ideas including Live Location tracking, Live Event broadcasting, Virtual Tour, Social Interaction.
