Inspiration

The elevator next to our office never comes. It's frustrating not knowing where it is.

What it does

A IoT device that let you know where the elevator is.

How we built it

SoC or MCU mounted in the elevator without modify the elevator circuitry.

Challenges we ran into

No enough equipment to realize.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

A few conceptual designs + some validation test.

What we learned

Bluetooth signal level can be used to track location and it is pretty robust.
