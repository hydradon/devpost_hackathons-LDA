Inspiration

The lack of innovation in the field of alarm clock and sleep

What it does

It allows user to experience a virtual space (like beach,mountains) and feel peace and serenity from within. It has an in-built alarm which allows the user to sleep and relax without tension.So, cut out the noise from the outside world and start sleeping/relaxing in piece.

Accomplishment I am proud of

I got this done completely (there are a few places here and there that needs to be improved though).I initially tried to make a dating app using Spark AR but that idea got completely shattered as Spark AR didn't allow integration of its filter to your own app.I am honestly really happy i didn't give up and kept going to implement this new idea.

Challenges I ran into

The biggest challenge was that I was a beginner in react-native which made it quite difficult to code for React-360 but I was able to do it.

What's next for VR-Alarm

Integrating the entire web app into react-360 app,without html5 and adding more scenery to the project. I feel a bit of smoothing in sound is also required for this project.
