Inspiration

I just wanted to make a game instead of an app for once so I though of a pretty simple idea and came up with devClicker.

What it does

A game where you click to earn money. Then use that money to buy stuff that increases your iq level. Then combine your wealth with your intelligence to build/buy other things that earn money for you.

How I built it

Used xcode, a lot of tableviews, and Stack Overflow.

Challenges I ran into

Its been a while since I have coded in swift. I often found myself going on stack overflow and other sites to refresh my knowledge.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I have the ability to create a basic game in a matter of hours.

What I learned

I learned the different concepts needed inside a game. For example, appeal to users.

What's next for DevClicker

I plan to add many more items to enhance the gui.
