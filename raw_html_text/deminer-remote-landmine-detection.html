There are 100 million active landmines in the world contaminating the size of Denmark five times over. Our thought was to explore the use of drone-based land mine detecting by combining real time data relay, GPS, metal detecting, and analytical heat maps into one system. Further development of this technology has the potential to improve lots of lives.
