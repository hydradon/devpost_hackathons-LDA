Inspiration

Our inspiration started with the difficulties enrolling into healthcare. We saw there was a shortage of Doctors, difficulty accessing care, fractured networks and 3rd party challenges.

What it does

Allows for perfect doctor match, book online, triage health data, access to doctors anywhere in world, mobile EHR data wallet.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Our first product built 2016 was approved by Booz Allen Hamilton, CMMS and HHS to integrate with Federal Gov't. Healthcare.gov, domiciled in Cheyenee Wyoming, received Open Blockchain Exemption Letter under HB70

What I learned

The challenges are massive
