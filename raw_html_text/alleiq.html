Inspiration

A team member has a son with 5 allergies, and it has been difficult to cope with the situation whenever the family wants to go out for dinner.

What it does

It adds a filter to third parties platforms; helping people with allergies find/ order food/restaurants that fit their needs.

How I built it

We used sketch to design a prototype it.

Challenges I ran into

we changed the product quite a bit, Having everyone to be in the same page was a bit challenging.
finding motives for restaurants to care about the issue

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

being open and respectful to everyone's opinion and backgrounds Team communication and commitment to finish this in 1.5 day being realistic in order to maximize the outcome

What I learned

No idea is a bad idea Think differently and be open to dumping the 1st idea

What's next for Alleiq

I'm looking forward to see our idea come to life and help other people who can benifts from this.
