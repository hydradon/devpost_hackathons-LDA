Inspiration

We tackled the problem of responsable behaviour in a smart city. We believe that the "smartness" of a city relies on the "smartness" of the citizens. We wanted to bring an incentive-based service, a "nudge" in a broad sense, in order to foster green gestures/behaviours, rewarding the most commited users. The rewarding system is based on a platform that gives real world vouchers to use in local businesses, based on the behaviour of the citizens.

How it works

It's a simple mobile application , where you can report problems you see when you're walking/hanging around in the city. The typical problems are dirty streets due to incorrect garbage disposal, broken lights, broken access doors to several public places. The user takes a picture of the problem : the geolocalisation used on the phone provides information to municipalities in order to optimize their cleaning services. Local businesses are able to generate vouchers using a real time bidding system.

What's next for Oaktion
