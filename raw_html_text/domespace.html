Inspiration

Athletes, sports coaches(high school, college, pros), MMA trainers, yoga instructors etc. can have an additional source of income by renting out local sports facilities and having paid training/classes. Facilities can also be booked for multi-use purposes like events, games, concerts etc.

What it does

Allows a user to book a facility based on available dates and listed price.

How I built it

Ruby on Rails and JavaScript

Challenges I ran into

  • Integrating Elasticsearch into my app, and using S3 bucket for my images.
