Inspiration

I missed my flight to the hackathon and was too dead to build out what I wanted to do. I tried to offer my help to other teams, but it was really hard to find someone who needed help, and everyone was keeping to them selves. I thought, it would be nice if there was a way for people to post what they need / want.

What it does

It allows people to post what table they are on, and what they need, so that other's can drop by for a chat.

What's next for DevHub

I'm pretty sure things can be done better than how DevPost does it. DevPost really has no features for activity or interaction, which is what hackathons should be all about.
