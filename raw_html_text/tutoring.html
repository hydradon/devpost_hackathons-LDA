TutoRing

I am frequently enrolled in online courses in webpages like Udemy and Coursera, and the content is great, but the interaction with the tutor is very limited and that has a negative impact on the course itself; the interaction you have in that kind of courses is limited to a chat and sometimes, the tutors take a lot time to reply and that reply may not even be for you. That's why we took the idea of TutoRing, and application that makes it easier to both learn and teach. The main asset of TutoRing is that simplification of the interaction between the tutor and the student.
