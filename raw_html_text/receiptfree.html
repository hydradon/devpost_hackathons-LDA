Inspiration

The idea get in our mind every time we go to the shop taking receipt and then throw it or it lost . We also thought about it from the environmental perspective and the amount of trees cut to do this wasted papers.

What it does

Having receipts on mobile by scanning QR codes instead of getting them printed.

How I built it

Using python as backend mongodb as database react as front end and three docker containers.

Challenges I ran into

Not all members write backend with the same language so we have to choose one and neglect other.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Expressing our idea with a good prototype in 24 hours.

What I learned

Working with new people and benefiting from their experiences in programming.

What's next for ReceiptFree

integrate with the real life and adding new features.
