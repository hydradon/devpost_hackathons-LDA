The bot which is going to be used is build by two plates ,which are having a rectangular bar at the center which will rotate at high speed and check the distance constantly which will check for the leakage using Laser based leakage detection providing a very fast detection of leakages of Pipeline systems. We are trying to use the force of water to move the bot and it will generate the amplified force to rotate the core faster. It takes the circle checking and then it will apply the glue. Then it will stop the flow and take some time to let it dry. We can also use helical structure or universal joint which can help moving the bot on the turns.
