Inspiration

[Alexandra Jordan, 11]: My family is big and diverse. Mom, Dad, grandparents, step-parents, aunts, uncles. We have to deal with me, my brother who has special needs, and all the different activities from school, to programs, to eats and entertainments. Who's got whom when, who's picking up which kids, who's attending what events ...?

What it does

Famtastic is an app that lets all the care-givers co-ordinate in one big family tracking where kids are when, which caregiver has which task covered, as well as going back and tracking what happened - for things like shared custody agreements and taking turns.
