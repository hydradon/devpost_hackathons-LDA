Inspiration

Hundreds of people lose their lives from texting and driving, along with causing thousands of dollars of damage. However, people have a need to respond and let others know their situation.

What it does

It's really tricky to text back someone without interrupting what you are doing. TTYL (talk to you later), gets the message across. This automatically responds to text during your busy periods letting your friends, family, or colleagues understand your currently busy by sending a gif, jpg, or text message respond indicating your situation.
