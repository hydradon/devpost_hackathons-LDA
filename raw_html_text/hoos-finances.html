Inspiration:

We identified the lack of proper financial and economic education for the large socioeconomic gap in the society and sought ways to aim to better the education system in that term. The solution that we collectively agreed upon was the idea of a virtual novel that would illustrate a typical financial lifecycle of a person starting from high school all the way to their retirement.

What it does

The virtual novel provides life choices for a typical high school student to select from as he/she journeyed their life. The catch is, every choice comes with a financial consequence and whether or not they made the best financially responsible choices will effect their overall well-being at retirement age, thus teaching them of the importance of every financial situation in the modern age. They would also be directed other online sources if they desired to learn more about any part of the story they are more interested in.

How we built it

We used ren-py, a free virtual novel building platform using Python to create our project. We used Figma to edit parts of the User Interface. We used Photoshop to edit images. We used Sketchbook Autodesk to draw the characters and some backgrounds.

Challenges we ran into

The main challenges that we encountered was deciding the right format for the best UX. Another part was the decision as to cut down on what part of the life cycle and the many options that would be available in real life to tailor the project into achievable scope for 24hours.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The working game as well as the personalized art that gives it a customized touch.

What we learned

How to use ren-py How to make digital art. How to convey a story.

What's next for Hoos Finances

The game would continued to be developed and curtailed to every culture/society as well as the financial state of the times since it has to stay updated to the day and age for the user to learn the most relevant information to base their financial choices upon.
