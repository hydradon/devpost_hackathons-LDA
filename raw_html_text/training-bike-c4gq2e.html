Inspiration

Did you know that the average person spends roughly in its life?

10 years working, 9 years watching TV, 90% in closed spaces and only 2 years practicing sports!

What it does

Based on this observations we decided to take advantage of such time in conjunction with technological devices in order to help on improving people's life through excercise. Taking cyclism as starting point we decided to develop a robust simulation of such activity offering the user an intuitive experience where it can choose one from many different routes to transverse at home through an stationary bike! Meet with friends or strangers on your way to a healthier life!

What's next for training bike

We´ll continue working on the development of this prototype, we´ll include reading pulse, routes virtualization video game and creating our own cycling roller .

We are confident that the project is a great idea.
