Inspiration

Companies like Equifax and Ashley Madison exposing people's credit card information and other personal information due to breach in their centralized storage systems.

Private Pay's addresses this issue by keeping consumer identity private and secure.

What it does

Private Pay is an private and secure payments platform for merchants.

Our initial "Private Pay button" that can be added to any pre-existing merchant website. Because this will use Blockchain / Crypto and Tor for payments and other secure mechanisms like tor, your personal identity can never be stored by these merchants.

How we built it

We built a prototype of that emulates a usage of the "Private Pay SDK". Our Prototype uses Poynt terminal with QR code scanning. This demonstrates the usage of Private Pay SDK.

What's next for Private Pay

We will keep building on this and making this a better SDK. In addition we will add more specific use cases of our SDK and find more partners. And then we will look even more into the business aspects.
