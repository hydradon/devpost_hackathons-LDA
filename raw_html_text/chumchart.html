chumchart

Make a D&D alignment chart with your friends. Send your friends a link so they can take a quiz. Once you and eight of your friends have completed the survey, ChumChart will generate an alignment chart of you and your friends.

Have you ever wondered how you aligned on the axes of morality and ethics? Well now you can find out with Chum Chart!

Chum Chart is a new service that allows friends to create entertaining and accurate D&D-style alignment charts. The group leader generates a link via our site, and shares it with his friends. All 9 chums answers 10 questions and paste a photo link to determine their spot and picture on the alignment chart.

Check it out live at chumchart.com!
