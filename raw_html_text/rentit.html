Inspiration We used to have to wait for weeks for books to come off of hold.

What it does Allows a user to find neighbors near them that are willing to rent them books. Simple for both sides of the transaction.

How we built it We used MapViews, CalendarViews, CameraIntents, and more!

Challenges we ran into Connecting our firebase database was quite tough. We also found most difficulty with the MapsAPI, which required an API Key that was hard to get.

Accomplishments that we're proud of Our very responsive and fluid app/homepage was our favorite accomplishment.

What we learned We successfully learned MapViews and their features.

What's next for RentIt We are planning to release our app to the Play Store
