Inspiration

Just having a great picture to upload on facebook or Instagram is not sufficient. It has to be uploaded with a great caption too. I have seen people searching all over the internet for that one perfect caption. We decided to solve this problem.

What it does

Lazy caption takes a picture from a user.......abbbrraacaadabraaa and you get the perfect caption.

How we built it

Lazy caption works on object detection. We detect objects in the picture, process their probabilities and get search for the caption in our database.

Challenges we ran into

Which objects to select, which quotes to put in the database. Pictures having one or more objects.

What we learned

Getting our hands dirty with IOS and Android permissions. Setting up a fancy search algorithm in AWS CloudSearch.

What's next for Lazy Captions

To make caption suggestions more and more relevant.
