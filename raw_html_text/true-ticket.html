Inspiration

Every big problem raises a big opportunity. We have been working on Blockchain for several months now and we decided to develop a system that will protect people losing money to ticket scalpers.

What it does

We defined every single ticket as a node in the blockchain system and record the history of every transaction in the node. Every ticket can have only one owner at any time. What we offer is similar to what paypal offers, instead of your payment we are sealing the tickets with trust.

Challenge

Delivering a blockchain product within 24 hours.
