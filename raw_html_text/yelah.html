Inspiration

As a farmer and a techpreneur, i needed an application i can use to communicate with others farmers without the internet.

What it does

Yelah enables farmers to chat, call,send images and files to each other within the range of 3km to 5km without having internet access.

How I built it

Yelah was built using javascript and it uses wifi connection,and with intense interaction with other farmers and agricultural stakeholders.

Challenges I ran into

most farmers have not adapted to technology

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Yelah version 1.0 is ready

What I learned

farmers want to connect, they are always looking for ways to interact directly with others farmers, customers and experts that can help solve their problems.

What's next for Yelah

we plan to lunch Yelah with a short number of farmers to start with, then we wish to extent it further more to other parts of the country, within a year or more, we plan to scale yelah to other part of the world.
