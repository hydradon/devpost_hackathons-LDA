Did you know that in addition to being an awesome module planner, a great way to find empty classrooms to sneak into and to locate your next class, NUSMods is also a game engine? Move aside Unity and Unreal, NUSMods is the next great game engine. To demonstrate this I have built the classic game Tetris in NUSMods. Behold - ModTris !
