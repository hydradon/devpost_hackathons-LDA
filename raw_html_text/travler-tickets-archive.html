Inspiration

Photos that were taken during the travel is well archived and saved in your iPhone. However, tickets, boarding passes, or even artworks that inspired you in the museum is hard to save but you still want to save it for your memory. Therefore, I came up of an idea of making a mobile application that archives and curates your tickets, boarding passes, artworks, and even more.

What it does

It allows you to organize your tickets, boarding passes, artworks, and anything you want to archive in the application. You can also share every contents in the way you want.

How I built it

I built it with adobe-xd and sketch. The animations were mainly built by XD.

Challenges I ran into

Making wireframes, prototypes, adding animation, and also doing heuristic evaluations (as a quick alternative of user testing) in a short time was challenging.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I'm proud of I put many user friendly interactions and design. Also I am proud that I overcame all the challenges I had!

What I learned

How to efficiently use Sketch and XD together. How to animate XD and use various functions for prototyping.

What's next for Travler Tickets Archive

More functions and fun sharing user flow!
