Inspiration

CuLearn sucks. We understand the struggle of trying to calculate grades at the end of the semester to see where we stand in our courses. To avoid any surprises and/or disappointments CourseGradeCalculator will do this for you.

What it does

The CourseGradeCalculator will keep track of your grades so that you can see your average for each course without calculating them yourself. This convenient CourseGradeCalculator will give you more time to focus on your studies rather than calculating.

How we built it

Android Studio was used to build this application.

Challenges we ran into

Only one individual in our group was somewhat familiar with using Android Studio, therefore this was our biggest challenge.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Completing a functional application overnight with minimal experience in Android Studio.

What we learned

How to use Android Studio.

What's next for CourseGradeCalculator

Making the application look more presentable, making it more robust and adaptable to different kinds of classes and assignments. Adding additional functions such as anticipated grade, editing or removing input, GPA calculator etc.
