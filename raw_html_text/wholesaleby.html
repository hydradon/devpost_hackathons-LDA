Inspiration

I was inspired by Quipu's mission statement.

What it does

Wholesaleby pulls data from the Quipu blockchain and finds goods/services which may benefit from wholesale purchasing. It then uses that data to automatically generate a letter template that can be sent to external providers or organizations that sell goods/services. This letter leverages the combined buying power of the entire community, based on the past transactions logged on the blockchain.

LIVE DEMO: https://altwallet.io/wholesaleby/
