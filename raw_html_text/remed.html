Remed - An app to help remind you to take your medication with minimal user setup!

Our goal is to help the forgetful to take their medication, but we wanted a more streamlined approach to setting the reminders. The long term goal is to have an app or a webpage for doctors also to simply enter the information for a new medication for a patient and have notifications automatically sent to their phone containing the important details about the medication. But for now users have to manually enter in the medicine name, frequency and time of day.
