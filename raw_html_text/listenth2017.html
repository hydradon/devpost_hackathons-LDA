Inspiration

Selina loves learning languages, design, and music. Jonathon wanted to make something related to improving recommendations. Dash said: OK let's do it.

What it does

LISTEN helps you learn languages on-the-go by curating playlists just for you!

How we built it

With lots of love. And cramming a lot of learning new (coding) languages.

Accomplishments we're proud of

Storytelling w/ illustrations and UI/UX The concept behind LISTEN!

What we learned

Prioritizing key areas to create a minimum viable prototype is essential. Also, shoutout to our amazing co-dancers from the Viennese Ball Opening Committee for both energizing us <3 and cutting into 1/3 of our hackathon time

What's next for LISTEN

Fleshing out more features on our wishlist: Making the playlists more personalized (e.g. being able to select which genres to include in your playlist) Pulling out new vocabulary from song lyrics Adding new languages ...and more!
