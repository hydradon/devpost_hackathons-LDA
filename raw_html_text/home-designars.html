Inspiration

Moving into a new home is an extremely exhausting. Floor planning is even more stressful. However, with Home DesignARs, the user can place and drop furniture with a little tap. This helps the user to better visualize how their home is organized from using their camera where everything is mapped out.

What it does

Places furniture without the single tap on your screen, making your home design a convenient and fun experience.

How we built it

With Xcode using swift and the ARKit provided by Apple.

Challenges we ran into

Since this was a fresh idea and we were not experienced in the swift language at all. This caused very frequent errors within our program for both syntax and the app storyboard. We have also never done any sort of AR, so this was a brand new concept for our team to grasp in the span of 24 hours.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Home DesignAR is a fully functional AR application, and its completion is the biggest accomplishment. Also, we have all learned about Swift's language and the concept of AR. The learning process during this hackathon is a huge accomplishment for all of us.

What we learned
