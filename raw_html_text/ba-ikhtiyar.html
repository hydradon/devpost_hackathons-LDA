Inspiration

Enablement of citizens through technology making it possible to do just about anything in the world today.

What it does

Enables the tansgender minority to earn decent income through a secure chain of networks and gain confidence to get integrated into the society.

How I built it

Through an android app.

Challenges I ran into

Ensuring it becomes a sustainable product

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Bridging the gap between between people who want to help and those who need it

What I learned

1) Retention of people of this community in the work force is a challenge 2) The gravity of the situation is much stronger than expected!

What's next for Ba-ikhtiyar

To add vocational training to the entire circle and grow into a for-profit organization.
