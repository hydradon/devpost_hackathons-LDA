Inspiration

ESCAPE was inspired by the Cinderella story: Cinderella wasn't allowed to go to the Royal Ball because of her stepmother and stepsisters and had to stay in the house. However, she ends up meeting her Fairy Godmother!

What it does

Python RPG in which you have to figure your way around to the Garden, while collecting the necessary items and avoiding the housekeeper.

How we built it

Trinket

Challenges we ran into

Defining the locations and constructing the loops

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Coming up with a fun and magical plot!

What we learned

Creating loops and if statements

What's next for ESCAPE: A Cinderella RPG

Images, audio files, and adding color + other font stats!
