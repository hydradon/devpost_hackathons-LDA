Inspiration

Rapping is hard work, why not let your computer do your shows for you while you're kickin' in the front seat, sittin' in the back seat?

What it does

Automagically raps for you, just type in your phat rhymes, and let our algorhythm "generate"[1] a dank music video for you to post your fans on youtube!

How we built it

Top sekrit (absolutely not available at https://github.com/disraption/rapture ) algorhythm takes your lyrics and uses the Apple voice synthesiser to speak your lines, then we use our proprietary (actually what's built into FFMPEG, but don't tell our investors that) shaping technology to put each rhyme in the right place and merge it, 100% MAGICK!
