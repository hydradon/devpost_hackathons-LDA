Inspiration

We chose to pursue this project due to one of our teammate's passion for kiwis.

Purpose

The site spreads awareness about kiwi endangerment and links to a foundation that saves them with your donations.

How we built it

We built this website with basic Web languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Challenges

As inexperienced hackers, we faced many challenges. We attended every workshop offered, and spent a good portion of our time solely on research.

We made it though!

Final thoughts

This was an educational and enriching experience for all of us: the knowledge and skills we accumulated from hours of research, hands-on application, and having to solve problems for ourselves will last us a lifetime.

For the future

We hope to improve the functionality and frontend aspects of our website.
