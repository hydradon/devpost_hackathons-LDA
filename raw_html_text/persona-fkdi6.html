Inspiration

We needed it at NewSpace.NYC to do diligence for companies entering our incubator.

How it works

We use FullContact API to get a person's social accounts, scrape those, process them through personality insights, and result a due diligence report.

Challenges I ran into

Our first API choice (ClearBit) didn't return the data we needed.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Our team is good people, good times.

What I learned

More bluemix, more watson, more fun.

What's next for Persona

Continue scraping more and more info and build out the report's thoroughness.
