Inspiration

We wanted to understand the current state of Dog states like are they Happy/Sad/Tired/Active etc through the use of Data accurately

What it does

It's an App which helps Pet owners know the state of their pet by just scanning pet's face and based on the data from Kinship API's and show corresponding Animoji's

How we built it

We used Kinship API's to access and analyze the Data, used Snapchat Lens Studio to create Animoji filters and write scripts corresponding to various behaviors that the pets will display.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to create animoji's and hook them up with Motion tacking of pet's eyes,face etc and show appropriate emoji's based on data.
