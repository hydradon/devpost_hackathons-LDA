Inspiration

Make it easier to keep track of the stuff that you purchase in store, and provide an easy way to find recipes for the food you buy.

What it does

Stores your grocery inventory in a database and allow tracking how much of each food item you have left in your fridge. Also allows you to search over 1.5 million recipes.

How we built it

Built with NodeJS, Express, and CouchDB for the backend, and KnockoutJS, Bootstrap, and jQuery for the front end.
