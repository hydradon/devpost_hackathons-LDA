This webapp provides security for people of all genders and backgrounds. Especially with the recent crime reports, safety is more important than ever. Thus PlzDontMug.Me. This webapp tells users how safe it is to go somewhere, and suggests the user to call an Uber if it is too dangerous. The crime rate is calculated through a sophisticated and clever algorithm on data from the City of Berkeley to provide an accurate assessment of the likelihood of bodily harm or being robbed.
