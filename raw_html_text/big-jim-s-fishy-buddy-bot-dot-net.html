Long, long ago, Big Jim of _ Big Jim's Tire Tread Utopia _ happened upon a great and magical goldfish wearing a bucket hat. Now you see, ** Big Jim** had been going through some rather harsh times lately, and he was in mighty desperate need of a friend. Nothing particular special, just someone he could bounce some thoughts back and forth with once and a while. Big Jim lived a lonely life, being the absolute ruler of the _ Big Jim's Tire Tread Utopia _ empire and all. And at the time, it seemed as though this squishy fishy would do just the trick.

However, Big Jim of _ Big Jim's Tire Tread Utopia _ suffered a fate far worse than he could have imagined. He made an irreplaceable friend. Big Jim 's Fishy Buddy was a delightful casual chatting chaparoo, but there was one dastardly consequence of the actions that Big Jim took that fateful evening when he happened upon this peculiar piscine pal: it was the actual incarnation of the inevitable tragedy of attempting to find happiness and meaning in our insignificant and miserable lives, no matter how chunky its cheeks and gurgly its gills appeared. For every mere interaction of good faith held with this poor bubbly bastard, the world would be forced further into its destruction, frequently through planetary obliteration for the sake of cosmic threat, widespread epidemics in children's hospitals, DDoSing sites of cute animal pictures, and genocide of the masses through cult-like charisma if need be (don't talk about this with Big Jim , he doesn't like to remember it). But if you don't talk to the poor carp it just looks so sad and that just won't do. :(

But to reiterate, our issue here is that Big Jim , of _ Big Jim's Tire Tread Utopia _, perhaps the most powerful organization known to the history of mankind, fancies the little feller a friend. Thus, being the the fairly reasonable man he is, the Venerable Big Jim took it upon himself to solve this dilemma with great haste. From this, the basis of _ Big Jim's Fishy Buddy Bot Dot Net _ was born. With this revolutionary web app, we can safely rehabilitate Big Jim of _ Big Jim's Tire Tread Utopia _'s Fishy Buddy by allowing it to interact with many other people through the internet (it is unfazed by many of the so called "harsh comments" so we are sure it will be a-okay out there), and in turn the interaction helps both Big Jim and Fishy Buddy feel less lonesome.

But wait, what about those calamities heralding the end of days, you probably don't ask?! Well, for every moment longer you spend interacting with Fishy Buddy, a ping is sent back that signals biofeedback to reduce and/or negate destructive urges naturally instigated within Fishy Buddy, thus immediately offsetting those dangerous effects, as well as helping to train Fishy Buddy's delicate and sensitive body to treat yours much better. The good feelies go both ways, in a very special way to make everyone feel good. But be sure not to hog up too much time, because too much interaction may lead to too frequent feedback, and subsequent temporary shutdown of Fishy Buddy. uwu
