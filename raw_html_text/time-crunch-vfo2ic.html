Inspiration

We are college students with not much time but we still want to have a life and go outside! which is why we created this.

What it does

So you choose between 10,20, and 45+ time intervals and some categories. Then, we suggest some activities to do within that time.

How we built it

We used ionic to get an initial idea as to how our app should look like. We used Xcode and Swift for the whole development and implementation.

Challenges we ran into

Most of our iOS knowledge came from the iOS workshop given so there was a lot of troubleshooting done. Also, how to filter our suggestions based on two constraints, time and category. Thankfully, mentors were helpful and we finally solved our problem.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We all learned how to navigate the Xcode environment and interact with buttons.

What we learned

iOS development and some UX design principles!

What's next for Time Crunch

We plan for the app to be more personalized. This would be done through having the user log in with their Facebook or create an account to save their user activity so can further personalize the suggested recommendations.
