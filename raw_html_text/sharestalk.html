What's next for ShareStalk

We are ShareStalk, a team that strives to promote reducing food waste in local communities. Our web application enables users to exchange portions of their food so that they are able to not only not waste food they would not use but also allows people to share their food and bring communities closer together. We used PHP to set up the database for our application, as well as used HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to set up and display the web pages. One challenge we ran into was our practicality and who would use our service, so we decided to narrow our user base to local communities where people would know one another so that trust would not be an issue. We learned how to integrate PHP into HTML, and we plan to expand our user base to colleges and companies when we receive enough credibility so that everyone is able to reduce food waste and share stalk with their community.
