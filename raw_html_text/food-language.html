Inspiration

When Rudy went to China, he was very difficult for him to understand the menu of restaurants because he didn't speak Chinese. That's where the idea was coming from.

How it works

It is really simple. Imagine that you're going to a foreign country, let's say Russia. You don't speak Russian, and you decide to go in a restaurant. The menu is not translated and there is no image. How could you order ? And if you have allergies or intolerances, how to be sure to not order something that you can't eat ? So, just flash a QR code on the menu and go to a app, with the menu of the restaurant directly in your language. And, if you have any allergies or intolerances, the app notifies the dish that you can't eat.
