Inspiration

There are a lot of people, including local and international, finding their flatmates due to the high residential costs in Hong Kong.

What it does

Users can simply register and then indicate their some information regarding their life pattern and the location where they are trying to find flatmates. Our service provides information of users so that they can find flatmate easily and recommends some matching possible flatmate bassed on the information provided by the users.
