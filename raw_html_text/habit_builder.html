Inspiration

The idea was concluded by combining the case studies related to Behavior and habit tracking section in the kit announced at the hackathon ,

What it does

Habit_builder is simple android application help people to track their habits with just click ! the user will set the app the first time with his/her good habits wants to build and bad habits wants to get over off and a widget will be placed on the user's phone sitting all the habits , once he/she did that habit will click on it on the widget , and a counter will increase by the number of clicks , all the data will be collected and analyzed to be shown as graphs to facilitate the tracking .

How we built it

after the brainstorming we did about the app and discussing the based features of the app , we separate the tasks into 3 ,and as we are group of 4 the tasks separated as follows : one handle the widget implementation , two of us implement the database and manage habits ,and the last one is responsible for the graph generating and finding the added value to the app .

Accomplishments that we're proud of

building the core of the app in a limited period of time

What's next for habit_builder

deploy a server to get in touch with friends, family members and share the habits statistics with them
