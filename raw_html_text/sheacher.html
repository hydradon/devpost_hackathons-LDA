Inspiration

To make education and emergency services easily accessible to young refugee girls while allowing educated women to service wide audiences.

What it does

An application which will enable the creation of a woman to woman collaborative network.

The goal is to have female instructors teach female students via Radio and allow female students to subscribe to the channels.

How we built it

Twilio, Swift, FB Login, Zapier, Raspberry Pi, Coursera API, Facebook groups, Google Groups

Challenges we ran into

Had a hard time making the transmission from Raspberry pi, and broadcasting messages from twilio also took time

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Transmitting on a single channel, Using SMS for notifications for low connectivity and for emergency notifications

What we learned

A ton about server side programming, about Raspberry pi and integrating various api's

What's next for Sheacher

Making the application standalone and improving on emergency notifications
