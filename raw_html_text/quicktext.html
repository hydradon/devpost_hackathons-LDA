What it does

guickText Allows small business owners, or really anyone, to send SMS messages to any numbers that are rendered on a page. We used Intuit Quickbooks to demonstrate how this feature could facilitate customer interactions and improve engagement. Nexmo sends and receives the SMS messages and routes them to our Python server which stores the message history.
