Inspiration

Online content creators have recently been facing problems such as demonetization and removal from platforms like PayPal. A platform that would allow any content creator to easily monetise their content on a per-item basis would open many new opportunities for the world of online content creators.

What it does

Blurry Cash allows anyone to make a content stream, like a Facebook Wall, and choose how much they want to charge for each piece of content, or to give it away for free.
