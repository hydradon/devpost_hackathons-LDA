Inspiration

We believe that the solution proposed here is unique, has its advantage considering that something like this does not exist and one stop solution where Doctors can get more insight into the future clinics. The Site will suggest the most economical solution for entire sites including the medical devices and workforce needed. Furthermore, we can assure effective and efficient operations.

What it does

The “Blue2White” solution provided by Apo Bank will help doctors to overcome the barrier for starting their clinical practice and make the journey from concept to implementation hurdle free. The data-driven solution for Doctors to increase their confidence and remove anxiety from day one.

How I built it

The proposed solution will use the data provided by Apo bank (data bank or surveys) and 3D Floor Plan creation and editing using readily available 3D and VR solutions to implement in the platform.

Challenges I ran into
