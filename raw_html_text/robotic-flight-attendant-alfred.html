What it does

How we built it

Our team retrofitted a standard cart to first be motorized and powered by a power supply. Next we designed a housing for the top of the cart to house the drink dispensing system and provide an asthetically pleasing experience for the users.

Challenges we ran into

When trying to set up our drink dispensing system, the windshield washer pump was requiring 10+ volts to pump the liquid up and out of the holding tank. We solved this issue by making our storage tank water tight and flipping the tank so that the water pressure could reduce the voltage required by the pump to displace the liquid.
