Inspiration

To alter the direction of societies viewpoints on health. To create a health website that does not just focus on weight It sets a goal for body fat percentage rather than a weight goal that fails to take into account how body composition rather than just weight has a greater impact on health

What it does

With the users input of their body measurement, the body fat percentage is calculated.

How we built it

We built the website through Javascript and html.

Challenges we ran into

Time crunch led to limiting the purpose and the design of the website. We started with multiple ideas to create an app that could help determine a fat loss plan over a month. However, our limited knowledge in javascript only allowed us to finish creating a body fat calculator.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

First hackathon that we started and finished. Self taught javascript in 7 hours through Youtube.

What we learned

We learned to compromise with our limited knowledge on computer science and design.

What's next for Body Fat Calculator

The next steps are to input the users ideal body percentage. Through that, a monthly/yearly plan will be set up to identify necessary steps for them to achieve their goal. Steps may include calorie monitorization, dietary restrictions, and possible workout plans.
