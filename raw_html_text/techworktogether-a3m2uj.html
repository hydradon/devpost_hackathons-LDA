Inspiration

Being an IT person, I need some friends with same interests to work together.

What it does

Allows a user to post their ideas, work on other's ideas and explore different projects.

How I built it

Used proto.io to make a prototype for our mobile app. It is supplemented with PowerPoint Slide.

Challenges I ran into

Discussing each and every feature of this app, like the working, user experience, future developments, future prospects.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Learnt new software, build an innovative project working in a team of 6 people. The mobile app will be helpful for many people.

What I learned

My team learnt team skills, worked on a very innovative idea. We also learnt to use proto.io software and played more with PowerPoint.

What's next for TechWorkTogether

Make a paid version of TWT where professionals and companies can post their ideas and invite people to work on them. Also add features to the person's profile like projects started, projects participated which help employers to look for prospective candidates.
