Team 4 - Big Green

Inspiration

  • Helping awesome nonprofit organizations like Growing Gardens

What it does

  • Geolocate local gardens
  • Analyze garden statistics
  • Gamify participation in the program

How we built it

  • React/Redux with Material Design on the front end
  • Neo4j/Flask served on AWS Elastic Beanstalk on the back end

Challenges we ran into

  • Efforts for full functionality would've prevented design work

Accomplishments that we're proud of

  • A proof of concept that demonstrates the viability of our application idea
  • Solid contributions that would make a good app if brought together with more time

What we learned

  • How to use React, Flask, and Material Design
  • How to work together

What's next for Gardenia

  • Proper authentication
  • Integrating the back end with the front end
