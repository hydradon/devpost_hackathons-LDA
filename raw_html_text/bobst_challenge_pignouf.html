Control a bobst machine mockup with eye tracking and gestures using Leap motion

We have been inspired by different Movies and series like Black Mirror and Minority Report for the finger tracking. For the eye tracking we thought that we can be faster by just looking where you want to interact. It allows us also to reduce the number of interaction to be more efficient. The most challenging part was to detect different fingers postures and combine both technologies Eye Tracking from Tobii and Finger Tracking from Leap Motion.
