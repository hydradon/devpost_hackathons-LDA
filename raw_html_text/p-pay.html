Inspiration- There are several payment solutions for Finastra and when it comes to a FI choosing a payment solution, currently all the products are scattered. In order to provide and achieve an organized way of combining all payment types into a single platform we came up with this idea P-Pay.

What it does- Providing a one stop payment solution by integrating all payment products of Finastra to provide a uniform experience for the customers along with added capability of budgeting for an enhanced customer experience.
