Inspiration

Meghann recently was bored in class and then bought bitcoin right as the market crashed last month. Since then, she has been tirelessly pestering Jason with updates on how much profit she has made. They decided there needed to be a more extra/bougie/loud/annoying way to look up the price of cryptocurrencies, as well as, to monitor coin wallets. Jason and Meghann don't have much money to play with fun hardware, so they figured they might capitalize on this opportunity and learn to program Alexa!

What it does

Check Bitcoin Price Can check the current price of bitcoin

Compares Two Cryptocurrencies Can compare any two currencies between top five cryptocurrencies. Alexa responds with the coin of the highest value and how much greater it is than the second coins value. The skillset also returns a simple card

Add Cryptocurrency transactions to a database This takes in a users cryptocurrency transactions and adds them to a dynamodb database.

Check Cryptocurrency Profits/Defecits This pulls all transactions from the database under a specific cryptocurrency and sums the investment and percent of coin you own. Alexa then calculates and says the profit or deficit based on current prices. This returns a standard card describing the earnings, investment, and value of your coin.
