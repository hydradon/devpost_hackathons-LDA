Syllacal is a web service that imports all the vital exam dates and deadlines from your syllabus into your calendar

This allows you to plan your semester in the most convenient way possible!

We used Django, CSS, HTML (Bootstrap), and Python.

First, Syllacal scans your given PDF. It then exports relevant information surrounding a date (ex. 09/10, 09-10, September 10) to an .ics file This can be converted to your calendar (iCalendar, Google Calendar, and more).
