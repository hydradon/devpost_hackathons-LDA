Inspiration

We wanted to allow people with brilliant ideas to connect with innovators who would be able to solve the toughest solutions. By turning crowd funding on its head, we provide an interesting means of solving problems that the public desires.

What it does

The website allows people to fund projects that they want done. Someone can post a problem or idea to which they would like a solution, product, etc and creators campaign to be selected to create the product.

How we built it

We constructed a website that allows people to effectively view ideas and support the ideas that they would like to see in action.

Challenges we ran into

We didn't let our limited knowledge of web development hold us back, but our biggest pitfall was our lack of software experience in general - which we didn't let us hold us back.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Developing a website with minimal experience and getting creative with both the design and backend. We didn't use any frameworks and built everything from scratch except for the imported npm modules and the programming languages/scripting languages themselves.

What we learned

We learned about the processes of developing a website.
