When people are the opposite sides of the world from each other, they take comfort in looking up at the same sky. Star Cross'd takes advantage of this physical separation and gives people the ability to not only communicate with each other, but to do so through a shared stargazing experience. People point their phones at a particular star in the sky and write a message to their loved ones on it. The message can only be read by looking at the exact same star in the sky.
