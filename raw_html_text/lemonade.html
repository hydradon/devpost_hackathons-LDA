lemonade

“I was served lemons, but I made lemonade.” Millions of people suffer from some form of mental illness. Many of which are afraid to seek help and treatment from healthcare professionals.

Lemonade is an online forum that provides a community for people that suffer from mental health disorders to share their stories, support, and inspire each other. This allows people to find others who have gone through similar experiences, providing hope and making them feel as if they are not alone in their struggles. We aim to promote a safe and nonjudgmental environment in order to make all users feel comfortable.
