What it does

OPEN GARAGE is one of the WMS( Warehouse Management System ). Our service has two unique points. Firstly, your garage is opened to the public. Everyone can see what you deposit in OPEN GARAGE. Of course you can see others’ garages. So you can know the products is actually used by real users.

Secondly, you can borrow others’ products. If you want to use garage function, you can use cheaper than any other’s WMS instead of opening your outfits to the public. When you find favorite one, you can ask the owner to borrow. If they accept your request, you can borrow it. You can try it before you’ve decided to buy or not. In addition, the owner can earn money depends on his accept.
