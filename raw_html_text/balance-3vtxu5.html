Inspiration

We were inspired by the TD speaker's quote "Invest in you." As young adults on a tight budget, we feel guilty for spending money on leisure. To us, "Invest in you" means saving money for the future but also taking time for yourself or spending it with others, which sometimes requires spending. With our budgeting app, Balance, we want to encourage young adults to spend their money wisely and on experiences that contribute to their well-being.

What it does

Using their income and transaction history, Balance recommends ways that the user can save money or spend wisely.

How we built it

Python, Yodlee API, Figma

Challenges we ran into

The Yodlee API and refining our idea and focus to solve a complex problem.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Having a demo-able product!

What we learned

The key to a strong solution is to narrow down our problem.

What's next for Balance

Doing more research into ways young adults can spend their money wisely to give more effective tips for their mental and physical well-being
