Luck Love Life

A psychological short game about morality and survival in outer space.

Best experienced in headphones.

About the project

Made with Unity and GIMP for the 2018 Hackers at Cambridge Game Gig.

The entirety of this project was created during the game jam.

The only assets not created by me are the audio assets taken from the public domain, and the fonts free for personal use: "A Song for Jennifer" and "Life is a dream".

Author: Jakub (Kuba) Perlin

Date: Dec 1st 2018

How to run:

Download the zipped folder from Github, unpack, and open the index.html in a Firefox browser.

How to play:

  • arrow keys (or A/D ) – walk

  • space – interact

  • M – mute
