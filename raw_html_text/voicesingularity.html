Inspiration

We came together with a single purpose: to apply the mathematics we had been studying in school to benefit humankind. Also, that scene from mission impossible where Ethan Hunt beats Phillip Seymour Hoffman and mimics his voice by recording his voice was cool and we wanted to bring that to reality.

What it does

It differentiates one person's voice from another and can mimic another person's voice through ongoing highly adaptive machine learning algorithms.

How We built it

We utilized Fourier Analysis, Decision Trees, Machine Learning, Signal Processing and Doritos.
