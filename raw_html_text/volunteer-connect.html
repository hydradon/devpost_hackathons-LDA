Inspiration

Based on personal volunteer experience that registering and applying for volunteer roles is slow, tedious, requires lots of paperwork and it can takes sometimes weeks to be notified about the role. Also, it is difficult to find opportunities in you area of interest and location.

What it does

One app for organisations to post volunteer roles. One app for volunteers to easily search and apply for these roles

How I built it

Prototype phase still in development

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Develop entire viability model in short time period

What I learned

Our idea does have some competitors in the market however we our app addresses the gaps our competitors are missing
