Inspiration

Housing market has always been a hot topic to predict prices. Here we try to gain insights and find the house according to your needs. We feel buying a house should suit your needs first and price is a later discussion.

What it does

It gives you insights on the various properties in Melbourne and analyzes the suburban regions to narrow down the right house. You can search for houses based on your family needs, no. of children and utilities around the place.

How we built it

We used Omni-Sci's GPU accelerated visualization and Pandas to create tables and preprocess data.

Challenges we ran into

Not enough datsets. Had to create synthetic data

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting some interesting insights from a small dataset.

What we learned

GPU accelrated visualization can help gain better insights.

What's next for Melbourne Housing

This can be expanded to every city in the world based on the data you get.
