Inspiration

My college ministry has been needing a website to do various things. The main problem they have is with organizing rides, especially for the large amounts of people who sign up for rides late. Automating this process would solve this.

What it does

Allows users to sign up as a rider or driver; pairs riders to drivers based on a few factors like location, house church, etc.

How I built it

I used mongo db for the backend and React for the front end.

Challenges I ran into

I've never used MongoDB before.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I used MongoDB.

What I learned

MongoDB kind of

What's next for Rides Organizer

Adding more functionality in terms of dropping the database at weekly intervals, sms functionality, etc.
