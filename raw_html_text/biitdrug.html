MedhanitAwaki

This project is about the development of an app that simplifies the process of purchasing off the counter as well as prescription drugs. It links the physicians, who prescribe drugs, consumers who want to purchase drugs, and the pharmacists that sell the drugs. It also allows the physicians and consumers to search the databases of all pharmacists who are listed in the database.

Furthermore, payment systems like such as the MBirr, payment with CBE or Dashen cards or banks internet payment systems will be included for local payments and credit/debit cards, bitcoins is also included for international consumers , who may be willing to pay on behalf of families and friends who live in Ethiopia, and Local Customers with access to Bitcoin.

Delivery options are available with a local delivery firm at a cost as well as through direct contact with the customer.
