Great ideas hit us in the most unexpected ways. As the process goes for most hackathon teams, we had trouble with our ideas from time to time. We thought of Chatbot first as the trend in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning in Vietnam is growing at a rapid pace. It turned out that Facebook no longer entertains more Chatbot in the house. A member of our team came up with a better idea, the inspiration of which stemmed from her dating and hook-up history: build a dating website. But wait, Tinder has done the same thing, and we were sure that it is much more superior to what we can create with a team of 6. Dating? What other form of dating can we have? Speed dating! In the matter of a second, Quick! was born.
