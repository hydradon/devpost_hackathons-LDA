Inspiration

I sit next to windows in office and it is hot

What it does

Whispers to your ears with breeze. FlirAir tracks your position with thermal imaging accurately, and it contains an absolute silent CPU fan which generate airflow directly on you. You can further customize the experience with FlirAir mobile app.

How I built it

FLIR Lepton. 140mm 1200rpm PWM super silent PC CPU/chassis fan. 2 servos for rotation and tilting. Use ice pop sticks to build the rack.

Challenges I ran into

We do not have the materials to build the rack. It is not easy to find a stationary shop for ice pop sticks. We did think of buy and finish 20 ice pops for the project. Also, the rack is difficult to build as the fan is heavy.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I can feel breeze in my office.

What I learned

python, swift and FLIR Lepton SDK.

What's next for FlirAir
