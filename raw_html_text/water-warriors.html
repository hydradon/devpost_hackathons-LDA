Inspiration

We wanted to inspire others to create change in helping Lake Erie become a better waterway.

What it does

We built a game that focuses on inciting change. The format of the game revolves around a high school student who kayaks leisurely. After training in Lake Erie for half a decade, they decided that it was time to fix the waterway. While looking for patrons to sponsor his initiatives in cleaning up the waterway, the kayaker petitions to stop pollution and to promote others to take action. We wanted to inspire people similar to the student to enact change.
