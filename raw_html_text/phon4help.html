Inspiration

Trying to learn languages on your own but you need to learn pronunciation. For instance, teaching my roommate to speak Russian without him knowing any Russian sounds.

What it does

Analyzes IPA (International Phonetic Alphabet) of the foreign word provided and provides similar sounding English words to help understand vowel sounds in foreign language.
