Almost everyone, at some point in their life, has found themselves visiting a doctor for a minor injury or musculoskeletal conditions. Doctors often suggest “at home” exercises to do on a daily basis as therapy and/or to keep the condition from getting worse. Sadly, the lack of an immediate threat from our current condition or an intolerable pain level makes most of us neglect doing the exercises and following up with the doctor. To everyone who can relate to this, medLeap is your savior! It uses Leap Motion to track the exercises you do and makes sure you do them regularly. It keeps track of your daily progress that both you and your doctor can see and comment on. Basically, medLeap makes sure your injury stays minor.
