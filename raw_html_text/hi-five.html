Inspiration

The 1 Second Everyday app ( https://1se.co ), which helps you capture the small moments in life. We wanted to gamify building good habits and having friends (or strangers) hold each accountable!

What it does

You and friend send a one-second video every day documenting how you continued progress on your habit. Once both people send a video, the high five is complete (woo!) and the daily videos are saved into a running gallery of how both of you are improving.

How I built it

Lots of typing. Firebase backend, vanilla HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript.
