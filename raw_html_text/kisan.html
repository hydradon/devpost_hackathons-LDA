Problem and Need

Agriculture Sector is backbone of Pakistan but due to lot of problems per acre yield is very low in Pakistan as compare to other countries. It account 21% of GDP It employs almost 53 % of the labor force of Pakistan. Farmers work day and night and still fail to get what they deserve. Why the Farmers in Pakistan cannot sell their products at their own price?

Solution

There is no platform available which connect small farmers directly with actual buyers (factories and ) to sell their crops.

Therefore a digital market is required which fulfill this need in our community.

Target Audience Small Farmers.

An App That will ask farmers for input of crops they like to cultivate and get recommendations about land conditions for the crop to grow
