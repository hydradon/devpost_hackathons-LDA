Inspiration

Ghost gear doing harmful damage around the world.

What it does

Reduce and eventually prevent any lost of fisherman's gear from happening.

How we built it

We worked as a team, coding with a united goal and delegated task according to our strengths in the team.

Challenges we ran into

physical and mental fatigue.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Creating the heat map in and dealing with unstructured SQL and using FireBase all for the first time.

What we learned

Time is precious. Careful planning of schedule for coding and resting is of paramount importance.

What's next for Catch

Implementing deep learning to the system so that the results will be more accurate with predictive-analytics. A chat system for fishermen to communicate among one another to exchange knowledge and experience of the sea.
