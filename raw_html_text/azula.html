My enjoyment of hacking is to ensure that everyone benefits from every hack I develop. Motivated my self to developed an artificial in very similar to Siri "iOS" & Alexa "Amazon" in less than 24 hrs! I want to make this free for everyone!

What Azüla does

Azüla is a voice-driven artificial intelligence personal assistant with a voice-controlled natural language interface that uses sequential inference to help perform personal tasks and increase users happiness by providing funny jokes and help set-up reminders, pay bills, math tutor and the capabilities are endless!

How I built it Azüla

  • CCS3
  • CSS
  • Jquery Animation (Animation of Azüla)
  • Sketch 3 (UI & UX Designs)
  • Modern JavaScript Utility Library (lodash.js) (Chrome Voice Recognition)
  • JavaScript Preprocessor
