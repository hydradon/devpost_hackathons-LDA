• JobJob - Easygoing job search.

Inspiration

We we're frustrated with current recruitment processes from both sides: emphatisizing both the recruiter and the applicant. The recruitment process is completely broken: Recruitment agencies bill a humongous 420 billion US$ in sales annually and the avg. time to hire is 12 weeks (25% of a year) from posting the job with finding the candidate pool, interviewing and qualifying eating the lion share of time

When we ran intoMicrosoft's Machine Learning, we saw an opportunity and cheerfully questioned our existence: How would Aliens tackle recruiting, a 100 years from now?

JobJob was born.

What it does

JobJob makes job search easygoing. We're building easy, integrable and smart web tools for recruitment agencies, HR managers and CEO's alike, who want to take their recruitment efforts into the 31st century. (yes you read right, 31st century). This is alien recruitment, after all.

We're building an app that enables job applicants to swipe job posts right and left created by recruiters. For recruiters we enable smart search, ranking, categorization and sorting of applicants through keyword, machine learning and algorithms.

We decided to target our tool to Recruitment agencies at first, since our front-end works at one and we have key insight on the industry; we want to enable them to increase their value through us. Plus the business and the industry is big enough.

Always be learning is the motto at JobJob.
