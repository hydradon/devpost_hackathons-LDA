Tech to care

There's lots of people in the world for whom a fall could be the end of their lives, and so they need to either give up their own independence or else live in fear at all times. The PebbleCare allows an individual to install an app, and, should they fall, the accelerator in the watch will trigger and, following a brief time to allow for override in the event of a mis-trigger, will text the up to nine individuals set up in the phone app, with a preset custom message and their current GPS location and address. This kind of rapid feedback takes the benefit of services such as LifeAlert and makes it as mobile as you are, traveling wherever you take your phone!
