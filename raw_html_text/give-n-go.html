Inspiration

Take our short questionnaire on our website, then we’ll help you figure out how you can make an impact on your community. Our goal is to help you find the volunteering opportunities that fit your personality the best.

What it does

After taking the quiz, our algorithm matches you to a project area with opportunities where your skills will be utilized. In addition, you can filter through your results to find a match that suits you. Once you are matched to a project you are given the option to sign up to volunteer for that opportunity or donate to the cause.
