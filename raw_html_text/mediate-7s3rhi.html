Inspiration

We are four engineers who wanted to build a device that augments human performance and benefits the consumers who use it.

What it does

Mediate allows users to record their days and ask questions of conversations that happened in the day and generates summaries of all conversations the user has. It also detects heart rate and emotional state of the user for mental health monitoring.
