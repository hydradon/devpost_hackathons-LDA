• AdBlocker with Speaker

  • Desktop Application

We like Spotify. We don't like ads. And we don't like paying for Spotify Premium.

Luckily, we had some time on our hands at UNL Cornhacks 2019 and decided to hack a solution to stop listening to Spotify ads. In their place, our setup plays songs of our choice that we've loaded onto a Raspberry Pi through a custom desktop application.
