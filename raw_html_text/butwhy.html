• User enters search term "Coffee"

  • Buttons respond and update to reflect the user inspiration.

  • The Uber button will take you to the nearest "Coffee" (location found using Google Maps API)

  • The iTunes button lets you discover a new artist, "Coffee"

Inspiration

We wanted to make some kind of project at a hackathon, for once. We came to BostonHacks hoping to be inspired by some of the APIs available for our use, and Button stuck out to us as one that could be easily reimagined to fit our bizarre ideas. We decided to make an app that aims to give you all the Buttons, or at least as many as possible.

Use

Are you looking for an easy way to integrate your whimsical desires into all of your favorite apps at once? What would you say if we told you we can give you a way to find the nearest or most relevant coffee, treehouse, green, or sunglasses in Uber, Delivery, Drizly, iTunes, Hotels.com, Jet, and more than ten other popular mobile applications?

"But... why?"
