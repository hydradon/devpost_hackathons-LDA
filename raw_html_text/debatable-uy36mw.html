DEBATABLE - a safe space

If you ever open the comments section on any form of social media over a political argument, chances are that someone's being attacked for expressing their opinion. Most times, even if an individual is hateful, a person cannot mentally grow because they are overwhelmed with hate around them.

'Debatable' provides a safe space for 2 people to have a conversation without having others badger any one of them. One person provides a topic of interest (such as the POTUS) and someone else of an opposing viewpoint can have a "debate" with them. Conversations stay civil, and if a person trying to converse feels uncomfortable with where the conversation is heading, they can always leave. Viewers are allowed to watch the conversation but are not allowed to take part in order to avoid unmanageable conflict.
