Inspiration

How to make something which would be useful in a daily life basis.

What it does

It just gives a sound alarm whenever it is near of something with different levels of tones for different distances, the closer you get to the subject the faster the tone gets faster.

How I built it

Using Arduino as the brain and an ultrasonic sensor to measure the distance, and a buzzer for the alarm sound,also an LCD just for the fun of using one and displaying the measured distance!

Challenges I ran into

Not to make the LCD refresh REALLY FAST.
