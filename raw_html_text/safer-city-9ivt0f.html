Inspiration

Some neighborhoods in Philadelphia have high crime rates, we want to increase awareness so people are more cautious when travelling through certain neighborhoods.

What it does

Allows users to explore crime in a specific neighborhood with reports that come directly from online community from your neighborhood

How we built it

Android, Firebase, EveryBlock

Challenges we ran into

crowdsourcing data, sometime there's not enough information or data to parse

Accomplishments that we're proud of

First time finishing an app using android

What we learned

How to use Firebase API, EveryBlock

What's next for Safer City

Scale it to other cities and neighborhoods
