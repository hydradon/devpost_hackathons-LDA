What it does

You enter in your program, and the term you're enrolled in. It tells you how much money you would have if you invested the cost of your tuition in bitcoin at the time that you paid it, as well as what the price of bitcoin was during each payment.

How I built it

I used node.js, and html/css. I don't have much experience with js so it isn't very good.

Challenges I ran into

It's hard to calculate the exact price of bitcoin for all the possible payment dates/programs/streams. So I made it really inaccurate by just taking the dates for fall/winter terms.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It looks good visually.

What I learned

How to efficiently use nodejs routes, and work with APIs.
