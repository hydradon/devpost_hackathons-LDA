HangryApp

Sometimes, we feel like you get so hungry and frustrated that going on an application to find good eats is the last thing you'd want to do.

So we figured a clean interface with some good ol' AI could fix that problem.

Our app analyzes your voice using an emotionally intelligent ai api, and makes quick suggestions based on your mood. Not too hungry? It will detect the calm in your voice and give you quite a broad range of suggestions. Stomach rumbling and Hanger taking over? HangryApp will sense that edge, and give you only the closest restaurants nearby, favoring faster food joints over slower ones.
