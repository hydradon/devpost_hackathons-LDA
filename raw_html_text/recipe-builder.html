Inspiration

As a university student, we usually do not have a full fridge of groceries to cook with. Therefore we must scavenge and try to make something out of what we have. We created a cool Alexa skill that helps with exactly that.

What it does

It prompts the user for what they have in the kitchen and provides them with a delicious and easy food to

How we built it

We used Node.js, lamda and AWS to create the Amazon Echo skill necessary for this function.

Challenges we ran into

None of us have ever used the Amazon Echo or AWS before so it was quite the challenge configuring it and making it work.

What's next for Recipe Builder

We would love to add more recipes based on the and allow the user to store food into it so the Echo can remember what food is available.
