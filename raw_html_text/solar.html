Project Solar

HackUMass Prototype
Calculates the estimated cost to install solar panels with user address

Inspiration

As a team, we wanted to work with everyone's common interest. After brief brainstorming, we decided to work on a project that is related to environment. The idea of finding sustainable energy may not be new, however, it is not easy for an average person to familiarize themselves with the intricacies of a photovoltaic power systems. We wanted to come up with more efficient, practical, and user-friendly way for environmentally-conscious people to estimate the cost for the installation of such system.
