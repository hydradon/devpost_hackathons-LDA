Imageless

Imagesless is a chrome extension designed to allow you to customize your very own internet experience. We allow you to filter out any images that match the key words that you enter into the extension. This extension works on a large variety of websites and will make happening upon unpleasant pictures much more likely. This can have many uses ranging from a parental add block to simply removing photos that remind you of your recently deceased dog.
