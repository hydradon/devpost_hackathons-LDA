Inspiration

Not knowing where to go on a train for an empty seat.

What it does

Counts people entering and exiting certain zone.

How we built it

2 micro pic micro controller, each receives data from 2 IR sensors, counting how many people entered or exited the area.

Challenges we ran into

Sensors not working properly - too sensitive, cross rays.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Overcoming all the problems we run into, one after another, while having the best logic solution in mind.
