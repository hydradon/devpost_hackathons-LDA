As a team we were inspired to work on something creative, funny and be proud of at the end of it. Visiting Philly from Toronto and being students immersed in Pop Culture we saw the opportunity to play off the feud between each hometown rapper.

The name of the game is Meek Mill's Worst Nightmare because the user plays as Meek Mill and is immersed in a 3D Hotline Bling inspired VR maze. Drake has become Meek's worst nightmare after having dropped "Back-to-Back". The objective of the game is to survive as long as possible in the maze. Listen for the music as it gets louder as Drake approaches, if he catches you the game is over. Being a busy rapper you need to maximize your sleep to be able to continue to make raps.

Do you have what it takes......?
