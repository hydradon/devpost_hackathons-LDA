Inspiration

As first year college students, we wanted a user-friendly one-stop location for students to access general information about adulthood that is often not taught in school. Essentially, this is the website we wish we had during our first semester in college.

What it does

This website provides pages of general "adulting" information that are organized, quick, and easy to read.

How we built it

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap.

Challenges we ran into

Text and image formatting on HTML, understanding GitHub repositories

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It works.

What we learned

New languages, GitHub, basic Command Prompt/Terminal, accessing databases.

What's next for Hacking Adulthood

Implementing more user-interactive features and further developing the user-profile aspect of the site. Researching and adding more practical information that may help young adults function in life.
