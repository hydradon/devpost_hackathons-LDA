Inspiration: We want to connect Baltimore's programs to the community in order to enable them to access the resources they need.

What it does: Streamlines the process for homeless/displaced/people in need for quick access to healthcare, encompassing physical, mental, and emotional health . Allows the user to gain information regarding facilities that can provide them with the specific care they need.

How we built it: We developed a Reactive webpage that utilizes various APIs and database management tools, such as Firebase, in order to develop a quick and responsive application.

Challenges we ran into: Some challenges we ran into include, setting up the database, processing of information, parsing data, creating a fluid layout, and maintaining overall simplicity.

Accomplishments that we're proud of: We're proud of the fluidity of the overall layout of the website. In addition to this, we're also extremely proud of the fact that we were able to parse natural language.

What we learned: We expanded our knowledge on full stack development skills.

What's next for Baltimore's Community Resources: Our future goals include dynamically loading new locations onto the website, adding on additional services beyond those provided, and to improve user experience while using the application.
