crash_code

The WebApp for beginners at coding! Learn the basics of Java, Python, and JavaScript through our interactive and instructive site. crash_code targets girls who may find coding daunting and makes it fun and simple to learn the basics in three common programming languages. Studies have shown that women who do not feel prepared for a task will be dissuaded from doing it. crash_code's goal is to help people feel more prepared and confident in coding.
