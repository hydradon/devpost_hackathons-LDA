STEMentors is aimed at connecting you, as a high school student, with current university students in STEM majors in order to give you an idea of what STEM is about. You will work alongside your chosen university student on a STEM project. With this mentorship and support, STEMentors will enable you to have an idea of what to expect if you decide to major in a STEM field
