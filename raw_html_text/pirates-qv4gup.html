Inspiration

Pirates, cannons, gold and explosions.

The East India Company are trying to steal the pirates' future, destroy the EIC main fort in the area and save your future of ... gentlemanly conduct and whatever the opposite of pillaging is.

How to play

Move the mouse left/ right to aim. Scroll the mouse wheel up/ down to change power. Left mouse button to fire.

Destroy as many EIC items as you can in 60 seconds.

How I built it

Using Unity and bought art assets.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Finishing, having a complete game loop.

What's next for Pirates

Maybe continue development for mobile, using gyroscope for aiming.
