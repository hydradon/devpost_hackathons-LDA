Almost anything can be bought online, but one thing has been missing. Until today...

Bringing cuddles to your door, CatsAndMore.store!

Thanks to the Yaas (SAP Hybris as a Service), we were able to quickly get a storefront running and focus our attention on what really matters. Browse our curated selection of cats and find the companionship you need. From LOLCats to Grumpy Cats and all cats in between, we're sure to have just the kitty you've been looking for.

The true power of Yaas shines with the integrated tracking via the Profile Graph API, which builds a customer profile over time. This profile can be explored to see which products a customer is interested in, and the storefront can be adapted to serve tailored results for every kind of customer.
