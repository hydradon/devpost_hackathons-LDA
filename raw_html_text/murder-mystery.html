Inspiration

We wanted to make something that was fun but simple.

What it does

Murder Mystery is a simple, text based game revolving around you, the detective as you search for clues about the murder of Judge Rodgers in his estate. The game ends after 3 days when the police arrive. By then I hope you know who did it...

How I built it

Murder Mystery was made using C++ and Windows Visual Studio 2019

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

This was the biggest project either of us have ever made and when we started only 1 of us spoke C++.

What I learned

Gavin learned a whole new language, I found out that the majority of my error messages were from me going to fast and forgetting things like quotation marks or semicolons.

What's next for Murder Mystery

We were asked to make a sequel where you are the murderer and you're trying to hide from the detective. We're not totally sure how to get that done just yet, but if we go to another hackathon we'll be sure to figure it out.
