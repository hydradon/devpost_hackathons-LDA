Inspiration

Caesura was developed out of a need for accessible and reliable education that anyone could contribute to. Students often use social network channels like facebook to help each other with tutorial and assignments, but they're inefficient and hard to access. Caesura combines a user's social network and their ability to share academic information, like tutorials and essays.

How it works

Caesura is a communal education resource that allows users to be students and teachers. It's a gathering place for curious minds that want to connect, learn, and create. Users can write seminars on areas of knowledge they specialize in, which allows other users to learn about new topics and ask questions.
