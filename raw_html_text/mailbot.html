Inspiration

Remembering people's email could be difficult, especially when they have the same names!

What it does

Once you send message to this bot, it automatically sorts out where does your mail needs to go.

How we built it

With machine learning, we feed a big chunk of dataset, trains and model. It first sees the name of recipient then check their most received key words. And the email goes to the correct person.

Challenges we ran into

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What's next for MailBot

Enhanced dataset. Bot will continuously learn every time one sends an email.
