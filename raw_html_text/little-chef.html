Inspiration

Anyone can cook. We wanted to follow in Chef Gusteau's footsteps and promote the art of cooking. Little Chef is a simple interface that curates recipes for each user profile.

What it does

Little Chef pulls details from a given profile to return appropriate recipes available online.

How we built it

React. At least we tried to.
