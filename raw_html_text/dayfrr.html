Made at HackGSU 2018. Discord bot that uses Google Vision and Yelp to recommend restaurants. Ever hanging with friends online and wanna go out to eat while and can't figure out what to eat then here's our solution! We use Discord.py to create a bot that services at the middleman to get user input. The Google Vision API Is used to "teach" the bot new foods by processing images and getting data from them and storing it for later use. The Yelp API takes user input or data from the google vision database to recommend a restaurant based on the inputted information.
