Inspiration

The importance of agriculture in the world cannot be over emphasized. Just like every other field agriculture comes with its own problems. Here we have come up with a solution to tackle the most frequent problems associated to agriculture via the use of technology and as such improving it yeilds

What it does

This device or robot will be fully automated and programmed to carry out monitoring activities within the farm land. With the aid of specialized sensors it can check the moisture of the soil, its fertility and it can also alert the farmer when fertilizer should be reapplied to increase the fertility of the soil via text messages and emails. Triggers a system to sprinkle water when the soil is not moist enough.
