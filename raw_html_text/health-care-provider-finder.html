Inspiration

To be able to quickly find doctors near you

What it does

It finds the closest doctor based on your location and then texts it to your phone with directions from google maps

How I built it

Integrated with San Francisco city data to get list of health care providers and then sends text message through Twilio

Challenges I ran into

I just started this at around 1 pm after the workshop I wanted was full. Also I was working alone and I'm a total newb when it comes to Alexa and Lamdas.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

That I finished something and it actually works!

What I learned

A bunch about Alexa and Lambdas.

What's next for Health Care Provider Finder

Lots more, I just got started. It could clearly be more interactive, so you can filter the list and it would give you multiple options based on different criteria.
