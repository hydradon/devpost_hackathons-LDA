Inspiration

For Senior : Feeling of loneliness and isolation can lead to serious issues for senior health. Understanding the causes and the risk factors that surround the senior will eventually help them a lot.

For environmental issue: What was so frustrating, aside from a mailbox full of ads, was seeing all this paper, ink and dye going to waste. The natural resources consumed, the toxic chemicals used – all for that piece of paper to reach your mailbox in a vain attempt to capture your attention… 44 percent of junk mail is thrown away unopened, but only half of that (22 percent) is recycled.

What it does

The idea to create an awareness involving all sectors of our society such as the community, grassroots, Government level that our seniors need our help. We will create platform where our seniors can actively participate and remain relevant to society thus boosting their self-worthiness. It is an eco-system that encourage business entities to advertise for a cause.

How we built it

  • We will invite a web and software developer to develop the desired program
