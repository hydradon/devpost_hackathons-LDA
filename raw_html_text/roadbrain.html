Inspiration

Poor maintenance of the road and high related cost

What it does

Automatically detects and maps potholes

How we built it

We created a mobile app to detect pathholes and send those interactions to a central database. There is a webapp with dashborad to view the data and prediction information

Challenges we ran into

Business model, integration with Here Maps

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The complexity of the technical solution provided given the time at hands and the business strategy governing its spread

What we learned

How to collaborate with people from very different backgrounds in a dynamic and time sensitive setting

What's next for RoadBrain

Collaborate with Eurovia to test the integration of the technology and build on additional features presented.
