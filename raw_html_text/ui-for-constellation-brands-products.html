Inspiration

We were inspired when we were told that we were the target demographic for Constellation Brands products.

What it does

If our project was implemented on a larger scale, qr codes or nfc chips would be included in product packaging. Scanning or tapping a code adds that tag to your user's "purchased" products. Each tag has a brand identifier, product identifier, and unique product id. For example: 52[SVK]56e3a15

Your product score is compared to your friends' on a leaderboard. This data can also be used to recommend products you might like.
