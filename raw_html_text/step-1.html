Inspiration

What's a P/E ratio? How about a Current Ratio? A company's Market Capitalization? For hundreds of millions of Americans, financial literacy is simply hard - and brokerages don't help. Current financial institutions provide vast swaths of data, with little insight for the average American. We're here to help.

What it does

We provide step by step stock research tools. We compare stock returns to benchmarks, and allow individuals to a gain insight into a company through the data points which matter to them. In addition, before adding a data point to their dashboard, we offer users the ability to learn more about that data source and why it is important.

How we built it

We built this by leveraging a suite of cloud products and APIs. We used Google Firebase extensively and used financial APIs to find troves of corporate data.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We're proud that our charts are well designed, dynamic, and support all Equities and ETFs that users would be interested in.

What's next for Step 1

We'd like to add Assistant Actions and Alexa Skills to gain more functionality.
