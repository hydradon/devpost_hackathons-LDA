Inspiration

Patient education is essential to ensure optimal outcomes for those who are transitioning to home care after vascular procedures. We sought to assess the efficacy of a novel mobile technology-based educational tool to increase patient knowledge, ease the transition to home care, and improve the health care experience.

What it does

Interactive media (a responsive module) that enhances geriatric patients' understanding of medical treatment and diagnosis

How we built it

Used html,css, javscript, 3dmodeling maya
