Inspiration

Many college girls keep track of how many drinks they have had on a night by drawing tally marks on their wrists, and often don't know how many drinks they can safely consume for their body weight. We want to help college students to drink responsibly.

What it does

Allows user to keep track of the number of drinks consumed and uses the commonly accepted Widmark formula to calculate blood alcohol content based on sex, weight and the amount of time spent drinking.

How I built it

In xcode I set up a form to collect user data. This data was used to run a counter and blood alcohol counter. The buttons were designed from scratch in adobe illustrator using vectors.
