Inspiration

Medium, Reddit, http://www.recommendmeabook.com/ , the hassle of researching everywhere just to satisfy my thirst for knowledge

What it does

Allows users to learn and contribute topics they want to share. They are able to read a summary of a topic and go to another page to see other information about it whether it be through the form of documentaries, podcasts, or articles. It encompasses various types of information about a topic in one place, saving the user from the hassle of researching through various sources.
