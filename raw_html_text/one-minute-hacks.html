Inspiration

Our world is filled with unlimited possibilities but how each of us perceives that world is wildly different.

There are millions of ways you can complete your own task list, whether it’s through tackling the most difficult tasks in the morning, hiring an assistant, focusing on batches of tasks in the evening power hour or completing all tasks that take less than 2 minutes immediately.

It’s all about energy and focus. Work smarter, not harder. The results compound over time and will most certainly lead to your most exceptional work.

Some of the simplest hacks this skill has gives you a chance to relook at how you do things daily in your life. The point of this skill is to help you get work done smartly than doing it the hard way.

The biggest inspiration for this hack is "If you want to be amazing at something you have to practice it every single day. That means you don’t take days off. Not even on Christmas." - Seinfeld’s Productivity Chain

What it does

One Minute Hacks is a collection of highly successful and simple life hacks that can get you organized and work smartly in just a minute.

Ask Alexa to open "One Minute Hacks", listen to the instruction and say "go" to begin your journey to quickly amplify your daily life.
