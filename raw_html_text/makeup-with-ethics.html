Inspiration

I got an app called "Buycott", which lets me scan the barcode of products to see if it fits the campaigns (pro-union, vegan, etc) I signed up for. After scanning some of my products, this got me thinking; what is in my makeup? What should I be looking for when I am buying makeup? What is veganism? Gluten-free? How can makeup be vegan or gluten free? So I took to the internet and researched the ideas behind the campaigns. And I highlighted this information on my website.

What it does

This website works to highlight three major campaigns that exists in cosmetics. The user can click a campaign that interests them and learn about brands that pledged to that campaign.

How I built it

Wix

Challenges I ran into

Time. Working with the website. Digging through the company website and other sources to determine if they really have followed their pledge.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I made a website. Figuring out 3D picture slideshow.

What I learned

Wix is the bomb. And very little companies are completely paraben-free.

What's next for Makeup with Ethics

To highlight major makeup brands individually and to add more campaign. And maybe add one line of code. Maybe.
