Through this app, we wanted people to learn facts around the world in one line

This app will show users facts about the world with several categories like natural, history, animals and so on.

We collected data from various sources and it has been built with Android studio and Firebase.

Data collection was the main challenging part for us.

Finally, good outcome and polished app make us proud of what we made.

Every good app requires patience and lots of efforts to make it nice.

We are planning to add user onboarding and kin integration with app and allow user to post facts by their own.
