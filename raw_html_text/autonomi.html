Inspiration- The large handicapped population traveling via airplane.

What it does- This eliminates the amount of wheelchair transfers, 3-4 people helping, resolves complaints, provides a luxury flight experience,

How we built it-The model is made of cardboard, superglue, tape, and various other simple materials

Challenges we ran into-target demographic, ways to make this work on an airplane, size requirements

Accomplishments that we're proud of-the entire project

What we learned- The disability traveling industry is very lucrative but very inefficient of how it treats and retains it handicapped passengers
