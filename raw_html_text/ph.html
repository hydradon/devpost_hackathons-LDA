Note-it-Now

Take control of your notes today.

Note-it-Now allows you to take images of your notes, and using a custom made pattern matching system, allows for one to bookmark notes and categorize them more effectively.

Note-it-Now attempts to automatically splice your notes into sections, thus allowing you to be more efficient.
