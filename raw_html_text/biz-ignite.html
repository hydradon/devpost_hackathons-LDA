Inspiration

Small Businesses are an important aspect to the job industry in America. It is important that there is a large density of small business in all aspects and fields of business so that corruption in economy, such as monopolies, can be avoided.

What it does

Helps a leader or Manager of a small business run and manage the business. it is a small Java application that manages data for 3 important aspects of a company (Employees, Inventory, Money Managment).

How we built it

Using Java for backend logic and the AWT and SWING libraries for UI

Challenges we ran into

Trying to Make an MVC environment with Data

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Making an MVC environement and algorithims

What we learned

More time planning and be content with what is planned

What's next for Biz Ignite

Its prototyped in Java, it would be cool to learn web development and move the concept of the application to that platform.
