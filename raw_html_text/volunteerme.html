Inspiration

Organizations often need volunteers, but it can be hard both for organizations to find enough volunteers, and for volunteers to quickly find causes that match their schedule and interests.

What it does

This web app will allow organizations to post events and tag it with a category, e.g. environment or healthcare. Users can find these events based on category as well as distance from their location. Users can also subscribe to categories to find upcoming events that match their interests. Organizations can also send email notifications to users.
