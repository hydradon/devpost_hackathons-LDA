We want to increase the awareness of african culture among our youths in africa, our culture is dying!

This is a filter effect to make you re-imagine your self as a nigerian god (Sango)

This was built using the spark AR studio, we did most of the user interface icons and textures on corel-draw. The patch editor was used to control when the hair should be visible and when not to. It also controls the visibility based our much we turn our head.

Some of the team members wanted to have the not part of the filter effect, some wanted wanted ( i wanted it).

We accomplished the project, and even solved our challenge. We created a screen tap toggle to disable and enable the hair.

Majorly Spark AR, its facial tracking is superb. We also learnt our to use the patch editor effectively.

We plan to populate it for other Nigerian Gods, then move out to different African countries.
