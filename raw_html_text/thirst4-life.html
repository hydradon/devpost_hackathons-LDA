Inspiration

What's the first thing you think about when hearing someone you just met is from Romania? Most certainly, the most common answers are Dracula, Hagi, Nadia! (and maybe Vadim?)

Even so, even if most of them know that Dracula is from Transylvania, not all of them know that Transylvania is in Romania.

That’s why we decided to make a website for those who are crazy about the myths and legends of count Dracula.

What it does

This is an spoof product website on which we sell one single thing: a small bottle of immortality potion made from the water that springs underneath the Castle of Dracula.

Clearly, this product doesn’t exist, and if someone wants to buy it, they will arrive on a funny page where we invite them to visit Romania themselves, so they could see the castle of Dracula in reality!
