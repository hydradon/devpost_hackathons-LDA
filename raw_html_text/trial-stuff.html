Inspiration

The Idea – Next generation wireless networks, have 3 basic requirements – fiber, colocation & power. Our vision is to build an app that uses the open data available to design a suitable network in a defined geography, ensuring adequate coverage in terms of ideal antenna placement, network usage density, capacity, speed and latency.

What it does

The app we build, will allow MVNOs to design the optimal physical network in a selected geographical area based on the use cases which will determine the type of equipment they already / wish to use.
