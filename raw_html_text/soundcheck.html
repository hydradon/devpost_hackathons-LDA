Inspiration

In the era of constant internet globalization and evergrowing communication channels, people still continue to face communication problems due to discrepancies between their perceived message and their true intentional meanings. In order to help gap the bridge between emotion and technology, this tool helps people gage how their message may be perceived based off of categories such as emotion and hate speech.
