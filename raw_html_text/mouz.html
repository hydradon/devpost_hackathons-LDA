Mouz

Inspiration

Have you ever wanted to play Fornite at lunch, but you didn't have a mouse? Ever had cramping hands while working on a long assignment in the library? Mouz is the solution!

What it is

Mouz uses heavily filtered readings from your phone's accelerometer to simulate a traditional optical mouse.

Implementation

Your Android phone communicates over websockets with a server on your laptop. The server emulates an OS mouse driver.
