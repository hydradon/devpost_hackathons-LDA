• Assist

Inspiration

We wanted to use Layered Neural Networks to give a more accurate prediction.

What it does

It calculates the probability that an individual will survive for 14 days after a stroke or stroke-like symptoms.

How we built it

We passed data from the International Stroke Trial into a 3-layer, supervised learning algorithm to train the AI.

Challenges we ran into

Small data set.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The AI is highly realistic and can accurately predict about 90% of cases encountered.

What we learned

Using basic neural networks to train an AI to predict more accurately.

What's next for Assist

To incorporate more recent and larger data set. Also be able to use the setup neural network and expand to other datasets.
