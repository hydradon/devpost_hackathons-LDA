Inspiration: After becoming disillusioned with the US Political and Financial systems, I had given up and was checked out. I didn't want to fight in a losing battle and never wanted to be a freedom fighter. But when I learned about Bitcoin, I knew this new technology would give us the tool we need to fight back, to get our edge, not by power or force, but by peacefully Opting Out of the system. And I went on a mission to tell as many people about it as possible. But not about the money, not about the tech, but about the revolution, the change that Bitcoin can help make to the world.
