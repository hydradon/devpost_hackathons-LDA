Inspiration

We realized that living together is not so pleasant when different roommates have different expectations about cleanliness, organization, noise level, etc. We wanted to devise a method to avoid the sort of tension that arises from these conflicts.

What it does

A user can join or create a "home," and within that home, roommates can "ping" each other icons that represent certain tasks to be completed ("Take out trash," "Do your dishes," etc). Users can view their pings on a Notifications panel and swipe them off the list upon completion of a task.
