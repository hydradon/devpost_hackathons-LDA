Inspiration

We wanted to build a device that would allow us to safely manuever our rooms at night without having the need to switch on the light or a flashlight and waking up our sleeping roommate.

What it does

Motion sensors track our movement and position and shine a dim light in that direction. App allows us to switch the app on and off and mark dead zones which teach the device not to shine a light in certain areas under any circumstances.

How we built it

Lucas: Project lead, Hardware, used motion sensors, LEDs and photo receptors. Handled the electrical component of the hack. Claire: developed code in Java for connecting Bluetooth with the Android Studio code. Aarohi: Designed the UI of the app, designed algorithms which allow the GUIs to function. Katie: Used SolidWorks to model the device and 3D print the parts.
