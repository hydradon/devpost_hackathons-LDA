System consists of 2 parts:

1. Mobile app consists of important knowledge resources, links and phone numbers database we compiled from several provided assets.

2. Mobile and desktop modules to help social workers and probation officers to monitor individuals reentering community after incarceration and drug abuse. The mobile app is to report offender emotional state and monitor behaviours that may trigger relapse or other illegal actions. User is expected to fill out his profile and run regular surveys. The results are reported to the backend panel for social workers and probation officers. They may determine existence of triggers and stresses that lead to drug use in the first place. The goal is to identify the feeling and circumstances that creates a relapse reaction. System automatically triggers alerts.

Early recovery and coming back from incarceration is a difficult time and the problem identifying the feeling that creates a relapse reaction is common. We want to help in it.
