Inspiration

The need to meet the demand for trained teachers in every country worldwide

What it does

Provides school managers the chance to register and manage fee files of an institution, help untrained teachers locate training programmes and colleges worldwide. Provide a chance for companies and organisations to partner with various institionsor programmes

How we built it

Coded using android and php overca period of 3 days.

Challenges we ran into

Lack of a good databasd server

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Successfully taking part in this hackathon and being part of potential spearheaders of meeting the gap between the need for teachers and available trained teachers

What we learned

Built on teamwork , and learned more about various existing education system from mentors We got to know about the existing high demand for trained teachers in various countries of the world

What's next for Karo

We look forward to fully implement the idea in our next version of the app(1.0.1)
