Inspiration

We came to the realization that lots of people have lots of common interests and we wanted to make them share their interests on the Internet.

What it does

It enables users to save what they want under some tags and search for the things (books, articles, videos, sports events and so on) that their friends or random people like, based on a search engine.

Challenges we ran into

The fact that most of the technologies used were unknown to us

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It helps us reach much better understanding of the current paradigm of web dev technologies.

What we learned

A lot of skills concerning web app development and RESTful architecture.
