Inspiration

We wanted to make something potentially useful, and though the dining were pretty terrible with regards to live feedback.

What it does

Allows users to post reviews and ratings of the five dining halls at Wellesley.

How we built it

We built it using Flask, Bootstrap, and too many hours worth of work.

Challenges we ran into

Actually downloading and using Flask, getting Flask and Bootstrap to play nice together.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Our hella nice home page.

What we learned

How to use Flask and how to design pages with bootstrap. (Hint: use templates)

What's next for Welp

Posting the menus for the dining halls, timestamps on reviews, the ability to post pictures on reviews. And actually hosting it online.

Also we're rookies so go easy on us.
