Inspiration

We were inspired by our passion for Anime and helping people choose between the thousands of collections available

What it does

It simply grabs the users mood and returns 3 choices of Anime along with their picture, genre, and a link to watch it for free.

How I built it

We built the app using Xcode for Mac and Swift 3

Challenges I ran into

We had trouble adding links, dealing with app crashes, adding images, and going to another screen when a button is tapped.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am proud of that this our first app, we are freshmen computer science science majors so we don't have much experience and also that we made an app that we actually liked.

What I learned

I learned the basics of Swift and Xcode environment.

What's next for Anime Mood

Instead of hard coding the anime recommendations we will work on grabbing info of anime from a website, also we plan on including table views to make the app more functional
