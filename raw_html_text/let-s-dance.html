Runner-up for the "Best Use of Machine Learning/AI (Comcast NBCUniversal)" award.

DanceNow enables you to map your dance moves onto celebrities through a combination of custom-trained of conditional generative adversarial networks (TensorFlow), keypoint pose tracking (CMU OpenPose), and frame-by-frame video processing (OpenCV).
