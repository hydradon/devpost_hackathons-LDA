Enhances your pictures with cute and "searchable" emojis, and sizes them perfectly to Insta specifications.

Challenges we ran into

The biggest design challenge we faced was trying to figure out a solution to display emojis. A long, long time ago Gabby wrote a web scraper that scraped 2300+ emojis, and then never touched them again. Obviously, displaying all 2300+ emojis would put a strain on one's browser, thus necessitating the need to search for emojis in some capacity. Our solution is far from perfect, but it works.
