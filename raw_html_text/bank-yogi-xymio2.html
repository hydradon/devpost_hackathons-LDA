• Bank Yogi

Inspiration

Founders look for creative ways to save money and share knowledge with others. The intent of this skill is to do the same, make users aware of the hidden benefits & perks offered through their banking institutes.

What it does

Bank Yogi skill provides users with information that can result in significant savings by helping users discover hidden benefits & perks offered through various financial institutes.

How we built it

We developed this skill using Node.JS/Lambda/Alexa.

Challenges we ran into

Reading through the fine prints for multiple financial institutes to help collect the data, that can help consumers save money and make intelligent decisions during their day to day life.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Launching the skill that can increase financial understanding for users and help them save money.

What we learned

Alexa can provide the information on finger tips and help making a bigger impact.

What's next for Bank Yogi

The complete end to end experience of saving money with Bank Yogi, and educating users on various topics.
