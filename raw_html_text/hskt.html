Inspiration

After analyzing what issues we as women have and how available resources are to help us over come them, lead to our decision to create a website where you could access various tools to get rid of said issues.

What it does

Provides different activities, information and tools to manage and overcome day to day stress.

How we built it

HTML CSS GOOGLE PHOTOSHOP

Challenges we ran into

We faced the challenge of finding a specific topic to focus on while incorporating the ideas of everyone equally so we could each display our talents and reach the widest audience.

Accomplishments

We are proud to have eventually come to a unanimous idea that works for people of all ages and utilized all members creativity and skills with what little knowledge we came with.

What we learned

We learned that coding to create a website takes time and no idea is a bad idea.

What's next for HKST

We see companies buying this website and providing it to their employees thus ensuring better health and better work output.
