Inspiration

We want to send sounds!! GIFs are lame and have no sound :(

What it does

Sends sound bytes from your favorite movie, song or video!!!!

How we built it

Python, flask, heroku and Pydub. We scrape a song library and we can query it to return an accurate match to the lyrics youre looking for.

Challenges we ran into

Heroku, when to position the song to produce the accurate lyric audio

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned

Make sure you rename the procfile when you change your app's filename for heroku

What's next for Aud.io

We use it to send audio memes!!
