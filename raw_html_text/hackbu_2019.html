Love Letters Online

a website built with 4 different tabs and two different functions
4 tabs: Home, Letters, Publish and Contact
home = home display page
letters = page where you can search letters based on their publish number
publish = page where you can publish your own love letter
contact = page that houses the web developer's contact information

functionality includes: posting anonymous love letters online and reading others love letters
