Inspiration

In this day and age there is one problem that everyone faces when it comes to emailing and talking to someone in a professional manner: they don't know how to do it. At least in my classes, one of the first things my teachers talk to us about on Syllabus day is that they won't stand for unprofessional emails or any form of electronic communication. From this, Professionalize was born!

What it does

Professionalize is a Chrome extension that uses Python that solves this problem by providing helpful tips to Professionalize badly written, grammatically incorrect, and unprofessional writing. This body of text will then be appropriate to send to an employer, professor, or anyone who you are trying to impress.
