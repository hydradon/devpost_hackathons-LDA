Inspiration

Stock market prices shouldn't be boring.

How it works

MrDuck shows you the past values of a chosen index and you have to determine if its going to increase or decrease on the next tick. Desktop application uses Java and Swing. The hardware feedback is provided by an Arduino and other salvaged hardware.

Challenges we ran into

Hardware setup and concept changed midway because of laser failure.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Making a controlled shocked device.

What I learned

Lots of useful hardware experience and the ability to improvise with limited resources.

What's next for MrDuck

Maybe make it a complete trading trainer...
