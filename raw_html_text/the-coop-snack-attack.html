Inspiration: We discussed during Hack Week that the Coop should have an inventory site that can let people know how much they have in stock.

How it works: The person just clicks on the item that they want and they can see how many of it is available. The manager of the the coop can live update that feed whenever.

Challenges I ran into: Trying to link the java script of our charts to the html.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of: Actually being able to tackle our challenge and accomplish our goal.

What I learned:

We learned how to simultaneously use different languages to create a pretty, sweet site ;)
