Inspiration

300,000 children in the United States alone live with epilepsy.

What it does

Provides a complete picture of the environmental factors that go into specific epileptic episodes.

How we built it

We used HTML, CSS, and javascript to create a web application that asks the user basic questions to log things like medication, mood, nutrition, sleep, etc.

Challenges we ran into

Coming to a consensus on a well rounded idea proved to be difficult. We also had to work on collaborative communication between various backgrounds.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are happy that we produced a prototype website that is live at https://path2peaceofmind.appspot.com/

What we learned

The variability of the development idea once it is first conceived. Through intense and rapid customer discovery, we rapidly iterated upon our concept to achieve a tangible outcome for a positive result.

What's next for Kapow!

We aim to develop the customization of the app to include personalized questions and a wider variety of avatars for increased functionality of the platform. We also will share this anonymous information with childhood neurological research. Include specialized settings tailored towards each child's individual case.
