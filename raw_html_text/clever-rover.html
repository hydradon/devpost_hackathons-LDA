Inspiration

Allec talked to us about the growing potential of robotics and inspired us to go above and beyond level of expertise.

What it does

It follows hand gestures and on commands will pick up objects.

How we built it

We spent the whole night trying to make the claw with many failures and many attempts.

Challenges we ran into

We realized that the claws did not work and tape did not hold parts as easily as we had wished.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We got it working with serial reading and two dimensional motion sensing.

What we learned

Hard work cannot be accomplished without the greatest attention to the slightest details.

What's next for Clever Rover

It would be to create a fully functioning robot arm working in cooperation with the ultrasonic sensors.
