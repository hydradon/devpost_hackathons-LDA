• Arkiver: Your Beautiful Gallery For Life

  • Upload and tag scans of old photos

  • Upload and view multiple-page PDFs and documents

  • Responsive web app works on all devices. iOS and Android apps due for release Feb 2015.

  • Share moments and collections privately via email (recipient does not need Arkiver account to view) or up to Facebook

  • Print pictures to Walgreens (other printing integrations coming soon)

  • Integrates with Aviary to edit pictures: crop, resize, add filters etc.

  • Filter by Collection, People, Location and other Tags and also File Type

  • Bulk Edit: Group moments into new Collections or add tags to multiple moments at once

  • Recipients can comment and "Like" moments you share with them

  • View all moment types (pictures, videos, notes, quotes and PDFs) in full screen

Inspiration

Our life stories are being lost. Despite dramatic advances in technology, our personal and family histories are more fragile than ever. We use mobile devices to snap pictures but seldom record the context around them. We communicate in ever shrinking formats, rarely put pen to paper and the files containing our memories are fragmented. Arkiver helps families save and share their life stories.

What it does

Arkiver is an easy and beautiful way to collect, organize, preserve, share and enjoy memories: Collect your photos, videos notes and documents from any device and online accounts like Facebook. Organize your memories and add meaning through descriptions & tags. Share your moments privately with family and friends in ways that make sense for you. Preserve your moments securely on cloud storage like Dropbox and by printing them to services like Walgreens.

How I built it

Started with proof of concept web app and incorporated a lot of user feedback to improve functionality and features. We have a full-time CTO and contract out iOS and Android app development.

Challenges I ran into

Maintaining focus on key features - there's so many cool new things we want to add to Arkiver!

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Launching the web app in beta. Developing the iOS app and launching it in beta.

What I learned

Good things take time.

What's next for Arkiver

Public launch of iPhone, iPad and Android phone and tablet apps in early 2016.
