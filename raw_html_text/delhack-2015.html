Delhack 2015

Team: Jeremy Keeler, Kyle Sullivan, and Natalie Lane

Project: Restaurant Heat Map+

Description:

  • A heat map that tracks how busy restaurants are in a given area. Users can navigate a map to see what restaurants are busy and also pick up sweet flash deals. Restaurant owners can view data about how busy their restaurant is and toss out flash deals to attract customers to their restaurant

Technology Used:

  • Postgres, Express, Angular, Node.js with a dash of javascript magic and some css styling. Don't forget bootstrap!
