Inspiration

I wanted a fantasy baseball game for casual fans. It would work for two players head-to-head, only require knowing the rosters of two major league teams.

What it does

Draft your team from a pool consisting of the starting lineups of your two favorite teams, or your team and a friend's.

How I built it

Server with Node.js. Frontend with vanilla JS.
