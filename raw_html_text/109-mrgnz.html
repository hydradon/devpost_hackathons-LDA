Inspiration

We were inspired by recent events in our country, regarding the safety of women of all ages who have been suffering harrasment or worse things just in the streets

What it does

Our project aims to prepare people to react in dangerous or life-threatening situations, going anywhere from a fire breaking out, to an earthquake or suffering a kidnapping

How we built it

We prioritized the user's convenience and safety, to fully make it a reliable experience, as we are using the smartphone at the palm of their hands to fullfil said purpose.

Challenges we ran into

Our main challenge was learning the means necessary to archieve our goal, as none of us had used Android Studio to make an app beforehand.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It works, we can help people share their current situation. Whichever the result from this hackathon is, we are sure we can use this concept to help people.

What we learned

Everything we needed, none of us were very experienced with app development and this was a great opportunity to get ouside our comfort zoe.

What's next for 109 - MRGNZ}

To develop our application to a point where it is not only a viable, but a powerful tool to help people feel safer and be safer.
