Inspiration

We have been in a situation where we have been looking for a space to work and could not find one.

What it does

The basic way of explaining the app would be Uber for coworking spaces. It allows people to find and book coworking spaces near them.

How we built it

MockingBot

Challenges we ran into

Could not get the API to work. The team had some problems when designing the app.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The final product. The idea, as we felt it will have a big impact, this is a product we would use and have been looking for.

What we learned

New skills in MockingBot and gained experience with API. The team got to use some design skills that we have not used before.

What's next for Salto\CW

We feel we could work with Salto to make the app and implement it. We believe this idea will be great for people who need spaces to work at short notice. Moreover, this could be a new opportunity for Salto as it will be a new market for them within the coworking area.
