Inspiration

The world can be very stressful, so I wanted to find a way to relax people with funny memes to share with friends.

What it does

It generates memes based on entered numbers and enables you to share them with your friends

How I built it

I used the Imgur API, funny meme quotes, and Android studio

Challenges I ran into

Making the share button work and able to communicate with different people

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Making the share button work well

What I learned

How to efficiently have devices communicate with different things

What's next for MemesWithFriends

Making a login and a more app centered chat-system. Maybe a search bar as well to browse.
