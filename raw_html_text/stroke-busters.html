Inspiration

When Richard’s grandmother had a stroke, all she wanted was the comfort of her home. She didn’t realize the importance of rehab and couldn’t be motivated to attend rehab sessions at the facility. She eventually became bed bound.

There are many more stories like this.

What it does

We offer outpatient quality care … at home

  1. Services for the whole person & family We don’t treat stroke. We treat you. We work with survivors of stroke and families on all aspects of recovery to regain full potential.

  2. Rehab equipment made mobile Outpatient facilities require large, expensive equipment. We bring the facility to you with our custom line of rehabilitation equipment that’s small, easy to transport, and easy to use. cer, b

  3. Business model = cash-based payment system

Concierge physical therapy services have been shown to produce cost savings to patients because it takes less time to recover. We have also seen a willingness to pay for a premium service for at-home rehabilitation services.
