ole-guacamole

Welcome to the Ole-Guacamole readme!

Simply use the drop down menu to select a grocery item to add to your list. When your list is complete hit the 'calculate' button. On the right hand side you can either hit the '=' button for a default store layout to appear where your items will be highlighted based on the aisle they are in, or you can create your own store layout and contribute to the open source framework of Ole Guacamole.
