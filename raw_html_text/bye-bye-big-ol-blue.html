We created a monster!!  Current practices are killing our planet and turning it into a hideous beast.  This piece was created in a fit of rage; denim was ripped and distressed, then stitched together to create an eerie representation of the monster, Fast Fashion.  
Just like the beings that inhabit this beautiful blue planet our team had to adapt and conquer the problems that arise when dealing with differences. Our desire to be the most fashionable and bring a haute couture look to life forced a sense of idieation and collaboration within the group.These differences most typically occur when groups are formed and their current practices and techniques are forced to interweave.
Overall we where forced to collaborate within our different ideas and come to our decision.  Death to the monster "fast fashion" and long live big ol' blue.  Only time will tell whats next for blue, we sustain or death do it be.
