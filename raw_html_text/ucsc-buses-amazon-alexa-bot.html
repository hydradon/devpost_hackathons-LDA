Inspiration

Sometimes when you are in a hurry and you have to go to a class using a bus, you would want to know when the next bus is coming. Now you can do that easily with the UCSC Bus Alexa Skill. Normally you would have to click endlessly on the Santa Cruz Metro Website, but now you can simply ask Alexa "when is the next bus coming to crown" and it will tell you when the bus is coming and which bus it is.

What it does

Tells you the next bus that is coming to the bus stop near you.

With Python and AWS lambda
