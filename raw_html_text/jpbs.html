Inspiration

We are inspired on all those people that doesn`t have a voice, that are silent or ,deaf-mute because we know that we all have things to say and things to achieve, our goal is giving all those amazing people a voice, literally.

What it does

JBP is a smart bracelet that register electrical activity from your muscles through sensors to detect the gestures of the sign language of the silent people and converts it to a voice that goes through a speaker so a person who doesn`t understand sign language can, without any problem what the other person wants to say.

How I built it

With Android Studio and Java and FREETTS.

Challenges I ran into

The sensors of the gadget we have aren`t precise enough, but we managed calibrating it.
