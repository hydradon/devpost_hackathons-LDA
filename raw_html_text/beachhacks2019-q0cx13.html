CSULB Hackathon 2019

Have you ever had a bad teacher? What about a bad textbook? If you say no, you're lying. When coming across a bad teacher, or a bad textbook, students take learning into their hands and head to the internet. This then becomes a sea of results for the best course claiming to teach you about this or that. It can quickly become a daunting task and take days just to find the right course for you.

Our product, StudySearch, implements a rating system for textbooks - for students, by students.

Market Value: Revenues from Ads and Amazon Affiliate links. Sustainability: High - new students every year mean endless customers Scalability: High: with the application of Google Translate, the website can be used by students throughout the world.
