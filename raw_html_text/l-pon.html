Inspiration

When you like something, you smile. So we took that idea and built an app around that.

What it does

You get multiple suggestions for your right clothing for whatever you are up to. You then are being filmed by your webcam. Once you smile, the particular piece of clothing is placed on your wishlist so you can buy it via stylight.
