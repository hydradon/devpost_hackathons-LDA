Inspiration

I like telling stories and making them fun and silly. I thought others would share this interest, but all of these stories go to waste. I created an app where these stories can be shared with children around the world. Authors can upload their silly stories with purple anteaters for anyone to read

Challenges

I hate HTML and CSS

How I built it

I used javascript and jquery for all the functionality and Parse as the backend.

Accomplishments

It actually works. I wish I could add more to the home page like top stories and most popular reads. Also, I wanted to add a way for authors to get more written feedback about their stories

Purpose

The Storybook App is an easy way to share and ready stories. It is safe for children of all ages to read. Authors can easily upload any stories they have written as long as it is in PDF form.
