Inspiration

Facebook, Eventbrite and other platforms filter activities and events based off of biases such as gender, age, and past user history. While these may have their advantages, they encourage users to stay within their bubble.

What it does

Randomises fun events for individuals in Stanford and Palo Alto. The prompts are gender-bending and creative. :) Guys may end up in nail salons and girls may end up wrestling!

How we built it

Front end languages.

Challenges we ran into

Understanding the workflow of ideation to creation.

What we learned

A simple idea can go a long way.

What's next for Bounce

Implementing other APIs and maps that allow users to engage in new activities regardless of location (places-near-me functionality).
