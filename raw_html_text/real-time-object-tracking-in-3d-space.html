Object tracking has a plethora of applications, from using it with CCTV cameras to find a missing relative with dementia in a crowd, to a run-away toddler in a shopping centre. Our software can be implemented on normal CCTV cameras already in place everywhere, making this an economically feasible project.

Alternatively, a stationary camera can be paired with a laser that can rotate in both planes of 3D space and be fed information about the location of a mosquito. The laser can then be used to fire at the mosquito and kill it. Malaria spreading mosquitos are a serious problem, affecting over 200 million people a year. Therefore, this application could save countless lives.
