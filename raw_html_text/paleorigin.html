What it does

Walmart knows basically where the food they sell come from. But consumers are totally in the dark before, during, and after buying their groceries. What if we can scan our receipts for QR codes, and we are able to trace every single food item back to its roots in, say, the farms of South Dakota? We aim to do just that!
