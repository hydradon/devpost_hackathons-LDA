Inspiration - The same way companies have various management systems to keep track of all their applicants, we as applicants should have the same type of management system of all the companies we apply to. Often times we apply to over 50 companies at once in search of an internship and the only way to keep track of them all is manually with an excel sheet...

What it does - TraeC. provides a management platform for users where they can keep up to date with all of their applications whether they applied yesterday, 2 years ago, has had 3 interviews already, or even got their offer letter already!
