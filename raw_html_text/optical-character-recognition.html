Inspiration

Are you tired of waiting days, weeks and even months for manga translations to be released ? Wait no longer , my optical character recognition program will save the day. come across some cool images on google but its not in a language you can read, dont worry i got your back !

Use optical character recognition methods to detect text in an image and extract the recognized characters into machine useable character stream.
