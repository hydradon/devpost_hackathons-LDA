Bicycles have always been a popular way of transportation. Today we have the technology to not only improve that way of transportation in terms of practicality but also make it more fun and at the same time productive. So, in response to Ericsson's challenge for the 2019 IOTHON event, our team presents an innovative use of IoT sensors, connected to the future 5G network, that will gather data from bicycle rides and process that data into useful information that will excite bikers and consult bicycle-related public infrastructure decisions.
