Animus

A platform to let your network read your vitals.

We all have loved ones whom we care about, however, we cannot always check up on their wellbeing, This is where our web-app Animus comes in!

Animus’ main focus is to monitor different vitals and medical trends of the user and share it with people they trust, such as family members and doctors. This information is private and only viewed by select trusted users.

1) Enter your vitals and press scan.

2) Make network connections with your friends/family

3) Authorize them to access your informaiton

4) Enjoy!
