StockBlock provides an innovative method to accurately model the fluctuations of the American economy, centered around the Fortune 500 companies. From preprocessing datasets to building a highly accurate linear regression system, StockBlock is able to utilize machine learning to predict future stock market trends. Using extensive training sets and libraries such as scikit-learn and matplotlib, we can provide investment advice with accuracy as high as 99%.
