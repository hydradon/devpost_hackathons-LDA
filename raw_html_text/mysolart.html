Inspiration and What it Does

Monitoring, Managing and Messaging Insured Solartis Travelers could be task oriented with thousands of travelers depositing, departing and returning at various time.

Whenever you create an account, the app will automatically assign you a random Profile Picture

This application has 3 major functions.

1.) Monitoring Solartis Travelers Movement
2.) Real-Time Notification and Messaging System.
3.) Solartis Customers Information Management and Easy Search System.

1.) Monitoring Solartis Travelers Movement

Whenever a Solartis Customer created an account with Solartis, each registrant is allowed to enter parameters like PolicyEffectiveDate, DepartureDate, ReturnDate, DepositDate etc. This dates are very important for all Solartis travelers as it will tell them when they will depart, return, make deposit and have their Policy renewed.
The major problem is that some persons might easily forget any of this events dates or even try to evade them for a malicious actions.

This applications will come to an aid to all this. This applications integrates a Dynamic calendering System that shows all the Solartis Customers on it displaying all their PolicyEffectiveDate, DepartureDate, ReturnDate, DepositDate respectively.

All that Solartis Admin or Solartis Travelers needs to do is used the calendar system to check all the traveling event. For instance clicking on Departure Date Calendar will automatically shows all the travelers of that Months, Date and their Solartis Reference No. etc. With this event Calendar, we ensure that Travelers those not missing any events thus keeping their traveling experience awesome going forward with Solartis Insurance.

The Calendar System has a Navigation Button to navigate to any months of your choice to find traveling Events

2.) Real-Time Notification and Messaging System.

Whenever a Solartis Customer created an account with Solartis, each registrant is allowed to enter parameters like Email and Mobile No. etc. This 2 major parameters are used to engage the travelers in the event of any updates.
This applications leverages Customers Email and Mobile Contact which is used to send an automatic Email and SMS Messages regarding any updates to his/her traveling events respectively. This Messages are send to Solartis Travelers regarding her PolicyEffectiveDate, DepartureDate, ReturnDate, DepositDate and any other events going on within the Solartis venerablecommunity. This will create the highest engagement between Solartis and their Customers going forward...

3.) Solartis Customers Information Management and Easy Search System.

Filling of Solartis Customers Data is very lengthy. To make the data capturing easy, I created a Multi-Step Applications that splits Customers inputted information's into 3 components. This will ensure that Customer will easily make registration with an ease.

As an Information Management System, The app allow users to do the followings:

1.) Esay search of Solartis Travelers by Solartis Reference No, Email, Phone Number, Names, Destination Countries, PolicyEffectiveDate, DepartureDate, ReturnDate, DepositDate etc .
2.) The app allow Users to have a preview of their various Solartis applications.
3.) Provision of Users Profile Data to access Solartis Customer Info in Details.
4.) Message History to enable Solartis monitor and reveiwed any messages send to a particular Solartis Customers in real-time. The message can be either Email or SMS or Others
5.) Easy Database Record Pagination to allow loading of Content with an ease.
