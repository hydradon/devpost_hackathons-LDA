Inspiration

One of the most unappealing aspects of guacamole is its puke-green color. I was wondering, what if I could make that even more unappealing?

What it does

Given the proportion of ingredients that you put in a guacamole, it will immediately give you a simulated rendition of its resulting color.

How I built it

I fought against Elm (elm-lang.org) until I laid defeated under a pile of redundancy and compiler errors, with a semi-working webapp.
