Our inspiration comes from personal strifes with procrastination.

What it does

Traction will periodically block access to social media sites and others that promote procrastination until you answer questions ranging in multiple subjects. Traction will be a perfect tool for students who want to study for upcoming exams or gradually finish homework over a period of time.
