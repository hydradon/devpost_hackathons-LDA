Get Stingy

As university students struggling financially, our app/web app aims to help those find free food or loots/#swags around campus to save cost! This app will help solve your problems by cutting down on food costs. This app is a one stop shop for your spending troubles.

Features include:

  • Saving upcoming free food opportunities.
  • Live feed of photographs of food and of the promotion/event

Finding free food couldn't be easier. Just enter your location and view the listings for free food. And for those who are more 'stingy', save download our app and create an account to be notified of food opportunities you can't miss out on.

Benefits of our service

Gain more exposure to your event or product in low traffic areas Gain insights to demographics of your potential customer's or patreons Promote your product or event to specific demographics

Currently, our target audience are university students but have the opportunity to be released for the general public to use.
