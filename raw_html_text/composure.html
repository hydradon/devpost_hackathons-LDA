Our motivation: As hacking, sitting hunched all day and all night at a hackathon, we decided to try to find a way to ensure we sit straight and stay healthy Our product: It uses bendable sensors to track the state of your posture and send that information to a web server to live update your information and your progress Our technology: It uses an electric imp to send information via wifi to a graph on our website. Biggest Challenge: The electric imp was new to all of us and it was great to finally get it working. Small Victories: Actually making a chart on a website live update with input from our hardware was a good feeling. Skills Learned: We collectively learned sketch, more about javascript and a lot of hardware basics Whats Next: Next we hope to include more sensors and make our data visualization more appealing.
