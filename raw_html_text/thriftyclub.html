As students with many cards and rewards programs, it is hard to keep track of what rewards we are missing out on or interesting deals. The ThriftyClub enables a ecosystem where rewards are all kept in one place as well as a place where one can battle out with their friends on spending. ThriftyClub enables one to demonstrate their thriftiness with the freedom of being able to shop more than usual. It is a platform that revolutionizes the consumer space in a new way.
