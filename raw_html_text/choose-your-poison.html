• Choose Your Poison

Inspiration

We were curious on how soy milk got its rich milky consistency. To our surprise soy milk has an awful additive ingredient called carrageenan. Although derived from a natural source, carrageenan is particularly harmful to the digestive system. Our website is inspired by the harmful ingredients hiding in drinks and processed foods that people should avoid or consume less of.

What it does

The website lets the user to search for a product either through text search or picture search.

How we built it

The website was built using HTML, CSS3, and SQL.

Challenges we ran into

We were challenged by the entire CSS3 language. We are first time programmers but we did the best that we could do.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We're proud of the research information we collected and the logo of our website

What we learned

We learned a more in depth understanding of HTML.

What's next for Choose Your Poison

We will continue to grow the database of the website and expand the search capabilities of the program.
