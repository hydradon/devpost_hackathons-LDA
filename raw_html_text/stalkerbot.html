• He's here to stalk you!

Inspiration

I keep forgetting to take my meds. I wish someone would remind me.

What it does

It stalks you. And reminds you to take your meds.

How we built it

A roomba with a cheap laptop, and a Xbox kinect.

Challenges we ran into

Too many. The roomba had battery problems. The roomba plug was poorly designed and couldn't connect properly.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

See video.

What we learned

Life is hard. Finding a stalker is even harder in life. That's why we made one.

What's next for StalkerBot

Creepy vocal messages. Face recognition so we don't stalk the wrong dude.
