Inspiration: automatization of the creation of multi-diameter shafts

What it does: Shaft Component Generator for Autodesk® Fusion 360™ extends the functionality of Fusion 360 by automating the creation of multi-diameter shafts. The add-in automates the creation of shafts of various level of complexity by configuring shaft sections and additional design elements, such as chamfers, fillets, grooves, etc.
