Inspiration

We want to make it easier for everyone, regardless of experience level, to design and manufacture knitted products on demand.

What it does

KNIT ENGINE makes it easy for people to design and manufacture knitted goods and textiles on demand.

Our intuitive, visually-oriented UI abstracts away the complexities commonly associated with the industrial knitting process.

We believe our platform lowers the barrier to knit product creation for customers of all experience levels, and can facilitate economic activity for designers, brands, knitting mills, and producers of raw materials.

What's next for Knit Engine

We plan to launch a public beta in 2018. Please follow us on Instagram ( @knitengine ) and Twitter ( @KnitEngine ) for more information.
