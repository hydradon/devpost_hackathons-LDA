I cried watching the election results Tuesday night. How could a man so outwardly cruel and spiteful become the President of the United States? How could people even vote for him? How could our nation be so divided? What are we missing? What can I do, and how can we move forward from this? There are so many conversations we need to have.

This project is a response to the tumultuous events of this week and my first time experimenting with tools (Sketch, Framer.js) that I've been wanting to play with for a long time.

Converse is app that attempts to reduce polarization and facilitate open discussion between willing individuals. It's basically Tinder for discussing social and political issues :)
