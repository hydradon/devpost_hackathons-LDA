Inspiration

Students often don't know what courses to take and don't know what courses are available. With School-Courses, this is no longer a problem.

What it does

Produces easy courses that one could take, to inspire kids to take different classes in the future.

How I built it

Using AWS Lambda and Alexa Skills Kit.

Challenges I ran into

At first, I was unable to produce output. However, after hours of struggling, I was able to finally get a great output!
