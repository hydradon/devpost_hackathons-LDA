• Banana-Bot

Inspiration

We didn't know what to do, but we had a banana and knew to go from there.

What it does

It crawls and screams.

How we built it

We attached a Photon board to a Lego chariot and the banana, then added servo motor arms and a speaker module/buzzer.

Challenges we ran into

The banana did not want to crawl.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We got the banana to crawl

What we learned

We learned how to use the Photon board to connect to Wi-Fi.

What's next for Banana-Bot

The trash can.
