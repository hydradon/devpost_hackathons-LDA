Disclaimer: this project is not blockchain related but STACK related

Inspiration

Problem: As STACK grows within the next few years, there will be many more offers(maybe in the 100's) that the customer will have access too. However, displaying these offers in a list/table/scroll view to sort through can be a turnoff for the customer and deter them from fully utilizing STACK. There must be a fun and intuitive way to sort these offers to all demographics of the market from young adults to seniors.

What it does

Solution: OfferSwipe! An offer swiping view, that lets users sort offers based on tags and swiping gesture(like Tinder), and stores the offers they swiped "yes" on for use. A fun game to play on the train ride home or waiting in line, where you win with STACK savings.
