Inspiration

I always need more information when I'm traveling or moving or whatever - and the unique way I've found is to use a combination of different apps. But it becomes difficult to use, a little bit overwhelming sometimes

What it does

Very simple. The app allows you to add pins to a map and add information to those pins

How I built it

With my hands - a keyboard - and a computer with Xcode.

Challenges I ran into

Many.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Coded a big part of the app in less than 2 weeks. I'm really proud of myself because I've not been able to do it full-time so...I think it's a big accomplishment to me. Especially because coding doesn't come easy to me as it does for some of my friends.

What I learned

I learned to use frameworks faster, also learned that I . And secretly, just between you and me, I'm kinda amazing :D.

What's next for Data Maps

Keep developing the MVP, especially when it comes to persistence, and in a couple more weeks (MAX) submit to the App Store. Also, add all the contributors and upgrade the graphics to meet my standards.

How to test

Download the app from the Github link. Open the Data Maps.xcworkspace with Xcode set the target as Data Maps and play. (Sometimes you have to manage the provisioning profile and signing certificate - if that happens to you, give me a shout or just change the team and bundle identifier).

Once running you can test with my account, it's right there for you or you can set up your very own.
