Mindful

A HackRU Fall 2019 Project targeted at improving mental health through engaging daily goals.

Summary

Many people look over their day to day mental well being, Mindful can help with that. Mindful is an iOS app that turns improving your mental health into an RPG! Collect EXP and level up your avatar by completing tasks that reduce stress and help you remain calm. Additionally, Mindful has meditation features built in. The app tracks your daily goals, so you can keep track of your progress. Mindful implements SwiftUI.
