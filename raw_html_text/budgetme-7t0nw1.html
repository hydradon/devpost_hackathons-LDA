• BudgetMe - Your only financial companion

Inspiration

We're thinking about how we can leverage the lives of millions in micro-managing their finances compared to the present world cumbersome solutions.

What is it?

FinanceDaily: A complete automated financial solution that advises a person on his/his day to day expenditure, fully personalized on his/her own behavior of expenditure.

FinancePlanner: Say you want to buy a new Macbook pro, we help you do all the math, relieve the pain of budget planning and give you the pinpoint data on how much you should spend every day in different liabilities (food, shopping, saving, etc) to regain the money you spent on the Macbook pro.

FinanceNow: A complete solution that leverages the data we collect from every person and curate them in the form of an index on a person's behavior in following. => MicroFinance. A platform to microfinance from your circles. => EvenFInance. A platform to finance from larger loan companies

Challenges We ran into

Docusign - Github is a little scattered and could use some cleanup. Our team had installed the SDK version 2.1 for Django Python but we were missing the key import: 'from docusign_esign import ApiClient, EnvelopesApi, EnvelopeDefinition, Signer, SignHere, Tabs, Recipients, Document' which was a little hard to find.

We have so many ideas we want to incorporate in our product but 36 hours is definitely not enough time to produce all of them.

What I learned

We learned that mixing and matching APIs can bring together a truly wonderful product. Thank you to all the sponsors who gave us access to their APIs and let us experiment.
