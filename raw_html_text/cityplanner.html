Inspiration

Imagine building a city with your friends and community with mixed reality

What it does

allows multiple people to build a simple city with buildings, trees, and roads

How we built it

with Unity for the HoloLens

Challenges we ran into

Shared Experience with mixed reality, snapping

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting multiple people to edit the same city at the same time changing scale to get into first person mode

What we learned

how to work with spatial mapping and world anchors

What's next for CityPlanner

Taking this technology into our other HoloLens applications
