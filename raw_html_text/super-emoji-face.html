We trained a deep convolutional neural network to recognize 7 emotions (Angry, Disgusted, Fearful, Happy, Sad, Surprised, Neutral). We then turned the emotions into emojis and used websockets to update the emojis in close to real time. Now you can see the emotions of your friends or other people you are playing against online without being next to them!
