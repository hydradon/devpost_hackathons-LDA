We wanted to build a site where students could directly sell their used furniture, clothes, books, etc. to other students.

This website allows for students to sell to other students. It is secure and will require a .edu email address to sign-up. It also will potentially come with a payment method like venmo to make transactions easy and secure.

We used HTML and CSS

Basically every step was a challenge

This is our first hackathon and none of us came in with any coding experience. We are incredibly proud of what we have created because it was truly a team effort and we learned many new skills.

We learned how to code!
