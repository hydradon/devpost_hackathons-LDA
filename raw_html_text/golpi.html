We have designed and created a multi-sensory extension to the classic Game of Life on a Raspberry Pi. It features a 7-inch touchscreen interface and most notably music generated from the movement of the Game of Life itself. Blinking lights and sensors complement the experience to offer even more immersion into the "zero-player game".

It is entirely coded in Python, using the Raspberry Pi GPIO and PyAudio libraries, with a touchscreen-friendly interface built in Tkinter.
