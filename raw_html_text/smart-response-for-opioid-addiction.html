Smart Reponse is a valuable resource for patients, family members, community members and first responders in efforts to prevent opioid overdose. We wanted to develop an engaging and personalized way to ensure that users are directed to the resources that best fit their situation and needs.

By leveraging SAMHSA's rich taxonomy and a faceted, pin-board style design we were able to develop an engaging and personalized user-driven experience that matches visitors to the rich content that is of most value to them.
