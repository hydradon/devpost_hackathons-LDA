Inspiration

We were inspired to make this website due to the need for a UTSU commons page.

What it does

UTSU Clubs and Events Page, similar to the Facebook groups that currently exist.

How we built it

We coded the website completely from scratch, using html and CSS.

Challenges we ran into

Since none of our team members know how to use JavaScript, we are still unable to take in the information from the form to submit events to the website.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Creating the website as a whole! Although we haven't even uploaded it online, were all still really proud that we have a product to present.

What we learned

We learned lots of new tricks to html, as most of us are a little rusty or are new to html. Plus, brainstorming and teamwork are your best friends.

What's next for UofT-Hub

If implemented, we would attempt to add JavaScript to our webpage and complete a sign in page to authorize people who are posting.
