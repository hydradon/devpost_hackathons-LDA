Human trafficking is a global problem and also one of the most prevalent issues today. We hope to help the fight against trafficking with our website.

We have an interactive quiz for users to take that determines if he or she is a victim of sex trafficking. It also presents myths and realities regarding human trafficking. We also have survivor stories to inspire new hands to join the fight against trafficking.

We had a breakdown with our code in the last minutes as well as group communication errors, but at the end of the day I think we all pulled it together and made everything work.

We are proud of putting together an interactive website that will hopefully change the world.

We learned that working together as a team is important, if any communication errors happen there could be a huge problem. Also we learned that we should proofread earlier, as we saw a breakdown in our code in the last few minutes.

A new fresh idea that can impact the fight against trafficking for the better
