Inspiration

While traversing government sites about rural empowerment, we were awestruck to find the job cumbersome and faced hardship in finding government facilities .

What it does

This website has a simple framework but highly equipped and strong in terms of knowledge it provides to rural population. It provides an easy and efficient way to access government policies and acts by providing direct links.

How we built it

We use html, css, javascript, bootstrap for front-end, php for back-end, and mysql for creating database.

Challenges we ran into

We could not make our website dynamic.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of providing a simple platform so that rural people can easily be notified about the education, financial help, medical aids and many more.

What we learned

We learned how to effectively work in group, we came to the level of competition and learned to work under pressure.

What's next for Empowering Rural India(ABD, Rural Empowerment)

We try to take this project in a bigger level and we try to improve it by taking feedback from the users.
