Inspiration

Bad travel experiences. We wanted somewhere that we can look before booking.

What it does

The map shows places places that black people can travel to and feel safe. It is based on reviews of other people who have travelled to that region.

How I built it

We used Google Map API, Javascript and CSS

Challenges I ran into

It was our first hackathon, so we had many challenges as most of us have never built anything substantial

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Having a cohesive project.

What I learned

I learnt how to collaborate and I can build projects

What's next for Black Travellers Heatmap

Add more locations and improve aesthetics.
