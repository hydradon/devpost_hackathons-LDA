We know Brampton's population is increasing rapidly. More and more people are coming into brampton not knowing what the city has to offer. People can find affordable houses on realtor.ca, and people can find services and facilities on other websites like GeoHub or the city of brampton website, but they'll have to keep switching between these website to find the right house in the right area. To solve this problem, we are introducing Welcome Home. Welcome Home is a website designed to help newcomers adjust to their new environments and make it their home. On this website you can find ways of transportation, you can find different public facilities, you will be able to find stores, and most importantly, you can find houses in the area you want. You will be able to look at all of this information side by side, which will make it quick and easy for newcomers to find their future home. Our website is translatable so that any newcomer will be able to use it. Overall, our website will help newcomers adjust to Brampton, and have the best experience possible.
