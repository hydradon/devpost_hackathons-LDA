Inspiration

inspired by pinball and a little bit of competition

What it does

It is a 2 person pinball game

How we built it

We used wood, 3D printing and laser cutting to make our parts and then we put it all together with cement, glue, and screws

Challenges we ran into

Broken motors, alignment between pieces and time constraints

Accomplishments that we're proud of

A working pinball mechanism that uses no electronics

What we learned

We learned that we can have fun!

What's next for Raging Dinos

This will be a great party toy
