Inspiration

I wanted to be able to create parametric rack gears and stack them in a row.

What it does

Generates rack gears with chosen parameters. You can also choose to make Helical rack gears with a chosen angle.

How I built it

Fusion 360 c++ API.

Challenges I ran into

Learning the API. Understanding how gears work.

What I learned

I learned a lot about the fusion 360 API. I am hoping it will develop fast with added functions and bug fixes so I can publish some more advanced features to the Add-In.

What's next for Rack generator

Wait and see. And support me by getting the app from appstore!
