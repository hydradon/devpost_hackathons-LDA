Inspiration

Today we were bit scared leaving our laptops behind while having food or going to bathroom.

What it does

We got a solution to our problem and credit goes to FemmelHacks !!! We build a Techie Guard , that attaches to your belonging and notifies you when it is being tempered with.

How we built it

We used Intel Edison Hardware and Grove Starter Kit along with Twilio API and node.js

Challenges we ran into

None of us had experience with hardware so it took a while to learn it and execute it. We faced a lots of issues while synchronizing device with laptop but at last we got it.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to properly execute the sensor and having a sms response immediately after that.

What we learned

We learned how to synchronize hardware device with laptop and execute respective API.

What's next for Techie Guard

Improve on efficiency ,security method. Also adding accelerameter.
