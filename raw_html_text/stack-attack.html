Inspiration

Have you ever wanted to drag and drop animals into a stack? Maybe not. I wanted to bring this insanely random vision to life in the form of a Unity game. The simple goal of this game is inspiring laughter and happiness in all who play it. It might help a user to learn about animals or gravity.

How it works

Drag grazing animals in order to make a stack tall enough to reach the goal!

Challenges I ran into

Physics collisions with animals were difficult to maintain.

What I learned

Basic Javascript and C#

What's next for Stack Attack

I hope to see this is the app store (in a 2d version perhaps?)
