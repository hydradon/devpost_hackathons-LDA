Inspiration

Presentation skills are important no matter where you end up: We want to help students and professionals everywhere become better speakers and storytellers.

What it does

Charism.ai analyzes everything about your speech: head and eye movements, talking speed, sentiment and word choice. Then, it provides you with insight on key information through various graphs, as well as offers recommendations for improvement! We use Machine Learning and Data Science based on ratings of speeches to predict whether your speech would be positively received by others.

How I built it

Technologies: Server: Node JS, Python for Analysis Microsoft Cognitive Services, Microsoft Machine Learning, Azure, MLab for Database
