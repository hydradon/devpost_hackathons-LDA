JINA is a platform with the purpose of exposing middle school-aged children to the world of computer science. By presenting the information in an accessible and interesting format, we hope to dispel some of the myths associated with the field of computer science and encourage more people, especially underrepresented racial and gender minorities, to pursue professions in the field.
