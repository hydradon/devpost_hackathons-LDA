Inspiration

Helping people record their emotion condition for a period of time, sharing their happiness with friends and help them get better when in bad mood by giving advice and joyful text and clips

What it does

Rating people's happiness level, giving encouragement and share option when they get high scores

How we built it

Building a Android App. Using Google face detection API to evaluate the level of happiness

Challenges we ran into

Deploying the Google API, importing different Android Studio packages.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Successfully building an Android App with functionality of detecting happiness level of people.

What we learned

The entire process of building an Android App

What's next for Smile

Ranking list and advice for emotion management
