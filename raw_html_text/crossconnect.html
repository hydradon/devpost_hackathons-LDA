Inspiration

Research can be narrow, focused and deep. It's easy to get lost in one's own rabbit hole and miss out on the newest developments in totally unrelated fields. But ideas across disciplines can bring powerful insights.

What it does

We facilitate that by having users import the tools, methods or techniques they're using in their own work. Our backend searches for papers featuring these keywords, irrespective of discipline, in the world's largest research database. We send a newsletter with the top hits from this to the user.

What's next for CrossConnect

We aim to search more sources for new updates, host our code on the cloud so it can listen 24x7, and make our newsletter timed.
