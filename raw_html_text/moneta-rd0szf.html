Moneta

Revision software for your browser!

Just load up the firefox extension and add some websites that distract you. Moneta will give you a revision question every time you try to visit them and only let you pass once you answer it!

Get it wrong and you have to wait for 30 seconds before trying again.

Moneta is named after the roman goddess of memory.
