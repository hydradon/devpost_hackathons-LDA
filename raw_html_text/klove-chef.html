Inspiration

Klove Chef is on the mission to bring back home cooking. With busy schedules, many people eat unhealthy fast food and take out on a daily basis. We want to make it easy for people to find recipes that are personalized for them, show them the ease of cooking, and reduce the fear of not knowing how to make a healthy meal at home.

What it does

In the same way that Google Maps helps people get to point A to point B, Klove Chef provides people with a natural way of finding recipes, shopping for ingredients and effortlessly cooking those recipes at home.

How we built it

Klove Chef was built as a recipe platform for everything food and beverage, through voice and natural learning processing.

Challenges we ran into

Voice technology is new to a lot of people. We needed to first educate our first partners on the potential of the technology and how rapidly it is growing.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Klove Chef has been a thought leader in the IOT space and Nestle awarded us as the "Next Big Thing" in 2017.

What we learned

Klove Chef is excited to see the growth of voice and the impact that our platform will have in the food and beverage industry. We have learned the importance of bringing empathy to any new form of technology and ensure that we are keeping things simple in order to get people to relate to the product.

What's next for Klove Chef

Aside from cooking, Klove Chef is excited to jump into the beverage space, both non-alcoholic and alcoholic. In addition, we are looking to explore other ways within the industry where we can leverage our technology. Down the road, we are looking to be the heartbeat of everyone's kitchen.
