Inspiration

Want to organize a protest but predict the result in advance? Use the Protest Outcome Predictor! Based on data provided from Binghamton University Political Science department, you can now protest SMART.

What it does

Predict the outcome of protest.

How I built it

Multilayer perceptron classification, a subclass of supervised machine learning.

Challenges I ran into

Cleansing data, since there were limited data and limited features.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Accomplishing ETL and prediction within limited time & manpower

What I learned

MLP is not effective for heavily biased data

What's next for Prediction Outcome of Protest With Neural Network(MLP)

Try different supervised machine learning algorithm
