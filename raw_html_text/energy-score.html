Inspiration

--> We used inspiration from the things we see around us on a daily basis. We chose electricity as our object and tried to find ways to use math to be able to create an output viable to the user.

What it does

-->The purpose of this app is to help the user decrease energy consumption in their house by keeping track of the home’s electrical usage per day and providing the user with their estimated monthly electric cost.

How we built it

--> We used android studio as our platform to build our app and used xml pages with java.
