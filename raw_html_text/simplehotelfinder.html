Inspiration

Searching for hotels is a pain – it requires clicking into each result, then returning to the results before progressing to the next one, repeat ad nauseam.

How it works

We assume you care most about the area the hotel is in and the bed itself, so we display a carousel of images and allow you do like or dislike each, then a short-list of search results is displayed in the traditional way.
