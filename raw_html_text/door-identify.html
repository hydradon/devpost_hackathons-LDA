The Idea

People with Alzheimer's Disease or other cognitive impairments may have trouble recognizing and remembering the faces of family and friends.

The Project

Whenever your doorbell rings, Door Identify takes a picture of your visitor and uses facial recognition AI to identify them among a database of friends and family (or mark them as a stranger). It then sends a push notification to your Android app and displays a profile of the person, ensuring a more comfortable encounter with your visitor.

The Stack

Door Identify involves three components. First, a doorbell camera is triggered whenever a visitor rings your doorbell. Next, a cloud-hosted NodeJS server uses Microsoft's Cognitive Services to identify your visitor. Finally, a mobile app created in Android Studio fetches relevant data through HTTP requests.
