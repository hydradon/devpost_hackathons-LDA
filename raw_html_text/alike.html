Inspiration

Each of us has witnessed first hand the increasingly inauthentic nature of social media. Driven by "what other people think", we've created online alter egos upon which mental health is attached.

The data gathered by insurance companies is a underutilized resource. At the same time, these organizations have long struggled with ethics and emphasize client priorities.

What if there was a social media that promotes organic stories, with photos, videos, text - that nobody else will ever see? A social media that harnesses the true power of technology and connects you to the people who share goals, interests, and qualities; letting you cultivate relationships without the fear of strangers.
