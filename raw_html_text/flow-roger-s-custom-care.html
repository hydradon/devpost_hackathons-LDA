Inspiration

There were some stories in our group of people losing connectedness with loved ones due to dementia. This neurological condition results in a decline of memory, reasoning, and/or understanding. The rising life expectancy and population growth, costs of long-term care.

What it does

Senses the user's physical, emotional, and mental states and provides them an intervention to calm and assist their needs, in real time. Such as, voice assistants modelled on familiar family member and adaptions in the environment.

How we built it

Virtual Reality, Artificial Intelligence, logo makers, google slides presentations

Challenges we ran into

Thinking about and observing how the emerging technologies would be integrated into the system.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Finishing deliverables in an effective and efficient manner Creating video prototypes Understanding the science of dementia

What we learned

Continuous empathy for dementia patients Being resourceful

What's next for Flow: Roger's Custom Care

Looking into/observing how it will impact other health conditions :)
