Inspiration

Every day millions of flights are completed with a lot of wasted layover time. We are making great use of this down time by connecting you with other around you who are also waiting around, looking for something to do.

What it does

Connects people based on simliar layover within your area and gives users the option to go on a date, right in the airport!

Challenges we ran into

No backend experience at all. At the end of it, we had a lot of mentor help to get the backend functioning. Front-end framework installation was very time-consuming and painful.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

With half of the team being first time programmers, we have functional mobile app that is cross platform, decently formatted and ready for demoing.

What's next for flyng.

Business connections, friendly connections and gps location
