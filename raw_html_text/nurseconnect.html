Inspiration

Optum and helping better people's live. Our goal is to help Nurses world wide by making their job efficient. Rather than them writing on a piece of paper, they launch our iOS app to record their meeting with a patient. From they can ask how they're feeling, if they're hurt, and/or more. Our product takes this recording and parses through it and obtains data from this meeting. With that said, we want to aim towards a paperless and more efficient experience for the nurse.

What it does

Answers a questionnaire hands free, especially when the nurse needs to interact with the patient.

How we built it

Java, Swift, iOS, pain and tears

Challenges we ran into

Need a lot of input for dictionary and word recognition, developing an algorithm

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We have a working back and front end.

What we learned

How to do voice recognition and to translate it into text for review.

What's next for NurseConnect

Get back end to interact with front end. Tackle a better algorithm. Adopt machine learning for the future project.
