As many immigrants seek a new and better life in Canada, many can feel quite alone and isolated - not knowing what to do or the steps to take to slowly adapt.

With that being said, we wanted to create a platform that encourages community based connections so that immigrants can help each other grow, learn and connect, making the transition easier.

We decided to create a mobile application to easily showcase the features and help make usage easier as well.
