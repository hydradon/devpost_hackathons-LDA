Inspiration

We wanted to help solve the problem of optimizing delivery routes in troubled areas

What it does

We first forecast areas vulnerable to food security crisis if they meet two criteria:

(1) High Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (NDVI) Anomaly rates;

(2) Rate of population growth exceeding national average

Step 1: Create Raster files of areas with high HDVI anomaly rates( negative)
