qwer-hacks-bru-print

Our project for QWER Hacks 2020! This website helps Bruins with living more sustainable lifestyles by giving them an easy way to track their carbon footprint through integration with UCLA Dining Halls and other UCLA Housing services. This is more of a proof-of-concept translated to a website, but we plan on creating an app to make it easier for Bruins to track their carbon footprint. This was coded using HTML/CS/React.js and Python.
