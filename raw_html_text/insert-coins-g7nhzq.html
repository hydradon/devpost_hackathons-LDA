Smart Energy Hackathon Project

GIZ challenge

there is a problem about electricity price on remote islands. Local people use diesel to run their electricity generator. Because of diesel price that is not stable and depending on the mainland, electricity cost is very high. So, many energy developers want them to use renewable energy instead of diesel. And how to do that, we need to reach the diesel price on islands and display these data online, To make the developers sure that what's they compete with.

Our solution

We developed the software solution system to reach the diesel price data from local people on islands by the mobile application. It always notifies them to send the data at least once a month and displays real time average diesel price at the moment. Moreover, they can see the progress on their island and communicate with their neighbor. After we get the information, it will be analyzed and display on the website, so the developers can access it.
