WorkspaceManager

To run, type: python wmGui.py into the command line

Our Goal

WorkspaceManager isn't flashy. It's practical. This app seamlessly integrates itself into your workflow, allowing you to open collections of applications all at once rather than one at a time. WorkspaceManager isn't something you'll notice is there -- but it's something you'll notice is gone.

Technical Difficulty

Perhaps the most difficult part of building WorkspaceManager is that it interacts with other applications. Using python, we were able to take advantage of command line tools in a new way.

User Experience

WorkspaceManager is a simple drop-down menu on the top right of your screen. It's there when you need it, and you won't notice it when you don't. A natural addition to any college students set of tools.
