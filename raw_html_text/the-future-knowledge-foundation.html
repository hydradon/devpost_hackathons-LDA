Inspiration

We were inspired by a teacher in Baltimore city who moved all the way from California in order to give his students opportunities thet he knew they wouldn't get otherwise.

What it does

It connects under privileged schools with much needed resources from all over the world.

How we built it

We used the website template software Wix, and CSS to create the artistic aspect of the website

Challenges we ran into

The Wix software isn't compatible with multiple computer users

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are very proud that we finished

What we learned

We learned the basics of CSS and HTLM and hacking skills.

What's next for The Future Knowledge Foundation

We hope that TFKF can develop into a substantial foundation in which corporations, individuals and small businesses can contribute to the implementation of our mission statement in the necessary schools all over the world.
