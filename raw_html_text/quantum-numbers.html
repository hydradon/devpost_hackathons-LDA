Inspiration

I'm currently taking chem 101, and the quantum numbers of an electron are something a lot of people have trouble with. I wanted to make a fun way to practice and learn about them!

What it does

It quizzes the user about quantum numbers, and displays whether they're right or wrong!

What's next for Quantum Numbers

I want to add functionality that explains the answer if the user needs it. Also, I want to have an interactive learning section, that explains more about quantum numbers and what they mean for electrons
