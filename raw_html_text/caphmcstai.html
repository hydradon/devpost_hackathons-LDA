• Our project

Inspiration

We thought that it would be cool to count how many cars stop at four-way mandatory-stop intersections.

What it does

It gathers footage from an intersection and counts the number of cars that come to a full stop. It then serves the number of cars that stopped and cars that didn't stop on a webpage through Flask.

How we built it

Soylent

But seriously: we first built the program for finding and drawing the bounding box around the car, then we built the algorithm for checking if the car stopped. Finally, we worked on serving the HTML page through Flask.

Challenges we ran into

Determining if the car was stopped Determining if the car was still in the frame Finding a way to serve the data through Flask

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Accurately determining whether or not the car stopped Accurately determining whether or not the car was in the frame Serving the data through Flask

Setting our mind to something and completing it.
