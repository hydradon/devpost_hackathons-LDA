Inspiration

Most teenagers' stress originates from school, so creating a schedule to organize their work and list their priorities would be a helpful way to reduce stress.

What it does

Allows user to enter in priorities and goals for the week.

How we built it

We used Java GUI.

Challenges we ran into

Most of our team encountered one specific error which was difficult in resolving. Also, merging our individual contributions ran us into some trouble.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Being able to figure out a general plan of what we wanted to achieve.

What we learned

Creating a fully thought out application requires a lot of planning and teamwork and there is always room for improvement.

What's next for STRL

Piecing together our original plan, including all add-ons.
