Inspiration

We wanted to challenge ourselves to make a game about anything. We also just wanted to have a good fun time. We chose to do one about a lemon.

What it does

2D side scrolling pixel game.

How I built it

We used the Unity Game Engine, as well as building all original assets and art on a web browser pixel art software called Piskel.

Challenges I ran into

Design decisions were a hard part of this project. We also had to decide how much to include and what to cut out since we were on a time limit. Since we are relatively new to Unity, at some points we struggled with debugging some issues we came across.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We made a finished product that a player can play and experience in a very short amount of time.

What I learned

We expanded our knowledge of Unity game engine as well as working as a team on projects. We also strengthened areas like level and game design.

What's next for Lemmie Run

We wish to further develop this game and hopefully bring it to a full length game people can enjoy and experience.
