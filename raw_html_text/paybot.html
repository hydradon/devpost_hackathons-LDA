Excerpt: Federal Register on 09/21/2016 and available online at https://federalregister.gov/d/2016-22379

AGENCY: National Institutes of Health.

ACTION: Notice.

SUMMARY: The National Institutes of Health (NIH) is issuing this policy to promote broad and responsible dissemination of information from NIH-funded clinical trials through ClinicalTrials.gov. The policy establishes the expectation that all investigators conducting clinical trials funded in whole or in part by the NIH will ensure that these trials are registered at ClinicalTrials.gov, and that results information of these trials is submitted to ClinicalTrials.gov.

DATES: This policy will take effect January 18, 2017.
