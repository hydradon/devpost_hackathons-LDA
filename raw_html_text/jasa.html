Inspiration

People need to decide what to eat every day. It's very important to know what restaurant we can choose around us. If we go abroad, it sometimes hard to find out delicious food. We want to have a map to find restaurants easily and quickly. No matter where we are or what kind of food we want, we can just check website and know which restaurants are recommended quickly!!!

Why JASA ?

JASA in Taiwanese means "Eat What ?". We hope that person who is bothered by what to eat can connect to this website. This website can give you directly information about the location, rating, category, and detail information.

What it does

A map which shows the most popular restaurants nearby. Every circle on the map represents a restaurant. You can see the image and name of the restaurant on the circle. The rating of the restaurant is proportion to the radius of the circle directly. The Bigger circle means higher rating. The color of circle's outer frame is affected by the number of reviews. When the number of reviews increases, the color convert to pink from black. The left column of the website can let you choose what kind of food you want to eat. You can key-in the location to search the restaurant at that place. You can also check where the parking space is.

Need inspiration ? You can select the element in this section first. Then, the website will update the information of the restaurant according to your choice.

Show me section This section is updated when the restaurant information is updated. It will show some categories according to the categories of current restaurant. You can turn off the categories you don't want to eat so that you can reduce the circle on the map. It makes you easier to find what you want to eat!!

Top 5 restaurant This section shows the restaurant with top rating according to current choice. If you want to know the detail, you can click the circle or top 5 restaurants you are interested in. It will show the yelp website and show the detail about the restaurant!!

How we built it

We use Javascript and Yelp API to achieve this. We draw a node for each restaurant after API returns the data of restaurant.

What's next
