Based on personal health data privacy, novel blockchain based infrastructure, and proxied re-encryption algorithms, we plan to mock Nucypher's encryption algorithm to support the goals of initiatives such as General Data Privacy Regulation (GDPR).

We aim to implement a mock up of Nucypher's mining/node proxy network to serve the encryption standards. We aim to illustrate a specific use case within an electronic health data permissioned environment. It will allow users (patients) and providers (healthcare organizations) to share data based on dynamic permissions in a secure environment.
