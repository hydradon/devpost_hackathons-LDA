Inspiration

26 billion pounds of clothing end up in landfills

Companies such as Eddie Bauer, Victoria’s Secret and Urban Outfitters have been accused of destroying their clothing instead of donating it

People often don’t know or trust organizations that claim to be charitable, in fear of them misusing donations

What it does

An app that facilitates transfers between companies or people to accredited nonprofits that repurpose or reuse the clothing
