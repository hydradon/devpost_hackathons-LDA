Mesa 88

Inspiration

People often don't know what to do with their free time or even when they are commited to spend time with someone who just answers to "What do you want to do?" with "Whatever". That's not the only issue, to keep track of our time is a must because it is the most valuable thing we have.

What it does

Gives you the oportunity to clearly know what kind of things you can do in your freetime around your city and has the tools to let you know how much time you will spend on your activities.

How we built it

Layout activities to get you through filters to let you decide an activity, then searches your decitions into a database and shows you what places you can visit, on which route, how long will it take and let you organize your schedule.

Challenges we ran into

SQLite is definetly not as pretty and easy as they paint it.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Actually make this happen in 24 hours.

What we learned

The most important thing in a hackaton is to have a clear idea.

What's next for Whatever

More tools to give the user more control with his time.
