Inspiration

Few people use the nest at hackathons.

What it does

It determines the time of year, the temperatures you prefer when home and away, and calculates the best temperature to keep the settings at to keep your electric bill down.

How we built it

We used android studio, parse api and nest api.

Challenges we ran into

The nest api was not well documented yet for android.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We finished, had it connect with the nest and learned math.

What we learned

We learned more android and how to connect to nest api.

What's next for nestBuddy

The sky is the limit, but not the temperature.
