Why I created this app.

The app has agency section and users section . Most of transport information's like fair cost, pricing, bus arrival and departure time of various agencies can change over time. how can those changes be reflected for users to see. if we build a static app by mere picking few static info. so we created a system that can allow all agencies to add and modify all available information. all this info are available to all users accessing the app. If you are a taxi agency like Uber, you can visit Biz Page Option to create info. a user trying to access taxi can access content from access option.

My multi-modal system work by allowing all agencies to add their info's via biz page from the agencies section instead of picking outdated infos. all this are available to all mexico city users in real time from Access option. . The video covers agencies and some part of users section.

Few Things to know ABOUT Agencies and Users(Citizens & Visitors).....

1.) City Updates Option: Enable Agencies and Mexico City Users to Share Text, Photos and videos regarding Mexico City Events, News,transportation and road incident in realtime.
2.) Update Profile Option: Enable Agencies and Users to Update their Profiles.
3.) Biz Page Option: Enable Agencies to Update their Companies Data.
4.) Access Option: Enable Agencies/Users to access all agencies provided data like bus/taxi arrival & departure time, pricing cost, booking,setting of bus/taxi arrival reminders,sms,email and chat communications etc.
5.) Sales Option: Enable Agencies to see people who book or place order on their products along with their Payment Info.
6.) Paypal Option: Enable Agencies to Update their Paypal Email Accounts to enable them to recieve Payments into their Paypal Accounts.
7.) Milestone Option: Enable Agencies to create a Milestone Payment and send to Users so that they can pay for products using Paypal Accounts.

8.) Your Purchase Option: Enable Citizens & Visitors to see all their orders and bookings .
9.) Payment Option: Enable Citizens & Visitors to see all payments details regarding all their orders and bookings .

10.) Reminder Option: Enable Citizens & Visitors to see all the Reminders already set from the Transport Agencies Profiles regarding the bus arrival and depature time for buses,taxi at various bustop. The default settings is 15 MINS. Depending on the bustop name you set, you will always get an alert 15 mins before arrival and departure time of any bus/taxi transiting that bustop. If you are an office staff, this 15 mins will enable you to close things and work down to the stations to catch a ride..

Transportations:

We created a Decentralized System that allow all the Taxi,Car,Buses, Train etc to updates their agencies data contents along with their Paypal Account Info that will be available for all the Mexican City Users. The app takes data like Transport agency Name,Taxi/Car Series,Locations,photos,Price Per KM of Coverage,mobile contacts,emails,Bus arrival and Depature Time at various bustop.

Assuming someone is at balderas and wants to know the bus arrival and depature time, He/She will just enter the bustopId or Bustop name eg Balderas in the searchbox and all the Buses and Taxi transiting the bustop will be showned along with their arrival and depature time. One can set up a Bus 15 minutes arrival Reminder. This Bus arrival reminder is good because if bus will arrive in balderas bustop by 5:30 Pm, You will be notified in 5:15 PM via Online Realtime Alert,SMS and Email messages..
Again one looking for a Bus,Taxi,Bicycle to hire will be able to hire/book a Car from Agencies Profiles by entering her Pick-Up Locations and Destinations. The app calculates the distance of the travel in KM along with the Price Per KM and User will be able to know her Fees. The User will be able to communicate with any transport agency via Chat System,SMS and can pay her fees using paypal account........
