Inspiration

When I was a young boy I used to be forced to paint, over and over and over again. I watched paint dry for 20 years. So I decided to create an app to alleviate my pain. GottaDryThatPaint.com is a real labor of love. I have never felt more connected emotionally and spiritually to a project before.

What it does

This app integrates SOMOLO social, mobile, and local. Ever had trouble setting up a paint drying party? NO MORE with our revolutionary site. We use the safest estimate of dry time for most all paints (Results may vary) to bring you the most enjoyable paint drying experience.
