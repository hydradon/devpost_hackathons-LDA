Inspiration

Our dorm, Random Hall, has a group grocery ordering service. Unfortunately, this currently involves a bunch of tedious manual labor for our Grocery Chair, mostly involving collecting and going through everyone's orders on a weekly basis, and manually adding their items to a group Peapod cart. We want to simplify this experience, both for grocery orderers and (especially) our grocery chair.
