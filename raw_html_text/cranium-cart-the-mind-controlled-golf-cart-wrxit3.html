By leveraging the power of the mind, we've enabled remote mind control - arguably telekinesis - over the most recreational vehicle of all: the golf cart. One actuator per pedal is driven by a link to an RC connection, which is using signal processing and machine learning to interpret the thoughts of its user: "forward" to go forward, "stop" for - you guessed it - stop.
