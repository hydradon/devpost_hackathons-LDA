Inspiration

We thought about the kinds of things applications are useful for and decided it was about time we started creating something useful but simple (at first). Hopefully this is the start of a series of inventive projects

What it does

calculates tip on anything you want

How we built it

used swift and xcode, lynda, youtube, and stack overflow

Challenges we ran into

learning a whole new language. we were just learning on the fly the whole time, dealing with the caveats of debugging in a language we'd almost never been exposed to

Accomplishments that we're proud of

we're proud of our UI skills, considering this is our first attempt.

What we learned

we learned how to use Xcode, patience with systems, and how to sit down and just work for hours

What's next for Tip Calculator

we want to smoothen it out by rounding the digits a little better, getting rid of leading zeros, and perhaps including a round to nearest dollar button.

Built With

  • swift
  • xcode
