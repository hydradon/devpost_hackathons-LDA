Inspiration

Imagine there is a app on your smartphone which memorizes your grocery shopping habits.

What it does

AJoure offers a simple and easy going solution for you to get informed about offers you really like to know. By knowing your refrigerator inventory and the text recognition of your receipt the app offers customized shopping tips by nearby stores.

What's next for AJoure

There are two main challenges WE are going to face. The implementation of an accurate algorithm which helps to analyze the collected data and offers the greatest value for the customer.
