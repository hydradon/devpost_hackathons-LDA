Inspiration

The idea of utilizing enormous food available in Silicon Valley to be available to students, old people and to less fortunate for free.

What it does

Person who is hungry and in need of food or grocery can directly send sms or text via app telegram and our chat bot will send food locations near them in a very user-friendly way.

How I built it

Core Database using Django and SMS part using Twilio. Telegram, Android app using Java

Challenges I ran into

Debug the application after each phase

Accomplishments that I'm proud of that

We were able to complete the application in time and learnt so many new things.

What I learned

About wit, Twilio, Android, Telegram and Django

What's next for SMileS

May be to provide future support to the community and make it more feasible and scalable.
