Inspiration

We have a hard time waking up in the morning, so we thought, "Hey! I would love to wake up to Donald yelling at me in the morning!"

What it does

This alarm clock app will wake you up at the specified time with one of many famous Donald Trump soundbites. You can either turn off the alarm by unlocking the phone and pressing a button, or snooze by shaking the phone.

However, snoozing comes with a price... A HUUUUUUGEEE price. You'll have to post one of Trump's many inspirational quotes with #MakeAmericaGreatAgain on Twitter to turn off the alarm.

How we built it

From scratch on Xcode with Swift.
