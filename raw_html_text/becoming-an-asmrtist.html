Inspiration

Have you ever wanted to getaway from the comfort of your own room? Us too. We love watching ASMR videos on YouTube and wanted to make something that allowed the user to create with their own ASMRtistry.

What it does

Becoming an ASMRtist takes the user through an interactive experience of what makes up ASMR.

How we built it

We used processing (java) to create the interactive experience through the world of ASMR. We utilized processing's video and minim audio libraries.

Challenges we ran into

Getting minim to cooperate with our original idea or having the user record parts of their audio at different times in the program. We ended up modifying our idea slightly for time's sake.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We got a working program! It's a prototype we came up with on processing over the past couple of days, and despite all the bugs and problems we came up with we managed to finish.

What we learned

Processing lags with a lot of videos, which makes sense. Also sleep is good.

What's next for Becoming an ASMRtist

Make a game out of it.
