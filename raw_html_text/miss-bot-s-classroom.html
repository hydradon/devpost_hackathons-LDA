What it does

Miss Bot learning how to code through jumping over elements and if she misses an element she falls directly into the black hole and her score gets reset. When users taps the screen after fallen into black hole, miss bot gets put back to its place where she left off.

How we built it

We built this project in the course of 7 days, learning AR platform from Scratch. We use background loops, audio, scripting, which the whole group was involved in. Cynthia designed Miss Bot's character, background, and elements.

Challenges we ran into
