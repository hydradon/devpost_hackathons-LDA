Inspiration

Bitcoin and the increase in the cryptocurrency market with the potential use of block chain in our everyday lives.

What it does

store and organized block chain currency based by using libnspv as a model to learn a develop a wallet that can be used by people around the world for easy conversions

How I built it

building upon the Komodo git hub samples libnspv to make an example of a wallet with local host self node transfer and exchange wallet.

Challenges I ran into

Running a project all by myself in a field I had little experience in, as I had never tackled coding in the development of wallets or anything real relating to crypto currency.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

making a decent product with something to present and not giving up no matter what unexpected events happened during the Hack-a-ton for me.

What I learned

A bit about smart chain and effective block chain through the enchantment of the Simple Payment Verification

What's next for Crypto Night and Day

To have Day and Night wallet a fully established wallet block chain that gives access to a diverse group of people around the world, giving many the Buiness opportunities they couldn’t have gotten without the access of a good crypto wallet.
