SAR

  • We want easily accessible resources at Dartmouth and ways for students to seek help for themselves and others in the community for responding to sexual violence. Through our app we provide a way to connect the user with friends or communities that can be source for them. Additionally, we provide steps to follow if immediate help is needed and understanding terminology used on Dartmouth's campus and legal system in the USA. We also outline the steps and procedures that will be taken if the user chooses to proceed with their case. Furthermore, we added a tap that allows users to help according to their time commitment: seconds, minutes, hour(s), and term(s).
