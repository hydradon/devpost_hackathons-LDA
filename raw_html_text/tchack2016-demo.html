Inspiration

Our inspiration came solely from personal experiences. Whenever we have food with a group, either one person pays, and the rest of the group venmos them back IF they remember, or we inconvenience the waiter/waitress by throwing all our credit cards in leading to extra reciepts and wasted paper. Also, people miss out on credit card rewards if they use venmo to pay a friend rather than paying the restaurant directly.

What it does

Our app uses the BRAINTREE sdk in order to let groups sort out how food should be split at the restaurant, and it lets them pay the restaurant directly with the taps of a few buttons. This app works by generating a unique order number for each transaction. The customers splitting the amount can do so by entering the 5 digit order number. They are then given a choice if the split should be done evenly between everyone on the order, or if the split will be done by item. The app will automatically decide which card is used based on the type of business at which the payment is being made, and the card that will give the customer the maximum rewards. With this app, paper money and credit cards will no longer need to be carried around. It is also better than apple pay as no hardware is required to do the transaction.

How we built it

We used Swift and x-code in order to build the application. We also did work in Python and JS for the server side of the app.
