Coffee Friends Nearby

While facebook users usually have hundreds to thousands of friends online, there is a limited chance of meeting friends face to face. We provide the service by which they can meet new friends who go to the same Starbucks. There are various advantages of bringing Facebook and Starbucks together. Most importantly, "the new friends" would be first verified by facebook. The easy access to the same Starbucks gives greater chance for the users to make use of offline connection such as meeting up for coffee, share used goods, give or get help, and so on. Coffee Friends Nearby would be able to provide the solution to the people with the need of making offline friends.
