Help Section: The application tests the user’s ability in trivia.

After starting, the user will be presented with 10 different questions from trivia. and with each, the user will have 30 seconds, as shown with a timer in the top right hand corner of the screen, to answer the question by inputting the number into the bottom of the screen.

The user will be given about 7 - 8 links to choose from, with the page title and the URL. Click on the URL to find out more about the subject to answer the question.

There will be a page at the end displaying how well you did.

Good Luck!
