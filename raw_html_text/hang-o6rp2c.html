Coordinating hanging out with Chi Alpha friends

It allows you to check in to a location so your friends can know when you are somewhere so you can coordinate activities.

By asking lots of questions and finally settling on using Amazon rds for backend data and react-native for the structure of our app

We went into this project with no prior experience of JavaScript hoping to gain knowledge. We spent a large portion of time trying to decide which tools and setting up said tools to use for our project. Half of the team learned how to use GitHub.

How open minded our team was to acquiring the knowledge and skills necessary for this project.

We learned about AWS' many different database solutions, react-native and java script, the different account login solutions, such as Firebase and Cognito, and the joys of friendship and the beauty of teamwork.

Google authentication/geo-location, and a more developed user experience are what the future goals for hang are
