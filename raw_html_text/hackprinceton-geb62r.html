FarSight Safety

Features *Over Extension Indicator *Struggle Detector *Person Detector *Fall Detector *Email Notification *GPS Location

Made with an Arduino as a sensor hub. The Arduino then sends alerts as it detects them over serial communications to the Raspberry Pi. The Pi acts as our main microcomputer and interprets the information. The Pi also takes images and uses open cv to locate the person using the walker. When a potential issue occurs, all family members on the email list receive an email notify them of the issue, the walkers current gps location, and the last image taken.
