• Queue gate

You don't like waisting time in queues, don't you?

Why: We'd like to remove the headache in one of the most common problem are all facing – queues.

How: Simple! Introduce sophisticated digital services to the physical world.

What: Queue gate is a digital queue system that allows people to avoid waiting for a queue in uncomfortable chairs while playing "Candy Smash*"

Well, somebody should ask following question: _ "But how about the hundreds of existing solutions that already in the market?" _

** Competitive advantage.** Today the _ banking services _ create an opportunity to turn the way old services were working. Queue gate charges every user* who don't face the calling: 1) Can't come? Cancel the queue and decrease the time for others. 2) Don't lose your turn! Get notified when the queue moves realtime. 3) We know when you arrive, don't worry! Our beacons detect automatically your presence. 4) Didn't show up and didn't cancel? Prepare the wallet at the end of the day. Missing the queue costs, so demonstrate your commitment and don't get charged.

Select ticket number -> arrive in time -> Voilà!
