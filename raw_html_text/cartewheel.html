The idea is to get cash delivered to people who might not want to go to an ATM to withdraw cash. Built during Cal Hacks 2015 (Cal Hacks 2.0). This was everyone's first hackathon. Perhaps as a result, we spent about half of our time coming up with an idea.

What it does

Requests for cash to be sent to you from an Uber.

How I built it

We looked at Uber's sample Flask app.
