Millennials love tech. Millennials dislike traditional banking. Even with online banking available, it can still be awkward to deal with finances the traditional way.

Enter Budgetbot. With Google Home nearby, simply talk to your personal financial assistant and let them deal with your banking needs.

Starting with a simple "Ok Google, start Budgetbot" you can easily check your balance, pay off bills and hear how your stock portfolio is doing from the comfort of your bed. Banking has never been more comfortable.
