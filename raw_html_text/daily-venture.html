Inspiration

Daily video and photo journaling.

What it does

Gives you a random tip/task to complete each day and capture the moment with your phone's camera. Then you can see all of your daily adventures in a grid style.

How we built it

We used Swift.

Challenges we ran into

The Swift language changes very frequently so when trying to solve our problems on Stack Overflow, the language was often outdated.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Accessing the phone camera and using a button to shuffle through an array of tips.

What we learned

Swift!

What's next for Daily Venture

A more comprehensive grid/calendar mode and accessing tips from different online sources to truly randomize the adventures one can take!
