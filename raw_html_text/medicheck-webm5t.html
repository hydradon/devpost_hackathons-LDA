Inspiration

A friend went to a hospital where nurses were having trouble managing his information. It took a long time to retrieve medical information from his family doctor, stressing out both our friend and the nurses. With our app, we hope to eliminate problems such as:

Efficiency Faxing records Remembering specific medications/brands of medication, etc.

Accessibility Different healthcare providers, doctors, and insurance Difficult for multiple doctors to have access to same information at once

Safety Drug history & interactions

What it does

A website that consolidates all of an individual’s medical information: Prescriptions Allergies Blood Work Surgeries CT Scans Accessible to different healthcare providers

How I built it

HTML / CSS JavaScript Python

Challenges I ran into

Originality Largest obstacle: coming up with creative, yet feasible, idea Eventually focused on current problems that affected others including ourselves and how we could solve them.

Experience Only one person on our team had prior experience with HTML, CSS, and Javascript, which made it difficult to carry out our ideas. A lot of time was spent teaching ourselves these languages.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Managed to create a working website that doctors can use to modify patient information. Additionally, this information can be shared with other hospitals and health services.

What I learned

HTML How to make a website from scratch

CSS Ways to create an aesthetic and provocating look to the website.

JavaScript Much better than HTML and CSS; one can actually use datatypes

Teamwork & Leadership Divide and conquer Maximize our strengths

What's next for Medicheck

App Development Improving the UI of the iOS and Android version of Medicheck so that patients can carry around their records to make medical visits faster.

Security Because Medicheck will have access to patient information, patient privacy and protection will be implemented.

Introducing Medicheck to hospitals and the public Getting doctors around the world set-up with Medicheck
