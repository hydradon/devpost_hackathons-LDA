Inspiration

First we wanted to resurrect some body from the past, but we realised that it is really BORING.

And then we wanted to have fun, we thought about resurrection real hero of nowadays. So:

MEET, MEME HERO OF 2k16 - HARAMBE (SAVIOUR).

What it does

World is gone to awful place to live and people need hope and REAL HERO to save their lives.

You meet some strange person that tells how you can resurrect Harambe by finding very special ingredients...

You have to pass impossible quests to gain some of them, like:

Help Clinton by not letting some guy (YOU KNOW WHO IT IS) attack her and helping her delete emails ASAP.

Emails are coming every 4s and if you fail to recognise if it's SECRET (should be deleted) or NORMAL - you will fail immediately .

And if you will not perform good enough, you will resurrect not Harambe, but some magical creature. And who knows what will it do to the world.
