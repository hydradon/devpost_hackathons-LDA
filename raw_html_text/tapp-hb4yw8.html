tapp: Relationship Analytics for the 21st Century

link

Relationship analytics are some of the most difficult statistics to obtain. People are very private about what their definitions of beauty are, and who they are attracted to. Our goal was to create a fun game which was able to make increase access to this type of data. And we did just that. "tapp" is a social media game where users provide reliable and previously inaccessible data, while enjoying doing it through an immersive game involving their Facebook friends.
