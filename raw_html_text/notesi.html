Notesi ( /noʊtsi/ )

Notesi is a service that enables students to upload and share notes for classes and specific lectures. If a student misses class, it's critical that they be able to catch back up. With Notesi, other students in the class can share their notes just by taking a picture, allowing sick or students unable to go to class to catch up on the content their peers consider important.

By aggregating multiple students' notes, Notesi allows the best explanations and the most relevant content to percolate to the top. It includes user voting, which means that well-written notes and well-uploaded notes can gain points, while poorly written, off topic notes can be ingored.
