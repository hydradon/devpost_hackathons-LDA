When we were standing in line waiting to be signed in to Juniper venue, we all noticed how unbearably boring and unproductive we were spending our time. This app is a solution to long lines! Imagine that instead of having to wait for someone to check off your name you can just sign in with a special code and let the staff know you are already here! The visualized part where every time someone checks in a small character with their name show up on a drawing version of Juniper venue isn't only interesting to look at, it also quickly conveys information about how full the building becomes and how many more are expected through sidebars with statistics!

sidenote: this project required two repositories because of the way that server was built to communicate with other devices, here are links to both https://github.com/WojtekTB/hackathonApp/settings/collaboration (frontend) https://github.com/WojtekTB/checkInAppBackend (backend)
