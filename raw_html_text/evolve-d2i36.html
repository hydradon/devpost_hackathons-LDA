Inspiration

Interest in AI and passion for Chemistry

How it works

A website will allow students to converse and chat with each other online in order to solve a chemistry problem. If help is needed, the AI will provide a step-by-step solution of the problem. In the fist few trials, feedback should be manually inputted by the users. This will allow the AI to learn and accurately provide solutions later on.
