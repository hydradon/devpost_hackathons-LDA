What it does

Pandora for gifs!

Upload your favorite gifs to find similar ones. Our algorithm parses gifs based on relevancy, upload count, subreddit point distribution, thematic similarity, and artist. The user picks their favorites and our machine learning code automatically updates the present gifs to tailor to your taste.
