Inspiration: On numerous occasions our fellow peers and us have been stuck in a situation where office hours and math tutoring have been inconvenient, unavailable, or their teaching methods were not effective as they could have been. As a result, the idea for this app arose to face the challenge.

What it does

Creates a skill improvement platform to connect within its student body Connecting students to create a friendly and better environment for academic purpose while preparing them with social skills for future employment.

How we built it

We collaborated on the infrastructure by pitching ideas, dedicating time to research, and using all resources.

Challenges we ran into

In the beginning the member of this team had no prior knowledge of each other. After getting into the team we found that we had to take time to get to know each other. Another challenge we meet was connecting activities that were created to work with each other.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of the content we learned and created. For a group made at the last minute, we feel proud out team's ability to work together and accomplish the amount of work we did.

What we learned

An large amount of java, android studio, and xml

What's next for Help-a-Bud

There is plenty more to be done , we aspire for this application to grow so that it can be helpful for the student and in the long run create professionals.
