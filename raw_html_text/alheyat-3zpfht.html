Shakeel's father got diabetes at a very early age of his life. Which then became a reason for his cardiovascular disease. His father didn't maintain a healthy lifestyle which includes doing exercise. We want to do public awareness among the people of Islamabad to use the publicly available parks to engage in a healthy regime which can save them from early age diseases.

The solution we are providing helps people of find the nearby park and will inform about the what type of exercise they can perform. We will also arrange public events which will be fitness competitions to create a strong community which cares and inspires about physical fitness.

Starting we are using our most common social media platforms which include facebook and youtube. We are also in talks with https://beatthegym.com/ who are willing to share their technology.

Motivating people to wake up early is a bit of a challenge. We aim to do this by creating video content. We meet with mentors working in different domains and with the help of their advice we were able to start the idea with a very simple tasks and built on it.

We aim to partner with sports bodies across the city and with their collaboration we will arrange public sports events which will help to promote different sports in the city. Going along we will also arrange paid public classes from trainers for special groups like yoga and therapy.
