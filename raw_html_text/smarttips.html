Smart Tips

What it does

Have you ever been through a situation, when you experience a very good service, you want to thank the waiter, but you pay with credit card and you do not have cash? That is a very common story.

5 of 5 respondents (waiters, restaurant managers) admitted the fact, that more and more people want to pay everything online. So, a big steak of tips is missing their potential receivers.

Over 2,3 bln rubles a month people in Moscow are leaving as tips. Can you imagine such an enormous money flow coming without any tracking from restaurant managers. But what if we could make even bigger and put everything online with clear monitoring and statistics?

SMART TIPS - is an app, that allows people to leave tips online, comment on the service and find beautiful places nearby.

What's next for Smart Tips

Tactics: test & learn mvp on a small customer-partner, then – distribution start with large franchises to meet the end-user demand. The bigger the franchise is – the heavier for them is the problem that we solve and the larger is traffic.

Step-by-step we are developing our product to the release stage, adding new features based on smart back-end solutions and HERE Api.
