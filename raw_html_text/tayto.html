What it does

A social network for users to search or make reviews about products. But that's not all. Users can keep updating periodically to show legitimate real-time and long-term results by reviewing products. Instead of reading blocks of texts, you can scroll through people's experiences with a product. Our app makes it easy for people to choose whether to try or buy a product.

How I built it

We built it on Android Studio.

Challenges I ran into
