• Make everyone your ATM

Introducing Cartera, a human network of ATMs.

Imagine an application that connected you to a network of ATMs. But here's the catch, they aren't real ATMs. They are your family, friends and community around you and they carry the cash you need to pay your bills, do your laundry or get a haircut. Simply post a request for an amount of cash you wish to withdraw from your account. Someone close to you (within a 5 mile radius) accepts your request gets you the cash and takes that dollar amount from your bank account. It's simple and hassle free! Interested in being a lender? You can choose to accept others' requests and earn reward points from Capital One! Banking is an old financial scheme. We'd like to make it a social trend!
