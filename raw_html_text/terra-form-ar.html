Inspiration

This app was inspired by the existing app, Horizontal Explorer AR, a potentially really useful app but with a not so great interface. This version is device agnostic and built-in web XR and can be deployed to a phone, VR or AR headset.

What it does

Terra Forma AR gives form to user-defined geospatial data as an AR overlay.
