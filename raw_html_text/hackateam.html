We noticed a problem

You register for a hackathon. You wait for weeks in anticipation. You wonder what you'll work on. The day arrives, but you're only given 30 minutes to find a team. Your team spends hours figuring out what each of you can contribute and what interests you share.

We crafted a solution

A lightweight web-app with a single goal: make it easy to search, create, and manage hackathon teams. Users can add their skills and search for teams in need of their expertise. Anyone can create a team and no one gets left out. Once you join a team, start sharing ideas right away without having to leave the app.

And more

Web scrapers to gather info on hackathons and hackers...a neural net to create the perfect teams.
