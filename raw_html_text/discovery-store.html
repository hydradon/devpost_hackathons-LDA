Inspiration

Aren't you tired of seeing always the same apps in the play store? Searching for some new funny apps always leads to the same apps everyone has already seen before?

What it does

Discovery App enables you to find new apps submitted by real users. Moreover you can share your findings and recommendations with other users, so that they can have as much as fun as you have with the app.

How we built it

  • Brainstorming about the idea and gather our ideas
  • Cluster ideas by must have and nice to have
  • Break the ideas down to a task list
  • Create a timeline upon the tasks
  • Assign the team members to the tasks
  • UI Design with FIGMA
  • Create the mobile application with flatter & dart
  • Create the application server with Spring Boot & Kotlin
  • Create a website scrapper to collect data from the Google Play Store

Challenges we ran into

  • Creating proper design screens

Accomplishments that we're proud of

  • Our structural way of working together
  • Having a running application at the end of the hackathon

What we learned

  • A hackathon is a nice way of getting connected with people sharing the same interests
