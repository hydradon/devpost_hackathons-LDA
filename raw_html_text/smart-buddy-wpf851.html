• Smart Buddy

Inspiration

Smart Buddy, is your true buddy, with whom, you can have fun while learning.

What it does

Here you can ask for -

  1. General Knowledge quiz
  2. Math
  3. Spelling

• General Knowledge quiz: - Smart Buddy will ask you 10 questions, each question will have 4 answers, out of which only 1 answer is correct. For each question you answered correctly, you will get 100 points, so for 10 questions you will get 1000 points.

• Math: - You can ask Smart Buddy, simple math calculations like addition, subtraction, multiplication or division of any 2 numbers, n then Smart Buddy will answer your questions.

• Spelling: - Here Smart Buddy will give you a word to spell. After each word, 10 sec. pause is given to write down the spelling. Before launching of the skill, get paper and pen to write your spelling. In the end Smart Buddy will give you the correct answer/spelling.

Smart Buddy is very simple to use.

To get started say, "Alexa, open Smart Buddy”

Or

"Alexa, ask Smart Buddy to start General Knowledge"

Or

"Alexa, ask Smart Buddy to start Math”

Or

"Alexa, ask Smart Buddy to start Spelling”
