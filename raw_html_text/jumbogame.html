What it does

JumboGames allows sports teams to invite their fans to compete for prizes by playing trivia games on the Jumbotron using their mobile devices as a game controller.

What's next for JumboGame

We're planning on meeting with the Solar Bears to discuss how we can make JumboGames work for a game coming up on the 19th of March.
