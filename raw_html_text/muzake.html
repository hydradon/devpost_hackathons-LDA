Inspiration

Kialo but useful for actual teams of people.

What it does

It allows you to create and structure a debate and use that information to inform people before they do a poll to come to a decision.

How we built it

Vue.js with Google Firestore and vue-router.

Challenges we ran into

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned

How to build a full stack app in Vue.js
