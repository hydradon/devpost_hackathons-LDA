Inspiration

Making clips is subjective and doesn't really have much say in community interaction.

What it does

HypeStamp is a two-part extension. Streamers can put a button overlay that users can click during hype moments. We then record the most "hype" moments and timestamp those in a VOD. Streamers, after they're finished, can switch the extension to a panel that displays the most hype moments from their most recent stream.

How we built it

HTML, CSS, node.js, MongoDB
