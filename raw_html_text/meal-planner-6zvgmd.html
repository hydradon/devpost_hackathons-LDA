Inspiration

Many students have trouble spending all the money in their Meal Plan account. The remaining will be belong to McGill.

What it does

Our app recommends them eating places according to their preferences to help them use up all the money.

How I built it

We implemented the app based on web using Vue.js and firebase database.

Challenges I ran into

All of us were new to Vue.js.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We have learned basic Vue.js in a few hours and use it to accomplish the app.

What I learned

Besides Vue, I learned how to coordinate with teammates and be patient when problem arises.

What's next for Meal Planner

Let users to post their location when they are in one of the places where they can use Meal Plan to allow other students to find them and help them to use up the money.
