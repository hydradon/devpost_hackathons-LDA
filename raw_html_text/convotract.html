Making official legal documents can be tedious. Especially if the conversation is verbal, it can be difficult to form it into an official contract. Our app, ConvoTract, can record conversations about contracts and uses machine learning and artificial intelligence to pick out keywords from the verbal exchange. These keywords are then used to create a contract which can be signed by each party.
