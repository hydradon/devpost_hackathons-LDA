Inspiration

67% of NYC youth under the age of 12 were incarcerated in 2016.1 out of every 5 youth (21%) who are brought before the court with a delinquency case are detained. Those incarcerated as a juvenile are 39% less likely to graduate from high school and are 41% more likely to have entered adult prison by age 25. With this criminal record, they have a difficult time getting jobs. Most companies do not hire employees without a clear record. That’s where New Leaf comes in. New Leaf aims to help prisoners and ex-prisoners restart their lives by aiding them on the process of getting jobs. Through resume building, education mentorships, and mental help, New Leaf helps prepare these people to be job-ready. We then work to help place all of our users in a job.

What it does

New Leaf aims to help prisoners and ex-prisoners restart their lives by aiding them on the process of getting jobs. Through resume building, education mentorships, and mental health help, New Leaf helps prepare these people to be job-ready. We then work to help place all of our users in a job.

How we built it

We built this application using HTML, CSS, and Javascript front end development, and Django, python and SQL for backend.
