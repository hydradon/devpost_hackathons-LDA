Inspiration

In times of need, technology can help us save lives.

What it does

A full-fledged platform for disaster relief, response delegation for normal citizens and first responders.

How we built it

Using HashGraph, Nexmo SMS texting, Esri ArcGIS mapping technologies, OpenCV, Arity Risk Management, Mapquest/Verizon Thingspace and Api.ai chatbot alongside a custom parsing library. We also used React.js and Node.js.
