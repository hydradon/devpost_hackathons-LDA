Inspiration

Allowing players to generate truly random numbers. In the UK almost 46 million people play the lottery each year. Most people prefer to go the lucky dip route where the lottery shop generates the random numbers for them.

What it does

This skill offers an alternative to the standard random numbers offered when buying a lottery ticket. The truly/premium random numbers are statistically drawn from atmospheric noise. The free offering uses the regular pseudo random generator from the python language.
