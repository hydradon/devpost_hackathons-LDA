Inspiration

Saudi Arabia is going into a huge transformation of technology. The history of cinema and entertiment in Saudi Arabia is just born. We have invented an application, to support the Saudi Nation and following the Vision of 2030.

What it does

The application is made to serve the whole kingdom of Saudi Arabia, specifically in up coming movies, where are the location of each cinema, and to help you to book the seat your prefer in each movie.

How we built it

It's a mobile application in both android and IOS apps.

Challenges we ran into

Timeline. Likewise it took us so long to create a professional design that is presenting the idea of cinema in Saudi Arabia.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The first professional application of Cinema in Saudi Arabia.

What we learned

There are many Imaginable ideas that can be transferred into reality.

What's next for Saudi Cinema App

We have a vision that we are able to move further in the future with the application to be serving the whole middle east world.
