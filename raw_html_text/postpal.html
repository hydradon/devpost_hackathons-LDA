Inspiration

There are over 1 billion package re-delivery attempts that occur in the united states every year, and last year alone, over 11 million US household have had packages being stolen from their doorsteps. Tremendous time, money, and resources are wasted, and the consumers received nothing but anger and disappointment.

What it does

PostPal is a secure, easy way to get your package delivered, at your convenience.
