Why: Jarvis - just a really very intelligent system - uses readily accessible data to ensure the right drug and dosage is given to the right patient at the right time by the right route.

How: Jarvis ensures seamless communication and coordination among the physician, pharmacist and nurse throughout the prescription, verification, preparation and administration of a medication.

Jarvis uses data available at the point of care to monitor the progress of a medication order, alerting the directly responsible individual at each point of the process, while ensuring patient safety along the way.

Jarvis incorporates drug formulary guidelines, as well as clinical guidelines and market knowledge, to increase the value of each medication order.

What: Jarvis is a cloud-based closed loop medication management system powered by a clinical decision support system.
