What is it?

When we came to HackRidge, we wanted to combine a software project with a tangible hardware device, and decided a projectile launcher fit our needs well.

What is it?

We made a catapult out of plywood, an Arduino, some servo motors, and an ultrasonic sensor. Taken together, they create a powerful (and pretty awesome) micro-controlled launcher.
