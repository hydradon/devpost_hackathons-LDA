Inspiration

We envision a interactive environment that improves productivity between people in a class or a business meeting to achieve better collective work

What it does

It allows the room administrator to host a class or a meeting to get real time interactive participation by the group as people can do their own interpretation and ideas and get shared to everyone or to the room admin
