Inspiration

We thought the presentation given by the ActivBody representatives were interactive and we felt that we wanted to expand on their app by adding personal user music integration.

What it does

Our app integrates the android music player / audio player manager so that the user can control the music play/stop using the device and play games that integrate their own music.

How we built it

We used android studio and kotlin to program most of the app.

Challenges we ran into

Getting the app to toggle music through the AudioManager, Switching Activities while preserving non-Serializable and non-Parceable objects, Understanding new syntax (kotlin)

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Developing our first Android app as a team.

What we learned

Kotlin and Android Studio

What's next for Activ5 Music

Integration of certain features into the Activbody app
