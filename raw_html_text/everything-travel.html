Inspiration

The problem for the tunnel seemed clearly, difficulty of public transport & excess traffic from cars. Thus it was clear to give residents the option to choose and provide to other residents combination of public & private transport to get to their destinations quicker.

What it does

Search for travel directions between destinations gives

Public Transport & any Ride Sharing options.

It allows to create Ride Share to offer to other residents. Allowing residents to provide options to transport both Wheelchair Residents Carry Bicycles for passengers

What I learned

** Real time chat among those who joined a ride share developed with Firebase**
