Inspiration

There is a lot of food waste at restaurants, events, college dining commons, you name it. What if there's a way to donate that food to people who need it?

What it does

Neighborhood Nibbles allows people to donate leftover food by posting what food they have left. Anyone can find amazing food that would otherwise have gone to waste.

How we built it

We used Node.js, HTML, CSS, MySQL, Jawsdb, and Bootstrap to create our amazing demo!

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We have very proud that we were able to collaborate in such a short time to solve a real problem using the knowledge and skills we have.

What's next for Neighborhood Nibbles

Push/email notifications, geolocation to filter posts by the user's distance, mobile app, social aspect which, for example, can connect a homeless shelter to a nearby restaurant that has leftovers often, and more!
