Inspiration

Stress, Depression, and Anxiety play a negative influence in many lives, including ours. Many people at our school have taken away their lives because of this.

What it does

We made an app that reduces stress, anxiety, and depression, hopefully reducing the risk of someone to suicide.

How we built it

We built with swift on xcode.

Challenges we ran into

We weren't able to use mp4 files because of the wifi.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to make an entire working app in less than an entire day.

What we learned

We learned how important time is, and not to procrastinate.

What's next for Resolving Mental Health Issues

We want to make the app more interactive, and be able to add audio and video.
