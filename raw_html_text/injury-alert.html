Inspiration

When I used played fantasy baseball, it was a huge advantage to pick up players based on the updated injury news. Injury Update texts you as this information becomes available.

What it does

Users subscribe to teams and get text messages when new injury news is available.

What's next for Injury Alert

I didn't implement the polling of the SportsRadar Injury API, and that would need to be added. I would also like to add subscriptions to players as well, so a user doesn't have to subscribe to a whole team if they don't want to.
