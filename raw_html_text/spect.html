Inspiration

All of us met here at LA Hacks and got hacking!

What it does

spect is an elegant photo voting app. Users are able to log in, upload photos into an existing category or create their own, vote on others' photos, and view profiles of other members.

How we built it

We used the Node.js Express framework to create it as well as Google Cloud Services.
