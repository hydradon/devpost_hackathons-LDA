Inspiration

There are a lot of interesting data sources out there that are relevant to real estate that haven't been explored too well yet, this looks into that more.

What it does

Uses commercial building contract data, sentiment analysis on reddit, and crime data to display a visualization in the form of a heat map, and used them as features to feed into an ML model to predict future trends.
