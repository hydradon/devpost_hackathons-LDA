Inspiration

After playing Tennis for years, we realized that it was often hard for the coaches to tell students what they need to work on. Usually, when my coach told me some exercises that I needed to do, or some areas of improvement I needed to work on, and when the important games were, I would often forget them. Now, with Ludus, we can track our progress.

What it does

Ludus is a unique, athletic-centered app which enables the both the coach and the athlete to communicate on order for a better experience in training and competitions.

How I built it

We designed the UI using Sketch 3, and we made an Android and iOS version of the app, using Android Studio and xCode, respectively.
