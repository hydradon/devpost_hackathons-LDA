Inspiration

One of our team mates is a Food Science major, so we used her knowledge of how food should be properly made and our dislike for on campus food options to create this website.

What it does

It allows students to leave anonymous reviews for each of the dining facilities around campus. It also allows students to view previous reviews.

How I built it

We tried to make an app. That failed, so we moved to a website.
