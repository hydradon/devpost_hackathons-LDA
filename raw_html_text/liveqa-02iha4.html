Inspiration

Most of the community leaders are struggling with an issue - members in their groups do not engage a lot in their group. It's astonishing in some communities that 80% of the members actually do not post anything. We think it's a critical problem for those leaders since they're so dedicated to operate their communities but do not get enough feedback. That's why we'd love to provide a solution that can highly boost the engagement in the community.

What it does

We provide a Messenger chat extension that helps people in the community increase engagement by doing live Q&A.

How we built it

We use Messenger API to create a chat extension and leverage Live API to create the video streaming inside the chat extension. Also, we use Facebook Login to allow users to create live Q&A session.

Challenges we ran into

It's our first time to use Live API. We spent over 2 hours studying and testing it.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We made it!

What we learned

We can do anything within 20 hours.

What's next for LiveQA

We will launch this Messenger chat extension and make it featured and allows 1.3 billion people to do live Q&A within the group chat.
