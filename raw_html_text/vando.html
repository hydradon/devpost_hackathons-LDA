vando

Globally one third of the food produced gets wasted every year, increasing the carbon emissions at one hand and increasing the hungry population at the other. Our idea is to address this problem through a peer to peer food sharing application. Further our app includes the clothes sharing and 'anything else' sharing. Food and cloth sharing will be free and anything else category can be charged.
