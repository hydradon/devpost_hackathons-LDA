Inspiration

There aren't any apps which simply just let you take notes on Android.

What it does

It lets your take notes in three different ways: bulleted lists, numbered lists, and plain text (no formatting).

How I built it

Natively on Android using Material Design specifications.

Challenges I ran into

Using the design support libraries from Google.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The way the app looks and how simple it is.

What I learned

Material Design in Android and more design Android.

What's next for Notes

Release on the Play Store soon and keep it simple.
