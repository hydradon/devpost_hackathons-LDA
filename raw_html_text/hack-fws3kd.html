Inspiration

Currently rap battles are very popular, so we decided to make something mobile, convenient, interesting and available for everyone

What it does

It's a game of poems: you have to pick up a line from previously chosen words, then you opponent has to make up a rhyming line from any words. The turn ends, the roles swap.
