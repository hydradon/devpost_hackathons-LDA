"If you got a problem, yo I'll solve it" -Vanilla Ice

When we saw T-mobile's three challenges, we were prepared to tackle one, perhaps two. We don't know what happened, but we woke up on Sunday morning and behold, all tasks were completed, and stunningly so.

Parking problems son? We slapped some camera's in your west parking lot and tracked cars using computer vision. The bad part? It works a little too well. Twilio is set up on our server to text you when lots fill up, and show lots that are not chock full.

People are reserving meeting rooms and not showing up? Say no more. Along with reserving rooms through text messaging, we trained Alexa to sit in a conference room and require employees to verbally "check in", or the conference shows up as empty on our handy dandy...

Monitoring tool. It sounds lame, but it's 3d and tracks people around the office. It can show open conference rooms and provides an overhead view of crowded areas of an office.

We are still diggging through the github as I write this, so we might find some more stuff we conjured up last night.
