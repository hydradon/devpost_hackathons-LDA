Inspiration

We wanted to make an app that promoted fitness and had some fun graphics (of cute dogs).

What it does

It tracks your steps and converts them into coins. Every hour you lose coins because you are feeding your pet. If you don't get enough steps, the dog gets mad.

How we built it

We used Android Studio (java).

Challenges we ran into

We were unable to incorporate APIs successfully

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Our graphics incorporations are great!

What we learned

We learned how to build an app in under 8 hours!

What's next for Steps for Dogs!

We want to be able to allow the user to spend their coins on fun features for their dog!
