Inspiration

My background is in sales and marketing. I notice that our client facing team only practices their pitch during actual client situations vs beforehand. Most sales people don't like to pitch to another team make and are too nervous to practice a pitch with their manager. They need a low risk way to practice selling.

What it does

This tool will simulate various encounters with clients, in this case selling someone a TV, but the use case can be very broad.

How I built it

I will leverage the tools provided in AWS in particularly Sumerian to simulate the environment and Amazon Lex to simulate human interaction.
