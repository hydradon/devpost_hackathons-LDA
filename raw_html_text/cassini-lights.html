Inspiration

What it does

The lights dance to the music, change based on your heartbeat and surrounding lights.

How we built it

Using a network of optical fiber network cables connected to Neopixels (controlled by an Arduino)

Challenges we ran into

Raspberry Pi not connecting to secured internet

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned
