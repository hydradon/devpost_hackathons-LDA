miss-direction

Get CLOSE to where you want to go.

MissDirection is a mapping engine that navigates you to a random location within a few blocks of your destination.

What it does

It's a Chrome extension that redirects attempts to get to Google Maps to a spoof that operates similarly to Google Maps, but instead of taking you to where you want to go, it takes you CLOSE by. It's misdirection by redirection. Get there and really be "only a few blocks away".
