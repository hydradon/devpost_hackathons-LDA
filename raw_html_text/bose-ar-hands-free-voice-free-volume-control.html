Inspiration

The inspiration for this app came when I was trying to listen to music and safely workout outside by maintaining awareness.

What it does

We use the multitude of sensors provided by the Bose Frames to allow users to control music and maintain awareness while doing activities

How we built it

Together, all three of us had close to zero experience with Android development and Java so we had a steep learning curve. We broke it down into pieces with some of us understanding the SDK, java and building the initial android app.

Challenges we ran into

Not knowing java, android development or anything about Bose Frame sensors.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud that we were able to control the phones volume settings through the headset

What we learned

We learned how to implement the Bose AR SDK with an android app. We also learned how to build an android app. None of us had previous experience in these areas. We also learned about the different sensors involved in spatial sensing

What's next for BOSE AR hands free, voice free volume control

Implementation in more AR devices
