Inspiration

I was inspired by the spaceX hyperloop contest. I realized that by making these great ideas open sourced, we can gather the creativity and expertise of a huge range of people to refine a certain idea to its highest potential.

What it do

It allows any organization or individual to list an idea they believe to be important. People can then enter design competitions for this idea, and the host selects a winner.

How I built it

I built it using internet resources and html
