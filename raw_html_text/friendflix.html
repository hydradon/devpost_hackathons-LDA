FriendFlix

FriendFlix adds a social aspect to Netflix. Sign in is through Facebook and it will show all of your friends. You will have two new lists at the top of Netflix, "Friend's Favs" and "Recommended by your friends". You can also recommend movies to your friends. If you friend doesn't have FriendFlix added to chrome yet, then they will receive a facebook message that allows them to install the extension and join the fun.

We will be hosting an informational website at http://www.getfriendflix.com/
