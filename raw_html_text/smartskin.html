We wanted to build a software for users to upload pictures of their pigmented skin lesions and get an automated diagnosis of the potential skin disease they might be having, and so that they could make an informed decision on the treatment procedure. We used the Google Cloud AutoML Vision to train a neural network from 10,000 labeled skin lesions pictures, reaching a precision that exceeds 90%.
