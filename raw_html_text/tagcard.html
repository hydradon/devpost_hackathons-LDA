Inspiration

Network security has always been a severe social problem. We decided to develop an app that stores everyone's basic information and tags into a single QR code.

What it does

Stores basic information and your tags into a QR code. The product can prevent people from socially-awkward by scanning others QR code to quickly get to know each other.
