Inspiration

Due to the recent lack of funding for research, we decided to cut out the middleman and create a way for scientists and contributors to work together

What it does

Power Science lets Scientists post their research and contributors to view the research in means to fund the projects posted. This will allow he future of research to continue on and push science to new heights.

How we built it

Tis site was built using weebly and html, both to customize the site to how we want it to be.

Challenges we ran into

trying to implement a login page using html, due to this we decided to use weebly as our host to help us with this.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of the idea that we thought of to create an interface that links Scientists directly to Contributors.

What we learned

We learned how to make and the difficulties that are involved in making a website as creative as one would like.

What's next for Power Science

We want to get in touch with companies that are willing to fund Power Science and take it to the next level.
