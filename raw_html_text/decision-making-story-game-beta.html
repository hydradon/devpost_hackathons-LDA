Inspiration

Often, great ideas start with small creations. We wanted to apply java and test our programming skills to create a simple program that would enable us to grow and solve more meaningful issues in the future.

What it does

This is a single player game where the player decides between two options and try to survive the horror game the longest.

How we built it

Acknowledging the unique skill sets of our team, we were able to divide the work up effectively and plan our programs efficiently.

Challenges

Challenges we ran into include deciding on a project idea to implement, fatigue in the night, and technical issues regarding minor problems.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of our versatility, team-working skills, and resilience despite fatigue and long working hours. Our learning abilities made us proud as well.

What we learned

We learned how the stages of project development and how to work under time pressure.

What's next for Decision Making Story Game Beta

Making the game more complete with more logic, customization, and smoother graphics will be coming up next.
