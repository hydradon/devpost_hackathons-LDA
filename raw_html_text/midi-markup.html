Inspiration

After listening to talks at SXGood about accessibility and technology, we decided to make a music web app that is accessible to seeing-impaired users.

What it does

MIDI Markup is a music markup language and synthesizer for visually impaired composers. It is simpler than other music markup languages and has a better interface with audio ques for seeing-impaired users.
