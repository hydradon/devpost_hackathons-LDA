Inspiration

Women are usually the minority group in CS classes and because of this it might be scary to ask questions. We decided to make an app that gives women a platform to ask their fellow female classmates questions.

What it does

Since we were given only three hours to build it we decided to come up with a Sketch prototype. It enables women to search for their school and then click on the classes that they are in. After that, they will be able to post questions and have their fellow female classmates answer them. They will also be given the opportunity to aid their female classmates.

How we built it

We used Sketch to build this.

Challenges we ran into

We did not know the right prototyping tool to use and since we did not have much time it was starting to get overwhelming. However, we pushed through and figured out how to use Sketch.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of coming up with an MVP.

What we learned

We learned how to use Sketch and the intricacies of UX Design.

What's next for Icebreaker

We are planning to make Icebreaker an iOS app.
