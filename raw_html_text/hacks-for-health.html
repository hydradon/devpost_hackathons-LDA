Inspiration

We wanted to explore Apple's native functions like HealthKit and because health is a big issue on many people's minds we wanted to do something to help them develop a healthier lifestyle.

What it does

Analyzes existing data from HealthKit (ex. weight, height) to calculate different measurements like BMI, TDEE, etc. and based on those results, gives you personalized suggestions to improve your health. Also saves data like height/weight every time unless you update it in HealthKit so you don't have to input it every time.
