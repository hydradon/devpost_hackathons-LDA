Welcome to our program! Our group decided to do our project on JPMorgan's Social Good hack topic. Our goal is to create an app that will help people in need, homeless people, and even teenagers to acquire easy one time jobs to earn a little bit of money on the side. This would be beneficial because it motivates people in need and also teenagers to work hard in order to finish more jobs and recieve a better rating to acquire more jobs. This app allows people in need of extra cash to pick up easy and simple side jobs that some people might think would be too lazy or inconvenient to do. Each person, job poster and job seeker, are also matched with their own personal rating that is determined by the people they have worked with. If we have the opportunity to expand this app, we would make the interface cleaner, add a map that displays jobs near by the user, and create a account system that verifies each person using the app.
