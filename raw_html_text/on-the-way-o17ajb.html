Everybody has this one thing that needs to get done somewhen...

For example buying Nails at the hardware shop to put this picture on the Wall lying around for weeks now, it doesn’t need to be done today, but it needs to be done.

This is mostly annoying because you need to leave the comfort of your daily-routine, you have to figure out where the next hardware shop is located from your home.

What people tend to forget while making this decision is that they are already on the way for most of the time, we have regular trips, we go to work, we go to the gym, we go to see friends, maybe it is much easier to get to the hardware shop on the way of your regular trips. But this is hard to find out.... on the way! will help you with that.

On the Way! is an app which helps saving time and nerves, here’s how we do it:

We gather your regular trip information to make us smart. Once we have this, we are happy to hear about your special trips you have and tell you when to do it the fastest way. No more searching around in maps, we know where you are on your daily basis, we know where you want to go exceptionally with the help of google places and other services we even know the opening times of these place.

Just tell us where, we will get you there.
