Inspiration

We wanted a simple and effective way to do our English homework faster.

What it does

T3xt is a word descrambler. That means that you can enter a string of characters and it will tell you if they make a word.

How I built it

To create T3xt, we used Python.

Challenges I ran into

The challenge my partner and I came across while creating T3xt was to eliminate permutations that were not in the English dictionary.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

After finishing the project with getting the result that we expected my partner and I were overwhelmed by a feeling of joy.

What I learned

Python is more than capable of running such a complex program and is extremely easy to learn and use.

What's next for Text de-scrambler

We think that we can sell T3xt to encryption companies after we upgrade it to break encryption codes.
