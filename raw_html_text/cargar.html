Currently, the general public face many issues while travelling in local taxis & vans. Taxi rates are not fixed and local drivers charge as much as the want. People cannot report problems because there is no such system where complaints can be registered against the local cabs. Some systems which exist are very expensive. The female community is specially affected by the transport services. They spend a lot of their income on transport expenses. In addition to that, they don’t feel secure while travelling in public transport.

We aim to provide the best motorbike and car taxi services for the local community, especially focusing on providing safe, affordable and efficient taxi service.

This service is the solution to most of the problems mentioned above. Since the drivers will be operating under a company so there is a little chance of any problem due to continues checks. In case there is any problem it could be addressed properly.

Service will be kept economic as compare to most of the competitors in the market. In addition to this, fixed rate system will be the biggest convenience for the customer as they no longer need to negotiate the price.

For this, our target customers will be students and working community who travel daily, don't have their own transport and no alternate solution other then vans and car taxis. Car taxis are expensive, with no standard rates while the vans are not convenient and appropriate for travelling.

We are addressing the problems mentioned above by providing the cheap, convenient and efficient alternate of car taxi and vans.
