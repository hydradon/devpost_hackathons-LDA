Inspiration As students we face time management problems every day. This app automise the scheduling process and helps people focus on their task. This app would be useful for small companies as well.

What it does It schedules tasks in an optimal way and adds websites to a blacklist to prevent the user to access them during the task.

How I built it Using Ionic and Cordova, and HTML/CSS, Javascript, Typsecript and Angular.

Challenges I ran into Including the Twilio API, we thought of notifying the user before the next task. Unfortunately me did not manage to implement it due to lack of resources and conflicting packages.

What's next for CalHack Including more features such as Twilio and cool graphics.
