We wanted to build an app that would give us a good intro to mobile development.

It attempts to change someone's mood based on what they want to feel and what they really feel

We built it through android studio

The challenges that we've faced were mostly aesthetics. We also had a lot of trouble writing specific tasks because we had limited prior knowledge.

We are proud that we have a running app.

We learned a lot of things including how to use the Android sdk and what it takes to write an Android application.

What's next for Da Feelz.... Not much for the app; however, each of us will most likely go work on different projects on our own.
