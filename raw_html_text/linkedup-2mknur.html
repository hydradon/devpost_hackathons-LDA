Inspiration

Our inspiration was valentines day combined with the fact that most dating applications are now apps and not websites. We wanted to make a website for our generation to find people who are similarly goal oriented.

What it does

Matches people based on age and what they are looking for, then displays information similarly to those on LinkedIn to show each individual the other's credentials like career, education, a resume, etc...

How we built it

Notepad++ and HTML!

Challenges we ran into

None of us had ever tried coding before Saturday, so that was difficult, but we had a lot of fun learning and can't wait to learn more!

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The entirety of LinkedUP is our accomplishment!! We are proud of how far we got and how much we learned considering none of us have ever coded before.

What we learned

How to use HTML, other languages of coding, different components of building a website or other application, and opportunities within coding.

What's next for LinkedUP

Working on creating a "back" to our website and a database so the information entered actually goes somewhere...then making wonderful matches for young and old professionals alike everywhere.
