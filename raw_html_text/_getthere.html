Inspiration

A real pain that affects our lives on a daily basis.

What it does

It gives you the optimal way to get to your destination based on certain criteria.

How we built it

We identified the problem and tried to fix it only by pressing a button.

Challenges we ran into

Very 'well' documented libraries and frameworks. Different kinds of non backwards compatibility in technologies. Understanding highly complicated mathematical concepts (such as Particle Swarm Optimisation)

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Modelling a process that affects all of us in a manner that allows you to manipulate it.

What we learned

It's easier to create a Front-End that catches the eye than to create a backend that simplifies the life of all of our users.

UI != UX

What's next for _getThere

Collecting the data we need in order for our optimisation system to facilitate the lives of our users.
