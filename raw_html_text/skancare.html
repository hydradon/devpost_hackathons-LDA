Inspiration

Skin lesions are difficult to diagnose and often simply overlooked. Skancare aims to bring awareness to skin ailments by utilizing machine learning to classify skin lesions.

Using Skancare

1) Upload a close-up image of skin
2) Submit the image, and wait for Skancare to analyze it

Challenges we ran into

• Learning how to train and deploy a model with AutoML Vision

Accomplishments that we're proud of

• Great coordination while working on different parts of the project

What's next for Skancare

• Using a larger dataset with more labels
• Displaying more statistical information
