LangBot

Uses Telegram, Wit.ai, Google Translate (with the help of RapidAPI), Twitter API, and Youtube Data API. Ask for the meanings of words/phrases, or ask for an example usage of a word taken from Twitter. You can also search for topic-specific tweets to further personalize the learning process, or even find a video in the language of your choice in a topic of interest.

Overall the goal of this project is to make it easier to find how foreign languages are used in more natural settings.

Queries must be structured in English, though thanks to the Google Translate API you can actually translate between any two languages.
