Inspiration

We make the head-tie frame on behave of those who love Head-tie But don’t have access to it in reality,

What it does

There are varies of head-tie which user can choose the color and styles of head -tie they want and take a nice picture or video with it

How we built it

We got the head-tie from Google image and it was edited with photoshop and Corel draw then we make use of the face-tracer section on our Spark Ar to find the face and we use the plane to position the head-tie to the position where it fit in to the head

Challenges we ran into

We ran into a lot of challenges when we are trying to make the UI button that is used to switch and pick the type of head-tie the user want to take a picture with and also after we finished the project we tested it on our mobile spark At player and we found out that the button are sticking to the picture after we click the caption button,so we have to go extra mile to find solution to that which took us a bit time to get that fixed

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of getting the project done and perfect after allot of discouraging issues coming up along the line of getting and implementing our idea into action

What we learned

We learnt a lot on how Spark At works and it can be used for apart from the frameworks and masks
