Were you ever annoyed when you have to physically place your phone or computer by the speakers whenever you want to blast some music? Or when you open an app and the music brutally cuts off?

We know. We understand.

Which is why we built Jukebox: a handy portable device that streams music from your playlist!

Hit play and let's get groovy.

Jukebox

Standalone Raspberry Pi based music player for parties/events

Goal

Our aim is to create a device for integrating tunes into infrastructure audio environments like classroom/auditorium presentation setups.

Tools

  • Raspberry Pi 3
  • python
  • GrovePin
  • LCD
  • Touch sensors
