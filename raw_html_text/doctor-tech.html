Inspiration

Our family members inspired us. Our grandparents had trouble several times in their lives trying to receive their prescription. So we decided this was the perfect business as an ode to them.

What it does

Doctor Tech as a medical app allows you to order medication, receive a possible diagnosis and in times of urgent need it will send a signal to a local EMS. It allows you to store your medical records in a blockchain which is hard to hack and will allow the patients to control who has access to their records.

How we built it

We used slidebean.com and google slides to develop our business presentation. We used visual studio to make our website.

Challenges we ran into

Budget and statistics

Accomplishments that we're proud of

To complete our business plan on a high note and to feel happy about what we have completed.

What we learned

That you cannot take this lightly. Also, that you have to do your research and must work as a team.

What's next for Doctor Tech

We plan to partner with Aliv, advance our app and to make it international.
