Inspiration

Students are constantly losing their belongings on campus, yet there is no dedicated platform for them to recover these items. They must turn to unorganised email chains and Facebook posts to alert others of their missing items and hope for the safe return of these items.

What it does

Register your lost items in our app, and receive notifications when potential matched items are found by others!

How we built it

iOS mobile app with Swift and Firebase.

Challenges we ran into

Without Apple Developer Account, we have issues testing our app in different laptops.
