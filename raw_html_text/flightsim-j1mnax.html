Inspiration

Being a pilot had always fascinated me and when I saw the opportunity to merge flight with my love for technology, I jumped on the opportunity.

What it does

Fli let's anyone feel like they are a pilot by giving them a First Person View(FPV) from the drone through the Oculus Rift, and letting them control the drone with your hands using the Myo. Our goal is to make the controlling as natural as possible.

Challenges I ran into
