Inspiration

We had a vision to browse the Internet without the need for a keyboard, instead opting for your own eyes as the controls

What it does

It's a WebApp that puts together some websites and demos to demonstrate VR without keyboards. It essentialy redirects you to a we

How We built it

We use AFrame to construct the VR environment and display websites.

Challenges We ran into

Hardware didn't support the software that we were trying to use. Also, animations are surprisingly hard. Also, connecting scenes together was a challenge, for example linking websites to objects within the VR environment.

Accomplishments that We're proud of

Our accomplishments that we have done together as a team have made us stronger.

What We learned

WebVR is hard. Unity is not always reliable. Monitors can do weird things.
