• Gameplay of Help Me I Hate You

Inspiration

The Keyword in the game jam was "resurrection". This was originally a prototype but turned into a short "finished" game.

What it does

A co-op shooter where you resurrect your allies who help you defeat enemies.

How I built it

We came up with all kinds of features and tested them in Construct 2. In the end we made one game in Unity called Curepitsa and this one in Construct.

Challenges I ran into

It's hard to make polished work in such a short time.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Everyone got to do things they wanted and whole team could discuss together.

What I learned

Game Jamming is more about realizing what ideas are good and less about creating a lot of content in a short amount of time.

What's next for Help Me I Hate You

A few updates to fix bugs. It'll stay in Scirra Arcade where people can play it.
