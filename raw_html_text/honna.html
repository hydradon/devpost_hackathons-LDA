Inspiration

1- Increasing sports awareness among women in Egypt.

2- Empower women participation in sports.

3- Achieving gender equality.

What it does

Honna is a Social Platform that aims to Connect, Mentor, and Empower Women in Sports. Our app has 3 main features:

  1. Connecting nearby women interested in the same sport.
  2. Raising awareness about sports using our educational feature.
  3. Empowering women athletes in improving their income.

How we built it

Using a cross-platform SDK (Flutter).

Challenges we ran into

  1. Lack of statistics about women in sports.
  2. Too many subpages to code in a short time.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

  1. Getting a brand new innovative idea in less than 3 days.
  2. Finishing the storyboard of our idea.

What we learned

  1. Learning a new cross-platform SDK (Flutter).
  2. How to manage teamwork in an effective way.

What's next for هُنَّ - Honna

Our goal is to release our app soon in the market, starting with an MVP.
