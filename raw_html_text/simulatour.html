Banyuwangi is one of the most famous city in Indonesia that has many Tourist Attraction. Banyuwangi provides beautiful yet meaningful journey. many kind of beaches, park, mountain that can cover the needs of tourist. Every district offer different kind of experience that can be choosen by tourist. Kecamatan Licin is one of Banyuwangi's Sub District that provides many kind of Tourist attraction. Kecamatan Licin is very potential in term of tourism. Kecamatan Licin have become one of the most wanted destinatination in Banyuwangi.

detailed information about destination attractions such as accommodation and transport is very important for a tourist. This information helps them to make the holidays run smoothly and the goals they want from the holidays can be achieved. Tourists need a platform that can facilitate the search for information about destination attractions and help them prepare for needs such as budget.

This application provides several features that can help tourists to find information and simulate the type of vacation they want. The first feature of this mobile application is trip simulation, this feature can help tourist to find and simulate the trip they want according to their needs. They can easily arrange the trip that suitable with their budget. This feature can also help the tourist to find the accomodation to support their trip by giving information about the accomodation. The accomodation information that can be provided are restaurant, homestay, hotel, and many other facilities.

The pilot project of this application is focused on Kecamatan Licin's Tourist attraction and can be expanded for further development.
