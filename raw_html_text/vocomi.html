Welcome to VoCoMi, the Voice Controlled Mixer! What would you like me to do next?

This is how our hack greets you. Play music with a purely voice and gesture-driven interface using Nuance TTS, speech recognition and speech understanding technologies (with a custom, overnight coded Voice Activity Detector), and the MYO armband.

Our hack lets you navigate sound sample collections with voice, and then compose a loop using gestures.

The video demos speak for themselves!
