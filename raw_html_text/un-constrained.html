Inspiration

What it does

Recreation of the scene of the accident to help emergency response allocate resources and priority level. Reliable collection and forecasting of weather data. Analysis of the collected data using VR.

How I built it

The sensor data was collected from the Nokia influx database and The VR headset was used for visualization.
