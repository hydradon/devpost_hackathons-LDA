Inspiration

The age old problem: do you ask out that person that you happen to be friends with or not? Humans aren't rational and its hard to measure risk accurately. Our game theory calculator takes care of such hard decisions for you.

What it does

Determines whether or not you should ask a friend out on a date. Read about more of the math here: https://neil-sehgal.github.io/ProjectEDEN/odo.html

How we built it

Simple combination of javascript, HTML/CSS, and game theory calculations.

Challenges we ran into

We didn't know how to use javascript and barely knew any HTML/CSS. As a result, our code is very janky.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We think the final product looks pretty nice!

What's next for Project EDEN

Applying game theory to solve even more problems
