Blind

You have been kidnapped. Having woken up in a dungeon with no recollection of recent memory, you notice that everything is a hazy monochrome. Little did you know: you've been robbed of color! Solve puzzles and gather pills that will slowly but surely regain your full sight, but is seeing the whole picture really what you wish for? Find out in this horror based retro point and click game!
