Inspiration

The main idea is to help people sell their products on a popular pages, instead of contacting the page admin to submit their product, they can use this bot to easily sell their products on the pages. and peoples can browse categorised products or search it.

What it does

Listing new products to sell, browse all product that peoples sell's on the pages using bot, search products, and share it on facebook timeline.

How I built it

1- Defining the main problem that new seller commonly faced when they try to sell their products. 2- Help peoples with same interest to connect, buy and sell on pages using bots.

Challenges I ran into
