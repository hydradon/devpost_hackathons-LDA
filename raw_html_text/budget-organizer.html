Inspiration

We wanted to help the residents of NB better understand where their money is going. Understanding our parents' frustrations with tax money, it would help them and other residents be more aware of their town's spendings. We saw how bleak and unreadable the current New Brunswick Budget PDF was, so we decided to take it upon ourselves to rennovate the display of info.

What it does

It shows all of the different places the money is spent and how much. Areas where more money is spent also has a detailed graph with subcategories of spending. It allows residents of New Brunswick to see where the money is spent.
