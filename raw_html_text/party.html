Inspiration

We were driving from Tijuana to Ensenada for this hackathon and once we got here we decided to go check out some parties on the first night but didn't know where to go or what events/parties where happening that night. This same issue has happened to us while on vacation, visiting friends at other cities and even in our own city during weekends when we can't find something cool to do. We decided to work on a project that could be both fun for the weekend and usable afterwards.

What it does

Party lets you find parties near you as well as share your own party publicly so other people can join and check out things to do that night.

How I built it

Party is an iOS app built with Objective C and Parse.

What I learned

Design a quick release mvp.

What's next for Party

Release mvp, get feedback, launch beta and promote.
