Daily Technomancer Mobile

Features:

-Uses the WordPress API to get articles from DailyTechnomancer.com.

-Displays the articles in a list; the format in which they are displayed can be customized by the user.

-Has options with which to manipulate articles and how they appear.

-Has Settings that the user may change to customize the way in which they want the app to function. Some customizable settings include: How often the app updates the data. How the app notifies you when a new article is posted.
