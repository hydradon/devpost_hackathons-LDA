Trash Trekker

A trash map for a beautiful city

Trash collecting is an inefficient process. To naively sweep an entire area for trash is a big waste of time, as the "brute force" path might use more time than another optimized path. Our idea is to use drones/ml to map out trash, and from there we can map the best path(traveling salesman)
