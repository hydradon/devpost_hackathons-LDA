Inspiration

Being at Superposition II, an all girls hackathon really supports and empowers girls who want to explore or continue a career in tech.

What it does

Our app is also based on female empowerment and centers around supporting and inspiring other women. Our app has feminist quotes, posts about female empowerment events, inspiring stories, female empowerment events, and political news.

How we built it

We have both a website and a prototype. We used HTML and CSS for the website and Justinmind to create the prototype of an iOS app.

Challenges we ran into

Originally, we wanted to code the iOS app with swift, but it turned out to be more difficult than we expected for beginners.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Our layout and features are really good actually. The app and website are both easy to use and have a really good design.

What we learned

We learned HTML and many different features when prototyping.

What's next for Already Perfect

We want to add more features to the app and website, including like, commenting, and saving features. We also want to link the news and and events to articles on the web.
