Inspiration

To save the money in the flourish app with your friends. --Set saving goals --Save for what matters to you --You choose how much to save. Building the habit is more important than the amount! --Save to earn Flourish coins --Earn coins by saving money and growing your balance --The more you save, the more coins you get!

Engagement of user: The user needs to invite friends and start saving with their friends else invite friends to the account.

The motivation for Saving:

One study found that 95 percent of those who started a weight-loss program with friends completed the program, compared to a 76 percent completion rate for those who tackled the program alone. The friend group was also 42 percent more likely to maintain their weight loss. Savings with friends will always push the limits.
