Inspiration

Finding a gift is a common problem, this website has the concept of finding it.

What it does do

You enter interests, and the search engine will list the top 10 recommendations.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Establishing the website to look presentable.

What I learned

Learned that its better to be prepared when entering into hackathons

What's next for GiftOasis

Development will continue when our team members are more experience with web development.
