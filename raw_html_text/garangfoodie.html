Inspiration

People might buy a lot of food and forget to consume them or they cannot finish eating before the deadline.

What it does

Registered users can share their unwanted food or trade food (?); Targeted for lazy users - they can just take photos of the food and our app will magically generate relevant tags for the food items.

How we built it

React native. Also used Computer Vision API to make it look cool

Challenges we ran into

Debugging React Native Applications is notoriously difficult. WiFi connection issues resulting in hours wasted trying to debug issues which was actually caused by the poor WiFi. Had a hard time dealing with Firebase Storage on React Native

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Learning about React Native and its difficult parts.

What we learned

React Native and Firebase

What's next for GarangFoodie

Make the app more complete by adding count down to expiry date and GPS location for exchanging food / giving food in the vicinity.
