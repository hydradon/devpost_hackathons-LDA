Inspiration

2 hours of thinking.

What it does

Peanut the Meme Collector tries to collect memes in a limited amount of time. One he collects a meme, the meme sticks on him. There are also boundaries that he should avoid from.

How I built it

After deciding on the project, learning from the tutorials, we implemented our idea to Unity. It took literally 24 hours.

Challenges I ran into

Time constraint definitely. Lack of knowledge, and having to go through tutorials.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We didn't know anything about Unity yesterday. Now, we built a game!

What I learned

Unity, C#, being a team member and coordination.

What's next for Peanut the Meme Collector

  • We will a high score screen popping up after 60 seconds.
  • Walls will be added randomly in the screen that the Peanut King cannot go through.
  • Forever mode (as long as you don't touch a wall or meme that is not bigger than you, you live.)
  • Random generation of memes.
