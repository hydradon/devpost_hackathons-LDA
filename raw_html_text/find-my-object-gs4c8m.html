Inspiration

I lost my bag somewhere. I need to solve this problem of tracking objects without any device.

What it does

This app will Take your name and mobile number to generate a card. You can print that card and stick to your obejcts. In case you lose your object , if somebody find the object , He/she will see the card and scan the card. When he/she will scan the card , The app will be downloaded or loaded in the mobile and that will tell tell the name and number of the owner . Also the app will have a call button to call the owner.

How I built it

I built this app using swift with xcode to build ios app. The app uses frameworks to generate qr code and the code is saved to photos , The app is having a qr code scanner which will scan the details in the code.

Challenges I ran into

The challage was to make it simple to use , For this i used the idea of QR code .

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

This app is working without any network authuntication or login.

What I learned

I learned to manage the project in short time frame

What's next for Find My Object

The app will export pdf file to user and also to directly know the owner with photo.
