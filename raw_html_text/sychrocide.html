An experimental cyberpunk couch co-op area game where you share the controller.

Two players control two characters each. Parallel universes have collided. Kill your opposite past self to secure your future.

Catch your opponent's past self (circle) with your future self (diamond), and avoid yours getting caught! Can you shape the future in your image by committing SYNCHROCIDE?
