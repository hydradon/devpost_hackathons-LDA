Inspiration

We know that a lot of teens look at memes when they are stressed out with school, life, or life in general, so we built this in order to have a collection of memes for teens to relate to based on what they're in the mood for.

What it does

It generates a random meme under each category, and the page can be refreshed for a new meme under the same category.

How we built it

HTML and CSS, and a little bit of Javascript.

Challenges we ran into

None of us knew Javascript, so we had to learn it on our own. We also had little or no experience with HTML and CSS.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud that we were able to make it a random generator, and that the website itself works.

What we learned

We learned how to create random memes per page and how to incorporate a button to refresh the page to reload a random meme.

What's next for Relatable Memes for Sad Teens

Adding more memes to our website, so that there will be more memes in our random meme generator, which will create an more enjoyable experience for our users.
