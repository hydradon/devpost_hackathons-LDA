Inspiration

Two of the guys in our group are former IDF soldiers, and they understand how difficult it can be. There are so many times when they want to give up, and don't get the support they need.

What it does

It gives a twitter, facebook, and instagram feed of supportive messages for our soldiers, and also gives a place to donate money to various causes, and read inspirational stories about other soldiers.

How we built it

Using texteditor, and learning more and more about HTML and CSS

Challenges we ran into

We didn't have much of a background in either, so it was particularly difficult to find a way to make it work, but we did it.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Having a working final project

What we learned

How difficult learning new things can be, but also how much we can learn and accomplish in a short amount of time

What's next for IDFSupportStream

Add some new features, improve the facebook feed, and get some more stories.
