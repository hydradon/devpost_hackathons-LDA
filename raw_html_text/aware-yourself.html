Mental health is an issue that should be on all our minds since it affects everyone. Unfortunately, due to sociocultural factors and the stigma around mental illness, many people are unaware of their condition or are afraid to seek treatment. In honor of the recent Mental Health Awareness Day, we decided to make it easier for people to evaluate their own risk for common mental health problems and find treatment options nearby.
