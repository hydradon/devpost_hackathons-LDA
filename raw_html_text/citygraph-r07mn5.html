Inspiration

Transparency is one of the great hopes for regaining confidence in politics and political decisions. We try to reduce chaos in the jungle of paper proposals and council decisions.

What it does

Citizens can follow the progress and development of proposals in the city council of Munich and understand which decisions are made when and where.

How we built it

With Flask, MongoDB and "homemade" Python-Crawler.

Challenges we ran into

Invalid HTML documents.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Developed an idea, concept and technical solution during the HackaTUM 2018 in Munich.

What we learned

How to deal with invalid HTML documents. And Jan-Ulrich Holtgrave a bit of Flask.

What's next for Citygraph

Making Citygraph a scalable, robust and modern solution to visualize decisions and progress in local politics.
