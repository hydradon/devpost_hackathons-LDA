RIseFaceDetect

Face detection greeting for Rise Manchester, StudentHackIV project. We wanted to create a cool and easy way for people to enjoy your stay at Rise Manchester.

What it does

Getting a camera stream it detects a face and matches it against its user database. It pulls up relevant information while checking you in e.g. where your friends are if they checked in and which rooms are free.
