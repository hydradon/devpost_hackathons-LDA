In-flight assistant

We created an app that allows travelers to submit their travel preferences before they even take-off. The app gives the option to pre-order meals and snacks, select a drink preference, and indicate if they would like to be woken if asleep when food comes. Our hope is to integrate the app with Google firebase, and create a companion app for use by flight attendants to easily view the passenger information.
