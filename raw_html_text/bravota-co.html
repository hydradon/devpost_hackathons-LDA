Inspiration

Everybody loves Tacos, we wanted to get word out about the ones we like, using a catchy URL.

What it does

Bravo Ta.co is a site that displays our favorite taco establishments, allowing the visitor to visit their respective websites and discover the magic of these tacos themselves. There is also a spot for recommendations by the visitor to expand our list.

Foundation

We used html/css to create a website.

What's next for BravoTa.co

BravoTa.co will continued to be maintained and hopefully expanded in the future to contain more popular tacos establishments! The website may be revamped in the future using our own custom code to include widgets and rating tools for visitors.
