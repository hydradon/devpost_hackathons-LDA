Inspiration

Living in a country with more than 2 millions individuals with reduced mobility and special needs whom, according to the Algerian Federation of People with Disabilities FAPH, suffer daily when it comes to spatial accessibility in both public and private spaces or while looking for an adapted means of transport. ( https://www.liberte-algerie.com/actualite/la-mobilite-spatiale-handicape-davantage-les-personnes-a-mobilite-reduite-221960 ).

What it does

We decided to work on a solution that help the inclusion of those persons with special needs by bringing them as close as possible to normal daily socials experiences. Our solution consist of a mobile app that allows people with reduced mobility to locate accessible places (both public and private) and identify a hurdle free itinerary considering the specific need for each individuals. Another important feature in our solution is the ability to find personal assistant in a really effective way by mapping volunteers and the people with special needs depending on their location.
