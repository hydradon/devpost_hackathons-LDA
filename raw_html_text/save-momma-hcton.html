You know when you are in that spot where you have to decide between two immensely important options and there is no way of deciding which is more important. And you end up hurting both your chances. Wow me too... so werid!!!

The funny thing is that computers are so good at this. They just need a weight for the situation quantified digitally and voualla, Decisions. Our app figures out where would an important piece of resource be so that we could get the best out of it location wise.

Think of it this way. Like ambulances are very scare in my country. There are a valuable piece of resource.

We need to decide which way there need to go when multiple calls are coming in. This is where the Humans choke up. We cant condemn another human to die so we rather both die and we be free of the guilt. Hence the computers. What if this decision can be made ahead of time. if we could track the demands of the ambulance we would already be on the way to the best chance of survival candidate. Maybe we even save time and rescue the others as well.

Hence our project in a nutshell. It is called Teresa because of Mother Teresa and we started out optimizing ambulances for mothers to be in labor. Then we pivoted to the broader idea. But not forgetting where we started WE VOW that if indeed this service becomes consolidated we will provide it to mothers to be in need especially In developing countries.
