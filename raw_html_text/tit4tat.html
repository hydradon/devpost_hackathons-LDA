What it does

Tit4tat is an easy, safe, and fun way to exchange your stuff locally. You can exchange anything you want such as video games, movies, parking areas even food and much more.

• Snap a photo and offer up your items in seconds • Use GPS to find thousands of listings within your neighborhood • Make an offer by sending a text • Update your post and send it to the top of the list • Facebook verifications • Messaging system hides your contact info • Tit4Tat doesn't share your information with anyone • Discover great stuff nearby by browsing photos • Search and filter to find relevant listing • Make offers and negotiate deals • Chat to share photos and meeting spots • Share listings to Facebook, make listing sell faster

How I built it

I built this app using Parse cloud as a backend and Trnql SDK for location services.

Challenges I ran into

Trnql really reduced the time for app developing, their super power SDK solves a lot of problems that most developers face while developing.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I never thought that I'd make any kind of apps like this, I'm so proud of it and I'm working on improving it.

What's next for Tit4Tat
