Inspiration

We were inspired by the presentation of the CakeSolutions guy (Alex i think).

What it does

It is a bell with a camera linked to it which detects your face and will do a specific action depending if it detects a person or not, and it send an email with a picture of the person in front of the door.

How we built it

Using pycharm and the opencv library.

Challenges we ran into

Finding a way to respons to your emotion, which we did not implement at the end.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud that most of the things we wanted to do we managed to achieve.

What we learned

Image processing, python, and how to work together as a team.

What's next for Future Bell

Transforming it into a product which will be in every household :) .
