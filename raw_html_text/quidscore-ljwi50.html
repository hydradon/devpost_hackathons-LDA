Inspiration

Quidditch is a hot new sport that is taking over the world. With its unique style of play, no web apps currently exist to cater to the sport's needs.

What it does

QuidScore primarily streamlines the process of scorekeeping so that any scorekeeper at a tournament can scorekeep a game simultaneously and have it all automatically linked together . It also allows the scorekeeper to keep track of the player who scored and comments regarding play -potential for stat-tracking. It also can be used as player check-in app at tournaments for it stores all the tournaments, games and teams in a database.

What's next for QuidScore

Quidditch is a growing niche market and there is great potential for its use in stat-tracking which opens doors to other ideas for apps. QuidScore could very much be the beginning of something like enterprise software but for the entire community of quidditch.
