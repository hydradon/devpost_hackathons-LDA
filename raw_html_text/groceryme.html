Inspiration

Why do people constantly change their diet? Health and environment are the most common factors. There has been a 600% increase in people going vegan for the environment, and millions of people changing their eating habits to live a healthier lifestyle. People want change. But why is it so hard?

What it does

You already have grocery list. Grocery-Me edits the list based on your personal profile.

  1. Create a personal profile: a selection of up to 29 dietary preferences
  2. Add your current grocery list
  3. "Personalize" (generate an alternative list automatically)
  4. Find relevant recipes

How We built it

We used React Native for the app development platform, allowing for both IOS and Android customers.
