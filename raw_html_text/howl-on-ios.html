• Apple Watch Design

Inspiration

The blue emergency posts are not immediately attached to you but your phone and your smartwatch are

What it does

This app runs both on your smart phone and you smart watch allowing easier access to the help you need

How I built it

Using Xcode and building it on the backbones of the newer swift language

Challenges I ran into

Updating to the Swift 2 standard and getting a fast one-to-one connection from phone to watch

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

That I was able to input a table value of emergency number from around the world

What I learned

The current method of development on the platform is very slow but there is potential for it to be like the boom of the smart phone

What's next for Howl on iOS

Keep improving on the smart watch market as it may grow due to the surge of these devices hitting the market so that help is accessed to your wrist.
