The CommUnity Angel simplifies the process of creating a social campaign for high-school and college orgs by providing all essential information and organizational tools in an easy-to-use mobile application.

The current problem for high-school and college orgs trying to market their events is there is no easy tool to gather locational, demographic, or any other additional data to help formulate a marketing strategy. Most tools are either too complicated for a general use, or they cannot provide both analytics and organizational assistance to the user.

The CommUnity Angel fixes this problem by providing all the essential information needed to run a social campaign. It helps the org track their KPI’s and overall progress for an event, create social buzz, and even provides key analytics to help improve marketing efforts in the future.

Lastly, the CommUnity Angel provides a platform for general users to find local events to get involved in the community along with access to a carpooling system and easy QR code Check-Ins.

--

The app is constructed using QML and ArcGIS AppStudio, which utilizes the ArcGIS Runtime SDK for Qt. Planned inclusions to bolster functionality is integration with the Esri tools of Survey123, Navigator, and BusinessAnalyst.
