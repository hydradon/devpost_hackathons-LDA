WE PARTICIPATE IN THREE CHALLENGES

Our submission for the IThinkUPC challenge.

It's difficult for a new student to find friends and fun activities to do in a new city. It's also pretty difficult to get your NIE, and other legal documents you're going to need, so this inspired us to come up with a solution!

We had several ideation phases. Then we designed the screens and made a functioning animated prototype that we're going to use for the demo.

We faced challenges along the way, it was the first time we're filming and editing a video, also animating some parts of the prototype were quite tricky, but we managed to finish it all in time while working on two other projects as well.

Check out our youtube video and our github page which contains the designs and the prototype!
