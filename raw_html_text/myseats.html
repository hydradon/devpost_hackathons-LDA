Inspiration

I am a season ticket holder for the 49ers and I was looking to get more data about ticket sales, as well as seat license sales. With some individual seats at Levi's Stadium selling for $250,000 per seat, the stadium started looking more like a real estate map than just a stadium. I came up with the idea to create a Zillow for seat licenses so seat owners could get an idea of what their seat is worth on the aftermarket.

What it does

Helps season ticket holders manage their tickets. Give them a real-time estimate of what their seat license is worth.

How I built it
