Inspiration

The need for identity. People anywhere could be without official identification at any time, and a thousand million people just don't have any government ID.

What it does

Provides verification of identity based on biometrics.

How we built it

Gradual database building from government agencies, ID2020 and private registrars.

Challenges we ran into

Many countries have laws that don't allow sharing their data with us.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Concept aligned with United Nations Global Sustainable Development Goals.

What we learned

Complexities and differences among different countries and laws.

What's next for ADAYA - Global Biometric Identity

Secure participation from RENIEC.
