f5-sunhacks-team: Sunhacks 2019 Season. Made by Gina Lee, Jennifer Guo, and Ethan Buranday.

We experience different kinds of responses, which are triggered by both our emotional and physical status. These statuses trigger different kinds of emotions. By answering two simple questions of how you are and how you feel, we can project an image that relates to your current feeling. With refreshingfeel.online, you can experience a new way to project and share your feelings through different images based on how you feel.
