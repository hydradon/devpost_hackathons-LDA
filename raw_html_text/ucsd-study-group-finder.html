Inspiration

Research shows that studying with your peers fosters learning and conceptual understanding and encourages creative thinking. From our first year experience, we found that it's hard to make friends in classes at UCSD.

Our app solves that!

What it does

The app matches people who are in the same classes and then by unique algorithms that match study preferences, including time of day and location.

How we built it

It's an IOS app, so we primarily used Swift to make it. For the backend and database, we used Google's Firebase. We used Google Drawing tool to create a raw framework of our UI/UX.

Challenges we ran into

As first time Hackers, we ran into many challenges in the last 36 hours. Learning about databases from scratch how to link Firebase and retrieve data on both server and client ends, efficiently, was probably one of the bigger challenges

What's next for UCSD Study Group Finder

If we had more time, we would implement a location feature idea. We came up with this idea in the very beginning while we were thinking of projects to do. We want to use the geographic and location preferences from each student to create an algorithm that can make study group connections
