Inspiration

Our own trouble finding a team for the PennApps hackathon

What it does

The user inputs what interests they have, what skills they are looking for in a teammate, and what skills they have. The web app sorts through its database of users and selects 3-5 compatible matches.

How we built it

Python, css, json, flask

Challenges we ran into

Connecting the front end and back end

Accomplishments that we're proud of

This is our first hackathon. We are really excited that we could make an app that we could continue to develop at future hackathons and which can be used by new hackers like us!

What we learned

Team work makes the dream work

What's next for HackMatch

Continuing to develop the front end, back end, and rest layer. Add features that improve the accuracy of the matches. Machine learning!
