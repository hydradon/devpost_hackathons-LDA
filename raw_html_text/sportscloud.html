Inspiration

Imagine being all alone on the night of the Super Bowl... Watching sports by yourself isn't just the same without friends or live in the stadium. SportsCloud is here to change that! Enjoy the suspense and excitement that you would have with friends or other fans all from your computer!

How it works

SportsCloud is like watching the game in a chat room full of sports fans who are watching the game at the same time. While the user is watching the sports, he can comment on his favorite parts along with other users and also see other user's comments as the match goes the on. The comments are arranged based on the time during the game that they were commented. SportsCloud allows viewers to track the exact moment that other sports fans' comments are attached to the sports game. SportsCloud, then, takes those moments when a sports game is commented on and generates a live streaming feed. This feed features the time marked comments with the corresponding comments posted by sports fans. Essentially, this would be like watching the game with friends or even live in the stadium, all from the comfort of your computer.

  1. SIGN UP - Sorry for the caps, but this is a necessary step, as the application may return an error message from your browser that may say "match not valid"
  2. Click on any topic you want in the header. There are different sports as well as an e-sport, League of Legends at the moment.
  3. Make sure you are still signed in, and click on play for the video.
  4. Click on the chat box at any moment to add a comment.
  5. Share the video and your comments on http://twitter.com/
  6. Be sure to check out all the other features in the video and explore the website itself!
