Inspiration

In a new age of technology and growing academic competition, some high school students may find it daunting as to how one may proceed.

What it Does

PLANiT provides a personalized academic "trainer" that provides feedback on the student's personal interests and merits to help generate a high school path.

How We Built It

As a team of eager learners, we collaborated using our various skill sets to bring out an app that we believe be of help to students struggling around the nation.

Challenges We Ran Into

The challenges we ran into included the programming aspect of various portions of our application, the proper type of feedback that would be provided by the application, and design execution.

Accomplishments that We're Proud Of

We're all eager learners that found an application we wanted to bring to other students to help them plan our their daunting futures. No matter the outcome, we can feel proud of our efforts.

What I learned

I learned how to develop all ends of web application development.

What's next for PLANiT

A more developed program that truly provides the vision this application wanted.
