Inspiration

As a developer it seems like many conferences are still word of mouth. Sometimes there are really good conferences even in your city or close to home that you just have never heard of. Developers should have a easy place to go and find conferences.

What it does

Search for conferences by language/topics, speaker, geographic distance, price, number of days, etc... Also setup alerts for new conferences that get entered in that fulfill those search criteria.

How we built it

Backend built in Node.js with express and sequelize. Frontend SPA built with Vue.js.

Challenges we ran into

Figuring out how many features we could include in the amount of time allotted.

Accomplishments that we're proud of
