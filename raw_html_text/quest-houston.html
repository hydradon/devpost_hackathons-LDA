Inspiration

We checked out what Houstonians were REALLY into by posting online and getting opinions on Reddit. How can we use this to help the city?

How it works

You can login to the app and start exploring the city. Find a list and get started!

Visit a location and check in! Check in to all locations to unlock a badge and become a pro city navigator!
