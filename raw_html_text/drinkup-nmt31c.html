Inspiration

I don't drink enough water, and I know many others do not as well. I use an app with friends for studying where we can share and compete for studying, which helps motivates me, so I thought why not do the same for drinking water? The health benefits from increased water consumption are tremendous!

What it does

Tracks water consumption, goals, view friends water consumption, compete with friends and motivate each other. You also get notified when your friends drink water!

How I built it

I used Typescript, Framework7, Firebase/Google Cloud Platform, CSS.
