Inspiration

Almost every girl has encountered that feeling of waking up in the morning without a clue of what to wear for the day, too tired to expend the mental energy of putting together an outfit that is both weather-functional and stylish. Finally, she throws together an outfit, only to go outside and realize that it is completely inappropriate for the weather.

What it does

Creates an outfit based on the temperature inputted by the user.
