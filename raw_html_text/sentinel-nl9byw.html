Inspiration

We wanted to solve the seriously anxiety-inducing problem of having to leave your belongings unattended for short periods of time.

What it does

Sentinel is an easy to use anti-theft alarm. Simply turn it on and walk away. If someone moves your phone, it will sound an alarm that alerts everyone in the immediate area to a potential theft. When you return, simply unlock your phone and deactivate the alarm.

Challenges I ran into

This was our first iOS app built in Swift!

What's next for Sentinel

Next, we're hoping to add risk detection capabilities so that you'll know how safe your device is before leaving it alone.
