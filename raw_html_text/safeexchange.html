Inspiration

Internet exchanges are always tricky. Neither party can ensure they’ll get what they were promised because of potential scammers. Secure Exchange aims to solve this issue of mistrust by facilitating these transactions in a transparent and secure manner. Using the Ethereum blockchain, our messenger bot facilitates payment and the exchange of goods and services to make individual commerce more trustworthy.

What it does

Our product, SafeExchange, is a messenger chatbot that facilitates a file exchange.

How we built it

Messenger API, DialogFlow, Blockchain
