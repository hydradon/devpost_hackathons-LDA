• PetFinder Logo 

    We got inspired by the fact that in every place of the world, there are people that have pets that has gone missing, and we know that every now and then people see pets on the streets, but have no means of contacting the pet's owner directly, so we're trying to help people to find their lost pets and also the lost pet's owners to bring them back to home.
PetFinder helps people by providing a free platform where people can register a picture of their missing pet, informing the last place and time they have seen the pet and also providing a contact number and e-mail, allowing other users that could eventually see these pets on the streets to contact the pet's owner and give back the pet.
We designed the platform using HTML5, Javascript, CSS and Boostrap to make a clean, easy-to-use responsive website, making it easy to use in any platform, pc or smartphone.
