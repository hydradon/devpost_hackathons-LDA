Inspiration

we realized that a lot of people get bored easily and more times than not, they don't know what to do. This app serves as a way for people to see events at a moment's notice and go enjoy themselves

What it does

It is supposed to show you the events around you at any given moment so that you can pick and choose what you want to do

How we built it

we wanted to make a simple user interface that was friendly enough but the time limit and lack of knowledge of proto.io didn't allow us to finish on time

Challenges we ran into

We had no idea how to use proto.io and it took a while before we figured out that saving your work overrides anything that your other partners wrote.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

we managed to make at least the first page

What we learned

We learned that only one person can properly work on proto.io at a time without messing it up

What's next for Escapade

We will continue to develop the app and actually create a successful prototype
