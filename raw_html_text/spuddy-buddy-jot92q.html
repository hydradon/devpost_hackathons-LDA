Inspiration

As students who study without reminding ourselves to take breaks and eat, we felt that it would be helpful to create a conversational chatbot that cares about your well-being and success

What it does

The most productive couch potato you will meet. Spuddy is an interactive conversational chatbot assistant that gives reminders to help you stay focused. This app provides customization that is previously unavailable in existing reminder apps,and solves the problem of bad time management, which is a very common obstacle in the life of a university student. Spuddy aids in goal setting, Spuddy will tackle your procrastination and lack of productivity problem, because... Spuddy cares about your success!

How we built it

We plan to build the app using Microsoft Bot Framework and C+. The informational website was created with HTML/CSS/Javascript

Challenges we ran into

Thinking of effective and unique functions as well as new and fresh ideas that other competing productivity chat bots don't have.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The name, the logo, the PowerPoint presentation, and the website.

What we learned

Emily learned HTML/CSS for the first time. We learned how to compromise and collaborate as a team, consisting of members who met for the first time, to work efficiently and productively.

What's next for Spuddy Buddy

We wish to develop the app for Spuddy Buddy and put it out on the market for the general public, so that in 2.5 years we can put it on a platform such as Slack or Kik Messenger.
