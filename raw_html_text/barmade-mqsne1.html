Inspiration

Having to wait in the queue at the SU bar for a drink.

What it does

This app/website allows students to order drinks from the bar without having to queue.

How we built it

We built it using notepad++ in the html5 language. These were uploaded to AWS to connect it to our domain "barmade.tech".

Challenges we ran into

How the business side of the app would work. Implementation of a database. Expectations Vs. reality.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Managed to get a domain name and a host. Also managed to get the basic layout of the website.

What we learned

How to host a website via the cloud.

What's next for BarMade

Further development and design, and if successful, expand to other bars.
