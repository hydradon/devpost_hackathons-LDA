Finding Your Ride Has Never Been Easier

Imagine you just got out of the bar. It's dark out. Your friend Kevin (it's always Kevin) is a wee bit wasted and is trying to hop in the first chevy that kinda sorta somewhat resembles his Lyft.

_ Hold your horses Kevin, there's now a better way _

LyftSEE uses VR from your phone's camera to place a bright pink pin over your Lyft driver. You can't miss it! Now there will never be any hassle hunting down your driver. Think of it as X-Ray vision for your ride!
