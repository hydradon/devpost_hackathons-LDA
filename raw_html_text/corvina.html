Inspiration

We got inspired by Rachael's struggle to find support and resources for managing her chronic illness, postural orthostatic tachycardia syndrome.

What it does

It organizes information and tools for sustaining the health of chronic illness patients. Includes features like virtual support groups, mentorship programs, virtual doctor's visit, patient advocacy, and educational courses.

How it works

Creates virtual support groups by matching people basing on their chronic illness, age, and personalities. For an extra fee, finds an advocate for the patient to help him deal with insurance bills, gather medical records, coordinate care.
