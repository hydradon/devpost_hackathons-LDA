Inspiration

We wanted to make an application that could help centers like St. Patrick's to easily connect with the community in order to provide more help to the homeless.

What it does

Involved is an application aimed at connecting individuals to the problem of homelessness. By signing up, you can begin to offer help through the form of opening your home, donating food, clothes, and toys to local events, and volunteering.

How we built it

Involved is a web application with a Go back end. The front end uses VueJS along with Bootstrap.
