Inspiration

There are a lot of people in the world that hate going to restaurants by themselves. This app helps you find someone to go out with.

What it does

It helps people meet new people by matching you with people with the same interest.

How we built it

We build this app with Android Studio and used firebase as our back end.

Challenges we ran into

Configuring firebase to work with our our situation and configuration.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were proud of how effectively we worked throughout the course of the limited 24 hour working span.

What we learned

Learned how to work with users and implemented a database storage system.

What's next for Minglr

A little bit of polishing up - integrating Google maps, Yelp API, and publishing it to the app store.
