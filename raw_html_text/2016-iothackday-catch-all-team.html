Inspiration & Purpose

This team is in place to make sure folks who come to the 2016 IoT Hack Day in Minnesota understand that they can feel free to bring a bunch of random stuff--whatever you have in your parts drawer...wires, resistors, capacitors, Arduinos, ultrasonic sensors, geiger counters, WiFi nodes, banana peels, etc.

We don't know what will come out of this. We will probably split into teams of 3 or 4 people per team. It will be fun. It will be hilarious.
