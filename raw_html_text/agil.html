Inspiration

Agil Grader takes the hassle out of hand grading multiple choice tests and gives you the analytical power of Microsoft Excel. This app is for a teacher looking to save time grading or do data analysis on tests.

What it does

Continuously processes a video feed and grades a bubble sheet if it is detected in the frame.

How I built it

JavaFX, OpenCV

Challenges I ran into
