Inspiration

Our inspiration is to help disabled people to feel more included in today's society, because we think there aren't enough places designed for their needs.

What it does

Magic Moments it's an app that pretends to ease the way disabled people live in Mexico. This app functions with the GPS location of the people and locations that use it, it helps disabled people to find hotels, restaurants and activities that fulfill their needs. For example if a blind person is looking for a restaurant with menus in braile this app will help them find a restaurant near them, this restaurants near them will provide information like how good their service is, if the food is good, the directions of the restaurant, etc. The information of the hotels, restaurants and activities are provided with reviews made by the users.

How we built it

We built the app with Justinmind.

Challenges we ran into

How to make a friendly interface for all users.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

That the app is really easy to use, any person that wants to use it will find it friendly and easy to use.

What we learned

We learned more about the necessites of disabled people and that there aren't a lot of places disigned for them.

What's next for Magic Moments

To develop the app for Android, Windows Phone and a website. Also, we are looking forward to expand our app to other countries.
