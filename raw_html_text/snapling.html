Inspiration

We sat on a bench for 20 minutes on a college campus. We recalled our struggles of learning a foreign language. We wanted to make the learning process simpler and more accessible. This is what we came up with.

What it does

Take a picture. We identify the subject. We translate the word. You learn a new vocab word.
