We were inspired by the selfie king Kurt Lee. Pay by selfie uses 'selfies' to identify the user and then asks to authorise the purchase with a NFC ring. Easy as that.

It's an app that talks with ibeacons to identify who's receiving the money and facial recognition to see who's paying.
