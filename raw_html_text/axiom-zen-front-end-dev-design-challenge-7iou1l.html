Inspiration

The challenge was designing a landing page for a non-profit organization. WWF is known worldwide as a famous organization concerning environment and wildlife preservation. I realy admire their work. That's why I chose to build them a page.

What it does

It's a simple single page application that shows some information about species and places WWF protects.

How I built it

I build this with HTML5, Javascript, jQuery, Bootstrap and CSS3. I downloaded a video from YouTube, some pictures from Google and infos from WWF official website ( http://www.worldwildlife.org/ ). With the project ready, I uploaded it to my Instance at Amazon Web Services so you can access it from http://ec2-54-86-85-188.compute-1.amazonaws.com/WWF/

Challenges I ran into

I had some problems to make the slides responsives on page "Species", so I had to do some researches on Google.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I think design and UI/UX is awesome!

What I learned

I learned more about WWF projects and actions and I'm thinking of adopting an animal ;)
