Inspiration

We are (honestly mostly Quin) a group of passionate and nerdy individuals who have spent way too many hours sorting MtG cards. We wanted to create an automated system that solves that headache for the thousands of nerds out there.

What it does

The card sorter allows users to input predefinied settings like cost, type, and card names into a UI and the sorter will read cards placed in a hopper and sort it into desired bins.
