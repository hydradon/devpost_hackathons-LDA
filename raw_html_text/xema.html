Inspiration 

Eczema is a chronic disease that affects over 35 million Americans, ranging from children to adults. The most common symptoms include itching and rashing, which can escalate to unbearable levels, causing sleep deprivation and poor mental health. Studies report that the annual economic burden of eczema, including productivity loss and impacts on quality of life, is estimated at $5.3 billion. Despite the vast market and target audience, there is currently no identified cause or cure.

What it does

This app records and evaluates the common triggers for the user based on their flare ups and what they came into contact that day. Through this app, users will be able to identify the causes behind their eczema and avoid such triggers in the future to help manage their symptoms.
