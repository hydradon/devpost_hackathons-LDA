Inspiration

We don't want to see anymore dead animals on the streets. There have been many times that we have seen stray animals but did not have the ability to stop and get them.

What it does

This app lets users log and share animals they have seen to a map shared on the app with other users and post to twitter or facebook and to the social media sites of animal rescue services closest to the location of the animal. You have the ability to change the state of the post if you are actively searching for the animal or are if the animal has been found. You can leave a description when you retrieve the animal with information on its whereabouts, like if you kept it at your home for an owner to reach out to you to retrieve or if you took it to a shelter and which one. Together we can get animals off the streets and into homes.
