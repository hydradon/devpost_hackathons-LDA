Inspiration

The main idea revolved around the concept of a hunter and a prey similar to the game "Tag"

What it does

Its purpose is to relieve employees at Zitcom from work pressure or stress and also to connect them to new employees in the company.

How we built it

We used a third-party software called Android Studio and Firebase(Google platform)

Challenges we ran into

The entire project was a challenge, as none of us worked with building android applications or made use of Firebase before.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The Login and registering of the user, building the application for real android mobile devices.

What we learned

We learned how to build an android application from scratch and also searching for effective coding methods from google.

What's next for HackathonApp

Add more stress-relieving activities and also a social app for the employees of Zitcom.
