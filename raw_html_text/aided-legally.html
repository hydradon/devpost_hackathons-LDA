Inspiration

My inspiration came from what occurred in the US last year, specifically Houston and South Miami.

What it does

My idea uses Blockchain to allow you to securely upload your worldly possessions when you begin your insurance terms. During times of emergency, my app can be used by insurance companies and the gov't to notify citizens of emergency services such as shelter, food pantry, clothing, temporary hospitals. After making it to safety, my app allows consumers to select and provide proof of damaged goods. This will streamline the process of submitting claims, and allow insurance companies to automatically submit funds to their consumers. My app will also allow for the storage of sensitive documents, supported by blockchain, such as social security cards and drivers licenses. Finally, my app will allow for those that are trapped to activate a beacon for emergency service personnel.
