TransVid

_ Dubhacks 2017: TransVid project _

In this global era, we often watch videos in foreign language and want to understand what they say. Youtube video does a great job providing translation along with transcript and subtitles. HOWEVER, what about those who can't read? People who haven't learned to read or those with visual disabilities will not be able to read the translated subtitles. So we created a chrome extension that translates and speaks. Users can simply choose the preferred language that the videos will be translated to, and they will be able to watch voice-over video.
