• Knowing details of car traffic past and present is of great importance in maintaining and upgrading America's road infrastructure. With this traffic dashboard, you can easily see and analyze traffic in your city.
  • The project uses City IQ nodes to gather information about the current traffic on the road.
  • This information is sent to the web application so users can see the latest information whenever they open the page.
