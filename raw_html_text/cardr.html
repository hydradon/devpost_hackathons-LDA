Our Journey and What it Means to Us

When meandering around the sponsor tables at BitCamp my team and I noticed how we were inundated with business cards and founds ourselves overwhelmed with the sheer amount of information to keep a track of. Furthermore, we consistently misplaced the business cards which is where the spark ignited the campfire.

What does it do

Upon visiting Cardr one will find themselves greeted with our easy, extensible, accessible user experience. Once loaded the user can click on the "Upload Image" button and select an image of a business card. This is where the campfire is lit, our deep learning API scans the card searching for the most relevant information and creating a virtual card for the user. This hub is where all the cards will reside waiting to be interacted with.
