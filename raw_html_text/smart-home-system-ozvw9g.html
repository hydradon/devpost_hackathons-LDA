Inspiration

Are you tired of having a fragmented home environment, fuddling through apps to control various things? Well, you have come to the right place; we are developing a new unified software environment that will make your daily routines a little less cumbersome.

What it does

Our Smart Home System integrates your alarm, lights, utilities, and speakers into one neat and easy to use app.

How we built it

We built it using Android and Android Studio. We have also created a simulation in unity which will interface with the Android application.

Challenges we ran into

Integration of the different protions of the projects, such as the app pieces and the unity integration.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The integration of all of the components in a simple and easy to use application.

What we learned

We learned how to work as a team and work with Android with minimal prior experience.

What's next for Smart Home System

Integration with Amazon Alexa and real time input data from outside sources, such as traffic.
