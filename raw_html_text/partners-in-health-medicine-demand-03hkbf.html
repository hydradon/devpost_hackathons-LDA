Due to the lack of proper technological infrastructure countries in local communities like Haiti, Russia, and Mexico, many health non-profits like Partners in Health are unable to provide the full health support to the countries. They lack the ability to accurately track inventory due to the lack of internet in these countries. We are allowing these countries to contact Partners in Health through SMS.
