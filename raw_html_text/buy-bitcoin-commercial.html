Summary - This video serves as a commercial which leads the audience with the call to action to “buy Bitcoin” or “search for ‘buy Bitcoin’”. It is meant to appeal to millennials and “main street” investors who feel that the traditional financial system is unfair, outdated, and not serving their best interest. In under 60 seconds, we aim to inform the audience that Bitcoin is limited in supply, secure, borderless, transparent, and has historically been a good store of value and investment.
