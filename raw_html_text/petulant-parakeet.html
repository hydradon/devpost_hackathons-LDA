Inspiration

What it does

Our project detects cars behind your bike and notifies you when you are being passed.

How I built it

It was built with arduino, Lidar-Lite, and bluetooth connecting it to an android app.

Challenges I ran into

Our lack of experience with hardware and android made this project a rewarding and challenging experience.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We are proud of finishing in time.

What I learned

We gained experience with hardware and android, and learned that lack of sleep does weird things to people.

What's next for smartBike

We plan on adding additional features to the android app and adding additional hardware components such as a smart headlight and security feature.
