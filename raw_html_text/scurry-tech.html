• scurry

Inspiration

fun and scary multiplayer game of tag

What it does

users join the web app - the first person is the monster. All non-monsters can see a map with other users, but NOT the monster. The closer you get to the monster, the redder your screen gets and the louder the heartbeat music gets. INTENSE!!!!!! When the monster is within a proximity of you, then you become the monster!

How we built it

node.js and mongodb hosted on a digital ocean droplet running nginx and pm2

Challenges we ran into

servers are dumb. iOS doesn't know how to HTML5

Accomplishments that we're proud of

it works! we have a game in the browser that tracks location and displays a map

What we learned

hosting node.js, google maps API, AR functions, web based geolocation

What's next for scurry.tech

IPO in a few years
