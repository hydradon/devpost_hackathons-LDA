Inspiration

Making life easy.

What it does

Regulates two independently controlled outlet voltages throughout a user-friendly interface embedded in an mobile Android bluetooth application.

How I built it

3D printed the box, manually joined all the wiring together, programmed the Arduino, and developed a mobile Android application to control the YetiSwitch.

Challenges I ran into

Integrating the Arduino with Bluetooth.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Developed a fully functioning product.

What I learned

I expanded my knowledge in micro-controller development as well as wiring and the mobile application development.

What's next for YetiSwitch

Adding schedule functionality as well as a more appealing and economic containment unit.
