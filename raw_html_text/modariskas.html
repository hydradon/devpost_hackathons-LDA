inspiration

reading the challenges proposed in this hackathon brought us to the one that talk about making school life much more easier to students , which land us to find a solution for the problem of finding the appropriate teacher for students both in virtual and real world , based on a specific and targeted information as : Location, Preferencies, Ages, Gender ....

this problem made many student fail in their school life and waste their money and efforts with the bad chosen private teachers , so we saw that it is a must to find a solution for this problem , which is ModarisKas

What is does

Online E-learning platform to smartly bring the perfect match teacher for student based on their infos (Location,Level,Preferencies ...) and help them to meet in both virtual and real world

How we built it

We worked as a team formed from 3 persons , one took the design job , another the backend work with laravel and the other one the frontend with Vuejs

Challenges we ran into

We learnt how to manage time , Team Work , working under pressure and new tech stacks
