Bathroom Buddy is an Alexa skill for many purposes. Number one, it is a life hack for parenting since it teaches young kids good hygiene habits. Number two, it helps with overall wellness since it teaches good life skills like washing hands, and brushing teeth for the appropriate amount of time while also making it fun.

Ask Bathroom Buddy any of the following and see how he responds. ~ "I went potty" ~ "Mirror mirror on the wall who is the fairest one of all" ~ "Magic mirror in my hand who is the fairest in all the land" ~ "Give me a tip" ~ "How do I brush teeth" ~ "Set a timer for me to brush my teeth" ~ "Sing to me as a wash my hands" ~ "How do I floss my teeth" ~ "How do I wash my hands"

  • The time to wash hands and brush teeth is advised by health professionals.
  • The description of how to brush and floss teeth is by Colgate. - https://www.colgate.com/en-us/oral-health/basics/brushing-and-flossing
  • The description of how to wash your hands is by Mayo Clinic. - https://www.mayoclinic.org/healthy-lifestyle/adult-health/in-depth/hand-washing/art-20046253
