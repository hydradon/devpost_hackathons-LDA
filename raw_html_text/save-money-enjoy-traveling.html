Employees in big companies travel a lot and this domain isn't optimized at all. Airbus needs this solution, it's already enough for an inspiration and a few days w/o sleep.

What it does

It collects data from the employees' calendars and booking system, analyze it and notices people about possible possibilities to save money and have a good benefit during next trip or a nice voucher before Christmas.

How I built it

Parsed data, improved data model, implemented an interface for managing data and visualized results.

Challenges I ran into

Data. It's almost always data. Always you need to do a lot to improve quality initial data.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Just as always, was very excited before the hackathon.

What I learned

Business needs fast, smart, quality and business oriented developers and business analytics. They can give a great push to big companies.

What's next for Save money, enjoy traveling

on next step, it needs to test on big real data and calculate savings.
