..we try to tackle the problem of wrong item donations after a disaster. often referred to as "the disaster after the disaster". our mobile application tells you when you should NOT donate your items.

at the same time the app tries to determine supply and demand of items in post disaster areas and encourages users in very close proximity to these areas to DO item donations.
