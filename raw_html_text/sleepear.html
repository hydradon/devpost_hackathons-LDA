Inspiration

Diagnozing sleep apnea these days is only possible in sleeping labs with special pressure transducers and many cables attached on the user. 85% percent of the population are undiagnosed even though the consecuences are severe (e.g. strokes).

What it does

With our app everybody is able to track the heart rate and respiration while he's sleeping. We use IMU enabled headphones to do so.
