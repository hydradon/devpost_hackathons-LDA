  • The Onion...because why not?

These days, who knows what real news is anymore? Is it Breitbart's fanatic 'alt-right' propaganda, or the New York Times' prejudiced liberal agenda? Now, with "Alternative News", its all up to you - just enter a keyword(s) that you'd like to read about, and choose from a traditionally 'left' leaning news source and a traditionally 'right' leaning one. Escape your secluded bubble of Facebook news and Buzzfeed, and expand your horizons by reading about both opinions.

In Android Studio, we used xml files for layout and java for the actual code.

We had a bit of trouble at first with WebViews, but turned that into buttons so now we're all good!
