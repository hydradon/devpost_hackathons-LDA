Inspiration

Media portrays the world as a hostile and dangerous place. Pages like Humans of New York show a different version of reality. The real reality.

What it does

Aggregates all the Facebook pages inspired by the Humans of New York project.

How I built it

Imported all the Humans of X pages, downloaded all posts, ran Watson Taxonomy to tag posts, used kinetise to create iPhone and Android apps.
