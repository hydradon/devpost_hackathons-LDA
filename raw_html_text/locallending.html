Local Lending

Invent:YU

We needed a solution to the problem of having to pay for things we only use once. Thus, Local Lending was born. It is a platform open to anyone who needs something temporarily, and doesn't want to pay for it, only to use it once.
You can search for items that other people are willing to share and offer your own items all on an extremely user-friendly and convenient platform.

We meant to use the domain.com domain that we registered; however, we were informed that the DNS would not propagate in time. ( http://locallending.org )
