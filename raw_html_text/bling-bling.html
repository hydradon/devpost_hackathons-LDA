We built a panic button for women and girls that can be attached to any piece of jewelry and gives the victim the power to record audio with a press of a button and call the police with a touch and hold.

Not only will the victim be able to discreetly contact the police she will also possess digital evidence of the crime.

Our solution is unique because unlike other safety apps, Hidden Treasures contacts the police and not your friends. Moreover it is portable, accessible, and discreet.

We choose this name because its a discreet panic button that goes on jewelry.
