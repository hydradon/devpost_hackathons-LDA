Anykura

(Table A3)

Anykura is a service to store your items safely at the most appropriate place for them. In emergency, you can abandon them in exchange of the immediate deposit money.

We target for people who cannot provide appropriate environments for their items but still want to own them.

Anykura uses Minikura's API & delivery system and the XDK from Bosch.
