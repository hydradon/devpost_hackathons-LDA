• Equal IQ - AI driven realtime audio enhancement

Inspiration

Our inspiration was to enhance the listener's experience while driving. As musicians and car enthusiasts, we understand that the car is the truest form to experience music, therefore we wanted to enhance that experience no matter the conditions so listeners can experience the music the way it was made in the studio

What it does

Our technology enhancing audio to adjust to the ever-changing conditions while driving.

How I built it

Equal IQ is built with Android studio, IOS, NodeJS, SDL framework, Dolby digital API AI-driven processing and gitch coding platform.

Challenges I ran into

Integrating both DOLBY and SDL framework, as well as API connections, sending information and interfacing AI enhancements with audio processing

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Being able to create a platform where it could truly change people's lives and experiences.

What I learned

Sorting issues with android and creating Dolby processing of audio

What's next for Equal IQ

We are grateful to have won Dolby's IO prize along with Ford's SDL prize. We will continue to Implement and improve the technology so we can implement and integrate!
