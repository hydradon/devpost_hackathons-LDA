Inspiration

To better the lives of foreign born individuals

What it does

JPU allows users to view businesses, make resumes with pre-loaded drop down boxes, and helps pair users with mock interviewers for their potential employers

How we built it

We used the AGILE development approach with each of us focusing on one feature at a time

Challenges we ran into

Running out of time, new to multiple applications because we used this event as a learning opportunity

Accomplishments that we're proud of

How much we managed to get done even though we had no clue what we were doing when we showed up

What we learned

How to work in a team setting with commits, pulls, pushes, and migrations

What's next for Juxta Position UniCorns

We hope to use this program for further semester projects

What problem does our product solve

The troubles of being a foreign born individual with a lack of knowledge involving how to make a resume, look for a job, and prepare for an interview
