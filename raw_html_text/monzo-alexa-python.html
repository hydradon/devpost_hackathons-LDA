Casually checking your bank account balance is tedious. Usually, it requires manually logging in to a website or banking app.

Amazon Alexa provides a convenient interface for casually and quickly acquiring information. Monzo provides a banking API. We combined them, making it super simple to check your balance, ask if you can afford to buy specific products, and perform other financial queries.
