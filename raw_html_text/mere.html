Inspiration

Daily commute, forgetting where the car was parked, car getting towed away, unable to reach/find car when its stolen, car information not handy, unable to track family members.

How it works

This app provides great benz user experience in keeping track of their car and their loved ones. It helps in providing live information on the location of the car where its parked, helps in tracking car when it gets stolen or towed away by giving timely alerts and notifications with options to call 911, Uber. Also, helps is tracking where the family members are based on the restricted access to the family members car location.
