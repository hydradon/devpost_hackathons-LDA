Inspiration

Companies have skills shortages and newly arrived refugees have experience and skills but often find it hard to get that first local work experience in Austraila.

What it does

Matches companies offering internship programs to skilled refugees

How I built it

Ruby on rails with bootstrap for the front-end

Challenges I ran into

I am new to Ruby on rails so I had to look up some things online since I wasn't sure how to do them.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Great presentation, pitch and finalised idea.

What I learned

More about ruby on rails and how to make a good pitch and develop an idea in a short period of time

What's next for Skills Connect

Winning the hackaton and developing our idea so that we can connect companies and skilled refugees to help the future economy of Australia
