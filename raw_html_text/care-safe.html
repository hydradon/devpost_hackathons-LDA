Inspiration

Innovating child safety in an aesthetically pleasing way.

What it does

will monitor a child's temperature and send notifications to the parents/guardians phone-through an app; telling them when they're baby's temperature rises or decreases.

How I built it

Using a combination of two soft, newborn safe fabric to create a flexible, yet comforting band with temperature sensory technology embedded inside the lining.

Challenges I ran into

Figuring out the correct blend of fabric for baby safety and the correct equipment for connecting the sensor to an app.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The entire research and creation process

What I learned

The details and precaution that comes with the safety of a specific demographic

What's next for Care-Safe

Possible expansion into cancer-patient safety; maybe even begin detection in heart rate, breathing, and possible poisons in the environment around the wearer.
