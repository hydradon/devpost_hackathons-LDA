Inspiration

We are living somehow in 2018 and have not yet managed to pay for groceries with our smartphones.

What it does

You simply scan QR-Codes generated by the checkout counter on your phone and pay!

How I built it

Our solution uses a combination of APIs, QR-Codes and existing payment services to enable smart payments without a big infrastructure.

Challenges I ran into

All of them!

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Integrating a ton of different technologies and services.

What I learned

QR-Codes can be awesome if used to their full potential!
