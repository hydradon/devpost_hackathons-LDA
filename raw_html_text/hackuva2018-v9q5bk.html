Watchdog By Devin Kim, Eli Lifland, Augene Park, Ian Rector

Watchdog was created to help clients feel safe in either the private or public sector. Security personnel can use our software to focus on potentially dangerous situations rather than having to waste time and energy manually looking at all security camera feeds on a traditional central monitoring system. If suspicious movement occurs, this software will highlight this area with a red border to imply the point of interest and place a box around the subject's body. Facial detection software will also trace the potential assailant's face. Objects are tracked as they move within the field of view.
