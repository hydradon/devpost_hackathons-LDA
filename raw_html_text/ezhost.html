Inspiration

Hosting large-scale events has always been a hassle with numerous middle men and we aim to simplify this process. We are the Airbnb of hosting.

What it does

A web platform where users can rent out events spaces and add-ons in an effective and simply process.

How we built it

We developed a preliminary android platform and the concept of the web platform.

Challenges we ran into

Due to the time constraint and lack of designers, only preliminary versions can be developed.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Our concept and android platform.

What we learned

We learned about android app development as well as how to pitch a case effectively within a restricted available timeframe.

What's next for EZhost

Detailed design of the platform(s) and determining the business side of the potential startup concept.
