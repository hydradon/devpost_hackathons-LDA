Inspiration

Our team members all had trouble meeting up with our friends and arranging reunions on WhatsApp, because we do it in a non-systematic way and we couldn't find the proper tool to do that.

What it does

The event creator can create an event on our platform and send an invitation link to their friends so everyone can specify when they have free time and the system will find a free time slot for the event and everyone.
