WaterConservationProject

It is predicted that by 2050, countries will fight each other for fresh water. In order to avert this apocalypse and save humanity from extinction, we built you an app to decrease your water footprint!

It's simple: download this app and prevent World War 3!!!

Our app, built by Harsha, Vishok (the veteran coder), Chaitanya, and Vamsi, is a user-friendly environment that assists users in setting water conservation goals that are both practical and effective.
