Inspiration

Our inspiration for making this app was that we wanted to have a real impact on problems that Houston face, as well as use some interesting algorithms or tech to accomplish this. We saw movies like minority report and video games like watchdogs, and thought we could try and predict crime around Houston.

What it does

The app uses a neural network to predict the probability of crimes happening at different locations throughout the day.

How we built it

We made the app using Android Studio, and made the NN with java.

Challenges we ran into

We ran into challenges with collecting and priming data for the NN, and android xml.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of getting the Neural Net working, and learning how to style google maps.

What we learned

We learned alot about front-end android design, as well as priming data for NN's.

What's next for safeHOU

We want to make an iOS app and possibly expand out of Houston. We also want to work with the police to improve the data available to us.
