Inspiration

Companies spend hours combing through social media sites to learn more about users are experiencing their products. Tools exist for brands to engage with their customers on sites like Facebook and Twitter, but it's particularly difficult to interact with them on Reddit.

What it does

Reddflag ingests posts and comments from Reddit, looking for mentions of a company and words related to that company. If the content matches, Reddflag scans its text for sentiment (was the poster generally positive or negative) and shows it to the brand. Brand managers can then respond directly to customers through the app.

How we built it

Our backend is a django app hosted in a Google App Engine Flexible Environment . We use Cloud Functions to ingest and process messages from Reddit and Google's Cloud Natural Language API for sentiment analysis. Our frontend is built in React.

Challenges we ran into

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We're proud to have a working prototype of our project that works end-to-end with a single brand!

What we learned

We learned a lot of new technologies (particularly on the Google Cloud stack)!

What's next for Reddflag
