Inspiration

Our goal was to build a fun and challenging game that was also educational. Due to the declining math scores of our children, it was also an obligation for us to be part of the solution.

What it does

Gameplay is very simple: Solved each math question as quickly as possible. Correct answers add power to your car making it go faster

How I built it

Built using Unity3d game engine and the Windows SDK for Universal windows support.

Challenges I ran into

MUMT Family play was our biggest challenge. This mode allows parents to play along with a child and help when they are stuck on a math question.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Developing a game that can help improve the math skills of families is a noble effort that I am proud to promote.

What I learned

Adding educational value while keeping gameplay fun and rewarding.

What's next for Whiz Racer

We want to add cars and customization as well as advanced math levels using Algebra and Trigonometry.
