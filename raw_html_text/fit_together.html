Fit Together

Fit Together is a cross-platform mobile app developed in Flutter. Since the intention of the app is to bring our community together and enable us to become better individuals, a cross-platform solution was the only solution in order to allow everyone to be included.

Execution

We chose the brand new Flutter framework because we saw it as an opportunity to try out a brand new technology and to challenge ourselves to learn a new framework and execute a project in a short time-span. We are a group of very passionate first year engineers, and are always excited to explore new technologies and solutions.

To aid with user authentication, we used Google's Firebase in order to sign up and log-in users using their email address and a password.

Additionally, the application uses SQL to enable users to host exercise events and for other users to view those events with a timestamp so that they can join in if they are interested.
