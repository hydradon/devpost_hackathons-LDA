Inspiration

We were inspired by the various ways celebrities use social media and how these celebrities influence the general public. They attempt to be philosophical while engaging in egregious self-promotion.

How it works

The user searches a Twitter handle and our back end fetches the information/tweets pertaining to the user and generates quotes/phrases using Markov chains.

Challenges I ran into

Coming up with a feasible idea was difficult.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The humor of the generated lines was surprisingly accurate.

What I learned

We learned how funny celebrities are, especially Jaden Smith.

What's next for Bloop

We will look into extending this project to other social media platforms.
