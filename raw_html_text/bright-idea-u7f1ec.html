Inspiration

Our goal was to build a safer light system for walkways, especially around campuses. As students who walk back home or to bus stops late at night, we have noticed that many walking paths are dimly lit and feel unsafe. We wanted to create a more energy-efficient and safe system so that students don't need to worry as much about late night walks.
