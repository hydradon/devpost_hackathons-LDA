Inspiration

Sometimes, we just need to know where our DD's are and what better way to do that than with a web app just for that.

What it does

DwDD is a web app aimed to help students and other event goers from having to worry about finding the right designated driver. This application finds designated drivers that are registered and willing to help with driving people to and from their destination

How we built it

We used, Python, Flask, HTML, and CSS

Challenges we ran into

Getting the application deployed to a web service within the timeframe.
