• Agrex

Inspiration

What inspires us is the increase in revenue on Agricultural product for export in Nigeria which grew in value by 54.9% in Q4 2017 (N44.7 billion) in comparison to Q3 2017 (N28.8 billion), and by 170.9% in comparison to Q4 2016 (N16.5 billion).

What it does

Agrex.com is an agricultural based e-commerce platform that connects local agricultural product seller/supplier with potential foreign buyers by providing secure payment transaction, easy and monitored transportation of goods from warehouse to the port. We help both farmers and sellers of Agricultural product to sell beyond boundaries.

How we built it

We develop the website with the use of HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript

Challenges we ran into

Bad Internet connection, Poor Power Supply

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The fact that we were able to work on such project within a short period of time. It really exposes us to real life dev hustle.

What we learned

How to collaborate and also learn new tools that can be useful for us as a developer.

What's next for Agrex
