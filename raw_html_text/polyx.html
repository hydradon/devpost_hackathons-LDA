We were inspired by Uber and Lyft, the two great services that brought inexpensive solutions and brought thousands of jobs with them.

This app allows a doctor to visit your home and have a doctor's appointment at your house! It also allows you to rate the doctor and patient based on their experiences.

We built the website with Html, CSS, and Javascript elements.

We started with an application but realized that it would realistically take longer to create a UI that was good enough for our customers.

We are proud of how well the website turned out.

We learned that HTML can be used for many things, even creating carousels. You don't need bootstrap anymore!

We hope to make an app next. This will further provide many benefits to our customers.
