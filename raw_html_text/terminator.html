Inspiration

We came to AGHacks without plan, when we managed to form the Team we decided to do everyone's best.

What it does

Automatically finds balloons lying close and shoots them with extra-sharpened nails.

How I built it

I (Piotr) was designer of OpenCV aiming software, Mateusz Mamala was embedded programmer, Mateusz Wydra was our wireless communication expert and Marcin was designer of shooting stuff.

Challenges I ran into

The biggest challenge was huge amount of data to be sent between remote control and platform.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am most proud of segregation of duties, that allowed us to create this awesome project.

What I learned

I learned lots of things about computer vision and professional photography from mentors. Also it was my first time I could test my python skills,
