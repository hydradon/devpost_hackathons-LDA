Inspiration

We want biking experience to be better and safer, at the same time maintain the cool looking that people would like.

What it does

The backpack has building in Arudino with sewn LED that is able to flash when there are things within certain distance behind. At the same time, bicylists can give tuning signal shown on the back for nighttime visibility or just to act cool. Sensoring the distance behind you and give signals when it's close to you. Give turning left/right signal when you want to turn so the people/car/bike is aware of what you want.

How we built it

Arduino with breadbaord and LEDs swen on backpack.
