Inspiration

Our passion for the environment! At our school's recent homecoming that was themed around trash, we gained inspiration about how our oceans were polluted.

What it does

Through drag and drop, players are able to make combinations and create a new element, with the end goal of discovering them all. At each new element, there is a fun fact to educate the players and teach them how to help the environment.

How we built it

Unity

Challenges we ran into

Dealing with Unity functions, features unable to be implemented

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Drag & Drop, Aesthetics

What we learned

Familiarize with Unity, challenges of creating a simple game, difficult to make aesthetic

What's next for Aftermath

More elements, full-functionality, meter bar, publishing as an Android and Web application, partnering with organizations, adding a tutorial screen, element tray scroll bar
