Have you ever gone into a grocery store and received $0.89 worth of change? It is annoying to carry all of that change in your pocket. So our team has developed a solution where you can easily take out your smartphone and have that change deposited into PiggyBank. When that money adds up, you can use the change you saved up in your PiggyBank towards that next transaction.

Our inspiration for this project was created because carrying change around is very annoying.

We built this using web development technologies. Such as: HTML 5, CSS3, Javascript, and Firebase hosted our backend.
