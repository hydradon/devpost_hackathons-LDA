Inspiration

A better Microsoft paint that could be used for educational purposes.

What it does

Users can collaboratively create and share images by moving their index finger in front of a webcam.

How we built it

Javascript, Python, OpenCV and Firebase

Challenges we ran into

Websockets and OpenCV stuff

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting the app to process finger detection and relay that information to the client in almost real time.

What we learned

Websockets and OpenCV stuff

What's next for AirDraw

Launch our app to a wider audience so that users can better understand conceptual ideas online without being confined to only typed text.
