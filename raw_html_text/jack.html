Inspiration

Our inspiration came from trying to solve the age old problem of making a party playlist that everyone is happy with.

How it works

JACK works by letting users upvote or downvote songs reddit style so that the most popular ones are queued before the less popular ones. Any user in the playlist can add a song at any time.

Challenges I ran into

The main challenge that we ran into was that the project, being very front heavy, involved a lot of JavaScript which only one member of our team had any prior knowledge of.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The app itself we think is worthy of pride.

What I learned

We all became more comfortable with web development in Flask and JavaScript.
