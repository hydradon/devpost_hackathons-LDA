Inspiration

We have organized over 100+ events in Seattle and hated the hassle in booking a space for our event. There will a lot of calls which needed to be made, a lot of searching, a lot of back and forth emails ... just a pain. Therefore we created a website for people to find a space.

Airbnb and Expedia were the websites that inspired us.

What it does

It allows you to input your search criteria, gives you event space results, allows you to select a space, and then finally book a space.
