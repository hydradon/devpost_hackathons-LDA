flint-water

Water For Flint is an iOS and Android app built to help make a difference in the lives of people affected by the Flint, MI water crisis. Water For Flint has several features that make it easy to receive help if you are a Flint resident in need or give a helping to those in need.

Current Features: Utilizes Google Maps web API to display a map with all free water locations in Flint News feed to keep up to date on important developments in Flint A timeline of events related to Flint's water crisis Counter that keeps track of how long Flint has been without clean water

Planned Features: Utilize Googlemaps API to provide directions to the user's chosen water bottle location. Utilize a public news api to provide a up to date stream of news in flint related to the crisis. Provide a list of ways to help to those user's unaffected but looking to help
