Challenge 1: Interactive 3D visualization of one of the building of the Poznań University of Economics and Business.

Inspiration

We always wanted to visualize the building where we are studying.

What it does

The user can walk through the building in VR.

How we built it

We created the models basically in Blender and put them together in Unity.

Challenges we ran into

Some models were more difficulty than others. It was also challenging to connect all models into one project.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of the accuracy of the individual models.

What we learned

We learned how to assign tasks to different skills.

What's next for XDevelopers

We want to go global!
