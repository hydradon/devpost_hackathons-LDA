Inspiration

Recently, I have been trying to learn how to write songs. However, I had some trouble learning the proper chords and scales.

What it does

This tool helps highlight the basic scale that is needed to begin songwriting. The major scale. The android app can tell the user which note they are playing, the chord they are playing, and highlight the major scale of that note.

How I built it

I first began by using a great amount of RectF's to make the white keys, which are red in this piano, and the black keys. The piano responds to touch by using the onTouchEvent command in Android java.

Challenges I ran into

I had a few issues with the sound playing and after loading the project as a whole multiple times, sometimes the prgram would cooperate in playing a sound.
