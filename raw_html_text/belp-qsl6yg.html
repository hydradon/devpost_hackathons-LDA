Inspiration

I am an active Yelper. I believe Yelp is a powerful platform to help customers find good businesses and to help good businesses attract customers. However, I (and probably many other people) do not feel comfortable treating Yelp as a social media platform. I believe fair and unbiased feedback is more likely to be left when users can remain anonymous.

What it does

Belp uses blockchain technology to allow for verified yet anonymous reviews by customers.
