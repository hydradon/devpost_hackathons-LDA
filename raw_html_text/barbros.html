BarBros

A simple app created in React Native that lets users find the perfect barber them!. You view what type of barbers are close by and you can view their profile to view their page and read their reviews. No matter times wasted going to a barber that doesn't cut that style you like. Simply go on the app and find the best barber that meets your needs
