Inspiration

Wanting to learn how neural networks work.

What it does

Estimates the probability (in percentage) of a car crash at any given coordinate in Montreal. It also estimates a number of different characteristics to the crash.

How we built it

The data is from the Quebec government official site. The numbers are based of a neural network trained with over 170000 different cases.
