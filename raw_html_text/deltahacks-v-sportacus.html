Inspiration

While we were prompted by the Exercise for the Community Triangle Challenge, we had this idea before from before. A lot of us in the group are rock-climbers, which is actually a two-man sport. You need one person to climb, and one person to work the ropes.

What it does

Sportacus allows users to create profiles, detailing their interests related to physical activity. Then, our algorithm finds people with similar interests and suggests connecting with those people. This enables people to find partners/opponents for sports really easily, and motivates people to get active.

How we built it

The core of the application is built with Flask using python.

Challenges we ran into
