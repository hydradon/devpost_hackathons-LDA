Inspiration

Urban Flooding is the inspiration behind our project.

What it does

Using turbidity wireless sensors, our website, SUDOL, collects data and processes the same to send out notification alerts(indicating high turbidity level due to intoxication via sewage waste) of illegal connections to the storm water drainage to government authorities. Also, provision is made to register complaints and book appointments for garbage collection.

How we built it

We developed a website called Sudol to manage all these services using html,css, javascript

Challenges we ran into

Majorly time issues, since the idea has been thought of in totality. We've tried to gather the essence of the idea in as much time provided

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We're proud of the final outcome of this project. And it feels like a cherry on the top that we have made something that will help India in its development.

What we learned

We researched a lot about the drainage system and sewers. Additionally, we touched the surface of IoT technology through which we wish to take our project forward.

What's next for Sudol

Sudol would turn out to be a national scale project, curbing all illegal activities and corruption amongst the municipal corporations of each state. We will connect our front end code to the backend resources to demonstrate the hardware implication
