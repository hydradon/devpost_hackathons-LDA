Inspiration

We live in Elche (Alicante), Spain, and we have suffered for long time the drought. We think it's very important to aware people of this problem.

What it does

It's a social robot. On top of ROS we've built several nodes for creating a conversational robot you can chat to.

How I built it

We've used Samsung Artik 10 as the main "brain" of our robot, in the past we've been using ARM based SBC, so we were prepared for this challenge. We've used our own board, called Jetpack, for interfacing Artik with the robotic servos. A webcam with built-in microphone for capturing audio and recognize speech, and online services from pandorabots for creating the dialogues.
