Questions for entering into the Entrepreneurial prize:

What is the problem you’re solving?

-We're giving a new avenue for people who may be socially awkward to signal that they are looking to socialize with new people. Our app focuses on spontaneity and flexibility. If all your friends flake out on you, your night out wont be ruined.

Who are users and/or customers?

-Young Adults 21-30, people who like to go out drinking and network with other people. More specifically it targets people who may be looking for others to network with at any given time.

What’s currently missing that they need and which your solution provides?

-A way to socialize which is as fun and spontaneous as a night out on the town should be.

Inspiration

The potential of augmented reality platforms such as Pokemon Go and Niantic along with proximity based socializing apps such as Tinder and Bumble.
