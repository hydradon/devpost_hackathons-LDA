Inspiration

We all like to travel right ? But as lazy people, we don't want to compare hundreds of flights, and make a whole plan. We can focus on vacations only, and not planning anymore !

What it does

From some basic informations (departure airport, budget and departure date), we will select for you a route at the best price possible (estimated). When you are satisfied with the result, ask for a precise route (price and times exact). You can even see you journey on a map !

Another feature : you can submit a picture link, corresponding to your ideal vacation, and it will analyse the picture to propose you a destination corresponding to the picture.

How we built it

We use a frontend composed of HTML, Vue.js, Javascript and flask. Then a Python algorithm is called in the background for the requests.

Challenges we ran into

Essentially optimizing the queries, as they might become really long.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The picture analysis was a big part of the work, and quite hard. Our frontend also makes us proud !

What we learned

We learned to work with REST API (in Python), and improved our HTML/JS/Vue.js skills

What's next for Skyscanner-CONpagnons

We are really looking forward to the next Hackaton, where we will take on another challenge.
