Inspired by 3 hour wait times when on hold with Comcast, we created an alternative to mind-numbing elevator music. With Dial Tone, you can make your own music. Callers press a number to record themselves laying down a beat, layer multiple tracks, and control the volume of each track individually.

Throughout the call, Dial Tone uses Twilio to record each track and compiles them together so you can always hear what your mix sounds like. After each call, a SoundCloud link to your tracks are sent by SMS for you to add to your mixtape.
