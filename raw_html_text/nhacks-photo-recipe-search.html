nhacks-photo-recipe-search on github

NHacks Hack, take a photo of food from instagram and get the recipe.

Uses Clarifai and Indico Machine Learning to determine food on Instagram and how to make them using web scraping and other APIs. Built to share and enjoy good food with good company.
