About

This is a WYSIWYG interface proposal for an email generator inspired by tools like mailchimp or sendgrid.

What it does

It helps MUSEFIND customers sent out custom emails and create emails. Furthermore, the customers will be able to manage campaigns, subscriber lists and templates.

How I built it

I used sketches and paper prototypes to create the concept of user experience and Adobe Photoshop to finalize the UI.

Notes

I tried to use the MUSEFIND bootstrap patterns for easy programming.

I took the liberty of changing the brand colors palette, making it shinier, in order to increase customer empathy.
