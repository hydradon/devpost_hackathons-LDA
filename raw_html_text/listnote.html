ListNote

Finally, digital lists that makes sense.

Listnote is designed to cater to two things: aesthetic perfection and seamless productivity.

Ditch the linear list. Unfurl dimensions of information relationships by drawing ties between relevant points. Alongside intuitive-for-once natural language processing, taking your online note-taking to the next level is beautifully effort-list.

While you take down notes, tags are being generated for each point - based on the document distance between the content in your notes. Natural language processing means that your notes are already being organized (dates within your notes, word definitions) with minimal effort.
