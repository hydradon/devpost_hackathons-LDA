Inspiration

We want to keep the Church praying. Sharing encouragement and calling for emergency intercession.

We found some churches allow members to send a request for prayer to a mobile number then whoever has that mobile forwards the message on to the list of members who want to pray. We found there were a lot of manual steps involved and issues keeping the list updated, passed on when someone goes on holiday etc.

We also found members wanted to be notified at a more convenient time and reminded to pray and there was stigma with opting out.

What it does

Allow members to submit prayer requests online or via a sms. The request is added to an approval list, once approved it will get sent out to subscribers based on their notification preferences.
