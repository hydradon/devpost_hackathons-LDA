Inspiration

Marches have become much more prevalent across the country. Even with this increase in attendances, there are still those who are hesitant to physically attend, or want to know more about the cause from a safe and comfortable space. This experience could also serve as a means for those who are unable to attend marches due to accessibility or health conditions to feel a part of the cause without being there in person.

What it does

You become a part of the Santa Cruz Women's March 2018 and can experience the atmosphere with music and people around you. Switch between locations and learn about causes, history, and organizations attending.

How we built it

We attended the Women's March in Santa Cruz and took 360 photos to create an immersive environment that VR users can experience. Photos taken from the event were loaded in the Android SDR.

Challenges we ran into

We're all VR newbies, the first steps were a challenge.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting a final product that really puts you in the center of it.

What we learned

VR is great! It was fun

What's next for March With Us

Attend other Marches to collect a range of experiences for people to choose from and learn about causes bringing people to the streets. The app could still use polishing too.
