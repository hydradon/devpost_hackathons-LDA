Inspiration

We are bored of eating on campus everyday, and the limited number of restaurants around campus (mostly fast food chains) provide us with minimum varieties.

What it does

Yamyneer connects students looking for a meal during lunch or dinner with locals or other students who are hosting meals at their place. They can choose from a variety of homemade food and options of dine-in, Pickup or delivery to a specific location on campus.

What's next for Yamyneer

We believe our idea will start off on campuses across the United Stated, but eventually will expand to communities and cities so that everyone can benefit from it. We are just at the tip of exploring the "Gig" economy of food.
