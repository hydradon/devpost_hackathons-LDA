Inspiration

Just an idea I've had for a long time

What it does

You put it the name of the food and how much. It helps you keep track of what you might already have in the fridge. Then you can tap "I'm hungry" to get relevant recipes based on what you have.

How I built it

Used the room example from android dev website as a skeleton to work off of. Then just researched and found what worked from there.

Challenges I ran into

There was a ton of new information to process. Mobile app development is very different than I imagined

Accomplishments that I'm proud of
