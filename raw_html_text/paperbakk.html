We made a social media application and website based on books and those who read them. You can see what your friends are reading, book recommendations based on your reading history, you can upload your own book, and borrow books from other users. We promote creative writing and give an opportunity to any type of writer so they can share their literature and stories with other users, without depending on publishers and distributors. It is a free app, anyone can use it to find books, and recommendations. It can help people recycle books in their community and share eBooks with others.
