BattleVision is a live streaming platform designed as an inclusive environment for online battles. Our goal is to combine a third party platform with Facebook’s new Producer environment to create a multilayered and interactive digital event space.

Designed primarily for Beatbox Battles, this platform can be used for a variety of battles/competitions, including dance, visual art, rap, poetry, tricks etc.
