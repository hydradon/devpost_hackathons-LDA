Sigtrap Presents: THE CRYSTAL CURSE!

Entries: Company Team Microsoft VR

Soundtrack: https://soundcloud.com/2-byte/sets/jamchester-music-tracks/s-UfWXP

Inspiration

Inspired by Crystal Maze, Knightmare and a bit of Indiana Jones

What it does

It's a social / local MP VR game where one player dons the headset to enter a virtual Aztec maze, and the rest of their team helps them out from the second screen. Or more likely shout over each other until the player plunges to their spikey death.

How we built it

Built in Unity using the SteamVR plugin.

Challenges I ran into

VR! Integrating a multiple-display solution was tricky both technically and in terms of game design, trying to give the player and the team enough varied information to force communication.
