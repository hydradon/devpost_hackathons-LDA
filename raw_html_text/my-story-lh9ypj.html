Inspiration

Tackling the 2019 Hopperhacks challenge of designing an app to fix a health issue.

What it does

Tackles two roots of the modern loneliness epidemic by combating maladaptive thinking and teaching social skills to children.

How we built it

After researching effective classroom techniques used to teach emotional management, we used Xcode & swift to create the app.

Challenges we ran into

Navigating through our different experiences with coding and pulling together a project in 12 hours.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Even thought we came from various backgrounds we were able to create an app that is not only functional, but aesthetically appealing.

What we learned

We all learned more about coding and gained an even greater appreciation for apps.

What's next for My Story

It’s possible that we pitch the app to companies after cleaning up the code, and are considering extending our reach to older teens with a journaling feature.
