This app was designed to help friends and family of opioid users, as well as community members who are concerned about their loved one's well being. It can also be used by opioid users or recovering opioid users to find resources to help themselves.

The app clearly outlines the steps to take when one encounters someone who he or she might suspect might be overdosing on opioids. The app has links to many helpful resources on how best to help those coping with opioid addiction.

This app was developed for the SAMHSA's Opioid Overdose Prevention Challenge.

The app can be used by downloading it from the Google play store onto most modern Android devices in the U.S. (check Google play for compatibility). It requires Android 2.2 and up.
