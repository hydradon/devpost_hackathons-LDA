Buying textbooks is often very expensive and as a new student it is difficult to find textbooks for the cheapest prices. I learned more about ISBN verification and web scraping to get information from websites. We used Ionic 4 with Angular to build the front-end and a JavaScript server to scrape information from the desired websites. We ran into problems with avoiding sponsored posts on Amazon when scraping information and with converting and verifying ISBN numbers.
