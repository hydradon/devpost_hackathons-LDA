Inspiration

Pizza is one of the most popular foods on the planet. We want to make it available off planet as well.

What it does

Our project lays out how to produce the ingredients needed to make pizza in space.

How we built it

During the Astropreneurship, Space Medicine, and the Dataverse Hackathon 2019, we researched methods to grow the three base ingredients of pizza, wheat, tomatoes, and cheese.

Challenges we ran into

Access to consistent, reliable, information.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Summarized ongoing research in three food production areas. Bringing them all together to estimate when space pizza will be available.

What we learned

Growing food in space, sustainably is extremely difficult, but if we want to enjoy Space Pizza we'll have to figure out a way
