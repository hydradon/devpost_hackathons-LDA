• Introduction

We recognized a large problem currently facing our youth was the lack of financial literacy during a developmental age, where even though we are allowed to enter the work force at age 16, we are provided with no information on how to budget. Thus, we choose to create a fun platform game to make financial literacy more accessible and appealing to a wider audience. Throughout, the game we provide important economic lesson, and definition to aid users to make more informed decisions on their budgeting.
