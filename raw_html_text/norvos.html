Inspiration

When I discovered TextSecure, I wanted to recommend it to my friends and others from a discussion group I am involved in. A lot of them though could not use the app because they don't own smartphones. I decided that a desktop TextSecure client would enable these people to communicate in a safe way while keeping them from the complexity of understanding encryption.

What it does

Norvos allows people to communicate in an encrypted way and exchange text messages as well as multimedia messages.
