REVOLVO

Rent a car anywhere and anytime in less than 5 clicks with the best price in the market

Background

You live in a flat and you do not own a car. One day in a Sunday morning, your mom living in 2 hours distance called you to come home for a family dinner. You realized that you need to a car to get there, but it takes too much time and effort to rent one. Not mentioning the long and manual verification process you need to go through.

Meet Revolvo

Our Seamless Process

  1. Download and register yourself using a photo of you & your driver license
  2. Get verified within a second, then choose your favorite car that is available around your flat
  3. Pay and go visit your mom! :family:

Stack Technology

  • Automated Driver Verification using Amazon Deep Learning technology
  • Distributed Blockchain Network that enables secure data storage & scalable Know Your Customer (KYC) process compared to traditional KYC
  • Mobile Apps written in React Native
  • Dashboard to monitor real time booking usage written in React & NodeJS

Our Visions

Seamless access to car booking so people can move faster, environmental footprints can be reduced

What's next?

Leveraging Machine Learning & Spatio Temporal data to predict the trend of car rental usages per-area
