Inspiration

We never realized the impact food has on green house gas emissions. The production and processing CO2 emissions alone are astounding. We believe that with this easily accessible knowledge, students can change their dietary patterns to significantly lower their carbon footprint.

What it does

The user inputs a dining vendor and food choice based on the vendor. Then an algorithm outputs an equivalent CO2 kg, car miles, and FoodPrint score.

How I built it

We developed a java algorithm based on research conducted that calculates equivalent carbon emissions in kg per meal based on criteria found in research. These included production, processing, and operations.
