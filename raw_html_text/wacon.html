Inspiration

The average Saudi consumption is 265 liters per person, which is 2x the world average consumption . And Household leaks can waste more than 1 trillion gallons annually nationwide.

What it does

Disaggregate the water usage in into Kitchen,Washing,Shower etc., from the Residential water meter data. Create insights to end-users and alerts when there is a water leak. Water balancing and feed the data to the central utility management system

How we built it

Using the Arduino & Cloud IoT platform the water management dashboard can be created.

Challenges we ran into

  • Building the dashboard.
  • Simulation constant flow of water (leak)

Accomplishments that we're proud of

  • The system works flawlessly.
  • Simple UX.
  • Beautiful UI.

What we learned

  • Manage and distribute tasks.
  • Dealing with Arduino and reading sensors reads.

What's next for Wacon

  • Apply Machine learning to disaggregate the water consumption using water flow trace signature.
  • Expanding the system to cover every aspects of water management and analysis.
  • Improve the user experience and automate some part of the system.
