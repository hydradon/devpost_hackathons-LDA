Inspiration

We found the Prudential Contextual Chatbot challenge as it combined aspects of database knowledge and aspects of machine learning and artificial intelligence. We as a group thought this would be a good idea to partake in this challenge as it would expose us to techniques, topics, and APIs that we have no experience with.

What it does

The Prudential Chatbox is an online chatbot for Prudential to facilitate a variety of simple tasks for their customers.
