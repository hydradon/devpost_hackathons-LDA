Inspiration

Our idea came from Annie's little brother who was wondering: "What if I could take a picture of anything and turn it into an emoji?". We decided to make it happen!

What it does

Our app let's the user either take or upload an image, and detects the edges of the object depicted. It then removes everything outside of the borders, allowing the user to create a sticker.

Challenges we ran into
