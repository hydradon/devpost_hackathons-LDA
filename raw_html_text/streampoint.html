Inspiration

We, as a society, constantly need to "rapidly create" (read: wing) presentations. Starting today, we have the technology to do so. You're welcome.

What it does

StreamPoint is the world's first on-the-fly presentation. StreamPoint generates a presentation as you talk, using natural language processing and automated vocal transcription, with content powered by the Bing Image API, Stream, WolframAlpha, and Azure. We've entered a new era of presentations and story-telling: show the audience without looking up anything, demonstrate physical components without passing anything around, and impress your crowd without memorizing (or knowing) a single fact.
