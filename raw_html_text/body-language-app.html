Inspiration: People with Asperger's and other social disorders have a limited capacity to read body language

How it works: Gestures are evaluated for indications of anxiety, confidence, and other emotions. The program returns a summary of what emotions the person is experiencing.

Challenges we ran into: Two group members left early, and we encountered difficulty in integrating all aspects of the project.

Accomplishments that we're proud of: We believe that we gained valuable knowledge and experience in the creation of this app.

What's next for SocialQ: We plan to add graphics and animation to the SocialQ app.
