Inspiration

The lack of 100% accurate bottle flipping in my life

What it does

It flips bottles with 100% repeatability (if it doesn't work the fault is yours)

How I built it

Laser cut plywood, 3D printed sensor mount, assemble, copy Arduino Sweep example, profit

Challenges I ran into

They told me the plywood was 5 mm thick, but it was 6. I had to do some additional filing and swearing

What I learned

1) Always have a caliper handy 2) Never share all your knowledge 3)

What's next for FLIPPOBOT L

I have received an offer from US government to include it in the next generation of military drones
