About mindvibes

We wanted to create something fun while learning new skills, so we decided to use our love of music to create mindvibes, a web platform where a user can input their Myers-Briggs personality type after taking an online or official quiz. Once the user knows their four-letter personality type, they can get a playlist that we feel relates to people who share their specific personality type. Hope you enjoy our project!
