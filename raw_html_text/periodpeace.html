• Period Peace

Inspiration

The period crisis of homeless women in NY. This app solves that problem.

What it does

Connects women with shelters that have tampons/pads and connects donors with organizations in need.

How I built it

I used Sketch to design the prototype and then used Xcode + Swift to execute. And used firebase for the database to save the users.

Challenges I ran into

Connecting people to get a tampon.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Able to have a good design. And the app functions good.

What I learned

How to access firebase with swift. How to make gradients in sketch. More about constraints.

What's next for PeriodPeace

The website version.
