Inspiration

It's difficult to think up the best pokemon that would fit in your team, or counter an opponent's team. Let pokeQuery do all the thinking.

How it works

Fill in a simple form, telling the engine the criteria of your desired Pokemon, and it will give you recommendations.

Challenges I ran into

Setting up the database was difficult because an API I relied on turned out to be incomplete. Had to resort to a under-clocked scraping script in the end.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The decisions of which filters get applied first was difficult, but make the app much faster. As a competitive Pokemon player, I am often satisfied with the results.

What I learned

Scraping other websites. Learned the MEAN stack

What's next for pokeQuery

Better options in the form. Multiple pages in result.
