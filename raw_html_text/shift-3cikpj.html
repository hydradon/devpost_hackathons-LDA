Inspiration

To help people avoid using clunky workaround like Facebook posts to find rideshares with people in their university community.

What it does

Implements an interactive calendar that streamlines the process of finding community members to share rides with.

How I built it

React, Node.js, Express, MongoDB

Challenges I ran into

Production deployment, coordinating database with front-end

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Succeeded in building a functional app in 24 hours!
