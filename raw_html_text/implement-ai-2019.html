• TBD

Inspiration

TBD or Total Body Development is a tool for maximizing your daily workout efficiency. Despite committing hours and hours to fitness, many people fail to achieve their goals because of poor form. TBD compares your workout form to an ideal example and guides your posture towards to the correct form.

What it does?

TBD leverages the wrnchAI’s pose estimation API to observe your body posture during your workout and analyzes the deviations from the ideal workout form to help users improve their gains!

How we built it?

The backend was developed using python and wrnchAI cloud.The wrnchAI cloud was used to estimate the user’s pose in the workout recordings. The estimated pose was then analyzed in python and evaluated using the ideal form for the exercise as a reference.

Challenges we ran into
