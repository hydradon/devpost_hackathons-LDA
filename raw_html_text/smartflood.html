Inspiration

In dealing with this flood disaster, Jakarta provincial government developed Smart City platform which involves public since it has an issue-reporting app known as Qlue and a flood map that crowdsources citizen flood reports from Twitter, called PetaJakarta. The government needs to input data from Qlue and Twitter and validate it. It would usually take a long time since the data is not informative enough, e.g. no detailed location and no water level.

What it does

Smartflood is an application that people can participate to input and get information in the same time. Input data consist of report of flood and the informtaion will be got by people is the best location shelter and evacuation route.
