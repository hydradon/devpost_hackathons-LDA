VRShopper

VRShopper is the future of shopping. Using the latest technology, you can shop from the comfort of your VR headset and phone. We built this application on the premise of how online shoppers do not get a tangible feel of what the product actually is like until it arrives. With VRShopper, we are able to render your living space with an overlay of the products in your shopping cart. This allows you to know how the product looks in your living space risk free, which not only expedites the process for making a concrete decision, but also saves us the hassle of shipping and handling in a hypothetical situation where you desire a refund. It's a win-win for everyone.
