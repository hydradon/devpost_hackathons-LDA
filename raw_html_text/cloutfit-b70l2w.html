Inspiration

The weather in Austin is too crazy. I don't have to time to figure out if its cold or hot outside and what to wear. I just want to wake up, look at my app, and wear what it tells me to.

What it does

Cloutfit helps users pick out their clothes for the day by checking which clothes they have available and by checking the weather

How we built it

Cloutfit was built in Flutter, a mobile app framework, and the underlying database was Firebase. We used Flutter because it exports to both iOS and Android. Github was also used for version control and to integrate our individual assignments

Challenges we ran into

Flutter is fairly new, so it has some bugs. Two of us were using Linux so the build files were messed up for the Windows users.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We have a fully functional login system and clothes storing database.

What we learned

We learned how to develop mobile apps and connect them to work with a database.

What's next for CloutFit

Integrate a weather checker for better cloth picking options. Also have a better cloth picking algorithm to have a better choice of color.
