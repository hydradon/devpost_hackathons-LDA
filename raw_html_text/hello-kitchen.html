Inspiration

Families and amateur household cooks needs to find inspiration of what eat on a daily or weekly basis and 80% of customer who enter a grocery store, actually do not know what to buy.

What it does

Hello Kitchen listens to which ingredients you already have at home, and uses that to recommend easy to cook recipes. The vision is to create weekly menus that minimises food waste and yet gives a high variety of dishes that are easy and fun to cook.

How I built it

By building a Java backend running Heroku and AWS, serving an Amazon Alex skill.

Challenges I ran into

Voice Interface is a rather new and exciting, but its also difficult to create intuitive voice interfaces.
