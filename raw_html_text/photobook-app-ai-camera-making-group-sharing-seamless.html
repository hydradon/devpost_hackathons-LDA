The Story of a Smarter Camera

We started off with a dream of creating a software product which entire world will use and benefit from. May be first of its kind from India !

We picked up a pain which most the us(smartphone users) are facing, which is the long process of sharing media to the right set of people once the media is created. Solutions like Google Photos and Facebook Moments are working on solving the same issue but we wanted make it even better and engaging.

Our solution is addressing the problem right at the root cause which in this is the camera. Our vision was to create a camera which will "share media with right set of people and also save media on cloud, the moment the media is created"

Our solution will suggest user where to share the media right before it is being created. Thus user can right away create and share media being created. We are creating this solution with the help of AI based suggestion , Live Camera Face Recognition, Location Identification etc., and we are bringing fun aspects with help of AR technology which is focusing on group based sharing.

We have features to which enable users to

  • create large Groups(Communities/Events) instantly. Users can search and request to join new events.
  • share media/information in that large events instantly
  • make media sharing in large groups engaging and fun with the help of AR The above will help not only faster media/information sharing among community members but also enable crowd based content creation with respect to that Community/Event.

With help of face recognition, we are planning to even make the Event registrations simpler. And also make event based sharing engaging with help of AR.
