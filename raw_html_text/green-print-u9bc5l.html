Inspiration: What's sustainable? Not our current lifestyle and our rampant and ever growing C02 emissions into the atmosphere.

What it does: Takes information about your mode of transportation, foods you eat, and potentially other activities to produce a score representing your "green print". A score representing how sustainable your actions are.

How I built it: We used Python to create an algorithm that calculated a users production of CO2 within

Challenges I ran into: Bringing the Front End to life and integrating it with our Backend.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of: Showing up to a Hackathon and trying to create something.

What I learned: Learned how to apply Pandas and Numpy with Python and and how to source data from a variety of resources to create factual information, statistics, and averages.

What's next for Green Print: Turning it into a full-fledged website that users can more easily interact with and also allows a more varying amount of information given.
