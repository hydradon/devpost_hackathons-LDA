Inspiration

Foody Call is an app that aims to connect people in order to sell or buy homemade food. The app is trying to promote healthy eating and reduce food waste. Besides its positive effect on health and environment, Foody Call is a nonprofit that donates all its revenue to homeless shelters. We believe everyone has a right to have access to healthy food!

What it does

Connects people in order to sell or buy homemade food!

How we built it

Behind the Scenes of Making Foody Call Foody Call is developed using Firebase. Other technologies used in making this app, especially its front-end were JavaScript, CSS, and HTML.

Challenges we ran into

None of the team members were experts in web development.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We managed to learn new technologies on the go, in a very short time and have built a functional web app while having a lot of fun. Making awesome friends!

What we learned

Teamwork and developed/learned new technologies, such as Firebase, JavaScript etc.

What's next for Foody Call

Android and IOS (indeed)
