Inspiration

My wife's goal is to produce media. Her son recommended she start with a podcast. She knew nothing about podcasts, but built a team and is now producing these devotions which are released each weekday. A weekly podcast is starting on Oct 21.

What it does

Spot On is a 10 minute devotional which gives a person one word for that day. Music and commentary included.

How I built it

Once she had the concept, the team came together to provide the content. The result is a high quality audio production.

Challenges I ran into

Personal challenges of the team members have been significant. However, the team is excited about the project and is finding ways to make it happen.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

That in the face of all the challenges, the team is getting the product out there and delivering high quality on time.

What I learned

I learned how to make a podcast and get it published. I give a lot of credit to our webmaster for setting us up to succeed in publication.

What's next for Spot On With the Word

Keep producing and launch the podcast interviews on Oct 21.
