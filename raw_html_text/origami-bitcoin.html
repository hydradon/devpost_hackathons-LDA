Inspiration

I wanted to make a video that is both visually entertaining and informative of why Bitcoin matters. I wanted to focus on 6 major features that Bitcoin provides to its users, which are that Bitcoin is decentralized, it banks the unbanked, it protects identity, it allows microtransactions, it's transparent, and it's a reliable store of value.

How I built it

Construction paper, sharpie, folding, and duct taping my selfie stick to a cardboard box so that I could record the entire video on my phone.

Challenges I ran into

Learning how to use Premiere Pro for the first time. Fitting everything into 60 seconds!

What's next for Origami Bitcoin

Origami structures with more than 6 pieces to actually fit all the use cases of Bitcoin!
