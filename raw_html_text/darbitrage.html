dArbitrage

Arbitrage is the simultaneous purchase and sale of an asset to profit from an imbalance in the price. dArbitrage (iOS) allows you to exploit price differences that exist between decentralized markets of Kyber Network and Bancor.

The app uses Kyber Network's iOS widget and Bancor's market price ticker API.
