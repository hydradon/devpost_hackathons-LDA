Inspiration

We love DIY projects, but often find ourselves searching for ones that use just the materials we have on hand. EcoDIY's goal is to provide users with eco-friendly DIY projects by empowering them to create crafts out of everyday recyclable materials.

What it does

Finds DIY projects based on the materials you have available.

How we built it

HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

Challenges we ran into

Creating the website and making the Javascript work.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Able to design an intuitive UI that is easy to use.

What we learned

UI Design and Web Development.

What's next for EcoDIY

  1. Users have the option of choosing materials from categories instead of typing them
  2. Grab DIY projects from the internet
  3. Allow users to upload their own DIY projects
