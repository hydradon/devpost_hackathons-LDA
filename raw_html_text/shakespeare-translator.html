Inspiration

Remember the old days when it was difficult to read Shakespeare? Well now, it's difficult to read Hobbes, and we've had enough.

What it does

It takes any text you want written in Shakespearean English and turns it into contemporary English. Hopefully, you can understand it.

How we built it

We used a Flask backend, and Google's APIs. That's it!
