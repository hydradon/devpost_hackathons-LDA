• AssistantBot

Inspiration

A smart bot for Slack that provide knowledge search platform that students can capture, learn knowledge and access directly to the knowledge provided.

What it does

Provide knowledge search platform for students who use Slack. Search platform including Google search, Wikipedia search, WolframAlpha computational knowledge engine, Youtube video search.Students then can capture knowledge that is created in inside Slack conversations and have access to expert-verified information directly in Slack.

How I built it

NodeJS with slack bot API

Challenges I ran into

Train the bot to be smart as human :)

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Create a chat bot and learn about AI and Natural language processing

What I learned

AI, pubic APIs, Slack chat bot

What's next for AssistantBot

Train it smarter.
