Inspiration

I learned that the best way to develop one's physical body is with intensive and critical feedback. There is no better way to push yourself than with a superior being incessantly berating your health.

What it does

Shia LaBUFF is a fully functional fitness application that tracks heart pulse, distance travelled, time running, and number of steps. Heartbeat information is measured with an AppleWatch. Data can be stored with Apple's new HealthKit framework for iOS.

How I built it

I got the classic Shia LaBeouf speech from online, split it into its different unique "sound bites", and set up a fitness algorithm to decide when to play the sounds. Steps are determined using unique X, Y, and Z calculations, distance travelled is calculated with the CoreLocation iOS library, and heartbeat is calculated with the new HealthKit framework.

Challenges I ran into
