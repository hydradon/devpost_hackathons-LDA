Inspiration

We saw a lot of articles about siblings fighting over parents' inheritance upon the parents' dismiss

What it does

Bliss On Earth is an E–will where you can make a will on an app by taking the pictures of all relevant documents or prized possession and to be signed by the person and his two witnesses. In the event, when the person cannot write his will, he can record or do a video of his possession
