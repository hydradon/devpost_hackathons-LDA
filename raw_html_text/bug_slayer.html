Bug_Slayer

We have attempted in creating an app for tracking the location of captive elephants. This will use Geofence. For now, the app has the login and registration pages for the CWLW officials, medical officials of the forest department. It will show all essential information of the owner and their elephants. It also has the Alert button for the 'unanonymous' user to send alerts while witnessing some untoward activity involving the elephant.
