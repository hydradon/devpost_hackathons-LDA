Inspiration

Due to increase in pollution in our society, every person should play a role in it's reduction. Here is where we come in, by pitching our idea for Smart Bins.

What it does

Basically we are automating the government's already in use sanitation system. We are aiding our government with automated and smart system.

How we built it

We are administering the garbage in the dumpsters and notifying the authorities in time. Further more, we are coming with a unique idea for a smart bins where it would generate an alert on it's status to the authorities.

Challenges we ran into

Finding the refined solution and conveying the solution to the proper authorities. The awareness of garbage solution to the people is essential.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Mobile and Web interface. Sensors

What we learned

Team and time management Pressure handling

What's next for Smart Bins

Data Mining and Data analytics.
