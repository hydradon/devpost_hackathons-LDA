Inspiration

Veterans have trouble traversing the maze of resources and we wanted to create an app that helps organize the information making it easier for them to find the right path. Secondly, we wanted to be able to connect veterans to other veterans for peer support, guidance, and brotherhood.

What it does

It tailors resources to a veterans needs base on an interview

Built it

Built on visual studio with asp.net

Challenges we ran into

Finding a database to access and connecting to a database

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The peer support system- The brotherhood

What we learned

How to use bootstrap. Working with a team and working on a time clock

What's next for Templers-Wellness Brotherhood

The next step is to implement ai to autonomously retrieve new resources and provide them to veterans that are in need of the resource. Working on connection with other members
