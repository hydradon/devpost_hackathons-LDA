Inspiration

I'm taking a class on image analysis this semester, and wanted to put some of it to practice!

What it does

Just put your bowl of cereal in front of the camera and let the machine fill it with milk!

How I built it

OpenCV and SciKit-Learn on a Raspberry Pi

Challenges I ran into

Figuring out the best feature representation for cereal bowls

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It works! It can properly identify cereal bowls a good enough percentage of the time
