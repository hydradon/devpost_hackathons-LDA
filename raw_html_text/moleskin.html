What it does

You can take a picture or upload one through your gallery and the program decide whether it believes the mole is malignant and benign.

How we built it

We used Android Studio to build an Android app along with Firebase as the database. We also used Jupyter notebook and Python scripts to parse data.

Challenges we ran into

Accomplishments that we're proud of
