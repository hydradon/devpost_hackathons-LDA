What it does

Enable customers to order custom drinks using their smartphone. Mixes juices and alcohol to create different mixes.

How we built it

Front End: HTML & CSS magic. Classic

Backend: Flask & Socket.io

Plumbing: Sink tubing from Home Depot

Valve control: CNC controller and stepper motors attached to the valves. GCODE through serial.

Cable management: Zip ties

Challenges we ran into

  • Making the plubming connections
  • Vacuum seal with glass bottles
  • weak stepper motors

Accomplishments that we're proud of

  • It can pour a drink

What we learned

  • Plumbing parts can be expensives when you try to convert 1/2 inch to 1/16 inch.
  • gravity is useful
