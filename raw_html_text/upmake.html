Inspiration- Celebrating diversity with the medium of makeup

What it does- A platform to connect individuals looking to enhance their beauty through customized tutorials by makeup experts.

How we built it- TEAM WORK! We used sketch and Marvel to put our idea together.

Challenges we ran into- Building the prototype

Accomplishments that we're proud of- We understood there was a gap between what the other services are offering and what customers want. We did a primary research to support our idea.

What we learned- The primary research concluded that there was a need of customized makeup tutorials for individuals, keeping in mind the skin type, skin color, features, etc.

What's next for UpMake- Build a tool that would automatically help identify the facial features and find the right customized solution for the individual.
