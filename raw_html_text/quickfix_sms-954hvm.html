Inspiration

FAQ on the SLC courthouse website. The use case was about people who have trouble proving their auto insurance is up to date.

What it does

Simplifies the process for citizens, insurance providers, and the courts in regards to providing proof of insurance. However, this platform is flexible enough to add other common quick fix citations.

How we built it

Matthew was the Business Guru, Calvin was the front end specialist, and Thomas was the primary back end developer.

Challenges we ran into

Coordinating work in such a way that everyone would be productive on a consistent basis.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are very proud of how many edge cases we were able to discover when utilizing the service.

What we learned

It was very intriguing to learn about the status of the Justice systems technology capabilities. There are definitely some limitations ripe for innovation.

What's next for quickfix_sms

With the continuing trend of companies providing APIs for the their services. We would love to evolve the service from processes like automated emails sent to insurance providers to real time API calls.
