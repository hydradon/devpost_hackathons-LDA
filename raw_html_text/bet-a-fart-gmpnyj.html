As international students, we have always had the issue of not being able to transport everything we would want to in our luggage. Enter Columbus. With Columbus, you can request for luggage from anywhere in the world by leveraging the extra space in other traveller's luggage. The advantage this form of transport offers is a huge difference in price compared to traditional transport services. And best of all, anyone can earn that good cash for just having extra space in their luggage.
