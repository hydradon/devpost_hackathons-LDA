Inspiration

Imaigine you can see the picture you have in your mind with your own eyes in your own room.

Imagine you can share this picture with an interlocutor.

Such opportunity will give you a lot of fun and will provide an absolutely new way of communication.

What it does

There would be two products:

  1. A web-site, rendering 3d-scene from its simple-text description

  2. An application for VR-glasses, rendering a scene from voice description and allowing to share this scene online with your friends

How we built it

For the hackathon we've chosen to create a prototype of the first product since it's more likely to be developed in 36 hours.

We've used pymorphy to pick out nouns and adjectives related to them, some stl-models that could be found in free access, react for the interface and threejs for rendering.
