Inspiration

Abusive relationships bring harm to many people. Whether its romantic, family, or even professional. Many individuals don't even know the extent to which their partner can manipulate and control their happiness.

What it does

It is a website that lets you to anonymously analyze your message history with someone. You can see different metrics that characterize abusive patterns in the communication. It is also a community for sharing information and reaching out to help others in need.

How I built it

It is just standard web tech.

Challenges I ran into

I could not find enough data over the time period to train any pattern classifiers or sentiment analysis.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I designed my first vector graphics for this project.

What I learned

I learned some design techniques, how to manipulate files/blobs via in-browser JavaScript, and about abusive patterns in general.

What's next for We Rise By Lifting Others

This type of NLP/AI is relatively innovative and would interesting to see how far it can be used to help people. This platform can be applied in different ways.
