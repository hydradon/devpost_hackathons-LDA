What it does

BotConnect helps match refugees/migrants (subscribers) with volunteer hosts (providers) to provide resources they may need in the upcoming days, weeks, and months.

Matches are based on location and personal interests, and users can always accept or decline any match requests. Once the match is initiated, users can chat with each other through BotConnect, to prevent misuse of the platform.
