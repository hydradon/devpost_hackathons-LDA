Inspiration

It's really hard to find people in a crowd, and it's really hard to keep giving directions to people who can't find you. We created a fun and simple way to locate your friends without having to zoom in on a map location or relying on pictures of your meeting point.

What it does

wya uses your phone's camera and augmented reality to pinpoint your friends' locations.

How we built it

wya is built for android. It uses the phone's camera view and phone GPS location to calculate the relevant AR feature positions to overlay on the screen.

Challenges we ran into

The biggest challenge we ran into was incompatibility with existing AR libraries. We overcame this challenge by writing our own AR framework from scratch.

Another challenge was the inaccuracy of the GPS sensors. We used low pass filtering to mitigate the effect.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We're really proud of our simple UI and the implementation of our own AR framework!

What we learned

We learned a lot about augmented reality and android app development.

What's next for wya

Our vision for wya includes:

  • improving location accuracy
  • support for finding more people
  • integrating Facebook friends
  • adding facial recognition (so you can find people you don't know too)
