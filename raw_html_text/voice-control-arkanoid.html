Inspiration

The topic is about art. We thought combining paintings and games is a good approach to bring users a sense of participation.

What it does

Voice-control Arkanoid is a simple voice-control version of Arkanoid. Users can move a paddle by their voice loudness and slow the speed of the ball by voice frequency.
