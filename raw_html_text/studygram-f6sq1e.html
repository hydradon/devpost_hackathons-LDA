We were inspired to make this application because we want to bring value STEM students together in a community for group polarization making students better, stronger, smarter and more efficient students. StudyGram makes it possible for engineers to share projects and use technologies like LaTex, to prove test and share information.

StudyGram makes is possible for students to connect with each other regardless of location and institution.

Making this application was very tedious and required consideration on niche issues that engineers face.
