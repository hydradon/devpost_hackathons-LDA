Inspiration

Seeing the Pulse 2 speakers demoed at the Harman area

What it does

It's a music game whose visuals are entirely on the Pulse 2 speaker's LEDs, controlled on an iPhone. As in similar music games, game is synced with the music.

How we built it

Using Xcode, Swift, and the Pulse 2 SDK

Challenges we ran into

Getting the sample code to cooperate

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting it working (and fun) on time

What we learned

Having fun is the most important thing!
