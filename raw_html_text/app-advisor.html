Inspiration

I and Gin are first-year students still trying to choose our classes. We thought it would be nice to have a web page that helps students like us.

What it does

Provides students information about recommended classes and TA hours according to majors and class years they choose.

How we built it

Use Javascript and HTML to design web pages that allow students to choose their majors and link it to another page where students can select their class years.

Challenges we ran into

Learning HTML from scratch

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting our first steps

What we learned

HTML, Arduino

What's next for App Advisor

Get more majors and the 4-grade levels. More detailed and specific advising.
