ARCHives is the newest innovation within the medical community created to increase the efficiency and productivity among dentists and their patients. Whether you’re switching dentists, seeking a second opinion or was recommended for a consultation with a new specialist who requires knowledge of your dental history - ARCHives delivers said history at your fingertips. The average patient may not be fully aware of medical descriptions, but direct contact with medical records can ease both the roles of the patient and the dentist. Dentists can now be fully aware of the situation and approach appointments more effectively.
