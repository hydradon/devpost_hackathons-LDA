Bike Bud

Introduction

The Seattle city has a well developed infrastructure for cyclists. Bikes are a great way to get around while minimizing our carbon footprint. However, we also need racks to park our bikes when we reach our destination. A common problem, similar to finding parking for cars, is that we arrive at our destination only to find that all the bike racks have completely filled up.

Solution

Enter Bike Bud. This application crowd sources bike rack availability from the public. Find open bike racks near you!

Features

  1. Find nearby Bike Racks
  2. Find your location
  3. Determine the availability of a bike rack
  4. Determine the time since last update
