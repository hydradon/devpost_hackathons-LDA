Inspiration

AR is limited in terms of ability to create content due to the lacking forms of interaction, despite VR creating new means of content creation and prototyping. We seek to unlock that for AR.

What it does

Enables everyone to design and create content in real space, onto the real world.

How we built it

Using ARKit and Unity, with AR image tracking and world.

Challenges we ran into

Camera auto-focusing away from the tracking icon. Networking the AR data between 2 phones

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Everything (:
