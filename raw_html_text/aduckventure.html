Inspiration

Both of us are quite fascinated with the mechanics of flight and thought it would be interesting to make a game involving flight.

How it works

The player controls a floating duck to gather all the coins and grapes.

Challenges I ran into

The physics of the flight and the angling of the camera presented significant difficulties.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We produced this with no prior knowledge of Unity, the game engine we used for this project.
