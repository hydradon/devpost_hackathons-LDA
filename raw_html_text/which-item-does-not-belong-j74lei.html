Inspiration

I feel that Alexa provides a huge learning advantage in classrooms and at home. With additional skills, the potential is unimaginable. A simple game offered by Alexa can help children learn more quickly and be beneficial to those with learning difficulties.

What it does it do

Which Item Does Not Belong helps with learning object recognition and association as well as speech pronunciation.

Three difficulties available: Easy, Medium and Hard. A simple gamified experience where you are presented with a list of objects based within a similar category. Choose the correct object that does not belong to move on to the next level and complete the game.

Whats next for Which Item Does Not Belong

Additional free levels and possible in skill purchase based on usage metrics.
