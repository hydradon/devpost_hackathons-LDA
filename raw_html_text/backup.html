Description

BACKUP IS A GOVERNMENT REGULATED APP, SPECIALIZED FOR EACH REGION, ACTIVATED WHEN IN STATE OF CRISIS.

Goals

THE GOAL IS FOR EVERY CITIZEN TO BE ABLE TO SEEK HELP AS SOON AS POSSIBLE; AND FOR RESPONDERS TO BE ABLE TO ASSESS CIRCUMSTANCES AND TAKE THE RIGHT ACTIONS IN AN ORGANIZED MANNER WITH FILTERED DATA ACQUIRED BY USERS.

Functionality

BACKUP ALLOWS ONE PLATFORM TO EXSIST FOR ALL TO TURN TO AND PUT THEIR TRUST IN.
