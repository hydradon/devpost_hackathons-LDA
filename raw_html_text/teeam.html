Change makes it easy to make regular donations and keep track of your donation history all in one place.

The Problem: People encounter barriers to making regular charitable donations: -Searching for trustworthy organizations -Too much choice -Managing multiple accounts -Keeping track of receipts -People envision large donations

Challenges: Many iOS tutorial were outdated and using the early version of the Swift programming language. We had to keep the syntax difference in mind during our version of implementation.

Accomplishments: Working together successfully without meeting prior to the project. Built a working MVP of our original idea that was originally fully fleshed out.
