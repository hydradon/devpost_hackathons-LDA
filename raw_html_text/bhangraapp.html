BhangraHero

Have you always been interested in learning a new type of dance but can't find a way to learn? Then BhangraHero is the app for you! It is an online tutor that allows you to learn the fundamentals of bhangra, and get realtime, detailed feedback!

BhangraHero makes use of the Microsoft Kinect's high end 3D tracking features to closely study each aspect of the user's motion, and is then able to both sequence these moves, and cross analyze the user's execution of moves with that of our team of bhangra experts!

Together, our app's detailed analysis and tested feedback techniques will make sure you look your swaggiest when you preform!
