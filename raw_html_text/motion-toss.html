What it does

It is quite simply just a game that lets you use the capabilities of the Leap Motion such as seeing your hand on a screen and grabbing/throwing objects.

How I built it

I used Unity, C#, and the Leap Motion

Challenges I ran into

When you attempt to throw, your hand moves faster, so the Leap Motion has a harder time tracking your hand. This often makes it pretty challenging to throw. I hacked my way around it as much as I could, but it's still far from perfect.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am pretty proud of being able to learn how to develop for a new hardware and create something for it in a very short time frame.

What I learned

I learned how to use and develop for the Leap Motion, one of the most exciting technologies of our time!
