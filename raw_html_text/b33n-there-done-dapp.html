Inspiration

Blockchain is cool, but complicated

What it does

We have built burner type wallet for events which utilizes blockchain technologies and hides them from regular user.

  • Quick onboarding as easy as reading QR code
  • Pay vendors with your data and collect different partners merchandise or NFT's
  • Instant off-chain payments between participants and event vendors
  • Collect proof of attendance NFT's
  • Attract audience to your projects by creating quizzes or encounter styled tasks
