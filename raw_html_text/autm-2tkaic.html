~INSPIRATION

We were inspired to create SolRBC product (the solar powered mobile ATM) as it enables users to "not have to carry any cash on the road, but never run out of it."

~THOUGHT PROCESS

Our team modified the standard ATM as we all related to the difficulties of using this machine if cash is needed.

~CHALLENGES

An obstacle we faced revolved around brainstorming ideas to make the standard ATM unique while being revolutionary, as many ideas which we thought of initially were already fabricated on the spot. We overcame this hardship by working together and combining each of our ideas to tackle both convenience and energy efficiency and finally designed this product.

~RESULT

It is without doubt that convenience is nonnegotiable, so this is why we strove to make this innovation as convenient as possible, while giving a little back to our Earth!
