Inspiration

Make people live with law more easy ans safe!

What it does

Provides access to the law projects you initiated, allowes you to track its stage, get related documents and information access. Starting new project is really easy -- you can see what to expect from it at the very beginning.

How we built it

We used Django for the server and android app as main product. We also used scipy on Python to do statistics for price estimation and web3 framework for blockchain to make documents downloads safe.

Challenges we ran into

For us it was a challenge to deal with law firm data, to make a good understanding of it.
