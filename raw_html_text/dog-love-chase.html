Inspiration

Dog Love chase is a mobile and web app that tells the history of a dog named Bingo that faces some challenges to save his love, Sandy. At the final he meets her and they live happy forever.

What it does

Bingo perform some movements and turn on some objects in the garden to put Killer far of his goals.

How we built it

We built it using Unity 3D, C# and Photoshop. Our team had two designers and two programmers.

Challenges we ran into

He needs to front a bad dog named Killer what is in the middle of its way.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud to build a prototype in less than 24 hours,

What we learned

We learned more about Unity 3D and mobile games.

What's next for Dog Love Chase

We expect to complete the game and them public in several app stores.
