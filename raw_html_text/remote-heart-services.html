Inspiration

The Thailand problem appealed to us as third world countires do not have easy access to healthcare services. This was an attempt to introduce an idea, to provide a solution to this problem.

What it does

A platform for remote & real-time EKG relay of data, for a cost-effective and early detection of cardiac disease.

How we built it

We got ECG & PPG anonymized data acquired from Misceo Grand technologies, visualised in side-chart. We employed Web-RTC enabled video calling using Temasys API. Data is accessed using Firebase. Machine learning is used in anomaly detection algorithm, to detect heart-beat anomalies.

Challenges we ran into

The potential of the project is big, and we had to narrow it down to a specific use-case. Additionally, it was a big learning curve in understanding how to interact with the electrophysiology data. Integrating the chart display software into the app was difficult, especially in real-time. It was the team's first time to use Firebase.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

  1. Setting up video conferencing
  2. Visualized the data taken from the sensor, on the smartphone screen
  3. Integrating the Fire-base with our web-app
  4. Integrating our solution with one of the biggest health and social burdens: heart failure.
