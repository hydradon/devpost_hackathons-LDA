Inspiration

I wanted to have a To Do list by my side wherever I go, laptop or mobile devices. Also, I want to bring house chores to a whole new level of fun.

What it does

  1. A personal to do list.
  2. Make house chores exciting.
  3. Contest: People can pool money together to see who can do the most chores and winner takes all.
  4. Penalty: Late fees are applied to overdue chores to keep users on their game. Money will be donated to local charities.

How I built it

The backend is built using Firebase. Frontend is built with HTML5, CSS3, jQuery and bootstrap.

Challenges I ran into

Some backend updating issues.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

My clean UI.

What I learned
