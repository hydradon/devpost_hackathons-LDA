Inspiration

Create a game involving satoshis and the lightning network that is provably fair that actually explains how it is provably fair. Thought it would be nice to use it as a teaching point about the power of hashing and could be a nice segway to people better understanding how bitcoin works if they were curious. The instant withdrawals and deposits showcase the power of lightning.
