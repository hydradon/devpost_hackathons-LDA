Inspiration

Artificial intelligence

What it does

Player can interact with an "Artificial Intelligence" in Unity, player can tell the AI to switch weapons, reload weapons, and more.

How I built it

Unity, VRTK, IBM Watson services

Challenges I ran into

Getting voice commands to register. IBM Watson provides 100 minutes of free use for the speech to text services. I ended up going over this limit and this caused our services to be cancelled and broke our entire project. I had to call IBM this morning and they didn't offer ended up. Another team member had to sign up for Watson services and we had to change the credentials to our scripts.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Finishing more than what we had planned, learned how to use IBM Watson in Unity

What I learned

We learned how to use IBM Watson text to speech, speech to text, and conversation services in unity to perform voice commands in Unity
