Inspiration

Booking rooms with Exchange is a pain. We hate pain.

What it does

You can book a room with a Slack command. You can distinguish the free rooms by the green light in front of the door, walk in and book with one click of an IoT button. The rooms frees itself if everyone has left using the motion sensor to detect it.
