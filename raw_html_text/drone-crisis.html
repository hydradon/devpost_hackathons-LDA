Inspiration

It's a similar aesthetic style as shoujo shumatsu ryokou.

What it does

We designed this project for people's entertainment. We tried to use this opportunity to illustrate the potential hazardous outcomes from the use of drones.

How we built it

We were originally planning to create a game that controls real drones. Because of hardware limitations, we switched to web end game apps.

Challenges we ran into

We encountered challenges related to the applications of homogeneous coordinates and coordinate space transformations.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We're proud of our visual effects and designs. We designed a custom physics engine and a complete particle system based on the WebCanvas APIs. We also utilized modern enterprise-grade deployment solutions such as Kubernetes, Docker, and Google Cloud.
