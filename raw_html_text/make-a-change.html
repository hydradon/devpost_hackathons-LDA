Inspiration

We are producing too much waste in the world which leads to global warming. It's affecting everyone of us and there is so much we can do to help!

What it does

My VR experience raises awareness of the topic and presents 5 simple steps to help saving the planet.

How I built it

Simple drag & drop with the Viond Builder and a few image editing programs.

Challenges I ran into

Internet connection and low laptop battery.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I think the experience is easy to use and understand. Everyone is able to take something away from it - even if they are tired or annoyed by the topic.

What's next for Make a Change

I hope that as many people as possible will watch this experience. And that this results in taking some important measures.
