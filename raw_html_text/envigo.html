Inspiration

Where we live, work, study and travel, the weather significantly affect our life, if there is device or solution that can help us to predict future environment change in order to better arrange our schedule, give us alert and notification for any environmental change

What it does

Our solution is to provide real time weather information to public

How we built it

We do it as an mobile app with linking data with Hong Kong Observatory

Challenges we ran into

Not much free open weather and transport data from government and private institution

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Develop IOT device and app within one day

What we learned

How to develop prototype or demo within short period of time with new members, utilizing free online development tools

What's next for Envigo

It is free to use for public, will add real time transportation data (need to negotiate with government, no free transportation data) To commercialize our service, will add premium version without advertisement Will promote our product to overseas market
