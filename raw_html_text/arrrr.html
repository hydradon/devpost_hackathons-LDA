Inspiration

We wanted to work on a project that would help bring validity to multiplayer gaming in an augmented reality setting. A big inspiration for our team was the ship to ship combat found in Assassin’s Creed Black Flag and later iterations.

What it does

ARrrr connects 3 players together in an augmented reality combat arena where they battle it out for supremacy of the seas. It is a fast-paced competitive multiplayer game where quick maneuvering and shrewd use of your ship’s broadside cannons are required to prevail over your opponents.

How we built it

The 3D assets were built in Maya and brought into Unity. The UI layout and design was done using Unity’s UI toolset, and the art and graphics were created using Photoshop. The game flow and interaction was built using C#.
