Jibo SDK & API Documentation

We, as faithful Texas Tech students and fans, want Jibo to be on the Red Raider team. Using the Jibo SDK, we developed skills for Jibo to execute in response to certain vocal commands - it will respond favorably to hearing the name of some colleges, and unfavorably to others. Jibo can show emotion and cheer on your favorite team all in one!
