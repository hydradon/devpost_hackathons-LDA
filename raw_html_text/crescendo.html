Inspiration

We used to get bored a lot without a good way to discover media objects. When we found music, videos, etc that we liked some times we weren't that comfortable posting them on platforms such as Facebook.

What it does

Let's you post media objects (articles, videos, or images) as yourself or anonymous for your friends to see. Let's you see how much people from different areas are interacting with you on the app via a map.

How I built it

iOS, Swift, Parse, HERE API

Challenges I ran into

Buggy XCode 7

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Learned quite a few things while developing.

What I learned

Using HERE API, search feature in iOS, video playback in iOS

What's next for Crescendo

Lots of users. College students will love finding all their new media on this platform.
