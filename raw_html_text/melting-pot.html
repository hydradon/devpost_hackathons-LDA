Cooking alone can be fun, but cooking with others is on a whole 'nother level! And imagine being able to meet new people while you're at it!

Melting Pot allows you to do just that! Just input what ingredients you have, and let our app handle the logistics. It'll find you a new friend to cook with, and a recipe that you two can make with your combined ingredients!

Melting Pot was built for Android using a Parse database and the Yummly Recipe API.
