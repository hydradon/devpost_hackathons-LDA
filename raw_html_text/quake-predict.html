Inspiration

Helping to reduce the amount of damage done by earthquakes by highlighting areas of danger.

What it does

By entering the information about yourself, Quake Predict will predict the size of a possible earthquake that can arrive in your area.

How we built it

By applying USGS' dataset we trained a machine learning algorithm

Challenges we ran into

We encountered many issues due to the sparsity of data, its formatting, model choosing, etc. that slowed our progress

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned

We learned how to fluently process data in Pandas

What's next for Quake Predict

Predicting the worlds next earthquakes and catastrophes with greater accuracy due to interpreting weather patterns.
