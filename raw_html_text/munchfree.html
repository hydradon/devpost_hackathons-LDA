Inspiration

Bring FSU student, who are financially restricted, the luxury of free food. It is a way to bring fellow students together to the share the joys of food through various free food initiatives

What it does

Website that takes events and generates them onto a calendar, map, etc. An event planner

How we built it

We built it using bootstrap, html5, css, javascript, google forms, google maps, and google calendar

Challenges we ran into

Syncing events to calendar and also syncing events to map

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Solving how to populate the map and calendar with information from a google form using javascript

What we learned

How intricate google apps are. Helped refresh members on html5, css, javascript, etc.

What's next for MunchFree

We would like to expand it to other universities across the country
