Inspiration

GoSave is a very easy tool for personal money management, It keeps track of what you spend your money on redundant expenditure

What it does

You can create your own daily cost plan on our web application. And then we will help to detect your daily use on your money and give a statistics result and recommendation to you! We also give your free coupon if you save enough money each month.
