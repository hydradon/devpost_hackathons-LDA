Inspiration

Ever really wanted to buy cheap vodka at an Iowa Walmart in the middle of the night?

What it does

Tells you if you can buy a drink where you are, right then.

How we built it

With a lot of javascript, a lot of research on state laws, and a failed database implementation.

Challenges we ran into

Finding easily parseable state information.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Modern JS with ES6 modules and arrow functions.

What we learned

Lots about local liquor laws.

What's next for canibuyalcoholnow.com

More precision on what and when and where you can buy certain things, more than just a Y/N answer.
