Inspiration

Keep your bike from being stolen without GSM and GPS

What it does

Creates a safe perimeter around your bike, that it cannot leave without setting of an arlam

How I built it

An ESP32 is connected to a gyroscope and acceleration sensor and tracks the bikes movement.
