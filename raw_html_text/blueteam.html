Inspiration

This was our first hackathon and so we weren't prepared at the start.

What it does

Our application analyzes the data provided by JetBlue and allows Users to get ordered data based on their preferences of what kind of trip they want to take.

How I built it

We built this app on Android so that users can make plans or see fares on the go.

Challenges I ran into

Many small bugs which took longer to fix then they should have taken.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

None as of yet. This hackathon was a great experience for us all to see how hackathons function. We will come prepared next time.

What I learned

There is a huge community of people who love to code.

What's next for BlueTeam

Preparedness. We still have a bit to learn about working on projects like these in a time constraint.
