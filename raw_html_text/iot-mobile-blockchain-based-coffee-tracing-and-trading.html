Inspiration

Coffee, the second most traded commodity in the world, is worth $100 billion. It impacts 25+ million growers world wide, countless service workers, small and large businesses, consumers and the environment, among others. The smallest players contribute the most and get lowest retribution. Supply chain is complicated and hasn't changed in over a century. It needs disruption to benefit everyone.

What it does

We are connecting the coffee supply chain from the farm to the consumer using IoT, smartphones and blockchain to create a platform that allows transparent coffee trading, reliable information flow, verification of production best practices and direct payment to the key players globally, starting with coffee farmers.
