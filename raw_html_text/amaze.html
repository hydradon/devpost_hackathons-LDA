Inspiration

We all love challenges, and this seemed a bit challenging.

How it works

Starting from a drawing program (paint, GIMP) and create the maze of your choice, then send it as a challenge to a friend through our email services and he will receive a link to the WEB App (the aMaze-ing game). The game itself is controlled by Leap motion. You move trough the maze with hand gestures and have to get from Start to End.

Challenges I ran into

Making the Front and Back ends, configuring django, figuring out on how to configure flask as well.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We are sending emails automatically, we have a donation page which has a form to send payment, working also with Paypal, We managed to get the Leap to take the right motions and work flawlessly.

What I learned

A lot about web programming

What's next for aMaze

Getting it popular and making it better.
