Inspiration

We were inspired by drinking games which we played already. We wanted to develop something new, a quiz game where the players can test their knowledge about alcoholism.

What it does

It's a quiz game for two players. It can be tested our knowledge about alcoholism by playing this game.

How I built it

Using Android Studio.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned

We learnt how to solve problems while making a bigger project. We developed our Java knowledge by declaring a lot of classes and instantianting objects.

What's next for Alcohol Quiz

Inserting challenges and "motivation" for players, extending the statistics. We are planning to motivate the players to drink when they don't know the right answer.
