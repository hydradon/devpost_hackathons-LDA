This is a simple price negotiatior bot for Amazon.in and flipkart.com the two giants for ecommerce India. This bot fetches the data from the first page of the product listings and compares both the websites and decides the final price with a pythonscript. The data gets saved as a CSV file. The CSV is then read by python t compare the prices.
