What is the problem that we are trying to solve?

  • The market for water level data is fragmented as every agency is publishing their data in a different format
  • There can be conflict of interests between agencies in different countries
  • Some data can be lost

What is the market for this solution?

  • Research institutes
  • Governments
  • Insurance companies, since it allows them to make better forecasts
  • Also, It could extend to other industries with additional environmental data

Why should we use a Blockchain to solve this problem?

  • It ensures immutability and insure that all data is recorded and reliable
  • We can gather data from all agencies on one platform
  • It allows standardized format

Accomplishments that we're proud of

  • A working cryptocurrency based on Ethereum
  • A blockchain-supported interactive information system architecture
