Objective Statement

A way to objectively measure and remotely monitor disease severity & quality of life in patients with chronic skin disease that reduces number of flares and office visits.

MVP

In order to accurately discover market needs, a MVP that consists of a Google Forms survey for skin snapshots with a Google Sheets backend and time-series plotting functions will be deployed in Dr. Afanasiev's dermatology office in the next few weeks. Existing patient and doctor interest has already been established through opinion surveys.
