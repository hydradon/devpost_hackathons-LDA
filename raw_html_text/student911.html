Inspiration

Students require time to study for finals. To help them prepare, we designed this website so to offer them resources both in the educational department, but also for their mental health and wellbeing, which is an underappreciated aspect for academic success.

What it does

Our website provides resources for many academic subjects, while also allowing students to rate resources and provide their own for their utilisation and others'. It also includes links to therapy services and lists of advice and tips to do to manage stress and their mental wellbeing.

How we built it

We used HTML and CSS to design the webpages and python Django web framework for backend
