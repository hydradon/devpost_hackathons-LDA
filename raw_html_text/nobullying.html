Cyberbullying, or harassment on social networking websites has become a growing concern. While the websites have mechanisms to report such content, a significant amount of indecency gets published and continues to intimidate and victimize the users.

NoBullying is a chrome extension that filters tweets suggestive of harassment in real time. It uses a SVM classifier to determine and hide the abusive content in a user's twitter feed.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Building something that improves user experience in a positive way.

What we learned

We became acquainted with this ever-growing issue of online harassment, and learned how a small extension in the browser can contribute towards curbing it.

What's next for NoBullying

Extend the NoBullying extension to other social networking platforms and browsers.
