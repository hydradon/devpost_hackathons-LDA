Colors matter very much in people's outfit. The right match really suit your mood, whereas the weird one will make you look really weird, not in a positive way. So we create an app to help people make the right decision when choosing apparels. Potentially this app can be used in many occasions.

It's a mobile app. Just tell the app what you have at hand right now, and the system will tell you which colors go well with it. Of course the mighty app will consider how you want to feel today.

Early on we wanted to build an app using augmented reality. Then we realized it's way more than that... and the light is too hard to control.
