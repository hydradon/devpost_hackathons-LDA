Inspiration

just as new crowdinvesting platforms are democratizing venture capital through access to startup investing for non-accredited investors, we want to democratize venture capital on the knowledge side

What it does

allow every investor, no matter their net worth, to be as informed as the larger institutional investors and venture capitalists who have a lot more money and resources

How we built it

Utilize natural language processing to summarize and analyze content, in this case financial articles. Assign tags to each article for sorting. Allow users to choose which tags they want updates on

Challenges we ran into

training data for NLP

Accomplishments that we're proud of

connected 3 services to make a new chatbot

What we learned

lots of twill
