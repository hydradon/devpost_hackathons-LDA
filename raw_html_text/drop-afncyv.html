Inspiration

We have inspired by the idea of making donation to homeless people, but what if you don't have cash. Drop is an application that will open a wider option to donate money for the poor through smart phone. People can now drop a small bucks to the homeless at the corner of their streets using their cellphone and we will deliver cash to the poor.
