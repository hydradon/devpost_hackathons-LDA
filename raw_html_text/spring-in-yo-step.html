Inspiration

Recently, our teammate had pulled many of her muscles and tendons, and she was having trouble walking on uneven surfaces. With this in mind, we designed a cane that would account for that.

What it does

It uses a system of springs and dashpots to rebalance the cane. It also gives out voice greetings.

How I built it

We built it using metal rods pvc pipes, raspberry pi, and Microsoft's Oxford project.

Challenges I ran into

We were having trouble using the APIs and raspberry pi, and we couldn't 3-D print all our parts.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We managed the mechanical part.
