Inspiration

Thanks for Reviewing my App. I always wanted to do something that will improve the Road travel Experience in Indore and Help saves lives lost in road accidents. This competition gave an inspiration to Reduce Accidents and increase awareness towards Road Safety.

What it does

My Safe Indore has been built to help the pedestrians, bicyclists, and motor vehicles in Indore in order to promote their safety. My App is capable to perform following functionality:

Signals : Signal will help people to find traffic signals in their area. It will help user to manage the time and it can reduce the delay and save time to be wasted.

Live Route Alerts : Its time to know whatever that is happening within Indore regarding Road events with periodic update status,photos. With this, you can always know the road situation before you start driving....As an example, If I am going though any Route and I stuck in traffic so I can notify other people by submitting live alerts. Also we can find the list of issue from this tab.

Save Life Alerts : If someone found any accident case on Road, They can submit information and inform people to save their lives.

Alert by Administrator : Admin can enter details of any construction work, Diversions. User can find the details. For any proposed changes in the traffic plan in your locality, one can send their opinions and suggestions online.

Real Time Traffic : It shows your current location on map. or plan your driving route with less traffic areas.You can find your Nearby Traffic status using Map.

AICTSL Bus Routes : Now a day, Public transport is safe way to travel. We have provided Bus Number Details with their Routes.

How to Drive : Driving Instructions to avoid Accidents.

Traffic Rules : List of traffic Rules, for better understanding.

Important URLs : List of Important Government Website URLs and Important Contact details.

Crossing : It will show Footbridge Crossings nearby you.

Speed Monitor : Speed Monitor tool helps parents to monitor their children location and the vehicle driving speed. App will be triggered email and notification to parents if driving speed is more then 80km/hour.
