Inspiration

Every day we watch the news and see images of fellow Muslims around the world going through hardships of life. Refugees crisis, poverty, hunger and limited access to education. We always feel helpless and needing to do more for our greater community. We want to give more than money we want to give effort, time and passion for solving our most pressing social issues and lifting the Muslim community up and sustaining its development. But, how can we do that? How can I find like-minded changemakers with a different skill set to build social projects?How might we invest the most valuable capital, our human capital to achieve sustainable development?

What it does

We can all rise together Using WeRise. An App built to connect you and other changemakers to create, collaborate and build social development projects to add value to the community. It will link you, your team and your project to social organizations so you can make a difference.

How we built it

We have built it using Andriod Studio, Android and photoshop.

Challenges we ran into

Transforming the idea into a product Creating a hybrid business model connecting social good and financial success

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Producing a prototype Refined the idea and enhance our problem statement. Focus our effort to provide an assessable solution. Find a unique position for our application among other competitors.

What we learned

How to work as a team How to build a business plan

What's next for WeRise App

Apply to Ontario Centres of Excellence to join a provider of a social accelerator program Reach out to social good organizations to integrate their projects into our application
