We all have had days that we have had many tests assigned in a week and they did not align. Teachers often schedule tests for certain days, not realizing that other teachers have given tests on the same days. This makes it hard for students to manage their time. This app makes it easy for the teacher to see what days other teachers have assigned their tests, and assign their tests accordingly to ease the pressure on the students.
