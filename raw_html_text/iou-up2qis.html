Inspiration

Simplifying and organizing individual interpersonal debts within friend groups or house/apartment groups.

What it does

Users can add a bill which will be evenly split among those that should be paying for it. Users can create groups, whether it be for just an apartment, or for individual, smaller things, such as sharing groceries, which determine by how many times the bill is divided and who gets an alert. You can also rate individual users to let others know if they're good roommates or not.

How we built it

We built the whole backend in Rails, and the front end is done by the usual suspects of HTML, CSS, and jQuery.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting it to work at the level that it is now in very little time.

What's next for iou

Slowly adding features, and polishing what's there. Adding payment integration with services such as Venmo or Paypal.
