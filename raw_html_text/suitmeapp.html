Inspiration

Nearly anyone needs to wear a suit at least once in his live. For all those who hate shopping and just want to get it done, we provide the perfect solution wit SuitMeApp.

What it does

I gives you a tinder-like way to choose your suit in seconds. We rely on the Zalando API to provide you the best recommendations possible, considering what you like.

How we built it

AngularJs

Challenges we ran into

Zalando API does not support direct ordering jet. So we provide you the raw links to order all you need for your perfect suit.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We already deployed a stable version, that provides you your suit in seconds.

What we learned

Lots about AngularJs

What's next for SuitMeApp

The social media plugin to show you the suits of your friends to prevent you from having the same outfit.
