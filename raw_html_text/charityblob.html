Do you have a soft spot for the children with no hope of a Santa? What about the fuzzy homeless kittens? Ever feel like cutting a check is too much effort?

If you answered yes to any one of these, Charity Blob is for you! You can easily donate to a charity of your choice without the hassle! The charity will be given your donation and you earn a blob on the map for your charity. Donating for your charity will increase it's popularity regardless of how much you donate so you don't have to write off your month's salary to feel you've made a difference.
