Inspiration

Solution for Smart Museum solutions

What it does

The SAM application is single touch point for museum support staff which provides dashboard for assets information and alerts/messages functionality for daily operation. It’s rich UI and real time assets information can help in reduction of operation cost and cycle time for museum assets. The application enables the IOT Strategy and journey for museum. Real time monitoring of the assets., Reduction in Cost of Operations Reduction in mean time for overall diagnosis & repair

How we built it

Its built on Predix - IOT platform, its capturing asset and location details by using GE "Asset" and "Time Series" services and then its generate the Dashboard using Predix analytics service for asset tracking and management.

Challenges we ran into

  1. 422 error code from Predix services didn't have proper message. 2) Steep learning curve

Accomplishments that we're proud of

  1. Built this app in a very short amount of time.
  2. It helps in solving day to day operation problem for Museum staff

What we learned

1) Power of Predix and its capabilties 2) GE's wide range of smart services and solutions 3) Industrial internet of things (IOT)

What's next for Smart Asset Management

1) Building more predictive analytics on current data for asset maintenance. 2) Integrated with SMS gateway for Alerts and Messages. 3) Telecom Service provider API integration fro precise tracking and handling of the asset. 4) Customer Feedback can be integrated for marketing and promotions.
