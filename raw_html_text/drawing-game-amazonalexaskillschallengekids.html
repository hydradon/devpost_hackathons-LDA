Inspiration

With a very art-enthusiastic niece that can't get enough of drawing pictures, she regularly asks people for suggestions on what to draw, with this skill, Alexa can offer up a range of creative, interesting scenarios!

What it does

Produces random art challenges ideas, in the form of either characters or environments.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

My niece was a huge fan and have since tested it with various family friends and their kids with positive reactions!

What I learned

One key area of interest was to ensure that commands were simple and with little ambiguity between calls to keep kid-friendly use in mind.

What's next for Drawing Game - #AmazonAlexaSkillsChallengeKids

More challenges, more fun!
