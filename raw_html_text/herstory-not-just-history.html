Inspiration

We wish to inspire other girls to pursue careers in technology, and to bridge the imbalance of gender representation in STEM fields.

What it does

Allows the user to interview the women and ask questions about their lives and work to hopefully inspire the user.

How we built it

It was our first time, but somehow we were able to complete the project through Unity.

Challenges we ran into

  1. no experience in using unity or C#
  2. had a lot of technical issues with hardware (oculus rift)

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We made our first VR project! We made our first Hack the North!

What we learned

How to use Unity to create some awesome projects. The importance of the development process, and the importance of having powerful computers to run graphics! :/

What's next for HerStory - not just history

Add a voiceOver Chatbot, add more women in tech.
