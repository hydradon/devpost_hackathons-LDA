FreshSearch

University students struggle to find meaningful positions whilst they are studying, to expose students to Canadian employers before they begin their careers will increase the odds of them considering such companies after they graduate.

Centralizing this service for all universities will expose a wider range of students to the unique opportunities available in Canada. Everyone believes that America is the land of dreams and infinite possibilities but having access to opportunities within the Canadian border can sway their decision.
