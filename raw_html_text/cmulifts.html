Here's the whole story. (its in the presentation)

inspiration

During Orientation Week I was looking for a running partner because I consider myself to be an avid runner, but enjoy running with others. One person on my floor was really into running; however, his skill level and speed was not equal to mine so we did not make good running partners. That's where our app comes in.

what it does

Our app matches people with each other who are looking for a partner to do physical activities with on the CMU campus. Users log in with their AndrewID and choose their preferences for activity, skill level, and time in order to match people with each other. Then our web application puts these two people in contact with each other.
