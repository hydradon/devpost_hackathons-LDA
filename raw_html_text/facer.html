Inspiration

Photos capture memories. As an event participant, I hope I can see all the photos taken by the professional photographers after the event.

What it does

It will capture the face of the participant at the registration counter. After the event, the organiser will upload the event photos and Facer will automatically detect and recognize the participants in the photo, and send them a link to all the photos that contains them.

How I built it

With React.js, Express.js and Azure Cognitive API.
