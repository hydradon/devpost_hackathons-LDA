About Jade

With an easy to use web-interface for heavier tasks and a minimalist and stunning android app for everyday use, Jade is the future for any and every organization that wants a centralized platform for social good.

Inspiration

Inspired by the CGI challenge, our team created the Jade application; a web application that allows its users to get personalized volunteering suggestions based on the user's preferences and available time.

How We built it

Our team of four primarily used Java through Android Studio and TypeScript with Angular.js for our web application. We all strived to use technology we have never used before, and we learned a lot. Adams was responsible for our fully functional database, Emmy took the lead on organizing the project, Jamie worked with typescript and design for the first time, and Davide was our mobile lead. We're all thankful for the opportunity this weekend -- thank you!
