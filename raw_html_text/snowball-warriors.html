Inspiration

Stanford weather's great, but there's times you just want to be able to have a snowball fight. Snowball Warrior allows you to have a snowball fight with friends around the world, anytime, anywhere.

How it works

Wearing a myo armband, make snowballs by making a fist, throw it at a partner virtually by letting go. Put on an oculus rift for a full immersive experience.
