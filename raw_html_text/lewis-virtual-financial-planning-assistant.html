Inspiration

Our vision was to help people of all different financial backgrounds have the knowledge and confidence to take the steps they need to prepare for their futures.

What it does

Provides data driven personal financial advice, facilitates goal setting and tracking, and recommends pertinent services and resources available to the consumer.

How we built it

Java backend prototype, proto.io frontend prototype

Challenges we ran into

creating new features on proto.io, brainstorming ways to analyze data and relate it to pertinent suggestions for the customer.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Creating a beautiful frontend environment, having a compelling pitch.

What we learned

Frontend development prototyping, critically analyzing customer needs and what features can best meet those.

What's next for Lewis: Virtual Financial Planning Assistant

Turning the frontend prototypes into a functioning app integrated with the Citi suite.
