FeedMe!

  • FeedMe! is a mobile food ordering app that connects local food trucks with hungry customers.
  • The food truck industry has grown 7.9% per year in the past five years, and has yet to truly impact the Atlantic Coast and Southeast. FeedMe! will accelerate the growth of an infant industry and broaden the use of food trucks domestically.
  • The services that this app provides are food truck requests for a specific food or available truck, food truck reservations, the location of currently operating food trucks, and a list of food trucks in your area and future events.
  • Competitors: Roaming Hunger
    • It has two separate apps for trucks(2.9 star rating) and customers(1.6 star rating).
    • The apps have trouble with updating the food trucks' locations.
    • Not all food trucks are on the apps.
