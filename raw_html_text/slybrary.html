Inspiration

A week ago, Parker decided that one day he wanted to create a library where he could share his knowledge with other people. To accomplish his goal, he planned on creating a database where he could place his old textbooks.

What it does

sLybrary is an open-source database that gives students the ability to upload and download college textbooks.

How I built it

MongoDB,Flask,Python,HTML

Challenges I ran into

Unfamiliarity with Flask, long setup time for the server, getting the server to communicate with the website

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Having a working database!!!

What I learned

How to use Flask, databases, and Jinja2!

What's next for sLybrary

Taking sLybrary out of the 90s!, adding download functionality, improve the parsing process, store the books in a better file format, and so much more!!!
