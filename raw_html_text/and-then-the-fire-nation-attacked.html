Starting out as a digital walkthrough of a house, our idea went from essentially Google HouseView to more of a Youtube point and click. We put together a bunch of already existing ideas and altered them so we can have a person simply take a photo or video of a room in their house and use the power of crowd sourcing to quickly and effectively itemize a persons belongings to help them submit insurance claims in the case of an emergency. Using a combination of youtube, html5, css, and javascript, we mocked up a basic test of what our idea would look in practice as well as a walkthrough website detailing our business. We are very excited about our idea and its simplicity so it can easily help people undergoing disaster. Machine learning identification was toyed with, but ultimately didnt work so it was put on hold for the future.
