What it does:

gally is an application that helps women schedule their usual workout split with intensity more appropriate to their hormonal level. Also for women who intend to go to gym but have no idea what to do, we offer free suggestion on how to split your workout and what to do in each session.

How I built it:

Well I was not able to build it so... But may I explain how my thing works. Customers sign up for an account to use the application. Then they fill out some information so that we can understand them and help them better. Needed information includes: basic information (name, age, weight, height), menstrual cycle information (average cycle span, normal period starting day), normal workout split option (upper-lower, push-pull, 5 major parts of body) and how the normally schedule their workout split through the week. According to the given information, we calculate to give the optimizing 4-week plan which consists of 3 stages: progressively overload, normal training and deload. Pressing into a particular week a more specific workout split comes out. And when pressing into a particular day there will be our suggestion for a full workout session, or customers can type their own to keep track of their progress. In period days, some people feel okay, but most feel either unmotivated, cramping or painful (the struggle is real, we understand). Hence we create some options to lower your workout intensity. Based on your situation or how you feel, you may modify your workout session, do some light cardio instead or even take an extra rest day. Basically, gally is like a workout calendar that suggests and reminds you of your strength status so that you can modify the amount of weight to lift each exercising day to benefit your health and optimize your goals
