Inspiration

Me and my team mate coincidentally met at the Go club, in which I introduce him to the game of go. I taught him how to play the capturing game, which is one of the variation of the game of Go. This is a great game for a beginner to start. We then met, again, coincidentally at hackathon and decided to make a game we played yesterday.

What it does

It plays the capturing game. two players share a screen and alternate the turn. When the winning condition is met, the game's background turns to white.

How we built it

We decided to use MVC architecture to represent the game and implemented it with Java. I worked mainly on model and he mainly worked on views.

Challenges we ran into

It was very hard to determine the win condition as it turns out to be a graph problem. We implemented it using DFS search.

What we learned
