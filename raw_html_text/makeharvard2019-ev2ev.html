Inspiration

Our inspiration was to solve the problem of EV owners worrying about range and charging. By creating an app to connect nearby EV owners with people in need of charging we solve exactly that. With the hardware in place for Vehicle-2-vehicle communications as well we can have short range and long range communications either between mobile phones and users or cars between cars.

What it does

The app allows users to register their location or vehicle as a pseudo charge station for electric vehicles in need of charging. This would hopefully entice people into purchasing electric vehicles since they would not need to worry about where they will charge.

How we built it

We built the app in Xcode using Swift and handled the Vehicle-2-Vehicle communications through Arduino microcontrollers. We used a flask server to handle location data, which was then read into our app as a location on the map.
