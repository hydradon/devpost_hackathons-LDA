Hitch Hacker

Hitch Hacker is the match maker service for lonely hackers.

It is important when going to hackathons, you have the right team. Having the right skills, interests, and ideas are critical for hackers to compete. Speaking from personal experience, having the wrong team can ruin the weekend. Hitch hacker is here to fix that. We provide a way for hackers to brainstorm projects and connect with users before hand. We connect users by their similar interests and skills to make the hackathon as enjoyable as possible.
