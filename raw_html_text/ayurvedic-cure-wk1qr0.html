Inspiration

Ayurveda teaches us to cherish our innate-nature With Ayurvedic Cure, Alexa will tend to your health problems with the help of organic and ancient auyrvedic remedies using in house organics. Ayurveda, developed more than 3000 years ago is now the best medicine a man can get in home, with the help of ancient herbs which are always available in a household, this skill brings along all the remedies which are easy and saves a lot of time and money.

What it does

This skill will help guide you to tend to your health problems with more than one home applicable remidies.

How we built it

we have first gathered information about the simple home remedies which are prepared using inhouse organic substances , then we have done the backend using node js , alexa-sdk library and used aws lamda for code storage.

Challenges we ran into

our main aim was to provide simple organic remedies which are safe to practice, we had put in lot of efforts for obtaining genuine ayurvedic practices which are safe to follow

Accomplishments that we're proud of

we are very happy to give users health tips , remedies for some common healthissues like hairfall , obesity , indegestion etc with our skill.

What we learned

Technically we have learned alot about the alexa skill development and off coarse we got to know health tips to keep our body fit.

What's next for Ayurvedic Cure

In near future we are going to add yoga asanas/poses , specific yoga poses/asanas for specific health problems.
