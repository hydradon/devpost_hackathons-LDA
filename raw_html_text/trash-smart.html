Inspiration

Trash is hard to keep track of and you never know when it is full and ready to take out to the curb.

What it does

It measures how much trash is in the can and tells you when it's full.

How we built it

using cardboard and paint and tape to make a trash can.

Challenges we ran into

Deciding on trash can shape, and getting argon to work properly.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Our trash can is very beautiful

What we learned

we should time manage better and pick groups better.

What's next for Trash Smart

Finalizing the design, working out the bugs, adding sorting system.
