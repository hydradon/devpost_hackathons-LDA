Inspiration

Private school/colleges extra curricular activities is always good if we compare it with the government school/colleges extra curricular activities. Basic reason is lake of opportunity.This website create a opportunity for government school/college student to show their talent and we are going to share it with the world.

What it does

This website allow us to perfectly show the hidden talent in government schools/colleges by providing a platform to government schools/colleges to show their talent with other schools,This website will also help us to start a monthly newspaper among the schools where only Government students/college are allow to show their talent.

How we built it

We built this website using css, bootstrap, jquery, javascript. html5.

Challenges we ran into

There is huge talent in government school/colleges but they don't have a proper platform to show their skills, this website will allow the students to show their skills. we first design this website statically we will built in php to convert this website dynamically

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Providing a proper platform to the students which will help to show their creativity hence the hidden talent will not go waste.
