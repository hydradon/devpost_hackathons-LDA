We as a group are introverted people. We do not like to go out to runs when a lot of people might see us. A lot of apps provide us with motivation to run and statistics such as how much we run. But, nothing seems to be able to push us enough to get out of our shells. So, we thought, why not built an app which caters to each person's specific needs. Some love the rain, some avoid pets. This led to the idea of developing an app that asks the user information about themselves, their likes and dislikes and using this information and the calendar on the user's phone, our app tells them what might be a suitable time to go running on a path of their choice.
