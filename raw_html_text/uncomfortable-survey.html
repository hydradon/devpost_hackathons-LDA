Inspiration

Taking surveys is a dull and boring process. Mindlessly clicking boxes requires no thought on part of the taker, and the lack of interaction can be draining. We have reinvented the survey to push the taker out of their comfort zone.

What it does

With open-ended questions, we require more than simple box clicking. Uncomfortable Survey is tailored to the user's answers, providing each taker with a personalized experience.

How we built it

Using the text analysis Microsoft API, we submit the user's data to the system of sentiment analysis.

Challenges we ran into

Our python expert was hospitalized at 2am.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to create a new survey experience despite the challenges faced.

What we learned

Always have a backup on the team for skills.

What's next for Uncomfortable Survey

Additional questions and routs, as well as a GUI to increase user friendliness.
