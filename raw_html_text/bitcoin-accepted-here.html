Inspiration

A better way to search merchants that accept bitcoin in the world

What it does

It helps users to easily find out merchants that accept bitcoin, by proximity, by countries list, by categories, by name, by map. It generates usefull statistics about the diffusion of shops in the world.

How I built it

I wrote a PHP parser that grabs all bitcoin points; i developed an Android app to view them.

Challenges I ran into

Coding with native PHP and Java, without any framework or library/addon.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Categorize most of the shops in countries/cities and categories.

What I learned

I improved my "academic" coding skills.

What's next for Bitcoin Accepted Here

A web version.
