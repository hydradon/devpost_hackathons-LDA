Inspiration

We grew tired of the complexity and the obsession with likes and followers from apps like instagram. We simplified the process of sharing images by allowing users to post a single image as well as a status, which is shared with their followers. This app does not tell you how many followers you have, nor does it keep track of likes. It just allows you to share images with friends without all the fuss.
