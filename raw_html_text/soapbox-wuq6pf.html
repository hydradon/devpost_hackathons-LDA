Inspiration

After going to many poorly-organized events, we decided to build a platform that allows event managers to have more control over their event/conference and have a better idea of how their conference is doing.

What it does

Soapbox provides event managers with an all-in-one app that helps them manage their event, gauge what attendees think of the event and provide them with analytics to have a better understanding of their own event.
