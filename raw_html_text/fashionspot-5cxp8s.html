Inspiration

Estefani is a fashion geek, which follows a lot of fashion designers, but she had a problem... None of those designers were from Puerto Rico, so she didnt have a way to acquire their designs and that made her very sad. But then it hit her FashionSpot.

What it does

Mobile app that lets designers from all over the world showcase their work through this platform, and encourage the community to apreciate and make it easy obtain their fashion.

How we built it

We used android studio as our IDE of choice to work on the mobile app and we managed all of our users within Parse (PaaS) and surprisigly our iconography was built with power point, which is not common at all (nailed It :p).

Challenges we ran into

Getting all of it to work toghether as smoothly as possible

Accomplishments that we are proud of

We managed to finish most of the features which are the ability to follow designers buy their designs and create a simple and beautiful UI that everybody loved.

What we learned

We learned how fast we can build a functional mobile application using a platform as a service and how it makes it easier for the team to help with the free tasks

What's next for FashionSpot

We hope to upload it to the Google Play Store and let designers from all over the world make their fashion global.
