What is it?

Pebble Trail is a navigational assistant that allows Miami residents to traverse the travesty that is our public transportation system with ease.

Sounds good, what do I need?

All you need is the Pebble smart-watch and a smart phone.

How I built it

Pebble Watch was built using the Pebble SDK, purely in javascript. It is dependant on the Miami Transit API.

Challenges I ran into

Other than tackling a foreign SDK, Miami's tracking services for its public transportation is lacking. The Miami Transit API is maintained by one person, and it is not always the easiest to work with.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Proud of putting forward a great hack with the team. Always a great experience to come out and produce something exciting.

What's next for Pebble Trail

More navigational features, and taking advantage of features exclusive to the Pebble Time watch.
