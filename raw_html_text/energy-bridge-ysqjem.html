Inspiration

We are solving the risk problem perceived by capital regarding community solar.

Problem

37% of U.S. households, or 43 million of us, are renters who generally don't have easy access to solar. Community solar solves this problem for renters and other groups for whom solar access is a problem.

So why hasn't community solar taken off?

Banks and other investors have said they love the idea of community solar but don't understand how to measure or manage risk in such projects.

Solution

EnergyBridge solves this risk problem by bundling renters into tranches and manage these groups to satisfy investors' risk sensitivity.
