Candy Crusher

We believe that Roboy should make your life easier and more fun. Therefore, we built a personalized candy thrower system using LEGO, Arduino, OpenCV, CAD and a Laser Cutter. Our system enables Roboy to automatically detect your face, follow you around and throw candies at you if you smile at him.
