• MaximizeWasteWaterRecovery

Inspiration

To save for future generation

What it does

Gives the food and agriculture industry a holistic visibility of the quantity of water used, wasted and recycled. Recycled water percentage gives them a visibility to correct them selves before sending polluted water into water bodies.

How I built it

I have built a simulator for the IoT devices and configured with the help of Azure IoT central and IoT hub.

Challenges I ran into

Its doable, but time is the challenge aspect.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Configured IoT devices to Blockchain and Azure IoT central. Executed analytics.

What I learned

How to save earth and water security.

What's next for MaximizeWasteWaterRecovery

TO continue building this solution to extend it to enterprise level.
