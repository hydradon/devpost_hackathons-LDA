AARFDog is a really good boy

You know that feeling when you're communicating with your teammates on a project and you start feeling more and more stressed out, and you find yourself thinking "gee, if only I had a dog to cheer me up"? Well stress no more! Introducing AARFDog, the automated slack emotional support dog!

Need a gif a a cute puppy? Just send AARFDog a message! If it hears that you're sad it will mirror your emotions in the form of adorable dog gifs! You can message it in private, or bring AARFDog to your group conversations to brighten everyone's mood!
