Buzzkill-app

A fantastic "serverless" voice recognition web app, built by and for HackGSU Fall 2018. Do you want to know whether or not to invest in this brand new cryptocurrency? Are you a clueless hackathon judge behind on the times and tech?

Never fear, BuzzKill is here! No dependencies or deployments needed -- just open this page on the web or download the files and launch away! Record those devilish entrepreneurs pitches in BuzzKill, and it'll tell you whether or not it's a run for your money. Give it a try!

Here's a sample pitch for you to try reading to BuzzKill: 

Hi, my name is Paul, and this is our project, BuzzKill. 
It’s a SERVERLESS WEB APP that utilizes the Google Speech API to collect keywords from a presenter's pitch. 
It scores the speaker in REAL TIME, as you can see from the trophy on the bottom.
