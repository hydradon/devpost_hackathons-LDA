Inspiration

Growing up in poor neighborhoods, we didn't feel safe traveling by ourselves.

What it does

The app notifies the user's loved ones or law enforcement when the user does not reach their location within a designated time frame.

How we built it

We used Android studio.

Challenges we ran into

Android Studio was unexpectedly difficult to set up and understand.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

One of the app's abilities is to send a text with the user's coordinates if it believes the user is in danger.

What we learned

How to use Android Studio and to collaborate with other team members with a time constraint.

What's next for Gotcho Back

Encourage others to look out for each other and to broaden the app's area of usage.
