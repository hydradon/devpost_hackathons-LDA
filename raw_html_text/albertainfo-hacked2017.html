I live in Alberta, we live in Alberta, but how much do we really know about our province?

In this project I wanted to explore the history of our province through the lens of data. So many things influence our lives and the lives of our neighbors. Often those that impact us the most create opportunities, barriers, and all sorts of small talk.

I thought it would be interesting to relate the story of our current events to intuitive data visualizations. For this project, I wanted to learn about R programming, specifically, R markdown and making effective static dashboards.
