• Improving the Labor Life through tech.

Inspiration

“Our mission is to remove the middleman” in contracting small-scale handyman services. Homeowners and workers dictate the terms of transaction virtually, thereby removing any face to face social anxiety.

What it does

Connects workers to People.

Problems

Workers: Cleaning Companies and Major Employers often consume significant profits. Workers can work during extra hours for additional income.

Health: Social anxiety arises between homeowners and workers during monetary transactions especially when both parties are face to face.

HomeOwners: Difficult to locate immediate services. Expensive for a particular task or supposed urgency of task.

Solutions/Benefits

Consumers : Lower costs -> Increased affordability Immediate Access to Services

Consumers & Workers : Negotiated Terms, Layout

Workers : Greater employment opportunities Greater Income

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Building an application on a complex Model View Controller architecture, using the industry standard NodeJS for the server side and well designed Consumer Front (Front-End) with a team mostly consisting of those unfamiliar with any project architecture, design or production.

Building a complex platform to resolve a very significant social problem..

What I learned

Better Product Development, Design, production and testing.

What's next for WorkMe

iOS release during second week of Feb 2016. Web release (Coming Soon).

Streamlined UI with seamless navigation and beautiful retina design, Secure Payment Authentication with fixed terms. Optimization for large consumer base.
