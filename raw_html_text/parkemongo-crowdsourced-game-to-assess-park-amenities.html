Inspiration

A fun and intuitive community-driven game to take care of our parks. In an effort to identify and assess park amenities, we ask users to submit data through the form of an interactive game. From questionaries to geo-tagged picture uploads, we create a compelling experience while being mutually beneficial for both parties.
