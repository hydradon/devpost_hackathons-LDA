What it does

CannaSource is a MITS (Marijuana Inventory Tracking System) compliant platform that integrates with existing seed to sale tracking, production and supply chain methods to create robust, secure and data rich information, using blockchain. This data can be leveraged for legal compliance reporting, internal analytics and metrics for production efficiency, quality control and consumer facing marketing.
