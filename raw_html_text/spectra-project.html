spectra-project

We all love to travel, explore, meet new people and learn about different cultures. We wanted something that helped plan our trip and allow us to meet authentic residents instead of falling into tourist traps. It also helps residents make money and participate in a cultural exchange!

Locol helps plan and organize trips so you can travel like a local without the hassle of booking with an expensive travel agent. We connect individuals who know the best restaurants, hidden beaches, or scenic walks that only locals go to!

Users can filter on price or just scroll through different trip options to find the spot of their dreams. Then they will read through the trip itinerary to book with the perfect tour guide. While on the trip, they can send "locol-snaps" using their phone's text messaging capabilities to share the experience with future travelers browsing Locol.
