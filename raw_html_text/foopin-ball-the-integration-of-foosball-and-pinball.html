• FooPin Ball!

Inspiration

Our inspiration comes from the love of classic and retro gaming, along with the motivation from SpartaHack 2017.

What it does

It is the integration of the beloved Pinball and Foosball arcade games. It will rid of your boredom with the touch of a potentiometer.

How we built it

Our resources were limited, and so we combined the ability of four Computer Engineers to construct this arcade game from Arduino, stepper motors, potentiometers, cardboard, and tape.

Challenges we ran into

The most challenging part was the limited resources, along with the 36 hour deadline for the project.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Working as a team, and dividing the tasks properly and efficiently.

What we learned

We learned the value of teamwork, along with constructive methods.

What's next for FooPin Ball - The Integration of Foosball and Pinball.

Well, this is an open source project for anyone to build and even expand upon. Thus, anyone can enjoy this crafty project.
