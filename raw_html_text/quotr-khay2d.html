Inspiration

We felt many online tools were not adequate enough for finding quotes needed to back up points in an essay.

What it does

Our website allows users to find specific quotes from a variety of novels that you can search for using key words.

How we built it

We used gravit.io to design the webpages, Gimp to design the logo, and Invision to put our designs into a presentation.

Challenges we ran into

Design the webpages was quite difficult due to inexperience in using the application.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Completing a finished prototype at our first hackathon

What we learned

We learned how to use gravit.io to design webpages, and constructing a prototype with Invision.

What's next for Quotr

Creating a more appealing webpages, and adding more text and analysis to the website.
