Inspiration

What it does

How we built it

Using both cyber and physical devices generating traffic related data streams.

Challenges we ran into

Integration of heterogenous data streams into one application.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Helping citizens to cope with transport noise.

What we learned

Not sure as yet.
