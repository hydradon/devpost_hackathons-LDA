Our web-app aims to empower users to clean up their local communities. Inspired by the #trashtag movement, we aim to connect communities to come together to clean up trash in their area. Users can submit a photo of garbage in their area and schedule a cleanup. Other users then search for cleanups in their area and can join a clean up effort. One of the greatest challenges that we face as a society is solving the global pandemic of pollution. We often hold a misconception that software developers don’t have the opportunity to help the environment with our work. We believe that software tools, like Cleanup Meetup, can and will prove to be an integral tool that will allow people to connect with one another in a way that incites the change that we aim to code.
