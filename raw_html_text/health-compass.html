There is currently no one-stop app solution which enables navigation of the NHS healthcare options , which also efficiently notifies patients of the availability of last-minute GP appointments

In the UK there is a huge and increasing demand on NHS services, particular within GP and A&E areas. Average wait for a GP appointment is 2 weeks and many patients can expect to wait over ~4 hours in A&E.

Solution: Health Compass - Multi-functional Mobile App Empowering Patient Choice. Real time patient notification of ‘last minute’ availability of GP appointments (ie. resulting from cancellations). App links into patient's NHS number and to standard appointment booking systems. Also broadens patient's awareness of all available to local healthcare options which are accessible to them. Promotion of multiple healthcare resource options aims to guide patients to easily accessible options and help alleviate pressures on busy practices.
