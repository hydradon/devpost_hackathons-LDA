Inspiration

Do you take notes on your laptop? Got textbooks on your laptop? Conveniently have your laptop open when you should be doing work? We do, and we wanted to stop getting distracted.

What it does

Sends one dollar to charity (or your prof if you feel like it) and a text to your legal guardian (or anyone really) telling them you're slacking off.

How we built it

A little bit of javascript, and a whole lotta sugar and caffeine.

Challenges I ran into

Getting twillo to work in a chrome extension. Get anything to work in a chrome extension.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Finishing.

What I learned

How to build a chrome extension, how to stack overflow more efficiently, how much coffee we can tolerate.
