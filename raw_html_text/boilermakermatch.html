Inspiration

We occasionally eat and exercise alone and want to make friends

What it does

Enter your name and phone number, select what you need a buddy for (exercise, eat in dining courts or off campus) and we will find you a match and display their phone number.

How we built it

We used android studio and cloud firestore from Firebase
