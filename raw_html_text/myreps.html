Inspiration:

Phone2Action Competition

What it does:

It shows who your local representatives are. It also contains a political themed game to keep the audience engaged.

How we built it

We used the MIT App Inventor

Challenges we ran into

The AppBuilder acted up a bit, we had trouble connecting our android device to the website, and we ran into time constraints.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of finishing. We are proud of even just creating an app because we are beginners. Being our first Hackathon, we are proud of having a finished product.

What we learned:

A new way to code. We also learned that there varieties of software available

What's next for MyReps

Adding more information for each state representative such as phone number, website, and map of districts.
