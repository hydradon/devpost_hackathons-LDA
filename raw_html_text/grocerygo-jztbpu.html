Inspiration

A quick item pickup does not become quick anymore when there is 1 cashier and 5 people waiting to get checked out. The self checkout kiosks are mostly out of service, does not register my item properly, does not understand on-sale items, and may or may not glitch out and require an associate to clear the error. Convenience shopping should also have a convenience checkout.

What it does

This app is an improvement upon the idea of self checkout through improved accuracy of items and costing. It takes an image and passes it through a model which can identify different products and help populate the checkout list. This app also has other features that developed from e-commerce such as creative discounts, coupon strategies, and data base generation for data mining.

How we built it

React Native - Front end cross platform for app development Flask/ Node - Backend Machine Learning - Google Cloud Platform Python - Creating dataset for training, testing, validating
