Creation of telYpath?

There are 8 pathologist an the entire country. That is the issue that Cambodia is currently facing today.

That is one pathologist per two million people. Cambodia is not alone in the need for pathologist. Countries such

as India and China are in need for pathology experts to help in diagnosis.

What is telYpath?

telYpath is a digital pathology cloud platform that utilizes an existing network of volunteer pathologist to provide

pathology diagnosis in developing countries.

The Goals of telYpath?

The goal of telYpath is to provide pathology diagnosis globally to countries lacking in resources and professionals to

provide patients with a quick and accurate diagnosis.
