Inspiration

Watching people die because of non-availability of required blood at the time of need made me start developing an app that would play a vital role in finding a matching blood group at the time of need.

What it does

Facilitates donors with the registration process so anybody can register details about his blood group, contact details and location. It enables seeker to look for or find, locate, and then connect with the donor.

Challenges I ran into

I had a very short span of time to get done with this kind of app which requires precision and dedication. I started interviewing people who ran into ‘finding a donor’ situations and on the basis of that I started developing each module.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I was a kind of guy who always thought of catching a big fish but working on and building this app changed me in a very good way. Now much of my focus is on developing apps and software which I think can serve humanity.

What I learned

I learned and explored new technologies. I learned that how to work the hard way in order to achieve what is required in a short span of time hence improved my programming capabilities.

What's next for Life Saver Application

Life Saver Application is designed to be easily scalable. I will keep on adding new features based on the feedback. and experiences.We can Register users through Sms based services, and will find the nearest persons on the google map if some One needs the blood groups according to their need in their specific areas.Further I will add Hospital lists and other cities.
