Inspiration

The idea has come to us when we were sitting in a public place then a handicap man without hands asked to join us, he asked us what’s our main activity, we explain to him that our life is about coding after a long talk he seemed interested and he told us with an emotional face that he can’t owing to his obstruction

What it does

we felt sorry about him so we decided to create a platform where this kind of people can say what they want to write and our programme generate the text, for example they say only the word HTML and the programme generate the basic HTML code (head + body).

How we built it

vueJS, JS, HTML5, CSS3
