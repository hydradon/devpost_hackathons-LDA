Inspiration

Who hasn't made lunch plans with friends, only to have the restaurant location change last minute? Your newly acquired taste for Italian is suddenly left unsatiated. The What's for Lunch? app helps prepare you for these last minute changes.

What it does

After inputting some simple search criteria, What's for Lunch? seamlessly presents search results for restaurants offering something completely unlike what you are craving.

How we built it

Using a combination of Xcode and sheer willpower, we built this app with the indecisive eater in mind.

Challenges we ran into

Oddly enough, software is much easier to write when it performs logical functions- allowing the user's inputs to trigger opposite responses was a challenge.

What's next for What's for Lunch?

We would love to expand into the dinner and breakfast markets!
