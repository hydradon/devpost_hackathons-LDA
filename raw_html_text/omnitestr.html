Inspiration

During Sean's last summer internship, his dev team was spending many weeks in identifying the bottlenecks of its products. The team was focused on improving the customer experience, and a very heavy focus was put on optimizing the User Experience by reducing the computation time perceived by the client. Due to this approach in optimization, the team members spent a lot of time in manually testing performances by clicking on div elements and checking the response times. His team wished it had an automated testing paradigm, but it couldn't find any available tool that offers both maximum automation and client-side testing.

What it does

OmniTestr performs client-focused automated testing by traversing through the DOM element trees with given level. Then every endpoints associated with the DOM elements are then tested extensively via automatically provisioned instances on Linode

Tech Stack

MEAN stack, MongoDB, Express framework, etc.
