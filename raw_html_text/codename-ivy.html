Codename-Ivy

Poison ivy identification app

What is it

It is a TensorFlow graph model that can determine if a submitted image contains poison ivy or not, with up to 99.9% accuracy.

Why we were excited about making this

As a group of freshmen, we were very unsure of what direction to go with our hack, but AI was intriguing to us.

What we learned

  • a lot about machine learning
  • a lot about dependencies
  • and a lot about anger management

We became very familiar with python and C#, and we learned how to use a device camera within a Unity app.

So, what now?

We would like to finish integrating our AI within the Unity app when an updated version of the TensorFlowSharp plugin is released. After the integration, we want to publish a mobile app that people can use to identify poison ivy while in the wilderness.

Usage Requirements

  • Python 3.6
  • TensorFlow 1.7
