Inspiration We got inspiration from our own life, when some of our team members had a trouble with buying furniture in her own home and it was hard to decide whether the furniture fits in her home or not.

What it does - With Fitin app you can visualize your selection of furniture inside the room, choose different colors and make a better shopping decisions.

How we built it - We used Unity, artoolkit, android-studio, 3D model.

Challenges we ran into - Didn´t ran into any challenge.

Accomplishments that we're proud of - We created AR app.

What we learned - We learned how to create AR applications.

What's next for Fitin - We would like to make other useful customer features
