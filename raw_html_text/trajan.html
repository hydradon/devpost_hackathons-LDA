Inspiration

Trajan was inspired from the disparity in current health record systems ability to preserve the doctor- patient relationship. Current health record systems force health care providers to focus on typing on a computer instead of focusing on the patient relationship. The name, Trajan, was inspired by the Roman emperor of the same name.

What it does

Trajan records a transcript and captures the essential patient information. In doing so, it allows for doctors to foster a better relationship between them and their patients.

How I built it

Trajan is build using Django, Html5, Css, javascript, and Python3.

Challenges I ran into

Challenges included integrating frontend and backend, getting voice translation software, and learning a new framework.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We are proud of learning a new framework.

What I learned

I learned how to use live reload, and use a new framework.

What's next for Trajan
