What it does

Artistic side scroller for those who love dramatic entertainment.

How I built it

I built it using Swift, xCode as well as powerpoint for prototyping

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned

Finishing up the other 5 levels and creating the animations for the scenes of the game.
