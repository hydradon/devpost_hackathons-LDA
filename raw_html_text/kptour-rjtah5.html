What it does

  1. In order to overcome language barrier user can select language of own choice i-e English and Urdu.
  2. User can view top sights and their surroundings.
  3. Once desired city is selected, user can view the complete detail of hotels, restaurants and other services of the city.
  4. On the other hand while travelling, with the help pf google map you can easily decide which way to go in calculated time shown to you.
  5. Complete history of city will be provided in this App.
  6. This App will be helpful with an Audio Guide feature.
  7. Last but not least in case of any emergency user can go to complain box feature, which will be handled by concerned authorities of city.
