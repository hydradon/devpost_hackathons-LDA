Inspiration

I've wanted to dabble in decentralized apps so what better way than to start with Blockstack.

What it does

Google analytics, but decentralized storage of metrics that you own.

How I built it

First was to understand and integrate with Blockstack auth and the Gaia decentralized storage. After that, develop the dashboard and syncing mechanisms.

Challenges I ran into

Auth stopped working, I think a package conflicted with blockstack and caused encryption to break. A simple "delete node_modules" and re-install fixed it all.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Doing my part to decentralize the internet!

What I learned

Blockstack, Gaia storage

What's next for nfogix

More stats and graphs. Custom event tracking. All currently under development :)
