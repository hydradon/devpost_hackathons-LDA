Inspiration

Most people don't invest in stock or Cryptocurrency because they are not only underbanked but also unprepared to evaluate market data or company finance reports.

What it does

Let's you look at text and finance data about potential investment through the Spritz api in a VR environment while performing deep learning on the brainwaves collected while you evaluate you choices. Provide feedback on how well you are investing.

How I built it

A web application that uses the Oculus Rift, Muse Headband, and the Qualcomm Dragon board to help people educate themselves and the choose the right investments

Challenges I ran into

Reading and finding documentation for certain libraries.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Teamwork.
