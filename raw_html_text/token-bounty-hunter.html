Inspiration

I loved the sticker/water-bottle/NFT thing at ETHDenver, which incentivized hackathon attendees to talk to sponsors.

What it does

It's a scavenger hunt. Users people search for other people at the hackathon who hold a given token. If they find that person, everybody who passed the bounty gets a share of the prize.
