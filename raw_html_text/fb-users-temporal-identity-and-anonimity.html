Inspiration and What it Does

Engaging Facebook Communities through Anonimity/ Users Temporal Identity(TUI) Post Status Updates to Free the Oppressed.

Millions of People around the world lives in fear. Some are even Oppressed and they can't share their Feelings on Facebook for the fear of being fished out and prosecuted by the bad ones,Politicians, Friends, Families and Relatives etc.

To this effect some Post on Facebook Groups can be better shared in Anonimity or by use of Temporal User Identification(TUI).

A friend of mine suffering from HIV says She is afraid of Participating in a Facebook HIV group discussion because She might easily be spotted as HIV victim by her Facebook Friends and there are so many similar cases...

While Anonimity might not be a viable option but TUI will be a good option for sharing data without knowing the real identity of the person.

Temporal User Identification(TUI) automatically assign the Facebook user a Temporal Username and Profile Pictures only for that Particular Post. This will allow Facebook Group Members to Share News, Informations, ask question etc without revealing its main User Identity which in turn will drive more engaging updates within Facebook community..
Share your feelings on Facebook without fear...

Engaging the fearful and Oppressed on Facebook.

Some persons are afraid of sharing their feelings on Facebook. For instance when it comes to health related issues, Political News, Environmental Events, most people will like to ask questions or share data in privacy while protecting their Identity from being seeing by Public or Her Friends on Facebook. The app has answer to data. it let FB Users share Post updates by:
1.) Using Anonymity (This will automatically replace Facebook Username and Profile pictures with anonymous Equivalents only for that Post Status Updates.
2.) Temporal Users Identity(TUI) : This TUI is a more better because will automatically give the user new look by assigning her a fresh Username and Profile Pictures automatically while hiding the Real Facebook Identity of the User posting the content.

With this concept, Millions of People can Share content on Facebook without fear. This will create an engaging Status Updates ever in the history

How to use this app:

1.) Login using Facebook
2.) Enter Post Title, Category and Descriptions
3.) Select How you want to appear in the Post Status Updates. You will be Presented with 3 options

A.) Display my Facebook Credentials/Identity: This will use your Facebook Data to display on the Post.
B.) Use Anonymity: This will give the User Anonymous thus hiding your real Facebook Identity on that Post.
C.) Temporal user Identity: This will automatically assign User temporal username and Profile pictures thus hiding your real Facebook Identity on that Post. User can now post or Share content on Facebook without Fear...
4.) User can Like and comment on the Post
5.)The User can Share any Post to her FaceBook Timeline as well as to her specified Facebook Friends in HIPAA Compliance Mode.

FaceBook Technology Used:

1.) Facebook Login Graph API: Used to authenticate Users
2.) Facebook Shared Button: used to allow user to share content to her Facebook Timeline
3.) Facebook Send Dialog Button: used to allow user to share content to a specific user in HIPAA Compliance Mode.
