Inspiration - Wanted to build a skill to easily access cult and popular movie quotes

What it does - This one accesses quotes from the movie Office Space, one of my cult favorites

How I built it - Used a model from an Alexa Skills course I took as the base

Challenges I ran into - Getting code to provide multiple quotes from a specific character or from random selection without closing and opening the app

Accomplishments that I'm proud of - Solving the challenge, with some help from the course instructor

What I learned - Some of what it takes to build a first Alexa skill

What's next for Cinema Quotes - Either multiple apps for different movies, or preferably multiple movies in this one app
