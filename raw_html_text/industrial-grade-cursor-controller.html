What it does

It's a smart mouse - it moves the cursor and clicks, BUT it also copies and pastes between devices

How we built it

We put an arduino in a box and put a bunch of wires in that box and put some buttons on that box. We also wrote a driver

Challenges we ran into

Originally, we were going to use an actual mouse, and have the arduino act as a buffer between that and the computer, but we decided to go a different direction after frustration. We also had a lot of trouble implementing the copying and pasting
