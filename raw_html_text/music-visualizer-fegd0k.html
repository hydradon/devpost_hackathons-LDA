Inspiration

We really like music and we're also visual people! Worked on a couple projects and got bored when they were done, so this came to mind!

What it does

Makes LEDs react to the music you're listening to!

How I built it

Using an Arduino and a Spectrum Shield, we're able to read music from our media devices and control an LED strip to showcase what our music LOOKS like.
