Inspiration

We wanted to help the community in a more unofficial way.

How it works

When you see a person in need on the street, drop a pin on Google Maps. Another person can see that pin, and hand-deliver food, coats, or whatever may be needed to that person.

Challenges I ran into

It was difficult to divide up the labor because our strengths are not necessarily what we enjoy doing.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We survived our first hackathon and completed a project.

What I learned

We learned that value of time management and resource allocation.

What's next for HomelessFeeder

The next step will be styling and adding a few more features that we didn't have time for.
