Inspiration

With flights going from anywhere to everywhere in these times, deciding a vacation destination has become a nightmarish task. Deciding on a destination has started taking more time than the actual vacation. Our product aims to reduce this mental load on the user, and present him with travel plans curated to his needs, that makes trip selection a child’s play, and the actual trips more worthwhile.

What it does

  1. Recommends exciting new places to travel to.
  2. Builds the search results around your budget.
  3. Easy one click ticket purchasing.
  4. Seamless itinerary automatically generated to help you get the most out of your vacation.'
  5. Tracks your American Airlines account so you can be in control of your vacation.

How we built it

Flask full stack with Tailwind CSS.

Challenges we ran into

Integrating so many APIs into one project in one night.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Sleek user interface with a polished user profile and account management. Intuitive workflow that streamlines users into minimal click ticket purchases.

What we learned

A great UI can make a good project become amazing.

What's next for Dream Vacation

Integration into the live American Airlines API so we can have more cities to choose from. Enable real ticket purchasing with your bank account linked to the your user profile.
