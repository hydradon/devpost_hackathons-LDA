• Ingrdnts in action

Inspiration

As a college student, we tend to have some left-over raw food in our fridge but we do not know what to make out of them.

What it does

It helps people (specifically college students in their dorm) to find out what they can make with their left-over ingredients by searching through our recipe database.

How we built it

We used the framework Laravel with Bootstrap, and languages such as PHP, HTML, SQL, CSS, Javascript.

Challenges we ran into

AJAX is annoying.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It works.

What we learned

Upon creation of the web app, although it looked easy, it was not. We also learned Laravel's environment.

What's next for Ingrdnts

Adding more features such as: expanding the database, add a user rate-and-review system so users can filter through top rated recipes.
