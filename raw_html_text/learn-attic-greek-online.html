I was inspired to create learnatticgreek.online from my experience with my Attic Greek class here at UMBC. While I took the language in high school, others in my class starting out may be struggling to remember names of types of grammar, let alone what they stand for. So I created learnatticgreek.online to lay out the basics of Attic Greek grammar, from noun and verb conjugation to accent laws, to give beginners a reference point to start on. I built the site using domain.com 's built in website builder. The builder was very intuitive, so I didn't face many challenges but I'd say that the hardest challenge for me was to ensure that I was providing accurate information. All in all, I feel like I learned more about web design and I hope that I can continue to use that as I dive deeper into coding.
