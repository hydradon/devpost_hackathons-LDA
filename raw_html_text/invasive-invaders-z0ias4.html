Inspiration

Malama Maunalua needed a sophisticated and intuitive way to store and visualize data. As well as an exciting way to get the community involved.

What it does

Our app effectively stores and displays their data, as well as inspires people to join the initiative by providing competitions and prize opportunities.

How we built it

We built it using HTML, JavaScript, and CSS

Challenges we ran into

We ran into a couple bugs while developing, buts that is to be expected. We weren't able to create everything we would of like but that was due to a lack of time and resources.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of the concept and how we "gameified" their needs in order to inspire the local community to aid the initiative.

What we learned

We learned that with time and effort we can make anything and everything we put our minds to. We also learned more about the three languages we made our app with. We also learned to make connections to real life situations with games and other subjects we are interested in and/or problems that need solving.

What's next for Invasive Invaders

We plan on adding on to our app, and continuing to make our product into our perfect concept so it could go on to become a standard in environmental aid.
