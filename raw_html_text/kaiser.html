• Kaiser

Inspiration

We wanted an easy way to compose UIs in a markup-style environment and have them instantly deployable to production.

What it does

Playground to generate a site in MDX, with built in deployments.

Friendly & flexible enough to compose and deploy UIs during your morning commute.

Support for sub-domains is half finished.

How we built it

We built it with a lot of coffee, less than average sleep, tandoori food, and modern programming tools.

Challenges we ran into

We couldn't get our more ambitious initial ideas up and running -- we had to pivot to a simpler and faster solution. The clock was running and we wanted to ship.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Shipping a working prototype in 24 hours.

What we learned

Get the smallest functional germ of your full idea up and running, and improve on it over time.
