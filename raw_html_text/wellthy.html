Inspiration

Symptoms of clinical depression are hard to trace in the early stages and in-between traditional therapy sessions (CMHA, 2018). We created Wellthy to facilitate the treatment of those being treated for depression by connecting them to their doctors in real-time.

What it does

Users can log daily fluctuations in mood, attitudes, and behaviors with reliable psychometric questionnaires. If you are being treated with psychiatric prescriptions, Wellthy is personalized to notify you when to take your medications, and your responses are tracked in order to manage your overall recovery progress. Between you and your professional healthcare provider, Wellthy keeps a confidential record of your treatment history to ensure your overall mental well-being.

How we built it?

We used Xcode and Swift to develop this application.

Challenges we ran into...

Working on Xcode was challenging for the team as 4 out of 5 of us had never done iOS dev before. Getting around the idea of working off lists in a class instead of utilizing a cloud DB was another challenge and finally ensuring that our design and structure is synchronized throughout the application was a time-consuming activity as well.

Accomplishments that we are proud of...

We committed to completing 3 major features and 1 minor feature and were successfully able to complete them during the 24 hour hack-period.

What's next for Wellthy...

Wellthy wishes to incorporate more advanced features including guardian emergency protocols. audio note-taking and analyzing ability in the journal section. Several quality updates will also be released in the next update :)
