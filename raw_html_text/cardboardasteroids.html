Inspiration

36 years ago, the world at large was astounded by the technology of video games through Asteroids. For many, this was their first video game, and for this reason holds a special place in their memory. Now, in the age of Virtual Reality, we have sought to recreate that fantastic first experience in a new, re-imagined way through the power of Google Cardboard. The graphics may be similar, but it's a whole new world of entertainment.
