  • Synchronous party playlist using Google Firebase

  • Stylized multi-line lyric visualization with Three.js

  • Background colors are calculated from album pictures

Lyricly

The project is still under active development. It was split into three different components:

GatherPlay: the collaborative playlist. https://github.com/Neo-Zhixing/GatherPlay

GatherRocks: music visualization. Reads from Spotify, audio files or environment. https://github.com/Neo-Zhixing/GatherRocks

Service - the backend NodeJS server built on top of Google App Engine. Requires MongoDB, Firestore and Redis. https://github.com/Neo-Zhixing/GatherPlay-Service
