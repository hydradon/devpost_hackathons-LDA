Upbeat is a service dedicated to helping new artists find their target audience and listeners to find undiscovered artists actually in line with their tastes.

There are 2 sides to the service

Artist: Artists can create a profile and tag 3 "related artists" that can be endorsed by listeners. The more endorsements from listeners, the higher they will rank in our algorithms

Listeners: Listeners can log in with their Spotify account - based on their listening history and the artist's self-chosen tags we return them a list of artists they may be interested in a tinder style interface

Swipe right to save to your Spotify library and left to just skip to the next artist! (And don't forget to endorse artists' related sounds if you think they are legit)

Upbeat helps new artists move on up!
