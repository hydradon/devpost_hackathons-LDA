Theme - Brexit & Greek financial crisis

Inspiration

Helping people in financial crisis with their financial needs.

What it does

Enabling User to see all their credit/Debit, next scheduled financial commitments and priorities on one dashboard and helping them to modify as per their need.

How I built it

Created objects to hold customer, account, bill, and merchant information from the Nessie API. Started work to post test account, bill, and merchant info from a local JSON asset to Nessie for in app use. Added notification on firebase to get ALERT in app, Created UI for Splash Screen and Prioritization screen with tabs and ViewPager

Challenges I ran into

Time and minor issues with posting to Nessie.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

First Hackathon experience, on spot deciding an app meeting peoples need in financial crisis, came with screens & designs, integrated API, Firebase.

What I learned

I learned about the Nessie API and how to better get started working on apps in a team environment.
