Inspiration

Always thinking exams and due dates are further away than they are.

What it does

Basically sets a count down for tasks leading to the due date. Example: It says its due in 5 days.

How I built it

I used html, css, and js. Vue as the framework. User sign in verified by Blockstack. Storage on Gaia.
