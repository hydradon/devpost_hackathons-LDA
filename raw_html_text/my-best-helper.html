Inspiration

I started this project to participate the Vanhackatown. I liked the idea of My Best Helper, and, decided to give a try.

What it does

Here, you can anwser the questions about your profile, and, after that it will give you a resumee, saying more about your personality.

How I built it

I Used Visual Studio Community 2015 with Xamarin Plugins, Windows 10, and mu Android Cellphone. I developed mainly using C#, .Net, Xamarin, and some Json Conversions.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

For my first hackatown, I'm very produd of my accomplishiment. It's fast, beautiful, fluid, and looks like a Quiz Game.

What I learned

I learned how to use better the Xamarin API, it's documentation, and other ways to test it.

What's next for My Best Helper

I'm thinking to connect it with a web server, so the more quenstions, answers and results can be added. I'm thinking to turn him into a full game, connecting it to facebook and improving the challenges.
