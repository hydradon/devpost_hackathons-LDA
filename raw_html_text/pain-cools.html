You know how there are many chronic pain patients/sufferers who feel they are alone on it, we provide a peer-to-peer connecting platform that helps with setting goals, manage challenges and provide rewards based on the latest insights and research. In fact it continuously learns from your habits and their effects and other people in the same position to improve.

Setting an ambitious yet achievable goal is a good way of distracting from the pain and regaining control. We help by breaking the goal into steps that you track in the app, getting rewards, support from peers and contributing data back to researchers to continuously improve the techniques and tools.
