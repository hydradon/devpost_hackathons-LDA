Inspiration

A friend of ours loves to make puns and we love to hear them. we thought it would be a good idea if there was a dedicated space to post puns and rate them

What it does

allows user to post their own puns and lets other people rate them. Based on popularity (punny and unpunny votes) their posts go up higher on the dashboard

How we built it

using meteorJS and mongoDB as our data base

Challenges we ran into

we couldn't get some ui components to work. we couldn't get the routes to work properly. sorting posts was a challenge too

Accomplishments that we're proud of

the whole app. duh

What we learned

meteorJS and perseverance

What's next for Punnity

maybe turn it into a phone app if its is received well at the hackathon
