Inspiration

I was inspired after realizing how long it can take for emergency responders to respond to a situation, and wanted to improve upon the situation.

What it does

When you dial 911, the operator receives your location in real time, medical information, emergency contact, and other important information before you can even say hello.

Challenges I ran into

Dealing with location data/gps, background apps, google maps

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The system actually works and in real time
