Inspiration

Kids love games. Food games are very popular. And games offer educational opportunities. The concept behind Gamify Food Literacy is to discuss how to combine popular food games and take advantage of the educational opportunities to promote Food Literacy. We’ll provide one model for how to build a Food Literacy game, how it could sustain itself through sponsorship and ad revenue, and how this particular model would benefit the lives of its target demographic both for themselves and their families in the future.
