Inspiration

The very popular social game called 2p questions

What it does

Based on pre-created themes, you upload images of people to the cup. The person whose cups the image is sent to tries to guess who the person on it is by not seeing the cup and asking only 20 yes/no questions.

How I built it

We used illustrator to design the app and have developed the application for android

Challenges I ran into

The cup refused to pair with the android devices on our team and it took a lot of trial and error and pouring of hot and cold water into the cup to get it to start working

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We've never created a functioning game so quickly. So definitely that!

What I learned

Working super fast and being able to communicate with team mates you barely know
