Inspiration

We all wanted to make a different game, so we joined forces.

What it does

Potion Royale functions as a multiplayer shooter that tests players' speed and accuracy in terms of shooter standards. The game is fast and frantic, allowing plenty of chaos and competition.

How we built it

We used Roblox Studio, a built-in engine for Roblox games built from Lua. The fireballs, ingredients, and assembly were all built from the ground up as unique properties in the world.

Challenges we ran into

As we all had different ideas from the start, sorting out a consistent game was challenging at first. However, once we all had a consistent idea of what the game would be, the development process ran smoother.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The crafting system, ingredient spawning, and collection are all unique parts of the game we're proud of. We poured hours into building those from the ground up, so we're glad to see them running smoothly.

What we learned

We learned to cooperate together, compromise our own ambitions to make a cohesive game, and how to build systems from the ground up with Lua.

What's next for potion royale

We hope to add additional features and parts to the game, to make it even better than our original vision was.
