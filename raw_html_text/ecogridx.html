Inspiration

The energy crisis in Pakistan lead us to look into ways we can reduce the load-shedding and outage periods for the people in similar regions, and providing users with insights into their energy history and forecasting

What it does

This provides insights to users that show perofrmance of solar panel units and forecasting for energy consumption and generation.

How we built it

Using public API's integrated with predix iot

Challenges we ran into

Predix usability, lack of knowledge, time constrains, legal issues

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned

Nuc, Predix machine, polymer, energy crisis situation

What's next for EcoGridx

Finish the project and sell it to Asia.
