Inspiration

The Office season 7 episode 9

What it does

It utilizes numerous social media apis to send messages from all social media services to the targeted person

How we built it

We constructed a web application that utilizes MongoDB, Twilio API, Messenger API, and Zapier.

Challenges we ran into

Nodejs is still kind of new to us and we didn't have enough time to accomplish as much as we wanted to.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Being able to utilize so many APIs at once

What we learned

Sometimes the easiest parts end up of the project end up taking the longest

What's next for Wuphf

UI fixes, adding Snapchat api to send snaps, and controlling the internal notification settings for the receiver's phone to ensure that the volume is at its max.
