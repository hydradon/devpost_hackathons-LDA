What it does

Talk to VR Kitty just like she was a real kitty.

Saying sit makes her sit, saying jump makes her jump, friend makes her grab her friend, and go away makes her run away (but wait for the surprise when she returns!)

How I built it

Unity engine, optimized for Oculus VR

Built With

  • unity
