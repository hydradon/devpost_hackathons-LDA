Inspiration

D.S.A.Z.A.F.N. simulates a city attacked by zombies who want to eliminate the masterminds , these nerds handle the defenses of the city using drones. Drones are you!

What it does

The game consist in stop the zombie invasión, these want to reach the great tree where it is found the master mind behind the Dron which is responsoble to put tanks, helicopters and cars to stop them.

How we built it

We use some Asets from the Asset Store and we create others objects to create the universe of the game.

Challenges we ran into

Being our first game as team's biggest challenge was to unite all our ideas to translate them into the game taking advantage of the interaction of HTC LIVES.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud to have made the progress of the game in only 3 weeks.

What we learned

Mainly we have learned to use the basic tools of HTC VIVE.
