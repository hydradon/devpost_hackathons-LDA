Team #: 24

Inspiration

The Inspiration comes from the current digital remote controller (i.e. slides switch, Surface Dial). We combined two types of the designs together to create a inexpensive and simple remote controller that won't affect user's hand gesture while you are doing the presentations.

What it does

Switch slides, pen adjustment when taking notes (or any kinds of simple operation to the device based on user's need).

How we built it

We used Partical Photon, force sentitive resistor, and 3D-printed rings.

Challenges we ran into

Programming. Have to learn Photon from the scratch.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

A simple, small device that can do almost everything.

What we learned

3D printing, modeling, hardware design, programming
