Inspiration

Room4 is a website platform, potentially an app, dedicated to helping people find emergency housing for those in need during disasters. This has been inspired by the “#roomformanchester” hashtag. Our Website tracks information requests via Facebook, Twitter, and our forms, and pairs hostess with hosters.
