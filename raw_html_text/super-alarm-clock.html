• The device disassembled

Inspiration: I have a hard time waking up in the morning and want to not have a hard time.

What it does: It turns on your bedroom light when it goes off, motivating you to wake up.

How we built it: With Arduino, particle photon, Bluetooth, and a lot of patience.

Challenges we ran into: The Bluetooth modules were finicky to work with.

Accomplishments that we're proud of: We got the Bluetooth modules to talk to each other.

What we learned: How to get Bluetooth modules to talk with each other much easier.

What's next for Super Alarm Clock: Make it play any song you like along with turning on the light.
