poweraid

Google Chrome Extension that provides an accessible and centralized crowdsourcing platform to donate to trending social causes! Scrapes Event Registry API for most recent news regarding natural disasters, natural hazards, and philanthropy and populates an extension feed with the top 5 ranked events. Then it produces a donation link to allow people to easily donate to the causes that they believe in.
