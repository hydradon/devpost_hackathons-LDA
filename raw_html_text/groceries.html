Inspiration

Two main factors:

  • Make one quick trip through the store and check off in order the items needed to be purchased. No running back and forth or getting confused looking for items.

  • Reduce food waste by getting recommendations on recipes based on items purchased closest to expiration.

What it does

Tracks a user's habits (granted that they take the same path through a store), and begins to arrange the items in the order that the user would come across in order.

Challenges I ran into

Properly sorting the items, and gathering expiration dates relative to purchase.

What's next for Groceries

More accurate habit tracking, coupons, deals, better recipe recommendations.
