What it does

Control number of available Link Types for particular Issue Types in particular Projects. Or even turn off linking for particular projects. That's it.

How I built it

Using Atlassian SDK, Java, Javascript and mixture of knowledge and experience.

Challenges I ran into

Right design. Changing concept while development was the biggest challenge.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Manage to deliver.

What I learned

Don't listen to that something is impossible. Even don't ask, just do your job.

What's next for Link Schemes for JIRA

1) Provide configurable sorting of link types
2) Assuring that these limits will be respected in whole JIRA including JIRA API.
