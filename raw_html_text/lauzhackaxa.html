"# LauzHackAXA"

With this web project Homely we aim towards connecting foreign students with local hosts to support in their cultural integration and serve the purpose of cultural exchange as well as fills the void of missing family back home. Share the joys, festivities and create an everlasting emotional bond. Many families, especially older couples or single and lonely natives might want to support a young kid, eradicating the feeling mutually, of loneliness and missing the dear ones; thus serving the motto of our project: No more lonely, go homely!

The future scopes are immense and if carried in the right direction, it will add an ever lasting value to the society.
