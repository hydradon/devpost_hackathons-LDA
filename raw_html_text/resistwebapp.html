Inspiration

We were inspired by recent political changes happening in our country and around the world. We wanted to find a way for more people to be able to join peaceful protest and resist unfair governmental laws.

What it does

Creates protest in your area and view protests.

How I built it

Our webapp is suppose to create a protest and store it an database. The values from our database will display a location in the google maps so other can see protests happening around them. We used firebase for our database. We used html, css, and bootstrap for our webpage.

Challenges I ran into

Trying to understand how databases work and how to configure the google maps api.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

we were able to get results from our database and our pages ran smoothy.

What I learned

How to use bootstap, firebase, and google maps api.

What's next for ResistWebApp

To create more social media integration to each location of where a protest is happening. And be able to join protests.
