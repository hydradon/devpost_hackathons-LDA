Inspiration

Now a public network platform for people to discuss and gain tips and knowledge from health expert, or even normal people who want to share their knowledge about health.

What it does

Using Cisco Spark APIs, I integrated with the basic web app that people can create new rooms, have access to rooms that were created by health experts to share their knowledge by create new messages in each room which is related to different fields about healthcare.
