Bundle

Bundle is about buying a setup created and curated by a professional. If you want that one artist, audio producer, or designer's gear, those professionals would create a "Bundle" and you could buy the entire "setup".

What it does

  • Choose a Bundle you want
  • Explore what the Bundle has to offer
  • Buy the Bundle, and the items will automatically be placed in your Amazon cart

How we built it

Front-end: HTML, CSS, JS, React, react-router Back-end: node.js, express.js

Challenges we ran into

  • We were a two person team so it was hard to fully deliver a complete experience
  • Designing and prototyping took way longer as shopping apps include many psychological "micro-decisions" we never think of until we start designing
  • Connecting our app with Amazon was an abstract and confusing task
  • Design was thought of much while creating this. Starting with a more complex design, and making sure it was cohesive with its own language was very hard.

Accomplishments we're proud of

  • Making the Bundle selection and payout work
  • Utilizing a router to guide the user from a landing page experience to the actual app
  • Connecting a backend to a frontend to create a more varied and complete experience
