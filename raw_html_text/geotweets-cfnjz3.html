GeoTweets is an interactive map that lets you explore the locations of tweets based on a given keyword. Users are able to locate regional trends on certain events, which allows them to analyze and interpret factors such as causes and developments.

Our data was scraped from Twitter and visualized through MicroStrategy Workstation.
