Inspiration

We ALL have that elderly relative who isn't the most tech-saavy (a.k.a. not at all), ESPECIALLY when it comes to complex mobile apps.

But does that mean they don't deserve access to today's life-changing tech?

NO! ABSOLUTELY NOT!

Recently, we have seen massive development of health/fitness apps for young to middle-aged people like _ MyFitnessPal _ ! But nothing for our elderly community! They are the demographic most susceptible to health issues, and thus the most in need of accessible healthcare technology.

As you can see, there's something very wrong with this, and we wanted to address it!
