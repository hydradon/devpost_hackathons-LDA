Problem

In the fast paced digital lifestyles we have created a dependency on Youtube and DIY tutorials. This produces a lack of community. We no longer share generational skills to be producers. We are only indoctrinated to be consumers. Everyone would prefer one-on-one mentoring for learning various uplifting practical skills.

Our Solution

Our platform is a high quality mentor matching application for learning practical skills. We connect you to well matched local mentors to learn something brand new and see your next passion project all the way through. Mentors will be able to share their skills while also making extra money and mentees will get invaluable guidance and advice. Teach a man to fish and he’ll eat for a lifetime.
