• A better standard for currency and Money

Land32, the core platform of Land.OT, is set out to digitally capture each sqcm of earth's spatial coordinates with unique cryptographic representation to correspond to an exchangeable store of value.

​

Built on the TODA protocol, a layer-zero blockchain enabling protocol for the decentralized creation and management of value and ownership, Land can become fungible and transportable making it a superior stable currency backed by valuable assets.

​

Land32 provides sufficient incentives for all stakeholders to participate, engage, contribute and benefit from a universal system that enables Nations, individuals, local and global financial institutions to work together towards global financial stability and prosperity.
