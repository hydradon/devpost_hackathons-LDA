Inspiration

We love playing Mad Libs, but the stories become repetitive after a while. What if you had a virtually infinite source of content, like the entire internet?

What it does

Turns any page into a game of Mad Libs by randomly removing nouns, verbs, and adjectives. Then you get to replace them!

How we built it

With a lot of pain. It's a chrome extension that gets a page, identifies parts of speech, and then randomly takes some away. Then it asks the user for words to replace them.
