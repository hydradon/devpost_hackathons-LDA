Inspiration

Mark Zuckenberg obviously.

What it does

Chatbot that allows an individual to order food from Dartmouth's very own KAF.

How we built it

We initially built it in php, but found that node.js was a more suitable framework to use in the end.

Challenges we ran into

We had issues with the chatbot bouncing messages back and forth. At one point, we had 10,000 messages in our inbox.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Our persistence and commitment until the end.

What we learned

Node.js, heroku, chatbots

What's next for dartbot

Conquer the Dartmouth food scene
