About

An exciting virtual reality experience at the airport. You are the air traffic controller and your job is to land airplanes and avoid crashes.

Innovation

GUI inside of VR looks out of place because we don't see floating GUI in the real world. However, we do see GUI on the devices we use. So to pull up the in-game menu you pick up a device and use it just like you would outside of VR.

Immersive

Using Cardboard spatial audio rendering creates great sound to feel like the planes are really flying overhead.

Entertaining

Who doesn't enjoy watching jets fly by as well as the occasional explosion?
