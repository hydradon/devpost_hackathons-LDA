Executive Summary

The Wellness App will be using wristband health monitors to receive all the information regarding the user's heart rate, dehydration, and body temperature information. The Wellness App is intended to be used by the Crew Leader who will oversee the data. It is the responsibility of the employee to wear the wristband. Any concerning data such as a low heart rate or if no data can be pulled will be alerted to the Crew Leader. From there, the leader can locate the individuals 's location and handle the matter. Additionally, all data collected can be exported directly to OSHA or saved fro archival use. We are currently finished with the design stage and will be creating the application in Android Studio using java.
