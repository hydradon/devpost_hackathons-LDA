Features

  • Multiple chatbots that provide emotional support in various scenarios, including advice on how to deal with triggers and what to do when warning signs appear (Insight 4)
  • Maps of local support groups and recovery sessions which provide crucial support for those that are still struggling with MAT and life during recovery (Insight 3)
  • Personalized profiles that allow users to interact w/ each other
  • Simple-to-read and easy-to-access database of important articles regarding recovery (Insight 1, 2)
  • Short video playlist for motivation and inspiration from people who have underwent similar experiences (Insight 2)
  • In-app calendar to keep recovery-related events exclusively inside the app so others do not have to see; support group meetings are easily added and other types of events can be included as well (Insight 5)
