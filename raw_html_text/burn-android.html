Imagine having a witty conversation with one of your closest friends and out of the blue You #getrekt with a burn. With no time to spare and no ingenuity left in you with this staggering wound, you pull up the Burn app. With support from anonymous crowds all over country, you manage to utilize their crowdsourced ideas to deliver one of the best comebacks you have ever had the privilege of reading. This Burn app offers and intuitive and fun experience of getting users from all over to assist in offering their wit and articulation in making conversations with friends a more exciting experience.

Features:

  • Facebook login
  • Firebase backend
  • First usage of custom-built NoSQL ORM library (Firebomb)
