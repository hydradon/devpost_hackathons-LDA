It all started over a brainstorming session ....

After shooting down several ideas, including geolocation for crime to compliment generators for upset users, we came up with the idea of optimizing one's vacation choice. Since there are many destinations one would like to visit, and wait times associated with those destinations (not to mention travel time from the current location), we thought we could create a minimum spanning tree using the Google Maps API. Our demo for the web-app will take over from there...
