Inspiration

We wanted to connect people though technology and music.

What it does

You can upload a little clip of you singing/playing a tune, and others will sample it and add it to a bigger project. So every song is a remix of a previous one!

How we built it

We used Adobe Muse because we don't have much experience with computer coding.

Challenges we ran into

Uploading it to domain.com has been our main roaadblock.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Even though we felt like quitting, we continued and stayed up the entire night to create a project we're proud of.

What we learned

We learned how to use Adobe Muse, and how much work you must put into a project to achieve your goal.

What's next for Jiley

We plan on going to other hackathons and expanding on this project.
