Castle Susan

a good castle – probably the best castle. it's a castle, called castle susan. it's a castle of dreams.

it made of cardboard and the drawbridge goes up and down when you press a button on the webby dashboard. also it has a high tech security system installed: there's a webcam taped to one of the walls

in the future we want to add google assistant / amazon alexa support, so you can control your castle using your voice
