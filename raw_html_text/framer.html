Framer

Android TV Backdrop Service for Modern Art

Uses a self-built RESTful API to serve images. Mats extremely high resolution art pieces and displays when the TV is inactive, turning it into a piece of dynamic wall art Concept similar to the Samsung Frame TV, which charges a ridiculous upcharge for the priviledge of their gallery and matting Added features build upon this concept, like matting color which adapts to the artwork Utilizes an extremely new and virgin territory in the Android SDK, the Android Backdrop Daydream Service
