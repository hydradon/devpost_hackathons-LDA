Inspiration

It takes a long time to request a tutor at Duke, hence we decided to create a way to cut down the time in between requesting a tutor and getting a tutor.

What it does

We link students with tutors by showing which tutors are available at which time to tutor specific courses. Students are also able to rate the tutors out of 5 as well as see the grades they got in the course that they are tutoring in.

How I built it

html, javascript, css, django, python.

Challenges I ran into

It was difficult to create a database and complete the login page.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The slick look of the website.
