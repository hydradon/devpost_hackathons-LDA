After my visit to Egypt this summer, I realized how I needed internet in an internet deprived place !

What it does

It's basically an Artificial Intelligence on a hotline that should assist you in finding answers to any of your questions. You can ask it :

  • direction
  • distance
  • time related questions.

How I built it

Call the hotline and ask your Questions ! You'll receive a text message shortly with an answer to your question.
