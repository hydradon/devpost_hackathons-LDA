Inspiration: In daily life we don't know the first aid steps of every emergency we face, hence to aware people about these important emergencies, and what to do in such situations this skill will provide assistance.

What it does: It provides information about the medical emergencies and what a person should do in case of these emergencies.

How we built it: Using NodeJS.

Challenges we ran into: Maintaining the long term persistence with the help of DynamoDB.

Accomplishments that we're proud of: To cover most of the day to day emergencies, which would be helpful to the users.

What we learned: Maintaining the long term persistence with the help of DynamoDB.

What's next for First Aid Info: To add more such medical emergencies in the next version.
