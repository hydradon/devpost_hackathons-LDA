In order to use this skill you will need to download the desktop client at: http://connectskill.tk/

This skill allows you to easily and seamlessly link your PC to your Alexa and preform custom actions like turning off your screen or opening an application. Linking your Alexa with your PC has never been more efficient and secure. Simplify say “Alexa, tell my computer to link” followed by the four digit security code displayed on the desktop app. All your information is stored using your unique and secure amazon ID meaning you have the safety and security of amazon protecting your computer. New features and action types are being released frequently and with the auto updating desktop client you will never miss out on the latest features!
