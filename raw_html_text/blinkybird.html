Inspiration

Using your mind to control things is the next frontier. This was also inspired by the thought that people who are quadriplegic can play games using facial expressions!

What it does

The goal is to be able to control the movement of a ball on the screen using blinks. The intensity with which you blink controls the height of the ball on the screen. Ideally, you must maneuver the ball through a series of obstacles (rectangles) that appear randomly. (Future: By clenching your jaw, you can freeze the screen and by widening your eyes, destroy all the obstacles!)

How we built it

Using muse and python.

Challenges we ran into

Muse battery life. Connecting Muse and python.

Accomplishments that we're proud of
