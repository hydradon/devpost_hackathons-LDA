Inspiration

I was making a snowboarding app and thought it would be cool to have my buddies listen to me while we snowboard. I built this as a third party so that other apps can connect real time music.

What it does

Everyone should be able to listen to music with people no matter if they are on Spotify, Apple Music, Pandora, etc. At the same moment as well!

How I built it

Sweat and tears!

Challenges I ran into

Encoding and decoding along with a mix of music

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Made the API with massive Deep Linking

What I learned

Deep Linking, mixing tons of music platforms together, Facebook Open Graph

What's next for Listen with
