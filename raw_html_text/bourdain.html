By utilizing Esri, you are able to determine points of interest in your neighborhood. Clicking on one of these points will provide a more in-depth view of the restaurant, such as how many users are currently at this location. You can begin or join a conversation to discover more about the restaurant.

Want to know what dishes are popular? What about how long the wait is? And how good is the venue for large parties? Powered by Hyphenate, these real-time chat room conversations provide insight to Bourdain in order to help you. With more and more discussions, Bourdain is able to figure out the answers to all sorts of questions, thereby becoming a local advisor to restaurants anywhere.

Bourdain is a learning AI advisor whom is trained through dynamic conversations happening at restaurants about restaurants by patrons.
