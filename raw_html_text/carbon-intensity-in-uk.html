What it does

Alexa will tell you carbon intensity in UK Areas. You can ask carbon intensity in Wales, Scotland, England, London, Midlands, Yorkshire. It is also enabled for Alexa UK Store.

Thank you to http://carbonintensity.org.uk to provide carbon intensity API

How can I use?

Example usage: Alexa, ask uk carbon intensity tell me carbon intensity
