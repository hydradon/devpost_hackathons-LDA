This project was started from college students and others alike opening the fridge and not knowing what to make. Everyone needs to eat, and cooking doesn’t come easy to some and other are just looking for new recipes to make. This give people another option for finding recipes, along with giving them options to save the users favorite recipes that they enjoyed on their profile. This application will show the most popular recipes from food2fork.com base on the ingredients given.
