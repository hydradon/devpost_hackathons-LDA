Inspiration

I tired when visiting my brother's house outside the city (Bandung), then he advised me to go to a massage parlor near the house. It turns out that there are all blind masseurs, and my massager is the manager, he is also a blind person and all are android users. They can use smartphones for calls, messaging, listening to the radio and even making an order (gojek - grab) ride sharing application. He said his problem with current apps for read money is quite slow, have to take money picture with right potition and accuracy is no guarantee.

What it does

this apps can help blind people for read money realtime , fast and ready for some combination, for example read several bills simultaneously and calculate the total
