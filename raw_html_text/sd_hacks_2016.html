Pebblim

An activity app for the Pebble Time Smartwatch. Using walking-steps, can gain increase the level of your Pebblim. Features custom drawn images and and special animation algorithms for the app. Faced interesting challenges when developing for a platform which is so restrictive on processing power and memory. Come check out our awesome 3D printed model of our very own Pebblim as well!
