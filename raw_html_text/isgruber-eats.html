Inspiration

We know Princeton (the town) well, but many Princeton students don't. This app connects Princeton students to our local knowledge of the area.

What it does

Text our chatbot to help you decide

How we built it

We used the Motion AI to turn our modified binary search tree into a conversation tree for a chat bot and then integrate it with SMS.

Challenges we ran into

Restaurants have many qualities and do not fit easily into binary search trees. At first, each node needed more than one child. Also SMS is hard.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Submitting something at our first Hackathon!

What we learned

A lot. How to integrate with SMS.

What's next for iSgruber Eats

Maybe iSgruber can help advise students on other things? Class selection, Housing draw, major/minor declaration.
