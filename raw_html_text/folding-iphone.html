Inspiration

A similar Android app developed by Sony. Besides, there are a lot of requests for an iOS client in the Folding@Home community, but the project leader Vijay Pande cited limited resources.

What it does

Start a background process and manage the intensity of calculations according to the device battery and charging status.

How I built it

With Ionic and Electron and JavaScript.

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Installing and running an ARM-based Android app on a Mac.

What I learned

Closed-source crowdsourcing projects make it difficult to develop clients.

What's next for Folding@iPhone

The key part is producing a precompiled C++/Assembly core code optimized for iOS/ARM64.
