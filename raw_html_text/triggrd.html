The opportunity

In the modern world people can easily get frustrated by the variety of options to spend their money on and they can lose track of the expenses. Therefore, we want to encourage them to start saving up the funds by letting them know which of their expenses are non-recurrent and can be invested into savings account.

Our solution

Our solution allows customers to calculate their personalized ultimate savings plan and set long- or short-term goals. We also not intrusively trigger their desire to start a savings account, guide them to the best solutions and suggest possible goals for their savings. We have developed a unique and smart algorithm, which labels, categorizes and sorts customers’ expenses into recurring and not-recurring, allowing easy tracking of their money.

How we built it

We propose one way of using our inovative system by incorporating it into a flexible, responsive and easy-to-use mobile application. We used real transaction data-set to demonstrate how our algorithm runs in the real life environment. Link to the dataset
