Inspiration

Far too often, we find ourselves in situations where we are sitting directly across from the love of our lives...and they don’t even know it. This app lets them know

What it does

Tired of online dating? Try Crush for a new offline dating experience. When you want to start a conversation with someone you’ve crossed paths with in real life, you can take a “snapshot” of people around you who are open for connection, and say hi to them later. After you say hi, any they say hi to you, BOOM.
