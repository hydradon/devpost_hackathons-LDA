Inspiration

Waste disposal and recycling are some of the most important issues of our generation. However, many people avoid separating the recyclable products. And who doesn't love free food, products and credits? So, we decided to combine the two in this super cool app.

What it does

This app allows you to live-capture a video of you throwing your recyclable containers into recycling bins and rewards you for it with points. These points can be redeemed for various offers like discounts, gift cards, sponsor raffles or even cash!

How is it built

It is built using Android Studio for the front end and Firebase as the storage database.

What's next for RecyclingRewards

Partnering with the Government for more recycle bins and scheduled pickups for big events and with companies to more and better incentives.
