Deep-Sea-Escape

Play as a lost fish avoiding other creatures of the deep. Swim as long as possible out of the way of larger fish to achieve the highest score. Original artwork, animations, game concept, and code. Sound effects and music imported from scratch library and youtube. Constructed by a team of one using block-based learning programming language.
