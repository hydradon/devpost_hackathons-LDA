STEWARD - Your fast command assistant by DKC Team, Finastra

Only the market moves and the strategies change… Traders and sales remain in the same seats doing the same things day after day.

Steward is aiming at facilitating and augmenting every repetitive tasks and interactions, man to man or man to machine, you can find in a trading environment. As such he answers perfectly the markets evolution for better and simpler information mixed with more speed, standardization and integration.

Literally, he makes the use of any solution frictionless, solving the multi-booking and the swapping between numerous platforms issues. He is also a key actor for information spreading, enabling both internal and external distribution of trade or investment ideas such as the one coming from the intelligent prediction engine embedded in Finastra’s TCM Sales workstation. Ultimately, he is a crucial compliance and best practice tool thanks to its interaction with risks and limits system or advanced solutions such as Finastra’s Detect.

Steward uses Voice Recognition, Machine Learning and is based on the powerful Finastra’s FusionFabric.Cloud APIs. You enable him naturally like you would write or talk to your broker, you can train him to enlarge his vocabulary base and you can script complex tasks.

Steward will become everybody’s natural assistant in your bank, making time for important tasks, reducing operational risks for non-STP flows and integrating more deeply your processes to answer your franchise requests.
