Inspiration

Ask any entrepreneur and they’ll tell you the same thing: it’s not the work that puts them off, it’s the admin side of things. Take a butcher, for example, or a lawyer, an oyster farmer, a beautician, an architect or even a urologist.

Listen to them talk about their profession. It’s stimulating. It’s stimulating because these people are passionate about what they do, but there inevitably comes a time when that enthusiasm starts to slip, and that’s when they start talking about their accounts. When we talk about accounts, we mean money, bank accounts, etc. It’s the crucible for any business, but too often it’s the professionals themselves who end up feeling the heat.

All entrepreneurs, regardless of the sector they work in or the scale of their company, have bank accounts. But that’s where the similarities end.

What it does

The manager.one banking platform is a one-stop-shop solution that’s as at home on your computer as it is on your mobile.

Its features have been designed by entrepreneurs, meaning it is capable of meeting their requirements:

  • Open an account online in just 5 minutes ;
  • Make regular, conditional and international transfers ;
  • Track your transfers in real time ;
  • Group your supporting documents together, simplifying your accounts ;
  • A bank card that you can manage using your mobile phone ;
  • The option of creating virtual bank cards ;
  • Customise access rights, granting third parties access to your bank card ;
  • Facial recognition, ensuring secure operations.

Conceived in such a way as to make life easier for entrepreneurs, our solution is flexible, meaning it can be tailored to meet individual requirements.
