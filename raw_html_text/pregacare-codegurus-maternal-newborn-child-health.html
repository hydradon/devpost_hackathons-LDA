Inspiration

To contribute to the development of Indian Healthcare

What it does

  • Tracks the ANC timeline based on WHO guidelines.
  • Continuously reminds the patient for ANC visits along with possible care centers.
  • Uses NLP(text-to-speech) to provide audio as an alternative medium for communication.

How we built it

Using android smartphone platform

Challenges we ran into

Synching with the server and access to MCTS database

Accomplishments that we're proud of

  • Implementing dynamic background notifications
  • Implementing text-to-speech technology
  • Incorporating personalized ANC timeline with reminders
  • Incorporating Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Yojana (PMSMY) into our app to spread awareness about this great scheme implemented under our Hon. Prime Minister Narendra Modi.

What we learned

  • Finishing a project in a given timeline
  • Programming under pressure
  • Team work
  • To never quit or give up

What's next for PregaCare(Codegurus, Maternal & Newborn Child Health)

  • Once we are given access to MCTS database we can sync it with our app and also use Data Analysis techniques such as clustering to identify regions where ANC is not widespread and needs to be promoted
  • We intend to access the PMSMY database to get locations of all hospitals and sync it with Google Maps to provide location tracking and directions for ANC visits
