Inspiration

Elderly Americans need more and more healthcare every year and sudden emergencies such as falls, take many lives early

What it does

In the case of an Alzheimers patient, the Apple watch component monitors for moment in the middle of the night and alerts family members in the event of a medical emergency such as wondering, falls, and sudden drops in blood-oxygen saturation, pulse, or blood pressure

How I built it

This app is build using watch OS 2, parse, and iOS 9

Challenges I ran into

Difficulties with Parse in Swift 2 and El Capitan issues with my macbook

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The push notifications from patient to caregiver

What I learned

A lot about health kit
