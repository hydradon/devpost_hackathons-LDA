Inspiration

Saw a picture on Instagram and thought about turning all of the users old pictures into a collage of them.

What it does

Takes a picture of your choice and will then take a hashtag of your choice and turn it into a collage based on RGB percentages

How we built it

With 2 people, teamwork and dedication, coffee helps. More seriously, we planned everything and tried to work on the project with, not against each other

Challenges we ran into

Two of our team member left without doing anything.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The website UI, elegant design, octoblu integration and use

What we learned

Html, integration of javascript, website formatting, quick decision making and recovery. Octoblu use and problem solving.

What's next for Collage Creator

Finish algorithm, sell to instagram as a plugin for the user experience.
