Inspiration

Mix robotics with mobile app dev

What it does

connects mobile app to robot wirelessly to draw a picture

How we built it

intel edison as microcontroller, receiving coordinates sent from smartphone(android)

Challenges we ran into

Many hardware issues just getting things to work with the internet/bluetooth. Had to switch out many times.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

configuring geometric output to control motors

What we learned

Arduino libraries, sending things over the internet

What's next for doodlebot

try to make it work over the internet and make robot controls much better.
