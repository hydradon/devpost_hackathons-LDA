Focused

Productivity and growth focused on chasing after a weekly focus

Our application facilitates a person's desire to improve their skills and life.

Goals need to be specific and simple for them to be achieved, so we refer to them as a focus . The app asks the user for a focus of the week, an overview of that focus, why they want to focus on this goal, and how they will do so.

This app has two parts: an Android app and a Web app. The Android app was built with Java using Android Studio and the Web App was created on top of the MEAN stack.

The domain we registered was "ikentfocus" on both Domain.com and the .tech.

On the web app, if you have not entered a focus for the week you will be prompted to create one. You can also view a history of the past weeks. It is very simple and bold to ensure
