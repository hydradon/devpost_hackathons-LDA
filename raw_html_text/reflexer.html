Inspiration

I was thinking about ways to entertain users when they were bored, so I developed this game to pass time!

What it does

Reflexer tests the user's reflexes as they attempt to click the button as many times as they can within a minute!

How I built it

Reflexer was developed with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Challenges I ran into

Developing the timer was challenging, as I didn't know how to do it at first.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am proud of creating a game that can help people pass time.

What I learned

I learned how to time events in JavaScript.

What's next for Reflexer

I hope to make the game more pretty in the future.
