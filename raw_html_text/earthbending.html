Inspiration

Last HackRU, we made a project called Firebending, and so this time we decided to figure out how to work with a dance pad and make Earthbending.

What it does

Creates an AR experience of Earthbending.

How we built it

We used Java2D, OpenCV for Java, and an old gamecube compatible dance pad.

Challenges we ran into

Getting the dance pad to send input to the computer.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Visualizing the user's motions to earthbend.

What we learned

Git.

What's next for Earthbending

More cool earthbending moves!
