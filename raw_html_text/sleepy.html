Sleepy

The app was programed using Swift in iOS platform. This app is able to measure user's sleep quality by obtaining the subtle change conducted by bed. The redundant data and useless data was filtered. All the useful data has been analyzed for the sleep pattern to determine whether our user is in deep sleep mode, light sleep mode or even a wake-up.

It is simple to use, and easy to understand. User just need to turn on the app, put it on anywhere in the bed, and that's it ! User can access their sleep data by simply turn on the app again when they wake up. A well analyzed chart will be shown to user so they can improve their sleep quality resoundingly. User do not have to keep the cellphone wake up all the time. The app could be either in background or even in the locked status.
