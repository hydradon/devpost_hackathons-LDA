PurchaseMate is a barcode scanning app which brings transparency to your shopping cart. By using your smartphone to scan the barcode of a product in a store, this app will give you easy access to the details of the corporation that produces it, including their donations to political and charitable organisations and their UN Human Rights rating. Our aim is to make socially conscious shopping easy and accessible.
