Inspiration

Saw that Pulse2 has a display, and PDM had a depth camera, and the rest is history

What it does

Displays a colorized depthmap on the Pulse2 speaker. Uses gestures to speak, which is recognized by Alexa to play music. The gestures can be used to control volume.

How I built it

Android Studio

Challenges I ran into

Pulse2 was lower resolution display that I expected, and Alexa can be challenging in high noise.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It's a cool hack :)

What I learned

Get the SDKs downloaded and working before staring the hackathon :(
