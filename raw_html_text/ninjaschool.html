NinjaSchool

Alexa skills app that allows kids to pretend they are training in a ninja school. The app will provide them with tasks, exercises, and activites to do as they gain ranks and glory!

Start the app by saying Alexa, start Ninja School . Follow the prompts from there.

The app will remember your progress and allow you to pick up again later.

Climb the ranks in Ninja School!
