Inspiration

Our friend has a mental illness

What it does

Our idea targets mental illness patients so they have a very straightforward path to getting help. The app records data over the course of the week and tracks your mental health.

How we built it

We built using Swift and Amazon Web Services

Challenges we ran into

We ran into challenges such as retaining information from APIs to point out therapist locations

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of how efficiently our MVC works

What we learned

We learned statistics about mental illness

What's next for iMIND

We plan on working on this project outside and hopefully expanding its range to other chronic diseases
