• Genesis

We believe that the most complicated dilemmas in this world are of a multi-dimensional nature. This being the case, we cannot hope to solve these problems with a one-dimensional solution. All knowledge was originally of a single entity, this being the case, true revolutionary breakthroughs can only occur through cross-disciplinary actions. Enter Genesis, a computational genomic sequencing platform that enables researchers to quickly and efficiently juxtapose different strands of RNA, and DNA to trace back lineages as well as predict future evolutionary mutations.
