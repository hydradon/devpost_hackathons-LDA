Inspiration

I keep getting sexiled, so I wanted to create an app that helps sexiled people find beds to sleep in.

How it works

Carleton Sexodus asks sexiliers to indicate the unavailability of the room they are using, and also input the info for the "empty bed" in the other person's room. Then, that bed will be marked as "available" for sexiled users to sleep in. (Unless the other person(s) in that room indicate otherwise).
