parkingBuddy

MTC hackathon

Parking Buddy Problem: We don’t want to go to a parking garage that is full and we have several to choose from Goal: Tells the user the status of parking garages that are nearby. - Are they full, or are there spaces still available?

There are markers on a map to indicate parking availability in parking structures Using Automatic Realtime ignition off event Color coded markers according to parking availability
