MosaicNinja

Ever wanted to make something cool? Ever wanted to have a large mosaic of your face? And ever wanted that mosaic to be uniquely you ? Mosaic Ninja is here! It generates a mosaic of your Facebook profile picture based on your Facebook likes. After all, you are what you like .

And thanks to Expedia, you are where you want to travel, too! They've been generous to offer us an API to hack on, and they have a ton of pictures of tourist activities in cities across the world. Become the ultimate tourist and have your Facebook profile picture be composed of scenes from around the world.
