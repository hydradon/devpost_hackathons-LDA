What it does

Raid Train is a panel extension that allows a hosting channel's viewers to nominate and vote for another streamer that viewers will raid. The app seamlessly allows viewers to nominate, vote, and view rankings. This is a great opportunity for the community to recognize and promote their favorite small streamers and discover hot new streamers in their community.

During a stream, viewers will nominate a streamer to cast their vote for to raid. Viewers can also vote for a streamer already on the leaderboard. The leaderboard will be updated in real time as the ranking changes during the host's stream! When the stream ends, the nominee in the top rank is ready to RAID! Viewers click the Raid button to raid the winner's stream. Find out who will get raided today and help your favorite small channels get recognized!

What's next for Raid Train

We're planning to add Bits integration to allow viewers to spend Bits to vote multiple times for their favorite streamer, allowing the host streamer to monetize their raids!
