We present a simple game for two people with a good memory and some reflex. The objective of the game is to look at the colors’ sequence provided by the RGB LED and try to repeat it correctly by pressing corresponding colorful buttons. In each round players have to repeat a sequence of n colors, starting with 3 and increasing after n rounds. Points are awarded to each player who repeats t correctly color in the sequence and the current result is being displayed on the screen. The game either stops after 25 rounds or after a player gets a 100 points. Player with more points win!

The project involves:

  • 1x breadboard
  • 1x Arduino Uno,
  • 1x RGB LED
  • 6x buttons
  • 3x resistors
  • 1x small OLED display
