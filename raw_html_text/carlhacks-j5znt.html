UniTab

UniTab was created to eliminate the clutter in your browser. It's as simple as it is straightforward: just click one button to consolidate all your tabs into one. We noticed that it was a common annoyance to have many tabs open simultaneously and created UniTab to organize these pages for future use.

Enjoy the internet!
