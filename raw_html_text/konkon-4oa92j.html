Inspiration

• Love the convenience of internet shopping • Hate missing first deliveries (receiving window too wide, worked late etc.)

  • statistical trends in delivered goods and manpower support the need for a solution

What it does

The app enables the delivery company to confirm the availability of the customer at the destination or to reschedule another time. -delivery company minimizes time & money wasted on failed attempted deliveries -convenient for customer because of instant rescheduling -positive environmental impact

How I built it

Create a clear goal for building teams. Build trust and openness with own team-member taking reasonable risks Creativity, innovation, and different viewpoints are encouraged constantly examine with ourself Schedule and attend team-building activities. Design your own

Challenges I ran into

Planning and communicating ideas with each other (language, conflicts, etc) Design - we don't have an experienced designer

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

-Arrived at a common consensus as a team and each team member were able to develop a new skill -We feel happy to have developed something which is beneficial to society

What I learned

-collaborating is not easy -we learned to appreciate each other's opinion, brainstorm, and improve them

What's next for Konkon

Our next step is to develop the app with an in-depth understanding of the company’s own delivery processes. We aim to maintain simplicity and adaptability in the fast-evolving delivery services market.
