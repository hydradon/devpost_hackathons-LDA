Inspiration

--- I have kids who find learning spelling words every week to be daunting so we devised a solution that will make them excited to learn!

What it does

--- Takes a child's spelling words and turns it into a game

Challenges we ran into

--- Leap motion sensor and/or the compatibility with our laptops makes it laggy. --- Limitations of 3js.

What's next for HackISU

--- Grab-able, drop-able blocks that line the word up to show the child visually how they spelled the word. --- Different games to keep learning fun and interesting --- Award system; trophies for spelling all words correctly
