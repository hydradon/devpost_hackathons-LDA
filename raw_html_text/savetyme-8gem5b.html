SaveTyme

SaveTyme is an app that is dedicated towards organizing what needs to be accomplished as a to-do list in the order of top priority to least priority. For each activity completed, a certain number of points are rewarded to the user based on the rank of the priority of the activity. This app will help students manage their time effectively through the use of making a plan and executing it, ultimately reducing their stress and allowing them to practice a healthier lifestyle.

The high school environment is constantly circulated with exams, caffeine, no-sleep, procrastination, and ultimately a boatload of stress. But one of the main causes of this stress is the lack of time management and prioritization - leading to unnecessary procrastination which can ultimately lead a student to their downfall rather than their success.

Our vision is to develop an app/program to allow students to create a to-do list, prioritizing their activities, to most efficiently use their time. While many may exist, this program specifically makes sure to include a form of gratification by rewarding the user every single time they have completed a task.

For completing their first priority, they receive 50 points; for their second, they receive 40 points, and so forth. In the future, with enough sponsors for our app, we can then allow the users to create accounts and accumulate points in order to win prizes that we are sponsored with. This will motivate students to keep crossing out their activities on their list; because not only are they getting their tasks done, but they know that they are being rewarded and appreciated for doing so.

Our plans for the future include introducing this on other platforms and interfaces that are more accessible by users, such as web apps and iOS/Android.
