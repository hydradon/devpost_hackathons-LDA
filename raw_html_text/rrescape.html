Inspiration

The wonderful place of Rush Rhee Library has long been the most mysterious place in U of R. The dark lights and ancient books, along with centuries-old attics are best places for storytellers. We now build a room escape game based on the environment and stories of Rush Rhee.

What it does

You are a student who studies in 500m of Rush Rhee Library during a stormy winter night -- you need to study for tomorrow's exams! Suddenly, a feeling of darkness came up to you: the power supply has been cut off. Then you hear screams and booms around the Library. After seconds the noises end and left a huge bunch of mess. You are trapped on the highest floor in Rush Rhee! Fortunately, you are the last magician of the world who understands ancient magic. You realized that the magic of eternal evil has been returned, which is hidden somewhere inside the Library! You need to explore further into the library and find out where the evil is. By using tools and interact with surroundings, you will explore the wonderful world created by us and understand the evil magic further!

We used the Unreal Engine to develop this game.
