Inspiration

Brian Cox, he gave us an interest in cosmology and this led us to looking into ways we can incorporate hardware into our project while only producing software.

What it does

It maps the positions of satellites in the current epoch around a 3D earth

How I built it

Node.js to parse the TLE data and send that to the client, p5 WEBGL mode to draw.

Challenges I ran into

Object picking in 3D is really difficult.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Creating something at all

What I learned

That 3D graphics are a lot more complex than one might imagine.
