Inspiration

We both love to bullet journal and wanted to share ways to create one without the hassle.

What it does

Fast Bujo allows users to document their schedules on an online form to keep track of their daily activities to habits they'd like to accomplish. This allows people who are new to planners and bullet journals to take a step into the bullet journaling community.

How we built it

We built it using domain.com and Sublime through html text files.

Challenges we ran into

We had little knowledge about html text files and creating a website from scratch. We had to go back to the drawing board a few times because our goals were too out of our reach.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud that we were able to connect the different pages together. We were able to get the skeleton of our website done.

What we learned

We learned that it takes a lot of patience to create a website that is functional and aesthetically pleasing.

What's next for Fast Bujo

We hope to be able to have a functional site for users.
