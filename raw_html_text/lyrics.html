Inspiration

Sometimes we listen to songs without paying attention to occurence of certain words in the song, and sometimes the word that appears the most often really describes the song.

What it does

We would like to present the occurence of repetitive words in a graph and have fun with some guessing game.

How we built it

We used JS library (React), firebase, firestore and make a website

Challenges we ran into

Most challenging thing is dealing with how the website will look and data conversions so that the chart can read the data and firestore can save the data.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Being able to make and retrieve data from the database.

What we learned

Everything.

What's next for Lyrics

Better design.
