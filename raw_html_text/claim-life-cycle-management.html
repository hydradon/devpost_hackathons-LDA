Inspiration

Our goal is to reduce claims processing time by 50%, increase Provider profitability, and positively transform the claims experience for insurance customers worldwide by leveraging the advanced AI/ML Modeling, OCR tools and techniques.

What it does

• Submit Claims on Appian Mobile Application
• Provide details and Upload Damage Images
• Integration with Amazon S3 for uploading images
• Instant Damage Estimator (Integration with Claim Genius)
• VIN Check (Extract VIN from Image and Validate)
• Body shop estimate document Processing (Document Data Extraction and OCR)
• 50% Straight Through Processing
• Adjuster and Vehicle inspector review and dashboards
• Dashboard to review the status of claims and notifications
• Accidental support Location finder for Rental/Towing company using Google Maps on Mobile

How we built it

• Built Appian Mobile application and web application for Claimant and Back office processing by Adjuster/Vehicle site inspector review.
• Leveraged the advanced techniques such as AI/ML model for damage estimation by evaluating the images, document repository on Amazon S3 for damage images and Amazon Rekognition Kit for OCR Processing of Body shop estimation data extraction.
• Real time integration with Claim Genius and Amazon S3 document repository

Challenges we ran into

• Real time test data. Since the model runs on the damage photos it was difficult for us to test with real time images taken at the accident site.
