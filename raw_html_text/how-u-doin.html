So our team wanted to create a platform that would allow users to navigate through interesting internet content depending on their mood in a really easy and interactive way. Users can visit and go through a series of simple questions and we get the user thinking about "how they're doin'". They have to be honest with themselves to get the end result they want! So if a user is happy, we offer an array of content that they can access straight from our website depending on what they like to see: memes, animal videos, etc! If the user is feeling stressed, they can access other forms of content: websites that help them cope with their stress. We are by no means attempting to medicate or heal any mental health concerns, but rather allow an easy-to-use and user-friendly platform that has content that is fun, relatable and accessible to everyone!
