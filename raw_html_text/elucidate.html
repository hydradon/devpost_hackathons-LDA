The inspiration comes from knowing that there are many caregivers out that wish to have better tools at their hand to be able to properly take care of love ones with the little time they have. Trying to remember and keep track of medications, appointments and all the doctors you or your loved doesn't have to be so hard with right application at hand. We want to make it easier for physicians, patients and caregivers to connect.

It gives the user a list of doctors near the address they input. They also have access to a list of insurances and conditions.

Importing the data from the Better Doctor API and displaying it properly. Converting an address to latitude and longitude coordinates and then trying to display the locations as google markers. Trying to use the 8base platform.

Displaying the list of doctors near the address input.

Learn to use google maps, 8base platform, and the Better Doctor API.

Trying to integrate medications and appointment reminders.
