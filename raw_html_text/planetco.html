Inspiration: I wish that I had learned more about finance and budgeting as a kid.

What it does: Teaches kids to budget by giving them a set amount of money to use and adding in things like "bills" and "emergencies."

How I built it: We built this using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Challenges I ran into: Getting it to work! The biggest challenge is figuring out how to push user selections to the final array.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of We are mostly beginners, but we got a lot done!

What I learned: I used Flexbox for the first time, and we all learned more about JavaScript!

What's next for PlanetCo: A second and third levels!
