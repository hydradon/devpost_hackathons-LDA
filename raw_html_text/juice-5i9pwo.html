Inspiration

To support growth of EV adoption for low-medium income households who commute long distance for work.

What it does

Allows the CCA to provide cheaper and greener electricity for EV charging

How I built it

Django on the back end and React front end

Challenges I ran into

workplace charging stations are harder to deal with given the limited time window for charging.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We identified over $60M of annual benefits available through lower electricity cost and LCFS credits that can be reinvested in the community.

What I learned

It's important for CCAs to promote growth of EVSE equipment to enable smarter charging to capture LCFS credits and charge cars with lower carbon content power.

What's next for Juice

Optimizing the workplace charging strategy
