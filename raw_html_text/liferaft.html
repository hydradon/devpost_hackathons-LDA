LifeRaft

HackDuke Fall 2015

LifeRaft is the ultimate tool for ensuring that you and your friends always feel safe at night. By seamlessly integrating location services for keeping up with your friends, status updates to check in on them, and rides by Uber so that you can always get home safe, LifeRaft will make sure that you and your friends will never float adrift.
