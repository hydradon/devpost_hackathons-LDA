Speech-to-LaTeX

What is it?

This python app records your voice and converts it to LaTeX !

How does it work ?

After recording your voice, Speech-to-LaTeX uses Wit.ai to convert it to text, the Wolfram Alpha API to interpret the text as numbers and finally generates the LaTeX.
