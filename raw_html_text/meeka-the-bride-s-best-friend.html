How I built it

In a year of operation, we already help more than 100,000 couples. And we learned a lot from it. The Meeka connect on Mecasei.com database and our support tool to extract and classify knowledge. The main feature, which is the chat, is built on top of Watson Dialog. All language classification is based upon Watson NLC. We are also experiencing things like Personality Insights to adapt the language to each customer profile. Every human-robot iteration happens in a rich app built on top of ionic and releases (still in beta) in Google Play and Apple Store.
