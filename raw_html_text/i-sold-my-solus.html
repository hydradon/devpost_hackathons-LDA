What it does

It creates a .ics file holding all course information, all from a user's copy and paste data from Solus/Quest/similar frameworks. A parser converts the information, and accepts further prompts to generate a calendar file. This can be used with iCal, Google Calendar, or other calendar apps that accept the format.
