Inspiration

make driving more fun and relaxing in rush hour

What it does

Corgi Co-Pilot is biometrics for your car, where by driving your car and using the steering wheel, your companion, Cara the Corgi, will help keep the driver aware and calm.

(Read your heartbeat and provide messages to lower heart beat)

How I built it

hardware sensor from metawear and ios and ford sync and app link

Accomplishments that I'm proud of
