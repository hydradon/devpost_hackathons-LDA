Inspiration

We were inspired by the recent movement to increase young voter turnout, especially with celebrities such as Yara Shahidi creating platforms to encourage teens to want to get involved in politics.

What it does

This is a website that compiles all of the essential information needed for voting/voter registration. It contains links for online voter registration, polling locations, registration deadlines, registration requirements, information on possible candidates, and much more.

How we built it

Sublime, GitHub, html, & css.

Challenges we ran into

Not being able to deploy our website.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Being able to code!

What we learned

How to code in html, css, and how to persevere through difficulties.

What's next for Your Voice, Your Choice

Expanding the prototype into a website and actually getting it out to our peers.
