Inspiration: The multitude of apps that try to teach people about recycling and fail.

What it does: The RFT app helps the user learn what can be recycled and what can't.

How we built it: Built on JFrame, Java, and lots of time.

Challenges we ran into: JFrame is notoriously uncooperative.

Accomplishments that we're proud of: JFrame started to cooperate.

What we learned: JFrame is not very nice.

What's next for RFT: Rewriting the app to be more expansive and not use JFrame.
