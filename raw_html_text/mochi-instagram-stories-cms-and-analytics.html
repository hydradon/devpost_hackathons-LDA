Mochi

What we did.

We reversed engineered Instagram's Stories API to create a modern CMS and Analytics tool for media companies.

Mochi allows you to schedule and upload an Instagram Story. You can then gather valuable insights for your Instagram Stories such as retention, views, and audience data.

After the event.

Mochi is lightweight enough to get started with one person at a company and will be robust enough to handle entire social media teams with owners, campaign assigning, and importing Snapchat stories to Instagram to keep your accounts in sync.
