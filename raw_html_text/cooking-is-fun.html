Inspiration

We were inspired by video-games for many aspects of our project, such as the theme of Cooking Mama, the controls of Octodad, and the overall vibe of Wario Ware.

How it works

We used the oculus rift, Leap Motion sensor, and unity to make the game. In-game, you attempt to make a soup in a kitchen.

Challenges I ran into

The hardware was not working originally, so we spent most of our time troubleshooting it.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We finished a game!

What I learned

We learned how to use unity and blender.

What's next for Cooking Is Fun!

We want to add more activities for the player to do.
