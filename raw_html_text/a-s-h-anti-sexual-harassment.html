Inspiration

Even in large organizations, such as Fox News and Uber Technologies, sexual harassment happens, in the forms of daily conversations and emails.

We would love to help these organizations to prevent this from happening.

What it does

For companies' employees to determine their received emails’ offensiveness, and report inappropriate contents, especially when it involves sexual harassment, with the help of sentiment analysis. Then the employee can decide whether or not to file a case to HR's server. HR will respond with a Twilio text message and start investigating.

How we built it

Front-end with Python Flask, back-end with Node.js, Twilio, Google API, AWS Elastic Beanstalk, AWS EC2

Challenges we ran into

Tried deploying through Azure Web App and Heroku.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

A user-friendly user interface.

What we learned

What's next for A.S.H. (Against Sexual Harassment)

This product can be widely used at companies, non-profits, military units and government agencies.
