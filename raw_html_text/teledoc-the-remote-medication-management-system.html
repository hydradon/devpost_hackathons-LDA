Inspiration

Our remote medication scheduler was inspired by observing the difficulty (and likely frustration) that medical patients experience when having to organize their schedules around taking many types of medication that have complex interactions and require specific timings of both administration of the pharmaceuticals and patient meals. These schedules may be easier to cope with while the patient is in medical car at a hospital, but this challenge is of greater concern when the patient is on their own.
