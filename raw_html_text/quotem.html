Inspiration

Our team wanted a social network where we could just look at funny quotes from other people.

What it does

People can use the app to tag quotes to people, and follow individuals to view what they are being quoted as being saying.

How we built it

We used Swift for the iOS app, Flask for the server, and Mongo for the database.

Challenges we ran into

Determining how to display the newsfeed of quotes for a user was technically challenged.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Creating a UI that looked nice.

What we learned

We learned how to incorporate persistence so our quote repository is highly resilient.

What's next for QuotEm

The Apple App store.
