• Game in play

Inspiration

Had an unexpected question about my hometown yesterday and met somebody from my home county. Made me think about Irish towns and how few people outside of Ireland (and some inside of Ireland ) would know the Irish names for these towns.

What it does

A matching game showing the English names of Irish towns and their matching Gaelic versions. Matches are randomised and players can say it's a match by pressing the green button, or if not by pressing the red button. A right answer flashes green a wrong answer flashes red.

How I built it

Green and red buttons to take yes and no answers. LCD screen to display text, and LEDs for feedback

Challenges I ran into

I had no idea what I was doing.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It works.

What I learned

How to create games using an Arduino.

What's next for Baile Match

Even more buttons.
