Bet It On Credit

betitoncredit.com

A quick, replayable flash game to teach you ways to improve your credit score.

You start out as a student fresh out of college, with a credit card and a student loans. Your objective is to raise your credit score from 600 to as high as you can maintain without going bankrupt. Every penny counts! Make sure to miss as few payments as possible!

The game takes place in 6 months blocks. In each of these six month blocks there is a scenario that you have to go through as well as decisions to pay off your current credit accounts. After each block of time, the result of you choices will be displayed and information relevant to the scenario and obtaining a better credit score will be displayed.
