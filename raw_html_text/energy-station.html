Inspiration We are inspired by the Virtual Assistant system which sends notifications to people as a reminder to do something positive.

What it does

It sets up daily routines, at most 5 positive things, for users to accomplish. The users can check the card when they finish the task. The more tasks they do, the more energy they will get. Their energy is accumulated can 10000 energy points can be converted to $10. The fund will be automatically donated to Canadian Mental Health organizations.
