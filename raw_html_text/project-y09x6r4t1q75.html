Inspiration

People who live in cities and villages with a bad or inefficient Sewage network infrastructure suffer from flooding around building.

What it does

We have two ways to solve this issue:

rebuilding the Sewage network infrastructure which costs billions of dollars our Flood Watch IoT-system which has the following features: a. cost-effective b. No need to change anything on existing infrastructure c. real-time accurate monitoring and help decision-makers to act fast and correct e. smartness and auto-detection of danger levels f. automatic control of water discharge pumps

How I built it

The combination between hardware which containe (Raspberry pi, Arduino, flood watch sensors) and software which contain (python, java, Firebase,android,swift,CSS ,java script) .

Challenges I ran into

The integration between hardware with software.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned

Team working, patience, Time keeping
