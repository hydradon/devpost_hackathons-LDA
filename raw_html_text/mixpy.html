Inspiration

My and my teammates are all python developers who feel like there could be better tools to share ideas and code with each other to facilitate rapid development.

What it does

Enables users to embed python-notebook-esque objects into their emails to send a friend an interactive coding experience.

How we built it

Using the powers of :

  • Sleep deprivation

  • Python

  • The ability to face bugs with courage

Challenges we ran into

We used javascript instead of a well-typed, rational language. Also, CSS.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Seeing past the tears clouding our vision and making a product that may make someone's life easier.

What we learned

Cookies taste just as good @ 2AM as 2PM

What's next for MixPy

I hope to continue development and see such integrations in more places as the number of hackers in the world increases.
