Mark your account number as you will need it in the next step. Invoke newly installed Alexa Skill with “Alexa, ask property advisory to setup a new device”. Follow instructions. Once your device got REGISTERED with the Talk To The House system, please login into https://www.talktothehouse.net and ACTIVATE your device. Once activated, you can start creating properties that you can attach to your device to play it back.
