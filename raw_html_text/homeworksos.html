Inspiration

We know university students need help on their homework sometimes, and office hour/your friends might not always be avaiable and thats what our app is for.

What it does

You can post a question on our website. And other people that have the answer can post an answer to your question.

How we built it

We built it using a question/answer format where we link the answers to the questions' key.

Challenges we ran into

A challenge we ran into was how to link the answers to the questions.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of having a functioning product despite having a rough journey.

What we learned

We learned that all objects in the data base has keys that you can use to find/recreate it.

What's next for homeworksos

Maybe adding a helpful/not helpful button to the answers so it is easier to find the answer people are looking for.
