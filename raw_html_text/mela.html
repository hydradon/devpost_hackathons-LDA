What It Does

Have you ever wanted to see all the pop-up events happening near you on a map? MELA is a mobile map that provides you the most accurate and reliable real-time information on where your favorite food truck will be tomorrow after work or which local band will play live at that coffee shop across the street. By making local pop-up events more known through our app, we hope to foster localized experiences where people never run out of fun things to do in town!
