Inspiration

Subways are where I do most of my thinking and where I plan my life. On one of my subway rides, while I was looking over the big group of people who were playing games on their phones, I came up with Choo Choo Choo. A game where it has a train that never goes out of gas and people who always needs to go somewhere.

How I built it

I wrote the main code for the game in 2 days and it took me weeks to design the game.

Challenges I ran into

Since this was the first game I made, I was new to a lot of things and I had to learn a lot in order to make this game.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of
