Inspiration

The annoyance of trying to figure out how fast you’re pacing while training.

What it does

projects a visual display of the pace you need to run. Worn on the chest.

How I built it

Laser cut plywood, attached elastic strap, wired up an LED.

Challenges I ran into

Time constraints.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Worked with a great team to conceptualize a potentially great product with a lot of market potential

What I learned

That a small group with diverse background can come together in 48 hours and build amazing products.
