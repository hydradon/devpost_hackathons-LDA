Inspiration

When we wanted to launch DevC Amman we thought what is the best way to share our event's details, using a new approach for members to be updated with an easy access with something technology related.

What it does

It helps every member to stay connected and up to date with their local circle's current and upcoming events

How I built it

Using React Native and GraphQL helped us having a smooth development process.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Everyone was very excited when they downloaded the application, it's intuitive, user-friendly, easy to manage and on top of that it's an open-source application form the community to the community.

What I learned

I learned that building a collaborative ecosystem is not that easy, yet once the kick-off is done it starts to have this positive domino-effect sense and such results are promising on a local and global scale.

What's next for Facebook Developer Circle - Events Application

We are planning to add multiple types of integrations i.e FB events, circle's lead profile, a survey for each event and we are planning to let the community decide.
