How many times were you driving around in a busy area on a Friday night and weren't able to park your car?

In New York, time is an asset.

We developed Parko with the intention to save time for our community. That means having more time to do what you need or want to. That means no waiting or driving around trying to find one spot. That also means not being late for events, work or taking an absurd amount of time .

Still a work in progress, our goal is to implement a platform where New Yorkers can help each other by exchanging parking spots with one another, including both street parking and driveways. This will save both time, gas and energy for New Yorkers.
