stepUP

Technology assists people in keeping fit. With devices like iPhones, wristbands and other wearables, people keep track of their workout progress and stay informed about their achievement.

Something’s missing. There’s no convenient way to brag about it.

StepUp is an app that let you brag about your workouts. It compares you against your friends in terms of step counts, creating leaderboards that update real-time. It is a great way to keep people motivated about keeping a healthy lifestyle.
