Inspiration

I took Arabic course back in uni and then taught the reading skill to some of my friends. I've realised that having a tool for independent practice is would be nice.

What it does

There are 8 lessons, each having from 3 to 4 characters. So you complete them by playing a game.

How I built it

On swift. My friend, who's a qualified teacher of Arabic provided me with all the sound recordings i needed for it.

Challenges I ran into

Gamifying an educational app to keep it both productive and engaging is the main challenge. Constantly trying to make it better.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I'm the winner of nFactorial Incubator 2016 Demo day (n17r.com)

What I learned

Swift programming, UX, gamification and many other things

What's next for Joode: learn Arabic Alphabet in two hours

So now i teach people the letters, next step is to teach them how to combine these letters to form words and sentences.
