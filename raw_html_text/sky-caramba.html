Inspiration

What's the most personalised way to get recommendations? By having a conversation! We combine the power of the Internet and machine learning, and the power of conversation.

What it does

You wear the Special Sombrero, equipped with a microphone. An iPhone app will have a conversation with you, as it works out what TV Shows you'll be likely to enjoy. As it's an iPhone app and handy dandy, you can keep it with you and keep updating its data to get improved suggestions

How we built it

Natural language processing, machine processing, elastic search, inverted indexes
