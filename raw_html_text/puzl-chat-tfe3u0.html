Inspiration

Honestly, I just wanted to build a social network for the iPhone and learn the challenges of building one from the ground up.

What it does

Puzlchat allows you to take a photo, edit it with some filters and add a question with answers. If who ever you share it with gets the question correct, they will get to see the picture until it is closed out. If they get the question wrong, they will never get to see the picture and it disappears forever.

How I built it

It was built by developing it in Xcode, objective-c and php and mysql for the backend and database.

Challenges I ran into

Too many to list, but one specific is making an if/else function in the backend in regards to if it's a question or a regular photo without a question attached.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Having a complete social network.

What I learned

I'm still learning a lot really..
