Inspiration

We wanted to promote healthy living by encouraging people to spend their days doing useful activities and rewarding their attempts at improving their health.

What it does

Sometimes, getting started on an activity is the hardest part, and you don't know what to do. Go app suggests activities for you based on your physical fitness and your current mood. Activities are suggested with a click of a button and it's up to you to decide whether or not you want to make the first move.

How I built it

Built using the MEAN stack written in Javascript source code.

Challenges I ran into

Decoding the framework to find the parts we needed to modify and make it our own.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Overtime, more popular activities are suggested as they are weighted by how many people choose to do the activity. We hope that over time, we can integrate a social aspect to this project and integrate better machine learning algorithms so that users can join up and meet each other.

What I learned

I learned about routing endpoints and services and better familiarized myself with JavaScript.

What's next for Go App

  • Social Media Integration
  • Perhaps Meet Up API usage to get certain details about events that may be suggested
  • Better machine learning algorithms to suggest better activities that cater to each user
  • Reward system
