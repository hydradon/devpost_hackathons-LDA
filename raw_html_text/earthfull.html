Inspiration

To help the helping hands

What it does

Connects the common people with NGO's to volunteer for events. Points are awarded for volunteers who turn up at the event.

How I built it

Technologies used are android and SQLite with which I was able to develop the app "Earthfull" on Android Studio.

Challenges I ran into

Developing on Android was fun and equally challenging as we were amateur to Android.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

In spite of being new to Android development we were able to put off the app successfully.

What I learned

Good learning and fun filled experience which helped us to identify our potential and gives us the confidence to develop more apps.

What's next for EarthFull

Allowing the volunteers to donate any items to the NGO's which would help the NGO's to give them to the homeless.
