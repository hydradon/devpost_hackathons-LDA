Inspiration

We liked Typeform's chatty style of presentation, giving you questions one by one. We immediately thought - why not have it as an actual chat?

What it does

Presents your typeforms over text to a group of users of your choice.

How we built it

It's a PHP web app using the Slim microframework, for quick'n'easy development and deployment in the 36 hours. We had a lot to learn during our time at the hackathon and so it was important for our workflow to be as frictionless as possible.

Challenges we ran into

Guy had never worked with OAuth before, and so it was initially challenging getting an access token from Typeform. Piers had never touched PHP, APIs, databases with PDO, or web dev before Friday evening - but has enjoyed getting his teeth into the challenge.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Making it work!

What's next for typeform text

Twilio supports a variety of programmable messaging channels, such as Whatsapp, LINE and GSM RCS. It'd be great to bring Typeform to those platforms too (which Twilio's APIs make super easy).
