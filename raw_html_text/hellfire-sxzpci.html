• HellFire

Inspiration

Inspired by old school shooters like Space Invaders and Galactica

What it does

  • No ads or in app purchases
  • Old school bullet hell gameplay
  • Simple and clean user interface
  • 15+ powerful upgrades
  • 7+ enemies with unique
  • 50+ waves of mayhem
  • Frequent updates adding new content and fixing bugs
  • Only needs 18mb of storage!

How I built it

Using Android Studio, Java, Google Play Store, and coding knowledge, this app was born.

Challenges I ran into

Converting the game from a different language, and balancing a game with multiple elements were the main challenges

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Having a completed product on the app store that has quite a bit of gameplay and fun packed into it for free.

What I learned

Solidifying my knowledge of Java and Android, while learning how to use Android Studio and the Google Play Developer tools.

What's next for HellFire

More content updates. For example: more upgrades, enemies, bosses, story mode, endless mode, etc etc.
