Inspiration

Nowadays developers are too AGILE to stop, read and classify their ICs. We provide a software solution that provides B2C supply chain management. By integrating the needs of everyday developers and globalizing businesses, we hope to provide an efficient and innovative way of changing the way development is done.

What it does

By using state of the art OCR technology and machine learning we enable developers easy access to valuable IC information.

How we built it

Integrating OCR APIs and by developing our very own REST API, the image is uploaded to our server by the end user. It is then parsed and analyzed with hundreds of permutations, giving the utmost accurate information.
