Inspiration: we're all athletes who face injury and want a way to live a healthier, safer lifestyle

What it does: provides athletes with stretching and strengthening exercises for injury prevention and rehabilitation.

How we built it: with Figma (app GUI) and Notepad++ (website)

Challenges we ran into: limited HTML experience and no Macs to create an actual app on XCode

Accomplishments that we're proud of: having done our project on multiple platforms

What we learned: how to deal with setbacks and still manage to do what we can with whatever we have

What's next for (B)A(K)E - Athlete Central: improving the app and actually coding it on XCode
