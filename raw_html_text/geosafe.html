Inspiration

Modern slavery in the 21st Century must be stopped.

What it does

Warns people when they enter dangerous hotspots (such as human trafficking) in real time with or without the internet.

How I built it

Using state of the art Geofencing technology for Android.

Challenges I ran into

Integration of Geofencing with the android app Finding a reliable dataset

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Helps create a safer world.

What I learned

Android Geofencing UX

What's next for GeoSafe

Expansion to crime zones and other unsafe areas. Real time data updates - emergency alerts such as terrorist attacks or natural disasters.
