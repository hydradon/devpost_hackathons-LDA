Inspiration

When looking at the challenges available, we chose Challenge 3, geared at reducing preventable mistakes in the pharmaceutical industry.

What it does

This is a block chain database that stores information and makes it readily available to all parties involved (doctors, pharmacies, patients). It is secure, fast, and provides the transparency necessary to help limit preventable accidents that can lead to increased hardship or death.

How we built it

We built the backend in Java, and the frontend through angular, connecting the two with js controllers.

Challenges we ran into

Not much information is available on block chains, so we largely had to build the database from scratch.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Developing our own blockchain structure and creating a clean UI

What we learned

How block chains work, and the increased need for software solutions in the medical industry
