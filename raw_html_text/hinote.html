Inspiration

We hate taking notes, but what's worse is when you know someone has spent hours reading through a page and now you must attempt to repeat their research. Our inspiration is the problem, we want to make life simpler.

What it does

Hi-Note allows you to easily take notes on webpages, load notes from top annotators on a page, and participate in the social network of knowledge.

How we built it

We used Firebase to host our data, a chrome extension to source the data with the help of awesome users, a Django application to present the great notes that are collected, and we deployed it all using google cloud platform.

Challenges we ran into

Everything, from highlighting html files to user authentication.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We made something that will be really useful for a lot of people, hopefully becoming a part of most student's lives and other related professionals.

What we learned

Sleep is important.
