Inspiration

I watch a movie or a commercial i would like to know what i am getting into before i start watching it. specially if i am watching it with family and kids. It would be nice to know that i shall skip watching this video between x andy range since it has bad content.

What it does

CrowdSource people emotions for a particular video. can be a commercial or a movie or a video clip.

How I built it

Frontend to sign waiver using docusign api. parse the video using aws Image reckognition and lambda functions.

Challenges I ran into

  • APIs weren't fully developed to implement our product sufficiently.
  • Performing emotion analysis on video stream comes with challenges such as deriving appropriate sample size.
  • Incompatibility based on weak documentation. ## Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned

  • Amalgamation of different APIs into a coherent web app.
  • Collaborating on a high speed development process ## What's next for HonestLook
  • Optimizing the implementing recognition algorithms on video streams.
