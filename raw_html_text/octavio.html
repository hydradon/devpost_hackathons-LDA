Inspiration

Github: exploring the idea of extending the Github's functionality as a platform to other audiences

What it does

Octavio is a web application interface that centralizes collaboration and focuses on music production. It features the ability to follow a music project through it's timeline and shows the history of all changes during the creative process.

How we built it

React, Mongo, Hapi, Nodejs, AWS, 7Digital
