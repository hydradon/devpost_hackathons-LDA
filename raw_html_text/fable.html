Inspiration

We were inspired by the personal stories that provide richness and depth to a location. Personal stories are the difference between seeing a place and experiencing the culture.

What it does

Allows users to upload personal audio stories and connect them to a certain location. Users should then be able to listen stories that are relevant to a particular place. The app can then act as an audio guide that clues users into the culture and mythology around a location.
