Whilst holidaying in the countryside I found it incredibly easy to get to sleep. Could it be the fresh unpolluted air, the all the holiday booze or possibly the long walks across the mountains. Perhaps but I can’t take all that back to modern life. So I’ll have to settle on the relaxing sound of sheep.

The skill provides a series of bleating sheep to count. The delay before the sheep bleats matches the speed at which you respond, so as your responses become less immediate the delay before the bleat increases. This should match your tempo and gradually encourage you to relax.

It was built as an aws lambda function.

I had initially worried about finding non-hilarious sheep sounds that didn’t sound like humans pretending to be sheep. But after listening to real sheep it turns out they sound like humans pretending to be sheep anyway!
