SortAI

An app for sorting garbage! (Dubhacks 2016)

When in doubt, take a picture of your garbage-to-be and let our app scan it and sort it for you.

Inspired by our own struggles with sorting garbage between 4 categories (Compost, Paper, Containers, Garbage).
