Inspiration

After working with business customer, we have realized a value in this service. It is a win-win situation for OEM and Customers. That was enough to inspire it to make a full fledge app.

What it does

It links OEM data with Customer operation/maintenance data and reduce fleet exposure to critical issues. It can lead to $$$ savings for both Customer and OEM.

How we built it

By using predix components like PX (polymer UI components), UAA authentication service, Java spring boot micro services, redis and postgres db.

Challenges we ran into

Documentation part of the platform to explore various services.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Finally able to achieve what we planned to do in this Hackathon. We were also able to do more feature addition because of better team coordination and expertise.

What we learned

Huge learning about the complete platform and end to end application building on Platform.

What's next for The Wings - On Wing Maintenance Optimizer

Launching in the market and bring value to OEM and Customer.
