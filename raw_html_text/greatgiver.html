Inspiration

We saw that shelters having a hard time to getting the goods that they needed, we decided to create a app that streamline that process.

What it does

GreatGiver.org is a web app aimed to bridge the gap between shelters and volunteers that wish to donate goods and their time. Shelter can request needed goods and People can donate extra goods.

How we built it

Starting with use case and using trello for agile method

What's next for GreatGiver

Update Login and Sign up feature, create individual pages for shelters. Connect with existing back-end server.
