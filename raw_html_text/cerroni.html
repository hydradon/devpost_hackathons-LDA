Inspiration

Affordable housing is a big issue for Metro areas such as Miami. Young professionals and most service professionals can't afford to rent or buy a house close to their jobs.

What it does

It's an airbnb alternative for students, service professionals, travelers, tourists on a budget

How we built it

A lamp app using Ethereum's smart contracts as a payout mechanism between the landlord/tenant.

Challenges we ran into

Leasing property on time for the hackathon

What's next for Cerroni

Launching the application
