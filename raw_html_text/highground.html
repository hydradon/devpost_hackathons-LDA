Inspiration

We got absolutely destroyed by the rain on Friday, so we decided to make a resilience hack based around flash floods.

What it does

Listens to the NWS alerts feed, and alerts users in areas at risk, suggesting a location within a half mile with a higher elevation to hopefully help them find safety in a flash flood.
