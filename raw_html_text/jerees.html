Inspiration

Our goal is to make it possible for those who are unable to move to be able to interact with the world in a way that is efficient, simple, cost-effective, and useful.

What it does

Identifies the location of the subject's eyes, determines where they are looking, adjusts a robot like structure to move and pick up the object.
