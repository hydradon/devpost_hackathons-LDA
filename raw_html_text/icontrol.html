Inspiration

Built at HackRiverDell
Throughout society, gaming is constantly being modernized. When the Xbox connect first came out several years ago, it marked a landmark in game history by allowing the player's body to be the controller. Today, we still can find this trace of technology through Miyo bands and Leap sensors. However, these objects can be annoying to carry. My app iControl allows ios gamers to have the same power as someone with a $200 miyo band by utilizing ar to track hand motion, allowing for games built on hand movement
