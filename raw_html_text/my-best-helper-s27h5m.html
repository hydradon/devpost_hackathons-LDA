Inspiration

Our team was inspired in creating an innovative onboarding process to the app myBestHelper. The main challenge was to make the user answer the 10 questions with a rewarding experience, increasing user engagement and making all the process fun and fast. Gamification was the key to achieve that.

What it does

Asks a series of questions, to determinate what kind of personality the user has. For now the personality is given randomly.

Challenges I ran into

To create contextual images getting together for all the 4 answers was a great challenge and mapping positions in the pictures, maintaining it responsive was not easy!

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The illustrations are gorgeous!
