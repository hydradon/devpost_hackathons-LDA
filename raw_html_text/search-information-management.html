Search Information Management API. (SIMAPI)

Our inspiration is the need to store and compile search results in an organized fashion. Our platform uses the power of NOSQL and nodeJS to tame the top search technologies to do our bidding, then return the results to a customized database. Bend the knee before SIMAPI as it tames the powerful search engines and multimedia sources of the internet into your personal search bot.

As business owners we need to stay on top of the competitive landscape. This toolset will compile and organize search engine results while you sleep for improved business intelligence. Stop searching the same topics. No more open windows and searching your history.

SIMAPI changing the way search results are provided to users is a huge opportunity for MONGO as it polls and clusters the results into your topic driven experience database tool. This API and javascript toolset is easy to run inside any NODEJS or JavaScript such as Sketch and Slack.
