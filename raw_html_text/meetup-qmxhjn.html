We wanted to revolutionize the IOS navigation system, so when integrated into our life it would become a simple easy to use app.We created a way for the navigation system to give an ease of mind to as many people as possible.For example three people trying to meet up. When looking for a place to meet, this app would pin point a location,such as a cafe shop, that is the same distance apart from each person.It also than allows you to track and see the estimated time of arrival for their friend or family.

We used Swift to handle the IOS portion of the app and also Parse.

With every successful test trial that we conducted we became extremely proud. Many issues came ahead, but we were able to dodge pass them and continue ahead.

MeetUp has the possibility of improvements and expansion. MeetUp can be used by virtually anyone, and with a touch of a button they will be on their way to meet up.
