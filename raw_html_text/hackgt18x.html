The idea for the project sparked from the goal of wanting to bring people together around a common interest, and one interest we believe everyone has in common is a good home cooked meal. We believed in reaching out to those around us who need help, rather that be friendship, socialize with your community, or simply have a good home cooked meal. We also believed in eliminating food waste concerns for people and give people an outlet to help others. Our app combines the ideals of reducing waste and sharing with those around you with the social wellbeing of meeting new friends.
