Our project extracts number of tweets sent over the duration of a week from local attractions in Chapel Hill, NC

(bars and restaurants) and ranks their popularity based on tweet count. The ranks are mapped onto a map based

on GPS coordinates. There is a heat map identifying the spots that are most popular.
