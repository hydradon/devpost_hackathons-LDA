Inspiration

People need help for little things and professionals are too expensive and inconvenient. Quick way to solve small problems Ex) Fixing plumbing, mend clothes, tutor, cleaning, fix skateboard, etc

What it does

Allows users to receive immediate response for tasks and services both big and small for a fee. Used by people in local areas so service will be fast and convenient Doesn’t require professional or freelance. Anyone is qualified Used in college environment and other Flexibility “Advanced Craigslist”

How we built it

Used Firebase for backend and database, Android Studio for Android app, and Vue.js for frontend website

Challenges we ran into

Firebase documentation was confusing, Android Studio was confusing

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We have a functioning website, and a functioning backend to power the Android app soon

What we learned

Firebase, Android, Vue, Java, Javascript

What's next for Feasy - Fast and Easy

*Implement credit card payment system *Allow for other users to offer services instead of request assistance *Include rating system to improve reputation of certain users *Incorporate camera so people could take pictures of issue *Messaging system *Gps system
