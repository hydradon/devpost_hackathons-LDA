Inspiration

We wanted to create an application/ website that promotes inclusivity to cater to people like us who have dietary restrictions and food allergies.

What it does

3 fold:

  1. Finds recipes for you using the food you already have at home for when you do not have time to grocery shop or when you are unable to order food online.
  2. Present a variety of recipes catered to your preferences to inspire what meals/snacks you would like to make for when you have time to plan ahead
  3. Finds restaurants around your location that cater to your preferences

How I built it

with Adobe XD

Challenges I ran into

We can build a front end design but needed more time to create the backend

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Coming up with this idea of inclusivity for people with different dietary restrictions and food allergies. We came up with an innovative way to reduce food waste for the social and economic perspective
