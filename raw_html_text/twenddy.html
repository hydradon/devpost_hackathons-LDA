Twenddy

It is important to note that there is a great deal of real time entertainment where users of Twitter often use more than one device at a time and so having a collection of the topic that are trending at any one time.

@Twenddy we seek to take that one step further, to accommodate for those people who wouldn't otherwise have visited twitter or not members per say, to collect the current trending topics with clear statistics of where in the world, frequency and volume of tweets on this.

It may be seen as a rather counterproductive or excessive, however, this is the essence of keeping people in the know and in tune with what's happening around them, even if it is to give them some interesting topics to bring up in conversation with their friends or an amazing fact about the sheer level of activity of those on the other side of the Pacific ocean!
