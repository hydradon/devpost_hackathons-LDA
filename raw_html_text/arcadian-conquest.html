What it does

You get to a move a red ball around a screen with some grass in the background. Originally intended to be a multiplayer PVP, RPG-like experience, we unfortunately fell short due to sleep issues.

How we built it

Very, very slowly with technology we had never used before.

Challenges I ran into

The human body's unwillingness to forgo sleep for more than 24 hours.
