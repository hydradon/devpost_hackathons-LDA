Inspiration and What it does

Introducing Medical Tools into Facebook Community For Diabetes, BP and Breast Cancer Patients.

Medical Contents are shared on Facebook by Post Status, Video and Photo Updates. Its time we Introduce the used of different Medical Tools directly on FaceBook..

Facebook is used by Millions of Medical Personnel, Medical Teams that uses Facebook to educate and Share medical data to their various Patients and their respective Caregivers.

How do we foster more in depth collaborations with all this Patients on Facebook along with their Caregivers and their Medical Teams. This is where FB Medical Tools comes in. This application uses BP Monitoring tools, Diabetic Monitoring Tools, Breast Cancer Tools, Share Status Updates, Video and Pictures to allow medical content sharing between Patients, Caregivers and their respective Medical Team.

Sharing this Medical Data between Patients, Caregivers and respective Medical Team requires Data privacy in HIPAA Compliance Mode.Fortunately to us, Facebook offers Sharing of Personalized information while protecting Medical Data Privacy in a HIPAA Compliance Mode using FaceBook Send Dialog API

With this FaceBook Send Dialog API Medical Information can be sent and shared only to the Medical Personnel that you want. This Applications was design to foster engagement into community of Blood Pressure(BP) Patients, Diabetics and Breast Cancer Patients on Facebook.

How to use this app:

1.) Login using Facebook
2.) Click on Medical Tools and Select BP Medical Tools
3.) Click on Record Your BP Data button
4.) Compute Your BP Systolic readings and Diastolic readings as obtained from the BP Reader and Submit.
5.) The App Compute Your result and tell you whether if you are having (HBP=>High Blood Pressure,LBP=>Low Blood Pressure,NBP=>Normal Blood Pressure, Hypertension etc)
6.) Use BP Calendar to See your BP Details on the Calendar, Your BP Readings,results, Time it was taken etc.
7.) Use Graph Statistics to see your Daily BP Readings in Real time.
8.) Use Facebook Send Dialog Button: to Share Your BP Data, Links etc to your CareGivers, Medical Teams on Facebook in HIPAA Compliance Mode.

What to Know about BP

Blood pressure Monitor readings have 2 major numbers along with Pulse.
The top number is your systolic blood pressure (The highest pressure when your heart beats and pushes the blood round your body system.)
The bottom one is your diastolic blood pressure. (The lowest pressure when your heart relaxes between beats.)
The third is Pulse which is just the Heartbeat.(Pulse Reading is just optional and if it is not Available in your BP monitor, just enter zero(0))

To Calculate your Blood Pressure, just find your top number (systolic Readings) and read across, and then your bottom number (diastolic reading) on the bottom. The point of intersection or where the two readings meet is defined as your Blood Pressure.
