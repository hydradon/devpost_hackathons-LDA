Don't you hate it when you watch one of those Youtube tutorials where the person is just typing in a notepad and you have to scrub like crazy just to find the one part you need? Don't you wish you could just Ctrl+F the stupid thing? Well now you can with Ctrl+Film! Just pop in a video file or a YouTube URL, and we scan the video for text and audio and print it out as text you can search. Wow!
