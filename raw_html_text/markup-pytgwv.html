Comment++

Comment++ is a Chrome extension that allows you to create and view comments on any page as you surf the Internet. The traditional home of comments is the bottom of the page, but often there are specific sections or quotes of pages that people would like to comment on specifically. Whether it's a New York Times article, a study guide, or your personal blog, you'll love sharing your experience with Comment++.

Features

Posting, editing, and deletion of comments Name tagging
