Inspiration

There's many online quizes to find out what celebrity you are, or what profession best matches your personality. I wanted to make such a quiz for kids to have fun while learning more about endangered species.

What it does

To play this decision tree game, answer a few questions and find out which animal you are based on your responses! After finding out which animal you are, you can ask for a description to learn more about the animal and its current status.

What's next for Endangered Animal Quiz

More questions and more animals will be in a future release.
