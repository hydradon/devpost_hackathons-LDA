Inspiration

Love for physics and destruction.

What it does

Shoots an eraser across the room at the desired angle.

How we built it

Used Arduino programming, 2 motors clear tapes, safety goggle headband, scrap wood, and hot glue.

Challenges we ran into

Motors were too weak.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Finishing on time.

What we learned

Programming with Arduino software.

What's next for Automated Slingshot

Developing a method to make the slingshot aim horizontally.
