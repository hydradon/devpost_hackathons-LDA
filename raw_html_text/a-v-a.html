Inspiration

Autonomous car accidents are occurring more frequently with the proliferation of technology. We wanted to leverage: Quorum on Azure, Solidty, and Ruby to solve this.

What it does

Tracks Linear Acceleration data and Posts it to Ethereum Blackchain through Quorum

How we built it

Ruby/ Rails, Solidity, Quorum, Azure

Challenges we ran into

Physics Equations on in Javascritp, deriving Linear acceleration data into a form manageable on the Blockchain

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Data Integrity and sped of throughput through use of Azure
