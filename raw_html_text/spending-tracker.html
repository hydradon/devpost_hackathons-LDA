Inspiration

We want to know how much money we're spending on different parts of our lives, i.e. food, clothes, etc

What it does

iOS app that allows you to choose/take a picture of your receipts, then displays nice graphics of the ways in which you are spending money

How I built it

Using Swift, Java, HP Haven On Demand API, and JSON

Challenges I ran into

Connecting front-end with back-end

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Developing a full project at our first/second hackathon

What I learned

Sockets, TCP Connections, JSON, API calls, Swift,

What's next for Spending Tracker

More data analytics! Averages of monthly and yearly spending and more data visualizations
