jukepod

Collaborative DJing for your car

How it works:

  1. Designate a phone to be the "host" of your Pod. You'll get a unique QR code.

  2. Your friends can scan your Pod QR to join your pod.

  3. Members of your pod search for and add songs to the collective queue.

  4. The host phone plays through the queue into speakers in your car or at a party.
