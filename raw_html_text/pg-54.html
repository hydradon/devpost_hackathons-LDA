Inspiration 

 Our parents are far away and are getting old. Checking in on the frequently can be annoying to our parents. We
    wanted a way to get information from them without pestering them unnecessarily .

What it does 

 PG-54 receives information from various sensors that are interfaced with household items that are frequently used. 
    It charts and graphs the information received on a local computer and on the cloud. This allows outsides users, such
 as loved ones to access the information and compare it to normal, habitual data and gives them an accurate guess
 about the well-being of the PG-54 user.

How we built it 

 Based on our parents normal behavior and interaction with things around the household. The most common items
    were interfaced with appropriate sensors to give information on their usage. Abnormally low usage is a red flag.

Challenges we ran into 

 Some challenges we encountered was charting the data in excel from arduino serial monitor and auto saving the
    data to google sheets.

Accomplishments that we're proud of 

We're proud about completing a project at a hackathon for the first time.

What's next for PG-54 

 Our next steps include introducing machine learning to decipher patterns based on item usage. We also want to 
    introduce email alerts when multiple red flags have been raised.
