Inspiration:

Distraught by the lack of recent innovation in the theremin industry, the SynthCOW team set out to create the most radical musical interface possible, breaking down the analog walls that have been constraining theremin enthusiasts for decades.

How it works:

Left distance controls pitch, right distance controls volume. Look ma, no hands!
