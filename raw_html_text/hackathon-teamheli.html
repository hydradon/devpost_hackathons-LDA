I was inspired by my own story of changing my life once and for all, from the moment I decided to start a healthier diet to the moment i stepped on the weighing scale and realizing that I've lost 22 kg and feeling my best, looking my best for myself.

This project will provide users with detailed information about physical and mental health, as well as suggesting exercises and meal plan. In addition, the "Fitness Dare" would give user a fun time challenging themselves

I built this project starting with coming up with the basic ideas of health then organized information to give user the best experience and putting in our creativity.

Not having enough experience to code further to create a more functioning website; Not having enough time; Coming up with ideas to make the project enjoyable

Seeing the web running smoother than we expected, extracting vital information to give users the most knowledge is such an accomplishment

I learned how to work as a team; some learned about the basics of coding such as html, css, python,...

Continue building the website to make living a healthy lifestyle more of a fun journey for users and creating a place for people to share their experience and knowledge, building a healthy community.
