• YEG Heat Map

Inspiration

In an attempt to determine the most hood neighbourhood in all of YEG (and to learn data visualzation), we built a heat map!

What it does

Seperates the city of Edmonton based on its neighbourhood borders and creates a heat map of total crimes committed in each neighbourhood from 2009 until now.

How I built it

Using data from the Edmonton Open Data Portal, we were able to extract the information necessary for our heat map. With d3.js, we projected a geojson map of Edmonton (also from the Open Data Portal), while adding the data of each neighbourhood to the map.
