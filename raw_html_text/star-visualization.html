Where is the data from?

CATest pulls information from the STAR Test Results page of the California Department of Education.

How do you help teachers??

CATest visualizes tthe STAR Test Results into a interpretive display to give teachers a way to track student info at scale.

But Why?

This was made with the hope to learn JavaScript and to try to help the broken education system.
