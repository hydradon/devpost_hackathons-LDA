• Can You Beat The AI

Inspiration

We wanted to understand the basic concepts of an AI.

What it does

It plays Tic Tac Toe with one user.

How I built it

We studied algorithms to win the game of Tic Tac Toe.

Challenges I ran into

Creating algorithms to account for each possibility.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Getting the program to work without any known bugs.

What I learned

Creating an AI takes a great deal of time and there are many possibilities to account for. We also learned that we need to adore efficient ways to let the computer choose rather than hard coding scenarios.
