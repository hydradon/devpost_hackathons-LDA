Inspiration

Analyze and change your mood using Amazon Alexa

What it does

Alexa takes a picture and analyzes your mood via azure emotion api. She tells you what your sentiment is and can adjust your phillips hue lights accordingly. After that she can play your defined playlist on spotify

How we built it

We used Amazon Alex for voice recognition and Lambdas to start the process of taking a picture on a raspberry pi. Afterwards the picture is analyzed by Azures Cognitive API.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It works :)

What we learned

Building Alexa Skills, using Lambda, Azure, Python and MQTT.
