What it does

Firstly, it shows a user how much they on average save when using public transport compared to using a car. The user can also see an explanation of how we came up with this figure. Secondly, and perhaps a tad more exciting, you can input a trip between two different stations in Munich (using autocompletion for station names) as well as the type of car you own, and get an oversight over pricing of different means of transportation for this specific journey.
