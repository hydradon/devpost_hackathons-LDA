Inspiration

We wanted to create something that improved everyday efficiency for everyone. Falling asleep/staying awake is a well-known problem among all people when working late at night.

What it does

Our app detects the level of drowsiness from a live photo. It then supplies relevant material (ie music) to help the situation. Finally, it logs the amount of time that you were sleepy or awake. We keep the data secure, make it easy to use, and leave the user satisfied.

How we built it

Android Studio TensorFlow CNN Model Trained with 3,000 Images - 94% Accuracy Scandit scanner

Challenges we ran into

Training the model and running Scandit

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Achieving 94% accuracy and getting the entire app to work.

What we learned

How to use Scandit for real-time interactions.
