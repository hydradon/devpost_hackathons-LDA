Inspiration

The concept of overpopulation and how with the increase of cars on the road there is more jaywalking

What it does

Timestamps people walking nearby and sends the information (along with street address) to the database (which can be used by local law enforcement)

How I built it

Built using JavaScript, Android studio and stdlib

Challenges I ran into

Time constraint and overall ensuring that the idea was feasible

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Leaning new technologies and working as part of a team

What I learned

JavaScript and Stdlib

What's next for SafeWalks

Improving its functionality and overall quality
