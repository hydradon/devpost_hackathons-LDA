Inspiration

We decided to create an entertaining game that allows two players to play and work together to complete the game.

What it does

This application is a 2D platformer with controls for two players, W, A, S, D, and arrow keys. They, together, have to avoid dangerous obstacles and reach the end of the level.

How we built it

We used Unity to create the application and used C# for the scripting.

Challenges we ran into

Have a second player, interesting obstacles, and camera following were difficult to complete. In the end, we overcame those challenges and finalized the product.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The complete game being finished is something to be proud of. Especially allowing 2 players to play the game gave a prideful feeling.

What we learned

We learned that there are many difficulties, but can be overcome with experimenting and planning.
