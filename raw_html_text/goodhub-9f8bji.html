There is a dire systemic inefficiency in sourcing talent for projects. This issue is greatly magnified in the non profit sector.

Volunteers need structure to better themselves and their skillset to make truly meaningful contributions.

GoodHub is the solution to the project sourcing ecosystem.

GoodHub is the aggregate for non-profit project sourcing.

Nonprofits can find the help they need.

Volunteers are provided with structure, guidance, and mentorship to make meaningful contributions.
