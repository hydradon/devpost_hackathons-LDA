Inspiration

The way we take and store notes photos is tedious and difficult to visualise when we need it.

What it does

It lets you see your notes organized by subject, with a specific title and date.

How we built it

We developed it with swift and used realm to store data in the device.

Challenges we ran into

Storing information locally and sorting photos by their date and time and storing them in their corresponding subject.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to develop an intuitive app that works and looks nice.

What we learned

More of swift and realm to store data.

What's next for VNotes

Update it so we can search by virtual notes and upload it to the cloud.
