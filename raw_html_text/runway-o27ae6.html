What it does

Runway is image classification for the layman. Our platform allows specific venues or item enthusiasts, or really anyone to build a sophisticated image classification model by uploading images and labels. Once finished, the can publish their model, and anyone can start using it. This way, local museums can provide more information about artwork, and parks can classify species of caterpillar.
