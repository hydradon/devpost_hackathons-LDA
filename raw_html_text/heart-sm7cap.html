Inspiration

Heart diseases is one of the top cause of death.

With ubiquitous smart watch and modern machine learning techniques, we are finally able to detect heart disease early.

What it does

Smart watch saves life by detecting heart diseases with machine learning.

Many smart watches have heart rate sensor. Wearers who wear it is providing continuous data input that is going to be valuable to machine learning. Since they wear it all the time, we might be able to provide earlier detection than the yearly checkup.
