What it does

Mobile app game for kids to play when bored in supermarkets - keeps them quiet and entertained.

Kids have to solve riddles by finding the product that the riddle describes. They then scan the barcode to see if their answer matches.

How we built it

PhoneGap, Javascript, jQuery, HTML, CSS.
