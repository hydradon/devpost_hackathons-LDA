BioClock

Android application

Every person has his own biological clock inside and each of those clocks run in its own pace.

This application analyzes your biological clock by doing a simple test (which is actually automatization of the test developed by German scientist Gunther Hildebrandt).

In only about 1 minute you can find out if you are an early bird or a night owl.
