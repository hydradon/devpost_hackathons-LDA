HackUTDVICoursebook++

Around registration time, we and nearly everybody we know are plagued by maintaining a multitude of tabs to check and compare data from schedule planner, utd coursebook, ratemmyprof, and utdgrades to choose our optimal schedules. Thus, we decided to try and minimize our work load around registration time and stay a bit more organized by creating this extension. Ideas for further development include summarizing info and displaying it in the popup, as well as integrating utdgrades and schedule planner into the extension.
