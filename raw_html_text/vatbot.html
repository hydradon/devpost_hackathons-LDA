Inspiration

Anxiety and Depression are one of the most common issues faced by our generation. After thorough research, we found that it is most dangerous in young kids who cannot express their problems and hence cannot get the help and support they require. To cater to this problem, our team decided to create a chat bot game that would interact with kids to detect any symptoms of anxiety and depression and alert their parents/ guardians.

What it does

It interacts with the user in a game setting whose premise is planning a birthday party through speech and analyzes the user's mindset along the course of the game using sentiment analysis.

How we built it

We used python libraries like nltk, google cloud, etc to build the chat bot.

Challenges we ran into

Training the bot to learn about symptoms of anxiety and depressions using vast amount of information and analyze it took time and effort.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to deploy the product as we had planned.

What we learned

Importance of collaborative learning

What's next for Vatbot

Making the bot intelligent enough to therapies the user and a gaming interface.
