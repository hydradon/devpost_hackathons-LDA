Inspiration

people always say you have good or bad "karma" based on your actions. But what if this was real.

What it does

tracks a person's good doing using sentiment analysis and rewards them based on good actions. The person can then spend their karma on items or services in the Karma Market - all items are submitted by users wanting to reward others for doing good.

How we built it

nG, JS, mongo and kairos emotional analysis api

Challenges we ran into

not being able to get the transaction on a blockchain with a cryptocurrency.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

fitting little pieces together and the brainstorming to implementation process

What we learned

talking through ideas can work out potential issues a lot quicker than just diving into the code

What's next for Karma

setting up the marketplace and moving the coin onto the ethereum blockchain with it's own smart contract
