Inspiration

St Andrews and Hugh Grant related story

What it does

A Hugh Grant character has to run and avoid obstacles through the city of St Andrews. This game entertains the masses with near infinite replayability and its whimsical nature.

How we built it

We used javascript, CSS, html and GIMP picture editing tool to create and host fully working video game project.

Challenges we ran into

Making characters and animations to do the right tasks.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Our program works as planned. The project is successful

What we learned

If you collaborate with your teammates and work non-stop for couple of hours, projects will eventually reach their final state.

What's next for Grant Pub Hunt

Add more levels, create a wider user interface and change places, characters (an option to play as a prince William).
