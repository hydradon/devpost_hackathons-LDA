Inspiration

We wanted to do something with VR and also Unreal Engine.

What it does

Allows you feel like you're water bending!

How we built it

We built it using Unreal Engine blueprints and and Oculus Rift.

Challenges we ran into

The most obvious challenge was attempting to simulate good-looking water.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We're proud of our teamwork, and pulling off something that seemed impossible! Namely, learning Unreal, working with an Oculus, _ and _ simulating a fluid!

What we learned

How to program using blueprints in Unreal Engine and what metaballs are.

What's next for Waterbending

Freezing the water!
