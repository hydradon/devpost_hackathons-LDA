Inspiration

Most consumers do not think of legal issues until the problem is right at their doorstep. We would like to help consumers be proactive about their legal health.

How it works

Consumers will be working through a treasure map, picking the "correct" answers would take you on a direct path to the end, but the "wrong" answers will take you on a side path to learn why your answer was wrong. A summary page be presented at the end of the treasure hunt to show the different lessons the person had to take.

Challenges I ran into

We wanted a creative game that would keep the consumer interested and engaged.

What's next for Legal Health Check Up

We would like this to be expanded into more components to provide a more comprehensive legal health check up.
