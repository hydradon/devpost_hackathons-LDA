Peddler is a bridge using which small businesses can reach out to previously untouched audiences and launch campaigns for targeted demographics. It leverages the power of the Messenger platform and the people connected to it for its core features. It’s extremely easy to sign up on the platform as either a customer/store owner, all you need is a messenger account. Imagine you just moved into new city or different part in current city, and now you want some grocery for today. Since new to the area, you dont have any idea about nearby stores and most of them are not even available online (Happens most of the time in India). but now you wont face that problem, thanks to Pedler. It has already all the details of nearby stores. With over 200M active users in india(being 95% of them on mobile), it very easy to setup account for stores and small businesses. While onboarding, it take details like products they are selling, store location, whether they do home delivery or not, their contact details, address etc. And now all this info in accessible to its nearby users.
