Inspiration

Income Inequality and decrease world productivity has lead to staggering effect on global economics. The Millennial generation has been defined by a sharing culture from Napster to Facebook. Recently, a more social and trust-less technology has arose "Blockchain". Therefore, we attempted to expanded that social economics of blockchain and the sharing to car reports.

What it does

AutoBlock is an application that allows Car Dealers, Insurance Companies, and private entities to report car incidents and historical information.
