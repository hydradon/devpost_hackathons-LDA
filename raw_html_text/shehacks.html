Inspiration

The Disaster and Health categories both require real-time information about people's location and needs. We sought to give individuals the ability to help each other, especially when there are few available emergency personnel.

What it does

In a post-disaster situation, when emergency personnel are focusing on people in life-threatening conditions, there are still people who need help. However, there are also people who were not as badly affected and want to help - they just don't quite know how. This service matches those two groups together, allowing people to help their neighbors with just the click of a button. You can also sign up to be notified via text message when somebody in your area is in need of food, water, first aid, or other supplies. if you are able to provide any of those things, you can simply respond yes and are given that person's location so that you can go and help!
