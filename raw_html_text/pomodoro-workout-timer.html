What it does

The pomodoro technique is a time management method where a person works for some set amount of time (for instance, 25 minutes), and then takes a short break (around 5 minutes). Our chrome extension blocks distracting websites during the work time, and suggests workouts for you to do during your breaks.

How we built it

The pomodoro workout timer is a chrome extension, made with js, html, and css.
