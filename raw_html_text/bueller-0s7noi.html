Bueller

Bash teachers all you want, but they're only human. Most of them have great intentions. Bueller is here to make sure those intentions become reality. This software gives real time analytics of the pace and clarity of every lecture straight from the students personal devices. Furthermore, the professor can peak at this data during lecture and adjust accordingly. Bueller makes lectures better, period. Finally, classrooms can have the quantitative data that they deserve. In the future, universities and schools could use this data to rate lecturers effectiveness. Additionally, students could use this data to get an idea of a lecturer's style before attending the first class.
