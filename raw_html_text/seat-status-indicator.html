Inspiration

As Singaporeans, we enjoy dining at hawker centres as it sells a wide variety of cheap yet delicious fare.

As students, most of us head down to the library whenever we need to do some serious studying.

Yet how many times have we experienced walking through the entire hawker centre or library without finding a single unoccupied seat?

What it does

Our team's Seat Status Indicator, or Chope Status Indicator (CSI hehehehe ), solves this by displaying the number of available seats in said public spaces prominently
