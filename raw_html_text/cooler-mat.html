It is a solar powered smart drink ordering gadgets; It measures the weight of the cooler (Styrofoam, plastic, or whatever) on it to calculate how many beers in it. When you get low on beer, it orders beers using Nexmo SMS/Voice API, and also publishes cooler temperature information at Verizon Thinkspace.
