Inspiration

My friend has a condition that he can't get solved and couldn't find a clinical trial to help him out.

What it does

Imports a patients entire medical record then automatically matches them to a clinical trial for their condition.

How I built it

MEAN application that is loaded with all of the clinical trials scraped from clinicaltrials.gov and a patient platform to do the matching.
