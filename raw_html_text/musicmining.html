Inspiration

Youtube and other platforms take a large portion of content creators money, and that prevents people from seeing their true revenue. We are completely transparent and this is a creator friendly platform.

What it does

Sends crypto which is mined by the browser of the user directly to the creator.

How we built it

We used flask for the web framework, html and js for the front end, and sqlite3 for our database.

Challenges we ran into

We had trouble with integrating the crypto mining, but we managed to do it, and our numbers update daily.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of creating a platform which could be a product used in the real world, not just the hackathon.

What we learned

We learned how to distribute work and manage different parts of a project.

What's next for MusicMining

We will implement videos on our platform as well.
