Inspiration

High school students are often overwhelmed when it comes to getting involved, applying to universities, time managing, while continuing to pursue their passion. As high school students we experienced this ourselves, we continues to feel the impact of this problem in university when high school students would approach us with questions on how and where to get involved.

What it does

A portal for extracurricular involvement, involv matches students with opportunities based on their interests, location and availability.

How we built it

Front-end: React App Back-end: Node.js

Challenges we ran into

Lack of web development skills

Accomplishments that we're proud of
