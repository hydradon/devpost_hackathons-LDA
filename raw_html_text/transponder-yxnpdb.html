Inspiration

Defeating message censorship of short-form status messages.

What it does

Transponder uses the EVM's contract log functionality to store short strings associated with an Ethereum address. Contract logs have a few valuable properties: 1) they are vastly cheaper to create than contract storage - 20x less gas!, and 2) they can be filtered and searched quickly.
