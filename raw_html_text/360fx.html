Inspiration

A lot of people enjoy adding filters / effects to regular photos, 360 degree photos / video could use the same thing! We're also exploring animated effects, since you need software to best view 360 degree photos anyway.

What it does

We made a basic 360 degree photo viewer from scratch and implemented some prototype effects on top of it. Works in mobile, desktop, and HTML5.

Challenges I ran into

It was a little difficult to deploy to the Dragonboard, but it works great there now.

What's next for 360FX

More subtle color-grading filters, more filters in general, explore animations more.
