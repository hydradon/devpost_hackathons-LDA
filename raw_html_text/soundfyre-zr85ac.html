Inspiration

All the struggling, independent musical artists out there.

What It Does

Musicians and musical artists can upload there music/song/album/mixtape/ORIGINAL HOT FIRE to the website and share it locally. Others are able to listen and discover new local music and like/dislike it to increase/decrease the "radius of fire" of each upload.

How We Built It

We used Parse on the backend and Google Map's API/JavaScript to handle the mapping and overall interface.

What's Next For SoundFyre

Full-fledged social media network for local musical artists and listeners and world wide usage.
