Have you ever found yourself in the situation of not knowing where to go for a drink or for something to eat? How many countless arguments have you had with family and friends have you had about where to go? Too many?

Why not let The Decidr make the decision for you? The only decision you have to make is to swipe left or right for food or drink.

Tapping on the icon takes you to a randomly selected bar or restaurant. It even has a map to show you how to get there, and step-by-step directions are also available.

Don't like the selection, give your device a shake and it will automatically select a new bar or restaurant for you.

So stop arguing and start enjoying.
