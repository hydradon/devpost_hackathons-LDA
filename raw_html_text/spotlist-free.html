We all love the new spotify feature "Discover Weekly". But we all hate that there is no way to listen to the previous week's tracks, or even earlier playlists.

Spotlist to the rescue !

Get your Spotify playlist uri, paste it in our form and we will make sure that every week we will archive your "Discover weekly" playlists.

Features

  1. Archive your weekly playlists
  2. See a list of all the songs
  3. Export each playlist to Spotify (limited to 10 songs per playlist - buy premium version for full export)
  4. At the end of each month we combine for you a monthly playlist, which you can export it (premium version only)

We hope you will enjoy it
