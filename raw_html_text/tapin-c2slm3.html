Inspiration

Speaking with data analysts from AB InBev about the lack of actionable data to maximize sales reps performance

What it does

Recognize emerging trends from real time sales data, increase the demand of products at retail level, streamline on-premise reordering and optimize brand portfolio.

How I built it

We used a combination of technologies including: iOS (Swift) for the iPhone app and Javascript ( jQuery, Bootstrap, several additional libraries) for the desktop web app.

Challenges I ran into

Creating an informative and engaging UI and UX within both the iOS and Web app.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Identifying Brand Metrics (Growth, decline, efficiency, saturation) and Bar Productivity Metrics (Action to take for over performing and underperforming partners)

What I learned

Do we need this? Theo anything for this or can we axe it?

What's next for Tap In

To have AB InBev hire our team to continue to build this!
