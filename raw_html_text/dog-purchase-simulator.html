Ulyana loves dogs, her housemates dog is really cute - and a corgi. * Who doesn't love dogs? I'm unsure. *

Press the button. Make money, keep watching your money go up. Eventually, you can purchase a dog.

Our team learned swift from the ground up, and utilized it to develop the app. We broke into teams. One person designed, one person made aesthetic designs and art. Multiple people contributed to code.

It is difficult to not only learn an entire coding language, and platform in one day on a machine you're unfamiliar with - but also code a single app on that very same language none of your team has experience in.

** The cutie page was an Easter egg, we were unable to dynamically store the dogs in a table in order to display the pictures of them under "my dogs" **

I am proud of almost completely learning a language to proficiency needed to work on an app as well as making an app with the minimum qualities needed to function.

You can learn a lot in one day if you put your mind to it.

Whats next you ask? Being able to look at your dogs. Complete functionality. Immense wealth. Complete stardom.

Dog Purchase Simulator will be the next biggest thing. Just wait.
