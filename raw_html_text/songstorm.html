It's a pain to share music , we all use different music streaming services (Apple Music, Spotify, Google Music, Soundcloud...) but in the end we manually type the song, or send a youtube link (that stops playing the moment we turn off our screen).

Songstorm is an Android and iPhone app that sends a universal music sharing link to your Facebook friends, no matter what service you are using. Your friends can now open the song in their favourite app!

How we built it

Android and iPhone native apps and a lightweight Flask backend serving the link sharing website and doing API/scraping calls.

What's next for songstorm

We want to release a public beta for other people to try, this is a service we need ourselves and think many others could benefit from.
