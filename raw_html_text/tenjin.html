Make you notes smarter, cleaner, and more efficiently.

TENJIN is a hybrid mobile and web app that allow users to effortlessly parse audio to text and automatically research articles and news in real-time. Using a variety of APIs to create a cohesive experience, TENJIN has a use for all ages.

ORIGINS

TENJIN is derived from Japanese folklore and mythology - Tenjin (天神) is the Shinto kami of scholarship. Tenjin's influence is now in high demand for passing exams, and so many students, and parents, pray for success at his shrine before important entrance exams, and return afterwards, if appropriate, to give thanks for success.

HOW

Instead of simply translating short clips of audio into text, TENJIN translates a live stream of audio from your phone to our web application. As the audio stream continues, TENJIN will gather articles, summarize research, and even output an outline based on the audio stream.

FEATURES

Along with thorough parsing, TENJIN was designed from the ground up with a Material Design philosophy to clear the clutter and allow users to focus on the research. Along with a faster experience, TENJIN introduces a suite of interactive features that allow users to bring their learning and lessons to life in new ways. Find relevant, popular articles with a single click. Read the stories of historical figures, accompanied by their portraits as you scroll through stories. Explore interactive maps, listen to audio captions, and even save individual parts of research to review later.
