▸Web app that analyzes your pictures and posts on your social media to generate a collage of your most 'euphoric' moments.

▸Used the FB Graph API, Microsoft Emotion API and deployed a node/express server on Ubuntu to gather and process data.

▸Selected certain images and posts based on sentiment after data analysis to create the collage.
