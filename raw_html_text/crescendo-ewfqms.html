Crescendo

Combines a touch screen with hand gestures to create an intuitive and fun music making experience.

The touch screen acts as a MIDI keyboard, enabling it to be used with the digital audio workstation of your choice. The touch screen is combined with a Leap Motion that maps hand movements in the x-, y-, and z-axis to MIDI controllers. In other words, the hand gestures are used to apply effects in real-time to the composed music.
