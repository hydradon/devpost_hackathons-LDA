Inspiration

Students eating alone because of conflicting class schedules with their friends, or hesitating to approach a familiar face because of fear of rejection and food wastage on campus.

What it does

It matches students based on their class schedules and similar interests and allows them to meet and dine together. I aims to reduce food wastage on campus by allowing students to donate their unused meal swipes to those in need.

How I built it

I used Java and XML

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I'm proud of
