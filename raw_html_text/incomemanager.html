IncomeManager

Philly Codefest Project 2019

With the website that we want to create, we will create a game where the player is given a certain amount of money based on a scenario to simulate what it is like to live in poverty. The player will go through a series of events that gives them an option to make a moral decision or an economic decision. The player must prove if they can live in the situation that people under poverty every day. Ideally, this will share a perspective of what it is like to live in poverty.
