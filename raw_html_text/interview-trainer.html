Title : AD - Interview Trainer

Team member - Matthew(380) - James (375) - Kristoporus(378) - William (229)

Inspiration

When it comes to find internship, students always scare, especially the technical interview. We offer a new concept of online interviewer using opencv that can detect facial, voice to text as well as machine learning APIs for grammar checking. We hope that this platform can be helpful for alot of students and improve their confidence.

What it does

Online interview, to train for practical skills(formal language, emotion)

How I built it

Opencv that can detect facial, voice to text as well as machine learning APIs for grammar checking.

Challenges I ran into

A lot of bug in integrating the opencv, nodejs, mysql

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Learn new things in only 1 day and manage to accomplish the goal task, although there are some challenges we are yet to overcome

What I learned

How to be patient to debug as well as teamwork and communication.

What's next for Interview Trainer
