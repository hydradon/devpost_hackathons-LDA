Inspiration

Being a person who sends a money abroad on regular basis, I have seen financial institutions charging hefty fees to convert money from one currency to another.

What it does

It provide the best possible currency exchange rate and makes it possible to remit the amount within small duration.

How I built it

I have created a set of algorithms which compute use mine & compute to achieve best possible exchange rates at given transaction time.

Challenges I ran into

To build a algorithms that will compete with current markets.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I was successfully able to provide less or equal currency exchange rate available in the market.

What I learned

Learned more about different technology and financial services.

What's next for Financial Service - Currency Exchange

To develop the investment strategies and offer array of financial services to community.
