Inspiration

MarioKart

What it does

Two Myo powered bots battle for world domination, one balloon at a time.

How I built it

Arduino, Myo, motors,

Challenges I ran into

Calibrating Myo movements for effective car driving movement and reliable Bluetooth interface between Myo, PC and Arduino.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The moment the vehicle correctly responded to our Myo gestures during first try....at 4am.

What I learned

The team learned about new hardware such as Myo, Oculus Rift and Muse. In addition, some of the team learned about the control of servo motors using Arduino coding and trouble shoot a non-functioning vehicle over Bluetooth.
