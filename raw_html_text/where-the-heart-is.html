Where the Heart is

Many who live on the streets battle the feeling that they’re inadequate or nonexistent to the rest of the world. We believe that fostering meaningful interactions between homed and homeless individuals vitalizes community health.

With this app, homed people can have moments with homeless people, get to know them, take selfies and fulfill their needs to gain points. The concept of gamification can be used for the greater good and make someone's day.
