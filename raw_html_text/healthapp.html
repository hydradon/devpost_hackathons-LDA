After reading that the number one problem facing physicians is patient compliance : how to keep patients engaged and disciplined about following their instructions. It's no wonder patients don't seem engaged with the person they only see for 15 minutes every 6 months. This app allows you to easily communicated with your physician and keep track of what you need to do to maintain health.
