Problem/OPORTUNITY

Nowadays, one problem that hospitals have to deal with is spending time with lots of paperwork when transfering a patient between hospitals, there is a need for optimizing this process by standarizing it, making it faster and much easier to the medic.

Value Proposal Our proposal to deal with this, is a new software platform that will allow the hospital personnel to perform the transfer by uploading the patient needed information to the cloud, making it available to the new hospital which is recieving him, and updating his status while on transfer.
