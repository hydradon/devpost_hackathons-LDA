Inspiration

Today we use Uber and Lyft to transport us around the globe. What happens when we scale it up, When we and technology progress we will need a new way of transpiration, I came into this Hackathon wanting to create an app that would allowed me to learn wireframe design with Marvel and learn more advance features of Photoshop so I create Hitch (like Hitchhiker Guide to The Galaxy)

What it does

It is a full UI and design of what an app would look like for an UBER like application that would provide for space transport. I mainly focused on design and making it look good. I also used animations to give it a slick look. I created 2 apps, one is an app customers would use and another pilots would use.

How I built it

I used Photoshop to prototype all my screens then animated them and edited them in Marvel to create a finished product.

Challenges I ran into

Getting a consistent UI and a real to life professional look was one of the greatest challenges. I learned a lot by getting past this and learned about backend dev.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The Screens look well made and high quality and the animations complement that

What I learned

I learned advanced features in Photoshop and how to use Marvel for full stack web and mobile design, a very demanding career.

What's next for Hitch - The Future Of Transport

Making it a functional app from a prototype that focuses on design, making the sensors in the phone adapt, It could be done easily with more time.
