Inspiration

We were inspired by the Greek Goddess Soteria, who was the goddess of safety and protection It would be prtrayed as everyday accessories like necklaces, earings, glasses and bracelets..

What it does

This accessory would notify family members or 911 in the case of the use of trigger words withing a certain time period

How we built it

We built it with an Arduino, grove, touch sensors, voice sensors and led light that would light up in the occasion of two trigger words, the app is connected to the device by bluetooth and it is the thing that would actually notify the police, the web would sell the project
