iLocate

Over the last few decades, technology has dramatically changed our way of living. We see so many screens all around us now, not to forget, the screen this text is being displayed on! Our Wi-Fi enabled devices have changed the way we work, travel and play. A plain old phone, which went on to become a ‘smart’ phone, is an ‘intelligent’ phone today. Why not use the ‘intelligence’ and travel ‘smart’?

iLocate is an app, that lets you make great friends! With the smart way of adding new contacts using QR codes, and an even quicker way of contacting your new contacts through messaging, create bonds and memories that last a lifetime!

Go to a new place, and find everything that is around you. Travel smart and carefree, with iLocate at your service!
