INSPIRATION: A sock with an LED light for athletes.

G O A L S is a wearable light that helps athletes travel the world.

We started off wanting to provide socks with a light and from there we realized that the wearable light market was bigger than just socks.

A big challenge we faced was the limited amount of time allotted to prototype. Finding the right materials and of the right size and strength was the major difficulty.

We're most proud of our accomplishment in responding to market need and seeing past our own biases.

We learned that simple has always been the best thing.

What's next for G O A L S? World domination!
