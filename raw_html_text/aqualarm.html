• AquAlarm!

Inspiration

We wanted to help people - snooze addicts especially - to wake up early in the morning.

What it does

It's an alarm clock that sprays water in your face and sounds a loud buzzer, and flashes reaally bright LED lights in your face.

How we built it

Using arduino, and some engineering.

Challenges we ran into

Finding hardware components, getting code to compile.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We managed to complete our design within the time limit, even though we had to face many challenges (like the aforementioned)

What we learned

How to work as a team, and some having patience when something doesn't work right or go your way.
