Inspiration

The overall hassle of the average parking experience. The MobilePay case

What it does

Allows you to checkin at a parking lot and paying automatically as you go, hereby making parking meters unnecessary. With the MobilePay integration you are able to pay in a smart way, and you can even share the parking expense with your friends!

How we built it

Using Swift 4 and Xcode

Challenges we ran into

iOS has a lot of restrictions, especially if you are a new Swift developer.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The design, the apple map zoning.

What we learned

A lot about iOS and Swift programming.

What's next for ePark

Parking lot database, MobilePay integration.
