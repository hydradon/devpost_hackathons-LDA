Inspiration

Nowadays the world is extremely dynamic and it is very difficult to find people with same biorhythms . So our application will provide assistance to help people to find friends with same sleeping cycles.

Nighting - the night dating

What it does

The application compares sleeping data from logged in person with other people that shared their sleeping data and makes a match of your sleeping buddy based on comparing the data.

How we built it

We build it with love, passion and bravery. We have divided responsibilities between group members so that everyone had his/her own part. We have Front-End in Angular2, Back-End and database is Firebase.

Challenges we ran into

Connection to Oura ring to mobile phone was challenging, also we found some bugs in Oura API, but support team fixed them really quickly.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to make a working prototype of sleeping data matching application in two days from scratch. We've solved lots of unexpected technical problems and worked non-stop on the limit of our bodies.

What we learned

First of all we learned a lot about sleeping cycles and tracking it with Oura rings. Most importantly we've learnt how important it is to maintain good team spirit and communication

What's next for Nighting

Now we compare only sleeping data, but in future we can expand it with activity data and readiness for a new day data.
