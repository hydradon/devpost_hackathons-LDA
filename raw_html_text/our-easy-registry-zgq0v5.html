Wedding registries, and baby registries allows the couple to make a list of the gifts/items they require for their new chapter in life. Our Easy Registry helps make this experience easier by allowing communication between friends and family to know what items have already been bought and prevent them from buying the same gift.

Our Easy Registry allows you to keep track of wedding registry items between friends and family to prevent the purchase of the same item twice.

We learned how to use Docker, create a container with an image and have it be run off of an aws EC2 instance and we learned how to use SQLite.

Our next step is to incorporate continuous integration using AWS and Jenkins.
