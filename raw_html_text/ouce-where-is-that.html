Inspiration

The inspiration came from the app Shazam, but instead of finding the song that is playing it finds the location of a video. We love to travel and are often inspired by the landscapes in movies, tv shows, random videos on the web.

What it does

It recognizes the location seen in a video and pinpoints it on google maps.

How we built it

We used clarifai to train models to recognize cities (at the moment only Paris and Nyc).
