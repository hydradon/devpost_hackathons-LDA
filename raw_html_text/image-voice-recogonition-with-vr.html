Da Vinci recommends it's users with the most suitable books. Moreover, the core functionality of Da Vinci is as a Human Library. Da Vinci listens to experiences and different journeys of its users and stores it into its library. The second segment of operation is enablement of the second category of users to listen to these stories that are collected by Da Vinci over the course of time. Da Vinci is programmed in such a manner that it narrates every story based on the storyline to make it sound compelling for the user.

Consider the example of a man who is highly enthusiastic, energetic and confident. Da Vinci listens to his journey and records to inspire a user who is less confident & energetic and has come to Da Vinci in search of motivational listening. Da Vinci is touching humans and enhancing their lives by means of its collective literature.

Da Vinci is also available on voice over the call and text where a user can look out for any book and hear a summary or continue to listen to his story on the go.
