• iPark Logo

Inspiration

Minimizing parking impact in San Diego

What it does

Provides a higher amount of parking spaces through crowd-sharing either in private driveways or through partnerships with restaurants or local businesses to increase business.

How we built it

Prototype is made using Wix web designing site, further development will be a personalized website as well as an application for user interface.

Challenges we ran into

-Liability (towing, bird collection, bikes collection) -Target Audience -Terms and conditions

Accomplishments that we're proud of

-Navigating around the challenges we ran into -Making a website demo that could potentially be launched -Intact team

What we learned

-Learned how to use database -Learned how to use each others strengths -Learned to collaborate well. -The fact that businesses are 'not' renting a parking space, but donating a designated space for a designated time in order to market to their customers through the promotion provided through our application.

What's next for iPark

-Application development -Narrow down target audience -Developing terms of use -MVP launch
