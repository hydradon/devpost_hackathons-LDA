When you walk into your office, Alexa will recognize you.

The way it works is every three seconds, an image will be captured of the front door of your office. When the image is uploaded, it will search AWS Rekognition for faces, and if one is recognized, it will query dynamodb for the employee details, including meetings.

When you ask Alexa to greet you, she will check who you are against DynamoDB, then read from your calendar and tell you about your meetings.
