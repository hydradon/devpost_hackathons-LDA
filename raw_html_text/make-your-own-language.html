We have established goals for the future of development and plan on working together more. With both of us working on this project together, we were able to move through the process and a reasonable pace, while still helping each other learn the code and GUI together. Even though we had some issues working together along the way we came together in the end. All while finding a way to communicate on a better level and also be understanding of each others perspective. We believe that the idea we have created has a large potential and will continue to work on it as a team outside of this weekend. Hoping to make long time friends and partners in development.
