Inspiration

Achim Jung, Lecturer at UoB, and his wonderful, confusing maths lectures.

What it does

Takes his panopto lecture recordings, transcribes them, passes them through a markov chain library to output a unique confusing lecture.

How we built it

Using Python hosted on a GCP VM, and a screenscraper to get all the recordings of his lectures, and download their .wav audio streams.

Challenges we ran into

Everything

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Everything

What we learned

GCP is so powerful, and markov chains are a really smart way of processing text in a seemingly natural way
