• OppoB

As university students and aspiring entrepreneurs, we find time and time again that it's increasingly difficult to find time to meet with our loved ones, hang out with friends, book meetings, and schedule our lives. Everyone's calendars are growing as fast as our lives are growing busier.

OppoB is the solution to that scheduling problem. By combining Google Calendar and Firebase, OppoB allows users to seamlessly merge calendars to find times that the most amount of people are free.

Doing fullstack development with two people proved to be a challenge that was overcome by teamwork, caffeine, McDonalds, and layered napping.

Thank you, The OppoB Team
