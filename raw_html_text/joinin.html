JoinIn is a website that can improve PittServe and connect to students’ LinkedIn, catered to Pitt students and all organizations, that consolidates the process of finding volunteers for your event, finding volunteering events to attend, and showcase a history of volunteer work on a profile.

Currently in its prototype stage, JoinIn hopes to make it easier to connect volunteers and organizations!
