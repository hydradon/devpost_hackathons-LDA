GivEasy is a company founded on the idea of optimizing life to its fullest potential while providing a helping hand to those around us. From its humble beginnings in the back of Roone Auditorium at the 2018 Columbia DevFest Hackathon three CS students and a lost econ major set forth to make the world a better place. Combining cutting edge technology and human empathy to create the simplest way to give and live.
