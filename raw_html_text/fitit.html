FiTit is a lightweight and flexible platform designed to help easily track fitness plans, from weight training to medical recovery and therapy. FiTit allows for a physician or trainer to prescibe a plan through the fitit.io website, to be loaded on iOS. Recorded data is then sent back and analyzed, allowing for open dialogue and a more personal and beneficial experience.
