Inspiration

As college students with a million things to do and very little free time, we were wishing for a way to schedule our to-do list that is concrete and automatic.

What it does

Takes tasks from a list with estimated times and adds them where there is free time in your calendar. Also, users can collaborate for tasks a group needs to accomplish.

How we built it

TaskIt is an Android app that utilizes the GForge and Google Calendar APIs.
