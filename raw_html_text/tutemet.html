Tutemet

“You can do anything — but not everything” — Keith H. Hammonds

Tutemet is a brain dump extension for VSTS inspired by the work of David Allen of Getting Things Done fame ( http://lifehacker.com/the-value-of-an-end-of-the-week-brain-dump-1740776196 ).

It helps you to stop thinking about work all day and at every moment. At the end of the day or week, type all of your ideas, todos and concerns on the tutemet work hub group. Enjoy your day knowing everything you have to do is in a safe place and pick them up the next time you get to the office.
