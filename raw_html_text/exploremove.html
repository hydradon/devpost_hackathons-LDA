Inspiration

Our goal is to get pre-teens, teens, and adults that have their eyes glued to computer/phone/TV screens all day to go out and about different places. No idea about what to do or where to go? No worries! This website will help you choose something that makes you happy and intrigued to get out and go explore.

What it does

ExploreMore recommends an abundant amount of activities in your local city, lets you personalize a "Bucket List", and offers more options if you dislike an activity idea.

How I built it

This is a website that is built with meteor using the built in mongo database.
