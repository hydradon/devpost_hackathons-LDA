Carbon Dioxide levels are higher now than they have been in the Earth's past 400,000 years. As Carbon Dioxide plays an integral role in Earth's ecosystem - regulating temperature and providing the means to photosynthesis - evaluation of this data from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration can help prepare our species for the future.
