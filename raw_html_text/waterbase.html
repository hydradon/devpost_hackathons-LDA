How we built it

We used Firebase to store data, xcode to develope the ui and GAMA ground water information for water information. ( http://geotracker.waterboards.ca.gov/gama/gamamap/public/ )

Challenges we ran into

We attempted to use Google Cloud Platform to fully service our app, but ran into a significant amount of complications. We attempted to train the GAMA ground water data in order to evaluate the overall quality rating of the water.

What we learned

We learned how to build an ios app using new and chalenging api's frameworks and technologies.
