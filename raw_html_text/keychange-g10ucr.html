Inspiration

With a quick Lightning Decision Jam we explored and developed a product idea as a Group and thought about problems which we observed ourselves.

What it does

keyCHANGE suggestes a variety of professions based on your skills and personality. In addition it directly provides job offers and professional advisors you can connect to.

How we built it

-brainstorm for design ideas -built basic webdesign -implement specific Content ideas from the business guys

Challenges we ran into

-lack of knowledge in programing -team formed without an idea and lost valuable time

Accomplishments that we're proud of

-having a complex business modell, professional plans for further development and user satisfaction -creating an appealing, responsive and intuitive website prototype despite our restricted experience in coding

What I learned

-utilise easy python commands -using Thigma, Trello -about new market opportunities

What's next for keyCHANGE

keyCHANGE is a website educating people about a potential dreamjob by… … categorizing you as a value-driven employee. … suggesting you types of jobs fitting your personality. … displaying job opportunities that lie in your field of interest. … providing success stories from advisors who know what you are going through. … directly connecting users with our advisors.
