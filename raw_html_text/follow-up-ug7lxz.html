What it does

Uses SMS message to gather important information about the patient after they have left the hospital and only escalates the interaction to a call with a healthcare provider when absolutely needed

How we built it

We used open source technologies and APIs such as: Heroku, Node.js, Twilio, RapidPro, FHIR API.
