Inspiration

Art is something that a person gets born with..In the era of technology, where each individual is keen to share their art and work, the privacy and quality of it matters a lot. Recent vulnerabilities related to individual's art and talent inspires us to create a solution and at the same time ensure that their work gets a global exposure.

What it does

What basically the project does, is that : A. It lets the artist publish their work by ensuring that their content get their very own patent. B. it lets the existing companies and market experts, find the suitable talent for their need C. It let people to inspire millions like them, to showcase their love through their passion

How we built it

We build it using:

  1. Front End Frameworks and Languages viz. HTML5 ,CSS3 ,JS ,Jquery. Materialized.Css,
  2. Back End Development: PHP, MySQL, Wamp Server
  3. ML and AI: Twinkle - A Chatbot System to guide the users through

Challenges we ran into

  1. Definitely,Implementing Backend server, localhost and the databases to work in sync with front end was a challenge
  2. Implementing chatbot was a challenge 3.Using appropriate logo, images for front end was a bit challenging
  3. Adding watermark process for each content, took us time

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are definitely proud of the fact that we worked as a team and gave our best effort to implement the solution

What we learned

We learned a lot of things, which would definitely be helping us in the future.
