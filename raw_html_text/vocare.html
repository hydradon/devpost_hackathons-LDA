Inspiration

We were inspired by the idea of volvo and thought it would be cool if you'd only need the phone to register.

What it does

You can register yourself (without the verfication part) and rent a car. While you are driving you can gain points by either cleaning the car or refueling it. Those points will be saved and provide a price reduction for you.

How we built it

For the app, we used React Native since it runs on iOS and Android. For the backend, we used Go.

Challenges we ran into

Our team lacked the knowledge to implement a verification of the car licence so we decided to leave that part out.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Most of the team members have never worked with react-native, but we managed to develop a working prototype.
