Inspiration

We looked at the two problems of fitness and climate change and wanted to combine them into an app related to our daily lives. As Rutgers students we have to commute to most of our classes on the bus to different campuses or take a bike. We wanted to show some of the health and environmental effects of how you choose to commute to class or anything that you need to commute to.

What it does

You enter in a location and based on that location it shows you have long it would take to get there by walking, bike, or car/bus and the effect of the method that you choose such as the calories burned when you choose to take a bike and your carbon footprint when you choose to take the bus or a car. (But none of this works in the current app this was just the plan)
