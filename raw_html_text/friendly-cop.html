Inspiration

Everyday while going somewhere outside, we see problems or crimes being done and some of us want to take some initiatives to solve them.

What it does

The key idea behind this project is a friendly cop application for the people to question the problems whatever they are facing in front of them in society by sending an anonymous report addressing the issue.

Challenges we ran into

However, this is not an easy task as one has to consider the privacy of the anonymous reporter being secured in the process and the issues reported are trustworthy in order to get the problem resolved.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

In conclusion, this application will become the backbone to maintain a highly secured society.
