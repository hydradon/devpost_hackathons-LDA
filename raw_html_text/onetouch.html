What's next for OneTouch

Actually getting a fingerprint scanner and implementing the program. OneTouch will store personal user information, such as annual salary or income, social security, etc. In the long - run, OneTouch will replace the signing of legal documents with fingerprints. An example is purchasing a house or a car; instead of signing a lot of papers and bringing in a multiple documents for proof, OneTouch will pull up all the required information through a fingerprint. This will save time, prevent fraud and identity theft, and make managing your personal information a lot easier. The innovation of OneTouch is exactly where the future is heading, and with OneTouch, we can build and get to that future even faster.
