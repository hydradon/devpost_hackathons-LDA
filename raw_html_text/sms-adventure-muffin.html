Inspiration

We believe that some people would appreciate a shift away from the ultra-immersive experience in gaming today. Take away the audio, take away the 3D, and let your imagination paint the picture while you explore a virtual world.

What it does

You can create a series of many inter-related experiences that people work through as they live the life of a fictitious person. Maybe it's someone in the Wild West. Maybe it's in the Starship Enterprise. Anything is possible.

How we built it

After some initial whiteboarding, goals emerged to simplify what gaming could be. Bring it lo-fi and let people think.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Extremely flexible system to build interrelated hierarchial data.
