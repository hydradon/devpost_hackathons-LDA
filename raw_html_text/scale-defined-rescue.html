• Scale Defined Rescue provides coordination, communication, and tracking for resilient disaster response

Inspiration

In an emergency , conventional communication breaks down just when it’s needed most .

What it does

By using virtual tools for real-time communication , emergency teams, providers , volunteers and the public are coordinated .

How I built it

Using real-time communication technology, AWS, and autocad Forge models and viewer, for visual and voice services that match people with information they have and information they need

Challenges I ran into

Considering how degradation of power or communication availability could be addressed

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Discussions and ideas about the nature of the problem and how to solve it

What I learned

These questions are open for coordinated teamwork
