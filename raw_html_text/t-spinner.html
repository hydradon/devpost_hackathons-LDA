Inspiration

T-Spins are extremely hard to set up in Tetris, and we wanted a way to help newer Tetris players to create them.

What it does

While playing Tetris, our app will suggest quick T-Spin setups based on your current and future pieces. Suggestions are shown by black pieces that if a block is placed in the suggestion, a T-Spin setup is made.

How I built it

JavaScript, HTML, CSS

Challenges I ran into
