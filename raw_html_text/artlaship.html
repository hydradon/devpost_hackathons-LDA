Often fans are at live music gigs and are unable to find out who a band is much less subscribe to their email list or tip the band.

What I'm proposing is a simple web app that delivers the information fans want and nothing else.

On one simple screen I want to provide this functionality.

Artist Name

Artist Bio

Links to other sites

Subscribe to band email list

Tip the band

Stretch goals

Ability to print QR codes that point to the band page that the artist can print on stickers.
