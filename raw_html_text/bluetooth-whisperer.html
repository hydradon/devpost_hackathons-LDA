Inspiration

Headphones always seem to get in your way with all their wires, but I don't want a whole new pair of bluetooth headphones, they're expensive!

What it does

Using the A2DP and AVRCP, the Microchip BM62 module transmits and receives bluetooth audio and commands, and uses an onboard DSP and analog hardware to output headphone audio.

How I built it

We interfaced a bluetooth module, the BM62, with a headphone jack and volume controls.
