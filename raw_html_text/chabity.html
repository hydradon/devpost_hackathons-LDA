Inspiration

We came up with an idea to create the ‘Chabity’ app (Charity + habit + 'y') - an app that allows companies & individuals to create charity events/fundraisers/charity items to users. A user would then pay/bid the product or participate in an event. The money would go directly to the supported charity. The amount of money collected by the seller would appear in their main profile. It's a great way to build public image and create social value.

What it does

We help bring charity projects to life by hopefully reaching people in need of help worldwide.

How I built it

We built it using coding, design and prototyping tools. The entire project is built in Java, JavaScript, and Swift, and Chabity processes everything locally on servers that we created.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

User Experience & User Interface Design went really well. We are proud of incorporating servers and code to our app.

What's next for 'Chabity'

Hopefully developing all the different features we have in mind to further expand the possibilities of our app, and reach as many people as possible.
