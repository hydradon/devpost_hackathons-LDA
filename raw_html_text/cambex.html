Inspiration

Coming from a different country, we always lose money when exchanging our foreign exchange. That way, we decided to create a decentralized currency trading platform with BlockChain. That way, buyers pay less and sellers earn more than they would otherwise.

What it does

It is an exchange trading platform, where you can see everyone selling/buying a specific foreign exchange. We use BlockChain technology to register the money transactions and verify them, having adapted the common algorithms to fit our ideas.

How we built it

With React Native, GCP and Flask. We created the FrontEnd and a Backend REST API.
