This app sets out to create a unique way of tracking and visualizing a person's week based on the amount of time they spend in certain locations.

There is still work being done to extend this application to mobile devices, eliminate redundant code, and create even more insights and visualizations.

Users save specific locations like "home" and "work" and the app takes care of the rest to see how long they are in each location throughout the week.

The coolest part of this project is the opportunity to visualize the data people create through the service to show them their week in new and exciting ways.

I plan to extend this service very soon to mobile apps across platforms to easily track people's locations and gather real-time data.
