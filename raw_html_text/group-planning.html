Ensemble

Planning a group trip is hard.

Between finding interesting group activities, choosing the best options, and making your way around town, the process can be exhausting.

Ensemble is a mobile-first component to the HomeAway website / mobile application that lets you plan the perfect trip with your friends or family.

You can find mutually interesting activities and vote on your favorites, build the ultimate itinerary, and take off.
