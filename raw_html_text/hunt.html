Inspiration

We loved playing the scavenger hunt during Freshers week in Uni. We wanted to create a more complex having an online version of it.

What it does

The player has to find out his next location by including riddles, making the player go through a series of tasks. You can join your friends for a game or join a random one. The user can to create his own game (involving the route and riddles) and invite his friends to join. It is verified wether the player is on the right location both by right location and picture (creator can review the image uploaded).

How we built it

We separated the tasks into backend, frontend API interaction and frontend UI. Each of us was doing their tasks while coordinating with the others creating an effective working environment.

Challenges we ran into

-separating the workload -coming up with the exact solution

Accomplishments that we're proud of

-we finished it

What we learned

That we work great together and each of us learned a new aspect of creating a web app.
