We wrote an application in python and tensorflow to make great image filters. Applies the styles of great artists to your instagram :)

Uses an algorithm and pretrained model described here and here ( http://arxiv.org/abs/1508.06576 , http://www.anishathalye.com/2015/12/19/an-ai-that-can-mimic-any-artist/ )
