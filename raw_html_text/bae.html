Bae is a web-based stock analyzer algorithm that uses FINRA's 20 years worth of stock data along with a series of algorithms to predict the best stocks based on inputs from the user. Using different weighted algorithms, Bae is able to compile the 20 years worth of data into a format that is understandable to end users and output a result based on their needs.
