I've been into automotive since I was very young. Recently I started to buy and sell cars yet found out that major car trading websites are all packed up with list and list of cars and numerous buttons on the side, which looks super "noisy" and by my standard, not user-friendly. Thus I came up with this idea and finally built this website. The core of this idea is to completely give up all buttons and lists in the car-choosing web page; the user will only see one and only one car at a time, clear and with everything they needed to know. Then, the user can choose to either get the car directly, dislike the car so it never shows up again, or rethink so the car is back to the "list", which is on the back-end. Either of these choices will refresh the page and shows up another car.
