Inspiration

Foundation was inspired by our goal of providing the best experience possible for those in need.

What it does

Our web-app connects individuals in need to services in the St. Louis area. Our app has certain tools that accommodate to many needs in order to provide the most effective outcome.

How we built it

Through Google's collaborative software, Editey, we used Twilio, HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap to create our end result.

Challenges we ran into

Twilio was definitely very difficult to comprehend and navigate. We also struggled with creating the most efficient and simple solution to achieve our goal.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The QR code and the language options are two features that we are very proud of.

What we learned

We learned that how great the need is for technology that can improve our society. Also, the importance of teamwork and communication are essential to an amazing result.

What's next for Smart Kookies

We hope to compete in many more hackathons and share our own ideas. We want to develop our talents to become the smartest Kookies out there!
