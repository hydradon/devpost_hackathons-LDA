Inspiration

Having a lively child, fear of a serious him needing an opertaion hospital is a worry that has grown alongside him. The lack of knowledge how such situation are handled, and knowing that I'm not alone with these questions. Was significant part of the inspiration to work on this project.

What it does

It is a concept for an service that uses gamification and real time tracking to relive hospital related anxieties and links together different stakeholders for smoother information transfer and communiation.

How I built it

Design thinking, Photoshop and Blender and the drive of hackathon spirit.

Challenges I ran into

Refining the focus taking account the stakeholder perspectives. Time running out within the large scope.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Clarity of the design that takes acount in different stakeholders with consice layouts and UI flow.

What I learned

Team skills, schedule management and efficient tool workflows.

What's next for Hospital Companion

Pitching with an interested party, prototype development and figuring out the prospects.
