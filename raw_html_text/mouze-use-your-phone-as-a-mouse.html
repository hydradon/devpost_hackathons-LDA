Inspiration

Our trackpad didn't work at the start of our project, and even though we had a mouse, some people might not.

What it does

It connects to a computer and sends messages to control the cursor based on the phone's accelerometer.

How I built it

node.js, css3, js, html5, robot.js

Challenges I ran into

Getting the accelerometer to work smoothly enough

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Sending data from a phone to a computer in realtime

What I learned

How to send data from a phone to a computer in realtime

What's next for Mouze, use your phone as a mouse

Make mouse movement smoother! And, make the desktop UI prettier.
