Inspiration

I wanted to make a game for the Force VR Gauntlet, a device that I made at a previous hackathon

What it does

A game demo demonstrating the Force VR Gauntlet capabilities

How I built it

We first had to sync the Force VR with Unity, which took half the hackathon to do.

Challenges I ran into

Getting the Force VR Gauntlet to work with Unity as well as dealing with the Kinect

What I learned

How to deal with Unity, never underestimate the difficulity of downloading a package, and a little bit of hardware

What's next for VR Feedback Game

Going to try to create a better VR Feedback game at the next hackathon!
