LA Hacks project by Andrew Kan and Jason Lau

Hi, I'm Andrew, a first-year at UCLA, and I built this project with my friend Jason. Anyone who has been on campus has probably seen all our efforts to conserving the environment: 2020 zero food waste goals in the cafeteria, facilities management cars everywhere, and of course, the multi-colored trash bins

I often found myself and others confused as to what bin to put my trash in, even with the nice fancy pictures on the bins. So we created this website using React to test people's knowledge of sorting trash. Take it for a go!
