Inspiration

At Weenat we consider water as one of the 21st century's biggest challenges. Agriculture accounts for 70% of the world water consumption. Smart water use has never been so important for todays farmers.

How it works

By using last RF technologies, strong cloub platform and we design and build our Weenat product :

  • Weenats work autonomously. Nothing to connect. Long-lasting battery life.
  • No hardware to setup. No software to install. Weenat multisensors work right out of the box.
  • Data is collected by your Weenat sensors dozens of times throughout the day, automatically and available 24/7.
  • Weenats use long-range wireless technologies, providing low-energy, globe-spanning network capability.

Challenges I ran into

At Weenat propose products for FARMER & nature related jobs to help them take better decision. Our product allow our customers to turn data into actionnable information by means of use our intuitive decision aided tools on every device from anywhere in the world. Our unique user interface and real time access to data allow our customers to act on the field to improve agriculture operation (reduce chemical product : direct impact on water quality) and optimize irrigation (save water).
