Inspiration

Program for students with serious intellectual disabilities at a team member's (private) high school

What it does

Offers information about issues and legislature pertaining to disability education in public and private schools, and shares resources for parents and others looking to get more involved with special education issues

How we built it

HTML, CSS, Javascript, and love

What's next for great IDEAs

Further development of graphic design (more engaging/interactive) and a more detailed page on how to get involved with and advocate for special education issues.
