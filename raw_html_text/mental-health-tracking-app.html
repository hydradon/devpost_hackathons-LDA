Inspiration

We understand what it's like to go through lows that are really challenging to face on our own, and it would be awesome for a place to vent our feelings to ourselves or to others.

What it does

Our Website provides an anonymous platform to post your about your current mental state. You can log and keep track of your mental state over a period of time

How we built it

Firebase to keep track of the user data and authentication, and used css/javascript to design the front end.

Challenges we ran into

None of us were experienced in any of these new technologies, so Firebase was a completely new challenge

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We got firebase working!!!

What we learned

Build a website that was able to use user data and how to design front end work

What's next for Mental Health Tracking App

To design an ai bot that will match up your post with others going through a similar "drop" in mood so you can read their posts and see how they overcame their low points
