Inspiration

We wanted to help Wyomingites take advantage of the 5 pro-blockchain laws that are now in effect.

What it does

The Secretary of State now has a ledger of corporate blockchain addresses.

How we built it

We used React, Node, and Mongodb to aggregate incorporation data and generate public wallet addresses for businesses.

Challenges we ran into

Different blockchains have different APIs, what works with one doesn't necessarily work with another.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The number of bounties/challenges we were able to incorporate into our solution.

What we learned

Wyoming is the place to incorporate if your company wants to conduct business on the blockchain.

What's next for Electronic Corporate Formation Portal

Expand functionality to facilitate token transactions between Wyoming companies.
