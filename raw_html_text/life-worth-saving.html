Inspiration

Suicide is an epidemic that is spreading across the nation and we wanted to stop people from committing suicide

What it does

This website raises awareness for suicide prevention

How we built it

We used a google site as a base platform for our website and then embedded html/javascript applets into the website

Challenges we ran into

Figuring out how to embed javascript into the website

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Embedding a map into the website and a subscription feature that sends a confirmation email to you when you sign up for the newsletter

What we learned

We learned how to use javascript, html5, and css to perform various functions in our website

What's next for Life Worth Saving

To add more centers to the map and integrate a phone number and direct sms communication with anyone who needs help
