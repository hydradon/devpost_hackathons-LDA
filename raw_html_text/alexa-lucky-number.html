Inspiration

I did an inspirational skill before. I thought it will be interesting to incorporate that with Interactive Adventure Game Skill template.

What it does

You will be asked to open one of the three boxes presented. Depends on the boxes choosen, you will be informed if you win or lose. If the box is locked, you will be asked to open one of the other two boxes. If you win, you will be given an inspiration quotes for kids.

How I built it

I use the Interactive Adventaure Game Skill template.
