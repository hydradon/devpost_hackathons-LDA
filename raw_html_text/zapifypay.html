Inspiration

When one goes shopping, time is normally wasted through long lines, sorting out money, and technical difficulties at the cash register's place. We wanted to create a hands free paying system where you don't need to hold or give anything out. This process can reduce frustration at lines and save time for shoppers.

What it does

It allows for contactless payment faster and easier than ever before.

How we built it

We built the application on three platforms: website, iPhone, and iPad.

Challenges we ran into

Understanding new concepts for the first time. The geotracking and bluetooth aspect was the most difficult as it involves careful programming. In addition, the hardware aspect of the DragonBoard was very time consuming.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were proud of being able to incorporate DragonBoard into our project.

What we learned

We learned a lot in a 24 hour period.

What's next for ZapifyPay

We hope it influences the future of spending.
