Inspiration

Through our research, we found out that it is very difficult for students to find their placement in coop/internship opportunities, as they find it hard to connect with different companies. This inspired us to come up with a app that connects employers and students.

What it does

This app is a central hub where employers can find potential coop/internship seeking students, and vice versa. This app will target specific students (from high school or post secondary) and it will help them find a perfect job. With this win/win situation, companies will be able to satisfy their needs, while students gain quality experience.
