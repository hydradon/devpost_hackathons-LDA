Inspiration

The amount of injuries/deaths that occur each year in the US due to distracted driving

What it does

Streams relevant info from the user's phone to a HUD to keep the driver focused on the road

How I built it

Raspberry-pi, Swift, Python, RedBull, Good Music, and Apple products

Challenges I ran into

Everything from interfacing between iOS and the pi, to figuring out how to transfer data across a multithreaded application.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Finishing the project
