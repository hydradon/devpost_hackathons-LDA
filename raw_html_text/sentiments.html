Inspiration

You know when you're in a horrible mood, and you can't seem to get out of it? Me neither. Sentiment is here to help with that.

What it does

Sentiments is a self care and mental health tracking app. It works by prompting you to choose an emotion, and then gives you a short mood-boosting prompt in response.

We used facial expressions, rather than words to convey emotional categories, because sometimes you can't put how you are feeling into words.

After your mood boosting prompt comes a daily log portion. Here you're able to log the things you're grateful for, a memorable quote from the day, put a few sentences (or paragraphs if you want!) about how your day went, insert a photo, and set your intention for the day.

Sentiments aims to help those struggling with mental health by reminding them that a bad day is just a bad day. Nothing more.

How we built it

Illustrations are done on ProCreate (iPad), UXUI/coding done on XCode/Swift
