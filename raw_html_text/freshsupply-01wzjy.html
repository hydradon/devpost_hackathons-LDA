Inspiration

We are inspired by the lack of trust towards contractors (and even to the customer invisible subcontractors)

What it does

Our app allows you to find the best logistic company fulfilling your quality standards

How we built it

We built it using a PHP server connecting to Daimler to access IoT sensor data feeding our iOS mobile App with the necessary information to create contracts.

Challenges we ran into

Defining boundaries and goals of our product. Also deciding between the use cases, as we had multiple ones.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Finishing in time and managing the loss of teammates

What we learned

How to structure a Hackathon

What's next for FreshSupply

Scale the product Add more features e.g. Blockchain
