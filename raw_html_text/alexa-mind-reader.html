Alexa -- Mind Reader

Can Alexandra The Great read YOUR mind? Pick any card from a standard deck and send positive vibes as she attempts to name it.

Watch the video first to see Mind Reader in action: https://youtu.be/fES-mUD8LjY

Inspiration

Remote telepathy.

What it does

Guesses any card from a standard deck by asking a couple of questions.

How I built it

Using ASK examples for reference. This was an initial skill to test out and learn the Alexa dev platform.

Challenges I ran into

Understanding utterances/intents; Debugging the code but there are some creative techniques you can use.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

First skill certified on the platform.

What I learned

The Alexa skill development and certification processes.
