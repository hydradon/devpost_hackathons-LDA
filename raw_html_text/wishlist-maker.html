Inspiration

We wanted to make an app for the holidays to help people organize the presents they want for the holidays.

What it does

It allows users to make a three-item wish list.

How we built it

We used Xcode in swift to build the app.

Challenges we ran into

We're beginners to app making so it took us a while to figure out how to do all of it.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It's our first app.

What we learned

The things that are seemingly the most simple actually can take a lot of complex thought and action.

What's next for Wishlist Maker

Add a database and network for people to share their wish lists for the holidays.
