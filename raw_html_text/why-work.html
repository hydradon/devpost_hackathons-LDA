Inspiration

A lot of the time, work takes the life out of me. I wonder why I keep at it. My productivity sinks to an all time low. But I have this set of phrases I use to remind myself why work is important. So I decided to make an Alexa skill out of it.

What it does

When you start the skill, it gives you a quip about why work is important and why you should persevere even if it seems tiresome. This simple life-hack helps me keep my productivity up.
