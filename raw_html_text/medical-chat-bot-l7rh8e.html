First Aid Chatbot

This is a chatbot that was inspired by my knowledge of First Aid and wanting to help those in need who have a loved one/someone they know in a medical emergency without prior knowledge of First Aid. Many medical emergency deaths could be prevented if the bystander knew some of knowledge of First Aid, so this chatbot can help to guide them through the steps needed for each emergency.
