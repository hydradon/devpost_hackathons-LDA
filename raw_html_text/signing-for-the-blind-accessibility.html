Inspiration

As technology has grown, we've often ignore Accessibility for the unfortunate, after meeting a doctor who's turning blind, I want to build more tools to help them, docusign being one of them.

What it does

It activates from google assistant to the app which helps to blind to sign documents

How I built it

Using Android, Google Cloud, Docusign and salesforce

Challenges I ran into

Trying to get touch events on Android

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Have a full functional demo

What I learned

Docusign goes very well with Salesforce

What's next for Signing for the Blind Accessibility

Intergrate and push the app to the market
