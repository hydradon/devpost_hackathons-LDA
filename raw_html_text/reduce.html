Inspiration:

Large amount of daily waste that everyone creates is shocking. In California,each person disposes 4.5 pounds/day in 2014. In order to solve this environmental problem, we develop rEDUce to educate future generations and make reducing a habit.

What it does:

1.Collect user input through our website in order to track students’ daily disposal of waste 2.Statistical analysis featuring daily or periodical waste disposed in student’s view 3.Ranking available in teacher’s view Teacher shows a part of the result and provides incentive in order to motivate student to reduce waste
