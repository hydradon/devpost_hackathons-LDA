Introducing Live Bible readings and Sharing as a Post Status Updates on Facebook for growing Communities of Christians

Bible API used: http://bibliaapi.com/docs/

FaceBook has over one billion Users who are Christians. How do we engage those Christians in a way to foster more collaborations in living a good Life and sharing discussions about God. We introduce Live Bible Updates.

All Christians on Facebook can now read Bible live, share God's good news with one another and post Bible Content as post updates to timeline directly on Facebook. This will create highest engagement ever in the history of Christians Communities on Facebook.....

Building Growing Communities For Christians.

God is the Creator of the whole Universe and Bible is the Center of Christians Faith in Christ Jesus. About 70% of Facebook Users are Christians. This app will unify all Christian's Community on Facebook into one entity. Pastors, Rev. Fathers, Pope, Evangelist, Lay People can now read and Share Word of God live on Facebook and this will in turn create the highest engagement ever in the history of mankind on Facebook.....

How to use this app:

1.) Login using Facebook
2.) Enter Post Title, Category and Descriptions
3.) On the Bible Section, Select any version of Bible and Chapters that you want to read live. After reading, you can share and post on your timeline as Status Updates. 4.) User can Like and comment on the Post
5.)The User can Share any Post to her FaceBook Timeline as well as to her specified Facebook Friends in HIPAA Compliance Mode.

FaceBook Technology Used:

1.) Facebook Login Graph API: Used to authenticate Users
2.) Facebook Shared Button: used to allow user to share content to her Facebook Timeline
3.) Facebook Send Dialog Button: used to allow user to share content to a specific user in HIPAA Compliance Mode.
