speakout

This site collects information from users about the content of positive or negative social identity interactions with other people. The information is a post about what happened, the content of the interaction, tags describing the nature of the interaction and a spot on google maps of where the interaction occurred. You need to log in and state your identity before you can make any posts. We were planning on implementing more of the stats page and upvoting for the posts but we didn't get to it because of time constraints.
