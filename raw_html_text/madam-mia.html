Inspiration

Arisan is one of culture from Indonesia. It is like Rotating Savings and Credit Association. Usually people doing Arisan by forming a group and gathering together in the leader's home. With Madam Mia, she can help Arisan leaders to manage their Arisan online, they can invite others from another state too to join the Arisan.

What it does

It helps Arisan leaders to manage their group online. The bot will recreate the usual things that happen when doing Arisan.

How I built it

I built it using Facebook Messenger Platform.

Challenges I ran into

I have some problem to integrate the bot earlier.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I found the idea and built it myself.

What I learned

I learn more about Facebook Messenger Platform. I also learn more how to find an idea that can make impact for a community.
