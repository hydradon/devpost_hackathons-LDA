Inspiration

I built an arcade machine over the summer and had to spend a lot of needless money purchasing tools for woodworking.

What it does

Allows users to register their powertools for renting to make money, instead of just having their powertools sit in their garage

How we built it

Separated modules for backend and frontend. Used vue.js and es6 modules and components to keep with current conventions. Node.js, express, and vue.js

Challenges we ran into

We tried using loopback but we gave up after a long time. we switched to just express

Accomplishments that we're proud of

User interface and experience. Bing maps integration.

What we learned

If something doesn't work stop before you waste too much time on it

What's next for ToolSeek

We want to finish the prototype and see how it may do by asking opinions
