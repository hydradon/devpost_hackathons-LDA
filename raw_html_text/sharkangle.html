Inspiration

Sharks are in danger in most parts of the world, with a quarter of all sharks and rays now threatened with extinction. We try to do something for shark and make some contributions to shark conservation and management.

What it does

The iSharkInfo takes an interactive process. Users only need to take a standard photo, select a proportional scale and choose a few points on the fish body, iSharkInfo will automatically analyze the information and tell you the shark body length.
