PINIT

_ Personal indoor navigation and indoor tracker _

The App target industrial innovation and sustainable cities & communities. We want to optimize citizens mobility and help them to reach their destination\ objectives more efficiently and accurately. The android app help citizens to identify locations within buildings on floor level. Citizen mark their start position and destination and the application will generate shortest path for navigation.
