The 6 of us met randomly this weekend, and are from different backgrounds The common problem in our group is expensive housing. Only way is to get a roommate. We all have roommate horror stories on the topics of theft, uncleanliness, and overall compatibility. The worst part is, we have no way to be sure that my next roommate will be any better How do you pick who you will trust for a minimum of one year, with your personal belongings and more importantly, your peace of mind Our solution SPACEMATE is a centralized platform that leverages different technologies such as AI, UX, UI for finding you the most compatible roommate options Our competition exists but they are non-intuitive database dumps, where user does most of the work Features Simplified Tinder-like matching system Profile Verification AI Recommendations, using supervised learning Dispute Resolution/ Corrective behavior strategies Monthly Review/On-going feedback - social proof
