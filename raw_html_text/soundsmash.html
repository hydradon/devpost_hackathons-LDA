Inspiration

I found inspiration for my idea when i realized how theres all these sound board apps that all do the same thing. They don't show u easily how to replay that amazing song/beat..

What it does

My IOS app uses amazing HD pictures as the tiles that your brain will remember and sound tiles that u can customize with the on board speaker.

How I built it

I used Sprite kit

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Im proud that i learn how to use the microphone to save custom audio and use it in a cool way

What I learned

I learn even more objective c and better ideas for sprite kit in the future.
