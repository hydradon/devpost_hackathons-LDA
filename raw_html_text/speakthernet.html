The FBI is in your phone and the CIA is in your microwave - but you wouldn't know it, because you can't hear the signals they're secretly beaming to spy satellites miles overhead - until now!

Speakthernet keeps you in the loop about every piece of data sent by your computer - and it's so intuitive, anyone can use it! Each and every bit is read out loud for your careful review as it's being sent over the air, literally! Advanced voice recognition technology inside the base station gives you industry-leading speeds of 458,000,000+ picobytes per second!
