Inspiration

Back in the day, there used to be a problem of having lack of access to content. More crucially, there was no way to access all resources on a single platform. With search engines and the internet, however, we often find too many links with too much content and no idea on where to start.

Too much content is an issue especially for the self-learner who has limited time - think college students, working adults.

What it does

Allig8r allows you to enter what you want to learn about and how much time you have available. Our app will generate an automatically curated list of links using our algorithm that will allow you to learn within time! These links will be beginner-friendly and easily-accessible to new learners.
