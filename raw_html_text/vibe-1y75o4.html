Inspiration

We wanted a music platform that worked for everyone in any mood and did not require the hassle of searching for thousands of songs to make the perfect playlist.

What it does

It allows you to categorize music into different moods and then curates playlists based the moods of being happy, sad, energetic and calm.

What's next for Vibe

We plan on implementing a facial recognition machine learning module where mood can automatically be determined to make it even more user friendly for the user to enjoy their music based on their mood.
