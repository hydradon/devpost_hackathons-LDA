Inspiration

As web designers it’s our job to turn a client’s goals into beautiful, intuitive, conversion optimized designs. This is easy enough when a client is able to communicate what they like and don’t like, but very often we’ll get vagaries like “I don’t like it” or “It looks too aggressive”. It’s hard to take feedback like that back to Photoshop and adjust the design to resolve those complaints. What if instead we could easily modify our designs during meetings and get immediate feedback on our work?

What it does

Alexa Site allows designers to edit the visual design of their websites with simple voice commands. For example you can say things like:

"Alexa, left align the text on the page"

And she’ll make the change on your website.

How I built it

Alexa Site uses:

  • Amazon Alexa Skills Kit
  • Amazon Lambda Functions
  • Internal Squarespace API
