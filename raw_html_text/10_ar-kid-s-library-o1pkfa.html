Inspiration

Problem Definition

*Parents who have work or study, they face difficulties to find the right place to look after their children during their absence.

*The difficulty of knowing the nearby schools and what is their educational programs.

  • Do not know the tuition fees and accompanying services (uniforms, buses, meals).

*Lack of sufficient information on the quality of the school's educational programs.

  • Registration opportunities for the schools are missing due to lack of knowledge of its registration dates.

  • Lack of communication data for the schools.

Proposed Solution Design and develop an application that helps to:

*Helping parents to find a safe environment to take care of their children while they busy.

*Make it easier for them to find schools that close to their home or work.

  • Introducing them by the programs provided by the schools.

  • Defining tuition fees and accompanying services.

  • Show pictures of classrooms and public utilities.

*Inform them of the assessment of other beneficiaries of the schools and their feedback on the services provided.

  • Inform them of the registration dates on the schools and available seats.

  • Enable them to communicate with schools to inquire.

Note: we mean by ( Schools ) in Inspiration: Pre-primary schools (nursery, pre-school and kindergarten)
