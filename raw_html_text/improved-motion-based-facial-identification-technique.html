Inspiration

FaceID on iPhones is super powerful but it is certainly a newer feature that only works for newer cameras and versions of iOS. I wanted to create something that works for much earlier versions of iOS and relies less on TrueDepth cameras or other newer hardware features.

What it does

The mobile app is essentially a gesture checker, in which you can turn, tilt, smile, etc. to complete a series of randomized gestures. After you complete these gestures, you are done authenticating yourself!
