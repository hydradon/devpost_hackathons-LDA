Help your brave explorers on their mission to find new settlements on a strange new planet. Our game follows the journey of these tiny spaceman. Our explorers need your help to navigating a safe passage. You do this by rotating 3D blocks to find the safe path for them. Your help is crucial to their future and ability to colonise!

We built our game in Unity and used MagicaVoxel to create the art assets and animations. All built in mind for Google Cardboard VR.

The team worked so well together! Moving forward with the project for release is the obvious decision! Its a great game and has so much potential. Looking at VR and in our example Google Cardboard I feel the barriers to entry are lowered with this tec.
