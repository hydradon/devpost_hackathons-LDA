Inspiration

Ever find yourself getting a new pair of shoes and not fitting even though it was the same size as your last shoe? That's because a lot of brands have their shoe sizes in different measurements in the actual inches.

What it does

Our app takes the brand and size of your current shoe and converts it into the users new desired brand of shoe.

How I built it

We used Android Studios with Java

Challenges I ran into

Deciding on the actual idea of what we were going to do. We ran into challenges of the design of the app and deciding what it was going to look like

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We have 19 popular brands of shoes including 3 men specific brands and 3 women specific brands that you can choose to convert from

What I learned

We learned how the functions of android studios and importing java into it.

What's next for Fyttr

Getting more brands to convert from and other utilities for the app
