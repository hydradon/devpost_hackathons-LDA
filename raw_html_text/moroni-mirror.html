Inspiration:

Wanting to add positivity to the users day day Moroni Wilson voiced the Magic Mirror in Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs

What does it do:

Captures the users emotion based on facial expression.

Displays: Date and time, upcoming calendar events, a daily message (ex: Hello, beauty), a news ticker (user may choose the news source).

How we built it:

Raspberry-Pi, old monitor, Old picture frame, privacy mirror film, cardboard boxes

Challenges we ran into:

Configuring various programs to work together.

Accomplishments we're proud of:

Interfacing different platforms such as software and hardware.

What we learned:

We learned a lot about the MagicMirror platform

What's next for Moroni Mirror:

Voice recognition, gesture control and touch screen
