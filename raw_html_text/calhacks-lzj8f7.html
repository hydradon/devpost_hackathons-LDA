Tactile game controller is an android application that allows users to play their favorite video games using their android phones as controllers, while not sacrificing the aesthetic beauty of a real controller. Thanks to the implementation of Tanvas, users are able to feel the resistance of a controller's buttons on their screens. This is helpful since it allows them to actually find their buttons by feeling the resistance on the screen, and provides a more satisfying controller experience overall, since the screen does not have uniform resistance, and makes the buttons stand out. The users are also able to modify the positions of the buttons to fit their needs.
