Inspiration

Everyday I walk past Enarc and was in awe of his ability to move things.

What it does

Allows the average UTD student, et al., to move boxes from the ground to the top of a construction site, as well as allows for automation of the process so as to function as a desktop background in wallpaper-engine.

How I built it

Javascript and html

Challenges I ran into

Learning Javascript's canvas ability

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The automation of the process became a major feature as it allows for a passive desktop background that slowly counts up

What I learned

Anything is possible with Enarc
