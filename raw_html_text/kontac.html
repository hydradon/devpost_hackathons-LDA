Inspiration

Say you go to a party or conference, you meet cool people and you want to keep them in contact, what do you do? You exchange phone numbers and forget about it the next day. You try looking them up on facebook but no luck. This is where Kontac comes in.

What it does

This app let you share your contact informations with people you meet. Simply type in your info, pick what you want to share and generate the QR code. You can share your facebook profile, Instagram, phone number, snapchat, linkedIn, and many more via QR code. Scan the code and save their contact infos!
