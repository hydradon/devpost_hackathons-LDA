PicBreaker

Uses the Microsoft Bot Framework

Interactive ice breaker with photos.

Icebreaker games, for the most part, have involved verbal or with writing activities of some sort. However, with the rise of chat bot technology and computer vision we thought this would be the perfect opportunity reimagine how icebreaker games are conducted for orientation events at campus clubs among others.

Organizers can upload photos with parts of it missing while participants have to physically find out where the photos are taken and complete the missing part. Under the hood, the backend uses computer vision and structural similarity index to compare participants' submissions against the original photo.
