Inspiration

If it's smart phone, the time of an accident should be able to do colleague's rescue support.

What it does

The safety contact by which a colleague is the person himself from smart phone in case of emergency. I'd like to make them notify a company through the internet easily.

How I built it

The safety notice function is added to a business software of existence.

Challenges I ran into

Even if the internet is overcrowded at the time of an accident, I'd like to enable notice reservation automatically.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The safety information which preserved reservation is uploaded automatically at the time of the internet recovery.

What I learned

Even combination of easy mechanism depends on an idea, and the wonderful effect can be shown.

What's next for i RESCUE

Securement of more than one communication tool and follow-up of rescue support according to the safety situation
