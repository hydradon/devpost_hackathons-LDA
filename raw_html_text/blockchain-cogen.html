• CoGen

Inspiration

With legal contracts you always have the danger, that one party does not fulfill their duties. This is why many court proceedings are necessary. We wanted to create self-enforceable legal contracts, so that duties and rights are executed automatically.

What it does

Transform any kind of legal contract into smart contracts.

How I built it

Smart Contract build on the Ethereum Blockchain with Solidity. Web3JS as interface between the back- and frontend. With HTML and CSS for the Frontend.

Challenges I ran into

Generation of a understandable use case.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Fully functional Dapplication.

What I learned

Legal Stuff, Smart Contract.

What's next for [Blockchain] CoGen

To make it generic, for every kind of legal contract.
