Inspiration

Doctors often do not receive feedback from patients after their visit. We come up with a prototype that helps doctors receive patients' feedback regularly and provides them treatment data and outcomes to improve their decision making.

What it does

The system includes a mobile app for patients to gather feedback and a web based dashboard for doctors, clinics, GPs, specialists, dentists and hospitals to get the quantified patients' conditions, treatment effects, as well as drug effectiveness. Medical workers can use this system to get more information from their patients; hospitals can use it to figure out their best doctors; medical research centres can use it to quickly find out the most effective drug.
