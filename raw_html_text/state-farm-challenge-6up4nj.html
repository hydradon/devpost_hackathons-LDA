Inspiration

Previous members from my team have partaken in the State Farm challenge previously at different hackathons and really liked and appreciated the way they do their challenges and so we wanted to do it again because we had such a good experience last time.

What it does

It builds onto the already existing State Farm app to help speed up and optimize the process along with analyzing the system of filing claims with insurance in a shorter period of time. In adition, we are creating a rewards system in which the environmentally aware State Farm customers are, we will in return donate to a charity of their choosing since they are using either less electricity, gas, or producing less waste.

How we built it

Created a mock up prototype of the app that shows how our design can be implemented and work in theory.

Challenges we ran into

Finding a way to combine augmented reality with a superior customer experience.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Creating a functional prototype of our app. Coming up with new, creative ideas that we believe have real potentional to be implemented in the real life.

What we learned

We learned how to view problems and issues through the viewpoints of both the customers as well as the companies.

What's next for State Farm Challenge

Develop further augmented reality and machine learning so that the camera can better recognize and analyze the situation.
