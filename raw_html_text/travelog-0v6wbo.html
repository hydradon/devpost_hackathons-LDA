Inspiration

Reading Travel Blogs take too long and it's hard to recreate others' itinerary based on very specialized blog entries. We want to make blogs more searchable by tourist locations so that it's easier and more possible for people to read short, informative blogs and be able to recreate trips. Writing Blogs take too long? You have to upload the picture, write the time and location and the detailed activity on each image/event? We take care of everything automatically. By generating locations and time automatically and creating a automatic travel timeline, all you need to do is to choose a category for your event (do/eat/sleep) and write a few sentences (if you want) to complete your blog and there you go! Share it with other people and save your travel stories online!
