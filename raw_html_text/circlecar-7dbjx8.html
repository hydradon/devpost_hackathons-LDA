Inspiration

We've been inspired by the challenging task and making something completely different from what we naturally do. Furthermore on our way to St. Gallen we realized that it is really a service we lack.

What it does

An easy and comfortable way to rent a car. That includes sharing the key to the car, paying for the car services and setting all services.

How we built it

Prototyped and built the necessary model, implemented base app.

Challenges we ran into

We were unfamiliar with the available car rent/sharing system in Switzerland. We worked in quite an unfamiliar field for us,

Accomplishments that we're proud of

That we are here for the second time. And we don't want to stop!
