PebblePositive

Inspiration

With all the negativity in the world these days, our team thought it would be nice to have a little sprinkle of positivity in your day.

What it does

The app is your positive companion that will keep you motivated throughout the day.

PebblePositive integrates a life philosophy of positive reinforcement and an appreciation of physiological dynamism to customize automated inspirational messages for the user. PebblePositive sends messages at strategic times throughout the day, based on the regular biorhythm and hormone levels.

Pebble Positive also allows the creation of smart alarms, which gently brings the user out of the sleep cycle to ensure a well and rested human being.

How we built it

The Pebble Time app was built using Pebble.js in the CloudPebble development environment.

Challenges we ran into

Pebble.js is still in beta and there is very little document on the framework. There is significantly less functionality available through Pebble.js compared to Pebble C, which as proven to be a extremely difficult to work around. There were also connectivity issues.

Accomplishments that we are proud of

The Accelerometer functionality created a better user experience and allowed for the detection of the human being's behaviour.

What we learned

We learnt the ins and outs of Pebble.js and the nuances that goes with it.

What's next for Pebble Positive

Pebble Positive is not just intended to be an app, but a framework to build upon a positive lifestyle. Smartstraps can be included to create a more accurate reading of the human being's mental state. With those features set, the possibility of gamification of different aspects of life can create better user response and conditioning.
