Inspiration

I was inspired by Apple Music radio. There're a lot of radios with top 100 in various types and styles ranging from traditional rock music to world pop. But when I was curious to search the top10 music in the states or even other countries. I couldn't even search for that. So I just made this website to search top 10 music world wide. To make it cooler, I am using map data service to get the country information.

How it works

  1. Search countries you want to know with search bar or just click on the land of the country in map;
  2. The top 10 music playlist will appear. Using the iTunes API to request the most downloaded music songs data. Store them into the mysql database.
  3. Click on the one you would like to listen to.
  4. Enjoy the song!
