We were genuinely inspired by how uninvolved the youth are in politics. We wanted to make politics appealing to younger people. And a game where you blow up candidates is a great way to do this! Because who doesn't like blowing up stuff?? Also we're just all for Bernie.

You play as Bernie the dragon and you shoot candidates that come flying at you with fire.

We built it in spritebuilder and Xcode.
