Left is an iOS app that lets you search for recipes that use the ingredients you have "left." Simply input your ingredients of choice and press "Get recipes" to see results.

Motivations

As a college student, it's hard sometimes to figure out what to do with leftover groceries. We don't want to throw them away, but we also have no idea what to cook!

Solution

Left allows you to search for recipes based on the ingredients you provide. With over 200,000 recipes available, you can cook anything you want using everything you have.

Found a recipe you really liked? Add it to your favorites so you can make it anytime without having to search again, or share it with your friends!

Challenges

This was my first iOS application, so I built it at the same time I was learning app development in Swift. The most challenging part of the project was learning how to use APIs to get recipe information, as well as learning how to handle networking for a smooth experience throughout the app.
