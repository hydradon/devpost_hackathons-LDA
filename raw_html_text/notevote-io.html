Inspiration

A more productive and efficient way to take notes in class, benefiting all students.

What it does

Allows groups of students to create a shared notebook, add sections to the notebook, and all add their own notes to a section. Each student can then vote on the other notes in that section. When viewing a notebook, the highest voted notes for each section will be displayed in the front. Students can then download these notes, or any other notes from each section that they feel are the best.
