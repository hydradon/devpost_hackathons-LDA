What inspired us

We were inspired to create this app after realizing the struggles of choosing a right playlist for the perfect workout songs to listen to

What it does

WorkoutTimer is an program that allows users to generate custom playlists based on what workout you're doing, based on the times of your workout.

How we built it

Java, Android Studio

Challenges we ran into

Windows/Mac connectivity issues

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Making a material design playlist (Jason), getting a parser completed within 12 hours (Serena)

What we learned

How to material design an app as well as improving Android Studio skills in general

What's next for WorkTimer

Implementation for mobile devices for an official release
