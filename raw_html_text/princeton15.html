Doc Louis's Training Program ( Dance like a fly, bite like a mosquito )

Doc Louis's Training Program is your personal coach who's mission is to make sure that you do not have to remember the exact details of your workout. Simply type in your workout program, and the coach will let you know what step to do, when and for how long.
