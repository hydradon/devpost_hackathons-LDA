What it does

It's a utility lottery system that motivates households to reduce their carbon footprint through reducing hydro and water usage. Eligible households that meet specified requirements will be entered into a draw for prizes. However, a $2 entry fee is mandatory for entry. Terms: italics Green Button Connect - Utility companies with consent can send utility data to third party apps. italics Energy Threshold - Basically comparing your household to other households of similar structure/situation. Lottery Rules (Guaranteed winner per month): Reducing your household's carbon footprint (compared to the previous month) will earn you draws. Get entries depending on which carbon footprint level you fall under. Get 2 extra draws by being the most improved household in your community each month. For each consecutive month below the energy benchmark (average carbon footprint ranking for households like yours), your entries will double.
