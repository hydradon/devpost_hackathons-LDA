Inspiration

News on social media is shared on a daily basis. In fact, for many users, social media sites are their only interactions with new. After reading the description of someone’s views or takeaways of an article, many users do not read the article, but they still form an opinions. Fake news detections and measures of companies like Facebook cannot address this issue. As a result, fake news can be essentially generated in the form of uninformed opinions propagating their ideas.
