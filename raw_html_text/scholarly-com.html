This site is designed to bring together all of the resources students need to keep themselves organized during their busy time in school. This website provides several personalized services for students including a centralized agenda, calendar, and GPA calculator.

The inspiration for this ORIGINAL website stemmed from the fact that a lot of students in high school are disorganized. Having one website for logging your homework, calculating your GPA etc. keeps students more organized and therefore more productive.

Attached are images of our website.

P.S. - We had difficulties integrating the GPA calculator into the site because there were too many differences between school grading systems to make the calculator truly universal.
