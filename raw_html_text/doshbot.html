Inspiration

AI, Bots, Blockchain. Ensure you always get the best deal based on your personality insights.

What it does

Doshbot, AI Assistant to help you save money or get better deals.

Simple App that integrates and pulls in your bank spending “transactions”, GPS location and mix them with your emotional tones, social propensities, and writing styles from your social activities - to help you save money or get better recommendations.

The system also ensures that only genuine best price reduction offers are received using an inbuilt reputation system ensuring positive user experience & higher satisfaction levels.

How we built it

The AI agent detects your emotional tones, social propensities, and writing styles in your communication through Facebook, Twitter and other channels. Then the agent analyses, classifies and filters your banking transaction history. The user can see all transactions with attached recommendations and selects one to be sent to local businesses for bidding to reduce the bill. But we don't and will never share your identity! Business can filter real time transaction and bid on your profile.
