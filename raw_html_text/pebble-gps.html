Inspiration

We are tired of pulling out our phones every 2 minutes to check the time and distance left to our destinations!

What it does

It returns the distance and time from your current location to your destination. The data is being constantly updated to keep the distance and time accurate.

How we built it

We used javascript.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned
