Inspiration: Huge difference in volatility between day-ahead and real-time power markets. For example, the approx. value of selling on the most profitable market in all zones in PJM territory is $650 million/year:

What it does: Use Machine Learning to better predict pricing.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of: Teamwork

What I learned: Differences between day-ahead and real-time markets
