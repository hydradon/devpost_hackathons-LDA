• SheCanTech

Inspiration

Women around the world are becoming more and more interested in STEM. Most women aren't sure where to start. Many women don't have the resources. And others would like to connect with like-minded individuals. Introducing SheCanTech.org.

What it does

SheCanTech.org strives to provide a global social media platform, in which to connect women of all backgrounds who are either involved or pursuing roles in tech. SheCanTech gives women an outlet for expression, showcasing their work, seeking employment, getting assistance, and connecting with other professionals in the space.
