Inspiration

The phone case makes up a large segment of the accessories market. People buy several phone cases each year to match their outfits. We wanted to reduce the amount of waste produced by creating one device that people could alter to match their mood.

What it does

It is a phone case that has a screen inside that allows for the user to alter the image displayed on the phone case.

How we built it

We built it with a raspberry pi.

Challenges we ran into

Bulkiness due to outdated version of the raspberry pi.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Creating a working prototype that perfectly embodies our inspiration of the ever changing chameleon.

What we learned

How to use the raspberry pi, create a phone case

What's next for Chameleon Phonecase

Make the phone case less bulky, consume less energy, increase brightness, and find materials that are more sustainable.
