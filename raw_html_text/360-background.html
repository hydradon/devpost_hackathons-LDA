Inspiration

I have seen a lot of AR, VR apps so i wanted to make similar app for Instagram and Facebook. For this reason, i made a filter.

What it does

360 Background is an Instagram and Facebook Filter created by using Spark AR.

How I built it

I have used Spark AR for creating the filter and Blender for 3D sphere model.

Challenges I ran into

Textures were very large for Instagram and Facebook limit so i tried to compress in high quality. If there is more space for these filters, i will add more background environments.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

In a very limited time, i have learned Spark AR features and i created a filter.

What I learned

I have used Spark AR for first time. I learned most of it's features.

What's next for 360 Background

I will try to add more image without losing quality.
