Inspiration

I asked my partner for ideas for the project. She said "Let's make Flappy Bird, but make it crappier." Then we laughed. Next thing I know, we were breaking down the logic for Flappy Bird, finding sprites for a turd and toliets and toliet paper, and finding a perfect IDE.

What it does

It's a phone app game where you control a turd and dodge toliet paper.

How we built it

We used Android Studio and Java to create the mobile app.

Challenges we ran into

We had trouble creating DetectCollision. The player never detected the enemy, even when they touched.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We made an app.
