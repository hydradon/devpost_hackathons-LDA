Inspiration

In hopes to see more women in the STEM field we want to create a less hostile environment for women to contribute their input in tech industry and see their ideas grow into fruition.

What it does

A social network platform that creates small group with commitment to make dreams into reality.

How we built it

Using Android Studio with cloud Firestore database.

Challenges we ran into

Coding challenges. Unexpected bugs.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We had ambitious goals and weren't afraid to challenge ourselves.

What we learned

Simple algorithm can be quite difficult to implement.

What's next for iDeaFactory

Continuation for promoting for women in the STEM field.
