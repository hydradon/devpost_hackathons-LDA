Inspiration

A client needed to pull in rich reports into Confluence and Tableau was their tool of choice.

What it does

Makes it easy for non technical staff to quickly and easily embed reports from Tableau into Confluence. This allows team members to bring greater exposure and support an open company ethos.

How I built it

Using Atlassian Connect Express, Node.js, coffee, and some tears.

Challenges I ran into

ACE uses jugglingdb by default and I needed a version of Node.js for a different package that wasn't compatible with juggling. As a result I spent a lot of time fighting npm and library compatibility issues till I got things working with sequalize.

What's next for Tableau for Confluence

Feedback from customers, and iteration to help customers customize it to meet their needs.
