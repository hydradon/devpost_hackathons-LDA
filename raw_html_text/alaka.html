Alaka

What is this?

Alaka can tell you who that actor is. You know, the one you saw in that movie? Alaka uses facial recognition to identify actors and subsequently searches for data about the match. Simply record a clip from the show you're watching and we'll take it from there. With Alaka, you'll always know who and what you're watching.

How does it work?

Alaka uses a facial tracking algorithm to track faces in a frame. When it determines a face is in an optimal position for recognition, it grabs the frame and sends it for processing.
