Inspiration

I wanted to develop a platform where people can share interesting places likes beaches, trails or even a corner store that is really good. I felt good places should be accessible to the community via the internet and not only the established places.

What it does

It shows you all the interesting places that people around you shared.

How I built it

Using ruby on rails, geokit, geokit-rails.

Challenges I ran into

Searching for places and marking them on a map

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The responsive and intuitive UI that look great on any device

What I learned

What's next for Lookup

Communities, Chat, Posts, Friends and rest of the social media bells and whistles.
