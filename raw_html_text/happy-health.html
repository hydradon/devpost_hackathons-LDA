Inspiration

Because Elderly People now a days have difficulties to get their meals every day because sometimes its impossible to get out doors, whenever they would order a meal on a phone call the options are simplle pizzas and prety much fast food that's really not that healthy for them. This is why a friendly and easy method to order food has come!

What it does

It is a friendly app which you will need to configure once, and then just sit back and check out the menu from the confortable seat in your house. you will know what time it will come in and what will the cost be. you can either pay cash or credit card.
