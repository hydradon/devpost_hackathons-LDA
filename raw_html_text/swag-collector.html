Inspiration

Collect both swag and memories, create and share stories around the collectors' items you find

What it does

Using the TomTom API, it let's start-ups, vendors, cafes and so on put their swag on the map, literally! Doing a small challenge, the collectors then get free swag, a memory and good karma.

And by the way, get ready for the the swag-treasure hunt, right here at Disrupt Berlin!

How we built it

Created a webpage as a portal for "locations" (start-up stands at Disrupt, for example) where they can upload their brand information and the "swag" they offer. Collecters can go to this site to see the information of their swag-travel ahead, and upload picture proof of the challenges they completed to get the swag
