Inspiration

We had some fantastic ideas that nobody on our team new how to accomplish so we went with something that we had at least a little experience in.

What it does

At the home page there are two options, one is to click a button to donate to Red Cross and takes you to their donation website. The other one takes you to a page with the map of the world and in it are circles that represent a tweet with a keyword of Hurricane in it. Each circle is a certain color based on what their reaction was. The four main colors are Red for mad/distressed emotions, Green for happy/optimistic emotions, Yellow for a mixed combination of emotions, and White representing a tweet that had no real emotion connected to it. Based on these graphics, one can infer different situations with emotions and begin to understand what exactly is happening.
