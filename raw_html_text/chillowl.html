Inspiration

We felt that there was a major issue in the world, that we are uninformed about the happenings around us, needing to look at many sources to get a vague idea of delayed events that have occurred around us.

What it does

OurChillOwl provides the world with a way to share information quickly with those around you. This helps promote safety, socialization, and happiness :)

How we built it

Hosted on NodeJS on back-end, utilizing google cloud services

Challenges we ran into

The project idea was intimidating due to merging technologies, languages, and differing knowledge and experience among our team members.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Everybody in our group has learned something crucial from this project

What we learned

Node JS, python, teamwork, front-end UX, sentiment analysis

What's next for ChillOwl
