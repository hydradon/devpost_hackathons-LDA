Inspiration

We wanted to learn more about the world of Augmented Reality.

What it does

Uses augmented reality and facial recognition to reveal people’s shared profile markers by pointing a phone camera at a group of individuals

How we built it

We built a face detection classifier using several Microsoft APIs (including Face Detect and Face Identification APIs), and Facebook’s ReactNative.

Challenges we ran into

  • Delivering an innovative AR app under time and resource constraints, with no prior experience with iOS, AR, ReactNative ARkit or 3D modelling
  • Optimising UI/UX. ## Accomplishments that we're proud of Building an app that can eliminate social hurdles and quickly reveal social and professional cues for conversation. ## What we learned Gained significant expertise in AR development, iOS development, and 3D modeling. ## What's next for Eagle Vision The future.
