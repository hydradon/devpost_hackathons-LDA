Block-Badge Demo App - ETHWaterloo, October 2017

This is a credentials verification platform example created for the ETHWaterloo hackathon. College is one option. Today, students have access to Udemy, trade schools, YouTube, Quora, Stack Overflow, Google, and more. These award course certificates that can easily be securely stored on a blockchain, and shared as wanted. This app adds a secure, decentralized option on top of Open Badges standard.
