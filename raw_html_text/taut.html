Inspiration

I was doing my chem homework and I had to look a whole bunch of stuff up. It would've been nicer if I could get electronegativities and molar masses, etc. all without leaving my notebook.

What it does

It's a combination of Slack and your Terminal with the note-taking features of Evernote.

How I built it

Electron, Webpack, and React

Challenges

Working with React, parent-child component relationships, forced immutability, working with poorly working APIs
