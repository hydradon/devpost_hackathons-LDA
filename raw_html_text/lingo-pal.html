Inspiration

Being bilingual is a valuable skill to have. We wanted to help people learn new languages by connecting them to someone who speaks that language!

What it does

Pairs you with a fluent speaker of the language you want to learn, and sets up a voice call between the two parties

What's next for Lingo Pal

Google translate integration and polish
