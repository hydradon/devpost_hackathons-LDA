Inspiration

We believe that if we can deliver some concrete methods and usefull application to kids and make their life and studies better and much more understandable.

What it does

It's a book and a smartphone application, two in one. The classic book feeling and a modern AR technology meeting point. Kids who interested and courious can experience much more with this package. In the app, you can scan the marks which you can find in the books and the app displays that for you in Augmented Reality.

How we built it

Built it for the lot of work. We used Unity and Vuforia for the AR part, Amazon Web Services for the authentication.

Challenges we ran into

Connecting Unity to AWS. Creating modells for the AR framework.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We have a very nice demo already, just 24 hours after we started learning this technology.

What we learned

A lot of things. Mostly modelling, scripting and designing a book in Word.

What's next for Eddie

We have a lot of ideas and things to improve and optimize. We up for the challenge. :)
