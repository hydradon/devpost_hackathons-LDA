Inspiration

For our project, we wanted to create a simple, accessible app that encouraged people to maintain a healthy lifestyle.

What it does

Our app helps keep track of the expiration dates for various food items.

How I built it

We used Xcode and Swift programming language to develop the app.

Challenges I ran into

The main challenge was learning how to create an iOS app, as it was a new experience.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We learned about version control and how to develop customized tables in Xcode.

What I learned

In general, we learned how to use Xcode.

What's next for Stay Fresh

In the future, we would like to utilize visual recognition to scan expiration dates on food products, instead of asking the user to manually input them into the program.
