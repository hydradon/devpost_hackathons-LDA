Inspiration

Have you ever needed money and not had a credit card free! It's annoying, that's why we created this app. How about people who want to help but can not? They can now help people, either family or friends, wherever they are? Moreover, lender can get money by lending people money by charging interest with theirs will.

What it does

It allows you to loan extra cash with each other and gain a small amount of cash.

How we built it

We use React for frontend development and express.js for backend development. To store data we use mongoldb. And for app engine, we use google app engine. We use Facebook and Google for login services. We use google for person API.

Challenges we ran into

Learning how to use React native, the use of color to design the app (the hexcode of the app), and design layout.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to work as a team to create a product within 2 days. Although there is still much more to work with the product, the concept and the groundwork of the app is just the beginning.

What we learned

React native and integrate many API into our products

What's next for My Lender

Make it into a market for people who want to trade
