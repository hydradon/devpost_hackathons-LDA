Inspiration

Attention monitoring system for online courses Using Bose Headphone & camera

What it does

Measures the attention of a student while doing the coursework/watching lectures.

How I built it

I designed an integrated computer Vision API, Bose headphone and API, webcam, web-based application that completely changes the learning experience of the student.

Challenges I ran into

I haven't worked with Unity before! I never worked with cloud deployment or didn't even know how it worked.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Gradually learning the mission pieces one by one and provide the proof of concept of the design that is implementable on a massive scale.

What I learned

systems-level thinking, "Use whatever technique/technology you want, make sure you are solving the correct problem!!"

What's next for Attention monitor and data interpretation

Actual Deployment of the system!!
