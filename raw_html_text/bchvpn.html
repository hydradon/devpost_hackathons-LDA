• Open BCHVPN website and slide the Money Button!

  • Get your credentials (and OpenVPN configuration)

  • Connect with an OpenVPN client

  • Connected!

Inspiration

Started as an idea during the BCHDEVCON Amsterdam 2018.

What it does

Allows you to connect to a VPN after making a payment in Bitcoin Cash.

How I built it

Combine Yours Inc.'s Money Button with an OpenVPN server.

See GitHub for more details, and some diagrams .

Challenges I ran into

Finding team members. Using Node.js. Getting the Money Button webhook to work.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Pitching the idea. Having a working prototype.

What I learned

Using the Money Button.

What's next for BCHVPN

Disconnect users after an amount of time (or make them swipe again!)
