Inspiration

Having a coffee machine running Linux and being able to ssh into it to brew a coffee

What it does

It runs a server on Raspberry Pi and receives brewing commands. It should also brew the coffee :D

How I built it

Connected a Raspberry Pi to two one-channel relays: one for controlling the water pump and one for controlling the water heater

Challenges I ran into

Fried the original chip and possibly some other internal circuits

What I learned

I learned how circuits work
