Inspiration and What it Does

Color your world!

Coloring is a great way to relieve stress and exercise your creative abilities. With Color Me, you will never run out of images to color.

Simply snap a picture of whatever you're doing, and add some fancy color to it. Or, you can choose one of our awesome pre-selected images to color and make your own masterpiece.

Using cutting-edge edge detection algorithms and image processing capabilities, we have brought coloring to your fingertips. We carefully designed and implemented an intuitive user interface to appeal to all audiences - both children and adults. With the camera feature, the range of possibilities is endless.

We are looking to integrate Color Me with social media so users can share their creations instantly.

The world is your palette.

What we learned and challenges

We learned how to develop an Android app and apply our software engineering skills to mobile development. We learned to debug and design our application. We built this app using Android Studio and Gimp for graphics. We ran into issues working with bitmaps and canvases as well as incorporating different screens available to the user.
