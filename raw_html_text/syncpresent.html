Inspiration

Let's not the presentations be eating up too much of your bandwidth weather you're presenting or attending! Present on (m)any device, even those which are not smart. Real time synchronization mechanism to avoid taking streaming video of your presentation. That means, the user can smoothly give stunning presentation while being on 2G or event slower cellular network from a phone.
