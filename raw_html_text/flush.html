flush

An Android app that displays bathrooms on the UW Campus. Click bathroom location for details. Shows bathrooms near you, allows you to sort by needs (handicap accessibility, baby changing stations, and gender neutral), and prompts for a user rating of the bathroom (1-5 stars). Feedback/rating function not completed yet. Uses Google Maps API. Compiled 90 buildings on the University of Washington campus. Bathroom locations and details need to be verified.

Created by Yina Zhu, Braden Sunwold, and Geoffrey Wukelic during DubHacks 2016.
