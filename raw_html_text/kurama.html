Inspiration

Nowadays, blockchain is one of the hottest topics in the world. It has done in resolving the problem in supply chain, bitcoin,... So that is reason why we choose blockchain track in JunctionxHa Noi.

What it does

Today, companies have their customer's data so maybe they can share it with each other to develop their services. Our program creates a lot of communities and each community has many companies connecting together. In each community, there is a common currency money and customers in companies will have chance to get this kind of money as a gift.

How I built it

We use BlockChain as a base and use smart contracts to develop the rules.
