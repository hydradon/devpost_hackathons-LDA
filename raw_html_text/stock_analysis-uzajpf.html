Inspiration

I always encounter in my daily life of people who are not in stellar socioeconomic conditions and believe that there is no possible way for them to develop a proper savings account for their financial future when they retire. This program is to benefit those individuals that have some income to spare each month but do not have the time to research stocks to invest in.

What it does

It just compares industry standard baseline stocks to any stock the user would like to compare it to. This gives the user a scatter plot and make an educated decision to about what stock or stocks to invest in for a period of time.
