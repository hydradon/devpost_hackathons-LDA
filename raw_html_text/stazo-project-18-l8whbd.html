Campass - Say Bye to Bored

UCSD is sometimes referred to as UC Socially Dead. We are Campass and we intend to change that. With Campass there’s never nothing to do. Our mobile application utilizes the Google Maps API to keep our users updated on what’s happening around them, so that your next adventure is always just a tap away.
