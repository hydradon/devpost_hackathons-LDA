What it is about

The medical reports often contain a lot of medical jargon commonly not understood by patients. They often lead to worries and create anxiety. This application not only allows them to look up their reports online, but also serves them in helping them understand their medical issues in a simpler language.

What are we doing

This is an ongoing project at Duke University, and our submission is part of Hack Duke 2016. It is all about trying to extract the medical jargon out of a radiology report and provide simpler explanations for each term to patients.
