Inspiration:

I love baseball and exploring datasets.

What it does:

The visuals show what months in the MLB season have a high amounts of homeruns.

How I built it:

I used Tableau to create all my data visualizations.

Challenges I ran into:

I was unable to export my Tableau images with the hovering tooltip showing the amount of games played and the number of At-Bat appearances by each MLB team.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of:

Scrapping data using MLB.com and baseball-reference.com

What I learned:

Creating data visualization requires time and dedication.

What's next for 2016 MLB Homeruns:

Comparing players ages and homeruns. Also, comparing the total of homeruns in dome and non-dome stadiums.
