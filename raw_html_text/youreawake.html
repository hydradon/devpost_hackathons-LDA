Inspiration

Our inspiration for "You're Awake" was a previous Terriblehack submission- the I'm Here app, which let someone know you have arrived after pressing a button on the app. We used a similar idea of redundancy, and incorporated it for another everyday activity - waking up in the morning!

What it does

Are you awake? Well, the You're Awake app knows you are! Once you've come to your senses in the morning, what better way to start off your day by letting You're Awake play an obnoxiously annoying alarm? Choose from a variety of horrible meme sounds including the 20th Century Theme Flute Version, Toxic by Britney Spears ShittyFlute, the Gopher Scream and more!

How we built it

You're Awake was built with Android Studio, in the languages Java and XML.
