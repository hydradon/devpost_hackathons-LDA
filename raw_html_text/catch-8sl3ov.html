Overview

Catch is like PokemonGo for music. As consumers start spending more time using their device in the real world, apps like PokemonGo and other proximity-based media services are catching on like wildfire.

How it works

Catch allows record labels or artists to place songs in the physical world using latitude and longitudes. As users come nearby, they are alerted of a song nearby. Using the heading reading from the BoseAR headset and the location from the user's phone, Catch allows a user to 'catch' a song by walking towards it.

As they movie toward the song it gets louder until they can finally catch the song and collect it.
