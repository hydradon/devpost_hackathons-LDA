Inspiration

Alexa looked very interesting and i wanted to get my hands dirty developing skills for her. Made few skills earlier, but this is my first complete skill along with APL.

What it does

Ask for Random Facts for a new interesting fact about the whole of universe as know to mankind, Random Facts provides fun the whole family can enjoy and is guaranteed to make you smarter. To start, just say "Alexa, launch Random Facts" or "Alexa, open Random Facts" to get a fact". At anytime, you can stop by saying "Alexa, stop"

How I built it

I used Amazon Alexa ask-cli to build the skill and added APL documents and screens.
