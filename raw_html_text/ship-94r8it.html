Inspiration

Dating in 2016 has two main problems:

  • It is difficult to find people looking for serious, committed relationships
  • It is painstaking to personally go through a myriad of potential matches to find the right individuals

What it does

ship crowdsources the match finding process, presenting YOU with all the BEST options.

How we built it

We used Ruby on Rails framework with React.js, Node.js and Stylus.
