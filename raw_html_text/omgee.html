Inspiration:

Anxiety related to health care

What it does:

Redefines patient's experience in a health care setting.

How we built it:

Microsoft band for heart rate monitoring alongside with an android companion app, a NodeJS backend with Socket.IO for real-time data streaming and front-end in AngularJS to have a very aesthetic and functional interface.

Challenges we ran into:

Socket.IO was new but we figured most of it with the help of each other.

Accomplishments:

We build a whole system in the course of less than 18 hours.

What I learned:

We have a huge capacity and talent pool on this team.

What's next for this project

Refining the existing technologies and gathering more data in order to deliver more effective care.
