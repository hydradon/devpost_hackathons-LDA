Personal Bias Blocker

A fun Chrome extension to remove information from websites as a way to make users aware of subconscious biases. Users can read a redacted version of news and then hover over text to view the original document. After comparing their reading experiences, we hope to make users more aware of their own subconscious biases.

-Created by Brandon Mar, Noah Ezekwugo, Lee Polla, Anthony Asencio, Tyler Fost
