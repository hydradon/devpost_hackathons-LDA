Welcome to Pear

I published Vita (also known as Pear), an education-focused app to the App Store that enables kids to learn math because I believe apps are a great way to teach things. The app lets you draw the answer to arithmetic questions just like you would do on paper, and it magically recognizes your handwriting. Pear (renamed Vita) was ranked as a top 100 educational game in 19 countries and received press attention from national Italian newspapers. I'm very happy with the success I am having with Pear and I look forward to building its next chapter!
