• IssCalc

Inspiration

Documentation in the emergency ambulance can be pretty though. The Doctor simple have no time, for a detailed amnesic based on the Abbreviated Injury Scale (AIS) to extract the Injury Severity Score (ISS).

What it does

The application's interface shows a body atlas, where the Doc can do a quickly pre selection by clicking one of the body parts. If a body part picked, the app shows a prefiltered list of the corresponding wounds. The items can be added to the ISS score of the person. In another view, the Doc is seeing a overview of the picked AIS code's and the resulting ISS.
