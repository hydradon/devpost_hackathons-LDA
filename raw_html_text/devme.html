Getting started with an idea is one thing, turning an idea into a project (and later into a product) is another beast entirely. DevMe facilitates ingenuity with a plan-- it brings non-technical users with a technical innovation together with developers, allowing for real-world ideas to become a reality.

DevMe provides a platform for both content creators and developers to list ideas and create development teams with the ultimate goal of bringing their ideas to fruition. Content creators can list their ideas (protected by an IP agreement) and accept applications as developers apply. Developers can build repertoire and gain easy-access to work opportunity by applying to and accepting a seat on projects.
