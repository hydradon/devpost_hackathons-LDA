Inspiration

This is dedicated to the girls and women without the resources, means, courage, or education to get help regarding their own health when necessary.

What it does

An app that allows female users to record personal, physical and mental symptoms/questions and receive feedback from verified gynecologists.

What I learned

There are many ways we can easily improve women's health with the technology we have available to us today.

What's next for Gynie

Donating feminine hygiene products through the app, making it more organized and specific, and maybe even connecting doctors to users through video calls.
