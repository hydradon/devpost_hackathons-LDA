Challenge:

Integrating analytics into the bar without compromising the bar experience

Solution:

Hardware - Adoption of a low friction sensor network, centered on the Weissbeerger system and the Smart Coaster
FOH Software - Integration of BetterBar Front-of-House Manager, which notifies wait staff of empty cups, warm drinks, user consumption, and ambient bar conditions
Owner App - Inclusion of bar experience data with the existing Weissbeerger app framework, comparison of successful bar conditions from other bars in the area and around the world.

How we define success:

- Perfecting the experience for the consumers
- Optimizing service of Front of House Staff
- Empowering bar owners to refine the factors of their businesses in ways that unlock higher top-line growth
