Inspiration

Inspired by the limitations of the current supper jio bot, we aimed to improve the current bot to extend to customised menus as well as stat management.

What it does

Allows people to order supper from a telegram group made of their own customised menus.

How we built it

We utilised telegraf to develop the bot while storing our data on firestore.
