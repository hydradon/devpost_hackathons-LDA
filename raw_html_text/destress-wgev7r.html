Inspiration

We built the app because we knew a lot of people who were stressed and they had no idea about how to control it.

What it does

The app gives an overview of stress and how it inhibits itself, and also how to meditate to control your stress.

How we built it

We used java in Android Studio.

Challenges we ran into

We had some problems in using the proper syntax to call functions and format everything correctly.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud that we came up with a good idea and a decent application that can help someone deal with their elevated stress levels.

What we learned

We learned that in order to be able to program fast and efficiently, it really is important to keep practicing.

What's next for DeStress

We hope to win the Creative Design Award. We also want to come up with more apps like this to deal with similar problems, like the ones addressed in this app.
