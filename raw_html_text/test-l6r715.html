• Marvel comics meets health records.

Inspiration

Going to the doctor isn't very fun for little kids. Super heroes though can make the experience better.

The Hack Project

Today, when a parent and child get to a doctor's office they have to fill out a paper intake form with the child's basic information. To make this experience more fun, I created an interactive comic book that the child and parent can fill out together.

Benefits

  1. Going to the doctor becomes more fun than before for the child.
  2. The child becomes more engaged with their health.
  3. The comic book format form can be edited and used for other purposes.
  4. Unlike paper, the information is digitized and doesn't have to be re-entered.
