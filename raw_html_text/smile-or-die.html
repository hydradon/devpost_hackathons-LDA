What it does

Nyan cat clone game with emotion control to train face muscles!

Game rules

Smile to change road line.

Eat cake and bottle to get plus one point.

Avoid dog or get minus one point.

Blink to enter night mode.

In night mode all earned points doubles.

Target audience

All! (Fun)

Presentation speakers (Training!)

People who suffer from face muscles disease (Health!)

How we built it

Android, google mlkit face detection and imagination!
