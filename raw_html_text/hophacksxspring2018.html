Inspiration

Inspired by Note Taker by the SDS, we want to provide students with the ability to receive instant and stable note taking.

What it does

Take photo of the blackboard and transcribe the photos into texts

How we built it

Use Android Studio and OCR Vision Google API (and attempted OpenCV)
