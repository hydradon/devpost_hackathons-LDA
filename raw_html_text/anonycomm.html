Inspiration

Many patients are hesitant or distrustful of the healthcare system. Our innovative communication technology aims to bridge a gap between members of marginalized community and medical health professionals.

What it does

AnonyComm provides anonymous communication between patients and physicians to initiate patient engagement, improve quality of care, and ultimately reduce healthcare costs.

How we built it

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned

What's next for AnonyComm
