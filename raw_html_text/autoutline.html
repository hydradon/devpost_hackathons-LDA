Inspiration

We have have too much lectures and book to pay attention to, and it has become our daily pressure. Autoutline frees you from being the slave of readings.

What it does

With NLP based algorithms, it takes in large chunk of reading and automatically gives out an outline.

How we built it

We used ReactJS and Node.js for web application, Python and Flask for backend, and Docker to containerize everything to make it more scalable.

Challenges we ran into

Finding boundaries within text
