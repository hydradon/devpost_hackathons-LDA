Inspiration

“Clocks slay time... time is dead as long as it is being clicked off by little wheels; only when the clock stops does time come to life.” ― William Faulkner

What it does

Time is what we want most but what we use the worst. You can review credit card statements to find out how you’re spending your money, but there’s currently no way to review how you spend your time.

With the Pinion iPhone app, you can review your time spent at predefined locations. You determine what locations matter most to you. Whether that’s your home, the gym or your favorite coffee shop; it’s up to you.

How I built it

Using Alohar's context-aware SDK, Pinion effortlessly and efficiently reviews your location and aggregates your time spent throughout the day. In addition, Pinion shows you distances traveled by mode of transportation. Whether you commute to work or you’re flying from one city to another, Pinion elegantly displays your travel history.

What's next for Pinion

We've developed the backend of the app and are now in the process of implementing the various charts to display the data in an interactive and intuitive way.
