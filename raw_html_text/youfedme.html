• Web-based pod simulator

  • Donors receive a notification when a pod is running low

  • Donors can see a map of nearby pods and their status

Inspiration

Lots of leftovers/spare food, and lots of hungry and homeless people in large metropolitan areas.

What it does

Our project enables those who have much to meet the needs of those who have less in a convenient way.

How we built it

React, Node, iOS, Firebase, and Swift!

Challenges we ran into
