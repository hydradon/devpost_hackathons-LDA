Dewmast: Decentralized Warehouse Management System

  • Current WMS’ suffer from data inconsistencies
    • Diverse parties: stores, vendors, warehouses (contract or in-house)
    • Competing applications, databases and manual emailing of spreadsheets
  • Dewmast: a WMS platform on the blockchain
    • Each entity (Owner, Location, Unit) is a smart contract
    • Reconciliation is done by each entity (smart contracts)
    • Lightweight client running on handheld devices
