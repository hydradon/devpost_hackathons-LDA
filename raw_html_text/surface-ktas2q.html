Inspiration

After playing a couple of fun games, i wanted to try it out by making my own version of a side scroller

What it does

The controls are simple, simply move your finger to move the plane to avoid the squares. You hit one, you have to start over and try again.

How I built it

I had to first create an endless generator of endless squares and make sure that the collisions work correctly. I also designed the art through illustrator. The coding went pretty smooth for the most part

Challenges I ran into

The initial square building and creating the whole grid system was quite challenging to accomplish

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I'm happy that I got to create a game where it's quite addictive for me

What I learned

I learned to keep following my passion and keep on going as hard as I can

What's next for Surface
