Inspiration

We aware of people who are motivated to work and want to learn continuously. As a students we have to develop our skills and knowledge all the time. We wanted to create solution and application.

What it does

It helps you to maintain your focus and have healthy breaks. You can also set tasks, which will be connected with actions sent via our Tap device.

How we built it

We used arduino for making a device. We used Ionic to build mobile app. We used graphql and node to create a backend on Heroku.
