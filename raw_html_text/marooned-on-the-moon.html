Inspiration

We were inspired to make a game loosely based of The Martian.

What it does

It is a text based game in which your choices affect your survival.

How we built it

We planned out the story first and then continued onto the making the game on JCreator.

Challenges we ran into

We had some trouble making the story stay realistic but also exciting and have an element of suspense.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to complete our task in the time provided which was a surprise to me.

What we learned

We learned that team work can prevail over a difficult challenge.

What's next for Marooned on the Moon

More improvements on the story are on the way and maybe even an inventory
