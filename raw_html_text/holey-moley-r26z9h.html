• Holey Moley

We now live in a world where it's all about preventative care.

Patients that take an active role in their overall health are healthier have a much more rewarding life.

We asked ourselves, “how can we help you be the healthiest you...for longer?”

Preventative health measures are not easy on your own. Only 8% of people are capitalizing on preventative care measures.

Holey Moley is the entry point to put people in control and at the forefront of this preventative care movement.

1 person dies of Melanoma every hour in the US. 1 in 5 people in North America will develop skin cancer during their lifetimes.

We are starting with skin health.

Holey Moley is a DIY Mole Mapping platform that allows people to document their moles over time, share the results with their providers, and connect with a community of people invested in their health.
