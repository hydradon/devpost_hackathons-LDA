Inspiration

As a person that listens to different music depending where I'm at like the library or commuting, I was inspired by the idea of an app that automatically changes my music depending where I'm at.

What it does

SpotUrSpot lets a user draw a geofence on a map and assign it a playlist. When you move into that geofence, the playlist starts automatically playing on your Spotify.
