Inspiration

People have difficulty finding community service opportunities in their local area.

What it does

Helping Hands is a social platform that connects people to non-profits and volunteer opportunities in their local area.

How we built it

Used Figma to make a prototype of the main screens and onboarding flow.

Challenges we ran into

Not having enough time to code an interactive prototype.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Being able to work on a project that will create a positive social impact!

What we learned

It was cool learning about React.js, as we're hoping to make this into a functioning mobile app!

What's next for Helping Hands

User testing and iterating on the prototype to make it interactive!
