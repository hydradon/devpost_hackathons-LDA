Inspiration

To provide a single stop workflow to connect people in need with service providers.

What it does

It provides a place to connect different people together and provide real time updates on the information.

How we built it

Front end in React, back end in Node and application is deployed on Digital Ocean.

Challenges we ran into

Organizing the ideas into reasonable workflows and prioritizing tasks in given short timeline.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to provide a complete workflow that connects everyone and we are very proud about it.

What we learned

Better time management and incremental development.

What's next for MonHackers

Look to see if we could build on top of what we built over the weekend.
