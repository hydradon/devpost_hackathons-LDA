Inspiration

I have definitely experienced days as an Electrical Engineering Major where I need to see beyond the struggle of now.

What it does

This simple app travels through powerful reminders from great women in history and in the tech. industry that can help to place today's troubles into perspective.

How I built it

Using Android Studio and photos that I have taken I compiled the completed project.

Challenges I ran into

Since this was my first time working with any code other than C or C++ the language barrier for Java was an issue but I was able to get a hang of the Android Studio enough to create something functional. We attempted to integrate snap kit but were completely unsuccessful

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am proud of integrating my own love for photography and nature into my first attempt at creating an app.

What I learned

I learned that there are a lot of other languages out there that I don't know yet that I should get to know.

What's next for Flower Empower App

We will be expanding to a daily use type of app, where a specific quote will be designated for each day of the year so that our users have new motivation every day.
