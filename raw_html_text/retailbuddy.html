Have you ever been shopping and you just can't balance all the price tags and discounts and items in your head, so you go to the checkout unaware of how much you're really spending? Let RetailBuddy do the hard work for you! RetailBuddy is a app that will calculate and keep track of all your desired items when you are shopping. Just scan the price tag of the item you're considering and RetailBuddy will add it to your virtual shopping list. You can see how much each item will be and what your overall total will be. With further improvements, RetailBuddy will be able to help keep shoppers conscious of ethical dilemmas behind any products they may be interested in buying, such as animal testing or employee discrimination, and offer them alternatives products/companies!
