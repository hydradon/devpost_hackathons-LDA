My inspiration was to create an app that will be used by students all over the world to have their doubts solved.

DoubtsSession is an app for getting your doubts cleared on the go, through audio/video calls across multiple platforms with other Cisco Spark users. The main idea is to keep your academic communications in a simple, secure space and get all your doubts solved from any part of the world.

I built it using Cisco Spark SDK for iOS. It is based on the example at https://github.com/ciscospark/spark-ios-sdk-example

Planned and possible future updates:

  1. An iPad only feature of taking notes (within the application) simultaneously while in a Doubts Session
  2. In-app personal messaging
