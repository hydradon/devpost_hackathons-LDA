Utilizing Android, we hoped to resolve the problem of tension in relationships whether it be a romantic relationship or that of a workplace. The issue that plagues these relationships are the passive-aggressiveness present in relationships and the lack of understanding about the other gender. We created an application that allows the user to track their mood and if chosen they can update their partner on their mood, which promotes conversation rather than the simple “I’m fine.” Graphs track the user’s mood over time, presenting a way for users to reflect on their emotional health. Indeed, to encourage conversation, we can provide the user with advice concerning the opposite gender regarding the workplace or personal relationships.
