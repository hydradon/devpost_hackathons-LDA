Inspiration

One of our team members uses a thermal camera to find lost dogs. If it can find dogs - why can't it find a drone?

What it does

When a drone controller is powered on a wake up signal is sent to the thermal camera network to search for and track the drone visually.

How we built it

Using a RPi zero, a thermal camera, lots of flying, and hard work.
