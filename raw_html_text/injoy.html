Inspiration

This idea was inspired by the fact that we came to Georgia Tech as exchange students. We had a hard time integrating to the new environment. Hence, we came out with a thought of helping people in general make new friends through the means of eating.

What it does

The first time launching this app, it will automatically know your preferences / hobbies. When you are going to dine alone in a restaurant, you can get matched with another person to dine together. During the dining session, it must be very awkward, meeting a new person, who does not know anything about you. This is where our second feature of our app goes into place, it suggests you a list of news headline and helps you kick start the conversation with a topic you are interested and familiar with. Result: no more awkwardness :)

How we built it

We use Facebook's graph API to know a person's favorite activity, preferences, and hobby. We process those data to produce a list of conversation topics.

Challenges we ran into

Accomplishments that we're proud of
