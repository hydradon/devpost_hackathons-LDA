Inspiration

11, 400 tracked deaths & many more untracked ones, last year in India alone due to potholes, bumps, improper roads

What it does

When a vehicle drives over a pothole/bumps/roadblocks, etc, the motion sensor records the severity of the jolt, tags it with the vehicle's geographic location, couple it with the satellite images for that location and uploads it to the cloud. Specialized software can then eliminate anomalies, cancel out duplicate reports, and generate an individualized “safety report” for roads.

How I built it

using combination of data sources — readings from vertical accelerometer (mobile phone) & GPS (mobile phone / sensor in combination with here maps) and satellite images (youmapps) — to automatically record nasty bumps in the road.
