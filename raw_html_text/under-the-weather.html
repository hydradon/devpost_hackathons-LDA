What it does

UnderTheWeather is a weather-reporting bot for Mastodon. It uses Watson's API to guess the emotional state of its interlocutors and respond appropriately.

Challenges I ran into

Twitter. They demand access to a phone number to even allow you to use their API. Unacceptable.

What I learned

At least three APIs and smarter JSON parsing.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Making something that might actually draw appeal to Mastodon as a platform.
