Inspiration

We were inspired by current events going on in America. We want to alleviate the fears and judgments people have and transcend boundaries.

What it does

Our app matches users randomly regardless of ethnicity, nationality, and gender. Once the users meet and scan each other's unique QR code, they can unlock the ethnicity, nationality, and gender of the other person (or find out by talking to each other).

How we built it

We used Android Studio and Git to develop as a team.

Challenges we ran into

2/3 of us were beginners to Android development.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud to have built an app with a social good idea as our motivation.

What we learned

How to develop an Android application as a team.
