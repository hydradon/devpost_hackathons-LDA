playlistr

A webapp that uses machine learning to make a Spotify playlist based off a group of users' information

Uses Gaussian Process Regression to approximate user tastes in music using vectors of music attributes obtained from Spotify's WebAPI

Never fight over music again. Use our app and we'll give you a playlist that fits everyone's music tastes.
