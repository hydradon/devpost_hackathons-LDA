Inspiration

As a musician, I am always looking for something to give me an accurate pitch, whether it be for relative pitch training or just to sing something for someone. With an echo in the room, now I don't need to pull out my phone to do it!

What it does

This skill currently outputs a random pitch. The idea behind this is specifically for pitch training. Alexa sounds a pitch, and you are slowly training yourself to recognize which pitch it is.
