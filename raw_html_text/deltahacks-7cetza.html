deltahacks

project by: julia cooke, tasnim bari, jenny feng, madeeha khan, and rumsha siddiqui

idea:

Our app's purpose is to encourage clothing donation and the realization that many people own more things than they actually need.

We designed this app as a way for people to "keep" their closet in their pockets by storing photos of every item of clothing.

Each day, the user indicates which clothes they've worn, and the app tracks those statistics.

At the end of 6 months the app prompts the user to donate all of their lightly or unused clothes.

implementation:

The app is meant to allow users to take photos of their clothes and then sort them into sorting categories.

The clothing buttons on the home page would take them to their "database" of clothing, jumping to whatever category they want first.

Scrolling up and down would allow them to access their entire "closet". They would select the clothes they wear that day.

The app would then track which clothes are selected, and the users would be able to access that information on the 'stats' page.

If they have not worn the item very often, the 'donate' button would generate a list of nearby clothing donation centres.
