Inspiration

We felt that in our daily lives, we don't think much about giving to charity. By making it a part of something we do often--like shopping on Amazon--we not only bring greater awareness to people in need but also help them with donations.

What it does

Whenever people make a purchase on Amazon, they can opt to donate a percent of their purchase to their charity of choice. This way, they can factor this donation into being part of their overall purchase and hence budget.
