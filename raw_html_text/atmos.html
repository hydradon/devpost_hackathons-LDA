Inspiration: The quality of air around us had led to many diseases, so we wish to make it better.

What it does: It tells the quality of air at a particular place and how to prevent the health problem caused.

How we built it: We built it using HTML and CSS. We linked Google maps to show the location.

Challenges we ran into: Data acuracy, time limit.

Accomplishments that we're proud of: Even if data accuracy was the biggest challenge, we managed to find a good amount of accurate data.

What we learned: We have learnt to work in a limited frame of time

What's next for ATMOS: We wish to make it a worldwide website, which will others to breathe better.
