Finding parking is a time-consuming , costly , and overall frustrating process. We're here to fix that.

Spotlight is a modern, cost-effective solution to the issue of finding parking. We are taking advantage of Computer Vision technology to help businesses increase revenue and drivers save valuable time .

Our product uses advanced detection algorithms to determine open parking spots across various parking lots. It then sends that data to a user friendly application that helps easily guide drivers to open spots.
