What is VroomLog?

VroomLog is a travel log that showcases the places you have visited and allows for the user to document their experience at that location. We utilized the SmartCar API to allow the user to pin the different locations of their smart car and rant about them.

Inspiration

We love traveling to places. We love experiences. We thought it would be cool to document and then share your traveling experiences with friends and family.
