Inspiration

Those little squishy balls at the end of pens. Simple gaming apps Prizes, glory, fame

What it does

A minimalist game revolving around a rotating sphere, in which the user must pass through the level - with a twist. It's not broken. It compiles. Nothing bursts into flames when you press play.

How we built it

Unity, C#

Challenges we ran into

Only one team member actually knew the software. Additionally, we had 4 team members, and ended up with a strong two on the final day of the competition. Coding is hard.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We actually built a working game, and even though it isn't an app, we're still pretty buzzed about our success.

What we learned

How to use Unity // how to write in C# // how to recover under the pressure of a lost workforce // Team management
