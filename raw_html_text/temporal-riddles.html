Inspiration

We were inspired by the Hackenger Hunt. We wanted to create a game where the person playing had to solve the riddles and type out the answers.

What it does

Our game is a series of time-related riddles

How we built it

We used python to program it. For the images we used adobe photoshop.

Challenges we ran into

We didn't have many ideas and we had errors in the code. Also, we ended up having to download multiple apps like adobe photoshop, pycharm, and others, and that was very time-consuming.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We're proud of being able to eventually work as a team and being able to have something to submit at all

What we learned

We learned that it's better to discuss ideas beforehand rather than coming up with them on the spot.

What's next for Temporal Riddles

Better quality, graphics, etc.
