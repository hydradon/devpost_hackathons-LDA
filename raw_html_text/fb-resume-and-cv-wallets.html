Inspiration and How it Works

Introducing Job Seekers Resume Page on Facebook to engage Employeers and Employees within Facebook Communities.
An Employer says to an Employee saying show your Resume/Cv Page on Facebook. Most People in the world are actively looking for Job. This applications was aimed at connecting Employeers with the employees all over the world directly on Facebook.

An Employees can now Create and have a Facebook Resume Page and easily share the link to Friends, Employeers etc. all directly from Facebook Applications.
This will ensure that all conversation on recruitment process will be taking place directly on facebook thus engaging billions of Job Seekers with their respective Employeers going forward on Facebook....

How to use this app:.

1.) Login using Facebook
2.)You will be presented with Create Resume/Cv Form. The CV/Resume muult-step Form has various Components About
Experience
Education
Skills
Interest
Awards & Certifications
Once you finished entering Data, Click on Create Resume button and you are in there.

3.)Click on View and Share Your Resume Button to Preview your Resume.
4.)Click Share Resume on Facebook Button to share your Resume.
5.)Use Facebook Share button and Facebook Send Dialog Button to share your Resume on your Facebook Timeline and to a specific Facebook user, Employers etc.. in HIPAA Compliance Mode respectively.
6.)You can also Use Email Option to share the Resume links to your Employeers Email Address all from Facebook...

FaceBook Technology Used:

1.) Facebook Login Graph API: Used to authenticate Users
2.) Facebook Shared Button: used to allow user to share CV/Resume to her Facebook Timeline
3.) Facebook Send Dialog Button: used to allow user to share CV/Resume to a specific Facebook Friends, Employeers on Facebook etc. in HIPAA Compliance Mode.
