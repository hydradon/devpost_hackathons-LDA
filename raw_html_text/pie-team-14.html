Inspiration

Practice Identify Emotions (PIE) Increase your emotional wellbeing with PIE. Record your feelings throughout the day and receive suggestions for increasing your ability to identify what you need to feel better.

What it does

Identify Feelings and what needs are being met with different experiences from a list of universal feelings.

  • When needs are met
  • When needs are not met

How I built it

  • Dreamweaver
  • Bootstrap
  • Django
  • FE Development code

Challenges I ran into

Put together a big app idea and started a large project. Could have used existing code and libraries better. We all chose things outside of what we have experience in.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We learned how to do some things. Planning and ideation phase was excellent.

What I learned

Prototyping the app the day before would have been useful. We created good design and good backend but we could have better communication regarding integration.
