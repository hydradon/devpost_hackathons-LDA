Inspiration

Being apart of Digital Leaders to Learners, me and my team was assigned in creating an app that will help the community of Atlanta.

What it does

ATL Connect is an iOS app that has multiple features, such as, connecting you with other locals, allowing you to only see locations in the Atlanta area, find jobs, housing and transportation, and also find entertainment within your favorite genre.

How we built it

Currently we built a prototype for the app using Sketch.

Challenges we ran into

trying to figure out the process of our the app should be organized

Accomplishments that we're proud of

being able to do a prototype in 48 hours or less.

What we learned

how to use Sketch, implanting maps within a prototype, how to work a prototype.

What's next for ATL Connect

to actually code the app through Swift and add new features.
