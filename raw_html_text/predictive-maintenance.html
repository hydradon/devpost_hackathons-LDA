Predictive maintenance

The problem with traditional maintenance approaches is their high cost. Waiting until a component fails means lost production time and revenue. In-person inspections are expensive and can lead to replacing parts unnecessarily, based only on the inspector’s best guess. Following the manufacturer’s recommended maintenance schedule saves on inspection costs but often results in replacing parts that are still functioning well and could continue to do so.
