Inspiration We are just beginners with coding and this was our second gaming code.

What it does This code is specifically for a few seconds of entertainment to a user who needs a quick distraction from his or her work.

How we built it We thought of old games like pac-man before, but a game that looked a little bit more 3D fascinated us. We used the same types of programming skills but expanded our knowledge on the visuals and functions of the roll, pitch, and yaw axes of aviation.

Challenges we ran into We had a few glitches where the user shadow of the plane would not coincide with the x and y value of the aircraft.

Accomplishments that we're proud of We made another quick game!

What we learned We can definitely do a lot more in the next 20 hours
