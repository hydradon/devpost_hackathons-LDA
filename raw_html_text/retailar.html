Inspiration

What it does

Displays phone details for displays at a store, gives directions through arrows on the floor, and lets the user play a game while they wait in line.

The phone display experience is gamified, giving points for looking at more phones and encouraging people to shop more.

The arrows on the floor show the shortest path to a destination using tiles on the floor as markers.

Try it out

Go to https://hacktober.netlify.com on any phone or tablet, and https://au.gmented.com/app/marker/marker.php on a laptop or PC. On the PC, generate a 3x3 QR code (all default options) with code "32" for the game, "7" for the phone details, and "0" or "16" for the arrows.

How I built it

Built in AR.js and A-Frame.
