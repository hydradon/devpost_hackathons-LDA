Inspiration

This is the first demo of a series of games that can run on Samsung GearVR. We hope to combine the virtual reality experience and head exercise, which can help people move their head when playing the game, so that we can help people prevent from cervical pain. With attractive game experience, people can do exercise without intention and it will make people much more healthy.

How we built it

We use unity and C# to create this game, and we also create the scene and models for the game.

What's next for Gamexercise

We hope that we can continue work on it and we will improve the graphic of the game. In addition, we want to continue our series of Gamexercise.
