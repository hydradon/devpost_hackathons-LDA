Inspiration

As a team we realize sometimes it's difficult to get aid on certain assignments especially in college when time is limited

What it does

A simple page that displays other people's work that they decide to upload as well as a live chat with a bot and a standby tutor that's there to help. With a tutor you are able to upload your work, receive feedback as well as set up meeting/ office hours.

How we built it

Google app engine - Twilio & LiveChat for Chat - Google Sign in API - Okta
