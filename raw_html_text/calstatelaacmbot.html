Inspiration

We need more club information at our college especially for Computer Science Students. Very few students in the department know about ACM. The ONLY Computer science club at Cal State LA!!

What it does

It informs students about clubs in the College of Engineering, Computer Science, and Technology. It tells them how they can join, find more information, activities, and projects done by the club

How I built it

We used the qnamaker.ai

Challenges I ran into

We were having trouble formatting the responses of the bot

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We figured out how to host the bot

What I learned

The bot has a home connection to the questions

What's next for CalStateLAACMBot

We will continue to add more information about the other clubs and more information about the college. Students will be able to ask the bot questions regarding where they can find paperwork, faculty, seek advisement, etc.
