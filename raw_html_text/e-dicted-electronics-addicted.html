Inspiration

Seeing a loved one become addicted to a f*cking Scrabble knockoff. The addictive elements added and not understanding what was going on when put away months of family time and life to play the game

What it does

Teaches people what video games are including to addict you by having the user follow an interactive story of adding addictive elements to a game they are developing

How I built it

Unreal Engine 4 and Hackathon food

Challenges I ran into

Bugs in the engine, rewriting the game a few times for feature creep

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Having it work at the end!

What I learned

I gained experience in Unreal Engine 4, learned a lot about loops and logic, and am better prepared to deal with feature creep by making it right in the first place. Also became up-to-date on uploading to the Android marketplace.

What's next for E-dicted (Electronics Addicted)

Better writing, a longer story, and more information and tricks scrupulous developers add, but first putting the HTML5 version on Blouir.com and releasing on the Android and iOS marketplaces.
