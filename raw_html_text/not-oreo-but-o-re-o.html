Inspiration 

 I like Oreo but only like the "re" part.

What it does

Why I can't make a useless and silly app, it could also bring fun to everybody when people feel bored and would like to kill time. Or for people in need to make their own customized O-RE-O asset. Aiming to make your laugh and built your own O-re-o on the computer. Do anything you like with your O-RE-O or O-O-O-RE-RE-O-RE-O-O-RERE. Enjoy how it looks and make it real. Install your own 3D asset as well.

How I built it

3D asset & node.js & javascript
