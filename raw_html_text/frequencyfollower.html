Caprisun Pathfinder™

H.A.R.D. Hack project taking on Northrop Grumman's challenge of making a robot that autonomously tracks down and navigates to RF Frequency broadcast node Table A4

We were a team of 5 and didn't know much going in, but learned a lot about RF, Raspberry Pis, Python, and circuitry, together <3

Hardware used:

  • Nooelec RTL-SDR Bundle
  • Smart Robot Car Chassis Kit - 4WD with DC motor
  • Basic Distance Sensor Sensor
  • Raspberry Pi 3
