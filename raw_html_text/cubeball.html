Inspiration

Rocket League Some game in a bar

What it does

Multiplayer (2-8) soccer game that people can get together and play with on their phones on campus. Cubeball breaks down social barriers and allows other students to take a few minutes to relax, play a game and make new friends.

How we built it

Unity to build our game. Twilio for receiving player inputs Flask for our webhook Cloud9 to host our flask server and to get player input from

Challenges we ran into

Getting user inputs from our flask server and having that update the players in Unity. Simple logic things

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting the game done!

What we learned

How to get JSON information from a WWW request.

What's next for Cubeball

More games that you can play with friends locally, through your phone
