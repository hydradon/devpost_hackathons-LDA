Inspiration

As a freshman in college, a desire to keep in touch with my family.

What it does

Utilizing iOS contact information, reminds users to reach out to their friends and family.

How I built it

Swift, XCode, and UIKit.

Challenges I ran into

Accessing the entirety of a user's contact list through Swift. Checking the last time that a text was sent between a user and a contact.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Not very much; too many hurdles to get past as someone new to the CS field.

What I learned

MVC, UIKit, and more.

What's next for ConnectLife

Progress through development over time until reaching a stable build ready for production.
