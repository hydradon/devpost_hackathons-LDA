Inspiration for SIMOGGA Solutions

The competition is fierce worldwide, companies compete with each other and the management focuses on the cost production while trying to keeping its energy footprint low.

This is the basis of the development of the SIMOGGA Solutions aiming at Visualizing, Quantifying and Optimizing the shop floor layout, the MRO processes as well the Warehouses.

We want to help companies to produce, maintain and recycle locally and keep the jobs locals

========================================

What SIMOGGA Solutions will do with GE Manufacturing Suite

We want to use the data collected by the WIP Manager and propose:

  • New shop floor layout increasing the capacity of the factory and reducing the production costs
  • Optimized MRO processes increasing drastically the usage of the material by reducing the time required to repair, maintain it
  • Optimized Warehouse management to ensure that the customer waits a minimum of time at the desk or waits for the delivery

We build desktop applications as well as public or private cloud based solutions depending on the customer’s needs.

====================================

SIMOGGA Layout design solution

  • We will integrate the data collected from various GE Brilliant Manufacturing suite with SIMOGGA Layout Design and propose optimized shop floor layout
  • We present dynamic shop floor layout proposing optimized shop floor layouts based on genetic algorithms mixed with the feedback from all actors working on the shop floors: unions, operators and managers
