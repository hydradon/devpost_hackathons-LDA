Your Private Search Engine

What’s your " private internet "? It’s all your stuff that’s scattered all over the place. Years of attachments buried in your personal and work email accounts. Documents stored in all your cloud accounts like Dropbox and Google Drive. Photos shared in your social network like Facebook and Instagram and photo storage accounts like Flickr. And all the people you know across multiple email and social accounts. It’s your stuff and all the stuff that’s been shared with you. It’s a mixture of your personal and professional lives online. And it’s private, just for you.

AnyCloud is an amazing new way to search, discover, and visualize your private internet - in an amazingly simple way.

AnyCloud is a meta service - it does not provide storage. Instead, you get a unified view into your entire private world – all your photos, all your documents, all your friends, across all your email, social, and cloud accounts, across your personal and work lives.

With AnyCloud, it’s easy to…

  • search for a file across multiple email and cloud storage services...simultaneously
  • find all the people you know, ordered by FriendRank, our algorithm that determines how close you are to them
  • keep current with the latest content shared by people in both your personal and professional lifes
  • discover all of the photos stored across email, social, and cloud accounts - in one convenient service

Integrations Supported

AnyCloud provides a comprehensive list of services available to link to your private internet.

  • Email: Gmail, Outlook, and almost any other email service available (IMAP)
  • Social: Facebook, Instagram, Google+
  • Personal Cloud: Dropbox, Google Drive, Microsoft OneDrive, SugarSync
  • Corporate Cloud: Box, Sharepoint Online, Citrix Sharefile, Salesforce Files, Hubspot Files, Barracuda Copy, Alfresco, OneDrive for Business, Jive, CMIS, SMB
  • Photo Cloud: Flickr, Picasa, Shutterfly, SmugMug, Snapfish
  • Developer Cloud: Azure Storage, Egnyte, Amazon S3

Features and Benefits of AnyCloud

Email-as-Cloud Storage - AnyCloud turns the attachments stored in your email accounts into your own on-demand, private Dropbox-like cloud storage service.

Unified Search - use AnyCloud to search your entire private internet with keywords the way you scan the public internet using Google. Find the people and documents you're searching for, fast.

Unified Friends - see a collection of your friends - from email and social networks - in one place, ranked by our proprietary FriendRank algorithm. Click on a friend to see his/her social profiles as well as all the content shared with you from multiple services - email attachments, photos, and more.

Unified Feed - easily discover new documents and photos that have been shared with you across all your accounts. Know the moment your colleagues upload the sales information or your kids add pictures to a shared folder.

Unified You - shows you all of your photos and documents cleanly sorted into the folders and groups they belong to, like a photo album from Facebook, a collection of files in a Dropbox folder, or the attachments to an email.

Share and Download - your content on AnyCloud is no use to you if you can’t do anything with it. You can, of course, easily download your documents. The share function is not yet active on this iteration of the mobile app, but will be soon.
