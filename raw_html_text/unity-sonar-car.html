The Sonar Car serves as a proof of concept for use of the Unity Gaming Engine along with arduino to facilitate complex control, feedback, and virtualization. While it was beyond the scope of this project, the sonar information that we were able to display in Unity could be expanded on to create a full virtual environment based on gathered sonar data allowing a user to view basic object and shapes in conditions of low visibility and without the use of camera or other sensor.
