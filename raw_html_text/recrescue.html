Inspiration: We were inspired by the JP Morgan Chase challenge and by the effects of past natural disasters and how they left many people in a panic.

What it does: This site is used to located food and gas sources for families after a disaster.

How we built it: We used html5 up to design a site that shows the basic locations and availability of resources needed after a disaster.

Challenges we ran into: With little knowledge of making an app we with the help of a JP Morgan chase worker we were able to put together a site.

Accomplishments that we're proud of: Learning how to use html more effectively

What we learned: How to get a job through chase and the process to make the site and develop our career paths

.

What's next for RecRescue: The next process would be to make an efficient site and app where people can find resources after a disaster so that there will be less havoc in the future.
