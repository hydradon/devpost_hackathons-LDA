Inspiration

Climate change is a huge problem that our world faces. We want to take advantage of social structures in order to make a difference.

What it does

Provides live and secure data to users regarding their daily and historical power consumption.

How we built it

Android Studio with a team of 4

Challenges we ran into

Requirement of contacting each utility provider in order to gain access to their data

Accomplishments that we're proud of

A great model that will attract many users to make a difference in a fun way.

What we learned

The development life cycle has ups and downs

What's next for imPower

Expanding to include all of the Green Button utility companies!
