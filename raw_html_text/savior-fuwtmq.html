Inspiration

We were brainstorming ideas for games when we came up with the idea for a create-own-adventure type of game.

What it does

The player enters the game and is swept into a storyline that they are directly part of. The player's decisions will decide what the fate of the character is.

How I built it

I wrote the entire program on Python.

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am proud of improving my Python skills and being able to create a program from just a basic knowledge of the language.

What I learned

I learned how to carry out conditionals efficiently.

What's next for Savior
