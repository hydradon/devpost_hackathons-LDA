Inspiration

Me and my partner have cofounded a blockchain marketing business where we create content for promising projects, but we realized that there is a lot of risk and uncertainty the online contractor takes in these situations. We wanted to remove that.

What it does

We intend for it to be a platform where people/companies can sell services which are done by contractors and verified by "validators", nodes not directly involved in a transaction who can profit from helping the network.

How we built it

We built it using solidity for the back end and VueJs for the front end.

Challenges we ran into

Determining how all parties involved in our platform would be economically incentivized to participate required some thought, as well as of course the programming in of itself.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Despite having no prior experience with solidity we were able to program a full stack web app centred around an idea that we sincerely think is viable and useful- and have a desire to continue development of it further!

What we learned

Getting the exposure to smart contract development and creating an application interacting with its logic was definitely an enriching experience.

What's next for ECOTA

We hope to continue development and create something with a true use in the future!
