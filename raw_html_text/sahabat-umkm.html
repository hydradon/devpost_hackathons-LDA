Inspiration

Many UMKM actors whose work combines the art and culture native to the area where they live. With the results of the work it should be able to lift their economy and cultural arts in their area. We see that UMKM are less developed in tourist attractions. Lack of knowledge to adaptation to the internet and technological developments experienced by UMKM. they are left behind, especially in their marketing products. So we want to develop an application to help UMKM in Banyuwangi better known by tourists who come.

What it does

This application will contain a directory of tourist attractions, as well as MSMEs who are in that place. Tourist visitors will easily find and buy MSME products in tourist attractions. For payments, you can use E-Pay BRI.

How I built it

we will integrate UMKM in the arts and culture by promoting their work on the website and promoting it on social media and etc. With a website-based application, we will build this application with an attractive and easy to use design.
