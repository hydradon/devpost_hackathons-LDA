Inspiration : Recourses for under privledged students

What it does : Gives students opportunity to earn points to use on recourses provided by non-profits, schools, and local businesses. It also gives students recourses for college prep, disabilities,

How we built it : Appery.io, Javascript, AWS, mySQL, Rest, XCode

Challenges we ran into : Making the app as simple and straightforward as possible, keeping page uncluttered and clear with language and icons

Accomplishments that we're proud of : Making an app in 24 hours

What we learned: How to better work as a team and make something we're proud of

What's next for SPARK : Outreach to teachers, schools and communitys
