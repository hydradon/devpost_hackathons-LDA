Texty

Are you travelling abroad? Or maybe you just have a poor Wi-Fi connection... Need to use an app, but you don't have Wi-Fi to connect to it? Well if there's an API for it, we got you covered! Textly allows you to send a simple SMS message and get a reply from our server giving you an answer to your question. Whether it be translations, currency conversion, stock prices, etc. - your answer is a simple text away.

Texty's your non-data assistant who helps you with basic things while you don't have data. Instead of querying for information with an internet connection, it instead uses texts over a mobile device's cellular network which are cheaper than buying data packages.

See our awesome launch page at http://williamyang.me/Texty/
