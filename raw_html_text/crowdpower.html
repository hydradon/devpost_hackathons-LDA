Inspiration:

Citizens who live in the city don't know what is going to happen or has happened in their surroundings. They used to have passive ways to get government information and, nowadays, it may be controlled by fake news. We want to create a proactive way for citizens to provide their opinions and get the truth from first-hand data.

What it does:

● Propose Ideas ● Gathering Votes ● Supervise Bills ● Care Surroundings ● Governmental Responses

How we built it

Design by Sketch, Develop by iOS

Challenges we ran into

No existing data base we can use. The data we are searching for needs 1. people's participating 2. government's bills from every different bureaus and cities and it may be difficult to collect since they could be in different forms.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We completed the design and 80% of iOS developing.
