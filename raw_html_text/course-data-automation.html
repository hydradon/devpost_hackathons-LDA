Inspiration

Looking for an idea for the Hackathon, we looked at UIPath and considered what could be automated.

What it does

It draws grades off of Blackboard and throws it in an Excel file. So far.

How we built it

Mostly through drafts and planning, trial and error when that inevitably failed, and online tutorials. And some caffeine.

Challenges we ran into

As first time users of UIPath, everything. Trying to efficiently extract data was difficult, so we did it inefficiently.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It works sometimes.

What we learned

We're not good at UIPath, but it looks like it could be powerful in the right hands.
