Inspiration

What it does

uni-Informal is a mobile app which does the following:- Register available Informal Schools and Courses Offered(all Information about courses) Provide this Information to app users where they can:- View the information Register or enroll to schools Make a review of the schools to either credit or discredit the school Share the Information to either friends or social media

Encourage teachers to form groups and register them with us for:- Inform other users of available groups Help them register at contribute Help Schools offer subsidized School fee for groups Enable school managers plan for the groups Enable the groups application for loans

Creates a Profile where the app user will:- View their courses they registered for Make the payments see their fee balances View Information about their next Training Make Saving via an e-Wallet See events in the groups they registered

Notification:- will notify user on the following Their payments (balances and when due) When the courses they registered for will begin Events that school or groups have organized Group Payments(Group Contribution) The training Information (What they need to plan for the next training all the full stack information)

How we built it

Using Android and php and using google notification Provider

Challenges we ran into

Accomplishments that we're proud of
