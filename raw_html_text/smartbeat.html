Inspiration

What if your phone could make decisions for you? If your body could think for you?

What it does

SmartBeat harnesses Fitbit's biosensors in order to determine which song should be played next.

How we built it

We dreamt it, we believed in ourselves, and through hard work, blood, sweat, and tears, we overcame the haters.

Challenges we ran into

Coffee ran out. Spotify API is not kind. Fitbit connection failed many times, and data was unreliable. Architecture needed to be edited continuously.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Making our vision a reality.

What we learned

Updating architecture constantly. Integrating cross-platform technologies.

What's next for SmartBeat

Integrating full body data to make simple decisions for you throughout the day.
