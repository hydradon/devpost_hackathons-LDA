Inspiration

We got inspired by the games we used to play as kids. We had lots of fun with them, and we wanted to try to develop one.

What it does

Two cheeses rolling down a hill, try to be the first shouting for it!

How we built it

With java using Android Studio.

Challenges we ran into

Changing between different set of coordinates, linear algebra, speech recognition, physics, Android API.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The first rolling cheese guided by our voice. Great design. Nice feeling with rolling down the hill. Fresh air for you and your cheese.

What we learned

java, Android Studio, to work 18 hours non-stop, to abandon non-succesful projects.

What's next for Cheese and Shout

Incorporate scores, online mode, scream-as-loud-as-posible mode, main menu, smooth hills, improve voice recognition.
