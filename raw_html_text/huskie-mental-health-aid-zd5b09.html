Inspiration: According to the data published by the Office of Student Development, as of April 1, 2015, at least 4 students have died from suicide. This is a 37% increase from the previous year. We cannot let this to continue.

What it does: It helps the students to assess the mental health and provide them guidance to improve mental health.

How we built it: We develop our prototype using justinmind prototyper and the app by MIT app inventor.

Challenges we ran into: To get a good psychological assessment scale

Accomplishments that we're proud of: Build of a good prototype on which we can work on to develop the app.

What we learned: Good teamwork, and UX design rules.

What's next for Huskie Mental Health Aid: To get help from psychology professors and build the app. Make it available to IOS and Android Platforms.
