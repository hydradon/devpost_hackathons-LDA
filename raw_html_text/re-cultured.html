Inspiration

What it does

Saves grocery store chains on carting cost, reduce dairy and plastic waste, create jobs in food industry, educate customers in how to save dairy waste at home

How I built it

start at commercial kitchen, set up product line, search for commercial space in low income community, train and expand, get into larger markets.

Challenges I ran into

Cheese training, testing product, source consistency

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

reusing dairy waste, reducing on production of more dairy by reusing it, training low income workers to give a trade

What I learned

Sourcing food is difficult, but we are using technology to help with that issue, cheese making is hard.

What's next for (RE)CULTURED

create more product lines, educate consumers on reusing milk to make their on yogurt through events, education workshop. Serve as example to other companies to learn how to reuse materials.
