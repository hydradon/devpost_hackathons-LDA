Inspiration

I really liked the SoHacks logo and looked for ways I could further improve it, and then I came up with the idea of making a 3D model that's ready for 3D Printing.

What it does

It can also be used for 3D printing or laser cutting/etching the logo. I estimate it'll take about 3-4 hours to finish printing the model.

How I built it

I used OnShape over the course of about 7-8 hours to innovate the logo.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I'm just happy that I finished the logo, it wasn't very easy at all.

What I learned

I learned how to recreate logos in OnShape and learned from the many mistakes I made on my journey.

What's next for 3D Logo

I'm looking forward to 3D printing this in the future, it should come out nicely!
