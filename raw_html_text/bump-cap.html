Inspiration

Employee wellbeing and safety is paramount but innovation and data/reporting in this area is lacking

What it does

Accelerometer + Gyro embedded into hardhat/bump cap, enables real time data collection of head trauma/impacts to ensure nothing material goes unreported. Data collection over time will enable development of a platform to deliver insights and create data driven initiatives around employee safety
