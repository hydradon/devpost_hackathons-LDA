Inspiration

Everyone wants amazing deals. 'Murica. Impulse buying.

What it does

You see deals from merchants around you. Once you click on a deal, you get a short amount of time to either buy the product or lose the deal forever.

How I built it

I used React Native, firebase, Braintree API.

Challenges I ran into

The lack of frameworks and features due to the newness of react native and figuring out work arounds to make things work.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I have something made.

What I learned

I learned react native and how to keep trying after you keep hitting dead ends.

What's next for Flash

Canada Post/EasyPost shipping, shopify integration, Sharing deals with friends gets you better deals or more time to decide. See which and how many friends bought the product you're looking at.
