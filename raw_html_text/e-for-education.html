We aim to demolish the rote learning practices in our education system.

E for education aims to promote concept based education in Pakistan at the grassroots level. We hope to augment the curriculum taught in public schools by creating animated video content for STEM subjects and provide parents with an opportunity to get involved in their childrens learning process. E for education aims to build an online self learning platform along the lines of khanacademy that provides demonstrative videos that students can learn at their own pace

We built the web platform using bootstrap, javascript and php.

We hope to cover the entire primary school science curriculum
