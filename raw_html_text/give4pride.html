Inspiration

Completing the challenge of social reform and hitting the large audience Rebuilding the broken bridge of humanity among humans Providing the large platform to help both, those who all are in need and all who want to contribute Easy and effective way of helping others

Sometimes people want to donate stuff but they cant find the right person to donate so they end up throwing stuff So from our application donors can see the NGOs that are in need of the materials by which donors donate their materials to NGOs So its good to give materials to the needy ones rather than throwing it away because things that are waste for one could be necessity of others Our application works with the policy that one man’s trash is the next man’s treasure

What it does

GIVE4PRIDE brings NGOs and Donors from all over the world to a common platform so that we can donate materials to the needy ones and share our love
