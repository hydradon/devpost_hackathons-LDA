Inspiration

We just wanted to make a fun platforming game with an interesting mechanic that we hadn't seen before.

What it does

It's got some simple platforming controls, the player can spawn a platform either vertical or horizontal which will dissappear after a set amount of time. They use these platforms to get across the various obstacles presented to them.

How we built it

All of the programming was done in Unity, The textures were made with Paint.Net.

Challenges we ran into

We were very inexperienced with Unity so, almost every step came with a large amount of difficulty.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were pretty proud of how placing the force fields turned out. It looks and feels pretty smooth.

What we learned

Well, considering most people in the group had absolutely zero experience in Unity, I think we all learned a pretty decent amount about Unity and it's what we can do with it.

What's next for Project ForceField

Not really sure about this, we had a few plans we wanted to get to, but due to time restraints they were cut out. Maybe in the future we'll revisit Project Force Field and put in a few of the things we had planned to.
