Inspiration

We wanted to make many very different projects, so we decided to combine our ideas into one game.

How it works

It is powered by the amazing Unity program. You can use the keys "WASD" to move around, the mouse to look around, and use ctrl or left click to shoot a red cube.

Challenges I ran into

Transferring files between teammates. Last minute technical difficulties have caused the temporary removal of a dragon. It took a while, and was completely modeled in blender. Animating the dragon.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

A cool unity project, random generating room arrangements, animated dragon model.

What I learned

How to develop in unity and use Blender, as well as c# and js scripting.

What's next for the Maze

Adding the dragon. A definitive goal to the game. More features!
