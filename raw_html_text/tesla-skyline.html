• The one and only Tesla Skyline

Inspiration

Just thought it would be nice to build a car that could run

What it does

It should be driving, and we have a stepper motor on top, which could have had a sensor that made it avoid driving into walls.

How we built it

Mostly with Lego Mindstorm blocks, and some self-made electrical cables and components

Challenges we ran into

We needed a gearbox because of the components available, so that made us unable to turn the car the way we wanted. So we needed a new way to turn. Which made us rebuild the entire back of the car. Also we had problems with our electrical components and with our servo motors of which one stopped working

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We build the car and it actually ran

What we learned

Think twice It's a good idea to have a planned finished design before you start building to avoid mistakes How a car turns and why it is a good idea to have an inner set of gears

What's next for Tesla Skyline

It will transport Elon Musk over the martian plane.
