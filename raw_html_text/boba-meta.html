Inspiration

Boba's been trending. With people from all sorts of places craving boba milk tea, there have been attempts to decide on the best boba franchise out there. However, such a feat is infeasible, especially since the quality of boba drink usually depends on the management of an individual boba shop, rather than the chain as a whole. Thus, people should instead be comparing boba shops to other nearby boba shops. And Boba Meta was born.

What it does

Localized tier lists for Boba shops.

How we built it

ReactJS, Mapbox, Yelp, GCP Firestore, Figma, Bootstrap

Challenges we ran into

Firestore asynchronous calls, drag-and-drop templates, small team

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned

What's next for Boba Meta
