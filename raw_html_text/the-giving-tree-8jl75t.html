What it does

Connects non-profit organizations who are in need to specific supplies to donors who have those supplies but might not necessarily know who to give it to for most effective use

How I built it

Use Django to build a web app and the Google Maps API to help the donor find the closest organizations

Accomplishments that I'm proud of
