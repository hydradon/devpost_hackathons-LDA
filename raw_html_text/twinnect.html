Inspiration

In the busy lifestyle,Sports players often don't get time to connect with fans,get to know what fans are thinking about them,get to know where the majority of fan following is there,etc The same holds good for fans as well.They miss out on important updates,news,images regarding the players . Even they get tired of tweeting to the players asking for a reply/retweet. Now it is all Easy using this application.

How it works

For the Sports Player

Get a easier streaming look at the tweets
Get to see the filtered fan tweets based on the location
Get to know what fans are thinking using sentiment analysis based on location
Get a good knowledge of your followers based on location
Post a trivia either by typing or at random and pick out winners based on the answer
Pick out winners based on the best post(Sentiment analysis)
Pick out winners based on most retweeted post
Pick out winners based on new follower monthly

For the Fan

Get to know the player info in a single platform like news,tweets,images and web links
Ask question to a player and expect a reply at the earliest
Ask for help from a player and expect a retweet of the same at the very moment
Get picked as winner by answering trivia's
Get picked as winner by getting most retweets on your post
Get picked as winner by getting a good score on your tweets
