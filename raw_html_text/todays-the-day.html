Today's the Day

A Chrome extension made at Hack@Brown 2018 by Angie Kim, Sophia Chen, Sarah Nathanson, and Rachel Wang ⚡️⚡️

There's a host of different new tab extensions available, but there aren't many that don't require you to add in a ton of information that you already might keep elsewhere. We decided to use the Google Calendar API to put already-established events into Today's the Day, making integrating the extension with your existing work suite as simple as logging into your Google account.

The extension can be accessed on the Chrome Webstore here .
