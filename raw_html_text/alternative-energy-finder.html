Alternative Energy Finder

HackTJ 2016

Edit: This app doesn't work because for some reason Google Maps doesn't work.

This android app will help users in the DC area find and compare clean and renewable energy companies

This app is currently unfinished. For now, the cost comparison and efficiency comparison pages are unfinished, and there will more than just text when finished. There will be more features in the future, including interactive maps that can be used to find the most efficient or cost effective form of energy, as well as detailed data for any known energy company in the DC region that can be viewed in the app.
