Inspiration

Whether it be related to academics, music, or sports highlights, we always want to watch YouTube videos without having to leave our current browser window. Watching YouTube videos without accessing youtube.com, will make life easier and less distractful.

What it does

We created a Google Chrome extension that allows the user to search for youtube videos by providing a topic. The user can increase the video's screen size, navigate between related videos, and exit the player located in the bottom right of the screen.
