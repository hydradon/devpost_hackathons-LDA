Inspiration

We wanted to do a cool hack with Oculus VR, but take it one step further by adding gesture realization using Kinect

How it works

Kinect will read certain gestures and translate these into keyboard inputs. These inputs are fed into the Oculus VR causing the player to perform certain actions

Challenges I ran into

Getting objects to interact with each other was pretty hard

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It works! And it's super cool!

What I learned

Tons about developing for Kinect and Unity.
