Inspiration

Once upon a time I attended UB Hacking 2017. There, Major League Hacking was having a contest for hackers to come up with the best domain name. After spending a lot of time learning and hacking, pondering what makes a good domain name in between, we finally realized the truth about hacking. No good hack can come about without good snacks!
