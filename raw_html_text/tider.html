Inspiration

Tide pods are an expression of our appreciation for how we choose to express our hygiene and we wanted to make that accessible to those who wish to participate

What it does

This application allows you to sign up for an account and customize your own Tidepod and order it with your specifications for you or your loved one

How we built it

We built the application on React and used Express as a backend

Challenges we ran into

Login flow, Registration, User lifecycle and persistance

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to conditionally change the tidepod's color through canvas and we are super proud of it

What we learned

We learned how to use React, persist session storage, and how to manage component state
