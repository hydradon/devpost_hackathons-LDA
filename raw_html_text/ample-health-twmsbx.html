For small group practices, there’s often forced to three two options that big buys are using to collect on overdue bills. One is the dunning process where they sacrifice front staff hours to create, print, stamp, and mail threatening letters, they sell those accounts to collection agencies often for cents on the dollar or they write off the bad debt altogether.

Doctors get to focus on treating the patient and not chasing payments...patients remedied of the burden of debt with various payment options.
