Restaurhunt connects food-lovers to a network of the top-liked food in the area. Users can upload a picture of their meal for others to see, and meal-seekers can simply swipe right to “like” and learn more about that dish. By tapping on the image, users can see how many likes that picture has, as well as the price, restaurant, and author of the image. Start uploading and swiping away!
