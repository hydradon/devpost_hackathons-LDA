Inspiration

We had an original idea for a project, but then we found out LähiTapiola had Wellmo which did pretty much everything we had planned. This gave us the idea to take Wellmo even further!

What it does

Gamifies keeping yourself healthy.

How we built it

We used LähiTapiola customized Wellmo environment to gain data from users activities to create a gamified experience on keeping yourself healthy. Currently using Wellmo and polar loop 2 pedometer data. Walking challenge!

Challenges we ran into

Time constraint and local development environments slowness with huge data chunks provided by lähitapiola. Lack of ready-made data export tools.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

During the last full day we chatted with LähiTapiola to gain more info about the possibilities of Wellmo. They gave a call to the developers who in turn gave us a secret gateway for the data that our development polar loop 2 collected.

What we learned

Minimum viable product development is the way to go. Should've kept it even more simple so the end result would've been more polished. Gamification of health data was a learning experience. Since we both work in software development we noticed that we should try something even more off the chain during these hackathons.

What's next for Prener

Making it more dynamic so people can use any device they desire to collect their activity data
