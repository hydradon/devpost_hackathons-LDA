Inspiration

We like data analytics. We liked the idea of playing with stock data. I really wanted to dabble in d3.

What it does

It's a stock analytics and visualization tool.

How I built it

We hosted everything from the app to the database on AWS. We used ajax and d3.js to make it a responsive web app.
