I created this as a fun way for people to get creative inside an already popular app -> Instagram.

Tap the screen to enable/disable the brush. Open your mouth to increase brush size. Move your nose!

I built it using Spark AR, published it on Instagram and people loved it. It sits at 27.6 million impressions at the moment.
