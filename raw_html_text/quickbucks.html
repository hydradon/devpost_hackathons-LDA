Anyone who walks down the halls of MSU's buildings will notice the endless flyers stacked on top of each other. QuickBucks is the answer to people who offer research studies and surveys but are looking for a more modernized way of connecting with their community and wasting less paper. QuickBucks is used for both,people looking for some quick and easy cash, and researchers looking to pay participants for their studies. Participants can look through a list of entries of surveys and their corresponding payout and easily get in contact with the author of the entry. The researchers can submit entries with a title, description, compensation, and any other information. Our group build this using Swift and Xcode for the app as well as Firebase for the database of surveys. In the future of QuickBucks, we hope to add authentication and user profiles to automatically filter out some surveys based on requirements. We also would like to add filters for tags and sorting for survey entries.
