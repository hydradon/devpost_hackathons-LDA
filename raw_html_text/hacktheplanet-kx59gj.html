How can we get more motorcyclists on the road with the help of today's technology?

We know that cyclists already use software applications primarily for personal interests, however the problem still persists - a lack of community among cyclists, let alone a reason to hop in . To us, riding a motorcycle is less of a means of transportation and more of a multi-sensory experience .

The greatest amount of untapped potential in the motorcycle market lies behind today's would-be customers. To these people, either owning a motorbike would be too big an investment or merely driving one might be too real of a danger. Our app aims offers both a customizably-scenic experience as well as a low-cost alternative to travel, but more importantly, a layman's guilt-free excuse to ride .
