Inspiration

Most students are age have it rough finding the time, energy, and money to cook our own meals. A lot of college students often resort to eating cheaper junk food. Our team of four didn't think this had to be the case, and with RecipeReady anyone can find the time to enjoy making healthy satisfying meals.

What it does

RecipeReady is an Android app that prioritizes the user's choice. It contains a search engine that looks at how much time you have and your tastes to determine the best recipe suggestions. RecipeReady keeps track of your favorite recipes, the statistics of how often you view each recipe, and the trends of the most popular recipes to give you quick recommendations.

How I built it

RecipeReady is an Android application made in Android Studio using Java. We used Android Studio APIs like Navigation View, Scroll View, Search View, and List View.
