Inspiration

Error in everyday diagnosis that stems from error in patient's memory of when the symptoms started/persisted and other lifestyle factors possibly contributing to illness

What it does

Tracks symptoms, activity, sleep, diet, and more up to your doctor's appointment from when you made the appointment, or for post operation monitoring

How I built it

Made an app prototype using android studio(java language) and google slides for business pitch

Challenges I ran into

Learning java/android studios in 36 hours, coming up with an original, helpful idea

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Making an app prototype from scratch in 36 hours with no prior experience

What I learned

Java, communication/teamwork skills, how to brainstorm creative and original ideas

What's next for FirstStep Symptoms

Further app development, meet with doctors to discuss implementing the app in their appointment-making process
