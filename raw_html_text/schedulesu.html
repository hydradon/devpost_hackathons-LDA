Inspiration

I am always having a tough time finding the perfect schedule that works for me when scheduling classes for a new semester.

What it does

The app will prompt students for their courses, preferred teachers, preferred times, and preferred day of the week and it will generate all possible schedules that work for the given criteria.

How we built it

We used the MEAN stack and AWS services for web hosting.

Challenges we ran into

The asynchronicity of node database calls and how to use angular to access endpoints and different routes.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of finishing the app in 24 hours and it is a viable, usable app for all students at rowan. We also learned a lot about angular and a ton of new ways it can be used.

What we learned

We learned a lot about angular.js and node.js and how different endpoints call different routes.

What's next for SchedulesU

We would like to try and peruse this app and offer it to Rowan for their students.
