Inspiration

I really wanted to learn some basic html this weekend.

What it does

It's literally just pictures of cats with corn for hats. It's nice to look at.

How I built it

I put the corn on the cats heads using MSPaint. The rest was just basic HTML.

Challenges I ran into

My team literally just left suddenly Saturday night (with our much fancier project), so I just started from scratch.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Centering the cats nicely on the page. It was actually a little difficult and took some tinkering. Also the name of the page. It's very punny in my opinion.

What I learned

Basics of HTML, and that cats are even cuter with corn on their heads
