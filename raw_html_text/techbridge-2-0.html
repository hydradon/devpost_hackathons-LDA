Inspiration

Having a one-year-old daughter, I wanted to get behind a cause that would inspire her to pursue a career in STEM

What it does

We built a web app in MEAN that would provide interested parents and volunteers the ability to enter their information saving Techbridge time with manual entry.

How we built it

We used typeform to create a great UX for our end-users. This information is then sent to Google Sheets via Zapier and then sent to their salesforce Org using Zapier.

Challenges we ran into

Being able to integrate Stripe without having to use Docusign.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Ability to integrate Salesforce and Typeform to provide a working prototype to the team.

What we learned

How much you can get done in 24 hours

What's next for TechBridge 2.0

We need to build the donations page to allow people to support the cause
