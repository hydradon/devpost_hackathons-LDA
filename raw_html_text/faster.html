Inspiration

fastER was inspired by a close friend's experience when he had to visit the Emergency Room

What it does

In a nutshell, fastER makes it easier and more efficient for a patient to receive emergency medical services. We use medical databases to assess which facility a patient should go to during an emergency. We prepare patients and health providers with expediency by allowing medical forms to be filled out in advance in case of injury. Finally, we allow hospitals to analyze patient priority based on the severity of injury to create a more equitable medical experience.

How I built it

iOS development and SQL databases

What's next for fastER

fastER wants to create more accurate and integrated services by reaching out to local medical facilities. We think that our service is scalable and vital to bridge the gap between injury and treatment.
