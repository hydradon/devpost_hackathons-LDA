• Wellness Daily Alexa Skill

Inspiration :

The inspiration was to build a platform that helps oneself to experience wellness and its incentivies to live a longer healthy life.

What it does:

You ask Wellness Daily Alexa skill for tip of the day, that can help you streamline your wellness goals. Wellness Daily is your daily companion on your journey to greater wellness and health. The skill automates daily wellness goals by giving a tip of the day - as what you do everyday matters more then what you do every once in a while.

How I built it:

Wellness Daily was built using Alexa Skill kit, Amazon Lambda and NodeJS hosted on Amazon AWS.

Challenges:

It was challenge to curate the wellness tip, as wellness is a complete integration of body, mind and spirit.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I'm proud to build this skill and the journey that lead me to bring this skill to help oneself to experience wellness.

What I learned

It has been a great learning experience. I have learnt about Amazon services such as Lambda, Alexa Skill kit.

What's next for Wellness Daily

My vision is to extend this skill to enable personalized wellness tips and allow users to set goal that can be measured and tracked. To build Holistic wellness management (including body, mind and spirit).
