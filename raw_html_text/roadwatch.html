Inspiration

We wanted to make something with open data that is useful to motorists in DC.

How it works

The user's Pebble Watch vibrates and changes its screen when near a traffic [obj], to alert the user, making him or her more aware, and safer. It uses data from DC's opendata

Challenges we ran into

Finding a good way to represent and query the location data.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It works!
