Inspiration

Yo! Everytime we reach an airport in a new country, we think about taking a taxi. Quite often, it turns out to be an expensive affair. We realize that there are 100s of other people like us trying to take a taxi to same parts of the city. YoNo eliminates the communication boundaries presented by a new environment by connecting people in similar geographical areas to convene as they wish by saying Yo! or No! . Once these people agree to convene with another person(s), they are added to a group chat where they can figure out the logistics.

What it does

You Taxi, Me Taxi -> We Taxi
