Inspiration

Want to improve quality of healthcare in NYC.

What it does

It categorize the severity of a patient by asking questions and generates a waiting time for the patient. It also gives an ESI measuring according to the severity.

How I built it

Used firebase, node.js, angular, express and built with love

Challenges I ran into

Implementing IBM watson for the chatbot was challenging.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

the teamwork, and endurance we were able to persevere

What I learned

planning is everything, and also various frameworks of javascript.

What's next for Get Healed

build a responder side app, and a database which can be utilized by any healthcare system to improve on quality of care.
