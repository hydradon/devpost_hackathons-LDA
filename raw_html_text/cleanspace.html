Inspiration

We got the inspiration from the TrashTag challenge and we realize the lack of centralized information about it. So decided to create a platform where volunteers can gather together and beautify public spaces.

What it does

This is a web application where people can upload images of areas needing to be clean. Create teams and beautify our common areas.

How we built it

We built our platform using React JS.

Challenges we ran into

We had issues at first coming up with the programing language we wanted to use. At first, we used WordPress but we couldn't edit how we wanted, so we decided to use React Instead and that solved our problem.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of our idea and plan to turn it into a movement.

What we learned

We learn that if we come together as a team, awesome things can happen. The idea of building more awareness to keep our community clean is big and help our environment as well.

What's next for CleanSpacenp

The next step for CleanSpacenp is to partner with other non-profits to keep spreading awareness and expand our community.
