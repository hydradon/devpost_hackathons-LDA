Inspiration

Despite technologies being introduced every now and then, particularly with digital banking, the number of unbankables still has not decreased drastically. We wanted to create an application that will also cater to users who will not need to have Internet access to do transactions.

What it does

Make payments digital and transactions offline, plus locate nearby local merchants.

How I built it

We made use of different technologies, highlight APIS provided by FusionFabric.cloud: Payments API, Bills Payment API, POS and Transactions API, Nexmo API.

Challenges I ran into

Addiitional knowledge for utilizing FusionFabric.cloud APIs.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We were able to create the prototype which includes user applicaiton and authentication, QR transaction of payment and receipt.

What I learned

Technical knowledge especially in implementing blockchain, focused sectors for potential market

What's next for FINASCASH

AI analytics for merchants, SMS mode of payment, linking of bank cards
