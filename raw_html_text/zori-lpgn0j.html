Inspiration

Struggles when deciding what to wear on certain days due to weather and current location for the day

What it does

Suggestions outfits for the user based on style, weather, location, event, etc

How I built it

We used HTML, CSS, Javascript (Jquery, Parse, Slick, Bootstrap), and different APIs including those from Google Maps, Facebook, and Simple Weather

Challenges I ran into

Creating the suggestion algorithm that takes into account style of the location along with weather conditions and the users' personal style

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Successfully outputting outfit suggestions, and connection the accounts users' are able to back to a backend database

What I learned

How to properly format websites and UIs, and how to integrate different APIs to successfully implement our goals

What's next for Zori

Creating a social media aspect that allows users to share their daily outfits, and further obtain suggestions based events, more specific times of days, etc
