PatientText

PatientText translates medical abbreviations to plain, understandable text.

Installations

PatientText is a Chrome extension and can be found in the Chrome Web Store.

Usage

While viewing medical records or test results, click the PatientText icon to replace medical abbreviations with their expanded forms.
