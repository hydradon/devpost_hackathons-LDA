HackRice

Strength Builder is a system that allows users to be able to quantify their rehabilitation progress. Usually Stroke victims are given 6 months to recover as much as they can. These are called the critical months. Once these are done, the recovery is entirely up to them. Some patients usually become very depressed, because they can not see their progress. This is very debilitating because you need to believe you can recover in order to continue recovery. We built this system so that people can visually see their progress no matter how small it is. This will hopefully restore some faith to the victims and allow them to continue rehabilitation so that they can regain as much of their abilities as they can and believe.
