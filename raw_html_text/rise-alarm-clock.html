Inspiration

The collective and motivated attitude of decisions made by a team. There's a certain aspect of commitment where you show up for yourself and for your teammates

What it does

An alarm is initiated for instances such as group meetings or events where it is pertinent that all members of the group are present and punctual. When invites are sent to group members, they 'commit' to the alarm, communicating that they will also be accountable for their attendance.

How we built it

Our back-end drew large inspiration from the desired user experience features, which led to our decision in building it on an Android Application platform. We meticulously refined the user experience to guide teams

Challenges we ran into

  • We originally wanted to use email verification as a method of user authentication, but we found difficulties finding the best methods in integrating this into our project.
