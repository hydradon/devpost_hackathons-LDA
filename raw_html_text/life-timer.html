• Life timer

Inspiration:

In general, people are taking their healthy lives for granted. on the other hand, general populations are harming themselves chronically. This program is designed to calculate how much time an individual has left by research. Sounds terrifying, doesn't it? But better safe than sorry. Keep a close monitor to your personal life with our program, a lifeguard that watch and protect you be healthy starting today.

What it does

First of all, we take your date of birth on record and we also gather information from participant's personal lives, then pull routine information based on general populations into consideration and calculate the most accurate time of an individual has left. Give hints to those who desire to live longer.

How I built it:

We built it by HTML website and host it on the Google cloud platform, we tried to use google firebase to simplify the process however is tough to set up. So we just simply switch to the regular virtual instance to host our server. Also, I created a link called: http://athenahack.org for this website.
