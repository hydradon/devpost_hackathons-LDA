We wanted to make an application that allowed users to gracefully crowd-source a playlist for their enjoyment.

We used Twilio to allow party goers to send music requests to our webpage. From there the requests are organized into a queue where the selected songs are played sequentially for all to enjoy.

Most of the tools we used were completely new to us. We all learned how to use several api's and libraries and gained little more experience with web development.
