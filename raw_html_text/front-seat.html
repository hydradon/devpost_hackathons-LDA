• Pick who rides in the front seat

Inspiration

Often kids, teenagers and even adults fight over who gets to ride in the front seat of the vehicle. This Alexa skill helps by randomly choosing who gets to sit up front.

What it does

Alexa collects the name from up to five passengers. She asks for animal names to make the process more fun. Once the names are collected, Alexa randomly chooses a winner.

How I built it

Storyline, a prototyping and configuration tool used for creating Amazon Alexa skills.

Challenges I ran into

Enabling being able to select from two to five passengers. Learning how to make an accompanying video.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Adding a variety of Alexa responses to keep the skill interesting for repeated use. Keeping the process fun. Also I created my very first video for this competition, and also uploaded a video to Youtube for the first time.

What I learned

Use of variables to manage the animal names. Specifically used one of the list variable types, AMAZON.Animals.

What's next for Front Seat

Adding some sound effects.
