Inspiration

The inability to find information about how specific foods at restaurants are rated. You can find a rating of the restaurant itself, but you don't know how a specific meal rates.

What it does

A web app for rating food of restaurants instead of rating the restaurants.

How we built it

Built using flask, sqlalchemy, python, html, css, and javascript.

Challenges we ran into

The team is inexperienced with web design and had to learn everything from scratch.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We made something that somewhat functions.

What we learned

We learned web design skills.

What's next for GrubGuru

We want to increase the functionality of our web app. We reserved grubguru.net to use as our app's Domain.
