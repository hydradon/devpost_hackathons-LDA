Oh no! A mean old wizard has stolen all the fairy tales and taken them to a secret island far away! Can you help them remember who they are to break the spell that holds them captive?

Fairy Tale Island is a cute narrative quiz game for kids. Explore the island to find classic fairy tales, who have forgotten who they are. Reveal their identity by answering questions about their tale and finally calling out their name! Of course you can always get a tip, if you're stuck ;)
