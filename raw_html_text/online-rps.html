Online RPS

We were planning to make an online 1v1 RockPaperScissors game which matches players to rooms automatically using a node.js server with socket.io, with an implemented "credits" system that would allow players to bet their credits on their matches (hence why we needed a login system with firebase). However, we didn't get around to completing the credit system. So, at the moment you can just use this website to play RPS against people online.
