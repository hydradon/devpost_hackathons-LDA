Inspiration

we were inspired by pollution in the city and what better represents the city than the MTA. We wanted to show that pollution affects the speed of the MTA due to transit fires

What it does

this site is a text adventure game. you get to choose what choices you make and then we show you if you negatively affect the MTA or positively

How we built it

we built it using cloud 9 the online coding platform. we divided the work, one did drawings, one did the CSS and one did the HTML for the site

Challenges we ran into

the challenges we ran into were getting things to stay in the position we put them in

Accomplishments that we're proud of

we are proud of the whole site. we accomplished almost all of what we wanted to do

What we learned

we learned about how much we affect our daily lives with pollution

What's next for SaviorCity

we plan on making SaviorCity into a big website that can be a teaching platform for all.
