Avi Taylor is a new kind of real world hybrid AI travel agent. Avi is an anime character who lives in the places you want to visit and can converse with you like a dear pen pal as you make travel plans together.

Avi is powered by API.ai's Dialogflow, with open-ended chit-chat powered by OpenAI's GPT-2 - an AI that is capable of writing novels. Avi's Unity 3D character has automated lipsync through SALSA driven by the audio stream generated from text to speech by Wavenet. Avi's travel results are powered by Amadeus API.

Here are some things that you can say to Avi Taylor:

"Dance for me"

"Set my home airport to LAX"

"I want to go to Paris"

"I want to fly from NYC"

Select each search result to view recommended hotels. (Coming soon, POIs nearby, too!)
