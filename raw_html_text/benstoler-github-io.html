TranSafe

We were inspired by Ford's discussion on how useful programming skills could be when applied to the real and severe problems faced by the Detroit public. We thought that one of the best ways we could improve their quality of life and their relationship with their government would be to create an app that interfaces between bus data and crime data, all information provided by the government, to inspire a much safer and effective means of transportation.
