Inspiration/What it does

GoodLife is a non-profit organization that seeks to make a difference for those who are homeless. By bringing leftover food from local restaurants and facilitating a tutoring program, GoodLife brings the necessary resources to help and support those in need. However, what makes GoodLife unique is that fact that our organization builds an unwavering support system to aid the homeless pursue their endeavors and make their own imprint in this world.

How we built it

We used HTML, Javscript, and CSS to build this website.

Challenges we ran into

We had trouble with integrating the Google Maps API.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of our ability to bring our vision to life and create something that has the potential to make an impact on the world.

What we learned

We learned how to code different aspects of websites and create functional parts.

What's next for GoodLife

We would like to continue working on this project and bringing it into fruition by creating an app version of it. We also want to completely integrate the google maps API and create a functional database.
