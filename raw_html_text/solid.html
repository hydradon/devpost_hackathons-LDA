Solid DApp

Solid is a Curated list of True Statements. It allows anyone to submit a statement and make it open for general public evaluation.

The public is invited to refuse other people's allegations, proving the statement is not true by staking a small fee in ETH, and the counter-argument.

All the statement refusal submissions are sent to a dispute resolution layer, that randomly select jurors to evaluate which argument is the winner in the discussion. The winner of the dispute wins it's staked fee back + the "challenger's fee", and the loser loses it's staked fee.

Solid incentivizes the fair discussion and exchange of opinions about any chosen subject, providing a decentralized curated list of statements and open discussion space, that rewards true allegations, and penalizes the fake news.
