Improving on existing technologies to integrate clothing with therapeutic remedies.

Infrared is already known to be effective in therapeutic treatment for muscle recovery, however, when it comes to wearables, the technology is still quite limited, and there is a lot of room for improvement. With our project vision, our plan is to integrate existing Infrared technologies for wearable infrared clothing. with the ability to be controlled wirelessly, this technology will help to advance this field of therapy, and to enhance the utility of wearables in our daily lives and in recovery progress.
