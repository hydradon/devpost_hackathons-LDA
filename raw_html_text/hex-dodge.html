Inspiration

I want to build greater apps that make a positive impact on peoples everyday life. That is why I produced this game to hopefully lower stress levels and entertain players.

What it does

This game main concept is to dodge the hexagons flying at you increasing in speed as you tap to change your direction.

How I built it

I built this app in xcode useing swift and did the graphics in inkscape.

Challenges I ran into

Completely learning graphic design in this apps production along with uploading it to the app store as it was my first app.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The overall minimalistic design and difficulty of the game

What I learned

I learned skills in xcode, swift, and inkscpae that can be used to produce more complex and advanced apps.

What's next for Hex Dodge

I plan on updating with new features biannually and going on to more impactful apps.
