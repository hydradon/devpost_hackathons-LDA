You are stuck on a dessert island with nothing but a golf ball and some sentient fruit. What do you do next? Fruit lawn bowling of course! The rules are simple, get your fruit the closest to the white ball to score points. The fruit with the most points at the end wins. Play with up to four people in by passing the headset or play against a variety of FruitBotz.
