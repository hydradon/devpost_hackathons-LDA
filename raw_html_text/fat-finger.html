Inspiration

We were tired on how slow corporate IT is to deliver applications to the business. We wanted to build a drag and drop platform so business users who are experts in their process can build and improve their apps in seconds.

What it does

Drag and drop visual application builder, super easy big finger apps designed for non-it people. Machine-learning to offer real-time operational "coaching tips" to users on the fly, dashboards to provide never before seen insights into operational activity.
