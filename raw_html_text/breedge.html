Inspiration

People love watching other peoples lives every day – think Youtube, Snapchat, Instagram. We feel like these people are part of our lives even though this one-to-many communication is very one-way. Almost like a real life friend that doesn’t talk back to us. What kind of friend is that?

What it does

With Breedge you can become more involved and enjoy a better community feeling by easily having conversations with people you may have only had a 1-way friendship with.

Breedge allows everyone to have a conversation with people who have many followers by only showing popular messages and allowing group voting responses.
