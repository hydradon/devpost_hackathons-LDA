Inspiration

So it's finally here. The new shiny computer you've been waiting for weeks. So you boot it up, download Chrome and your favorite editor... and start hacking!

But... oh, wait! All your settings that you spent so many nights tuning up to the most insane detail are gone.

What it does

Meet Crispy Sync, or as you'll get to know it, crispy . It's your one-stop shop for syncing all kinds of .bashrc , .gitconfig , and many more!

Wanna see how easy it is? Just crispy upload-app bash and all your bash files will be uploaded and kept up to date as you keep improving your configuration!
