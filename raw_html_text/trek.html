Inspiration

The inflating expenses on ridesharing apps like Uber that puts a lot of burden on drivers leaving them with very little in profit.

What it does

Peer to Peer payment between, where riders can set the fare they can pay and drivers can choose the most feasible route.

How we built it

Client Server architecture with a DB in between to store metadata that would bog down our blockchain.

Challenges we ran into

We ran into a few issues with debugging our smart contracts while running the testnet through metamask but quick switched platforms to fix the issue.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of our teamwork and ingenuity.

What we learned

We learned a lot about some of the issues with Blockchain at scale and some of the propose ways to deal with it.
