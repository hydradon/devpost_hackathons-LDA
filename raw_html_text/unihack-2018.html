SafeWay

We're aiming to make the streets safer for you by providing a safety-based navigation service. Whereas popular services such as Google Maps and Apple Maps route you to your destination purely based on "efficiency" and speed (it's not efficient if you get hurt), SafeWay gives you the best of both worlds!

SafeWay uses the latest crime statistics to calculate the safest route, always getting you home safely.
