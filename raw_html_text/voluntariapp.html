VoluntariApp

Voluntari was born on the idea that we all waste time, and time is precious. Time can be spent on many different ways and we have a choice of what to do with it. Voluntari helps you take the best decisions and use your time on what really matters; helping others. At the end of the day, helping others is helping yourself.

Voluntari helps you

  • Find your inner volunteer
  • Be the change our world needs
