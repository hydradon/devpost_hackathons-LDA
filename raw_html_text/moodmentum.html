moodmentum

The emotional influence of the internet and social media browsing often cause heightened stress or anxiety for the receiver of the content. We strive to use a simple mechanism - a Chrome extension that is activated upon opening a new tab - to help chrome users be more aware of their mood as they browse the web by providing cute cat gif responses to each mood. The simple reminder that creates awareness of one's emotional state of mind helps the user acknowledge his/her emotions before those emotions cause backlash or further stress.
