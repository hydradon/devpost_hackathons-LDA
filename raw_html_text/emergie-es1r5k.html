We are Emergie, a logistic optimization platform, offering a solution for fleet transport to improve energy efficiency and save logistic cost. One crucial issue of energy inefficiencies in fleet transport is a utilization loss from a lack of space management, such as an empty backhaul. With Emergie, we create a logistic management solution for routing decision, space management and collect all logistic data, which not only benefits a fleet space owner, but also save CO2 emission and reduce fuel consumption for environment.
