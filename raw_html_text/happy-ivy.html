Inspiration

Want to aid people with bi-polar disease.

What it will do

Research shows, people who suffer from bi-polar disorder have greater difficulty keeping up with daily tasks and responsibilities. Our app, will serve as a reminder to stay on top of these tasks and will reward the user along the way. After completing tasks, they will earn points which will help grow a plant inside the app and help strengthen the reward system.

How we plan to build it

Adobe XD, Xcode, swift
