What it does

Drunculator calculates the amount of alcohol that should be bought for the party. Basing on the parameters, provided by a user about a future party, Drunculator makes a clear list of what to buy to achieve the desired level of euphoria.

How we built it

Sitting and coding for 32 hours

Challenges we ran into

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Made a ready-to-use product in less than 30 hours. It is a fully functional multiplatform app, that strikes right into user's needs.
