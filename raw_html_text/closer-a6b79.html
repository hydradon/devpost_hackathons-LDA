Very often, old friends come to Silicon Valley and we have no idea they are here. Sometimes, two friends are visiting Paris at the same time but are not aware they could meet. The problem extends for hardwares as well. On operations of many industries such as oil or automobile, engineers can not track their equipments and devices on a real time basis.

Closer? solves the problem of proximity identification for people and devices.

It shows you who are the people or devices that are changing status in relation to your current position.

Whenever a device is crossing a limit distance, independent of the movement direction, you are notified.

People can simply access the system anonymously, type the id of a device and done.

With Closer? we hope to provide people an easy way of knowing where are the things they most care about in real time.
