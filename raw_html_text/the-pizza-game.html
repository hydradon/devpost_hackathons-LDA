Inspiration

I'm an artist, and I like to make games. Both Kermit and pizza are iconic (college meme-wise and in American media), so I wanted to make a game including them.

What it does

This game is an asteroids reskin.

How I built it

Using GameMaker, its scripting language, and Photoshop for the graphics.

Challenges I ran into

I was going to make a level 2 that was a reskin of snake, but I ran out of time! A lot of the challenges were involved in enemy movement: the sprite animation, spawning points and timing, tracking AI.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I'm proud of my results graphically! The Kermits look very nice. The way that the enemies track the player also pleases me.

What I learned

This was my first time using GameMaker- I'm surprised that it's more powerful than I realized.

What's next for the pizza game

Level 2- Snake (Maybe a level 3?)
