Inspiration

The fact that even the most specific queries can lead to unuseful suggestions and the lack of an independent tool to fix the issue/

What it does

Allows the user to like/dislike suggestions based on his query and later fishes out interesting trends/analysis in visual forms.

How I built it

We built an app on Android.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of
