Inspiration

This idea was inspired after I read articles about women undermining themselves through "little phrases" such as "just" and "I'm sorry". I then realized that I am guilty of making these mistakes.

What it does

Unapologetic is a custom keyboard that alerts you when you use the words/phrased, "just", "I'm sorry", and "Sorry". This is to encourage users to be more conscious of when they use the "little phrases" and think of alternative ways to phrase their sentences.
