Inspiration

After cancellation of the FIT program in Ontario, corporations lost the economic incentive to implement photovoltaic systems. The cancellation of 758 projects led to the demise of the solar market. The biggest barrier to solar energy is its high cost. Most corporations, small or big, cannot afford the immediate expenses of the project implementation. Ultimately, the concept of renewable energy through solar will remain unattractive amongst the private sector, as energy usage continues to grow and our earth continues to deteriorate. Which is where imperium comes in.

What it does

Our mission is to provide clients with an action plan that clarifies why they are an ideal client and shows the projected impacts of the transition and then most importantly, facilitate the interaction with investment seekers who would mitigate the upfront costs and ultimately lead our communities to a better environment.
