Inspiration

Inspired by The Bail Project, a 501(c)(3), nonprofit organization, we look to stop mass incarceration starting from a key source: bail.

What it does

It crowd funds bail for people who share their stories on the app.

How I built it

App made in Android Studio with backend powered by Firebase.

Challenges I ran into

First time making a mobile application.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

For the most part, the app looks clean and has some functionality.

What I learned

Probably should work with a team.

What's next for HeyBailMe

Implement a better way of user and payment authentication. There needs a way for users to get follow-up notifications about the cases they support.
