• EmpowHer:SF

Imagine you’re brainstorming ideas for volunteer day with your coworkers and the only options your team come up with are beach clean up and soup kitchen.

But did you know that in 2016 alone, there were 500 cases of human trafficking right here in our beloved city of San Francisco?

We came up with a simple solution of creating a web app that bridges the needs of women’s shelters in the city with whatever, you, SF working professionals, want to donate. While at the same time, increasing awareness of the issues by encouraging sharing on social media.

This will ultimately solve the biggest challenge that safety houses in San Francisco have, which is lack of funding, while tapping into the power of working professionals in the city.
