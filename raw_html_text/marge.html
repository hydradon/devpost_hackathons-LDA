Marge, the ScaleFactor chatbot, is a personal financial assistant. She helps business owners stay up to date with their important financial information, especially when they need it most: on-the-go.

Marge helps users run their business by answering important questions that can be found in a user’s accounting file -- things like “What are my sales for the month?,” “How much do my customers owe me?” or “What’s my cash balance?”. Users can chat with Marge 24/7 in the ScaleFactor platform and via text message.

Marge is more than a customer service chatbot. She understands and delivers on requests for financial data. She also prompts users to take important business actions, such as paying a bill. Users have human-like interactions with Marge and trust her deliver accurate information when they need it.
