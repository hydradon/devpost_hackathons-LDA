Inspiration

We were inspired to create an app that will help youth better manage their finances. As students, our biggest expense is going out to eat, so we centre our design around food expense.

What it does

It helps you keep track of daily food expenses and provides the lowest prices on products based on store locations and whether they do price matching or not.

How we built it

It was built through Figma.

Challenges we ran into

Challenges we ran into was learning how to use Figma and learning how to hyperlink our pages together.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Accomplishments that we're proud of is being able to come up with a solution to a common problem all students face and being able to execute our idea as a team.

What we learned

We learned how to use Figma and Principle animation skills.

What's next for Savour

What's next for Savour is building an iOS and having it available for students usage.
