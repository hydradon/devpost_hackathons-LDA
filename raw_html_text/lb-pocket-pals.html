Inspiration

We were feeling pretty pressured under the stress of the hackathon, so we made something to help relieve our stress!

What it does

It loops through motivational quotes and cute photos of dogs, don't worry about where we got the pictures. The music is also a nice touch.

How we built it

We built it with HTML, CSS, JS, and json files. Moreover, we used domain.com to host our site for the hack.

Challenges we ran into

Brainstorming a project that was possible with our knowledge and under the time restraints given.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We're proud we came up with an end product after iterating through a lot of different ideas.

What we learned

We learned a lot about hosting domains, front end development, and back end development.

What's next for LB Pocket Pals!
