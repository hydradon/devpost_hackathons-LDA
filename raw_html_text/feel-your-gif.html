Inspiration

We really wanted to play around with AR, and initially wanted to combine the two features. However, we ran into problems regarding how the webcam is accessed (it cant be used by both AR and Giphy/Clarifai at the same time), so we split up the project.

What it does

AR gifs will project a gif of your search query onto a marker (in this case, a phone) with a 2d or 3d rotating object of the gif. Emotion gifs tries to guess your current emotion based on your facial expression using our own trained Clarifai emotions, and plays a soundtrack to it
