Helping to find missing children

About: Type: Web App The app allows the gaurdian(s) of a missing child to: Create a new profile to log in and fill out a form for missing child Update profile with additional information (eg. a more recent photo) Profile of missing child then adds to a map which shows last seen areas of missing children The app allows all users to: View maps with missing children hotspots View news feed View missing children Post to page

Built: Using html, javascript and css

Challenges: Group Members were all new to all languages mentioned Lack of knowledge disallowed group from further enhancing and bringing forward certain ideas to app

Accomplishments: Group all learnt basics of all languages mentioned

What I learned: The need for an app with our ideas

What's next: Group will continue to keep in touch to make app better
