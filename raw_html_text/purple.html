Purple is a modern financial planning website focused on making investing for your future simpler. After entering their basic financial information, new users are able to create their own customizable retirement plans.

Purple supports multiple types of investment instruments, including 401(k)s, IRAs, bonds, and traditional savings accounts. All of the user's inputted data is used to calculate a projection of their retirement savings as well the particular level of risk for their financial situation.
