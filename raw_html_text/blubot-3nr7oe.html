bluBot helps identify what can and can’t be recycled. If you’re unsure of whether or not something can be recycled, it’s always better to throw it in the garbage instead. Contamination in recycling streams leads to a less efficient system, costs cities lost revenue, and leads to recyclable material that needs to be trashed instead.
