What does it do

Application is used by medical professionals to identify cardiac rhythm management devices (CRMDs: pacemakers, ICDs, CRTDs) simply by looking at the Xray of the patient.

How does it help doctors

Help in identifying the type of device

Quick and easy identification of the manufacturer of the device

Useful in devices at end-of-life where the programmer is unable to communicate with the device

What does it offer beyond current practice

This application is meant to simplify and expedite the work of medical practitioners by providing timely access to information and device identification.

What does it offer beyond current practice

The next step will be to take a picture of the xray and the device and use image recognition and machine learning to identify the device.
