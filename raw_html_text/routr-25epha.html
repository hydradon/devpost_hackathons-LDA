Have you ever wished for a fun, addictive game to play in your Avast browser when you have no internet connection?

Routr is a simple but engaging network-building game. Connect nodes to allow packets to travel around, and don't let too many packets build up at a node.

Watch out for infected nodes, which may spread infected packets around your carefully-crafted network! Add firewalls to your network, and use the Avast antivirus tool to disinfect nodes.

How long can your network survive?
