Expense Buddy is a mobile application created with Flutter, Dart, and Firebase to bridge the gap between personal finances and group expenses. Our group, Evelyn, Hadeel, Nadeen, and Sarah, worked together to develop an application with the goal to make budgeting for group activities easier.

The ultimate goal was to provide an easier way for young adults to manage their money while focusing on having fun.
