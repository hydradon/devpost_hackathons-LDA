• Pronoun Checker

It's a Chrome extension that lets you record which pronouns your friends use! Then it'll display their pronouns next to their name on Facebook Messenger and detect when you might accidentally be misgendering them. If it sees you use someone's name and the wrong pronoun at the same time, it'll give you a friendly reminder of which pronouns they use.
