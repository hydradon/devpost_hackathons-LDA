• Arduino small scale solution where we could simulate energy generation/usage levels with knobs to feed data into the trading engine

Inspiration

Currently, homeowners with solar panels cannot compete on a level playing field with industry monopolies in energy generations when trying to sell their excess. We wanted to build a solution where competition is fair for everyone, no matter how big or small their energy use/generation.

What it does

GenerationZ is a solution where energy producers who have excess put a 'sell' order onto the cloud platform, and any energy user (the vast majority of the population) put in 'buy' orders or buy up existing sell orders, very similar to how a stock exchange or crypto exchange works.

How I built it

We built the trading engine off the open source solution liquibook, and built a small scale model with an Arduino microcontroller.

Challenges I ran into

Researching current market solutions, code issues

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

A working proof of concept and an idea we want to build off of

What I learned
