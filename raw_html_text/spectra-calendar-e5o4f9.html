Inspiration: The creators were inspired by the bullet journal to create a new calendar that allowed you to customize and organize your life in an interactive and aesthetically pleasing way.

What it does

This application’s goal is to promote productivity and progress in a customizable way. Users are able to write daily tasks, record memories, schedule appointments, and much more.
