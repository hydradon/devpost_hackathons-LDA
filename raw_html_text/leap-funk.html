#LeapFunk is my first time working with the Leap Motion Controller and Three.js . This 3D browser game is inspired by a bunch of popular android games under the name 'space tube' with the objective being pretty straightforward, like in any other arcade game - stay alive as long as possible and evade the obstacles coming your way. Control is fully gesture-based. Apart from basic controls, the roll of the hand towards left or right causes a rotation of the world in the respective sense, giving more space to play around with. Features currently in development are fist gestures to burst through the obstacles, power-ups and some aesthetics. Would love some inputs about the game or code. Cheers!

May the funk be with you!!

PS : Mind the collisions with obstacles; they might be a little marginal. I'll fix that soon and add keyboard control to play without the device too.
