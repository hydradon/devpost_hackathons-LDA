• Gameplay

We wanted to make a horror game that had mystery and combat. It would have had sprites moving randomly around under the black screen overlay. The only viewable piece would be a hole that showed your convertible going through the forest. We tried to include some quirky elements in the sprites as well as the background. With more allotted time we would have added sounds and worked more on the graphics and collisions.
