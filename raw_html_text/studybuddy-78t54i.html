In order to help students achieve their academic goals, we wanted to create a platform to meet other students with the same aim. StudyBuddy allows user to enroll in a course they would require assistance in, and help find other people who would be interested in finding a study partner for the same course. This way, it's a win-win situation for everyone who wants to excel in their college life!
