Inspiration

CryptoCasino came out of our idea of wanting to compete against others in a high risk versus high reward style game.

What it does

CryptoCasino is a game run on an Etherium smart contract that allows players to compete against each other with the hope of receiving the overall satisfaction and reward of winning the entire pot of ether.

How we built it

We developed CryptoCasino on the Remix IDE.

Challenges we ran into

The main challenges we faced involved securely transferring ether from each player to a single location and then transferring the entire amount to the winner.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud that the game we created is secure, fun, and shows the great potential for Etherium usage in future entertainment.

What we learned

In the development of this project we have learned countless skills related to smart contracts, Etherium, and blockchain overall.

What's next for CryptoCasino

In the future of CryptoCasino we hope to add more functionality and flexibility in the games and ways to compete making the game more enjoyable and engaging.
