Best Route

BestRoute is a website that tells users to when to leave at an optimal time in order to avoid traffic and other commuting complications.

Audience

BestRoute is for any commuters - students and adults.

Experience

A user goes onto the website and inputs where they are going and what time they have to be there. BestRoute then sends them a message/push notification for when they should leave. extra: shows BEST ROUTE

Technical

Models

  • User

    • phone number
    • destination (address)
  • Trip
    • time
    • final/end destination
