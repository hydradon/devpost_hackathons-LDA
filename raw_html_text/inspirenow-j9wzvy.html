Inspiration

Helping individuals and students reach their full potential. Also encouraging people to persist through adversity.

What it does

It changes one's life by giving them motivational fuel. It does this through a webpage as well as a text service where individuals can receive motivational texts.

How I built it

Team was looking to solves the challenge of helping individuals reach their fullest potential. Idea was was implemented using HTML, CSS, twilio and PHP.

Challenges I ran into

Formatting the website as well as maintaining an effective content management system.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I'm proud of the fact that students and individuals can motivate themselves at anytime or place. I am also proud that this application can help improve our world and people's drive to do things.

What I learned

I learned how to use twilio and also strengthened my web development skills.

What's next for InspireNow

InspireNow will be taking on social media to attract more students and individuals to the website. We will also be adding numerous ways for users to motivate themselves other than quotes.
