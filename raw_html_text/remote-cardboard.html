Inspiration

Have you ever gotten bored of your phone's limitations? Well now you can use your phone, laptop, desktop, and more, with our remote cardboard device that fits in your pocket. You get everything all in one place and you're able to access them from anywhere.

What it does

Allow a user to mirror multiple devices from a single affordable interface.

How we built it

With love, sweat, caffeine, yerba mate, and lots of ngrok.

Challenges we ran into

Making our device compatible with multiple interfaces.

What we learned

Websockets != sockets
