Inspiration

To provide a more affordable way to get much needed extensions to the marketplace that were reasonably priced so that any Infusionsoft user could afford them.

What it does

Product Overview Ever try to use Zapier and just feel like it only got you 70% of what you needed? Or need one "must have" feature and find that that single feature costs you $79 a month or more?

MyFusion Helper was built out of necessity. You see, first and foremost, we are Infusionsoft users ourselves. We own three different businesses running on three apps ourselves, and as an Infusionsoft Certified Partner, we have helped and supported 100's of Infusionsoft clients.

What we saw was a problem in the industry - a void. Campaign Builder is incredibly powerful, but it does leave us all wanting certain features, extensions or "extras". The problem is that in the Infusionsoft market space, it seems that most apps solve just a single problem and charge a premium price.

Our solution?

Our mission is to provide hundreds of the most popular and needed extensions all in one "gotta have" library that is as simple to use as it is powerful. While we don't have 100's of helpers yet, we do have more than 30 and growing, and have already become the largest collection of Infusionsoft specific extensions available.

We are adding features on a frequent basis, with new helpers being released monthly. If you don't find what you need, then just contact us at our helpdesk and let us know what you need - help [at] myfusionsolutions.com.
