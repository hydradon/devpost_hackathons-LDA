Inspiration

We were inspired by the misconception that you couldn't live a sustainable lifestyle without paying a little more for it. x

What it does

This app debunks the myth that a sustainable lifestyle is expensive. It educates students on what they can do to help the environment, and how they are helping

How we built it

We developed the app on xcode using swift

Challenges we ran into

This is only our second time using Xcode to make an app, so it was really hard to learn how to use. We had trouble coding a lot of necessary functions

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We actually got to develop the app and include a substantial amount of information that will help at least educate the users.

What we learned

We learned how to do API, swift, xcode, and environmental studies

What's next for Better Life
