• Can It Fly

Inspiration

This project is based on a game we used to play as a kid. The skill I’ve built is based on game I used to play with my friends when we were kids. The game is simple: one kid says the name of a bird or animal and the other kids have to say whether or not it can fly.

What it does

This is a great way for kids to learn bird and animal names.

What's next for Can If Fly?

I will add information about the birds and animals, such as why they can or can’t fly and their country of origin.
