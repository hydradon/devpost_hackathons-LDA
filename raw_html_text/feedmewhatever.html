Inspiration

Too much good food out there

What it does

Lets you take a picture of a food item that you saw on TV, book, magazine and tells you where you can find that item (or something similar) in your local area

How I built it

Ruby on Rails mostly

Challenges I ran into

Using proper Active Record models and creating the right associations between them. Also since I was the only one working on this I didn't get a lot of time to work on the UI/UX.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It's a working prototype!

What I learned

SQL Joins, Active Record methods etc

What's next for FeedMeWhatever

Better UI/UX
