Inspiration

Brickhack

What it do

You play a brick, and you have to outrun other bricks, triggering traps and infecting them with a blue color. If the bricks are hit by a trap, they die. If the bricks get infected, and you touch the big infected brick, they die. If you touch a small brick, you die.

How I built it
