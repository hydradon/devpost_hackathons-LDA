Most people are intimidated by the stock market because they think that the only way to be successful is through endless math computations and the in-depth analyses. While that is one way to look at stocks, it isn’t the only way. Instead of creating the next great stock prediction tool during MHacks: Refactor, this hack simply tries to find stocks that are trading at either their extreme low or extreme high.

As many famous investors have said, “buy low, sell high” ... or more simply put “whatever goes up, must come down”. Instead of creating the next great stock prediction tool during MHacks: Refactor, this hack simply tries to find stocks that are trading at either their extreme low or extreme high. If it is at its extreme low, then it is time to buy. If it is at its extreme high, then it is time to sell. It is the Law of Averages - something is much more likely to go up after it goes down and vice versa - applied to finance.
