Idea and Motivation

We all see headlines, news articles, friend's tweets, instagrams and facebook comments about virtually any company/industry in the world. We are curious to see how all these sentiments/output influences market value and stock prices on a day to day basis.

Challenges we ran into

It was difficult to actually use the engagament scores because these were only applicable if a post had been released that day, for dates when the company did not post, the score would be 0, which was a bit meaningless in terms of what we were looking for.
