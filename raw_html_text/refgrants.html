RefGrants

Problem Set “Many immigrants and refugees who have college degrees are unemployed or working far below their skill level due to many cultural and structural barriers.”

Solution Job portal that provides core training, coaching, networking and resources. Use Machine Learning algorithm to match attributes of users like, skill set, language proficiency, work experience, to two sets of organizations: one which sponsor H1B and the other doesn’t. Resume Screening.
