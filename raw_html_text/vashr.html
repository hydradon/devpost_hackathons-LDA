Inspiration

Use fleets of drones to detect hazards like sharks, medical emergencies on boats, swimmers in distress, active shooter situations, etc. Then, once a hazard is localized, a rescue drone with an appropriate payload is deployed to the location and using a skycrane, delivers the target payload

What it does

detects hazards, localizes target areas, deploys rescue drones
