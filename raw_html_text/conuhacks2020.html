We have all experienced a moment in our lives when we have been wanting to tell the person connected to the speakers to put our favorite songs, but have been too shy to do so. With partyHub, we thought about creating a web-app where all guests can join a session and add songs to a queue. This allows for a fair share of the music and makes listening to music more interactive with friends. We used the Angular framework, Node.js and MongoDB to built this responsive web-app. The challenge faced was working with a new framework, where we had to overcome a big learning curve.
