Express-o

Coffee-to-go

Express-o is a new innovative, fast, and sustainable way to get coffee. We plan to implement this product on college campuses first, and expand to densely populated cities. Coffee is often a necessity and highly on demand on college campuses, however time is scarce which demands a quick stop by Starbucks or the like to satisfy our coffee needs. To solve this problem, we are introducing Express-O, a sustainable way to ditch the paper cup, save time, and satisfy your coffee needs.

Using Express-o, you can remotely order and pay for your drink, and schedule, and pick it up as soon as they arrive at the vending machine by scanning their QR code. The machine will be able to make the drink and store it until the customer arrives to ease the morning routine for many busy students and workers. After scanning your QR code you are required to present a mug, or a cup, otherwise you are required to pay extra for a paper cup.

Coffee while saving the environment? I'm a fan.
