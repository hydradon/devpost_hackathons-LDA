Inspiration

ConSensual is inspired by Cindy Gallop (founder of Make Love Not Porn) who pitched the idea of a private, consensual sexting app.

What it does

It allows couples to share intimate video and photo content safely and consensually.

How I built it

We are a team of strategists, marketers, and UX designers. We built a clickable prototype and business plan rather than a coded project. But we would love to collaborate with developers and the larger Make Love Not Porn team in the future!
