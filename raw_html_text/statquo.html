The idea of StatQuo was slowly growing in our minds over the last time.

With StatQuo we wanted to provide a free-data platform to share informations with everyone who's interested in using them, for commercial usage or even for private charts, just for fun. It's great, how easy you can insert datas from our huge database and provide a great monitoring through a variaty of different charts.

You can scroll through the latest posts and gather the information you need, while you enjoy the great design of StatQuo and the entertainment, those statistics will provide you!
