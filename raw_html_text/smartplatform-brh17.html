As enthusiastic coders, we have participated in many hackathons in the past, and we have noticed that most of the hackathon projects with good potential go nowhere after the event either due to lack of resources to expand the scope of projects or due to lack of mentoring from the right set of experienced people and researchers. And we have observed that same is the case with other hackers too. There are a lot of good ideas with the potential to take off but they just die down later. This project creates a platform that brings together researchers, students and industry professionals to collaborate and develop research, hackathon ideas and projects to an advanced level prototypes.

This project helps hackathon teams to know the potential of their ideas by introducing their ideas to various firms and venture capitalists. This also focusses on bringing investment management firms and tech firms to invest in the high potential research that's being pursued by professors and post doc students at universities.

A one stop platform for all the researchers, professors, hackers, industrialists and firms all over the world to collaborate with each other and produce quality research, projects and work.
