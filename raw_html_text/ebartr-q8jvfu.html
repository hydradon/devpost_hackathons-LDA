eBartr

eBartr is an innovative new app that enables people to easily trade goods and services. eBartr connects people who need things with the people that have them, helping consumers save money by forgoing costly purchases used for single occasions. eBartr also helps people in tricky situations (flat tire, dead battery, etc.) get access to the help they need in a timely and cost effective manner.

eBartr will be targeted primarily towards college students because their inherently social living structure and frugal lifestyle is more in line with the app's community-oriented philosophy. We want people to use our app as an opportunity to resolve a challenge they face without spending money and through social interaction.
