Inspiration

Passwords suck, and we want an easier and better solution for webapps that require low security logins.

What it does

Mustela allows you to sign in using your face! Your phone will snap a picture of your face, compare it to a stored copy, and allow you onto your account if you match!

How we built it

We built Mustela using Azure's Face API, Postgres, and node.js, with a frontend in Android.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Live face detection, identifying your face without clicking a button.
