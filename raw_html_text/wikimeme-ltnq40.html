Inspiration

As seen by our team name And I O.O.P. , we love memes and spreading our love for memes. With a team member who has some experience in Web Dev, we decided to make an online database for memes.

What it does

The website home page has a carousel that presents currently popular memes and a navigation bar that lets you find memes based off of its name or the year in which it was popularized. Users can also submit memes and image examples that are not currently found on the website so it stays as up-to-date as possible.

Challenges we faced

Only one of our members had prior experience in HTML, CSS, Node.js, and MongoDB. Even so, none of us knew how to create a database and have a website access it or how to host a website using Azure.
