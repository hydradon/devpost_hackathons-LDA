The game was inspired by the constant pollution of Ogoni community and other communities by oil spillage in my country. It creates awareness about the dangers of pollution and climate change on Human civilization. It was built in Corona Sdk using the LUA programming language. There wasn't really many challenges, except the lack of an artist, I built the entire game myself.

What's next for Earth's Plague

Hopefully, I'll expand on the game, and create a more engaging narrative, that would sensitize players on the effect of destroying nature.
