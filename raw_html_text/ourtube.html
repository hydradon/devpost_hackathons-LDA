Inspiration

Some people don't want to pay for Spotify premium. We are those people.

What it does

Ourtube allows users to easily and intuitively download songs from YouTube, store them in playlists and play them all in the app.

How we built it

We started by designing our app's user interface. After committing to a design, we separated the project into different tasks, which we distributed among all members evenly.

Challenges we ran into

Internet speed, sleeping environment and starting off were major challenges we faced.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Fighting capitalist greed since 1969 one step at a time.

What we learned

We learned many interpersonal skills such as teamwork and communication. Our technical learning included React, Javascript and electron

What's next for Ourtube

Bypass all capitalist limitations on current music services such as Spotify, YouTube premium and Bandcamp.
