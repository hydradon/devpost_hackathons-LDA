• PLAN

Inspiration

A timely alert mechanism to natural disasters.

What it does

PLAN is an application that would provide the alerts service to people in case of natural disasters, plans recommendation and features that will be of great use for people when they are in these situations, so we can be prepared and take appropriate preventive measures and thus prevent or reduce the damage that can generate these phenomena of nature.

How we built it

Built with tools for developing mobile applications as Android Studio, using programming languages like Java, JavaScript, Web services management and libraries specific use.

Challenges we ran into

Update delivery data quickly

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Participation in other hackathones, experience and entrepreneurial spirit.

What we learned

It is necessary that with the help of new technologies are innovative projects that provide services for people, to make intelegentes cities. PLAN is for smart cities .

What's next for PLAN

Being one of the first applications that help reduce damage caused by natural disasters.
