Lacking in funding for investing.

A democratized investing application that allows friends, families, or anyone to come together and invest in stock together even if they have low budget.

We incorporated many different types of technologies such as the Banking API from CapitalOne to stock price data from FINRA.

Using Big Data, improve backend, and beat mutual funds!
