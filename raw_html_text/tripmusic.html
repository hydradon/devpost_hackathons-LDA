Inspiration

This is our entry for the Travel Hack Hackathon - 2015 - Sao Paulo.

What it does

It gets the artists you like from your Spotify profile, cross the list with the artists schedule and suggests travel destinations where you can watch them live around the world, according to the the lower plane ticket prices.

How I built it

We are using Python/Django and JQuery

Challenges I ran into

Assemble a great team on the spot

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Making a complete, working application in 24 hours

What I learned

Spotify and Sabre APIs (tried to use bandsintown but were not able to get authorization)

What's next for tripmusic

We don't know, we are here just to show-off.
