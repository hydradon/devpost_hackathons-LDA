Imagine, that you are just sitting in the downtown of the city and you would like to visit some places which are around and you have only 6 hours to complete your journey. You start our app and you pick what you would like to see based on your own preferences, you write the time you need to finish your trip at and pick the place you would like to end up in. You can also put if your budget is limited. Our app will offer you three main journeys, based on your preferences and also on current weather. You can pick one which you prefer the most and it will be kept offline even when you leave the public wifi.

Our app saves your time, money and offers you only places and events you are really interested in. And moreover, it works anywhere in the world.
