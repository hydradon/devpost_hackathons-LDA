Drunk driving is definitely a problem in the United States. Many people are unaware that they are at risk simply because they "don't feel drunk". We created an app that tracks your BAC. Every time you take a drink, just select one of the options and your BAC will be updated. At the end of the night simply check the app and you'll know if you are safe to drive. If not, the app will indicate "unsafe to drive" and a button will automatically take you to the Uber app for a safe drive home.
