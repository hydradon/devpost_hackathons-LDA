What it does

VOLINKS is a charity project aiming, first of all, to link and put in touch all associative actor at the national scale by introducing a new and simple concept: virtual association or going virtual in the charity sector, this will be put into practice by offering a virtual space using a web platform where all charities can take place and benefit from a matching adapted to its needs with volunteer, collaborator, and sponsor. On the other hand, VOLINKS also aims to broaden the individuals involved in the charity movement for a better result by giving them the chance to take part as a volunteer or propose a solution to any real environmental anomaly and get all the help they need to overcome it.
