Inspiration

Community water center posed a compelling need for a web portal that provides aggregate data on water quality and government relations for affecting local water quality

What it does

Connects users with lists of local free and clean drinking water (user sourced), locations where water can be purchased, lists of recent drinking water violations, provides contact information and meeting dates for local waterboards.

How we built it

We used node.js to create a simple web application and incorporated API data from yelp, epa and google maps
