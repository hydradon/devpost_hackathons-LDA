Inspiration

Once upon a time (5 years ago) bunch of students made a small game "Shake the world" at small hackathon at the small town Kaluga. That game was released and after that it was forgotten. Now our grown up team decided to make a new and trendy game, which has more sense and has more fun .

What it does

Have you ever shaken your phone? Why? Well, we offer you a game where you can explore cities , learn short ways (if you want to), conquer buildings , group up with friend or strangers to destroy your enemies by shaking you phone in different ways! We offer you a way of healthy and active way of leisure.

Accomplishments

Now player have almost full functionality, without social networking and granting achievements.

What's next for Shake The World

It seems that this variation of games is popular and easy to monetize. We got some ideas of making this game more interesting .
