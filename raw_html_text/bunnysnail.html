• Bunny Snail

Inspiration

We saw a sea slug that looks like a rabbit on the internet and gave us the inspiration to form "bunnysnail".

What it does

It is a website that holds three different games we created using Construct 3. Every game was made by very different people, but are all coming together to support similar causes.

How we built it

Construct 3.

Challenges we ran into

None. Learning Construct 3 and FTP.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Athena got her sprite to stop flopping on the ground and have a proper walking animation. Tuzette implementing the wall collision logic for her bunny massacre. Sarah counted all 123 tiles that make up the image.

What we learned

Everything that we did here at the hackathon was solely based on things we learned during the hackathon. No previous experience influenced our ability to complete our projects.

What's next for BunnySnail

Make more games for the site!
