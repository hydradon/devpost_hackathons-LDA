ExtremeBoratDefenseAR

Extreme Borat Defence is the only educational Augment Reality game for iOS that is dedicated to preparing the average Joe for the event of an attacking Borat Swarm.

In the game, a cactus totem is spawned on the player at start up and the Borat swarm attacks from all angles with the objective of destroying the totem. Players must tap to shoot their gun to slay the enemies before they destroy the totem.

Before we decided that this project was the optimal use of our time, we started out seeking to build a different iOS AR app. This app would create a portal and allow the player to walk into and explore various 3D worlds made in unity. While working on this our interests changed and we adjusted the scope Saturday afternoon to make Extreme Borat Defense.
