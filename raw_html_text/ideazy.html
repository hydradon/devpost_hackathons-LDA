Inspiration

Our inspiration stems from the lack of competitive edge from internet presence and finds a unique and effective way to gather information about their customers.

Built by

Eli Harris, Adam Short, and Dakota Williams

What it does

IDEAZY uses the noninvasive information from the customer's driver's license to equip businesses with information about their customers and enables effective marketing decisions

How we built it

We built it on the framework Laravel for the backend web, xamarin for the cross-platform application, and multiple javascript libraries including vue.js and jQuery

Challenges I ran into

The biggest problem our group ran into a lack of a working iPhone cable and integrating Eli's Querify library

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Creating a market ready application

What I learned

Collaboration, teamwork, and the importance of git

What's next for IDEAZY

We hope to continue developing and selling IDEAZY to businesses
