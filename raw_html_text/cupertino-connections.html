Inspiration

We wanted to make an app that could alert the whole city of Cupertino about incidents, like accidents, burglaries, and so on.

What it does

This generates a map of all the incidents in Cupertino and lets people report their own incidents.

How we built it

We used Swift and some external Google APIs.

What we learned

Perseverance is key! Don't give up.

What's next for Cupertino Connections

We plan to expand this into more features such as automatic tracking, 911 calling, and more.
