Captain-Conundrum

My first publicly released iOS game created at Make School Summer Academy 2017

This game is a top-down space shooter similar to the likes of Space Invaders and Galaga, but made for iOS. You tilt the screen to move left and right and tap to shoot out lasers. Your goal, as the captain of this ship, is to destroy as many of the obstacles as you can without taking too much damage. Along the way, you'll find power ups to enhance the game even further. You even have the option to customize this very ship. Try to go for a high score, or perhaps, try for a low score!
