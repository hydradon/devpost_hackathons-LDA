Inspiration

I felt this is the best time to remind my fellow country men that we should be united. A lot has happened to our country this year and I believe together we stand, divided we fall.

What it does

Its a facemask that shows unity and hope again for Nigeria.

How we built it

We used Spark AR studio, Figma, Microsoft PowerPoint.

Challenges we ran into

We tried to import a 3D object and we never knew the specifications, we figure it out later. Getting used to the tools was also a challenge but we later got used to it.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Building a facemask that shows the unity of our dear nation.

What we learned

Persistence is key. Take one lesson at a time when embarking on a new project.

What's next for Nigeria

More positive social facemasks.
