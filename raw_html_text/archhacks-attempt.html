Inspiration

Many people don't know enough minute details about their health to succesfully diagnose themselves, or be aware of possible disease risks, especially if they don't frequent a physician. This app would be able to help inform this demographic of their potential health issues, and be able to track ways to improve their odds of mitigating serious ailments

What it does

In theory, it would combine a series of health related API's (Infermedica, basehealth, better doctor), to compile simple human data into more medically applicable results, providing users with information on doctors near them to help with their possible ailments.
