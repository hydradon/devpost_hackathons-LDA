• Breadboard, Arduino, and Quadcopter Controller!

What it does

You can use your hand to control the movement of the quadcopter.

How we built it

We track the movement of a user's right hand with a kinect 2. We control the quadcopter by modifying its controller and connecting it to an arduino.

What's next for Kinect-Copter

Using subtle hand movements to control the movement of the quadcopter more precisely (e.g. rotating the hand to rotate the quadcopter.)
