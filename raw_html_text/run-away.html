Inspiration - To teach and introduce younger generations about technology

What it does- Educate younger generations through problem solving and critical thinking

How I built it- Using C-Sharp on Visual Studio window form

Accomplishments that I'm proud of is the Story line:

Main character is walking home from school. Someone or something comes up and knocks you out. You find yourself in a haunted house. When you wake up, a few seconds later, a ghost appears before you and tell you in order to to escape the house, you must solve riddles and games to defeat the robots.

What's next for Run Away!- Implement and introduce our application to the younger generations so that they can start learning.
