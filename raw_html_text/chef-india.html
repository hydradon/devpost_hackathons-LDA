About Chef India:

Most of the recipe/cooking skill's posted on India have a linear layout i.e. when a recipe is invoked it just say's all the listed ingredients and instructions then just stops. If you missed a step you just have to hear or see the whole instructions again making the whole experience very inefficient as it can done a whole lot faster by just googling it . Also most of them do not have a proper SSML incorporated in them , making the speech output very fast or hard to understand . Even if they use APL or display card they are just some instructions written on the screen again that can be done more efficiently if we just google the recipe .

While Chef India do not make the same mistakes . It uses a non linear layout making every step in instruction separately accessible. That means if you want to hear just the step one or just the ingredients of a recipe you can easily do that . I have also incorporated a video demo of every step that make learning process a whole lot easier. There are audio cues incorporated in this skill where Alexa will wait while you are cooking. They also work as placeholders so that your skill doesn't get disable while you are working . Currently there are only five Indian recipes available in the library as I had time only to work on five of them but I will keep on adding the recipes in future.
