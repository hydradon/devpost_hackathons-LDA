Squid

Have you ever been on a road-trip and someone has annoyed you, again and again and again to play some song that they like? Or are you tired of being denied your choice of music because the person with the speaker is too lazy to queue your song?

Download Squid!!

With our application, you can send song requests via Bluetooth to another device with the app, and Squid will manage the queue automatically.

No one gets annoyed, everyone gets a turn
