Inspiration

Karen has way too many tabs open when she's browsing the Internet for shopping items.

What it does

This extension becomes a comprehensive shopping cart by injecting Javascript into shopping sites and then performing data mining operations to provide an overview for users to compare prices and quality.

How I built it

Tears, no sleep, caffeine.

Challenges I ran into

Debugging is always a fun aspect of a programmer's life.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Being able to have a somewhat decently facing product.

What I learned

That using third party APIs for product information can be a very painstaking experience if you are not careful with your XML, JSON, and PHP interactions.

What's next for Kartful

More sites? A more developed interface? There are plenty of opportunities to scale and we have only slugged through 36 hours of development for this product so far.
