What it does

We are predicting the effect of rising carbon dioxide levels based on historical data and climate trends. We display a simulation of the problems this may cause.

How we built it

We are using machine learning to help predict the real effects of rising carbon dioxide, and presenting this to the user with HTML5 canvas animations.
