Inspiration

We unfortunately live in an urban area where air quality fluctuates frequently due to pollution and other worsening environmental conditions. As poor air quality can lead to adverse health effects, being able to monitor current, local air quality indices (AQI) will allow awareness of potential health hazards while also encouraging people to take the necessary safety precautions.

What it does

When it comes to daily ways we take care of ourselves, checking the air quality is not a priority despite its health effects; therefore, we created an AQI map that displays current air conditions for any location through Google Maps so that users may know the air quality of places they plan to visit. This way, it is easy for people to be aware of potential health hazards while doing an every day task such as looking up directions.

How we built it

Our project was broken up into two parts: the front and back. The front part dealt with creating an interactive webpage that displays the information acquired from the back part.
