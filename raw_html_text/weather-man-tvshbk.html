Inspiration

I hate waking up not knowing what to wear to work! I have to scroll so much (my finger hurts!) and there might suddenly be rain or sleet at the end of the workday that I have to take into consideration.

What it does

It analyzes weather data from your location and let's you know what clothing you should wear according to your own temperature preferences.

How we built it

We used Swift.

Challenges we ran into

We had to learn swift and Xcode on the fly. There were issues with the UI and Xcode being inconsistent and unreliable.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We finished a whole application that you can put on your phone!

What we learned

We learned so much about swift and APIs in just one day.

What's next for Weather Man

We'd love to add in the extra functionality to add custom items that are unique to the user's wardrobe!
