Inspiration

Guitar Hero, stock prices

What it does

Tiles with different types of tiles come in flying from right to left. As soon as they enter the "Action Area", the player has to swipe or tap the screen in order to destroy the tile and make the graph rise. --> It's all about your fast decisions! If you can master our game, you are a true TraderHero!

How we built it

We have used Unity and C#

Challenges we ran into

  • We had both little experience with unity and C#, so it was mostly learning by doing
  • To design an easy understandable but enjoyable game experience
  • We had to figure out the right scales for the difficulty of the game to increase

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The game is actually fun! And we achieved a super simple solution.

What we learned

We learned a lot more about Unity, and about the whole process of designing a game.
