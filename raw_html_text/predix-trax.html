Inspiration

What it does

It tracks and monitors Model T's and other assets with, temperature, humidity and, GPS location, it may also send alerts via text. There is also a camera function to view the asset.

How we built it

We used javascript to access the API's. We also used Predix and a Rasberry Pi as web and apache servers. Then we got a Raspberry pi camera to continually take pictures of the assets.

Challenges we ran into

Some of our challenges include learning javascript and learning predix the day of the hackathon, understanding the API's for google maps and using and putting together the Model T data.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We're proud of the team that came together and the web app that we've made.

What we learned

We've learned at least one new language for each of us. We also learned to look at how to use the languages and hardware in different and creative ways.

What's next for Predix Trax

Hopefully we will continue to keep in touch and make more web apps that could change how companies or even the world works.
