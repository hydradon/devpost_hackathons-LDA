Inspiration

No more bad days due to forgetfulness.

(Also -- once upon a time, my roommate forgot to lock the door. She will never forget again.)

What it does

Gives user location-based reminders Sends push notifications as reminders when entering specified radius around a destination Contains easy-to-read agenda and comprehensive map containing drop pins of all event locations

How we built it

We used Android Studio, Parse, Facebook Login, and the Google Maps API.

Challenges we ran into

Geofencing and Android intents

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Global scale reminders (Messaging, data, and roaming rates may apply)

What we learned

Learned Android basics, Google Maps API, Parse API, Facebook login API.

What's next for Remind Me

Location search, performance optimization, scalability, stability improvements, feature additions, galactic expansion
