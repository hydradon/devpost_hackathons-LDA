Inspiration: We care about the children of our community and we know that the insecurity has been increasing, so we were searching a project that can help us to solve this problematic.

What it does: It give to the family the location of the kid when he or she goes out of the school, and register the digital signature to know when he arrive and when he left.

How we built it: We create a model of how it works.

Challenges we ran into: We didn´t know how to create chatbots, we were so tired and hungry.

Accomplishments that we're proud of: We learn how to create and program chatbots :).

What we learned: How to work in team.

What's next for Pignus: Make the project grow.
