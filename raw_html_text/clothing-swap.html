Inspiration

We wanted to make a marketplace to make clothing recycling easy. We built this for people that have clothes they want to get rid of and those transitioning who need a new wardrobe. Our product offers an environmentally friendly alternative to getting clothes and getting rid of clothes.

What it does

Allow people who are transitioning to trade old clothes and receive new gender-affirming ones Allow people to get clothes that they might otherwise not be able to afford Give people an option to reduce their waste

How I built it

Swift: General app programming and UI Firebase Authentication: For signing in with Google Firebase Realtime Database: Store and retrieve data about requests for clothing

Challenges I ran into

Started in React, then pivoted One member has experience with Swift, none have experience with Firebase Figuring out how to integrate Firebase with our project Realtime database Setting up sign in with Google and integrating it with the app General suffering and despair

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Proud of what we built and what we learned!

What I learned

A lot about the technology we used and general process of development.

What's next for Clothing Swap

Planning on expanding this to be a multi-million dollar fashion empire.
