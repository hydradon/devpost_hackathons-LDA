Expensepedia makes it easy to track your spending.
All you have to do is take a picture of your receipt and the app will type out the items for you to categorize.
Then see how much you spend on food, entertainment, clothing, and more during the month with our clean UI bar graph. Track your monthly spending over the year to get an overview of where your money is going.

Developed with Android Studio, Microsoft Azure API for text recognition, and Firebase.
