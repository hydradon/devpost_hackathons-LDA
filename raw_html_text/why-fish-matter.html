My inspiration was based on the need to understand how to prevent fish extinction.

The community will be involved in a program that raises awareness on how to prevent over-fishing and how to replenish the fish population.

My challenges were the limited time to research this topic and providing more detailed information.

The biggest accomplishment that I'm proud of is how some of my members were interacting with this topic, which made it more creative and educational for all.

I learned how to understand the limitation of fishery and how it is necessary for a community for survival.

The future of Why Fish Matter is to continue its mission to provide programs to the community to best serve the economy.
