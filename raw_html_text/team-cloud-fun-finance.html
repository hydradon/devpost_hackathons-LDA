Challenges I ran into

For the first case, Co-Saving. Split the cost to all people who involved
We found 3 problems:
1, The first, When you go out with your friends, drink beer then sing karaoke, take a late meal.You don't want to share cost every time u finish an activity, you want to do it after the whole party end.
2, The 2nd is when you go out by the group, there always will be someone join late, someone needs to leave soon. While u must share cost exactly.
3, The 3rd, assume that you are the one who organizes the party. But you need to leave soon. What other should do?
Goal-Saving:
There are 2 problems we try to solve
1, Millennial is needed help to stay on the saving track.
2, Saving as a group, transparent is most important. Only recording is not solve the problem
