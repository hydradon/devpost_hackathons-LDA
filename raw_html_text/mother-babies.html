Inspiration

We think the memories are important for every person, so we decide to create a place to locate the photographs permanently

What it does

Save the pictures of babys. This helps to mothers to have an album of the best moments in the life of her children. Doing this, we guarantee that the photographs keep save.

How we built it

It was used MVC pattern to keep the escalability for futures features. Ruby-on-rails was used for this, is perfect for this kind of projects.

Challenges we ran into

We had some challenges with database.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned

We learned to work in team and we get some skills.
