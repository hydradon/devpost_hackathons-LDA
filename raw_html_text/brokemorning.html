Inspiration

It is easy for me to over sleep in the morning. After I asked my friend to wake me up, it changed my life, so I want to help people like me to wake up easily.

What it does

"Sleep in and you'll go broke. Wake everyone else up and become rich." People get morning calls in the morning, if they don't wake up and pick up the phone, they will be charge money. On the other hand, people who have waken up can call others to wake people up and earn money.

How I built it

I used Node.js as a server, and the Twilio service to call people.

Challenges I ran into

I was having trouble using Twilio for a while, until Sam, the Twilio guy helped me. Also, running a JavaScript app on a mobile platform is not convenient as I originally thought.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I'm proud of the idea. I can help many people, and a lot of people love the idea and want to help. Also I used Twilio for the first time.

What I learned

Steady Friendship and lovely experience.. alright, I learned How to use Twilio.

What's next for BrokeMorning

keep building it and wake millions of people up bright and early in the morning.
