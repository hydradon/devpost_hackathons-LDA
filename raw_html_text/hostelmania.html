Inspiration

Actually, i went to kota for coaching of competition examination and it was previously decided that which coaching class i have to choose. But when time came for selection of hostel/ pg's there was huge tension. It was summer time and me and my dad was roaming around for good hostel . It was time consuming task and at that time i first thought about this venture.

What it does

In this app, we try to put all hostels and pg's which are located upto 3 to 4 km area from your respective coaching class. We personally check hostels and pg. And we give ratings to it depending on food, room, and environment of study. We also give images of rooms provided by hostels and pg's.
