Vaccination protects us from serious illness and complications of vaccine-preventable diseases which can include paralysis of limbs, brain damage, and death.

It important for individuals to keep updated of their vaccines since it can be a matter of life and death.

Introducing Ivory, a secure android app that allow users to record the dates of their vaccination. It also reminds users of their next vaccinations.
