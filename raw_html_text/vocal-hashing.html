Inspiration

I believe that when people watch a News or Tv show, they constantly want to know about the ideas of other people. Lot of people including me open Reddit, twitter, facebook and try to find that topic extensively and read it. I say instead stay relaxed and ask alexa.

What it does

The skill basically provides you with the latest hashtags or trends. It gives you best trending hashtag suggestions and allows you to pick from those hash tags and listen to them.

How I built it

I built it using the Node.js, amazon dynamodb as backend data base and s3 bucket service to load the file.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned

I got better at using alexa and dynamodB. I gained a lot of knowledge and experience in Node.js.

What's next for Vocal Hashing!
