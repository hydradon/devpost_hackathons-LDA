• Title of VR Application

Inspiration

As college students, we are no strangers to feeling stressed out and tired. We wanted to create a solution to this.

What it does

It is often hard for people to relax in a new environment; however, our application asks the user various questions to determine an environment that is catered to their needs and transports them to that area. Calming music is tailored to every situation at hand, and you can just get your hakuna matata on.

How we built it

With as little sleep as possible and too much caffeine.

Also Unity, Microsoft Paint 3D, Blender, and using an Oculus Rift.

Challenges we ran into

Collapsing from lack of sleep.

Also, the Oculus Assets Package was new to us so there was a learning curve.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It works, which we didn't see coming.

What we learned

Sleep is important.

Also, more about Unity coding and the Oculus Rift.

What's next for ReVRie

We're all taking naps.

Also, add more scenarios, more questions, more music, and include the WRLD Unity SDK if possible.
