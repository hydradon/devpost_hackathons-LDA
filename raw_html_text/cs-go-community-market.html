CS:GO Community Market

A decentralized market place for trading in-game items in Counter-Strike: Global Offensive.

  • Universality: combine the usage with a universal token such as TODA can be efficiently traded between many in-game currencies Security and speed:
  • Decentralization
  • Economic freedom
  • New economics for the gaming industry

We would like to acknowledge the APIs that we used in this project:

  • https://todaq.net/ TODA and TODA-as-a-Service platform (TaaS), decentralized protocol for ownership management
  • http://CSGOBackpack.net Get the information of in-game items in CS:GO
  • https://8gwifi.org/ Generate a key pair using P-256
