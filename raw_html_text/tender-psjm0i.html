Inspiration

A study shows that the average American couple spends 132 hours (5.5 days) a year deciding on what to eat.

What it does

A simple web app that helps you and your friends plan an event by voting on nearby restaurants.

How we built it

Over the Flask framework, using Google Firebase and Yelp Fusion API, and hosted through Azure.

Challenges we ran into

Deploying a Flask app through Azure and making better use of Firebase.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The web UI, learning how to use Flask, and for some, learning python. We are proud of the product we designed and developed in the span of 24 hours.

What we learned

Azure deployment, python, Flask, Yelp API
