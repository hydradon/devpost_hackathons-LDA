Inspiration

** _ Hello Neighbor _ ** was inspired by the way that the internet connected online communities to create an app that could connect real world communities. ** _ Hello Neighbor _ ** serves as the front page for your neighborhood, allowing users to connect with those around them to look for help within the community.

What it does

** _ Hello Neighbor _ ** is a community networking app designed to strengthen the neighborhood by allowing people to share their skills to help others. Users can go through posts by a map view or by scrolling through tagged posts to see what help they can offer their neighbors. When a user sees a post by someone they can help, they are automatically directed to dialing the person's number. If you need help - your car breaks down, you need ingredients to finish dinner, or you're unable to do heavy lifting and it's move in day - all you need to do is make a post to be connected to those around you.

How I built it

** _ Hello Neighbor _ ** was built using Android studio, java, Firebase, and google maps API.
