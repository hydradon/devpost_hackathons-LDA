Guessify is a tool that challenge you on how many song names you know. Discover the music and compete with your friends in our addictive dynamic multiplayer mode. "Name That Tune" TV show moved to the 21st century!

Guessify contains two parts: Android app and a server backend (for the Multiplayer mode). Using Spotify as an input our app allows to play a part of the song and check if you guessed the name correctly. In the multiplayer mode all users answer to the same song-question and the first is the winner.

We are especially proud of the multiplayer mode and a nice-looking UI. That makes the app appealing and playable.

Try it with your friends and have fun!
