Inspiration

Our main objective is to reduce factors that discourage voting for first time voters using 2 methods: 1) prevent cognitive overload by only providing essential info 2) decrease opportunity cost of voting by decreasing time needed to gather info

How it works

You enter your location and the web application returns -current elections in your district -candidate list in the elections -candidate's campaign pages
