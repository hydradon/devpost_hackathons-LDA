What it does

Play chess collaboratively with other players. Together, teams will decide on one move to play against the opponent. This game format is both instructive for beginners and fun for more experienced players.

What's next for hivemind

Hivemind can be extended to host a wide variety of games. Other features would include playing against AIs, creating private rooms, and more customization.
