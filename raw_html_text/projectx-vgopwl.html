Overview

Once, We saw a girl singing on Facebook and one line of lyrics caught our interest. It was about how a girl shouldn't come home after 7 p.m. That's our motivation to build this mobile app. Our goal is to connect two people to go home together when it is late in the night.

This is the project for Dubs Hack 2018
