Inspiration

In order for America to be the greatest country it is, we need to ensure that all citizens have access to medical care.

What it does

My app allows for you to select your primary physician and securely, via blockchain backbone, chat or video chat with them from all around the world. This is great for those that are traveling, that live in remote areas, or immobile elderly meet with their physicians. My app will allow for you and your physicians, secured by two personal keys held by each other, to access and discuss reports and x-rays remotely. My app will also allow for the elderly to call for an uber like pick up service, driven by an off-duty EMT. It will arrange home visits and delivery of medication.
