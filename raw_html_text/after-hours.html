After-Hours

After Hours is an exciting new app that allows users to find Happy Hour information from differing venues relative to their location.

Our approach to Happy Hour App

With the many hours we put in at work or school, relaxing and decompressing over a cocktail, a glass of wine, or a cold beer is necessary. When traveling to new cities or even new states or countries, it would be useful to have an app that would pull all the local Happy Hour information from nearby restaurants and bars. Additionally, it would be useful for users to be able to post reviews based purely on a venue's Happy Hour, and for owners of establishments to be able to update their information to keep it current.
