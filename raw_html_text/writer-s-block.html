Inspiration

As a writer myself, there are several moments when I feel stuck. At these times, some friendly motivation is always welcome.

What it does

Gives you a motivational quote from a famous writer about writer's block, to help you tide through your own!

What's next for Writer's Block

I hope to make a contextually aware skill that helps writers with different quotes based on what kind of writing they do - e.g. fiction and non-fiction writers may be motivated by different things.
