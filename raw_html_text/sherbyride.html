Inspiration

SherbyRide is an incredible app that allows you to tour Sherbrooke depending on your mood and profile.

Out with your family? You probably want to stick to easy, paved and flat trails with parks, benches and open spaces to take breaks near by. On a mission and up for a challenge? If you are looking for a more intense workout choose an "extreme sports" profile which will offer a more intense ride on inclined and/or longer terrain. If you are looking for a romantic outing with your significant other, then you will love our very special "couple" profile that offers the best rides to enjoy together... May you be by bike, by foot, on rollerblades or on a kick scooter, SherbyRide will give you the best paths around Sherbrooke.

Know real time were restaurants, parks, wifi, awesome attractions, bus lines (almost ready) and districts data sets (to file a complaint if you have one - just click on the "chialage" button! ;)

Discover Sherbrooke in new way! Download SherbyRide on your Androïd and enjoy today!

Check out our mobile friendly website: http://bit.ly/1HEhnfW and follow us on Twitter @HoverDrone (account recycled from Sherbrooke's participation in Nasa's Space Apps Challenge two weeks ago).

How it works

Open the app. Choose your profile. Enjoy your personalized ride ;) Easy as that. We have integrated data that indicates local attractions (restaurants, parcs...), wifi hotspots, bus lines and municipal buildings close to your bike trail.

May you be on a bike, a skateboard, rollerblades or a kick scooter, SherbyRide will indicate the optimal trails for you to take.

Ouvrez l'app. Choisis un profil. Amusez-vous ;) C'est aussi simple. Nous avons intégré du data qui inclut les attractions locales (restaurants, parcs...), les bornes wifi, les lignes de bus et les bâtiments municipaux (voir le bouton "chialage" qui vous permet de porter plainte si vous êtes proche d'un de ces bâtiments :) Le tout s'affiche en temps réel pendant votre trajet.

Que vous soyez en vélo, en skateboard, roller ou trottinette, SherbyRide vous indiquera les chemins optimales à prendre.
