Inspiration

we are very frustrated about the insecurity of my country, many womens are killed every day and houndreds of robbery to pedestrian occurs every day... so we want to make a real change in our community to make this situation change, our family could be the next victim and we don't want that

What it does

It discreetly listen the surroundings and conversation, and if it detects a problem situation it starts to take pictures and send localization along with the pictures to the two first cops nearby, we control the situation using AI to get the information of the police who attends the case and the person who needs assistant, this is perfect for public transportation or when you are walking along in the night.

How we built it

we built in unity because everyone in the team knows how to use it and it's very easy to use thank to we don't need to worry about UI
