Inspiration

Allow tour guides to tailor presentations to specific groups. Also help keep personalized messages private.

How it works

Using Bluetooth on Plantronics, broadcast voice from the tour guide to the group, and from individuals in the group to the group and tour guide.

  • Allow recording of presentations and conversations.
  • - Produce a printed tour report using voice to text.
  • Allow text-to-speech to broadcast prepared presentations.
