Inspiration

The heterogeneity of the IoT systems is a barrier to develop the new Port of the future. The Logistics and Transport field is a market where obsolete technology is still ingrained. Moreover, the innovation is a need to satisfy and improve the performance of every operation in this area. Particularly, maritim transport and port management are key in a port environment that have to be improved with the inclusion of new technologies.

What it does

To overcome the interoperability problem, allowing IoT systems to work together in a seamless manner and with security as a must, the application of a innovative solution is needed. SymbIoTe is the optimal interoperability solution to succeed in the creation of the Port 4.0.
