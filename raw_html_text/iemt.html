Inspiration: My mom has epilepsy and one of her biggest difficulties is remembering if she took her medicine or not. She's at a very high risk of under-dosing and missing a dosage or overdosing and taking it twice because she doesn't know if she took it or not.

What it does: It is an virtual pill box. You can list your medications, dosages, and times they need to be taken

How I built it: Android Studio

Challenges I ran into: Trying to save the data In the app.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of: Actually getting it done.

What I learned: How to save data on my app.

What's next for IEMT: Publishing to help not only epileptics but people with memory challenges.
