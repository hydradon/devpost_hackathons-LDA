A Moment of Science

A distractingly science themed new tab page for Chrome. Have a laugh and feel empowered with a tweet from a super cool female scientist, snack on a little bits of knowledge with a pretty and informative short from Vimeo, and sit back and enjoy a podcast about scientific discoveries and misadventures.

To test it out, download this crx file and drag and drop it onto your browser.
