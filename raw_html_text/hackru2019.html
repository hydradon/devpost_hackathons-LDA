mpg

We wanted to utilize all of the API's within the hackathon, and we came up with the idea of something universal that everyone deals with - filling up their cars with gas.

We wanted to tell people how many miles they'll have left based off of how much gallons they filled, and from there you could do so much more. But that's just one use-case.
