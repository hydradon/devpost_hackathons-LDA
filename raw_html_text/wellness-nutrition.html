Inspiration

One of our group members took Introduction to Nutritional Science last semester. A thought that popped into their head was: "Why isn't this common knowledge?" In order to promote awareness and knowledge for the nutritious vegetables we created a INSTAGRAM filter in order to reach our target demographic.

What it does

Randomly recommends you a vegetable to eat for a week.

How I built it

With Spark AR and Photoshop

Challenges I ran into

Learning a new studio kit, understanding how to use photoshop.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We finished it.

What I learned

How to work in a group effectively.
