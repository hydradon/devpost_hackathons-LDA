Inspiration

It is difficult to share resources and discuss when collaborating online . With Runway, all members can look at the same material together in a shared space to better understand what every other person is saying.

What it does

Provide a shared space for team members to come together and hold productive discussions.

How I built it

Used Jade, CSS, JavaScript, CoffeeScript, node.js, angular.js, mongoDB

Challenges I ran into

Implementing authentication (sign up and sign in) and drag and drop to upload files.

What's next for Runway

Implementing tag feature, uploading various content types
