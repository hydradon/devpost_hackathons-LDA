Inspiration

After learning about payday loans from a family member and doing some research Payday loan volume is estimated at around $40 billion a & 10 - 12 million American adults get one payday loan a year

Lending circle alternative that connecting lenders with borrowers directly

An angular web app connecting to an ethereum smart contract layer

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

First blockchain application for all of us!
