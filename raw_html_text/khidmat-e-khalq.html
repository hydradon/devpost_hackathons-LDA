Inspiration

Mobilizing the community is a sustainable and long term solution for Pakistans problems. However, the concept of organized community service is not established in Pakistan. This is because on the one hand the general public lack information about the Who? When? Where? How? Of community initiatives, while on the other hand non profits need to spend large sums to publicize their initiatives and attract volunteers. We aim to eliminate these problems and help Pakistanis become a part of the solution instead of the problem.

What it does

Non profits create a profile and upload their projects. Volunteers create their own profiles and search for projects that they can get involved with

How we built it

We created a web app using MySql, node.js, angular.js and w3css
