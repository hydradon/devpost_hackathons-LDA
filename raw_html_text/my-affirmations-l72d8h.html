• My Affirmations

Inspiration

I was inspired to create My Affirmations when I started learning about positive affirmations and felt a difference in my life by using them. I wanted to be able to get a dose of positivity whenever I caught myself in a negative mood to remind me not to sweat the small stuff.

What it does

My Affirmations gives you one new affirmation to repeat each day. You can also save your favorites and hear them again whenever you want!

How I built it

I built it with AWS and Javascript.

Challenges I ran into

Curating a large list of empowering affirmations.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I'm just happy that I built something I can use myself and I'm excited to see others using it as well.

What I learned

I learned that positive affirmations are a powerful tool to have and they can really make a difference in your life.

What's next for My Affirmations

I'm planning on adding more features and continually making it better and more conversational.
