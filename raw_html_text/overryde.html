overRyde

Comparing prices of uber vs. lyft rides!

Exposition

On the way here, two of the developers wanted to rideshare because they were unable to reserve a spot on the bus to the hackathon. In this tragedy, they quickly shored up their intelligence to find an alternative. And that alternative was ridesharing since they did not know enough about the advanced public transportation

Rising Action

The two quickly arrived at a dilemma: what ridesharing service should they use? They had used Uber and Lyft before, but in their short experience, these services had huge fluctuations and choosing one over the other was a nerve-wracking experience. To address this potentially disastrous financial loss for scrappy college students on a budget, we have addressed this issue with our skills and abilities.

Climax

We have used our abilities to create the ultimate price-estimate comparison guide between all services provided by Uber and Lyft.
