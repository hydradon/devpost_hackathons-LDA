Inspiration

internet, school and street bullying

Every one of us has gone through depression caused by bullies.

What it does

It plays calming music, inspires people with happy pictures. Takes a survey to see how bad the depression is. It was supposed to have kind of a waze option, in which people could report regions, streets or places in which they got bullied or harassed. It also lets people get one on one talk with strangers that allows the users let the emotions out, or they can get help from professionals.

How I built it

We build it using tools like html, java and wix.

Challenges I ran into

Finding good visuals and implementing our ideas in code in only 12 given hours.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We have cool music, cute pictures, funny comments, and inspirational website, which can actually help people.

What I learned

How to upload music and moving pictures.

What's next for Sit-in Happiness

Make the waze stop street harrasment idea real using google maps API and just improve the design of our website.
