Inspiration

Oftentimes we do not know where to go next while traveling, and it takes a lot of manual labor to go from one app to another to approximate travel time, waiting time, and finding the recommended places.

What it does

Provides recommendation for where to go next based on your current location, allotted time, chosen activity, and time of the day.

How we built it

It is built using Google and Foursquare APIs to approximate travel time and recommend places.

Challenges we ran into

Getting the website (mobile compatible) to load quickly.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It works!

What we learned

Building the website with html/css, building the backend, getting APIs to work.

What's next for Next

Polishing the UI/UX and extending to a mobile app.
