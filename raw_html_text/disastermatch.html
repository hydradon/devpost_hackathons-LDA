Inspiration

Inspired by the recent natural disasters around the world, from the devastating hurricanes in Texas and Florida to the heavy flooding in Asia.

What it does

Victims of natural disasters and other catastrophic circumstances should fill out the "I need help!" form in order to notify nearby people who have the resources and capabilities to perform any form of assistance.

How I built it

HTML/CSS, Javascript

What's next for DisasterMatch

  • adding a map to pinpoint victims
  • converting the website into an app for easier mobile access
