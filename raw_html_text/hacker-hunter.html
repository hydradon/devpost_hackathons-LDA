Inspiration

Some hackathons you just don't know where to start. Whether it's from a lack of teammates, ideas, tools, or mentorship, people can easily find themselves stuck and unable to get off the ground.

What it does

Matches your Hacker profile against profiles of other Hackers, project ideas, resources like APIs, and local mentors. The best suited are presented to you to give a good starting point for you weekend hacking.

How I built it

Java backend; Database and GUI needed.

Challenges I ran into

I don't know much about database management or UI.

What's next for Hacker Hunter

A fuller implementation and perhaps broader use across MLH and in other applications.
