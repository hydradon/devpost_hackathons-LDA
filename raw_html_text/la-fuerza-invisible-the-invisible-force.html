Inspiration

We are really interested in recognising the vibration of the sound in a tactile way. Our goal is to elevate sound experience using frequency alignment.

What it does

This system translates sound vibrations into physical and visual representations to give a fuller sensory experience to the listener. Our prototype can be taken on a larger scale for musical performances and can also function as a playful installation in smaller spaces.

How we built it

● Uses the voice as a controller in order to trigger events through a frequency spectrum analyzer. ● also uses localized vibrating devices across the body in order to improve our relation and perception to spaces ● Digitally created bodies that are exposed in projections and are modified using a program that’s connected with the voice analyzer ● It also has a mobile voice processing device that enhances the tonal dynamics of the user, helping the user travel the phono frequency spectrum. ● It has a dynamic floor surface that can interact with any material like dust, or water in order to increase immersion.
