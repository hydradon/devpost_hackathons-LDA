Inspiration

Yesterday I found $30 worth of good food expired because my roomates and I didn't pay attention to their life span. This hasn't been the first time and I'm certain other university students share my pain

What it does

Keeps track of your food (particularly fruits and vegetables) by telling you when the food will expire and how long you have upon purchase to save it for

How I built it

Using Clarifai API, I managed to use the camera to take photos of groceries. Thus, I can search up expiry dates of foods more easily than typing in.
