Inspiration

I always find it frustrating that I have to open my phone to add an item into a calorie app, so using Alexa, we can easily track calories just by saying what you ate and the most popular option will be entered.

What it does

Assuming a daily 2000 calories, you can tell alexa what you ate and she will say how many calories you have left for the day.

How I built it

I built is using AWS lambda in Javascript

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I'm proud of
