Inspiration

We wanted a way to keep track of our spending's but wanted to make it as easy as possible.

What it does

You take a picture with the camera, it get's OCR'd and then auto fills a form so the user can easily track where they spent how much money. Provides the user with a graph on a monthly basis to track monthly spending's.

How we built it

Used Android Studio and sqlite to make it.

Challenges we ran into

Getting the OCR to scan the receipt properly. Some receipts didn't display total properly.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Git and Trello. Our workflow.

What we learned

How to implement OCR technology to scan receipts and auto fill typeforms.

What's next for receiptron

Alerts on overspending. Ability to see tax/tips.
