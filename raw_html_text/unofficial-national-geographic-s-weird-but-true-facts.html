Description

These facts might be weird, but they are all true! Unofficially from National Geographic's Weird but True facts, find out things you never knew!

What it does

Weird but true is a voice interactive experience to help kids explore the world and the creatures in it through facts.

Inspiration

This app is aimed for kids of ages 6 through 13 who want to explore the world through facts about topics they love. We originally wanted to beta test this with local elementary schools (to introduce kids in lower income neighborhoods to new technology and to engage them in learning with fun facts). After we beta tested it, we wanted to contact National Geographic to see if we could make it go from being "Unofficial Nat Geo" to official.
