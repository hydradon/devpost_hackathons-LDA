Inspiration

In the present scenario we come across many platforms which can make a user socially or professionally active but there is no platform which can focus on overall development of user. Another thing which we are tired of seeing is the extra features and plugins on various platforms which we don't even use and our web application becomes heavy due to this. So we came up with an idea called NervesX.

What it does

NervesX is a platform which provide user a platform for overall development. User can customize the website in whichever way he/she wants. User has the right to control the operations other can perform on his/her post. Secure Social Interaction and Overall Growth are the main motive of this platform.

How I built it

NervesX is built on VueJs, Django, GraphQL, Django-channels and RestAPI.

Challenges I ran into

The big challenge while making NervesX was it's huge and complex module.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

NervesX is not a one night thing but we have completed the majority of its part in 24 hours. So we are proud of this.

What I learned

How to tackle problems and how to build such a big module is what we have learned.
