What it does

We leverage new technologies to make 1 on 1 debates more fun and easily accessible to the new generation.

How we built it

We use BotKit inside Facebook Messenger to connect debaters in real time based on common topics. Then by using an Angluar.JS broadcasting screen we gamify the experiences. While a debate takes place, other audience can upvote and like the arguments. The debater with the highest number of upvotes wins the round.

Challenges we ran into

Our biggest challenge was time. Deciding on an idea, mapping product features out, and choosing the primary feature to exhibit in the demo took up quite a bit of time.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We formed the team and decided on the idea at the event. We are happy that we utilized the strength of each person on the team to produce our demo.

What we learned

We learned how to release a working prototype in 24hrs. We learned how to work as a team. None of us had participated in a hackathon so this in itself has been a great learning experience.

What's next for Let's Debate

It is a demo. We will leverage the feedback we will receive from the audience and the judges to build a proper prototype.
