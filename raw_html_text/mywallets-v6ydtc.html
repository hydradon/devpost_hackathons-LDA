Inspiration

The invention of the Internet and printing press resulted in massive amounts of information, which led us to develop ways to sort through all of that information. The development of distributed ledger tech has resulted in massive amounts of wallets and we believe it's time to develop a way to sort through all of them.

What it does

MyWallets provides a dashboard for managing digital wallets.

How we built it

It's a react web app that interacts with API's of various exchanges.

Challenges we ran into

Settling on a project and tailoring it to the challenges. Paring down the project to decide on a reasonable proof-of-concept.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Introducing a new approach to digital asset management.

What we learned

Find a compelling problem, look to history for insight, and keep your solution as simple as possible.

What's next for MyWallets

Create a visually-attractive onboarding journey, implement an API for wallet integration, and extend the arbitrage feature to include popular exchanges.
