Inspiration

Super hexagon but with bees and honey combs!. We are a part of the Game Developers Association (GDA) here at OU. We wanted to showcase how easy it is to make a game, so we made a simple game that is similar to Super Hexagon. It took us around 30-45 minutes of work to create Super Honeycomb.

What it does

You avoid the walls and go for a high score.

How we built it

Unity and C#

Challenges we ran into

We had issues with learning Unity and had to use a lot of online resources as a reference. We wanted to make a working game with the minimum amount of work to showcase making a game.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to reach a playable game really fast. We finished the game in less than an hour!

What we learned

How to use Unity and C#.

What's next for Super Honeycomb

Level selection, difficulty selection, music, better art, and QOL improvements.
