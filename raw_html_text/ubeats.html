Inspiration

We like music. We encounter music in our daily lives and wanted a simple way to create our own.

How it works

Camera scans paper and registers shapes. It continuously scans for the shadow of the users fingers and when they overlap with the shapes, it play the registered sound.

Challenges I ran into

Calibrating the camera. Unstable foundation.

What I learned
