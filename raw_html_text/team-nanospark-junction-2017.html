Inspiration

A possibility to make an impact on the future of dental and healthcare

What it does

The collection and analysis of vast amounts of data and an API to serve both of these enable research and predictive care of dental issues. It also enables the creation of new services for Planmecas partners.

How we built it

The API itself and the data it uses are only an idea, but during the hackathon we have built a small demo of a possible product that would take use of the data and API with the Unity engine to show our vision of future possibilities.

Challenges we ran into

Regulation of patient data and the large scale of our idea forced us to truly think it through.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We managed to work through the problems we faced when iterating on our idea and were able to gather valuable feedback to further improve it.

What we learned

Sometimes it is better to go for an idea even if you think it's too massive or a daunting task. We are proud we had the guts to swim against the stream in hackathons and created a distruptive solution for the client company to create new business in the future

What's next for Team NanoSpark Junction 2017

Our wish is to see our idea come to fruition in the near future through fast development and easy to launch parts to gather valuable feedback during the whole development process.
