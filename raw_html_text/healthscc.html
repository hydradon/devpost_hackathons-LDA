HealthSCC, your private health assistant !

HealthSCC is an online health calender which helps patients to keep track of their health conditions and customize their own medication plans. This calender will also automaticly send patients notifications of upcoming events . HealthSCC is also a platform where doctors can manage patients' information and provide patients with timely medical advice.

The front-end of HealthSCC is developed with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Bootstrap, and we implemented its back-end with flask and sqlite.

With user friendly interfaces, patients can easily manage their health events and update their personal medical information in time. The overview of everyday events and periodic notifications provide patients with daily health care so that patients will not miss any important health event.
