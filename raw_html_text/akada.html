What it does

Akada's purpose is two-fold. It serves both to demonstrate the versatility of the Kaeon FUSION, and to provide a proof of concept for a service that would allow individuals to earn the necessary credentials for their chosen career path without attending college.

How I built it

I built the Akada site entirely using Kaeon FUSION, a new language I've been developing that can handle the full stack with a single syntax.

Challenges I ran into

I had to iron out some glitches with the Kaeon FUSION interpreter.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It may actually be the best looking website I've ever made.

What's next for Akada

I'm hoping to expand its scope and eventually release it as a much more sophisticated product.
