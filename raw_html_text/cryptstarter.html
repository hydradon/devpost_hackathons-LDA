Inspiration

Go Fund Me and Kick Starter are awesome tools to raise money for important causes ranging from funding a company to paying medical bills. We sought to build a decentralized app that helps you raise money for important causes.

What it does

Uses the Ethereum blockchain to store raised money in smart contracts, and distribute to campaign owners.

How I built it

Using react and Ethereum blockchain.

Challenges I ran into

It was very difficult to test the execution of smart contracts.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We created a web app thats able to create campaigns, and then allowed people to donate money to campaigns.

What I learned

Blockchain concepts, as well as how to write smart contracts using solidity.

What's next for CryptStarter

We want to expand our app to work on mobile!
