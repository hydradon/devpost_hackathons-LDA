We are all busy - from kid's soccer games to squeezing in lunchtime meetings - but we also want good food within our time constraints. Wouldn't it be great to have your food waiting on the table when you arrive at a restaurant? Wouldn't it be great to just eat and leave, and pay for the bill on your phone after leaving?

That's what Aftab does. Find your favorite restaurant, order ahead, and pay after.
