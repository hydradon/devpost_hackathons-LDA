Inspiration

Maplestory

What it does

Provides achievements to kids wearing their back braces to keep them encouraged and actively want to wear their braces, as well as track how long they wear them

How we built it

Unity and a lot of coffee

Challenges we ran into

Inexperience with Unity and C# development. This was also a rather impromptu group and as a result we had to figure out whether our skill sets would be suited for the development environment. The unfamiliarity with the IDE and development environment lead to many frustrated attempts at correcting things like Aspect Ratios and Variable declarations which in other settings would be relatively easy.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Artwork and assets turned out really well. Interface was decent.

What we learned

C# is a challenge that we will be better prepared for in upcoming endeavours

What's next for Super Vertebros

Adding many more assets to keep children engaged, as well as an expansion to the unlock system and further fleshing out of Parental controls.
