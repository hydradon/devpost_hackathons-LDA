Inspiration

Finding a way to spend your time waiting for your medical appointement.

What it does

It allows people to play a game together while waiting. With the simple principle of a rythme game, everybody can join a game and have fun playing or watching.

How we built it

We used Unity 2D and the Wii remotes.

Challenges we ran into

A lot of bugs... Especially with the controller management and the music analysis.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It works ! And a nice interface.

What we learned

How to overcome our tiredness spending all night long reading Unity documentation.

What's next for Heart Heart Revolution

The music analysis API we used was quite weak and we should either find another one, or develop one ourselves.
