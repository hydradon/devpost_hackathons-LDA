Inspiration

Shared universe is a growing trend in TV shows. In particular, Arrowverse has four shows that frequently crossover with each other. It is great as long as we are watching as they air. However, when someone wants to catch up, it is extremely hard to keep track of them.

What it does

Traditional TV trackers keep separate progress for different shows, our app can merge spin-off shows' progress and keep track of them all at once.

How we built it

TraktKit for TV show database, TMDB for posters, with Swift on iOS

Challenges we ran into

UI design of main screen

What's next for CatchUp

More functionality, better UI, and synchronization across devices
