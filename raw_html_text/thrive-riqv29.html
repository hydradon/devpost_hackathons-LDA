Thrive!

Inspiration

As college students, we strive for success. However, we often find that we spend too much time scrolling through social media. You cannot succeed if you don't put in the time, and it is our mission statement to elevate users' productivity and give them the chance to live to their full potential.

What it does

Thrive brings you the ability to track your browsing history in an intelligent manner.

How we built it

We started with the development of a chrome extension and a Python Flask server, tying in a php server for email functionality.

Challenges we ran into

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Our product is functional, marketable, and will help users achieve their full potential.

What we learned

We used the Agile Development method and pair programmed in teams of two. Together, we learned JavaScript and Python!

What's next for Thrive

Full integration of our Python server into the project and the ability to specify triggers for sending texts and emails.
