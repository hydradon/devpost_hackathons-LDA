Inspiration

When we were younger, we were big gamers and we hated the people that used the money to win. So we decided to build a model to find out who is more likely to be spenders in these games because they have a huge edge on the one that doesn't spend money on a game.

What it does

You input a few data and the model will predict if that person is going to be a spender or not.
