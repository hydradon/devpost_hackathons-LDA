PedPoints

Innovation/Creativity

How unique is this app? Is the idea similar to an existing product or does it have its own spin to it? Did the app have any special features or advanced technologies? Was the idea ambitious and did they develop an effective business model? How well did the app solve the social issue?

User Interface / User Experience

How does the UI look? It is professional? Does it have a certain theme and does it carry throughout the entire app? How is the user flow? Is it easy to use? Is the workflow intuitive or is it cluttered and not make sense?

Overall Quality

How finished is the product? Did the team scope their app’s features well given the time frame of the Hackathon, 24 hours? Is it just a mockup and prototyped or has it been developed and is the demo live? Did they face any challenges? How did they overcome them? Were there features that weren’t quite useful, resulting in a cluttered product?

Pitch / App Impact

What is the target market like? Would they have viable customers? Would people use or buy this app? Was the team cohesive and able to effectively explain their product and answer questions?
