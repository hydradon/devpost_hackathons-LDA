• NeckRelax

Inspiration

These days more people suffer from neck pain and sour due to long time use of computers and cell phones. Is your neck also pain or sour after long time work? Here is a skill to guide you through some simple exercises to relax your neck.

What it does

This is a skill to help you do some simple relaxation movements to stretch your neck.

Four easy exercises are involved:

  1. rotation;
  2. side bend;
  3. look up;
  4. look down. You can just follow the guides by the skill to relax your neck in a few minutes. Any time and any place.

How I built it

The skill is built with Alexa Skill Kit SDK and run on the AWS Lamda service.

Challenges I ran into

  1. Design a suitable procedure for controlling the exercising steps;
  2. How to describe the motion as compact as possible;
  3. How to control the time length for each step and proceed as normal.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Health is the highest priority for everyone! I am proud to build this skill that can make people keep fit.
