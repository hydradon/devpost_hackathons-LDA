People who are afflicted with both limited mobility and poor body temperature regulation may find getting up to change the thermostat for their comfort difficult. Nest, a smart thermostat, allows one to control the temperature in a house remotely. We have implemented a wearable system which allows Nest to regulate the temperature inside a house to stabilize around the body temperature of the wearer - effectively removing the need to manually adjust the thermostat for comfort.
