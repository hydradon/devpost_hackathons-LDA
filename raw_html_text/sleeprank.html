Inspiration

Due to the fact that lack of sleeping time negatively influences people's performance and health, it is critical to build an app to encourage people to pay attention to their sleeping habits. To achieve this goal, we model a "sleeping habit competition" based on personal social network. Users can choose whether to publish their sleeping record to social network and also gain the access to their friends' data.

What it does

Users link their accounts through Facebook so that their friends' data will be available as well. The app can rank the sleeping time, and display the top three "winner" in the sleeping competition and their own ranking.

How we built it
