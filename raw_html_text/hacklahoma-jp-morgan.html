Inspiration

Due to a widening gap regarding information between small entrepreneurs, either for-profit or non-profit, we've developed a platform that pairs them with investors based on matching criteria (e.g. mentorship, funds, stage of business). In particular, we've targeted entrepreneurs in lower-income counties of texas in order to help them, and their county, achieve financial self-sufficiency.

What it does

Platform that pairs entrepreneurs with investors in low-income counties within Texas based on a matching criteria.

How we built it

We used R (Markdown and Shiny), Google Sites, and GitHub.

Challenges we ran into

Lack of expertise in web development and integrating different software together.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Developed a working prototype that showcases the potential of the platform.

What we learned

Web development, R, Systems engineering
