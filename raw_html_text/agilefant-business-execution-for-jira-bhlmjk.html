Inspiration

Agilefant enables you link your business plan activities with the work that is managed in JIRA. Product and portfolio management, roadmapping and Scaled Agile Framework are all supported.

What it does

Agilefant makes it easy to split your vision into smaller parts until they are granular enough for teams using JIRA. Progress on epics and issues accumulates automatically from JIRA to Agilefant.

How we built it

The integration is implemented using JIRA REST API and webhooks.

Challenges we ran into

  • Linking issues to stories ('Story' is work item in Agilefant)
  • Reading customfield values and linking them to properties in Agilefant

Accomplishments that we are proud of

  • Two-way integration between Agilefant and JIRA (stories linked to issues)
