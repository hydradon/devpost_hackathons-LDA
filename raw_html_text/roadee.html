What it does

An app to help you plan an exciting road trip!

How we built it

Each of us had roles which we specialized on, yet we all worked extensively on most parts of the product.

Challenges we ran into

Time. Time. Time. Its always a struggle to complete the tasks which you have prioritized above the others. In the end, we are extremely satisfied with what we were able to accomplish in such a short period of time.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

I am personally glad to have grown intimately acquainted with the Web2py framework, and the super cool Ractive Javascript template for data-binding which we used along with ajax calls to make our website load and react to user movements.

Additionally, we had a strong focus on design, planning, and database management (aided by the MVC architecture built into the Web2py framework). Drawing upon our own experiences as users of technology and web-based applications we were able to narrow down which features were absolutely necessary to include within our shortened time span for development.

What we learned

While we all developed a much greater understanding of the Web2py framework javascript templates, and design, for Joshua and Sidney this was their first hackathon competition!.
