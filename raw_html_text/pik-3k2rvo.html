Inspiration

Life is full of experiences, captured with photos after photos, but when we wanted to share those experiences, popular image sharing apps such as Instagram gave us the same photo collage layout every time. We wanted the entire control over how we share our experiences - not just the photos, but also the presentation.

What it does

Pik gives you a blank canvas, which you can slice with your finger to form any arrangement of picture windows. Then, you can fill these picture windows with pictures saved in the phone's gallery, taking pictures from the camera, or with solid colors chosen with a color selector. You can undo and redo changes, make more splits as you go along, and save the final image to the phone's gallery.

What's next for Pik

We will be expanding Pik to allow more complex cuts, higher resolution images, filters/settings, and social media integration.
