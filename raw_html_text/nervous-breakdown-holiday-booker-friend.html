Inspiration

I have a friend who saves money for extended periods of time. When she has a substantial amount she packs her bags and goes to the airport and asks where there are cheap flights to. I got really stressed at the 20 hour mark of this hackathon and wished I could book a holiday. I couldn't be bothered to go through the long process of booking a flight and checking if I could afford it. I decided to create a text messaging service that will suggest a flight to you. Hopefully I would be able to link it to a banking api to allow it to pay for it completely.

What it does

It helps you find flights to go on holiday and can book them for you.

How I built it

Using Twilio to communicate through text and SkyScanner to get flight information
