Inspiration

We wanted an instantly accessible educational resource. So, we created a mobile application that allows face-to-face, in-person tutoring.

What it does

It connects students or parents of students and tutors through a mobile app.

How we built it

We used Squarespace to make a webpage with php in the background to manage the database.

Challenges we ran into

Lacking app development skills was a problem we ran into. We also had communication issues at times and misunderstandings as we were new to hackathons.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Finishing our idea and presentation.

What we learned

Team work is important as well as communicating and doing research prior to entering the hackathon.

What's next for Tutor Finder

To create a fully functional product.
