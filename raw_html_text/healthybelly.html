Inspiration

We want to provide the customers the new way to get the nutrition contents of the item in the menu by simply using our technology to scan the menu.

What it does

Our app will scan the item in the menu and it will find the nutrition contents base on our database. Using this data, we can calculate how the nutrition value that would help our users choose the right food item according to their healthy diet plan.
