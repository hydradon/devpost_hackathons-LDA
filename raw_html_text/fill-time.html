Inspiration

I was looking for something that could easily impact someones life as well as the environment positively... i realized that people use water everyday and sometimes while they fill their containers, they just forget about it and alot of that goes to wastage.

Source Of Inspiration: Quora Answer

What it does

Fill time simply helps by giving the user an approximate time to which they can easily work with by setting an alarm, timer or just reminding themselves that its time to close the tap - its easier to know at the very least when the container is going to be full.

Challenges I ran into

Trying to determine the exact time it would take for a container to fill. This skill assumes that the rate at which the liquid fills a container is constant from start to end.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Making something simple that can make a huge difference

What's next for Fill Time

Continue adding more features for flexibility purposes and support for more containers.
