Inspiration

We were inspired by our own personal experiences with projects that felt out of our skill-scope. There were times were it was difficult to find the help and/or mentorship that we needed to pursue a task, which ultimately led to the dismissing of a potentially great product.

What it does

It is a networking platform for students to connect with other students to find and work on fun projects and engage in collaborative learning.

How we built it

Brainstorming, low-fidelity prototyping, Sketch, Adobe Illustrator

Challenges we ran into

How to engage young audience to user the app? How to make it simple, intuitive and user-friendly?

Accomplishments that we are proud of

We created our own tone of voice and style guide. We focused on the user research.

What we learned

The importance of planning before doing. Time management.

What's next for LinkUp

For further development, we could incorporate sections on the application for more professional networking (connect students with professionals in industry)
