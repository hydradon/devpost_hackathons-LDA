Inspiration

Emojis and a more fun and creative work environment that utilizes social media aspects

What it does

The app makes it easier for associates to give feedback on how they feel in the workplace, through a simpler and easier to navigate layout, a feed where associates can view others' comments and addition of pre-made responses or comments to submit.

How I built it

Our team used Android Studio and github for version control.

Challenges I ran into

Our team had some challenges understanding the existing app and integrating its components into our app.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The feed with the company's responses

What I learned

UI design is crucial for user experience and motivation to use an app

What's next for HackGSU-AnthemApp

More improvements in the UI to make it more visually appealing and easier to navigate for the associates. Also, adding different views to the app such as the manager view that consists of more charts that will be useful.
