Inspiration

One of our underclassmen had to do online practice for AP exams and needed a timer, so we did this so he wouldn't cheat by gong to other tabs.

What it does

Implements a timer that can be incremented by the buttons provided, and in the end it rings and cannot be exited unless gone to another tab.

How we built it

Used the google get started tutorial for chrome extensions.

Challenges we ran into

We didn't really know javascript.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We actually finished a functional extension, considering this is my first hackathon.

What we learned

How to actually make a project (my own project for once).

What's next for TimerExtension

Reusability and stacking timers and breaks.
