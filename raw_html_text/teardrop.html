Inspiration

Our team started out with plans for an entertaining iOS app written using the Swift programming language. With limited to no experience with Swift or mobile devices among our team, this feat proved to be a touch too ambitious in this limited time frame and we all shed one collective team tear. Hence, TearDrop was born.

What it does

Add a tear drop to a selfie.
