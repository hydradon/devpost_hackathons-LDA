Inspiration

For save lives of San Jose residents.

What it does

It collects information related to minor accidents and road violations prompting city officials and the transport to take appropriate measures and it warns residents of dangerous streets.

How I built it

An Android application to push violation information and a web application for up voting and prioritizing by the residents or drivers.

Challenges I ran into

Data visualization and tight android-service integration.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The applications altogether and the services we expose could be used by any 3rd party application for better governance.
