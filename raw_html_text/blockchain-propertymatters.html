Inspiration

Property title document forgery, resulting in the owner going through a lot of stress

Transferring property in Czech Republic and many other countries takes a long time

What it does

Fraud costs a lot & property transfer times are long

People want their property secured & they want to transfer quickly

Our solution uses smart contracts on a blockchain to reduce fraud, reduce transfer times & reduce work for the Cadastral Office.

How we built it

We built a smart contract with Solidity and then worked towards connecting that to a UI, as well as working on a sustainable business model.

Challenges we ran into

Blockchain environment challenges and getting whole infrastructure working

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Learning how to code a smart contract including learning online and from mentors.

What we learned

SmartContracts, Remix,...

What's next for [Blockchain] PropertyMatters

Presenting and working on finer details and improving UI as well as interviewing relevant parties - including the related government authorities - to seek feedback and provide trust in our platform.
