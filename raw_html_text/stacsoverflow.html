Inspiration

Make CS Student's Life Easier!

What it does

Allows students to post questions related to coursework and answer others' questions.

How we built it

Using Django for back-end, Auth0 for authentification and HTML&CSS for front-end.

Challenges we ran into

Managing a database with different models (users, questions, answers).

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We have a working prototype.

What we learned

How to organize and manage the development of a real-world platform, which could turn out to be really useful.

What's next for StacsOverflow

Expanding it to the whole department online and many other universities after, such that CS students could get help each other. Also, our platform could employ a competition (e.g. user of the month), where the most active users would win points for giving out correct answers, motivating students to work harder.
