Inspiration

The sponsor poses the problem as navigating certain 3D viewing software but having limitation with using hands to touch the screen or control the mouse, so our team operate under the assumption that all hands are tided up and we thought, why not use the foot?!

How I built it

Accomplishments that I'm proud of
