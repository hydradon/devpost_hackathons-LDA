Inspiration

Anyone who has things to sell can become a business owner, including people who want to get rid of unwanted items, to people who wish to provide a service.

What it does

Treasure makes selling locally, and decluttering your life, extremely easy. Our workflow focuses on establishing relationships with the vendors

Treasure allows users to sell locally so buyers know exactly what they are getting, when they are getting it and they can discuss the product with the seller.

How I built it

Red bull induced bursts of coding genius.

Challenges I ran into

One of the issues we faced was figuring out the best UI possible to make it as intuitive as possible. We didn't actually start developing the app until Saturday morning do to partner issues but once we got going, we were fine.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We were proud of creating an easy to understand workflow.

What I learned

We learned about OAUTH and building scalable backend systems

What's next for Treasure

Integrating a chat feature, transaction logs.
