TAMUhack2019 Project

IEL is an interactive way for students who are learning algebra to better grasp logarithms and equations in the form y = ax^n + b through easy to understand and thorough explanations.

After prompting user for three numbers, the program computes an equation of the previously mentioned formula, then explains each step of the process, asking questions to the user along the way to ensure they understand the material.

Coded on Python 3.7, using a Tkinter GUI.
