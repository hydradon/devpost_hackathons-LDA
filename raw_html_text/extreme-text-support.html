Your personal assistant is waiting!

Inspired by Microsoft's Clippy , we wanted to make our own very helpful word processor assistant. We built our project using NodeJS. We ran into a few challenges along the way, such as the usual mistake of wanting to add too much without enough time, but we feel we've been able to create something simple that might make somebody laugh. We feel we've both gained a lot of experience with full-stack web development through this project. Extreme Text Support will be the first product to go under our company name, Affsoft .
