Inspiration

In times of need, communities need to rally together in support.

What it does

Give+Seek is where we provide unconditionally to those who need assistance and ask without hesitation from those who are kind. People can submit items or services to give and ask for help when they need it, so that others with compassion to spare can reach out to help.

How we built it

The front-end is built with Angular, and the back end is built with Express (NodeJS) hosted on Google Cloud Platform.

Challenges we ran into

Using Angular to communicate well with the back end, constructing the data flow and architecture.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Making a great ideation diagram and sticky note board that everybody was jealous of.

Making something to connect communities!

What we learned

It's important to empathize with the users of your product and see what would best help them when they need it most.

What's next for Give+Seek

World domination.
