What is it?

As you explore the known galaxy with Robo, your reconnaissance space robot companion, who is tasked to assist you to gather various materials, you will discover that space is the perfect place to learn. Design & Build your own Robo: Challenges focused around upgrading your companion Robot by collecting the necessary materials .

How To Play?

Input the commands via mouse clicks while looking at the buttons. Execute the commands to watch the robot go. Analyze the asteroids to find one with titanium.

What it does

Teaches kids about logic, science, programming, and critical thinking skills.
