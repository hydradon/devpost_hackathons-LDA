Inspiration

There are a lot of fancy new multiplayers out there these days. But the most memorable ones are ones that we can share with people right beside us.

What it does

Provides tons of intense fun. The goal of this game is to dig and pop up underneath the opposing mole. If you pop up underneath a rock however, you are knocked out and you lose. Also, your energy metre controls how long you can stay underground for, keep an eye out for it!

Player1: Up/Left/Down/Right, Right SHIFT for dig/pop Player2: W/A/S/D, E for dig/pop

How we built it

Unity, Photoshop, Web

Challenges we ran into

Making a game on Unity for the first time. Understanding input limitations when it comes to two players. Various bugs that made the game unbalanced.
