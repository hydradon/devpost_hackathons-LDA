Inspiration

Inspired by the constant efforts in the BVG branding and marketing material to better engage with a broader audience and present themselves as a younger and boulder corporation and understanding how games like Pokemon Go can change the way we percieve a city and its people, we developed a discovery game for Berlin.

What it does

Each public transport station got challenges that will reward the user. Those rewards can be used to buy tickets.

Challenges I ran into

User location, map implementation, trigger achievements based on location

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Our super cool map :)

What's next for BVG Discovery

Create lots of fun challenges and implement iBeacon technology
