Inspiration

My Wedding Icon (MWI) is an invitation app that helps you aptly capture the information for your big day without any hassles. My Wedding Icon makes sharing information about your upcoming event as easy as A-B-C. All you need do is register on the website, fill in your wedding information – photos and text, etc in the place designated by the right icon, create your app with the chosen icon to match, download the customized app with your information in it and then, share the invitations with family and friends. Having to share your information – photos, videos, venues, preparations and all just got a whole lot easier.
