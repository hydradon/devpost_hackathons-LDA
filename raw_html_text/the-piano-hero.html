Inspiration

We wanted to do this in real life: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2KdUL62_tFc

What it does

It allows people to play the piano using their hands. It is based on the guitar hero concepts and it has a sequence of notes that you need to play. If you follow the sequence correctly a .wav file will be generated and recognised by shazam.

How we built it

We used leap motion to recognise your hand movements and based on that you can play the piano.

Challenges we ran into

Synchronising lyrics with melody.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The graphical design of the Piano hero and the possibility to play it with your hands.

What we learned

How to change the pitch of a recording file.
