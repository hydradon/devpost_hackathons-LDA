What it does

It's pretty straight-forward. You click, the internet turns off, then it turns back on. No more reaching into the menu, saving 3 (3!!!) clicks for this pretty common annoying thing.

For those unfamiliar and wondering why this would be useful, often when in an area with poor connection the quickest and surprisingly effective way to get connected again is to turn the WiFi off and on.
