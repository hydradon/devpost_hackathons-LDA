Inspiration

I knew from the very beginning that we were destined to change the way people think about driving. You see it in cars all across America - people taking their eyes off the road, and their hands off the wheel to mess with phones or other navigation equipment. Countless PR campaigns have tried to warn people of the dangers of this, but to no avail. Something had to change.

We approached the problem from a different angle - what if the devices aren't the problem - what if the road is?

How we built it

We used Unity to create the TurnVR interface, and we used a modified version of the BeamNG.drive video game to simulate real word driving and crashing.

Challenges we ran into

Getting the trigonometry to work out with all of the direction finding was a big challenge.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

This is the very first navigation system designed to be wholly immerse and all-encompassing.

What we learned

Driving with this thing is even harder than we could have imagined.
