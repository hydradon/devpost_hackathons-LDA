We were trying to create a fun and casual app to try to appeal to getting the unicycle prize. Something basically useless and stupid but funny.

Our app is basically a clapping simulator. It simulates applause every time you tap the screen. It keeps track of how many times you tap & keeps a high score! Also, it gets louder the faster/more frequently you tap the screen.
