Inspiration

We wanted to create a three dimensional space for organizing thoughts. VR/AR came to us as the perfect solution.

What it does

Place and remove asterisks in augmented space with gestures or voice!

What it doesn't do

Unfortunately we weren't able to implement our original vision of creating a virtual space for annotation/ word ideation. This would be a potential next step for the project.

How we built it

Unity and Visual Studio

Challenges we ran into

Neither of our computers ran the required drivers for virtual reality development, so we spent a lot of time on setup and learning the technology.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Making something that works.. sort of.

What we learned

The intent of this project was to learn, and we definitely accomplished that. We learned how to navigate Unity, program in C#, and much about virtual reality development/system requirements!
