Inspiration

Well, we started as "How many condoms you are gonna use watching a movie" (CUZ SOME MOVIES ARE JUST TOOOOO BORING or too sexy ;P ). Then, we planned to go a bit 'subtle'. Here comes the 'N' Chill . A web app helps you select the best movie for your date night (or saves you from "condomplating" in the middle of the night).

What it does

Takes in the title of a movie as input, based on the genre, MPAA rating, IMDB rating and Rotten Tomatoes rating of that movie, 'N' Chill outputs the chance of you 'getting laid' on your date night.

How we built it

Using JavaScript, we fetch a JSON object for each movie from OMDb (The Open Movie Database) and take the genres, ratings to the back-end where the score is being calculated.
