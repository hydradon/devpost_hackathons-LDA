Inspiration

Core principles are:

  • Transparency
  • Trust
  • Simplicity
  • Impact

What it does

Enables the collection of funds that finance local projects which empowers the population and develops the country. Users (investors/donators) will be able to put money in, track and monitor the development of projects and the impact being achieved. The tracking and monitoring mechanisms will be detailed, specific and insightful: showing the business plan, how and when money is spent, progress against plan, and completion points.

How we built it

Web platform (with the possibility to develop a mobile app as well: Android, iOS). React, GCloud. Safe digital payment

Challenges we ran into

  • How to choose impactful projects
  • How do we encourage diaspora and local people to donate/invest
  • Ho do we keep track of the real progress of projects
  • Mitigate downtime and power problems
  • Understand local problems and actual needs

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Streamlining our idea, how we went from 1billion ideas to one working . The uniqueness of our propositions Teamwork

What we learned

Assign roles and responsibilities at the onset Avoid working silos The need to gain 'local' insights
