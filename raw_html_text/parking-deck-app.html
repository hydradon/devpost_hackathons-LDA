Inspiration:

We want to make it easier for college students to find parking at the parking decks on campus because usually on regular school days and during special events such as basketball games, football games, etc., the spaces in the parking deck get filled. When the parking spaces get filled, people have look for a new parking space to get to class on time or to watch the game. We wanted to solve this issues by creating an app that will tell NCSU students how many spaces are available at each of the parking decks so that NCSU student know prior before coming on campus that they can park in the deck. This app will help save people time and gas money.

What it does:

The app tells how many free spaces there are in the parking deck you choose at any given time of the day, the frequency of how many people are waiting in line to get into the parking deck, which parking decks are within your location, and the peak times for parking in the deck.

How we built it:

We built it through android studio and eclipse.
