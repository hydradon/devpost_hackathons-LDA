VirtualDeck

Leveraging virtual technology to create a more immersive experience for fans. Our MVP was to create an immersive emotional experience with Google CardBoard.

Using Google Developer tools for Google Cardboard and the game engine Unity, our team built an app to utilize 360degree panoramic photos simulating an immersive VR experience.

Challenges we ran into: our team does not have mobile developers, or experience with Unity and VR before hackathon. Always more time needed.

Accomplishments that we're proud of getting Unity and VR to work on the Google Cardboard.

What we learned: quickly narrow scope, collaborate, and have FUN!

What's next for VirtualDeck:

1- Create 3D Model of Cards in Google Cardboard 2- Use beacon/gps tech to create geofences and locations to unlock limited edition cards. 3- Ability To Earn More cards with more visits. 4- Vote on creating instant Highlight Card while in arena. 5- Create Community of Fans that can trade cards they don't have.
