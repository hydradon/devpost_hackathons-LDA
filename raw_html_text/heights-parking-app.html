How I built it

We used Android Studio as well as google maps api to develop this application

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Working prototype with big plans for the future

What I learned

You can't change the world in 24 hours

What's next for Heights Parking App

In the future we hope to connect someone looking for a spot with someone leaving a spot as well as have the map distinguishe between legal and illegal parking spots
