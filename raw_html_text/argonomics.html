Inspiration

As students who spend hours every day at our desk, we've noticed a significant disparity between the physical health of our programming friends versus our friends in other programs. Moreover, we've noticed that after a day of bad posture-induced pain, our mood is significantly worse.

As such, we've decided to produce an application that encourages people with a sedentary lifestyle to improve their every day life through a small, but rewarding change: improving your posture. By improving workstation ergonomics, we drastically cut the probabilities of developing health issues due to bad posture

What it does

Throw your measuring tape away! Our application uses AR to suggest the optimal desk and chair height for their work station.

How we built it

This application was built using Unity, C#, and AR Core.

Challenges we ran into

We had two main challenges:

  1. Understanding how AR works This issue was fixed by a lot of hard work and a little bit of mentorship from the developers at Unity

  2. Calculating vertical distances This issue was solved by reducing the scope of our product to horizontal planes such as the desk and the chair
