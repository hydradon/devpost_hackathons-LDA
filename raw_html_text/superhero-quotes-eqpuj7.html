The whole story behind this tiny effort

Kids love superheroes, don't they? Superheroes can be very influential among them. Kids try to imitate their actions. I thought why not harness this in a positive manner, teach them good things about superheroes and instill good manners among the children. Alexa will tell a quote said by a famous superhero which children can listen and parents can explain the meaning. Such small influences matter a lot when a child grows up.
