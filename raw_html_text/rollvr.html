Inspiration

We wanted to make a VR game. And the deadline.

What it does

It's a basic VR game made for the Samsung Gear VR. You play as a ball, and you tilt your head to change the direction you're rolling.

How I built it

Our team built this application with a Gear VR and Unity's C#.

Challenges I ran into

Unity is not a fun program, and VR does not have much documentation and support online.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We made a VR game.

What I learned

We learned how to make VR games.

What's next for RollVR

Polishing could be done, but otherwise, this project was used for experience and learning.
