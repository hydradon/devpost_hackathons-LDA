Inspiration

My daughter and I love music and we thought the other kids should enjoy learning about the musical instruments too. And what better way to learn music than playing a musical game with an educational Alexal skill. That is how we came up with this idea.

What it does

It helps kids to learn different musical instruments and how they sound. They can listen to different instruments and guess what it is.

How I built it

The skill was developed using Java Alexa Skills Kit SDK.

Challenges I ran into

The only challenge I ran into was to make all musical sounds into the proper format that Alexa accepts. It was an easy process after going through documentations.
