Inspiration

We always felt the necessity of attaching small drawings/diagrams to a Trello card in our team. Especially if you are a part of an engineering/programming team- this becomes a must. We didn’t like the idea of going back and forth to a diagram maker website to attach small drawings.
We felt the necessity of a lightweight in-app diagram tool, so we made Draw in Trello.

Under review process

This power up is submitted for approval and still in the review process. So currently it is not available publicly.
