Introducing TEN/TEN - the perfect solution for your personalized wardrobe. In Today's world, people spend their time and energy to find suitable preferences out of their wardrobe. Sifting through their entire permutation is a painstaking task. Their limited knowledge on fashion and latest trends makes it even more difficult for them to make an optimal selection.

TEN/TEN is an reccommendation engine that factors in user's taste, schedules, their current wardrobe, weather, venue and occasion for providing day on day clothing choices. Our solution helps users to arrive at an ideal speedy wardrobe selection thereby reducing time and energy invested.
