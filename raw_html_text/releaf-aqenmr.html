Inspiration

We wanted to create an app to address mental health.

What it does

Anonymously connects users with others who understand and know what they're going through.

How we built it

We didn't sleep, typed a lot of letters and consumed a lot of food.

Challenges we ran into

WIFI, data modeling, framework that we are not masters at, and languages we don't mainly use.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are very proud of our UI/UX designer!

What we learned

How to quickly prototype a product and make an implementable web application.

What's next for Releaf

Deploy the web app and improve our current product.
