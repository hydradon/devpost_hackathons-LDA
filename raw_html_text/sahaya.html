Appold

Bringing smartphones to everybody

A fantastic android application to do easier the life of oldest people who has not knowledgement about smartphones and how to use it. With Appold you can search whatever application installed on your phone and doesn't matter if you don't know how is written, because with a perfect approximately Appold know what application you want. You can also have a shortcut with the six telephones that you want to have a call only with tapping that you want. The third thin that Appold have is a magnifier, which allows to read newspapers with small font size or whatever you want. Finally have a pill reminder. That works with all the pills that you have to take during all the day, organized with Morning, Afternoon and Night with the hour programmed and also with an alarm that reminds you it.
