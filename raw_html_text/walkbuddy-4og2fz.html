Initial seed started while interacting with the representatives of the NNEDV, who expressed the nationwide need for an application that helps connect NGO's with the survivors digitally.

SafeBridge is the solution to connect with the survivors in a secure, haslefree and cost-sensitive way without leaving any digital footprint. All the organizations can just plugin and start digital connections.
