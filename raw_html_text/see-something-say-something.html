"See something, say something!"

Inspiration

All of us go about our lives noticing things we'd like to change or wished someone would change about our neighborhood. This app provides a great feedback system to let everyone know about a problem in a particular area.

What it does

It allows users to login, and provide precise geo-tag based feedback on any particular issue.

How I built it

With a lot of patience! I literally had to Google: "How to make web apps".

Challenges I ran into

I couldn't get the angularfire to bind to my Google map html file.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

This is my first and solo attempt at a web app and I had only 10 hours to put in. I am really happy I learnt so much in this time frame. The idea is very special to me.

What I learned

Everything! I learnt, HTML, Angularfire, Google-Maps, Javascript

What's next for "See something, say something!"

I will finish this project after the hackathon and make it a useful app for all.
