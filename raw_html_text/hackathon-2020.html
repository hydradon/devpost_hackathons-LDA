Our Vision

We want to encourage communities to share their favorite places with each other, whether it be a good restaurant, a garage sale, or simply a pretty view in the area. Stumble encourages its users to explore their community and meet each other. We believe there are true mavens in communities that have a natural ability to distill the best a place has to offer. Even more, this inspires more tastemakers and explorers. Become a part of your community by really getting to know it. Then help others get to know it too!

What it does

We allow for users to add specific locations within their community with a description of what they are, and why someone would want to go there. The main page will show all nearby posted locations, and allows people to get directions to them, and share if they have been there as well. A feed view also allows for users to see the latest posts in the community in a more traditional social media app style.

What we want to add

We would like to improve the features of the posts, allowing people to comment on existing posts, add new images, and vote on what are the best. Adding tags to the posts describing what category it is (restaurant, event, garage sale, etc) and the ability to sort by them is also on our to do list.
