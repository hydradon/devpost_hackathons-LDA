Inspiration

http://i.imgur.com/Jy0B6IF.jpg http://i.imgur.com/TTjzrGo.jpg Dora was doodling on the board all day. Destroying her Pikachu was fun! Now this little moment is a game that exists on the internet forever.

What it does

Technology often takes away from human interaction; rather than isolating users away from society, this game brings people together through creativity. Players use teamwork to draw a picture given a word to draw.

How I built it

Caffeine and internet.

Challenges I ran into

Updating a server real-time was a pain. Firebase provided incredibly fast updates that worked for a large amount of people simultaneously.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

NODE.JS.

What I learned

Drawing on a trackpad is difficult. Also I learned a bit of backend and Firebase.

What's next for Supersketchy

Multiple "sketch-rooms", login, scores, "accomplishments", etc.
