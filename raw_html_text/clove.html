What it does

Clove is a platform that allows users to post pictures of the products they buy from clothing stores as promotional material for those companies. In return the companies that are registered on clove must be socially responsibility and make donations to those in need.

Challenges we ran into

We wanted to incorporate computer vision to verify that the images being posted were valid, but ran into difficulties with that.
