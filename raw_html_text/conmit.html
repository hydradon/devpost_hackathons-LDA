Inspiration

I always go to the movies, only to find out that my friends have cancelled at the last minute. We wanted to create an app that would solve the problem.

What it does

Creates lists of events for people themselves and their friends. There are ways to chat and people will know who will be able to attend to the event.

How we built it

We built through marvel app making and inputted our ideas of what we want to see become a reality.

Challenges we ran into

Marvel maintenance made us have less time to work on our project.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Our creativity and idea behind the application.

What we learned

We learned time management skills and how to make an interface.

What's next for ConMit

Take this application and make it a reality. We also would like to add a location feature using google maps.
