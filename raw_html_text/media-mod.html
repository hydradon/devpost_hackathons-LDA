Inspiration

After talking with artist-in-resident, Danny Lee, we discovered a serious need for a better solution for when artists put out a call for new music to songwriters/producers.

What it does

The service provides secure file sharing, standardized contracts, and escrow of funds in the collaboration of music between artists and songwriters/producers.

How we built it

We built it with Node and the Ethereum blockchain.

Challenges we ran into

Working with Smart Contracts on the Ethereum blockchain proved to be difficult. As well as getting the blockchain to communicate with a traditional dev environment.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The biggest accomplishment was understanding and utilizing the blockchain.

What we learned

A lot about blockchain, and decentralized apps.

What's next for Media Mod

Next up is to refactor the codebase to make it more ironclad and begin adding in more features.
