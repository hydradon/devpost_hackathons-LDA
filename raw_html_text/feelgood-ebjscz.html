Inspiration

As a junior in high school, the college process just started, and students are very stressed or anxious or even depressed. At times like this, they need a website where they can just experience positivity.

What it does

At FeelGood, we provide you with access to an unlimited stream of positivity that will enhance your state of mind. Whenever you are feeling down, cheer yourself up with FeelGood, a platform designed to bring you joy with just a few simple clicks.

How I built it

We all worked on one computer. We divided our work so it's more manageable –– some people were in charge of writing, some were in charge of media, and some in design, etc.

Challenges I ran into

We were a little unsure of the direction. First we wanted to do mental health, then it changed into a general website for positivity, and towards the end we changed back into it being a mental health one.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We are very comprehensive in our efforts –– we made a logo using Adobe Illustrator, we created an Instagram, facebook, and twitter account that is updated daily about our account.

What I learned

Anything is possible!

What's next for FeelGood

We would like to create the website so that it can be accessed on the phone as well. And in terms of spreading awareness, we would also like to expand to include the negative side of mental health.
