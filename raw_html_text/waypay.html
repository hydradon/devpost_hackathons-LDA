The problem?

What can we do to improve the use of urban public transport?

What it does

Our solution is an electronic ticketing solutions for public transport networks. The solution allows the users of public transports to seamlessly pay for their ride via our bluetooth sensors.

Thanks to waypay, public administration can set dynamic pricing within their networks and set rewarding tariffs to people using public transport during car traffic peak hours.

Simple, transparent, seamless.
