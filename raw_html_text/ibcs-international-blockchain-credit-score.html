Who we are

  • We are a group of talented champions who organically met and came together from the 2018 World Crypto Economic Forum.

Inspiration

  • We are inspired by helping the underbanked receive lending and secure loans. This will be extremely helpful to those who need microlending. In developing and third world nations, they are adopting mobile payments quickly.
  • I could not get a mortgage for buying a house with crypto so we wanted a system to build an international credit score.

What it does

  • Decentralized blockchain verified Credit Score Rating and Peer to peer loans

How we built it

  • Built with Solidity on the ethereum blockchain.
  • Node JS server to communicate with blockchain

Challenges we ran into

  • The challenges we faced is putting together this entire project within 24 hours.
  • We ran out of ethereum test-net gas several times building the smart contract

Accomplishments that we're proud of

  • The number one accomplishments are the friendships we made through this.

What we learned

  • We learned how to use solidity and how to launch a token in 24 hours.

What's next for Sugar Score

-Mortgage payments with crypto done peer to peer -Car payments with crypto peer to peer -Token sale
