Our main goal is to combine physiotherapy and VR entertainment. We had a physical balance board and we attached the HTC vive set to the board. Our main challanges were how to make excercises of physiotherapy more enjoyable and how to communicate to the user the proper way of performing the excercise. The setup can be used for researchers and for fun. Our future plan is to develop more game levels in order to improve trunk stability and brain function.
