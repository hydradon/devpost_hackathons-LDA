Inspiration

I bet Rahul his skinny little arms couldn't throw a hard-hitting high five like I can.

What it does

Open the app and create a new game! Invite a friend with a game code and you'll be placed in the same game as them. High five, and check your screen to see who has the fastest high five! Want a rematch? Hit the rematch button!

How we built it

We used node.js, the device motion Javascript library and socket.io to make this work.

Challenges we ran into

How do we properly secure the phone to a wrist? Our teammate Nat devised the ingenious wrist mounts used for sick high-fiving.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting sessions and multiplayer. Having a slick UI.

What we learned

High fives are cool.

What's next for HIGH SPEED FIVE

Rockets mounted to the wrist mount. Officially sponsored high-five tournaments.
