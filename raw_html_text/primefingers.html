Inspiration:

What inspired the conception of this project is to enable businesses and products to get quality feedback on their products(apps, webs, prototype).

What it does :

PrimeFinger helps businesses and products to get quality feedback on their products(apps, webs, prototype).

How I built it:

We built it using web technologies to get user feedback

Challenges I ran into:

Developing a model that would work in our economy, making the process as simple as possible, integrating the designs by the frontend developer into the product and keeping up with deadline.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of:

I was proud to configure a Linode server and point a .tech domain to it and also been able to provide a platform where products can get feedbacks and reviews from their prospective users

What I learned:

I learned how to work with servers and hosting companies

What's next for PrimeFingers.

Pushing the product and adding more features, while improving on the features that are not working yet.
