Inspiration

We wanted to make something with the awesome Hacker Boards provided by MHacks. With a piezoelectric buzzer, a breadboard, and hours of debugging, we settled on playing some retro 8-bit music.

What it does

Plays the Mario theme. The two LEDs on the board flash in sync with the frequency of the notes.
