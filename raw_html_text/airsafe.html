Inspiration

I thought this up while looking around for ideas for hackathons on the internet.

What it does

TL;DR - Detects plane crashes and reports it to the authorites. Not an app, just a simulation.

It uses your phones GPS (not at this moment, once again just a simulation), accelerometer and other sensors to detect unnatural movement in a plane/helicopter/something in the air. Once it does, it will detect if it is a possible crash, and sends vital info to the authorities.

How I built it

At first I was going to have it as an app. However, when I built the app, I ran into many challenges, such as not being able to access virtual sensors correctly. So I switch over to Unity to build a simulation of what may possibly happen in a scenario.

Challenges I ran into

  1. Not really able to make an app, but making a simulation.
  2. Simulation errors (took up most time)
  3. Building the simulation itself

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

  1. I got a working copy of this complete
  2. The simulation covers most errors or faults that may lead to a plane crash therefore making it seamless.

What I learned

  1. How to create simulations for certain scenarios
  2. A lot about sensors in Android.

What's next for AirSafe
