Inspiration

Early response, CPR administration, and effective AED use can help save lives for tens of thousands of people who die from cardiac arrest every year . With average 911 response times of 11.5 minutes , and rates of survival decreasing by ~7-10% every minute , "zeroth response" care is more important than ever.
