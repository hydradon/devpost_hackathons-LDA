• Friends Who Travel Tother

Inspiration: As college students, we all understand the struggle of trying to coordinate a group trip. JetBlue's services prove to truly satisfactory and we believe adding this feature will make their services even better.

What it does: Makes planning group vacations efficient and convenient.

How we built it: Using CSS, HTML, and Javascript.

Challenges we ran into: We were short on time and couldn't implement all the great ideas we had.

Accomplishments that we're proud of: Working together and collaborating on a great idea that we put all our efforts into.

What we learned: Working in a group with a time constraint can be stressful but it allows us to grow and learn.

What's next for jetBlue Group Trips: We hope JetBlue agrees that this feature will be very helpful and will implement on their website and app.
