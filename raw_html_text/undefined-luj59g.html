Our major source of motivation was Carey Anne Nadeau, founder of Open Data Nation. Her success story inspired us, and encouraged us to build this app to help women in getting into Entrepreneurship.

It is basically a web portal, where sponsors and entrepreneurs come together. It is a platform where entrepreneurs can share their ideas and get funded by sponsors by getting in contact with them.

We used the following stack: FrontEnd- ReactJs, React-VR, Backend - Node.js, Database- Elasticsearch

Experimenting with new technologies and integrating with different apis.

We were able to integrate many small modules within a short span of time.
