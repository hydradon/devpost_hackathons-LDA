Inspiration

Currently we face these majors problems. Deforestation Desertification Extinction of species Forced migration Soil erosion Oil depletion Ozone depletion Greenhouse gas increase Extreme energy Water pollution Natural hazard/Natural disaster Metals and minerals depletion

Each problem is somewhere related to human actions. Inspiration behind this app is to make everyone realise how we are exploiting and destroying the nature.And how it will affect our future generations.

What it does

It allows the user to have a one on one conversation with mother earth. And listen from her first hand how humans are exploiting and destroying her.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am proud that i am creating something which might inspire someone to do some good for our planet. And also bring more people together in this cause.

What's next for Dying Earth

Along with the problems, exploitations and destructions caused to her, our beautiful earth will explain the user how he can take actions to save her, prevent her and nurture her.
