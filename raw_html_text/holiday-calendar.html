What it does

A calendar which shows public holidays in a year. To listen public holidays in the year, just ask alexa "Alexa, open holiday calendar"

How I built it

I built it with Alexa APL

How can I use this skill?

Enable Holiday Calendar from alexa skill store. Then just say to alexa; Alexa, ask holiday calendar tell me all holidays Alexa, demande à calendrier des vacances d' afficher tous les jours fériés
