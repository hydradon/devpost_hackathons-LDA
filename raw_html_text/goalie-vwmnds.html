Inspiration

We wanted to create a motivational app to inspire users to workout by using a rewards system.

What it does

The user orders an item from Amazon and they create a goal. They will receive their order after they complete their goal. If they do not finish their goal, they have to pay added shipping costs.

How we built it

Swift
