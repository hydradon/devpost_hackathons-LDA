Inspiration

Nowadays, cryptocurrency usage is reduced to a small percent of us. With KashBot we aim to make the process of sending Bitcoin accessible to everyone.

What it does

It helps the user in the process of sending money in a conversational way. Sending money to your contacts is now as simple as sending a message. KashBot manages the connection to your wallet and your balance.

How we built it

KashBot is built over the Coinbase API. It also uses the XE API to perform the currency conversion. The conversational engine of the bot is built over the Microsoft Azure Bot Service infrastructure. Finally, we used Firebase in order to manage friends and their addresses.
