Inspiration

We created an app to help those who need support when things get difficult, feeling isolated, and alone. Whether thats a blind person that want to connect with someone, or a Mom with a kid thats autistic.

How it works

One tap to listen to incoming messages. Double tap to record and respond.

Challenges I ran into

Technical challenges building the app - where our team is design oriented. And the inclusion of the speech-to-text with the machine learning aspect of it behind the scene.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Simplicity. One button. All the technology and chrome is pushed to the back.

What I learned

Voice and conversations builds trust.

What's next for AT&TAP

Machine learning algorithm in the backend that allows to connect people who need support with one another.
