Inspiration

We thought NFC was a pretty cool idea and thought it would make it very convenient to basically replace paper business cards

What it does

It allows you to enter in your personal information and beam it to other people with the app to store it on their device and easily make calls, send emails, or visit linkedin

How we built it

We used android studio

Challenges we ran into

We had some problems with implementing NFC due to its complexity, and the implementation of multiple activities presented some problems at times as well, as well as updating existing information from a recognized device

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We successfully implemented NFC

What we learned

We hate android studio
