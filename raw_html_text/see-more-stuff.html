Inspiration

I was aiming to do something completely different, but found that I had no idea where to begin, and so I decided to do something without the need for user registration and login

What it does

You enter a city or landmark and have the option of looking up weather information and places to eat around there. I would have added directions as well as points of interest and activities, etc. but ran out of time

How I built it

Simple Rails app with Twilio

Challenges I ran into

It was hard to differentiate between when a user was typing in a city and when they were requesting the weather or food. So hard, in fact, that I couldn't solve it. I would say the app is about 50% working.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of.

I'd never done a Rails project before, so the fact that I made a thing that kind of does a thing is already pretty satisfying to me.

What I learned

I am not good at Rails.

What's next for See More Stuff

I'll go home and actually attempt to get it working when I can think straight and my leg isn't twitching.
