Inspiration

We were inspired by how often we seem to dream a nice dream but then forget about it completely in the next 5 minutes. We're hoping we could help keep these dreams more memorable through the use of tags and images.

What it does

The webapp allows users to save and keep track of dreams through the use of images and tags. These tags are used to find trends across multiple dreams, such as a recurring theme.

How I built it

We built the app through a framework called Meteor JS.

What's next for Subliminal

We're hoping to add more ways of recording dreams, such as through a microphone.
