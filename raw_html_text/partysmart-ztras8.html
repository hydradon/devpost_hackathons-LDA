What it does

Party hosts can register their parties using their name, address, date of party and phone number. Neighbors can anonymously submit noise complaints by entering their address and phone number. The system automatically texts the party host with a warning immediately and the complainant in 20 minutes to ask if the noise issue has been resolved, and if they reply "no," it texts the police and party host to notify them of police being contacted. Admins can log in and view all registered parties and complaints and backlogs of information. Future iterations will display a map/list view toggle of the information, and will highlight "problem areas" or houses that have multiple incidents reported.
