Inspiration- Our inspiration for this project was that we recognized that there are cravings for different foods depending on a person's current mood. We came up with this idea during the team formation/ideation activity.

What it does- Our app lets you click a button (emoji) that describes your current mood and it gives you a food choice based on your mood that is chosen from a list.

How I built it-Xcode/Swift

Challenges I ran into- Adding the icon for the application, random generator code

Accomplishments first hackathon and successfully creating our first app
