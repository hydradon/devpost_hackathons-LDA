Inspiration

We wanted to create a new, innovative way that could help users find the exact locations of cheap, high-quality products through the recommendations of their own peers.

What it does

Our product Recognizes the product with one of Apple's CoreML Models, then creates an annotation onto a Google Maps display detailing the product's name, location, and price. It also displays products reviewed by other users. Finally, an Augmented Reality Label is created with the name of the product and the distance from its location to the user's current location.
