Inspiration

It's extremely important for people to socialize their dogs with other dogs, but many of us our lazy about that.

What it does

BarKode rewards you for taking your dog to meet other dogs, and also for taking them to pet-friendly places. More importantly, it will help users get reunited with their lost dogs by making it easier for someone who finds a lost dog to contact the owner.
