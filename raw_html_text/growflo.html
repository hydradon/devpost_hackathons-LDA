Inspiration

Some of our friends have gardens that they have to keep track of in their head. This can be very annoying to keep track off and is prone for forgetfullness and clumsiness. We want to make this task easier!

What it does

Welcome to GrOwFlO! This app will ensure that flower lovers are able to take care of their plants while doing something they always do: check the weather. Users are able to type in almost every city in the world and are able to get the temperature reading and a message explaining how to take care of their plant in that type of environment.

How we built it

We built the project using Android Studio and the Yahoo-Weather API.

Challenges we ran into

This was our first Android Project, so we needed to learn a lot about Android Development in general.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Making the app and having a lot of cool features including showing the weather, setting the alarm, and a points based system for using the app!

What we learned

Lots of android development from the whole team.
