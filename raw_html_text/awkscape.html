Inspiration

With the evolution of technology and social media, the amount face to face communication in modern society has declined engendering a new social phenomena, fear of awkwardness. Today we are here to solve your problem. Have you ever been in a awkward conversation that you want to get out of, or feel like a loner while hanging out with your friends because everyone has someone to text but you? We are here to solve your problem!

What it does

Provides assistance to those who are socially awkward by providing phone calls to save you from talking to unwanted people and text message to make you popular.
