We are pleased to present you our new extension: Kouch Dota 2 for streamers!

Many players stream Dota2 to show their game. They communicate, joke and offer various insights: which hero is best to play, where is it best to earn gold and other tips and tricks. We decided to go further and show the audience exactly how the streamers plays:

from development of abilities to where and how does they get their gold. And how good is the streamer compared to meta average. Curious how the streamer moves and where exactly they get so much gold? Well, we developed cool heat maps that show exactly that, plus where where they die and kill!
