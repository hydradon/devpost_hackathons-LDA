Inspiration

I'm a online video fan in youtube... so I follow different channels and always find myself searching new contents.

What it does

This is the command list:

  • hubot s | search - search videos/channels
  • hubot lc | list categories
  • hubot mpc | most popular by category
  • hubot r | related - show related to
  • hubot n | news - search news using

How I built it

Using glip hubot library.
