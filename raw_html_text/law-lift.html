Lawlift is an application to educate and support people seeking refuge in Australia, hence the forms for applying are very challenging especially for people with poor English and hardly enough money to afford lawyers. Furthermore, Lawlift will potentially reduce the delays asylum seekers experience whilst submitting incomplete or incorrect forms.

The application was inspired by the lengthy and complicated legal application process inflicted upon refugees in Australia. The Chatbot will undertake some of the preliminary information gathering activities to assist legal agencies to help refugees and accelerate the overall application process time.
