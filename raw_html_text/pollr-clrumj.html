Pollr

HackNY Fall 2017

Pollr is a Spotify playlist voting webapp built in Django!

By signing into Pollr, your Spotify is linked to the webapp. Pollr will generate a playlist for itself and give you your Spotify user ID. By giving this ID to other people and navigating to /<user ID>/vote , users can vote on the song they want to hear next on the playlist. Whenever you think you've got a decent audience, you can hit "Call the Vote!" on the webapp to wipe the slate clean and add the most popular song at the moment to the playlist.
