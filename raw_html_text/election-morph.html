Inspiration

The election is coming up, and not just any election. This might be the most important election in our lives, so we better now who to vote for.

What it does

It's a website that asks you policy-related question that you can answer on a scale from 1 (strongly disagree) to 5 (strongly agree). Based on your answers, you're told which of the two main candidates your political views are better aligned with. According to this, a pictures morphs into either Trump, Clinton or a creepy mix between them.

How we built it

We're employing web technologies, namely Javascript, Html and Css. The Morph images were generated using python.

Challenges we ran into

Gifs are a pain in the neck sometimes... Plus installing lots of libraries that we decided not to use because we ran out of time.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

A good looking website with an entertaining morph feature.

What we learned

Things about machine learning, morphing, some Javascript and most importantly: GIFs.

What's next for Election Morph

Next Tuesday when the picture will irreversibly morph into one of the candidates.
