Inspiration

Pokemon Go users are lonely and dont know were to go and are lost nowdays. They need some social guidance. And then: Kaz said "Let's use the Metal Library."

What it does

The app provides users with valuable data and data visualization on where to find the rare pokemons by location, by type and time when they appear. Users also will feel happier with sense of belonging to the global social pokemon catchers community.

How we built it

This cutting-edge technology app uses latest Swift with super cool Metal and of course Firebase. Social updates about where to catch rare pokemons are delivered by Twitter API.
