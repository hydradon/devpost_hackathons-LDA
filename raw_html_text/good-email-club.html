Inspiration

Every morning we wake up and have to deal with tons of emails. What if we could hear the good news first, starting our day off on a good email.

What it does

Analyzes your unread Outlook emails and finds the most positive emails with the help of IBM Watson's Tone Analyzer.

How I built it

Email content via the Outlook API is passed into IBM Watson, which returns traits about the message. We score them and play those emails with the highest scores.
