Inspiration

The Arduino that replaces your mother :) Uses Intel based Arduino 101.

MomDuino: your mother in an Arduino. Reminding you by phone and by speaker/LED, to carry an umbrella if it rains, wear sunscreen if it’s sunny outside, and more. There are two features which the device enables. First, it will inform you exactly what it feels like outside your house, including whether you need to carry sunscreen, wear sunglasses or bring an umbrella. These situations are called out to you when you attempt to leave your home. Second, it will wake you up at a specific light setting by detecting the brightness outside and sounding an alarm. Overall, MomDuino’s goal is to be the nagging mother you need.
