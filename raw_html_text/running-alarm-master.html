If you have trouble getting up in the morning for your morning run, or just have trouble getting motivated enough to get out the door, our app will make you move a certain distance to turn off your alarm. Using your current location, the app calculates when you move outside a certain radius of your starting point. Yes, it sounds annoying! Which is perfect to get you up and out that door. It's easy to use, user-friendly, and sports a minimalist modern design. Check it out!
