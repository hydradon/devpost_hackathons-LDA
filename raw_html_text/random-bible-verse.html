Inspiration

I was inspired to make this because I didn't see a random bible verse on the skill store. And while I do my best to read a verse a day from the Bible, sometimes I just don't. I thought, why not make this skill so I can hear the Word of God whenever I am near an Alexa device.

What it does

Alexa reads a random verse to anybody asking her to do so.

How I built it

I used javascript, and the alexa sdk documentation.

Challenges I ran into

Making Alexa read the Chapter and verse as a ordinal statement, not a normal statement. Also getting Alexa to read it with the correct pausing after saying the verse.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Just getting it working:)

What I learned

How to convert an API response to Alexa voice.

What's next for Random Bible Verse

I am thinking of being able to ask this app to read me a random verse in a specific topic, such as Fear, Love, Patience etc.
