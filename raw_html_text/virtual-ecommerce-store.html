Inspiration

eCommerce is a huge business but many times customers are making decisions based on a few different images of something. We wanted to make a solution that helped online shopping have a similar experience to shopping in real life. By being able to look at 3D models, users can get a better idea of the scale of the product they are thinking about and look for details a photo would be unable to capture.

What it does

Users enter the store and can add items to their cart. When they are ready to checkout the Capital One API processes payment and Twilio is used to send a payment receipt to the buyer's phone.

How we built it

Unity, Capital One API, Twilio

Challenges we ran into

Unity's UnityWebRequest and WWWForm was being a potato

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Making something in less than 36 hours

What we learned
