Inspiration

Patients are dying around the world due to the inefficiency, exorbitant costs, and shortage of ambulance services. Emergency medical services should be reliable, affordable and quick. Patients should not need to wait more than 10 minutes to even get to the hospital

What it does

GoAmbo is an emergency ambulance service. We combine the efficiency of a cab service like Uber/Ola with the necessity of a sound ambulance service in developing country like India. Any person in need of emergency services can simply click a button on the app, and our trained drivers will arrive within minutes to take you to the nearest hospital, using GPS optimization
