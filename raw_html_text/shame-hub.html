• Program working. The boxes show the timestamps, the content of the posts and a link to access them.

We all had moments in our lives where we felt the need to post something more politicized in our Facebook account, and never thought about the consequences that it may bring in our future. As Facebook is a fairly open social media, anyone could read these posts, whether it is your close friends, family, or even your next boss. With that in mind, we decided to build a web app that analyses your Facebook posts and shows you a list of them that contains some keywords or topics that may be of your interest to delete them, like controversial topics and bad words.
