Show Me Bobola!

Why use real brush and pain? If pixels on a computer screen can be changed by the program.

By imaging a brush in our hand we can make the famous Bob Ross drawings in digitals format.

With image recognition and Leap Motion, we built a fully functional tool. To give you a new experience of drawing.

Why we did it? We all wanted to experience something new and learn something while having fun.

The main goal was to create something new for people to enjoy and have fun with.

The name comes from the Tombola toy we used to test it.
