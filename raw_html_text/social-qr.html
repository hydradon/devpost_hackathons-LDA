Inspiration

We became tired of having to ask more than once for people's various social media platforms so we decided a single step process for all social media 'requests' would be beneficial for humanity.

What it does

It scans a QR code and adds the person who 'owns' the QR code on all social media.

How we built it

Using swift and xcode with firebase as a potential backend

Challenges we ran into

Learning how to write in swift and use xcode.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We didn't cry too much and we have a skeleton of an app, with some psuedocode and limited functionalities.

What we learned

We learned lots of iOS development skills.

What's next for Social QR

The continuation in the development of Social QR.
