Inspiration

No Objection Certificate (NOC), is a legal certificate issued by any agency, organisation, institute or, in certain cases, an individual. Most of the times for any new setup (Business/Infrastructure), investors have to get NOC from Pakistan Government Agencies.

While approaching the government authorities, users usually face inconvenience in terms of time delays and obscure processing which hurdles the industry growth in our country. The problem can be addressed if we could provide a solution which can facilitate the investors by improving the customer relationship and process advancement.

What it does

Our service provides general public, who are dissatisfied with the current procedure of NOC processing by government sector, a convenient, reliable, time saving and transparent online solution to their problem.

How we built it

Currently we have developed a prototype iOS app using Swift. We have also developed a prototype website using wordpress.

Challenges we ran into

Currently our main challenge is to get proper collaboration and funding from the government. As to facilitate the general public with Government processes, we will have to involve the higher authorities from the Government. This will require proper training and marketing as well.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

In a limited time of two days, we were able to come up with the solution for a issue which is faced by general public on a large scale. Our project will be a milestone in fostering innovation and promoting sustainable industrialisation. This is also a step towards better infrastructure.

What we learned

We learnt how to work on solving civic problems in a limited amount of time by collaborating with like minded people.

What's next for eNOC

We plan on :

  • Scaling the project by incorporating more sectors for NOC
  • Providing localisation eg urdu, and chinese etc.
  • Providing analytics to FBR for tracking investments all over the country
