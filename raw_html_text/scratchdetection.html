Fast vehicle damage assessment through automated visual inspection using your smartphone

For Private Users:

By taking pictures of your vehicle, we can quickly and automatically find damages, scratches or bumps. This helps you with insurance claims or gives you security and transparency when using car sharing.

For Business Users:

You can use our technology for monitoring the state of your vehicles more easily and quickly.

Shipping Companies

Your trucks don't have to return to your company for damage assessment or state monitoring, our technology enables you to do this on-the-go.

Automotive Logistics Companies

Our technology gives you a way to insure yourself against false damage claims for the cars you transport.

About our Technology at this Hackathon

We trained a convolutional neural network (TensorFlow ImageNet v3) to detect scratches and bumps on cars. Our app is built using Ionic, in the Backend we use an express-server, some python scripts and graph.cool.
