Inspiration

We have a vested interest in education and being able to work with JA Arizona is a huge opportunity to give back to the community. Helping volunteers find schools to take their passion will allow the education system to flourish. This way, both volunteers and students benefit.

What it does

The mobile application will allow volunteers to add classes they wish to help out in and the admin will be able to manage the schools listed.
