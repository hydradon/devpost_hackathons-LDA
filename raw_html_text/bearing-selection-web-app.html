Inspiration

The amount of time I realized it took to look up bearings for my desired application.

What it does

Searches a bearing catalog given the bearing company name, and the desired loads and outputs available bearing products with their associated part number, safety factor, and Life ratings

How I built it

Excel, html, CSS

Challenges

I ran into several function errors to reference tables at the beginning

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The fact that I was able to get this app to calculate Life Ratings and Safety factors by performing calculations with formulas

What I learned

How to use excel to calculate Life Ratings and Safety Factor

What's next for Bearing Selection Web App
