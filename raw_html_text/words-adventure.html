• Words Adventure: Alexa skill for kids

Inspiration

Amazon has made great strides in terms of voice technology. The opportunity to create for voice and make a difference in children's lives ultimately inspired me.

What it does

Words Adventure teaches kids key components of the English language such as nouns, adjectives, verbs etc, through a gamified quiz session each spanning for five questions. Whenever a kid get's the right answer they're congratulated by a by bazinga! SSML audio, bringing out the game-like nature of the quiz.
