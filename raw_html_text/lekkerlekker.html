What it does

Forget standing in front of huge shelves not knowing what to do cook with an egg plant or exotic chinese fruits. Start lekker lekker, directly pick your favorite recipe and all the ingredients are automatically added to your shopping cart. If you like, a supermarket nearby will delivers your groceries within hours.

You still have some vegetables and some food left in your house and don’t know what to do with it? That’s not a problem. Tell the app what it is in your fridge, click „suggest recipe“ and lekker lekker will provide you a perfect meal and which ingredients you still need to start cooking. Proceed to checkout and the delivery man it’s on it’s way. You didn’t waste food, saved money and your friends are enjoying a delicious meal at your next dinner party.
