Inspiration

We are Cervelio, an international and skillful team from Canada, US, Italy and Finland, here to challenge the concept of what a bus stop can be. Our team is developed as a result of winning European Innovation Academy in July 2016 with a startup idea (Cervelio). Ever since, we have worked together to overcome numerous challenges. We were recently joined by an additional team member for the sake of Junction 2016. We are passionate about finding new and creative ways to make human and technology interaction more natural. We have challenged Emotion AI, Bio Sensing, and now AR.

What it does

We are creating an interactive, comfortable and entertaining space within the familiar environment of a bus stop. We are bringing convenience, timeliness and relevance to the user experience at the bus stop. Our plan to utilize HoloLens technology in implementation of our vision.

With our demo we will cover the following features:

  • Weather
  • Music
  • Community feel
  • Bus route
  • News & entertainment
  • Purchasing on the go

How I built it

We courageously took on the challenge to work with the HoloLens. We also implemented Unity, Visual Studio to achieve our final goal.
