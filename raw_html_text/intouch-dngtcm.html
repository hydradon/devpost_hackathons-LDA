Inspiration

With college students who face daily stresses and challenges in mind, we created an app to help improve anyone's lifestyle.

What it does

InTouch is designed to drastically improve anyone’s well-being. Whether they’re struggling with depression or anxiety, or simply want to better their life, InTouch is made to keep track of the building blocks to a healthier, happier lifestyle. It functions by monitoring four essential components: diet, sleep, mindfulness, and happiness.

How we built it

The app was built using Xcode and Apple's programming language, Swift.

Challenges we ran into

Time. Brainstorming, designing, writing, implementing, and bug-fixing an iOS application in 24 hours was our greatest challenge.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

InTouch is designed to be as simple and effective as possible. The simple, non-intrusive design makes it intuitive and easy to navigate.

What we learned

This was the first major Swift project for several of our team members making the it range from learning how to create a button on a ViewController to learning to implement complex graph and animation APIs.

What's next for InTouch

We want to implement a community aspect to the app where you can share your progress and chat with other users.
