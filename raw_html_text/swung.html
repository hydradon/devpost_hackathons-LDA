SWUNG

“A place for singles to become doubles”

This is a dating service for baseball fans!

Tired of relationships with people who don't understand America's Pasttime? Exhausted with dating fans of your rival team? Then Swung is for you! Suggests matches based on Baseball in general!

Created by Chris Daley, Jon Farrell, Nathan Burgess and John Williams for Boston Baseball Hackday 2017
