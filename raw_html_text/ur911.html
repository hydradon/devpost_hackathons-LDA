Inspiration

In some emergency situations, calling 911 is not an option but texting is. Unfortunately, most emergency call centers do not accept text messages.

What it does

ur911 allows you to text to it, will dial the nearest call center and read out your text message.

How we built it

ReactJS front-end hosted on Heroku. Python back-end hosted on AWS lamda
