Inspiration

Increase engagement and viewer contributions by letting your audience interact and play with you directly through your stream

What it does

Playground is the ultimate audience engagement extension. Create custom engagements, and let viewers contribute and participate in your stream. Share the love and give your viewers the recognition they deserve when they contribute to your channel!

Chat Capabilities: Upon completing a successful engagement, a message will be automatically sent to your channel.

How I built it

We are using React.js for the front-end And Node.js for the EBS + MongoDB, on AWS ec2.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Streamers like the products and keep using it!

What I learned

Engaging between content-creators/streamers and audience is something that gets bigger and bigger

What's next for Playground - Engage your Audience

More features are coming! Bidding, voting and more! - requested and driven by our community.
