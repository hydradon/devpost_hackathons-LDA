What it does

Tone Detector will collect all of your sent messages in public channels and feed them to IBM-Watson Tone Analysis. Every Monday the user is sent a message from Tone Detector containing the statistics of how their messages were received.

How we built it

Built using javascript, node.js, slack API, and IBM-Watson API

What's next for Tone Detector

We want to integrate the ability for a user to communicate directly with Tone Detector to send text on the fly to be evaluated. For example, to feed it emails you plan on sending. Before you send the email you can ask tone detector to give you stats on how the email may be perceived.
