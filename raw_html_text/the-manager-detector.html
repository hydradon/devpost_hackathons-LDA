What it does

Imagine you are bored with work and want to open some NSFW tabs in the office, you have to watch out for people around you. The manager detector has a motion sensor that detects when people cross the sensor, then it will shut down all the non-work related and save you from your boss.

How I built it

With Arduino knowledge and internet
