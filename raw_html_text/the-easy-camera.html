Inspiration

My friend has a hard time using her new camera she got and shes left handed

What it does

It makes it easier for the person to take pictures and may appeal to younger audience, also will come for both right and left handed people

How I built it

I used a program called Sketchup 2016 which was a great help on how my design will look

Challenges I ran into

A challenge I ran into was the placement of the button, I wanted it make it easy for the user to turn it on and off and also take pictures

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I made it also for left handed people, I noticed that most of the supplies that I use as a right handed person may be hard to use for a left handed person. I did some research and found out that many left handed people die using right handed tools

What I learned

I learned the most of the things we use are meant for right handed people, I also learned that there is no camera meant for left handed and right handed people

What's next for The Easy Camera

I want to make it a real thing and test it so I can see some of my mistakes and make it easier and better for the user to have a better experience
