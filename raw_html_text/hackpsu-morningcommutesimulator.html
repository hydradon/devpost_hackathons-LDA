HackPSU-ShootingStarsBreakout

This is a groundbreaking new game for the HTC Vive set in a space themed universe. The player uses their paddle to hit the legendary Powerball into bricks for points. The game lets you interact with the physical world, by grabbing the ball as well as the bricks, with sick tunes to match the fun gameplay.

Check out footage of Rodney Wells playing the game alongside gameplay here! https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SpOB_gBznCY&feature=youtu.be

Music is "Shooting Stars" by Bag Raiders.
