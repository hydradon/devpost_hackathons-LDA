• PRO SAFETY

Algeria is a country which is still in development because of that it contains a lot of problem and one of them which is really sensitive is that 40% of medical errors victims are due to lack of information (Source: "Secretary General of the Algerian Organization victims of medical errors ) and more over a lack of care about other diseases such as Aizahmer we found that more than 100 000 people have this disease in Algiers; So to prevent this problems we though about making PRO SAFETY service which includes small device which contains a unique QR code for every costumer. This QR code will refers to the full costumer's health histogram ( Diseases, medicals.. ). This small device will also contains a GPS module that will help our costumer to easily find their own children or even old parents who have Aizahmer ( as an example ). Our service also contains a website and two mobile applications ( One for the costumer and the other will be for the concerned authorities - S.A.M.U., Hospitals.. - ).
