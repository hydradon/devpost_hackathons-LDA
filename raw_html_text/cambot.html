Inspiration

What it does

Our robot takes pictures and moves on its own.

How we built it

We used an arduino with a motorshield and the nodemcu 1.0.

Challenges we ran into

We attempted to use the dragonboard in our project however our first dragonboard was broken. We replaced this one, however it would not connect to WiFi. Our nodemcu chip would also not connect to WiFi.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of being able to get the various different pieces to work together.

What we learned

Using different forms of serial communication
