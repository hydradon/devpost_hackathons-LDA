Inspiration

I love going out and meeting new people! So much that it sometimes keeps me from getting my work done. So why not blend the two? Here's Social Study , a find-a-study-group network that connects people, places, and work that needs to be done.

What it does

By pressing a button or sending a text, I can set up an impromptu study sesh wherever I want! Or, I can take a peek on Twitter or reddit to find a Social Study near me.

How I built it

I used the amazing Wi-Fi connected Photon combined with IFTTT.com to link my inputs to the platform's posts.

What's next for Social Study

Next up is integrating location sensing into my hardware and more detailed Social Study profiles (subjects, interests, etc.)
