Inspiration

Share the happiest//most enjoyable thing in ur day

What it does

Offering an opportunity for users to note down daily happy//valuable moments in life.

How we built it

We used firebase to create a sign-in platform for users. And we built UI tableview to browsing different "cards" posted by users. Users could design their cards' contents and colors through single interactions with our interface.

Challenges we ran into

Lots of BUGS How to write delegates Viewcontrollers management

Accomplishments that we're proud of

User Interface & to let users make every day count ~

What we learned

Test and build incrementally Good design is really important

What's next for hapi

Add a calendar to demonstrate different colors for your day. Upload your cards to firebase and update your friends' cards into your desktop. You can comment//like others' cards.
