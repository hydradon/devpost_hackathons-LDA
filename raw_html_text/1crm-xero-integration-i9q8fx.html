• 1CRM - XERO Integration

Eliminate double data entry between XERO and 1CRM

Our integration is a 2 way sync between XERO and 1CRM . Integration syncs Accounts (Companies) , Ledger Accounts , Taxes , Currencies , Products , Purchase Orders , Invoices and Payments between 2 systems .

As challenges we had to over come , small differences between XERO and 1CRM that even being small are big challenges ex Tax Inclusive / Tax Exclusive from XERO that is not supported this way in 1CRM

We are proud to produce one more fully functional integration that even if had some issues now works smoothly and accomplished this with quite limited human and financial resources .

To answer your question as "I learned" .. I and we learned your api , for me personally quite well :) can't say I've learned anything more from this integration since we integrated quite a good number of other accounting systems before :)

What's next for 1CRM - XERO Integration

As next steps for this integration we aim to make it easier to use for users , have a better error tracking for it and eventually integrate more modules if would be possible .
