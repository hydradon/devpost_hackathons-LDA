Inspiration

We wanted to figure out our eyesight without going to the optometrist.

What it does

Test out your eyesight with 8 different levels, each level with progressively smaller letters.

How I built it

Using Swift

Challenges I ran into

We had some difficulty getting started, as we didn't really understand how to make an app, and our initial idea turned out to be too difficult for us to work out within the time limit. We also had some trouble with working out a random letter generator.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Learning how to use Swift in Xcode, and being able to make a finished project after having to scrap our original idea and starting four hours behind.

What I learned

We learned how to use Swift to make an app.

What's next for SIGHT

Next, we would like to have an option for vocal input, and find a way to calculate the length from eye to phone instead of using average arm length.
