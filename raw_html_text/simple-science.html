Inspiration

I wanted to create a skill to to help make science fun for young people.

What it does

It gives basic chemistry, biology and physics facts to 7 to 11 year olds to help them learn science easily.

How we built it

In JavaScript, using Alexa Lambda function.

Challenges we ran into

Writing the facts took a long time, and making sure they were simple but interesting.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Making a cool skill to help kids learn science

What we learned

Making an alexa skill is fun!

What's next for simple science

Adding more facts to expand the skill or expanding the skill to have facts aimed at other age groups learning science.
