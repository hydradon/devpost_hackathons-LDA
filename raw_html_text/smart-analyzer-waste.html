  • Problem Statement

An application to measure, analyze, compare the waste produced by manufacturing plants on day to day basis. This will be a dashboard which will contain the data related to the waste produced by manufacturing plants such as the metal scrap, defective hardware parts, chemical waste, nuts and screws etc. This will display an interactive graphical analysis of everyday waste which will allow the engineers in the plant to steadily improve the overall product quality and profitability by applying simplification methods to reduce the waste.
