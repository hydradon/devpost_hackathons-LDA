What it does

When you stand in front of an artwork, simply take out your cell phone and take a picture. The app will then show its information on the screen with a "headset" icon. Click on the icon then switch your focus back on the artwork while listening to the audio guide provided by the app.

How we built it

Java / Google Search / Wikipedia / TextToSpeech API / Cloud Vision API / Android Studio

Challenges we ran into

Lack of sense in aesthetics while designing UI.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We've learned about mobile app development, UI design, Google text-to-speech API, Cloud Vision API, and Android Studio.
