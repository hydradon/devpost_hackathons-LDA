Inspiration

The lack of Trust in Hotel Booking Platforms

What it does

TRENT aims to restore the right values by providing transparency in Hotel Booking Platforms through the power of Blockchain.

How We built it

Using Truffle and Ganache we will demonstrate how we can use a distributed ledger to make clean and transparent transactions. These transactions will include critical information that customers do not usually see in typical hotel booking platforms.

Challenges We ran into

Uncovering the core details that were missing in typical hotel booking transactions. Repairing the trust tension between Customers, Hotel Owners and the Middlemen.

Accomplishments that We're proud of

We managed to create a Smart Contract within 24 hours of the hackthon without any prior experience in the subject.

What We learned

Blockchain is a disruptive technology that has the potential to reshape the way we do things

What's next for TRENT

We will address the technical aspect and reinforce the business model of our startup
