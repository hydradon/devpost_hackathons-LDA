Inspiration

Hardware and arduino kits from the MLH hardware lab were available to us. I love hardware, so I decided to make a project.

What it does

There are five different sensors/devices that I was able to use -- a piezo buzzer, LCD RGB Backlight, temperature sensor, light sensor, and touch sensor.

How I built it

I used online resources and coded my own custom actions.

Challenges I ran into

Connecting the arduino was a struggle.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I made the piezo buzzer play "location" by khalid.

What I learned

Hardware is a fickle thing.

What's next for Arduino101 Sensorpack
