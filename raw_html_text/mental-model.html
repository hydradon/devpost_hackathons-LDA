Mental Model

Inspiration

With 1/5 Canadians suffering from mental illness, including friends and loved ones of mine, I've always been passionate about mental health. However, assistance remains inaccessible and distant to many of those in need. I try to promote accessibility to mental health care with technology, through projects such as this, and my experience at Medimo Labs.

How I built it

This project was built with React, Bootstrap, D3.js, and Neataptic.
