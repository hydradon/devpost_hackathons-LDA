Inspiration finding help for people who live alone and have family that worry about them but don't live with, specifically aimed at people with heart problems

What it does it's an app that sends and receives alerts through a heart monitor bracelet which is connected via bluetooth.

How we built it Mit app inventor

Challenges we ran into we had problems with coding the actual program to work like we wanted to and we had problems deciding what our target audience with this would be.

Accomplishments that we're proud of we succeeded in making the app work to an extent

What we learned how to develop an app and a level of some coding.

What's next for MHRAS to develop a heart monitor bracelet and help it connect to the phone and run the program to see if it works and de-bug.
