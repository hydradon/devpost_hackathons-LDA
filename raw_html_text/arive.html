Inspiration

I get home from work everyday exhausted with the desire to take a much needed nap but I do not have that opportunity as I could risk missing my bus stop

What it does

It sends you a simple text message when your bus arrives at it's destination.

How I built it

We used Google's geocoding Api to compute user's desired destination. We also used Next bus apis to track live bus locations in the Toronto Area (TTC). XMLParsing api was also implemented

Challenges I ran into

Deploying text messaging through Twilio

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Pulling an all nighter!

What I learned

Twill and XMLParsing
