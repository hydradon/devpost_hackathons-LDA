Inspiration

We wanted to develop a platform that would empower communities to combat the perils of natural disasters. With a focus on the granular social network at the community level, we look to inform & support communities in ways larger technological platforms can't.

What it does

EmpoweredBy.Tech provides 2 very important features:

  1. Community Posting Board

The Community Posting Board provides a channel for people to spread information and knowledge at the focused community level.

  1. Resource Readiness Map

The Resource Readiness Map offers a user-friendly map with markers representing valuable community resources that can be borrowed, bought/sold, or donated to those in need.

How we built it

The website is built on the MERN stack . React.js is used for the frontend with the support of Node.js to serve pages. Node.js and Express.js power the backend functionality. The React app & backend are hosted on the Google Cloud Platform App Engine . MongoDB Atlas is used as our cloud storage solution.
