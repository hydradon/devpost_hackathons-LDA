Inspiration

It is a common problem for undergraduates in universities to find research opportunities, especially in university, SUNY Stony Brook. Therefore, we wanted to develop a web app to facilitate undergraduate involvement in research projects across disciplines by providing a direct undergraduate-to-researcher interface.

What it does...

It allows undergraduate students to directly contact graduate students, postdoctoral researchers, and principal investigators to participate in their research projects. Researchers are able to post availabilities in various research projects to their department webpage and undergraduate students can browse this webpage to find areas of interest. This, we hope, could be used to decrease the overall time cost of searching for opportunities felt by students and researchers.

How we built it

We used html, css and JavaScript for front-end and php to save the data as .csv file.
