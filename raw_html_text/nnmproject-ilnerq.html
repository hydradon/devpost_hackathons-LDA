The Problem

As you may know, supporting & monitoring of the equipment at the hard-to-reach areas are difficult and expensive. That's why we decided to use a drone as a great way of supporting, collectioning data and checking the factories and other industrial organization at the remote an dhard to reach places.

The aspects of working are:

1) Visual checking of the equipment 2) Monitoring of the equipment with the sensors 3) Gathering of information

The advantages are:

-Hight usability -Lower cost -Permeability -Acceleration of working processes -Automatization of working process -Autonomy of working process -Ecological safety -The possibility of taken collective decision

The main working processes are connected with sensors system, transmitterin and recieving of data, and etc.

We ran into Smart Services & Sustainable Choices challenges by Outotec (the track is Industrial Internet).
