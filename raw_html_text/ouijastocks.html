OujiaStocks

Inspired by xkcd 1600 . Helps you choose which stock to buy next using a Ouija Board and historical end of day prices.

Built on node.js, jquery, yahoo-finance for npm, express.

If you minimize all the JS and HTML, the logic ends up being under 2 kB. (for Datto)

You can find it at http://ouijastock.biz
