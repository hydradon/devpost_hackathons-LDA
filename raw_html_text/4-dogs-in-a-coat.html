Inspiration

I wanted to be able to practice speaking foreign languages even when it was just me and my Amazon Echo

What it does

This App gives you the opporunity to listen and then respond to real life conversations in french, which massively improves over more traditional language learning methods such as rote memorisation. Allowing you to have a living and breathing dynamic knowledge of the french language

How I built it

I used the amazon skill flow builder

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I'm proud of
