Inspiration: As a lead of a DevC, I see developers mostly learn for the internet. There is no easy to get certified on any skills. There are udacity and other platforms but you have to go through a course first and then try the exam (if any).

InternetAcademy.xyz is a simple solution to keep developers engaged via messenger platforms. Developers can get certified for any skillset, improve their skills, challenge friends in learning and get updates of recent happening with technology they love.

We are an expert team of developer bot from Bangladesh. We have built that in roughly 2 hours or so. The demo is inside one of our excisting bots so the jury panel can test this easily.
