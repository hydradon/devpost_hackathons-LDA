MyAdvisor - A virtual advisor that is always available 24x7

There is a stigma associated with feeling depressed or blue or down.

Most people do not feel confident in seeking help for these conditions

** However, they may feel comfortable talking to a virtual advisor that shows empathy **

_ MyAdvisor was built using Amazon Lex, Amazon Lambda and Slack. It is meant to be a lightweight application that can be run on any device - mobile or desktop _

The next step is to continue to enhance this application and add more natural language processing smarts. We also plan to seek help of a professional psychotherapist to make the dialogs and responses more meaningful.
