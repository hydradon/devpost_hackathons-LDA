Inspiration

The bubble tea food event in the 2017 HTN Schedule.

What it does

It's a simulation of a Bubble Tea Shop, where you create bubble tea to satisfy your customers before time runs out!

How we built it

We split up the work into different classes, and researched and collaborated to code it through android studios.

Challenges we ran into

There were lots of walls and learning to do as none of us were very familiar with coding with android as well as working with git.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We actually created a working app within the span of 36 hours!!! It has cool animations and we got a progress bar to work!

What we learned

We learned many things about android and java programming, and personally, I learned a lot about git.

What's next for Bubble Tea

We will probably add more add-ons such as perhaps sound and more food options.
