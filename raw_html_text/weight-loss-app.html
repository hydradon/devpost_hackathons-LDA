Inspiration

The inspiration came from a friend who wants more from a weight loss app. Other apps lacked on educating how to lose weight.

What it does

It's a calculator that estimate how long and the best path to lose a desired amount of weight based on your current weight, age, sex, and activeness. It will also give hints on bettering a user's current diet.

How we built it

We used Android Studio to develop the application.

What's next for Weight Loss App

Unfortunately, we did not finish the application. Its development will continue past HackRU.
