Inspiration

We wanted to get the feel of a full blown application during a 24 hour hackathon. So we created a full web application, custom RESTfullAPI, and tied several other APIs into our application while challenging ourselves to keep the application as small as possible.

How it works

The site can be found at the included link. You can access the API by going to http://brickfacts.me/api . We also created a pebble application that can be accessed through the pebble appstore and integrated twilio to receive facts by texting (585) 209-5554. Our developer documentation can be found at http://brickfacts.me/apiman .
