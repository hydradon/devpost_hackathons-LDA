Inspiration

I work in community development locally in the Erb West and Paulander community centers funded through the non for profit Carizon. I have first hand seen the positive impact these community centers have made on the neighborhood and how important it has been in the development of a strong, united community.

What it does

The app notifies neighbors and provides a calendar view about the events that are occuring at the center in an easy to view.

How we built it

We built it using Xcode

Challenges we ran into

Learning how to use Xcode

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The design of the app logo and its connection to the local community

What we learned

We learned that prototyping is an important part of the process

What's next for CommUNITY

Possibly continuing the development of this app and implementing it into the Erb and Paulender Communities.
