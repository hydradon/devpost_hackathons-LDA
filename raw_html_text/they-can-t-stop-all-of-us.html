Inspiration

Memes. Storm Area 51 happened Friday September 20, 2019, the same day Sunhacks started. Because we could not go to Nevada, we decided to bring Nevada to us.

What it does

A VR simulation of the raid on Area 51 including Naruto runners heading to a small facility that may or may not hold an alien.

How We built it

Built using Unity and a little bit of Blender to manipulate the models. All of the assets used to make the desert setting, facility, and models were downloaded from the Unity Asset store.

Challenges We ran into

Finding a computer with the hardware to operate the Oculus Rift.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Creating an operating VR game in a strict time frame with software and hardware we were unfamiliar with.

What I learned

How to work and program with Unity and Blender.
