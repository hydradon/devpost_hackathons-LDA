Inspiration One of the worst things that can occur to a person is hearing the recent news of a loved one’s recent lethal drug overdose. This can occur for a number of reasons; some may have conditions such as alzheimer’s and forget their proper medication amounts, or perhaps the person was just thinking and acting in a rash manner.

What it Does The system is designed to securely keep track of medications given to people from all walks of life. Activated through voice commands from an amazon echo, the system dispenses the proper medications during given time periods. The greater system includes a QR code system and facial recognition to authenticate prescriptions for medications, and features the takkstrip, meant to keep track of the medicine dispensed through its weight.
