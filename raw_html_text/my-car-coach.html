Inspiration

One of the harder things you will learn in live is to be a really good driver. My Car Coach helps you with that!

What it does

It analyses metrics like the acceleration, speed and pressure on the break-pedals to give you a feedback on your driving behaviour.

How we built it

We use paho-mqtt to read out the data from the car. This data gets processed in our backend and the final result is sent to the front-end over websockets.

Challenges we ran into

Asyncio in python is not that easy!
