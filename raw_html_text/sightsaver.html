As busy students at Montgomery HS, we spend a large number of hours working on an electronic device and staring at a screen on a day to day basis. This in turn causes many of us to experience notable eye discomfort and deterioration of vision. We took this common issue that many of us face and aimed to create an application which would resolve the screen-staring experience by prompting the user to do visual exercises at regular frequency. The app also provides information on the benefits of different eye-resting exercises. Our design and coverage makes it usable for all kinds of clients, whether they are an office worker or a student, and serves as a resource to help with the maintenance of one’s eyesight.
