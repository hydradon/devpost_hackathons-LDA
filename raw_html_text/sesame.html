Inspiration

Voice recognition/natural language processing app that accepts users' request and satisfy their needs somehow

What it does

Currently, the app can capture expressions like "get me the textbook for CS350 class" or "I need a ride from Waterloo to Toronto next Monday at 7pm". The vision is to allow users to naturally speak to the app and the app will get what the users want by matching them to sellers or service providers.

How I built it

make use of the powerful Wit.ai natural language processing API to capture the intent of the request and the entities associated with the request

Challenges I ran into

Training the AI bot and determine the different ways a user can express the request

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Training and completing the basic function by myself

What I learned

Getting more familiar with wit.ai
