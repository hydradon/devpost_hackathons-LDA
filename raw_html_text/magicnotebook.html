Inspiration

We want more efficient ways to study

What it does

1) allows you to take notes converting your handwriting to text 2) Enter in any math problem and it will automatically solve it for you in steps whether it be Integrals to addition 3) Enter in your book and page # to page # and we will also return relevant information that can help you study
