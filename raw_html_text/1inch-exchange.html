Inspiration

To build an aggregation token swap to offer a solution to buy for the best price common tokens on several exchanges in only one transaction.

What it does

Offers at small amount of trades the best exchange with lowest price and on higher amounts spilts it to multiple exchanges to get the best rate.

How I built it

Without sleep :) Build with technologies: Angular, Solidity, Ethereum, Smart Contracts, using exchanges

Challenges I ran into

Getting price from Bancor Network, which was not possible because of a bug which i reportet to Bancor and fixed it.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

To have a running MVP wich solves problems.

What I learned

Working with Bancor and other exchanges.
