With this app you can practice your english vocabulary and learn new words using pen and various drawing activities. This is the first exercise. You need to listen to the recording and select correct object by drawing contour on the photo.

In other exercises you may need to:

  • connect corresponding terms in pairs,
  • spot words from the letter matrix,
  • or look at the picture and draw a line connecting words in a correct sentence.

Drawing activities are simple and effective way of improving your English, especially if you are a visual learner. Have fun !
