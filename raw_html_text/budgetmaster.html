Saving money is hard. Whether it be keeping track of how much you've spent already, or finding cheaper alternatives; it takes a lot of time and effort. BudgetMaster aims to combat that. BudgetMaster keeps track of your weekly spending and helps generate useful analytics to be able to provide you with with a simple yet effective solution to such an annoying problem. BudgetMaster is also capable of analyzing your habits and generating fresh alternatives that can help you save money!
