Inspiration

When travelling with others, such as on a tour bus, it becomes exponentially difficult to collaborate with others in deciding what places to visit. Since the team likes to travel is not in favor of this problem, we designed our own solution - We Tour.

What it does

Users can login, if they do not have an account they can register and make one. Now, they can choose from a list of local campaigns/trips happening near them that they are interested in and/or will be going on. Once a trip is chosen, a new list of available touring locations is shown and the user can select which ones they want to visit. When everyone is done choosing adding their location preferences, and algorithm that determines the tour path is run and the trip begins.

How I built it

We built the app in iOS (Swift), Android, and Node.js.

Challenges I ran into
