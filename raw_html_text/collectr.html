Inspiration

It started out with the goal of analysis in mind. AI, Machine Learning, that sort of stuff. Along the way, we realized that some of these open source apis were too difficult to use for small scale applications. So we instead converted our app into a data visualizer. However, we liked the logo so much we kept the twitter bird.

What it does

Explores the earth in a retro style low poly environment, taking you to different countries across the world.

How I built it

We mainly used Flash CS6, with ActionScript3. Flash seemed like the best choice for a GUI heavy application.

Challenges I ran into

The biggest challenge was trying to simplify our app and focus it to something more realistic.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We are very proud of how it looks, and the experience that you get using the app.

What I learned

That staying up for 36 hours is very doable if you replenish your caffeine levels every hour.
