What is hARdball

Never miss a second of ballpark action again with the hARdball app. Using Augmented Reality, the users phone becomes a gateway to enhancing the fan experience of both the action on the field and all ballpark amenities. Simply launch the app and point the camera at the field. The Field View enables users to identify the players on the field and click through to real time stats and player specific info. The Stadium View gives fans the ability to easily locate restaurants, bars, shops and more anywhere in the stadium. They can even pull up menus, product offerings and other ballpark info. With the hARdball app, baseball fans everywhere can finally keep their eyes on the game and get a heads up view of the entire ballpark experience.
