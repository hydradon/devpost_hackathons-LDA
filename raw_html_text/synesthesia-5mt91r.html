Inspiration

Mochi needs a light display for his room. He wonders: can computers feel?

What it does

Listens to music, learns some features, and dances its lights off.

How we built it

Coupling some lovingly connected LEDs with neural networks that learn to generate images from music features. Controlled with a raspberry-pi. It listens and dances!
