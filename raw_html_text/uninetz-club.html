Inspiration

With the aim in building network from the very beginning of the freshman year and bring about new ideas to the companies so they can pursue the projects in their organizations or even sponsor for start-up ideas.

What it does

The student uploads his personal/university projects, company look into the description, if interested they contact the student to purse the projects further. This way the company gets new innovative ideas from the younger generation while the students get the opportunity to build their network.
