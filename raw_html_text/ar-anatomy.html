Inspiration

To create more effective learning environments for students who have to learn in a hurry

What it does

Displays labeled animations of organs on the body using AR codes

How we built it

Unity for engine, Vuforia for AR recognition

Challenges we ran into

AR codes not working, missplaced text, no animations

Accomplishments that we’re proud of

Making something that can be used for almost any learning environment!

What we learned

Basics of Unity and Vuforia, use of whiteboards, importance of team happiness

What's next for AR Anatomy

After display the team will probably distribute it for free so anyone can change the text and animations to make other learning environments and hopefully the CDC will find inspiration on this project
