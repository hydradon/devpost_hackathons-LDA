Inspiration & What It Does

There are many homeless pets out there that aren't being adopted. With holopets , people can see all the pets that are up for adoption and interact with them through an augmented reality experience. Then, they "love react" to the pet they want and a text is sent to their phone with the link to the pet's adoption page online. Through this, I hope that people are more likely to adopt these pets and provide them with loving homes.

How I built it

I used Unity to set up scenes with 3D models of different animals and animate them.

Challenges

I had to develop the entire app before testing & debugging it because I didn't have a HoloLens with me.

What's Next?

In the future, I hope to add more kitten and puppy breeds and design a sleeker user interface.
