Inspiration

Invest in funds, stocks and ETFs as fast as your tap. Young generation should invest as easy as sent smiles. Messengers are the most popular apps in the world by usage. Chatbots available without install a new app.

What it does

In any popular messenger as FB Messenger, Viber, Telegram, WeChat exists our chatbot that firstly asks a few questions for an understanding mentality of a user finance situation and after offer specific funds to buy. In one tap with an attached and tokenized a payment card, a user could pay for it right inside in the chat.

How we built it

We used Messengers API for building a chatbot, Tap2pay for realizing payments inside messengers and API of Just2Trade and OP Bank for suggesting funds and stocks

Challenges we ran into

Visa

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We made seamless and smooth process to buy funds and stocks. Integrated it in a chatbot. Made investing easy for young people.

What we learned

How to compile messengers technology, payments via cards, tokenization, marketing

What's next for Investy

Collaborate with popular and trusted Brokers and Investment Funds to acquire new customers and got a commission fee per transaction.
