Inspiration

Our driving will to help others.

What it does

It allows for homeless persons to have a refined search feature for homeless services. It makes information easily available for those who need it.

How we built it

We used the MIT-APP Inventor to construct our application.

Challenges we ran into

We were not very experienced with MIT-APP Inventor and had trouble learning and building our app at the same time. We also had trouble debugging our demos.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to push through the hardship of actually creating a functional application.

What we learned

We learned how to value time given to complete tasks.

What's next for Homeless-RPS

To expand the product and offer more functionality.
