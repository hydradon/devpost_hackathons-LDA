Inspiration

Nowadays we do not pay much attention to scientific news around us. What is the reason that we do not read articles? I think because they are not so inviting. I thought that if they would be presented as quiz then more people would be interested in reading articles.

What it does

Provide ten daily quiz question every day.

How I built it

I built in summer 2016 when I started learing iOS development. I built it using Swift and XCode. The backend was Parse API.

Challenges I ran into

The most difficult challenge was to generate 10 questions every day. For this reason there was a need for writer who would create questions for a quiz.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of
