Inspiration

Humans have the tendency to make simple things complicated. I've developed a program intended to guide the user back to comfort. Let's sit back and Lax.

What it does

Guides the user to take steps in doing a brief relaxation exercise for a quick de-stressor

How I built it

C++, Finger-Knuckle Grease, and Optimisim

Challenges I ran into

Setting a timer. within c++ to allow for a more realistic feel.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Finishing the product in c++ within the time period. and creating something that probably everyone that competed may have needed the past day.

What's next for Lax'

Conversion from C++ to an app for iPhone and Android. This improved app may also use wearable technology to better time the exercises and decide whether or not a loop for longer breathing beyond the initial minute is necessary.
