Inspiration

With most flashcard apps you would have to be on the app to do it. This lead to many students not doing flashcards. With our method we text you the flashcard and then you can answer from a multiple choice set.

Every answer you get right gets you a point, and a point let's you play tic tac toe (still with SMS) against fellow classmates.
