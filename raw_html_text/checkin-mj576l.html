Inspiration

Everyone has his own hobby, like a dance classes, driving lessons or any other activities to be attended on periodic basis. Idea is to create a suitable solution for both sides (Trainers that organize classes and attendees) that will be easy to use, reliable and helpful.

What it does

As an attendee you will be able to track all your activities in a single app. As a trainer, you will receive fast registration feature along with attend statistics.

What's next for CheckIn

Developing blockchain registration to enable new business subscription strategies for organizations is next our next step. It will allow our service to offer customers with cheaper subscription plans, allowing everyone to attend their favorite hobbies. It will also provide organizations with new customer flow and stimulate healthy competition.
