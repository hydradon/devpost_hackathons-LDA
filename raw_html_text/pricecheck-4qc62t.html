Inspiration

A lot of students struggle with getting into college. Or then apply to unreasonable places. We were inspired by our own inner struggles along with what our siblings went through in the past. We wanted to know where we could go to college based on your PSAT/SAT scores, our ACT scores, and our unweighted GPA.

What it does

Our app sorts out reasonable colleges based on PSAT/SAT scores, ACT scores, and unweighted GPA. It will create a list of colleges where your inputs are greater than the average for all three categorizes. It also has a search bar that searches google for any colleges that are not in our database.

How we built it

We used android studio to build this app. Split up the work in order to speed up the process of creating the app. We used multiple IDE's to test out certain functions such as Eclipse and JGRASP. This allowed us to find our what was wrong in certain parts of the code.

Challenges we ran into

Due to us splitting up the work, it was difficult for us to synchronize all of our work onto one platform. We also had a tough time web scrapping information from different websites. We also were unable to read a file from a android studio. We found out that the data must be inside the phone to access by the phone.
