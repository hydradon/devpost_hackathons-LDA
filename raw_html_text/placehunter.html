Inspiration

You have free time in new city, maybe it's short break in conference or time between two flights and you want to see the best places in your area but you don't know which ones.

What it does

It allows you to find new places, earn cool looking badges and compete with friends who gets more badges and visited places.

How I built it

API created in Ruby on Rails and web application in ReactJS (mobile friendly)
