Inspiration

Rising Mental Health Issues among Youngsters leading them to as extreme as Suicides

What it does

  • Provide Randomized Small Content to Normalize User’s Emotions
  • Dynamically delivering content on the basis of User’s interactions
  • Content from various Domains and Various Formats based on availability on the Web
  • Each Session to the user acts as Fresh

How we built it

  • Dynamic Web Crawling based on Real-Time interactions
  • Use of Information Retrieval and Freely Available Content to alleviate Emotions
  • Use of Distributed APIs to improve the Speed, Efficiency and Scalability
  • Provide a simple, native and interactive Android based Frontend

Challenges we ran into

  • Had to spend more time on Problem Analysis than Implementation of Solution
  • Lack of time to implement many impacting features
  • Lack of good Free Content

Accomplishments that we are proud of

  • Glad to have worked Comprehensive analysis and Solution to an existent social mental health problems
  • We are proud of collaboration
  • Proud to have developed a prototype of application successfully

What we learnt

  • Learning about the enormity of Mental Health Issues
