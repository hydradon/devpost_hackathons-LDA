Inspiration

Have you ever had problems finding the perfect location to meet with friends coming from different cities or different countries ? Have you ever had troubles finding where to meet with your clients for a meeting ?

What it does

Then Travel cruise is the tool for you : it finds the best location for every participant whether you want the lowest price for everyone, the fastest mode of transportation to meet with everyone !

How we built it

It is a platform made with Bootstrap, Google Maps API which uses a back-end made with Python, Javascript and rome2rio API.

Challenges we ran into

Making the UI communicate with the backend was the most challenging part and having the server running all the time was quite challenging.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

End-to-end implementation of the platform.

What's next for Travel cruise

Next use case is increase the number of destinations and integrate workspaces to facilitate the meetings.
