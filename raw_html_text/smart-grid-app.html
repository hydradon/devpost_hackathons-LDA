Inspiration

Energy suppliers constantly need to balance energy and demand. Proposition: Can we shift demand to periods when grid carbon intensity is low?

What it does

Uses time series to display current grid carbon intensity based on energy sources. Allows users to schedule appliance tasks to minimise carbon footprint.

How we built it

Using Predix Timeseries and Analytics

Challenges we ran into

Calling analytic from front end.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

A great concept and analytic, with future room for development

What we learned

A lot about using Predix's Analytics services.

What's next for Smart Grid App

More advance modelling and planning, world domination!
