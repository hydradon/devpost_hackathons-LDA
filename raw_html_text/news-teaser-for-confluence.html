Looking for the best way to announce or share your news to your Confluence users? The News Teaser add-on for Confluence provides multiple macros to display news from blogposts and pages in a beautiful way.

Includes

  • News Slider macro - Use the News Slider macro to display news from blogposts and pages with thumbnail and excerpt in a simple and beautiful slider.
  • News Teaser macro - Use the News Teaser macro to display news entries with thumbnail and excerpt in a Pinterest like cards layout or as simple list.
  • News Ticker macro - Use the News Ticker macro to show off your latest breaking news from blogposts and pages in a simple news ticker.

Feature Highlights

  • Easily collect news entries from Confluence blogposts or pages and optionally filter them by spaces or labels
  • Choose whether you want to display a summary, an excerpt or the complete content as news text
  • Specify which image attached to the corresponding page or blogpost you want to display as thumbnail
  • Easily display social stats like the number of comments and likes to see which news are the most engaging
  • Fully customizable macros to adapt the output to your needs
  • All macros are responsive, touch-enabled and fully mobile compatible
  • Works best in combination with the Custom Dashboards plugin to display newly published posts in a stunning way on your dashboard.
