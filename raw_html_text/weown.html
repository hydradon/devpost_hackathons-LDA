Inspiration

Real Estate investment is a rich man's game, using blockchain and smart contract we will solve that problem by democratizing by creating liquidity real estate.

What it does

Tokenize crowd investment in real estate properties, it allows fractional ownership of income producing investment real estate on the blockchain.

How we built it

Using smart contracts on blockchain, we tokenize each property through their own ERC20 like tokens. And we give people ability to invest using crypto currency.

Challenges we ran into

Deciding which blockchain to use, permissions, and privacy.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We built out a working platform and smart contract.

What we learned

We learned how to blockchain to access broader global investment market

What's next for WeOwn

We want to do a real life transaction to prove our process, as a POC.
