Child Care Licensing Violations in Harris County

Ensuring all families have equal access to child care that meets basic standards

Our team used data from DFPS to understand how quality child care centers are distributed throughout Houston.

Our key activities include:

  1. Analyzing number of child care licensing violations
  2. Creating a visual map of all of the child care centers
  3. Displaying map and analysis on an accessible website
