Inspiration

We really wanted to use the latest technology to make something for the people who are not able to use as much as we do. We always wanted to do something good for them.

What it does

Our cane is a good integration of hardware, software, and a simple but portable cane. It is made especially for the blind population. It has sensors and rumble motors that help the blind people know that there's an obstacle near them and vibrates the cane in order to alert them. Not only does it tell them that there's an obstacle in that direction, but the intensity of the vibration helps them to know how far is it from them.

Apart from the cane, we made a software using AWS that identifies objects, people, their expressions, and other stuff and reads them to the blind people. The main idea is that the software will be on their glasses (with a camera) and the speakers on the end of the glasses that sit on their ears.

How we built it

We built this cane using an Arduino Uno, an Ultrasonic sensor, a buzzer, and a ruler (for the prototype). We connected everything at the proper place and put the right software into it to give them the most accurate results.
