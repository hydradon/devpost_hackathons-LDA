PivotPoint16

The U.S. Department for Housing and Urban Development has been faced with organizing an overwhelming amount of data which has become increasingly more imperative to consolidate as the homeless population increases.

We saw the opportunity to make public housing inspection scores more accessible to those who need that information by presenting the data in a user-friendly graphical webpage.
