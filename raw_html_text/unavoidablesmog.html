The topic we choose for our project is sustainability. The air pollution in the world now is becoming heavier and heavier. It's bad for our health. Revealing deep problems through a happy gaming experience is our goal. So we create a video game aiming to raise the awareness of environmental protection. In our game, the player can control a cartoon figure by using the keyboard. Players use the arrow keys to control the cartoon character's direction. The player's goal is to spend more time in the circle. During the game, the computer gradually increases the difficulty of the game by adding random speed to the cartoon characters. The blue in the circle represents the clean sky, while the outer circle represents the heavy haze. Once you enter the haze area, the cartoon character will “die” and the game is over.
