The average consumer still charges their smart phone throughout the night despite the damage it does to it's battery life. On one hand, they want to wake up with a fully charged phone, but on the other they don't want to have a crappy short battery life a year down the road. The overnight charger solves this. Its a smart charger and alarm clock that won't overcharge your phone at night, and will only start charging so that at the specific moment you need to wake up, you'll have a fully charged phone.
