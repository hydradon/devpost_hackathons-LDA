Inspiration

I wanted a quick lookup for my area's sunrise and sunset. My program allows for this because you can enter just a part of your address, and it autofills the rest for you.

What it does

You enter any part of your address and it calculates the sunrise and sunset personalized for you.

How I built it

I used Python to do the algorithms, and made a web app using Flask

Challenges I ran into

I was working by myself, I was in 8th grade, and this was my first Hackathon.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I managed to get the really complex algorithm for the sunrise and sunset in the class Sun().

What I learned

I learned Flask and improved by skills in HTML

What's next for Sun Calculator

I hope to be able to launch a fully functional Android App
