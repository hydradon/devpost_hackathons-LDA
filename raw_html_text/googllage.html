Googllage

Ever want to see your face made out of java logos? We got you covered. A bitcamp logo out of potatoes? Sure! Why not? With Googllage see any photo you choose transform into a collage of anything your heart can imagine!

Welcome to Googllage. Googllage allows you to create a collage/mosaic of photos using pictures from the internet. To use: choose a photo file, then enter a search term. A collage representation of the photo will be created using photos searched from internet using the given term.

A little about us: We're just a few high schoolers from Montgomery County, MD who have loved technology for a long time but are fairly new to hacking. We had a lot of fun coding this and we learned so much (including how useless sleep actually is).
