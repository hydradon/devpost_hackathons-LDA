We notice that it is a necessary need for people to get access to the education they need immediately before an exam so that they aren't stressing.

We find that the way to do this the best way is through a messenger bot, which allows for efficient communication and instant access to study guides.

In the future, we will hope to have implemented more subjects and more guides to make the bot more functional and complex.
