Inspiration

Halloween's just around the corner! It's time to scare children and be festive!

What it does

It's a first person horror game, where you are the horror. You also control your ghostly corporeal form with your voice. Say "boooooooo" and watch yourself fly.

How we built it

We used Unity and the available Oculus SDK to build this game.

Challenges we ran into

Slight differences between the two dev computers led to strange bugs.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Alternate control schemes are the best!

What we learned

The right source control is important. Microphone input isn't that bad to set up!
