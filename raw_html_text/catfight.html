Inspiration

I have an obsession with cats and I love to search lol cats. I thought I should make a game based around this.

How it works

You move around, trying to dodge the opposing cat, who will swipe at you.

Challenges I ran into

I wrote my code in a rush, leading to redundancies in code which slow down additions/changes to code.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Getting a semi-working AI system, getting the grid system to work.

What I learned

Spend time making things scalable first, coding the bulk of it later.

What's next for CatFight

Better AI that is less prone to moving into you, better collision detection.
