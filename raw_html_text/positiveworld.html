• PositiveWorld

Inspiration

I don´t want to have a dark world whith a lot of problems. So the only thing you cab do is to wake the people what happens if the live like they live right now. Everybody throws garbage out without note what they do to the environment.

What it does

People can ask different categories and get a text about the selected subject about what will happen if people continue the life like now and what they could do to make the future sitiuation not true.

How I built it

I biuld it with the Amazon Alexa SDK in NodeJS and the Amazon Wevservice. Therefore I managed the interaction model so that people could ask every type of sentence to make it as a clear experience.

Challenges I ran into

A big challenge was how to say people how important it is to save the environment and that they should change their own behavior and not say: "Only when I do it, nothing would be happen on the big places.".

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Clear descriptions and a very valid Interaction-Model to have a great experience.

What I learned

People are hard to "manipulate" in form to let them change some situations because people are lazy and don´t care about the environment.

What's next for PositiveWorld

More possibilities of how to save the environment and how to change peoples minds.
