CalendarMatch

Find shared availabilities with your buddies in a platform-agnostic manner!

How-To:

  1. Visit bitcamp.timofeo.com
  2. Start planning'!

During his pledging process, one of the team members realized that there was no tool that sufficiently aided planning across multiple schedules. Another team member has had difficulty scheduling practices between many busy college students.

We decided to build a tool that aimed to fulfill that need, and CalendarMatch was founded.

Cool features:

  • OAuth 2.0
  • Google Calendar (and more...) support
  • Create unlimited groups with your friends
  • RESTful API to create your own service against ours

Our stack consists of a HTML/CSS/jQuery front-end, interacting with a Node.js+MongoDB backend. This all is made possible by DigitalOcean and the Google API. Huge shoutout to them! Check us out on GitHub!
