We all know the feeling

You are out of smalltalk topics. Awkward silence is what's up... Smalltalk Helper is a fun little web app that does its best to help out!

How Smalltalk Helper helps

Smalltalk Helper proposes some potential conversation topics by using APIs to find a) something current and relevant or b) a random fun fact to talk about.
