Inspiration

We wanted to create a Visual Novel. Our inspiration is a Visual Novel called Butterfly Soup and we wanted to create a fun story with lovable characters.

What it does

You can click through each scene to meet all of our characters and understand the premise of the story.

How we built it

We used Renpy to code the project, drew all of the art, and composed a theme song that plays throughout the story.

Challenges we ran into

Each team member have very different talents, so coordinating all of the parts of the project and combining our individual styles was challenging. In addition, learning a new framework was a challenge since we were unfamiliar with Renpy.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of having a varied soundtrack and producing a lot of artwork by different artists who were able to match each other's styles. We managed to write a cohesive story in a short amount of time.

What we learned

A new coding medium and how to customize our own Visual Novel.

What's next for Boiling Point

We want to finish the story and make it complete with two different paths the reader can take to see different perspectives of the story and develop our characters.
