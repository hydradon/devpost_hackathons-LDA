Inspiration

We wanted to use Leap Motion to change how music tech works. You have to buy super expensive instruments to be able to play.

What it does

Simple music studio built in nodejs and express, with frontend leap connection with react.

How we built it

Leap Motion with Xesto and node.js with react.

Challenges we ran into

Whole technology is not so stable yet, but it's certainly interesting!

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It kinda works.

What we learned

Using leap motion is cool, jet-lag - not so much.

What's next for LeapMusic

More development are more gestures supported, uploading your own sounds, and better UX/UI
