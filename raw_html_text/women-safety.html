Inspiration

We were inspired from the event Karina Vetrano of Queens who lost her life from jogging. Our main inspiration is to give safety for all the girls by building an application.

What it does

It is an app which shakes and gives notification to the people who are relatives and important people.

How we built it

We used firebase for the database and made an app by using android studio. For the location we also built the app which gives your location.

Challenges we ran into

Challenge was to send API key to your relatives so that they know your location.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It was able to notify the shaking thing and also gives the location.

What we learned

We learned about how the google API works and gives notification to the user.

What's next for Women-Safety

the whole app is to provide the safety for the women by shaking and sending notification to the people that are close to her.
