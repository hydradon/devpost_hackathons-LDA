Inspiration

Our idea sparked from the sheer bitter feeling of boredom. Surely there are times when you and your family and friends wanted to leave the house but had no idea what to do. This web application will collect information on all events happening in your city and bring them to your fingertips. Not only is this a helpful tool for planning events, it can also enlighten you to new interests.

What it does

A web application to assist in finding events happening near you.

How we built it

Javascript, CSS, HTML, Open Data, Bootstrap
