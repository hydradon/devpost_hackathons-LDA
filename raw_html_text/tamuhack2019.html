Maroon Noise: a custom white noise generator

By Maggie von Ebers and Kevin Wang for TamuHack 2019

This project aims to be better at blocking your own environment noises than typicaly white or pink noise. It takes an initial sample of the ambient noise around you from your microphone and creates "maroon" noise based off of it. Next, it will update every few seconds to any new, annoying noises around you as it loops continuously for your listening pleasure.

This project runs from the command line with command "python main.py". It will continue to run until CTRL-C is pressed.
