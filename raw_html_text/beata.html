Inspiration

Being afraid of getting possessions stolen when left unattended

What it does

An alarm system that goes off when the system experiences a jolt of movement

How we built it

Combination of breadboards, an Arduino Nano, various sensors, and a speaker

Challenges we ran into

Finding an acceleration threshold that would not trigger with an accidental bump but would with slow movement of the device Size Bluetooth connectivity

Accomplishments that we're proud of

A working product that is not affected by small vibrations from accidental movement

What we learned

Bluetooth capabilities and coding 3D Printing Wiring

What's next for BEATA

Decreased size iOS Compatibility/Dedicated App GPS Tracking
