Text 'Buddy' at 13476702260 and find out what your friends are thinking!

Need IBM Watson to tell you what people are really thinking when they send you text messages? text 'Buddy' at 13476702260 to get a real sentiment analysis and personality profile on the message 'Your Friend' sent. Buddy will give you an analysis of the message, a summary of what they are feeling, followed by the most appropriate animated GIF for the emotion! Most appropriate use for this application is to determine is a potential 'date' hates you or likes you based on the text message you send to 'Buddy'!
