What it does

OnTrack contains relevant information for patients taking Methadone, Buprenorphine or Naltrexone.

Relevant information such as side effects, interactions with other drugs, important warnings, and general things to know.

At the same time it provides a stream of real time, nearby events from support groups.

There is also an emergency system in which the user can call their physician or an emergency number with ease.
