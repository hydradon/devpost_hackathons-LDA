Inspiration

With recent police reports in areas close to the university, we have created a resources to help encourage safe habits amongst UW’s students that live near campus. UW offer its students a free shuttle for those who stay on campus late at night. This website would be an attempt to improve student safety on campus by allowing them to notify NightRide drivers that they are at a bus stop, so the students are not accidentally missed while waiting for the NightRide.
