Inspiration

Books are awesome, but they can get uncomfortable to carry around, not to mention that it requires effort to remain focused when reading. Vite is the Pebble Watch app made to solve those problems.

What it does

It takes advantage of speed reading techniques, e-ink displays and portability to provide a comfortable and natural reading mean.

How I built it

PebbleCloud with javascript.

Challenges I ran into

PebbleJS as a beta technology.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The app works and it makes it easy to read long texts very fast.

What I learned

Pebble framework.

What's next for Vite

Phone integration and data logs.
