Torny - Fencing Tournament Manager

Torny was made in 24 hours at the 2016 HackRU hackathon.

Torny is a web app that manages fencing tournaments. Torny allows people to create, register and view results of tournaments. Once you create a tournament, you can input result data and Torny will automatically organize the tournament, calculate winners and make the tournament public.

How was Torny built?

With blood, sweat and tears. Also, Docker, Django, Bootstrap and python.
