Inspiration

When we met our teammates for the first time, we found we all had a common interest in yoga! We want to encourage everyone to try their hand at yoga and experience the same benefits as us. As stress takes a toll on all of us, this app is sure to help improve mindfulness and keep your worries at bay!

What it does

It is a platform to give unified information about yoga and encourage everyone to follow this practice.

How we built it

We used HTML and CSS along with some Photoshop to create most of the elements on our website. With team members who had interdisciplinary backgrounds (design and cog sci), we used our knowledge and unique skill sets to come together and build something unique.

Challenges we ran into

We were all relatively inexperienced and there was a big learning curve. We spent most of our hackathon learning how to use different software.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We participated in our first ever hackathon!

What we learned

As beginners, this was our first hackathon and we had no experience coding outside of class! We learnt a lot about HTML, CSS and design. More importantly, we learnt how to work as a team, manage time and a lot of technical skills.

What's next for One Body

To add more to our platform such as healthy living recipes and a resource for mental health. We are also working on adding a badge for users to receive after completing each level, and are going to add a sign in feature that tracks progress and reinforces good yoga habits.
