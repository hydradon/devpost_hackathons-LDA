Inspiration

We heard that scoring CVs was difficult, since companies get inundated with thousands of them at once. Hence, we're scoring CVs automatically.

What it does

Give it a folder of CVs, and it'll score all of them. We take into account the language inside the CV, as well as the proper nouns mentioned (e.g. their university). We will also have a "buzzword" detector, which penalizes the CV if there are too many buzzwords, and not enough verbs or nouns.
