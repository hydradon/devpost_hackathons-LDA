Inspiration

Our team believes that people is brilliant, has the potential to grow up and all of them are connected with their emotions.

What it does

A Hybrid platform that provides the students the opportunity to stand out through a variety of entrepreneur projects where they can collaborate with other students, creating important networking.

How we built it

We used 'Angular platform' to create the development environment, and a no-relational database 'Firebase'. Typescript was our language for development.

Challenges we ran into

The lacking of time was one of the biggest challenges for the project; there was the risk that we couldn't complete its development, however we decided to took it.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud with the effort of each of the team members. We created a product in less than 36 hours fully functional and user-friendly. No matter what happens next, we are inspired to keep learning.

What we learned

The trust in the members of the team its the most important value for us. It's OK having high expectations but we have to be realistic about the project's scalability. In every project we develop, we must take into account that its quality is essential.

What's next for Omelet

After this first version, the next step will be creating a startup that its objective will be having a big impact in the entrepreneur's student community,
