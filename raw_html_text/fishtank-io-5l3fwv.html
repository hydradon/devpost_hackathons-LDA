Inspiration one of the member's younger sister always forgets to feed her fishes leading to the death of one them. So, we thought of a solution that automatically feeds the fishes.

What it does

It feeds fish automatically at a given time set by the user and at a frequency also given by the user.

How we built it

Using an IOT ship connected to a weight module that gives data to control the amount of food. A servo motor moves the trap to let the food fall into the tank.

Challenges we ran into

Water proofing.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Completing the presentation in a short amount of time.

What we learned

The way fish tank filters work and how to add more modules to the project.

What's next for fishTank.io

Improving the actual design and make a real prototype.
