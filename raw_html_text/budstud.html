##Our Inspiration Sometimes it is hard to know others in your college courses, especially in large classes. This app was inspired by the want to study with classmates and to have a central place to meet. This is much less of a hassle than trying to find someone during class and provides relief from the stress of making friendships in class. Perfect for your typical nerd!

## What it does

  • Study buddy finder - Tinder for studying
  • Finds a person/people wanting to study the same subject as you or just wants to sit and study with you.
  • Make profile I- nternational
  • Study with someone who speaks your language
  • Input: level of language proficiency
  • Preset semester classes
  • School/university
  • Group size
  • Location (Google Maps coordinates)
  • Subject (Biology)
  • Specific class (BIOL 101)
  • Accountability Buddies
  • Study Atmosphere (quiet/music)
  • Language (for international students looking to study with someone in their native language) -
  • Lists available group study room locations

Goal

  • Study material together
  • Study in the same location

Accountability

  • Level you want in your accountability buddy
  • Your accountability buddy level
  • Preferred study location
  • Quiet/loud
  • music/no music
  • Lists available group study room locations

## How we built it We have been using Java and Python to build this app. The app is not yet complete, but we have general code for the main parts of the app.

## Challenges we ran into * All of us are beginner coders. The problem was we went for something that was greater than what we could currently do. The biggest problem was not being able to code in the same languages as one another and trying to learn a new language. We also had trouble using app development programs that we tried, like Google App Engine and Android AppBuilder. Instead, we ended up writing code within our respective app editors.

## Accomplishments that we're proud of We are proud of the template that we created. We are also very proud of the idea that we came up with. This app would be very helpful for students in college. We definitely learned new skills!

## What we learned How to make a markup How to use GitHub Resources available for app building Java coders learned Python and vice versa Team communication like Slack

## What's next for BudStud The next step for BudStud is to get it to become a working app by integrating the elements and creating a more aesthetically pleasing user interface. We would also like to extend it for highschool students as well, even though it is much easier to communicate with kids in your high school class since those classes are typically smaller than college classes.
