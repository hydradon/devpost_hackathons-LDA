Inspiration

We built Silio in order to be more spontaneous, spur-of-the-moment, surprised. Too often, we realize that we have a bit of free time in our days, but don't know how to spend it. We spend too long trying to figure out what the best way to live in a moment is, until that moment passes by and is lost. We wanted to create an easy to use tool that gave us the best recommendations on how to spend our time.

What it does

Silio is a bot that lives on facebook messenger. If you start a conversation with her, she'll ask you how much money you're willing to spend, and how far you are willing to travel. Then, Silio will quickly generate an itinerary of exciting places you can visit that fits your budget and travel constraints.

What's next for Silio

Silio works best in the Westwood, Santa Monica, West Hollywood Area. Soon, we want to expand the reach of Silio so that she can plan amazing itineraries for people anywhere and everywhere.
