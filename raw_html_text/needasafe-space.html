Inspiration

We are inspired by the current wildfires in California that have shut down power to more than a million people. There should be a platform that provides temporary shelter for people from a natural disaster, escaping abusive situations, or experiencing sudden and unexpected homelessness.

What it does

NeedASafe.space allows people to post their shelter and/or housing on our service. Users can live at the shelter/housing for up to five days at no cost. Instead of worrying about finding a safe, warm place to sleep, access to food and water, and staying clean, users can focus on finding a permanent solution to their situation.

How we built it

We built our website using HTML, React, Tailwind, and ant.design. We utilized Firebase for our login and data storage and hosted our website on Azure.

Challenges we ran into

When we build the React application, all of the files were initially built under the wrong path. This broke the app. We found it challenging to build an authentication page so we used Google Sign-In instead.
