Inspiration

Setting up and maintaining an optimal Facebook place entry is a lot of work. There are so many choices and fields to fill out. Then there are contents to create. What images to include? What words to write? This Facebook app will automate and streamline the process as much as possible by suggesting action items and contents for you.

What it does

User enters keywords with optional location and criteria such as ratings, rating counts, likes, checkins, or any permutation of the four criteria. The app then looks at contents plus data related to business and locations such as business hours and facility types. All this data is analyzed by a recommendation engine in real time to suggest the best action items and contents.

Challenges I ran into

Currently trying to get Page Public Content Access that will allow for more public, non user-identifying data to be analyzed to better suggest actions and contents that will increase ratings, likes and checkins for place owners. But the process to get public page contents seems bugged ( https://developers.facebook.com/support/bugs/348573335673386 ).
