Inspiration

I was inspired by the JP Morgan challenge to create something in order to inspire women to join STEM.

What it does

It is a website that I made for a hypothetical company that inspires women to join and stay in the STEM field. The website opens to a landing page, there is a page for the about which is what the company does. I then added a section where there is a list of careers in Science, technology, engineering and mathematics. There is also a section where I included multiple women who have made ground breaking advancements in STEM. When you click on their pictures there is a description and a link to their wiki page. This will serve as inspiration of women who have changed the world. I then included ways to encourage the next generation of girls in STEM. I included a list of helpful tools and resources that anyone can use. I also made a form where you can contact someone if you are interested in setting up an event focused on inspiring women.

How I built it

I used a empty website template and added all the information and features on it. I then registered the domain name and uploaded it on google cloud platform. The domain name is www.inspirewomeninstem.com .
