Inspiration

Using thermal imaging to enable or enhance the users’ experience.

What it does

FLIR One Adventure is a context-aware outdoors oriented activity app (camping, hiking, hunting, fishing, animal watching, etc.) with powerful local, social, environmental and safety features. It automatically understand where the user is.

It has the following features:

  1. Track Your Activity
  2. Safety and Security Scan
  3. Weather
  4. Places

Track Your Activity while hiking, camping or hunting. Record your activity with the app, map your route, know where you're going, see where you've been. Listen to your own music from within the app. Record GPS activities. App displays a nice summary of your outdoor activity and stats.

Share your activities in Social Media with your friends on Facebook, Twitter.

Safety and Security Scan feature is useful for spotting animals, campsite area security, locating lost hikers, scanning areas at night before venturing out, rescuing people and other numerous uses. It uses infrared / thermal imaging which helps you see in difficult environmental conditions including smoke, dust, fog, rain and snow.

Weather helps you find the best time for your outdoor activity based on weather conditions before you venture out.

Places helps you find nearby Points of Interest and restaurants.

App is designed to be simple to use and keep you engaged as well as safe. It is a perfect companion for outdoor activities .
