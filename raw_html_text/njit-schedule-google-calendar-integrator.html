Inspiration

As Seniors within NJIT we have always dealt with constantly fulfilling this meticulous task of scheduling every semester. We thought this would be meaningful for future and current students.

What it does

This schedule translator uses UiPath, robotic process automation a free software for the community. The student will log into the NJIT pipeline and pathway scrapes information from registrars page and from the student's schedule and organizes it directly to their google calendar.
