Inspiration

I often hear similar lines in songs separated by decades and I wanted to know who inspired whom

What it does

Takes a line from a song, and outputs a list of similar lyrics in ascending order by date

How we built it

A simple website and JavaScript and JQuery

Challenges we ran into

One of our teammates wisdom teeth came in so he couldn't come I had to learn how to use Rap Genius's API, which is pretty bad

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Actually finishing the project. It gives a list of similar songs based on dates, it's pretty cool

What we learned

Webdev, and I got much more comfortable with JavaScript

What's next for Empirical Spiritual Lyrical Finder

We could definitely clean up the front-end, and have more options for search criteria.
