Inspiration

Smaller companies need more connection with community outreach.

What it does

The website is owned by One Stop Outreach that facilitates outreach opportunities and register companies and their employees, allowing the companies to select community projects they want to part.

How I built it

Used bootstrap template, java script, pulled information from json using fetch commands, used photoshop and html.

Challenges I ran into

getting the json to load

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

ALL of it! We all worked really on this project and brought several skills and aspects to the table.

What I learned

Non coder learned html and javascript, new coders took their skill farther and senior level coders learned new concepts.

What's next for One Stop Outreach

Expanding their market to other business and growing features on their website. Having an algorithm that helps companies pick opportunities based on specific parameters.
