Inspiration - As a kid finding info about elements such as the element Ionic Energy , Shells etc was not easy as we had to look up the table frequently

What it does - Gives you a list of elements to access easily and a list to show their Isotopes. Also a learning section which has a set of flashcards to Quiz yourself or others.

How I built it - Ionic (Hybrid) Framework. The project can generate both iOS and Android Apps

Challenges I ran into - Consolidation of 2 data sets - Isotopes Data set with Ionization Energies.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of - Building it cross platform

What I learned - Swipe Cards interface.

What's next for i-SoTope - More info on the section for each element. Challenging Quizzes with Social Integration.
