Inspiration

There are lot of homeless people who starve because of poverty and on the other hand there are lot of food wasted by people after having food from the restaurant --- What if there was a way to utilize these two aspects and help homeless people get food.

What it does

Matches up homeless people with other people ready to donate food.

How we built it

Using Twilio and Google Maps API in python

Challenges we ran into

Integrating both sides of the audience of this service

Accomplishments that we're proud of

A final working prototype ready to be scaled

What we learned

The power of using basic technology like twilio to help humanity

What's next for Serve-It

Scale it and see it in action
