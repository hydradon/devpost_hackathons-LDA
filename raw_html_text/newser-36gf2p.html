Inspiration

The disappearance of local news outlets has brought many dire consequences that range from increased corruption to news manipulation through fake news.

What it does

It generates a content based on the user (reporter) input then it's hashed and put into a syndicate contract, there reporters can claim ownership and receive payments system a logarithmic growth scheme

How I built it

I used Tensorflow and Texar to generate the news, flask to serve the content, truffle. MetaMask and ganache to deploy and test the contracts

Challenges I ran into

Getting a decent dataset was tough, and computer power for the training as limited

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The content is decent enough for the limited amount of time

What I learned

Self generated content is a BIG problem that need to be addressed ASAP
