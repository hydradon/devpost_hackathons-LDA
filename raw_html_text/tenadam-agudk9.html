Ethiopia has rich local medicines. There are a lot of such medicines and the new generation doesn't know them very well. In most cases, they provide a clear and nice treatment with almost no side effect because they are naturally made. To engage the users we will collect use health issues and provide the proper local medicines. In doing that, we will also store the user health information with GSP location and eventually come up with different predictions and analysis on the health of the society.
