Inspiration

While millions of news articles are published every day, I realized that relevancy and terseness was key. Identifying that this solution could be implemented by machine learning & pattern recognition led to the birth of 1News. Delivering news in its simplest form and fresh is what 1News aims for.

What it does

Summarizes the latest news articles into 1 sentence. Categorizes it. And can be searched by keyword.

How I built it

REST, Python, HipChat REST API.

Challenges I ran into

Identifying keys components to reduce server load, optimizing the summarization.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Building the product as a modular project.

What I learned

Learning and building for HipChat. APIs endpoints and rich developer docs.

What's next for 1News for HipChat

  • Expanding 1News with additional categories
  • Saving topics and keywords
  • Feed tracking and push notification of saved topics
