Inspiration

on 2nd of September, 2016 I traveled from Egypt to South Africa to start a two year pre-university program at African Leadership Academy. When I arrived on campus I started noticing some flaws within the residential life.

What it does

I decided to create Kampasi which is a platform with the aim of enhancing students' campus experience through offering online tools. These tools include Prep signup, Sign-out sheet, Feedback system, Tutoring program, elections system, and more..

How I built it

I built Kampasi with PHP based on laravel framework.
