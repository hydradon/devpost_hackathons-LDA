Inspiration

With the recent loss of 7 hikers in the Zion National Park, a flash flood warning system could help.

What it does

Flash Alert starts by collecting and associating historical data from rain fall, stream data to formulate a predictable method for flash flood severity.

How I built it

Using historical weather data and IBM Data Scientists to figure out the movement of the rain pattern, and then mapping it to a new function from ESRI and other government data.

Challenges I ran into

Need time and resource to generate the variable rate in each "flow region".

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Get the concept formulated because of all the great help from weather, esri and IBM.
