PROBLEM

Infrastructure problems in a developing country is unavoidable. The massive amount of infrastructure problem leads to priority issues. The people and the government needs a medium that can channel their thoughts in hope of resolving the most important infrastructure problem first.

SOLUTION

We created an app that can convey the people’s complaint about the infrastructure problems regionally and rate it so the government of that particular region can decide which problem should be tackled first.

HOW IT WORKS

•User sees a real-time problem of an infrastructure •User then takes a photo as proof and post it through Lalin •Other users seeing that post can upvote if they agree, comment, favourite to keep track, and report the post if found bogus •Lalin has two ends, one for the people and one for the government •Lalin then sorts the post based on the most upvoted and notifies the government end of the app •Seeing this notification, the government can decide whether to resolve it or not •If the government choose to resolve it, the government will open a thread in the forum section of our app that will be sorted regionally •Users can then check the forum section to verify the policy the government chose to go with and give feedback on the process or result.
