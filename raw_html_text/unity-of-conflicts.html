This creative art installation project is where Art, Physics, Computer Science, Astronomy, Philosophy of the East and the West, Biology, Economics, Buddhism and Humanity intersect. These 9 fields don't look like they are related, but because of blockchain concept, it bridges all 9 fields together. The project is the beginning/the mother of the 9 fields. At the same time, it is the result/baby of the 9 fields.

The Klein bottle structure is a Symbol of Conflicts & Harmony, which also symbolizes blockchain concept.

This project is based on Dogethereum Bridge Art Project from Maker Space Workshop, it is a temporary public art which will travel around major cities in U.S.
