Inspiration

The doors in school are far too boring and we wanted chaos and excitement when making the decision to pull or push a door.

What it does

It triggers a voice to mock you for incorrectly pushing a pull door, or vice-versa.

How I built it

The change in accelerometer reading triggers a fetch request to a web server that plays a random voice line out of a directory.

Challenges I ran into

Accelerometer reading was too small

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Explored many different things we had not done before.

What I learned

Many things.

What's next for Push-Pulling on a Pull/Push Door

Add a face recognition to take a photo of the victims and keep track on a scoreboard.
