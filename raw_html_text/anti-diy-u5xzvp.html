Inspiration

Uber, and other service based industries. Furthermore, there were complaints among university students about being unable to perform errands on their own, creating a potential market.

What it does

Sorts out low-level jobs for people nearby, and starts off in a local capacity before later on expanding continuously.

How I built it

As a website, using databases and connecting them via PHP, as well as displaying those who are available for immediate employment.

Challenges I ran into

Implementing the PHP and sorting the job offers due to the tricky interface, as well as figuring out how to sort the functions of the website such that it met all requirements.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Collaboration with teammates.

What I learned

How to use PHP to sort database entries as well as connecting two sides of a database, customer and employee.
