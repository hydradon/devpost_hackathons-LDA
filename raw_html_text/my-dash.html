Inspiration

I wanted to learn about car maintenance and best practices.

What it does

It tells you preventative car tips and general maintenance facts.

How I built it

I linked it to Mojio in order to get a user's driving history. I built it using Amazon Lambda and the Alexa sdk.

Challenges I ran into

I had difficulty fetching data from Mojio's server.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I used account linking with Mojio so users can get their driving history and information.

What I learned

It can be tricky authenticating and authorizing users. However, account linking makes the process easy.

What's next for My Dash

I would like to pull more data from Mojio and customize the car tips based on each user's driving history and location.
