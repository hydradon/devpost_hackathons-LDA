What it does

artDrop is an easy way to leave a drawing, or a message anywhere in the world. It can be thought of as a mobile graffiti app. Graffiti, without the destruction of property perhaps. It also comes with a fantastic augmented reality feature for viewing sections of map in a whole new and exciting way. Meshed LiDAR data makes it all happen, baby.
