Inspiration: Many often find themselves in jam-packed parking lots and struggling to find a parking spot. This is time consuming, incites frustration and can lead to various undesirable situations especially when there is an important event.

What it does

We have created an app that informs users of vacant parking spaces at their destination of choice.

How I built it

This is achieved by integrating machine learning technology into existing cameras and connecting the camera to our customized app that informs users of available parking spaces.

Challenges I ran into

Trying to generate innovative ideas that are feasible

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Learned how to generate innovative ideas and build an app from the get-go

What I learned

We have learned about the ideation process, UI and UX and how to collaborate among ourselves.

What's next for Camlot

Implementing the machine learning algorithm to understand how to identify different parking spots based on preferences and car model.
