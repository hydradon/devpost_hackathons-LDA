Inspiration

We believe there is a lot of room for improvement in the communication between students and teachers. In fact, most of us have heard teachers say that our questions during class is probably the same as that of many others in class. The number one question goes along the lines of "what does that mean?" or "can you explain that again?" However, not every student feels comfortable sharing their questions with the whole class, so this is a good opportunity to use technology in favor of them.

What it does

When the student presses the button (on the pencil case), it sends a pre-defined message via wi-fi to the teacher's computer (our website).
