Inspiration

The popular game charades and the lack of nearby people who want to play it.

What it does

It connects people online, potentially strangers, to have fun acting for each other in friendly competition.

How we built it

A website (HTML, CSS, Javascript) and using TokBox API.

Challenges we ran into

Learning how to make a website and server from scratch.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Video connection and a pretty UI.

What we learned

How to make a website/server and how to work together as a team.

What's next for CruzActs

Further development and features.
