Inspiration

Within the Wellesley community, a fellow student sent out a request for a classmate to take notes for her, for money.

What it does

A platform for students to collaborate on schoolwork and help all access the material, regardless of whatever reasons may prevent them from attending class.

How I built it

Front-end: bootstrap to add various widgets to the interactivity of the site Back-end: used django views to create individual pages, models to store the data

Challenges I ran into

Front-end: newbie problems p2 Back-end: Learning django as I went along; numerous newbie problems

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Front-end: cobbling together a website and working finally with divs Back-end: getting it to work

What I learned

Front-end: a thing Back-end: another thing

What's next for NoteLuck

  • upvoting good notes
  • built-in screen reader
  • user logins
  • notifications for users when classes are updated, and reminders to upload notes
