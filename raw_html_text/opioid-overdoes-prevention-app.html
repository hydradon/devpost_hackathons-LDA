Opioid Overdose Prevention App *Innovative Mobile app that helps people understand what to do if a family member or friend overdoses on heroin or opioid pain medications. *Easy and Intuitive interface for the Five Essential Steps for First Responders. *A slick, clean user interface design to display information for Patients, Family Members and Health Professionals. *Information is updated in real-time, so user does not need to download the app again to get the latest information. *Easy navigation and quick display for information. *Conversation Starters – to help bring up the conversation with people struggling with addiction *Need some to talk to? “OP Anonymous”, an anonymous social network that provides a peer support system. *It helps you connect with people who are going through the same thing, helping in long-term recovery from addiction. *App works on the iPhone and will be downloadable via the AppStore for Free.
