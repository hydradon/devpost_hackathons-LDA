Inspiration

The Decade of Action for Road Safety (2011–2020) UN General Assembly Resolution 64/255 ( Aimed at reducing the number of deaths due to road traffic injuries. The 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development has recently reiterated this priority by setting a target for 50% reduction in road traffic deaths and injuries by 2020.
