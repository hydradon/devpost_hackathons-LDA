Inspiration

We came here as individuals but we are going to leave as a team.

What it does

We are making for You a multiplayer chatbot for gaming. You will be the hero in your game and you can compete against or cooperate with other online players. All the process is made through chat. Bot is the advisor for your realm. In addition there will be quests and rpg system to make it even more attractive.

How I built it

We invented our own domain specific language to implement to help us to deal with complexity of our game. We made a story line for a client to try different tasks in their specific guest.

Challenges I ran into

Greatest challenge was to limit to overcome how to implement something that bring endless combinations. In addition, story line took more time as expected.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We learned a lot about our team and found our strong sides, we had fun in hackathon while doing it. We made this product happen

What I learned

We learned to ask more help from the mentors. We learned to pivot fast. We failed fast, thanks to that we have now this awesome product for market.

What's next for

We are going to continue advancing our AI logic. Afterwards we are going publish this outstanding product for the customers. Find customers and get even more feedback.
