PastTense

is a social web app that lets users find or create messages to send to their future self, and others.

Not only that! But users also have the ability to keep the message as private or make it public (which is default)

So what happens when the message is left public ? Well on our website, every new message that people are publicly sending themselves and others gets pop'ed in real-time for everyone to see. This is really cool because it gives people an insight into what others are thinking and feeling.

What can be done with these pop'ed messages? If any other users like the message, they can have it be sent to themselves, users can also like cool messages.

What counts as a message? Right now, messages can be in the form of text and image.
