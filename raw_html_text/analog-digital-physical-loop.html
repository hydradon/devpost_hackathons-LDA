Inspiration

The only inspiration for this project was to win the Diode challenge for the Hackathon, and to gain experience in Arduino.

What it does

The circuit operates on the concept of a physical loop where energy is "conserved". The circuit alternates between analog and digital signals through 4 different methods to keep a perpetual signal transferred. Entering the loop either at the photoresistor or at the sound detector, the loop is traversed from sound detector to LED light array, to photoresistor to buzzer and back to the sound detector.

TL;DR once you start this loop, it cannot be stopped.
