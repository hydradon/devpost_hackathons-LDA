Inspiration

Smart housing

What it does

Our platform allows students to post and complete task to earn coins which turns into cash to help them pay for high-costing rent in the long run.

How we built it

We built the frontend with PHP and backend with flutter.

Challenges we ran into

Time was not enough to make a complete app. And coming up with ideas to scale our platform which we overcame later on.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We created the UI/UX design which illustrated well of how our platform works.

What we learned

We learned to have fun and not stress too much over one thing.

What's next for ST Coins

We shall see.
