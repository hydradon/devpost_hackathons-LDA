Inspiration

Our original idea proved infeasible and this one was suggested as a joke. We went with it anyways.

What it does

It lets you withdraw and deposit money into your bank account!

How we built it

We used the Nessie API from CapitalOne and JavaScript to make a web portal to access your bank data.

Challenges we ran into

The Nessie servers weren't working for the majority of the hackathon and we thought that our code was messed up.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The website skims money out of your account and puts it into ours.

What we learned

It's a bad idea to use a third-party banking app.

What's next for Totally Legal Online Banking (100% legit!)

A mobile app.
