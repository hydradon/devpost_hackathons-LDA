What it does

Precisely is a platform which helps you search for and discover the latest academic and professional opportunities, personalized for you! We cover latest jobs, internships, scholarships, conferences, competitions, fellowships and much more. Precisely intuitively learns your interests and brings you a personalized feed of the latest jobs, internships, scholarships, conferences, competitions, fellowships and much more - all in one place and in multiple languages! Our AI-powered platform matches your interests with the opportunities like international internships, conferences, fellowships, conferences and scholarships to separate the music out of the noise.

Currently, we offer opportunities for scholarships, internships, and fellowships and more in 15 languages! And we assure you that no matter what region or country you belong to, you will soon be able to see opportunities your language! Just comment your language and you will be surprised to see how lightning quick we are!

The scholarships, internships, jobs, conferences and more on our platform are manually curated and handpicked from tens of thousands out there. To add to it, you can also read in-depth details of the scholarships and other opportunities like eligibility, requirements, funding, location etc.

Unlike other applications and websites, we do not just cater to only one region, rather, we offer national and international opportunities for the youth across the world in Central Asia, South and South East Asia, Middle East, Europe, the Americas, Africa and the Oceanic region!

How I built it

The Alexa skill was built in NodeJS on AWS Lambda using AWS Dynamo DB as Database. There is also a php based backend which the Alexa Skill connects to for getting the data which is generated from our machine learning, data analytics, and deep neural networks which cater to personalized feeds.

Challenges I ran into

Finding quality content and connecting Alexa skill to the android application. Also, Use of state handlers perfectly has always been one of the toughest tasks in Alexa Development and this skill heavily depends on state handlers.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

This is an Alexa skill which I will be using myself daily.

What I learned

Working with state handlers is always a new challenge.
