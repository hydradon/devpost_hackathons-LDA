Chalk

Write it down, get it done.

Inspiration

Do you find yourself overwhelmed with all the things you have to do? Don't know what to do first? That's alright, neither did we, until we made Chalk. We created Chalk to help plan and manage our schedules.

What it does

Just tell Chalk:

  • what you need to get done
  • when it has to be done by
  • how important it is to you

and Chalk will help you figure out how to go about your week.

Chalk will take care of it, so you don't have to.

What's next for Chalk

Improve the task distribution between different days. Implement a Profile page to customize account and profile settings.
