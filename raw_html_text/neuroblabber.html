Neuroblabber

People always think that machine learning is something complicated, something for PhDs and so on, we bring machine learning to the comfort of their couch! Just say the word, build your network, get your training data with 0 code. With visualization this tool is also useful for the scientific community, because it provides insights into the characteristics of neural networks during training which can aid in designing of the architecture. It also makes creation of neural networks possible for people with disabilities.
