Inspiration Awesomeness

  • Eroding Education Infrastructure: misaligned economic incentives between universities and students has resulted in skyrocketing costs, archaic education standards, and a bubble that is about to pop.

  • Unprecedented Automation: Modern technology has finally caught up to existing B.S. skill levels. Many fresh grads are graduating without valuable, future-proof skills and capital. Retraining is going to be a huge job market.

  • Rise of Nontraditional Opportunities: Opportunities like Lambda School, Make School, trade school, vocational opportunities, and more are making the skills-job market more liquid and changing what "a traditional education" means.

We propose a different model for the educational infrastructure of American society, enabled by the Internet to allow for unique skills and circumstances to better align with sustainable employment opportunities.

ML and data processing allow users to find the personalized path that best fits their life situation and aspirations--not "just go to college"--to achieve their dreams.

Economic incentives are aligned between the platform, employment opportunities, and education opportunities.
