Inspiration

As freshman we have found it difficult to stay fit and watch what we eat. And many other students at UCLA face the same problem. This will help the on campus community stay fit and be healthy.

What it does

It counts calories of all food items available on the Hill (residential area) at UCLA. Users can create their own accounts and maintain a record of what they eat.

How we built it

We built it using JavaScript, Python and Django mainly.

Challenges we ran into

We faced a big challenge when we could not get a proper server host for our website. Also, we had to use react which was brand new for us.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

That as a two man team we did our best in our first ever hackathon. We created something better than what we initially thought would be produced, and we are proud of it.

What we learned

We learnt how a hackathon works, and learnt many new techniques and languages (like react).

What's next for Healthy Bruins

To make a fully functioning website where anyone can access it and stay fit and healthy.
