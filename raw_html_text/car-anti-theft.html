Inspiration - Increasing number of car thefts

What it does - The App keeps you connected with your Ford car 24*7. Sensors detect Motion as well as start of the engine and send an alert to your phone with a 360 degree image showing the car and surroundings. You can then accept or discard based on the image received. If you accept the alert, the app will call 911 immediately and start the geo tracking of your car on the app.
