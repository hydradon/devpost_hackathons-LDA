Inspiration

Current market tools do not provide a safe and anonymized way to report street harassment.

What it does

Enable users to create anonymous safety reports while viewing previously reported incidents in an area

How we built it

This app was developed on Android Studio with SQL and Python Flask for the backend. We integrated with Google Maps to provide a seamless experience for users to visualize safety reports.
