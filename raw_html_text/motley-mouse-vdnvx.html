Motley Mouse came about as a response to the growth of access of educational resources. Not only are costs down, but more solutions are being created to solve technological problems. The challenge we face is navigating these resources when there is such a wealth of information readily available.

We curate tweets from trusted sources related to education and categorize them for the consumer. Whether you are a teacher looking for a new lesson in math, a student learning to code, or a parent trying to teach your kids to read Motley Mouse is your solution to find what you need quickly and easily using tweets as a concise delivery method.
