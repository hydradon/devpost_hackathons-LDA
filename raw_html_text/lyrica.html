# Lyrica

Please use the heroic link - the APK might be a little jank.

Ultimate fun app for wannabee freestyle rappers like myself.

How to master the art of free styling like a playa:

  • Play any backing track over YouTube, SoundCloud, etc.
  • Or for convenience, cycle through some royalty-free tracks built-in
  • Hit 'Rhyme' to cycle between rhymes of the same syllable sound
  • Hit 'Pivot' to change to a new syllable sound.
