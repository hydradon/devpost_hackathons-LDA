Inspiration

We are college students who have struggled to find peers to study with. We wanted to build an app to help connect students and build an academic community.

What it does

Connects students within courses in respective universities to share notes, discuss classes, form study groups, and find tutors.

How I built it

We used the MIT app inventor to create our app and used Ninja Mock to create mock ups of what we envision our project to look like.

Challenges I ran into

We've never created an app before so we had trouble getting started, getting our ideas into code, and working the simulation.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We are proud of putting an application together despite no prior experience.

What I learned

How to put an application together starting with just an idea.

What's next for Study Buddy

We hope to get our application to how we envision it and launch our application so that other students can benefit from it and further their education.
