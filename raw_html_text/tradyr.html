Want to get last-minute tickets to your favorite artist? Got sick right before an expensive concert? There are plenty of reasons why someone might want to buy tickets from or sell tickets to other fans. But there is a big issue: Who can be trusted? Scamming is all too common these days. People will sell fake tickets or take tickets and take off without paying. There is no reliable way to establish trust . Tradyr aims to assure you that what you're buying is real and actually gets you in - without charging everyone involved half the ticket price in fees alone.
