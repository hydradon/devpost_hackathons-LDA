Inspiration We felt it inconvenient to share our parking spot with roommate as we always need to keep schedule with each other and to move our car.

What it does Our web app allow you to lock/unlock your car; track location of your car; get notification when your car is out of a certain range.

How we built it We used Javascript to write our front end. Java for our back end. We also implemented Smartcar-API in our web as required.

Challenges we ran into All of us are new to this area and have almost no pervious experience. We develop our web from scratch and learning API from zero. At first, we tried to use Google Cloud Platform and spent all day on deployment. However, due to technical difficulties, we couldn't finish it.
