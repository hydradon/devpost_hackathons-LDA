Inspiration

We were inspired by BBQing a steak which ended up charred and on fire. Also numerous times where chicken would be half-cooked and the propane runs out.

What it does

FuelMe lets you use thermal imaging and a Flir One camera to accomplish 2 tasks:

  1. Examine your propane tank using thermal imaging to check the levels, and then find propane refill stations near you!
  2. Value add: Examine the inside of your food using thermal imaging to ensure it's cooked to perfection!
