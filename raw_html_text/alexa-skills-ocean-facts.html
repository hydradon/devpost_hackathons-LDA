Inspiration

Developing skills for Alexa is always fun.

What it does

Ask for Ocean Facts for a new interesting fact about the whole of universe as know to mankind, Ocean Facts provides fun the whole family can enjoy and is guaranteed to make you smarter. To start, just say "Alexa, launch Ocean Facts" or "Alexa, open Ocean Facts" to get a fact". At anytime, you can stop by saying "Alexa, stop"
