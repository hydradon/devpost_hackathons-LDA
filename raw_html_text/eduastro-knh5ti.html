Inspiration

Normal simulation programs for astrodynamics take too much time for calculating a solution. We wanted something more visual and simple to teach kids about the fundamentals of the Solar System.

What it does

It is a model of the Solar System. The motion of the bodies can be animated and there are some interesting news from twitter. Expect some surprises from there!

How we built it

We built it with lots of passion, perseverance and too little sleep time.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We believe that we have obtained a good model approximation for the Solar System that can be really useful for teachers at school. It's online and free to use!

What's next for EduAstro

Improve the accuracy of the equations while maintaining performance.
