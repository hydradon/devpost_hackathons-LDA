Inspiration

The opportunity of being part of an event this big and help people. Combine technology and social problems.

What it does

It serves as an alarm, a button or something that sends data and location to policeman. This in order to ask for help, also it displays information about policeman activities on your city, sharing posts and serve informing all the users.

How we built it

Using Android-Studio we build the application.

Challenges we ran into

As being the very first hackathon we assist to, all the new interactions and short time for doing the work represented a hard challenge.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Of being able to help other people with our application.

What we learned

Team Work, fast and by several mistakes, how to build an application using android-studio

What's next for Botón Aux

The informative arm that has the app is very ideal for growing at tons of ways.
