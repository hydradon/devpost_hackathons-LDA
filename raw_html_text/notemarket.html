Inspiration

As Vanderbilt students, we have no effective way of receiving notes when we miss class for whatever the reason may be.

What it does

It's a note exchange market that can be filtered by class, professor, and date missed.

How we built it

Challenges we ran into

Implementing the capital one api.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Our ability to come together and finish our project even with the limited WebDev we knew.

What we learned

We learned a lot more WebDev than we knew coming in.

What's next for NoteMarket

Helping students share notes not only for classes, but helping students prepare for tests by creating a study guide specific section for NoteMarket
