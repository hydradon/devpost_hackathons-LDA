What it does

White Knight is a decentralized solution, for sharing your incident about harassment. It allow you stories to remain completely private shared with no one unless you choose to at a later point, or you can connect with HR departments, police departments, not for profits, and civil action groups.

The use simply goes to a White Knight DApp page and types in their details, the Webpage automatically creates a wallet which is stored in their phone. The user then has the option of telling their story, and sending an encrypted copy to whomever they want or only storing a hash so they have a record of incident. This record is time stamped and can be shared with the court and law enforcement at a later point.

How I built it

It runs on Hyperledger, Node.JS, React, CouchDB and Stone Paper ThreeLefts Proprietary Blockchain Platform.
