Inspiration

We want to help people find venues for professional photoshoots, or Instagram side hobbies.

What it does

Hotspot helps photographers share, search, and review venues for taking pictures.
Hotspot also uses sentiment analysis to automatically categorize reviews as positive, negative, or neutral.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of our sentiment analysis and team dynamic, but we are especially proud of our team member who came in with no Django experience!
