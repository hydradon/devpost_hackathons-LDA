Inspiration

How to find and locate high-value assets, people and things in situations where they might need help

What it does

Customizable, scalable, hardware data acquisitions with indoor and outdoor data capabilities to transmit sensory data to the cloud at variable frequency. A data visualization and management system (web and mobile) with customizable easy features for corporate sized to family customers.

How we built it

Searched and searched for a solution that would work in many different situations/problems. Chased multiple existing solutions to find a solution that would work for Spotless, utilise IoT and have general market applications (a valuable solution for multiple uses/users)

Challenges we ran into

Finding where the value can be found in a fairly easy to build (low barrier to entry) infrastructure solution. As a small team, we would have liked to have had more time to proof out all aspects of our solution. But most of all finding a real problem to tackle with a solution we were proud of that fulfilled scope and provide personal satisfaction.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Pushing, pushing, pushing - eureka !

What we learned

don't build a solution until you can really agree on a problem that the whole team is passionate about solving and understands
