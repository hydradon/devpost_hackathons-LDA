Inspiration

My phone would often remain silent or on vibrate mode even outside of class. With the help of this application, the user is allowed to set a time from which the phone will start to be silent or vibrate. This app eliminates the human need to constantly remember to tun on the ringer after every class or meeting. Further development of this app will foster an idea of a repetitive alarms with multipile alarm times.

How I built it

We used research to come up with the code and reach a proper end. Our prior knowledge in C++ also helped to increase our understanding of Android Studio.

I'm proud of the way my group and I adapted to such a new enviroment. We had no prior knowledge or experience in Android Studio yet at the end of 36 hours we were able to yield a functioning hack.

What's next for Silent Hours

Silent Hours will become an application that will empower the user to choose a time frame in which their phone will be either silent or vibrate. We will further develop it to be able to handle multiple alarms.
