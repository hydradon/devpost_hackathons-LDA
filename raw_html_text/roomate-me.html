Inspiration

This was designed because it can be challenging to find roommates after one moves out

How we built it

We built it using adobe dreamweaver

Challenges we ran into

We wanted to incorporate VR into it for virtual touring of apartments but was having a hardtime rendering images. WE have never worked with VR and really wanted to experience it.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We coded the whole thing almost from scratch and felt good! Our Website looks really nice.

What we learned

We learned the structure of VR code, and the premise that it runs on. We also looked into virtual environments of touring houses to draw in inspiration for our project.

What's next for Roomate Me

We want to develop it into a more robust website and maybe actually finish it and make it fully functional one day.
