Inspiration

We were inspired by the popular meme of Ugandan Knuckle and his user-created catchphrase, "Do you know da wey?" It was also a part of the CTF we took part in this hackathon.

What it does

It is a two-player game in which each player can control their own Ugandan Knuckle. The Ugandan Knuckles can spit, and winning requires hitting the opposition three times.

How we built it

We built this game with the Unity engine, using Visual Studio 2017 for writing scripts in C#.

Challenges we ran into

There were many times our team had to backtrack due to minor issues and at one point switched ideas because of >> getting stuck on a particular issue involving a class we were unfamiliar with.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It was our first experience with a game engine, and having essentially finished within the allotted time, that alone was accomplishing for us. Constantly looking into the scripting API and identifying necessary elements were crucial.

What we learned

We learned that even small projects like this game can take a lot of work and manpower. We have also learned how stressful working quickly under a small time-frame can be.

What's next for Ugandan Knuckle Fight

The sequel. But in serious terms, probably more games of this nature for practice, and possibly moving on to much >> more advanced things in the future.
