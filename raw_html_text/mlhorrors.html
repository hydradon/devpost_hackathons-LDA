Inspiration

We all love the Werewolf game don't we? Wouldn't it be better if it had sound effects and awesome photoshops of the MLH team?

How it works

The app run on one device for the Game Master, and on as many devices as needed for the participants (at least 6-8 recommended).

Challenges we ran into

Integrating BrainTree, persuading Gradle to stop whining, keeping devices synced together via GCM.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Speedy communication via Google Cloud Messaging and push notifications; fully-functional RoR backend; well-built Android app, the sound effects.
