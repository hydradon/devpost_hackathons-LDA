Inspiration

Young people aren't communicating with their government and are not aware of political issues. We help Selfie Activism will inspire them to become more politically active.

What it does

  1. Pick an issue you're interested in
  2. Take a selfie for the postcard
  3. Write a message on your stance on the issue
  4. Use your location to find your local representatives
  5. We send your postcard (physical!) to them using the Lob API!

How we built it

We made it in Objective-C with Lob API to send the postcard. We scrape issues and their descriptions from 5calls.org

What's next for Selfie Activism

We're launching a social campaign as well as improving the app.
