First Impression Matters !

Most visitors experience begin at the airport terminal looking for local transportation. For those looking for a taxi, confusion and chaos are around the corner. Saddled with a cargo load of regulations and rules, taxi rates have become all but impossible to estimate beforehand. With the advancement of ride sharing apps, the consumer has come to expect a level of service and information that is currently not provided by the government regulated taxi service.

Fair Taxi addresses this information asymmetry, by providing the tourist with an easy to use mobile application. Once you arrive, at your destination, you can still relay on Fair Taxi. It has a virtual meter feature that can be used when using regular taxi services.

Finally, empowered with View PR API, Fair Taxi is able to provide the tourist with the latest and greatest information available for gastronomy options and nearby attractions.
