Inspiration

As part of the team-building exercise they had us fill in mad-libs from copypastas. We thought, why not cut out the work for the organizers since it's obviously an arduous task to decide which profane words to remove from "SCAV bothers me"

What it does

It uses google cloud's part-of-speech tagger, along with NLTK's stopwords, to decide which words are candidates for removal and replacement with blanks.

How we built it

We used the Google Cloud Natural Language API to tag the parts of speech in a copypasta, then used our super secret algorithm (it's not that secret)

Accomplishments that we're proud of
