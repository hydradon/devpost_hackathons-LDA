Inspiration

Learning a language is something that needs to be practiced every day and you need to immerse yourself as much as possible in.

How it works

As the person trying to learn the language, you can send a message through our app in the language that you're trying to learn, and the people you send it to get the translated version of that in the language that they speak. When they respond in their language, you get their message translated into the one you're trying to learn.
