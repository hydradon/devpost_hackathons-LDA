Inspiration

What it does

Makes physiotherapy fun

How we built it

Combination of Unity and C# coding, and long hard work!

Challenges we ran into

Generating the game in Unity given the short timescale provided

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Promoting fun physiotherapy for young children.

What we learned

Basics of C# and teamwork in app & project development.

What's next for C-Force

Integrate competition through the app, and a leaderboard based scoring system to compete with friends.
