Problem: Young adults do not have enough incentive and discipline to be able to manage their money, especially with ease of functions such as ApplePay or AndroidPay Solution: Don't budge is a mobile app that allows you to set a general monthly budget and a budget limiting you from specific stores to stop you from using mobile pay functions

  • When you have reached your monthly budget, Don't Budge sends notifications reminding you not to overspend
  • You can set specific smaller budgets for certain stores that will be recognized based on location - when reaching these smaller budgets, notifications will be sent to keep you from spending
  • Daily facts will be shown to increase financial literacy
