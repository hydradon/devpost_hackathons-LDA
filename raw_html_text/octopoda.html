Inspiration

We were frustrated with the long process of searching for sources, saving notes elsewhere, and then creating a separate document for writing the final paper. Our goal was to streamline the research process by bringing it to a central area.

What it does

Octopodus allows users to import articles directly to the site in a simplified form. Specific text on Octopodus can be selected and annotated. Annotations are saved on a separate page where they can be easily viewed and accessed.
