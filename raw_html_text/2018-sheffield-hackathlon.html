Our website - reunite.me - is designed to reunite students and staff with their stuff!

It's one page (ListingPage.html) that displays all the lost and found items that are in the database. If you find something someone else has lost all you have to do is click the button on the post and it'll send an email to them. There's also the option to add a post to the database which you can do via a pop-up window.

You can easily search through the website for posts that contain keywords that are relevant to you - eg. lost, found, keys.
