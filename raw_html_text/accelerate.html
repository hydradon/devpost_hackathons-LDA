Inspiration

Just want to fully develop your phone's hardware haha.

What it does

When you press the start button, the app starts to measure the acceleration of your phone. Try your best to accelerate your phone(like shaking, throwing, or even striking), then press "stop". You will see your score and you can see the global rank.

How I built it

Using the xcode and firebase.

Challenges I ran into

It is hard to retrieve data from the accelerometer.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I successfully established the app and the UI design is elegant.

What I learned

How to design an app and realize it.

What's next for Accelerate

Maybe I will add some practical part to allow users to use it in some small research projects. The app will also be able to draw an acceleration/time graph.
