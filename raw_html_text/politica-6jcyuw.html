Politica - Rate your politician!

Politica is a social platform in which the world-wide community becomes well informed of the politicians world wide. Then, you can participate polls, add details about politicians, read up on the latest news, or participate in discussions with other community members.

You can choose to be anonymous or you can verify your identity to have some credibility.

Our ecosystem is made of:

  • A website
  • An sms bot (a live demonstration will be provided due to restrictions of application used)
  • A forum
  • A server (php, a little sequel) -unfinished app

Inspiration - We thought hard and decided one of biggest political problems present, was that there was no platform to see review and discuss politicians nationwide and locally, which is when we thought of politica

What it does: in a dew words: review discuss and poll politicians world wide, nationally and locally

Accomplished that we are proud of: We are very proud of what we accomplished today as none of us had ever attempted such a big project

What we learned: We learned that coding is extensively time consuming, database management, and how hard backhand is

Whats next for Politica: We all decided that our idea was a very viable solution to the problems present in the politics industry, and that we would work on this even after the hackathon to better our solution

We also had a companion android app in the works but due to server issues and time constraints, we scratched it.
