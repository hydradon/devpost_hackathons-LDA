One day, all of us were sitting around in a room bored. We really wanted to play some games of soccer, but no one really was available to meet with us. At that point, we really wished there was a way to compete with other individuals. Because of this experience, we decided than an app like Coordinate would be nice such that anytime anyone is bored, one can simply sign up and compete.
