diabEATes

When you are struggling with diabetes or allergies you have limitations on what you eat. When we go to the store, we cannot easily determine whether we can eat selected products without a risk to our health. With a simple bar code scanner we provide customers with a clear UI if this product is risky, average or safe to eat. dieabEATes also provides you information about any allergens a food product might have.
