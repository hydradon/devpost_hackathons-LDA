Inspiration

More than 400.000 people and organizations across the globe already use Moqups to create wireframes and prototypes for their web and mobile projects. Since many of our users rely on Atlassian's products to manage their development projects, we have gotten numerous requests for a better integration. As such, we decided to provide an easy way to use Moqups directly inside JIRA.

What it does

Moqups works from sketching out ideas to making low-fidelity wireframes to orchestrating highly detailed mockups that are readily accessible from JIRA issues.
