Inspiration

Everyone has issues finding parking to popular sporting events, concerts, and big cities.

What it does

Users can rent out their vacant parking spaces and book other's vacant parking spaces for a fraction of the cost of traditional parking.

How we built it

Android Studio, Github, Butterknife, Firebase, Google Maps API, and lots of elbow grease!

Challenges we ran into

Merging the project was hard.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We merged it and got a working project! Plus we learned how to use Firebase and sharpened our Android skills.

What we learned

Everything.
