CultureShock

Participant project in Hack Puebla 2016.

Culture Shock is a new platform game, based on four famous ancient cultures, with the purpose of spreading the basics of these civilizations: egyptian, aztec, nordic and japanese.

Through an interactive, user-friendly application, the player will be able to choose one of the four characters and battle. Different attacks and weapons are available depending on the selected culture.

Culture Shock es a great opportunity to approach the traditions of different civilizations through a party game.

Players: 2-4
