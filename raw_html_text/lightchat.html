Inspiration

Need for a lightweight peer-to-peer chat to host and get to know Bluemix.

What it does

Connects users to each other with increased ability to provide IBM integrations and data analytics.

How we built it

Node.js backend, Angular-ish front-end, Bluemix hosting.

Challenges we ran into

Lack of knowledge of MEAN stack, short deadline.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Got to know and play around with Bluemix.

What we learned

A lot.

What's next for LightChat

Integrate translation service, maybe some sort of game aspect.
