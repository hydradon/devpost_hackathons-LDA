Inspiration

Nowadays more and more people are working on a desk and a lot of them do not have the correct sitting position and health habits. Because of that while years pass they face more and more health problems like back pain.

What it does

The device is detecting your sitting posture and reminds you when you have a bad one. That way we help you develop good healthy habits. It has also integration with Skyscanner API that suggests you flights to a random destination for you to relax at the cheapest price.

How I built it

We use a library called OpenPose that detects your posture and we have an algorithm that detects bad sitting posture.
