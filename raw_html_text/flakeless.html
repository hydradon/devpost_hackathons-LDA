Inspiration

It's like a when2meet for friends to plan vacations together.

What it does

It provides a consolidated trip itinerary with specific flight, hotel, and transportation information all geared to accommodating as many people’s preferences as possible.

How I built it

We split up into teams to complete this project. Two of us worked on the backend, making sure that the PHP and JavaScript communicated with each other and made the right API calls. The rest of us worked on the front end, and built the pages that users would be directly interacting with.
