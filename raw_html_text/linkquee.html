Inspiration

We were inspired by the Vueling challenge because we though not only queues are not fun but boring, but that you could also meet new people and at the same time, make Vueling win a client base out of Social Networking and Gamification.

What it does

Users can enter the website on every device possible, being able to register, and this way entering the Vueling Club. They can play quiz games, where they can win Avios, Vueling's official coin. With them they can unlock prizes, like flight discounts or maybe snacks at the bar because they are winning real existing coins from Vueling: Avios and they are also part of the Vueling Club being able to receive email marketing! The social feature is that all people that are from a Vueling flight or not that do have internet can enter the app, making a connection between them where they can talk and filter users base on their interests(Business, Love, friendship, or someone to make tours with when they arrive.)

How I built it

We built it with an API based on azure and node js and Vue js as the website.

Challenges I ran into

Making everything related to the user's flight destination, and connect them all together!

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Making a full API and app that does work, and very interesting quiz games that evolve gamification for Vueling and makes them win more clients on the Vueling Club. Also, due to the social connection people retain good memories thanks to Vueling, which will bring them to Vueling in the future!

What I learned

Vue js and Node js

What's next for LinkQuee

Making it nicer and add more features like chat whatsapp like based messaging system or AR Way finding to help users search Vueling offices on an airport.
