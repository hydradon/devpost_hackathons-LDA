Inspiration: We were inspired by having a lack of ability to show work when doing homework, in class work, or even trivia.

What it does: Students and teachers can generate questions and users can send them their response with written work via email

How we built it: We used Xcode, Swift 2.3, 3, and Google Firebase for database.

Challenges we ran into: We ran into problems using the app on iOS, but overcame these struggles with good critical thinking.

Accomplishments that we're proud of: We're proud of our ability to allow our ideas to evolve as the hackathon progressed.

What we learned: We learned to have a positive mindset when facing challenges, and also using Xcode and Swift from scratch for the first time.

What's next for Growth Mindset: We are hoping to make cross-platform the app and make it more aesthetically pleasing and possibly sell it to schools.
