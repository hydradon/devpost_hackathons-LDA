Inspiration

My friends and I like making games with our Comp Sci teachers in them so I decided to finally make one of our joke ideas: Fahrenbacher Go, a game dedicated to Matthew Fahrenbacher.

What it does

You get to be Mr. Fahrenbacher and capture other teachers around Niles West.

How I built it

I used Java to make the project.

Challenges I ran into

I was having issues with the audio and making the player stand still while the background moves.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

A title screen as well as a game screen.

What I learned

How to make stationary players with moving backgrounds.

What's next for Fahrenbacher Go

Better graphics, more gameplay (maybe battles?), multiplayer. I would have done a lot more if my team showed up.
