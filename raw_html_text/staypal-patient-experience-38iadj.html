Inspiration: Inspired to enhance the connection between patients and those who care for them.

How it works: Simple input of important data by the patient, easily visible to nurses and actionable in real time to improve care and patient satisfaction.

Challenges we ran into: Lots of innovative ideas but what to focus on that would really make a difference!

Accomplishments that we are proud of: Integrating insights from HCPs at NYPH to deliver an innovative solution. A group of H4B and NYPH volunteers working collaboratively.

What we learned: Technology can be leveraged to strengthen the connection between NYPH personnel and the patients they serve.

What's next for StayPal Patient Experience: Full development of the platform and ongoing enhancements and rolling out across all hospital units.
