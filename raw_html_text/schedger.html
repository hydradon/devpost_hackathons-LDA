Schedger

Schedger tracks various information to help college students and presents it back to them in an organized, easy to read app. Information is split between: Tasks : things the user needs to do before a certain deadline. Events : which tell the student what they have to do at certain times for the coming week(s). There are regular, manually-input events and automatically-generated "GenEvents," which are tied to a task. GenEvents disappear once their related task is completed.

An AI automatically generates GenEvents during valid open times in the user's schedule, showing the student the best times to complete tasks.

Users are able to add, edit, and delete tasks and events
