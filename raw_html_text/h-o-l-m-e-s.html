Inspiration

ACG organizes the world's medical information making it useful and valuable at the point of care.

What it does

Using our patented directed machine learning rules engine, H.O.L.M.E.S., we are building an encyclopedia of medicine.

How we built it

We invented a new computer language, PALATKA-C, which was created to handle the intense algorithms required by medicine and machine.

Challenges we ran into

There were two major challenges the team ran into and both have been solved: How to teach a machine everything about medicine. How to build a graphical user interface of medicine for every disease, every test, and every procedure.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We now have a fully functionally system that is being used by providers and hospitals. Our team has developed a unique process of crowd sourcing the organization of medical knowledge into a question-answer expert system.

What we learned

Although true AI is a dream for the future, we have shown that directed machine learning is a pathway to automating medicine. H.O.L.M.E.S. thinks like a doctor so providers and facilities can do the right thing for the patient.

What's next for H.O.L.M.E.S.

We are actively seeking to take ACG H.O.L.M.E.S. to the next level. ACG wants to do the right thing for the patient and our partners.
