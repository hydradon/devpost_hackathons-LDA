Inspiration

We were inspired by the problem many consumers have when they own a lot of makeup. We find ourselves unable to keep track of everything and we end up making poor purchasing decisions based on the lack of management.

What it does

We created an app that will help makeup professionals track their inventory and we will notify them when to restock their products.
