Inspiration

At our school, we are assigned various texts ranging from AP Government textbook chapters to biology lab reports. Who has time for those when you could be exploring artificial intelligence and data science? With this app

What it does

This app will take in a picture of a text and will summarize the content and output to a PDF, making it concise and readable.
