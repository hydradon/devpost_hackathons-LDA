Token

Token allows you to ask your phone questions like "How many times have I gone to McDonalds in the past month?" or "What's the average amount I spent on food?" and Token, using our natural language processing, parses through that, and spits out the answer! We spent a lot of time making sure that the questions you could ask were both versatile and useful. On top of that, we focused a lot of effort on the design of the iOS app, so that users who have never used the app before could immediately get a hang of it, and enjoy using it for long and repetitive periods of time.

Token connects to your accounts via Plaid's API and was coded entirely in Swift (2.0).
