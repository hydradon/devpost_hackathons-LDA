New Day Nature is a mobile app that allows users to take pictures of Eco spots that they find aesthetically pleasing and share them on the app along with its exact location. The picture is then available on the app and others can favorite it to save for later. If it is spot that you're interested in going to then all you have to do is open the location pin which will launch on google maps and you can easily find that spot. This app is an awesome way to discover places near you that would would possibly never hear about. But not only do you see it, you don't have to worry about being unable to find it. And in the near future you would be able to get a push notification that you are passing by an eco spot and easily go visit that eco spot. It's a cool way to connect with nature and your community by easily posting on the app or favoriting other spots that interest you.

A problem that this app will also help to fix is regulating funds to the appropriate eco spots by seeing how much that location is used by the general public and for what activity.
