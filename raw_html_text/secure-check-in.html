Inspiration

To make the airline booking and check-in process more secure.

What it does

Our system was designed to let airline passengers book and check into flights using multifactor authentication.

How we built it

This was built using a wide array of hardware and software, including Android Studio, NodeJS, and the Qualcomm DragonBoard.

Challenges we ran into

We ran into challenges interfacing the dragon board with other hardware, which was likely our largest challenge.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are quite proud of the concept that we have developed that can further be created into a very safe and powerful system.

What we learned

We learned a lot about hardware and IoT device interfacing and integration, along with multiple amazon web services.

What's next for Secure Check-in

The next step is to fine-tune the development of the system, and increase system robustness and accessibility.
