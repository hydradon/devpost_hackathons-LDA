Inspiration: Being young adults ourselves, we wanted to help our peers who are new to America. We hope that this site makes their American experience better.

What it does: communiTEEN Immigrant is available in English, Spanish, Dutch/German, Bosnian, Chinese and French, all of which are spoken by large immigrant populations in St. Louis. There are pages on recreation, religious opportunities, jobs, and education for the users to learn more about life in St. Louis and integrate as smoothly as possible.

How I built it: We built this app using HTML, CSS, ruby and javascript.

Challenges I ran into: working with APIs, pushing to gitHub

Accomplishments that I'm proud of: Translating six languages and implementing google maps seamlessly.

What I learned: our team works really well together.
