• Was soll ich machen (What should i do?) Logo

Inspiration

I would like to have a skill that can give me a tip in different life situations.

What it does

The skill gives suggestions against boredom. The boredom tips take into account the age and gender of the user. The skill also contains a random number generator in which a virtual coin is flipped. You can ask about the meaning of life and get a geeky answer.

As purchase option question and answer tests are to be offered, which are to be helpful with the decision making. Currently, a relationship test is available in which users can assess the quality of their partnership with their boyfriend or girlfriend.

How I built it

I programmed the skill in node.js 8.10, use an external database for answers and use AWS Lambda, Cloudwatch and DynamoDB.

Challenges I ran into

The development of ever new tests and the evaluation of these tests in order to generate a suitable answer for the users will be my biggest challenge.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am proud that I can now monetize individual intents.

What I learned

I am proud that patience and diligence pay off.

What's next for Was soll ich machen? (What should i do?)

Further tests will be added during the Challenge.

A subscription model is also to be offered to users, with which users can access future tests.
