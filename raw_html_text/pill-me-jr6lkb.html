Inspiration Manage medication for people who cannot manage them themselves.

What it does

Dispenses medication in controlled amounts. Reads medication labels. Sends notifications reminders. Uses a medication query API to determine information about a specific medication in the system.

How we built it

Through an iterative process of brainstorm: first we developed a rough prototype in which we aimed to lay the groundwork for the fundemental features of the project; then an app was developed to control the device.

Challenges we ran into

Everything, especially that we didn't have parts.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Finding said resources/parts to create a working prototype.

What we learned

Bring parts next time.

What's next for pill.me

Global domination. In terms of in-home pill dispensaries of course.
