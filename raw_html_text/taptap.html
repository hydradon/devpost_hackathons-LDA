TapTap

TapTap allows you to control your browser at the tap of a button. TapTap performs a custom action depending on which tab is currently active. Control YouTube, Vimeo, Spotify, SoundCloud, Netflix, Hulu, and many more websites by using the button to play and pause conveniently. On text based sites like Wikipedia, TapTap helpfully scrolls down so you can continue reading.
