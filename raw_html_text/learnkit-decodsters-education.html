Inspiration

Because we have gone through the phase where resources are not easily available at right time from authentic persons. We wanted to provide an interface where it is very feasible for the students to gain instant links and description of the content that they want.

What it does

Basically, the website is all about the sharing of various resources like notes, various external links, etc. The resources are provided by various professors from all over the institute, where she/he can stay in touch by providing students the necessary materials.

The student in turn can access these resources quickly and efficiently and he doesn’t have to worry about the authenticity of the resources.

How we built it

We used the CMS, wordpress for creating a custom theme, which will blend into a ERP.

Challenges we ran into

In less than 24 hours were about to make a complete website as well as an android app. Also, debugging the lines of code was also a huge task.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Android app wrapper around our site was developed in far less time than we expected.
