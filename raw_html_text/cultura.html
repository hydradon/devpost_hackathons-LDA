Cultura is a app that works within the refugee communities and beyond to provide a way to share their culture with others over food. By eliminating barriers to being social, Cultura seeks to help refugees by helping them create a sense of community they've often lost since coming to Australia, as well as connecting them with settled migrants, indigenous populations, and sympathetic Australians. Over food, refugees often talk about their problems, and realise that the problems they face aren't simply unique to their culture, race, or faith, but are universal. Cultura - Empowering refugees to find solutions to their problems and create community over food.
