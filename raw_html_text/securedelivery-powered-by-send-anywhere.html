Companies, musicians, bands, publishers integrate Send-Anywhere into their shopping cart. SECURE DELIVERY OF PURCHASES

It works simply, and securely, completely automatically and unattended. Once a user completes their purchase, they are sent a Purchase Confirmation email with a one-time-use URL. The URL can be used to download the product once. The advantage to businesses is that they can feel comfortable that the product is delivered, and the URL cannot be passed to others, or posted on the Internet. SecureDelivery with Send-Anywhere can manage inventory, manage data streams and provide secure delivery of all kinds of digital products.
