Inspiration

Dating websites suck. Let's be honest here. You ever meet up with the love of your life in Spain, only to find out you were really in love with a middle-aged 40 year old man? Just me? Alright, well that doesn't change the fact that despite so many options, it's so hard to find the one . There are also more bots than people, and people who don't know what they want or need.

What it does

You can't lie about your genome. Put simply, we take your genome and pair you up with people we think you'll be most compatible with.
