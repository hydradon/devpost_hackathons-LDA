Plan My Ride

Plan my ride is a tool to easily step through your route and mentally prepare for your ride. It allows you to visualize the journey without having to step through google Streetview manually. This means you can see where the steep inclines are, where to expect parked cars as well as memorize the turns you will take to get you from origin to destination.

The major pain point is that without this tool you would be required to load the starting point in google Streetview and step through the streets using their clunky interface, all of this whilst remembering where to go.
