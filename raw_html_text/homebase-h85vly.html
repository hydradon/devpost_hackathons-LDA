Inspiration

Mall billboards, large communal maps, online surveys, machine learning

What it does

Provides a 3-step package for attacking homelessness;

  1. A communal map to let homeless people know how to provide themselves with emergency shelter.
  2. A client feedback form to let shelters better understand what their clients want/need.
  3. A occupancy forecast for shelters to better anticipate capacity. ## How we built it LAMP stack ## Challenges we ran into time constraints and feasibility of training a machine learning model for predicting homeless movement patterns ## Accomplishments that we're proud of building the thing in a short amount of time ## What we learned How to bootstrap (albiet poorly) ## What's next for HomeBase Integration with Machine learning; Large scale deployment
