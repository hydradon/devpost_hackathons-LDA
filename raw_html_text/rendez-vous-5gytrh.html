Vision

Meeting a friend should be a straightforward endeavour . No more exchange of messages back and forth, no more time consuming phone conversations, just a press of a button .

Rendez-Vous is a social app that actually makes you more social, by helping you meet your friends!

How it works

Rendez-Vous let's you invite your friends , choose a time and location , or give multiple choices for your friends to choose from.

Receive notifications when you are invited, and when your invites are accepted.

Watch your friends as they approach on the map .

What's next for Rendez-Vous

The Android version is ready and fully functional. The iOS version is currently under development. Global launch is planned for an ubiquitous deployment on both platforms.
