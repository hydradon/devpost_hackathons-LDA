Why

Protect the Pack with the original cryptographic handshake::Howl

What

The Pack shares the perception of the territory (interface) and alerts one another of potential subversion via subtle state change.

How

Peer-to-peer hashing of Document Object Model (DOM) nodes after/during runtime. The files sent are not hashed, but instead the final interface state, after potential attempts to alter the interface.
