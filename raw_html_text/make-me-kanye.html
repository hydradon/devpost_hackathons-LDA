Inspiration

Interest in computer vision and Kanye West's semblance.

What it does

Takes images of people, analyzes their emotional state and places an appropiate Kanye face on top.

How we built it

Uploading an image to a Flask application that pre-processes the image, sends it to Google Vision API to analyze face geometry and emotion, then locally manipulates the image using PIL before saving the images and redirecting the user.

Challenges we ran into

Not having any experience with the Google Cloud Platform, computer vision, or image manipulation.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Applying computer vision to a real application, and bringing Kanye to the masses.

What we learned

Google Cloud Platform, Google Vision API, and Kanye is love, Kanye is wavy.

What's next for Make Me Kanye

More Kanyes, more people, more happiness.
