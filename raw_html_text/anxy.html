Inspiration

The inspiration for our project came from both of our recent years experiencing anxiety. We thought this technique of identifying stressors and slowly working on them was a good way to relieve anxiety.

What it does

Anxy is a web app that aims helps individuals struggling with any level of anxiety by jotting down their stressors of the day, what category it belongs in, and how they plan to resolve it. It will help promote healthy self-reflection and practice them in thinking of how to ease their anxiety, one stressor, one goal at a time. In addition, it has a statistical functionality, showing the user what category caused them most anxiety per week or month so they can have a holistic view of what stresses them out the most, and be more cognisant of what to be aware of and work on.

How I built it

We used Docker for MySQL and Flask. We built a RESTful API in plain Flask with an Angular.js and Boostrap front end. We built the front and back end separately, and connected them together afterwards.

Challenges I ran into

We had trouble connecting the front and back end and handling dependencies

We learned that connecting front and back end is no easy task. We also learned a lot from each other about front and back end, respectively.

What's next for anxy

We hope that anxy could take off and can be something that can help others with their anxiety or other mental issues. Otherwise, we are excited to work on it on our own after the hackathon because we're confident we can make it into something great and something to be even more proud of
