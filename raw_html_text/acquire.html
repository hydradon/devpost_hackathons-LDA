Inspiration

For the last few weeks we have made a massive spreadsheet of job applications for software engineering interns. When it comes to internship position it's all a number game, the more places you apply the better.

What it does

Acquire searches the web to find you all the best internship opportunities and compiles them all in one clean interface. Unlike other job sites, we handle keeping track of your internship from start to finish. That mean you don't need to keep a massive spreadsheet! Easily get links directly to the employers application and keep track of details such as if you have applied and what stage you are in the interview process. Did you find a job opportunity that our system missed? Easily add it and everyone else will get access as well! The master list of internships is shared among all users!!
