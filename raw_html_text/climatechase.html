While American voters say that they care about climate change, polls of voters actually reveal that it ranks relatively low. This is the result of many direct results of climate change -- changes in agricultural productivity, higher amounts of armed violence, etc. -- are not percieved to be effected.

This game attempts to introduce to the public the relationships between unforseen variables. Given the opp to invest in different green energy resources in a simulation, the public can see the output result on various metrics they SAY they care about very much.
