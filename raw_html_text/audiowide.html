Inspiration

Our team found common ground in our love for music and decided to help others connect through music. One of the greatest problems we face in the area of music is that we can't find good recommendations for unknown groups. Another huge problem for artists starting out is that it's hard to be discovered even though there may be many that love their music. We wanted to fix that.

What it does

Our website allows users to discover new music. They begin by adding in their favorite artists and genres and based on their favorite artists a page is made so that they can look at similar artists. These similar artists are voted on by other users. This way lesser-known artists can be discovered. They can also look through different music genres and see the most popular artists by each genre and a few of their biggest hits.

How we built it

We began by figuring out what pages we wanted, and we each took our own part to make. We each coed our own parts and later put it all together which wasn't always perfect but now it works out most of the time.
