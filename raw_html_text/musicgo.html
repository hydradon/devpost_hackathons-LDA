Inspiration

I always find problem in listening to music from multiple music services as all the songs are not in one app and I have to switch back and forth.

What it does

It takes your songs from spotify and your saved playlists from youtube and that's it you can listen and watch to your favorite songs and videos at one place.

How I built it

Using Android, Node.js on server side,RapidApi and Youtube Api

Accomplishments that I'm proud of
