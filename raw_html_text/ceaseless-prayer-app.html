Inspiration

What would it take to pray for every person on earth? From a Facebook experiment, we discovered it would take less than 1% of the world's population to pray for everyone on earth. Inspired by 1 Timothy 2, we invite all believers to experience the joy of praying for others, like their friends, family, coworkers, and beyond.

What it does

The app shows you 3 contacts to pray for every day, so that over time the user prays for everyone they know. This hackathon, we focused on improving the "joy" aspect of the iOS app, by implementing a celebration screen when a person prays for all their contacts. We also implemented the user onboarding, so they understand how to use the app.

How I built it

iOS native app, UX design

Challenges I ran into

Staying focused on the things that improve the experience without "gamifying" the prayer experience too much.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Improving the experience will help the user feel happy when using the app, and will help improve the social sharing of the app so others can find out about it too.

What I learned

Paper + pen + conversation = good ideas that I would never have thought of

What's next for Ceaseless Prayer app

Pushing out to production the changes; implementing what we learned for the Android app as well
