Inspiration

In the Bay Area, bubble tea has become an obsession, filling all social media feeds. We wanted to make a social media app so boba lovers can share photos, reviews, and drink recommendations with other users, as well as find boba stores in their area.

What it does

Our app has many wonderful features:
-users can post boba reviews, recommendations, and photos
-posts are on the feed for all users to see
-map of all the boba places in the bay area
-customize profile with drink and store favorites

Challenges we ran into

We got rejected from the app store the first time!

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We're proud of making our idea, which we've had for years, a reality.

What we learned

We learned how to persevere through errors and bugs and push out a super cool project.

What's next for Boba Beacon

Stay tuned for new features, we're planning on expanding to the world!
