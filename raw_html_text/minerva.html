Minerva

Minerva is a Daily Journal. But it is also a social network. With Minerva you write about your day-to-day activities, and we will connect you with people that are going through the same problems you are. We do this by using Natural Language processing to summarize what you have written, and then use Sentiment Analysis to gauge your mood. When you are connected with someone, you can talk about your life and hopefully learn together and become better people.
