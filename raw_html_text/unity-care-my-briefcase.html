Inspiration

Current and past foster youth have following issues:

  • Do not always have a permanent location/home
  • Lack of immediate access to critical documents
  • County backlogged - Approximately 6 months wait time for records. Could be longer if county records are needed.
  • Can miss opportunities for required documents such as school enrollment, employment, housing, etc.
