Inspiration

First attempt at creating a VR project at my first game jam on a mobile device I hadnt previously used

The theme was "You've already won"

What it does

It has the player in the position of someone receiving a letter "You have won... To claim your prize..."

If the user completes the steps and sends the letter back they will be sent another letter... and another...

They do have the option to walk away by binning the letter and getting on with their life.

How I built it

Built in unity in a 40 hr game jam

Challenges I ran into

Deploying the project to windows phone took some time. Creating a VR project was easier than expected, but still a challenge

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Actually having a working game to show.. that fits the theme

What I learned

Lots about deploying to windows phone and creating a VR project
