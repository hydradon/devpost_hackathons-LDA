Inspiration

CS industry is very difficult to navigate and there is too much information to know, especially, for newbies . Using Memory Palace method & WebVR , we are making it easy, accessible and fun to navigate. We, as human-beings, tend to explore new things and remember information better by interacting. So VoadMap's WebVR is the best platform for it.

What it does

It is a website that you can easily go either on your smart phones or laptops. You will be brought into an immersive interactive VR world, where you can walk and look around to get information for software-engineering industry. At some points, there will also be narration with audio to make it more interactive and redirection to other online resources.

How we built it

Using, A-Frame(WebVR Framework), HTML & Javascript

Challenges we ran into

Solving UI/UX problems & getting the right data

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We learned and had fun a lot!
