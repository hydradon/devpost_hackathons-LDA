• PollMe

Inspiration

Conventional TV and online forums and polling mechanisms are both less interactive and inconvenient. With this application we aim to create a rich experience for the new age audience who like to stay connected and voice their opinions real time.

What it does

It provides an interactive service that brings together real time polling and opinions on one platform. It is a one stop application for both content providers and the audience. Content providers can stream real time questions and PollMe can bring its audience to the channels they wish to subscribe to with just a mere scan of an image!

How we built it

We built it using android studio, firebase and TensorFlow for image classification.

Challenges we ran into

Building the image detection modeling, data structure planning and real time poll.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We overcame all challenges we faced and implemented the functionalities we intended to design.

What we learned

Scalability issues, architecture design, real time database

What's next for PollMe

Scaling and creation of forums and marketable deployment
