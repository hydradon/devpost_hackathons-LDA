Inspiration

To try out Alexa APL

What it does

Ask for cricket facts, and learn about amazing things you did't know about your favourite game.

To start, just say "Alexa, launch cricket facts" or "Alexa, open cricket facts" to get a fact".

At anytime, you can stop by saying "Alexa, stop"
