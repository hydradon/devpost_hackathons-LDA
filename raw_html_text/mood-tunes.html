Inspiration

We decided to create Mood Tunes because we wanted to share our love with music and help ease daily stresses.

What it does

When you call the phone number, Mood Tunes will play a different song and message based on your indicated mood (on a scale of 1-5).

How we built it

We used Twilio, python, and flask. For our website, we used HTML,CSS, and javascript.

Challenges we ran into

It was our first time using Twilio and we went through many trial and errors.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We're really happy that the calling feature works!

What we learned

We learned that we're capable of building something awesome with our limited skillset.

What's next for Mood Tunes

We hope to create more features for calling, such as more songs or voice recognition.
