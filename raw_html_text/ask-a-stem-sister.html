Inspiration

What inspired us is that there aren't many women in stem majors, and our mission is to connect and support STEM women in their acknowledgement of personal and professional atmospheres for women in STEM fields at various companies and university departments.

What it does

People can navigate to our universities or companies tab to write a review on their personal or professional journey with those organizations. Individuals should feel free to include their overall satisfaction, perceived gender equality, and whether they would recommend their experience to other women

How we built it

We used php, javascript, css, and html

Challenges we ran into

Some people on our team never coded before, and it was difficult. We persevered through and helped each other and collaborated with other members who have had more experience.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of ourselves for doing something we've never done before and getting out of our comfort zone.

What we learned

We learned to work together in a team and strengthen our leadership skills. We never met each other before, and we made new networks. It was awesome working together!

What's next for ASK A STEM SISTER

In the future, we might make a start up software company for this social media.
