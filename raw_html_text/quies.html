Inspiration - Our inspiration starts with ourselves, being high school students and struggling to sleep adequately. We wanted to help all people that face this extremely common problem that affects our productivity.

What it does - Quies is built to calculate your sleep cycle length to help you find the perfect time to go to sleep and wake up, to ensure a more restful sleep in a shorter amount of time.

How we built it - We built it with Justinmind (a prototyping software)

Challenges we ran into - Figuring out how to code Xcode in such a short amount of time.

Accomplishments that we're proud of - Our presentation is really well executed, and the benefits that our project provides caters to a wide audience.

What we learned - We learned how to use to use a prototyping software and trying to execute preliminary Xcode.

What's next for Quies - We want to provide our users with a higher availability of pairing devices, connections with medical professionals, and more in depth nutritional analysis.
