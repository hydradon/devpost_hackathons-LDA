Inspiration

The disguised happiness of being independent and self serving often departs adult individuals away from their loved ones which leads to the cases of mental difficulty. According to the statistics, one in five adults today are facing some kind of hardships. Any opportunity to serve our community would be the most delighting thing for us to do.

What it does

It is a friend which detects your mood through your expressions, verbal and written communication. Provides you personalized music, videos and suggests you any event and activity happening in your surrounding that might soothe you. It is a friend which inspired you to be a friend to those facing difficulties and also seek help ourself. It is your caretaker that would signal your loved ones in case of extreme situations.

How we built it

We used Django to build the basic framework for the web application along with Bootstrap, HTMLand CSS for responsive UI. Google Cloud API is used for detecting the locations of your mentors nearby.
