Inspiration

Have you ever been out with friends or colleagues, and no one can decide where to eat ? You go to Yelp and get lost among the tons of choices!

What it does

PickMe quickly and smartly gives you one option: the best option and book it for you .

How we built it

PickMe is built on the Facebook Messenger API .

Challenges we ran into

Understanding the user's messages and providing robust dialogue capabilities.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The AI is able to detect the user's need and pick the best option to respond to it. The interface is also sleek and very user-friendly.

What we learned

It's important to focus on the core of your product and keep things simple , especially on short time frames.

What's next for PickMe

More sophisticated language models. More personalization, and use of user profiles.
