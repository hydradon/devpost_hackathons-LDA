Inspiration

Every person has extra/spare belonging which they don't use. We want to encourage them to easily donate them to needy & deserving people with the help of technology.

What it does

Donor posts their spare belongings, we collect them and give them to local organizations, who have access to needy & poor people. We keep tracking the donation and let the donor know about it.

How we built it

We built an android prototype app over this weekend, which works exactly as we aim. We have built APIs & server based database using node and MongoDB, respectively.

Challenges we ran into

How to collect donations from donors.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

E Kindness is based on how to help poor people instead of how we will make money out of it. We made the prototype over this weekend.

What we learned

We learnt about idea validation, mostly.

What's next for E Kindness

  1. Partner with other organizations who work in the area and have access to needy people.
  2. Allow needy users to make their wishlist and nearby donors can fulfil those wishes.
  3. Gather volunteers
