Inspiration

As students ourselves, we believe that the best way to succeed is to help each other out. Our goal was to build a website that includes every single pieces of information a student would look for in a course. Usually, you would search individually a course description, outline, rate my teachers, exams, the list goes on and on. With our website, we merged all potential resources to your advantage.

What it does

Simply get in our website and start searching for a class you need to find out more information about.

How we built it

We build it using Concordia's new Open Data API. This enhanced our effectiveness into gathering information such as course ID & course outlines.

Challenges we ran into

Upon posting our website live, we ran into server problems & php connection.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

In our first hackathon, we were able to all share the work load equally and help each other in order to understand & achieve our goal.

What we learned

We learned that team work truly is valuable when it comes to code. As we all are becoming Software Engineer's, this was an experience we can look back on.

What's next for ConUPlugs.com

Expansion, promotion, currently we only support ENGR, COMP & SOEN classes but we want to expand to JMSB and optimize our website.
