Skill Description

The Last Coin is a kid-friendly strategy game based on the mathematical game, Nim. Take turns against Alexa to grab coins from the jar. If you grab the last coin, you win!

Try and beat Alexa on both easy and hard difficulty!

Play now by saying, "Alexa, let's play the coin game".
