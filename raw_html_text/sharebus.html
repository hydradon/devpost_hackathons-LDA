The existing public transport network, as a rigid structure, is unable to satisfy the constant demand of a dynamic society. In this framework we can not provide a rigide solution to a dynamic question. We propose a flexible public system that works as a complement to the existent public net unifing the paths that currently are overlapping them-self in individual private vehicules. This is a demand-on service that offer a door to door journey and complement the actual transport network in Barcelona. This service is available in the Barcelona Metropolitan Transport APP and eliminate the disadvanteges of the old on-demand services due to the restrictions that we apply when we are going to book the travel. This restrictions are:

  • A minimum of a 2 kilometers journey
  • A minumun of 15 minutes of improvement respect to the basic transport network
