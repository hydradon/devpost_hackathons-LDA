Inspiration

Studies have shown that writing down three positive things about your day can improve your outlook on life. We wanted to build something beautiful around this idea, and help people struggling with depression and mental health issues.

What it does

Grateful encourages users to write down three things that they're grateful for every day. Users can also tag friends in their submissions – we'll text them to let them know that someone was grateful for them today.
