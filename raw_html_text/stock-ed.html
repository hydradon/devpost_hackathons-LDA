What it does

Stock-Ed is an app that is compatible with mobile devices that takes in your risk tolerance, time frame, price range, and desired industry, and it returns stocks that match your request. After that, you can click on different stocks and headers to view details on the stock, and teaches you about stock.

How we built it

We built Stock-Ed with UI software such as InVision and Sketch to create an informative prototype showcasing all of our product's functions
