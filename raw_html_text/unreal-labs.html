Inspiration

We say we carry our clients to the next level, provide them the the best possible tech. Still, we present ourselves in 2D: Texts, forms, pictures... We would like to bring our projects, teams and offices to 3D.

What it does

We present our office space and along with it, the actual projects and tech stacks in web with HTML5 format. So, we can provide our community, potential team members, customers and clients a 3D walk-through in our offices.

How we built it

A good plan, task management and Unreal Engine.

Challenges we ran into

Cutting the MVP, not enough CPU, long build times and demanding materials and lighting in Unreal Engine.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Great team work and problem solving.

What we learned

Light and material limitations with WebGL, importance of reflection cubes to improve rendering performance.

What's next for Unreal Labs

Full building with rooms and team members.
