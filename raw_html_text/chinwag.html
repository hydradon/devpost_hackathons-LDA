CHINWAG

What started as a tool that would put Albert Einstein, Muhammad Ali and ourselves in a group chat pivoted to a Sales Development Representative tool where companies can compete for customers dollars in a group chat.

PITCH

Chinwag is a bot that chats with companies to find the best deal for customers. We are providing high quality leads which will save companies much money and increase conversion rates. It will also save consumers so much time.
