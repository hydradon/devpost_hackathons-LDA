Inspiration

People have difficulties to spare time & visit a super market for buying groceries. Enabling technology like voice assist in E-commerce can make people lives easier.

What it does

Order groceries with the help of Google Assistant.

How we built it

With the help of Google Assistant and Dialogflow.

Challenges we ran into

Lack of experience with Google Assistant App development.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Providing a solution to a real life problem related to working class and students of Ingolstadt . Bringing an idea to life in 48 hours.

What we learned

Development of an App for Google Assistant. Able to build a prototype from an idea.

What's next for GroceriesOnTheGo

Collaboration with other super markets. Extending the grocery list for family members or roommates . Providing a better choice like value for money to customers by using AI . Integrating with other Google products like Google lens (training the agent) and with Google pay (financial transaction)
