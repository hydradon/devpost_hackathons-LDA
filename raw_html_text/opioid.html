Inspiration

Our inspiration was from our local community and those around us which struggles with the opioid epidemic on a daily basis.

What it does

"Platforming with a problem" is designed to teach kids about the dangers of opioids and the spiral it can lead to whilest constantly being engaging.

How I built it

The game was created in gamemaker2.

Challenges I ran into

Free version of game-maker 2 only allows you to create 15 objects.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Creating a working game. Dealing with the barrier of 15 objects. Solving irritating bugs.

What I learned

Game-maker 2. Object based programming.

What's next for opioid

Fixing moving platform clipping. Having more than 15 objects to expand the story.
