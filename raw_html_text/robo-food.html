Inspiration

A competitive multiplayer platforming game, with a relatable and easy-to-understand concept. The stress of fast food never gets easier- the only thing for it is to take out the competition!

What it does

A simple platforming game Player 1: A-D = Left-Right, C = grab, Space: jump Player2: Left-Right = Left-Right, K = grab, Up = jump

Bugs/Readme

If for any reason you are unable to grab the item, walk away from it, and: Player1: Press E Player2: Press L Then try again.
