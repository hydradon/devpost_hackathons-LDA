Pricify

This application designed on OutSystems using google cloud, google spreadsheets, and python is designed to take subreddit titles for sale based subreddits. It builds them into a spreadsheet to be downloaded onto the app and display the unique identifiers of said subreddit titles such as buildapcsales and display key information in identifying the item on sale, the type of item on sale, the new price as well as providing the link attached in order to give direct link access to the product page for said item in a browser.
