Inspiration

The 45th President and his love for Twitter.

What it does

Using Markov Chains and ALL of Donald Trump's Tweets as input. We generate new tweets that are similar to what Trump would actually say.

How we built it

We made our backend using NodeJS and express, and HTML/CSS/Jquery for the frontend.

Challenges we ran into

Designing the front-end of the application took longer than expected.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Taking some NodeJS libraries that none of us have used before and implementing them without much issue.

What we learned

We went into this project with no knowledge of Markov Chains, and now we have the capability to implement them.

What's next for MC Donald

We plan to take what we learned about generating Markov Chains and apply it to more complex data, such as banking.
