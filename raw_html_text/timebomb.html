What is Timebomb?

As students and hackathon organizers ourselves, we were looking for a way to keep track of upcoming dates and deadlines. Designed as a browser extension, our countdown page will appear every time the user opens a new tab. Users will be able to create cards that count down to important events in their lives.

What's next for Timebomb?

Tags will allow users to organize their countdowns by categories. All countdowns with a specific tag will have the same colored card, helping the user to differentiate all the upcoming dates in their busy lives.
