Inspiration

We wanted to expand our knowledge of JavaScript by pushing ourselves to our limits and collaborating with each other.

What it does

It's a web hosted game made using mainly JavaScript. You make the main character jump over enemies and work to get the highest score.

How we built it

We divided tasks and tried to help each other with anything that we had trouble with. It was made in JavaScript.

Challenges we ran into

Lack of JavaScript knowledge.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The incredible amount of experience and knowledge learned.

What we learned

How to work with collisions and rendering graphics. We also learned how to implement music into a game.

What's next for Minion Jump

Maybe a crouch function.... or World Domination, really just depends on how we're feeling.
