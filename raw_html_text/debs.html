Inspiration

An effective course of action always begins with information. To support greater situational awareness of small unmanned aerial systems (sUAS) threats, we are creating the Drone Emergency Broadcast System (DEBS). DEBS will provide public notifications whenever dangerous drones are detected. The intent is for DEBS to become open source intelligence that aids both DoD and civilian communities in detecting and responding to drone threats.
