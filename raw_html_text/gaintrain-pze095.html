GainTrain

All aboard the Gain Train

Here's the deal, I've used a lot of workout apps in the past that allow you to track what you do, but none of them were quite perfect. I figured "Hey, I'm a CS major at GaTech, might as well make my own!" And thus the gain train was born.

The ideas were simple: do what all the workout apps do well, but add in every feature that I've wanted, such as:

  • Linking a spotify playlist to a workout
  • Competition with friends!
  • "Streak" of consecutive days worked out
  • Custom reminder of how hard the last workout was
  • Ability to challenge a friend to a "competition"

But as all plans go, this was too ambitious and this did not get anywhere close to finished. Firebase was a confusing beast that took too long to figure out. Maybe next time!

This submission is very unfinished, but I thought it'd be fun to attempt it, so I'm submitting it just for fun!
