Inspiration

Feel me was inspired by the fact that I had hard time finding places to play and communities to connect with that had a similar feel but different "genre." I, as a dj and producer, pride on being able to transcend generes and work in feels when I play shows. I needed an app to help me transcend those boundaries through visuals and branding.

What it does

The idea is to analyze artists personas and online profiles to select various "vibes" in their visuals, lyrics and music in order to help place themselves in communities and events.

How I built it

HTML, javascript, ajax, cloudinary, tivo jquery
