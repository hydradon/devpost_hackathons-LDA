The Story:

I've been obsessed with productivity and self-improvement for a long time. Being able to build a chatbot that actually tries to ask you sensible questions about your workflow has long been a dream of mine. I told myself I'd show up to HS Hacks, learn JS, and finally build the damn thing.

And I did!

Productivity Bots is designed to be a collection of different "bots" that anthropomorphize the actual planning and productivity techniques we use.

Currently, there is Plan-Bot, who walks us through a planning scenario where things go wrong, as well as a half-finished Pomo-Bot, who just sits still and sets a 25 minute timer.
