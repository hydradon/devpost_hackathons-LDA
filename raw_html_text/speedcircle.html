Inspiration

The NeoPixel LED Ring caught our eye and we wanted to make something cool with it.

What it does

Randomly displays a group of three LEDs on the NeoPixel Ring. The user must move the analog stick connected to the Arduino to control a pixel of their own on the ring. If the user is able to hit the group of LEDs with their own pixel within the limited timeframe, they earn a point for that level. There are multiple "attacks" per level and five levels that become increasingly faster as the game progresses.

How we built it

Developed on an Arduino Uno with an Analog Joystick and NeoPixel Ring with the assistance of the Adafruit NeoPixel Library at https://github.com/adafruit/Adafruit_NeoPixel

Challenges we ran into

Since the hardware was borrowed, we were unable to solder wires onto them, causing some loose connections. However, our device still ran smoothly.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Tracking the analog stick on the LED ring and finishing the project on time

What we learned

Prior to this hackathon, we were very unexperienced with Arduino. Now we feel much more confident with the environment
