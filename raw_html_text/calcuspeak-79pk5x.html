Background

Imagine having to solve a complex mathematical problem without the aid of a calculator. Nearly 10% of Americans have some form of visual impairment, and they must live with this reality.

Solution

CalcuSpeak is a calculator that allows people with visual impairments to interact with the world of Mathematics.

Usage

Download the repository and use Flask to run locally and say a mathematical equation. In a few seconds, you should receive a spoken response.
