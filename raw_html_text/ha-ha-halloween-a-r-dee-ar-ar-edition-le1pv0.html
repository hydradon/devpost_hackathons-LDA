A digital sign delivers a stream of halloween jokes using the Arduino and Neopixel blocks and point your mobile phone at the scrolling text. Using Wikitude image recognition and Unity the app recognizes the joke text and delivers an animated graphic punchline in Augmented Reality. A physical button generates new jokes on the digital signage.
