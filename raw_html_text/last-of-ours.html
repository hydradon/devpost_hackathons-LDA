A digital mirror of the world’s endangered animals

The non-fungible Living Animal Social Tokens (LAST) on our blockchain is a digital mirror of the latest population estimates of real living animals, one to one.

ADOPT THREATENED SPECIES Purchase magic eggs that hatch wild animals or visit the sanctuary to adopt your favorite species.

BECOME A GUARDIAN Raise awareness and contribute to organizations that are helping to restore the balance.

EARN TOKENS Earn newborn animals as their population grows out of extinction.
