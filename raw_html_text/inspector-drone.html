Inspiration

Safety is first. Mining is a very dangerous working environment. Nobody wants to work there and if an emergency or accident happens, may occur casualties or fatal injuries. We want to increase and improve safety inspections and first responses, make them human-less. So we do not put other human in danger and rescue teams can asset the situation earlier.

What it does

First response drone

How we built it

Fast heavy lifting drone (teal drone) + Smart glasses (Epson 400 BT)

Challenges we ran into

Autonomy of the drone, weight lifting drone

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Connect the smart glasses + drone and the remaining hardware

What we learned

Drone technology, smart glasses technology, safety regulation in mines

What's next for Inspector Drone

Implement the project plan and buy the materials to build a working prototype
