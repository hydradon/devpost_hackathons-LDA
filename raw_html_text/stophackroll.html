TechRoot

TechRoot is a web-based platform that allows targeted users – Students – to display their technology projects created for funding. This platform is only used by students to fund technological projects for both hardware and software they have worked on during hackathon or even in school.

Through this platform, students can

  • Fund their projects and ideas to the public
  • Sell their funded and made projects to the public

TechRoot will only be available in Singapore for students studying here to put their projects on the platform. It is available to the public as well to fund their projects.
