For this project, we sponsored by Kinship Pets. Kinship Pets provided their APIs to our team to work to develop a better & improved dog care services. We used the following APIs: food nutrition, dog parks & clinic location. While we want to take best care of our dogs without limited data during diagnosis and avoid the hassle on dog health details.

Our team came with the idea of a virtual vet app for personalized dog care by using enhanced vet diagnostic with weekly data and analysis. By giving the right care at the right time, our platform would alert pet owners when to bring their pet(s) to a vet based on current conditions while giving a one click search to vets.
