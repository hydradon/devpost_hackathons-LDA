Inspiration

We wanted to engage stream viewers by letting everyone own a virtual pet that follows them around from channel-to-channel, to fight the streamer's pet.

What it does

You can team up with other viewers to play a clicker game to kill your streamer's pet. Every time the streamer's pet dies, it is replaced with another higher-level pet to fight. Also, when a user subscribes to a channel, the pet levels up.
