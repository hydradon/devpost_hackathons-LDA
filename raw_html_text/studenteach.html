Inspiration

That moment when we are as a student are doing the home in the library or nearby and stuck on a question and we don't know what to do, and I cannot advance further in the homework exercises and the friends forum is no help, then why not teamup with some one who can help you and you can help him?

What it does

As Knowledge Seeker - Find a Teacher/Student who is willing the answer your question or give you a class in certain subject at the same moment by nearby location and face-to-face learning. as Teacher, they gain broad access to student who are maybe willing to pay for private classes and maybe can be helped by other students strengthen their weaker subjects.

How I built it

With some advices from the mentors, with MIT - APP Inventor and Firebase NoSQL DB + authentication

Challenges I ran into

no knowledge with NoSQL or firebase, nor android. problems with how to build the data in the DB and how to access/modify.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We learned new stuff! we are first year students for C.S it was fun to experience developing in first hand.

What I learned

We learnt how to use DB's, especially firebase's NoSQL's Json's Like trees.

What's next for StudenTeach

We hope to achieve Country-Wide recognition with Student Association across all campuses in Israel. or maybe even worldwide.
