Inspiration

  • Helping students be more prepared and active for doing assignments and managing their time.

What it does

  • Regularly takes the important information regarding assignments (due dates, assignment number) and sends it to the Google Calendar of the user.

How we built it

  • Python, OCR , Linguistics Analysis , webkit2png, Google Services

Challenges we ran into

  • Too many specific corner cases to account for. Difficulty with machine learning.

What we learned

  • How to use APIs and combine different files.
