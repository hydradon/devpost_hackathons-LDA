Challenges I ran into

As my first Hackathon, it was quite difficult to work in a group and try to split up the project into parts that were of equal value. Furthermore, we had never made an app on Android Studio before or had any experience in such a field, so the coding was quite difficult in learning how to do certain actions and approach what we wanted to do.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am proud of the fact that we were able to come as a team and finish our project. Although we lost hours of sleep and would have been happier to gain rest, we were able to divide the project into equal parts and learn some new material that hopefully we will carry on.
