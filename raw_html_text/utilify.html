In an effort to make tracking utilities more simplified and accessible, UtiliFi aims to allow a user to monitor and record the real-time usage of water, gas, and electricity in their home through the usage of IoT-enabled sensors, while also creating a projection of their upcoming bill.

Our future goals with this project are to allow users to become more ecologically-conscious of their utility usage by comparing it with regional and national usage averages for each category or utility and alarming the user for unusual usage cases (i.e. suddenly using 30L of water at 04:00, gas usage over an extended period of time).
