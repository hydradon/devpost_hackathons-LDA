Inspiration

Many students in New York do not have access to the resources to be able to travel across the country to visit a college in California. Our goal was to allow high school students to visit a college without needing to travel and without the extreme filtering that colleges place on their advertisements.

What it does

College students can upload pictures and emails of their college to their college's page on our website. From there, high school students can then view those videos to truly see inside of the classroom and college life atmosphere.

Challenges we ran into

There were many bugs that we ran into which we solved by being persistent.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of working through the night to figure out the bugs We are proud of figuring out file uploads to our website

Market to college students to be proud to show off their college on such a website Expand the UI to different accounts to have their own profile Users to store their colleges of interest Figure out a way to monitor to ensure that videos not safe for underage students are not posted.
