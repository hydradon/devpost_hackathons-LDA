Inspiration

What does a "like" mean in social media anymore? Has it become something less meaningful? We wanted to bring more human emotion to the "like" button and to emojis.

What it does

Using your laptop's webcam, we capture your reaction from an Instagram post on your newsfeed as either positive or negative and like posts on your feed accordingly!

How we built it

We used the Indico.io and Instagram APIs, along with Python, Flask, Webcam.js, Javascript, HTML, CSS.

Challenges we ran into

Too many to name.

What's next for Smile to Like

Deployment and possibly an Android and/or iOS app!
