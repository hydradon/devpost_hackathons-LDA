Inspiration: Air flight service when receiving food and drink can oftentimes come with many pain points. The amount of time taken by the flight attendant to manually cater to each passenger, the safety hazard in blocking the aisle, and the quality of distribution are just three of many current issues.## Inspiration

What it does: While seated, the passenger places an order off of a kiosk positioned on the headrest in front of them. After placing the order, it is sent to the prep station manned by the flight attendant. The flight attendant prepares the passenger's order and once completed, the order is placed onto the conveyor belt system. The conveyor belt delivers the passenger's items to their seat, and this is all done without the need for anyone to physically move across the plane

How we built it: 3D Modeling, 3D Printing, UI Prototyping
