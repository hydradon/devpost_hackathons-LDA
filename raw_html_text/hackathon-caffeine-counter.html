Inspiration

A last minute effort to preserve an idea that was originally for another medium--it is a mobile-friendly website that will measure your caffeine input and let you know when to stop.

What it does

The user selects a caffeinated beverage that was consumed (assuming it is one serving/one can), and the website will keep track of how much caffeine was consumed
