Max My Body is a product that will fundamentally change the way that parents athletes and coaches look at nutritional education. The possibilities are there for the future of nutritional education, but the for some reason the majority of the apps created only look at the "adult" experience for nutrition.

What about athletes that have a "built in" compliance, the athletes and parents that are fighting and playing for pride, honor and a possible opportunity to play sports at the next level of athletics, when are we going to listen to the needs of this segment?

Well MAX is doing this, now!

Our company will speak directly to the pre high school/ high school and post high school athletic experience, creating an environment that is rich in content and resources.

Parents of the athletes can now communicate with a sports dietitian or nutritionist before the start of a season, during the season and most importantly after the season. Creating results and habits that should form a lifetime of nutritional education.

How it works - Subscription based model

Bronze level - free teaser level MAX clients will sign in to the app and create a profile, once created the profile is active and the client can download generic nutritional templates from the MAX educational library.

Silver level - 1st paid level MAX clients with an active profile can now schedule a client nutrition session with a sports dietitian for "Meal Planning" optimization.

Gold Level - 2nd paid level MAX clients with an active profile can use the "real time" meal planning function by incorporating wearable devices during their athletic activity in order to record individual caloric burn rates. The caloric burn is important because the meal planning data base will "pull" food items into the player profile based on the total caloric burn and the nutritionist guidelines for meal planning.
