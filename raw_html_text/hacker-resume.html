Inspiration

As programmers we wanted to summarize our online presence as developers. We think it can improve one's value in the eyes of her future employer.

What it does

Hacker resume is automatic generation of developer's resume based on their online activity to showcase their skills. Hacker resume connects to GitHub, StackOverflow and Wakatime. It combines data from them and present it in simple way.

How I built it

We chose Python and Flask for their simplicity
