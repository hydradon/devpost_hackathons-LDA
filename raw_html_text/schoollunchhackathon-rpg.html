Web forms can be confusing.

To overcome that, I created a method to submit the form where the parent is guided by a mentor the entire way, in the medium of an RPG world.

Step by step the parent is led through the application process, visually seeing the application filled out before their very eyes with the spawning of child and adult avatars and a progression of mentors.

A clear, retro, and intuitive interface that the current Gameboy-era parenting generation is sure to love and understand!

All applications are saved into a postgres database with an admin panel allowing admins to browse and download individual forms. They are also available via the standard Django admin panel in a JSON format.
