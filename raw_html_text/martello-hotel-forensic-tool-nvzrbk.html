Inspiration

Our team wanted to build a tool to solve a real-world problem. Our goals were to develop a tool with ease of use.

What it does

Our app takes in a Json file that holds data of where users had moved in the Richcraft Hotel. By clicking icons on the hotel map, data is displayed in chronological order. The data can then be used to identify who may be the culprit of crimes.

How I built it

Our Application was built using C# as a front end and C#, ASP.NET core as the backend.

Challenges I ran into

Our main challenges were getting our application to transition smoothly

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We are proud of our app's ease of use, decent UI and visuals. We wanted to make our app's layout with an intuitive design so anyone could use it.

What I learned

We learned how to be Full stack developers, using C# and ASP.Net

What's next for Martello-Hotel-Forensic-Tool

We want to use our knowledge with UI design and continue to leverage our skills.
