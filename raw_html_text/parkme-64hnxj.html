Inspiration

Airbnb's easy-to-use service and the need for simple parking

What it does

ParkMe enables business owners and home owners to sell their empty/unused parking spaces. Drivers looking for a convenient way to park can reserve a parking spot through our application and pay directly online.

How we built it

We used Android-Studio to build our app. We used the Google Maps API to display a map of available parking spots where the customer needs it and Firebase for storing ParkMe hosts' and ParkMe customers' data.

Challenges we ran into

We were unable to finish the application because it was a bigger project than we expected. We had trouble with using the Google Maps Geocode API and figuring out Android Studio

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We made the skeleton of our envisioned app

What we learned

Set easier goals for hackathons

What's next for ParkMe

Finishing the entire application and turning it into a real business
