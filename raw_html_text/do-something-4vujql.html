What it does

Do Something gives you the ability to find something to do to make a real word impact in improving the world and its people. You will be prompted with a task you can perform to bring in change and you can choose to do it or ask to get a new task.

It is powered by DoSomething.org API so that the user will always have something to do.

What's next for Do Something

Additional sources of tasks can be integrated into the skill.
