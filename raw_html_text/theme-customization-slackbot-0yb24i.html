Inspiration

We wanted to create a simple way for people to customize their Slack platform theme with the colors of their company/organization logo.

What it does

This slackbot automatically generates the main Hex color codes of any image. Call the slack bot, paste in the url of your image, and press enter. The Hex color codes for the main colors of your image will appear along with a "Switch sidebar theme button". Simply press the button and your customized theme will appear!

This can be used by companies, student organizations, and anyone who uses Slack.

How We built it

We used the Slack and ColorTag APIs and Node.js
