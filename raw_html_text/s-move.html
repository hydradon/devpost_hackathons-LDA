Inspiration

Finding interesting things while traveling the world can sometimes be very hard. An app which allways shows you what you can do and visit at the places where you are, would be realy great.

What it does

s-move findes places & events nearby you and based on your interests.

How we built it

We build s-move including Google Maps to get access to all the places all over the globe. Aditionally we create our own webcrawler who finds events on the internet which we also include in our app. Finaly we add the users interests which can be added by the user himself, or can be retrieved through connections to social networks like facebook.

Challenges we ran into

  • Implementing apps with frameworks which are still in beta.
  • Never done webcrawling before.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Writing a prototype app with an technologie we never used before.

What we learned

  • Programming in dart.
  • Using the google API

What's next for s-move

Partnerships with ticket websites. Providing travel routes based on your intressts and timeframe.
