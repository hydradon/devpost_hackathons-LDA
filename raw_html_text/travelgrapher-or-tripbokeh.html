TripBokeh. "Uber for Local Travel Guides"

We want to help travellers to have wonderful captured moments by letting our travelgraphers do the travel documenting (pictures, videos, groupfie, selfie, wefie) for them. Travellers are social people, they take photos and videos and immediately upload to social media without considering the quality of the images or videos they share. With professional videographers/photographers as tour guides, they can capture the perfect moment and with great aesthetics.

_"bokeh" is all about focus an aesthetic quality in photorgraphy. _
