Inspiration

Motus helps you enact the change you want to see in the world. Everyone on our team felt that many times, it's hard to find others passionate about making the same changes you want to see. That's why we created Motus. We want everyone to have the voice and the opportunity to go out and make an impact in their community.

What it does

Motus helps everyone get together to shake up our world. Whether that means attending an event, joining a movement or signing a petition - you have the ability to see what is going on in the world around you and easily find opportunities you are interested in joining. Not only can you see ways that you can positively impact our world, but you can create your own movements and see them grow in support and importance!

What's next for Motus

Getting more individualized feedback and results for each user so they can see what's going on immediately around them! This would involve showing events nearby and figuring out what opportunities are relevant to the user based on what events they follow and the movements they join.
