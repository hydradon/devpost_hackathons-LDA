Inspiration

Some rural area doesn't have proper supply of energy. We want to accumulate electric usage data for the producer to predict that they should invest on more energy here in this local area, or they can stay with what they are doing.

What it does

It accumulates the data from user. It give score to the user on how are they using electricty. It cooperates data together and summerizes to the producer party.

How we built it

We will it using elm and webpack. And we will use elixir for the backend.

Challenges we ran into

The challenges was to find what information we shall gather from users. We want to gather as less and as valuable information as possible.

What's next for eHand

We need to find what we need to develop more to incentize it.
