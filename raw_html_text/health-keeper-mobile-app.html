Inspiration

In today's busy life, people often forget to take their daily medications and set appointments for health checkups. According to a report, 50% of patients do not take medicines as prescribed and 44 out of 100 prescriptions are never picked up at the pharmacy.

The app helps in addressing these concerns. Like other medicare applications, the app lets you set reminders for daily medications and daily exercise. However, unlike other applications, it also lets you scan your medical records thereby reducing the need to maintain physical records. It also lets you share your fitness achievements. This acts as a motivating factor for your friends and family and keeps you motivated too.

What it does

● Medications Management ● Activity Tracker ● Sharing achievements ● Set reminders and appointments ● Scan medical records

Here's why everyone should use it: ● Simplicity: The app is simple to use ● Health app integrated: Keeps track of your measurements and syncs them to your Fitbit, apple watch, etc ● Family interaction: Share your fitness achievements with your close and dear ones ● Reminders: Set reminders and appointments that are color-coded for ease of use.

How I built it

The prototype was designed on Figma and XD

Challenges I ran into

There are many medicare applications that exist today. So the team brainstormed on new features that can make the app interesting to use and that motivates users to record their daily fitness habits.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of
