Inspiration: waking up in the morning too exhausted to even spread the toothpaste on our toothbrush.

What it does: Squeeze toothpaste out.

Why it's important: Imagine any commercial with a big glob of toothpaste. That is way too much tooth paste! A dentist recommends a pea-sized drop to brush your teeth. Save money on toothpaste. And it's more sanitary too!

How we built it: SnappyXO kit.
