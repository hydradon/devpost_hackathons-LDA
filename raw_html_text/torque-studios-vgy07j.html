The Game

Polarity is a basic feature of the universe, for every action there is an opposite. Good and bad, up and down, positive and negative. Every decision you make decides your future and you literally shape your own path. Polarity is a game where you create your path, switching between both the positive and the negative and effecting your journey. No action is bad.

Controls

Mobile Touch and hold to jump Swipe up or down to pole shift

Desktop Press and hold spacebar to jump Press the return key to pole shift Esc to quit
