Smart Elevator Control will observe a people flow in a large office building and automatically call elevators to the most frequented floors. Also statistics can be viewed, a graphical overview of the current elevators can be shown, emergency controls can be triggered and on-demand elevator calls can be made. If a couple of employees leave for example a meeting and walk towards the elevator, this will be detected before they arrive at the elevator and an elevator will be called in order to save them time. Also the administration of the building will get interesting insight about the elevators and people moving by from the application. In case of emergency, they can also manually control the elevators
