Inspiration

Being college students away from home and wanting something more than cafeteria foods.

What it does

Homcook brings people who want a home-cooked meal and people who cook extra portions together. Whether it be a pick up, delivery, or a dine in, Homcook will help you set it up.

How we built it

We built the app on android studio and used Firebase to support the backend.
