Inspiration

Drawing is fun. So I wanted to make an art competition app.

What it does

We send you a noun to draw

You and your competition have 30 seconds to draw that noun ... __ on your phone! __

We send your masterpieces off to Google Vision Services.

If those fine algorithms can make heads or tails of what you drew, we give you a few points

Highest combined score after 7 rounds is the winner!

How we built it

Python and C#. You know, I was at the foo bar last night, and some guy called C# dull. That may be true, but it still hurt my feelings a little bit. Anyway, we also used AWS EC2 instances to host our servers. AWS is pretty cool.
