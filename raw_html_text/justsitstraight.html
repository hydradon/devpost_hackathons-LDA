Inspiration

Most of us spend a great deal of our day in front of a computer screen. In the long term, being too close to the screen causes eyesight problems. Leaning towards the screen or to the sides can cause back problems and deformation, so why not avoid this?

What it does

Our app is making sure that you are sitting straight and still. Simple as that, yet powerful and you will thank us later.

How we built it

Python, OpenCV, sweat and perhaps some back problems from all the testing.

What's next for justSitStraight

We want to make a standalone, more user-calibrated app from this! Currently this has dependencies and needs terminal to be set up. Also there is much more regarding custom notifications or we could be tracking more subtle movements.
