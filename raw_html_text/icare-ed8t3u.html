Inspiration

Caregivers are people who give their all to provide for someone, while also needing to keep the needs of themselves and their families in mind. This kind of pressure can lead to headaches and other illnesses that could easily be remedied. Our app aims to alleviate some of that pressure

What it does

Provides a one stop shop for caregivers to view everything relevant regarding their patient

How we built it

Using React, local storage, and chart-js

Challenges we ran into

We wanted to use firebase originally, but couldn't figure it out
