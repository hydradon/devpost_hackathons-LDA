Inspiration

We wanted to see the world in a more creative and dynamic viewpoint that enhances creativity for all.

What it does

A holographic rectangular prism is spawned into space. The user utilizes a gesture to launch pink fireworks into the augmented environment, allowing the user to spark their imagination in real life.

How I built it

Effectively utilized tutorials online and our imaginations! (actually used Unity and Visual Studio)

Challenges I ran into

Using new technology and installation. (really though)

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Not wiping out any OS

What I learned

HoloLens is the future

What's next for HoloLensFun

More dynamic objects with complex gestures. Hopefully making people smile (at this).
