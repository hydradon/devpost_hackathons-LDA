Inspiration

Events are more clunky than they have to be. We made events instant and convenient. Think of it as a snapchat for events!

What it does

Sends out instant event invites to friends. Runs on the web, and is accessible to everyone. Instant sharing with open links. Integrates with BrainTree/PayPal to let people pool in money/fund events that are being organised!

How I built it

PHP, Javascript, Coffee and love

Challenges I ran into

Integrating the BrainTree API

Accomplishments that I'm proud of
