Inspiration

Arcade machine.

What it does

The idea of this project is to allow a gripper robot to move not only in 2D, but also in 3D. For this purpose we use 4 ropes attached to the robot that is hanging right beneath the cealing, while the gripper hand hangs from below and can fetch objects.

How I built it

Recicled 'the claw' and added new servos to it. 3D printed new hooks.

Challenges I ran into

Lasercutter was unavailable, 3D printer destroit the first couple of prints, motors didn't act as expected. Oh, and too little time.

What I learned

A lot about the motors and Raspberry Pi
