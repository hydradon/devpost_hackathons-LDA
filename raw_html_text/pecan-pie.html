SwampHack Project 

  Truckers around the world travel far distances with very little technological help. Our technology aims to improve a trucker’s overall work life while driving. We developed a desktop application in Visual Studio C#. The technologies we have used include openCV,Google Directions API,Cascades Trainer, a government database for crash data, multiple cameras, arduino, and Excel. To hear how we implemented all of these technologies into our project, be sure to stop by our table!

Peyton Marinelli, Ahsan Rohan, Julian Berrio, Tristan Jaramillo-Pritchard
