What it does

Creates a blockchain that hands over team management to the team. Reports, Reviews, feedback are essentially done by the team collectively through the Blockchain and Neural Network. Features

  • REGL - DSL REPL the allows you to collect data from the blockchain and store it in your own department specific DB to perform analytics. Also uses Pytorch to do deep learning
  • CockroachDB - For analytics
  • PseudoWhite paper - Outlines what this Blockchain seeks to do and how it does it conceptually ## How I built it built on Ark.io with Python, NodeJS and SQL ## Challenges I ran into Using the Ark.io platform and learning new ML techniques ## Accomplishments that I'm proud of learning more about blockchain ## What I learned
