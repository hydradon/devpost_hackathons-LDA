Inspiration

Create a Web game with ThreeJS and train an AI on it

What it does

Its a game were you puzzel and try to get 6 or more balls connected. The Player can decide to play against an other player or against a bot. You can watch the learning progress of an AI

How we built it

We build it with Angular and ThreeJS, and most importantly with a reinforcement learning JS module based on TensorflowJS.

Challenges we ran into

Time management issues. Use ThreeJS to model a 3D world. Slow Windows Surfaces.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

That we Finish the GAME and built an AI learning on it.

What we learned

Appreciate better time managment

What's next for BlockChain-AI

Integrate Online Multiplayer. And more game logic.
