The Pomodoro technique advocates 25 minutes of work rewarded with 5 minutes of play. The overhead of recording and managing your "sessions" can be drastically reduced. Enter Amazon Echo. Amazon Echo and Alexa, the voice service powering it, is always on. It sits there, waiting, listening, thinking. Coupled with Firebase, it is the perfect tool to collect long term data on your study habits. Furthermore, we have designed a web service to visualize that data to generate more insight.
