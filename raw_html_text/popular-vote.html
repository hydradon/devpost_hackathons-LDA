Inspiration

The USA has historically had terrible rates of voter turnout. We wanted to increase civic engagment with a platform that makes it easy for voters to give their opinion on politcal topics.

What it does

Popular Vote is an app that allows politicians to check the views of their constituents on divisive topics.

Android, Coffee, Javascript
