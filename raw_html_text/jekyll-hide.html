Fixing Digital Copyright Issues

Have you ever felt scared to publish something cool, online ? Somebody can easy copy your work and claim credit , right?

We fix this problem

We use State of the Art Artificial Intelligence (AI) algorithms to hide your copyright information in your Image itself . That way , your copyright will always stay wherever your Image goes.

How it Works

  1. You take your Art/Photo/Work Image
  2. You take in your Copyright Notice
  3. Pass them to Jekyll & Hide
  4. It knows how to 'Hide' the copyright in your image.
  5. Nobody except you knows about its existence in your image
  6. If somebody steals the image , you can ask Jekyll&Hide to Reveal your copyright from the stolen image.
  7. This is proof that that person had stolen your image.
