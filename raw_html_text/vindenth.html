Inspiration 

 Since MacOSX is the only platform without a virtual assistant, we took it as our challenge to create one, or the base of it.

What it does 

 Just like any other assistant, Charlie will be able to open apps, tell you the how is the weather, give you your location, etc, with the use of voice commands.

How we built it 

 We built Charlie using XCode as the engine and Swift as the programming language.

Challenges we ran into 

 The hardest parts were fixing the unexpected crashes and failures during the programming, and giving it a voice.

Accomplishments that we're proud of 

 Our best accomplishment is literally making it function with voice command

What we learned 

 To develop an idea that's simple, fast and functioning in a short time span.

What's next for Charlie 

 Most probably, advance to its completion.
