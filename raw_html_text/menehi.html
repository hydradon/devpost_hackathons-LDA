"Menehi" is Javanese for "Giving"

Do you feel bad when you pass by a homeless person but don't have cash on you?

Menehi is a cross-platform app that allows you to make donations to local charities to help the homeless individuals in the area. Simply register a debit or credit card with your email address to have the ability to help the community, regardless of the amount of cash in your wallet. It only takes two taps. Menehi also gives a map of local shelters, food kitchens, and other support systems so that you can help people in person in other ways besides financial.

Created at HackDuke 2015.
