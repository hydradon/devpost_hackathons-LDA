The world isn't changed by one mind, one man, one moment. Our society is grown on the labors of thousands, the change of many, and the collaboration of mankind. Small changes can add up to big difference.

This was the mindset we held when we developed sents

Sents is a mobile application that helps many small pennies make a _mountain of change _. We capitalize upon today's technological trends to build a simple tool to help a greater good.

The application is simple. Swipe right: donate a penny. Swipe left: view other charities.

Sents gives you a sense of satisfaction with limited sacrifice. You donate a penny as the difference is made with many. Swipe in your free time, swipe on the bus, swipe throughout the day to catalyze years of difference.
