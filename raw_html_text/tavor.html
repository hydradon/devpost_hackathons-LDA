Inspiration

We've encountered a lot of problems when we're planning a trip - Not having easy access to information about plane timings , hotel vacancies constantly being unavailable and transport from the destined location being a heavy burden on our shoulders . Hence, we have came up with this idea of having artificial intelligence to take over our burdens and assist users in creating a pleasant time planning for a trip.

What it does

Checking and paying for flights, booking a room in hotels, everything can be done by the bot.

How we built it

We built it by having a program that retrieves dynamic data in real time to gather the most up-to-date information and provide it to our users.

Challenges we ran into

Encountering all kinds of errors that took up a lot of precious time .

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We've integrated multiple Application Programming Interface (API) to create a Itenary System for users to help plan, organize and better their traveling experience.

What we learned

Teamwork and time management is very important in order to build a coherent team. Also, always do a quick research on the different APIs because sometimes, things do not go according to plan.

What's next for Travor

Expanding the project to allow third-party companies to contribute and sponsor to the developed idea. Furthermore, including more services for our Artificial Intelligence technology to provide better user experience for everyone.
