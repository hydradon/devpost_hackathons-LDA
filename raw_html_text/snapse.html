Snapse is putting health analytics back into doctors' hands

With the rise of evidence based medicine, clinical studies has become the roots of medical rules. But clinical studies are very expensive and time consuming processes. Existing health analytics are compromises between answering predetermined clinical questions or needing technical skills to use. Moreover, there is no easy way to deal with data quality issues (incompleteness and inconsistencies).

Snapse bring :

  • a simple model of clinical question
  • a query engine based on medical notions rather than SQL columns
  • a built in tool to help doctors manage their data quality

Snapse is an easy, versatile and real time health analytic tool

With Snapse, our ambition is to spark the brain that will change healthcare.
