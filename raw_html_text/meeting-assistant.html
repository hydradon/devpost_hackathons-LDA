Inspiration

It is often hard to fulfil the needs of all participants of the meeting to find the suitable room for everyone. We help you handle this case. Just imagine when we need to meet the preferences of multiple employees: do you suppose to leave them argueing? Our smart solutin excludes this possiblily, now everyone would be happy! In addition to that, the reservation system takes into account the busy times of the meeting rooms to level the load among them.

What it does

Taken the list of meeting participants it uses intricate algorithm to suggest the best meeting rooms taking into account their needs. Each employee enters their needs and they are automatically taken into account keeping the whole privacy!
