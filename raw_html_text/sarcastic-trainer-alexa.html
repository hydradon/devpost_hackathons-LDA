Inspiration

Everyone needs a little demotivational reality check every now and then.

What it does

Alexa becomes your own personal fitness trainer, counts out or times your reps of push-ups, jumping jacks, sit-ups, and planks. She also can't resist throwing in a snide comment or two.

How we built it

AWS Lambda Function and Alexa

Challenges we ran into

Making Alexa count at different paces (or count at all)

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Currently, there is no functionality that allows Alexa to count at different paces, but we were able to accomplish that in our skill. Being able to integrate this functionality in such a fun skill is something that we're really proud of.

What we learned

Some of us had never used Alexa before, so we learned how to write a skill. Others learned how to write a more complicated skill.

What's next for Exercise with Alexa

Integration with a motion sensor app, such as the Amazon Fire Phone, that allows for Alexa to track each exercise in real time. Alexa will respond as she sees fit to every half-hearted pushup or well-done sit-up.
