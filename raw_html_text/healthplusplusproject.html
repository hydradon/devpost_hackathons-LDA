We heard Matthew Strehlow's pitch and were inspired by his idea to tackle the problem of paper medical documents in developing countries. Given that it is unfeasible to perfectly transfer documents without loss of information from paper to digital, we chose to prioritize user friendliness and fast algorithms to increase the number of documents we can process altogether. Our solution aims to be simple to use, yet still highly effective.
