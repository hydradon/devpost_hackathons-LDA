Yo!Yumm is a feature-rich platform that enables entrepreneurs to launch a food ordering marketplace like foodpanda and GrubHub. It is developed keeping in mind the latest trends of food ordering market and the variety of needs of the customer. The customizable script of Yo!Yumm allows one to expand a business and add as many revenue channels as required to maximize the profit margins. Below we have mentioned some of the key features of Yo!Yumm the online food ordering marketplace. 

Yo!Yumm streamlines the process of business expansion in the market with diverse demography with its multilingual feature.
Yo!Yumm comes integrated with marketing module with blog integration, affiliate program, and on-site advertisement.
Yo!Yumm allows you to run customer loyalty program to engage new customers and increase the market penetration.
The admin panel of Yo!Yumm is packed with sophisticated analytical features to provide customers insight.
YoYumm has been integrated with pre-order/ pre-booking feature which allows customer to book a particular order beforehand.
