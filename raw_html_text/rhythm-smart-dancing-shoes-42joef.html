Inspiration

I’ve been dancing the last 6 years (salsa, bachata, tango). I’m not a natural born dancer, and like everybody, it’s difficult to memorize, follow and know where and how to step. Specially if you're a guy and you have to lead, you cannot focus on how to move your feet, body and lead your partner at the same time. The classes I have attended are usually too crowded, I have to follow a schedule and most of the cases are too expensive, about $30 per class.

Now that I know how to dance better, I can see beginners struggling with their first steps, and they keep asking me, how did I learn to dance. I always respond it's about practice, but I wish I had a tool like these smart shoes that could help me during the path to be a dancer.

What it does

The shoes has three functionalities:

  • You follow a video to see how you should move your body and the shoes gives you vibrating clues to know where to step.
  • The vibrating clues help you to practice without a video.
  • Through the 6-axis sensor we could know how well you're performing the steps

How I built it

You just need an Arduino 101 and 5 vibrating motors like this https://www.adafruit.com/product/1201 , you could also add motor drivers to make the motor vibrate in different ways to give different kind of vibrating clues.
