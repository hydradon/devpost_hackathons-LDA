Polaris Share is a platform for promoting and providing sustainable education straight from home. We aim to:

  1. Connect students in need to people with old books to give.

  2. Connect students in need to people with study material from universities with strong academia.

  3. Enable verified students to buy used laptops or desktops from people willing to sell them at nominal price.

Join our platform and help us achieve the dream of a brighter and smarter future. We make sure you reach someone who deserves.
