Inspiration

Want to create an interactive musical quiz to spend friendly and musical evenings with friends around Alexa.

What it does

Access thousands of song clips. Play with friends or family and find the title and artist name of the musical excerpt to earn the most points! Once the musical style has been chosen, a musical excerpt of a few seconds launches, try to find the title or the artist of the musical excerpt! If you find both, you will earn a maximum of points.

How I built it

Skill encoded in nodejs, uses Amazon lambda with a MongoDB database, uses Amazon dynamodb, APL is used to present the menu of musical styles and illustrate the skill. The sounds are stored on Amazon S3.

Challenges I ran into

Dynamically create snippets of a few seconds using ffmpeg.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Proud to have passed the certification after many returns from validation teams who have tested all the cases. Thanks to them :)

What I learned

I learned how to use APL in a skill

What's next for Music Quiz

Create a database of important sound extracts, manage the multi player, more screens design in language APL.
