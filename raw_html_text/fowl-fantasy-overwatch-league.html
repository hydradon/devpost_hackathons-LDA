Inspiration

Global eSports will top 1 billion dollars or the first time in 2019. We wanted to provide another awesome experience for the fans to get more involve with the fast-growing industry. Thus, a better fantasy experience, exclusively for eSports.

What it does

Created a platform that provides customers to join and participate in fantasy eSports league

How we built it

We built the backend in elixer and Phoenix with postgresql. The front-end is built with grommet & react.

Challenges we ran into

Abundance with features, coordinating between Overwatch API with our own League platform. We also learned a brand new framework and design system.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Working MVP!

What we learned

How to separate the critical features from the secondary features and prioritize them.

What's next for FOWL: Fantasy Overwatch League

Expand full functionality for FOWL, then go into other eSports markets.
