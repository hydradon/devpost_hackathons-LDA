Inspiration

The classroom of the future will use bio data to track students' attention and will use it as a feedback to improve lecture structure and delivery

What it does

It combines sensors to measure environmental parameters such as noise, temperature or light intensity, with individual Fitbit and Muse headbands to track student's heart rate, movement and attention (via brain activity).
