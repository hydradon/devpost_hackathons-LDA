Lumos: A sensor-based adaptable smart lighting solution.

Using a sensor-packed board, we collect the data from it and funnel it through a reinforcement learning algorithm that powers the house lighting. The algorithm already has a pretty good idea of what's best for you, yet it will also learn more based on your expressed preferences, as you use it.
