I wanted a simple way to find the closest place near me where I could get a samosa. I started with a web page that uses the current location and the Yelp API to find restaurants in the vicinity that serve samosas. I added more categories like burritos, brunch, froyo and sushi. This is for people who don't want to download an app. It is inspired by the NextBus app in the San Francisco Bay Area.
