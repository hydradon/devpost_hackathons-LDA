Inspiration

Assets are influenced by market sentiment, however, it's been difficult to leverage on this sentiment to make stock decisions.

BlackRock has tried social media analytics but experienced problems with noise.

To overcome this, we adopt a multi prong curated approach from various curated sources,

What it does

Our product detects potential alphagen signals through text analytics and sentiment analysis of social media, especially by key figures who have influence over the market.

We take in data sources from 3 areas, influential figures, and 2 social media analytics sources from quandl.

We backtested it, and it achieved significant returns
