Inspiration

If done right, an intelligent tutor recommender engine can remove the need of middle-men, allowing parents to seek the tutors they require to teach their children.

What it does

With a beautifully designed frontend of everyone's favourite Pokemon - Chespin, the website's form takes in user's requirements and send it to our unsupervised recommender model, thereby getting top 5 recommended tutors back to the user.
