City of San Jose - Walk n' Roll

Walk n’ Roll San José is funded by the Metropolitan Transportation Commission through its Climate Initiatives Program with the goal of increasing walking and biking to school, fostering a healthy and active lifestyle, and reducing traffic congestion and related air pollution, leading to a safer environment within school zones. Currently, data is being collecting manually from the various participating schools ( Data CollectionForm ). The current process is both cumbersome as well as burdening on the participating schools.

As part of the Opportunity Hack , the team is looking at improving the data collection and processing. This repository provides the code that has been developed in the hackathon.

Goal

Build an application that helps with automating the data collection process as well as provides insightful analytics & reports based on this data.
