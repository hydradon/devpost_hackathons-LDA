Inspiration

Working on Blockchain track, we built this application in hope of tackling many problems consumers may face with in their experience in Loyalty programs, such as too many loyalty programs to join that requires physical cards, too short time period to accumulate points and not being able to send points between addresses. Besides, we also find it crucial to work on consumer's big data to ensure and enhance their experience.

What it does

This project provides users with:

  • Different types of Loyalty points (Tokens) accumulation using QR codes (address)
  • Point earned and redempted History tracking on App
  • Rewards/Gifts suggestions based on Customer’s big data
  • Platforms to send/share points between not only two but several people
