Discover Ah Wha Gwan® - Awareness Simplified, Knowledge Amplified ™

A news content display marketing service that broadens awareness without having to be constantly handcuffed to a smartphone - allowing you to still be connected but by being disconnected - hands-free.

AWG is a not an app or website, but a new media experience...

It allows for at-a-glance discovery of content from various established reputable sources in the form of visual oriented slides, that highlight need-to-know stories in a bite-sized format, and can be utilized in a number of scenarios, such as libraries, computer labs, offices, lobbies, etc. With so many websites/apps, our time is severely constrained to constantly be checking so many areas - hence the need to simplify.

AWG was engineered to eliminate the traditional GUI...

GUIs can make/break a solution. So several years ago, when AWG was conceptualized as having no GUI, it took a great deal of explanation as to why. We believed that less can be more, and a focus on efficiency would produce better results over the long-term. Hence, by meticulously combining the right combination of technologies with a focus on minimalism, we engineered a solution that is not limited by the traditional GUI challenges of platforms. The integration of voice commands using Alexa was "one of" the long-awaited final pieces of the puzzle, that allowed for a simple and cost-effective hands-free interaction model.

Many people are infatuated/addicted to mobile apps and websites...

It can be difficult to get people to appreciate that innovation does not have to revolve around a smartphone. Now with the abundance of research data available about the negative impact of tech on mental health, relationships, and society that social media/smart phones have created, AWG has a greater opportunity to drive the vision of a hands-free discovery and awareness model to increase awareness of the importance of mindfulness. link

but a unique media model that has stood the test of time when other technologies have lost relevance. In an ever shifting technology landscape, one has to be very selective of where to place one's efforts to ensure development efforts will remain useful and relevant.
