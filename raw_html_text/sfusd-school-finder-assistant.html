Inspiration

What it does

Chatbot gathers information from parents about what their needs and preferences are. Then an optimized list of schools is generated for parents to compare and learn about the different schools. Parents are able to bookmark schools and apply when they're ready.

How we built it

Chatbot experience - designed in Sketch, prototyped in InVision School recommendations - HTML, CSS, jQuery, Python
