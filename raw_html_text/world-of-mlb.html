What it does

Looks at birthplace of MLB players from 2018 season

How I built it

Good old fashioned copy and paste from Baseball-Reference website to Microsoft Excel, then pivot table. I have also created json object from the spreadsheet but I ran out of time

Challenges I ran into

Time! Data! (Don't I do this every year?)

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned

U. S. Virgin Islands, Aruba , United Kingdom, Guam, Hong Kong, Lithuania , Nicaragua, Saudi Arabia, and South Africa have each one player that appeared in MLB in 2018 season.
