Inspiration

Friends and family of women on campus have no way of knowing their daughters or friends are safe without contacting them eveyday.

How it works

This mobile app tackles this challenge by incorporating new advanced technologies and a uniquely designed algorithm within the app for safety of user.

What's the story behind?

What Women Do Now

Walking in dark: Woman sends a location and a message to her friend to tell a friend or a family member about her whereabouts and her arrival time.

Taking a Uber: Woman takes a photo of an Uber driver photo and sends a message to a friend about her arrival location and her arrival time.

Taking a flight: Woman takes an early flight to avoid arriving late at night in a stranger town.

Taking an elevator: Woman takes a floor below or above to avoid the same floor pressed by a guy stranger.

Having a date: Woman asks a friend to call in 10 minutes in a case she doesn't want to continue.
