What it does

Allow you to add THE MOST IMPORTANT TASK OF THE DAY ( the only one, with the highest priority) to the task and reminds it to you in the morning through Google home mini, without you getting distracted with your phone.

This will help you save time in the long term, because you won't be wasting it anymore!!!
