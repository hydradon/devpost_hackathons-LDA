Inspiration

We were inspired because we thought that it would be an easy way to standardize the way people collaborate, since often the meaning of collaboration can be unclear with ambiguous academic integrity policies.

What it does

It matches you with tutors if you need one, allows you to set up collaboration sessions with your friends, allows you to use whiteboard sessions online to keep track of your collaboration with your classmates and allows you to see who's in your classes and arrange to meet.

How I built it

Built with basic HTML.

Challenges I ran into

Pairing it with jade, mogodb, node js

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Parallax scrolling and google maps

What I learned

I learned that it takes more than 24 hours to make a website with everything you want.

What's next for CollabSpace

Finishing the website, setting up login information, and bringing it to CMU campus.
