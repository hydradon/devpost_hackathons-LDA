Hi-Riot is a smart doorbell built on top of the Matrix API. It contains a web camera for seeing who is at your door, and the ability to lock and unlock your door through the Riot messenger. It also has an alarm feature in case the person at your door is not friendly.

Future features for the doorbell could include facial recognition for identifying the visitor.
