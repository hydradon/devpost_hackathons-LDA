  • Text sizes according to window size

Inspiration: So you have CustomerHub and you love it. It's easy to maintain and you get a great member area for a fantastic price. But your clients want to log in from their phones or tablets and they come back and say, "The content isn’t mobile responsive or even mobile friendly." When you sell membership content, you must make it available on tablets and phones. It's ALL THE RAGE! The client experience with your product can make all the difference to a "repeat purchase".

What it does: We've created Genie themes so you can install our quick script, use our cheat sheet for updating your content for mobile.. and KahBlamm…Mobile responsive (and tablet) CustomerHub.

How we built it: Javascript, CSS with database validation for active subscriptions via Infusionsoft's API.

Challenges we ran into: Not all customers are willing to do a content audit for external content. We're continuing our mission to update styles that will adjust elements for a variety of popular third-party embedded assets.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

To date we've launched two of the growing theme libraries for CustomerHub and will continue to offer more.
