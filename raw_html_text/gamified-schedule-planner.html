Inspiration

A youtube video about gamification + Skinner's psychology and a bit of luck during a discussion.

What it does

Adds video game elements to keeping up with your schedule to make it more effective and engaging

How we built it

We built this app over about 24 hours (more or less) at a hackathon, using Java.

Challenges we ran into

We needed to use a lot of things we didn't know before, and how to use.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to research what we needed and figure out how to do what we didn't know previously.

What we learned

How to use arrayLists (some of us didn't know), ways to find the local time, how to make tabs, and GUI related syntax.

What's next for Gamified Schedule/Planner

We may try to finish it and fix bugs, and hopefully make it what we foresaw when planning.
