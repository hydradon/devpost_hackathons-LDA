Inspiration

Inspired by other teamwork-based games like Keep Talking and Nobody Explode where players needs constant communication in order to complete simple tasks.

Our objective

Three players are required to run a pirate ship. There are random events where players will need to perform in order to keep sailing and save the captain from getting scurvy. These random events are other pirate attacks, ship maintenance, and equipment maintenance. In order to take down another pirate ship, the players will need to communicate to align the ship in the right direction and then fire the cannon. If this task is not successfully completed after a few seconds, the ship will be taken over and the captain will be forced to walk the plank.

What it does

Currently, we have a working app with a GUI interface but the back-end is not connected with the front-end client and server. So if you download our project, do not expect a complete game as of right now (4/14/2019). The mobile app can only be explored if diving right into the downloaded project.
