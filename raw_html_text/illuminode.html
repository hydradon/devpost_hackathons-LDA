Inspiration

We wanted to inspire creativity in the community.

What it does

It uses a live real time database hosted by Firebase to allow multiple people to edit the story - however they can only see the line above theirs, not the whole story, which leads to twists and turns. There's also web scraping to find interesting prompts on the web, as well as use of Firebase online storage and calls to archive and view past stories.

Challenges we ran into

There were many bugs, as well as designing the original structure. We did not have any experience with Firebase and only one member had experience with Android.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We managed to create an app well out of our comfort zone and learn several valuable skills across multiple platforms along the way.

What we learned

We learned how to use firebase, pull from specific databases and manipulate them, make calls to and from online storage to a device, scrape data from webpages, and lots of android syntax.

What's next for Illuminode

We would have liked to add accounts so that users could view their past stories and contributions. We were considering doing an alternative approach using a tree rather than an arraylist to show many different viewpoints and diverging paths, which would be interesting for politics and social discussions.
