All of us should control our budget (especially students :D), but most apps demand lot of engagement from the user. For example, usually you have to remember when, where did you spend your money and how much - at the end of the day it's easy to forget or during a day it's annoying to control it. Our app does it for you! It knows places where you usually spend your money and allows you to quickly add your expenses, right after you leave the store.

P.S. No, it won't drain your battery ;)
