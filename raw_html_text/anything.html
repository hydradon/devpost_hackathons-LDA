Inspiration

We are a group of indecisive students who can never decide on what to eat when we go out.

What it does

Anything is an app that unloads the burden of choice from you and your friends. The app generates a list of restaurants nearby based on the location and dietary preferences of the users. The user would then choose one restaurant and rank it. Restaurants with the most votes would be chosen as the suggestion.

How I built it

We designed our wireframe in Justinmind prototyper.

Challenges I ran into

We were all from business backgrounds and did not have much development experience. Hence, we decided we would just create a wireframe mockup of our app.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We successfully designed a wireframe of our app in 24 hours

What I learned

UI/UX concepts and best practices Rapid prototyping and iteration

What's next for Anything

We plan to do some market validation to identify if our problem is big enough and if there is actually a demand for our product. If so, we plan to learn about Android development to create an actual prototype.
