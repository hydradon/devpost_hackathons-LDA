Often times potential customers are discouraged from the struggle of waiting in long lines at restaurants for food or stores for clothes. Most of this struggle comes from the constraint and physical chore of standing in the line for the next available cashier. Fortunately, we built Rush, a web application that allows customers to scan a QR code upon entry in the store and queue up online. Now they can sit or shop while they wait.
