• Boss

Inspiration

Most people must have such an experience, when you are playing games or do some personal things in office, you absolutely didn’t recognize you boss is just behind you. Oops! Been caught!

What it does

When your boss is passing by and look at your computer, the camera can recognize your boss’s face and pop up a working page. So the boss will not find that your are doing something that are unrelated to the work. It can also be used at home to prevent your parents peeping your computer.

How we built it

Python, API

Challenges we ran into

How to capture the image from a webcam How to make the machine know whether it is the boss’s face.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Complete this project within 10 hours, basically achieve the function we want

What we learned

The use of API, methods of solving problems, teamwork, cooperation

What's next for Bossface

Currently, the Bossface can only popup a image about working page. Next we will make it popup a real working page or a normal webpage.
