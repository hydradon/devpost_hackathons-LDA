Inspiration

Women are severely underrepresented in tech, and our team wanted to make a tool that would enable women to enter the field.

What it does

This site allows women to find internships and mentos in tech.

How we built it

We used a google sites platform with HTML coding.

Challenges we ran into

We struggled with the technical code due to our lack of coding experience.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are so proud of making the animation.

What we learned

We learned about how to operate in a time crunch.

What's next for Women in Tech

We want to share this with our friends and others who support women entering tech.
