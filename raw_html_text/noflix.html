Inspiration

Dedicated to boring weekend evenings staring blankly at the tv.

Dedicated to hours spent with your friend being unable to decide on a place to go.

Dedicated to that wanderlust you've been hiding your entire life.

This is our rise against those endless nights of netflix. This is Noflix.

Noflix asks you basic questions such as price range and location, before an UBER drives comes pick you up and takes you to an unknown surprise adventure.
