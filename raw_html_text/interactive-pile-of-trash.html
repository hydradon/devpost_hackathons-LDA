How I built it

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

None of this was made before the hackathon and none of it was even thought of until an hour post arrival. Doing it all by myself with occasional guidance when puzzled by certain c# syntax.

What I learned

Never Give up, even an inexperienced programmer like myself can make something decent if He puts the time to it. There is always a solution, and take breaks every few hours.
