Inspiration

In our society we suffer from extreme biases and the diverse traits of extroversion and introversion , which make meeting people, making new friends, socializing with co-workers...

What it does

An online chat app that pair random users _ anonymously _ (gender neutral, neutral ethnicity) expriencing a "blind audition" and help them get to know each other without any prejudices by suggesting topics to talk about selected specifically to _ uncover their personalities _ without revealing any backgrounds or profile's details.

How we built it

Android app with android-studio linked with a firebase real-time database.

Challenges we ran into

brainstorming the idea, researching competition, no team designers, lack of ui/ux knowledge, implementing firebase's ml-kit...

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Idea, making a great team, our app ofc

What we learned

ml-kit, design, firebase, pitching...

What's next for Social-Blind

implementing ml models to suggest idea suitable for both persons (recommender system), changing the randomness of user's chat to users more suitable to user's personality.
