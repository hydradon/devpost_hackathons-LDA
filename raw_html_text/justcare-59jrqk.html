justcare

Everyone deserves better healthcare. People of color are suffering from a healthcare system that misdiagnoses, dismisses, and sidelines them. justcare fills this gap, by giving patients the data they need to find a responsible health provider near them. We keep hospitals accountable through our rating system, which specifically targets obstacles people of color tend to face in the healthcare process. In the future that justcare envisions, it doesn't exist. Search. Evaluate. Just care.
