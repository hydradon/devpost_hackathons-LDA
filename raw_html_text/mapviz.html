The world is awash with data, but this data is meaningless without analysis to understand it and make decisions based on it. Data and analysis needs to be presented to people in understandable ways too for the message inside to be communicated. Visualisations are an excellent way to analyse and present data, and can help reveal patterns to analysts and audiences. Adding interactivity makes the data even more accessible.

MapThis aims to provide a user friendly, interactive visualisation of geographical data. Analysts and researchers can upload datasets and MapThis will generate an interactive map of the data.
