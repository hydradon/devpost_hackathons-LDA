Inspiration

  • Everyones been through a situation where you're out with friends, and at one point, you're all a bit bored and you want to know what else is _ trending _ around you. With FOMO you see all the trending locations around you, why you should go there, and what people are saying about them.

What it does

App that allows real-time interactions with live trending locations. People can look at what aggregated social media data, through machine learning and image analysis says about a location (is it a scenic place? Are people having fun there? Is it in the news?) and the interact in real time with other people to see what they're saying about the location.

** Also automatically switches to a mobile version if on mobile**
