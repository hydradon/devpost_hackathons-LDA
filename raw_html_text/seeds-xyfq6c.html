Inspiration

We know that nowadays, there is a hughe lack of knowledge about how to reduce our footprint and be more ecolgyic. Even there is a lot of information about how to be more ecologyic, we thought that an App that includes games should be a better way to learn and practice our choices to improve our consume.

What it does

Basicly, it includes 4 sections: the first includes some basic tips to improve everyone's footprint. The second one is a game that make you recycle. The third one is a simple plataform game for everyones fun. Finally, the 4th section is a quizz to check your knowledge.

How we built it

We used C# and Unity

Challenges we ran into

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned
