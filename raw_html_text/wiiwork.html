Inspiration

This past November 5, Just Dance 2020 was the final game of the Wii and marks the passing of the Wii from a family game console to a workhorse at the office.

What it does

The WiiWork Suite of professional business tools provide the modern office with the worker productivity solutions, offered at a reasonable subscription cost of $50/month per user.

What's next for WiiWork

The WiiWork Suite is expanding to include:

  • WiiChart
  • WiiShow
  • WiiMail
  • WiiCloud
  • WiiWrite
  • WiiChat
