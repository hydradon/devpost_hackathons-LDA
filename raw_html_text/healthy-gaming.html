Playing games has always been a favorite past-time of ours. However, the youth of today takes gaming to a higher level: with intensive gaming sessions that harms their bodies. Our mission is to remind and empower the next generation of gamers while keeping them in optimal physical shape. Usually when he saw them play the games, he realized that they usually got addicted to the games and never did anything productive. As a result, we wanted to make it possible to play games, but also build healthy habits. If we did this, productivity would be achieved and there wouldn't be as much addiction.

To accomplish this task, we created a website with many programs that could be installed into games to create a healthy habit. Programs such as MInecraft’s toothbrush mod encourages Minecraft Gamers to develop a habit of brushing their teeth in game, which will in turn translates to a healthy habit in real life.

We built our project with HTML and Java.

Our challenges included designing the webpage, using Java to develop programs for our games, and putting the websites and the programs together. We had trouble designing the UI as well as the logo for our websites and writing codes for specific events in Java. Java was especially difficult for us, because we did not know how to make MInecraft mods beforehand.

We are proud of our website and programs, and we hope it will help Gamers develop healthy habits.
