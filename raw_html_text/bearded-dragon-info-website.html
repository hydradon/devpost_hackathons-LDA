Inspiration: I own a Bearded Dragon and one day his basking light burned out. I didn't know what kind to buy so i had to google it last minute at the store, but there were so many websites to go through. Then i wondered how much info about my pet i didn't know. I wanted to learn more about what kind of diet he should have, what lightbulbs to buy, things like that. i decided other people would want to know too, and making a website for it was the perfect opportunity to learn more about Beardies, and teach other people at the same time.

What it does: It teaches people how to care for Bearded Dragons, what cages to buy, what to feed them, what they are.

How I built it: i built it using a website called codepen.io, using HTML and CSS.

Challenges I ran into: It was challenging to learn the codes, and i had to get every single letter right or else it wouldn't work.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of: I'm really proud of how well it turned out, I wasn't expecting to put this much time and effort into it, but it definitely paid off. I'm proud I stepped out of my comfort zone and learned something new that i thought would be difficult.

What I learned: i learned how to code, make a website, and how to make friends

What's next for Bearded Dragon Info Website: I'm definitely going to add even more information and facts as i learn them. I want to add more pictures and make the website more professional looking.
