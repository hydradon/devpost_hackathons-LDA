ABOUT MYCODEDAY

MyCodeDay was created to fulfill a need -- a place where organizers for CodeDays can interact with attendees, and attendees can easily view any information about their event. That's where MyCodeDay comes in - with a simple, intuitive, and easy-to-use web app and iOS app, it's easier than ever to access a personalized source of information about CodeDay.
