Inspiration

Worked with virtual assistant, hard to allow him/her to be effective without giving them all of your passwords. Needed a way for them to access accounts without having to share passwords.

What it does

Allows you to make account, enter info, and give access to others who have the extension. Displays privileges you have to access accounts. Allows user to set timer after witch stand in accounts are automatically logged out.
