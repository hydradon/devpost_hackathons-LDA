Inspiration

Over 80% of ICOs are scams. Energy applications of blockchain are susceptible to the same level of fraud and security vulnerabilities. Unlike other blockchain applications, energy requires physical devices to provide truth (like smart meters), impacts real infrastructure (blackouts, asset destruction), and is bounded by regulatory and contract complexity.

What it does

We're launching the world's first research organization/foundation to ensure the resilience of valuable energy blockchain application.

We provide:

  • Smart contract review and standardization
  • Penetration and scalability testing
  • Physical "oracle" security testing
