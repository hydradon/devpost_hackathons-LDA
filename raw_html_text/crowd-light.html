Inspiration

Presentation devices failed during the JacobsHack introduction session. With our project, we wanted to create a synced, Go-based slide-sharing platform. Also, think of the big crowd of people attending a concert in an arena. Now the DJ can develop a smartphone-based light-show and have thousands of people attending it by just loading a website.

What it does

Sync any amount of screens, sort them into patterns and start your fancy light show.

How we built it

Backend: Google Go; Frontend JS, Python script for planning and controlling the light show
