Inspiration

The idea was to backup your own instagram media to anywhere. In the middle of the project, I expand the possibility to save and send on any instagram media owned by other users.

How it works

Login with your instagram account, and select the images you want to send. Thats all.

What's next for Jupook

Jupook will be able to get not only the instagram media, but all of the social media such as Facebook or Flickr.
