How do authors play with the tides of emotion throughout a piece? At what points do they tug at your heartstrings to make you laugh in joy or cry in sorrow? Wouldn't you like to know?

So did we.

visuEmote aims to leverage the powerful machine learning algorithms of indico for sentiment analysis and provide visualization of the emotional trends throughout a book (or even just a piece of text). By segmenting the text into small chunks and conducting indico's powerful sentiment analysis and then visualizing the data with d3.js, we provide an insightful visualization that allows you to easily view and appreciate the emotional trends behind a text.

An interesting fact, did you know that Dudley in Harry Potter & the Sorcerer's Stone is frequently associated with negative sentiment? Come check us out to learn more!
