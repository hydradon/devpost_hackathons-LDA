Inspiration

We have devoted over a year to creating our free-to-play VR gaming platform _ Ripple Effect _, which is playable on the HTC Vive and Oculus Rift.

What it does

_ Ripple Effect _ includes 12 free-to-play games. We are currently developing content for other studios and brands. The platform allows for other developers to upload their own simulations for distribution. We anticipate releasing our own token for the multiverse.

How we built it

Using Unity we developed original models and gameplay mechanics. We have now used those mechanics for other clients to build their own simulations.

Challenges we ran into

Releasing a free game every week for 12 weeks and integrating a block chain concept within the model.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Over 25,000 downloads and being runner-up as the Best Startup in Los Angeles

What we learned

Teamwork and communication is the key to producing successful content.

What's next for Valhalla

A potential token sale and the release of a number of new simulations within _ Ripple Effect _.
