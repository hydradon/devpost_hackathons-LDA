While playing Fortnite Battle Royale made by Epic Games, use this skill at the beginning of the game to get a drop location, or to get a challenge. Getting a random drop location may have you land in a convenient spot, or it could have you start the game skydiving across the entire map! There are many challenges that can make the game much more interesting, rather than just using the same weapons over and over again, or just building to over power everybody else. No matter where you land, or what challenge you get, just make sure you get that Victory Royale!
