Inspiration

To help people learn how to recycle properly and reduce waste production.

What it does

As well as being fun, our game teaches people how to recycle in the real world.

How I built it

Jet used Unity and C# for the development. Xcode to build to iPhone. Jin used Photoshop for the art and design. Game content was made from the research that came from the EPA.

Challenges I ran into

Making the presentation for our game.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We finished.

What I learned

How improper recycling affects, not only the City of Houston, but all of the U.S.

What's next for Recycle Redo

Partner up with state officials to get our game into schools.
