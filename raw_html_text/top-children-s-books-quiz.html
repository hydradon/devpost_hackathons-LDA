Inspiration

I was an avid reader as a child, often surpassing over 5-10 novels in a week under the warm blankets of my bed. Now, as a junior in college, I'm often reminded of those days when I pick up a book, but there are simply too many classics to remember. Enter "Top Children's Books Quiz."

What it does

"Top Children's Books Quiz" is a fun Alexa game that teaches and quizzes children on over 40 of the top children's books of our century! These were handpicked from https://www.nypl.org/childrens100 and encompass several of my favorites that I cherished as a child.

How I built it

I built this with AWS, Amazon Lambda, and the Alexa Skills Kit.
