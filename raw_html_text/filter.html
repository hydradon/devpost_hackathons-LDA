Inspiration

We have hundreds of contacts in our contact lists, but sometimes you just don't need some of them but they still clutter your contact list.

What it does

Tag your contacts, and filter your contact list with these tags

How we built it

Android app, SquiDB as backend

Challenges we ran into

Learning android from scratch, debugging android, teammate leaving halfway through

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We made our first android app, and it more or less works!

What we learned

Android development

What's next for Filter

Hard filter and soft filter options, multi tag filtering, adding more features that are often seen in regular contact lists such as sorting, etc
