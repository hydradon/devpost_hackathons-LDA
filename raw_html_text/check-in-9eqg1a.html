Inspiration

How can we keep people safe and give their loved ones peace of mind in a world with increasing safety concerns.

What it does

This application allows many people to follow-up on their loved ones. Compared to a simple call or message this is a many to many connection, where many people can get peace of mind that one person is safe.

How we built it

We built it using react-native and firebase and expo

Challenges we ran into

Learning a new technology, firebase errors, the short time frame to complete everything.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Learning react-native within 24 hours and building a prototype. Coming up with a unique and useful idea. Working in a team.

What we learned

React-native, ideation, firebase

What's next for check-in

We wish to implement location services and further polish and make it user-proof
