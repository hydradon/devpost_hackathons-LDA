ByoMotion

Use a MYO arm band to help patients recovering from arm injuries and ailments. Developed for Steelhacks 2016.

Reason

To track the progress of patients recovering from arm injuries and ailments. Data is processed and displayed showing their progress.

Technologies

C++, MYO Armband, Python.
