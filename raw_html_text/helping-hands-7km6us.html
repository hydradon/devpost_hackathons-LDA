Application Category:

Architecture, Engineering, Construction (AEC) & Development

Inspiration (Problem I solved):

According to Proptech, It Says that the building construction processes in New York City have not changed significantly in the last several decades with many Engineers using the same building patters, techniques etc. which results in repeative mistakes and that informations and know ledge gathered are not shared down the lane which will help to prevent future mistakes, reduce cost of constructions and make current contractors more competitive..

Here is how I solved this problem. I created an application called Helping Hands: A real-time communication and Social Networking System Designed to help all Building Engineers to Connect, make friends, chats one to one with other Engineers, Share files privately, ask questions, get response and Share Informations in other to avoid Repeating the same error and Future Mistakes while promoting Knowledge and information gathering at large.
An Engineer who is Building a house can share his knowledge with others, Share Building plans in (PDF Formats), Images, Videos etc. Can also easily ask questions to get response from the Building Engineering Community...

Community for Graduating Engineers:

This Helping Hands applications was designed to also help Students, Graduating Students of Building Engineers to foster colla boration as well. Building Engineering Students can also Post Contents, Ask Questions and get help on their School projects from other experienced engineers in the Community..

Helping-Hands Features Integrated:

1.) Post Status Updates : Easily Share what is in your mind with other Engineers in the community

2.) Sharing of Building Photos, Videos, Plans(in PDF) : Easily Post and Share building informations including building Plans,Images, Videos and get responses from the community.

3.) Asking Questions and Getting Responses : Easily Ask any Questions and get reply from other Engineers:

4.) Searching Local Engineers & Connecting.. : Easily Search and Connect with other Engineers in your Locality, City, States etc. You can search Engineers by names, phone no, email, city name, states etc and be connected with them in real-time

5.) Friends Connection System Easily send and recieve real-time connection request. You can build your friends/contact list. Easily have wealth of Engineers in your contacts for future collaborations.

6.) 1 to 1 Realtime Chat Communications Easily chat with your Friends one to one, Share files(PDF,IMAGE,Ms Word, Excel etc) privately all in realtime....

7.) Gets Realtime Notifications for any Post Updates : Easily get a Real-Time Notification alerts for each Post status Updates.

Some Real-Time Notification Icons & Its Functions.

2.) : This Real-time Message Icon displays real-time One to One Chat Message with Files Sharing Options.

How to use this Application

1.)Call up a browser Eg. Chrome, Firefox,UC Browser and Create your Account. You will be required to Enter your Social Security Number(just anything),Passport, National Id Cards and other details for one time background Check..

2.) Once Logged In, You can start Posting, Sharing Files, Asking Question, Commenting on other Peoples Post.

3.) Fully Integrated Search Box to enable you to Search other Engineers in your Locality, Cities, States. You can aslo search them by names, Emails, phone number. Once connected, you can send and recieve real-time friends request and can easily build your Friends list or Contacts. You can chat with your Engineering friends one to one, share files securely, know when they are offline and online all in real-time.
4.)Fully Intgerated SMS and Emailing System to allow you to send SMS and Email messages in realtime all from your friends contacts profiles.
