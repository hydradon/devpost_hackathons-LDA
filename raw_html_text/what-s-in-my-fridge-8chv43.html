Inspiration

Mouldy bagels at work (CLEARBRIDGEMOBILE) made us think about all the food we buy, an amount of which we may forget about and waste.

What it does

To mitigate the problem mentioned above, we created an app that tracks the expiration dates of food items and notifies you when they are about to go bad. In addition, it also has a shopping list that can be populated through the list of items in your 'Fridge', so that it can be integrated into a user's routine, with plans to implement Speech-to-Text with Google Home or any other assistant.

How we built it

Android Studio.
