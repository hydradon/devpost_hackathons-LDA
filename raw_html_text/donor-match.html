Inspiration

We wanted to make a system for the hospitals to find the right donor in the shortest amount of time.

What it does

It uses blood donation and organ donation database and geotags the users. Thus when a hospital is in need of a certain blood group or an organ donation, they could quickly locate the nearest donor
