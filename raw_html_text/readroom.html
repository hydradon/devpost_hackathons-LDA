ReadRoom

Keep track of what you've read and what you want to read

Start by hitting "Search" and typing in a query. Check the books you want to add, then hit "Wishlist" or "Collection" to add the books to your lists.

"Wishlist" contains books you want to read. "Collection" contains books you've read.
