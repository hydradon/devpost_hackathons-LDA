Inspiration : For certain types of businesses, like service companies, the staff budget is one of the largest expenses for the organization. Maybe you feel like you could be saving more or doing better, but you’re not sure where to start or how to regain control of your money.

What it does: A good budget is a realistic spending plan that you create based on your needs and preferences.

How we built it: by pulling data from mirro.io/contracts and modelling them into forecast of data based on historical changes. Projecting the new salaries using the actual costs for reference. Add the burden percentage below the new salary to calculate how much salary increases will affect the overall budget.

What's next for: compare individual data from your forecast with a benchmark from your industry in order to identify your strengths and your weaknesses .
