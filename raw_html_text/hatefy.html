Inspiration

We, as a mankind, are the worst human beings all over the Earth. So - why bother with nice and tidy applications which let us, let's say, to 'Like' something? We need a tool to hate - Hatefy.

What it does

Hatefy allow You to make an account and then publish your bad, dirty and critical thoughts without any limits. You could browse hates published by other users and filter them by hashtags. However, the most exciting (and disgusting) part of Hatefy is achievements system. With Hatefy you're allow not only to express the darker side of your nature, you can be even granted for it! Feel free to insult everything you can imagine and discover amazing achievements like Rosemary's Baby or Daredevil!
