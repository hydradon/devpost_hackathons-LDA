NicePik

MangoHack APP

Imagine you're at a cruise. You have this badge that you have to carry with you at all times, just in case you want to purchase margaritas, which they still get charged to your room, not the actual card. So why not enhance customer experience and get rid of that rfid card, and use the customer face, which accurate recognition of it? This is what NicePik is trying to achieve, circumvent an annoying card (for this specific use case) and let user just use their face, no username no passwords, just their smile.
