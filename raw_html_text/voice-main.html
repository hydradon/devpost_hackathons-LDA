Inspiration

For all the advancements in the field of Computer Science, which made almost everything around us possible and fun, coding virtually remains the same. Apart from the physical monotony that drains a coder we are looking at some bigger health risks in the coming future such as Repetitive Strain Injury(RSI). We need to save our fellow Computer Scientists from being victims to this syndrome and that is why, we decided to do our bit. It not only serves the purpose but makes coding cooler than ever before!

What it does

You tell us the logic; we give you syntactically correct code :)

How we built it

A little help from our friend "LUIS", a lot of coffee, love and passion! ;)

Challenges we ran into

Accomplishments that we're proud of

  • Sleeping for just a couple of hours during the hackathon :D

What we learned

  • Power of good team dynamics.
