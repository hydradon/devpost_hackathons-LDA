What is Hack UofT ?

Its an app where lovers of technology can gather as much knowledge about Hackathons, Internships and Events.

As UofT students, we constantly find ourselves asking our friends how they find all these "hackathons". We wanted to create a hub for students to easily see all upcoming events. Like that, people will be asking you how you find all these hackathons.
