This is the first app from the smart city series that we have design with context in mind. The plan is leverage existing system to launch multiple apps covering different regions of the world.

Major features:

1. Phrasebook system + Smart location = Geo-based translator Picture yourself travelling across China or India with dozens of languages being used. Smart phrasebook will give you translations in the local language depending on the region you're staying.

2. Travel app + Smart People = Connect with travelers nearby Discover travel buddies and connect with other like-minded travellers across the globe. Set your profile with matching preferences. Than see other travelers around you and invite them to chat.

3. Guide + Smart Weather = Better trips recommendations

Context enabled features including connecting travelers in dynamic social network is made possible thanks to Trqnl API.
