Inspiration

We wanted to provide quick access to vital Georgia Tech services that some new students may find trouble finding.

What it does

A "chatbot" that allows users to access specific questions about various Georgia Tech services ranging from Health, Academics, and Transportation.

How we built it

Utilized a simple GUI and Java.

Challenges we ran into

Storing all the information in a time efficient manner.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to build something fun together as a team.

What we learned

Teamwork, dividing duties among team members, etc.

What's next for HackNC

Looking forward to incorporating some hardware.
