Inspiration

In today's century, kids would rather play games and watch movies than study. A survey shows that most teachers use digital games in the classroom.

What it does

It randomly asks questions based on the category selected with three wrong options and a right answer. The player then selects the answer before shaking the device for the next question.

How we built it

We built our project using Android Studio. (Using Java and XML)

Challenges we ran into

Linking our XML files using a button was a major challenge.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

As first year students, this is our first game that we have produced and worked on. We are proud we made it work successfully.

What we learned

We learnt new skills in Android studio, for example, we learn't how to like the button with another XML file.

What's next for How much do you Know???

We plan to expand the game, covering different areas of study for children, rather than just colours.
