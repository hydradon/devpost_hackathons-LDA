Inspiration

There are too many dating apps that focus on liking a person by how they look and promoting hook-up culture. We feel a relationship should be built on more than that.

We have created a social networking site where individuals could connect with people based on their common interest in books.

What it does

On Tindle, each user rates the books they have read. Through collaborative filtering, we extrapolated the a dataset of 50 thousand users and 9000 thousand books, and matches users who rated books similarly.
