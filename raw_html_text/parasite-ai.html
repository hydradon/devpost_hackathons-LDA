Inspiration

What it does

We took a service oriented approach. A Flask web app holds the neural network for detection, and is hosted on Google Cloud App Engine. It communicates with Client iOS app and 8Base, where business logic and predictions are handled. Detects malaria from phone photos in real time using a hand-made convolutional neural network.
