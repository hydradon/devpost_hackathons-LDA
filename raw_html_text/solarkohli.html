Inspiration

The inspiration was to use the millions of users of Facebook, Slack, Kik and other social communities and educate them about Solar in a intuitive way using a bot called SolarKohli.

What it does

It takes inputs from users like knowing if the user owns a rooftop, get's to know if a user is interested in solar energy and then promotes use of solar energy.

How we built it

We built it using ruby, git, wit.ai

Challenges we ran into

Training the bot was a massive challenge.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Having a bot on the fb messenger app live

What we learned

Great insight about the solar field.

What's next for SolarKohli

Going into domains like pushing ads of solar panels and having a more trained ai
