We learnt ReactJS in a introductory course. We were fascinated with the abilities of ReactJS and decided to come up with a project of our own using ReactJS.

Our project is a simple expenditure tracker website. We used Firebase as our backend database to record user's expenditure. We also used Firebase Authentication to secure access.

We learnt from tutorial videos and applied it to our own project.

ReactJS and Firebase were very new to us so we struggled with the syntax and integrating the platforms together.

This is the first webpage we have built.

We are now more familiar with ReactJS and Firebase and also about interactive user interface.

After this project, it had definitely motivated us to come up with more projects of our own.
