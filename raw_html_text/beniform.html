Inspiration

Suggestions from the Community Foundation Santa Cruz County

What it does

Transforms applications for eligibility to SNAP, WIC, CACFP and Food Bank easily – As the family transitions from WIC to SNAP the necessary updated information is automatically sent to the respective agency so compliance is easy and there is no gap in services.

How I built it

I built it using Android Studio and Pyze SDK
