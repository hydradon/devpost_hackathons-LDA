Inspiration

an easy way to maintain having a low carbon footprint https://www.figma.com/file/Y4I3ISoyuPWE87HpxSEg4ZYQ/Level-Zero

What it does

Goals: -make it easy for people to offset their carbon footprint -make people aware of their carbon footprint
