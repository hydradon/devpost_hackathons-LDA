What it does

“MeeSeva” in Telugu means, ‘At your service’, i.e. service to citizens. It is a good governance initiative that incorporates the vision of National eGov Plan “Public Services Closer to Home” and facilitates single entry portal for entire range of G2C& G2B services. The objective of MeeSeva is to provide smart, citizen centric, ethical, efficient and effective governance facilitated by technology.
