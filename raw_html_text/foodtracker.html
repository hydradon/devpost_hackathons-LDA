Inspiration

The unhealthy eating habits of many people these days

What it does

Each of the 5 food categories are listed. People can use the stepper to mark when they eat a serving. The label becomes red if they eat to much.

The image of the food (ie bread or vegetables) become more opaque as the user comes to completing their daily needs for that category.
