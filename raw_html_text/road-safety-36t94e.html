Inspiration -> This application was designed for the safety of people travelling in their vehicles or walking down their streets

What it does

The application has the following features:

  1. Sends the emergency alert to selected contacts with exact coordinates and complete address .
  2. Sends the emergency alert to all saved contacts with exact coordinates and complete address .
  3. Locates all the nearby hospital and starts the navigation on one click in case of emergency.
  4. Locates all the nearby car repair shops and starts the navigation on one click in case of emergency.
  5. Contacts the Hospital on one click.
  6. Contacts the Police on one click.
  7. The last and the most important feature of this application is, in case of major accident in the vehicle when the user cannot access the android application, we have used an piezo-electric sensor which calculates the frequency of accident and sends the emergency alert with exact coordinates and complete address to the emergency contacts saved in the device.
  8. Automatically connects to wifi as soon as we start moving in a vehicle.

How we built it

Android Application We used the concept of Google Maps, Google Api's, accessing data in Json format with the help of Volley library, maintaining proper database of the user and the emergency contacts.

Arduino Uno We designed the circuit using Arduino Uno where we used piezo electric sensor to calculate the frequency of how hard the sensor was hit.
