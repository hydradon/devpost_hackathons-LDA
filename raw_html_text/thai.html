Our project is about informing diners about food's flavors and portion size when they are taking orders in restaurants. It is really common that customers leave the restaurants plenty plate waste since the food is not matching with their expectations e.g. too spicy/sweet or there is simply too much food to finish (it happens to Madhack too, btw). However, individuals have different standards when it comes to flavors and food portion size. Our project aims at providing a customized measurement scale for users through machine learning - predicting individuals' food preferences by learning other people's preferences. We hope that by giving consumers a general idea of the flavors and portion size of the food items on the menu, they can make better decision that can minimize unwanted food and therefore, food waste.
