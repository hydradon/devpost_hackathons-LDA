Inspiration

Creating safer communities by providing security to businesses that need them, and giving cops who want to earn extra cash an opportunity to do so.

What it does

Connects off-duty cops with businesses that need security. Cops are able to look through listing posted by businesses and apply to the ones that work with their schedule.

How we built it

We used Mean stack along with HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

Challenges we ran into

Creating a direct messaging system from scratch

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Creating a direct messaging system from scratch and being able to build a functional site that does everything we wanted it to do.

What we learned

We learned how to use a new stack.

What's next for cops 4 you

Launch our app, and get real users(businesses, and police officers) working together.
