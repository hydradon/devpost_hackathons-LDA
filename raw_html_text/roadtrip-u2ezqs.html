Inspiration

We were inspired by the copious situations in a car where friends couldn't decide who to listen to next on their Spotify playlist, or the moments where we suffered in silence while listening to an acquaintance's choice of artists that makes you wanna die inside (#neversaynever). So we built an app that makes it easier for you.

What it does

It's an app that takes two artists (you enter an artist you're in the mood for, and your friend enters an artist they're in the mood for) and returns a multitude of options for you to explore.

Other features include a Discover page with the top 20 songs, outside links to artists' bios, and the ability to save your searches and export them to Spotify.
