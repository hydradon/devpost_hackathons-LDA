Inspiration

  • The 36 million self-represnted litigants who have difficult access to legal services

What it does

Our app addresses the challenges and barriers to access, reduces the time required to enter the forms, and presents the information in the form to the user in a more understandable format.

  • So we’ve built a mobile app provides personal access anytime anywhere you need it. (Mobile View)
  • We auto-populate repetitive form questions (Show a form item)
  • And let you literally say what you mean using voice-to-text built into the majority of smartphones
  • Finally, we improve understanding by providing questions in plain english

How we built it

  • We divided the project work into our areas of specialization. Walked through the customer's journey to understand the challenges and we divided the work so that each person could begin work. Dan, Judd, and Taylor developed the app architecture, Bob and Mathias studied the user flows and interface requirements, while Katherine provided the overall subject matter guidance and project inspiration.
