This application is specifically developed to facilitate the Traffic Police Authorities to take action against traffic violations that goes un-accounted for. This application is not a “Police Specific Application” and can be used by anyone to record an ongoing violation. This app will send images/videos of traffic violation to police server. It will help members of the police to monitor traffic irregularities, such as parking in spaces reserved for special needs or driving on the hard shoulder, over speeding etc. The videos/images will be sent to police server where process on videos/images will take place to find the License Plate number of the Car involved in the violation. After the recognition process, where the owner of the car will be identified, a violation ticket will be sent to the owner’s home. This app have many benefits • Time saving. • Ease for police. • Evidence of video. • Anyone can post a Traffic violation to the server.
