What it does

Every day, you check boxes saying what environmentally friendly actions you've performed that day. It keeps track of them in terms of points, and our front ui keeps track of goals and a visual "tree" that grows as you accumulate points.

How I built it

It's an android app, written in Java.

What I learned

How to make an android app, how to design an Android app

What's next for EcoTiles

Adding more functionality, like Google maps search for local recycling centers, comparison among other members, etc.
