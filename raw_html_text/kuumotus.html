Inspiration

An endless jumper game, with a twist - inspired by the melt theme!

What it does

The snowman is at the bottom of a volcano that is starting to erupt. WASD and space move the snowman, left mouse button spawns (with a cooldown) ice blocks to jump on. The idea is to escape the lava and flames that is approaching underneath. Score is given based on height. The game can be also be played as 2 player co-op so that the P2 controls the mouse and spawns blocks for P1 to jump on.
