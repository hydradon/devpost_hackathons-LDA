Inspiration

I am trying to address this for HipChat, using natural language understanding to generate a summary of current status of the room. You take a break from your HipChat Room. When you come back you have no idea what conversation is going on. Now with Summary For HipChat you can generate a recent summary of your room.

What it does

It process the chat conversation, try to understand entities, concepts and keywords to generate easy to understand graphs. Summary For HipChat actually brings Natural Language understanding in the scene to provide sensible summary. That is relevant for practical purpose.

How to use?

Simply install the plugin and hit /report . That's it!
