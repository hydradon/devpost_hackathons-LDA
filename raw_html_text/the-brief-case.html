Inspiration

We were inspired to do a shared VR experience because we believe that many current VR experiences are isolating.

What it does

The Brief-Case is a co-operative local multiplayer game that emphasizes teamwork and communication.

How we built it

The back-bone of this project was the Unity game engine and Google's Cardboard API. Those in combination allowed us to make an interactive experience that was connected players in a immersive and accessible way.

Challenges we ran into

Designing the puzzles such that they could be picked up on a first try, and be engaging for both players, while not being too difficult as to bore the players. Another challenge was working with VR and getting used to developing for a different perspective. (For example, the scale of objects can be disorienting if it is not accurate.)

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Having early play testers pick up the game and immediately understand and enjoy the game. While the game only includes only 3 puzzles, those 3 puzzles are very strong and show a large potential for future designs.
