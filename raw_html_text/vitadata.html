Inspiration

VitaData is a decentralized health data crowdsourcing platform to monitor, aggregate and share data for medical/pharma research purpose based on blockchain technology.

What it does

The platform allows data contributors to

  1. provide continuous data without revealing their ID;
  2. label their health behavior data for better data quality;
  3. own/control what data can be used for medical/pharma research; and
  4. earn tokens for contributed data.
