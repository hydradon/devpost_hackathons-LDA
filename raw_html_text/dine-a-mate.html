Inspiration

Only one out of five people step away from their desks for lunch. Getting outside the office increases productivity and creativity, but most people don't want to eat lunch by themselves at a restaurant.

What it does

This app will allow people to grab lunch with other people that share similar interests instead of eating lunch alone in front of their computers.

How we built it

JavaScript PHP MySQL wamp vue.js Google Maps API

Challenges we ran into

Time constraints, exhaustion

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The app works!

What we learned

The complexities of creating a web app from scratch in 24 hours

What's next for DYNA-MATE

Hopefully, many people will be now lunching together and our team will be proud from a distance
