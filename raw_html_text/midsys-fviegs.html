Inspiration

Startupbootcamp hackathon. Child birth related problem is a great challenge where mortality rate is still high in many countries. We wanted to do something to improve the situation.

What it does

Caring before, during and after childbirth of pregnant women by connecting them to midwives and providing useful interactive contents.

How we built it

We created an android app platform where midwives and woman can come together to make the process of child birth a wonderful experience

Challenges we ran into

Integrating fitness bands with mobile app

Accomplishments that we're proud of

created a prototype highlighting the look and feel of the app

What we learned

we learned how to work in small time and build something functional. Group work and efficient timing.

What's next for midSYS

Make the app fully functional with real data.
