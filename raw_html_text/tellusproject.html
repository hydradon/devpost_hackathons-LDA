Description

The project is a submission for ConUHacks 2020 hosted by HackConcordia.

Problem

According to Canadian Mental Health Association, 49% of people who went through anxiety and depression have never seen a doctor. This application will help the user find a diagnosis and will locate the nearest institution that will provide professional help.

What we learned as a team

We all have learnt something about teamwork, definitely! Each member of our team all learns something new about Python and its frameworks.

Challenges

We encountered difficulties while working to integrate Python's logic process to the front-end side. We have tried Eel, Tkinder, and Django. We got a good time with Django but in the end, we used Php for a better integration with JavaScripts. It works well!! But definitely could be improved.
