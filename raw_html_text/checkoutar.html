Inspiration

Buying a new phone can take long and be tiresome, but it doesn’t have to be. We’re in the information age! In fact, most people walk into the stores already knowing what they’re looking for, and still have to wait in long lines. We’re here to improve that!

What it does

Search for a few phones, compare the loose end features like phone size, and buy in an automated way. It’s NOW that simple

How we built it

Apple AR Kit is a native iOS AR toolkit. Jazz core enables us to integrate with AWS. When an order is placed, Twilio is used to send a text. T-mobile’s DCP apis power our app’s product listing, detailing, and more!

Challenges we ran into

Learning AR Kit, linear algebra, Jazz and DCP api were challenging

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We have something to demo.

What we learned

Most of 3D models are not free and expensive. ARkit is powerful.
