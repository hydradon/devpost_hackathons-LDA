Inspiration

Our inspiration for this project was to help people save time through having a way to get places faster.

What it does

This project takes advantage of third party transportation services, specifically Lime and Bird, and integrates them with Google Maps to help people get places more efficiently and faster.

How we built it

We built this app by scraping the locations for different bikes and scooters from the corresponding apps and integrating them with map kit. We made both a mobile and web app for this project.
