Inspiration

The gameday experience is something else. The whole experience of tailgating, going to the game and the joy when your team pulls through cant be matched. Nobody likes the ticket spam on facebook when last minute plans come up, which is why I created SC Ticket.

What it does

Easy access to buy and sell usc student section tickets.

How I built it

Used swift, firebase, and cocoa pods.

What's next for SCTicket

Scale it to buy and sell other things not just tickets.
