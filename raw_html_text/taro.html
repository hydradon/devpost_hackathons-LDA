What it does

This service will teach planner which project is most appealing to audiences in advance. So, the video distributor (planner) could gather a lot of supporters and investors in the project without fail.

Unlike today's Cloud funding, this service focuses on the video distributing communication, and participants (ex. supporters and investors) can join the project by not only investing but also other support (ex. tips, lend some tools, etc...). In other words, movie distributor (planner), movie viewers (audience), movie supporters, and investors will help each other in a same project.

Why this service is necessary?

These days, more and more people try to appeal their value by distributing video. (Ex: YouTuber etc)

However, almost all of planner can't tell "What type of project could match audience needs". In addition, viewers (audience) can't know "Their support was effective or not".

This service has a possibility to solve both's troubles at the same time. By indicating audience needs to a planner and giving a feedback to the audience.
