ExTree

Collaborative contribution platform

  • An entertainment app for creating short funny/interesting stories anonymously.
  • If a user at some point contributed to a story, he will be send an SMS with a final version of the story once it is finished.
  • The user also has an ability to have a look at best stories on the app and the stories that he/she has lately contributed to.

Implementation

  • Data model (general tree) is flexible and can be adjusted even during the app usage.
  • Parse is used as backend.
  • Twillio is used as one of the ways to deliver the resulting stories to users.

Built With

  • node.js
  • swift
