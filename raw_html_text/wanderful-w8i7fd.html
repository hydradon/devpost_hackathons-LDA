Often times people want to get up and be more active, but they do not know where, when or how to start.

Wanderful connects people who want to explore the great outdoors, enabling them to take on new adventures together!

We used Google Cloud Platform with Python through Flask framework for back end development, html for web app design, and MySQL to store out data.

Learning how to use MySQL and connect front end to back end.

Putting our heads together and doing our best to get our app online.
