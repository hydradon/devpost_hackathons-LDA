Inspiration

In a group chat: A: Hey wanna do lunch? B: Okay C: sure D: Sounds good! E: k ... A: How about at 1:00 pm Wednesday? B: sure C: I'm free D: nah E: can't do Wednesday ... A: When are you guys free? B: Tuesdays and Thursdays work for me, but not from 11:30 am - 1:30 pm C: Wednesdays only, but all day, and also anytime that's not Wednesday D: I'm free pretty much whenever E: when are you free?

and so on! How frustrating!

What it does

It makes scheduling a meetup between multiple people, like lunch, really super easy and not a hassle at all. And it tells you where!

How we built it

Challenges we ran into

Learning how to code and develop for mobile platforms in Xcode and Swift and a bit of Android How to differentiate ourselves from existing "easy scheduling" apps, how to be easier to use than them Developing for two different platforms simultaneously, Android and iOS

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Location, auto-zooming in Google Maps Straightforward, streamline, very simple design

What we learned

Demo-oriented programming, Google Maps API, Xcode, and Swift

What's next for Sched n Me

Databases, remote connections, better interfaces, cooler, more "gamified" look
