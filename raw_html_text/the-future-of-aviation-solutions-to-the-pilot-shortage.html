Inspiration

There is a large number of pilots that are retiring and not enough new pilots to fill the positions. This is a problem that will have widespread consequences if efforts are not made to correct it.

What it does

Our solutions help create more pilots and makes it easier to become a pilot.

How we built it

We interviewed people with expertise and went with our own personal experience to conceptualize and flesh out the solutions.

Challenges we ran into

We are not experienced pilots so we were relying heavily on research and expert testimonials.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We came up with viable solutions to a big problem.

What we learned

We got more knowledge about the airline industry and the massive scale of the pilot shortage.

What's next for The Future of Aviation:Solutions to the Pilot Shortage

We will share our love of aerospace and inspire more young people to get involved with aviation.
