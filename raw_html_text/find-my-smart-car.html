Inspiration

After finding multiple articles of people losing their cars for months and frustrating personal experiences of forgetting where we parked our cars, we decided to create a solution with cars that are able to send their locations.

What it does

After logging in through the Smartcar client portal and registering your vehicle, you are then able to use our application to locate your car with instant directions from Google maps. A visual and description (from computer vision) of your car's surrounding is also provided.

How we built it

Starting with the Smartcar api and the server it provides, we implemented info from there onto a fresh web app.

Challenges we ran into

Retrieving data from the car and making sure Google streetview works with any arbitrary location.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Making multiple api's work together.

What we learned

Implementing api's and learning how to use them.

What's next for Find My Smart Car

Develop a mobile app.
