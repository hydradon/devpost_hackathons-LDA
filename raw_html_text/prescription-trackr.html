What it does

Tracks the different prescriptions the user has. Including the purpose of said medication, when it needs to be taken, the doctor that prescribed it, and what it's supposed to help with.

How I built it

Microsoft's Xamarin.

Challenges I ran into

Saving data so that it's avaliable after the app is closed.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I managed to create a reasonably functional (lol) application, by myself, in 12 hours.

What's next for Prescription Trackr

Adding notifications and photo support.
