Inspiration

Twitter is a great platform for connecting with people, however sometimes it can be time consuming and become a distraction. Humbee is designed to use augmented reality to view tweets around you.

What it does

I experience the world around me and Twitter at the same time. Tweets pop up around you. Think Pokemon Go, but for Twitter

Challenges I ran into

I am new to the augmented reality(AR) field, so getting the application to work was a challenge.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The augmented reality and map integration works.

What I learned

Doing augmented reality work within iOS is ALOT easier than doing it in Unity and then porting it out to iOS

What's next for Humbee

Wokring out the bugs with the Twitter integration
