I've heard the air can make us sick. After conducting research and finding real world examples of this phenomenon,I thought this is a great opportunity to tell the story on the state of air quality and what is being done, and, what we can do to reduce the number of days the Air Quality Index (AQI) exceeds 100.

This application pulls air quality and health related data (static and live) from various government and private agencies and presents the data in a story format communicating complex data in a understandable fashion.
