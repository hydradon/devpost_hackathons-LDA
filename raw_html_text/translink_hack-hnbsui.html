This mobile web-app is designed to allow users to identify busy buses so the Lower Mainland community can better select buses for their commute to avoid traffic. Businesses will benefit from location-based advertisements for commuters since they can better use their time during the commute. Today the app is actively tracked, where users indicate if a bus is busy; with a growing user-base, we will switch to a passively tracked system where the phone's GPS will send us signals if the bus is busy.
