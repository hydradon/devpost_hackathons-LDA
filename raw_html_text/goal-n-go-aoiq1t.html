Inspiration

Motivation can be a huge problem. We wanted a simple way for students to keep their promises to themselves and also reward themselves.

What it does

We incentivize students to keep on top of their goals by breaking down goals into subtasks that can be achieved in a given time block. Once students accomplish the tasks, they can reward themselves with a purchase from Jet.com via Button.

How I built it

Built from the ground up using the Ionic ecosystem for Cordova and Angular.js as the lingua franca.
