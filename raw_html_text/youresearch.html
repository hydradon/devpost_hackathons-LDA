Many university researchers struggle to recruit participants for conducting research study experiments. Even though participation is often compensated with money, a lot of them don't end up participating in research studies. Lack of awareness and poor communication between channels are often the reasons behind why there is a low turnout.

YouResearch allows you to easily connect with university researchers and find studies that match your eligibility criteria and availability. It gives any researcher to post his/her study in a one-stop shop to ease the way people can participate.

YouResearch was built using the MEAN stack hosted on Azure cloud web service running an Azure Database, and the code can be found on github link below.
