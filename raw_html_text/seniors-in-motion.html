Inspiration

Inspired by a summer job at a retirement home. Seeing groups of seniors all perform the same activities with a wide range of results, and in some cases even hurting them. We knew there was a better way.

What it does

Detects the joint motions of the hands and bones with the LEAP-motion camera.

How we built it

Built the back-end with Python and the LEAP-motion visual API.

Challenges we ran into

Trying to visualize the project.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Putting out a final project.

What we learned

Two heads are better than one.

What's next for Seniors in Motion

More statistics and data to improve personalized exercise routines which will best lead to a full recovery. Need more data, doctors, and research in order for this vision to become a reality.
