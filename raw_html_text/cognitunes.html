Ever wished that your Amazon Alexa would play just the right music to fit your mood without you having to tell it exactly what to play?

Just ask Alexa to open Cognitunes, tell Cognitunes what's happening or how you're feeling, and Cognitunes will start a playlist to fit your mood.
