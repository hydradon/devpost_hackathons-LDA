Inspiration

NCWIT challenged us to create an app that would facilitate more face- to- face connections between their aspirations in computing network at conferences, universities, and workplaces.

How it works

uses a database table to keep track of member's contact info and event plans. Adds the capabilities to share to Facebook and other social media platforms.

Challenges I ran into

originally started with cordova but pivoted into android studio, also databases are difficult to parse when there is too much information, and working with fragments in Android Studio can be cumbersome

What's next for NCWIT Networking App

Added functionalities for more connectivity- ex. connect with people around you, people who have been to events in the past with you, people who work with you
