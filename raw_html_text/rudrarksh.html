Inspiration

What it does

The product helps the user to predict risks quickly in realtime and share insights about it. The product helps the user with mitigating the risk in a short term.

How I built it

We built with platform based approach for mitigating different risks from various sources of data providers. The information is generated, assessed for risk in real time and creates a product offering customized for the user.
