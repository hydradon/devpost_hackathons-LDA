Inspiration

The smart home is supposed to make your life easier, right? Well, as of now, it’s almost more complicated to manage your smart home and the various connected devices than it was before. That’s why we invented Infini.

What it does

We don’t really have to say it, but we will. Smart home accessories are undoubtedly becoming one of the most popular things to buy for your house. The ability to make once “dumb” things “smart” is a convenience we didn’t know we needed until a few years ago. There are a mix of devices on the market, and apps that go with them, but the tedious process of managing all of them needs to be a thing of the past. It’s time to stop settling.

Infini is the FIRST app-based smart home hub that has an open API, because it’s the philosophy we believe in.

The easy-to-use interface and beautifully designed touch screen device make managing and organizing all of your smart home devices and internet apps simple. Installation of Infini is a breeze, and accessibility is unlike anything you’ve ever seen before. Keep scrolling to learn more.

By using Infini, you are able to wrangle all of your smart home apps that are on your phone into one convenient smart hub. We’re helping you save time, stay organized and bring your connected home to the next level.
