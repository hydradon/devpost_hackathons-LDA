Inspiration - Tic Tac Toe

What it does - Nine simultaneous games of tic tac toe that turns into one large game of Tic Tac Toe

How we built it - We built it in java with eclipse, we used libgdx to create our game engine and render images/shapes

Challenges we ran into - We originally started a different project, but that ended up falling through when three of the group members got sick. After getting some much needed rest, our team decided to switch gears and throw this game together.

Accomplishments that we're proud of - our title screen

What we learned - we learned that Tic Tac Toe can be fun

What's next for Mega TicTacToe - Incorporating better AI to play against
