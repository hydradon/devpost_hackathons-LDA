Inspiration

Toronto floods, on every hard rain fall. We need to increase the amount of water permeable surfaces in Toronto.

What it does

An app that allows users to take selfies with their green infrastructure. From the selfie, a reward amount is calculate, and the funds are sent to the user from the incentive provider.

How I built it

We have the backend smart contract deployed on the rinkeby network We have web scraper scripts to extract dataset related

Challenges I ran into

We could not get Fortmatic to work on a dapp that's deployed on the skale network.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We got the app to deploy on the skale network We integrated Fortmatic We are using real datasets for reward calculation logic We are solving a real local issue

What I learned

Learned how to deploy on a L2 blockchain

What's next for Flood Chain

If significant interest is garnered for development of a production app, that could be arranged.
