Inspiration

Attentive places allow us to come together to collaborate on new ideas and new projects. We love this city, and the people who are helping us build the future. Attentive.in helps us discover and share the best places in our city to be productive and meet with collaborators in order to get things done.

How it works

It is a platform for sharing your favorite places in the city. Using a geo-based content platform you can add descriptions about your favorite places to work and share them with friends.

Challenges I ran into

Mapping API limits and content creation.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We have a user platform (request an invite at javier+attentive@intentive.info ) so people can log in and publish their favorite places to work from.

The search engine is super quick and helps you find relevant locations.

Collections group together locations in an easy to share format.

What's next for Attentive.in

Contextual visualizations and metadata on all the locations
