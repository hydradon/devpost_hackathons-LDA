Inspiration

In the spirit of apps like Robinhood, we wanted to make investing and managing cryptocurrencies appealing to the regular user by bringing it to them in an easily accessible app.

What it does

Brings cryptocurrency exchange and investment to the common user, presenting information with a simple and easy to read interface.

How I built it

The backend was built using Ruby on Rails, and the front end was built on barebones JS, HTML and CSS.

Challenges I ran into

Making an Android app from a web app from primitive technologies.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We have a great way to visualize the state of cryptocurrencies and how they relate to the user, hopefully making them more accessible to more people.
