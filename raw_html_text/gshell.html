GShell

This is the monorepo containing the GShell core package and all its plugins

The 'What'

GShell is a production-ready, lightweight and easy-to-use abstraction of the main components of a microservice, making it easy to spawn a robust microservice architecture without spending hours on boilerplate.

About

Initially developed in a hackathon by Carlos Rodríguez <@crodriguezanton> and Ramon Pans <@Kylar13> as an internal tool to be used at Goin <@gointech>
