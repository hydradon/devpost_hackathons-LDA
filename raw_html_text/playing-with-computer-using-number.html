What it does

First, you will set the last number. After that computer will start game by choosing number. Each turn, user and computer can choose one or two or three numbers. If computer say 1,2,3 , you will able to put 4 or 4, 5 or ,4,5,6. (choose 3 number adding 1). After that, computer will choose 1 or 2 or 3 numbers. EX If you put 4 after computer, computer might choose 5 or 5,6 or 5,6,7. You will choose the number again. Anyone who type last number will lose.
