Inspiration

College students struggle with classes for many reasons such as Tutoring Centers not being open 24 hours, Not all students learn from failure and Teachers expecting the students to learn everything on their own.

Many College students resort to websites like Chegg to get homework solutions and tutoring, but websites like these charge large fees monthly that can add up to a large financial burden.

We made this app to help support students struggling with school work at zero cost.

What it does

Our Chrome Extension accepts problems as text input and finds chegg solutions for the problem without needing a chegg account.

How we built it

We built it in Javascript, JSON, and XML in a Chrome Extension.
