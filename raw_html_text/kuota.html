Inspiration

There's a hundred different organizations wanting to know you, but there's only one you. How do you give your info to everyone? --Kuota

(Filling out a dozen forms to get SWAG at Cal Hacks)

What it does

Broadcasts your info to all accepting organizations at a certain event. Events/organizations presented are curated by your current location.

Quota is good for small-big organizations, sponsors, booth-runners @ hackathons and conventions looking for a unified,integrated, and end2end check-in platform.
