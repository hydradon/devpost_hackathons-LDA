Inspiration

According to Pew Research Center, nearly half of U.S. adults use voice assistants already; among people 18-49, it’s 55%. Meanwhile, the technology is showing up on more and more devices—computers, phones, thermostats, appliances, televisions, and, yes, smart speakers like Google Home and Amazon Echo. While it’s super difficult to accurately predict what’s coming (just look at this collection of voice assistant predictions and stats), I believe this is a fundamental shift in the way people interact with machines, and we need to start designing for it now. So I thought to take this opportunity to build Alexa skill to perform almost all thigs related to Insurance policy using Power of Alexa ?

What it does

V-Insurance Alexa skill solves your all queries related to Insurance policy. You can get insurance Quote. You can buy insurance policy and can download documents using Alexa skill. No need to type, no need to fill multiple forms. Just talk with Alexa and done. User need to link account so that Alexa will get basic user details along with email address and mobile number

How I built it

I have built using Alexa Skill kit, nodeJs, Express, Amazon lambda, and Solatris Insurance API

Challenges I ran into

Account linking with Alexa to get user information

Accomplishments that I'm proud of
