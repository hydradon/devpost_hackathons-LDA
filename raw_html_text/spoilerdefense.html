Inspiration

We hate spoilers, everybody does!! So we decided to stop this...

What it does

SpoilerDefense is a Google Chrome Extension with the purpose of hiding spoilers in your Facebook news feed. Just select your favorite tv series and the plugin will search potential spoilers while you are reading news in your home page.

How we built it

Using artificial intelligence algorithms to detect faces of the characters of TV series, we use this information to prevent that the user sees unwanted images on his/her Facebook news feed.

Challenges we ran into

Using new APIs for all the team. Using AI algorithms.
