Subtle

  • The joy of dating, combined with the goodness of consent.

  • Anonymous dating is great - it allows you to meet new people without any commitment from either end, you simply match with someone and decide whether or not to continue the conversation in an instant.

  • The problem with websites doing it currently, like Omegle, is that you instantly match with a naked guy and before you can skip, you catch a quick glance that haunts you forever. And this happens multiple times until you finally match with someone decent who turns out to be a bot in the end. And in the rare case that you actually do find someone nice, you have to leave the platform to continue talking to them which hinders your overall experience.

  • Subtle is here to solve all these issues. Subtle is an anonymous dating platform that matches you with people but opens only a text interface, to begin with . Once you get to know the person, you request them to unlock a trust level , which allows you to move to an audio call and similarly, you can move to a video call through mutual consent by adding another level of trust to the call.

  • This avoids all the haunting visuals and ensures that you see people only after you have validated that you'd like to see them. Also, Subtle allows you to maintain the context of the conversations in which you have unlocked a level of trust so that you don't have to move between platforms once you have found a good match.

  • With Subtle, you get the excitement of meeting new people minus the sudden naked video streams.
