Inspiration

Unemployment services are in need of human interactional support services and community

What it does

Neuvo is here to help direct employment-related questions to viable answers from specific industry specialists

How we built it

NEUVO is built to come into play when main services of Työmarkkinatori fail to help unemployees. A good governmental service is built to cover all needs, hence having two circulations: citizen with government and if possibilities run short, supports communication between citizens.

Challenges we ran into

Legal limits, corruptional issues, data security, system transparency, user comfort, simplicity of usage, polite reach to all parties

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Pioneers in this field with governmentally supported citizen communication services

What we learned

Finnish current employment and tax systems, Regional trends on subject, possibilities for future system enhancement,
