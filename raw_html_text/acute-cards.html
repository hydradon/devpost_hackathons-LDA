Inspiration

Helping empower parents to be more involved in the care of their infant.

What it does

Curated cards that guide parents on interventions they can do to help their infant.

How we built it

We built a website, integrated it with google home, and created an app in the Epic playground to autopopulate flowsheet in the EMR.

Challenges we ran into

Issues with access and developing an app in Epic's playground

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Physical product that is approved by a NICU nurse

What we learned

How to collaborate with a fully interdisciplinary team to create a workable product that benefits all sides of the problem.

What's next for aCute Cards

Expanding to include cards for other pediatric inpatient units.
