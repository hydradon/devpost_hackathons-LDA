Inspiration

this project was founded on the needs of two Local nonprofits one looking for a method to grow its beach clean up solution. the other looking to connect art projects with solving real world problems to scale.

What it does

Node waste educates users to reduce waste material contamination, allows users to join campaign in direct effort to divert waste from landfills, incentivises users to be more active in this new way of life respecting the planet.
