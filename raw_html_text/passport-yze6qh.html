Inspiration

Worker in UAE unable to return to Abu Dhabi because Passport was withheld by his employer

What it does

It creates an opportunity for a more secure, and more convinient future without the hassle of working about forging identity or identity theft.

How I built it

Using HashtaX as the basis for the Block Chain used to secure the information inside

Challenges I ran into

Teammates left the group, no knowledge of JavaScript, no knowledge of HashtaX

What I learned

Understanding Block-chain better, as well as improved knowledge on Raspberry Pi and JavaScript

What's next for Passport

Passport could potentially be the future of identity, where everyone is who they are, and no one could forge a stolen/fake identity. Passports becomes something that you permanently hold without it ever getting lost or withheld
