What is Socialize?

Socialize is my project for the Shopify VanHackathon Challenge. The main goal of this app is to provide a central panel to manage marketing actions and campaigns in social networks, like Twitter, Facebook, Instagram.

How it works?

When the Shopify user create an account in Socialize, the app will fetch all products in sale in the store. Then, the user can create publication campaigns for each product, which will be posted on social networks (only Twitter at this moment). The user will be able to monitor and manage what people are saying about his products and also about his store.
