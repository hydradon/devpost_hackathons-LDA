Inspiration

The inspiration for Bonfire Fitness came from personal experience. After experiencing the difficulty that many naive beginners in the fitness industry face when dieting, I wanted to provide those new to the industry with the correct resources to create both a healthy mind and body. That is exactly what Bonfire Fitness does.

What it does

It takes user data- height,weight, level of daily activity, and gives the user an idea of what he/she should expect to eat during a weekly basis. We even tailored the app to help people who want to gain weight, lose weight, or maintain weight using sample meals with nutritional values included; the end goal, of course, was to help people not experienced in nutrition to improve their bodies in a safe and healthy way.
