VillageSim

A simulation of a village at both the daily and century level.

So the village starts off with 50 people. These people have names, ages, hair colors, and other things like that.

Every day, people will interact with each other causing them to start to like, hate, or not care about certain people. Eventually people will get married and have children passing on genes and family names.

However, people don't live forever and eventually. Some people may even be murdered. As time goes on, the older generations will die out and be replaced by newer generations.

You can run controlled simulations and choose to go day by day, month by month, or year by year. Or you can run the uncontrolled simulation and see the results of centuries.
