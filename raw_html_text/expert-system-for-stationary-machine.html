What it does

It appears to reduce queues in hospitals by non-human consultation. Main case is to show a user right way to the doctor he needs, by completing simple questions' line. I think some people(i'm not an exception) goes to queue wrong doctors' door and then go to another queue. For example, my granny never been in Kosice and fall out of the stair and hit an arm, she came to the hospital but without knowledge of where surgeon is, she started searching by walking random doors, she thinks that where a queue is, there are a doctor(recepiant) she needs. And after staying in queue she got the knowledge but she needs to stay another queue again. So, i believe it will help all ages people to improve their health and improve quality of medicine.
