Inspiration

Make shopping easy for everyone through an assistant AI chatbot.

What it does

Your full-on shopping assistant takes picture of the desired product and gives recommendations from multiple e-commerce websites based upon your preferences.

How I built it

Microsoft BotFramework, Azure bot services, ShopStyle API, VISA API, SeekXR and CloudSight combined with love and dedication gave birth to this project. Can be used on various messaging platforms, gives recommendations of products similar to what you capture an image of. Enables you to buy the product and checkout with VISA with a single click. It also adds an reminder to your calendar and sends out the receipt to your email.
