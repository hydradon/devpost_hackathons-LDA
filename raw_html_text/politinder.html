• PoliTUM Startpage

Inspiration

Saving Democracy or at least helping people to easily inform themselves not just about the parties, but about the people.

What it does

Our service provides an awesome opportunity to get to know our politicians in detail. In contrast to many others just asking for their opinion for some topics, we decided to use past decisions in referendums as a kind of groundtruth.

How we built it

We used a huge variety of different services, tools and frameworks to assemble our product

Challenges we ran into

sleep deprivation, brainstorming, communication, smaller issues

Accomplishments that we're proud of

as software devolpers, it was a challenge to build a good working frontend

What we learned

a lot about developing in a team with lots of different and new frameworks

What's next for PoliTUM

more questions, more politicians
