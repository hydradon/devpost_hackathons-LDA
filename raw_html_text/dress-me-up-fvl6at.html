Inspiration

As Women, we love to shop, shop and shop! But how many times have we worn the same piece of expensive clothing more than once a month?We dont like repeating clothes at functions,parties and other social events. We dont mind wearing our friends clothes so why not expand this friendship all over?

What it does

The application shows you all the dresses available at the moment and you can either choose to rent an item or put an item out for rent.

How we built it

We designed the layout using HTML and CSS. We used Firebase as our database and Javascript to connect the front end to the Database.

Challenges we ran into

A lot of functionality for setting up accounts and validating the fields. Getting data updated live from the database

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The design of the application The idea to prefer to show as much information as possible without loading a new page

What we learned

We learnt connections and problems faced when you connect to databases and the checks and validations necessary.

What's next for Dress me up

We would like to take it to the next level by using social networking sites like twitter to advertise your item
