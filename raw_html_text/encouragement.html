Inspiration

Seeing how some of my friends lacked encouragement and gave up on projects half way through.

What it does

The app displays encouraging words every time you press the start button.

How I built it

I used unity and c#, I used random.range to generate a random number between 1 and 10 and that number would then determine what string would be used.
