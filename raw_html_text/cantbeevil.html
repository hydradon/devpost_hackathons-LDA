Inspiration

Blockstack challenges all-knowing services like Facebook and Google that infringe on your privacy. But to what extent is it true that their proposed alternative Can't Be Evil?

How It Works

We're making an interactive exposé about the privacy implications of decentralized apps - an investigation into decentralized apps, privacy and security, delivered using the medium of a dapp.

Learn hands-on what dapps get to know about you, based on the actual data available to our open source Blockstack dapp. Subscribe to the project on Product Hunt to support and get updated about our progress.
