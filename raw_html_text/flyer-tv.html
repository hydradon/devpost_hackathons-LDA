A Fire TV app for viewing travel related content along with information for arranging travel to featured destinations.

With an almost endless supply of popular travel related content available on TV streaming platforms, Flyer TV sets out to provide viewer with the resources needed to compare and book travel to the vary locations that they are watching.

The app allows video browsing and playback for travel related content and reads content metadata to identify locations featured. Using the United Airlines API , Flyer TV finds all available flights from the viewer's home airport to the featured location and filters for the cheapest possible fares as well as options over holidays and presents them to the user.

Flyer TV is and Android TV app build in java with data managed on InterSystems .
