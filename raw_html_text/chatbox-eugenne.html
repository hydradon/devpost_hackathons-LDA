Inspiration

We wanted to create a person who you can talk to and make you laugh during class or when you just need comfort

What it does

He provides support on telling time through GMT time and also he provides jokes to give you a laugh

How we built it

We built it with Cisco Spark by connecting boxes and little coding to create a chatbot that is functional

Challenges we ran into

What if someone does not say certain things the correct way would he still respond

Accomplishments that we're proud of

He is someone you can see as a friend with limited resources

What we learned

That anything can be created you just have to have the mind to do it
