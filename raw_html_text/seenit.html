Inspiration

We wanted to do something alcohol related, but Swift said we would give Rutgers a bad name ): We thought really really really hard about what we wanted to do that would be beneficial to everyone and pretty awesome too. Michelle took from her own experiences and realized that when she was in Maine, she wanted to take a scenic route down the highways, but there wasn't an efficient way to do it. That's what Seen It does!

How it works

The user inputs their location and their chosen final destination. Seen It provides them with a list of amazing things to do that has been highly reviewed on Google with the directions to the sites they want to see along the way.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of
