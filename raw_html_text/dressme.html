Inspiration

the possibility of changing your look with a literally infinite number of styles

What it does

it takes a photo of you, your chosen style and changes your top and bottom with closes of your style

How I built it

we took a pre-trained segmentation neural network to detect person's top and bottom, a GAN that changes them, we deployed them of the server and created a UI for uploading photo and selecting style

Challenges I ran into

deploy nets on server

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

another use case of GANs

What I learned

how to develop react app

What's next for dressme
