Inspiration

The inspiration for this game was primarily from Splatoon and another Roblox game called Doomspire's Brickbattles.

What it does

Turf Wars has two teams, 10 players max on each team. The map is split up into 22 different sections called Turfs where teams present.

How I built it

We built it using Roblox Studio.

Challenges I ran into

Since we were just amateurs and still learning, we ran into many syntax and logical errors in Lua.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We are proud of our creative map design along with our unique turf capturing system.

What I learned

We learned more about GUI's and new Roblox libraries, services and features.

What's next for Turf Wars

We will try to get a second map built along with fixing all the most important bugs. We want to add more weapons to Turf wars along with more features.
