Impact

Impact is an app that allows you to look through and learn about trusted and highly rated non-profit organizations. With a click of a button, you are brought to the donation page where you can make an impact on issues and causes you are passionate about. The list of organizations has been carefully created so you can trust that these charities are not only transparent and accountable, but are also effective in using your contributions. The featured page is regularly updated so you can see which organizations are most relevant to current events. Impact is the perfect answer to when you ask “How can I help?”.
