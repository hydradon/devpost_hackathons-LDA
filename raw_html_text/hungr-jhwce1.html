Inspiration

We have always been looking for free food so we thought of an app that can serve us food for free and at the same time prevent food waste.

What it does

Notifies students whenever there is surplus free food after an event and organizers can post about it anytime. Students with edu accounts will get the notification.

How we built it

Android Studio. Using java and firebase database.

Challenges we ran into

Firebase realtime database connectivity.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The idea and serving all the students looking for food, learning, and also teaching students without android and firebase experience.

What we learned

Firebase, android, teamwork

What's next for Hungr

To add image detection features to make it work by one click and let the students know about free food events by notifications.
