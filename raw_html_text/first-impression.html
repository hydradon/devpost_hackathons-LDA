Inspiration

Within five years of release, about 76.6% of prisoners are arrested again. The prison system takes these people out of society and then expects them to reintegrate and become good citizens upon release. However, after leaving prison most people feel lost and could be left without transportation, source of income, or even housing. The only way to combat chronic crime is to take active steps in providing resources to help people become productive members of their community.

What it does

First Impression provides valuable information and resources including: job opportunities, financial resources, transportation information, reentry programs, legal resources. First Impression links directly to job opportunities that don't discriminate against people with criminal records, and also provides professional attire sources, resume builder and interview information. We have extensive reentry program information and also provide information to aid in a healthy transition to everyday life.

How we built it

We used JHipster to create a spring-boot back-end with angular front-end. Python is used to scrape information from the web. H2DB provides a database to store all of the information we provide. We use google-cloud platform and hosted it on kubernetes to create a highly scalable web app.

Challenges we ran into

We wasted a lot of time trying to get authentication working using keycloak, but eventually decided that it would be better to forgo a login system so our app could be as accessible as possible

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The web scraping Having an app that looks good Feeling like we could make the difference for someone thinking of committing crime to survive Staying up for 24-hours

What we learned

Deploying an app using JHipster Don't use keycloak for anything other then a built in function and even then....

What's next for First Impression

Aggregate more information on the site, possibly reach out to businesses and organizations for specific needs.
