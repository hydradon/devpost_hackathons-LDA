• Trackin Network - Paid to Play

We were inspired by the idea of introducing cryptocurrency to the gaming market and developing a p2p community around gameplay stats and crypto.

Trackin aka TRK rewards users with KIN for achievements in their game play and will facilitate p2p interactions with KIN

We are currently building using a combination of Web and Mobile App views

We are still in the process of building and look forward to sharing our story and what we learned!

We are looking forward to seeing users introduced to crypto for the first time and seeing TRK potentially making waves in the crypto and gaming space.
