Inspiration

I love writing on whiteboard, but it lacks memory, I want to save all my whiteboarding moments

How it works

It works by sensing touch using ir frame that is mounted on a normal whiteboard

Challenges I ran into

Resolution of ir touch is low. drawing perfect curve with low resolution data was the challenge.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned

I learnt about different touch technologies and how they work, I learnt coffee script

What's next for Portkey

Making this whiteboard ready for market
