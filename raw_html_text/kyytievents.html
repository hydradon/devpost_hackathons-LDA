Inspiration

Talking with Kyyti and Finnair people about their needs for hackathon projects. We came up with idea about finding events and travel destinations that really interest users.

What it does

When you link your Facebook and Spotify accounts, it generates the music and events you like and according to that app starts to offer events that user might like. Perfect when travelling abroad to new cities and finding events you didn't even know there was. Check our UI sketches to see more!

How we built it

Our developer got sick with high fever, so we couldn't finish the app, therefore we have only UI skecthes our designer made during hackathon with Photoshop.

Challenges we ran into

Major challange was our developer getting sick.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Our idea is easy to implement and finalizing the UI sketches with user-friendly flow.

What we learned

Don't get sick during hackathon!

What's next for KyytiEvents

Depending on Kyyti's and Finnair's interest, we can start making app real, after our developer gets healed.
