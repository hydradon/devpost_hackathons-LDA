Inspiration

VR, 3D movies and live shows, live concerts anywhere and international movies access around the world.

What it does

The app/portal lets you watch new released movies from cinema or film production companies, live music or concerts, live sports, live TV or entertainment shows anywhere in the world in VR 3D view from comfort of your home. It's like you're there and part of it.

How we built it

We outsource some of the VR related stuff and other coding is in-house

Challenges we ran into

the only challenge we are facing and we are solving which is never been tried, is playing normal flat videos in 3D stereoscopic format without creating or converting content into 3D first.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Developing solution

What we learned

VR needs more research and development support. there are a lot of opportunities to make it better and more realistic.

What's next for VOOD VR, A Virtual Teleportation App

Having partnership with either Warner Bros or ATT or both to bring next generation entertainment to people homes. Also we will build an innovative hardware solution which will solve many VR related issues.
