• LOVESENSE MOCKUP

Inspiration

Taking care of yourself and knowing how your loved ones are doing

What it does

Lovesense connects loved ones and trusted partners to one single well-being service

How we built it

With the combination of open source applications and private sector funding

Challenges we ran into

We prioritised the business model and the value proposition over technical implementation.

Accomplishments that we’re proud of

We are proud of that we have responded to society scale trends of raising average age and urbanization

What we learned

Lovesense could fill the market gap between competitors. There isn’t a service which could connect cost efficiently several different devices and services into one service.

What’s next for LOVESENSE

Prototyping, validating, interviewing and incrementally improving the service.
