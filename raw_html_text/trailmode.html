Inspiration

Inspired by how unprepared my devices are in-sync with me while I'm Hiking, Biking, Paddling, Horse riding, Bird watching, Photographing, Horticulturing, and/or doing other exciting thing while perusing a trail outdoors. Need better mobile experience that's tailored to my activity on the trail!!

What it does

Right now, it looks cute, really cute, In the next 3 hours Firebase will be closely in-tune with dynamically choosing the best TrailMode for your mobile/tablet while on the trail: In short this means the following.

  • Choosing "must-have" functionality and using the Ionic Wrapper to access native code on iOS/Android giving you permission to turn-off functionality you don't want while on the trail.
  • Choosing "user-submitted" features and tools that work only within the TrailMode app and give you the ability to better experience your activities while on the trail
  • Optimizing your phone live while you're on the trail.
