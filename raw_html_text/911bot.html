Inspiration

When a friend of mine fainted i didn't knew what to do until help arrives. This feeling of in competency is the worst feeling possible. 911Bot is our vision to how all 911 services will look and behave in 5-10 years from now. "Whoever saves one soul, Scripture accounts it as if he had saved a full world"

What it does

The first minutes of any emergency situation are critical for the survival of the participants. 911 bot connects you to 911 and passes all the needed information about the case and then connects the call. In the meanwhile it guides you step by step about what are the first action that are needed to do in order to improve the survival chances of the injured people.

How we built it

We built it using meteor.js and the facebook messenger api

Challenges we ran into

Defining the tree of actions and creating the cases

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Making the first functional chat bot that saves lives

What we learned

Twillio is even more awesome than we already knew, That there's a great domain extension available out there (.club)

What's next for 911Bot

Deep integration with 911 services, Smart "triage" algorithm and case prioritizing, Smart and more effective routing to relevant operators, Case management system for operators
