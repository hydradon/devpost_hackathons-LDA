I created Good Steward so that it can provide tips and tricks to responsibly manage problems you may encounter in the world. Ask it how to deal with issues like spiders, ants, bees, weeds, poison ivy, tires, and magazines.

You can also log actions you do daily like recycle, compost, or garden.

Future enhancements may include having a weekly or monthly stats listing for actions performed.
