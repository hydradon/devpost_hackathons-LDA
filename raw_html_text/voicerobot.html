Inspiration

How about just using your voice to control the Mekamon

What it does

Allows full control of all aspects of the Mekamon Robots including Movement (Translations, Animations & Kinematics), Ability (for gaming) & Mode settings. Allows for new highly engaging game play using just your voice

How I built it

BLE peripheral controlled from a Central also acting as a gateway to Alexa via PubNub.
