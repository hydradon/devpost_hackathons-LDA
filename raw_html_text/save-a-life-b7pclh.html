Inspiration

We have learned and seen multiple news stories that reflect police brutality.

What it does

** It is a free app created by students to combat police brutality. There have been lots of scenarios where the police is taking advantage of minorities beacuse they feel have complete power.

How I built it

We used the website Mockingbird to make the app and created an platform to help myself.

Challenges I ran into

Not knowing how to create an app nor being able to create a product in time frame.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am proud of my team for working hard together and being able to create an app,website, and powerpoint to show our ideas.

What I learned

I learned that drawing and brainstorming is important to discover the best ideas. Also that dividing the work will speed up the process a long with a set checklist.

What's next for Save a Life

The next step for Save a Life is to share it to consumers and have social media platforms expose the service
