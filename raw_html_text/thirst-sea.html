What it Does

Most people don't drink enough water during the day. This game records both your water consumption and gives you an incentive to drink more water!

About the Game

This is a water consumption tracking game, made fun with fish collectables! As you drink more water during a day, fish will come visit. Click on the fish to add it to a fish book and to record it.
