What it does and How we built it

Have you heard of Dwarf Fortress? Have you ever wanted to customize or randomize your own Dwarf Fortress Abomination? Welcome to Monster Mash (C)(R)(TM 2018 - All Rights Reserved) This repository includes a script that scrapes the information from all of Dwarf Fortresses wonderful creatures that take the form of text files. Attributes are parsed from these files that allow you to make you very own creature, complete with a website UI. BUT WAIT!!! You can also visit the community page to see what random monsters everyone else has been making! (As long as they are named)
