Who we are

The Knowledge Roundtable is an online marketplace for tutors and students. We have over 900 tutors listed, and our network is growing fast!

Our mission is to help facilitate important connections between students and educators. We simplify the tutor search process so you can focus on learning.

The problem

Searching for tutors on conventional directories is cumbersome and unpleasant. Finding a great tutor is quite difficult when using traditional tutor services.

The solution

We are simplifying the process with a streamlined interface, which allows parents to filter their results with simplicity, then swipe through tutors to select the ones they'd like to contact.
