Inspiration

Woebot created by the godfather of AI made us feel inspired to help the community with chatbots. We use Siri, Alexa, and Galaxy everyday, but none of them apply to us educationally.

What it does

It repsponds to students questions about : 1. What textbooks to use, 2.Which teachers to reach out to, and 3. Emotional assistance and guidance in scenarios of extreme stress and anxiety

How we built it

We made a Java servlet with Tomcat server. We also made a Mongo DB database with all of the professor's and textbook's names but didn' get a chance to integrate it with the servlet.

Challenges we ran into

We had trouble trying to integrate the database and the servelt.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to deploy a servelet with Tomcat.

What we learned

-mySQL database, working with servelets and apis
