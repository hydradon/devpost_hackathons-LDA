Play a game of Rock'em Sock'em controlled via your foot and arm!

Our website, built with AngularJS the css framework Foundation, lets two competitors create a session using their Twitter handles and start playing with our bots. The webpage displays the scores as the players' bots hit each other, which are in real time thanks to Firebase. The first player to 5 points wins! Once the game is over, our Twitter account (@beatembots) tweets the final score at the two players. Also check out our past scores table to see who beats 'em all.
