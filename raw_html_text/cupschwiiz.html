• Scan for refund

Inspiration

WIth 8 Million plastic and paper cups being trashed each day in Switzerland (almost one per person!) we need to be drastic and cut the plastic.

What it does

While maintaining the convenience of one-way cups, we help you to live green and clean. Buy the coffee in a reusable cup. While enjoying the beverage scan the QR code on the cup for getting the refund as soon as you drop the cup in the queue-free quick-drop bins. No need for new apps: use TWINT or Swisspass or enter your paypal account. The cup gets recognized via RFID, as soon as it is dropped in the bin, which is linked to the QR code. Alternatively return the cup at a coffee store in order to get cash.

How we built it

Code: https://github.com/mpschr/START19-DCN

Tools: Python, R, Open Data from Swisscom and SBB and admin.ch as well as a online website tool.

Challenges we ran into

The mobile cell journey data set for entire CH must be purchased from Swisscom

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Perfect User Story: No registration needed, no unnecessary data collection, full convenience, simple pricing and access for everybody

What we learned

There is a lot of potential in waste - we can save up to 670 trees per day. It can be easy to make a difference
