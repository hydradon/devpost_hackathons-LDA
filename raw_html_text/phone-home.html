Inspiration

For those times that you just want to go home. No need to check anything yourself. Phone Home calculates the best ride and gets you on your way. Inspiration to predict when you need a ride from thinking about how to use Sense360

How it works

Your home address is estimated form Sense360 or you can enter it manually. Open the app and the best ride is calculate. All you have to do is request that ride. Phone Home only integrates with Uber, Lyft, Sidecar, Flywheel, EZCar, and Via currently. Text based (thanks to Twilio!) predictions on when a user may need a ride is calculated server based on the average amount of time a user is usually at a location. Phone Home will text the user 5-10 minutes before we anticipate them leaving. Timestamps of when a user uses Phone Home at locations is also taken into affect to determine when to text them.
