Inspiration

My grandfather used to say that once in your life you need a doctor, a lawyer, a policeman and a preacher but every day, three times a day, you need a farmer. -- Brenda Schoepp

Identification of newer technologies as part of the HiMSS - APM taskforce to identify technical solutions and governance of healthcare usecases centered around outcome based payments

What it does

A private blockchain solution based on the Qtum blockchain integrated with an E-commerce storefront targeted towards small business operators and promoting consumer choice driven incentives.

How I built it

Downloaded, and built Qtum, implemented a smart contract to automatically pay and document how much of the consumer's percentage of a food dollar is going to the agricultural producer, and prototyped integrations with two E-commerce systems.

Challenges I ran into

Finding an odoo plugin and a working Magento plugin that supported bitcoin-rpc proved to be more than could be accomplished in 24 hours.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Writing a 14 line Qtum Solidity contract that worked the first time. Mapping a solution to integrate two extremely important and complex chains - food safety and healthcare outcomes.

What I learned

We ended up with a solution to a much bigger problem than either of us realized at the beginning

What's next for HealthBucks

Find a health payer to issue tokens and start collecting data
