Inspiration- being constantly frustrated by finding cool pictures online but never finding good prints of them

What it does- streamlines the process of creating posters and having them shipped to you

How I built it- using html, css, javascript, and the Walgreens and PostMates APIs

Challenges I ran into- the APIs were much more difficult to implement than we anticipated, resulting in a bit of a rush at the end of the event.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of- making a sleek chrome extension and a visually pleasing website.

What I learned- never to underestimate any seemingly small aspect of a project.

What's next for JiffyPrint- who knows!
