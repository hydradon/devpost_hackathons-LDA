Inspiration: People do not know what they can or cannot recycle

What it does: inform user of which plastics are locally recyclable

How I built it: Using android studio to develop a mobile app that interface with Mongodb database hosted on AWS cloud account and leverage custom-built national database to recycle statistics

Challenges I ran into: database connectivity with Microsoft product

Accomplishments that I'm proud of: successful completion of a minimum viable product (MVP).

What I learned: android mobile app development.

What's next for Team_22: make it more exclusive by adding more countries
