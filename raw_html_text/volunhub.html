Inspiration

Our product, Volunhub, is built around the story of a high school student Jenny. She wants experiences to broaden her skills, and she also needs 40 hours of volunteering to graduate high school. There are thousands of people just like Jenny, who want to volunteer but have trouble finding the right opportunities.

We empathized with the users and focused on the central problem. Then we developed a solution that connects volunteer candidates with non-profits.
