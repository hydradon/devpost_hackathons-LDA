Inspiration

The Chandler Christian Community Center currently has a web site with lots of good information on it but it is hard to navigate, has a good amount of out dated information, and it does not look good on mobile devices.

What it does

We revamped their web site making a fully responsive web site, cleaned up the information on it, and make it easier to navigate.

How We built it

AngularJS, Bootstrap, MongoDb, CSS 3, Html 5, Javascript, Heroku, NodeJs

Challenges I ran into

Mis-understanding of requirements that originally drove us down a different route.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We are re-grouped and turned out a great web site that they could go into production with today.

What I learned

More up front design and requirements gathering. Need more face time with the non-profit.

What's next for Chandler Food Bank - Helping Those In Need

Turn over the site to the Chandler Food Bank.
