Inspiration

I got spooked by a friend. Instead of manually sending the ASCII image to seven of my friends, I decided to create a website that would do the job for me and spook many more people.

What it does

This website uses frightening imagery of the undead to incite fear upon the watchers. Upon threats of 'skilentons' (not 'skeletons') eating the viewer, they must send seven links to seven of their friends, sending them to the same website that would spook them and force them to share with seven of their friends. To maximally spook the victims, clickbait article URLs are generated to mislead the viewer into the website.

What's next for Spooker

World domination of spooking and scaring.
