As members of the Science Olympiad Team of SBHS, we decided that publicity was important to our club to increase attendance. Therefore, we decided to make this website.

It has information pertaining to our team and how you can reach us if you need to or have any questions.

We used HTML and CSS to create our website, and used JavaScript for some features.

We had some issues with the CSS and it didn't look right a lot of times.

We used JavaScript to create a moving picture slideshow on the homepage.

We learned that while coding something may seem easy or shallow, it's no simple task and takes a lot of careful effort and time.

In the future, we will try to get a domain for our website, and we will also add more information and features, such as competition history.
