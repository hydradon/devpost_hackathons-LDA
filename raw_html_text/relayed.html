Relayed is a web application which automatically matches local businesses with online influencers. It creates of a database of influencers based on various social networking stats. Relayed parses influencers social accounts that sign up and look for a specific criteria . Details like: account of followers, how much attention they get per post, how localized their followers are, etc. The influencers are then put into categories based on categories and we now have a database of influencers for the ability to advertise items effectively. The next step is the business then creates a profile and puts in various details as well. Business name, business location, target audience, type of business etc. Relayed then automatically matches the business with a specific collection of influencers. Relayed then create a custom campaign based on the information provided by the business.
