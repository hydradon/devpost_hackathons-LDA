Inspiration

Ineffective and overworked teachers are often why students from rural and/or underperforming Schools and Colleges find themselves unable to compete with their peers with much better teachers.

We decided to level the playing field.

What it does

TeachMe is a subscription-based smart classroom that enforces quality standards for students. Schools interested in the service we provide pay for their students to enroll and learn from the nation's leading teachers in our smart classrooms.

With real-time live streams and Q and A interactions with Professors and his or her students, we offer School's top-notch standards of education for less.

How I built it

For the frontend we have used bootstrap, react, pure CSS and HTML5. For the backend, we used nodejs / javascript. Webrtc for live video streaming.

Challenges I ran into

Integrating webrtc in the platform.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned

Webrtc, pure CSS.

What's next for TeachMe

Testing services for schools for online examination. Forums for every classroom.
