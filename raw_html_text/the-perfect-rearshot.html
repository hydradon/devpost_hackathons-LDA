Inspiration

Try to take a selfie with the rear camera... NOT EASY!

How it works

Point your back camera to your face and when it detects your face the app starts making a sound and when the face is completely centered it makes a different noise indicating the face is centered.

Challenges I ran into

Time difference of exactly 12 hours, Singapore to New York was a major factor.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We built the app in less than 12 hours and some of it was done in a car on I-95 and the other was half drunk.

What I learned

NOT TO PROCRASTINATE

What's next for The Perfect Rearshot

Enable aut0matic photocapture seamlessly easing back selfie. Also make sure the size of the face isn't too large.
