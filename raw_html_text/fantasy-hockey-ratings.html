Inspiration

Ever have a hard time finding the player that fits your needs in fantasy hockey? Current tools available to the user are primarily ESPN articles and tools behind a subscription paywall. I wanted to make an easy to use tool for fantasy hockey owners (like me!) to use.

What it does

fantasy-hockey-ratings provides an easy way to find players that fit your criteria in terms of head-to-head categories.
