Inspiration

Inspiration is to let gamers learn how to cook while playing game.

What it does

A game with fun experience on how to cook multiple food items from different cities. With rewards and good experience.

How I built it

It is totally designed in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop built in Unity with C#.

Challenges I ran into

Animations with the static images so have to design multiple sprites for single animation.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

A really good response for beta version on Google Play Store.

What I learned

So many things, Most of it how to work with Facebook pixel and Audience Network.

What's next for Cooking CIty - Mad Chef's Restaurant

We are trying to build it in 3D. So the experience is more immersive.
