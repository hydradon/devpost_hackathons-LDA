SocialTutor

This web app is designed to pair together those that love to teach, and those that want want to learn. I-Know-This allows you to select topics that you may be knowledgeable about, and be placed onto a databse for others to contact you with questions, or to set up study / tutor sessions. I-Know-This is a perfect application for sharing talents, and finding individuals who may need tutoring services.
