What it does

When you visit a city, you often ask your friends there what are events to check out, places to eat and things to see. They would list it to you, but we thought this experience could be more seamless and engaging. TripFlow allows locals to go on it and it will query their Facebook events, social media to list points of interest on a map. You then select points of interest to form a travel guide.

People can view the most loved travel guides for different cities. It differs from existing travel apps in that, TripFlow is more about events and what locals would do in the city vs tourist attractions
