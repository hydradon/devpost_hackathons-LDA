Inspiration

Often we find ourselves doing the same old routine when going to the gym. Progress is extremely hard to make when you're running out of ideas on what to do.

What it does

At this current moment, it provides the user with 68 work outs for specific targeted muscle groups.

How I built it

A huge spiderweb organized with each muscle area -> actual muscle -> workouts provided -> diagrams

Challenges I ran into

The main challenge was making this app as simple as possible with fewest words possible, but also informative enough to give the user enough ideas on what to work out on.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Well, this project was started 3 hours into the Hack-a-thon, and was made from complete scratch. The app's idea is to provide every single workout in an aesthetically nice and user-friendly environment.

What I learned

A ton about work out plans, and the story board used in Xcode.

What's next for Gainz

Well, this app is easily updatable. We'll just keep uploading more and more workouts to provide users with a crazy variety of workouts. We also plan to update this app for not only body building, but also for weight loss and nutrition, catering to different people's goals.
