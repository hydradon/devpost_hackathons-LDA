Inspiration

Not all areas are safe. We want to help you avoid dangerous places. Helpful for tourists who are new to the area.

How it works

The app shows the map around you using ESRI API, and splits it into neighbourhoods. The criminal zones (red) differ in color from safe zones (blue).

What's next for CrimeRadar

  • Closest police stations and ambulances.
  • Possibility to share your location data with emergency contacts.
  • SOS call.
  • Ability to report an incident.
