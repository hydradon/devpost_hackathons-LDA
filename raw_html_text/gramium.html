Inspiration

Gramium is trying to help rural people to develop skills and get loan for their small business. NGO help to get small loan to this rural people and also become guaranteer for their loan. As there are so many people are getting help from Gramium, It is hard for Gramium to keep track of all portfolios on traditional notebook and Excel sheets. There are two main problem. Because of their traditional operations methodology, they are not able to scale much. and second is, they are facing financial issues because of loan defaulters who fails often to pay loan instalments on time.

What it does

Our system notifies the loan taker about load loan payment dues and deadline.

How we built it

We used Twilio and django .

Challenges we ran into

None.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Made alert system successful with admin portal live.

What we learned

Team Work. Collaboration.

What's next for Gramium

With technology, they will scale and reduce the defaulter events.
