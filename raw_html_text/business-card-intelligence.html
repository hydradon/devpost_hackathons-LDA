Inspiration

Business cards an important part of society to connect with others, especially in a formal setting. We aimed to develop a mobile application that solves the difficulties of managing your contacts through a physical business card.

What it does

Through our mobile application we were able to allow a user to create an account, select an image from the gallery on their mobile phone and use machine learning to identify texts on the business card.

How we built it

We built it using Google's ML Kit, Firebase and Java.

Challenges we ran into

Because it was a team of two, making a native mobile application took longer than we thought.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are happy to have learnt the use of the ML Kit and the impacts it can have.
