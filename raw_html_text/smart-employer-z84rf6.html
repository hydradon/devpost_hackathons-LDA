Hostages of the Binary Dungeons wanted to urge the skilled yet unemployed population of Singapore to start working from home.

The app 'Labo - The Smart Employer' provides a platform for the consumers to reach out to the producers of servies and extend their skills.

We initially worked on Android Studio but we had a shortage of time and hence couldn't complete the app on it as we were working on CTF too. Therefore, we used Thunkable to create the app.

We learnt authentication on Android Studio, using firebase models, integrating visual and speech recognition.

An extension of the app would be to extend it to the disabled.
