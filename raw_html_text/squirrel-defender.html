Inspiration

I got an internship at the ISU's Virtual Reality Applications Center this semester and I now have a new passion for AR and VR and wanted to put my skills to the test.

What it does

its a 15x15 ft. game board that you are able to see in the real world around you. You set turrets up to shoot down jayhawks to protect to HackISU Squirrel.

How I built it

I used Unity and Visual Studios.

Challenges I ran into

Building and deploying the game onto the Hololens is a major pain. Also difficult to test applications.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I did create a simple tower defense game.

What I learned

I learned how to create a video game using Unity and learned a lot of new tricks with using Unity and Visual Studios

What's next for Squirrel Defender

Trying to get it to fully work on the HoloLens
