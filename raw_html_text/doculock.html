Inspiration

Everyone is moving towards a paperless world and yet there is no central place to have all of your personal data such as your ID, Social Security Card, bank statements, pay stubs, etc... It would also be great to not only get what they receive such as the credit score or background check in return, but to also have a central place to see who has your data and restrict access after a certain time.

What it does

It puts all of your data and documents in one place where companies or individuals can easily request it and then give you what they received in return.

How we built it

We spent many countless hours running on little sleep putting together the front end and back end.

Challenges we ran into

Limited time and being a weekday we had to work on our day jobs while hacking. We aimed for the stars on this task.

Accomplishments that we're proud of
