Inspiration

Since we live in the Residence Halls, the dining hall is the best option for food. We identified a few areas where we could improve the experience using a mobile app system

What it does

1) Rate the dishes a. Individual Dishes (Imported from the website) and be rated and reviewed. 2) Connect with people who want to dine together 3) Voting polls for future options: top-voted dishes can be recommended to the dining hall to serve again 4) Feedback / Suggestions tab

How we built it

We used Android studio, with possible future Firebase integration
