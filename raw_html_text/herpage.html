Inspiration

Inspirational women around the world kicking bums and taking names.

What it does

Provides a short boost of inspiration throughout your daily websurf.

How we built it

We used the MediaWiki API to get the summarized biographies of women who excel in their fields. The women are chosen randomly when you open a new tab. Then, the summary and photo of these women are displayed using HTML and CSS.
