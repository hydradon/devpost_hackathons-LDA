Exploring the mysteries of the vastly unexplored land in Nevada, Area 51 Raid simulator aims to prepare common folk for the event of the century. Avoid the entire US military and make it out alive.

Ubisoft's HackersNest API enables small platform game development and through its simplified graphics and physics engine and it makes it simple to develop games in the C++ environment. At the beginning of our 36 hour ordeal none of us knew anything about game development and C++ and ended the weekend learning how to develop a game from the basic graphic rendering and implementing a physics engine to adding obstacles and adding different level of difficulties.

Collision detection, sprite generation, asset modelling, animation, basic physics used in game and implementing a repeating background are some the topics we covered with our game.

Our idea influenced by North American meme culture and covered areas which Ubisoft's challenge wanted to explore.
