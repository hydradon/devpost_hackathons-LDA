What it does

facr is an iOS app that analyzes your smile and expressiveness while you practice for a speech and gives you an score of your performance.

How I built it

Using the emotion recognition SDK from Affectiva and KDCircularProgress Cocoa Pod

Challenges I ran into

The app is built in Swift and the SDK in Objective-C. The main challenge was to make them work together using a header bridge file.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Built an MVP in 24 hours

What I learned

Design a product that people would use Make Swift and Objective-C work together Integrate a facial recognition SDK into an iOS app

What's next for facr

Record the video of your speech to be able to replay it and see your score while replaying.
