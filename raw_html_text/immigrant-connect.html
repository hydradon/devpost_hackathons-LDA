Inspiration

Two of the developers are first-generation Americans and understand the struggles of connecting in an unfamiliar community. So they wanted to create a system for which immigrants could better and easily find a footing in America

What it does

Connects immigrants with common factors through online communities. Provides them with events, updates of immigrants lives, and the ability to interact with one another

How we built it

Django + React.js

Challenges we ran into

Getting Django and React to work together

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The overall design of the application. Works on both Mobile and Desktop

What we learned

  • Bootstrap 4
  • React.Js
  • Django

What's next for Immigrant Connect

  • Adding user-curated immigrant resources.
  • Multi-Media posting with Videos, Links, etc
  • Adding auditory and visual elements to enhance the community experience
