• My News

Inspiration

We live in a fast age. People don't have time for selection data which they like or need. Publishers post their news/articles every minut and we need to create a dashboard with everything in one place.


What it does

You can very quickly sort articles (with simple swipe) from you favorite website/blogs and you can read it on phone or send it to a desktop to later (after work, after launch). It's mobile friendly because you can easily block all images in articles.


How I built it

We started 4 ways how to build the whole project. Veronika started to create a template for android app and bridge between desktop and phone. Denis built another side of the bridge and desktop version. Dušan drew our logo and project image. He also prepared whole visual look of My News. I was working on a presentation, business model, and marketing strategy because we wanted to create something bigger that just simple app. We want to create a concept.
