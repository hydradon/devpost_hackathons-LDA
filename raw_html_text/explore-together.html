Inspiration

Find the local places of attractions in the city. The application is perfect for local individuals seeking new adventures in their city, tourists visiting the city or bored passengers waiting for their flight on long layaways

What it does

.The application finds out the popular destinations at the given city.

How we built it

.We used Amadeus API, Android Studio, Java, Google Maps API, Google places API, Firebase, Google Authentication, Google Cloud.

Challenges we ran into

.Making different versions of framework communicate with each other

What we learned

. Android application development which was new for us.

What's next for Explore Together

.We would love to integrate Explore Together with Google street.
