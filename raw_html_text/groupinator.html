Groupinator

Goals

  1. Group people into groups easily
  2. Pick a random person easily

Why?

  • WiFi may be unpredictable and disrupted

  • Mobile data is too expensive :(((

  • Many RNGs (random number generators) are web based

Cryptographically secure random numbers

  • No human bias or error

Loads offline

  • Uses service workers to at least load a UI without Internet connection

Environmentally friendly

  • Reduce use of paper by using Groupinator instead of drawing lots

  • dockerfile
