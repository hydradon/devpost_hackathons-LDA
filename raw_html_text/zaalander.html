What it does

Zaalander pulls items that match your profile from the Zalando API. Each item has a cost and everyone who likes an item contributes 5 cents to a bucket. If your contribution makes the bucket reach the cost, you get the item. The app works in a similar way to Tinder, you are presented with the items and you have two options: passing, or liking and contributing 5 cents to the bucket.

How I built it

I used the Zalando API, IBM Bluemix, node-red, Cloudant, and Kinetise.
