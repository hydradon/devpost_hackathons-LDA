Inspiration

The things we got inspired was by people who have emotional instability people who may need help with getting their emotional instability back to something more sturdy. Helping as many people who may get bullied or hurt by negative influences.

What it does

It helps all kinds of people who may feel as if they may get up to a point to feeling suicidal. It helps them find a safe haven to talk to other people and get positive feed backs onto how they may get back to the light (aka back to feeling like a normal kids again)
