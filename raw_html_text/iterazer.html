Inspiration

Iterazer is an existing app for creating complex structures and fractals, with uses in concept design, idea creation and pattern making.

The original app was nominated for quite some prestigious awards, is available for Android, iOS and Windows and can be found here: http://iterazer.com

What it does

Creating beautiful fractals based on very simple, intuitive controls. WILL gives it even more capabilites, because it allows users to draw their own patterns and use them to create complex art.

How I built it

Using Unity3D and lots of research.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Lots of users tested it - many without prior experience with multitouch devices at all, ranging from 3 to 85 years - and all immediately understood what to do and how to create. That is great feedback!

What's next for Iterazer

Actually pushing WILL support to the stores once it is thoroughly tested and optimized for more devices.
