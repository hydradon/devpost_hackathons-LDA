Inspiration

The theme: Resolution. Subthemes: Weird, Games as Art, Dreams, Education, Health and Learning.

What it does

This is a simple and funny one-button endless runner jumping game.

How we built it

The game was built in Java, using the libgdx framework.

Challenges we ran into

Working in parallel with source control was a challenge we overcame, as well as fine-tuning the physics of the jumping mechanics to ensure a fun and fair game.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The game was finished and ready to go in 13 hours! Tomi's little brother loved the game.

What we learned

Definitely don't work for 13 hours without sleeping, and take breaks in between to ensure that your code doesn't become a mess (especially around hour 9)

What's next for Fat Boy Szn

Possibly adding new features, porting to mobile, we have no idea!
