Inspiration

A post on social media can have a huge impact on how people perceive you. So we wanted a way for users to understand how their next post will affect them.

What it does

It is a chrome extension that predicts how many likes and comments your post will get as you type it. Also it will tell you how it affects your overall personality trait.
