Inspiration

Students commonly share meal swipes, and this app could provide them with a way to do so easily and conveniently. Furthermore, the app provides a more beautiful and powerful platform for all their dining needs. It is also a powerful social networking tool, connecting students to people they may otherwise have never met, through food.

What it does

Connects users with meal swipes to those who need them.

How we built it

Java applet (functionality) and proto.io (UI/UX)

Challenges we ran into

Integrating the functionality with the UI, but we plan to do so with more time

Accomplishments that we're proud of

the UI/UX and the extensive code library

What we learned

making an app is hard but rewarding

What's next for SwipeDaddy

release it to the Brown community!
