In Element Tower, you begin in a simple rhythm game where you gather the four basic elements by typing with the beat. In this phase, you use the Q, W, E, and R keys to collect each element. After 30 seconds, you switch to the defense phase, where you use your elements to defend the tower from slimes. Each tower shot uses a different element for a different effect. In this phase, you use the mouse to aim and click to shoot. Fire - High single target damage and burn Earth - Explosion dealing damage in an area Air - Huge knockback to one slime Water - Slows a slime temporarily
