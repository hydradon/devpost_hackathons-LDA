About

Most calendar apps use a dull grid layout for scheduling. With Centric, we created a new design to display activities intuitively. Centric uses a wheel-based interface to make it easier for users to plan the day's events. This app is geared towards students, with the ability to add weekly classes as recurring activities and a to-do list to keep up with deadlines. Finally, Centric can also be customized with themes to personalize your calendar.
