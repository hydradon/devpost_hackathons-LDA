• During heartbeat

Inspiration

Inspiration was the theme of the game jam "resurrection" and Rammstein's Spieluhr.

What it does

You move a cube inside a heart while only seeing the platforms every heartbeat. Collecting hearts increases your pulse. If you run out of pulse you die and lose.

How I built it

I used Javascript and create.js framework to build the game

Challenges I ran into

Creating randomly generated levels that aren't impossible is difficult.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I made a finished game that looks and feels nice.

What I learned

That you can finish a simple game in only like 16 hours

What's next for Resuscitation

I am going to upload it online and see if people like it
