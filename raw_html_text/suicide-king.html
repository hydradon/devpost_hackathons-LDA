• The lethal Queen

Inspiration

Every Playing Card has a story. King Charles the VIII, often known as the Suicide King, only it wasn’t suicide. One day a loyal courier sent a message to the king hidden in a deck of cards. The king opened the gift and seeing the message gathered the people he suspected to his court.

What it does

You as a player is required to use playing cards from real world, find clues and identify the murderer!

Challenges I ran into

We had a hard time getting watson to distinguish between hearts and diamonds. This made us retrain multiple times and we still could not get watson to distinguish between the two. Perhaps we need more data set to distinguish between the two.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Getting image recognition to work for different suits and connect something in real world space to objects in virtual space.
