Inspiration

WarioWare

What it does

Just play the minigames, yo

How we built it

Using THREE.js library in JavaScript, using libraries adding support for the Leap Motion

Challenges we ran into

A lot of challenges, most of them involving a battle with getting the THREE.js widgets to do what we want. Each of us working individually on our own minigames also caused a bit of a challenge when we had to put them all together!

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Proud of getting this thing to work, and the amount of progress we were able to make with it in such limited time.

What we learned

We learned to stay away from prototype libraries if at all possible, lest they cause problems.

What's next for Sleight of Hands

Hard to say! We would need Leap Motions of our own to continue development on this project.
