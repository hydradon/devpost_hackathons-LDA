  • Introducing Meut, pronounced "Mute".

You’re at a silent rave and it’s lots fun, but you spot a friend and you want to talk. So how do you? How do you communicate inside headphones? Harman/ JBL has taking a huge step to solve this problem, letting the world in when you want it and only when you want it. But a social interface that allows you to view who at a party and select who you open up to and only who you open up to drives takes another step toward an immersive social experience. and technology helps, and a social interface that lets the world in or out is the next step.

Now we can welcome anyone we want into the party in our heads- the spontaneous, joinable, fist pumping milly rockin party paradise. Meut is a silent rave social platform that allows users to meet and party together with headphones wirelessly. Passerbys can simply join in (pay-to-party), see who’s in attendance, who’s flirting, and to message others people.
