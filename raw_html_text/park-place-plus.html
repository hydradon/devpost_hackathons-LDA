Inspiration

Finding parking is a nightmare

What it does

Finds available street parking

How we built it

Utilized Google cloud services/Maps API with web design

Challenges we ran into

Lack of available API/Technology to perform certain tasks we wanted to do such as grabbing street segments between two intersections by location without requiring multiple points

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to simulate receiving signals from "sensors" placed on parking spots on the street and get all available spots with a certain radius from our current position as well as positions clicked on the map

What we learned

The capability of Google cloud services and Google Maps API

What's next for Park Place Plus

Installation of proximity sensors at street parking locations
