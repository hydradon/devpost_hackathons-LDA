Inspiration

Our inspiration comes from trying to help people overcome obesity. Over 17% of children and teens in America struggle with this health problem. If we can help these teens out in the earlier stages we can eventually lower the percentage of people who have obesity in America. If a teenager is obese in his adolescent years they are prone to be obese in their adult years. This helps to explain why over 35% of adults in America suffer from obesity. Our mission is to help out these people by educating and tracking the health of these teens.

What it does

Helps provide pragmatic advice by providing articles about health which the user can use to apply to their own lives. Also, the user is able to track their weight loss and mood.

How we built it

We are in the prototype phase using Google Slides.

Challenges we ran into

A challenge we ran into was thinking about the sensitivity of the apps features. Sometimes asking for too much information could make people feel more uncomfortable.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of the sketching for a functional app and all the features that help the app to become useful for people struggling with obesity.

What we learned

We learned how to develop different parts of an app with a purpose of ease and functionality.

What's next for Health Teen Obesity

We can use the iOS Fitness app in combination with our app to help provide more information about the user.
