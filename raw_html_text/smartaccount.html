SmartAccount

"A smarter way to access accounts"

Smart Accounts is a web-based platform for teens to access social media accounts in one tap more conveniently while keeping their accounts more secure. In today's world, it is so inconvenient for teens to exchange social media accounts day after day since they are so many new apps like LinkedIn, Instagram etc and we have come to make lives easier. The solution of what the platform is and how it helps, that being it’s a website to connect all of one’s social media on one page and does away with memorization of one’s account and their contact information. Our vision for the future and how this product could influence the future by being used to compile all forms of data from a person and store them on the blockchain for a more secure and insured future.
