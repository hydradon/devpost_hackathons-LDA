There are many Pebble watch users across the world, and there are probably even more investors. We've yet to seen a light weight and convenient solution to check on investments while you are not with your phone or laptop. Opening a phone or laptop just to check on stock prices is tedious. This has led me to put two and two together and make a financial investment app. It was meant to be simple and lightweight as there aren't currently any "good" Pebble stock market apps right now.

StockMark is a simple Pebble watch app that is made in Pebble.js. Users can simply enter their stock ticker symbols through the configuration page and it will load on the watch. Currently, version 1.0 only supports stocks listed on the NASDAQ exchange.
