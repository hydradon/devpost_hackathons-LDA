What is SoundSketch

SoundSketch is an interactive drawing program that lets the user choose a song that they draw along to. Using FFT nodes in the HTML5 Web Audio API, SoundSketch analyzes the chosen tune to dictate the width, color, and behavior of the brush. You can choose to draw in opaque circles or lines, if your brush marks align with the treble or bass of their song, and what two colors you want to oscillate between. Additionally you can save your artwork at any time to share with your friends!
