Inspiration

More often that not when you're continuously working on a computer, you tend to have a bad body posture which ends up making you look like Quasimodo from Hunchback of Notre Dame; and also, it hurts your back. So we were inspired to create a tool that would help, or in our case, force the user to have a perfectly straight back.

What it does

BackPropagation basically detects if the user is slouching or not via the web-cam. In case you are slouching, it sends you a reminder to straighten your back by switching off your screen and playing a sound.

How we built it

The machine learning model is generated with TensorFlow; which was then incorporated into a node.js script which can turn on your camera and compare it with your model.
