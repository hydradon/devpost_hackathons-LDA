Inspiration People are scared to do CPR. With this app, their hands are free yet they have guided instruction to perform it.

What it does It gives instructions on giving CPR through compressions. The pulse is the beat of when to perform a compression. (3 options - infant, child, adult)

How I built it - I built it with Xcode using WatchKit

Challenges I ran into - Haptic Feedback

What I learned - More about developing for Watch Kit

Built With

  • watchkit
