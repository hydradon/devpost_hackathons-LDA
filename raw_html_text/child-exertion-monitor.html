Inspiration

Children with congenital heart diseases that are not able get surgery are required to greatly limit their activity level. The children have to be constantly monitored to make sure they are not being too active, which can be exhaustive for parents and even more difficult for parents when the child enters schools.

What it does

Monitors the activity level of the child using a pebble, and sends SMS messages when high levels of activity are detected.
