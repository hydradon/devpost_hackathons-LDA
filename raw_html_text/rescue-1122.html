Rescue 1122 APP

Rescue 1122 app give access to citizen to report the accident or any misshapen to Rescue 1122 team for quick response. Citizen Can report through different way.

  1. Report through call 1122
  2. Report accident through image or video
  3. Report through SMS  Reporting through app automatically identify the location of accident through gps for quick response.   User can submit any observation to rescue team if anything wrong going in his surroundings.   Through this app citizen get alerts if any accident happen in surrounding area.   Feedback mechanism is embed in system get feedback from public regarding the service delivery also improve services.   Web base dashboard is integrated with app for monitoring all these activity and also give director to rescue team from headquarter.   Rescue team have its app interface through which headquarter give director and get feedback from accident location.   Develop this rescue 1122 soon will available for public on app store to start reporting.

Audience: Citizen Will use as end user and Rescue 1122 Team will use as administrative
