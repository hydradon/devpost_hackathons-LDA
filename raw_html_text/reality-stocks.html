Inspiration

Leap Motion has been around since early 2012. Not many developers have worked on huge projects. That's why Team Reality Stocks had thought about working with complex hand tracking technologies that aren't really available to the public. It's not very known so we gave it a try and we were blown away by the results. WE WERE CURIOUS. WE WERE HUNGRY and that's why we're building hand technologies cause it looks cool.

What it does

Reality stocks makes viewing and interacting with stock market data more engaging (and fun). Our site is designed to keep the user informed about real time development of stock prices, and makes it easy to get organized. We offer an overview of different company's stock prices as well as the option to zoom in and get more details about a specific company using the Leap Motion.
