Inspiration

When spectating important football events or halfheartedly taking part in America's political process, it's important to make an informed decision before you jump on a bandwagon.

Smackdown gives you an inside look at the fan bases' smack talk expertise before you decide who to root for today.

What it does

Smackdown analyzes current tweets about the teams, and assigns each tweet a Smack Score. The team with the higher aggregate Smack Score is winning the Smackdown.

What's next for Smackdown

Smackdown plans to sit down with a nice bowl of guac and enjoy the Superbowl later today.
