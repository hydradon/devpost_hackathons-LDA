Inspiration

Rhythm games are cool. VR is cool. Why not?

What it does

Hold the controller within the spheres and click the trigger in time with the beat! The more accurate you are, the better your score.

How we built it

We used Unity and Valve's SteamVR plugin to work with the HTC Vive.

Challenges we ran into

It turns out making a hollow cylinder in Blender is really hard.
