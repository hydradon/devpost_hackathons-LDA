Inspiration

My good friend and team member has said this phrase many a time to promote Mr. Sean from MLH's (aka Sean Victory) wonderful work at various hackathons around California. So, we decided to create a fun and easy way to keep track of how many times she mentions him. Now, you can do the same for yourself.

What it does

Every time you press the button at the bottom of the screen, the count will increment. Get to 100 for a special surprise!

How we built it

HTML, CSS, and JavaScript in Sublime text.

Challenges we ran into

Connecting our github to the domain.com domain name was very difficult, but we got there in the end.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Shedding some light on the radiant Sean from MLH, and hosting a website for the first time.

What we learned

Domain.com is very difficult to use. Sean from MLH is very charming.

What's next for Sean from MLH

We'd like count some other frequently-said phrases in the future.
