Inspiration

Are you tired of being asked if you want a receipt? "Over 250 million gallons of oil, 10 million trees and 1 billion gallons of water are consumed each year in the creation of receipts for the United States alone, generating 1.5 billion pounds of waste." -Huffington Post

We wanted a better way to manage receipts in our daily lives instead of throwing them away immediately.

What it does

Allows banks and companies to incorporate our API and push all purchases made through your accounts to our platform.

How we built it

We use Golang on our backend. AngularJS on the frontend. Mongo as the DB. Everything is in docker.

Challenges we ran into

CORS.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Built a functional full stack platform.

What we learned

Working with CORS (Cross Origin Resource Sharing). Improving UI design.
