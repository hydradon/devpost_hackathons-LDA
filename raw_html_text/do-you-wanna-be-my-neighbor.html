Inspiration

My teammate and I move to new neighborhoods almost every year. And finding a good quiet place is very hard to find. We needed something like this. A website where we can unleash our anger about the place we are currently living in and make sure that the next place we move into is not the same or worse.

What it does

allow people to post comments about their neighborhood and look at other neighborhood

How we built it

HTML5, CSS3, Node.js, JQuery and JSON

Challenges we ran into

every year we say "I'm never developing an Alexa skill again" and then we do!!! we tried to do three projects and none of them worked so we ended up with this cool website that helps people find good neighborhoods.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

we got something done

What we learned

never code an Alexa skill at a Hackathon again.....

What's next for Do You Wanna Be My Neighbor?

make it more functional
