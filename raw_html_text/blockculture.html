Inspiration

My inspiration was my grandpa, he does farming in India and I was thinking about him when I was choosing my idea.

What it does

It basically is insurance for farmers using digital coins, helps farmers predict droughts and sell crops.

How I built it

I didn’t build anything yet but what imagine it to be is an app on your phone or a website. That has all the features of a betting system, a way to convert cryptocurrency and a way to sell your crops.

Challenges I ran into

I didn’t have a teammate to support me, and my idea was rough and I had to refine it to make sense.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The whole idea of a cryptocurrency that acts like insurance for farmers I really feel proud of.

What I learned

I learned the basics of blockchain and how cryptocurrency is formed. I also learned how things like bitcoin operate and how it could be hacked and ways to prevent it.
