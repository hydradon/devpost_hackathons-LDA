Inspiration

As international hackers and we had difficulty touring around the city on our own so we thought of creating this application for frequent travellers who want to learn more on what's around them

What it does

The app connects users with tour guides in the country who can give them a more personalized/thematic tour based on their interests of the country.

How we built it

We designed it with: Sketch and Canva Front-End: Zeplin, HTML, CSS, SASS, Javascript Back-End: Ruby, Ruby-On-Rails, Postgresql, React-Native, Google-Maps-API

Challenges we ran into

Deciding on the project

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The conflicts we had, we resolved it in good manner and managed to work on things that we wanted to do for the project.

What we learned

The importance of team management skills

What's next for AdvenTours

Adding more key features into the app such as: Scavenger Hunts, Messages section, and more...
