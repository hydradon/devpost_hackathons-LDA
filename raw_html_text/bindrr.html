Problems

Investor

Many people have the money to invest but don’t know how to start or they are afraid due to trust issues.

SME(Small to Medium Enterprise)

Many Small to Medium Enterprises are well known or experts on their fields, however they don't have enough opportunities or funds to grow.

What is Bindrr?

Bindrr is an intuitive, trustworthy, social investment platform for Investors and Small to Medium Enterprises which connects and assists them to obtain mutually favorable investment deals and opportunities.

Investor Application

With Bindrr, the investors can discover various SMEs and investment opportunities that fits their preference. Investor can swipe right if they like the SME and otherwise left. Once liked, investors can be able to communicate with the SMEs. With the app’s intuitiveness, investors can easily invest on their ideal deals. Investors are also given simple information such as basic stats and review to assist them on getting the best deal. If the investment is done, the app will utilize the funds transfer API on FFDC to transfer the fund to the consignee or SME.

Business Application and Tech4Good

With Bindrr, the SMEs will be empowered and will be given opportunities to showcase their business to investors and create personalized or customized business deals or investments that will support their business growth. When it's time to pay the investor, the app will again utilize the funds transfer API on FFDC.

Monetization

Finastra will get a percentage of the total deal that is invested on the business or SME.
