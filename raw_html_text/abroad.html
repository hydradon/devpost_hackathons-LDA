Inspiration

Have you and your friends ever wanted to book a flight trip together but unfortunately couldn't find a destination which wouldn't lead to a fortune being spent? If so, this is an answer to your problem.

What it does

The web app finds the cheapest round trip for you and your friends.

How we built it

  • Front-end: React, CSS and HTML.
  • Back-end: Python.
  • Communication: Flask.

Challenges we ran into

  • Developing front-end.
  • Developing concurrency.

Accomplishments that we are proud of

  • Teamwork.

What we learned

  • Queues for the food are smaller towards the end.

What's next for Abroad

  • Expanding the suggestion section with accommodation, car hire, food, etc.
  • Making a filter for the flights with fewer CO2 emissions.
