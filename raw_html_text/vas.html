Inspiration

The problem that we saw in hospitals, disabled people don't like to be dependent and ask others for help. they prefer to do things by their own without the need to ask someone.

What it does

Helps disabled patients to position themselves on the hospital bed without any human help just by their voice.

How we built it

  • Raspberry
  • Microphone

Challenges we ran into

  • Lack of equipment.
  • Connecting the raspberry device with the mic.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We're proud that our idea touches the humanity side, and serves an important category in the community, also it's exclusive and never been done in Saudi Arabia

What we learned

Working with a team and accepting other opinions, searching skills
