Inspiration

Reverse Pac Man with a Low Poly Spin

What it does

A farmer tries to pick and protect his lemons before the horses invade his lemony orchard and steal his lemons. He can scare them to take some back.

How we built it

Splitting up the team by strengths and using Maya, Unity, Garage Band, and Visual Studio as the lions share of the development software

Challenges we ran into

a big memory leak from our path finding Dijkstra's Algorithm.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Fixing a big memory leak from our path finding Dijkstra's Algorithm.

What we learned

How to make horses in Maya. Also path finding grids. As well as good and bad ways to generate a traversable map. Finally, version control in Unity.

What's next for Lemon Stealing Horses

Refining our algorithms. Improving sound and rigs. Polishing up performance.
