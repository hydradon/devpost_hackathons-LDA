Inspiration

Changing habits is very complicated especially when it comes to technology, but if technology is that hard to overcome, why don't we use it against itself? recursive thinking, isn't it?

What it does

In order to deal with it, we decided to create a web platform associated to a PWA application. It's about putting articles from different fields and push people to read them.

How we built it

Each field got a series of questions to be answered so the user can evaluate his knowledge in the specific discipline. We added a family option, a parent can add his kids so they can read articles and take questions separetly, with a system of competition between family members to encourage them to always read more and more.

Challenges we ran into

Change the habit where every member of the family remains drowning in front of a smartphone in social media by the desire to read more in order to surpass his family on knowledge ===> FAMILEDGE

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Create a competition between family members, around each other's knowledge. "who has the most information on this area?"

What we learned

How to think about a project impacting humanity in the long term.
