Inspiration

Blockchain technology allows for highly secure and private storage. To take advantage of this, companies and people can use Invenstory to keep track of their supplies. The blockchain retains a history of what was put onto it so a story is created for each item.

What it does

Invenstory allows users to create inventories of their belongings or work supplies.
