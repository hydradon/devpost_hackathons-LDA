Inspiration

We found an interesting picture of Obama's face on Mao's head. That inspired us to do such a project.

What it does

Face Swap - It recognizes and swaps faces of two people in front of the camera in real-time.

Motion Capture - It captures human skeleton and body movements, and the program can react to certain body movements.

How we built it

Face Swap - python, opencv

Motion Capture - python, opencv, openpose
