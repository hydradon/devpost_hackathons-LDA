In an attempt to make virtual reality more immersive, the Jedi Training Simulator is a game where the user holds a Wii remote that appears in their virtual environment as a Light Saber from Star Wars. The user then swings his or her arm in combat with incoming robots; movements correspond and show up in the virtual environment. The light saber can retract or extend by pressing A or B respectively, however, to defeat the robots the light saber must be extended. The directional pad on the Wii remote allows the user to move around in the virtual world to find the best angle or perspective in the battle field.
