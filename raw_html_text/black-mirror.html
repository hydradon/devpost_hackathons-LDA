Inspiration

Eric at ViewFin brought up an interesting problem of digital asset transactions over the blockchain and how transactions are between two digital identities but there is no way to assign digital identities that are verifiable to people.

What it does

Creates a biometric locked mobile device based ID for you that can then act as a place to store all your records.

What we learned

This is quite a large undertaking that will need a lot of cooperation from various governments and government entities.

What's next for Black Mirror

Exploring a way to work with a small forward thinking government to establish a global network of ID and data verification mechanisms.
