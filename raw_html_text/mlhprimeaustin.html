**Our story begins in the fall of 2016. 4 freshmen from very different backgrounds teamed together on this very day to form the most intelligent, most API intensive application ever. We present to you, on this very day, Volun-near.

Our application is designed to ensure the safety and security of you and your loved ones. It takes the form of both a beautifully designed mobile application to give users an effortless and simple user experience

**What it does

Our application is designed to make volunteering less boring.... by inviting a friend! Or a random stranger with a similar passion! From our database of volunteer opportunities we get opportunities for each unique user and match each user with another user based on their choices and preferences.

**Challenges we ran into

Having a backend that needs to sync up with our frontend was excessively difficult. Our IP messaging was extremely had to implement.

**What I learned

I learned how to deploy a Python webserver using flask.

**Features User database, IP chatting, user matching, sponsored volunteer events
