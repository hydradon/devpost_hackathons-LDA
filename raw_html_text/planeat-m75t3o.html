Inspiration

The common problem plagues teenagers and young adults across the world. Where do you go to meet up. As it always has been, these are the years where we most often seek social interaction, yet the means for doing so has always been more difficult to decide upon than it seems.

What it does

Planeat takes location and personal preferences data from each user involved in a plan to meet up, and generates a ranked list of options deemed suitable for all parties. Doing so provides personally tailored results optimized to where ever any user may be.
