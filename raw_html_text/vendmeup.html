Inspiration

Cryptocurrency is growing, and some people may have cryptocurrency for purchases instead of carrying around cash.

What it does

Lets you make a purchase from a vending machine with your e-wallet.

How we built it

Backend is most Solidity and standard web dev in front.

Challenges we ran into

Solidity is a newer language, so we had trouble debugging errors at times.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

A working dapp live on the test network.

What we learned

Lots.

What's next for VendMeUp

Connect to a Raspberry Pi and be able to actually dispense things.
