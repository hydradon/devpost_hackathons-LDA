Inspiration

Making E-sport meta part of the everyday life

What it does

We're creating a platform and a framework for creating collectible cards our of your favourite E-sport scene. Being integrated with the in game statistics and tournament results the cards are given their stats with better players becoming the better cards.

How we built it

Apart from small demos and mock-ups, we haven't really started developing the actual system yet. Only planning and design. More than coding, the important aspect in building a platform like this is to form partnerships with different game companies, brands, e-sports teams and players.

Challenges we ran into

Licensing issues are of course a problem, but either partnering with an e-sport game, game companies, or having the content be community managed to avoid these issues.
