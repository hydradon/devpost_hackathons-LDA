Inspiration

We wanted to help people to learn new languages easier as we (two japanese's and one spaniard) needed to do.

What it does

It gives you the most important things you need to know about Japanese or Japan and also of Spanish and Spain for the Japanese people.

How we built it

We built it fully in Swift.

Challenges we ran into

We wanted to had a lot of sections in our app so we could not finish with everything.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We could finish the translator, that took us a lot of time to make it work.

What we learned

How to use Swift better, managing Views with cells and some things in spanish or japanese that we did not know.

What's next for Bannou

It gives you all the things related to how to get visas, recommendations, common words and helps you to learn a new language, and also has a translator between the latin alphabet and the japanese ones.
