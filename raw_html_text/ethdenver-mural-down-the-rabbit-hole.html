Where education, artists and the blockchain universe intersect

In order to create a diverse and innovative community around blockchain technology, we need everyone on board! Through this interactive installation, "Down the Rabbit Hole", people can visualize why and how activity on the blockchain happens. We're a collaborating group of makers, artists, designers, engineers and developers to begin the journey of cultivating an environment where anyone can feel the pulse of the blockchain.

What did you actually do?

  • We run a whisper node to visualize the amount of messages being sent through addressable LEDs
  • We demonstrate PoW through a mining experience that users that participate in!
