Inspiration

There are over 850 student organizations at Iowa State and there is no way for students to lookup events for all clubs. If someone does come to a club event, most clubs use paper to record their attendance which can be easily lost and makes it time consuming to send an email to everyone that attended.

What it does

Our app allows student organizations to create events that any student can search for and also get the details. It also allows students to view their involvements and tailors their experience to these involvements.
