Inspiration

Losing things in the midst of a messy workspace is super annoying. Also , for example , something that happens a lot is when you get a new water bottle when you forget where you left your last one. This creates unecessary waste in our lives.

What it does

My app tracks objects you are working with and lets you know where they are when you need to. It reminds you to use previous objects such as a water bottle if you reach for a new one.

How I built it

Used machine learning , OpenCV , android

Challenges I ran into

Had to develop completely on a remote system because my computer would not run Android Studio at all

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Finished a prototype that clearly shows the idea effectively
