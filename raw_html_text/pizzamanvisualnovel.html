Initialy when we started work, we began developing a top down procedurally generated dungeon crawler. We quickly realized, however, that this would NOT work in a way that would let us turn in a working game by the end. We scrapped that development, and instead began working on this one, a visual novel, choose your own adventure type game based around the concept of getting free pizza, something we thought up when eating the pizza on Saturday. In the end this is what we got, and i think its pretty okay
