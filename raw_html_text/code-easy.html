Inspiration

Being international students pursuing a career in tech, we realized that there are lots of individuals in our country that do not have access to academic resources, hence we decided to develop a mobile and progressive web app to enable users to access the same resources offline without the limitations of unlimited data/wifi or laptop.

What it does

Our product will give students from underrepresented countries access to learning materials that can help them to be a success in the field of Computer Science.

How I built it

We built it by utilizing tools such as Xcode, Swift, Javascript, Html, Css.

Challenges I ran into

Some of the materials we did not understand, therefore, we utilize videos from youtube.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

This is our first Hackathon! therefore we are proud of each other that we went through with it until the end. We also made an amazing product to demo.

What I learned

Hackathons are not has bad as we thought.

What's next for Code Easy

We plan to add future implementations that we couldn't add in the time frame granted to us.
