Inspiration

Hip hop heads have become obsessed with Kanye West's 'Silver Surfer Intermission' for both its beautiful storytelling and undeniable musicality. So I felt that I would offer people the chance to share its greatness with the uninitiated.

What it does

It receives a phone number as input, processes a $1 charge using Stripe (a man's gotta eat fam), and then makes a phone call using the voice of Max B himself.
