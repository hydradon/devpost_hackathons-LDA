The need of Automated Analysis of abundance of incoming data is critical to avoid human error and inefficiency. This is the main inspiration behind the hack.

The Hack captures data sent by Citrix-webhook, logs that data in a google sheet, pushes the data into Google's Big Query which in turn is integrated with Google Studio to generate visually appealing and informative data charts.

I established servers to capture data flowing from the Citrix webhook, logged it into the google cloud and ran it through SQL queries to generate information out of the raw data.

Understanding the incoming data and extracting meaningful information from it.

Finding more meaningful information that can help Citrix take important and critical decisions.
