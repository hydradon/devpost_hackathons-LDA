Inspiration

When I was a child, my friends and I would always have a fascination with dinosaurs. Many of the other kids my age would also be interested in learning about these majestic yet giant creatures that once roamed the face of the Earth.

What it does

The skill is extremely simple to use. All the user needs to say is, "Alexa, tell me about dinosaurs" (among other variant phrases), and Alexa will respond with a child-friendly fact about dinosaurs.

How I built it

I built the skill through the Alexa Developer Console and Amazon Web Services (AWS) Lambda.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am proud that I have created a skill that I wish I had in the past when growing up as a child.

What's next for DinoFacts

I will continue to update more fun yet interesting facts.
