Inspiration

Studying is hard, and the process of making flash cards can be very manual. While Quizlet exists for sharing flashcards, the cards are not catered to your specific notes and writing style.

What it does

Reads through your notes and autogenerates flashcards based off your exact wording/style.

How we built it

Node JS Backend, HTML/CSS frontend
