What's next for Warm R US

Climate change is the number one global issue; rise in temperatures cause more severe weather, crises with food and resources, and the spread of diseases.This project provides information about rising temperatures and CO2 emissions that occur in the U.S. Utilizing the datasets of annual average temperature and annual CO2 emissions in the U.S. from 1900-2013, our team created visualizations for readers to see the upward trend and connection of the consequences of CO2 emissions on rising temperatures.
