Inspiration

Solarmove is a Swiss based startup working to promote access to solar energy by lowering the cost of PV installations by promoting self construction. Sumthesun is a Kosovo based startup whose objective is to offer people interested in solar energy a platform and a costumer friendly calculator that can compare different products and services. The collaboration born in Berlin builds on both ideas and focuses on offering access to large information regarding PV materials and services, promoting ownership and a DIY approach in the choice of the installation

What it does

We have developed a centralized market place that allows clients to choose from different solar products (panels and Inverters) in a transparent way. We have developed a calculator that informs clients about the different products available to build a PV installation, and plan to expand our calculator with more features in the future.

How we built it

We gathered data on prices and materials from Swiss wholesalers and built an interactive database where clients can choose the PV installation according to their needs based on brands, prices, colors.

Challenges we ran into

We will need to access a great amount of data to offer this services to different markets worldwide. Time management was also an issue.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Our collaboration and ideas developed for the future

What we learned

We have learned how to collaborate in a multicultural team with team members from different backgrounds and skills

What's next for Diysol

We will advertise our platform and calculator and reach out to producers to include them on our websites, so that they can be directly contacted by clients.
