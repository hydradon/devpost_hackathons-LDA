• Virtual Fitting Room a new way to try out new clothes!

Inspiration

We have see the potential Instagram filters have now a days, so we decided to join a chatbot that offers the service to try out new clothes so you can decide better what to buy online.

What it does

A chatbot that salutes you and waits for you to send an image to fit, or suggests a new clothe for you to try virtually.

How I built it

I used Facebook Pages, Facebook Messenger, chatbot technology and Spark AR

Challenges I ran into

Spark AR has some difficulties to publish to pages that represents productos or companies and publishing this filters are quite difficult to do. Creating a need base on what people may want was difficult, because it offers new service for people.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It does what it says, gives the opportunity to people to try out new clothes virtually.

What I learned

A lot of code in chatbot technologies and spark ar filters.

What's next for Virtual Fitting Room

Sell out the chatbot experience to any e-commerce that offers clothes for women.
