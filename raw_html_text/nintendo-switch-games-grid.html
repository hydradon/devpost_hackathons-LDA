Inspiration

I wanted an easy way to access information about games on Nintendo platforms without having to go to the wikipedia articles.

What it does

It uses BigParser's tools to have a grid that lists all of the games on nintendo platforms games along with plenty of information about them.

How I built it

I copied and pasted the information from Wikipedia into spreadsheets, then consolidated them into a single spreadsheet and uploaded the spreadsheet to BigParser's website.

Challenges I ran into

Some of the wikipedia articles on Nintendo consoles' games weren't consistent in their headings, so I had to tweak with that.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I acomoplished gaining the knowledge of how easy it is to create a BigParser grid.

What I learned

I learned how to create a BigParser grid.

What's next for Nintendo Games.grid

I may create more grids for games on Sony's or Microsoft's consoles. I can also add more columns with more information.
