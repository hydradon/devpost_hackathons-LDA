Inspiration Wanted to improve security for the smaller airlines at a cheap price.

What it does It is a grid system of drones that can scan the perimeter of the airline that can sense motion and trigger a loud alarm to scare any unwanted creature away from the airline

How I built it We took apart a drone and replaced it's circuit board with an arduino board.

Challenges I ran into The team doesn't have enough experience in arduinos to complete the prototype

Accomplishments that I'm proud of Learning more about how to use the arduino and having the ability as a team to have a consensus on the design of the drone.

What I learned I should look for more help when needed at this hackathon, and it's great to work as a team.

What's next for Watch Dog Drones Hopefully it will take flight at a small airstrip to show it's potential.
