Inspiration

Walking into a restaurant or library and realizing there are no seats constantly

What it does

Finds restaurants that are open and available seats.

How I built it

We developed an Android app to detect when a person is seated in a chair using the accelerometer. Then, we developed iOS and Android apps that displayed information like restaurant hours

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned

What's next for CMfood
