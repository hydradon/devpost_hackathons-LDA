Inspiration

Inspired by student experiences and challenges with subletting

What it does

Provides a start to finish UX design with panoramic views of the interior of sublets , while providing structure and transparency to the subletting process

How I built it

Html, CSS, Javascript, Jquery

Challenges I ran into

Creating continuous stitched panoramic views

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Creating said continuous stitched panoramic view

What I learned

Learned about panoramas, the technology involved in spherical panoramas (Google streetview), the stitching process of panorama and webdev process

What's next for Sublet

Rating and feedback system for increased liability of users, filters/sort options, spherical panoramic views that the viewer can control, listing of nearby amenities (bus stops, grocery stores, etc) and respective location display on map
