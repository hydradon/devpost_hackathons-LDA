Inspiration

Majority of the video content on the internet is created by amateurs and they are usually in need of editing tools/utilities.

What it does

It has three amazing features.

1) The Mask : A feature which protects the privacy of a lot of people who accidentally show up in live broadcasts

2) Green Screen : A feature which provides an inexpensive alternative to a physical green screen, and empowers amateur video content creators

3) Stand by : A feature which allows users to suspend a Facebook LIVE session, giving them more control over what’s being broadcast and thus protecting their privacy.

How I built it

Using Spark AR

Challenges I ran into

-Implementing the switch over between features with just a Tap, Swipe and Long press

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

LiveStudio effect has been approved by Facebook.

What I learned

As a developer, i learnt Patch Editor, Reactive Programming
