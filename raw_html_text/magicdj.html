Inspiration

To let someone else play the song at your speakers, we would have to give them the remote control, also if there is a party, there is usually only one DJ who sets up all the songs and don't let anyone to touch his controls. Now, anyone can be a DJ and do the magic.

What it does

MagicDJ allows anyone to control the music. Whether you are dancing at the private party or having fun with your real friends at your house, anyone can play their favorite songs by tweeting or texting the song name with certain hashtag.

How we built it

We have used Node.js for back end, Twitter API, Bose SoundTouch API, Twilio API, The music is played
