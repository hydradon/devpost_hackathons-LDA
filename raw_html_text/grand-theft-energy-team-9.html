Grand Theft Energy - Team 9

This is the code repository of the famous Team 9, the winner team of the second YB Hackathon 2019.

Inspiration

The goal was to create a great Hackathon Project that matches the following requirements.

Develop a game for ewbBern that encourages customers to act economically and sustainably. Develop a game for ewb in which the player is encouraged to act sustainably and/or economically in a fictitious world. ewb's portfolio of products and services in the areas of " self-sufficiency in energy " and "mobility" is integrated into the game and ideally plays a central role. The aim of the game is to show the player the added value and long-term consequences of his decisions for the environment.

What it does

We designed the game "Grand Theft Energy" with a two-layered story. First to create a simple game fun to play. Second to analyze the emotions of a player during the game to prove that acting ecologically is fun too.
