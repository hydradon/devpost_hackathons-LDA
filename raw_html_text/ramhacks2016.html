• Frontpage playing fullsize video

  • Form for submitting a new video

Inspired by the jukeboxes found in American diners, StreamLine is a website that allows users to DJ the frontpage. Users submit an initial deposit and a YouTube url, and that video is played for one second for every cent deposited. When the deposit is spent, the video reverts to a default video, which opens way for sponsors to play ads until another user submits a video.
