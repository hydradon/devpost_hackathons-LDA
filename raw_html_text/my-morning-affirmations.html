Inspiration

Last year my friend introduced me to the world of affirmations. I started practicing affirmations since then. And benefits are mind blowing. Thee are so many people who are not aware of affirmations and do not even understand how they work. My attempt is to create a skill where users can understand what is affirmations , what are the different categories of affirmations, how can you practice affirmations and what are the benefits of affirmation practices.

Law of Attraction experts almost universally recommend using positive affirmations. However, perhaps users have struggled to design affirmations that work for them. Alternatively, perhaps they’re new to the Law of Attraction and are not sure how to start using affirmations in the most productive way. That was my inspiration.

What it does

My morning Affirmation skill is one of a kind skill that helps users focus on the most important aspects of your life through affirmations. Just follow the process and attract happiness , abundance or whatever else you want.

How I built it

I have built it using Storyline platform.

Challenges I ran into

You can provide one affirmation to user and they can concentrate it. but i wanted to provide bunch of affirmations for one category or one aspect of person's life. and what is the best thing that compliments affirmation practice is Meditation. so how can i get best of any affirmation was challenging for me.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Finally i have created a skill for myself and people like me who want to get the best of affirmations.

What I learned

Developing a skill like this has been challenging but the development process was the best experience. How to keep users engaged has been a great learning.
