inspiration

Help improve the Community in Bryan/College station by incentivizing people to do more community service. although many people have thought about wanting to help out in their community, not a lot of the general public volunteer without some form of incentive. For example, most volunteers are young people whose main incentive is to fulfill their yearly hours for graduation at their school, something that not a lot of them enjoy because they feel forced by the negative consequences if they don’t volunteer. We feel that by using incentives, we can give them that push to go out and use their time and skills to benefit the community.

What it does

Companies can upload volunteer opportunities to the site. The users can create an account through which they can register for opportunities and earn points based on the number of hourse they log. They can redeem points for prizes.

How we built it

We built it using html php mysql and javascript. We thought that this would be the best way to create an interactive website with a functional UI, so that it is easier for the end-user.

Challenges we ran into

fetching data from database. Lack of experience in server-side programming. We were only able to make a local database as opposed to the cloud. We also felt short on time due to our inexperience at a hackathon.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

made a nice user interface

What we learned

the basics of building a website

What's next for aggie community service finder

we want to add a leaderboard where users can rank based on their points, and compete against friends to earn yearly prizes such as vacations.
