Inspiration

We wanted to transmit the feeling of excitement you get when you get on a cruiser for the first time, so you can decide whether or not the experience is for you.

What it does

It´s a system that simulates thru virtual reality the whole experience of being on a cruiser.

How we built it

We utilized Virtual reality as our prime focus, utilizing Unity to allow us to make the program.

Challenges we ran into

We did not know how to use Unity.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We learned how to properly use Unity in less than 24 hours.

What we learned

VR and Unity.

What's next for Cruiser-VR

If we were to win this hackathon we would love to continue the whole idea and sharpen it up so that it actually finds its desired use.
