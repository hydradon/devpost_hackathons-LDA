AUXY

A modern-day jukebox. Think Kahoot meets Google Drive meets Spotify. AUXY is is a collaborative playlist that brings people together via music. Some features include: authenticating within Spotify, hosts being able to create a key for their “party”, users being able to add songs to the collaborative playlist, and depending on the selected mode, other users can up/down-vote songs.
