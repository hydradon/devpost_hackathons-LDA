Inspiration

More than 70% of an average person’s diet consists of processed food…. so we decided to do something about it.

What it does

  • Scans a food item to tell you the Food Score
  • Food Score - how healthy a food is from 1-10
  • Calculates eco-points to inform you on how you are impacting the environment
  • Lets you compete with friends!
  • Informs you of local farmers markets!

How we built it

We used machine learning and a core ml model to help recognize the products that people are eating. Based on this analysis, we give people nutritional and environmental rankings as well as tips on if they should eat that food or not. Additionally, we used the GoogleMaps API in order to show the nearest farmer market locations that people can go to.

Challenges we ran into

It was difficult training the ml model and integrating the map.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We created a successful ml model that helps recognize food.

What we learned

We learned how to use Xcode and machine learning while learning to integrate an ml model and google maps.
