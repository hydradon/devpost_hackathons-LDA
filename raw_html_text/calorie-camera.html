Inspiration

A lot of friends like to lift and take their health seriously, and as such, they always count calories as they eat. With an app like this, it would make counting a lot easier!

What it does

It takes a picture of your food and identifies what could be on it and tells you the amount of calories of each object.

How we built it

Intense Android Studio sessions

Challenges we ran into

Incorporating and learning how to use the Clarifai and Nutritionix API

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The app is able to recognize most of the objects in the picture, and outputs nutrition facts accordingly.

What we learned

How to code in Android Studio and how to use API's from other developer's effectively

What's next for Calorie Camera

More features such as health tracking and more personalized data.
