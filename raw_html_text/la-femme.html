Inspiration

We were researching and came across a statistic that stated that there are only 6 feminine product non profit organizations, yet thousands of food organizations and drives for the homeless. Because the needs of women are overlooked, many homeless women can't receive the feminine/maternal products they need. This is why we are here to help.

What it does

It helps connect women within a community by allowing them to share maternal and feminine products with each other. This then forms a bond so special that it empowers both women. Additionally, we have a city portion of the app where you can find shelters as well as bins to donate any feminine and maternal products you might have (this is also open to our male audiences if they'd like to help as well).

How we built it

We built our website using Cloud 9 and coded it with Bootstrap, HTML, and Javascript.

Challenges we ran into

Because of the time constraint given, we encountered the challenge of how we would fit all our content including an app and website into our presentation.
