Inspiration:

Lane wanted to buy a delicious bagel, when he realized that he had forgotten his wallet, and therefore, his credit card and cash. He was left with only his phone. It was then that he realized that it would be convenient if an app allowed him to exchange the funds from his online bank account to another's for cash.

What is it:

omnicash is a peer-to-peer atm. If you have forgotten your credit card at home and do not have money on hand, through omnicash you can connect with others nearby using your phone and request cash. You can think transfer funds from your bank account to another's in exchange for physical currency.

Accomplishments that we're proud of:

Though the product wasn't as polished as we had aimed for, we were proud that we made something that people could test out.
