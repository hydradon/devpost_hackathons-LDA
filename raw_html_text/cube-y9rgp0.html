Inspiration

The lack of games available for people with disabilities.

What it does

It is a simple simon-like game with a 3D cube. To make the game accessible, each side of the cube has a different sound and the cube can be rotated by the eye movement.

How I built it

Xcode

Challenges I ran into

Smile tracking to select side of cube

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Building the concept of an accessible game

What I learned

OpenCV

What's next for C.U.B.E.

Improving eye tracking and smile selection.
