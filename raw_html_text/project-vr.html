Inspiration

Events such as Swamphacks takes a tremendous amount of work to plan and coordinate. In addition to traveling, event planners must find a venue that will host their needs. To save time and Money for either a hackathon or weeding Project VR plans on solving the needs of the customer.

What it does

It allows individuals to have the experience of visiting a venue while saving money through the use of VR.
