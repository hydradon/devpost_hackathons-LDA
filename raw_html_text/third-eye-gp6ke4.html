Inspiration

Smart Surveillance System for Social Good. Our inspiration was to control the crime rates which are on a hike in daily newspapers and news channels. Nowadays, Unwarranted government surveillance is an intrusion on basic human rights that threatens the very foundations of a democratic society. The Purpose is to gain confidence in government services and to maintain transparency between government and people. A major concern is for the goodness of the people.

The main motive was to contribute somewhere in the development of the nation.

What it does

The proposed system includes the specialized mechanisms for Camera Tampering, Long Time Tracking, Counting the number of peoples. An alert (siren) will be generated each time anyone tries to destroy/tamper the camera by any means.Also, an alert will be generated if someone enters the chamber by covering his/her face by any means.A mail will be sent to the nearest police station and bank admin so that immediate actions can be taken.The entire mechanism takes place in real-time, thus decreasing the time complexity to a great extent making the system an efficient mechanism to prevent any anti-social activities.The space complexity will be reduced by processing only suspicious frames of a video and storing only such videos.

How I built it

We used a combination of Machine Learning and Web Development to build the project. The Technologies used in this project are OpenCV and Flask. The primary approach for the proposed system consists of - Background Subtraction, Object Detection and Tracking, Feature Extractions, and Pattern Matching.

Challenges I ran into

The project follows a methodology of creating a system that is cost-effective and has fewer hardware requirements. Thus, without making changes to the existing system this system can be used for ATM surveillance. The main challenge the team faced was the availability of data. Since, the idea was to make the system capable of real-time video processing, at every phase of design and development, the overall cost was kept low so that the system becomes effective. One limitation was the hardware requirement(availability of GPUs) for training purposes.
