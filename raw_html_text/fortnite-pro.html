Inspiration

As high schoolers, we are able to see the extent at which the gaming community positively affects the lives of our peers and their every day lives. One game, Fortnite, has been especially dominant in the gaming industry, and Discord, a communication tool for gamers, has also been prominent. Wanting to combine the two resources, we decided to make Fortnite Pro, to create a more informative Fortnite experienece.

What it does

Fortnite Pro streamlines the gaming experience and adds Discord-level sociability. It allows Fortnite players to take advantage of Discord, being able to find user stats, generate random tips, rank fortnite users in a discord channel, and much more.

How we built it

We built it using node.js, javascript, and multiple Fortnite APIs including fortnite-tracker and fortnite.wikia.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Being able to integrate multiple APIs and manipulate multiple sources of data was our greatest challenge in the hackathon.

What's next for Fortnite Pro

Better visuals and more personalized functionality.
