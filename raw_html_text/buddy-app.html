Inspiration: Everyday activities are often a lot more enjoyable in the company of others. For example, as students, many times we don't want to eat alone (or rather, don't want to be seen eating alone). We also understand everyone has extremely busy schedules that don't necessarily align with those of their friends. With these issues in mind, we created Buddier, a site that allows you to find a buddy for certain activities like dining in restaurants, or going out for a jog, all while spontaneously meeting new people.

What it does: Once you specify location and an activity, Buddier will match you up with others in the area who chose the same activity. You can then message each other, look at their ratings, confirm a meeting location, and become buddies!

What's next for Buddier:

  • Additional features and activity options
  • Algorithms to better match individuals based on location and interests
  • Monetization opportunities
