Inspiration

Our vision for the future of digital business agreements is that every interaction will be a face to face. When sending business documents and contracts online, it is harder than ever to determine who is on the other end of the computer sending you a digital signature.

What it does

Using the "Live Contract" platform, users can review documents in Office 365, chat about them face to face using Twilio's Video webRTC platform, and sign them digitally using DocuSign! The end result is a more productive, collaborative and transparent business environment, where the barriers once introduced by conducting business negotiations online are now a thing of the past.
