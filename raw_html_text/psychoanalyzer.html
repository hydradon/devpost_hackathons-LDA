Inspiration

Noticed a lack of technology/analytics in helping people with their mental health.

What it does

To help patients keep a diary of their emotions/feelings every day, and to provide insightful analytics for the psychologist. This helps amplify the psychologist's ability to help the patient.

How we built it

Python/Bluemix/Flask/HTML

Challenges we ran into

Making sure different files connected, getting our database to work correctly.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Finishing it. Creating some analytics for it. Real life applications that could help people.

What we learned

'How to use all the technologies involved, except SQL. Specifically, how to create a proper web app using Flask.

What's next for PsychoAnalyzer

Creating a better UI/Flow and adding more analytics/AI to the data. Additionally, making the diary side more interactive. We are considering making an AI that talks to the diary user directly.
