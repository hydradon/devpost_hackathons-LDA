Inspiration

The coin jar.

How it works

Penny saver keeps track of your Venmo account balance daily. If there are extra pennies in the amount ($4.37), it will deduct the pennies (37 cents) to a Penny Saver account. At the end of the month, you may have $20-30 that you can use for fun or to cover unexpected expenses.

You can contribute more money into your Penny Saver account by paying the pennysaver Venmo account, or ask for some back by charging the pennysaver Venmo account.
