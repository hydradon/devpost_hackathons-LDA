Inspiration

Tuna is a big community in fish, about 386.260 ton potension in Samudra Hindia Indonesia. Many fisherman is to lazy to make a note so that our goverment lack in fish data. Other than that, counting and sorting still do manually by fisherman or distributor.

What it does

This system can capture data for every ship such as length, thick, and count. System also can sort fish based on length and send the captured data to android device.

How we built it

Built with hard component intel galileo, stepper motor, servo, and ultrasonic sensor. We also use a simple conveyor model to simulate the real fish counting.

Challenges we ran into

Challenges is to calibrate the sensor to adjust our requirement.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We proud can built a simple system in 24 hours.

What we learned

Many things such as integrated a data from microcontroller to android device. How to make a clean, effective and efficient code.

What's next for Automated Fish Counter

  1. Validation fish identification method with ultrasonic sensor.
  2. Hardware implementation that suitable for fish industry
  3. Visualization Information more attractive
