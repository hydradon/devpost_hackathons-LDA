Inspiration

A Behavioural Economics Based Financial Prediction Report for Companies and Investment Bankers

What it does

Bamery is a new financial tool for companies, investment bankers, startups and more. It provides predictive financial reports on the future trends of yield rates, stock prices, and more, based on social trends and sentiments towards particular institutions, corporations, organizations, and governments.

What makes us different?

Traditional economics is based on three fundamental assumptions:

  1. Everyone has exactly the same information
  2. Humans always make rational decisions
  3. Humans will always act in self-interest

But these assumptions are in fact severely flawed because humans often include perceptive bias and emotion in their decision making process. Behavioral economics is the study the effects of psychological, social, cognitive, and emotional factors on the economic decisions of individuals and institutions and the consequences for market prices, returns, and the resource allocation.

Bamery is a ground-breaking tool in its usage of geographically contextified sentiment data to provide investment bankers and companies a new perspective when determining risk, picking stocks to include in their portfolio, and making investments.

What's next for Bamery

Bamery will get bigger and better. The next step is to scale production up to and become an enormous data-mining and machine-learning operation.
