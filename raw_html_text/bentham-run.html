• Bentham Run

Inspiration

Three words: JEREMY . BENTHAM'S . HEAD

What it does

It is a 2D Platformer where Jeremy Bentham run towards a flag for no reason.

How we built it

We divided the task amongst us by Artwork, Music and Gameplay

Challenges we ran into

The time limit and our limited experience with Unity made it difficult for us to build our first 2D platformer game.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

` Sound Making the scripts and composing an original music for the game. Artwork Making the pixelart and trying to make most of the graphics from scratch. Programming Being able to animate Bentham and watch him jump, run and fall.

What we learned

Use the collab feature in Unity
