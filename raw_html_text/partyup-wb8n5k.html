What it does

Allows users to find parties nearby and filter them based on categories (Music, House Party, Film/Cinema, School Functions, etc.) and find ones that fit their interests (Games, Networking Meetups, Alcohol, BYOB, Open Mic Night, etc.)

What's next for PartyUP!

Add features that allow for hosts to hide the address and location of their parties, and charge a cover for the information. Allow users to find other party goers attending the same party and chat with them in small groups. Add party host/attendee rating systems to alert other hosts or party goers of scammers and bad guests.
