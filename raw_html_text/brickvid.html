We wanted to develop a way to come up with our own video recommendation system, but we also wanted to have a way to replace existing video recommendations for example on YouTube and display ours instead. We as a team were attempting to develop a video recommendation system that might perform better than that of YouTube's.

We used javascript to develop the Firefox plugin and to display our recommendations over YouTube's. We also used google cloud platform (GCP), python, & scikit-learn to pull video descriptions from a couple thousand YouTube videos and perform sentiment analysis & clustering on the data to come up with our own video recommendations.
