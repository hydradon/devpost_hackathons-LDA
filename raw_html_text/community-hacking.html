Inspiration

We set out to make something that encapsulates the essence group hacking and Hackathon culture.

What it does

Community Hacking shows the love for creation and the people of Hamp Hack who go literally without rest to create.

How we built it

We recorded people around Hamp Hack while the competition was happening and captured the hard work of each team.

Challenges we ran into

Deciding wether we wanted to code and make a program or try to create something a little unconventional for Hackathons.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Creating a concise depiction of what Hamp hack is all about.

What we learned

Bring hardware suited for the challenges that we want to face.

What's next for Community Hacking

Continue to pull hackers together and show the outside world how great Hackathons are.
