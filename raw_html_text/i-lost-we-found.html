Inspiration

Many people lose their items and cannot find it.

What it does

It allows you to broadcast the information of your lost items and also allows you to broadcast the items you found.

How we built it

We built it with create-react-native-app and React. We assign different components to each of us, and we bring them together.

Challenges we ran into

No seats nor table, slow wifi at first, no wifi all day, and ran into technical issues like syntax errors and installation issue(setting up the project).

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The app is halfway done, and we have all the functionality working correctly. Make our table : image of our table-> link

What we learned

We learned React-native and Ignite and corporate with each other

What's next for I Lost We Found

extend the functionality of the lostView and foundView such as adding the search bar and categorize items.
