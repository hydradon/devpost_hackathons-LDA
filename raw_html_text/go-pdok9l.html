Inspiration

You're in Rome for a day. You want a whistle-stop tour from the best tour guides in Rome without the hassle of booking onto a tour and following a set route. Cue, Go.

What it does

Record a short audio tour at a location eg "You're standing outside Kings College Cambridge, it was founded in 1441......." Upload the audio with automatic geotaging. When anyone walks past this point they can click on your marker that you left to hear the audio tour.

You can see all the available hotels in your area on your Go map. You can leave an audio review if you wish to as well.

How I built it

  • Native android with node back end
  • Hosted on Azure with aws for storage
  • Uses the Skyscanner API to get all available hotels in the area
