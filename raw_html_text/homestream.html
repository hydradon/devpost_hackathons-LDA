Inspiration

Is your Utility bill too high? Usually leaves you feeling: Confused Frustrated Do you blame your roomates? Wished you could track your usage? or even pay as you go?

What it does

Stream payments to Utility Companies/Entities: continuously (example 0.13 $/sec) efficiently (avoid peak hours) safely dynamically (through multiple markets) using the interledger protocol
