Inspiration

  • Every time I finish a meal with friends, we always take a long time splitting the bill according to what we individually ordered. I thought an app that could calculate by choosing your own orders in a receipt can help speed up that process.

What it does

  • Calculates how much you have to pay in a receipt after a meal with friends so you can send money (like venmo) to him/her fast.

How I built it

  • Swift

Challenges I ran into

  • TableViewCells for Items being reused created huge problems for me.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

  • Clean, simple UI
  • Applied app to real world circumstances and it worked.

What I learned

  • Coding logic improved

What's next for FairShare

  • Improving UI
