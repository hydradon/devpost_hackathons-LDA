coolspots

Friends have trouble choosing where to go eat because of personal preferences. Introducing coolspots, the web application that allows friends to start a smart poll where past personal preferences are taken to account, but a Tinder-based, swipe-left & swipe-right mechanism, will be used to make a rank-based choosing based on mutual benefits.
