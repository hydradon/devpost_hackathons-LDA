What it does

You can add your daily rules or routines to the list. At the end of the day ask it to calculate your score. It will ask ever rule you respond with yes or no.

For example: you have rules like Don't tell lies or work hard or eat less. You can add it to dusky list. Night when you are going to sleep calculate score. Tell her weather you told lies or you worked hard or you eat more. It will tell how much rules you followed every night.
