Inspiration

I wanted to make something with APL and saw the need for a skill with high quality animal photos and sounds.

What it does

Displays beautiful images on your Alexa Screen Device while making beautiful animal sounds.

How I built it

NodeJS, ASK, APL, Lambda.

Challenges I ran into

Took a LONG LONG LONG time to comb through 50,000 royalty-free/commercial-use sounds and images, to get the best 1000!!!

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The quality of the sounds and photos and the way the skill works with APL.

What I learned

How to use APL!!

What's next for Animal Parade

Additional integration, perhaps ISP.
