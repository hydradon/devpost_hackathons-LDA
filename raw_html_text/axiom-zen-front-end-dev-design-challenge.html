tricky## Inspiration I work as volunteeer at Clube de Leitura Caruaru (CLC) as designer and social media. They asked me to do a logo redesign this year, and now I saw an oportunity to do the logo redesign and a website proposal to them. The new logo represents the diversity in our group and the love for the books.

What it does

The site proposal is about to make easier people to connect, know about CLC and to be informed about all the events.

How I built it

For the first time I used Adobe Fireworks to do the wireframes. For this project, I challenged myself to use something new, so I used this program besides Adobe Illustrator to make the site proposal.

Challenges I ran into

As I work for the CLC, I had almost none difficulties. But do this alone, without discuss ideas with someone was a little challenging for me. But was an awesome experience.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I'm really proud of the final project and the time it took to finish (24h!).

What I learned

Besides know another adobe program, I learned how a group is important to discuss ideas.
