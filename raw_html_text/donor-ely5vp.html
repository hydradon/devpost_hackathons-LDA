When people sometimes suddenly need any vital organs or blood they cannot find it easily. This problem is more acute in developing countries. In those countries you cannot find blood donors or any organ donor easily. As a result people die from blood losses or any organ failure. This problem inspired me to develop such an app.

People from all over the world can access this application put their information about donating their organs or blood in a large global database. People around the world can then contact the person who enlisted them as a donor. In this way the process of saving lives become easier.

I built it using android. The database was built with SQL.

This is an individual project. I am happy that I could complete it.

Enhancing the User interface would be the next step for donor.
