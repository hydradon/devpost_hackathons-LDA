• Magical Readings

Facility to help drama professionals and students to learn and study their lines is now available.

The “Magical Readings” Alexa “skill” (voice-driven app) has passed certification by Amazon. We’re planning initially it will only be available to a restricted group of invited users Details of 'Pre-Release Volunteer Launch' are available via a link below.

Currently, twelve of Shakespeare’s most famous plays are included, with more planned in the near future.

It has been thoroughly tested internally by our staff, and by drama professionals, with some of the features they requested now added.
