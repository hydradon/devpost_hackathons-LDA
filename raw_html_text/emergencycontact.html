Inspiration

Many people cannot reach out to their emergency contacts during their emergency and a result they die or get injured badly.

What it does

It will send a message to the emergency contact they provided on the app and also send that person's current location to the contact.

How we built it

We have used android studio to build this app.

Accomplishments that we're proud of
