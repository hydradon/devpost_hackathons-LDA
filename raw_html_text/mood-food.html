Inspiration

We all live in low-income areas. There are places that serve healthy foods in our neighborhoods but people may not know about it. So we made this website to help people in neighborhoods like ours and any neighborhood get access to healthier foods.

What it does

Mood Food utilizes IBM Watson for natural language classifying to determine the categorical mood of the user and process it into an efficient healthy food choice specifically for them!

How I built it

It's a full stack MongoDB, express, react and node.js application that utilizes IBM Watson for natural language classifying to determine the categorical mood of the user.

Challenges I ran into

Building the back end was difficult but we managed to do it.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Being able to build the back end with IBM Watson.

What's next for Mood Food
