• ConcertCam makes your cell phone concert videos sound better.

  • Information and data about cell phone concert videos.

  • Data from our algorithm finding a match within a 2 hour long live show.

Inspiration

Crowds film concerts. How do we enhance that experience?

What it does

ConcertCam automagically enhances the audio quality of cell phone videos taken at concerts by replacing the recorded audio with a board feed.

How we built it

Using Azure, machine learning and a proprietary FFT audio syncing algorithm.

Challenges we ran into

Building a robust algorithm which cuts through noise and crowd sounds in order to synchronize proved technically challenging.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We produced that algorithm.

What's next for ConcertCam

We plan on integrating UJO for automated rights management.
