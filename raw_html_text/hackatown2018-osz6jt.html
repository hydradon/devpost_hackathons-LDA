For this hackathon we designed and implemented a web application called watchMTL that aims to make our cities more intelligent, and safer. By anonymously reporting incidents that you see happening around you, you retain your privacy, but at the same time potentially help save many lives. We hope this application will also help people feel more compassionate towards other residents, and take the wellbeing of their community into consideration, for a greater future and a safer nation.
