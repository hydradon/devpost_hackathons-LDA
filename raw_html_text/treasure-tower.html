Inspiration

Our inspiration for this game was Hotline Miami and Indiana Jones.

What it does

Using WASD and right click to shoot, the player ventures through paths to get to the next room, avoiding guards, and ultimately collecting the tower's hidden treasure.

How we built it

We used Unity to create our game, and Krita for some of the art assets.
