Juggling morning coffee, toast and trying to check if the trains are on time on your phone? Now you can get live departure status from stations across the UK by simply saying, Alexa, open train departures.

Train Departures will give you a brief audio overview of trains from your station, "Delayed" means the train is more than 10 minutes past its expected time, "Late" is less than 10 minutes, "Cancelled" is cancelled (obviously)

If you have an Echo with a screen eg a Spot, Show or FireTV Train Departures will also show you a live departure board with times, visual status indicators and the platform number when available.
