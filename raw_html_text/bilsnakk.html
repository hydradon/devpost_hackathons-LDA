Inspiration

I was about to drive to the airport in the last minute. Once I got to the car, I realise that other car is blocking my car. I wish there was an easy way to contact the owner of the car.

Bilsnakk is the app solve parking problem and many more!

What it does

WIll Bilsnakk can any user contact any owner of car in Norway.

How we built it

We used react-native, with native-base to make both Android na iOS apps.

Challenges we ran into

App use the latest Siri for a recording messages while users are driving. Working with the SiriKit gave is quite a challange.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

In addition to nice app interface, it is possible to send messages using Siri. User can also scan the licence plate using his mobile phone camera.

What we learned

Our mentors challenges us in marketing, business model and marked research.

What's next for Bilsnakk

We have to polish app and get it out there in Google and Apple Store.
