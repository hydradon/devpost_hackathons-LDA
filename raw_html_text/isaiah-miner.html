Inspiration

I got into mining a month ago, but realized there is no easy way for a non-technical person to get into mining or even owning crypto.

What it does

How I built it

Java. And custom made PC.

Challenges I ran into

Getting the hardware just right, and facilitating bitcoin transaction signing.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned

Team management, Git version control, JavaFX, hardware building...
