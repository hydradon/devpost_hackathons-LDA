Inspiration

Friend built a light suit but it was rudimentary and hard to handle. We decided to stick to the light suit idea and throw in different modes and music analysis.

What it does

It makes lots of flashes and can change modes by interacting with a Pebble smart watch or by a phone app. It can cycle through different modes that range from preset shows to real-time music recognition capable of immediately responding to audio input and creating impromptu audio-visual experiences.

How I built it

Science.

Challenges I ran into

Running out of energy

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Successfully incorporating the Pebble, Android app, Arduino, and LEDs into a project that surpassed all expectations!

What I learned

How to build Android applications, Pebble applications, embedded digital signal processing, Bluetooth integration, how to make cool clocks

What's next for Light suit

Marketing.... and a more streamlined version of the suit. Create buses for wires and better latches to hold the lights on the body.
