drug_reminder

HackMed 2018 project

We are building a text messaging service that will send you personalised text messages whenever you need to take your medicine. In the initial text message, we provide warnings on food and drug interactions as well as toxicity information. The following text are simpler reminders.

Ideally rather than asking the user to input their drug information, we would have an automatic transition from prescription template issued by their doctor to our service.
