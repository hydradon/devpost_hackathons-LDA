What it does

Enter your address, and it will create in itinerary for a you so you can have a spontaneous day filled with activities and great restaurants around you. It also tells you the estimate of each Uber ride to get from one activity to the other.

How I built it

Tornado for backend. Bootstrap for prettiness. Used Yelp, Uber, Google Maps API.

Challenges I ran into

Making it gorgeous. I haven't succeeded...

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It's kind of gorgeous, so I'm still kind of proud.

What I learned

Design is really important, and not as easy as I thought.

What's next for LeadMe

Link Uber account and Facebook account. Have personalized trips but remembering the trips you've been and your interest on facebook. Make it social and be able to have spontaneous trips with friends or even strangers.
