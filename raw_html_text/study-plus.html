Checking for classroom availability in a specific University, given the class times.

For example, what we've decided to do, is given two selected times, we will go into the database and retrieve all rooms where there are no classes occuring within the time range. We would be able to select a specific campus, building, time and then we will retrieve all classrooms that will fit our criteria.
