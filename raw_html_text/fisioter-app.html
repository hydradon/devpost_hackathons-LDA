What's next for Fisioter-App

Inspiration

Bring services of excellence to everyone.

What it does

The wearable detects bad posture in people, telling them in real time that they should be fine.

How I built it

It was developed using an arduino one and high precision flexion sensors. The Mobile Application was developed on Android Studio.

Challenges I ran into

The efficient communication between the development card and the mobile application was not easy, we had to work very hard.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We succeeded in working as a team. I can say that 100% of what we did was productive and unique.

What I learned

We learned that there are no impossible, although sometimes what can limit people are the resources with which they have.

What's next for FisioterApp

Use it to use physiotherapy, for people who are in rehabilitation not only of the back but of other parts of their body.
