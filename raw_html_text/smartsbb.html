Inspiration

The current routing algorithms today do not do a good job at offering drivers parking options that suit their needs. Therefore it occurs to us that we could utilize IOT technology and a smarter routing strategy to facilitate daily commute.

What it does

Whenver you buy a train ticket and you want to drive your car to the station, we will assign you a free parking lot based on a lot of requirements that fulfill your needs.
