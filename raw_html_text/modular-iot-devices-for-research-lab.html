Inspiration: Getting locked out of the lab, and frustration of the building things from scratch.

What it does: Currently, the first two modules are a face recognition module, and door actuation module.

How we built it: From various supplies left behind in the lab from previous projects.

Challenges we ran into: Planning out how everything is going to flow, and leaving options for the future.

Accomplishments that we're proud of: Built the first proof of concept, and got the ball rolling.

What we learned: The rabbit hole is deep, no matter how simple the problem should be.

What's next for modular iot devices for research lab: Adding more devices to the network. Things to do improve, the door lock, actuation/sensing. Improve facial recognition module, add garden module etc. Whatever the people in the lab feel like making.
