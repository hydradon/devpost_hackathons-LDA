emote

Web app that tracks your day to day emotions and shows you how you compare to others.

This application will track how your mood changes over time and how your emotions have shaped your life ultimately. It also shows you how you stand against others who also use the service to show you statistically what other people are feeling the past month of results collected daily.

Image is captured live with a webcam integration and results appear right away.
