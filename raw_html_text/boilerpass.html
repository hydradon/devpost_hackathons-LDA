Inspiration

2FA applications rely on immediate interaction, and often don't have ways to see a history of approvals.

What it does

This app doesn't just approve 2FA authentication requests, but also logs and saves approval events as well. It can also work in the background and be disabled.

What I learned

We're always looking for an excuse to continue learning Android development.

What's next for BoilerPass

To expand BoilerPass, we could add native UI for acceptance entries, and also add options to delete entries or clear all entries.
