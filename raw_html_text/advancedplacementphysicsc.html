Monetize

Our project lets you take a picture of yourself and transform it into a stunning work of art! We were inspired by the works of Claude Monet, the impressionist artist. We have options for watercolor and stained glass styles, and we choose random color palettes to apply to your photos. We also have a default option for a more realistic look. The best part about it is that the impressions are generated right before your eyes! See yourself like you've never seen before!
