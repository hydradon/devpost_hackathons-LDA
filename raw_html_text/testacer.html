Inspiration

Having hard time finding a group of classmates to study with.

What it does

Allows students to meet other classmates so that they can study together for a midterm, work on homework together, etc.

How we built it

We used git, Firebase, Swift, and Xcode to create the iOS mobile application.

Challenges we ran into

Most of us have different backgrounds, so it was hard dividing responsibilities and combining our work together.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Just in two days, we have at least managed to create something where students going to the same university and taking the same course can meet together!

What we learned

We learned a lot about fundamentals of iOS programming, including how to manage asynchronous tasks.

What's next for TestAcer

I'd like to eventually build a full version of it, as it's always been an app that I've wanted to create since a while ago.
