Inspiration

We were under pressure and hungry ..so we made a game to find food in VR. Also inspired to give the kids in hospitals a game to entertain with discovery and mystery in their dull hospital room.

What it does

You have to find food "treasures" using the project Tango HMD to navigate the room

How we built it

Using Unity and the community assets (for a start). Relying on the Tantgo project.

Challenges we ran into

Very short time, harsh setup and configuration. Unity/Tango/Cardboard learning curve in few hours

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It's working and fun

What we learned

Time runs fast and Unity is a great versatille tool to explore. Tango is near future, lots more to come
