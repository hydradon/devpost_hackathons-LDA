• Zrilo Business Insights

Inspiration

How do businesses and their advisors make the right choice of a Xero Add On? Who takes the business on that journey to getting off and running? Once there, how do they reap the rewards of the Xero ecosystem business apps working together for their business, advisory or franchise?

Solving these problems is the inspiration for the Zrilo Business Insights platform.

What it does

Recommends Xero Add Ons and Xero/Add On solutions for a list of industries End business owners and Advisors on behalf of their clients can generate integrated cloud app recommendations based on specific industry and business related questions. Recommendations come with a network of preferred cloud integrators for businesses to get help.

Provides businesses, advisors and franchises with individual business, industry specific dashboard views combining financial data coming from Xero and operational data coming Xero Add Ons

Current industry dashboards Field Service - ServiceM8/Xero Professional Services - WorkflowMax/Xero Wholesale & Distrubution - Dear/Tradegecko/Xero

Provides multi-business owners, business advisors and franchisors with multi-view benchmark reporting across the entire business domain

How I built it

It was built with Angular and modern low latency global scale PaaS covering data, compute, communication and IAM

Challenges I ran into

Learning new technologies and working with tooling in early stages of their maturity

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Building a global scale platform with flexible reporting architecture that covers a wide range of progressive use cases.

What I learned

A lot about javascript programming, cloud platforms and APIs. I learned its a great time to develop apps with these amazing frameworks & platforms

What's next for Zrilo Business Insights

More Xero Add Ons and Industries for Recommendations More Xero Add On integrations for Industry specific Dashboards and Benchmark Reporting metrics
