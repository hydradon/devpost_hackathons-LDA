Inspiration

You're hungry and you don't know what to eat. You get FOOD WISE. Find the best foods around you. Didn't you ever want a menu that told you what was good? Now you can have it.

What it does

Provides you the best menu item recommendations around you. You will see all the food items from menus across all restaurants around you, and always have the best options no matter where in the world you are.

How I built it

Ionic Framework for CSS and Angular. Firebase for the backend. Node for API requests.

Challenges I ran into

Getting routing states to work. Finding your location. UI.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Firebase Integration. UI. Getting routing states to work. :)

What I learned

Learning to work with people from different countries and workplaces.

What's next for WISE

Social integration. Distance tracking. Better food recommendation algorithms. Star ratings of restaurants. Better UI. Photos of the foods that you will be eating.
