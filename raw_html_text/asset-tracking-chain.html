Inspiration

Asset tracking tracks history of an item, helps with quick action when there are recalls, reduces costs with shared ledger implemented, and increases social responsibility (avoiding blood diamonds, using manufacturers that do not use underage workforce)

What it does

Tracks all touches to an item from inception

How I built it

Modeled using IBM Hyperledger Composer

Challenges I ran into

Although the tool has templates, some level of development knowledge is needed

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Understanding, planning and doing high level design

What I learned

More about Hyperledger Composer tool, Challenges faced by industry

What's next for Asset Tracking Chain

Subscription based asset tracking product that implements shared ledger– building the basics that can be used across industries Industry specific features – focus on pharma, retail Interoperability
