VoiceCapsule.com is a virtual interactive communication tool used with all Alexa devices.  VoiceCapsule.com is designed for individuals to post audio and video recordings that they can share w/ friends, family or associates.  By combining VoiceCapsule.com with the Alexa Skill Voice Capsule, users can now have virtual interactive conversations with the recordings.

Alexa Skill Voice Capsule Features are:

  1. Alexa Skill Voice Capsule works on All Amazon Echo Alexa devices!  Audio will play on Amazon Video Devices and the Audio from Video recordings will play on Amazon Audio only Devices.  Record once, play back on all Amazon Echo Alexa devices.
    1. If on Amazon Show or Spot - if there is a video it will play the video, if there is not a video recording, it will then play only audio.  
    2. If on Amazon Echo, Dot, or Tap - if there a video recording, Voice Capsule will play the audio from a video recording.  
  2. Individuals save or upload audio / video responses on  www.VoiceCapsule.com
  3. Individuals have the ability to decide if a recording will be private or for a specific person or for everyone in their contact list on VoiceCapsule.com.  
    1. Private can only be listened to by the account owner.  
    2. Contact List is everyone in the StoryTeller’s VoiceCapsule.com contact list.
    3. Specific person is the only person the StoryTeller invites to hear their recording.  
  4. To use the Amazon Alexa Skill Voice Capsule
    1. Ask Alexa to open Voice Capsule and to speak to a specific person
      Example:  “Alexa tell Voice Capsule that I want to talk to Jim Wyatt”  
    2. Then begin asking questions.  
      Example:   “What is your favorite book?”  

The Voice Capsule concept has been in the making for over 20 years.  Have you ever wanted to talk to someone and you know they are not available?  The original concept was to have family and friends record their life stories.  But, we needed to find an easy way to share those stories today and with future generations.  With the Amazon Echo devices Alexa provides an avenue for those stories to be shared.    

But as we began programming, we realized Voice Capsule can provide so much more.  Every user can transform Voice Capsule into what would be most beneficial to them and whomever they share their recordings with.

Amazon Alexa Skill Voice Capsule is a Life Hack because life is better when you can have virtual interactive communications such as:

  1. Virtual bedtime story reader.  Even though you are on a business trip, your child can ask the Alexa Skill Voice Capsule to have you virtually read their favorite story.  
  2. Virtual Assistant makes life easier for those working from home or anyone needing to log activities or events and then search for them.  Your potential client could ask the Alexa Skill Voice Capsule to show them one of your completed projects.
  3. Diary or journal.  Ask Alexa Skill Voice Capsule to tell you what you did on a particular date.  
  4. Story Journal.  Ask Alexa Skill Voice Capsule to have Mom tell you about her favorite recipe.


The Alexa Skill Voice Capsule allows users to virtually interact with memories, stories and information shared by themselves or others on VoiceCapsule.com.  By using the Alexa Skill Voice Capsule users can have virtual interactive communications with audio and video recordings posted on VoiceCapsule.com. 

Voice Capsule is unique because it allows you to Virtually Interactively Communicate with yourself, friends and family beginning now and anytime in the future.  Voice Capsule also provides an audio search engine for your posted recordings.

Voice Capsule is a Voice Time Machine because you can make recordings today and then you are able to talk to your younger self anytime in the future.  How cool would it be if you left recordings today and your great-great-etc grandchildren could have a virtual interactive conversation with you.

Voice Capsule works by
The StoryTeller (VoiceCapsule.com Account Owner who makes recordings):

  1. The StoryTeller must have a VoiceCapsule.com active account  
  2. The StoryTeller must post audio or video recordings to VoiceCapsule.com and declare whether the recording would be private or shared with their selected contacts
  3. The StoryTeller requests VoiceCapsule.com to send an invite to their contact to go listen to their Alexa Skill Voice Capsule recording


The Listener (Alexa User):

  1. The Listener must have a VoiceCapsule.com active account
  2. The Listener must add the Voice Capsule skill to their Alexa account
  3. The Listener must link the Alexa Voice Capsule skill to their VoiceCapsule.com account
  4. The Listener tells Alexa to Open Voice Capsule and which Storyteller they want to talk to (You can listen to your own recordings or only those recordings after receiving an invite from another Storyteller)
