Inspiration

Inspired by the current Opioid Crisis in the Canadian Healthcare system.

What it does

Creates a ledger of patient drug history and allows the medical professional to input the healthcard number and DIN to see if the drug being prescribed is being over-prescribed or if it interacts negatively with other medication they are on.

How I built it

Build using classes and text files in java.

Challenges I ran into

Structuring the drug database and ensuring the files were read in properly with varying list lengths.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Making it run in under 10 hours from start to finish at our first hackathon.

What I learned

Hackathons are fun!!

What's next for PharmaSafe

Development to include allergies and other conditions.
