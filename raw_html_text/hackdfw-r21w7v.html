Overview

Cook Pal is a way to automate the way you cook. You don't have to worry about wasting time trying to figure out what you are going to eat each day. Simply update your pantry with your ingredients in one of our easy and quick methods. Let your handy Cook Pal search among thousands of recipes and tell you what is feasible.

Tropo

TropoPHP is a set of PHP classes for working with Tropo's cloud communication service . Tropo allows a developer to create applications that run over the phone, IM, SMS, and Twitter using web technologies. This library communicates with Tropo over JSON.

Future development

Cook Pal has great potential in the IoT field. It could be integrated with other devices to facilitate the ingredient update process. From bar code scanners to image recognition, this application could easily be more connected.
