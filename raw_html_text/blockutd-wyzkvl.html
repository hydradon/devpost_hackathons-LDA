Inspiration

We wanted to try learning something completely new, and thought of going down the finance route while keeping the project personal to us

What it does

Many productivity apps reward you for being productive. That may be a good incentive, but we felt that it wasn't good enough. blockUTD blocks the user from using social media, and in the case that they still use social media, they are fined a small fee to a friend who also uses the app.

How we built it

We used Android Studio

Challenges we ran into

Accomplishments that we're proud of
