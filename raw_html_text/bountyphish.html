BountyPhish is a premier social engineering bounty program. Similar in structure to existing bug bounty programs like HackerOne, BountyPhish is a trusted third party that enables ethical attackers to prod organizations for vulnerabilities. Unlike other bug bounties, however, BountyPhish focuses on the most common attack faced by today's companies: social engineering.
