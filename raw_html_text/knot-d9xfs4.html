Inspiration

We wanted to promote learning and fun in the process of immigration.

What it does

A comprehensive platform that provides tools for learning and adaptation before making the journey, but also a way to reach out for help if needed.

How we built it

We used a variety of platforms including web, mobile, and even augmented reality to demo the full vision of Ellis.

Challenges we ran into

We only had a short time to create a working prototype. Github won't hold files over 100mb. 3GB+ repo zipped and put on Google Drive and shared publicly.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Completed a substantial working demo of the Ellis platform that includes several interactive pieces.

What we learned

Using a lot of extra time Friday night to continue brainstorming on a concept was time well spent.

What's next for Ellis

We'll be awaiting feedback and decide on any next steps after that.
