Web Window

With Web Window, you will be able to experience a 3D view of the web without requiring a bulky VR headset. We have created a series of demos demonstrating how camera tracking can improve your web browsing experience.

Maps

Have you ever been looking at a map, and wanted to see just around the corner? Using Web Window, you can peek around the map as you would with a tabletop map; using your head!

Shopping

Shopping for furniture online has never been less stressful! With a 3D model, you can intuitively peek around the object and inspect all sides!

Video

Love to have your favorite Twitch stream in full screen, but still want to see the chat? With Web Window, just shift your gaze to the right, and the chat is right there!
