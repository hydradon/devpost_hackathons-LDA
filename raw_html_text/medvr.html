Inspiration

The lack of a interactive way to study and perform medical procedures.

What it does

Helps medical students learn basic to advance procedures through VR.

How we built it

We used a combination of the Oculus Rift, Leap Motion, and Unity Software to design the prototype.

Challenges we ran into

Integrating the Leap Motion and setting up the physics aspect in unity.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Integrating the Leap Motion with Oculus Rift

What we learned

How to make 3D games and simulations in Unity.

What's next for MedVR

To polish and add more functionality by working with medical experts and to roll it out to students in medical schools.
