Inspiration

Wildlife trafficking is one of the largest drivers of extinction the people and hunters don’t have enough awareness how much danger could this lead . This causes a lot of chaos in our Eco system .

What it does

An application and website can connects all conservationists together. People can report trades of wildlife animals and violations to them.

How we built it

We made a static website demo using HTML, CSS, Jquery, Javascript, Bootstrap.

Challenges we ran into

How to make our idea useful for people who is so far away from wild life environment. How to make people interested in our product.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Completing the demo before our presentation. Participating in this hackthon and meeting new people.

What we learned

We learned more about wildlife in Egypt, and the threats it faces. We learned more about how to approach problems and find solution for it.

What's next for The Hunters Hunters

Making this website dynamic with a proper backend.
