As students who are soon to enter their first year of university, we thought it would be a good idea to make a way to find a group of peers that we could study with.

It connects students, teachers, and tutors that take/ teach common classes so they can answer questions and share past assignments/ tests.

We build our website using HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

As students who are new to engineering, we had to teach ourselves how to create a website from scratch.

We're proud of the design of our website.

We learned a lot about web development.

We plan to continue working on our project so that it may one day be used for other students.
