Inspiration

Fixed assets represent the largest single asset category on the balance sheet, yet rarely do fixed assets command management time that is proportionate to the magnitude of the investment.

What it does

Fixed Asset Connect helps to eliminate ghost assets and zombie assets. Using this App, all of your company’s fixed assets will be tracked through every stage of their life-cycle, from acquisition, depreciation, maintenance, revaluation, asset split and transfer, to disposal.

What's next for Fixed Asset Connect

Add Internet of Things and Machine Learning capabilities to identify assets from photos and to recommend preventive maintenance based on actual usage.
