Inspiration

We love Drones and wanted to do something different

How it works

Simply open the app and you can see via Esri your location as well as those of the Gimbal beacons it reads, using Nexmo . Click the location on the app and the drone will go to the desired beacon. We can send from beacon to beacon through the app with ease. All of this, of course, is hosted on Atlantic.net's cloud servers.

Challenges we ran into

For one, we don't have a huge drone, so for the scope of this hack we are using a minidrone. Getting the communication together!

Accomplishments that we're proud of
