What it does

Each user has their nationality stored and based on that we get the power of their passport (the countries they could visit without VISA). They have a starting amount of money and time limit which they have to use to their advantage in order to win the game. The goal is to visit every country without a VISA without running out of money or time. For every destination there are three different packages consisting of real-time price data for flights and hotel options extracted from Booking.com. The logic behind this is that the cheaper the package that you buy, the longer it takes you to reach your destination and rest (more time is subtracted from your countdown timer). However, for every country you visit your passive income grows higher.

How we built it

We built the project using PHP, HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, MySQL, XAMPP and Amadeus API to get real-time price data.

Challenges we ran into

It was hard to choose which API to utilize since every different product has its own advantages and disadvantages. We chose Amadeus API because it does not require you to be a business company and we can get 2000 free requests to the API. We feel like we made the correct decision.
