carsNcows

Have you ever wondered how much carbon emissions humans produce? Well wonder no more! carsNcows is a user-friendly platform designed to spread awareness of the greenhouse gases emitted by automobile transportation and beef and dairy consumption. Simple minimalism and fun graphics combine to create an engaging but educational experience that never gets old. Try carsNcows today!
