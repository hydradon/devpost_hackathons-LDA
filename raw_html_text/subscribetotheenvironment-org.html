Inspiration

We feel that it should be super easy for every individual or company to make a positive impact on the environment regularly. Technology makes it easy to subscribe to our favorite services, but there is no way to subscribe to our favorite world (Earth) so that we can leave a positive legacy. So we're making it possible.

What it does

You can start a monthly subscription at different amounts, either as an individual or as a company. The subscription payments get distributed to vetted charities and projects that are having a positive impact on the environment. You can also control how your subscription amount gets distributed, and you can pause at any time.

How we built it

We built it with React and Node

Challenges I ran into

It was difficult to settle on a project that we felt would use technology to make a really positive impact in a sustainable way and that we felt had a USP that people would connect with.

Accomplishments that we are proud of

Going through many ideas and motivational ups and downs before anchoring down and implementing something simple and elegant.

What we learned

We learned about team formation and motivation and how important it is to really be excited about the project and it's impact if you want to get it done.

What's next for SubscribeToTheEnvironment.org

We want to connect with trusted charities and partners and facilitate the flow of subscriptions to meaningful projects.
