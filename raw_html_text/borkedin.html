BorkedIn

LinkedIn, but for dogs.

Profiles

  • Search for doggos who are up and coming in their careers (many have been featured in We Rate Dogs).
  • See dogs' service credentials (therapy dogs, police dogs, support dogs, seeing-eye dogs).
  • See which skills from our data-driven matrix they have been endorsed for (based on Twitter retweets).
  • See profiles of similar puppers in their network.
  • Send shmackos and pats to dogs to feel the love.
  • See links to donate to causes they care about!

Parks

  • Suggest parks to take a meeting (playdate) with a doggo.
  • Browse parks and street view data from six cities around the world (Nashville, Chicago, New York, Los Angeles, Seattle, and Calgary, courtesy of public datasets).
  • Preview parks in virtual reality before visiting them in person.
  • See meetups and events that are occurring in parks.
