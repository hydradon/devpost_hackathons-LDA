Inspiration

We felt there is need to make food ordering more convenient at the wheel.

What it does

Orders food for pickup or delivery right from your car.

How I built it

Cloud based speech recognition and restaurant search, handled by a state machine in spring boot.

Challenges I ran into

GM Lib. IBM-Watson speech recognition was inaccurate.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

End-to-end solution in 3 days.

What I learned

Teamwork, Spring State Machine, Google Cloud Services

What's next for Food Finder

Actual ordering of real food items, Choosing between pickup and delivery, optimized voice filtering, integrate touch
