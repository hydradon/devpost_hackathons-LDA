What it does

ARNeighborhood (pronounced Our Neigborhood) is an Augmented Reality educational tool designed to encourage people to learn about the history of their communities. Our goal is to create a tool that augments the user's experience by giving them an interactive way to learn the unique stories waiting to be discovered right around the corner.
