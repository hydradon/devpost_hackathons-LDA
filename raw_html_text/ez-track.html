Inspiration

Having family members that suffer from diabetes.

What it does

EZtrack is a device that automatically keeps track of your blood sugar and sends the results to your phone. It gives suggestions on how you should deal with your sugar level. It will remind you throughout the day on when you should check your sugar level or estimate where it should be. Below is a demo of the app where you plug in your sugar level and the app tells you how to approach it.

How we built it

We designed a site map and wire frames. Then we used a bootstrap template and filled in all the content. We conducted research on our topic. we also used javascript to create a prototype glucose checker.

Challenges we ran into

Time management and learning new technology. Also using git hub and azure.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting the website completed, completing the prototype, and working as a team.

What we learned

We learned about bootstrap, azure, how to make a site map, some javascript, roles as a team, more about diabetes, and team project management.

What's next for EZ Track

The design of the watch, mobile apps, and devices.
