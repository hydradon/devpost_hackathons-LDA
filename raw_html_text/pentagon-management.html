Inspiration

In the bay area, with booming tech companies all around us, we saw the need to help project managers organize their work and their team.

What it does

Allows managers to add employees, their skillsets, and their ratings. Combined with the task that needs to be done, Pentagon Management gives automatic recommendations for the optimal employees for the job.

How we built it

We used html, css, and various other frontend utilities.

Challenges we ran into

Hosting it online.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

making a good ui and ux, along with hosting it succesfully.

What we learned

How to use html and css to our benefit and more efficiently, along with hosting.

What's next for Pentagon Management

Connect more backend algorithms.
