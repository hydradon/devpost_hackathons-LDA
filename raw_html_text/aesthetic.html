Inspiration

Most of the people nowadays are not able to take good picture, people waste their time to take lots of picture before they actually get a picture that has a quality they wanted.

What it does

Our application helps those people to take good picture by considering weather, location, angle, and referencing good pictures from instagram.

How I built it

We built it by using 3 api, which is google map api, openweathermap api and instagram api.

Challenges I ran into

The challenges we encounter are the way to integrate all the functionality into one pieces, and also the integration between back end and front end.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

This is the first android app that we have designed.

What I learned

We learned how to make an android application app and how to use api. Apart from that, we also learned that how to design an UI by considering multiple aspects such as usability.

What's next for Aesthetic

Add more functionality that let people take good pictures much more easier
