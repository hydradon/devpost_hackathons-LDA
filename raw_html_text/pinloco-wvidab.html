Inspiration

We were inspired by current photo sharing apps like Instagram, but we felt like the current location tracking technology has not been used to its fullest capacity.

What it does

Once users have signed up and logged in, they will be able to see a map of all the photos of other users in the area around them.

How I built it

We built the app using Swift, MapKit, and Parse.

Challenges I ran into

We were unable to retrieve location data from previously taken images.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We were able to save to and pull from Parse to produce a map of all the locations that the user's photos have been.

What I learned

MapKit and Parse

What's next for PinLoco

More users and privacy settings
