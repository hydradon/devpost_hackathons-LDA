As academic students, we tend to find ourselves feeling burnt out, exhausted, and drained. We’re usually too busy juggling all our tasks at once or not doing them at all. In fact, according to the American Psychological Association, “Between 80 and 95 percent of students procrastinate on their schoolwork.” This app will help students manage their stress, climb off the never-ending rollercoaster of procrastination, and decompress their daily tasks with an intelligent platform.
