AmISick?

A skill on your Alexa device that allows you to help identify medical issues based on your symptoms?

Exactly! Also, a lot more...

AmISick? is your friendly mock-doctor skill to help to identify medical problems based on your symptoms. The user can enter body location, sub-location of symptoms, along with age and gender, based on which the skill suggests a list of symptoms. The user can then enter one or more symptoms, and AmISick? would determine possible medical issues with their probability (in %) based on the combination of symptoms and provides useful diagnosis information. AmISick? is not a substitute for professional medical advice, but a tool meant to supplement it.

The skill is powered by ApiMedic, one of the largest & well-maintained medical databases & medical API.

DISCLAIMER: This tool does not provide medical advice, and is for informational and educational purposes only, and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, treatment or diagnosis. Call your doctor to receive medical advice. If you think you may have a medical emergency, please dial your local emergency response phone number.
