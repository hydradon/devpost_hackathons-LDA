Inspiration

Have you ever been in a class where you know nobody but wish to study with somebody? StudyBuddy helps you connect with others in the same class through an online forum! By going through the forum or making your own forum posting for the class you wish a study buddy for, you can easily find new people whom you wish to study with!

What it does

Allows you to connect with others in your classrooms and create study sessions with new people

How I built it

We used JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Node.js, and mongodb to dynamically display, store, and update information.

Challenges I ran into

Connecting, reading, and writing into the database

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Creating a web app that allows you to connect with other individuals

What I learned

Enhanced our experience in front-end and back-end development as well as increased on knowledge in database creation and manipulation.

What's next for StudyBuddy

Creating a chatroom so you can chat with other people!
