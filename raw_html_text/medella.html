Medella is an IOS application that provides lower-income families with the ability to reach out and receive the health benefits they deserve. Through the use of our secure backend database, users are able to locate free medical clinics and chat with a medical bot at any time right in our application. We also developed many machine learning models to detect various diseases. We implemented these same models in our app for our users to use to detect if they had these such diseases. We developed this project to not only help others but to also raise awareness.
