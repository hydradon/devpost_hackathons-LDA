Inspiration

Most of us have stickers on their laptop. And who does not think they have the coolest. Well, know you can find out if you are right with the use of our app.

What it does

Using OpenCV to calculate a 'hacker score' based on the stickers you have on your laptop cover.

How we built it

The project was build with Android Studio and OpenCV.

Challenges we ran into

OpenCV was a pain to get to work.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud to have a project version running before the submission deadline.

What we learned

It was a fun project to learn about OpenCV and App development.

What's next for Swagathon

Spread the word and get people using the first version of the app. Recognise different stickers and count the numbers of Hackathons attended.
