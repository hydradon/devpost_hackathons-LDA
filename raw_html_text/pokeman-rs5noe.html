Pokéman takes advantage of the Moves API to give you automatically updating motivation to stay active. Train your avatar pokémon by walking, cycling, and running. Future features include ability to increase certain attributes like strength and speed by doing certain activities and evolving pokemon by reaching certain levels. Also a more comprehensive pokemon choosing is forthcoming.
