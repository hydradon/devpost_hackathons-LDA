Inspiration

Studying alone in the library is no fun when you just need some classmates to collaborate with. Connecting people to help them learn!

What it does

Takes your class schedule as input and shows you who's studying your subjects at the library.

How we built it

We performed a lot of whiteboard prototyping and design discussion before settling on our idea. Once selected, the project was built collaboratively using Android studio.
