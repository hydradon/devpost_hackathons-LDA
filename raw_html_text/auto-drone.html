Inspiration

Robotics projects.

What it does

The drone will fly autonomously, eventually being able to follow a robot running on the ground.

How we built it

The drone frame is made out of open-source 3D printed parts.

Challenges we ran into

Programming the electronic speed controllers, reading data from a MPU6050 gyro board.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting the logic able to guide the drone, in theory.

What we learned

We learned how to program an ESC and a MPU6050 Gyro.

What's next for Auto Drone

Next for Auto Drone is to become air-borne. As of now, it will stay grounded.
