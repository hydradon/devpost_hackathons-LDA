Inspiration

Personal experiences and a desire to help others become comfortable with social struggles.

What it does

Allows users to keep track of daily social challenges. They can also communicate with other users through an online forum, and also suggest more challenges to be implanted.

How we built it

We used XCode9 and Swift 4 to program our application.

Challenges we ran into

Saving user data for when the app closes. Setting up online forums.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

A simple UI that is easy to understand and achieves our goals.

What we learned

We learned how to use different Swift functionality such as URLs.

What's next for Speak Up

Plan to implement further suggestions and features, and possibly expand this idea to help individuals in other areas such as physical health. We also plan to design this app on both IOS and Android.
