Inspiration

Identity theft and privacy issues are plagues to one's life. We believe individual identity should reside with the individuals, and not at someone's databases.

One's identity is a person's ultimate asset, comes with birth and expires at death. Technology should be the safeguard of one's identity and prevent it from abuses or misuses.
