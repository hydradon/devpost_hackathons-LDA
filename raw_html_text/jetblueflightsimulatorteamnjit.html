Inspiration

We wanted to give travellers an idea of what a comfortable jetBlue flight feels like before they book.

What it does

It simulates different experiences a passenger would have on the plane; what the seat they are assigned to looks like, the food and drink options and other fun perks.

How we built it

We made the digital models in Adobe Illustrator and the webpage in HTML and CSS.

Challenges we ran into

We had a hard time connecting the webpages to each other. We also had a hard time attaching a GIF that was a part of the experience.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The fluidity of the pages and the graphics for the simulations.

What we learned

Webpages are not hard to create but very time consuming. It would also be helpful to have the same graphics to create more fluidity throughout.

What's next for JetBlueFlightSimulatorTeamNJIT

Create more awesome websites and maybe even extend into VR.
