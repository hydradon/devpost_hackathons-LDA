Inspiration

Being college students at a college with no TA's we saw a need to connect students with others who have previously taken tough classes. With our mobile/web app, students can hire past students of classes as tutors to help them with the test, homework, or just general reviews.

What it does

Allows students to select tutors based on class and professor.

How I built it

We built a mobile and web app using PHP code-igniter.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Having a finished project.

What's next for Instant Tutor

Work more on the review side of the app allowing users to rate tutors.
