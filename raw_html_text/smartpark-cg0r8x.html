Inspiration

The poor parking system in our town.

What it does

It is a smart parking system in which you open the mobile app and book a spot just like at the cinema. When you arrive you open the app scan the QR code and enter. When you leave you automatically pay with the account linked to your app profile.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud that we can fix the poor parking system and replace it with a more advanced one.

What's next for SmartPark

A system that allows SmartPark to be implemented in big parking lots with more than one entry and one exit point. We would like to add the possibility to book the parking space by time without affecting the whole network.
