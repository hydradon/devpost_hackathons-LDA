Inspiration

We love snow cones. Don't you? Our mission is to change the world, one snow cone at a time.

What it does: with the control of android application: a user can select which flavor, up to 7 different options and combinations, to add to their snow cone.

How I built it

Brains and duct tape.

Challenges we ran into: Wait time for 3D printing, ensuring waterproofing the system to ensure no damage is caused to the hardware, as well as working around the limitations of the motor.

What's next for Ice Ice Baby to automate the ice crushing part.

Since we had some missing hardware, we were unable to automate the ice shaving proecess. The next step would be to implement this.
