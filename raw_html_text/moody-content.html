Inspiration

Moody content inspired by peoples moods. We think when you have bad mood some AI help would be better to change your mood-

What it does

Moody content app provides you content such music, video, pics etc, according to your mood. It tracks your face and get your emotional situation. According that situation it tries to provide you best content that matches with your mood.

How I built it

To build mood content app we used imagga emotion recognition api and we have use nodejs express server, javascript and html5 to build prototype web site.
