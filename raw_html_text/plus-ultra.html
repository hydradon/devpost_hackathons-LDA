Inspiration

Our parents are immigrants. The Canadian heritage challenge made the most sense to us in terms of finding a solution to the issue of "isolation" faced by new comers.

What it does

One conversation with our chatbot has the potential to increase opportunities for collaboration and cooperation between English-speaking and French-speaking individuals in Quebec by facilitating a friendly event in an equal footing environment. Nonetheless, the app also incorporates features from the Canadian Heritage prototype mobile app.
