Inspiration

Reaction time test can help identify burnout and can stimulate cognitive skill. I used to play baseball and now that I became a software engineer I use my knowledge in sports to make productivity more fun and safe.

What it does

Create your pomodoro with 4 task and work/rest duration time and after finishing a task during your rest period you will be able to test your reaction time playing for only 30 sec.

How I built it

I used a simple logic and speed variation simulation different pitches. Also I use jquery for bracket animation.

Challenges I ran into

Handling client and server side.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I was able to auto populate the bracket automatically as player finish playing
