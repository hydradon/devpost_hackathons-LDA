Inspiration

The current demand for battle royale and PvP games encouraged us to create an online simple browser game.

What it does

Asteroid Online allows users to enter a PvP asteroids based scenario with other players from around the world.

How we built it

Vim/JS/Node and Tears/Blood/Sweat

Challenges we ran into

Sleep and fatigue.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud that we started with a simple skeleton and at the end of 24 hours, we have a product ready game.

What we learned

JS/Node/Sockets/IO

What's next for Asteroid Online

Asteroid Online will spend its rest of its days, as a repository on Github.
