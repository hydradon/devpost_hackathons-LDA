The project is designed to help solve the problem of excessive use of the car This is an application together with a chatbot, which allows you to give an "aventon" to your friends according to the needs of each one, in addition to the route you take, as well as the destination to which you will arrive, in a few words , you can share your car with your closest friends, family and colleagues at work or school, this is to reduce the emission of gases, acoustic pollution from excessive use of the horn, as well as the traffic that is generated at hours peak, in addition to helping the citizen's economy The application was made in android, with the help of kotlin, the database was made in firebase so that it would work in real time, the chatbot was made in dialogflow, and the backend with javascript, and api's were used google maps, in addition to facebook developer.
