Food is something we love but something we do not want to get sick from.

We have developed a solution to Track perishable food items from production all the way through to the consumer. Any one can scan the food item and get a report on freshness of that product.

We have sources Salesforce to use as a development platform for our solution.

Challenges we face includes not readily available IoT devices to securely capture data points and send remotely to our data collection points. It would be something we will develop on later.
