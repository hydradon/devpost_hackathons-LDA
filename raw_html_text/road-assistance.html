Inspiration

We always wanted to something that will improve the Road travel Experience in India and Help saves lives lost in road accidents. Ford Indore Road Safety Hackathon gave an inspiration to realize this dream.

What it does

It collects road data from various sources and inform users (pedestrians, bicyclists, and motor vehicles drivers) about:

  • Accident prone areas
  • Nearby emergency vehicles like Fire brigade, ambulance and Police vehicles
  • Traffic rules violation prone areas
  • Bad roads (i.e. roads with potholes)
  • Closed roads

How I built it

It is built in the form of a mobile application (available for iOS devices at present). A web application and Web API is also exposed to be used by sensors and individuals to update real time road data.

Challenges I ran into

My complete vision for road safety is vast and selecting on the features to be included for this competition was the biggest challenge.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am able to make this application right from idea to implementation in record time with all possible distractions possible.

What I learned

Together we can achieve more.

What's next for Road Assistance
