Inspiration

It took me multiple days to go in every grocery store in Stockholm in my firt week here. I know that many people don't have that free time. So, I've decided to do something about it.

What it does

Our project makes it easy to compare the prices of the products you're interested in.

How I built it

We've scrapped the prices on the stores websites and saved them in a database. Then, we used shiny to display the results in an user-friendly way.

Challenges I ran into

Scrapping the data was by fas the hardest part.

What I learned

Building an entire applicaiton in a few hours with people we never met before.

What's next for stores_prices

Get more data and more statistician feature to analyse the results (e.g. correlations between days of the week and sales, prices and type of food...), georeferences...
