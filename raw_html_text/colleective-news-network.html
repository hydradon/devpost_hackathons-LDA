What it does

Allows a de-centralised approach to news reporting so that people are treated equally. Uses Wisdom of the Crowd and a Reputation Model for reporters and editors to allow the community to decide who serves them best.

How we built it

Challenges we ran into

What we learned

Learning a TDD framework may not be for hackathons but bean bags definitely are!
