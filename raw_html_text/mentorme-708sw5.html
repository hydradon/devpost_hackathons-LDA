Inspiration

We want to make the mentor resources more accessible for students from a different background.

What it does

Provide an online group chatting platform for mentor and mentee.

How I built it

We used html/css/bootstrap for our frontend and we used MongoDB and javascript/meteor.js as our backend.

Challenges I ran into

We have a problem with creating different chatrooms for our website.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We have one chatroom that functions well.

What I learned

We learned how to use meteor.js for both the server side and the client side. We also learned how to use bootstrap to design our UI.

What's next for MENTORme

We want to make filtering for the chatroom so that mentor and mentee can easily find a chatroom of their interest.
