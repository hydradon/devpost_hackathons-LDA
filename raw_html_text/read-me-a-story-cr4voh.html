What it does

Alexa will read a short story upon the user's request. Currently, the short stories come from the following website: http://www.eastoftheweb.com/short-stories/index.php?p=web/library/fiction/all&pg=1&ob=length&obv=1-2

What I learned

I learned the basics of the Alexa SDK and AWS Lambda, along with NodeJS.

What's next for Read Me a Story

Ability to search various short stories online requested by the user!
