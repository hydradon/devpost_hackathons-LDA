Inspiration

We wanted to create a program that directly impacts the people around us and ourselves. As high school students, a big struggle that we and our friends face on a daily basis is the difficulty to focus on our studies. However, during our 4 years at high school, studying well is an absolute necessity for everyone.

What it does

Our program enables us to interactively study and focus by giving the user something fun to do while helping them memorize.

Challenges we ran into

One challenge we ran into was the creation of the collision class for the player model against the walls.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Actually finishing it and the visual appeal
