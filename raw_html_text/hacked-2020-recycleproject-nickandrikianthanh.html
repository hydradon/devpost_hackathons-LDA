The team consists of Nick, Andrik, Ian and Thanh. Our project idea was inspired by recent changes to what can be recycled in a home. Over the course of HackED we are learning how to use Tensorflow to process an image captured on a USB webcam by openCV and then communicate with an Arduino on the sorting machine to place each item in the correct area. The primary challenge has been learning how to effectively process an image and automatically determine whether it is recycle, compost, or garbage with significant accuracy.
