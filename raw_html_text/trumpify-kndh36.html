Inspiration

We thought it would be fun to highlight the ridiculous aspects of Donald Trump's speeches and books by transforming normal sentences in to their trumpified equivalents.

What it does

Accepts sentences and returns their trumpified versions.

How I built it

Python, python, python. We created a language model (word2vec) based on Trump's books and speeches, tagged parts of speech and then calculated the cosine similarity between the input words and Trump's words.

Challenges I ran into

Some python modules gave us issues, such as Flask.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We finished on time! And you can share on social media
