We wanted to create a social platform combined with a game where students, peers, and all individuals can engage and interact with others within your community. We wanted to recreate Pokemon Go and utilize features to be applicable to day to day use rather than be a game that would die off. As a team, we learned how to face and overcome obstacles including learning a completely new language, teamwork and collaboration skills. Through day to day life, we've seen that people are constantly using technology but find themselves in a centralized tech world, thus, we wanted to create a way to appeal to this target market. This is why we created GeoExplr.
