Inspiration

We all like to play Magic, and we wanted a better app to supplement our addiction.

How it works

It acts as a comprehensive life-counter. A card price tracker partially courtesy of TCGPlayer, and a Venmo component for transactions and trades.

Challenges I ran into

Battling API, making realistic goals, networking on android devices

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Making a magic app that we would use above the current ones.

What I learned

A better grasp of Android, GitHub, and APIs

What's next for MTGLifeApp

More features, Google Play
