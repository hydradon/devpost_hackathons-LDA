The bot is to perform shopping in an online grocery store which has a messenger on its Facebook page.

The bot is built on the Amazon Lex service. The bot has individual intents for store's departments , processed with AWS Lambda function (implemented on Java), with a persistence storage in the DynamoDB database.

  • aws-lambda
