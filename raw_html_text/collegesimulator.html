Inspiration

Society of Women Engineers outreach events

What it does

Simulates a lab bench to give users the real college experience

How we built it

We used Unity and Oculus Rift. We got asses from the Unity Asset Store and utilized the mentors at the event!

Challenges we ran into

Computer compatibility issues with VR/Oculus.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to design the room and have a player that can look around in the simulation. We learned a lot about Unity and VR development being first time developers.

What we learned

We learned A LOT! Unity Core Concepts and how to build a room in the editor. How to look for resources and teach ourselves from scratch.

What's next for CollegeSimulator

Apply to VR successfully and add more courses and various environments!
