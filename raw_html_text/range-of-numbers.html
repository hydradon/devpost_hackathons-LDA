Range of Numbers

This Skill is made for Kids from preschool to second grade to teach them the range of numbers between 1 and 100 to get a better understanding of the decimal system.

Group of tens and units have different sounds.

A deep sound represents tens, a high sound represent units.

Example 23 = 2 * deep sounds (20) + 3 * high sounds (3)
