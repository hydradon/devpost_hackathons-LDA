The Official Party Network

Ever wondered whether your local club is bumping on a weekend? Have you ever heard of a whole bunch of parties but don't know which one to go to?

We've used revolutionary technology to develop your #1 party and event social solution.

FEATURES:

  • Use the interactive map to see what parties are active and bumping in your area
  • Check in to local events and parties so your friends will know where you are
  • Check where your friends are checking into for the night
  • Create events for your friends to join and see where the biggest parties are at.

Using a Firebase database management system we are able to update locations and events in real time.
