Inspiration

Optimizing Productivity and Mental Health Can you really have the best of both worlds? Studi aims to use real-time data to find your perfect balance.

What it does

Studi recognizes and tracks a user's emotional health during a _ Studi Session _. This data can then be used to provide user feedback on how to optimize the effectiveness of their studying, while also considering their mental well-being. For example, if our system detects a prolonged period of stress, it will send you a reminder to take a break or practice self-care.

How we built it

We utilized Microsoft Azure's Emotion API for our emotion recognition. We connected this to our website for a user-friendly experience.

Challenges we ran into

Each component of our project seemed to work fine independently, but when it came to making them interact with each other, we encountered several bugs.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Putting forth an idea our entire team is proud of and using technology to solve a real-world problem.
