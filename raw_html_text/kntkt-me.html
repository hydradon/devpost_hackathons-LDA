Inspiration

People will never get rid of paper business cards so let's combine them with online ones

How it works

For now it lets users to connect via FB and upload own business card, which will be matched later on when others will be uploading your card as well, to get access to your online contacts.

Challenges I ran into

That thing should really go mobile, while we're missing mobile developer in the team. To find a good working SDK for images matching (and offering generous trial mode) was also a bit tough.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It's damn simple but working :)

What I learned

It's better to find team mates before the event

What's next for Kntkt.me

We'll definitely put a mobile version and will develop the concept further!
