Inspiration

A life hack I knew I could use, and doctors, lawyers, parents planning a surprise party, people who live in apartment complexes with doors that let conversations out.

What it does

Plays a modified form of noise especially fingerprinted to compete with the human voice ranges

How I built it

sampled a lot of people reading audio books, averaged then applied it to a sound plugin I made.

Challenges I ran into

keeping the distortion low on the alexa devices, but maximizing loudness at the same time

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

it works well

What I learned

Its a life hack
