Inspiration

We were inspired by an event on our way to the hackathon. We had gotten to the hackathon at different times but realized that the large crowd obstructed us from finding each other. Later, we were texting, and calling but we had no exact way of pinpointing each other. We realized that there is a potential for an application that can connect people together.

What it does

Connects people together.

How we built it

Andriod SDK, Java, Firebase, Google App Engine

Challenges we ran into

We were unfamiliar with APIs, and struggled with some code.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We finished!

What we learned

We learned about databases, andriod, and concurrency!

What's next for parsee

We plan to continue implementing features into parsee and making it into a better overall application.
