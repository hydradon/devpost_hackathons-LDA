Inspiration

What it does

1.) Open URL from you mobil phone or laptop. Where prompted enter your mobile number to generate a security code. Shortly you will receive an SMS with your security code. 2.) To complete the polling ballot enter your security code on the main page of URL and cast your vote. 3.) To view the status of ballot. Re-enter your mobil phone and security code.
