Inspiration

Our childhood tamagotchi toys.

What it does

An iOS application health tracker for steps that is inspired by a tamagotchi.

How we built it

We wrote the app in Swift using Xcode. Our design was made in Sketch and Photoshop.

Challenges we ran into

This is our third idea (there was not the proper hardware for Oculus Rift and installation issues with Beaker Notebook)

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned

We are resilient, creative, and able to produce!
