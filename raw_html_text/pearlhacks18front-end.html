Front-end for female in the workplace website

The goal of Women at Work is to provide easily accessible information and empower women to make educated decisions in where they want to work.

What it does

This website allows women to search a company and find information of its treatment of women.

How we built it

We used HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as well as an API to find company logos.

Challenges we ran into

Our biggest challenge was finding data and collecting it. In the end, we hard coded our data but have plans for future features.

Accomplishments

We are very proud of the functionality of our website, especially coming in without HTML, CSS, or JavaScript experience.

What we learned

HTML, CSS, and Javascript

What's next

We plan to be able to scrape websites for future data sources.
