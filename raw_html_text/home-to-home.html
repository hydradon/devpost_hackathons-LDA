Inspiration- As someone who has adopted pets from shelters in the past I know the joy that these animals can bring to someones life. Sadly 2 million animals are killed each year due to the lack of adoptees

What it does- This application connects shelters with people interested in adopting pets. Using a map based system the application connects to 'change makers' who can share information about adoption opportunities with their network. This is time sensitive information in which users are shown time left before animals are sent to shelters.

How we built it- We used HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Ruby on Rails and Google Maps API
