Inspiration

We wanted to make a challenging and fun-making app. Simple to use, simple to be charmed by it.

What it does

It measures the maximum height you can throw your phone upwards in the air. Evaluates your total score due to your device model. (The better device you use, more risky you are). If you think your phone flew a lot, submit your score and compete with other people. Expect some prizes if you are the best, and challenge your friends to show them your skills.

How we built it

The most difficult part of the project was coming up with the idea. Since then, we used our skills, other participants' experience and a lot of patience to accomplish our goal.

Challenges we ran into

Working with different types of sensors of the devices was the main challenge we ran into. We had to get to know how exactly they work and come up with tough algorithms to solve the problem.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of our idea, our level of teamwork, and that we managed to reach our goal.

What we learned

Besides of using our skills and improving our experience, we learnt a lot from other participants with different skills and same enthusiasm as ours.

What's next for Why not ?!

We are going to refine our project a little bit and then launch it on the marketplace.
