Inspiration

Sitting in the financial aids office is a huge waste of time, we want to maximize the efficiency of being able to go out and go to classes or eat or even do homework.

What it does

Essentially, this app gives people tickets based on the ETA of past visits. Allowing students to estimate how long of a wait a visit to the financial aid office would take.
