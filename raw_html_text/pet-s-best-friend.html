Inspiration

I have friends who usually travel with their pets. In a city away from home, it's not always easy to figure out what are the closest resources for their pet to stay happy and healthy.

What it does

Pet's Best Friend allows you to look up nearby clinics, dog parks, and look at nutrition options fit for your dog. Whether at home or away from home, the assistant can help you quickly look up places that will help you best care for your dog.

How I built it

I used Google's Dialogflow and Sketch.

What's next for Pet's Best Friend

More intents will be built in the future to support Fido's ongoing needs!
