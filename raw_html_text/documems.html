App Name

DocuMems stands for DocumentYourMemories

Inspiration :

Being avid travel lovers, our team wanted to create an app that would allow users to document and record their travels for future recollection. Unlike computers, humans don't have perfect memories, and we believed it would be useful to have a designated app for storing travel info to remember the tiny details for years to come.

What it does

Our app allows users to create new trips, and record the trip name, trip participants, date of trip, length of trip, and a memo to go along with it.

Challenges we ran into

Both of us have never created an app before or used android studio, so we had a huge learning curve learning the entire process.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We learned so much!!!!! From beginning with no app development experience, we were able to learn android studio, create a workable app, AND survive on practically NO sleep!

What's next for DocuMems

We have so many future ideas for this app! Ideally, creating a new trip will allow you to add specific events to the trip. For example, on a road trip across the US, you could add events such as "Visiting the Grand Canyon", "Weekend in New York", or "Spontaneous Ice Cream Stop". Each event would have access to camera roll and google maps to associate the trip with photos and location of your travels. Ultimately, the app would connect to Shutter-fly to allow to users to make photo books or print their favorite photos directly from the app.
