Inspiration

My calendar is already packed with meetings and events, I just need a way to send myself a reminder. I love TODO lists but sometimes a reminder doesn't always = a TODO item. JustRemindMe is a simple way to send a quick scheduled blurb to yourself by the method that makes the most sense. Reminders come in either text, voice or email format.

What it does

Schedules reminders or notes for future use.

Challenges I ran into

Time, i should have sent a reminder to myself to start earlier ;)

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Just getting the proof of concept together in a short time frame.

What I learned

The Nexmo API made the communication part of this app a snap. The heavy lifting was the interface and pieces and parts around the scheduled reminder.

What's next for JustRemindMe

This is in beta state and has a few bugs/enhancements that need to be addressed. Those improvements coupled with tweaks to the UI will make this a cool app to have handy.
