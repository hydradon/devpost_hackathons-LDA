Inspiration

The inspiration came from a zombie survival/Hide and seek type of game.

What it does

"Zombies" or NPCs are spawning across the map and the purpose is to avoid being caught by these characters.

How I built it

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned

3D Unity library is just as massive as the 2D library.

What's next for justShowered
