Inspiration

SnapAndSay: I want to see how well clarifai works for educational purposes.

FaceBlaster: I wanted to do something like this for a while.

What it does

Take a photo, select the concept in English. Say the word in spanish and test if it translates back to the concept in english. Or translate the concept to spanish and have it spoken to you.
