Inspiration

Many don't eliminate the transport of their goods, since they can deteriorate or break during re-laying. We want to help track the status of your orders throughout the journey. To make you feel comfortable.

What it does

This application monitors the status of the order throughout the driver's travel path. According to the sensors shows the driver where he has a problem, if such appeared.

Challenges we ran into

New technologies for reading parameters from sensors and IoT devices.

What we learned

Work in a team, compose business models, work with IoT devices.

What's next for 1010ts

Modify the application and the web version to make this process more transparent than it is now.
