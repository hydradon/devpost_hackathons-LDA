Shedd.

Make new friends. Do fun shit together. Stay Well.

Suicide kills 6 Aussie blokes a day.

We know that depression can lead to suicide, and loneliness often leads to depression. By making it easy to address loneliness, Shedd aims to reduce the suicide and depression rates in Australian men.

Shedd brings Tinder, Men's Shed and the Black Dog Institute together in one iOS application.

Log-in, identify your interests, availabilities and travel range, and our algorithm will find and suggest matches with similar blokes near you. From there, it's as simple as confirming your activity (golf, fishing, pub etc), the timeslot, and then turning up.
