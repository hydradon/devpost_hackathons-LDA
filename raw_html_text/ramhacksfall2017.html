The Inspiration: Inspired by helping students to continue education and reduce cost of education.

This web application connects students with students to exchange textbooks and supplies for points. These points are used through gamification.

Most of us never seen Javascript before. 4/5 of us were beginners in coding. We've learned a lot and plan to integrate this into Alexa, Google Home, and Siri. We weren't able to because of wifi issues and had a roadblock with our database (firebase) working with semi-object orientated javascript with Node.js.
