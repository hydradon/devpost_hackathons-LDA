Creating a mobile app that will let students scan a title from there book and get 2D / 3D Animated content right there explaining the concept in a better way.

we developed the app using Natural Language Processing and Artifical Intelligence that will maintain a database of what students are searching for and our highly dedicated team of Animators will produce the content which is being searched by the students.

we have more than 450 animated videos of 9th grade STEM subjects and these numbers are increasing every day.

the challenges that we face were recognizing the characters from handwriting so we standardize the text of the book and built our model on it. but in future, we can have a highly trained system that can even recognize the texts written from hands and play relevant videos
