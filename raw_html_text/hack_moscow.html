Inspiration

Technical support is an essential part of many successful businesses. It exists to relieve your clients' headache and to solve their problems. But the question is: whether you get the greatest possible benefit from your technical support?

What it does

We suggest a solution that provides business with technical support workers rating which is based on emotional satiation of clients after their converation with operator. Moreover, this solution gives you data about potentially disloyal clients who are displeased with your service.
