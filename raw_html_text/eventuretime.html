Inspiration

No plans? No problem. Ever get to a new city and feel totally lost? The authors were tired of travelling the world just to be unable to find good events because of a lack of friends in new cities.

What it does

Eventure Time is an online platform that allows you to find any event near you based on your interests. Simply login to Facebook and instantaneously see events you might be interested in. Search for a variety of different events such as networking events, concerts, sporting events and many more! With the click of a button, you can even get an Uber to your desired event. Eventure Time is powered by Django, with the Indico API providing natural language processing to suggest events you might be interested in.
