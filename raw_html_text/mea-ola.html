Mea Ola is the Virtual Pet of Today!

This falls under the category of web projects and games

Mea Ola is a virtual pet that lives in your browser as a Google Chrome Extension. It interacts differently depending on the pages you visit, and can be used to challenge other virtual pets.

It is a venture by four RIT students, and covers many technologies, from basic HTML and CSS, to Node.js and Express, to Unity and Blender.
