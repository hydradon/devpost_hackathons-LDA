FacePalm sums up our emotions at the end of HackBaroda. We started our Hackathon journey by trying to build a real-time tweet analyser/visualizer , which would predict the Location (from where the Tweet was made) based on the different contexts a Tweet may present. 16-18 hours into the Hackathon , we came to the conclusion that this was something quite significant in today's research , so obviously it turned out to be difficult. Hence, we FacePalmed.

And then we struck upon the idea to develop a portal where people (connected in a network) can share their own embarrassing stories/facepalmies with each other. There will always be someone to listen to them and make fun of them.
