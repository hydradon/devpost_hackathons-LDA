Trust & Self-Improvement

  • No excessive sandboxing or isolation: run the provided job binary directly
  • Deploy on a variety of providers, depending on the actual spot market situation
  • Optionally include means for providers to optimize the job and their resources: job source code, guranteed amount of similar (with a minor patchset) payload to be purchased

Economic game changer

  • Provider may resell the job he optimized to the same network for better price
  • Provider may subcontract partial optimization to the network as smaller jobs
