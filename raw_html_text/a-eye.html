Inspiration

The idea of crossing any street without seeing anything terrified us and we thought of a solution to help blind people to detect iminent threat.

What it does

A-Eye is designed to detect if an object (a person, a car, a ball, ...) is approching toward a camera that will be placed in the glasses of a blind person or of someone with visual dificulties. The process of the data will be made on his cellphone

How we built it

The object's detection is made with the azure computer vision API, a proof of concept was developped in Python 3.6.

Challenges we ran into

Learning to use the Microsoft API.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The whole project ; developping a proof of concept in very little time.

What's next for A-Eye

Developpement of the hardware (glasses) Deep leaning program to make the A-eye more smart by defining the right Beep context. We could also adjust the sensibility of our program to become more effective.
