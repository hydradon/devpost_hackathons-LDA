Inspiration and what it does

We were inspired to create a social media where wanderers, city dwellers, and business in the travel industry can connect all around the world. Wanderlust is a web-application that satisfies both users and companies. Specifically focuses on those who are indecisive about their travels and curious about seeing new sites. For a business point of view we advertise and help users find hotels, restaurants, local-food, tour guides and many more.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We have successfully created a web-app, created with stress, sweat and accomplishments.

What we learned

We learned about the marketing scheme of tourism and travel throughout this project. We also learned about how we can combine social media with a marketing application.

What's next for Wanderlust - A visual and interactive way to wander

Our future ideas include contacting with companies such as air miles and getting users discounts for their travels as well as creating a forum site for issues about the web-app.
