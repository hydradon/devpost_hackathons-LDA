Fetch Bot is an interactive facebook messenger bot, made in collaboration by Johnathan Brunelle, Stu Wilcox, and Andrew Ning for Hack Western 3.

Made using Node.js and Heroku

Visit the app Here , and send it a message to start a conversation!

(Project is still awaiting Facebook approval, so the bot is not yet open to the public. Ask one of the Developers for a demo!)

Commands

  • doge
    • Sends a picture of Doge
  • coin flip
    • Flips a coin for you
  • emoji
    • Sends a random emoji
  • yes or no?
    • Helps you decide Yes or No
  • wall-e
    • Sends a picture of Wall-E
  • mp3
    • Sends a download link to a surprise song
  • video: " Your Search Term "
    • Searches youtube for you
  • calc "your math expression"
    • Performs a calculation for you
  • roll
    • Rolls a dice for you
