Inspiration

Every morning, one of our team members had to request an Uber to the train station for work. We thought it would be useful if we could schedule ride requests ahead of time and even repeat them!

What it does

Goobr allows users to request their Uber rides ahead of time as well as set up recurring rides throughout the week.

Challenges I ran into

Spring framework was hard to figure out.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It looks amazing!

What I learned

Choice of framework is very important.

What's next for Goobr

It would be really nice if we can finish integrating front-end with back-end. Also find a time to get it on a goobr.co!
