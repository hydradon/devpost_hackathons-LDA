Inspiration: Inspired from experience and observations of students who cannot buy food because of busy timetables.

What it does: An website that enables Monash Clayton students to receive delivered campus food when they don’t have time to physically get the food themselves.

How we built it: Using HTML and Python.

Challenges we ran into: Coding in HTML because of no prior experience.

Accomplishments that we're proud of: Making parts of the interface and trying to complete the challenge.

What we learned: Learned about time management and basics of HTML coding.

What's next for Monash Eats: Expanding the business in the future and adding more features to the website.
