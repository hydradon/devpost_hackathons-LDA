Inspiration

  • The brain is actually a highly flexible and plastic organ that changes based upon our experiences, emotions and behavior
  • A correlation between a healthy person's mental training or exercise and their brains capacity to manage greater volume and more complex information.
  • Neural circuits not actively engaged in task performance for an extended period of time begin to degrade.
  • Super-M is a proven method for brain training that improve the memory capability.
  • the integration of MyScript improve the brain training experience by providing a familiar way for the brain to interact with the application.

What it does

It is a game to train your brain and considerably improve your memory capability. The level of difficulty is incremental my the increase on number of elements as long as the player progress. If the player fail the number of elements decreases by one.

How I built it

It is built using Cordova/phonegap multi-platform with MyScript CDK.

Challenges I ran into

Support multiple screen size.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Integration of MyScript in my multi-platform application.
