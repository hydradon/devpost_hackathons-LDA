Inspiration

We found way too many floppy drives and upon remembering how useless they are, we decided to make an orchestra out of them.

What it does

It plays midi files out of floppy drives. Quite entertaining.

How we built it

Our electrical engineer built everything, whilst our software failed over and over again.

Challenges we ran into

High expectations, people wanting to hear the orchestra, and an unstable audio library for java.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Music was made

What we learned

Never again shall we try and use a library that is not well documented (...we say for the 3rd time)

What's next for Floppy Cups

A long-term implementation
