Inspiration

Phone calls are an old, yet so respectable method of communication. We have decided to bring it up to the Information Age standards.

What it does

It speaks for itself - and for you, too! Text message goes in - an automated call, complete with an ability to answer the receiver's basic questions, comes out.

What we learned

Spending nights coding is nice - albeit tiresome. Said nights taught us basic neural network training, some Python, and a whole lot of web APIs.

What's next for Loudr

Improvement! We will grow both personally and professionally, as a strong team.
