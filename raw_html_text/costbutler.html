Inspiration

Inspired by reading a study that said that over two out of three ticket prices will drop at some point within 24 hours of the original search or booking with an average saving of 14% and that The US Department of Transport requires that all domestic and foreign airlines offer a 24-hour free cancellation policy for tickets purchased at least a week in advance of departure.

What it does

We know that flight, hotel, and rental car prices change every minute, but most people will never go back and check rates after they have made their travel plans. Costbutler continuously checks your travel plans after you book. If you are eligible for a refund, or are able to rebook your flight or hotel and save money, we'll let you know how to do so.

How I built it

Costbutler works through email. Forward any travel confirmation to trips@costbutler.com- we parse the receipt, and then alert you if the price drops.
