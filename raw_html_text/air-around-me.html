Air-Around-Me

Air Quality Indexing was recently launched by the Central Pollution Board. The Application came about as an idea to access the continuously generated data by various stations across all major cities.

Considering geolocating users is not a distant dream anymore , We want the application to serve as a tool to simplify these data values and present the Quality of Air in his vicinity.

Lastly we went ahead and analysed the past dataset and provided a facility to predict the next day's Air Quality.
