TravelSight:

Transtat Collective API

Description of our tool:

This tool will analyze historical flight data from major airports and airlines to predict future delays. The prediction will be displayed in a chart which will show delay vs arrival time and another charts which will break down the delays into 8 key categories. A twitter feed and tripadvisor feed is used to showcase up-to date information on the destination airport.The tool is targeted towards frequent flyers specifically involving business travel.
