Inspiration

Many times we don't remember the channel numbers and tend to go through the tv guide. Now, the work is much simpler with just a alexa command. Something like, "Alexa, ask direct tv, where can i find fox sports ?".

What it does

For the before question the response would go something like "It is on channel number 219". Instead of going through the tv guide, the number is just a command away.

How we built it

The skill was build using node.js using alexa SDK.

What's next for Direct TV

Under this series, will be releasing tv guides of major providers such as cox tv, dish etc.
