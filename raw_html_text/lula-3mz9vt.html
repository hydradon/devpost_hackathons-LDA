• Lula

Inspiration

The long and awful time it takes to complete a purchase or complete shopping in a mart or shopping complex due to the queuing time and the time it takes to log items purchased by the customers as it can make the customer bored or tired and change the mart he/she is using thus destroying user-client relationship.

What it does

The app uses a QR scanner and NFC to tag and get items, adding them to the shopping list but before then the user would be assigned a unique code or ID that makes sure the shopping cart or information isn't leaked or tampered with and the data sent to the client or store. the service then makes sure the user has flexibility in terms of shopping and can use the NFC reader to pay or checkout if using an NFC enabled mobile device. it can also use an online payment service to pay the fee as per the shopping. after payment, the customer then moves to the counter to identify items and the number based on the info of the payment and is cleared.

How I built it

The app was built using the react-native slack with some in-built libraries and external modules to integrate the NFC, code scanner and the payment option. certain design inspirations were gotten from dribble but the main integration as by using a core library.
