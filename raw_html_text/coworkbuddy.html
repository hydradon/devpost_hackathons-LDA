API Used

MASAI Mobility Community CoWorking Space API

What it does

CoworkBuddy helps travelers make decisions as they they are given information and a visual representation of available coworking spaces in the city that they are staying in. The service is also completely worldwide, so it can help users staying in Los Angeles or Ho Chi Minh City, and anywhere in between.

I built it with javascript, jquery, html, and css.
