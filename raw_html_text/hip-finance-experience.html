What it does

We created a new medium for bank services. Rather than going in to speak with a teller or calling the bank (neither of which are available 24 hours) to get something done our users will be able to simulate the bank experience through Virtual Reality. If you decide to order some checks or view your transactions at 2 in the morning after a Netflix binge or gaming session this is the service for you!

How we built it

Everything is made in Unity.

Challenges we ran into

None of us have used VR before so much as develop for it. This was a new learning experience. Pretty much everything we did caused problems.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Everything works! :D

What we learned

Introduction to Unity and VR development.

What's next for Hip Finance Experience

If the project were to continue (and we had longer than 48 hours) our primary goal would be to implement a live chat feature between bank tellers and the consumer. Ideally, this would be an application available on systems such as cell phones, PS4 VR, and also available as a web application with compatible VR headsets.
