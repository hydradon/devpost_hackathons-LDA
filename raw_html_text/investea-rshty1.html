Inspiration

Young people are generally not interested or even ignorant towards saving money. Our website attempts to incentivize younger people to save money by allowing them to visualize the growth saving even a small amount of money can have over time.

What it does

Visualizes the growth of your savings through images so that people can see their money grow.

How we built it

HTML and CSS front-end PHP SQL backend

Accomplishments that we're proud of
