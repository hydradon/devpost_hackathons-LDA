Inspiration

Members of our team had a project on Biomes. During the project we would have liked to have support in creating our project.

What it does

The skill answers questions about biomes. The answers range from the climate of the biome to the animals living within the biome.

How we built it

We used Alexa developer and the AWS.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned

We learned how to use java script.
