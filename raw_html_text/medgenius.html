What it does

MedGenius is a mobile app that enables patients to easily access information comparing the quality of care provided by local doctors and hospitals. It helps patients in their decision making process of choosing providers. The app uses multiple public health datasets. Based on their specific needs and criteria, patients can search for and find high quality clinics using visual representations of quality scores, a five-star comparison rating tool and search percent matches. Ratings gives patients a quick way to compare quality care metrics across providers. App has an intuitive and easy to use interface and uses cognitive search to help patients easily find clinics with the highest quality based on their needs.
