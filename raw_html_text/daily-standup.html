Inspiration

Remote work is taking over the world. It is one of the biggest workplace trends. Remote work helps both employees and the employers. For employers, remote work means access to the best talent all over the world and reduced costs. For employees, remote work means that they can work for any startup / company across the world. Physical location is no longer a barrier. This may also help to solve the unemployment issue in the developing countries.

Some of the challenges that remote work brings:-

  • Building rapport
  • Collaborating with other distributed team members
  • Scheduling standups / meetings

What it does

Daily Standup empowers the remote teams by powering their daily standups / meetings. It helps to bridge the gap between the distributed teams. It supports both one to one as well as group video call. Hence remote employees can communicate or collaborate as if they were in the office.

How we built it

Daily Standup is powered by Agora Web SDK and ReactJS
