Inspiration

I ran out of space on my dropbox and wanted to merge my google drive together with it to have a more uniform storage space with greater capacity.

What it does

It essentially stores files for you in both drives without you knowing

How I built it

I built it with python and flask for the backend and html and jinja2 for the frontend.

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned

I learned a lot of web development and what it takes to build a good looking site
