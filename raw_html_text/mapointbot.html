Inspiration

I am inspired of what is a distress call mean to humanly possible. Call for help is one of the crucial role of us human, to ask for help is a dozen.

One of the examples:

  1. Ask your location.
  2. Ask suggestion of what activities you are going to have for the day.
  3. Ask people's location.
  4. Ask flight status.
  5. Post help to social media in realtime in connection to your location.

What it does

Mainly focused in determining your location and bot making suggestions.

How I built it

I built it through nodejs and aws lambda.

Components have the following:

  1. Lex
  2. Facebook integration
  3. Slack integration
  4. Google Map Geolocation

Challenges I ran into

  1. Manual deployment to AWS Lex website.
  2. Thoroughly debugging directly to AWS lex website.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am able to learn bot's algorithm and able to help users in the future through developing another application.

What I learned

Bot's algorithm.
