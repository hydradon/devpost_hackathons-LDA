Inspiration

When trying to plan a weekend trip with friends around the nation, the first problem was finding a city that would be most affordable for all of us. Small World hopes to help get over this initial hurdle!

How it works

Enter the airport everyone is flying from and it will call Sabre API's to find mutual top destinations and then calculate the total fare cost for everyone.

Challenges I ran into

Making simultaneous async AJAX calls and processing the callbacks that result.
