Inspiration

There are few sport/movement tracking wearable device targeted for senior citizens in the market. Most of the personal alarm and fall detection alarms have the location limitation.

What it does

Our product is a wristband, a combination of personal alarm, fall detection alarm and motion/sports tracking device, based on LORA technology to make it connected at home and outdoor without relying on home hub, wifi, mobile phone or mobile network.

How we built it

The wristband consists of Lora module, MCU, GPS, and accelerometer. The accelerometer tracks the motion and movement.

Challenges we ran into

Utilization of the data collected and come up with personalized physical exercise recommendation could be a challenge for us.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Utilizing the advantage of Lora network to fill in the gap of the market

What we learned

The microshare.io looks good for us. We will look into further.

What's next for SmartSenior

We will build an MVP and try it out in the market.
