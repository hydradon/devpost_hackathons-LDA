Inspiration

Too many ruined pairs of undies... And potentially helping couples who are trying to get pregnant.

What it does

in order to anticipate your ovulation time, we collect your body temperature every second from a temperature sensor connected to the Arduino.

How we built it

We collect data from the sensor, then send the data from arduino serial port to python. Store this information on FireBase and update the information onto a Heroku server.
