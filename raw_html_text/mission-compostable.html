Inspiration

We want to become better citizens of this planet. Just like how people keep track of calories when they are on a diet, we want to enable people to keep track of the waste that they need to depose of.

What it does

Every user is expected to scan the item they want to dispose of, and our web application will be able to determine to which bin it should be thrown in (either garbage, green bin, black bin, compost), and display it to the user. Then we will push this information to our Firestore real-time database. Users will be able to view their daily/weekly/monthly waste disposals through a dashboard, which displays it through data visualizations.
