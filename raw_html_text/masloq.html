What does Masloq mean?

Masloq is an Arabic word which mean cooked in boiled water.

The purpose of the project

The main idea of this project is to help those who get busy most of the time because of work, or students in their academic life and do not have time to prepare healthy meals. This application shall be an easy way to connect customers with food providers.

Objectives:

1- Ability to specify your calories daily need to meet your current diet program. 2- Ability to make your meals get delivered to you daily as fresh meals delivery. 3- Variety of healthy food options. 4- Good for all ages and genders. 5- Convenient prices.

Future work:

1- Allow other food suppliers to integrate with our system as meals provider. 2- Add more diet programs to fit most the customers need. 3- Collaborate with other organizations and affairs to offer special discounts, such as students and veteran discounts.
