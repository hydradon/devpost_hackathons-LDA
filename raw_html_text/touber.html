Inspiration

I always wanted to do something related to helping potential students and visitors at college campuses.

What it does

It is a lookup service for college visitors to find on-campus tour guides, when it is not possible to schedule an official admissions tour. Visitors can request a tour guide to give a personalized, convenient tour of the campus. After the tour, the visitors have the option of donating/tipping the student tour guide for their service.

How I built it

LAMP Stack Web App PhoneGap converts the Web App to a mobile Android or iOS app.

Challenges I ran into

CapitalOne APIs. Bugs with purchase API.
