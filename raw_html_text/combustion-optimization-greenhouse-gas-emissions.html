Inspiration

Global Warming is a major concern causing impact to the human life and greenhouse gases emitted by the coal fired power plants are a major contributor to it. We would like to have a healthier environment for the future generations.

What it does

The solution monitors real-time data of GHG emissions from the Power Plant boilers and provides an advisory on how to minimize it and improve boiler efficiency.

How I built it

The solution is built using GE Predix platform and core services, along with custom analytics.
