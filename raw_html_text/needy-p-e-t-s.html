Inspiration

Tamogochi, Angry Birds, Pokemon Go

What it does

Needy P.E.T.S. is an AR experience where players throw special treats at a pet home (a cat tree or doghouse) to keep swarms of robot cats and robot dogs happy!

How we built it

Built in Unity for ARKit. Assets created in Maya, Sketch, and Photoshop
