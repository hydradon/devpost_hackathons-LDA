Inspiration

22% of the US Population receive SNAP (food stamp) benefits each year. They have tremendous purchasing power in aggregate, yet remain under-served by consumer technology. Currently, Food Stamps do not work online , on mobile, or anywhere in the digital economy.

What it does

Allows people on Food Stamps to shop online, on mobile, and on apps like Instacart, DoorDash, & Postmates. Right now Food Stamps don't work anywhere digitally.

How I built it

Messenger, Snatchbot Integration, Marqeta, Stripe

Challenges I ran into

Computer vision integration for OCR reading of EBT card

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Responding bot, bot is live

What I learned

How to create a messenger bot

What's next for All_ebt

Set up banking infrastructure for end to end transactions. i.e EBT Card to Visa Virtual card
