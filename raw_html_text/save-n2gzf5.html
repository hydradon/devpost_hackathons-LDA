Inspiration 

Wanted to find a creative and fun way to save money. Also we wanted to enrich our skill sets.

What it does 

This app helps people keep track of their purchase history while finding areas that they could potentially save money.  Our app makes it fun and provides inspiration for people to try to save money in different areas of their lives.

Challenges I ran into

Learning new technologies.

What I learned 

How useful Jenkins and JHipster are.

What's next for SavE 

Implementing additional features to increase fun. Also, implement financial goals, social aspects, and donating saved money to charity.
