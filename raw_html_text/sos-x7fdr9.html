Username is a users characteristics who needs users needs because insight

Jane Doe is a person living in an area prone to Natural disasters who needs an in-depth way to provide 911 related services and information on where to find safety in the case of an emergency because at any point a natural disaster could leave them in need and phone lines get really busy when something like that happens

IDEATING

How can we solve this problem?

We can create an app that would let them send an SOS message to the authorities with their geographic location and it will also provide users with real-time weather information in their area to alert them of a upcoming natural disaster, provide local places to seek shelter (safe zones), provide pick-up point(s) locations in case the area needs to be evacuated, get in touch with people close to them who are affected by the same circumstances, mark themselves as “safe” on all their social media easier, alert an emergency contact of an impending storm…..
