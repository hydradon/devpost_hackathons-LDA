Inspiration

As students at BMCC, we noticed some problems to register for classes and being advised. Thus, we thought of creating a website that can help students at BMCC to improve their experience in terms of registering for classes and getting advised.

What it does

Through our website, students are able to create a plan for their two years at BMCC. They select classes based on their level. Once they completed a class and met the prerequisite for the next level, the class will open up.

How we built it

We used CSS, HTML and Javascript.

Challenges we ran into

The time was almost too short to make it perfect and the lack of getting coffee affected our brains.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We managed to create a website that looks like the mockup that we created in advance.

What we learned

We learned to work as a team and time management.

What's next for kartoshka
