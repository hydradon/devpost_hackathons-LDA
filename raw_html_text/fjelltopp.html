Inspiration

Have you ever wonder what is the name of the mountain on the other side of the valley?

What it does

App shows mountain tops directly on camera on your iPhone or Android.

How we built it

We use React-Native and Viromedia framework.

Challenges we ran into

AR technology is still very new and not always vet precise.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We managed to test AR for quite a bit of scenario.

What we learned

We learned more about HERE API, Entur API of Riksantikvaren API.

What's next for Fjelltopp

We are about to publish app in Google Play Store and Apple Store.
