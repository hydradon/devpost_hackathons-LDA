SMARTDash

Our project for HackGT 2016. Helps users set responsible financial goals and stick to them. The source is available at github.com/chsahit/SMARTDash and a demo is available at http://9d36afed.ngrok.io . The user can monitor how their affect their fiscal health and how to meet the goals they want.
