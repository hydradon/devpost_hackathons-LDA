What it does?

QuickPoll allow you to create and responds any day to day question that needs outside opinions in your local area. Can't decide which newest smart phone should you get this Thanks Giving? Pop on QuickPoll and see how many people have agreed with you in just a few clicks!

Creating surveys and opinion polls can be a real hassle, so can filling them out. That's why our main goal is to have an eye pleasing pocket tool that is simple yet succinct for quick and efficient usage.
