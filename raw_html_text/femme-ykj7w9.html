Inspiration

We wanted to give women a resource to learn about their rights.

What it does

Femme is a web application that masks as a period tracking app but its main goal is to give women awareness about sexual harassment laws, underage marriages, and other laws concerning women issues. Femme also helps women connect with each other on an anonymous chat feature called community.

How we built it

We used React.js, JSX, and CSS.

Challenges we ran into

We wanted to have an interactive calendar but couldn't accomplish it.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of our product and how it helps women. Also, we are proud of our features, especially insights as it was our main highlight and turned out to be how we anticipated it.

What we learned

We learned more about React and polished our skills in Javascript and CSS.

What's next for Femme

We want to make an iOS/Android App for Femme in the near future.
