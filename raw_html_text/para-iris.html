The project helps paralyzed people to communicate with the help of their eyes by just looking into the screen. The screen displays several options for communication like food water restroom etc. The person has to just gaze at the screen with several options in front of them,just with a glance at one of the options the message will be forwarded to the caretaker. This software is connected to the caretaker's mobile phone using the IP and number of the device. This software is very helpful for paralyzed patients. This software constis of an app in the caretakers mobile so that the caretaker can know the status of the patient and his needs.
