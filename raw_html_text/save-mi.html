• SaveMi

Inspiration

Our friend Miller is deathly allergic to many things. We can help him by keeping track of potential allergy sources he may have come into contact with based on photos he took throughout the day to provide warning and information for medical professionals.

What it does

Take a picture with your phone, the app will identify potential allergy sources on the picture through cloud based computer vision. A list of potential allergy sources will be kept in an online database complete with time-stamp for easy access, and a notification message will be send to a slack channel whenever new allergy source is identified so friends and family get sufficient warning as well. After you are done, share the moment with others on SnapChat with a simple tap!

How I built it

Built using android studio. API requests composed with std.lib and image labelling through firebase google vision API. Snapchat integration is with snapchat creative kit.
