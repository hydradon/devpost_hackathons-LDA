Inspiration

As high school students who often fall victim to bad time management, our team decided to create an app that makes scheduling both easy and efficient. Several sources stated that 86% percent of high school students and 92% of college students tend to procrastinate on assignments. Creating an app that would eliminate procrastination and poor time management can greatly improve efficiency and productivity.

What it does

The app allows the user to create tasks and events and it creates a custom schedule that divides a task up evenly for the user until the due date.
