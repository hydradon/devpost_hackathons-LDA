Inspiration

During the beginning of the Local Hack Day, people talked about their hobbies. For the most part, they were indoor activites. Additionally, it was a rainy day, so I was thinking about what to do indoors to stay safe.

What it does

It gives the user a few options for food, activities, and movie genres. When the user generates a link that will search that type of cuisine for the user within the user's area.
