Someday at work are exhausting. Your back hurts from being hunched over at the desk. If only there was something to ease the pain. Well, now their is.

What it does

This vest has a heating pad and mini vibrators to sooth the back. Targeting the lower back and the shoulders.

How we built it

We designed a vest that would contain the technology on the inside, turning the mechanism on through a switch in the pocket. The arduino controls the vibrators.
