Inspiration

TD's sponsor challenge! We were inspired by the idea of AI technology helping bring a positive social change to things people do every day – such as banking.

What it does

Forecas is a web based application that empowers Canadian’s financial confidence by encouraging them to spend better both short term and long term. Historical and present spending habits are interpreted and analyzed, then predictions of the future long term trend is shown. Immediate advice and tips on what you can do now to be more financially secure, alerts users on the dashboard.

Things we're proud of

What we learned

New technologies, languages and working under pressure.
