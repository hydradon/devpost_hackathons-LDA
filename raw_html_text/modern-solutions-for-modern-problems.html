Inspiration

As much as I love tech and technological innovations, I have a strong penchant for social justice and civil service, and this challenge seemed to combine the three.

What it does

In the end, this project will help generate a stronger ability to gauge and predict where efforts to protect against homelessness need to be centered. Efforts can include short-term acts such as providing temporary housing, or long-term acts such as lobbying for the repeal of single-family-zoning laws.
