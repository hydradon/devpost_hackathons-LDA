Fermi, learning through teaching.

Fermi is an interactive application that allows university students to collaborate throughout their academic career. This application not only helps you succeed in your academic endeavors by connecting you with other like minded individuals, but rewards you for it too. Fermi works on a point based system that gives points for participating in study sessions, tutoring other students, and getting tutored your self. These points can later be redeemed for discounts and even free meals at your favorite local places to eat.

The mission of Fermi is to be a useful tool which encourages collaborative work and good academic performance of our fellow students. After all, the students of today are the future of tomorrow.
