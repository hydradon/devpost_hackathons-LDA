Inspiration

We realized that it is very difficult to reach out for help from older students and people with experience when you are a student in school. This website makes it easier for mentors and mentees to reach out and help people achieve their goal.

What it does

Mentors and mentees fill in what they are specialized in and work together to accomplish an aim. Mentees can ask to improve academically, psychologically and receive general advice.
