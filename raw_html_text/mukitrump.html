Inspiration

Paulig Muki and our lord and savior Donald Trump

What it does

Fetches quotes regarding Trump, selects one random quote, draws it next to picture of Donald Trump and sends it to Muki. Repeats every minute if kept running in background.

How we built it

With android studio
