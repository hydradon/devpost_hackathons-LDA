Synchronising Canvas and Your Google Drive

When using Canvas it often becomes frustrating for the end-user who has to constantly maintain synchronization between what their instructors are posting and their own file systems. This leads to missed information, nonsensical errors, and general confusion when students do not know what version of a particular file they are looking at.

We wish to change this. Canvas File Sync promises to bridge this gap between instructor and student by facilitating seamless integration between online coursework and students' Google drive. In fact, after the initial setup, this once great source of confusion is reduced to a background task that they never see or have to think about.
