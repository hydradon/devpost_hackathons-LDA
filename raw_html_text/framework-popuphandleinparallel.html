Inspiration - Multi threading mechanisms

What it does- Handles the multiple popups in parallel . Popups may populate at any moment during the execution of worflow. Timeout for each element exists of popups activity is shared in parallel.

How I built it - Using Uipath

Challenges I ran into - Identifying the selectors of the popups while executing the process in real time business cases.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of : This idea is effectively used in my project which identifies 8+ intermittent popups within 5 seconds and handles it.

What I learned : Multi-threading in UiPath
