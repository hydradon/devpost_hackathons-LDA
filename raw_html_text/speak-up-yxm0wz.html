Inspiration

NYC is the center of immigrants. Often times immigrants' voices are unheard. We wanted to change this and give them a voice!

What it does

Speak Up is a platform for documented/undocumented immigrants and non-immigrants to submit their issues and concerns without disclosing their identity. Also, the users are able to vote for the most concerning issues and most voted issues will be directed to the personnel in charge of those concerns.
