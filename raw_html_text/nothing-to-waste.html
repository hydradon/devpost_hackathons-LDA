Inspiration

The climate crisis has been a really hot topic these last years, and with good reason. Everyone should be aware of the magnitude of the problem and we wanted to provide a way to be a bit more eco-friendly in our everyday life.

What it does

It stores information about the food you have, and warns you if something is going to expire and its objective is to reduce the food you waste.

How we built it

We learned from zero to code in Java and we used android studio.

Challenges we ran into

It was a lot of information to assimilate in a very short period of time.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

How far we've got, taking into account that we started from the ground up.

What we learned

Java Script and android studio.

What's next for Nothing to waste

It has a lot of potential. It could suggest you recipes to mimimize the waste of food and many other things!!
