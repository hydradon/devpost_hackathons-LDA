Inspiration

To help high school students through Support My Club by increasing their reach via social media and reduce their manual work by automating certain aspects of their day to day work.

What it does

  1. Automate the process of sending an email to the donor which is currently done manually.
  2. Increase their donor base using social media like Facebook.
  3. For existing donors, recommend relevant items to donate further.
