Inspiration

Its inspiration came mainly from brainstorming about education for mentally challenged children.

What it does

It provides localized and engaging educational content to parents of mentally challenged children. This also helps in identifying the exact type of challenge the child is facing.

How we built it

We used Android technology for building the mobile application.

Challenges we ran into

Finding authentic data for Pakistan about mentally challenged children. Improving the User Experience for parents of mentally challenged children. Creating localized content.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Creating this application and content in such a smart time

What's next for Adapted-Virtual-Education

Next plan is for expanding this platform to all kinds of physical and mental challenges children are facing.
