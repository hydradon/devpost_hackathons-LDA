Inspiration

I wanted to build an app that allowed me to improve the lives of people on a day to day basis.

What it does

Detects emotions, age, and gender of people and cumulatively suggests appropriate activities for mood enhancement.

How I built it

Android Studio, Microsoft Azure Face API, Honeycomb API.

Challenges I ran into

Difficulty extracting data from the Face API and getting the appropriate data for emotion, age and gender initially.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

First time implementing facial recognition in an application, and I'm really happy it worked successfully.

What I learned

How to properly use APIs and create effective layouts in Android Studio.

What's next for Mood Detector and Enhancer

Build Functionality of the app further and keep on working to succeed on it.
