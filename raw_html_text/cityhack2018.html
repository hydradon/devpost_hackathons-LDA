TubeAmp

TubeAmp is a WebApp that creates Spotify playlists based on journeys on the London Underground.

It can be invoked remotely by sending an SMS message to 07418 340 203

It can also be accessed at TubeAmp.me

Messaging TubeAmp

Sending TubeAmp the start and end stops on your trip will result in TubeAmp returning a link to a Spotify playlist as long as your journey, with tracks named after - or "inspired by" - the stations on the way.

Invoking TubeAmp online

Inputting the start and end points of your journey into the website will result in the website redirecting you to your Spotify playlist!
