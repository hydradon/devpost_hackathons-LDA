Gab

We are aiming to make new arrivals feel truly welcomed by their new fellow countryman by encouraging one on one conversations betwean new arrivals and Australian residence.

Hate can be very loud, we want to shine the light on the positive by showing the new arrivals that there is a large community of Australian residents who welcome them with open arms.

Not by marching the streets, organising big events but by something simple. Helping with those mundane questions that can arise on a day to day basis.

With automatic language translator and automatic smart profile matching it makes it easy for anyone to ask questions and be connected to the right people.

Gabs might start out as a simple question but who knows what will grow from that. Sky's the limit. All we know is that we want to connect new arrivals and Australian residence by closing the gap and simple... gab.
