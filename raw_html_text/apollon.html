We thought about events with music, like at a party where there is a stereo system, but normally only one person is in charge of choosing the songs. Could it also work in a democratic style? Could people choose the next song together?

We think they can by using APOLLON. It is the music manager of the future which enables all guests to participate in creating the playlist.
