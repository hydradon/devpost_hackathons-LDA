Inspiration

South Park Season 19 Episode 5

What it does

SafeSpace parses your Twitter feed, removing any tweets that are negative or rude.

How I built it

SafeSpace is built using Symfony2 on top of the Twitter API and the HP Haven OnDemand Sentiment APIs.

There is also a literal "desk-top" client that runs on Intel Edison, and shows you your positivity stream all day long.

Challenges I ran into

Apache and Symfony Routing weren't playing nice.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It works and it's finished!

What I learned

All the inner-workings of Apache config files
