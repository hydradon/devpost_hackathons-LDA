This SBUHacks my team and I decided to delve into Machine learning. We pulled an open source project from online that takes an image and stylizes it based off of the style of another image, so we decided to turn this into a web app. Re:Define takes a photo of you and processes it, turning it into the same photo just with a new style to it.
