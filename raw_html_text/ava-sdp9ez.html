As Penn students, we juggle a lot of school work, activities, personal care, and much more. CAPS chat is a convenient chat service that lets you connect with a peer or CAPs counselor to voice whatever you have been feeling. Feel free to chat us to talk about your stress, anxiety, depression, or other related issues. We are here for you!:)
