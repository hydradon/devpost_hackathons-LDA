Look at the book Alexa Skill

The Alexa Look at the Book skill reads an RSS feed from Desiring God for Look at the Book bible study episodes and makes this list available through a custom APL document powered skill. The skill allows the user to choose an episode with its APL powered screen or through voice, saying "play the latest episode".

Once a user chooses an episode an APL video component plays this video with a pause play button. The user can then watch the episode and the session ends or say "go back" to go back to the episode selection screen.
