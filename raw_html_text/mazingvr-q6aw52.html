Imagine you open your eyes and find yourself within the center of a supernova, staring at the entrance of what looks to be a maze. With no other signs of life in all directions, you have no choice but to enter.

MazingVR is a maze exploration game set in a zero gravity space environment. Your goal is to escape the maze while collecting all the space orbs along the way. Exploring our beautiful yet ominous maze is the stuff of dreams…and nightmares.
