Inspiration

Not having a dashcam and not wanting to buy one, I have decided that the only reasonable thing to do is to make my own app.

What it does

It creates a recording of your drive from one place to another, recording both front and rear cameras. If the phone detects there's a crash, it will automatically create a backup of the video and send it to the cloud.

How I built it

Using android studio, some duct tape, and a car phone holder
