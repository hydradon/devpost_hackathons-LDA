• Deep Focus

Inspiration

Wanted to have a way to turn phone on Do Not Disturb, but somehow let very important messages, voicemails, and emails through

What it does

Uses IBM Watson to get the sentiment analysis on the data, see if there is a large sentiment/tone value, and let it override the Do Not Disturb

How we built it

Node.js, Twilio, IBM Watson, Koding, Radix, Caffiene, and willpower

Challenges we ran into

Making a good UI without a UI developer.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Seeing this come together, learning Nodejs.

What we learned

A while bunch of corner cases in Node.js.

What's next for Deep Focus

Getting it out to the masses!
