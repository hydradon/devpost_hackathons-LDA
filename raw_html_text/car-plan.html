Inspiration

Carpools are economically smart, and environmentally friendly. My partner Austin and I have both been in many carpools due to our long commute to TJ every day, and we realized that people seemed to always have trouble remembering when there day was, or even finding a carpool near them. We wanted to solve this problem with our great app: CarPlan.

How it works

CarPlan allows users to create a unique account, then find local carpools around them. They can request to join the carpool groups, and once joined they will be reminded when they are supposed to pick up or drop off the other members of the group. Users can also create their own carpool groups, and invite other users to join their groups.

We ran out of time and were unable to fully implement some features of the app, but we are planning in the future to build in more social features allowing users to message each other on the app, plans for finding the most efficient route from pick up areas to all members' houses, and all members' houses to a drop off area.
