Inspiration

Buying online is risky - there’s no way to try before you buy. Items can show up smaller or larger than expected. We wanted to build something that helps make that process easier.

What it does

EtsyCanvas lets you decorate your space with unique art pieces from shops around the world. All from your HoloLens.

How we built it

We made this with Unity and the HoloToolkit.

Challenges we ran into

Building our scoped featureset in the required amount of time was tough, especially since none of us had built for the HoloLens and only two of us had programmed in Unity before.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

  • Sticking objects to walls
  • Sounds play when you interact with objects
  • Great UI design
