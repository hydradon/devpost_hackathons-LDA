Inspiration

Distracted driving is the cause of billions of dollars in damage and thousands of lives lost each year. We aim to help decrease those numbers by incentivizing leaving your phone off while driving.

What it does app that prevents texting while driving

We give you money for keeping your phone screen locked! We collect data about your driving, in exchange for which we give you money, but only if you keep your phone locked for the whole drive

How we built it

Android studio, cURL

Challenges we ran into

Everything!

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Verifying screen is locked, collecting data, interpreting data, etc. It was a lot to take in for our first app!
