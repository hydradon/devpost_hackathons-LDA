Flood Watch IoT-System

Problem illustration: People who live in cities and villages with a bad or inefficient rain-drain network infrastructure suffer from a life-threatening risk which is the flood.

Solution: We have two ways to solve this issue:

  1. rebuilding the rain-drain network infrastructure which costs billions of dollars
  2. our Flood Watch IoT-system which has the following features: a. life saving b. cost-effective c. No need to change anything on existing infrastructure d. real-time accurate monitoring and help decision-makers to act fast and correct e. smartness and auto-detection of danger levels f. automatic control of water discharge pumps
