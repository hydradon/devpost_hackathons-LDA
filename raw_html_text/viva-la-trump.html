Inspiration

I wanted to make something with a technology that I don't usually use.

What it does

The webpage detects your hand and their motion through the Leap Motion Sensor, displays them on your screen, and gives you points if you pinch your hands.

How I built it

I built it with Javascript and getting ideas from the Leapjs examples github

Challenges I ran into

Time, Javascript.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I made a functional app for the Leap Motion in just a day!

What I learned

Javascript sucks, and I probably won't be using it again for a while.

What's next for Viva la Trump!

Making interface prettier, adding some more features, and general cleaning of the code.
