Inspiration

Most of us listen to Spotify or some other streaming service while working on our daily assignments. But when you remember that one song that you just need to hear right then, you have to hunt down the spotify tab among 100 other tabs. Where's the fun in that? That's why we created StreamSpot. All you gotta do is go to the omnibar, press 'S' (to invoke StreamSpot), type in the song name, and voila! We'll play the song and even add it to a playlist if you desire.

Challenges we ran into
