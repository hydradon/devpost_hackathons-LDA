Rutgers-Ratio

Discover the details of parties ahead of time so you pick the best one

Users can enter where their party is located and the details about the party. The parties show up as markers on google maps and users are able to view information about the party (address, frat, date, whether you need to pay to get in, and what ratio they will need to get in). Users can view all the parties around them based on their location, and use google maps to give them directions to the party!
