Inspiration

Every time you get lost in a huge mall you realize there are no maps available or solutions to help you find the way. We also had pre-Chistmas shopping flashbacks.

What it does

The app helps to navigate in a building (e.g. malls, universities, public buildings). You just need to choose your destination and position your phone downwards on the one of the markers placed on the floor. On your screen you will see an arrow pointing in the right direction.

How we built it

With great effort. And magic! And Unity/Vuforia.

Challenges we ran into

After some inconviniences with a few devices, everything went "with flying colours". Well... almost. To be fair we struggled with markers, but thanks to some brilliat ideas we managed to finalize the prototype.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Great planning, excellent cooperation and bright solutions!

What we learned

We learned how to work under pressure with a limited amount of time.
