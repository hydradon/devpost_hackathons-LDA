Inspiration

Love board games? Play them competitively? Well as you may know there is often a time required as part of competitive games such as chess. Our application is designed to allow for users to add n players to a chess timer so that there is a timer that limits the time it takes for each player to take their turns. Needed chess timer with n player support for competitive board games

What it does

Is a chess timer with n player support

How we built it

we made it over the course of ~10h this weekend
