Inspiration

College students want to do good in the world, but we might not always have cash to spare to donate to a good cause. Meanwhile, students also throw away money on name-brand products when the store brand is identical.

What it does

Charity cart helps you find a cheaper, but identical, store-brand version of a product and lets you donate the price difference to a charity of your choice.

How we built it

Frontend: Typescript, Angular

Backend: Python, Flask

Challenges we ran into

Cloud deployment, code generalization.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Great search capability on frontend, charity API integration, savvy use of Wegmans API.
