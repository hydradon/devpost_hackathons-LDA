Inspiration

There are plenty of apps monitoring a specific part of your health, all of them focusing on short-term-health-/fitness-goals. Imagine our world in 10 years - tons of data collected by various sensors allow us to depict a way bigger image of the long-term-health of a person if they are well combinated. By also including events from other sources like the 'Gesundheitscloud' health-line gives the user a deep insight into his medical life. Furthermore health-line can then predict future scenarios for the user by looking at similar health-lines.

What it does

health-line finds past events in huge amouts of health-data from different sources and can then predict future scenarios for the user by looking at similar health-lines. Thereby it offers an easy-to-use GUI.

How we built it

We built a webapp using several web-technologies and SAP-HANA database in the backend.

Challenges we ran into

Finding good metrics for measurements and visualisation
