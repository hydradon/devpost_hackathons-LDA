Inspiration

We wanted to encourage dog adoptions by providing a fun experience to find their favorite dog. We also wanted to create a community for dog lovers and owners by giving them fun and quirky questions to vote on and compliment our furry friends. We then use their responses to create fun data visualizations.

What it does

For everyone:

  1. If you are interested in dog adoptions, we show you adoption dogs with the questions to vote on.
  2. You can save dogs that you are interested in adopting.
  3. If you are not interested in dog adoptions, we will just show you dogs from your city.
  4. You can use the leaderboards to find the voted most athletic dogs and to browse more dogs as well!

For dog owners:

  1. You can upload pictures of your dog for other people to vote on.
  2. If your dog is voted in a question, you will be noticed via text and feel loved.

How we built it

  1. We fetched the dogs using Petfinder APIs

What's next for Boo
