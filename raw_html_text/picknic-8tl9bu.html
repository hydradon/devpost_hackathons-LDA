Inspiration

The mystery of 320,000 trees. It was a dark and stormy night in the CS center at a campus not far from the center of Edmonton. "If you drop a tree from a database, and there are no event listeners, did the tree actually get dropped?" I pondered the mystery over the coming days. I decided to solve the mystery, so I rounded up my best men and set off for the land of Startup Edmonton.

What it does

Locally sourced data and free range programmers provide high quality cruelty free picnic recommendations.

How we built it

Minimal amount of crying, maximum amount of keyboard to face action.
