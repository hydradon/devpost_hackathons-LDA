Inspiration

All of my friends who extremly indecisive.

What it does

Chooses a random restaurant nearby and gives you step by step directions to it. It doesnt tell you what you chose until you arrive!

How we built it

We used Google Play Services to get the current location and send it to the Yelp API to get a list of restaurants. Once we randomly select one, with a given radius, we use Google Maps and Google Directions API to show step by step directions to the unknown restaurant. The Map is drawn with help from Google Roads API to make them smoother and more real life to actual driving paths.

Challenges we ran into

Making the directions as accurate as possible and gathering the correct GPS Data.
