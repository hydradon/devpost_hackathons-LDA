Inspiration

Brian, Tanzeela, and I all have 19 P. The stress of checking swipes, checking menu, and trying to exchange swipes from all three different web browser is annoying.

What it does

Three basic functions: Buy & sell swipes in real time Check dinning hall menu Check how many swipes would keep you on the right track

How we built it

We used Android Studio and Java!

Challenges we ran into

It is illegal to sell swipes. Thus, this app emphasizes that students "donate" swipes to fellow Bruins.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We built a app on a idea! That is pretty cool!

What we learned

Coding is harder than we thought, but with good food and friends, anything is possible! We can swype to that!

What's next for Swype Right

We hope to expand our app by including functions where students' can input their weight goals, calorie intake, so that they can eat at the dinning hall that would allow them to reach their calorie intake.
