Inspiration

the whole event was about diversity so I made a game that gives every one knowledge about other countries

What it does

the game consists of two teams of three players from different nationalities.

As the game starts it randomly chooses a country gives you some extraordinary facts about that country, and then asks a question about it the team that gets it right gets a point.

THEN the real action begins after the question there are physical challenges that has a connection to the chosen countries,I had foot ball for Brazil with the vibration sensor and ninja game for Japan with the touch sensor if a team wins the question and the physical challenges they get to conquer that country and the game continues till a team has the most conquered countries.

How I built it

I built with 600+ lines of codes, arduino 101 and base shield,arduino sensors and LEDs and donut box Which unfortunately I found empty.

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments

that I'm proud of I actually made it work and a lot of the participants loved the game and the game made them connect and make new friends and have appreciation towards other cultures

What I learned

I learned a lot about other cultures traditions And that you can do a lot with a donut box
