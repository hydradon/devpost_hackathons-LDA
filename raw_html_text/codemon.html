Inspiration

We wanted to make a website that would help kids learn programming in a fun way.

What it does

You solve coding problems to gain experience for your Codemon.

Challenges we ran into

Domain.com didn't work and we couldn't use login APIs without a custom domain.

What's next for Codemon

Future additions could include more Codemon, more levels of evolution, and adding friends to battle with.
