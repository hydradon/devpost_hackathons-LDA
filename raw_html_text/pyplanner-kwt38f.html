PyPlanner

A smart scheduling system that lives in your Google calendar and intelligently integrates with your preexisting scheduled events. This python script will read your current class and work schedule from Google's data. All you have to do is run the script, allow access to your calendar in your browser, and input your task goals .

Each task goal is something you want to accomplish and a time frame.

  • Study for 3 hours for my test next Monday
  • Go to the gym for 100 hours this year
  • Work on improving my coding skills for 20 hours this month

Next time you open your Google Calendar, on your phone or computer, you will see we have scheduled blocks of time for you to accomplish these goals.

Our algorithm maximizes efficiency by breaking up lots of man hours into manageable chunks. We make each day as equal in work load as possible.

  • Have Fridays off? We will give you a few of your tasks to fill up your day.
  • Have class, work and a plane to catch? We won't assign you any tasks that day.

We want you to stop wasting time and achieve your goals.
