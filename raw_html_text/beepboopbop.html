Inspiration

Just like making games.

What it does

You move around with one joystick and shoot with the other, try to dodge incoming red missiles and survive for as long as you can for a good high score.

How we built it

Swift 3 Xcode Project using SpriteKit Library, also used AnalogJoyStick.swift from online source.

Challenges we ran into

Getting anything to work.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It's a fun game that we enjoy playing.

What we learned

Prioritize the hardest parts of the hack.

What's next for BeepBoopBop

Updated gameplay thats less choppy and perhaps more gameplay mechanics and enemies.
