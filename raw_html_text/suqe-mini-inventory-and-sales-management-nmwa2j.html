What it does

Manage mini market inventory 

                   inventory of purchase price 
                                                                            inventory of selling price 
                   availability of inventory in stock
                   inventory that are not available in stock

Manage credit or debt service 

                contact of credit customers 
                                                                amount of credit that the customers take  
                amount of payment that the customers paid

Store information about supplier 

               supplier contact 
                                                            supplier type(item that they provide)

report generation regarding sales and inventory information 

              report of soled items quantity and price  
                                                        report of daily,monthly or selected date profit
