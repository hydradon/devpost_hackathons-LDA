The purpose of the “Kids Care” app is to ease transition from hospital to home. It helps parents take care of a child once, once he/she has been discharged from the hospital. It is an easy to use app with a intuitive interface. It has four key features:

  • Medication Reminder
  • Discharge Checklist
  • Connect with Care Team
  • Emergency Contacts

"Home care as if your Care Team is there"
