Inspiration

We would like to empower students to find better deals on textbooks, rather than continue to be subjugated to the expensive prices at the bookstore.

What it does

Allows UCSC students to list offers to buy or sell textbooks, and helps them meet up and negotiate a fair exchange price.

How we built it

Using Android Studio.

Challenges we ran into

Creating a backend server is extremely challenging. It was our first experience using Android Studio.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We made a button.

What we learned

How to make buttons.

What's next for MyBooks

Expand support to other universities.
