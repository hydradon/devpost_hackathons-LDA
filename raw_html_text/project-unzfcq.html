Inspiration

Believe it or not, the retail industry is DYING. Online giants such as Amazon are taking over! Retail stores are struggling to keep up and need to embrace new way of visualizing data. Our product aims to provide in-depth analysis of product placement and an insight into the customer mind. It's like “putting yourself in their shoes”, literally.

What it does

HoloAnalytics uses data about customer locations in your store to generate a heat map of popular paths that your customers take. This is done by generating a cluster map using Azure machine learning. The map is projected onto the floor, and allows you to walk through the store seeing dominant areas and paths. You can then look at an item and ask for additional financial metrics to see how well the product is selling. It's easy to then see how these products are affected by their placement. You might see that a certain product sells well when placed beside another, or in a certain area.

How we built it

Our project was built in the Unity game engine and programmed in C#. Using the Azure API, we were able to generate a heat map displaying foot traffic in a store. Furthermore, we used the Vuforia API in order to have additional information displayed when the Hololens camera is pointed towards the product pricing labels.
