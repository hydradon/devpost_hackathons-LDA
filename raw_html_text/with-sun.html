Inspiration

Just to be able to get up at the right time in the morning to take a photograph at sunrise.

What it does

1)Set alarm for the next Sunrise or the next Sunset. 2)Shows weather(temperature) conditions, with a more human context, such as, indicating whether to take a water in case of very hot weather 3)Shows Flickr Images for Sunrise and Sunset from around the world.

How I built it

Use trnql SDK 1)access sunrise and sunset time. 2) Weather changes

What's next for With Sun

1) I have released to Google Play. If there is traction, I will make the UI more appealing and add social alarms.
