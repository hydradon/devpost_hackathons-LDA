blick

Blick is a web application that allows you to buy things from stores around the area where you live.

Has ever happened to you that you need to buy something but you just can't? Maybe you are busy with work, taking care of your children, sick or just tired. Well, Blick is the answer to your problem, buy online the things you need from stores around the area where you live and save time and effort just with some clicks.

Inspiration

Sometimes we want to buy something but we simply doesn't feel like going to the store and buy it, so we think in developing an application that allows us to buy what we need from our home.

What it does

It's a web application that contacts the client with the business registered in the app and allows the client to make their request in an easy way.

How we bult it

It was built wiht the help of the languages mentioned before and using Django framework for the back-end part and Bootstrap for the front-end

Challenges we ran into

Managing Django with data bases and learn how to give sessions with the users

What's next

Deploy the application and give it promotion in order to have business registered so the clients can start shopping and also promote it to have a bigger reach.
