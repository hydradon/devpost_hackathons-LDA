Inspiration

Personal problem with poor eating habits and schedule. Lack of time and ability to cook fully nutritional meals, while keeping up with a busy university life schedule.

What it does

The smart-home cooking machine that uses algorithms to predict the cooking time, and goes through every step of the cooking process until your meal is ready. The best part is that it does everything without your supervision. The machine is connected to the app that also uses algorithms to smartly come up with all possible healthy recipes to cook, based on the type and amount of groceries you have in your fridge.

The only thing you are required to do is put all the products in the machine. Just whsk it and enjoy your home-cooked meal in no time!
