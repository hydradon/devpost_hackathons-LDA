INTRODUCTION

Have you ever - looking at a stranger - admired a bag, a pair of shoes, a jacket of somesort, heck, even perhaps socks?

Wondering where they bought it, where you can get your own? It's not the easiest of tasks to ask a stranger, or ask an

intangible object, like an image.

Trendin is a new initiative aimed to fix this issue.

With the entrance of Trend.in, something like this now becomes a lot easier. It combines crowd-sourcing information from users, in order to form a data-base to store tags including information

such as websites to purchase the said item, or any other miscellaneous information.

PURPOSE

  • User can upload an image and tag items, or follow tags and discover where to purchase items, simply by having the

picture there.

DESIGN

  • Key points were minimalism, modern design
  • Because of the focus on images, we kept images to a minimum in order not to crowd it
  • The images are left as the centre-piece, and everything is left simple
  • bare essentials

FUNCTIONS

  • Tagging
  • Upload image
  • Follow tags
  • directed to buy items

FUTURE DIRECTION:

  • Enable onto mobile devices
