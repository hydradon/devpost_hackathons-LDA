chicknskratch

https://5ft3squirrel.github.io/chicknskratch/sketch.html We created an instrument that uses finger positions from Leap Motion to determine the volume of the Chicken Song, as well as a live visualization. The right hand controls the volume of the sound according to pinch strength, the spread between thumb and other fingers.
