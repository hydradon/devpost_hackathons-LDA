Inspiration

We want to build the most widely used financial platform, which enables people to save money in a disciplined manner and guarantees a good ROI, along with the flexibility to access huge amounts of cash when in need.

Opportunities

  • America Saves too little. More than a quarter of Americans have no emergency savings. 67% have less than six months of emergency savings.

  • No Company, Product or Service in America addresses this issue efficiently. This gives us a wide window to dominate and capture the market.

  • Obtaining funds from a bank or any other service during an emergency is difficult.

  • Hassles and time taken to get a loan.

How I built it

  • Extreme programming
  • Divided modules. Defined interfaces (REST Endpoints)
  • Frequent integrations and BVTs
  • Each responsible for their own area of expertise

Challenges I ran into

  • Scalable web auctioning system.
  • Synchronizing complex mysql queries in an async framework like node
  • Scheduling cron jobs on the backend node system to start the bidding and make automated payment transfers.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

  • Getting a good scalable system up and running in spite of complex modules.
  • Not giving up on efficiency and correctness despite a short frametime.

What's next for Pennypool

  • Build a profile of the user. Use intelligent ML algorithms to recommend potential investment options .
  • Associate every person in the group with a trust rating which is a true measure of how reliable and timely a person has been in the payments. This would help people choose their moneypools/groups wisely.
