BMOre is centered around customer experience. We aim to enhance and address customer wants and needs. BMOre is a Twitter data scraper to help the company understand clients through their positive and negative tweets. In doing so, we are able to understand the company's social media presence to help build better customer relationships and a better banking experience overall.The platform is created using python scraper, my sql storagem and html/css. We take tweets and turn them into clean data to truly outline the REAL EXPERIENCE of BMO customers.
