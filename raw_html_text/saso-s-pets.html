Inspiration

I have 4 kids myself and I am always interested in mixing the fun of games with the importance of learning. There have been many popular and successful mobile apps in this space and I wanted to try and bring that into an Alexa skill and utilize some of the unique features of Alexa as well.

What it does

Professor Saso has a lot of pets that keep him busy. Players are asked to help feed, bathe, and put the animals to sleep. To accomplish these tasks the players must answer math facts, spell animal names, or answer animal trivia. Get enough right answers and you can complete the challenge and earn merit badges to unlock more animals.
