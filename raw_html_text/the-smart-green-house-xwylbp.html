Inspiration

The idea came after we thought about how we should move agriculture to the city and produce healthy food at the lowest cost

What it does

A plastic house equipped with intelligent techniques to facilitate ablution and control with the technology of agriculture outside the soil

How I built it

The chassis was made of iron and covered with plastic sheets and for easy control Ardiuno was added with automatic sensors and remote control.

Challenges I ran into

Participated in the National Association of Scientific Clubs at the University of Setif 1 in 2018 and participated in the competition of the House of Contracting at the University of Guelma for 2018 and participated in the National Exhibition of Scientific Research Projects in Algiers 2018.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Ranked first in the pool of scientific clubs. And ranked third in the competition in guelma .

What I learned

I learned from the competitions the experience and how to plan a successful project and how to translate good ideas into real projects. I learned that combining competencies in creating a project generates a successful project from all sides
