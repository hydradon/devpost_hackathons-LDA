Inspiration

Type 2 Diabetes is a preventable disease disproportionally impacting communities impacted by poverty.

What it does

Takes the player running through the circulatory system of a human with diabetes, exemplifying through creative lens the impact that diabetes imposes on the body.

How we built it

Using Unity, and coding in C#1

Challenges we ran into

Collaborating with our teammates through Github or the UnityCollaboration.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

All the little things; side-scrolling, character mechanisms, audio, design, and researching more on the Type 2 diabetes.
