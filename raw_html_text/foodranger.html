Inspiration

So much perfectly good food goes to waste from restaurants throwing out their food at the end of the day. People would like discounted food and food banks would like food donations. FoodRanger solves this problem by creating a platform where the three can interact and food can change hands.

What it does

It allows restaurants to post their leftover food, and people can deliver it to food banks with a discounted meal as an incentive

How we built it

We used node.js, Express, Esri ArcGIS API.

Challenges we ran into

Getting the Esri API to work, time constraints

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting the Esri API to work, having everything somewhat work

What we learned

How to get the Esri API to work.

What's next for FoodRanger

Polish the app and change the world.
