What it does

It is an Outlook Add-In attached to appointments. It reads the location of an appointment and after entering a start location, gives you trip alternatives including the price and means of transportation. You can book directly from within your calendar.

How I built it

It is an Outlook Add-In on appointments. It is using the location from an appointment. The start address textbox does auto completion on your Outlook contacts (via Office 365 API). The backend is the API service of http://www.1clicktrips.com .
