Integrating war casualties into society The project is based on four activities

  1. Training war casualties on the computer course
  2. Office Management Course
  3. Reporting cycle
  4. Creating a job opportunity that will enable them to re-engage with society and gain their rights with a decent life and the right salary for their potential The project is planned to be in five large towns in the Syrian north for two months
