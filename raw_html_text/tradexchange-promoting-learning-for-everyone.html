Inspiration

We noticed that students at CMU often wanted to learn a new skill, such as an instrument or game but faced difficulty in finding someone to help. We wanted to resolve this and built TradeXchange!

What it does

Allows CMU students to offer or request a session with other students.

Challenges we ran into

Time, since we decided on a solution at a later stage during the hackathon.
