• RoomMe App UX Summary

Inspiration

Inspired by the problems with the roommate compatibility search around the Davis area for UC Davis students. Problems in organizing and fast effective way in finding a roommate to fill a room or a person looking for rooms around the Davis city area.

What it does

It makes searching for roommates easier through personality tag matching similar to dating apps algorithm based matching.

How we built it

We built it using Swift for IOS development and Adobe illustrator and Invision for UX and prototype testing.

Challenges we ran into

Slow internet, creating a lag in the completeness of our project.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The concept of our app is very helpful for students since every student experience issues in roommate compatibility and room finding in Davis. Facebook groups are usually unorganized in hosting these types of services.

What we learned

We need to plan accordingly in downloading libraries we need before the event due to the slow internet.

What's next for RoomMe App

Development of Swift code and launching of the app through the local UC Davis student body.
