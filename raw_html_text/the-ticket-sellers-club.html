Inspiration

Selling and buying concert and sport tickets should be frictionless.

What it does

The Ticket Sellers Club is an AI powered chatbot that centralised supply and demand of second hand concert and sport tickets.

Simply talk to our Ticket Sellers Bot on Telegram (check it here) to start buying and selling tickets faster than ever before.

The Ticket Sellers Club allows seamless peer to peer ticket transactions.

How we built it

We used IBM Watson's natural language classifier and Telegram API. We added a swear word library connected to Urban Dictionnary's API to make the bot more "human".

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We scrapped Craigslist's second hand tickets inventory to make the bot usable from day one.
