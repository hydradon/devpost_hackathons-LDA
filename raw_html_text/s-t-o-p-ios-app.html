Inspiration

The reason why we chose to pick this topic as our IOS Application is because we know that Domestic Violence is not only something that women face but is also something that anyone of any gender can go through. Through this project, we hope to raise awareness of this issue as it is becoming more and more prevalent.

What it does

Our application allows for not only Domestic Violence victims to receive help, but also spreads awareness to almost anyone who wants to aid the cause.

How we built it

We used many different languages for our final project.

For our IOS Application we used Xcode and Swift and learned the language in less than 12 hours and coded the entire application from scratch. For our Website we used Sublime Text as text editor and Github pages for our website hosting. The website was used to accompany the app.

Challenges we ran into

We were initially clueless about using Xcode as it was very overwhelming, but together as a team we overcame this hardship and succeeded in building our first IOS Application.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Putting all of our work together and used 4 components to emphasize one idea.

What we learned

We learned a total of 3 different languages: Swift, HTML, CSS.

What's next for S.T.O.P iOS app

We hope to add and implement more content and see it being used by people in need.
