• This is Phi.

Inspiration

To provide a positive change in people's lives.

What it does

Phi lets you communicate about your day and how you are currently feeling. Phi's intention is to provide delight to you by listening to your feelings and at times provide you with a little bit of laughter. If while you are talking to Phi, you no longer want to talk, you can go ahead and say "I don't want to talk ," and Phi will respect your wishes. Additionally, if during your conversation with Phi, you would like to start from the beginning of the conversation, you can go ahead and ask to "start over."

How we built it

We designed a voice interaction flow, developed Phi with node.js on Amazon lambda, and then tested Phi with real people for user feedback.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting the user flow right, integrating APIs and Amazon DynamoDB, releasing Phi to the store.
