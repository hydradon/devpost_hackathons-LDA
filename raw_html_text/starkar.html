The Inspiration for this project is that, the need for Augmented Reality in the growing fields of E-Commerce, where the customers' only problem is the inability to view the products in realtime into their environment, and also how it fits well into them in case of apparels.

So, we are creating an AR project where we have planned to integrate all E-Commerce portals' APIs and make our product reach a global audience, where the people would be able to visualize whatever they want from the sites we have partnered with.

** PLEASE LOOK INTO OUR VIDEO DEMO**

So, not only for E-Commerce portals but we have also planned for providing it as a 'Software As A Service' for local Furniture and Apparel dealers and manufacturers.

Reasons why customers are unsatisfied of buying furniture and apparels online : 1) Poor Imaginary Visualization of the product 2) Poor understanding of the fit of the product into customer's desired environment.

** PLEASE LOOK INTO OUR VIDEO DEMO**

Benefits of using our application : 1.First-of-all, it makes much more people to get attracted to our store. 2.Now it makes people to visualize the product in 3D and also by placing them into their environment. 3.People could experience the fit of the product in them. 4.It results in more number of successful orders. 5.Makes the Return rates lesser which results in huge savings for the company.

How we built and challenges : We built the AR models first of all using SparkAR and then, expoted it as FB Camera links and added as backend in our application which we developed using Android Studio. And the major challenge was we didn't find any API which made our project a bit weak than what we thought of.
