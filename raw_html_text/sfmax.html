Inspiration

Disneyland has a similar app that helps you plan out an entire day while taking into consideration wait times at lines and what you're interested in.

What it does

You pick how heavy, expensive, and which category you want to explore San Francisco in.

How I built it

Ruby on Rails with the Google Places and Uber APIs.

Challenges I ran into

Parsing the Google Places API result.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Parsing the Google Places API result.

What I learned

How to parse the Google Places API result.

What's next for SFMax

Styling and real-time generation of a trip with multiple transportation sources, including MUNI, BART, and Lyft.
