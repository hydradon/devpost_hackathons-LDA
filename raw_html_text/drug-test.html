People, especially college students, can go to a party without knowing that they are being affected by drugs. For instance, they may drink, sniff one kind of drugs without realizing it. Moreover, some drug users think they are no longer affected by drugs after a while. The app will help you better protect yourself.

What it does

It uses AI to detect if someone is affected by drugs by looking at the user' face.

How we built it

We implemented transfer learning with inception v-3 model and deployed the website using Flask.
