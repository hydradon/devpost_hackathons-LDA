Inspiration

We were inspired to build this project out of the need to multitask during your morning routine.

What it does

Our smart mirror allows you to view current weather, time, news, social media, etc.

How we built it

We begged homeless people for their cardboard boxes... got a random people to buy nail polish remover for us... and web built this mirror.

Challenges we ran into

Supporting the monitor in the unit was one of the biggest challenges!

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Through research, we were able to find a cheaper alternative to an observation mirror, mirror film, which can cost hundreds to thousands dollars. We are also proud of our engineering feat: being able to support a monitor on top of multiple cardboard boxes and spacers.

What we learned

WE LEARNED THAT YOU CAN DO ANYTHING IF YOU BELIEVE IN IT ENOUGH!!!

What's next for Smart Mirror

We plan to refine this product and put it on Kickstarter.
