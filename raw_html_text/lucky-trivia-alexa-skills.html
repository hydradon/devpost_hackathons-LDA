Inspiration

To try out Alexa APL and also to win the prizes.

What it does

Trivia/quiz game with new and creative questions you'd never have seen before! Be it on history or geography, film or TV, riddles or puzzles, guessing the sounds or you tell! New and unseen questions added everyday! Just Say, "Alexa, Play lucky trivia" to start the game! You can also pass the question for which you do not know the answer! Just say - "Pass"

How I built it

I used Amazon Alexa ask-cli to build the skill and added APL documents and screens using the APL Authoring tool offered in the Alexa developer Console

Challenges I ran into

Adding APL feature to the skill. APL resources and docs, and videos on twitch channel helped a lot.

What's next for Soccer Game Facts

I will add ISPs offering for the skill and add more user engaging features such as trivia questions every week.
