Our goal is to bring solar power to the people by enabling residential solar companies to target homeowners intelligently.

Sunspot uses property-specific data to estimate energy usage for lead generation.

Team AWEsome used data from USPS, NOAA, EIA, UScensus, realtor.com, and zillow.com as sources of property-specific data to build a regression model that projects annual energy usage. From there, customers can identify relatively high energy users in a given geographic area, incorporate other input data to tailor the model to their business needs, and use outside metrics such as cooling and heating daily degrees to indicate areas of high need for solar.
