Inspiration

MLB has seen its attendance lower. Parking can be a problem. Long lines at the concession stands. The Grand Slam App, was created create ease of use.

What it does It Creates

Bundled Tiered package experiences using existing databases such as; scheduling; ticket purchases, but also creating custom databases for parking and concession stands.

How I built it

Using Adobe XD for the Design and Prototyping.

Challenges I ran into

Unfortunately, the API for Development was unavailable. Our team continued to build-out the UX/UI Design, to give more of a since of the experience.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I'm most proud to of the animations. They came out nice.

What I learned

That Hackathons are really fun!

What's next for The Grand Slam Mobile App.

Moving forward we could include at the top tier levels options such as; Stadium Tours; exclusive autographs sessions; and rewards points program.
