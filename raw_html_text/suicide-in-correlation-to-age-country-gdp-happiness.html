Inspiration

Mental health is a topic that is near and dear to our heart. Some of us struggle with mental health and shinning a light on the subject is always important.

What it does

Educates the users about the grim reality of suicide.

How I built it

Using Anaconda, specifically jupyter notes I compiled and plotted the statistics in a visually compelling way. We integrated this information into a website built on HTML CSS and JavaScript.

Challenges I ran into

Idea crafting and setting up environment. Having to start fresh half way through the hackathon.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Our team of devoted coders.

What I learned

How to plot data in jupyter notes using Panda and matplot with seaborn.

What's next for Suicide in Correlation to Age, Country, GDP, & Happiness :-)
