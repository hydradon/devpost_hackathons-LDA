Inspiration

We wanted to make a fun way to help kids learn about STEM subjects with both hands-on experience and game-elements.

What it does

Teach teens STEM subjects while growing their HackBot to be the very best! Battle other bots to level up!

Answer questions to train your (wireless!) HackBot (e.g. EE and MechE, shown) online and watch them battle. Learn how to build real-life upgrades on this modular robot and watch your bot rumble and shoot laser eyes as it attacks its opponent.
