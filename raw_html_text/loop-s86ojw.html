Inspiration

Inspired by the recent paris & beirut attacks, we wanted to create an app where you ask an AI(personal assistant) what news to follow and it updates you on developments in real time. You can also inquire more about specific events.

What it does

Send "follow ___ " and we keep you updated on real time updates about the news you filled in the blank. When you type "stop following [what you specified in _ __ ], the app will stop following the news. You can follow several news concurrently.

How I built it

node.js backend

firebase for database

obj-c for iOS app
