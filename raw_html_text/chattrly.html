Inspiration

There was no good solution for temporary teams in college when group coursework was required.

What it does

Chattrly allows you to form teams that last for as little as one month, or to keep the group operating as long as you want.

How I built it

PHP/MySQL/Ajax/Bootstrap

Challenges I ran into

Syncing messages in an efficient manner

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Getting messages to sync effectively

What I learned

Programming is hard sometimes.

What's next for Chattrly

I'm going to finish Chattrly in its entirety and see what user adoption rates look like. If they're acceptable, Chattrly will stay around.
