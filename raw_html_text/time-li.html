Storytelling is the glue that holds cultures together but stories are unfortunately frequently lost. Timeli is a way to change this. A way to preserve and explore the stories that make up the timelines of our lives, communities and cultures We do this by: Providing an interface that audio records and transcribes your stories Allowing you to browse stories from your entire community in a timeline visualization
