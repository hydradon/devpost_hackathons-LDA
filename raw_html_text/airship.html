What it does Airship?

With Airship you can find, book and buy from low cost flights to many other setup conditions the users have… For example, you want to find a flight to Japan we’re your maximum budget is $990, in this case the algorithms are going to try to find everyday that combination, and if he finds it, he either can buy it to you through your Visa Card: https://youtu.be/8YPdlsnXkXs or send you an automatic alert where you can buy them. Also Airship has been trained to help users with the United Airlines MilagePlus program, benefits & enrollment. You can see how it works: https://youtu.be/uIqJqMsaoLw and

Try it on FB Messenger: https://m.me/302903173787559

How I built it

The solution is a mashup between the integrations of multiple APIs: for the payments we use Visa API, for the speech recognition we use Google Speech API, for the Natural Processing Language (NLP) we use Dialogflow, for the real time data flights information we use United Airlines API and for the storage of the reservations we use Google Sheets API. All of this APIs, integrated through cloud functions to our back-end in Firebase and FB Messenger.
