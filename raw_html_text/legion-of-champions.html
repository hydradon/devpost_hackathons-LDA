Legion of Champions is an offshoot of my main project: Storyteller. It allows the player to select their champion, travel around the world, engage in challenging boss fights, and seek treasure and glory beyond one's wildest dreams. All done in HTML with a bit of CSS and JavaScript, this RPG was, though tedious with multiple attempts to utilize PHP only to abandon using it, quite an experience for myself. Watching Disney-Pixar's "Ratatouille" inspired me with the phrase that "anyone can cook". Granted, it doesn't mean that everyone can cook, but anyone willing to put in the work can achieve great things that were believed impossible. As for "Legion of Champions", the bare bones of the RPG are complete, but the cast will be added, and expect more updates on the game soon.
