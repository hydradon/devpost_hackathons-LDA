Inspiration

I really enjoy listening to music, but sometimes get sick of the songs on my playlist, the more I listen to them. This project still plays the same music from a single playlist, but it mixes it up a bit by only playing certain parts based on their "hype".

What it does

The user simply enters in what type of music they like - for example, "Hip Hop". The app then crawls through Youtube and gets a playlist from the keywords, combines the audio from the videos in these playlists, and mixes it so that the songs only play for a small amount of time, and transition into each other based on their tempos and "hype" factor.

This can be for making "hype" videos (e.g new launches), music for a party, or anything in between.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of
