Inspiration

Terminator movie.

What it does

Use a device that has a camera to recognize some Image Targets and projects different augmented elements on the device screen over the real images.

How we built it

We used Vuforia SDK in Unity3D. Vuforia comes with support for AR elements such ARCamera or ImageTargets and Unity3D helps us to build a virtual environment on top of the real world.

We focused on presenting some of the key elements of the AR world when some predefined images are recognized by the device: play a movie, interact with objects (open, close elements), keep on-screen information like the weather, new emails, clock etc.

Challenges we ran into

There were a lot of challenges, for example, it was much more difficult than we thought to configure the objects to stay in place, in a virtual 3D world and interact with them.
