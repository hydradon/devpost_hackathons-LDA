Inspiration

Microservices architectures and Docker containers

What it does

Introduces an embedded microservices and containerized approach to multi-app development in RIOT

How we built it

Challenges we ran into

Time constraints

Accomplishments that we're proud of

What we learned

It's a good idea to get a prototype down early and improve, rather than designing everything at the start.

What's next for Jerryscript_app

Use as a basis for a prototype for app development in RIOT, including OTA update, containerization, and build configuration.
