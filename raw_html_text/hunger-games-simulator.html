Inspiration

We both love the read books. One of our favorites is the Hunger Games by Suzanne Collins. We thought we would share our love of this series with everyone else.

What it does

This simulates the 25th annual Hunger Games. You can put in the names, and watch as the game unfolds right before your eyes.

How we built it

We used an ArrayList to hold all the names and generated random outcomes for the games.

Challenges we ran into

We wanted to do this as an iOS app, but both of us do not have Macs, so we're not able to fully implement our initial thoughts.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

This is our first hackathon. We were able to reach our full potential with this app.

What we learned

We learned the basics of Swift and Xcode to develop an iOS app

What's next for Hunger Games Simulator

Develop a fully functional iOS and Android apps to implement this Hunger Games Simulator.
