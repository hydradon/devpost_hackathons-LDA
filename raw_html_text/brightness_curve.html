Inspiration

I walked outside one day and my phone went to full brightness even though half bright would have sufficed.

What it does

The (concept) app allows you to adjust the standard brightness curve to your liking.

How I built it

Brute Force , Research and Android Studio

Challenges I ran into

Creating the GraphView class, Messy code, lots of bugs

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It works to a degree, Learned how to control the hardware on my phone, and Staying up all night making it

What I learned

With enough time and research you can make anything.

What's next for Brightness_Curve

Easier Customization and control of the curve, More Linear and Exponential Function Support, Smoother Transitions, Sexier Design,
