Inspiration

We were inspired by the idea of holograms that we could touch. We wanted to make the virtual as physically real as possible.

How it works

Haptic Touch gives users of Leap Motion physical feedback when they touch objects in virtual realities.

Challenges we ran into

We ran into challenges when trying to find ways to merge arduino controlled feedback with events that happened in the virtual reality of Leap Motion.

What I learned

We learned how to interface with Leap Motion as well as how to create our experiences with Leap Motion.
