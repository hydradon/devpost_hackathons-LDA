Hackamp is a reliable and open source network for anyone in the community interested in computer science but don’t know where to start or already set their path. Based on the website’s functionality, we are able to test the knowledge and level of interest the user might possess going into the realm of computer science and/or tech-based events. Providing a platform that consists of different events, workshops, and info sessions, we’re sparking the mind of a potentially interested candidate in the field.
