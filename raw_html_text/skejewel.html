Ever been in a meeting with others and realized that you have a really hard time dividing up the tasks? Do you want a way to keep track of all the tasks your teammates have completed? Don’t worry because Skejewel is a revolutionary new web application that can be used to simplify your life. All you need to do is to enter your project names, team member names, and the tasks you would like to see complete!
