Inspiration

We all experience the time where the busy schedule of everyday-life prevents us to pay attention to our basic health needs. Today we put an end to this problem.

What it does

Salamat is a multi-Purpose Application to help you keep track of your most important body-needs during the day. We are featuring: Body-Mass-Index Calculation Daily hydration amount Vitamin-D requirement Daily Calorie-intake need Mass Convector calculator

How we built it

We used android studio to perform most of the programmings.

Challenges we ran into

The main challenge was to create the application in smaller pieces and out them back together as we faced many errors to archive the task

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of creating a useful application which follows a user-friendly UI and demonstrates our Android-Application_Development skills.

What we learned

We worked in a team and strengthened our knowledge around Android-Studio, and Application Development.

What's next for Salamat

We are looking forward to add more features to our application and be able to track individual's health- journey.
