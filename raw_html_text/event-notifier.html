Event-notifier

It's a pain to keep track of every single thing going on at large events, whether that be Hackathons, Conferences, or even a social!

Organiziers also have a tough time reaching out to the correct people they need to!

Event-notifier simply provides a schedule users can subscribe to using their phone number. Pick and select events you want to be reminded by! Only get reminded by things that really matter to you.

How it works

  1. You select events to subscribe to
  2. Put in your North American cell phone number (without the international number and without formatting - e.g. '2894893743')
  3. Wait for SMS messages to remind you 15 Minutes before the event starts! - Checks are made per minute.
