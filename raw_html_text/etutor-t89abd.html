etutor

English tutors network

Problem

No English - No Job

It is not urbanize generation, it is globalization. Now a day, without English, people will find difficult to have a good job. English is not a bonus cite, but a required quality.

Learning English is not cheap

Even with a middle class family, learning and examination fee of English is still high. Each student usually can only manage to follow a training program or join in an English center.# To move to another place, more money is needed.

Solution

connect experienced tutors to students

Students will be able to submit their speaking and writing examples according to the format of popular examinations such as IELTS, TOEFL. The experienced tutors will correct and give advice to these students. Because this system don't require any class room and tutors can work when they have free time, the cost will be cut down. We believe that, a small change can make huge difference. Many tutors can help you get out of your routine and have new achievements.

machine learning is on the ship

Although experienced tutors is strong point of our system. Machine learning is also on the ship. We use machine learning to connect the right students to the right tutors. And also figure out what is students' strength or weakness by their speaking and writing example, which will help students more effectively and personally.
