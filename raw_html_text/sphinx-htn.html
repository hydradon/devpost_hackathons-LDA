What we did and why we did it

Inspired by our love of riddles and curiosity for Blockchain technologies, we built a webpage which lets you participate in a global contest to solve a challenge. You pay an entry fee to enter the competition which goes to an award prize and the first person to solve the puzzle wins it all. Behind the scenes, a smart contract handles the payment logic.

How we built it

Standard html, css and js for the front-end, solidity and ethereum tooling for the backend

Challenges we ran into

Coming from not knowing much about the blockchain ecosystem and smart contracts, we had to work hard to learn how to integrate these technologies into our page and get the product working
