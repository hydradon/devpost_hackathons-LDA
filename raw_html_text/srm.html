Elify: Your Social Relations Manager

Enter Elify: the best online open-source tool to help convert your acquaintances into best friends, with a friendly reminder to keep in touch with your peers by analyzing the frecency of your interactions. Even better, Elify stores all relevant information about your friends, allowing you to get suggestions on how to best connect your friends who don't know each other, yet they could be best friends, all thanks to you and Elify! This is done by running similarity algorithms based on interests, hobbies, current projects and more. Stay tuned for more cool modules coming your way, including facial recognition and machine learning!

To get started, sign up with a button on the top right, or log in with an existing account and start adding people you care about the most.
