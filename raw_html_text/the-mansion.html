The Mansion is a choose your own adventure and click-game app. My inspiration for this app was actually quite simple. One day I got bored, I decided to make something creepy out of a maze app. Because Code Studio doesn't really have an "is touching" type block I couldn't do a maze. Instead I made The Mansion. I'm proud of myself for figuring out all of the different sounds and all the timing for the different sounds in the app itself.
