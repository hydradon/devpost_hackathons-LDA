Kids Diet Tracker

Problem: Children’s imbalance diet. AS a 5 years old kid’s father, I have a headache (as many other parents) :
My son do not like to eat green vegetables!!! How can I know his food is healthy or not? How to track his diet record in a long term ?

Solve this problem : 5 simple steps

  1. Take a photo before my son’s each meal .
  2. Recognize food nutritions automatically .
  3. Show me daily & weekly nutrition intake charts.
  4. Give my son rewards when he is following healthy diet daily, weekly, monthly and yearly .
  5. Warn me by email when his healthy-eating-index exceeds the alert value .
