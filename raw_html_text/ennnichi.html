Inspiration

"Ennnichi" means "auspicious day" in Japanese. People who are walking around may be a person of fate. Having said that, everyday is "Ennichi".

What it does

This product "ennnichi" is a matching service that shoots people I meet in my daily life and tells me who I want to meet.

How I built it

I build this product with Hackathon Team Member. We Held Hackathon with Airbnb and build this product just one day. Most people don't have any knowledges of AWS, but we built this product.

Challenges I ran into

We ran into challenges of how to register informations of people who take picture and processed with Rekognition. The answer was to use "collection", but we spent time to resolve it. Furthermore, we can't check which collections and people are registered now with management console.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

To spread AWS to those who don't use AWS usually.

What I learned

AI APIs of AWS is very useful. I can use them more comfortably.

What's next for ennnichi

To tell you the truth, we don't finish to create this product, so we desire to complete this.
