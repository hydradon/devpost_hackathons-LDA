One day, while the twenty year old Irene Ye updated her prescription glasses for the tenth time, she suddenly wondered to herself, “If only there was a place where I could sell all my old frames?” and that’s how the idea of GlassCycle came to be.

But GlassCycle also has an important twist, we’re a charity working hard to provide new glasses for underprivileged children and teens who just can’t afford them. So whilst you enjoy your new glasses, you can also feel good about helping someone else achieve clear vision!
