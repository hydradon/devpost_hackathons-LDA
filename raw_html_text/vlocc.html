Inspiration

My teammates and I wanted to try something that was out of all of our reaches

What it does

It is incomplete, but soon it will have a peer-to-peer connection with other users on a site in which you can share messages, images, and eventually video. Using block-chain technology, security is a key component of our project.

How we built it

Python, with the help of Json and Flask libraries

Challenges we ran into

All members were unfamiliar with many of the concepts and components of networking and server-client messaging. Also, Flask and Json were completely new to us at the start of Brickhack.
