Inspiration

After paying a jaw-dropping amount at the University Book Store, I decided to move the entire process on-chain to make prices transparent, cheaper, and trust-less.

What it does

Allows sellers to sell their books on the public blockchain and buyers to purchase the cheapest available book. It also includes an escrow method of payment for trust-less transactions
