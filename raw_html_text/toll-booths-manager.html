Inspiration

In developing countries there are many toll booths, these toll booths are less transparent, leading to protests and traffic congestion. Suitable blockchain technology to solve this problem.

What it does

It helps the government and people manage toll stations, investments and revenues are transparent.

How I built it

I use Tron blockchain network because the performance of Tron network is quite good.

Challenges I ran into

Because I cannot deploy directly at the toll booths, I can only do one simulation.

What's next for Toll Booths Manager

I will improve my application and I hope early blockchain technology is applied to toll booth management.

Smart contract management: https://tollbooths.herokuapp.com/admin/ (To manage smart contracts, you need to use a "unique private key" to log into Tronlink. The hackathon managers may request a private key, I will provide a private key of smart contract)
