Problem,

I have been suffering from eczema for 22 years and have not find a cure until few years ago and I managed to reduce it dramatically. The discovery was to keep the temperature of the affected area of my skin, usually the elbows and knees, at a constant level, and to avoid any sudden fluctuations, which is difficult especially with todlers

Solution,

so I took my experience discovery a step ahead and developed android app, called Pismire, that uses thermal imaging technology, Using Pismire parents and adults can view and monitor human skin temperature seamlessly, and recieve smart decision service. So without any further ado let's dive straight into the demo.

Business

UK population is 64 million, 11m are children and 1/5(2.2m) has skin Eczema according to NHS, 8/10 develops it before the age of five, where they can't speak and understand, so parents will have great potential same as adults to use Pismire and reduce Eczema i.e. there is huge demand to such solution.
