Inspiration

Personal experience.

What it does

Our project locates the nearest disposable systems for users and provides an easy-to-use interface for admins.

How we built it

We used Figma to prototype and storymap the user's journey.

Challenges we ran into

Exploring new programs including indoor mapping development kits.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of building an application that can help provide a means to reduce our carbon footprint, while providing an enjoyable user experience.

What we learned

We learned how to go from a simple idea to a full fledged functioning prototype and the overall design process.

What's next for Find My Bin

We are investigating future additions that can help elevate the application experience through routing, item classification features, and providing advanced data analytics.
