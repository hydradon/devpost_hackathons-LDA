Inspiration

We wanted to be able to help people find tickets to travel locations easily by getting prices sent directly to them through their email

How I built it

We used python and flask to create a web app that would take in the required information from the user and add them to a list that would send a weekly email containing the cheapest plane tickets to a location of their choosing.

What I learned

This was a new experience for our group, with everyone working with technologies that were new to them and we got to build our first attempt at a web app.
