Inspiration

We wanted to help people make educated decisions for their real estate.

What it does

Our service starts with the user's address, and provides a full data analysis on the environment. The analysis includes walking, driving, and public transit times to nearby living facilities such as grocery stores, pharmacies, and public transit. We also showcase demographic and pollution data.

How we built it

We downloaded and cleaned data from Finland's open datasets, and leveraged Google's Places and Distance Matrix API to gain accurate insights into the data.
