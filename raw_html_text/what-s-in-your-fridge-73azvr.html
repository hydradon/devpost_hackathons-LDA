Inspiration

I'm usually home alone and there isn't much food in the fridge. My parents aren't home till much later, and I get super hungry. Most of the recipes online require a huge amount of other ingredients. I want something quick.

What it does

With the ingredients that you have entered in the app, our app filters recipes from an online database and presents it to the user in a user-friendly manner.

How we built it

The app was built using Xcode with a project creating using Swift 3.

Challenges we ran into

Without prior knowledge in Swift and the lack of proper tutorials, it was difficult to understand what we wanted and implement what was possible.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We have successfully created a complex application that uses various aspects of the possibilities and iOS application can do.

What we learned

-Swift 3 -PHP -PHPMyAdmin

What's next for What's in your Fridge?

-saving recipes -adding a grocery list for ingredients -sharing recipes with friends -"Surprise Me" feature that presents an AI turn-wheel to user that randomizes the different kinds of recipes
