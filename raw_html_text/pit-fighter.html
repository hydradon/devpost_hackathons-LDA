Inspiration

Pit fighting / gladiators. We thought it would be fun to make an arcade game about fighting in an arena!

What it does

You enter the arena, kill the monsters, buy any upgrades, and continue fighting more.

How we built it

We built it with the unreal engine (which took 7 hours to install by the way) and just had fun adding as many features as we could to the game before the deadline

Challenges we ran into

There were problems with saving our variables and sorting out hitboxes.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We enjoy our game! We were able to link an xbox controller to it and it's just so fun to play in our opinion. We have a shop system and an entire 3D arena to play in! We also made the game get much harder/easier according to how well the player was doing in the game.

What we learned

I learned how to use the unreal engine from having no previous knowledge while Ciaran learned to use parts of it he had never used before.

What's next for Pit Fighter

More pits, more fights, more monsters. Cool stuff basically.

xbox controls

move - joystick, attack - x, open shop - right shoulder, heal - y, upgrade health - b, upgrade damage - a

pc controls

move - WASD, attack - space, open shop - u, upgrades - use the mouse to click on them
