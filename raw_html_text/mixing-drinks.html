Inspiration

Being able to work towards another Amazon challenge milestone in a fun and exciting way.

What it does

Alexa will read off a recipe of a mixed drink.

How we built it

You ask Alexa to "open mixing drinks", then you can say our select which drink you would like to make from this list of drinks. Alexa will read out the recipe with the drink ingredients in parts. You can also choose to construct your drink with optional garnishes, and even watch a video on how to make the drink.

Challenges we ran into

Not being able to include every optional for the actual finished product.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Having some good video moments of perfectly executed drinks and lime slicing. Even got to add gummy bears for a sweeter effect.

What we learned

How to make a nice list of drinks and be able to craft them in a fun and interesting way which allows others to make a drink of their own.
