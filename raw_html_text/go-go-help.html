Inspiration

We've always found that the homeless and lower income brackets and disproportionatly affected by the lack of internet services. We believe that their lives can be vastly improved if they were able to access basic information that those in higher income brackets take for granted.

What it does

Go Go Help ensures that those with basic SMS phone texting can message our number and get the information they need. Those who are homeless cannot plan ahead for the weather, nor check themselves into nearby rehab centers, nor can they discover the bus schedules on the fly. However, with Go Go Help, these members of our community can easily access each of these things at just the push of a button.

How we built it

We used Twilio and our own web scraping to grab information and process requests made by the community members.

Challenges we ran into

Discovering how to use these techniques and API's as well as proper server setup with Structure and Twilio

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Developing a solution that will positively impact our entire community!

What's next for Go Go Help

More available services such as appointment check in, homeless shelter discovery, Craigslist job integration, and linking to soup kitchens.
