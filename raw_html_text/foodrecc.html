Inspiration

We love food and wanted to try harnessing the power of Google Cloud Vision to discover new restaurants

What it does

foodRecc takes photos from a user's Twitter feed and recommends restaurants based on photos of food

How we built it

We built foodRecc using Google Cloud Vision, Twitter, and Zomato APIs

Challenges we ran into

We initially wanted to get photos from Instagram accounts, however Instagram recently changed their cryptography and made it more difficult to scrape photos

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Completing this project, using 3 APIs simultaneously together in a smooth manner in the span of 24 hours.

What we learned

Setting up a good development environment and installing Python dependencies can be difficult at times. Instagram is not very developer friendly and actively makes it difficult to scrape images from their platform.

What's next for foodRecc

The world

In all seriousness, we would like to get Instagram integration and improve the recommendation system to be less naive
