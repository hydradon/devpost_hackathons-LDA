What it does

Bluetooth enabled human interface device that accepts multiple forms of input. This device would allow someone who isn't able to use a standard mouse to control their computer or tablet.

Design Thoughts/Requirements

  • Mouse and Right/Left Click HID
  • Microsoft , MacOS, iOS iPad compatible
  • Bluetooth
  • 1st concept could be joystick for design safety, 2nd concept is hands free

Hardware

  • Bluetooth enabled micro controller
  • Gyroscope
  • Magnetometer
  • Joystick
