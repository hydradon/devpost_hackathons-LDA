Inspiration

Having several projects and important services hosted on DigitalOcean, it is important that I am aware of whether there is an outage or if there is maintenance scheduled. Having to navigate to the website every time I want to check can cause a loss of time while I am trying to work. So having a simpler solution was necessary.

What it does

This skill essentially brings the DigitalOcean status page to the Amazon Alexa, allowing you to do everything the website does. Features:

  • To get the current status of all services, say 'get the status'.
  • To get the status of a specific service, say 'get the status of [service name]'.
  • To get any current or scheduled maintenance, say 'is there any maintenance going on'.
  • To get a custom report of your favorite services, first add something by saying 'add [service name] to my report'. Then, say 'get my report' to hear the status of your favorite services. This report is saved, so you can just ask for your report whenever you use the skill

What's next for DigitalOcean Status

To be published to the store and to be used by developers who are short on time!
