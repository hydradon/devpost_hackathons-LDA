Problem: The rapid increase of obese/overweight teens in america.

Solution: An app that allows user to find healthy food options; Allows user to search for healthy eating restaurants/food markets

This app lets people use foursquare as a gps to find the best places to eat that wont affect your weight and your wallet. It also tracks history about what you've eaten, places you go too alot, shows suggestions, and recommendations from friends.
