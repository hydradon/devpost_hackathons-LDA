Welcome to Sentimeter

Have you ever wondered what kinds of vibes you’re getting from your daily music, internet, and news browsing history? What if you started watching a YouTube clip of dancing chickens hoping for a laugh, only to find out at the end that the chickens get run over while crossing the road?

SentiMeter is here as your most reliable sentiment measuring stick to help you figure out the moods of videos, songs, ads, journal pages, news articles and more BEFORE they can rain on your parade or push your fun night past the “funnyness” boiling point! Simply hover over a link and before you can say “bippity boppit-” we’ll have already calculated for you just how happy, funny, and provocative that one-click-away experience might be. Come back to your personalized SentiMeter at the end of the day and we’ll give you a tally of all the vibes that hit your dashboard since you last woke and started clicking.

So how does it work? Our sentiment analysis uses third party natural language processing, audio processing, and text categorization to quantify mood induction from visual and audio experiences.

Sound cool? It gets better: we're a Chrome extension, so you can download Sentimeter for free and effortlessly track the sentiments you take in everyday through news, social media, and even music. Take control of the kinds of vibes that penetrate your day from the world’s virtual cloud machine and lift your SentiMeter scale today!
