Cozy Flat Mail Reader VR

by Mark Müller berlin3d@icloud.com CEOs Pet Software http://www.ceospet.com

Read Mails and view Videos / Web pages from the mails in the relaxing privacy of your cozy flat in virtual reality.

For Oculus Rift DK2

Left Mouse Button: Pick up / Throw Right Mouse Button: Drop

To use the mail functionality you have to get an API key at https://context.io/ and add your mail accounts. For now only the Inbox folder of first account is used. Enter your key and secret here to access your mails.

If you dont have an context.io account yet, there is a test mail lying on the couch table.

The Mails appear as silver balls. Throw them into the fan to extract the links from the mail. Throw the links onto the TV to view them.
