Made a bot for game called Rocket League. The goal of the game is to score on the opponent's net with your car. The bot chases the ball and aims for the opponent's net if it is on the attack and will defend its net when the opponent was last to hit the ball.

An injector is used to get in-game values from the Rocket League client. These values are read and calculations are done to create user inputs for the game.
