echocash

Echocash is an application built for users in a hurry- whether you need to pay Joe back for that coffee he bought you earlier when you lost your card, or if you need to subtly remind Paula that she still owes you three dollars- this app is built for situations like these. Simply speak to your Amazon Alexa-enabled product, and after some setup, sending and receiving money through our service becomes a breeze! When in full production, the application and website are fully enabled, but for the purpose of this hackathon, we chose to build simply, and to just display the critical. Given here, you'll find our logo, statements, and a sample page where our development team is displayed. Explore our code, and have fun using Echocash!
