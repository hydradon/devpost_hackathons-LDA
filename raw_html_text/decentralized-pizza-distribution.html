Cheese wasn't an original topping on the pizza when it was invented ..

Cheese was added to pizza to hold on the toppings ..

Americans have adopted cheese on pizza as if it were a requirement without option ..

This dApp is created to pre-sell & crowdfund the distribution of no-cheese pizza ..

Using restaurants and private commissary kitchens we plan to sell pizza using tokens which are earned through user/consumer participation ..

We re-invest profits to pay for free distribution of pizza to homeless citizens or hungry citizens ..
