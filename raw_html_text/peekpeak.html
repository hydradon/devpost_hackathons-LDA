Inspiration

We wanted to cut down energy usage during the peak power time to both save overall energy, therefore limiting reserve power plants from being used as well as save customers money by conserving energy, especially during peak hours.

What it does

It monitors the ways to reduce energy consumption in the home during peak hours and allows you to choose areas to save energy

How we built it

Using a Dragonboard with Arduino software and an Android App

Challenges we ran into

Getting communication between the app and the board

Accomplishments that we're proud of
