Purdue-Study-Groups

Struggling in a problem set but too busy to attend SI or office hours? Or are you simply looking for a buddy to study with, or hang out with? Purdue-Study-Group is for you! In this app, you simply have to input your free time and the course you are struggling in, and the app will find coursemates with similar free time who are willing to hang out with you. You will get their email and contact number (chatbox and social media integration to be implemented).

We used React Native for the front-end and Firebase for the back-end.
