Inspiration

We were inspired by the horror stories that we read about the tube and the long and sometimes spooky history of London.

What it does

Brings you on an unforgettable adventure, searching for clues of the notorious Jack the Ripper who has been transported to present-day London, freely roaming along the night tube. You have to find clues, solve puzzles and navigate the tube to track down this elusive killer.

How I built it

We used the Anvil platform for the web app, css for the front-end and python for the backend.

Challenges I ran into

There was a period of time when the navigation buttons were not working, and after a period of troubleshooting, we found out that it was because the buttons were not linked to the code.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Creating my first complete web app, learning python as the hackathon progressed.

What I learned

Coding is not easy at all, but quite fun!

What's next for Chasing the Ripper

Playtesting! Share with friends and family, lots of fun (5 minutes) guaranteed!
