Inspiration

The beepBeep robot serves as a wonderful proof of concept and model for larger scale projects. These projects can inlcude medical applications, and numerous hmi applications.

What it does

The beepBeep using its compainion app is able to take directions from a user, remember and replay those directions. Using it's sensors it is also able to aviod dangerous situations, and prevent against poor quality users.
