Inspiration

Detecting Walking patterns and fall detection in elderly people staying alone or young children in house.

What it does

Recognizes walking patterns and finds out a position when a fall is detected as well as trains to find a general walking/sitting pattern.

How I built it

I modified the apis available from myo to generate the data from the myo armband in real time and then use it to generate the required data as well as train the datasets which is done in matlab. The analysing for any fall detection generated a text alert.
