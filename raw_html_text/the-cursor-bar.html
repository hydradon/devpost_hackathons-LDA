Inspiration

The new 2017 MacBooks were released this year with new specs, and boasted a new feature called the touch bar. This android app allows your phone to become a touch bar for your windows device.

What it does

Brings shortcuts on your phone therefore increasing productivity

How I built it

Using Android SDK and a python server

Accomplishments that I'm proud of
