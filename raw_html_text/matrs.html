Inspiration

We realized that currently donating things to people affected by natural disasters had two problems: it was slow and inconvenient to do, and if we were to donate, we're not sure of where the money goes.

What it does

The app links you to Amazon, where you personally buy stuff that the disaster victims need, and those things are directly sent to non-profit organizations that would give them to those that need them.

How we built it

It was built in Unity using C# with AWS, Facebook Login, and Cloudant DB

Challenges we ran into

Integrating AWS, Facebook Login, and Cloudant DB into the app

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Be able to integrate AWS, Facebook Login, and Cloudant DB into the app

What we learned

How to work with a team, how to use the technologies above, and how to work in this fun and hectic environment

What's next for Matrs

Add more social media platforms for sharing users donation, and more E-commerce sites for making users easy to donate
