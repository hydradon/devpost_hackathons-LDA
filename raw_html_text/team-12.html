Inspiration

Help the " Food for hunger" organization to automate and ease the process of signing , maintaining, analyzing their data.

What it does

Automates the otherwise required manual entry of Volunteer Registration, maintain, analyse and visualize the report management. In short it is report management and visual analytics tool.

How I built it

Android for Volunteer registration, PHP for Web Server Management, PostGress for DB Management, Apache for hosting Web Server.

Challenges I ran into

No digital information and hence had to retrieve data from manual paperwork.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Visual Analytics, Automation into the DB, Information Retrieval

What I learned

PHP, PostGress, XAMMP, Interacting with People, Team Atmosphere
