Many people nowadays spend money they don't have, maxing their credit cards, lowering their credit score, and causing an overall unhappy and financially poorly managed lifestyle. What our application accomplishes is a way to manage all of a specific individuals funds, labeling amounts of money they should be spending per month on necessities and leaving room for wants. If you have never written a budget for yourself before, this app is for you.
