Inspiration

Choosing what to do when we have a limited time is a challenge for most. We created What's Next to help those who feel overwhelmed by their tasks and can not decide what to do next.

What it does

The main goal of the app is to be a simple and quick user experience. After opening the app, the user can enter the current time they have on hands, and the app will return a random task the user previously put, that matches the time frame.

How we built it

We built the app using React-Native, which allows it to be compatible with both Android and iOS without having to do cross-platform developing.

Challenges we ran into

None of the team members were proficient in react-native, in fact, most of us have never heard of it. So it was overall a challenging experience getting the wheel rolling.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We accomplished to have a presentable application, that we can improve later on. We also are really excited to use the finished app ourselves, so it's a good motivation to work on its completion

What we learned

We learned how react-native works, along with all its dependencies.

What's next for What's Next

Complete our What's Next app and publish it to public!
