Inspiration

There are several "apps" that help us to track food or workouts. However, there isn’t one that focuses on improving the overall engagement between Clients and Trainers. All these "apps" are independent and are not well integrated to benefit both Trainers and Clients.

What it does

At WorkoutQs, our mission is to become the de-facto platform for Personal Trainers. We are focusing on reducing the gaps in trainer-client engagement, thereby helping Trainers and Clients reach their goals.

Trainers can focus on creating workouts and we will use technology to improve client engagement.

Clients will have access to workouts from a global pool of Trainers and they can engage on workouts with various technology choices.

We are already live on Web and in Alexa as a Feed. We are currently working to provide the same through Google Home, VR, etc.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Our facebook and google marketing had a positive response with people signing up for our services.

What I learned

Marketing and engagement related metrics are an important aspect of measuring success.

What's next for Workout Queues

We are in beta stage and are going to focus on more integrations to drive client engagement.
