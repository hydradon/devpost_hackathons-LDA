Inspiration

We've faced the struggle of going grocery shopping without knowing what's in our kitchen already.

What it does

It tracks the food in your storage places in your kitchen.

How we built it

We used Xcode and used multiple youtube videos and websites to learn about how to use Xcode.

Challenges we ran into

Making buttons turn into images was quite difficult, so we had to find alternate solutions to doing so.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Making it work in the end.

What we learned

How to use Xcode and Swift.

What's next for FoodTrack

We want to add a database and find an efficient way tot turn buttons into images.
