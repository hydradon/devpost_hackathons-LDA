Inspiration

Gamechanger's charity and the need for more donations to help kids around the world

What it does

Opens the flood gates for the donations, bring them on....

How we built it

All on AWS, cloudformation template, lambda, sqs, serverless, and used Jmeter for performance/stress test

Challenges we ran into

Getting their codebase working to do an integrated test

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We have a working prototype, and stress test to go with it, this can be easily turned into a product. We have a dashboard to with the prototype as well.

What we learned

A ton, collaborating on cloud projects, learned a ton from the other participants

What's next for Donation Floodgate

Productize, enhance, gamify and just open the doors for not just Gamechanger but all NPO's out there
