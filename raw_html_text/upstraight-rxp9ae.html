The idea came out as a necessity for us in the first place. We are a team of computer enthusiasts and thus the majority of our time is spent sitting, very often with a bad posture.We were told sports might be the solution, however doing exercises wrong might be even worse.

This is where we come in: UpStraight is a smart portable suite of sensors, web and mobile apps that track your back posture both in the gym and daily life. Moreover based on the data collected, predictions are made on recommended exercises and potential future affections (eg: scoliosis)

The solutions combines multiple programming languages(Java, Python, Javascript, C), cloud computing (Azure), and flex sensors together, into an architecturally wise, scalable suite of applications.

We are still in the development phase, thus any feedback would be highly valuable. Don't hesitate and meet our team at the table #28.
