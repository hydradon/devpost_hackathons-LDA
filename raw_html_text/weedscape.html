Inspiration

With the recreational Cannabis industry becoming legal soon, there is going to be a large influx of demand coming from consumers. Along with that, there will be many workers needed to be hired on and they must be trained on knowing how to grow and maximize yield from the crop.

What it does

Shows workers how to properly care for cannabis plant to maximize yields.

How I built it

Built with Unity and ARKit

Challenges I ran into

Finding and creating usable 3D assets

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Made it interactive
