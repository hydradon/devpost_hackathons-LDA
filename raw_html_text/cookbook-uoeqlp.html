Inspiration

Every summer, I become passionate about cooking, however it would always come to me in spurs or on the fly, so often times I would only have 20 minutes to cook something, or would be lacking in a lot of food ingredients. It would be so difficult to find recipes for a limited amount of ingredients, and surfing the web or looking through books for recipes eats away at your time analyzing every recipe. So, being able to quickly search up recipes based on specific criteria became the basis for our project.

What it does

CookBook allows you to save your favorite recipes to your phone! It stores ingredients, prep time, directions, even serving size!

How we built it

We used Android Studio to make this.

Challenges we ran into

Databasing, api's, first hackathon for some, inexperience

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Getting so much to work in a short time.

What we learned

Android studio, using api's, gradle, xml
