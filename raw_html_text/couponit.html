CouponIt is inspired by the problem of many people not taking advantage of coupons. We want to make it as easy to take advantage of coupons as possible. We intend for a customer to walk into a retail store, see the location of the best deals that are personalized specific to buying preferences, and buy the product. https://docs.google.com/a/berkeley.edu/presentation/d/1DlAByPcKIXqnh2vdTlsyX2HRziqYeANa3ZO8rlcTGHw/edit?usp=sharing
