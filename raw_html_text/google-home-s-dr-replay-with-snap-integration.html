Inspiration

In deliveration for building a skill for the Google Home with practical day to day application, we settled with this design for the platform.

What it does

It can record with a buffer space of 15 minutes, the last conversations that happened around the device.

How we built it

Utilizing the platform and a raspberry pi, we decided to integrate the two to provide an asynchronous integration of the replay feature outside the Google home device. The Google home provides a REST integration.

Challenges we ran into

Many, including utilizing an appropriate microphone for the raspberry pi.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We managed to create a test Google home skill and also more about team building skills.

What we learned

REST, Google home API, snap sdk integration, project design.

What's next for Google Home's Dr Replay with Snap integration full integration with the platform.
