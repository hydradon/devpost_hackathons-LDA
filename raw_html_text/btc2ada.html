Overview

BTC2ADA is the first swap of its kind.

It allows anyone to easily send a bitcoin lightning payment and receive ADA.

Why is this important?

Cardano is a 3rd generation blockchain that is tackling difficult problems such as scaling and consensus. The bitcoin lighting network is tacking the scaling problem by having off-chain transactions and on-chain settlement.

BTC2ADA provides an easy way to get ADA and have inbound liquidity on the lighting network.
