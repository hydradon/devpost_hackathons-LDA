Inspiration

It's probably not the most talked-about topic, but people spend half the movie wondering where they've seen the actor. and If you're really committed to finding it out, it'd would take you no less than 3-4 steps to figure it out. That's where the inspiration to build this app engendered.

How it works

You just have to upload the picture of the actor and our service under-the-hood performs some kickass face recognition, figures out who that actor is and gives you some of their most famous movies. Pretty simple, huh!

What's next for MovieRazzi

We're definitely going to grow our face recognition data to allow for more accurate data sets. We are also going to work on a natural language processing search and voice search that recognizes dialogues!
