Inspiration

There are plenty of apps that allow you to track your period and remind you to take birth control, but they could always be improved.

We came up with the idea of an app made for the benefit of women -- one that will allow you to track your period, remind you to take your birth control and reward you when you do so.

Beyond that, we wanted the app to include sexual education resources, and allow you to connect with nearby health centers.

We aimed to develop a user friendly and intuitive app.

What it does

Birth Control Reminder + timer Period Tracker Find a Doctor WebMD Settings Redeem Points

How we built it

Build in Xcode Coded in Swift Images and logos made in Photoshop

Challenges we ran into

We came in as complete beginners and taught ourselves app development from the ground up.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of the minimalistic and simplistic UI and actually coming up with an app even though we're complete beginners

What we learned

We learned how to make iOS applications. We learned Swift and how to navigate Xcode.

What's next for Period.

We hope to develop it further with better functionality and even better design. We hope to add more customization options such as color preferences and giving the user the ability to tailor alerts to their wishes. We also want to make it compatible with Apple Watches.
