Nonogram

SBUHacks 2018 Project

This is a nonogram game (also known as Picross). It is a picture logic puzzle where you must color cells in a grid according to the hints on the top and the left of the puzzle. The numbers mean how many cells will be colored consecutively in groups, separated by unknown number of spaces. You can play random puzzle or create your own by uploading a picture on your choice. Have fun!
