Inspiration

Our inspiration came from our past experiences with morally conflicting situtations. We want to make it easier for people to provide reasons for being late or backing out of upcoming events without having to think too hard.

What it does

Our app customizes reasonable excuses for users to give.

How we built it

We constructed our app in Java and android studio.

Challenges we ran into

Using android studio was challenging and slightly daunting because we have never used it before. We also ran into some difficulty creating a domain for our app.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud that we created a website, got our app to work, and met our goals.

What we learned

We learned how to make an app and put it on the market. More importantly we learned that strange ideas can bring into fruition of feasible apps.

What's next for Gotta Blast!

Next, is to continue adding on to our database and creating a sleeker user interface. Over all we wish to provide a more entertaining and lighthearted approach to conflicting situations.
