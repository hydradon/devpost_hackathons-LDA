Inspiration

Nowadays, music is an indispensable part of our lives. Music comes in many categories and genres, and it is much more convenient to have something to make song suggestions in terms of categories, and even your moods.

What it does

This is an user-friendly website, which suggests songs relating to user's inputs (purposes, feelings and music genres).

How we built it

We built it with enormous effort in researching many websites. Programming languages involved are html, javascript, css and even python :'D

Challenges we ran into

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We almost perfectly finish our idea!!!

What we learned

This hackathon will be one of the most unforgettable experience of us since we have learned a lot about code as well as how to working with each other as a whole group.
