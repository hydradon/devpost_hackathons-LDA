Kingdom Line Faith and Service Hotline

This app supports a website and a twilio-api based hotline. The goal for this project is for this app to serve those who seek advice, be it advice on faith, family, substance abuse, stress, or relationships.

The website has a homepage with information about the organization and the app. There are also pages for Coaches and Donors to sign up on. Coaches get added to an admin approval page immediately upon signing up. Admins can then go in and approve coaches. Upon approval, candidates immediately get added into the queue. Donors can sign up with their payment information.

Users call the phone number, which immediately rings all available Coaches.

Use

-Call (773) 985-3318 to ring available coaches on the hotline
