Busmate

Travelling often means exploring new routes and places. In a bus this often means you have to track your location on their phone to not miss your stop. But with Busmate there's no need for any mobile apps; all the useful information about the bus trip can be found from the screen located in the front of the bus.

Busmate is an application for the travellers in the bus. It shows important information about their trip and the area surrounding them. For example, it shows current speed, upcoming bus stops, nearby upcoming and occurring events and any landmarks worth mentioning.

The application view itself is a real time map, which helps passengers have a pleasant and interactive journey.
