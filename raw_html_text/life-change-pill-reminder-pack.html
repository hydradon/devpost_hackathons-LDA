This is our life change pill reminder pack.

It's made for patients who are diagnosed with a new condition when they newly have to start taking daily medications. It can hold up to four different pill bottles. It is unique to current technology because it utilizes typical pill bottles and would not disrupt pharmacy workflow.

It uses TakkTile sensors to know if the medication is taken.

It will remind the patient to take their medication by turning on a light and alert their caregiver if they don’t take their medication within the hour.

Built With

  • takktile-sensors
