Inspiration

After learning about how freedom of speech is limited for many people, We decided to build a chat app that circumvents government censorship.

What it does

It uses blockchain and cryptosystems to send and verify messages on a decentralize peer to peer network.

How we built it

We used the bitcoin public/private key cryptosystem, and the blockchain for the message ledger.

Challenges we ran into

Understanding network layers and the blockchain.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We managed to learn about tons of new stuff.

What we learned

How blockchain works, how cryptography and public/private key systems work.

What's next for Nector

Fixing the flaws.
