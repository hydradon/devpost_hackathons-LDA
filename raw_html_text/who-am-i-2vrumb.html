Who am I?

To play "Who am I?" Alexa needs to know the player's age to set the difficulty of the riddles accordingly. Then, Alexa will pretend to be someone (or something) and give a hint about herself. The player tries to guess who she is or can get another hint. This goes on until it has been discovered who she is (or no more hints are available).

Currently, the following age groups are distinguished:

  • 0-10 years (animals and objects)
  • 11-19 years (characters and celebrities)
  • 20 years and older (historical characters and celebrities)
