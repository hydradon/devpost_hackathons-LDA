Inspiration

  • Only 42% of bacteria are correctly identified.
  • 60% of antibiotics are incorrectly prescribed.

What it does

Performs a faster, more accurate pathogen diagnosis for severely infected patients, in order to provide proper antibiotic treatment

How it works

  • Utilizes new technology to provide a cheaper, more accessible, faster treatment
  • Leverages robust bacterial characterization macroscopically towards more efficient allocation of medical funds and more precise epidemiological insights for more effective international epidemic response
