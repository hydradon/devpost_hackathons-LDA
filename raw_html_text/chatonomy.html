Our inspiration is the lack of wanting to give numbers to people we may never see again, but have to communicate with to get things like school projects done.

Our application allows groups to send anonymous messages together without requiring the need of exchanging phone numbers.

We built it by integrating Node.js, Twilio, Javascript, and Google Cloud together into our project.

Integrating the APIs into our project and getting the networking set up to gain the ability to distribute messages across our test number were two of our toughest challenges.

We accomplished a flexible structure for our project that we will easily improve upon later.

We went _ very _ far out of our comfort zone and learned a new language, programming style, platform, and environment.

We are going to improve upon our code and continuously add more functionality to the project in the future.
