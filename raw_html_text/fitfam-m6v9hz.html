Inspiration

A Tinder-like app which helps make friends and help meet people with similar interests and schedules so they never have to work alone.

What it does

Allows people to swipe on fellow students at their college with similar interests and schedules so they can make a workout fam!

Challenges we ran into

None of us have ever used Android Studio! None of us had experience with databases or FireBase.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Having an app even created that swipes! The database is connected!
