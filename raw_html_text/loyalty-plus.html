We are an interlinked loyalty network packed with built-in smart contract features for dividends, rewards and experience.  Loyalty users can add, spend, receive or exchange points with other loyalty point retailers without ever knowing transactions are happening on the blockchain. We are leveling the point playing field. When users share their information, they will have more choice and flexibility in how the use they get rewarded and use their points. For companies, our blockchain and smart contract cut the costs of running existing programs. With our opt-in program features, companies can launch redemption programs and reduce points liability on balance sheet.  Our opt-in program features also achieve program satisfaction by shifting focus on “Experience” drivers not “Monetary” drivers.
