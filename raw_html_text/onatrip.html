Inspiration

Do any of these sound familiar?

  • "I want to get boba, and then go to a park to drink it, then go to dinner."
  • "I want to find a restaurant with a bar nearby"
  • "I want to find sushi places within walking distance of a karaoke place"
  • "I want to find a grocery store near a coffee shop that I can easily get to on public transport"

How would you currently solve these problems on Google Maps? If you were in a new city, it would probably take several minutes of viewing the different maps and trying to figure out visually where would be best. We built a better way!

What it does

Allows you to perform more freeform queries on maps. (Such as any of the above problems!)
