Inspiration

I love the way technology and education have combined to revolutionize learning and I am eager to help children learn new skills anywhere and anyway they'd like!

What it does

Jr. Wallet teaches children 8-12 smart financial decision making through an interactive game that models real life situations. Players are encouraged to leverage time, money and satisfaction to find the most optimal decision in each situation.

How I built it

I built it using Xcode and Swift.

Challenges I ran into, Accomplishments that I'm proud of, What I learned

I learnt Swift and XCode starting Saturday and it has been an amazing experience that has only motivated me to continue building on Jr. Wallet and creating new apps.

What's next for Jr. Wallet

An updated UI, new levels and a timer to make levels more competitive.
