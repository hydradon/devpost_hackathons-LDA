Swap

We wanted to make the process of exchanging business cards easier for professionals. We knew that there needed to be a more seamless way of collecting business cards and following up with people we meet in real life. So we created Connect, an RFID business card connected to the cloud. This enables you to do two things:

  1. Personalize your own digital business card
  2. Store all your contacts business cards with easy access to their information during follow-up

How we built it

We built it using Android, Android Studio, NFC Tags
