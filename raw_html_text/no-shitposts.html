Inspiration

Increase in toxic posts on facebook over the past week

What it does

It replaces toxic posts on your facebook feed with cute images related to the topic of the post

How we built it

We used the Azure Cognitive services to analyze the emotions and topics of the facebook post text and for any posts that fall below a threshold we replace them with cute images.
