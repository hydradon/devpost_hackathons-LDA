Inspiration

Solving the problems of trying to find street parking.

What it does

This app displays the parking spaces available on the street that you want.

How we built it

We used XCode to build the iOS app and node and express to build the api server.

Challenges we ran into

We ran into some threading issues that caused our app to lag, we also had difficulty creating the size of the app and making the pins correspond to different colors.
