Inspiration

When I was at Geisel library I wanted to study cse100 with someone. I didn't know anyone who was also studying cse100. I decided to change this problem for students forever.

What it does

App has two features. This first feature lets you find people with similar interests within a one mile radius. This allows you to quickly meet people and make new friends spontaneously. The second feature allows you to create and find events within a two mile radius. If you and your friends are looking for something to do right now, find an event closeby and go there.

How we built it

Our current progress is laying out the design. Over the next few weeks, we will be developing it on the iOS and Android platform.

Challenges we ran into

Coming up with a simplistic yet functional design.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Coming up with a way of seamlessly forging new bonds.

What we learned

The amount of people an individual meets is finite. We owe it to ourselves to have a circle of friends who connect with us deeply.
