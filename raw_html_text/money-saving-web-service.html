Inspiration

We see a lot of people who don't know how they can or should save money for some big specific goals that they have and thought there should be a way to help connect the people who have experiences with those who don't

What it does

Right now we only have an unfinished interactive website but if we can developed it, it would have a system where the user can choose how they want to save money, by choosing to plan out their saving step on their own or they can look for a varieties of suggestions taken from the users database about how other people have saved for their own goals
