* Description: *

Have you ever been stuck on what to eat and need somewhere close by and fast? eatRNG is your solution! Using the power of the Google Cloud Platform, we access close by food locations with your filters, displays them and choose one at random. With this technology at your hands, never get stuck on where to eat again!

* Problems we faced: *

One of our team member's computer died for half the hackathon until it updated itself. :(
