Inspiration

Students are normally afraid to ask for help from teachers, and even still, the information they receive is confusing.

What it does

It connects students and student tutors, so students can better understand concepts in school.

How we built it

We used Google's DialogFlow conversational AI platform, and Sublime Text Editor for programming a HTML file.

Challenges we ran into

The conversational AI is obviously not perfect, so it was difficult to get it to work effectively.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We successfully made a chatbot AI, and embedded it into our webpage.

What we learned

We learned the intricacies of conversational AI, and how to get it to work efficiently.

What's next for Stutor: Student Tutor Platform

Hopefully, we will extend it beyond the current functionality, and create a more effect chatbot AI.
