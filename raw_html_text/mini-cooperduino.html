Inspiration

Free time and a dream.

How it works

Gutted a mini Mini-Cooper RC car and replaced it with love but more importantly a bluetooth adapter and Arduino board. Now we use our phone to control it with the gyroscope as if it were a steering wheel.

Challenges I ran into

Soldering the board and soldering it outside against the elements.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It works! And has three user modes to choose form which function super duper well. You are going like the way it runs, I guarantee it.

What I learned

We learned how to connect to bluetooth and not sleep.

What's next for Mini Cooperduino

A mini helicopterduino.
