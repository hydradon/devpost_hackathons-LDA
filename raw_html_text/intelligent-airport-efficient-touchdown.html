Inspiration :Exploring the unexplored Predix Platform and solving real world problems.

What it does: Calculates and Reports impact parameters based on key issues responsible for Turn around/go around problem faced during airplane landing.

How I built it : By leveraging Predix Platform ,reusing predix services and apps to build INTELLIGENT AIRPORT :EFFICIENT TOUCHDOWN reporting tool.

Challenges I ran into : All being new to Predix,more theoretical and less practical experience gave us some challenges to come up with the end result.

What I learned

Predix Platform , Predix Apps, Predix Services, Postgres , Risk taking and better team collaboration.
