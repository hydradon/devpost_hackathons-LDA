Inspiration

Ever heard the old adage: if you don’t kiss your homies goodnight, are you even homies? But what happens if they’re in another city, or state, or even another country? How are you supposed to kiss them goodnight then?

What it does

Kiss Your Homies allows you to enter a phone number and send them a kiss goodnight via text no matter the distance between you.

How I built it

Built with Android Studios.
