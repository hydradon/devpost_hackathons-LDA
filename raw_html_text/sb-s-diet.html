Inspiration

In the world of technology, people have forgotten their daily diet due to many accesses to technologies. However, we, SB's diet, designed a game that brings awareness of people's food choice and diets.

What it does

We made a game that helps people to balance their daily calories intake.

How we built it

Through the use of Java, awt, javafx, we created an algorithm of calculating calories to help people balance their weights.

Challenges we ran into

We have trouble sharing resources and time management since this is our first hackathon. However, we all learned from experiences and we know that team cooperation is most important in the Hackathon.

Accomplishments that we're proud

We finished the game on time and we proud of our whole team work process.

What we learned

We learned how to work in team, resepct each other's idea, and follow each step to accomplish the same goal. We also learned how to acquire skills within limited time.

What's next for SB's Diet

We will implement more features if we have more time. We will also incorporate animations in the future to make the ui look better.
