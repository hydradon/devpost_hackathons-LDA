PiggyHack

This app aims to be the Uber for piggy back rides, allowing people to request piggy back rides to and from places. This app will revolutionalize the way people think about transportation. Via this app, those without the ability to run their cars in the ground for extra cash can now use their bodies to not only whip themselves into shape, but also earn extra money in their free time piggy backing people around the world!

  • Using React Native, we built a web app from the ground up
  • We integrated the Giphy, Twilio, and Google Maps APIs into our program
    • We used Giphy to produce GIFs related to piggy-back riding
    • We used Twilio to notify people when their ride is arriving
    • We used Google Maps to show people where they are and request travel destinations

So don't get carried away, but it's no pig deal. It won't break your piggy bank ;)
