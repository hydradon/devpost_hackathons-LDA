Inspiration

We were talking about friends' chats and came up with this idea.

What it does

Evaluate the emotional level of you or your friends based on your chat history.

How we built it

Used flask & Microsoft API.

Challenges we ran into

Flask front-end back-end communication. Front-end data processing.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

A Minimum Viable Product finished in 10 hours.

What we learned

We learned how to use flask and how to help people with an unstable emotional state who is unaware of that.

What's next for Emotional IQ

Improved functionality, improved UI. separate frontend and backend.
