Inspiration

We were inspired by how overwhelming the presence of oppression is in our current time. We realized how we could make an impact if we helped teens become more aware of not only the bigger oppression's that the news covers but the "micro-oppression's" that everyday people go through but aren't deemed as important

What it does

It allows you to access a website that connects you to other people like you and gives you a broad diverse experience, We also have alongside it a phone app, We find that if you have a personalized experienced it gives it more meaning. We also believed that if it could be taken anywhere you were then it would be great

How we built it

For the website we utilized Bootstrap, Notepad++ a script editor to do all of our Html5,CSS, and Javascript.

Challenges we ran into

When we began to approach the creation we had a difficult time figuring out how to implement the Api's into our idea. Also getting one of the main features of our app to work

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We successfully completed both of our objectives as a team with minimal arguments and fluent work and a lot of fun.
