Inspiration

Patients don't have easy access to their own data in. Every time they go to a pharmacy, their prescriptions have to be electronically sent to the pharmacy, before they can go there.

What it does

It stores the patients' information on a blockchain such that it is accessible only by individuals authorized by the patient themselves with the help of a QR code. Every time they go to a pharmacy, they have to create a QR code.

How I built it

I first had to build my own blockchain where I could store the patients' data, and then I had to create an app that could read a patients' QR code.

Challenges I ran into

Creating a blockchain was the hardest challenge as this was my first-time experience working with blockchain technology.

What I learned

I learned a lot about the blockchain technology and how useful it can be in the field of healthcare
