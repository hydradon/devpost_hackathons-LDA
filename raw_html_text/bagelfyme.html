Inspiration

Team member received a "free daily bagel" award from Panera and struggled daily in deciding which bagel to eat that day.

What it does

The app takes carefully researched questions to determine the optimal bagel-human connection.

How we built it

We built the app entirely through Android Studio

Challenges we ran into

Both team members were fairly new to Android app development, so just getting things to work in general was a challenge.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are very proud that, firstly, it runs. But on a more serious note, the concept of switching between activities was daunting at the beginning (due to prior experience at a different hackathon), but we made it work and celebrated accordingly

What we learned

We learned the importance of break statements and that app development wasn't as daunting as we originally believed. Also constraints suck. A lot.

What's next for BagelfyMe!

The addition of a blueberry bagel.
