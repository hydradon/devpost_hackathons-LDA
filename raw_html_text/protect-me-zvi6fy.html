Inspiration

This project aim is to help people who can't defend himself or they are too scared to even try to do it.

What it does

This application tries to approach people who suffers of bulling to the teachers or volunteers. They will try to help them using the different features of our app.

How we built it

We have used SCRUM methodologies. We also divided the project in three Sprints and 3 sections (FrontEnd, BackEnd and Mobile)

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We have learned a lot more about the technologies used in this project.
