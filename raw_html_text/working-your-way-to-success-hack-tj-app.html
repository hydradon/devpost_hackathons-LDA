Inspiration

As TJ students, we are often thought as a group of teenagers that are unfit, so we need to bring up the TJ reputation and promote healthy living in the TJ community.

The app gives users a pre-planned workout for each day of the month, based off of the preference of the difficulty level and duration of the workout of the user.

How I built it

To build our app, we used jgrasp, a java-based software to create the app

Challenges I ran into

One challenge we faced was creating a successful code in a limited timeframe

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The app is user-friendly and promotes healthy living for all ages

What I learned

What we learned was to make an app that is good for all and have successful communication skills with other team members. Also, we learned to keep the app successful.

What's next for Working Your Way to Success Hack TJ App

We want to launch the app in both android and iOS versions.
