Inspiration

Scrolling through my Instagram and was irritated by how many people upload a image of the food they having at a restaurant or at home. I wanted to create something that lets them know of the health pro and cons of the food they eating.

What it does

Application that helps users select healthy food recipes based on the ingredients in the grocery cabinet

How I built it

Used php and mysql to create this application. I used the source to image capabilities that the Openshift cloud service provides.

Challenges I ran into

Still being new to Openshift, I had trouble getting started and ran into quite a few issues that affected my progress. Linking databases in openshift to the web app. Technical assistance API's didn't work the way they should've.
