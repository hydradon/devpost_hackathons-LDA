The U of M is almost a small city onto itself. Navigating it as a new student can be incredibly daunting and challenging. However, someone is here to help. Enter - the Gopher. Gopher is a (WIP) Android and iOS app that allows you to look up, see information about, and organize your classes at any campus, and we eventually hope to add bus routes and walking directions for each of your classes.
