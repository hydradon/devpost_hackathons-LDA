Inspiration

Lots of solar projects are being installed, and there is no solution to operate them efficiently. This leads to costly uninformed operations.

How it works

In the SunHub marketplace, you will receive the best price bid for your solar project's operational issue. The platform will perform a cost-benefit analysis to determine when it makes sense to send a contractor to a solar project based on severity of the operational issues they are having (underproduction, soiling, inverter downtime, arc faults, etc.) and contractor bid pricing. We also pre-qualify contractors that bid into the SunHub marketplace so you will receive quality service.
