Inspiration

If you're anything like me, you're easily distracted. You fidget, you look everywhere, and your fingers are sore from the constant twitching. Well, here comes the solution: the Distraction Solver 3000, a tool with uses limited only by its battery life.

What it does

The DS3000 has 4 distinct modes. Upon startup, all LEDs will blink white, until a mode is selected by holding down the button with number matching your desired mode. To exit any mode, simply press all four buttons at once.

Modes:

1) Music: Each button plays a short note, that can be held down for a longer note or mixed with other notes for hours of creative fun.

2) Color: For a more soothing experience, this simply increments the color of the lights. 2 increases the red value, 3 the green, and 4 the blue. 1 resets the lights to white.

3) Game: For something a bit more challenging, the device will flash a certain color, and the user must press a corresponding button within 1 second. Again, 2 for red, 3 for green, and 4 for blue. The lights flash white if the user has lost.

4) Timer: This is a simple timer. Buttons 2 and 4 increment by 60 seconds, plus and minus respectively. Buttons 1 and 3 do the same in 1 second increments. Holding Buttons 1 and 3 simultaneously starts the timer, and it blinks blue when done. Note: Timer mode cannot be exited while the timer is in progress
