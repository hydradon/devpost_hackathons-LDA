WorstCase

Worst case is a app developed at Swamp Hacks 2020. Intended to help victims in disaster situations, the app provides relief reponse teams the ability to inform the affected people about provide aid, include supplies and location. The app also enables victims of such tragedies to locate this aid based on their current needs.
