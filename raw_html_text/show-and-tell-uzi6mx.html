We can say that for as long as we've been students, teachers still are restricted in their learning methods. We can see it in those that use remote controls, or those that use their whole class to move around and speak. We have been restricted to the spacebar that control the visuals of the lecture. Introducing hands free, voice activated presentation technology, Show and Tell. Using an IOS app as a microphone, Speech is converted to text, That text is parsed in our servers for commands such as "Begin" (Brings the presentation full screen), "Next" and "Previous" (Taking you through a journey of knowledge). This is all done through
Browser Automation. Honestly, we had no idea what we were going for, but we did grind to see our idea through. I learned most importantly how to learn in a team setting. Share the workload. Show and Tell will be discontinuing its services by next week. But we did have big plans like turning mathematical explanations into equations for people to understand in real time.
