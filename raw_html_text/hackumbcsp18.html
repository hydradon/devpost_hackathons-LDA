Health issues are often difficult to analyze on our own, we try to do our own research and may get carried away. We google a runny nose and the first thing we might see is a dangerous and rare health condition. I used sklearn's decision tree and used symptoms as the features list and the issue name ad the labels list

Rendering on mobile devices may not work and the ui may also be really bad, and the background image is not compressed which is another reason rendering may take so long

Enable the Flask api

python backend.py

Enable the React frontend

npm start
