Name: Markets Manifolds [WIP]

Leveraging depth in VR to enable visualizations of changing market trends through time.

Modern financial data visualizations typically come in two forms: time series visualizations and snapshot visualizations of trends. We built a data visualization tool that captures both simultaneously by leveraging VR to provide manifolds representing changes in financial relationship by extruding relationships through time/depth.

The hope is this tool will enable more intuitive capture of information from large, complex data sets.

First take applications include extrusions on fixed income yield curves, futures yield curves, and moving average volatility vs moving average return curves
