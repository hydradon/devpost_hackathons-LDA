After scrapping our original idea to create a dating app for iPhone due to a first long night of very little progress on a giant project, our team decided to start over on day 2 of the Hackathon and create an Apple Watch. None of us had ever created an Apple Watch app before (though we all had experience with iOS apps), but we had an idea and a will to learn, and we were able to pull through!

Craving coffee? Coffeed shows a feed of the nearest by cafes on your Apple Watch to satisfy!
