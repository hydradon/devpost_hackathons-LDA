Inspiration

As our team loves to travel, we wanted to do something that could be helpful to us and others who love exploring as well.

What it does

Our app provides general packing guidelines based on the weather of your travel destination, the purpose of your travel, and how long your trip is.

How I built it

We built the web app using node.js and HTML, learning together as a team.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We're proud how our team rose to the challenge of quickly learning Javascript and node.

What I learned

We learned Javascript and node - this was a new language for some of our team members.

What's next for Packed

We'll add more specific location-aware features - like suggesting to pack flip flops if you're traveling close to a beach or coast.
