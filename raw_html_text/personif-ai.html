• Personif.ai Redesign - 2017

Inspiration

The mental state of patients in long term hospital stays or assisted living facilities tends to decrease with time due to lack of continued social interaction.

What it does

Personif.ai is an AI-driven health monitoring system that serves as a companion to patients and adds a social dimension to digital healthcare.

How we built it

A backend in Flask and Python with a front-end in HTML/CSS and Javascript comes together to create an avatar you can interact with.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We managed to take a chat bot to a whole new level by trying to solve a real issue that affects many people.
