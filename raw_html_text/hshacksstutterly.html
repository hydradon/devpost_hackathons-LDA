Inspiration

We were inspired to build this idea because as a child I(Sreehari) have suffered from stuttering and I haven't found an app on the market that helps this situation.

What it does

It helps to decrease stuttering through repetition and aids students with the knowledge of what words and sentences they have trouble pronouncing. It uses google speech API and charts to give students a comprehensive list of words they struggle with.

How we built it

We built it in android studio and xml.

Challenges we ran into

Configuring the Google Speech API to track stutters. We also had trouble creating the algorithm to determine stuttering. Furthermore, we had lots of challenges with implementing a Navigation Drawer in our app for a great UI. Firebase was a hard topic as well.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Successfully able to solve a real-world problem with technology, something we're passionate about.

What we learned

We learned how to import libraries, use Firebase API's, the Material design spec, and use the Google Speech to Text API.
