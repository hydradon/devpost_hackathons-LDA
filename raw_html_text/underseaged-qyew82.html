• Maisha and Kifo(Life and Death)

The year is 3232 AD, life as we know it has changed and the entire world has been plunged into an eternal darkness. In an underwater post-apocalyptic era where only the sea peeps have emerged, all is lost, technologies from the past have been forgotten and the only thing that serves as solace is the hope of finding the Lost Artifacts to restore power to the Sea peeps village. As endless wars raged over differences in culture, an evil tyrant crab ruler has taken over the village and claimed the sea for his own. Two warriors have risen to the occasion, Maisha and Kifu, part of the legendary Sea Peeps now must undo what has been done and help recover the artifacts for this civilization to be no longer UNDERSEAGED
