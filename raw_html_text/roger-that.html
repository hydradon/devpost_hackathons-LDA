Inspiration

Based on our personal experiences and the desire to improve the daily life of every one.

What it does

RogerThat! is an app that allows users to independently install and maintain their smart devices through the assistance of image recognition technology. Image recognition allows us to identify different/complicated components that are foreign to the non-technical user, and to tell them in easy and friendly terms the different steps required for installation/maintenance. We believe that RogerThat!'s use case is not only applicable to the installation and maintenance of Wi-Fi routers, but also Rogers' TV and home phones.
