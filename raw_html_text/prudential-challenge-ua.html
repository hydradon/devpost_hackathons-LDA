Inspiration

With our knowledge in full-stack development skills, we believed we could properly showcase our skills in the Prudential insurance application.

What it does

It takes users voice and transcribes into a form that is stored in a real time database.

How we built it

We used Google's Firebase for our backend and MaterializeCSS for our Front-End.

Challenges we ran into

Backend was really difficult to configure.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We got the front-end and back-end connected seamlessly. The real time database works as a charm.

What we learned

How to use Firebase API and OAuth with material design.

What's next for Prudential Challenge - UA

Adding support to scan documents and convert to text.
