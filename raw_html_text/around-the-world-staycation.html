Inspiration

Museum software to allow users to explore far away places in 3D Virtual Reality which they would be unable to see otherwise.

What it does

We have constructed a VR 3D environment within Unity utilizing Oculus Rift

How we built it

We built this project using Unity and C#

Challenges we ran into

No one in our group had ever had previous experience with Unity, C#, or VR technology before this Hackathon event.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are extremely proud that we were able to put forth a project without any prior experience in these tools!

What we learned

We learned fundamentals of VR development and gained substantial experience in Unity.

What's next for Around the World Staycation

We hope to present our project at the end of the Hackathon. We hope to do more 3D modeling in Unity in Virtual Reality in the future!
