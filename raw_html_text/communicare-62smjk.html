Inspiration

We drew inspiration from the North Dakota Access Pipeline protests in Standing Rock. We recognize that many relief organizations have trouble coordinating between themselves, and could benefit with a visual tool showing where their resources would best be put to use.

What it does

Our website allows relief organizations to keep track of their own supplies and locations while simultaneously view other organizations in the area.

How we built it

We built a web app on heroku that integrated google maps and pulled data from mongodb on ec2.
