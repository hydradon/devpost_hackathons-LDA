Inspiration

One of the teammate's family member was diagnosed with Alzheimer last year.

What it does

ElderAlly is a companion app to help elders with Alzheimer’s never miss a thing. When signing up, family members can add reminders to help their relative who may have forgotten to take their medication, doctor’s appointment etc. Memories can also be uploaded as trivia questions to help an elder to revisit his most memorable life events.
