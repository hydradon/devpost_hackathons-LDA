Inspiration

We wanted to make something cool with leap motion so we made this workout demo. Its also a proof of concept to show that VR workout is possible.

What it does

You could punch a bag or you could lift dumbbells or kettle weights(or throw them for a high score!)

How we built it

We built it using the unity game engine, utilizing Oculus and Leap Motion SDK's to use and interact objects with unity.

Challenges we ran into

Interaction is buggy but we made it a more stable environment to get the job done

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We worked as a small team and finished on time.

What we learned

Learned how to use oculus and leap motion and integrated into unity.

What's next for Fitness VR DEMO alpha

If we get funded, we will work on integrating more equipment, integrating a HUD for more usable UI, making combat dummies and tracking health related factors like calories burnt, exercise duration, steps and heartbeat using smartwatches such as apple watch, fitbit etc. We also could add a self defense training course but we are not licensed yet.....
