Description

Make a Difference is a platform that helps people get justice, fight for their rights and start different initiatives for the social good.

Everyone can create initiative they are interested in. For example one person can initiate building a bend in front of their apartment or another can create an initiative to fight a Payments giant because they are holding people's fund without a reason. The initiatives can be of any kind with the purpose to do social good.

The Dapp is available on web, mobile devices and tablets. It is fully secured on the blockchain through the Blockstack platform. All data is protected and encrypted.
