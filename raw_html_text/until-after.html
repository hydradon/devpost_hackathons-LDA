Inspiration

I don't know about you, but personally, I have a lot of cool ideas and honestly just crazy thoughts that I think of but would never say out loud. After I pass away, I want to be remembered, i want my work to be remembered. So when I pass away, i would love to pass on a life journal of all my secrets/ideas/thoughts/files to someone close to me, because why not!

What it does

It's a web app that allows you to journal and upload files into your private account and when you pass away, your close chosen friend can activate your account.
