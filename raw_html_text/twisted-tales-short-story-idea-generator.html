Inspiration

We were inspired by personal struggles to come up with ideas for short stories. Writing short stories is a great way to exercise your writing skills and creative thinking, so we wanted to create something that could help spark that creativity.

What it does

Our website allows the user to choose between multiple different scenarios, characters, and settings to create a basic outline for a short story that they can then use to spark their own creativity.

How we built it

We used html, css, and jquery to create our website.

Challenges we ran into

We ran into the challenge of having a group of people with varying levels of skill regarding coding, however in the end our process allowed everyone in our group to grow and learn new skills, regardless of their original skill level.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of having a finished product that is very close to our original design. We met many snags along the way but we persevered and created something that we are proud of.

What we learned

Each of us learned various skills, however collectively we learned how to accommodate each other and collaborate despite our differing backgrounds.

What's next for Twisted Tales: Short Story Idea Generator

Next we want to create another page for poetry to continue creating ideas that spark creativity in writing.
