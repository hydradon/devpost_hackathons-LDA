Inspiration

In University of Toronto, mental health issue is one of the most serious issues. Many students are suffering by this issue. We, as a team of four University of Toronto ECE students, decided to create web application to help people with improving their mental health, or just helping them relax after stressful days.

What it does

Inspiration quotes, wellness journal, calm/meditation mode with soft colors and music.

How we built it

We built it as an Angular single-page web app.

Challenges we ran into

Merging branches and finding files on Github.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of making all the features work with Angular, and the Google Cloud OAuth with Firebase.

What we learned

We improved at creating webapps with Angular.

What's next for Steady

We may extend the journaling features and allow users to upload photos that make them happy.
