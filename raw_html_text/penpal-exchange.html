Inspiration

Thinking of the many kids who have trouble making new friends. Having a stronger social support network at school gives them more confidence in themselves and also are less likely to be a target for bullying.

What it does

Make new friends in a safe and low-stress environment.

How we built it

Microsoft Bot Frameworks

Challenges we ran into

Creating options such as Keyboards or Cards thru the Microsoft Bot Framework that would work on the Kik platform

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Kikbot is working on kik.

What we learned

40% of American teens use Kik. That is a very highly engage demographic and the best way to get them talking are with stories or options.
