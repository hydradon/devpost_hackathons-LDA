Inspiration

What it does

Extractive summarisation task. By utilising a neural network(BERT) and a clustering algorithm (k-means clustering), it highlights the important sentences in the transcript of a specific YouTube videos (which could be lectures). It can also cluster sentences with respect to Euclidean similarity. That is, it can show how sentences can be grouped in terms similarity in an unsupervised fashion,

How I built it

  1. extract a transcript from a YouTube video using youtube_data_v3_api
  2. pre-process the transcript
  3. feed the pre-processed transcript to the neural network (Google's BERT), which will then translate the sentences in the transcript into numerical vectors.
  4. cluster the vectors(sentences) by measuring the Euclidean similarity between the sentences
  5. pick the sentence that lies at the center of each cluster, and highlight it!
