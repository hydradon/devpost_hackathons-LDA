That's the Expression

Asperger Syndrome is a developmental disorder characterized by significant difficulties in social interaction and nonverbal communication. Our project is a learning application to help Asperger Syndrome patients by testing them in different situations. It is divided into two parts wherein the first part is for testing a patient's emotions with the random images, and in another part, we are taking one small quiz on fix interval during learning to check its response.
