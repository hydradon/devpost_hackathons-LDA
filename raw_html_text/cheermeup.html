Inspiration

Stress is part of our everyday lives. If stress is not managed properly it can negatively affect our mental and physical health. We know it’s not easy to get rid of stress so we created a tool for people to lower stress through the power of simple conversation. Our vision is to provide a better way for users to deal with stress so that they can live a healthy and productive lifestyle.

What it does

CheerMeUp is your virtual stress manager in a form of a google smart home app. Our tool uses google home to help users cope with stress through conversation with recommendation to help lower stress.

How we built it

We used Google Cloud Platform (GCP) to set up our project environment. Our Google smart home app was created using dialogflow on GCP.

Challenges we ran into

Our entire team was new to Google Cloud Platform. We had some difficulties connecting to NLP.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are extremely proud of learning a new technology and completing a functioning home app.

What we learned

As a team, we learned how to work with GCP, specifically intents and entities in Dialogflow. We also learned how to use dialog to design conversations.

What's next for CheerMeUp

As a team, we are looking forward to implementing Natural Learning Understanding (NLU) to be able to process a wide range of responses from the user.
