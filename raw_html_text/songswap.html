We initially discussed how often it is that we do things with friends: scheduling trips to the movies, bowling. We then came to a point where we discussed our music tastes. We often find that the sure-fire way to find music we're likely to enjoy came from word of mouth-- from people of similar interests; thus we conceived SongSwap.
