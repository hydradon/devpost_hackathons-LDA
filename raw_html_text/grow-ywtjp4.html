Inspiration

Taking Ap Psychology in High School we learnt about the ongoing mental struggles people all around the world go through today. We are deeply moved by their will-power and want to help them take a more positive outlook on their life. We recognize that it is difficult for people to express their feelings and emotions irl, or on a hotline and we believe this app can make it easily accessible for those who do not feel comfortable using a hotline.

What it does

An app that logs your daily statistic with a bunch of data to tailer the positive messages to the patent personally. Once the tree is fully grown, you can look back at the hard work you’ve done to get there, basically you are “growing”

How we built it

We used thunkable an online app builder.

Challenges we ran into

A lot of bug fixes in the thunkable platform we had to overcome.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We created an app that works within 24 hours that captures our main goal.

What's next for Grow

In the future, for every tree grown on the app there will be an actual tree planted, so it’s a win win situation
