Piggy Bank

Piggy Bank makes a small charge to your credit or debit card once every month or week depending on your preferences. You then keep a list of items that you would like to save up for and you decide how to split your money amongst the list.

Current Functionality
Create Account
Log In
Create Goals
Database Interaction

Things to Add
Sub Goals : Provide sub goals that need to be completed in order to save the money. If these goals are not met then the amount that would go to this savings goal is instead sent to either a charity, back to us, or a friend. These goals are user defined and could be anything. For example, you could set a goal to loose a certain amount of weight over a period of time, and, if you don't meet your goals, you won't save that money for that cycle.
Money Split : Currently the user is unable to define the money split
Tutorial : The user needs more direction their first time visiting the site.
Charging : Currently the app has not been set up to make charges to a credit card
Charge Interval : There needs to be an event that causes the charge to take place and the user to be credited.
