Problem Statement :

  • Our project aims to help middle school-aged child refugees to get education by bringing school to them.
  • It also aims to educate someone who wish to learn certain skills in the refugee camp.

Solution Overview:

  • A simple app to provide peer to peer learning by searching nearby refugees having those skills and willing to teach.
  • The key idea behind is: “Not everyone knows everything but everyone knows something”

Audience : Anybody who wants to learn but especially for girls who are often most vulnerable.

Why Unique? Requires minimal internet connection and ensure safety of girls
