ThoughtsAround_WomXnHacks

We believe that places around us inspire us. Every experience is an adventure and sharing it with others makes it even more special. Thoughts Around is a web application which encourages you to venture out into your surroundings and find things that excite you. You are able to upload your experience to the website while you're in the moment, right from your location, to encourage others around you to venture out too. If you're someone who is looking for motivation, you can jump to anywhere in the world to see the thoughts left behind there.
