So what does this do?

Resumémon takes in your resume and generates a Pokemon out of it. If you upload two resumes (yours and your friend's), you can have them battle each other! The stronger your resume, the stronger the Pokemon that's generated.

What's considered a "strong" resume then?

We use custom heuristics to determine how good a resume is.

But why?

Confident in your resume? Now you can 1v1 your friends and show them who really belongs in the Elite (Big?) Four.
