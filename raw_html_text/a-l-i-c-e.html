Inspiration

Countless accidents across the world

What it does

Allows a remotely controllable 4-wheeled robot to monitor environments safely without nearby human intervention by taking in various sensor data

How we built it

Arduino + Android + Cardboard

Challenges we ran into

Trying to use an Edison at first as well as the bluetooth connection to/from the Android.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Making bluetooth work and integrating the entire system

What we learned

Arduino in-depth materials

What's next for A.L.I.C.E.

Stronger casing, more sensors, and other improvements to the robot
