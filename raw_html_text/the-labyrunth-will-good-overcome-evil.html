Inspiration

I find the notion of having powers incredible, and I decided to make a game that has a unique combination of powers: gravity reversal, phasing through objects, and rewinding time.

What it does

It is a fun, enjoyable game for people who are looking for a challenge.

How I built it

I built it over 8 weeks through the MakeSchool summer academy using xcode, swift, and spritekit.

Challenges I ran into

I found coding time reversal to be extremely difficult, as there are numerous different things to reverse separately besides just position.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I'm proud of shipping my game to the App Store.

What I learned

I learned a lot about Swift, xcode, sprite-kit, and the App Store shipping process

What's next for The Labyrunth: Will good overcome Evil?

Implementing cosmetic upgrades is next for my game.
