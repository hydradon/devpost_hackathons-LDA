Inspiration

I was inspired by when i was playing group games at school with teachers.

What it does

Icebreakers explains group games for different group sizes. Shows 60 rebus puzzles along with their answers in individual and pdf formats. Includes a fully functional timer for use in group games.

How I built it

I built it with xcode and swift using the integrated tools along with some pods such as iconic (icons) and a pdf reader.

Challenges I ran into

I had difficulty in displaying the rebus puzzle pdf sheets in the app as well as integrating the group games sheets stored on a website.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I am proud of making the integrated timer and the group games menus.

What I learned

I learned how to make menus, timers and tabs in xcode while making Icebreakers.

What's next for Icebreakers

Icebreakers will be improved upon by adding more variations to the existing group games along with adding more group games/rebus puzzles.
