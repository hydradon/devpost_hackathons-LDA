Decisions, decisions, decisions. Where should we eat? Its a website where users can create dinner date groups with their friends. Each member of the group can enter in the distance they’re willing to travel to a restaurant, a price level, and the type of cuisine. The website will then display the best results based on the average distance, price level, and cuisine type! Indecisiveness no more.
