Inspiration

We were inspired by the quest for knowledge and our deep passion for natural language processing

What it does

Given an URL, GRO carefully deciphers the company homepages in order to extract useful information (company name, address, industry branch)

How we built it

We started with a word embedding approach and tried multiple deep networks as well as classical machine learning approaches

Challenges we ran into

Quality of the data acquisition, sleepless nights and strange keywords

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Trying out multiple approaches and successfully implementing a few
