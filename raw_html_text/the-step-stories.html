Welcome to the The Step Stories. I’m other word the El Paso Stories. We aim to foster a family environment through encouragement and community involvement.

Our first question was, what four things make up a family? Motivation (Quotes), Inspiration (Stories), Inclusion (events) and beauty (pictures) all this together creates love.

And that’s what this app represents love for and within the city.

Inspiration -

In times of need & resilience, we all want to belong to something. We want to embrace and take care of the city that we are a part of.

What it does -

The step stories allows you to spread love, engage, volunteer, and inspire your very own El Pasoans.

How I built it -

Finding a sense of purpose within our community & community engagement.

Challenges I ran into -

Losing Momentum & app saturation.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of -

To be able to create a community involvement opportunity.

What I learned -

Although belonging to a close community, there is still much room for interconnectedness.

What's next for The Step Stories -

Continue its market research & implement its service.
