How it works

You have a browser based application shows live status of the parking facilities near you. Choose a location you want to park your car and we'll book it for you. We estimate the time required for you to reach the parking lot and save your booking for that time.

On booking a slot, you'll receive a QR code that will be the authentication token. On reaching the parking lot, authenticate with the code and park your car. Isn't that simple!
