Inspiration

We want to spend a couple of minutes clearing your head at soothing places. It's good for the mind, especially given today's demanding careers in tech and startups. We would like to show spots of 'zen' to everyone, to enjoy and benefit from them. Breath in, breathe out, then back into the rush with a clear head.

How we built it

We used datasets of geographical data such as green and water surfaces, noise, and air quality and calculated opportune places of high overall hygiene.

Challenges we ran into

Transforming the datasets to match geographically was a challenge, e.g. projection issues. Some data sets were not dense enough (air quality, very few measurement stations)
