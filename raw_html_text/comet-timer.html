Problem

Many students are stressing out because they can't seem to manage their time efficiently. The stressful atmosphere created by the upcoming events and deadlines places pressure on the students and causes depression.

Solution

With Comet Timer, an extension and an android app, students are able to maximize their productivity with the 52-17 method, which is a research conducted by Stanford, concluding students are most efficient when they work for 52 minutes and rest for 17.

Features

Comet Timer uses this method of 52 minutes and 17 minutes by sending out notifications when the time is reached. It also has a built in To-Do list, which allows users to easily remember their activities throughout the day, and a page with a game to help the user relax.
