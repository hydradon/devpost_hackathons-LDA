Inspiration

I love chocolate. Everyone loves chocolate. Sometimes though we do not know what chocolate to eat, so we set out to make that easier.

What it does

Shows chocolate produced by many different companies around the world to easily see what your taste buds are craving.

How we built it

We used Android Studio (Java, XML) to build the app

Challenges we ran into

Being able to make different navigation menus and just learning Android in general

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Learning how to use Android

What we learned

Android

What's next for ChocolateNavigator
