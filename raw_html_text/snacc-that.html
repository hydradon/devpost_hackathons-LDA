Inspiration

you want food? you get food.

What it does

What the app does is essentially it allows you to spin a wheel of what kind of food stores you want to eat by filtering through the different food stores.

How I built it

We built is using Android Studio, Flask, and Node.js.

Challenges I ran into

We ran into some front-end problems with the Android Studio, and some problems with scrapping for the backend. Additionally, we ran into some problems with our words in Figma not curving.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It's simple and easy to use. We got most parts of our original design working.

What I learned

How to use Figma for photo editing and logo designing. Also, I learned aspects of ascetic design with Android Studio.

What's next for Snacc.that

Get support and build a bigger team to make a more well-supported app.
