Inspiration

Representation matters for all ages. We know from personal experience and through research that a lack of representation can be detrimental to minority children. Our app, Starring You, allows children to take charge of their own story.

What it does

Starring You allows users to create an account and customize a main character which is featured in a story of the users choice.
