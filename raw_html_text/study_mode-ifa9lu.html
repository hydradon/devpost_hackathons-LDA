study_mode

Our team found ourselves constantly struggling to find a straightforward way to form study groups and decided to create our own solution. While this application fulfilled our academic and social needs, it also allowed us to foster a new connections with others and expand the community of passionate learners on campus. This idea came about prior to HackTX, but we never found the time or space to actually work on it. We also had very different backgrounds and skillsets that inhibited any significant progress on our project. Through this hackathon, we were able to learn from each other and from open source projects to create a minimal viable product that we plan on further developing and testing in the future.
