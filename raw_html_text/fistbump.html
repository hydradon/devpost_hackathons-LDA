Inspiration

We liked the app Bump. While listening to spotify, we wanted to share a song with a friend by bumping their phone with ours.

What it does

FistBump asks which social media platforms a user wants to let others follow/friend them on. When two phones with FistBump open bump each other, they ping a server that sends the social media authorization info from each phone to the other.
