Inspiration

Talking to those involved in the educational community it seemed that there was just a missing link for low-income families trying to keep their children active and engaged even in their time off from school. Despite a large number of programs there was just no way of knowing about all of them.

How it works

Recess is a simple Android application that allows you to search for activities for your children near you, as well as see which ones are popular, and which ones are well regarded.
