• Education Calendar Feed

Alexa is now able to help with education priorities. Ask Alexa when is the next assignment due or what is the next class today.

We use great technologies such as amazon lambda, Alexa SDK, Google Calendar API and behind the scenes logics to connect all the dots and provide the great experience for users.

Alexa Education Feed is designed as a skill which is also published for free so people can use it in order to keep track of their education priorities.

Checkout our video demo where we ask Alexa with real data calendar and it provides accurate dates and events based on our education background.
