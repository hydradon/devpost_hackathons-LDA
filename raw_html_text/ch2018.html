Welcome to Strobe_Snake!

Strobe_Snake is a remix on the classic game of Snake! With the main difference being the flashing lights, score, and timer! Of course, we want everyone to have fun and enjoy our product, so we added a WARNING screen at the start AND the ability to play the game without flashing or strobing lights for those who are photosensitive. Please enjoy the game!
