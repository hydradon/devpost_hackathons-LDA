Inspiration

Construction delays have effected us all one way or another.

What it does

BuildAR uses GPS tags and Blueprint data to augment construction sites and provide guidelines for construction workers.

How I built it

This prototype was built with Vuforia and Unity for Android OS.

Challenges I ran into

Creating proper image overlay and finding assets was a big issue.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Time delayed augmented reality overlay on a marker.
