Inspiration

There are several betting companies that provide basic abilities to place bets on major eSports events. However there are not sites offering the user the ability to watch eSports, view player stats and place a bet before the event starts within a single page.

What it does

Combined Twitch.tv with William Hill APIs (signin, place bet, view odds) Allows you to place a bet on App, Website (directly or through twitch bot)

"WE BUILT THIS CITY ON ROCK AND ROLL"

Split development between Web app and iOS clients for front end and another working on node services for the backend. The other two members worked on UI, user journeys and pitch.
