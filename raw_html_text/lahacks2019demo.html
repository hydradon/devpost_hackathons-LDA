LAHACKS2019DEMO: LearnPic

Language Learning app in Android!

We decided to make an app where you can take a picture and the app translates all the objects in the picture for you into a different language. That way, if you forget the word for something in a different language, or you just wanted a new tool to assist you in learning a new language, you could use our app!

We decided to develop this for Android, as the back-end was nicer than swift (with Java). We also used xml for the front end, and we used 3 APIs: Google Translate API, Google Cloud Vision API, and the Android Camera API.

The toughest parts of this project were figuring out the APIs and merging our moving parts. Eventually, we managed to figure everything out and got a working product.
