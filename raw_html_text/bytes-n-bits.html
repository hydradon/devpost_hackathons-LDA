Inspiration

The theme of data privacy is a growing issue in our modern world, and we believe it is our duty to raise awareness for not only the more mature of audiences, but younger generations to come.

What it does

Informs, educates, and aids in the protection of our digital community regarding cyber security, through games, products, quizzes, forums, and education.

How I built it

Beginning from a fundamental idea about a educational website, we discussed the methods that would render it more interactive and memorable towards all audiences. Cyber security is a developing issue that affects the whole gamut of citizens throughout the global network.

Challenges I ran into

Just like any other computer science project, there were countless glitches and bugs that we encountered in the process of coding the website and its implementations.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We were proud of what we were able to accomplish given the limiting time period for coding.

What I learned

We learned how to manage time, incorporate different platforms to construct a multifaceted website that can be utilized in unique ways.

What's next for Bytes N' Bits

We hope to be able to continue this project, even after this hack-a-thon has ended, as it is a relevant and pressing issue that needs a solution.
