Under the guidance of assistant professor Ms. Jyotika Pruthi @The NorthCap University Gurgaon I developed an android app for road safety of pedestrians,cyclists and motorcyclists in Indore.

Anyone can find which roads are blocked for road repairs or public events. One can see what are the different traffic rules and how much is the fine for a particular offence. For any proposed changes in the traffic plan in your locality, one can send their opinions and suggestions online. One can contact numbers for senior traffic officials in case of emergency, report accidents and recieve quick police assistance.
