Activities such as payments and transactions through the internet have proliferated to an extent one could barely think about. This evidently is because of the involvement that the customers have put into such interesting implementations. One of such implementations happens to be a one-click digital payment method that has grown like a wildfire in a few foreign countries. Our objective is to make this mode of transactions much more simpler and secure, so as to make it a huge success in our own country. This method that we have come up with includes much more reliable techniques which helps one gain the trust of their customers. Simply put, we've chosen ECC methods of encryption over RSA and also have put forward a procedure that helps one switch their account to a secondary device from their primary device using One Time Passwords. This is our new initiative to help get away from spammers and identity hijackers. With strong support provided, we would like to annex a few other ideas to the same project, do our best to make it the safest and possible way for our fellow countrymen to use this technology efficiently. We'd also like to do deeper research on this subject for, knowledge is power. Thank you!
