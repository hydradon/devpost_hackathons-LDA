Inspiration

Ultima is our a.i. companion, that while capable of doing allot of things, it has never been used for Healthcare and on a Robot. So we saw this as an opportunity to innovate and solve a 4.4 billion dollar problem.

We coded from scratch our A.I. in 24 hours!!!!

Now it can diagnose someone who feels sick via speech and help them identify if they have a life threatening or a non-emergency problem.

In the case of an emergency the assistant can call an emergency contact or first responders via voice.

Today the average cost of a hospital visit is around $1,200 dollars, only 11% of Emergency room visits result in hospital admission, and 65% of issues taking people to the ER are considered serious, even with these numbers ER visits have increased 27% making Healthcare less affordable and more inaccessible for all of us.

What it does

Using the Autonomous bot platform, it speaks with the user via TTS-STT and allows our A.I. to access a massive medically validated database that will allow a user to perform a high level diagnosis before making that call or driving to the ER.

In case of a serious Emergency using NEXMO Ultima can call automatically Emergency contacts or first responders, without the users interaction allowing the user to concentrate on what matters the most, their health!
