Porject

By: Isabella Andrade (basically all the code), Jessica Fox (story), Andrea Griffin (presentation and some artwork), and Ashlyn Kim (some artwork).

Lovingly made by people who have no idea what they are doing.

To see the project live, click here https://Porject-1--boopmehnose.repl.co

The main difficulty was implementing what we wanted into the program with the syntax that twine provides. The formatting and commands were strange and hard to get used to, but we had adapted by the end of the day. There was a lot of deleting and restarting from scratch, especially since many of the approaches we took ended up failing. The final and current version is the one we am most satisfied with, and we believe it is exactly what we could have asked for.

Our art providers worked diligently throughout the entirety of the day, and we can thank them for their lovely contributions.

The author of the story behind this was a DnD master, so we owe our story to such experience.
