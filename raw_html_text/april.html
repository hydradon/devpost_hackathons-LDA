Inspiration

Everyone is going to be old one day, we would like to build something for elder people to benefit their life.

What it does

April empowers the elderly to lead a active, personable, reliable, independent lifestyle

How we built it

We build it with Alexa and computer vision, machine learning, deep learning

Challenges we ran into

Learning how to work with Alexa and doing the deep learning

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Camera detect when Tylor wakes up Remind Tylor to have the medication and make sure he has the right one Remind Tylor’s personal schedule for each day Help arrange visits to see doctors Ensure healthcare measurement. April will use a camera to watch medication taking habits, remind and ensure correct dosing Alexa will make conversation with people right after they wake up Cognitive questionnaire

What we learned

How to work with a team, how to code in Javascript, build skills with Alexa, doing machine learning and deep learning

What's next for April

April will record the medication taking situation and how they feel after that, send the information to the doctors for reference. Address Tylor’s emotional needs, connect friends to listen to each other and share words of encouragement can positively influence their morale
