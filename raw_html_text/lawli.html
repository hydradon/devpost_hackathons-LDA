Inspiration

Inspired by the hefty bills lawyers charge when they read, also by the clutters in news and blog articles.

Like many people, I don't like reading a long winded article that it repeats itself, when essential information can just be covered by a few sentences. I understand that's how writers get paid by word count, but too frequently it's a waste of readers' time.

You can even try Lawli app to demystify legal documents like the Mueller Report, just saying.

Summarize anything you want summary for by a push of button.
