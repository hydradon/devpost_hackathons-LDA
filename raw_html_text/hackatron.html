Hackatron

Hackatron is a bundle of software that will help any group throw a hackathon seamlessly and easily. Our suite of apps allows for full integration of third parties such as Slack, Google Calendar, Twitter, and Remind. Our applications together give easy access to any passionate group to handling during-event notifications, reminders, as well as mentor relation using a Slackbot!

Hackathons are meant for making an awesome event, not worrying about web design and logistics prior to the event Let hackatront help you concerntrate on the important things: the culture, the people, and the passion
