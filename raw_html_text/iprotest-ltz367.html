It's goal is to spread awareness to all people and be able to share information about protests with all kinds of people, on all kinds of platforms.

We built it using a program named wix, making our own goals, and hopes for our resulting website.

We are proud of our overall project. Because this was our first hackathon. We are proud that we were able to create an entire website and work together, through the night, creating something that looked aesthetica and had a good end result.

We learned how to work hard, and to remember to work together as a team.

We hope to spread more awarness, maybe even creating an app, sharing with people knowledge about protests/important issues.
