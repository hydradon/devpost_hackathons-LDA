Youth nowadays have entirely new mediums to express their feelings that generations above them never had the chance to use. When a young person goes on the internet, they send each other incoherent pre-written messages and send stock images as ways of expressing kinship. There will always be young people, but you, you will only get older. Do not let youth slip away from you and use StockStory! A program aimed at understanding the youths of present day and the way they communicate. Run our program, and input a copypasta or any written story (of reasonable length of course), into the text box that appears and see and hear what it is like to be young again!
