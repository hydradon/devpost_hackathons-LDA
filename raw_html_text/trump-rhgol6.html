Inspiration

On the brink of sleep, a single idea surfaced in my mind. Trump. Trump. Trump... The world needs to be able to appreciate his hair!

What it does

Users brush Trump's hair to earn points, which work towards checkpoints. At checkpoints, Trump succeeds at a point in his political campaign. User's also are given the option to send a tweet to Mr. Trump after completing a checkpoint.

How I built it

I built it using Swift, Xcode, and SpriteKit.

Challenges I ran into

iPhones aren't really powerful enough to simulate hair (at least the way I was rendering it). It took a lot of time and effort to optimize this so that it runs semi-smoothly.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It's Trump! What more can I say.

What I learned

I learned how to better use SpriteKit, and I learned a lot more about the Swift Language.

What's next for Trump
