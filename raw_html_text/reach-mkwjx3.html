A social platform that engages individuals with their local community by creating opportunities for people to socialize through events and activities.

Problem: Lack of opportunities for individuals to find social events & activities in their local community. This is something supported by research and surveys we conducted.

Our Solution:A social platform that engages individuals with their local community by creating opportunities for people to socialize through events and activities. Businesses also have the opportunity to reach out to individuals in the form of discounts and specials as well as outreach events.

How are we different: On our platform, events are spontaneous, planned no more than 72 hours in advance, and can easily be found and filtered. We also give any interested business an opportunity to reach out through outreach events, employment sessions, deals/discounts, and the sponsorship of pick-up events.
