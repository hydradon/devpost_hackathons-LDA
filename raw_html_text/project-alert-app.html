Inspiration 

 After seeing multiple posting of crimes in our community, we wanted to make an app that would keep people safe. Our motivation is that people should feel safe wherever they are.

What it does 

   The app provides a quick way to alert authorities and emergency services

How we built it 

  Using Xcode 9.3 & Swift 4

Challenges we ran into 

 Integrating and learning new functions and frameworks

Accomplishments that we're proud of 

 It works!

What we learned 

 how to Apply sounds and basic animations. We learned how to obtain free public domain pictures for commercial use
