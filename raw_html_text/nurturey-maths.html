Inspiration

My 8-year-old son. And how I help him practice mental maths with him in the car during school runs.

What it does

Asks mental maths questions to kids via Alexa. Manages their response. Questions are capability/level appropriate

How I built it

During design, I kept asking my son to use the skill. I identified how a young kid will interact and what will he expect from Alexa during the conversation. His expectations were different to those of adults. That's how we fine-tuned the communication capability of the skill.

Challenges I ran into

Developing new skill sets to design communication with Alexa.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

My son loves the product. He is in the demo video.

What I learned

Alexa skills can be built with capability to manage conversations - end to end - even with a child

What's next for Nurturey Maths

Make the platform more sophisticated, with rewards and progress reports for parents.
