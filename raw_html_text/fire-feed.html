Inspiration

We wanted to build something that lets you share with the community around you. We both love reddit and yik yak, but felt like both of those apps needed some improvment. This hackathon we saw the chance and grabbed it.

How it works

You can sign up with your email, and then post pictures, no matter if funny or serious to a geofence around you.
