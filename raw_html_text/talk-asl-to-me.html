This glove talks to a processing program which guides you in making the correct shapes with your fingers to fingerspell. It checks your fingers' positions, your hand's orientation, and your hand's movement. I spent the first day troubleshooting a hardware issue in the glove, which took a huge chunk of time that I could have spent on more letters.

This is a really fast and potentially fun way to learn letters. You get live feedback, and there is a large potential for gamification. I had a lot of fun building this project. Thanks to the guys at the NSA and Telecommunications Lab table, they helped me out a ton!
