Inspiration

Many centuries back our interactions were limited to people around us. Today we interact with social media memes, news articles, international politics, pen-pals, and many other things on the internet. Trust thus has become an important issue. Trust is high with people near us, as we know them for a longer time. But how should we trust sources on the internet? How can we avoid exaggerated news and data manipulation by companies like Google and Facebook? Our game aims to explain this issue

What it does

Trusting Trust is a 5-minute psychology like game. The winner is rewarded with Ether crypto.

What's next for Trusting trust - Crypto

Based on similar concept, we will be launching a chess game.
