Inspiration

Our inspiration was the #MeToo movement and the stories of women coming forward to tell their truth. The issue of gender and racial representation in STEM is a very personal and important issue to all of us. We want to examine that issue at an in-depth level and try to provide solutions and tools for analysis.

How we built it

We built with the ShinyApp feature of R and through the website building with webnode. Some scripts for data analysis were also written with Python.

Challenges we ran into

Some challenges that we have run into was working with R and trying to get ShinyApp to work. We also faced an immense time crunch.

What we Learned

We learned how to work with R and as a team. We also learned the extent of the lack of representation in the tech industry. We hope to use the tools and resources that we built to address this issue on a broader level and work with the industry in addressing this lack of representation and issues of harassment.
