Inspiration

Tired of paying exorbitant shipping prices for products with a marginal value.

What it does

Aggregates a list of people ordering from a particular retailer; thus others can place an order with you and save out on the cost of shipping.

How I built it

It was built on Ruby-on-Rails.

Challenges I ran into

Building the Chrome Extension, and populating the database with test data.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We finished on time, and the team learned how to use Rails.

What I learned

Most of the team had never used Rails before, so it was a great learning experience as all of us got exposed to Rails for the first time.

What's next for ShareShip
