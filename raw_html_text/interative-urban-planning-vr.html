Our inspiration

Our inspiration is the enormous potential of Virtual Reality in various sectors of industry impacting people from all walks of life.

What it does

VR+ provides virtual reality & 3D based visualizations for all types of virtual tours for architectural firms.

How we built it

Using a combination of open source and proprietary software packages.

Challenges we ran into

Application optimization for smart phones

What we learned

The integration of Geographic Information Systems, Satellite remote sensing and Building information modelling enhanced with the power of 3D Mapping and Virtual Reality will give architects a unique way to visualize and market their products and services.

What's next for VR+

To create products in the domain of Augmented Reality.
