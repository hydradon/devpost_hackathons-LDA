The transition of money matches to the gaming community has left many behind. Big time players and companies are given the freedom to play and win-- preventing the general population from a growing income source. The Blockchain E-Trade creates an environment supporting a pure peer-to-peer permissioned betting system. Meaning anyone can trade with a wallet in minutes, without the traditional hastle associated with a centralized network. BET enhances both the gaming experience and the thrill of a calculated win. Participants of all skill levels have the ability to earn crypto. Our service allows for the general population to compete for and interact with gaming sponsorships and prizes.
