Inspiration

Growing concern of self-diagnosis for serious respiratory related difficulties.

What it does

Senses the temperature of your breath and co-relates the sensor response to probable dental and pulmonary issues in your body.

How we built it

Using sensors from Grove. Arduino Connectors Coffee Cups LED Display Bubble Tea Straw

Challenges we ran into

Vibration sensor didn't have a wide range of data to display. Light sensor is too sensitive in nature. Touch sensor is binary in nature and hence, we had only the temperature sensor to use.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It works. Does correct math. Displays results up to 2 decimal precision. And it was made within 1 day.

What we learned

We learnt a lot about oral health and various diagnosis that can be performed on human breath.

What's next for Just Breathe

Use chemicals and accurately detect the extent of oral and pulmonary disease.
