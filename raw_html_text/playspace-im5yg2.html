PlaySpace

A social network that connects people through music and gives soul to city spaces.

Features

There are three main aspects to this application

  • PlaySpaces
  • Explore
  • Listen

PlaySpaces

Allows the user to manage its own PlaySpaces . A PlaySpace is a set of music connected to a specific location. It can be tailored to fit certain criteria.

Explore

The goal of this feature is to visualize the different PlaySpaces (the mood of places) in the city.

Listen

In this mode, you can listen to the mood created by other users passing by. It allows to connect people with similar musical tastes.
