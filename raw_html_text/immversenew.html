Inspiration

I remember doctors having all these things there to distract kids from the undoubtedly horrifying procedures they are about to go through. So I thought, why not use the inherent "wow factor" of VR to accomplish that task?

What it does

Swaps between mildly stimulating environments.

How I built it

Very slowly

Challenges I ran into

Unreal got really gross with lighting the main sphere.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Making really cool effects with the materials system

What I learned

Hollow, reflecting surfaces make unreal's lighting really bug out.

What's next for Immverse

More environments, gimmicks with the main sphere
