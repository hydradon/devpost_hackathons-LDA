Inspiration

Our team is passionate about health, and through our brainstorming, we identified a vulnerable and exploited element of women's health. We are seeking to rectify these vulnerabilities and provide a menstrual and mood tracker that protects the users' data.

What it does

you are here tracks menstrual and mood information from its user and predicts future menstrual and fertility dates. It also tracks and displays mood indicators based on DSM 5 criteria for common mental health conditions. All data is stored locally to ensure user privacy and safety.

How we built it

you are here was programmed in java.

Challenges we ran into

It was challenging to complete all functional aspects of the project and prepare it for mobile deployment in such a tight time frame.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud to have a foundational version of the application with visual representations of all functionalities.

What we learned

We improved our java skills in a team environment.

What's next for You Are Here

We hope to complete features of the app such as the interactive calendar and mood data displays in the future.
