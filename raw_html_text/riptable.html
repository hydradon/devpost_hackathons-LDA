Inspiration

People's vocabulary words while learning a language often come from many different sources in different formats. RipTable makes it so they can extract it extremely quickly and intuitively from any website to a common study platform.

How it works

RipTable integrates into any website within a user's browser, turning the table. Each table becomes selectable and interactive, allowing the selection of multiple columns and the removal of rows.

Challenges I ran into

Parsing of the tables and getting all the colors to display quickly and fluidly was the biggest challenge

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The interface is extremely user-friendly and intuitive to use. Anyone can pick up all their data and move to another platform hassle-free.

What's next for RipTable

RipTable will be integrated with into a language learning ecosystem for everyone to easily pick-up whatever foreign language they are pursuing.
