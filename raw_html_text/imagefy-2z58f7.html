Inspiration

Are you seeing some product you want, but don't know how to search for that product? Take a picture of the desired product let us work for you! Our motivation is to make the shopping experience more humanized giving the ability for the user to send a picture of a desired product and receive selected offers by a specialized team.

What it does

It allows users to take pictures of desired products and receive offers from various shops.

How we built it

The project was built on three platforms: IOS, Android and Web. Through the native applications of iOS and Android users send their desired products for analysis of Shopify team. Using the Web platform, Shopify team selects products and sends to users.

Challenges we ran into

The biggest challenge is to create an unforgettable and unique experience for the user through an intuitive and easy layout.

What we learned

We learned a lot about Shopify API.

What's next for Imagefy

The next step for the Imagefy is to integrate various shops on the web platform to offer a wide range of products.
