Inspiration

Despite the continued popularity of games like guitar hero, there aren't any good ways to play today's songs unless you buy the newer, not a good version of the game.

What it does

Upload and convert any music file into its individual music notes. This is then transcribed into "SitarZerø" notation. From there, you can play our desktop game using our Patent Pending SitarZerø Sitar. You can even play the sitar without hooking up to or playing the game!!

How we built it

Utilising sensors and other parts for Arduino. Also using phaser.js for game dev.

Challenges we ran into

Communicating between our Arduino and our game's front and backend.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Building a full-fledged Guitar.

What we learned

The power of friendship. Also how to hate promise objects.

What's next for SitarZerø

Wireless controller, more detailed gameplay, VR, difficulties, multiplayer, and all other functionality that would make this a modern game.
