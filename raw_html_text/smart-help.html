Inspiration

It emerges from the need to provide first aid information at the moment in an accurate way to a person who needs it, considering that a qualified person is not always around or has the knowledge about the emergency.

What it does

It determines the kind of emergency and provides a guide of first aid while the professional assistance is on the way, if it’s the case.

How we built it

By an expert system, designed in Java and executed in Android.

Challenges we ran into

The extraccion of the overall knowledge and modelling of it and the cultural and religious belief too.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It’s an application that by implementation of Artificial Intelligence gives solutions and accurate and precise attention.

What we learned

We obtained first aid knowledge mainly.

What's next for SMART HELP

Update or add other basis of knowledge to the base which is taken by the expert system.
