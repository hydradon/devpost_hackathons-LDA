Inspiration

Five hours into the hackathon, and still, nothing. No start and no idea. One of our friends came up and recommended we do something funny because people love funny, so we thought of memes. After a bit of a conversation, we decided that we would make a website where you could make your own memes.

What it does

Our website allows any person with a computer to make a funny meme.

How we built it

We first went to the website building workshop where we learned the fundamentals of HTML and CSS. Then we spend the next multiple hours coding, checking, and learning new things to do.

Challenges we ran into

We don't really know how to code, and also one of our partners got bored and left 3 hours in.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Learning the basics of HTML and CSS.

What we learned

HTML and CSS
