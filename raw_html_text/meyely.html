Inspiration

I work in industrial automation mainly computer vision and industrial scales. The price of cameras, sensors have gone down and computing power has gone up. Yet the prices of vision systems still remain high. It mostly has to do with sensor quality and close software.

What it does

It maps the volume on the shelf through 6 ultrasonic sensors and identifies SKUs by color.
