Big Red Hacks

Road Rage Submission for Big Red Hacks 2017

Two Computer Scientists, one Game Dev, and one Software Engineer walk into a hackathon. They plan to make a game, only to find out the entire hackathon is based on education and fintech! Down, but not out, our heroes continued on to make a game anyway to express their frustration. For fun! For anger! For redemption! For screaming! For ROAD RAGE!

Tired? Upset? Come find us, and let's shout it from the rooftops. Just be sure to bring your laptop!
