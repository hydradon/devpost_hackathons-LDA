Inspiration

Music trivia. Current sites that give you trivia practice, like Quizlet or Kahoot, are plain and boring. We wanted to make pop music trivia enjoyable.

What it does

Fun game to test your knowledge on the greatest hits of the last decade!

How we built it

We built it using Flask, React, Google-Cloud, AWS and Genius API's.

Challenges we ran into

Front-end design we ran into a bit of trouble with react states and finding the right layouts for our app

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Backend service to use the API, deployed with GCP.

What we learned

How to use Genius API's, authentication, and how to come back from losses.

What's next for allow.it

Building out full back-end services, and dockerizing for scalability, per-user authentication.
