high tech swimwear and the latest in sports gear.

This swim suite tracks your GPS location so that when your swim is over you can check your speed and distance. It also warns you when you are getting to much UV light. Lastly it also has a magnetic band that wards off sharks.

Using fabric and arduino boards and sensors plus lots of skill we brought the idea to life.
