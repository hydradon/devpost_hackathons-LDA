Inspiration

Sites such as etheroll offer simple luck-based crypto games. We wanted to turn this experience into something multiplayer.

What it does

The classic Jewish game of Dreidel comes to the big screen in a whole new way - create and join online rooms to stake ETH against other players in hopes of coming out on top and winning big. In the game of Dreidel, there can be only be one winner.

How we built it

A lot of blood, sweat, tears, solidity, and javascript.
