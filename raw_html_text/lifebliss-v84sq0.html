Inspiration

Alexa skills are basically known for making our life easy. Just thinking about some ways of getting health tips or hacks to make our day to day life easier. LifeBliss is the answer

What it does

LifeBliss would love to hear from you about your age. Because, the health plan can be prepared for individuals based on their age. Apart from that, LifeBliss likes to know about, how long you are sitting while at work. This is to determine your physical activity during your official hours. Considering both of these parameters, LifeBliss will provide you some life hacks for your health and fitness

How I built it

Golang is the language which i used to build the skill. Obviously, this code runs over the Alexa-skills-kit
