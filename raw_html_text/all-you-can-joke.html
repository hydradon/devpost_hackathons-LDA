Inspiration

Not everyone thinks about creating something that can put a smile (rather laugh in this case) on other's face.

What it does

A service of lame jokes and juxtaposed puns that will tickle your stomach to laugh by giving you a completely different answer to your questions powered by iOS app, web-app and AI assistance including Alexa and Siri.

How we built it

We used HTML, CSS, CANVA, AMAZON-ALEXA, 3-D Printing, Google-cloud, Domain.com.

Challenges we ran into

First and foremost, the most challenging part was brainstorming for project ideas. There were other couple of problems that the team faced like the part where we had to adjust the co-ordinates in the 3D printer for the first time, implementing the Front-end together with the iOS app and connecting back-end with Amazon Alexa.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Overcoming the challenges we ran into and completing the project on time. Also, everyone had to work with something completely new skillset and we're proud of the new things we have learned this weekend.

What we learned

3-D Printing, Connecting Amazon Alexa with back-end, networking, team-collaboration.

What's next for All you can joke

Expand the database for jokes/pun and improvise on the User Interface.
