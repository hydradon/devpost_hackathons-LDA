Inspiration

The widespread concentrated poverty in Detroit urging for better management on finances

What it does

This iOS application calculates your spending trends based on categories as wells as your flucuations in spending every month.

How we built it

We used Capital One's Nessie API to analyze the purchases and relationships between the merchants and the customers. We used Alchemy API to categorize specific purchases into generic groups. This helps us to save space and helps us to simplify the UI.

Challenges we ran into

The debugging involved in XCode 8 was a huge obstacle that we had to get around and jump over many times.
