Inspiration

Using artificial intelligence to detect sentiment from prose. Trying to understand emotions and how they are all connected, and some connections to colours too.

What it does

It takes your mood (an emoticon :-( or 0-100, or 'meh', 'bored' etc) and suggests a tv show to watch

How I built it

I took the data from sky, passed it through Watson alchemy language, saved it to mongo. Using twilio, receiving a message and returning a suggested tv show.

Challenges I ran into

Data limits with watson (I had to mock a lot of the results).

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

getting twiliio integrated and getting results

What I learned

How to use the watson SDK.
