Inspiration

Chance to work with a new technology.

What it does

Website controls a Roomba's movements and gathers sensor information of the Roomba in realtime.

How we built it

We used a flask server that communicates with a Roomba through a serial port.

Challenges we ran into

Having to share the Roomba with 3 other groups during development and testing.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Learning new technologies for the first time and getting the webapp to work.

What we learned

Jquery, serial port connections, and Flask

What's next for Room Room

Various possible applications stemming from the current control established.
