• Scan2Win

Inspiration

Our inspiration is to motivate people to continue visiting stores through generating some exclusive experience which online shopping can´t deliver. Through gamification and a contest factor we want to use the "christmas shopping period" to improve the shopping experience.

What it does

Every advent-week an the customer can unlock a random puzzle which he has to solve to find the hidden QR-Code within the store. Every customer has to chance to win prices.

How I built it

We used android studio to build some prototype app. We also used zbar libary to implement the QR scanning process.
