Inspiration

I was inspired by the many people who want to shop online, but are afraid to do so.

What it does

It allows the user to enter their birth month, and then determines their birthstone. Then, it provides a link for them to buy birthstone jewelry.

How I built it

I used Android Studio and Java.

Challenges I ran into

The app initially wouldn't run. Then, the text was overlapping.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I made an app! Also, I got the text to not overlap.

What I learned

I learned how to use andriod studio.

What's next for Birthstone Jewelry

The app will remain forever in my memory.
