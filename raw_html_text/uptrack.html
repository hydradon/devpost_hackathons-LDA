What it does

When you are in uptime mode, it will (optionally) disable certain sites (ex. social media) of your choosing in order to promote productivity as well as track your usage of active programs (so you can see where your time is going!).

When you are in downtime mode, it will disable tracking as well as return access to any sites that were restricted in uptime mode.
