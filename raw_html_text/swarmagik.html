Inspiration

Swarm is a super annoying app by Foursquare where people "check in" to place to earn points. We wanted to meme our friend who use it.

What it does

When a friend checks in to a place on Swarm we get a photo of that place from the web and use ImageMagik to overlay a photo of them.

How we built it

Bit of JS, bit of APIs, bit of ImageMagik. Boom.

Check it out on Telegram: https://t.me/swarmvisbot
