• Savvy

Why is that people who are talented(Savvy) have such a hard time getting paid for it. From Makeup Artist, Musicians, Dancers to even Mechanics. Everyone has a skill they want to show off. Best part get paid for it. Savvy is an app that allows you to capture video content demonstrating any talent/skill you may have. This will be shared to users around your area where then they can hire you based of your talent. Once you have assigned a job to another user you will receive a receipt where you can leave feedback on the job done.
