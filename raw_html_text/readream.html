Inspiration

Nightmares sparked our initial interest in attempting to understand the underlying causes of dreams. To make a long story short, we could not come up with a clear-cut definition. However, we did learn that when we share dreams with our friends (or vice versa), our dreams (or theirs) became an open-ended story with multiple insights, analyses, and meanings.

What it does

It allows you to share your dreams as well as give insights to other users' dreams.

How I built it

We used css, html, RoR, Ruby, and Javascript.

Challenges I ran into

The challenges we faced are formatting the web page and overall coding.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We are proud of our design of the website.

What I learned

How to work with others as well as how to use time efficiently.

What's next for ReaDream

Making a private page by using user authorization and using APIs.
