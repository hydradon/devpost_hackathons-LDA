It all started with noticing youngsters in our community having heated debates about major subject areas. However to our surprise incorrect information was being argued, so within the 60 seconds listening to these youngster debate the idea "flashed" in our thoughts and thus 60 seconds was born.

It is a fast paced Q & A quiz game where the question is given and the player has 60 seconds to answer each question and reap up points. Separate from the time as a factor the player also is given 3 lives , where if the answer is incorrectly selected they would lose a life. The twist is that the answer are animated and reappears at different locations on the screen as you play.

A challenge was trying to pull the information from the database, which we ended up solving in a speedily fashion.

The speed at which we finished this game and the proper functionality of the game

Patience, Coding paradigms and the power of team work

What's next for 60 Seconds: We plan to push this app to be integrated in the Tablets for Schools program as well as provide CXC students with a fun , portable and informative way of preparing for exams
