What it does

  • Fully automatize the continuous improvement process of value chains by gathering, analyzing and interpreting available information

How we built it

  • A central container hosted on predix that collects sensor information and value chain characteristics, which are analyzed and visualized on a responsive dashboard

Challenges we ran into

  • Data cleaning and stitching as well as interpretation of production information from an unknown domain
  • Evaluation and interpretation of inferred Machine Learning insights

Accomplishments that we're proud of

  • Achieved basic understanding of predix platform and the value chain business
  • Generation of interpretable outcomes

What we learned

  • Importance of skill distribution within a diverse team
  • The positive impact of a Minimal Viable Product (MVP)

What's next for ACCELERIX

  • Refinement of the UI / UX
  • Development of a more sophisticated prediction approach
  • Invention of a real-time data analytics pipeline
