Inspiration

Everyone wants to be Donald Trump, and everyone loves his hair. Who wouldn't want to shoot his hair at Hilary Clinton to help him defend his great wall? It's truly revolutionary.

What it does

This app lets people experience the ultimate feeling of being Donald Trump.

How we built it

One of us programmed while the other did the art for the game.

Challenges we ran into

We had multiple syntax errors, Photoshop stopped working at one point, and water spilled onto one of our computers.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to create a decent game within 24 hours.

What we learned

We learned that games become 500 times better when Donald Trump is involved.

What's next for Trump Defense

Next, we will move onto launching Donald Trump's hair at Rosie O'Donnell.
