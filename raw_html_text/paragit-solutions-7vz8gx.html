Inspiration

What it does

The project consists of two solutions, a hardware diagnostic device and an assisting software. The solution will be able to more precisely assess Parkinson’s disease cardinal symptoms with respect to UPDRS scale which is further described in the attached PP-presentation.

How we built it

PoC was made with a simple breadboard and conductive textile, afterwards we moved further and developed rev.1.0 PCB.

Challenges we ran into

The business aspect of the product as well as R&D in regards of attenuating movement artifacts.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The overall business strategy as well as promising results which is further described in our PP-presentation.

What we learned

We learned how to involve different aspects of business go-to-market strategies as well as team-building.

What's next for Paragit Solutions

The next for Paragit Solutions is to further develop the hardware as well as finalizing the analyzing software. Our next plan is to further validate our data with 50 Paragit Sleeves.
