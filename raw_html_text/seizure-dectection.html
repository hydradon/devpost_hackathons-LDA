Seizures happen because of sudden, abnormal electrical activity in the brain. Affect 3 million adults and ~500,000 children in the U.S. Most seizures last from 30 seconds to 2 minutes and do not cause lasting harm. However, it is a medical emergency if seizures last longer than 5 minutes or if a person has many seizures and does not wake up between them. Alert the people around the patient!

We used data and build a machine learning model and an android app to call when fall.
