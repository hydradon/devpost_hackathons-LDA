Inspiration

We wanted to make something that would allow people to express their creativity and has relevance in today's society.

What it does

The project allows users to create a meme by adding witty top and bottom texts to either a picture from a selection of popular meme backgrounds or their own uploaded images. There is also a function to generate dank meme texts on top of the memes.

How we built it

We built it using Java Swing.

Challenges we ran into

We were not familiar with some of the concepts needed for the project. For example, we did not have much experience with filechooser and had trouble exporting the custom memes.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to resolve all of the challenges we ran into along the way. We are also proud of the fact that the project has relevance in our lives.

What we learned

We learned about importing and exporting images, and we became more familiar with graphics.
