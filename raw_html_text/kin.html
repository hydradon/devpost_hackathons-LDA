Inspiration

Our inspiration is students at our school not completing their homework. And we believe its because parents don't check-in on them. So we want to solve this problem by making this app/website for parents to be able to check-in on their children whether they are at home or at work.

How it works

Teacher will submit the homework he or she assigned in class each day. Then the parents will get a notification showing that they child have homework. Then they can remind their child to do their homework and keep track if the he or she is doing the homework.
