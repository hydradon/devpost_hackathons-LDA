NYC GOVChain This is the entry for the Consensus Hackathon dated May 11 - 12, 2019

Problem: Inefficiencies of Real Estate Documents
Solution: Decentralized Private Permissioned Blockchain using Quorum adding immutability and privacy

This app has 7 nodes representing 7 different government insitutions ( Department of Buildings, IRS, Office of the Mayor, Department of Finance, Department of Licenses, OSHA, and Department of Consumer Affairs)

User/Client: Government Institution (Department of Buildings - Node 1)
