Problem Statement #1

We urgently need a tool that can streamline the fisheries data collection process, and enable fishermen to play a role in data collection.

Solution

Neptune's Fish Market is a mobile application that allows fishermen to sell their captured fish online to motivate them to enter the data of their captured fish

Impact of Neptune's Fish Market:

  • The government will save huge amounts of valuable time, money, efforts, and resources
  • The fisherman are now able sell their catch in the general public without needing the help of the middleman
  • The consumers are able to save money through direct buying from the fishermen

Core Functions

  • Image Recognition
  • Image from Camera and Saved Pictures
  • Search the database's existing records through pictures
  • Measurement of the Fish
  • Search Optimizing per Picture Search

Supporting Features

  • User-Friendly Interface
  • User Accounts - Registration, Login, Edit, Account Settings, Logout
  • 2 Account types (Fisherman and Consumer)
  • Fisherman Account can post fishes to sell (1 day duration of post)
  • Fisherman can specify the price, tags, location, and measuremen to the sale post
  • Consumer can search fishes to buy based on location, name of fish, and tags

The Future of Neptune's Fish Market:

  • Multiple Language and Currency Support
  • Specific and Extensive Database Support
  • Improved Image Recognition Capability
  • iOS Support
