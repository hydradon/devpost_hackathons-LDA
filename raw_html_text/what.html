Inspiration

We was inspired by the firm Terma to do an AudioVR challenge.

What it does

WHAT 'is' an augmented communication application. Use cases are in aeroplanes ect.

How we built it

On an html js base with frameworks such as WebVR, WebAudio, WebRTC and Aframe.

Challenges we ran into

WebAudio has some limitations due to the inability to modify remote MediaStreams.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The loading screen and the WebRTC voice communication.

What's next for WHAT

A looooooooooot of refactoring.
