Get together with your friends & family faster, and have some fun without the planning hassle. Seriously, coordinating times to get together is an unnecessarily complicated process. Spend more time enjoying and less time planning.

With Airtime, scheduling is easier, faster, and free. Like air.

As a host, you can

  • Create, edit, and delete events
  • Select the dates you are available to host the event on a calendar
  • Invite friends via username or email
  • See the best dates for everyone
  • Send an invitation with finalized date via email
  • Add the event to your calendar
  • Cloud-based to sync your events between accounts

Once you're invited to an event,

  • Input your available times to meet within the timeframe given by the host

Use AirTime the next time you're planning a

  • Team dinner
  • Pickup volleyball game
  • Birthday party
  • Reunion
  • Brunch with friends
