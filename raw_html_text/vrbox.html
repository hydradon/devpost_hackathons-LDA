Inspiration

In the beginning of the hackaton I had a completely different idea. Since I have started I faced too many problems, but along with problems more ideas visited my head. What if one has an absolute freedom, but reality limits him/her?

Description

VRBox is a sandbox in virtual reality. You can do whatever you want with whatever you.

Highlights

For now, you can grab objects, resize, rotate, move and spawn them. There are two main scenes - home scene, where your objects are located, and spawn scene, from which you can get new objects.

Comments

This is my first Unity/VR project and I had a great experience working with HTC Vive. I want to thank Connor Smith and HTC company who provided a VR Lounge for SD Hacks.
