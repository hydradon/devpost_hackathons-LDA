Inspiration:

A professor at UNM needed an app to help speed up foot injury treatment, analysis, and process.

What it does:

Analyzes a picture of foot wounds to estimate the time required for healing.

How we built it:

Image analysis and mathematical modeling using Java and Android studio.

Challenges I ran into:

Learning about different foot injuries and healing characteristics.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of:

Learning a new application for image detection and mathematical modeling.

What I learned:

How different wounds can be estimated and how everyone heals differently.

What's next for HeelHeal:

Possible business with associate professor and his research.to develop the app more.
