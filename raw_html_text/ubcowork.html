Inspiration

As college students, we have some knick-knack jobs that require an extra hand and services from another person. UBCOWork connects the user and the service provider, both of which are students helping each other and they can earn some quick bucks from that too.

What it does

UBCOWork is a platform built for students who want to earn money by helping the community or get work done and pay for it. A student has two options, either pay for a service or provide a service to earn money.

How we built it

We used Swift language on the Xcode Editor to build an intuitive iOS App within 24 hours

Challenges we ran into

None of our team members had made an App before, so it was a completely new learning experience. We couldn't figure out the backend too quickly. Our main purpose was to get the concept out!

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Learning a totally new language and building a mobile app, all within 24 hours, I would never have imagined that. Our team worked hard to learn constantly and implement the skills.

What we learned

We learned a new language and a way to express ourselves. Diving into the new world of App Development was a great experience and we cherished every moment of it.

What's next for UBCOWork

We need to make it fully functional and lift it up from being a concept to a utility. We would love to expand this platform into different universities and start a subscription model around it.
