Inspiration

We wanted to automated trade across DEXs

What it does

Tannatci is an application which allows me to do the following:

  • DEX agnostic - Share liquidity and price across all DEX
  • Assets and private keys always with you
  • Create condition-based trades that execute no matter where you are

How I built it

The user pre-signs a transaction for the condition based order for future on-chain validation and settlement once the condition is satisfied.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We managed to glue all pieces together and prove the concept works
