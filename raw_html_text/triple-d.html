Inspiration

According to the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) approximately 30.000 people die in traffic crashes yearly in the USA, including an estimated 10,000 people who died in drunk driving crashes, accounting for 33% of all traffic deaths in a year. Our goal was to reduce this number by creating an app which provides an easy way to record and report dangerous drivers on the road all around the world. Users of the app will receive notifications via email when a dangerous driver is near them.

How it works

Imagine that you are driving and a car in front of you is making weird maneuvers. What can you do to avoid an accident?Just use TripleD, make a recording and send it to the local police station. You can also notify your family and friends about this dangerous driver, keeping them safe. Using the key received in mail, message or on any other communication channel you can download the video recorded by the sender using TripleD.

Challenges I ran into

The biggest challenge was to create a simple app, which is easy to use and satisfies the user's needs.

What's next for Triple D

We would like to add some new features into the app, like voice control for navigating between the features of the app without interacting manually with the user interface. In the near future we would like the publish it on the Appstore, making it accessible for free for all the people on the go.
