Inspiration

Many people are unaware of how resources are being allocated as it relates to health care in their localities. So many preventable cases arise because of unequal distribution, therefore we are on a mission to bring awareness to the public.

What it does

It highlights areas in which the quality of health care is lower than, on par with or above the national average, in hopes of leading the public to seek effective policy change.

How we built it

We decided to use Angular and Node to parse and display heat signatures on map surfaces, after analysis and sanitization of big, open datasets.

Challenges we ran into

Integrating Maps API with Angular 2 and parsing CSV files with multiple different headers

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Being able to finish the server and client side.
