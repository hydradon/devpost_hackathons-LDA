Nowadays religions are one of the most common topics in the world. There are lots of believers and atheists, but everyone don’t know that there is a celebration every day. So we decided to develop an application, which would inform people quickly about all of these holy holidays. When you install our application, you have to open it at least only once and that’s all. Our program will work in background mode and it will send you notifications every day. During the development process, I was using Android Studio and Java. It was really difficult job, to devise something really cool in 36 hours and develop it. In future we’re going to add some really cool functionality. For example we’re going to add a birthday reminder and a regular reminder. You will be able to add your own events to the calendar.
required min sdk:android 5.0.1 PS. My application says that today is the birthday of tights. .
