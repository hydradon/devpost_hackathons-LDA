Inspiration

Inspired by the crazy number of students who are uneducated about their own alcohol tolerance, and as a result get themselves into dangerous situations.

What it does

Allows you to keep track of the number of drinks you have consumed, while giving an estimate of your blood alcohol content and a corresponding wait time before drinking another.

How we built it

Using android studio and java!

Challenges we ran into

Only one of our teammates had used this software before, so sharing ideas between the various skill sets proved difficult.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Using all of our individual experience to create an application to make drinking safer for everyone.

What we learned

Emulators are very problematic!

What's next for DRUNKR

We would like to add tons of functionality, such as personally altering your body statistics, partnering with other companies to add increased inventory and analyzing your historical use to educate the user on healthy drinking habits.
