Inspiration

Organizations need to protect the personally identifiable information of their employees, members or customers, or else they risk incurring significant financial costs and a blow to their reputations in the event of a data breach. JIRA is one of the core systems where organizations store information. Therefore a solution for protecting PII in JIRA is crucial.

What it does

PII Protector for JIRA add-on monitors sensitive PII (personally identifiable information like credit card numbers, social security numbers, addresses, etc.) stored in Atlassian JIRA, reports it, provides admins with a convenient UI to manage it, to audit access to it, and optionally to hide or to erase it. PII Protector for JIRA is aimed to be a complete solution for PII in your organization JIRA instance. You won't need to worry about PII leaks or unwarranted access anymore.
