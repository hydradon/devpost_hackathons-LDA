Inspiration

The root of wastage is consumers making 'trigger happy' purchases without considering their past purchases.

What it does

Our app serves as a reminder to consider similar past purchases and how frequent you use them right before you add the item to our shopping list.

How we built it

We used android studio. Room library to handle SQLite database which stores the item data.

Challenges we ran into

Communicating data from activity to fragment in another activity

Accomplishments that we're proud of

It worked!
