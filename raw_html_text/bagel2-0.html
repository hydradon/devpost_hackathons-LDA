Inspiration

Being indecisive hipsters, we always face an issuing for finding last minute tickets for our favorite concerts. We know hustlers are ready to sell the ticket for 2x the price but it leaves us no choice but to buy since all of our friends have decided to join the fun.

What it does

It helps you connect with resellers who are willing to sell their ticket for a reasonable price. With the use of ChangeJar payments API, you can easily transfer money from peer to peer.

How I built it

We built it using a combo of Angular and Ionic

Challenges I ran into

Setting up the ChangeJar API. Challenges of integrating Google API

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Having a working prototype!

What I learned

Learned more about how Angular works and the built-in functionality Ionic has

What's next for Bagel2.0

Bagel2.0 would expand and have the functionality of integration Innovapost API, being able to send your ticket as a package if you are not able to pick it up in person
