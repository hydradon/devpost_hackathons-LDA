Inspiration

Boomers to Zoomers aims to build community connection amongst youths and seniors through meaningful activities. We strive to provide a positive and encouraging platform for youths to develop leadership skills, connect and learn from seniors while providing companionship and assistance to seniors who needs them.

What it does

Boomers to Zoomers is an app that connects boomers and zoomers through a social media platform where they can connect and partcipate at events together.

How we built it

We built B2Z using HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Firebase API.
