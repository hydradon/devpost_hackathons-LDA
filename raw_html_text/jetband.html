Inspiration

Seeing how wearable technology can make our lives easier and how Jetblue needed a way for its consumers to have easy access to flight information and be able to easily buy it.

What it does

Informs consumers of affordable flights they would likely be interested in and catalyzes a seamless travel process.

How I built it

It was built in Android Studio. A lot of fiddling with the Microsoft Band API. A little help from mentors and Google.
