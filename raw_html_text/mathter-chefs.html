What we learnt:

How to manage expectations of a project. Making necessary simplifications to get a workable solution.

What we built:

We successfully developed a web app that uses the food detection algorithm that we developed using python. In addition to that physics of heat transfer in food items was also applied to calculate cooking times and to develop heat map data for different types of meats and habitat (Earth vs Mars). Interface between three different components of the project was also successfully established!

Challenges we faced:

We wanted to measure the thickness of the meat cuts but ran out of time to do so. Heat transfer analysis for different types of shapes such as cylindrical, spherical also couldn't be implemented due to time constraint.
