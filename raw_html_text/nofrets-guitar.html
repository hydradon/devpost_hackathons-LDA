Inspiration

This project was inspired by the Bonfire packing list suggesting bringing a guitar. One group member wanted to bring their guitar, but couldn't because one of the strings was broken. As a result, we were inspired to build a guitar that never required strings.

What it does

The project replaces the strings on an old guitar with touch-based switches, and the strum is pushbuttons
