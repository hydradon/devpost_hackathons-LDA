Inspiration

This website was inspired by my experience working as a tutor and my need for a platform to reach potential students.

What it does

Helps connect struggling students with tutors at their school who are familiar with the student's subjects and teachers.

How we built it

We used sublime text to integrate html, css, and javascript to create a framework for our site.

Challenges we ran into

As a two member team, we struggled because of lack of javascript experience.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We managed to create a multi-page site with clickable buttons.

What we learned

We gained a lot of javascript experience, learned how to implement it into a website, and overall improved our ability to program and design a site.

What's next for Kennari

We intend to continue working on our website. It could realistically be used at our school to facilitate tutoring, and with a bit more work, it could be fully functional for a variety of students.
