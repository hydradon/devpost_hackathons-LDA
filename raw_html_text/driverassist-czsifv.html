AssistDriver

Team Mates Jitendra Singh Kapil Vyas Sukhbir Singh Supriya Saraogi

Objective: To assist drivers in avoiding collision by notifying any vehicle nearby Description: AssistDriver helps the drivers to drive safely in an economical way. Suppose a driver wants to change a lane and if it is not safe to change the lane, the application will display visually about incoming traffic either on the left or right. Use Cases

  1. Change Lanes: If a driver wants to change a lane and there is a vehicle in blind spot or very close, application will display the vehicle.
  2. Take turn: Same concept applies while taking turn

Primary goal of application is to give an economical instrument to drivers

Built With

  • arduino
  • node.js
