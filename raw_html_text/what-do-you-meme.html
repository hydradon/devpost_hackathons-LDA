memefully

We constructed this project because Mr Max Lee inspired us to be more appreciative of MEMES . We built this project so as to be able to educate youths and future generations to be able to read bite-size news in a more interactive way. Through numerous hours of churning out different ideas and verifying them with our mentors. We managed to come out with this idea. It was built on out blood sweat and tears.

The challenges we faced snowballed one after another and it reached a all time low when one of our group members left us and gave up on the project. From then on, we were a 3 man group.We coped and adapted and improvised!
