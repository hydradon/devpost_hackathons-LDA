its 2017 and every human being is now employed by the Robot to label its training data. Making it smarter.... and smarter. We are continually watched at work on our macbook cameras. If we display happy emotions…… we hear the pleasant music! And if we get angry...we are punished with terrible music.

We developed an app that connects to your macbook camera and takes 1 picture of the user every 5 seconds and sends that to a custom model built on the Clarifai API to to determine if the user is happy or mad. We use this information to adjust the music.
