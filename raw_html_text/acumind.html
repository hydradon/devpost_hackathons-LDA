Inspiration

We wanted to use technology to better the welfare of users. Mental health is becoming an increasingly prominent issue. Acumind accomplishes this by giving users the power to be more aware of the state of their mental health.

What it does

Analyzes and assesses user's mental health based on their twitter feed

Challenges we ran into

Utilizing an API to analyze the sentiment of the user's posts. Originally we had wanted to use GoogleCloud but we ran into irresolvable problems.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Integration of Watson API

What we learned

Assessment of user's mental health based on their chat history.
