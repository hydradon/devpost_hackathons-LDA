Inspiration

I share my apartment with my classmate. We usually pay our bills and share the expenses. I wanted a decentralized solution that would help monitor my expense without sending it to a centralized server.

What it does

It's a personal expense manager to monitor and get insight into your daily spends

How I built it

I used Blockstack to create the dApp which give the data in the hands of the user.

Challenges I ran into

As the Blockstack is frontend on the challenge is to share data between multiple users and keep the data private at the same time

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

I was able to develop cryptography powered app for managing expenses.

What I learned

I learned a lot about cryptography and why giving the data in the hands of the user is important.

What's next for Paise

I would like to add Split Expense feature to share expenses like food, bills, etc with friends and pay them using Lighting
