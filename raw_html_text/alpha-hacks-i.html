Inspiration

When delving into the business world it is known that for one to succeed, it is important to establish healthy connections with consumers. Well, how can that be done? Many businesses invest a significant share of their income to develop advertisements and ways for customers to believe in the company more, with that being done, it is certain that they need to know that they work and not create a negative image of themselves. Persistent growth comes from continually sustained customer relations rather than time to time encounters, for a company to be robust fundamentally, it has to go through the conventions of disagreement and the general consensus for it to adapt and create the most appealing version of itself.

What it does

A user can upload ads to the server. The ads are displayed on a page, which flips through each ad every ten seconds. Viewers' emotions are detected and sent to the server, and the server stores how people react to each ad.

How I built it

We used clmtrackr, a library for face analysis, to detect emotions. We used node.js to set up the server.
