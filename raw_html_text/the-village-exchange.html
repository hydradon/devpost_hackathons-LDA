Inspiration

A picture is worth a thousand words.

What it does

Users submit photos and captions under one (or more) of four categories: Events, Places, Foods, and Other. Athletes can share through words and pictures what they love most about their country and why. For every picture submitted, The Village Exchange sends the user a random picture from an athlete from a different country.

How I built it

I built this app using JavaScript.

Challenges I ran into

Making sure new random pictures are sent each time a picture is submitted.

What's next for The Village Exchange

Expanding it to general users.
