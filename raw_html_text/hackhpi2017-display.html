Inspiration

Constant measurement of children growth is very important in the early years of a child. To discover growth problems before they get critical, it is crucial to record the history of the childrens growth.

What it does

Our App and Hardware Kit makes it easy for parents to measure their childrens growth. They will get reminded in regular interval to measure their kids.

How we built it

Swift, Python, PythonBottle, OpenCV, Raspberry Pi, LED Matrix, LEGO <3, Coffeine

Challenges we ran into

Accomplishments that we're proud of
