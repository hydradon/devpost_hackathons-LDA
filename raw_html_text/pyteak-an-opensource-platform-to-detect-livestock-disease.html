• Pyteak detecting sick chicken

Inspiration

diseases in livestock spreads swiftly, especially in chicken. within a few days, a disease in one chicken, had it not been detected, can spread to others and infect all of the chicken in the barn. Pyteak comes as a solution to detect sick livestock in huge barn to prevent the spread of the disease.

What it does

Using computer vision, pyteak can detect sick chicken and distinct between healthy and sick chicken, even in industrial amount

How we built it

We built it using tensorflow but currently we are migrating to pytorch

Challenges we ran into

we currently have some difficulty of searching enough image and video of chicken

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Pyteak has won a 3rd winner in intel OpenVino hackathon Indonesia in august 2019

What we learned

Pyteak still needs to be improved in a few aspects

What's next for Pyteak - an opensource platform to detect livestock disease

Pyteak sees a clear way ahead, from the business side and usability side. Pyteak needs more data to train. From the business side, Pyteak can be used to sold the system or smart barn.
