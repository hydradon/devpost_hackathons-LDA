Inspiration

How does management know if you're burnt out? If you're dealing with personal issues? If you're stuck on a problem and need mentorship. Traditionally, timesheets and issue trackers monitor employees by the amount of work they get done, but these tools ignore burnout, which can have drastic effects on productivity and emotional wellbeing. This application aims close the gap between human resources, management and their employees by checking in periodically.

What it does

The application allows users to regularly post 'reactions' to their current tasks. This acts as regular team updates to progress. Managers can monitor overall team wellbeing in real-time, and human resources can quickly identify the problem.
