Inspiration

We wanted to use Ethereum and cryptocurrency to provide a way for local governments to invest in their residents' education.

What it does

Learning Bee provides a way for local governments and organizations to mint and distribute tokens to users who watch educational content and complete bonus quizzes. The users can then redeem their tokens for sick rewards such as transit fares and community centre credit.

Technology Used

Burner wallet is used to store unclaimed tokens. Fortmatic is used to claim tokens.
