DevCommons consist of two developer zone and community solutions namely DevFITAID and Duaqs

DevFITAID is an application for developers or any potential developer out there looking to enhance their programming skills. It is meant for personal use for daily workouts.

Duaqs–that’s “Squad” spelled backwards– is a messaging and collaboration tool for developers.

More details on Github: https://github.com/mmaro/devcommons
