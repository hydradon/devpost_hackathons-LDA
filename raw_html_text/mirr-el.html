Inspiration

Mirrel, powered by Mirro: For high performance teams that want to add a hint of color to their daily routine. Mirro encourages a culture of feedback inside companies that cultivate shared values and open communication.

Mirrel is the liason between the online world of team dynamics and the real world of self validation, encouraging free expression and creativity at the workplace.

What it does

Friendly autonomous robot for fast announcements dispatchment, offering a fun & engaging alternative to spreading sugar, spice and a touch of magic inside companies that use mirro.

You can count on Mirrel to locate teams around the office and share out loud kudos given in mirro.io !

What's next for Mirrel

Functionalities can be extended to various scenarios that include:

  • make public announcements more fun and engaging
  • test to speech capabilities
  • gathering feedback after meetings
  • nudging your colleagues to give you constructive feedback
  • connect with Outlook calendar and announce company events & gatherings
  • recognizing colleagues while interacting with them
  • finding colleagues around the office
  • make special deliveries around the office (who wants a beer? )
