Inspiration

We wanted to create an app that was going to help women feel safe by having a quick and discrete way to contact an emergency contact.

What it does

This app displays three colors menu items that correspond to the level of notification desired. When the menu item is selected and email is sent to the emergency contact.

How we built it

We coded in c to talk to the watch. We wanted to show three windows: the splash window, main window, and the selection window. From the selection window we hard coded in an email to send . We got the phone to connect to email with java script.

Challenges we ran into

All of our group members had different coding backgrounds which effected our decision to code for wearable technology which none of us had used before. Furthermore, the resources were limited for Pebble as well as the development community was not as developed.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We're proud of coming up with a discrete way of helping women in troubling situations. We are also proud that we were able to work on a wearable technology that none of us had used before.

What we learned

We learned how to develop in a platform that we had never used before.
