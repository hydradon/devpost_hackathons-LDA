Food-Mood

Tinder for restaurants

Have you ever spent too much time deciding which restaurant to eat at? Look no further, food-mood is a quick and easy solution. Just enter a query (can be cuisine, food, etc.) and swipe left or right on restaurants as they show up one by one. When you've swiped right three times, you will be asked to pick a restaurant or have the app generate a random one

APIs Used

Google Places - GeoDataClient, PlaceDetection Google Maps - Location Services
