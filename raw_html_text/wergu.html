weRgu

Twitter is a platform where people can share their opinions or stories instantly at any time. As it is with people, not everyone agrees with everyone else's opinion. weRgu was created out of the need to provide a platform for constructive debates about tweets regarding a certain topic.

weRgu uses NLP techniques to analyze the sentiment of tweets regarding a user-specified topics. Once these tweets are analyzed, they are presented to the user, along with a chat box, which allows them to converse with other users in real-time (using Firebase). The entire web app is hosted on Azure.

This impacts the way people socialize. weRgu takes discussions that would occur in a malicious fashion over social media and acts as a non-biased mediator between multiple people so that they can have a discussion in a calm fashion while not clogging up anyone's timeline!
