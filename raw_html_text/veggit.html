Inspiration

We wanted to give users the opportunity to find unique fruits and vegetable either because they are bored of their everyday foods or they want to become healthier.

What it does

The home page displays the current top rated fruit and vegetable The random fruit or veggies pages will give you nutritional information, helpful tips, fun facts, and where to buy. Additionally, you can rate the fruit/veggie if you have already tried it before The member page stores your favorite fruits/veggies

How we built it

Wix and javascript

Challenges we ran into

scoping issues

Accomplishments that we're proud of

the aesthetic and easy use of the website

What we learned

Wix is not great because it does not allow use of php causing us to be very restricted
