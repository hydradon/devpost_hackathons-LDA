• Panny is the future of travelling.

  • Panny lets you know how long more you have during the flight.

  • Panny is also able to know which are the available restrooms, and notify you when it is empty.

  • Avoid awkward moments with the flight attendant and order your food via Panny.

  • Nearing the end of the flight, Panny lets you know where is your next connecting flight, so you won't feel like a lost sheep after alighting

What it does

An intelligent virtual flight attendant that provides travellers with a personalized and comfortable flight experience built with Panasonic InFlight API.

Problem we are trying to solve

Sometimes talking to a flight attendant might take time to attend to us, and does not provide immediate feedback. With a virtual personal assistant living within the In Flight entertainment system, we can let Panny do some of the work for them!
