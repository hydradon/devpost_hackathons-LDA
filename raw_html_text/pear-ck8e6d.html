Inspiration

  • People with wealth gap and no access to tuition

  • Knowledge ecosystem

What it does

Pear matches prospective students to knowledgeable tutors, and encourages all users to participate by incentivising knowledge sharing and acting as a tutor through an internal economy.

How we built it

ReactJS with Materialize CSS frontend

Django + Django-REST-Framework backend, SQLlite database
