Inspiration

International fishing law gives constraints to which fish fishermen can legally keep depending on the species and length of the fish. Unfortunately, for one man fishing operations around the world, it's almost impossible to comply with regulations, because you need one pair of hands to hold the fish, one pair to measure the fish, and one pair to write down statistics.

What it does
