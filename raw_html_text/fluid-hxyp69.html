Inspiration

I tried to paint with water on the Processing canvas first. The animation of water failed, and led me to the simulation of particles and use Myo to control their moments.

What it does

The feedback information from Myo that I use is orientation and gesture. Orientation controls the position of the flock of particles and the gesture is used for signals of zoom in and zoom out.

How I built it

Using algorithms for particle generations, and Myo signals for controlling.

Challenges I ran into

Convert the roll, pitch, yaw into x, y, z coordinates, sending signals with Myo to control the motions.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

What I learned

This is my first time in hacksthon, and I learned to use processing for the first time.
