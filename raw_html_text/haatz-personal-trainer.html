Inspiration

As our group is formed of people who are keen on sports (gym too) and we can't afford a personal trainer, we thought that this might be a good application for other people like us.

What it does

The application can be used in the gym or home to exercise.

How we built it

It is a mobile application, made in Android Studio with Java.

Challenges we ran into

We wanted to make a small device, but due to the lack of hardware, we had to use the mobile.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

None of us used Android Studio before or developed any mobile software; and one of us didn't use Java before either.

What we learned

We learned how to develop a mobile application.

What's next for Haatz Personal Trainer

Haatz Personal Trainer has space for a lot of other functions, like aerobic exercise, complete all the group muscles and add more bodybuilding exercises.
