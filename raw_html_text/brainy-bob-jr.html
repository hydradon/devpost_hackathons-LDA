Inspiration

Robots are really fun, but alas they may take over the world someday.

What it does

Brainy Bob Jr is a robot that moves. When he senses something is in front of him, he spins and turns right.

How I built it

Brainy Bob Jr is built using MakeBlock and an Arduino board

Challenges I ran into

It was tough to get Bob to turn.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

When we finally got Bob to turn!

What I learned

Screws are hard to screw in. We learned how to use Bob to show temperature.
