What is Company Simulator?

  • A game app that simulates the player as the CEO of a new company.

  • The objective of the game is to grow the company by balancing investments in hiring more workers, advertisements, and upgrading the infrastructure of the company.

How to Play

  • Players have to manage their investments and make a profit while also dealing with random events such as sudden booms or busts in the market and stock market crashes.

  • Players will have to make sure they can make enough income to profit while paying for worker costs, upkeep costs of their building, and pay for taxes that scale with their income level.

Our Objective for This Game

  • This game is meant to teach players to manage their money and make smart decisions to increase their profit.

  • For example, players can decide if it is more efficient to hire more workers or upgrade their building in order to make more money.

  • While playable by everyone, our target audience would be young adults who have never had to manage more than just spending or saving their allowance.

Our Inspiration

  • We learned that many people our age do not know how to manage their money at all and we wanted to make a fun way for people to learn at least a little bit of money management.

Problems We Ran Into and What We Learned

  • We ran into issues displaying the text on screen and we ran out of time to include everything we wanted.
  • We learned that organization is key to completing a project of this scale.
  • We also learned many AndEngine Functions.

Future Installments

  • In the future we wanted to add additional options to invest in other companies and work on different types of infrastructure upgrades.
