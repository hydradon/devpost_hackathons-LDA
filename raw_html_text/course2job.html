Inspiration

Course2Job helps people to develop valuable skills through providing information on online courses in the form of a news letter.

What it does

Users receive tailored emails periodically and based on their responses and click rates, more information is sent related to what users are interested in. Based on user responses, job opportunities relative to a particular skill they are learning will be then sent through another news letter.

How I built it

We use Sparkpost messaging platform for sending and monitoring users interaction with our email newsletters.

Challenges I ran into

Setting up web hosting.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Developed an useful app.

What I learned

Learned about Sparkpost, its API and Python SDK.

What's next for Course2job

Improve job recommendations by connecting directly through company job portals.
