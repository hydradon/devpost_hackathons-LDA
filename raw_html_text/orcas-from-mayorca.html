Inspiration

Last year my team abandoned me with out even saying goodbay. This time i found wonderful team that managed to stay till the end, and was very interested on making this idea happen.

What it does

It uses android camera to capture a photo of a table with markers. Then photo is sent to server, and server processes it with computer vision, and hosts the picture. It is possible to request picture and coordinates of markers.
