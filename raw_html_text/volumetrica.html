Inspiration

With the rise of 5G, we are building the next iteration of MTV!!

What it does

Capturing the artists in 3D volumetric camera rig, we display it using augmented reality technology.

How we built it

We downloaded the captured video from open source platform for prototype purposes. Then, we created scenes around the volumetric models so that users can swap scenes through swiping. We have also did appropriate product placement to drive additional revenue for the artists.

Challenges we ran into

debugging Unity, finding the volumetric video and 3D content

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We even fit in Game of Thrones into our pitch! This app can only be achieved through 5G.

What we learned

Volumetric videos with AR technology

What's next for Volumetrica

Demoing to different record companies and hopefully get our first record on board!
