What my Alexa Skill does

Open magical surprise boxes on the Echo Show and Echo Spot!

It's free to open these surprise boxes and explore the cool toys you'll find inside!

Use this Skill on the Echo Show and see the presents unwrapping before your eyes. Fun for kids of all ages!

Inspiration

Because I'm older than the intended age range for this hackathon's user audience, I decided to invest the time into doing some user research to figure out what kinds of Alexa Skills kids may want. After talking to a few kids and their parents, many kids expressed enjoying watching unboxing videos and liked getting presents, however, parents were hesitant to allow their kids to watch unboxing videos.

How I built it

I used Javascript as my language and I created the unboxing animations in After Effects. The imagery came from icons I licensed for this project.

Challenges I ran into

Developing Skills for display-enabled Alexa devices is still relatively new, so there aren't a lot of examples out there to reference.

Accomplishments and learnings I'm proud of

This is my first Skill I've shipped that is optimized for display-enabled Alexa devices.
