Residence App

Platform that allows residents to share a qr code with friends so they can acces the private residence in a faster way.

It works by generating a link so the guest can open it and a qr code will be created with his information. The guard will scan at the entrance and he now has the data of the guest, so they don't waste time asking questions and writing down the answers.
