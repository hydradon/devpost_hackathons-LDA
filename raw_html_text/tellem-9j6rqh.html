Inspiration

Brands are spending top dollar on artificial influencer content . Real consumers are already talking - just not heard within the brands' target group . Consumers use social media to tell their network about brands and products they love. So far, marketers can not reap the maximum benefit from these contents . On the other hand, users have more to gain from their brand advocacy .

What it does

In its current stage, our service offers brands a way to select tweets of signed up users and promote them on their behalf within the brand's target group.

How we built it

  • Ruby on Rails 5.0.0
  • Twitter API
  • Twitter Ads API
  • Susy frontend framework

What we learned

Twitter ads API is pretty well documented and straightforward to use.

What's next for tellem

Possibly integration of all media types from Twitter, Instagram , Facebook , Pinterest .
