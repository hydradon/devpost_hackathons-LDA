A mobile-friendly website that can store and retrieve information on public services to assist people in housing transit. The goal is to make it easier to find friendly, clean, and safe facilities for the target users using crowd-sourced data.

  • Showers
  • Bathrooms
  • Phone Charging
  • Wifi

Created in response to a challenge issued by the 2017 Portland Hackathon.
