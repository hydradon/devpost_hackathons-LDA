Team Number

Our team number is 18.

Inspiration

When we arrived here we had no clue about what to do. Some of us suggested an app, others a videogame, and we ended up making a musical videogame.

What it does

It gives the user a sequence of notes and he/she tries to remember them without knowing exactly which notes they are.

How we built it

We built it using Unity, some graphical editors and other minor programs.

Challenges we ran into

Phone features implementation, we hadn't developed for mobile before. Also lack of knowledge about some of Unity's functionalities.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Completion of the app, all that we learned and our teamwork. Our first completed app.

What we learned

Unity, C#, coding organization, collaborative working and teamwork.

What's next for Music Training

We will expand the functionalities of the app, organize the project more and implement better artwork and more gamemodes. We will improve the app and then publish it.
