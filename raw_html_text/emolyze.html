Inspiration

The TV series 13 Reasons Why

What it does

Our system is able to do face and emotions recognition for people dataset. The system is able to warning the appropriate authorities about situations according to the configurations that was previously given. For example: determine major depression by the configurations that was taken from the American Psychiatric Association.

How I built it

I used two libraries, one for face recognition and the other for emotions recognition and I combined them. moreover I used AWS DynamicDB in order to save the data was extern from the camera.

Challenges I ran into

Combining the two libraries of the face and emotions recognition was quite a challenge. Learn in such a short time those libraries, DynamicDB and couple of more small thing was a challenge too.
