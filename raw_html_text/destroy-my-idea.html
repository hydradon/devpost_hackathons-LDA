Inspiration

After coming up with countless ideas they all continued to fall down when it came to competitor analysis. Disheartened as Unihack was in full swing, we joked about how it would be more productive if there was a website where you could post and idea and people could link you to other apps/solutions/ideas/youtube videos that showed your idea had already been created insert light bulb moment

Thus Destroy My Idea was born, out of frustration for the lack of original ideas, we did a competitor analysis and found there was no dedicated service for this idea. Sure there was reddit and rate my start up idea, they required a lot of energy just to put a post together that would get decent amount of feedback, and the UX was terrible.

The other thing that was lacking is they were often targeted towards start-ups rather than general ideas, which means that our service allows you to rapidly prototype apps, start-ups, book ideas practically anything and get feedback on it.

How it works

Our web site offers three services:

  • Instant Feedback: Instantly gain feedback from users across the world. Discover where your product lacks in innovation or doesn’t fit the markets needs. No limit to what post, lightweight designs, app concepts or start-up ideas. If you can think it we can tell you it’s a terrible idea.

  • Crowd-Sourced Competitor Analysis: Get insight into what competition exists and use this information to calculate market positioning and strategic management. With wide variety you will be equipped with the knowledge needed to position yourself strategically and identify opportunities and potential threats. Exploit strangers around the world and pay them nothing to do your dirty work.

  • Identify Issues: Identify where your product, start-up idea or app may have issues often overlooked by those closest to it, practice your Shark Tank pitch – get feedback and when you win send us a share of your seed funds.
