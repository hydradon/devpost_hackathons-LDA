Inspiration

Our inspiration was that we all like the current memes that are being shared everywhere and are so trendy, but what about the memes that get classfied as 'dead'. Who is to say those memes are not still funny? Would it not be great to still have access to seeing these memes and even categorizing them by month and year. Well, memecemetery.com is where you can visit these memes and pay a laughing tribute to them.
