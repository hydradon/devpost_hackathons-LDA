What it does

imports data
  about various events/parties/performances/festivals
      related with arts/culture/entertainment;
  from external websites
      like Facebook/Meetup.com/Eventbrite/Festicket;
  or from personal contribution
      bringing WordOfMouth info into Buddy.com website/mobile;

helps people to
  find the events that seem interesting
      find buddies to go to those events together,
      easily get updated with exciting events/performances;
  meet a community of similar interests
      find fellows in similar tastes,
      expand networks;

promote the industries of
  art/culture/music/dance/film/theater/entertainment;
