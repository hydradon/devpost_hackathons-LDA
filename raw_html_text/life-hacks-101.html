Inspiration

So we see a lot of people struggling in our daily life just for completing small tasks which are very small and easy. But they don't know the proper way to do that or there might exist a strange yet very efficient approach to doing that task which they might not know yet.

What it does

My Alexa skill tells some really cool life hacks, that can make our life way easier. These are simple but really cool hacks which reduce your effort doing a simple task. For example, ironing a collar of the shirt may seem a difficult task to someone but a simple life hack can save time. There are similar yet useful life hacks in my skill.
