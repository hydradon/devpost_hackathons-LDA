Inspiration

Ever since the Citizens United court case, I've been yearning to give a voice to the up and coming corporate artists, so I'm creating the biggest concert ever!!!!!!!!!

What it does

It prompts the user to enter a stock price and then makes a song based on their historical stock prices

How I built it

I built a web app using Flask and jquery. I used Quandl and MarkIt On Demand APIs as well as many open source libraries for music

Challenges I ran into

Admitting companies are more talented at making music than me

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Giving a voice to a minority

What I learned

I became much more comfortable with using 3rd party APIs
