Inspiration

I live with my grandparents, and my grandpa really struggles with remembering to take his medicine. A lot of medication management apps on the market require you to type in manually the name, instructions, dosage etc., but most medicines have long complicated names that aren't easy to type out. Also if you're on a lot of different medications, it takes a lot of time to get set up.

What it does

Scans prescription pages, parses for relevant information, generates reminders for when to take the medicine.

How we built it

In XCode working in Swift.

Challenges we ran into

Getting the notifications to work.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The OCR working was pretty dope.

What we learned

Swift/XCode has a lot of weird undocumented that are a pain to fix.

What's next for pill pal

Polishing up the emergency contact system. Adding support for other major pharmacies.
