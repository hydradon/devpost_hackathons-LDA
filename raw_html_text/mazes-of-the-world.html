I thought it would be fun to create a voice game that challenges your memory and makes you think. I believe this game challenges you mentally. I enjoy puzzles and games that challenge my mind. Mazes of the World will challenge you to remember the direction you are going in while also remembering the number of moves you have taken. If you haven't completed a maze in a certain number of moves then you are on the wrong path and you need to reassess where you are. All the while you need to avoid traps as you are going through the maze. If you fall into a trap you will go back to the beginning of the maze.For a consumable purchase you can escape the trap and go back to your previous position. There also is a subscription option.
