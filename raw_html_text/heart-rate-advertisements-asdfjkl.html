Inspiration

First, we saw teletubbies. Then, we wanted to see more teletubbies.

What it does

When your heart rate increases, the advertisements in the relevant categories are shown. Otherwise the category is skipped.

How I built it

Magic. The Kinect sensor detects your heart rate. CSV file contains productID, Name, Price, Discounted_price, and category ID. The math under the hood using R statistical libraries determines your heart rate based on changes in the infrared sensor. Different categories for advertisements are included, including electronics, clothes, general, food, stationary.
