Inspiration

In 2014 the Super Bowl had a light show that blown my mind. I saw how this technology could advance and make it an immersive experience for any show/concert. Years have passed and I haven't seen adoption of technology like this and I want to know what would it take to build a cheap light wearable device. Think of IoT controlled glow-sticks/bracelets.

What it does

Lights up in at least 1 color given a queue or condition (ie. musical beats, intro outro, location)

How will I build it

1+ LED 1 Bluetooth module 1 Bluetooth emitter
