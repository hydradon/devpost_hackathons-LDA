Inspiration

We are Inspired for solve the problems of traffic vehicular like traffic jam and wrong control of traffic lights in the cruises caused by an old and unsuccessful System for traffic control. Our mission is improve the Traffic Control in the cities, converting in the best and efficient cities with a good quality of life. Our goal is reduce unnecessary expenses of gasoline, save transportation time, reduce the environment contamination and solve all problems caused by wrong traffic control.

What it does

It makes more efficient the traffic that we live in Guadalajara, controlling traffic lights depending the users, walkers and drivers. Also the system is intelligent which means that it learns in the march.
