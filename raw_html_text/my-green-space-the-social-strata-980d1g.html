How we built it

Social, useful and easy to navigate. That were our key words for this project. Our target is simply to allow the user find what he wants in one look.

For this reason, we chose a opened menu, showing all the navigation options in a few clicks.

The visual language was inspired in the most popular social and messaging apps. All to ease the learning of the new users.
