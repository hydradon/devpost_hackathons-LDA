Inspiration

Alot of older people especially those with alzheimers/ dementia often get lost

What it does

installing the app on the phone you can set up to 5 contacts on the app. If the old person is lost someone whos helping them or they themselves just need to push the big red button on the app and a text message will be sent out to the 5 contacts with the exact location of the phone.
