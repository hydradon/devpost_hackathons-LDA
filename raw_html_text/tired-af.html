What it does

Tracks your energy level in real time via brainwave monitoring.

If you're getting tired, it will feed you caffeine or makes you exercise to ensure you're operating at 100% productivity at all times.

It also serves as an alarm so if you choose to take a nap in the middle of your work, it will ensure that you wake up on time as your Pebble alarm won't shut off until you're truly awake via motion monitoring.
