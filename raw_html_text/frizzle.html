Inspiration

People waste too much time on sites they go too only because they are idle or bored.

How it works

When you visit sites that you usually waste time on, Frizzle suggests alternate sites to visit. Our friend Franklin the Turtle swims into the page, holding a suggestion for other places you should go.

What I learned

I learned a lot about formatting elements arbitrarily located on web pages and interfacing across them using a Chrome Extension. We also utilized machine learning to improve article suggestions based on user preferences.

What's next for Frizzle

Next will be even more accessibility and controls for people to pursue additional information in their daily lives, within time that would otherwise be wasted staring through a screen.
