Breadth is a cross-platform web app developed to bring environmental concerns to the public. Breadth uses a variety of government and private API data to visualize location-specific water/air/disease reports.

Breadth was the result of three UC Merced students concerns over our environment and the lack of information the general public has access to. Years of uneducated abuse of our planet has caused significant damages, one being the effect on our health. Breadth uses location-based health and environmental data with usability, interface and social impact at the core of its design.
