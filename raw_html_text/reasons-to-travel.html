Inspiration

One of the most important life hacks you can have these days are ones that improve your health. And few things are as healthy as taking a trip outdoors.But it's so hard to actually get to it! I felt having a reason to get out, that outshines the great excuses we come up with to not, would be incredibly helpful.

What it does

Every time you invoke Reasons To Travel, it responds with (you guessed it!) a great reason to get out and have fun!

How I built it

This was built using a python backend hosted on AWS Lambda, and the Alexa Skills Kit.

Challenges I ran into

Getting great reasons to travel, of course! But with a bit of help from the world wide web, I was able to find some pretty convincing ones.

What's next for Reasons To Travel

Adding more and more reasons to travel!
