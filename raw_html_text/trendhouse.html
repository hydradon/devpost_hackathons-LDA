• Acceleration ecosystem with TrendHouse

Inspiration

A quantum house to find my tastes and trends in music and more

What it does

Making a place to find your trends of music, films and restaurants with the security of a distributed ledger

How I built it

Using corda as a tool to generate tokens and a 'data blob' where my trends are stored. Using the api of qloo to connect the data of music and film and restaurants

Challenges I ran into

The connection between trends, the ethical debate of security

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The hyper-acceleration of customer experiences A token to connect artist to customer and put tags of space and time to create market trends

What I learned

The complexities of data privacy

What's next for TrendHouse

More validation of the model with different APIs
