Inspiration

We are inspired by the idea to economically empower the transgender community and widows by providing them stitching opportunities.

What it does

An online platform where we are providing online stitching services and employment. People can visit and select clothes of all varieties. We will especially focus on the quality of the product to provide relief to the customers and will also provide the opportunity of stitching their clothes, of which style and Design they want with quality tailors. The measurements of their clothes will be saved with us, so next time, when they want to stitch their clothes, these measurements will be useful. The phenomenon of AR (Augmented reality) will be used to get their measurements it takes a scan of the body using the phone’s camera.

How we built it

Mobile application for Android and IOS with React Native and Website
