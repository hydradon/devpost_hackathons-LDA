Observers and Drawers listen up! Your mission is to replicate the masterpiece you see before you. (Well, fake it till you make it, right?)

Observers! Toggle on your photographic memory, for you have to memorise the design and verbalise it to the Drawers.

Drawers! Listen hard to the observers, and replicate the painting. Remember - you can only paint one colour in each phase.

Oh one last tip! The more the colours, the higher the multiplier on the score. Fortune favours the brave.May the best replica win!
