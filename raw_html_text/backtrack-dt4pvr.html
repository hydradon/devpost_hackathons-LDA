BackTrack

Android app devoted to helping users keep track of important events. If you found a new favorite restaurant but forgot its name or you just need to remember a special event for a special day, BackTrack can help make your life easier. With this app, users can keep record of the time, date, location, and description of any kind of event that they participate in. They do not have to worry about forgetting anything again!
