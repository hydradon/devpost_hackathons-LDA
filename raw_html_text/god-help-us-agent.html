GodHelpUs

A truly great mobile app should come with top-notch customer support. But for most of today's app developers, integrating customer support into their apps is still a manual and complicated process. GodHelpUs gives mobile app developer the ability to provide emotionally intelligent customer support in their app through an easily embeddable component. Through the embedded component, the user can get help from a live customer service agent. GodHelpUs intercepts each message, analyzes the message's emotional characteristics, and provide the customer service agent recommendation on the best way to respond. With GodHelpUs, the mobile app user no longer has to feel lost and helpless.
