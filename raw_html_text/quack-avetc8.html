Inspiration

We noticed that in everyday life people don't talk to each other anymore... Everybody is communicating and interacting primarily with people alike him. We are all living inside our own filter-bubbles.

We want to change this!

What it does

With Quack we provide an app that enables everyone in an urban environment to use his time on public transport to get to know people.

Once enabled, Quack looks for other users around that are also willing to have a conversation with a person they dont know, yet. In case there is a "match", both are notified and have the opportunity to chat, in order to arrange to meet.

How we built it

We used firebase and Google Cloud Functions for the backend and built the frontend using the ionic framework.
