Inspiration

People in Tallahassee need one place to access all the different events, parks, trails and outdoor activities we have here. Cars are a big problem in our world, so we wanted to make these resources accessible to everyone & connect them to the alternative transit options to get them there.

What it does

This is an app that allows users to choose what they're interested in, browse events or locations where they can participate in these activities, and links them with the weather and transit information about each opportunity.

How I built it

Angular, Ionic, JavaScript and the Trailahassee, ArcGIS, and WeatherSTEM API's

Challenges I ran into

We had never built an app before, so it was very challenging.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We did it!

What I learned

We learned the ionic framework, how to integrate great API's to make Tallahassee better, and that naps fix everything.

What's next for Trail-Hacks-App

Keep getting better and getting people more engaged with our city!
