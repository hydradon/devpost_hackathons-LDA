• Tracking multiple people at once.

Inspiration

While playing sports non-competitively just on the street, its difficult to keep track of what is happening. Currently it is only a desktop application.

What it does

This program allows to user to utilize any camera at their disposal to monitor a game in real time and can be used to provide definitive results.

How I built it

The people tracking is done by detecting moving objects which are the players and it finds the shapes that would most resemble human dimensions.

What's next for Real Time People Tracker

In the future this can be extended to more sports and to the mobile platform.
