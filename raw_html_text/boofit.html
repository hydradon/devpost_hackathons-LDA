Inspiration

A research paper a teammate and I discovered during our math capstone.

What it does

It simulates this experiment with the user. They choose how much they want to wager, in exchange for a larger starting amount and then play the game to its conclusion.

How we built it

Reach-Native, Expo, and lots of patience.

Challenges we ran into

React-Native is its own challenge. We also had to make a prototype and figure out how to pass data between screens and make asynchronous calls to functions.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to make a minimally viable product.

What we learned

We learned a lot about react-native, navigating pages and handling data in JavaScript.

What's next for BoofIt

In the future we would like to make a fully fledged app. Complete with better styling, actual account management and possible cryptocurrency tokens users can play with.
