Inspiration

Using leap to take Rock, Paper and Scissors to the next level.

What it does

Uses client's hand gestures to play Rock, Paper and Scissors with other people. It also allows the users to put a wager on the match and pay each other using Venmo after the winner is adjudged.

How I built it

Using pure JavaScript frameworks

Challenges I ran into

Accuracy of the sensor when identifying the hand gestures and the game's dynamics within a web application

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We kept going regardless of obstacles

What I learned

It's more important to have fun during the development process

What's next for Leap-RPS

A comprehensive analysis of one's performance and earnings through a universal leaderboard. Turn Rock, Paper and Scissors into an upcoming sport.
