Inspiration

We think data is beautiful, and we wanted to help monitor our air quality. We attended the Louisville Open City Data workshop and thought the APIs looked cool

What it does

An Android app that displays the local AQI, or 'Air Quality Index' for health concerns. It can also calculate your average distance from 311 service calls all around the city.

How we built it

Used Android Studio to build the app which uses various APIs from Louisville's Open Data website.

Challenges we ran into

Importing data, the APIs were very inconsistent with how they returned their data.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Got to finish our project!

What we learned

Learned about and used Louisville's Open Data project, which was really cool.

What's next for Air Pollution Indicator

Maybe better UI, or more interesting data visualizations/options.
