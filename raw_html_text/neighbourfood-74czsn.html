Inspiration

Eating-out is always expensive and inspiring cooks don't have the money to open a restaurant. What if we merge two market and let users dine at people's homes for affordable price and cooks can earn extra income.

What it does

Think of this as Airbnb for dining

A local community that lets you enjoy authentic dining experiences in people's homes in your neighbourhood.

Cooks can focus on their cooking, neighbourfood will take care of their operation by giving them live support and take-out material

Chef can expand their reputation and brand through our app without investing in a local restaurants

Diners can eat healthy meal instead of eating process food from restaurants

For health issue, we would send food and health inspector

People in the suburb don't have access to local restaurants, they can discover new foods through neighbourfood and meet their neighbour

Book a private meal with the best home cooks
