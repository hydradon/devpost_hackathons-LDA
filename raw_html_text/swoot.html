ICE Heat Map is a crowdsourced heat-map of live ICE activity and an ICE activity alert system.

ICE is the U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement, and are responsible for finding and handling deportation of undocumented immigrants today.

Immigrants living in America today live in fear of encountering ICE agents.

Our app populates live ICE raids on a heat map based on user reports.

We then have users sign up to receive text or calls to alert them if theres ICE activity in their area.
