  • The sad reality

It all started when we’ve watched a video clip on the young boy Omran from Aleppo, Syria who looked suffering with a bloodied and a dusty face while sitting in an ambulance, so shocked he couldn't cry. That kid reminded us of all the kids that are suffering on the other side of the world. The team members therefore decided to make a Game that can serve as a wake call to everyone around the world. The next step is to add occasional ads, generating income which can be donated to charities and humanitarian organizations.
