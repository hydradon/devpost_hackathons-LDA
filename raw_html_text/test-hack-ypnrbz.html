This is an html hack for hackBCA III - A simple web site built with html about myself.
It's the "new and improved" version of an html site I programmed over 3 years ago , and I'm very happy with the progress. Upon doing this hackathon, I realized that nothing was motivating me to make something highly technical, but I still had the power of web design to guide me. To me, creating art is central to my self-expression, and a site having a good design is also important to me while surfing the web. Most sites nowadays are business-oriented, whether people make revenue off of advertisements or are selling a product. Why can't we build more sites that are just about ourselves? This site is pseudo-interactive , meaning any interactions placed on the site don't actually go anywhere.
