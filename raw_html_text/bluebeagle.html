Welcome to BlueBeagle.

A website designed to answer your questions and provide you with the resources and information you need to identify, prevent, and cope with addiction. Whether you or someone close to you is at risk of overdosing on opioids, BlueBeagle provides you the information you need. We created a website which is mobile first in design to have the broadest reach. We have designed the site to be as easy to use. With features like color coordination to keep you oriented, tabs for each type of research you are doing, and links to external information, this is a comprehensive site to help keep you aware and informed about opioid overdose.

FIVE STEPS is a tab which lays out explicit directions of what to do in case of a medical emergency.

BLUEBEAGLE EXPLORER enables the user to search resources by category in either a general or specific manner. Additionally, it provides two types of searches with direct links to these resources.

OVERDOSE RECOVERY provides resources for overdose patients and their loved ones. It provides support groups and networks for support, education, as well as medical and faith-based facilities.

SAFETY ADVICE explains opioids and how to prevent overdosing on them. You will also find data on side effects and how to proceed should you or someone around you experience them.

FACTS FOR COMMUNITY gives general facts about opioid addiction and other resources.

USE CASE has starting points for someone who wants to start using the website.

Family, friends, and caregivers of opioid users can find everything they need to recognize, prevent, and treat overdose with BlueBeagle. They will also find additional resources for networks and groups that can help provide support and education. We believe that these components and the way we have presented them on the site provide everything needed to get to the information quickly and in an intuitive manner.
