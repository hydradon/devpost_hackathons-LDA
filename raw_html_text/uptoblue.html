UpToBlue

** With UpToBlue It's All Up To You **

UpToBlue is the ideal control system for a parent who needs sanity on a long car trip, a teacher who would like their students to pay attention, or anyone who is performing sensitive work. For example, if you're in a car and your two kids in the backseat have their phone turned way up, and the glare from their backlights is obscuring the rear window, you can just dim those screens from the dashboard.

UpToBlue is a bluetooth control system for cell phones and other devices. From a master controller, a user can perform actions such as muting specific devices (or all devices), changing screen brightness, and disabling/enabling sms and voice communications as needed.
