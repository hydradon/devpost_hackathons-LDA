Inspiration

We wanted to make an app that would allow for more natural relationships to develop between users. We were tired of the negativity associated with the current crop of dating apps and wanted to attack it by making conversation fun, flirty, and still, fulfilling.

What it does

First, it matches you with a person that is similar to you in personality. Then, it provides you with quirky pickup lines and a nice topic to start with.

How I built it

Used Cloud9's IDE. HTML+JS+CSS for front end and Node.js for backend.

Challenges I ran into

Finding good pickup lines. The balance between appropriate and interesting was difficult to achieve.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Finding a neat way to understand someone's personality.

What I learned

How to use node.js to make cool backends.

What's next for Fumble

Better personality training by using neural nets.
