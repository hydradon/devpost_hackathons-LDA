Inspiration

8 out of 10 Australians believe loneliness is increasing; issue of tackling the growing role of technology as a method of socialising

60% of Australians reported they often feel lonely

Individuals travelling alone may experience episodes of loneliness. Taking the same route to and from work/school, having someone’s company goes a long way.

What it does

Never make the same journey again Travel Buddies provides a platform for individuals seeking companionship to provide company for each other during their regular/spontaneous travels.

Connects people travelling alone, whether they travel on a train, bus or on foot. Easier to find time to connect with others as they are not going out of their way to seek companionship Facilitate greater social encounters and alleviate boredom associated with solo travels Provides a safety mechanism for vulnerable travellers

React-Native front-end with firebase backend
