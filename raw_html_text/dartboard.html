Dartboard is a website that aims to generate your ideal trip made especially for you. Just simply enter the location where you will be staying, the duration of your stay, and how long you want to spend touring each day. The site will then calculate the best places to visit every day for the entire trip, accounting for attraction ratings and travel time. Dartboard starts with a simple search or a selection from the most popular destinations and ends with a cleverly designed itinerary equipped with links to Bing Maps directions and Yelp reviews for each event of your trip. Finally, planning to travel to a foreign place is simple and stress-free. Dartboard utilizes Flask, Bootstrap, Jinja2, jQuery, Bing Maps API and Yelp API.
