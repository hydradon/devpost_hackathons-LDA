digital solutions dedicated for farmers face a lot of obstacles like illiteracy and digital ignorance.. and from this point, I have got inspiration to make flexible digital solutio that overcome these obstacles. Fellah is an audio-based social media platform dedicated to farmers, and agriculture services providers, it make using application easy for newbies throughout a minimalist design and taking advantage of audio content.

What's next for Fellah

My aim is to expand Fellah impact on farmers by decreasing illiteracy and empowering their digital presence.
