Inspiration

Even after years of technological advancement and medical innovation, Cancer remains a threat disrupting lives around us. We hope to provide some respite to survivors and ongoing patients through our game which would give them a feeling of empowerment over Cancer.

What it does

The game involves the player's character to shrink and travel inside the body, hunt down and BLAST away cancerous cells ! Using Virtual Reality, we aim to give the player a truly immersive and almost realistic experience.

How we built it

We have not submitted an actual working game due to our lack of background in Game Development, however, please consider the power point presentation we have included in the submission - we have tried to give a good idea about the layout of the game.

Challenges we ran into

The problem we are trying to resolved was a great challenge in itself, since we wanted to come up with something that can really cater to the emotions of Cancer patients and sympathizers.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

  • Creating a means for motivation, a sense of feel-good for current Cancer patients

  • Giving Cancer survivors a chance to relive the triumphant feeling of overcoming the ordeal

What we learned

Mentally and emotionally healing Cancer patients is as crucial as physical treatment, so we believe our Game is indeed special in that aspect.

What's next for Cancer Terminator

Exploring other health conditions such as Depression, Anxiety and trying to find similar Gaming solutions to tackle them.
