Inspiration: 

 The DJ Wave project was inspired by a group of new young hakers that wanted to create a project in 24 hours

that was new and fresh.

What it does 

 The DJ Wave is essentially an advanced jukebox. Place your hand over the sensor and move your hand up and

down to select a song. To pause the music simply place your hand close to the sensor until the screen says pause. To turn the volume up or down hold the button and move your hand closer to the music up or move is further way to bring the party down.

How We built it 

Our team built this project using an arduino board, an ultrasonic sensor, the aduino IDE programmed using C, Processing programmed using javascript, an RGB LED, and an LCD screen.

Challenges I ran into 

Without a speaker to connect to the arduino we faced a big challenge. This meant we had to run two different programs at the same time that would use the same data and use our laptop as the speaker.

Accomplishments that We're proud of 

Our team is very proud of having a project that works. We got an entire project up and running within the span of 24 hours that was original and our own.

What We learned 

We learned that it's all about getting your hands dirty. Sometimes you can start with something small that works

and just build from there. Don't worry about the perfect idea at first.

What's next for DJ Wave 

Our team is going to continue to ride this wave and see where it takes us.
