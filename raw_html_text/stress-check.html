Inspiration

With high amounts of stress and mental health issues, especially among college students, the team decided to find a way to help cope with it.

Description

Stress Check has two components. The mental health IOS app is a tool for people under stress to find resources to help themselves. This app has a hotline directory for users to call or text in crisis, emergency, or suicide prevention hotlines. The GSR sensor is controlled by Arduino Uno to monitor and graph changes in stress levels.

Built With
