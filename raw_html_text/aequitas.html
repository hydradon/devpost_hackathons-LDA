What it does

Aequitas was inspired by college students on a budget. The program in theory should allow users to budget based on a list of products that they need.

How I built it

Aequitas was written in C# with a MongoDB backend.

Challenges I ran into

With no prior experience with MongoDB and a substantial lack of documentation on c# application interactions with MongoDB , It became a challenge to do simple things like even connecting to the database.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The cohesiveness of my team. :)

What I learned

Gained a deeper understanding of C# and its frameworks,MongoDB, and application structure.

What's next for Aequitas?

The team will to continue to work on Aequitas in hope that it can bring value to the good tech space
