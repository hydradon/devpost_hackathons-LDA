Inspiration

We wanted to build an entirely new chat experience.

The market is flooded with chat apps but they all fail to do one thing: capture your emotions when you see a message.

What it does

Our chap app hides messages until a user taps to view them. When the user does this, the camera turns on and captures the user's expression/feelings. This is then shown to all participants, making it a fun and social app.

How we built it

Build using Node.js, stdlib (serverless), Kotlin (Android app)

Challenges we ran into

The team consists of only two people, so time was an issue.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We built most of the chatting back-end from the ground-up.

What we learned

A lot. Serverless requires different types of thinking.

What's next for Emotichat

Group chat isn't supported yet, and we are hoping to add it in the future.
