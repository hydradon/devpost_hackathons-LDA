Our Idea

We tried to implement computer vision to recognize different medication, then connecting it to a database to provide important details regarding the medication. PharmMed gives the patient a personalized way to track the medication they use for their needs, while being able to connect the records with a doctor.

What we did

We were able to get the website and front end working, as well as implementing Google Maps API to showcase nearby pharmacies in Long Island.

Future Goals

With more time, we would implement a chat bot to talk with doctors in real time, work out bugs in our Google Cloud Vision, utilize OpenFDA's database.
