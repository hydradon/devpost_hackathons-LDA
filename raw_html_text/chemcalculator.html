Inspiration

Many sophomores are struggling with time management in their chem honors class. This app is designed to help do some of the calculations for them to save time.

What it does

It calculates the number of sig figs in a number and the molar mass of a simple chemical

How we built it

we used Android Studio and BlueJ

Challenges we ran into

Calculating molar mass of more complex chemicals with coefficients

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Developed an app in 6 hours
