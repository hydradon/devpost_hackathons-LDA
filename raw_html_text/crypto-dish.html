Inspiration

There are so many fusion dishes waiting to be created. We make it happen in crypto world.

What it does

It tokenize traditional dishes from different cuisine. Make it into fusion dish. You create your own unique fusion dish. People bid the likeness of your dish. Your unique dish could value a lot if most of people like it.

How we built it

We use Ethereum, OpenSea and ERC721.

Challenges we ran into

We got stuck deploying it on testnet

Accomplishments that we're proud of
