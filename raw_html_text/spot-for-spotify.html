Inspiration

We saw an opportunity to work with the Spotify APIs and we were curious to what we could do, and ultimately we made this interesting project.

What it does

Lightweight web application that creates a custom Spotify playlist from sentence input.

How I built it

On Dreamweaver, cloud9, and notepad++

Challenges I ran into

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We figured out how to use bootstrap!

What I learned

We learned how to apply our knowledge of javascript into a practical application.

What's next for Spot for Spotify
