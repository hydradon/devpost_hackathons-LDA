Inspiration

What it does

Delta Voi keeps customers of Delta updated on their flight schedule as well as informs them of events and restaurants in the cities they depart from and arrive to.

How I built it

Html, css, and javascript was used for the frontend. Python and jquery were used for the backend.

Challenges I ran into

We ran into challenges pertaining to connecting the frontend and backend.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We're proud that we worked as a team, explored new areas and learned new material.

What I learned

Customizing a customer's travel experience requires a lot of information and API's.

What's next for Delta Voi

We would love to continue to expand on the options that users can explore for what's going on in the cities they are departing from and arriving to such as languages and time zones.
