Inspiration: Finding good sites for commercial rooftop solar is tough. We make it easy for developers to screen, market, and execute legal agreements for rooftop solar development.

What it does: Simplifies the site acquisition process by starting with Owners who have expressed interest in solar by signing Rooftop Lease Options

Accomplishments that we're proud of: building the first site development tool that takes a few simple criteria and translates them into sights that have signed lease options

What's next for Sun-e-Lease: submitting this concept to the Powerhouse Accelerator
