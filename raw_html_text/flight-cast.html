Inspiration

Apps that contain everything without leaving their application.

What it does

Weather and nearby locations of city depending on flight ticket

How we built it

We used flask as our main framework, and utilized multiple API's(google Maps, OpenWeatherMaps,AAirlines API)

Challenges we ran into

Finding non-proprietary api for our application.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

The things we are most proud of is stitching our project together. Each of us worked on a different part of the application and then combined them together.

What we learned

We learned the importance of having modularity between files, that everything doesn't always workout how its supposed to be, and the way to deal with it is to analyze the problem and adapt to it.

What's next for Flight Cast

We would like to implement way for the User to make accounts, show users their flights purchased while actually implementing a way to show cool historical events/cool things to do in the destination. That way users feel as if the airline really want them to enjoy their stay.
