What it does

This application establishes an efficient connection between those who have a car and those who don't. It allows individuals who are driving to a shopping centre to become in contact with someone who wishes to retrieve shopping items, but doesn't have the means of transportation. The individual driving would be paid by the individual requesting.

How I built it

Use MongoDB as database, Python Flask as backend server, JQuery JS as frontend with Google API Login system is with FB

Challenges I ran into

Creating a connection between the customer and the driver. Whether to have it real time or a schedule.
