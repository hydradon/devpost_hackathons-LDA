Readme

Worked with Leap Motion and Computer vision to create a new innovative way to interact with Bose Soundtouch API speakers. We utilize gestures, and computer vision to effectively and efficiently control the functions of the Bose speaker. By rotating and/or swiping your hands on top of the Bose speakers allow you to change everything from volume to weather aware playlists.
