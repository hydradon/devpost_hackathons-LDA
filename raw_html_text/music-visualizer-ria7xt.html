A web application that visualizes music. https://muvis-app.herokuapp.com/

Welcome to Muvis!

This music visualizer works by streaming songs from Soundcloud. To get started…

  1. In the top left, enter your zip code into the weather drop down (if it’s raining you’ll get a surprise!).
  2. Find a song on Soundcloud and copy the url (or try our choices below).
  3. Paste the url into the text box, push play, and enjoy.
