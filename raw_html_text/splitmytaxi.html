Split My Taxi provides safe, efficient, reliable transit to all Detroit residents, so they can get to school, work, and activities securely, on time, and in an enjoyable, clean environment.

All residents can sign in with Facebook/Google, and share the ride with fellow residents. SplitMyTaxi searches for residents going the same way and lets you share your ride! By using SplitMyTaxi you can share your private car, taxi, or other mode of transportation and save money, reduce traffic congestion, reduce pollution, and meet Awesome Detroit residents!
