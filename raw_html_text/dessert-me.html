Inspiration

As a baking enthusiast, I decided to make this to give myself and other baking hobbyists lots of dessert suggestions.

What it does

You can explore many recipes just by typing in some ingredients you are likely going to use.

How I built it

Ruby on Rails MVC, and Materialize library for front-end aesthetics

Challenges I ran into

API call integration with RoR

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It actually works.

What I learned

RoR! (the basics at least)

What's next for Dessert Me

Enhance search criteria such as the quantities of ingredients, as well as nutritional factors.
