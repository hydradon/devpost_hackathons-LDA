What it does

reHab+ allows one to undergo a series of workout routines for your hands and arms with the help of a Myo device. The connected mobile app stores data in the cloud which can be shared with and further analysed by your physician to oversee the progress your have made in you recovery or rehabilitation.

Inspiration

My Grandma experiences pain getting her hand to grip her glass of coffee. Physicians told her to perform multiple Hand and Arm exercises to help bloodful across the targeted areas needed for a better grip.

How I built it

Using the Myo SDK for Android I made a series of screens with training/workout animations and detecting hand/arm motion in real-time.

What's next for reHab+

Using the advice of health professionals develop a fully-fledged set of training routines that can actually be used by patients to have them rehabilitate their hand and arm areas.
