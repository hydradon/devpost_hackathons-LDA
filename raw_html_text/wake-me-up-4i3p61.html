There’s something about donning a university hoodie that, every morning, turns us students into the conquerors of the snooze button. So, to alleviate this epidemic of missed lectures, we’ve created SnoozeMeNot.

SnoozeMeNot is an alarm clock up that won’t shut up until you scan a QR code that you registered with the app. The app works with a vast majority of QR codes (including barcodes), so you don’t even need to print any codes – just use the barcode found on everyday objects like toothpaste.
