Inspiration: Sleeping through my alarms. We realized that an innovative alarm would help us wake up better.

How it works: You set an alarm and the alarm will not shut until you answer five language questions correctly. This forces you to work your brain early in the morning and wakes you up. It also teaches you a language to boot!

Challenges I ran into: Integrating the quiz and alarm together

Accomplishments that I'm proud of: This is my first hackathon and app and I am proud that I could create a workable version of my app

What I learned: I learnt about programming android apps

What's next for LangApp : More Languages, a larger database, better interface, store alarm times so you do not need to enter

them repeatedly.
