What's next for ReportAmigo

We're working on a Machine Learning recommendation engine that leverages innovative proprietary models to give business operators and advisors alike automated financial recommendations to better run their businesses. The model learns and gets better over time. Kind of like Pandora for financials: you create the station ("pick a model, provide a couple very easy parameters"), then Amigo will give you "songs" (recommendations over time), teams can give Amigo feedback (thumbs up/down type experience), and he gets better over time.

We'll also likely continue to add more features around forecasting (using budget / longer term, different sorting), and enhance other features and reports too.
