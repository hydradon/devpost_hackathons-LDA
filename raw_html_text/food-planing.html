Inspiration

We wanted to create a useful and transversal application for all the people. Food is one of the most important issues of humanity. We think that there not exists in the app market a complete food planner, that goes from creating a suitable food schedule to giving the best tools to buy it.

What it does

It's an app for food planing, it prepares all your week meals based on your preferences and your available time for cooking, makes the shopping list and shows the nearest supermarkets or you can make an order via Glovo app.

How I built it

We build it using Android Studio
