Our inspiration is to cat owners become closer with their cats.

User speaks into phone then phone can output either pig latin or a pig latin/cat meowing hybrid.

We build it with Android Studios.

Challenges we ran into was splicing the meowing with the user's input.

We are proud of how much we were able to accomplish in such little time.

We learned how to successfully convert speech to text and vice versa.

SpeechToMeow will work towards making it compatible with more pets.
