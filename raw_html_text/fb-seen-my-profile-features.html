Accessing Your Popularity on Facebook via Facebook Seen My Profile Features.

Inspiration and What It Does

Many Facebook Users has been trying to know how Popular they are via accessing who view their Profiles. This application is an answer to that. It Lets FB Users to know who view their Profiles, time it was viewed,number of times it was accessed by a Particular User.

This app is powered by Seen My Profile Calendar that allow FB Users to know who view their Profiles, Time it was viewed and number of time it was accessed by a particular User on Facebook.

The application also has a Statistical Graphs Representations that shows who seen your profile by day, week, month and year.

This application is very interesting as it will tell FB users how popular they are so that they can catch-up with other FB Members.With this Popularity in mind, FB Users will now able to promote personal status updates, which can trigger more Facebook Ads to generate more revenue, This feature will tempt users to pay and promote their posts so that more people will see them thus growing Facebook community going forward.

How to use this app:

1.) Login using Facebook
2.) Once You Login, Your Facebook data will appear among the list. Click on View Profile of any user to see content
3.) Statistical graph : will show you statistical counts of number of times your Facebook Profiles was viewed in a day, weeks, months and a year.
4.) Seen My Profile Calendar: will show you Facebook data of who view your profiles like FB names, pictures,number of times he views it in a day and time it was view .

Facebook Technology Used:

1.) Facebook Login Graph API: Used to authenticate Users
2.) Facebook Shared Button: used to allow user to share content to her Facebook Timeline
3.) Facebook Send Dialog Button: used to allow user to content to a specific Facebook Friends. in HIPAA Compliance Mode.
