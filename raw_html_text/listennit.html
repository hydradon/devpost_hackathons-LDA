Inspiration

We wanted to be able to listen to subreddits all day, simplifying the process by taking the songs all into one playlist. The idea is to be able to listen continuously, whether when you're working, relaxing, or trying to get into the creative zone.

What it does

All in one intuitive page, you search for subreddits and then you start listening. There's no log-in or hassling processes.
