Inspiration

What it does

Our program will take in a route you plan on taking, and find artists that are performing around that route. This helps you find any local artists during your trip and big named ones in town.

How I built it

The program was built using a django backend and integrated with google-maps, spotify, and ticketmaster's api.

Challenges I ran into

Reading through all of the various API's documentation and get authentication working was challenging.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

The overall project is very complete and am proud of my first finished hackathon project!

What I learned

I leared a lot more about authentication and interacting with API's I also got to work with google maps and interacting with data that maps provides.
