Inspiration

Ever leave the house on a rainy day when you forgot to bring your umbrella

What it does

Alerts the user when it going to rain that day by beeping. Displays realtime weather data on the umbrella. Collects temperature and GPS for data analytics.

How I built it

Stitched a PYNQ Xilinx board to and umbrella.

Challenges I ran into

There were some challenges with initially getting the Xilinx board set up.
