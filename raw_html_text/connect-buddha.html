Inspiration

You can't trust public connections with your secure information like your credit card info, personal information, and passwords; who knows who's listening?

What it does

We built a way for anyone to use public wifi with a secure connection through a Raspberry Pi and OpenVPN

Challenges I ran into

Getting the Pi to function as a router. Spinning up a web server locally on the pi. Figuring out how to connect the server to the Pi's operating system.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

All of the above!
