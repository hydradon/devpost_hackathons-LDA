Inspiration

We're on a mission to have everyone love their work life. There are 3 billion meetings per year in the U.S. alone, and most of the time spent in them is frustrating and wasteful. We're going to change that.

What it does

Worklife helps teams have highly effective meetings. It streamlines the process - before (create/share agendas), during (real-time collaborative note-taking) and after (shared notes and action items) the meeting. Then, we help ensure you close the loop from one meeting to the next so nothing slips through the cracks.

How I built it

Node.js and Angular.js

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Creating a beautiful, dynamic document that's special purpose built for meeting agendas, notes and action items with real-time collaboration.
