The DayToDay apps allows you to easily create day to day low-key short-term events and invite close friends and family to join you. The Category tab of the app allows gives you a pre-selected set of common event categories to choose from After Choosing an event category you can then invite enter the details of the event you are creating

The “My Events" tab, allows you to see all current events you created

and under the “Invites" tab, you can see all events your friends invited you to

Enjoy connecting with friends day to day
