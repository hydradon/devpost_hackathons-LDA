Inspiration

To help Action Emploi Réfugiés (AERé) resolve obstacle on information translation issue of refugees. It changes the way that people used to donate, we donate money, clothes, food. Now you can donate your language knowledge, to help refugee to get better understanding of job information that post in French/English.

What it does

We build a crowdsourcing translation platform and job information crawler. The job information crawler get us information on Action Emploi Réfugiés job posting site, and our users can translate those job information from French/English to Arabic/Albanian etc. They will get points which can used to redeem rewards. And we provide API to Action Emploi Réfugiés, so that they can use the translation in their work to help refugees.

How I built it

node.js, express.js, MongoDB, Scrapy, Python.
