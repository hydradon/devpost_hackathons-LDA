Inspiration

I got the idea to write this article while trying to open my first channels, and struggling to decide with who and how much capacity to commit to each channel.

What it does

Educate people about the current solutions they have for liquidity problems they could face using the Lightning Network today, the main ones being Lightning Loop and LndHub.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

It is my first article about any topic ever, and I'm very proud that it is about such an interesting and innovative topic.

What I learned

I learned that it is very tricky to write an article with you own thought without rewriting what other have written before. In fact, I originally wrote a piece about the Liquidity problem in the Lightning Network, and realized afterwards that it was a close copy of this article I had read a week ago : https://blog.muun.com/the-inbound-capacity-problem-in-the-lightning-network/ That's when I decided to do the second part, which I submit here!
