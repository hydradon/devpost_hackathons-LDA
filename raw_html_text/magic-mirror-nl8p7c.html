Magic Mirror

UCR Rose Hack 2020-Use computer vision to find the perfect makeup products for you!

No matter what kind of skin tone or type you may have, we believe that you can find the right cosmetic products for you through the use of our web-based app! We use a Google Cloud-based online database to help you find the exact foundation to complement your natural beauty. This app can also be used on your browser without the hassle of downloading and installing another application on your phone!
