Inspiration

To better train the residence and fellows for minimally invasive image-guided procedures.

What it does

It helps to perform and learn minimally invasive image-guided procedures without compromising patient safety.

How we built it

We used Unity3D editor and Windows Mixed Reality.

Challenges we ran into

3D designs and 2D transformation.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We have a working training MR tutorial for residence.

What we learned

This is hard, and doable, and takes some experience programmers.

We learned a lot.

What's next for Interventional Virtual Radiology

We will do touch up and refine until have a fully functional prototype and present it in the Society of Interventional Radiology.
