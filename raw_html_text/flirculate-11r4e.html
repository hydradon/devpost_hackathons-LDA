Inspiration:

Prohibitively high cost of blood sugar testing, combined with knowledge that thermal imaging is a viable alternative.

How it works:

Compare fasting & post-prandial circulation in hand to detect abnormal blood flow.

Challenges I ran into

Compatibility issues with Intel devices.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Functional prototype with statistically significant results.

What I learned

FLIR's power in providing an alternative view of the world.

What's next for FLIRculate

Incorporate user information (BMI, height, weight, age, etc) to tailor results.
