Inspiration

Solar energy, recently, has been on the rise in terms of fast development in Vietnam, according to the Ministry of Industry and Commerce. Not only does it make use of green energy, but it also helps residents take control of their power bills.

What it does

The web app serves as a marketplace where solar module companies can publish and advertise their products. Meanwhile, customers can have a variety of selection as well as a preview of their investment roadmap including payback period. The mobile app is there for end users to monitor their investment, power production & consumption so far.
