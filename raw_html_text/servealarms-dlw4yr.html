Inspiration: The mere happiness from being able to help the elderly.

What it does: You can send an alarm to a loved one, and it will ring at the time you need it to and say a message.

How we built it: Using Android Studios, Firebase.

Challenges we ran into: pulling from firebase, and getting the alarm to ring.

Accomplishments that we're proud of: Getting something pushed to firebase and getting it on another device

What we learned: A ton about alarms and pushing to firebase for android

What's next for ServeAlarms: Getting customized messages and alarm types
