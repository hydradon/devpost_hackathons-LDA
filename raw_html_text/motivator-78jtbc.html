Motivator

Motivator is a reward system between parents and children to foster children's productivity. Parents can assign task for children to do with reward points. Upon finishing the task, children will acquire the reward point. The children and parents can set goals with a corresponding point. When reward points has reached the goal, the children are rewarded with the goal.

Issues

In the beginning, we used Node.js and firebase, but we have issue processing the authentication for Google accounts. After hours of struggle, we decided to re-build our project using flask.

Impact

Many parents face the problems with their children not focusing on finishing a task. Therefore, this website serves to motivate the children to complete their assignments.
