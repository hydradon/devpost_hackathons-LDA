Inspiration

BASEBALL!!!!!!!!!

What it does

This project allows baseball fanatics to mingle by having their first date at a baseball game.

How we built it

We used codepad to have our team contribute snippets of code to build our website. We then transferred our code to the WebStorm IDE to publish our website and link the pages together.

Challenges we ran into

All members of our team had never experienced a hackathon before and since we had time constraints, it was fairly difficult to stick with an idea and produce a decent project

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We were able to make a somewhat functional website!

What we learned

We all gained a bit more insight into making websites using HTML, Javascript, and CSS.

What's next for HomeRun

We shall finish our website and turn it into an app
