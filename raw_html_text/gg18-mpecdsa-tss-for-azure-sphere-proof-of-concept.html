Proof of Concept for the integration of Azure Bot Framework with a distributed wallet where chatbot capable of communicating across all major chat apps like Telegram, Facebook Messenger, Slack, Teams etc becomes you interface to the distributed signer. And on top of that - offline voice control with Snips

Major use cases:

Disributed wallet where you can send funds to social network accounts! Distributed signing of smart contract with deployment - from your favorite chat!

Crypto custody for everyone in the era of secure IoT!

Proof of concept for running Genarro Goldfeder 2018 threshold ECDSA on Azure Sphere based on the implementation by KZen networks

also partially based on bits from Azure Sphere Advanced Samples

and samples by Microsoft
