Inspiration

Our inspiration for this project was how a lot of people do not care about what they do to they environment, and what products they use. We wanted to stop this and help people become more passionate to the environment

What it does

Our website helps lovers find each other on a website that allows them to plant a tree on their first date. As the tree grows, it symbolizes the relationships growth as well.

How I built it

We built it using CSS, JS, and HTML5, programming languages which none of us have had prior experience with. It was super fun to learn them with the help of mentors and peers.

Challenges I ran into

Challenges we ran into was formatting the website to look good, The login system, and getting the system to include the profiles of others,

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We successfully applied a login system, and made the thing look okay. It was also super fun and a good learning expirience

What I learned

I learned that while projects may seem difficult at first, with hard work, anything is possible. We asked Nithilan specifically, and he was very helpful in teaching us on connecting it to google.

What's next for Dating Tree

Improving the website by cleaning it up, and creating an even better login system. We are excited for what the future entails.
