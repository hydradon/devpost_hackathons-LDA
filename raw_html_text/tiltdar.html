Inspiration

Have you ever played a game of League of Legends and wondered how "TILTED" the enemy team was? Well do I have the app for YOOOOOU!

What it does

Tiltdar allows you to determine the TILT level of your team and the enemy team in real time.

How we built it

Tiltdar takes advantage of the Riot-API to gauge a player's TILT from their previous match history.

Challenges we ran into

Firewalls suck and bad documentation is annoying.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

Actually completing this cancerous project.

What we learned

How angry people can actually be while playing League of Legends. Wow.

What's next for Tiltdar

Use more information to more accurately gauge TILT and provide more statistics and data
