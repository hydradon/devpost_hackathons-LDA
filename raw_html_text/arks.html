Inspiration

Our inspiration that water is the basis of life and also preserves the golden drops of waste.

What it does

It is a full system depends on AI to detect leaking water by using a cam (computer vision) and sensors (IoT). It has a web application that gives a full vision to manage the water in homes, hotels… etc.. It has ability to predict the water consume flow using sensors and a prediction model. This system is able to send SMS alerts if there is a certain leaking.

How we built it

Key words of what we used ( Machine Learning and Computer Vision, Google Colabs and Google drive, Laravel, HTML,CSS, Bootstrap, Photoshop, illustrator, Sensors, Webcam, SMS).

Challenges we ran into

Linking between IoT sensors and backend, a small dataset that collected.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud of the work overflow/supports and the smoothness between the team members. Most importantly, we invest our time effort for NEOM lifestyle to save the priceless treasure.

What we learned

How huge are the efforts to save water for a short time, everything is possible.
