TEAM 12 -- A DOZEN GARDNERS

The Seedsonal Planner is an app that notifies the user to plant when their preferred crops are in season. It also serves as a searchable database for gardening tips, growth cycles and other information about different crops. Additionally, Growing Gardens organizers can log in from an admin page and customize notifications to users. This feature will allow them to direct donated resources to specific locations as well as inform the community where to get those surplus zucchinis Oprah is handing out.
