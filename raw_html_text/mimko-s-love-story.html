Inspiration

I was trying to think of what to write, and decided to think of something that would make my friends laugh.

What it does

It's a short Visual Novel meant to either make you cringe, burst out laughing, or just stare in confusion. There are two choice menus and a total of 4 different endings. It'd have best effect if you played this game without viewing the screenshots, and go in blind.

How I built it

I used the Ren'Py engine to code the visual novel. I also used royalty free music and sfx, as well as online pictures to help build the story.

Challenges I ran into

Had to come up with scenarios on the spot because I wasn't really sure what I wanted to do. Also having to familiarize myself with some Python syntax in order to achieve certain effects I wanted in the game.

Also, the file to play the game is too big. Please go to the provided link to download the zip files and extract from there.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

Coming up with an overall silly plot that might make most people think I am crazy. I also came up with some art myself within the tight deadline, although it is not exactly very good quality.

What I learned

The basics of Python as well as familiarized myself with the Ren'Py engine.

What's next for Mimiko's Love Story

I may add more scenarios and memes for more silly fun times. Also I hope to work on a GUI theme for this game to help make it fit its joke theme even better.
