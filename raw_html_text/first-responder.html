Inspiration

 Minimize response time of emergency services to the spot of incident  Average response time is around 10 minutes. In some cases, it is as low as 8 minutes, in other cases can be twelve minutes. Difference of life and death First Responders who are aware of recent accidents in their immediate vicinity may be able to provide on-site assistance at a sooner time than officials responding to the scene Currently, there are not many major mobile networks that allow First responders to keep up to date with all the instances of accidents and situations going on around them.

What it does

 Developed an iPhone app to be used by a pool of users and a pool of service personnel  User could be a victim or a witness  App takes the location of the spot of accident based on input from the victim or a witness  Service personnel who are active and in the vicinity of the accident are sent a notification  Out of the ones who accept to provide service, closest one is chosen to attend(The first responder)

What's next for First-Responder

Initially, it can be deployed in a not so densely populated city. Extend with greater capabilities which include being able to serve a larger user space and a very big network of network personnel. Integrate police departments and Hospitals dispatchers in order to maintain a coherent picture of what’s happening on the ground Smart allocation of personal – based on First responders routines, contact First responders if you know where they are, or if they have a certain skill set, etc.
