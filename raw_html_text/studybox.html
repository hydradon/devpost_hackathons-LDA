Studybox - the tool to achieve your study goals and be never distracted again

What it does

The way the box work is very simple: you put all things into the box, that distracts you. Than you close the box and choose the time you want to stay away from this stuff. When you activate the timer, the times run down and your able to open the box only when the time is at zero again. So when the LD is shining green.

How I built it

The core of our development was the arduino uno. Around this we arranged the tools we need to make our idea work. So we need a display, buttons, two servo motors and a battery pack. We thought about the software and the way we can combined them together.
