How to Use

The first time you enter the app, you will have to enter your phone number so you can be securely logged in. You should receive an SMS which will contain a PIN code. Use that PIN to continue. After this one-time login, you won't have to log in again. On entering the app, you'll see five different "boxes". You may click on any one and enter the chat UI. In the General Box, there will only be conversation when certain conditions are met, such as when it is raining and there happens to be a warm and cozy coffeeshop nearby. The Welcome Box provides a brief introduction to the ap.
