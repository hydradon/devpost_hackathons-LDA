Inspiration : Get acquainted with new techniques with micro-controllers, my first robot.

What it does : It can sense light, sound, temperature, motion sensor, happy or angry (such as good weather, good light it's fun, someone senses it, shows emotion, or hits it ...)

How I built it: Phase-by-step design, micro controller and sensor coupling, linking and linking to Genuino 101 hardware, module testing and component programming, module connectivity and complete programming.

Challenges I ran into : Design lack of equipment, short time, not enough tools, do everything by hand.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of: Know the Arduino and program it, get the micro controller engine, create the first robot.

What I learned: Understand arduino and Arduino programming, microchip and micro controllers, designed the first robot.

What's next for FBOTEVALUATION : Finishing products, evolving decorative products such as human figures or pet, implementing the next steps of the project.
