Cloud-9 Presents

ASSET PROTECTION THRU MIND-CONTROL & BIO-FEEDBACK

Problem: Operator driven assets suffer accidents with loss of life and significant damage due to operator in-attentiveness.

Solution: 

Stimulate operator to pay attention and monitor operator's EEG signals to determine level of concentrations.

Send EEG and Lack of concentration Event data to cloud along with GPS location and vehicle data.

Add weather data in the cloud and save to Time Series and APM.

Provides data for metrics to determine problem areas in operators, vehicles, routes, weather, etc.

Provide feedback to operator to pay attention.

Utilizing: 

Predix Time Series
Predix APM
Edison Board
Grove Display and Sensors
EEG Sensor
