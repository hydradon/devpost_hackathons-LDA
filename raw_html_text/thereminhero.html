ThereminHero

Have you ever wanted to be a superhero who plays a theremin? Well sorry, with this you can't, but at least you get to pretend to play a theremin.

Hook a Raspberry Pi up to your laptop or screen of your choice, and move your hand to varying distances from the photocell to play notes on a C scale.

Controls and Options:

  • ESC to quit
  • up/down arrow keys to change pitch
  • i to change instrument sound
  • m to mute
  • backspace/delete for colorblind mode
