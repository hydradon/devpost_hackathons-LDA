Inspiration

As students and entrepreneurs, we recognized that students are in need of a new and innovative way to change the way students learn. Our purpose and essential goal for BrainWars is to enhance the learning experience by providing a multiplayer platform where students can learn.

What it does

Our web-application allows students to connect you to classmates and friends for a multiplayer battle. This enhances the learning experience by bridging the gap between learning and interesting.

How We built it

We used Meteor.JS to take care of both the back and front end of our web app and hosting it. We also used CSS and Bootstrap to make it aesthetically appealing and intuitive to access

Challenges We ran into

We were relatively new to using meteor. Due to wifi issues we had to find a way of databasing our API calls and making our web app more efficient
