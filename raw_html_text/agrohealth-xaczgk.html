AGROHEALTH CARE CONNECTS (AHCC) MISSION: Brading the gap between farmers, veterinary doctors and government in such a way that the farmers can have access to the veterinary doctors when there is an outbreak of disease/emergencies. "Connecting Veterinary Doctors to their customers (farmers)" As well as bringing the farmers, veterinary doctors closer to the government. Essentially, providing the government with a database of farmers wherein the farmers can get benefits that beckons from the government. The crop and animal detection will be carried out via a detailed and comprehensive database platforms, that will enable the Veterinary doctors to access the info provided. It will also be an easy platform for farmers to use in terms of uploading their basic info and profiles. The farmers will be required to send in signs and symptoms that they noticed. These, in turn, will be uploaded.
