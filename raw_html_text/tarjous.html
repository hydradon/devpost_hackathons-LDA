Inspiration

We often got lost in stores looking for a particular product.

What it does

Tarjous improves customers' shopping experience by showing them the shortest way to their desired products inside a store. The customers just need to search for a particular item or provide a list of items and Tarjous will find the shortest route you can take to buy all your items. Tarjous also makes it easy for customers to spot discounted products.

How I built it

The user search for a product, then using Kesko's API, we get a list of available products in the store. Then using Proximi.io's API, we then place all the products on the map. Based on the user's selection, we provide shortest the route to the product.

Challenges I ran into

How to accurately get user's and product's position.

Accomplishments that I'm proud of

We built a great application within the shortest possible time with limited resources.

What I learned

We learned about the various ways of accurately capturing the indoor location.

What's next for Tarjous
