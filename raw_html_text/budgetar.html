Inspiration

Finances are hard.

What it does

Family Fiduciary maximizes your income and your family time.

How we built it

This project was built using Unity3D and the Magic Leap SDK with future goals of integrating banking APIs and a cloud-based Database.

Challenges we ran into

1) Learning curve involving Magic Leap and Unity environment in beta environment. 2) Hardware and Hardware simulation issues; behaviors that should not happen and whose solution could not be identified 3) Project Management with a brand new team with unique skillsets and time constraints with a new, beta technology.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

You can see your money growing in front of your eyes.

What we learned

We learned about the potential and limitations of AR.

What's next for Family Fiduciary

To integrate with banking APIs and refine our game application
