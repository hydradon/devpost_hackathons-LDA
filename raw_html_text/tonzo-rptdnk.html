Inspiration

The inspiration was drawn from being in an rigourous engineering program, and seeing others having issues but having no one to talk to.

What it does

It's a chat bot that attempts to alleviate your anxiety, depression by allowing a channel in which you are able to communicate your thoughts and feelings.

How we built it

We built the app using the Microsoft Bot Framework's Node.js SDK, intergrated LUIS NLP and CockroachDB. Angular was used for the front end.

Challenges we ran into

We hit some roadblocks trying to use our Python knowledge to write JavaScript. It also took awhile to receive our Azure credits to begin hacking.

Accomplishments that we're proud of

We are proud that we were able to integrate Cockroach DB and get the LUIS NLP working.

What we learned

Focus more on creating the Minimal Viable Product and worry about the details later.

What's next for Tonzo

Scheduling appointments with physicians and recommend locaiton to seek help.
