Inspiration:

We wanted to create awareness for phishing and the importance of phishing awareness/ready-ness as cheap as possible..

What it does:

We created a phishing campaign and phished Hackathon goers.

How we built it:

Open-source tools such as gopish, mail-in-a-box, domain, HTML/CSS development, social engineering, and AWS ec2 t2.micro free tier only.

Challenges we ran into:

HTML, email spoofing, payload distribution, AWS mail/send blockage, and bypassing spam filters.

Accomplishments that we're proud of:

Successfully phished people at the hackathon through social engineering.

What we learned:

How to code in HTML/CSS better, social engineering tactics, AWS.

What's next for Phishing Campaign:

Production.
