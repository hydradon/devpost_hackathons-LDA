5C Party Locator Authors: Georgia Kenderova, Xander Koo, Hugo Ward

Locates parties at the Claremont Colleges.

Users click on map to put a marker down on the map, and can remove them if they click the marker again. Other users that come onto the website can also see those markers. Map is rendered using the Google Maps API, and locations of markers are stored in the cloud using Google's Firebase API.

Markers will stay on the map for an hour (configured to disappear after 30s for demos).

We three are all classmates in CS 51.
