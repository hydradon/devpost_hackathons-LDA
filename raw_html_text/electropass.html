We started the night with a simple project in our minds. We wanted to demo simple RSA encryption encryption, possibly through a simple messaging app, maybe add in a couple of cool easter eggs.

We definitely did not expect to have our minds blown away with OpenBCI . We saw those sleek 3D printed helmets and heard the promise of turning your body’s internal energy into something that can be manipulated in a computer to perform tasks? We were so blown away from this, we knew we HAD to include it in our project in some way.

We rushed to think of a way to possibly combine our two newly found interests into one beautiful beautiful hybrid. WE WOULD MAKE A PHYSICAL ENCRYPTION SYSTEM USING THE OPENBCI AND BASIC PRINCIPLES OF SCIENCE AND ENCRYPTION.

This is revolutionary.

No longer must you "type" passwords, now you can simply think them. Don't want a plain-text password? Try an emotion or a wave of your hand!!! It all comes easy with ElectroPass

Sit back, relax, and let us tell you the amazing story of how ElectroPass will change your workplace, your home, and the future.
