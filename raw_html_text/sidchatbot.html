Inspiration

Nowadays, we can not reject that chat is one big part in our daily life. We use chat for work, for education, for appointment or event for shopping.

Let's focus on shopping, when we desire to buy something, most of the time we always ask a simple question like shown below:

From the example we found that it take a long response time. Therefore the merchant might lost their customer.

So we introduce a chatbot that response to the customer as soon as possible with useful information.

What it does

Our chatbot will ask for the basic information from customer for example, product category, brand, model or customer's budget. After gathering enough information, chatbot will recommend product best product based on customer's intput.
